Graters For Herbalism

Graters are kitchen tools used to shred or grate food items into fine particles.

In herbalism and home apothecaries, graters are essential for processing dried herbs, roots, and other botanical materials into smaller, more uniform pieces.

This helps in increasing the surface area of the ingredients, making them more effective for infusion, tincturing, or making salves.

Graters also aid in creating consistent textures for herbal preparations, ensuring better potency and ease of use.
