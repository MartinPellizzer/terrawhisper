Sesquiterpene lactones have a mild antispasmodic effect that contributes to better blood flow regulation by relaxing smooth muscles lining the walls of blood vessels, reducing resistance to blood flow. Triterpenoids promote blood flow because they contain molecules with a steroidal core structure, which can help lower inflammation and improve the function of the vascular system in carrying blood throughout the body.
