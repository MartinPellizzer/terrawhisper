1. Reduces Inflammation

"Turmeric reduces inflammation" refers to its ability to lessen swelling, pain, and redness caused by irritated tissue, largely due to curcumin content.

The following list includes the main medicinal constituents that give Curcuma longa this benefit.

  • Curcumin
  • Demethoxycurcumin
  • Bisdemethoxycurcumin
  • Turmerone
  • Ar-Turmerone
  • Calechin
  • Quercetin

Curcumin is a well-known anti-inflammatory substance, part of the turmeric root that helps reduce inflammation, hence providing health benefits. Demethoxycurcumin and bisdemethoxycurcumin, along with curcumin, belong to the same family of compounds derived from turmeric and have similar effects. The oils turmerone and ar-turmerone are also derivatives with anti-inflammatory properties found in turmeric. Furthermore, catechin-related antioxidants such as catechin (calechin) and quercetin contribute to the overall health benefits of turmeric, including its ability to reduce inflammation.
