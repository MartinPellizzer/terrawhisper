9 best funnels for herbalists

In the world of apothecary, a funnel is a crucial tool used to safely and efficiently transfer liquids and powders between containers.

This product is a long, narrow tube with a wide mouth at one end and a smaller opening at the other, designed to minimize spills and mess. Apothecaries use funnels to prevent contamination and preserve the purity of their ingredients, particularly when handling expensive or rare materials. By using a funnel, apothecaries can accurately measure and pour substances, reducing the risk of errors and waste.

This simple yet essential tool is a staple in every apothecary's kit.
