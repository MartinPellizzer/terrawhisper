11 best mixing bowls for herbalists

Apothecary mixing bowls are specialized containers used by apothecaries to accurately measure and mix medicinal ingredients.

These bowls typically feature graduated markings, allowing apothecaries to precisely measure and combine different substances. The bowls' materials, such as glass or ceramic, are often non-reactive to prevent contamination of the ingredients. Apothecaries use these mixing bowls to prepare remedies, potions, and other medicinal concoctions with precise measurements and proportions.

By using apothecary mixing bowls, apothecaries can ensure the quality and efficacy of their products.
