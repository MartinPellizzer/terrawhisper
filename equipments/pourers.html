9 best pourers for herbalists

Apothecary pourers, also known as measuring pourers, are small devices used to accurately measure and dispense liquids in apothecary settings.

These pourers typically consist of a cylindrical body with graduated markings and a spout or nozzle at the bottom, allowing for precise control over the amount of liquid dispensed. Apothecaries use pourers to ensure accurate dosing and measurement of medications, as well as to minimize waste and contamination. By using pourers, apothecaries can maintain high standards of quality and safety in their work.

This is particularly important in apothecary settings where precise measurements are crucial for effective treatment.
