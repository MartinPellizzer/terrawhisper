13 best scales for herbalists

Apothecary scales are precision instruments used by apothecaries to measure the weight of medicinal substances with high accuracy.

These scales are typically made of brass, copper, or other durable materials and feature a delicate balance system to ensure precise readings. Apothecaries rely on these scales to accurately measure the weight of ingredients in their formulations, ensuring the correct proportions of active and inactive ingredients. This is crucial in creating consistent and effective medicinal preparations.

By using apothecary scales, apothecaries can ensure the quality and efficacy of their products, providing reliable treatments for their patients.
