Airport Aesthetic With Medicinal Herbs

The airport aesthetic with medicinal herbs blends modern design with ancient healing traditions.

Airports now incorporate lush greenery and aromatic plants to create a calming environment for travelers. Medicinal herbs like lavender, chamomile, and eucalyptus are used to soothe stress and improve air quality. These natural elements add a unique visual and sensory experience to terminal spaces. The trend reflects a growing interest in wellness and holistic design in public spaces.

This fusion of function and beauty redefines the modern airport experience.
