Tannins are known for their anti-inflammatory properties that can help regulate the contraction and dilation of blood vessels, which contributes to better blood flow and eventually leads to lower blood pressure. Lastly, saponins also demonstrate cardioprotective effects by acting as natural vasodilators, thus reducing blood pressure. In all, these constituents possess multiple beneficial properties that work together in synergy to promote a healthy heart and maintain optimal blood pressure levels.
