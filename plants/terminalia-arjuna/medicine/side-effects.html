Terminalia arjuna, a plant commonly used in traditional medicine, can have potential negative health effects if used improperly.

First, it can cause digestive issues, such as stomach irritation, nausea, and diarrhea, due to its strong medicinal properties. Second, it may lead to irregular heartbeat and fluctuations in blood pressure, especially if the individual has a pre-existing heart condition.

Third, it can potentially interfere with blood clotting, increasing the risk of bleeding and bruising, particularly in conjunction with other medications that affect blood clotting. Fourth, it may have hormonal effects, disrupting the menstrual cycle in women and testosterone levels in men. Lastly, high doses of Terminalia arjuna can potentially harm the liver and kidneys, leading to damage or failure over time.

Therefore, it is crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen, especially with a potent herb like Terminalia arjuna.
