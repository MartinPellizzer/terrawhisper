Gentiana lutea, also known as bitterwort or yellow gentian, is a popular herbal remedy known for its potential benefits to the digestive system.

However, if used improperly, it can cause several health issues. First, overconsumption can lead to stomach discomfort, nausea, and vomiting due to its bitter properties. Second, it may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and result in bleeding or bruising.

Third, pregnant women or individuals with kidney or liver diseases should avoid it, as it may exacerbate their conditions. Moreover, long-term use may cause skin irritation and photosensitivity, making the skin more sensitive to sunlight. Lastly, large amounts of this plant can be toxic, leading to severe health problems, including heart rhythm abnormalities and seizures.

Therefore, it is crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before using Gentiana lutea for medicinal purposes.
