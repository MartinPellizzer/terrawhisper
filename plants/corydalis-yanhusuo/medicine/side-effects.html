By Martin Pellizzer
0 minutes

Corydalis yanhusuo, a plant commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine, can have several potential negative health effects if used improperly.

Firstly, it may cause digestive issues such as nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain due to its alkaloid content. Secondly, it can lead to skin irritation and rashes when applied topically.

Thirdly, in some cases, it can result in respiratory problems, including difficulty breathing and shortness of breath. Fourthly, overdose can result in hallucinations, seizures, and even coma. Lastly, it can interact with other medications, especially those that affect the heart or blood pressure, leading to potentially dangerous complications.

Therefore, it is crucial to use Corydalis yanhusuo under the supervision of a healthcare professional.
