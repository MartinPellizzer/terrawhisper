Atropa belladonna, also known as deadly nightshade, can have serious harmful effects on the body if used improperly.

Some possible health side effects include dry mouth, blurred vision, rapid heartbeat, and delirium. In severe cases, it can lead to seizures, convulsions, and even coma or death.

Additionally, ingesting large amounts can cause anticholinergic syndrome, a condition characterized by hallucinations, dilated pupils, and loss of balance. Long-term use of Atropa belladonna can also result in liver damage and other serious health problems.

Therefore, it is crucial to handle and use this plant with caution, and it is highly recommended to consult with a medical professional before using it for any medicinal purpose.
