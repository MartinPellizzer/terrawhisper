0 minutes

Paeonia lactiflora, also known as Chinese peony, is a plant that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

However, if used improperly, it can have various negative health effects. First, it can cause digestive issues, including stomach pain, bloating, and diarrhea. Additionally, it may lead to respiratory problems, such as difficulty breathing, coughing, and wheezing.

Prolonged use or high doses can result in hormonal imbalances, which may manifest as irregular menstrual cycles, breast tenderness, and mood changes in women. In severe cases, it can even induce liver toxicity, characterized by jaundice, dark urine, and fatigue. Lastly, it can have damaging effects on the reproductive system, potentially resulting in infertility or decreased libido.

Therefore, it is crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before using Paeonia lactiflora for medicinal purposes.
