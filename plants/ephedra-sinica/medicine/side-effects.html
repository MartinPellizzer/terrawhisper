By Martin Pellizzer
0 minutes

Ephedra sinica, also known as Ma Huang, can have several negative health consequences if used improperly.

First, it can increase heart rate and blood pressure, leading to serious cardiovascular problems. Second, it can cause nervousness, anxiety, and insomnia due to its stimulant effects on the central nervous system.

Third, prolonged use or high doses can lead to seizures and even fatal heart attacks. Fourth, it can cause digestive issues such as nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain.

Lastly, it can interact dangerously with other medications, such as blood pressure drugs, and should be used under the supervision of a healthcare professional.
