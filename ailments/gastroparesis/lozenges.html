10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Gastroparesis

Herbal lozenges are often used as a natural remedy to alleviate symptoms of gastroparesis, a condition characterized by delayed gastric emptying.

These lozenges typically contain a blend of herbs such as ginger, licorice root, and slippery elm, which are believed to support digestion and reduce inflammation in the gastrointestinal tract. While they may help soothe irritation and promote a sense of comfort, they are not a cure for gastroparesis and should not replace medical treatment. Some individuals find relief from nausea and bloating by using these lozenges regularly, though their effectiveness can vary among patients.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before incorporating herbal lozenges into a treatment plan, especially if other medical conditions or medications are involved.
