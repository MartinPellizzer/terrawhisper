10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Acute Bronchitis

Herbal lozenges are commonly used as a natural remedy for acute bronchitis, offering soothing relief for throat irritation and coughing.

These lozenges often contain ingredients such as echinacea, ginger, licorice root, and thyme, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. While they may help alleviate symptoms, they are not a cure for bronchitis and should not replace prescribed medical treatments. It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal lozenges, especially for individuals with allergies or those taking other medications.

Overall, herbal lozenges can be a supportive option for managing mild symptoms associated with acute bronchitis.
