Eye Bags Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Eye bags is a common condition characterized by the appearance of dark circles or puffiness under the eyes, often caused by fatigue, allergies, or fluid retention.

Herbal remedies have become increasingly popular in treating eye bags due to their natural and non-invasive approach, offering several benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving circulation, and promoting relaxation.

Certain herbs like chamomile, lavender, and peppermint are known to calm and soothe the skin around the eyes, while others like ginger, turmeric, and ginkgo biloba help to reduce puffiness and improve lymphatic drainage.

Herbal preparations like eye creams infused with extracts of green tea, rosehip, and alfalfa, as well as herbal teas such as licorice root and dandelion, can be used to treat eye bags, with the former providing antioxidant and moisturizing benefits, and the latter aiding in the removal of toxins and excess fluids.
