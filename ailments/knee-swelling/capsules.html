1. Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Curcuma longa, commonly known as turmeric, is a popular herbal remedy often used in the form of capsules to address knee swelling.

The active compound in curcuma longa, curcumin, possesses potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that may help reduce inflammation and pain associated with conditions like osteoarthritis. When taken as capsules, curcuma longa provides a convenient and standardized dose of curcumin, making it easier for individuals to incorporate into their daily regimen. However, curcumin has low bioavailability, so it is often combined with black pepper extract (piperine) to enhance absorption and effectiveness.

While some studies suggest that curcuma longa capsules may offer relief for knee swelling, they should not replace medical treatment and should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
