10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Chronic Bronchitis

Herbal lozenges are commonly used as a complementary therapy for individuals with chronic bronchitis, offering a natural alternative to conventional medications.

These lozenges typically contain ingredients such as eucalyptus, thyme, licorice root, and ginger, which are known for their anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and soothing properties. They can help alleviate symptoms like coughing, mucus production, and throat irritation by reducing inflammation in the respiratory tract. While they may not cure chronic bronchitis, they can provide symptomatic relief and support overall respiratory health.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal lozenges, especially if you are taking other medications or have underlying health conditions.

6. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.
