10 Best Herbal Mucillages For Hoarse Voice

When used for a hoarse voice, herbal mucillages may help to lubricate the throat, ease coughing, and promote healing of the mucous membranes. They are often used in traditional medicine and can be found in various forms, including teas, lozenges, and topical applications.
