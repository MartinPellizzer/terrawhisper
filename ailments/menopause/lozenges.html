10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Menopause

Herbal lozenges are increasingly popular as a natural remedy for managing menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes, dryness, and mood swings.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like black cohosh, sage, and chamomile, which are traditionally used to support hormonal balance and soothe discomfort. They offer a convenient and discreet way to incorporate herbal support into daily routines, especially for women who prefer non-hormonal treatments. While they may not replace medical advice, they can complement other therapies and provide relief for mild symptoms.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before starting any herbal regimen to ensure safety and effectiveness.
