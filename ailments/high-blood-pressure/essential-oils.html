11 Best Herbal Essential Oils For High Blood Pressure

Herbal essential oils for high blood pressure are specialized plant-based extracts that have been used for centuries to help manage and treat hypertension.

These oils, which include lavender, bergamot, chamomile, frankincense, ylang-ylang, geranium, and cypress, possess various benefits that contribute to their ability to lower blood pressure.

The benefits of using herbal essential oils to treat high blood pressure include their ability to reduce stress and anxiety, improve sleep quality, and promote relaxation, which are all essential factors in managing hypertension.

By incorporating these oils into your daily routine, either through aromatherapy, topical application, or inhalation, you can experience a reduction in blood pressure and an overall improvement in your cardiovascular health.

Related Study

According to "Endocrine, metabolic & immune disorders drug targets", essential oils derived from Moroccan medicinal plants, such as those belonging to the Asteraceae, Lamiaceae, and Apiaceae families, have been found to have antihypertensive properties, operating through mechanisms such as vasorelaxant impact involving signaling cascades or blocking/activating systems like ACE, RAS, or diuretic activity.

1. Valeriana officinalis essential oils

Valeriana officinalis essential oils, also known as valerian root oil, have been traditionally used to treat high blood pressure due to its relaxing and sedative properties, which help to calm the nervous system and reduce stress levels.

The bioactive constituents of valerian root oil, including valerenic acid, isovaleric acid, and valeranone, have been shown to exhibit vasodilatory and antioxidant effects, which contribute to its ability to lower blood pressure.

By reducing the heart rate and promoting relaxation, valerian root oil helps to alleviate the symptoms of high blood pressure, including anxiety and insomnia.

The benefits of using valerian root oil to treat high blood pressure include reduced blood pressure levels, improved sleep quality, and a decrease in the risk of cardiovascular disease.

2. Ginkgo biloba essential oils

Ginkgo biloba essential oils have been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including high blood pressure.

The properties of this herbal preparation, such as its vasodilatory and antioxidant effects, help to improve blood circulation and reduce blood pressure.

The bioactive constituents, including flavonoids and terpenoids, help to relax blood vessels and prevent the formation of blood clots, which can contribute to high blood pressure.

Regular use of Ginkgo biloba essential oils can lead to reduced blood pressure, improved cardiovascular health, and a lower risk of cardiovascular diseases.

Related Study

According to the "Journal of Clinical Pharmacology", Ginkgo biloba essential oils, in the form of extract ingested over a period of 3 months, were found to decrease systolic blood pressure from 125 +/- 15 to 118 +/- 12 mmHg (p < 0.05) and in diastolic blood pressure from 86 +/- 10 to 68 +/- 10 (p < 0.01) in normal glucose-tolerant individuals.
