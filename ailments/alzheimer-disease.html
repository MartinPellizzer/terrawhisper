Alzheimer’S Disease Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that causes the degeneration of brain cells, leading to memory loss, cognitive decline, and a range of other symptoms.

Herbs such as Ginkgo biloba, Bacopa monnieri, and Ashwagandha are commonly used to improve cognitive function, reduce inflammation, and promote brain health.
