10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Pink Eye

Herbal lozenges are often used as a natural remedy for symptoms associated with pink eye, such as irritation and discomfort in the throat, though they are not a direct treatment for the eye infection itself.

These lozenges typically contain ingredients like echinacea, chamomile, or licorice root, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. While they may provide temporary relief for associated symptoms, they do not address the underlying cause of pink eye, which is usually a bacterial or viral infection. It is important to consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment, as pink eye can sometimes require antibiotic or antiviral medication.

Herbal lozenges should be used as a complementary therapy rather than a substitute for medical care.
