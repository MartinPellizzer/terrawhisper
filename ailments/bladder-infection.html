Bladder Infection Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Bladder infection, also known as urinary tract infection (UTI), is a bacterial infection that occurs in the urinary system, specifically in the bladder, causing symptoms such as pain, burning sensation while urinating, and frequent urination.

Herbal remedies have been used for centuries to treat bladder infections due to their antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

Some effective herbs in treating bladder infections include Urva Ursi, which helps to prevent bacterial adhesion to the bladder walls, Juniper berries, which aid in flushing out bacteria from the urinary tract, and Uva Ursi and Corn Silk, which have diuretic properties to increase urine production and flush out bacteria.

Herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures made from these herbs, as well as Echinacea, Buchu, and Saw Palmetto, can be effective in treating bladder infections, with the added benefit of being non-invasive and gentle on the body.
