7 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Hyperthyroidism

Herbal essential oils for Hyperthyroidism are plant-based extracts used to alleviate symptoms associated with an overactive thyroid gland.

These essential oils have been found to provide numerous benefits, including reducing inflammation, promoting relaxation, and stabilizing the thyroid gland, thereby helping to alleviate symptoms such as rapid heartbeat, weight loss, and anxiety.

Some examples of herbal essential oils used to treat hyperthyroidism include Peppermint oil, which helps to calm the mind and body, Bergamot oil, which reduces anxiety and stress, and Frankincense oil, which helps to reduce inflammation and promote relaxation.

Additionally, other essential oils such as Lemon oil, Geranium oil, and Rose oil are also used to treat hyperthyroidism due to their ability to balance hormones, reduce stress, and promote overall well-being.

1. Silybum marianum essential oils

Silybum marianum essential oils have been traditionally used to treat hyperthyroidism, an overactive thyroid gland disorder.

The anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties of this herbal preparation help to reduce thyroid gland inflammation and promote overall thyroid health.

The bioactive constituents of Silybum marianum essential oils, including flavonoids, silymarin, and sesquiterpenes, have been found to inhibit the production of thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) and reduce thyroid hormone levels, thereby helping to treat hyperthyroidism.

Regular use of Silybum marianum essential oils may help to alleviate symptoms of hyperthyroidism, including weight loss, anxiety, and increased heart rate, providing relief and promoting overall thyroid well-being.

2. Lavandula angustifolia essential oils

Lavandula angustifolia essential oils have been found to possess properties that can help alleviate symptoms of hyperthyroidism, including its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.

The herbal preparation helps to treat this ailment by reducing anxiety and stress levels, promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality, which are often disrupted in individuals with hyperthyroidism.

The bioactive constituents of Lavandula angustifolia, including linalool and linalyl acetate, contribute to its therapeutic properties, which help to balance thyroid hormone levels and reduce oxidative stress.

The benefits of using Lavandula angustifolia essential oils to treat hyperthyroidism include improved overall well-being, reduced symptoms of anxiety and insomnia, and a lower risk of complications associated with hyperthyroidism.
