13 Best Herbal Creams For Dental Plaque

Herbal creams for dental plaque are topical treatments that contain natural ingredients, such as herbs and essential oils, to combat the buildup of bacteria and plaque on teeth.

These creams benefit oral health by reducing inflammation, preventing the formation of tartar, and freshening breath, making them a popular alternative to traditional mouthwashes and toothpastes.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat dental plaque include clove oil-based creams, which contain antibacterial properties to kill bacteria and prevent plaque buildup, tea tree oil-based creams, which have antiseptic properties to reduce inflammation and prevent infection, and aloe vera-based creams, which soothe and calm the gums while reducing inflammation.

Additionally, other herbal creams such as eucalyptus, peppermint, and chamomile-based creams are also used to treat dental plaque due to their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help prevent plaque buildup and promote healthy gums.

Related Study

According to "Journal of ethnopharmacology", creams for dental plaque can contain extracts from medicinal plants such as Camellia sinensis, Melaleuca alternifolia oil and Azadirachta indica, which have been shown to reduce plaque and gum inflammation scores in clinical trials.
