10 Best Herbal Lotions For Motion Sickness

Herbal lotions for motion sickness are topical remedies that incorporate natural ingredients believed to alleviate symptoms such as nausea and dizziness.

These lotions often contain calming herbs like ginger, peppermint, and lavender, which are known for their soothing and digestive properties. While they are not a substitute for conventional treatments, they may offer a gentle, non-invasive option for those seeking alternative relief. Application is typically done on the wrists, temples, or neck, where the skin is thin and absorption is more efficient.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using any herbal product, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.

1. Ceylon cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum)

Cinnamomum verum, commonly known as true cinnamon, has been traditionally used in herbal remedies for its calming and soothing properties.

When incorporated into herbal lotions, cinnamon can help alleviate symptoms of motion sickness by promoting relaxation and reducing nausea. These lotions are typically applied topically to the wrists, temples, or neck, where the aromatic compounds can be absorbed through the skin. The essential oils from cinnamon may help regulate the body's response to motion by influencing the central nervous system.

While not a cure, cinnamon-based lotions offer a natural and alternative approach to managing motion sickness symptoms.

While research on its specific efficacy for motion sickness is limited, many users report relief from symptoms when applying the lotion to the wrists or temples.

7. Ginger (Zingiber officinale)

Zingiber officinale, commonly known as ginger, has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of motion sickness due to its anti-emetic properties.
