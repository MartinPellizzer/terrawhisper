10 Best Herbal Decoctions For Oral Thrush

Herbal decoctions for Oral thrush are a type of natural remedy that involves steeping herbs in hot water to create a soothing liquid solution, which can be used to treat the symptoms of oral thrush, a fungal infection that causes white patches and discomfort in the mouth.

These herbal decoctions offer several benefits, including being gentle on the skin, non-toxic, and cost-effective, making them an attractive alternative to conventional medications.

Examples of herbal decoctions that can be used to treat oral thrush include calendula, which has antifungal and anti-inflammatory properties, and myrrh, which has antimicrobial and soothing effects, while licorice root, sage, and tea tree oil also have antifungal properties that can help combat the infection.

Additionally, other herbs such as marshmallow root, slippery elm, and echinacea can be used to soothe and protect the mucous membranes in the mouth, reducing inflammation and promoting healing.
