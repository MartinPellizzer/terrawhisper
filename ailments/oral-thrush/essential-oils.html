9 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Oral Thrush

Herbal essential oils for Oral thrush are a natural and effective treatment option for this fungal infection, which causes white patches and irritation in the mouth.

These essential oils have antifungal, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties, making them ideal for treating oral thrush.

Some examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat oral thrush include Tea Tree Oil (Melaleuca alternifolia), which has potent antifungal properties, Eucalyptus Oil (Eucalyptus globulus), which helps to reduce inflammation and combat bacteria, Oregano Oil (Origanum vulgare), which has strong antifungal and antibacterial properties, and Clove Oil (Syzygium aromaticum), which has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to soothe the affected area.

By using these essential oils, individuals can experience relief from oral thrush symptoms and promote a healthy oral environment.

According to "Frontiers in bioscience (Landmark edition)", essential oils for oral thrush may be effective, as lemon balm and oregano essential oils showed the best anti-Candida activity with minimal inhibitory concentrations below 3.125 mg/mL, potentially offering a natural treatment option.
