10 Best Herbal Lotions For Overactive Bladder

Herbal lotions for overactive bladder are topical treatments that aim to provide relief through the use of natural ingredients known for their soothing and calming properties.

These lotions often contain herbs such as chamomile, calendula, and lavender, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory and muscle-relaxing effects. While they are not a substitute for medical treatment, they can complement conventional therapies by helping to reduce irritation and discomfort. Some users report that applying these lotions to the lower abdomen or pelvic area can alleviate symptoms associated with overactive bladder.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before using any herbal remedies to ensure safety and effectiveness.

These lotions are often applied topically to the lower back and pelvic area, where they are believed to support the musculoskeletal and nervous systems involved in bladder control.
