10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Dry Lips

Herbal lozenges for dry lips are natural remedies designed to soothe and moisturize chapped or cracked lips using plant-based ingredients.

These lozenges often contain soothing herbs like chamomile, calendula, and licorice root, which have anti-inflammatory and hydrating properties. They work by melting on the lips, releasing moisture and creating a protective barrier against further dryness. Unlike traditional lip balms, herbal lozenges may also provide a calming effect, making them ideal for individuals seeking a gentler, more holistic approach to lip care.

Regular use can help improve the overall health and appearance of the lips while reducing irritation and flakiness.

7. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herbal remedy often used in the form of lozenges to address dry lips.
