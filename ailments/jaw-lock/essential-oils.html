10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Jaw Lock

Herbal essential oils have gained popularity for their potential to alleviate symptoms of jaw lock, a condition often associated with temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorders.

Oils such as peppermint, lavender, and eucalyptus are commonly used due to their anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which can help reduce muscle tension and pain. When applied topically, these oils can provide localized relief by stimulating blood circulation and relaxing the jaw muscles. It is important to dilute essential oils with a carrier oil before application to avoid skin irritation.

While they may offer supportive relief, they should not replace professional medical advice or treatment for severe or chronic jaw issues.
