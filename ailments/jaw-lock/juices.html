10 Best Herbal Juices For Jaw Lock

While it is not specifically marketed for jaw lock, some herbal practitioners suggest that its calming effects on the nervous system may indirectly help reduce stress-related tension in the jaw muscles. Herbal juices made from vitex agnus-castus are often consumed to promote overall hormonal health, which can have a positive impact on conditions like bruxism or temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorders. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using vitex for jaw-related issues, as it may interact with certain medications or conditions.

Overall, while vitex may offer supportive benefits, it should be used as part of a holistic approach rather than a standalone treatment for jaw lock.
