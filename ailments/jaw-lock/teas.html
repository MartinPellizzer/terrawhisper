7 Best Herbal Teas For Jaw Lock

Herbal teas for jaw lock, also known as temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorder, refer to a range of plant-based remedies that help alleviate pain and discomfort in the jaw, face, and neck.

These teas are beneficial in treating jaw lock as they promote relaxation, reduce inflammation, and ease muscle tension.

Examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat jaw lock include peppermint tea, which helps relax the jaw muscles and reduce pain, ginger tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and ease discomfort, chamomile tea, which promotes relaxation and calms the mind and body, and lemon balm tea, which has a soothing effect on the muscles and nerves.

Additionally, other herbal teas such as lavender tea, passionflower tea, and valerian root tea can also be used to treat jaw lock due to their calming and relaxing properties that help reduce stress and promote overall well-being.
