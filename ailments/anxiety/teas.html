11 Best Herbal Teas For Anxiety

Herbal teas for anxiety are a type of natural remedy made from plants that have been traditionally used to help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, such as stress, nervousness, and restlessness.

These teas offer numerous benefits in treating anxiety, including promoting relaxation, reducing stress levels, and improving sleep quality.

Some popular examples of herbal teas for anxiety include chamomile, which has a calming effect due to its high content of apigenin, a natural anti-anxiety compound; lavender, which helps regulate the nervous system and promote relaxation; valerian root, which has a sedative effect and can help improve sleep quality; passionflower, which has a calming effect on the nervous system; peppermint, which can help reduce stress and anxiety by promoting digestion and relaxation; and lemon balm, which has a soothing effect and can help reduce anxiety and promote better sleep.

Additionally, other herbal teas such as ashwagandha, ginseng, and rhodiola rosea are also used to treat anxiety due to their adaptogenic properties, which help the body adapt to stress and promote balance.

According to "Pharmacological research", teas for anxiety that have been found to be effective include Silexan, Kava, Ginkgo biloba, and Withania somnifera, as well as Passionflower and Saffron, which showed potential for further study.
