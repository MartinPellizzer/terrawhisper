10 Best Herbal Capsules For Oily Hair

Herbal capsules for oily hair are natural supplements designed to balance sebum production and promote scalp health.

They typically contain ingredients like neem, aloe vera, and green tea, which are known for their antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. These capsules work by detoxifying the scalp and reducing excess oil without stripping the hair of its natural moisture. Regular use can help prevent dandruff and maintain a healthy, balanced scalp environment.

They are a gentle alternative to chemical-based treatments, making them suitable for individuals seeking a natural approach to managing oily hair.
