10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Jaundice

Herbal lozenges for jaundice are traditional remedies that aim to support liver function and promote the natural detoxification process in the body.

These lozenges often contain a blend of herbs such as milk thistle, dandelion root, and turmeric, which are believed to have hepatoprotective properties. While they may help alleviate symptoms associated with jaundice, such as yellowing of the skin and eyes, they should not replace professional medical treatment. It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal lozenges, especially if jaundice is a symptom of a more serious underlying condition.

Overall, herbal lozenges can be a complementary approach, but their effectiveness varies and should be used with caution.
