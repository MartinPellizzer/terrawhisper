10 Best Herbal Juices For Jaundice

Herbal juices have been traditionally used to support liver function and aid in the treatment of jaundice, a condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and eyes due to elevated bilirubin levels.

Common herbs such as dandelion, milk thistle, and burdock root are often recommended for their detoxifying and hepatoprotective properties. These juices are believed to help stimulate bile production, improve digestion, and promote the elimination of toxins from the body. While they may complement conventional medical treatments, they should not replace professional healthcare advice, especially in severe cases.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before incorporating herbal juices into a treatment plan for jaundice to ensure safety and effectiveness.

1. Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa, commonly known as turmeric, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential benefits in treating jaundice, a condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and eyes due to elevated bilirubin levels.

The active compound in turmeric, curcumin, is believed to support liver function and enhance the body's ability to process and excrete bilirubin. Herbal juices made from Curcuma longa are often prepared by combining turmeric with other liver-supporting herbs like milk thistle or dandelion root to maximize their detoxifying effects. These juices are typically consumed on an empty stomach in the morning to promote optimal absorption and metabolic activity.

While some studies suggest curcumin may aid in liver health, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using turmeric-based remedies for jaundice, as they should not replace conventional medical treatment.

Herbal juices made from silybum marianum are often consumed to detoxify the liver and improve bile production, which can help reduce the buildup of bilirubin that causes jaundice.
