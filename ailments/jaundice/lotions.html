10 Best Herbal Lotions For Jaundice

Herbal lotions for jaundice are traditional remedies that utilize natural ingredients believed to support liver function and aid in the detoxification process.

These lotions often contain herbs such as neem, turmeric, and dandelion, which are known for their potential hepatoprotective properties. They are typically applied externally to the skin, especially on the forehead, chest, and abdomen, to help alleviate symptoms associated with jaundice. While some people use these lotions as complementary therapy, it is important to consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Herbal lotions should not replace medical care, especially in cases of severe or persistent jaundice.
