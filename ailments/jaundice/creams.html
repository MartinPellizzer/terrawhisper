10 Best Herbal Creams For Jaundice

Herbal creams for jaundice are traditional remedies that aim to support the body's natural processes in reducing bilirubin levels.

These creams often contain ingredients such as neem, turmeric, and aloe vera, which are believed to have detoxifying and anti-inflammatory properties. They are typically used as complementary therapies alongside conventional medical treatments, especially in cases of mild jaundice. While some studies suggest potential benefits, the efficacy of these creams can vary, and they should not replace professional medical advice.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using any herbal remedies to ensure safety and appropriateness for individual health conditions.
