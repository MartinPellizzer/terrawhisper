7 Best Herbal Creams For Jaundice

Herbal creams for Jaundice are topical preparations made from natural herbs and plants that are applied directly to the skin to help treat and manage jaundice symptoms.

These creams have been shown to provide several benefits, including reducing bilirubin levels, soothing skin irritation, and promoting liver health.

Examples of herbal creams that can be used to treat jaundice include Silymarin cream, which is extracted from the milk thistle plant and helps to protect the liver from damage; Turmeric cream, which contains curcumin and has anti-inflammatory properties; and Dandelion root cream, which stimulates liver function and promotes bile production.

Additionally, other herbal creams such as Ginger, Peppermint, and Calendula have also been used to help alleviate symptoms associated with jaundice, including nausea, vomiting, and skin rashes.

Silybum marianum creams, also known as milk thistle creams, are a natural remedy used to treat jaundice, a condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and eyes due to elevated bilirubin levels.

The bioactive constituents of silymarin, including isosilybin, silydianin, and silychristin, have been found to possess antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which contribute to their hepatoprotective effects.

The benefits of using Silybum marianum creams to treat jaundice include improved liver function, reduced bilirubin levels, and a decrease in the risk of liver damage.
