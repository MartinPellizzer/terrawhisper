10 Best Herbal Creams For Jaundice

Herbal creams for jaundice are traditionally used in various cultures to support the body's natural detoxification processes and alleviate the symptoms associated with jaundice, such as yellowing of the skin and eyes.

These creams often contain natural ingredients like neem, turmeric, sandalwood, and aloe vera, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory and hepatoprotective properties. While some people use these creams as complementary therapy alongside medical treatments, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before relying solely on herbal remedies for jaundice. The effectiveness of herbal creams can vary, and scientific evidence supporting their use for jaundice is limited.

Nonetheless, they are often preferred by individuals seeking natural and holistic approaches to health.

1. Curcuma longa

Herbal creams containing curcuma longa are often used topically to support liver function and may help in the management of jaundice by promoting detoxification and reducing bile stagnation. These creams typically contain curcumin, the active compound in turmeric, which is believed to aid in the regeneration of liver cells and the normalization of bilirubin levels. While some studies suggest that curcumin may have beneficial effects on liver health, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using such creams, especially for individuals with severe or chronic liver conditions.
