10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Jaundice

Herbal tinctures for jaundice are concentrated liquid extracts made from various medicinal plants believed to support liver function and aid in the treatment of jaundice.

Commonly used herbs include milk thistle, dandelion root, and burdock, which are known for their detoxifying and hepatoprotective properties. These tinctures are often used as complementary therapies to conventional treatments, helping to improve bile flow and reduce bilirubin levels in the blood. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal tinctures, as they may interact with medications or have side effects.

Overall, while herbal tinctures may offer some benefits, they should not replace medical advice or treatment for jaundice.
