10 Best Herbal Capsules For Jaundice

Herbal capsules for jaundice are commonly used in traditional medicine systems such as Ayurveda and Chinese medicine to support liver function and promote the elimination of bilirubin from the body.

These capsules often contain a blend of herbs like milk thistle, dandelion, turmeric, and boldo, which are believed to have hepatoprotective properties. They are typically taken as a natural alternative or complement to conventional treatments for conditions like hepatitis or gallstones that may cause jaundice. While some studies suggest these herbs may aid in liver health, their efficacy and safety can vary, and it is important to consult a healthcare provider before use.

As with any supplement, herbal capsules should not replace professional medical advice or treatment for jaundice.
