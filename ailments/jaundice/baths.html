10 Best Herbal Baths For Jaundice

Herbal baths for jaundice involve the use of specific plants known for their detoxifying and liver-supporting properties, such as neem, turmeric, and fenugreek.

These herbs are believed to help reduce bilirubin levels and support the body's natural processes in eliminating toxins. To prepare an herbal bath, the selected herbs are typically boiled in water, and the resulting solution is used for soaking the skin, allowing the beneficial compounds to be absorbed through the skin. This traditional remedy is often recommended as a complementary therapy alongside medical treatments for jaundice.

While herbal baths may offer some supportive benefits, they should not replace professional medical care, especially in cases of severe or persistent jaundice.
