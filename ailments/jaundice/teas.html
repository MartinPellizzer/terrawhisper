10 Best Herbal Teas For Jaundice

Herbal teas have been traditionally used to support liver function and may help in the management of jaundice, a condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and eyes due to elevated bilirubin levels.

Certain herbs like dandelion, milk thistle, and ginger are commonly recommended for their potential detoxifying and hepatoprotective properties. These teas are believed to aid in bile production and liver detoxification, which can help reduce bilirubin buildup. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal teas, especially for individuals with severe jaundice or underlying liver conditions.

While herbal teas may offer supportive benefits, they should not replace conventional medical treatments for jaundice.
