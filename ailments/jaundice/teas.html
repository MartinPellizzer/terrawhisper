10 Best Herbal Teas For Jaundice

Herbal teas have been traditionally used to support liver function and may aid in the treatment of jaundice by promoting bile production and detoxification.

Common herbs used in such teas include dandelion, milk thistle, and ginger, which are believed to have hepatoprotective properties. These teas can help alleviate symptoms by improving the liver's ability to process bilirubin, a pigment responsible for jaundice. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal teas, as they may interact with medications or have contraindications for certain individuals.

While herbal teas can complement medical treatments, they should not replace professional medical care for jaundice.

1. Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa, commonly known as turmeric, has been traditionally used in herbal teas to support liver health and aid in the treatment of jaundice.

The active compound in turmeric, curcumin, possesses potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce bilirubin levels in the blood, a key factor in jaundice. Herbal teas made from curcuma longa are often consumed with black pepper or ginger to enhance absorption and effectiveness. While some studies suggest potential benefits, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using turmeric tea as a treatment for jaundice, especially in severe cases.

Overall, curcuma longa herbal teas may serve as a complementary therapy to support liver function and alleviate symptoms associated with jaundice.

2. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum, also known as milk thistle, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used to support liver health.
