10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Body Odor

Herbal lozenges are natural remedies designed to help manage body odor by promoting oral hygiene and freshening breath.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like peppermint, sage, and licorice root, which have antimicrobial and deodorizing properties. They work by neutralizing odor-causing bacteria in the mouth and throat, reducing the emission of unpleasant smells. Regular use of herbal lozenges can contribute to a more pleasant personal scent and support overall oral health.

However, they are most effective when used in conjunction with good hygiene practices such as regular brushing and hydration.
