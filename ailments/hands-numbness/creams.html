7 Best Herbal Creams For Numbness In Hands

Herbal creams for Numbness in hands are topical preparations made from natural herbs and ingredients that aim to alleviate numbness, tingling, and other symptoms associated with conditions like carpal tunnel syndrome, peripheral neuropathy, and Raynaud's disease.

These creams offer numerous benefits, including improved blood circulation, reduced inflammation, and enhanced nerve function, making them a popular alternative to conventional treatments.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat numbness in hands include capsaicin cream, which contains the active ingredient capsaicin found in chili peppers, to reduce pain and inflammation; St. John's Wort cream, which has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties; aloe vera cream, which soothes and cools the skin; and arnica cream, which reduces swelling and promotes healing.

Additionally, other herbal creams like peppermint, tea tree oil, and ginseng are also used to treat numbness in hands due to their ability to improve circulation, reduce pain, and enhance nerve function.
