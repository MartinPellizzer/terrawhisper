10 Best Herbal Lotions For Numbness In Hands

Herbal lotions for numbness in the hands are natural remedies that aim to improve circulation and reduce tingling or loss of sensation.

These lotions often contain ingredients like ginger, eucalyptus, and peppermint, which are known for their warming and stimulating properties. They work by promoting blood flow to the extremities, which can help alleviate the symptoms of numbness caused by poor circulation or nerve compression. Many people find these lotions to be a soothing and alternative option to pharmaceutical treatments.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare professional before using them, especially if the numbness is a symptom of a more serious underlying condition.

1. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's wort, is a herbal plant that has been traditionally used for its potential therapeutic properties.

While it is well-known for its use in treating mild depression, some studies suggest that it may also have benefits for nerve-related conditions, including numbness in the hands. Hypericum perforatum herbal lotions are formulated with extracts of the plant and are applied topically to the affected areas, aiming to improve circulation and reduce nerve pain. These lotions are often used as a complementary therapy alongside conventional treatments for conditions like neuropathy or carpal tunnel syndrome.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using St. John's wort, as it can interact with certain medications and may have side effects.
