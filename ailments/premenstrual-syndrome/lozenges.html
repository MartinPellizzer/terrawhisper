10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Premenstrual Syndrome

Herbal lozenges have gained popularity as a natural remedy for managing symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS), offering a soothing alternative to conventional medications.

These lozenges often contain a blend of herbs such as chamomile, licorice root, and peppermint, which are believed to have calming and anti-inflammatory properties. They can help alleviate common PMS symptoms like bloating, mood swings, and headaches by promoting relaxation and reducing stress. While they may not cure PMS, they can provide relief for mild symptoms and support overall well-being during the menstrual cycle.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before using herbal lozenges, especially for individuals with allergies or those taking other medications.

1. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus, also known as chaste tree berry, has been traditionally used to support hormonal balance and alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

Herbal lozenges containing Vitex agnus-castus are formulated to provide a convenient and targeted way to consume this adaptogenic herb. These lozenges are often recommended for women experiencing mood swings, breast tenderness, and irritability associated with PMS. Clinical studies suggest that Vitex may help regulate menstrual cycles and reduce the severity of PMS symptoms by influencing hormonal pathways.

As a natural remedy, Vitex agnus-castus lozenges offer a complementary approach to managing PMS, though they should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

2. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale, commonly known as ginger, has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms associated with premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

Studies suggest that ginger can effectively ease PMS symptoms without the side effects often associated with pharmaceutical treatments.

As a result, many women are turning to ginger lozenges as a safe and accessible alternative for managing their premenstrual discomfort.

3. Foeniculum vulgare

Foeniculum vulgare, commonly known as fennel, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential benefits in alleviating symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

Fennel herbal lozenges are often formulated to provide a soothing effect on the respiratory system while also supporting digestive health, which can be affected during the menstrual cycle. Preliminary studies suggest that the essential oils in fennel, such as anethol, may help reduce bloating, nausea, and mood swings associated with PMS. These lozenges are typically made from dried fennel seeds and are designed to be dissolved slowly in the mouth for optimal absorption.

While more research is needed, some women find fennel lozenges to be a natural and gentle alternative to conventional PMS remedies.
