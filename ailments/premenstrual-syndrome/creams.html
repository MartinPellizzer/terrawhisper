10 Best Herbal Creams For Premenstrual Syndrome

Herbal creams for premenstrual syndrome (PMS) are topical treatments that incorporate natural ingredients such as chamomile, lavender, and calendula, which are believed to have calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

These creams are often used to alleviate physical symptoms like bloating, cramps, and skin irritations associated with PMS. While they may not address the hormonal fluctuations that contribute to PMS, they can provide localized relief and promote relaxation. Many women find these creams to be a gentle, non-invasive alternative to conventional medications.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal creams, especially if you have allergies or are taking other medications.

1. Vitex agnus-castus

Herbal creams containing vitex are formulated to deliver essential oils and active compounds directly to the skin, potentially offering localized relief for mood swings, breast tenderness, and bloating associated with PMS. These creams are often favored by individuals seeking natural and holistic approaches to managing menstrual discomfort. While research on their effectiveness is limited, some studies suggest that vitex may help regulate progesterone levels, which can influence PMS symptoms.

As with any herbal remedy, it is advisable to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially for those with underlying health conditions or who are pregnant.
