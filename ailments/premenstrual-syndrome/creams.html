11 Best Herbal Creams For Premenstrual Syndrome

Herbal creams for Premenstrual syndrome are topical creams or ointments made from a combination of herbs, essential oils, and other natural ingredients designed to alleviate the symptoms associated with Premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

These creams work by providing relief from physical and emotional symptoms such as cramps, bloating, mood swings, and fatigue.

Some examples of herbal creams used to treat PMS include Dong quai creams, which help to regulate menstrual cycles, Ginger creams, which reduce inflammation and ease cramps, and Peppermint creams, which ease digestive issues and reduce stress.

Other examples include Wild yam creams, which reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, and Chamomile creams, which soothe and calm the mind and body, making them a popular choice for women experiencing PMS.
