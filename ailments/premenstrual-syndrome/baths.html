10 Best Herbal Baths For Premenstrual Syndrome

Herbal baths for Premenstrual syndrome are a holistic approach to alleviate the symptoms of PMS, which is defined as a group of physical, emotional, and behavioral symptoms that occur in women in the days leading up to their menstrual period.

Some examples of herbal baths used to treat PMS include lavender baths to promote calmness, chamomile baths to soothe irritability, peppermint baths to ease nausea, rose baths to boost mood, and ginger baths to reduce inflammation and cramping.
