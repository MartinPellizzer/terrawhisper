The bioactive constituents of Zingiber officinale capsules, including gingerols and shogaols, have been identified as key players in this therapeutic effect, as they possess potent anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

The benefits of using Zingiber officinale capsules to treat migraines include reduced frequency and severity of attacks, improved quality of life, and a decrease in the reliance on pharmaceutical medications.
