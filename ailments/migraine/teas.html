9 Best Herbal Teas For Migraine

Herbal teas for Migraine are a natural remedy that involves brewing and consuming teas made from various herbs to alleviate migraine symptoms.

These teas are beneficial in treating migraines as they often contain anti-inflammatory and anti-spasmodic properties that help reduce pain and alleviate symptoms.

Examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat migraines include Feverfew, which contains a compound called parthenolide that helps reduce inflammation and pain; Ginger, which has anti-inflammatory properties that can help ease nausea and pain; Lavender, which can help relax the mind and body, reducing stress and anxiety that can trigger migraines; Peppermint, which can help ease nausea and relax the muscles; and Willow Bark, which contains salicin, a compound similar to aspirin that can help reduce pain and inflammation.

By incorporating these herbal teas into their daily routine, individuals can find natural relief from migraine symptoms and reduce their reliance on prescription medications.

According to "Phytotherapy research : PTR", teas for migraine treatment may have some benefits, with limited evidence suggesting that certain herbal teas such as feverfew, butterbur, curcumin, citron, coriander, and chamomile may be effective in reducing migraine symptoms, although further high-quality research is necessary to confirm their efficacy and safety.
