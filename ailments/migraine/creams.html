11 Best Herbal Creams For Migraine

Herbal creams for Migraine are topical preparations that combine natural herbs and essential oils to provide relief from migraine symptoms.

These creams benefit sufferers by providing quick and localized relief from pain, inflammation, and sensitivity to light and sound.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat migraine include feverfew-based creams, which contain parthenolide to inhibit the release of pain-causing chemicals, and ginger creams, which have anti-inflammatory properties to reduce swelling.

Additionally, creams containing lavender oil, peppermint oil, and eucalyptus oil are also used to treat migraines due to their calming, cooling, and decongestant properties, which help to ease tension and reduce pain.

Related Study

According to "Phytotherapy research : PTR", creams for migraine treatment using ingredients like menthol and peppermint oil have shown positive, preliminary findings as an acute treatment.

The benefits of using Foeniculum vulgare creams to treat migraines include reduced frequency and severity of episodes, improved sleep quality, and enhanced overall well-being.
