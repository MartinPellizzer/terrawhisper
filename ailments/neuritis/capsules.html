1. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used for its potential anti-inflammatory and antidepressant properties.

Herbal capsules containing Hypericum perforatum are often used to support nerve health and may help alleviate symptoms associated with neuritis, which is inflammation of the nerves. The active compounds in St. John's Wort, such as hyperforin and hypericin, are believed to contribute to its therapeutic effects by modulating neurotransmitter levels and reducing oxidative stress. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using these capsules, as they can interact with certain medications and may have side effects.

Despite its popularity, more clinical research is needed to fully establish its efficacy and safety for treating neuritis.
