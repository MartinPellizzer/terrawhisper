13 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Nail Fungus

Herbal essential oils for nail fungus are plant-derived extracts used to treat fungal infections affecting the nails, typically caused by overgrowth of microscopic organisms such as dermatophytes, candida, and yeast.

The benefits of using herbal essential oils to treat nail fungus include their natural antifungal properties, which can help to inhibit the growth of fungal organisms and promote healthy nail growth.

Examples of herbal essential oils that have been traditionally used to treat nail fungus include tea tree oil (Melaleuca alternifolia) due to its high content of eucalyptol, which has antifungal properties, lavender oil (Lavandula angustifolia) for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, oregano oil (Origanum vulgare) due to its high content of carvacrol, which has potent antifungal properties, eucalyptus oil (Eucalyptus globulus) for its decongestant and antifungal effects, lemongrass oil (Cymbopogon citratus) due to its antimicrobial and antifungal properties, and neem oil (Azadirachta indica) for its antifungal, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory effects.

By incorporating these herbal essential oils into a treatment plan, individuals can potentially alleviate symptoms of nail fungus and promote healthy nail growth.

According to "BMC complementary and alternative medicine", essential oils for nail fungus, particularly thyme red oil and pine oil, demonstrated good activity against Candida spp., including fluconazole/voriconazole resistant strains, suggesting potential benefits for the treatment of nail fungus.
