13 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Heart Pain

Herbal essential oils for heart pain are plant-based extracts that have been used for centuries to alleviate symptoms of heart-related discomfort, such as angina, palpitations, and anxiety.

These oils offer numerous benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving circulation, and promoting relaxation, which can help to alleviate the emotional and physical symptoms associated with heart pain.

Examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat heart pain include lavender oil, which calms the mind and body; bergamot oil, which reduces anxiety and stress; frankincense oil, which reduces inflammation and promotes relaxation; geranium oil, which balances the body's energy and reduces emotional stress; and ylang-ylang oil, which promotes feelings of calmness and relaxation.

By using these oils, individuals can experience relief from heart pain and improve their overall cardiovascular health.

Related Study

According to "Applied nursing research : ANR", essential oils, specifically lavender aromatherapy, can reduce pain after coronary artery bypass graft surgery (CABG) by decreasing the mean pain score and the need for analgesic intake.
