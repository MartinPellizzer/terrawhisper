9 Best Herbal Creams For Peptic Ulcers

Herbal creams for peptic ulcers are topical treatments that utilize natural ingredients to heal and soothe digestive issues associated with peptic ulcers.

These creams are beneficial in treating peptic ulcers as they reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and provide relief from pain and discomfort.

Examples of herbal creams for peptic ulcers include aloe vera, which is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, calendula, which accelerates wound healing, and slippery elm, which soothes the digestive tract.

Additionally, other herbal creams such as chamomile, licorice root, and marshmallow root can also be used to treat peptic ulcers due to their anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.
