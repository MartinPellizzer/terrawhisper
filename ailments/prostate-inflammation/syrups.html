1. Pygeum africanum

Pygeum africanum, also known as the African plum tree, is a traditional herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to support prostate health.

Its active compounds, including steroidal saponins and triterpene acids, are believed to reduce inflammation and improve urinary function in men with prostate issues. Herbal syrups made from Pygeum africanum are often used as a natural alternative to conventional treatments for benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH). These syrups work by inhibiting the enzyme 5-alpha-reductase, which helps lower dihydrotestosterone levels and reduce prostate enlargement.

Clinical studies suggest that regular use of Pygeum africanum syrup may alleviate symptoms of prostate inflammation and improve overall urinary health.
