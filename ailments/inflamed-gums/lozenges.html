10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Inflamed Gums

Herbal lozenges are natural remedies designed to soothe and reduce inflammation in the gums, often containing ingredients like echinacea, sage, or licorice root, which have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

These lozenges work by delivering soothing compounds directly to the affected areas, helping to alleviate pain and discomfort associated with inflamed gums. They are particularly beneficial for individuals seeking alternative or complementary treatments to conventional mouthwashes or medications. Regular use of herbal lozenges can support gum health by reducing bacterial buildup and promoting healing.

However, it is important to consult with a dental professional to ensure they are suitable for specific oral conditions.

1. Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable ingredient in herbal lozenges for inflamed gums.

These lozenges typically contain dried sage leaves that release active compounds such as flavonoids and rosmarinic acid, which help reduce gum inflammation and soothe irritation. The natural astringent properties of sage can also help tighten gum tissue and reduce bleeding, promoting faster healing. When used regularly, sage lozenges may offer a natural alternative to conventional mouthwashes or medicated treatments for mild to moderate gum inflammation.

However, it is advisable to consult a dentist or healthcare professional before using herbal remedies, especially if symptoms persist or worsen.

2. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, is widely used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.

Glycyrrhiza glabra herbal lozenges are formulated to target inflamed gums by reducing swelling and irritation through their natural compounds, such as glycyrrhizin and flavonoids. These lozenges can help alleviate symptoms of gingivitis and other gum-related inflammations by promoting healing and reducing bacterial growth. The soothing effect of licorice root also helps to numb the pain and discomfort associated with gum inflammation.

As a natural alternative to conventional treatments, these lozenges offer a gentle and effective way to support oral health and gum recovery.

When used regularly, cinnamon lozenges may offer a natural alternative to conventional gum treatments.
