9 Best Herbal Teas For Chickenpox

Herbal teas for Chickenpox are a natural remedy that involves brewing herbs and plants to create a soothing, antiviral drink that helps alleviate symptoms associated with the illness.

These herbal teas are beneficial in treating Chickenpox as they possess antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce fever, itching, and the severity of the rash.

Examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat Chickenpox include chamomile tea, which helps calm the skin and reduce inflammation, calendula tea, which has antiseptic properties to prevent infection, and peppermint tea, which helps cool the skin and relieve itching.

Other herbal teas such as echinacea, licorice root, and ginger tea can also be used to boost the immune system, reduce fever, and ease discomfort associated with Chickenpox.
