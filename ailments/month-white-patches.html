White Patches In Month Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

White patches in mouth, also known as oral lichen planus, is a chronic inflammatory condition characterized by the presence of white patches or lesions on the mucous membranes inside the mouth.

Herbal remedies have been used to treat this condition due to their anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and soothing properties, which can help to reduce the discomfort and promote healing.

Examples of herbs used to treat oral lichen planus include aloe vera, which has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce redness and swelling, calendula, which has antiseptic properties that can help to prevent infection, and turmeric, which has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that can help to reduce inflammation and promote healing.

Herbal preparations such as aloe vera gel, calendula cream, and turmeric paste can be applied topically to the affected areas to help to soothe and heal the lesions, while herbal teas such as peppermint and chamomile can be consumed to help to reduce stress and promote relaxation.
