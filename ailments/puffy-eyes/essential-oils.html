11 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Puffy Eyes

Herbal essential oils for puffy eyes are natural remedies derived from plants that help to reduce swelling, inflammation, and discoloration around the eyes.

These oils offer numerous benefits in treating puffy eyes, including reducing puffiness and dark circles, improving circulation, and providing antioxidant properties to combat free radicals.

Examples of herbal essential oils used to treat puffy eyes include peppermint oil, which helps to cool and relax the skin; chamomile oil, which soothes and calms the skin; lavender oil, which promotes relaxation and reduces stress; eucalyptus oil, which has anti-inflammatory properties; and rosemary oil, which improves circulation and reduces puffiness.

Additionally, other herbal essential oils such as geranium, bergamot, and frankincense can also be used to treat puffy eyes due to their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce swelling and promote healing.
