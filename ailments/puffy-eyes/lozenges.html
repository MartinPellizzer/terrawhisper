10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Puffy Eyes

Herbal lozenges for puffy eyes are natural remedies designed to soothe and reduce under-eye swelling by incorporating ingredients like chamomile, licorice root, and green tea, which have anti-inflammatory and calming properties.
