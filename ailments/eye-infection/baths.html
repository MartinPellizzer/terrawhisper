10 Best Herbal Baths For Eye Infection

Herbal baths for eye infection are a traditional and natural remedy used to treat a range of ocular conditions, including conjunctivitis, blepharitis, and keratitis.

These baths, also known as eyewash or ocular irrigation, involve immersing the affected eye in a solution of warm water infused with herbs such as calendula, chamomile, and eucalyptus, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The benefits of herbal baths in treating eye infections include reducing inflammation, soothing discomfort, and promoting healing, making them a popular choice among those seeking a chemical-free and gentle solution.

Examples of herbal baths used to treat eye infections include a solution of cooled green tea and aloe vera to calm irritated eyes, a mixture of peppermint and rosemary to reduce inflammation, and a concoction of echinacea and goldenseal to combat bacterial and viral infections.
