10 Best Herbal Syrups For Upset Stomach

Herbal syrups for upset stomachs are natural remedies that combine soothing herbs with sweeteners to ease digestive discomfort.

Common ingredients include ginger, peppermint, licorice root, and fennel, which are known for their calming effects on the gastrointestinal tract. These syrups are often used to relieve symptoms such as nausea, bloating, and indigestion without the use of harsh chemicals. They are typically easy to consume, making them suitable for children and adults alike.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially for prolonged or severe stomach issues.

2. Foeniculum vulgare

Foeniculum vulgare, commonly known as fennel, is often used in herbal syrups to soothe upset stomachs due to its carminative and antispasmodic properties.

3. Cuminum cyminum

4. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its soothing properties, particularly in addressing upset stomach issues.

The herbal syrup derived from licorice root contains glycyrrhizin, a compound known for its anti-inflammatory and antacid effects, which can help alleviate symptoms such as indigestion, bloating, and heartburn. This syrup works by coating the stomach lining, reducing irritation and promoting a sense of comfort. It is often used as a natural alternative to over-the-counter medications for mild digestive discomfort.

However, long-term use of licorice root syrup should be approached with caution, as excessive consumption may lead to side effects such as hypertension or fluid retention.

Matricaria chamomilla, commonly known as chamomile, is a popular herbal remedy used in the form of syrup to soothe an upset stomach.
