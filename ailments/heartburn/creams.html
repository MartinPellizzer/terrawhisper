7 Best Herbal Creams For Heartburn

Herbal creams for Heartburn are topical ointments or creams that combine natural herbs and ingredients to provide relief from the uncomfortable symptoms of heartburn, such as burning sensations and acid reflux.

These creams benefit individuals by providing a quick and non-invasive solution to heartburn, unlike traditional medications that may have side effects.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat heartburn include Aloe vera gel, which soothes the esophagus and reduces inflammation, Ginger cream, which helps to relax the muscles and reduce acid production, and Slippery elm cream, which forms a protective barrier to prevent acid from irritating the esophagus.

Additionally, other herbal creams like Licorice root, Chamomile, and Fennel are also used due to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to calm the digestive system and reduce heartburn symptoms.

1. Glycyrrhiza glabra creams

Glycyrrhiza glabra creams, derived from the roots of the licorice plant, have been traditionally used to treat heartburn and related gastrointestinal issues.

The anti-inflammatory and soothing properties of these creams help to reduce inflammation and irritation in the esophagus and stomach, alleviating symptoms of heartburn.

The bioactive constituents of Glycyrrhiza glabra, including glycyrrhizin, flavonoids, and saponins, contribute to its therapeutic effects by stabilizing stomach acids and improving digestion.

Regular use of Glycyrrhiza glabra creams can provide relief from heartburn symptoms, promote a healthy gut microbiome, and support overall digestive well-being.

2. Zingiber officinale creams

Zingiber officinale creams, derived from the rhizome of the ginger plant, have been used to treat heartburn due to their anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.

The bioactive constituents, including gingerols and shogaols, help to reduce inflammation in the esophagus and alleviate symptoms of heartburn by relaxing the lower esophageal sphincter.

The anti-acidic properties of these constituents also help to neutralize stomach acid, providing relief from heartburn and acid reflux.

Regular use of Zingiber officinale creams can provide long-term benefits, such as reduced frequency and severity of heartburn episodes, and improved overall digestion.

The bioactive constituents present in Alpinia galanga creams, such as gingerols and shogaols, have been shown to possess gastroprotective and anti-ulcerative properties, which help to prevent damage to the mucosal lining of the esophagus and stomach.

The benefits of using Alpinia galanga creams to treat heartburn include natural relief from symptoms, reduced risk of complications, and minimal side effects, making it a promising alternative to conventional treatments.
