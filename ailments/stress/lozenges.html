Herbal lozenges for stress are natural remedies designed to help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and tension through the use of calming herbs such as lemon balm, valerian root, and chamomile.

Many herbal lozenges are free from synthetic additives, making them a preferred choice for individuals seeking natural wellness solutions.

3. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, is a popular herbal remedy used in the form of lozenges to help manage stress and support respiratory health.

These lozenges contain glycyrrhizin, a compound known for its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties, which can soothe the throat and reduce irritation. The adaptogenic qualities of licorice root may help the body adapt to stress by regulating the hormonal balance and supporting adrenal function. However, long-term use of licorice root lozenges can lead to side effects such as increased blood pressure due to its effect on sodium retention.

As a result, it is recommended to use these lozenges under the guidance of a healthcare professional, especially for individuals with hypertension or other underlying health conditions.
