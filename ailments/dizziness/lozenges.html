10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Dizziness

Herbal lozenges are natural remedies that may help alleviate symptoms of dizziness by promoting relaxation and improving circulation.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like ginger, peppermint, and licorice root, which are known for their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties. They can be particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing dizziness caused by colds, allergies, or inner ear issues. While they are generally safe for most people, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before use, especially for those with chronic health conditions or who are taking other medications.

Overall, herbal lozenges offer a gentle and accessible option for managing dizziness through natural means.

1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba herbal lozenges are commonly used to support cognitive function and improve circulation, which may help alleviate symptoms of dizziness.

These lozenges contain standardized extracts of the ginkgo biloba leaf, known for their ability to enhance blood flow and oxygen delivery to the brain. The active compounds in ginkgo biloba, such as flavonoids and terpenoids, are believed to contribute to its potential benefits in reducing vertigo and other forms of dizziness. While some studies suggest that ginkgo biloba may be beneficial for certain types of dizziness, particularly those related to circulation or aging, individual results can vary.

As with any herbal supplement, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before use, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
