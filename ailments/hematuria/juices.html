10 Best Herbal Juices For Hematuria

Herbal juices have been traditionally used to support kidney health and may offer potential benefits for individuals experiencing hematuria, which is the presence of blood in urine.

Certain herbs, such as nettle, dandelion, and parsley, are believed to promote diuretic effects and help cleanse the urinary tract. These juices can support the body's natural detoxification processes and may reduce inflammation in the urinary system. However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal juices, as they can interact with medications or exacerbate underlying conditions.

While herbal juices may complement conventional treatments, they should not replace medical advice or therapy for hematuria.
