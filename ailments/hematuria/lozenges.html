10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Hematuria

Herbal lozenges have been traditionally used in various cultures to support urinary tract health and potentially alleviate symptoms associated with hematuria, which is the presence of blood in urine.

These lozenges often contain natural ingredients such as cranberry, uva ursi, echinacea, and licorice root, which are believed to have antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties. While some studies suggest that certain herbs may help reduce urinary tract infections that can lead to hematuria, more rigorous clinical research is needed to confirm their efficacy and safety. Herbal lozenges are generally considered safe for short-term use, but individuals should consult with a healthcare provider before using them, especially if they have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.

It is important to note that herbal lozenges should not replace medical treatment for hematuria, which can be a symptom of more serious underlying conditions.
