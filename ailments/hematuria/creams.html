10 Best Herbal Creams For Hematuria

Herbal creams for hematuria are topical treatments that incorporate natural ingredients believed to have anti-inflammatory and healing properties.

These creams are often used as complementary therapies to support conventional medical treatments for blood in the urine. Common herbal ingredients include aloe vera, calendula, and chamomile, which are thought to soothe urinary tract irritation and promote tissue repair. However, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before using these products, as they may interact with other medications or underlying conditions.

While some users report symptom relief, scientific evidence supporting their efficacy for hematuria remains limited.
