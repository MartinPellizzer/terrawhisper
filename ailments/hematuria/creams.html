10 Best Herbal Creams For Hematuria

Herbal creams for hematuria are topical treatments that incorporate natural ingredients believed to have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

These creams are often used as complementary therapy to support kidney health and reduce urinary tract irritation. Common herbs such as calendula, chamomile, and aloe vera are frequently included due to their soothing effects on the skin and mucous membranes. While some studies suggest that these creams may help alleviate symptoms, they are not a substitute for medical treatment of underlying conditions causing hematuria.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal creams, especially if hematuria is persistent or accompanied by other symptoms.
