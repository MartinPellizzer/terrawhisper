10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Hematuria

Herbal essential oils have been explored as complementary therapies for managing hematuria, which is the presence of blood in urine.

Certain essential oils, such as chamomile, calendula, and lavender, are believed to possess anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that may help reduce irritation and infection in the urinary tract. However, it is important to note that essential oils are highly concentrated and should be used with caution, typically diluted in a carrier oil to avoid skin irritation or toxicity. While some anecdotal evidence suggests potential benefits, scientific research on their efficacy for hematuria remains limited, and they should not replace conventional medical treatments.

Always consult a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially for conditions like hematuria that may indicate underlying health issues.

Essential oils derived from Urtica dioica are typically prepared through steam distillation and contain compounds such as alpha-pinene and camphor, which may contribute to its medicinal effects.
