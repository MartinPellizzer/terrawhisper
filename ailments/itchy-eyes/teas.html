9 Best Herbal Teas For Itchy Eyes

Herbal teas for itchy eyes are a natural remedy that refers to teas made from various herbs and plants used to alleviate the discomfort and irritation associated with itchy eyes.

These teas offer several benefits, including reducing inflammation, soothing dryness, and calming the nerves, thereby providing relief from itchy eyes.

Examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat itchy eyes include chamomile tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties, calendula tea, which has antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, and peppermint tea, which cools and soothes the eyes.

Additionally, other herbal teas such as licorice root tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties, and eyebright tea, which is known for its ability to reduce redness and swelling, can also be used to treat itchy eyes.
