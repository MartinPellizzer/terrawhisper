10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Edema

Herbal lozenges are traditional remedies that may offer relief for symptoms associated with edema, particularly when the swelling is related to respiratory or throat conditions.

These lozenges often contain natural ingredients such as echinacea, licorice root, or ginger, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. While they are not a primary treatment for fluid retention caused by heart, kidney, or liver issues, they can help alleviate localized swelling in the throat or mouth. It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal lozenges, especially for chronic or severe edema.

Overall, herbal lozenges can be a complementary approach to managing mild edema symptoms when used appropriately.
