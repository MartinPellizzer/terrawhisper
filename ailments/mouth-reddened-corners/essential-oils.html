10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Reddened Corners In Mouth

Herbal essential oils, such as tea tree oil, lavender oil, and calendula oil, are commonly used to soothe and heal reddened corners of the mouth, also known as angular cheilitis.

These oils possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and moisturizing properties that can help reduce irritation and prevent infection in the affected area. When diluted properly with a carrier oil like coconut or jojoba oil, they can be safely applied to the lips to promote healing and comfort. Regular use of these essential oils may help maintain the skin's natural moisture balance and prevent recurrence of the condition.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially if the condition persists or worsens.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis, commonly known as pot marigold, is a herbal plant widely recognized for its soothing and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular choice for topical applications.

Its essential oil, derived through steam distillation of the flowers, contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and triterpenes that help reduce inflammation and promote healing. When applied to the reddened corners of the mouth, calendula essential oil can alleviate irritation and redness caused by conditions like cheilitis or dryness. However, it is important to dilute the essential oil with a carrier oil to prevent skin irritation, as undiluted essential oils can be too potent.

Regular use of calendula essential oil may help restore the skin's natural barrier and improve the overall condition of the affected area.
