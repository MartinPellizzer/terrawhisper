11 Best Herbal Creams For Constipation

Herbal creams for constipation are topical preparations made from herbs and other natural ingredients that are designed to help alleviate symptoms of constipation by softening stool and improving bowel movements.

These creams offer numerous benefits, including being easy to use, non-invasive, and gentle on the digestive system.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat constipation include aloe vera cream, which soothes and hydrates the anal region, castor oil cream, which stimulates bowel movements, and triphala cream, which helps regulate bowel function.

Other herbal creams, such as peppermint, chamomile, and ginger creams, may also be used to treat constipation due to their ability to relax the muscles, reduce inflammation, and improve digestion.
