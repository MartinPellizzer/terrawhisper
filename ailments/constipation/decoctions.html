By increasing the water content and softening the stool, Glycyrrhiza glabra decoctions help to facilitate easier passage through the digestive system and alleviate constipation symptoms.

Regular use of these decoctions may also help to maintain a healthy gut microbiome and prevent constipation recurrence, making them a beneficial natural remedy for this common gastrointestinal disorder.
