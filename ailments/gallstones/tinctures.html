10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Gallstones

Herbal tinctures for gallstones are concentrated liquid extracts made from various plants believed to support liver and gallbladder function.

Common ingredients include milk thistle, dandelion root, and garlic, which are thought to promote bile production and reduce gallstone formation. These tinctures are often used as complementary therapies alongside conventional treatments, though their effectiveness can vary. They may help alleviate symptoms such as pain and inflammation associated with gallstones.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal tinctures, as they can interact with medications and may not be suitable for everyone.
