10 Best Herbal Lotions For Gallstones

Herbal lotions are topical treatments that may be used as part of a holistic approach to managing gallstones, though they are not a substitute for medical treatment.

These lotions often contain natural ingredients such as milk thistle, dandelion, and ginger, which are believed to support liver and gallbladder function. While some people use herbal lotions to reduce inflammation and promote bile flow, scientific evidence supporting their effectiveness for dissolving gallstones is limited. It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using any herbal remedy, as gallstones can lead to serious complications if left untreated.

Overall, herbal lotions should be viewed as complementary rather than primary treatments for gallstone-related conditions.
