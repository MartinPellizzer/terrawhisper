Its active compound, L-DOPA, is a precursor to dopamine, which can help improve mood and increase libido. Mucuna pruriens herbal tinctures are often used to address low female libido by enhancing hormonal balance and emotional well-being. These tinctures are typically prepared by soaking the seeds in alcohol to extract their bioactive components, making them easy to consume.
