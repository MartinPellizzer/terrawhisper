1. Mucuna pruriens

Mucuna pruriens, also known as the "velvet bean," is a traditional Ayurvedic herb that has been used for centuries to enhance libido and sexual health.

Its active compound, L-dopa, is a precursor to dopamine, which can help improve mood, energy levels, and sexual desire. Herbal tinctures made from Mucuna pruriens are often used by women experiencing a loss of libido due to hormonal imbalances, stress, or menopause. These tinctures are typically taken orally and are believed to support natural hormone production and enhance sexual vitality.

While some studies suggest potential benefits, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before using Mucuna pruriens, as it may interact with certain medications or have side effects.
