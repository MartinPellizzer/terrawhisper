Its seeds contain high levels of L-dopa, a precursor to dopamine, which is known to influence mood and libido. Some herbal lotions formulated with Mucuna pruriens are marketed as natural supplements to help restore or enhance female sexual desire by supporting hormonal balance and emotional well-being.
