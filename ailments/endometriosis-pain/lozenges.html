10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Endometriosis Pain

Herbal lozenges for endometriosis pain are natural remedies that may help alleviate discomfort associated with this chronic condition.

These lozenges offer a convenient and targeted way to deliver curcumin directly to the oral cavity, potentially enhancing its absorption and bioavailability. Some studies suggest that curcumin may help alleviate pelvic pain and reduce the size of endometrial lesions by modulating inflammatory pathways.
