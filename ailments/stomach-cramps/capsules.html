1. Foeniculum vulgare

Foeniculum vulgare, commonly known as fennel, is often used in herbal capsules to help alleviate stomach cramps due to its carminative and antispasmodic properties.

The essential oils in fennel, particularly anethol, are believed to relax gastrointestinal muscles and reduce intestinal spasms, making it effective for digestive discomfort. Herbal capsules containing foeniculum vulgare are convenient and provide a standardized dose of the herb's active compounds. They are typically used for mild to moderate stomach cramps, bloating, and gas, and can be a natural alternative to over-the-counter medications.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially for pregnant women or those with existing medical conditions.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using these capsules, especially if you have a medical condition or are taking other medications.
