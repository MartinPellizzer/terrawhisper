10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Frozen Shoulder

Herbal lozenges can be a natural and soothing option for individuals suffering from frozen shoulder, as they often contain anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving herbs such as willow bark, ginger, and turmeric.

These lozenges work by delivering targeted relief to the throat and chest area, which can help ease the discomfort associated with restricted shoulder movement and pain. While they are not a cure for frozen shoulder, they may complement other treatments like physical therapy and heat therapy by reducing inflammation and promoting overall comfort. Herbal lozenges are generally safe for most people, but it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before use, especially if taking other medications.

Overall, they offer a gentle and accessible way to manage symptoms and support the healing process.
