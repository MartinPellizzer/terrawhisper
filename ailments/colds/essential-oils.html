1. Eucalyptus globulus essential oils

The bioactive constituents, including menthone and bornyl acetate, exhibit expectorant and bronchodilatory effects, making it easier to expel mucus and promote airflow in the lungs.

Regular use of Eucalyptus globulus essential oils has been reported to help alleviate cold symptoms, reduce the duration of illness, and provide relief from coughs, sneezing, and nasal congestion.

By using Echinacea purpurea essential oils, individuals can experience benefits such as reduced severity and duration of cold symptoms, improved immune function, and enhanced overall well-being.
