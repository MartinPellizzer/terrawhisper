9 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Dry Mouth

Herbal essential oils for dry mouth are a natural remedy that involves the use of concentrated plant extracts to alleviate the symptoms of xerostomia, or dry mouth.

These oils have been found to have numerous benefits in treating this ailment, including reducing inflammation, fighting bacteria, and providing a soothing and refreshing sensation.

Certain herbal essential oils, such as peppermint, eucalyptus, and tea tree oil, are particularly effective in treating dry mouth due to their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to combat oral infections and soothe irritated tissues.

Additionally, other oils like myrrh, frankincense, and lavender oil can also be beneficial in promoting saliva production and reducing stress, which can contribute to dry mouth.
