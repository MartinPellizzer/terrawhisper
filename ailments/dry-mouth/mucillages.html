10 Best Herbal Mucillages For Dry Mouth

Herbal mucillages are natural substances derived from certain plants that have a thick, gel-like consistency, making them highly effective for soothing and moisturizing dry mouth.

These mucillages, such as those found in aloe vera, psyllium husk, and marshmallow root, form a protective barrier over the oral mucosa, reducing irritation and promoting hydration. They are often used in herbal remedies and oral care products to alleviate symptoms of dry mouth, also known as xerostomia. The soothing properties of these mucillages can help reduce discomfort and improve the overall sensation of oral moisture.

Due to their gentle and natural composition, herbal mucillages are a popular alternative for individuals seeking non-pharmacological relief for dry mouth.
