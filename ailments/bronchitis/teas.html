13 Best Herbal Teas For Bronchitis

Herbal teas for Bronchitis are a natural remedy made from various plant extracts that help alleviate symptoms associated with the respiratory condition.

These teas are rich in antioxidants, anti-inflammatory compounds, and expectorants that help loosen and clear mucus from the airways, making breathing easier.

Examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat bronchitis include peppermint tea, which helps ease congestion and coughs, ginger tea, which reduces inflammation and soothes the throat, and thyme tea, which possesses antimicrobial properties to combat infections.

Other herbal teas such as eucalyptus, licorice root, and slippery elm can also be used to treat bronchitis due to their decongestant, anti-inflammatory, and soothing properties, respectively.

Related Study

According to "The Korean journal of internal medicine", teas for bronchitis, specifically HL301, which is a combined product of seven medicinal plants, were found to be effective in treating acute bronchitis, as shown by a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial with a higher improvement in bronchitis severity score and other efficacy variables in the HL301 group compared to the placebo group.
