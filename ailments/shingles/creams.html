11 Best Herbal Creams For Shingles

Herbal creams for Shingles are topical ointments made from natural herbs and plants, designed to alleviate the symptoms of shingles, a viral infection caused by the reactivation of the varicella-zoster virus.

These creams have gained popularity due to their potential benefits, which include reducing pain, inflammation, and itching associated with shingles.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat shingles include creams containing capsaicin, which helps to block pain signals, as well as creams infused with aloe vera, tea tree oil, and chamomile, which have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.

Other herbal creams used to treat shingles include those containing St. John's Wort, calendula, and comfrey, which are known for their antiviral, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties that can help to speed up the healing process and reduce the severity of symptoms.
