Jammed Finger Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Jammed finger is a common injury where the finger gets pressed or bent against the bone, causing pain and swelling.

Herbal remedies can be an effective way to treat a jammed finger, offering benefits such as reduced inflammation, pain relief, and accelerated healing.

Certain herbs like arnica, calendula, and ginger have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties that can help alleviate discomfort and promote recovery.

Preparations such as arnica gel, calendula cream, ginger tea, and turmeric oil can be applied topically or ingested to provide relief, while willow bark and meadowsweet can be used to create a warm compress to reduce swelling and ease pain.

What causes jammed finger?

The primary causes of jammed finger are direct blows, falls, or sudden twists that put pressure on the joints or bones of the finger, causing them to become misaligned or stuck.

Secondary causes may include repetitive strain from activities like typing, playing musical instruments, or sports, which can lead to overuse and inflammation.

Individuals with lifestyles that involve frequent heavy lifting, bending, or overhead activities, such as construction workers or athletes, may be more prone to jammed fingers.

Furthermore, people who engage in contact sports like basketball or football may also be at higher risk due to the physical nature of these activities.

What herbs heal jammed finger?

The medicinal herbs used to treat jammed finger are Arnica, St. John's Wort, and Calendula, which have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties that help reduce swelling and alleviate pain.

Arnica, in particular, contains sesquiterpene lactones that inhibit prostaglandin synthesis, while St. John's Wort contains hyperforin that has anti-inflammatory effects.

Calendula, on the other hand, has triterpenoid saponins that promote wound healing and reduce inflammation.

The therapeutic actions of these herbs work synergistically to promote tissue repair, reduce pain and swelling, and facilitate a speedy recovery from jammed finger.

What preparations treat jammed finger?

The herbal preparations used to treat jammed finger are a combination of cooling and anti-inflammatory teas, such as an infusion of a certain plant known for its soothing properties, a warm bath made with the flowers of a specific herb, and a topical poultice of a plant that reduces swelling.

A tea made from the leaves of a plant that promotes blood flow and reduces pain can also be applied externally to the affected area.

Additionally, a certain type of herbal oil or salve can be massaged into the skin to ease stiffness and discomfort.

These herbal preparations can be used individually or in combination to alleviate the symptoms of a jammed finger.
