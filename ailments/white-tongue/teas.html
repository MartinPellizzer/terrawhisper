10 Best Herbal Teas For White Tongue

Herbal teas can be a natural and soothing remedy for a white tongue, which is often caused by poor oral hygiene, dehydration, or fungal infections like thrush.

Teas made from ingredients such as chamomile, green tea, and licorice root have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that can help reduce irritation and combat oral infections. Regular consumption of these teas may help improve overall oral health and promote a healthier tongue appearance. It is important to maintain good oral hygiene practices alongside using herbal teas for best results.

However, if the white tongue persists or is accompanied by other symptoms, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional for further evaluation.

1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential benefits in oral health.

When used in herbal teas, licorice root may help soothe inflammation and reduce irritation in the mouth, which can be beneficial for conditions like white tongue. The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties of licorice root may help combat the bacteria and fungi that contribute to the formation of white tongue. However, it is important to note that licorice root should be used in moderation, as excessive consumption can lead to side effects such as high blood pressure.

While licorice tea may offer some relief for white tongue, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare professional before using it as a treatment.
