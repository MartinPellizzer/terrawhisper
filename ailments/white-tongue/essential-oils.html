11 Best Herbal Essential Oils For White Tongue

Herbal essential oils for White tongue are natural remedies derived from plants that are used to treat the condition of oral candidiasis, characterized by a white, patchy coating on the tongue.

These essential oils have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help to soothe and balance the oral flora, thereby alleviating the symptoms of white tongue.

Some of the most effective herbal essential oils for treating white tongue include tea tree oil, which has potent antifungal properties, peppermint oil, which helps to reduce inflammation and freshen breath, and eucalyptus oil, which has decongestant and antibacterial properties that help to clear the oral cavity of fungal and bacterial infections.

Additionally, other herbal essential oils such as oregano oil, clove oil, and lemongrass oil may also be used to treat white tongue due to their antifungal and antimicrobial properties.
