1. Ginkgo biloba essential oils

The bioactive constituents of ginkgo biloba essential oils, such as bilobalide and ginkgolides, have been shown to inhibit the formation of beta-amyloid plaques, a hallmark of Alzheimer's disease.

The benefits of using ginkgo biloba essential oils to treat Alzheimer's disease include improved memory and cognitive function, reduced risk of dementia, and enhanced overall brain health.
