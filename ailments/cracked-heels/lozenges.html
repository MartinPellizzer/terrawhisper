10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Cracked Heels

Herbal lozenges are not typically used for cracked heels, as they are designed for sore throats and oral discomfort rather than skin conditions.

However, some herbal formulations may contain ingredients that can be beneficial for dry or cracked skin when applied topically. Ingredients like aloe vera, calendula, and chamomile are often found in herbal products and can help soothe and moisturize the skin. For cracked heels, it is more effective to use specialized foot creams or ointments containing urea or lactic acid.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional for proper treatment and to ensure safe and effective care for severe cases of cracked heels.
