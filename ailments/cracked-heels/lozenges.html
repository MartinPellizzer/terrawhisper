10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Cracked Heels

Herbal lozenges are not typically used for cracked heels, as they are designed for sore throats and oral discomfort rather than skin conditions.

However, some herbal formulations may contain ingredients like calendula, aloe vera, or chamomile, which can have soothing and healing properties for minor skin irritations. These ingredients can help reduce inflammation and promote skin regeneration when applied topically. For cracked heels, it is more effective to use moisturizing foot creams or ointments containing ingredients like urea or lactic acid.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional for proper treatment of severe or persistent cracked heels.
