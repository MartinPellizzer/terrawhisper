1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis herbal tinctures are made by soaking the dried petals of the calendula flower in alcohol, creating a potent and soothing remedy.

These tinctures are known for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making them effective for treating cracked heels by promoting skin healing and preventing infection. When applied topically, calendula tinctures can help soften and moisturize dry, cracked skin, reducing discomfort and encouraging the skin to regenerate. They are often used as a natural alternative to commercial foot creams, especially for those preferring herbal treatments.

However, it is important to dilute the tincture properly before application to avoid skin irritation and ensure safe use.
