10 Best Herbal Baths For Eye Irritation

Herbal baths for eye irritation are a natural remedy that involves soaking the eyes in a solution of herbs and water to alleviate redness, itchiness, and discomfort associated with eye irritation.

These baths offer several benefits, including reducing inflammation, soothing the eyes, and promoting healing.

Some popular herbal baths used to treat eye irritation include aloe vera, chamomile, calendula, and green tea, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
