11 Best Herbal Tinctures For Cough

Herbal tinctures for Cough are liquid extracts of herbs that are used to treat coughs and respiratory issues.

These tinctures offer a natural and effective alternative to pharmaceuticals, providing relief from coughs, sore throats, and congestion.

Some popular herbal tinctures for cough include Thyme, known for its antimicrobial properties, Echinacea, which boosts the immune system, and Ginger, which reduces inflammation and soothes the throat.

Additionally, other beneficial tinctures include Slippery Elm, Licorice Root, and Wild Indigo, which help to calm the mucous membranes, reduce coughing, and fight infections.
