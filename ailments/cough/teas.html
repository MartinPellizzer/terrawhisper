9 Best Herbal Teas For Cough

Herbal teas for Cough are a natural remedy made from various herbs, plants, and flowers that are used to soothe and calm the symptoms of a cough.

These teas are rich in antioxidants, anti-inflammatory properties, and expectorants, which help to loosen and clear mucus from the lungs, making it an effective treatment for coughs caused by colds, flu, and other respiratory issues.

Some examples of herbal teas that can be used to treat a cough include peppermint tea, which helps to ease congestion and soothe the throat, ginger tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties that help to reduce inflammation and ease coughing, chamomile tea, which calms the nervous system and helps to reduce anxiety, eucalyptus tea, which has decongestant properties that help to loosen mucus, thyme tea, which has antimicrobial properties that help to fight off infections, and licorice root tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties that help to soothe the throat and reduce coughing.

Overall, herbal teas offer a natural and gentle way to treat a cough, without the harsh side effects of over-the-counter medications.
