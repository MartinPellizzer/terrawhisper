10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Hot Flashes

Herbal lozenges have gained popularity as a natural remedy for managing hot flashes, particularly among women undergoing menopause.

These lozenges often contain ingredients such as black cohosh, sage, and red clover, which are believed to have estrogen-like properties that may help regulate body temperature and reduce the frequency and intensity of hot flashes. Unlike pharmaceutical treatments, herbal lozenges are generally considered safe with fewer side effects, making them an appealing option for many. However, their effectiveness can vary, and it is important to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially if taking other medications.

Overall, herbal lozenges offer a gentle, alternative approach to alleviating the discomfort of hot flashes.

1. Chaste tree (Vitex agnus-castus)

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chaste tree, has been traditionally used to support hormonal balance and may help alleviate symptoms of menopause, including hot flashes.

Herbal lozenges containing Vitex agnus-castus are designed to be convenient and easy to incorporate into daily routines, offering a natural alternative to conventional treatments. These lozenges typically contain standardized extracts of the herb to ensure consistent potency and efficacy. Some studies suggest that Vitex may influence the hypothalamus-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis, potentially reducing the frequency and intensity of hot flashes.

As with any herbal supplement, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare provider before use, especially for individuals with existing health conditions or those taking other medications.
