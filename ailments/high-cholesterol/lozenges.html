10 Best Herbal Lozenges For High Cholesterol

Herbal lozenges are natural supplements that may support cardiovascular health by helping to manage high cholesterol levels.

These lozenges often contain plant-based ingredients such as garlic, ginger, green tea extract, and fenugreek, which are known for their potential cholesterol-lowering properties. While they are generally considered safe, their effectiveness can vary, and they should not replace prescribed medications without consulting a healthcare professional. Some studies suggest that certain herbal compounds may help reduce low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol, often referred to as "bad" cholesterol.

As with any supplement, it is important to read labels carefully and be aware of possible interactions with other medications.
