Psoriasis Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Psoriasis is a chronic skin condition characterized by red, itchy, and scaly patches, resulting from an overactive immune system.

Herbal remedies have gained popularity in treating psoriasis due to their potential to reduce inflammation, promote skin healing, and alleviate symptoms.

Herbs such as Aloe vera, Chamomile, and Calendula are often used to soothe and calm the skin, while Turmeric, Ginger, and Willow bark are valued for their anti-inflammatory properties.

Herbal preparations like ointments, creams, and teas made from herbs like Echinacea, Licorice root, and Silymarin can help to reduce the severity of psoriasis symptoms and promote overall skin health.
