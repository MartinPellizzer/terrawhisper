10 Best Herbal Capsules For Amenorrhea

Herbal capsules for amenorrhea are commonly used as natural remedies to address irregular or absent menstrual cycles, often containing herbs such as chastity berry, ginger, and black cohosh.

1. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chasteberry, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used to support hormonal balance and is often recommended for women experiencing amenorrhea, which is the absence of menstrual periods.

The active compounds in vitex, such as flavonoids and iridoids, are believed to influence the pituitary gland and regulate the production of luteinizing hormone, which can help restore regular menstrual cycles. Clinical studies suggest that vitex may be effective in improving menstrual regularity, particularly in cases where amenorrhea is related to hormonal imbalances or stress. Herbal capsules containing standardized vitex extract are available and are typically taken as a supplement, with dosages ranging from 40 to 100 mg per day, depending on the formulation and individual needs.

As with any herbal remedy, it is advisable to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially for women with underlying health conditions or those taking other medications.
