10 Best Herbal Lotions For Cystitis

Herbal lotions for cystitis are natural topical treatments that may help alleviate the discomfort associated with bladder inflammation.

These lotions often contain anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial herbs such as calendula, chamomile, and echinacea, which are believed to soothe irritation and reduce infection risk. While they are not a substitute for medical treatment, they can complement conventional therapies by providing localized relief. Some herbal lotions are designed for use on the lower abdomen or genital area to target the affected region.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before using any herbal remedy to ensure safety and appropriateness for individual health conditions.
