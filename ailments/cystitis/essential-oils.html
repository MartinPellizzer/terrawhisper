10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Cystitis

Herbal essential oils, such as tea tree, lavender, and eucalyptus, have been traditionally used for their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which may offer potential benefits for individuals experiencing cystitis.

These oils can be diluted and applied topically or used in steam inhalation to help reduce bladder irritation and discomfort. However, it is important to note that essential oils should not replace conventional medical treatment for cystitis and should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional. Due to their potency, improper use can lead to skin irritation or allergic reactions, so proper dilution and application methods are crucial.

Overall, while some essential oils may provide supportive relief, they are not a substitute for medical care in treating urinary tract infections.
