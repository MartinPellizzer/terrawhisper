These herbal formulations are often preferred by individuals seeking alternatives to conventional antibiotics due to their potential for fewer side effects. However, it is important to consult a healthcare provider before using them, as they may interact with other medications or have contraindications for certain health conditions.

While some studies suggest they may provide relief, their effectiveness can vary, and they should not replace professional medical advice or treatment.
