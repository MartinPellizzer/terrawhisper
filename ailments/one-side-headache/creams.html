7 Best Herbal Creams For One Side Headache

Herbal creams for one side headache are topical treatments made from natural herbs and plants that aim to alleviate pain and discomfort caused by migraines or tension headaches that occur on one side of the head.

These herbal creams have numerous benefits, including natural pain relief, reduced inflammation, and improved blood circulation.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat one side headaches include Feverfew cream, which contains the herb Feverfew that has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties; Peppermint oil cream, which helps to relax muscles and improve circulation; Ginger cream, which reduces pain and inflammation; Willow bark cream, which contains salicin, a compound similar to aspirin; and Lavender oil cream, which promotes relaxation and reduces stress.

By applying these herbal creams to the affected area, individuals can experience relief from one side headaches without relying on pharmaceutical medications.

1. Bacopa monnieri creams

Bacopa monnieri creams have been traditionally used to treat unilateral headaches, also known as one-sided headaches, due to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and neuroprotective properties.

The bioactive constituents of Bacopa monnieri, such as bacosides A and B, help to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the brain, which may contribute to the development of one-sided headaches.

By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, Bacopa monnieri creams may help to alleviate symptoms of one-sided headaches, including pain and sensitivity to light and sound.

The benefits of using Bacopa monnieri creams to treat one-sided headaches include reduced frequency and severity of attacks, improved quality of life, and a reduction in the need for pain medications.

2. Curcuma longa creams

Curcuma longa creams, derived from the turmeric plant, have been traditionally used to treat unilateral headaches, also known as one-sided headaches.

The anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties of Curcuma longa creams help to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation in the affected area.

The bioactive constituents, including curcumin, demethoxycurcumin, and bisdemethoxycurcumin, work together to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, thereby providing relief from headache symptoms.

The benefits of using Curcuma longa creams to treat one-sided headaches include reduced pain intensity, improved quality of life, and a natural alternative to conventional medications.

3. Lavandula angustifolia creams

Lavandula angustifolia creams have been traditionally used to treat one-sided headache or migraine ailments, primarily due to their analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and calming properties.

The bioactive constituents, including linalool and linalyl acetate, in these herbal preparations help to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation, thus providing relief from the symptoms of one-sided headaches.

These constituents also possess a sedative effect, which helps to calm the nervous system and reduce stress, further contributing to the treatment of one-sided headaches.

The benefits of using Lavandula angustifolia creams to treat one-sided headaches include their non-pharmacological nature, lack of side effects, and ease of application, making them a popular choice for those seeking alternative remedies.

4. Achillea millefolium creams

Achillea millefolium creams, derived from the dried flowers and leaves of the yarrow plant, have been traditionally used to treat unilateral headaches, also known as one-sided headaches.

The analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties of Achillea millefolium creams help to relieve pain and reduce inflammation in the affected area, thereby alleviating the symptoms of the headache.

The bioactive constituents of Achillea millefolium, including flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and phenolic acids, contribute to its therapeutic effects by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory mediators and modulating the pain signaling pathways.

The benefits of using Achillea millefolium creams to treat one-sided headaches include their non-invasive and non-pharmacological nature, making them a suitable alternative to conventional medications for those seeking a more natural approach to pain management.

5. Capsicum annuum creams

Capsicum annuum creams have been traditionally used to treat unilateral headaches, also known as one-sided headaches, due to their anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The bioactive constituents present in these creams, such as capsaicin and flavonoids, help to reduce pain and inflammation in the affected area, thereby providing relief from the headache.

Capsicum annuum creams also possess vasodilatory properties, which help to increase blood flow to the affected area and promote relaxation of tense muscles.

Regular application of these creams can provide long-term benefits, including reduced frequency and severity of one-sided headaches, as well as improved overall well-being.

Related Study

According to "The Cochrane database of systematic reviews", Capsicum annuum creams, also known as Capsicum Frutescens, have been found to produce more favourable results than placebo in reducing pain, although the quality of the trials was generally low and additional research is needed.

6. Ginkgo biloba creams

Ginkgo biloba creams have been traditionally used to treat one-sided headaches, also known as migraines, due to their anti-inflammatory and vasodilatory properties.

The bioactive constituents present in Ginkgo biloba creams, including flavonoids and bilobalide, help to improve blood flow to the brain and reduce the frequency and severity of headaches.

These creams work by inhibiting the release of pain-producing chemicals in the brain, such as prostaglandins and leukotrienes, and also by relaxing tense muscles that may be contributing to the headache.

The benefits of using Ginkgo biloba creams for treating one-sided headaches include reduced pain and inflammation, improved sleep quality, and a reduction in the frequency and duration of headaches.

7. Mentha x piperita creams

Mentha x piperita creams, also known as peppermint creams, have been traditionally used to treat unilateral headaches due to their analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The menthol content in these creams helps to relieve pain by blocking the production of pain-causing chemicals in the body, providing quick relief from one-sided headaches.

The bioactive constituents, including menthol, limonene, and beta-pinene, possess potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to reduce swelling and alleviate pain in the affected area.

The benefits of using Mentha x piperita creams to treat unilateral headaches include their non-invasive and non-addictive nature, making them a safer alternative to conventional pain-relieving medications.

Related Study

According to Phytotherapy Research: PTR, Mentha x piperita creams, which contain peppermint oil, show positive, preliminary findings as an acute treatment for one side headache.
