Eye Strain Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Eye strain is a common condition characterized by physical fatigue and discomfort in the eyes, often caused by prolonged use of digital devices, reading, or other visual tasks.

Herbal remedies have been used for centuries to treat eye strain, offering a natural and effective solution to alleviate symptoms.

Certain herbs such as bilberry, chamomile, and feverfew are particularly beneficial in treating eye strain due to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which help to soothe and protect the eyes.

Herbal preparations like eye compresses infused with herbs like calendula, lavender, and ginkgo biloba can be used to reduce eye strain, while herbal teas like peppermint and green tea can help to relax the eyes and reduce fatigue.
