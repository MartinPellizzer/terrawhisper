Herbal teas for Gerd are a natural remedy that involves brewing tea from various herbs to alleviate the symptoms of gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), a condition characterized by the backflow of stomach acid into the esophagus, causing heartburn and discomfort.

Some examples of herbal teas that are commonly used to treat GERD include peppermint tea, which helps to relax the muscles and reduce inflammation, chamomile tea, which calms the digestive system and promotes relaxation, licorice root tea, which soothes the esophagus and reduces inflammation, ginger tea, which aids digestion and reduces nausea, and fennel tea, which helps to relax the muscles and reduce bloating.
