10 Best Herbal Baths For Dry Eyes

Herbal baths for dry eyes are a natural remedy that involves soaking a clean cloth or pad in a solution of herbs and warm water, which is then applied to the closed eyes to provide relief from dryness and irritation.

This treatment has several benefits, including reducing inflammation, soothing the eyes, and promoting the release of tears to help moisturize the eyes.

Examples of herbal baths that can be used to treat dry eyes include chamomile, which has anti-inflammatory properties, and calendula, which has antimicrobial properties that can help prevent infection.

Other herbs, such as green tea, peppermint, and aloe vera, can also be used to create a soothing and nourishing herbal bath to help alleviate dry eye symptoms.
