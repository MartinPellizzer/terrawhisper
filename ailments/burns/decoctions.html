13 Best Herbal Decoctions For Burns

Herbal decoctions for burns are a traditional and natural remedy made by infusing herbs in hot water to create a soothing topical treatment for burn injuries.

These decoctions have been used for centuries to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pain, making them an effective and beneficial treatment option for burns.

Examples of herbs used in burn decoctions include aloe vera, which accelerates wound healing and reduces scarring, calendula, which has antimicrobial properties that prevent infection, and chamomile, which calms the skin and reduces inflammation.

Additionally, other herbs such as tea tree oil, lavender, and plantain are also commonly used in burn decoctions due to their anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, which help to promote wound healing and prevent infection.

According to this study published in the "Journal of ethnopharmacology", decoctions for burns, specifically the use of Abu-Khalsa (Arnebia euchroma) ointment, showed benefits in healing second-degree burn wounds by reducing healing time and severity of pain, with lower side effects compared to silver sulfadiazine cream.
