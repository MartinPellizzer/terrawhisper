10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Postoperative Recovery

Herbal lozenges are increasingly being used to support postoperative recovery due to their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties.

These lozenges often contain natural ingredients such as echinacea, sage, and licorice root, which can help alleviate sore throat and reduce inflammation in the respiratory tract. They are particularly beneficial for patients undergoing procedures that may lead to throat irritation or dryness. Unlike traditional lozenges, herbal versions are generally free from harsh chemicals, making them a safer option for individuals with sensitivities.

Incorporating herbal lozenges into a postoperative care routine can contribute to faster healing and improved comfort during the recovery period.
