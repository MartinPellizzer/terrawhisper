10 Best Herbal Capsules For Postoperative Recovery

Herbal capsules are increasingly being used as complementary therapy to support postoperative recovery by promoting healing and reducing inflammation.

These capsules often contain natural ingredients such as turmeric, ginger, and ashwagandha, which have been traditionally used for their anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting properties. They may help alleviate pain, reduce swelling, and enhance the body's natural healing processes after surgery. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before using herbal supplements to ensure they do not interfere with prescribed medications or surgical outcomes.

When used appropriately, herbal capsules can be a safe and effective addition to a comprehensive postoperative care plan.
