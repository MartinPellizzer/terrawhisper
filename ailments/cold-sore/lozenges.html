10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Cold Sore

Herbal lozenges for cold sores are natural remedies that contain ingredients like echinacea, sage, or peppermint, which are believed to have antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties.

These lozenges are designed to be dissolved in the mouth, providing localized relief by reducing pain and discomfort associated with cold sores. They may help to shorten the duration of outbreaks and promote faster healing by supporting the body's natural defenses. Unlike some pharmaceutical treatments, herbal lozenges are generally considered safe for most people, though they may not be as effective for severe cases.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal lozenges, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
