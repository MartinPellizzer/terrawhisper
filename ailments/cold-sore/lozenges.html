10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Cold Sore

Herbal lozenges are a popular natural remedy for alleviating the discomfort of cold sores, which are caused by the herpes simplex virus.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like echinacea, sage, or licorice root, which are believed to have antiviral, anti-inflammatory, or soothing properties. They work by providing a cooling effect on the affected area, reducing pain and promoting faster healing. Unlike prescription treatments, herbal lozenges are generally considered safe and can be used as a complementary approach to managing cold sore symptoms.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare professional before using them, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
