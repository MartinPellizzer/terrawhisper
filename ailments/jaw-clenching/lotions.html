10 Best Herbal Lotions For Jaw Clenching

Herbal lotions for jaw clenching are natural topical treatments designed to soothe and relax the facial muscles, particularly around the jaw area.

These lotions often contain calming herbs like chamomile, lavender, and calendula, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and stress-relieving properties. Applying these lotions can help alleviate tension and discomfort caused by bruxism or stress-related jaw clenching. They are typically massaged gently into the jawline to promote relaxation and improve circulation.

While they may not cure the underlying causes of jaw clenching, they can be a beneficial complementary therapy for those seeking natural relief.

1. Valerian (Valeriana officinalis)

Valeriana officinalis, commonly known as valerian, is a traditional herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to promote relaxation and ease tension.

When formulated into a soothing herbal lotion, valerian can be applied topically to the jaw area to help alleviate symptoms of jaw clenching and bruxism. The calming properties of valerian are believed to work by gently reducing muscle tension and promoting a sense of calm in the jaw muscles. This natural remedy is particularly beneficial for individuals who experience stress-related jaw clenching during the day or night.

While it is not a substitute for professional dental or medical treatment, valerian-based lotions can serve as a complementary approach to managing jaw tension and improving overall comfort.

The active compound, glycyrrhizin, has been shown to have a calming effect on the nervous system, which may alleviate the stress that contributes to jaw clenching.
