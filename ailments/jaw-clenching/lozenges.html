10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Jaw Clenching

Herbal lozenges are a natural remedy designed to alleviate the discomfort caused by jaw clenching, offering a soothing effect through the release of calming plant-based ingredients.

By dissolving slowly in the mouth, they provide a long-lasting relief that helps reduce tension in the jaw muscles. They are particularly beneficial for individuals who experience bruxism or stress-related jaw clenching during the day or night.

Incorporating herbal lozenges into a daily routine can be an effective, non-invasive way to manage jaw discomfort and promote relaxation.
