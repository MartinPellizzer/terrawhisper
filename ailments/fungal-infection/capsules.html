These bioactive constituents help to reduce the severity of symptoms associated with fungal infections, such as itching, redness, and inflammation, by disrupting the fungal cell membrane and inhibiting the production of fungal toxins.

The use of Eucalyptus globulus capsules has been shown to be beneficial in treating fungal infections, promoting wound healing, and reducing the risk of secondary infections, making it a popular herbal remedy in traditional medicine.
