Examples of herbal essential oils used to treat dry cough include Eucalyptus oil, which helps to open airways and reduce congestion, Peppermint oil, which provides a cooling sensation and relieves coughing spasms, Thyme oil, which has antibacterial properties that combat infections, and Ginger oil, which reduces inflammation and eases coughing.
