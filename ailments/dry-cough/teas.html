9 Best Herbal Teas For Dry Cough

Herbal teas for dry cough are a natural remedy that involves brewing herbs to create a soothing and calming liquid that helps alleviate dry cough symptoms.

These teas are beneficial in treating dry cough as they provide relief from irritation and inflammation in the throat, while also promoting hydration and relaxation.
