11 Best Herbal Creams For Periodontal Disease

Herbal creams for periodontal disease are topical treatments that combine natural herbs with traditional medications to combat the symptoms and progression of periodontal disease, a bacterial infection that affects the gums and bone supporting the teeth.

These creams offer several benefits, including reducing inflammation, fighting bacterial growth, and promoting healthy gum tissue regeneration.

Some examples of herbal creams used to treat periodontal disease include those containing aloe vera, which has anti-inflammatory properties; tea tree oil, which has antimicrobial properties; and eucalyptus, which has natural antibacterial properties.

Additionally, creams containing herbs like turmeric, ginseng, and echinacea are also used, as they have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to reduce gum inflammation and promote healing.

1. Melaleuca alternifolia creams

Melaleuca alternifolia creams, derived from the leaves of the Tea Tree plant, have been used to treat periodontal disease due to their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The bioactive constituents, including cineole and terpinen-4-ol, help to combat the growth of bacteria and reduce inflammation in the gums, ultimately contributing to the healing of periodontal tissues.

These creams work by penetrating deep into the gum pockets, reducing plaque, and preventing further infection, ultimately leading to the resolution of symptoms such as bleeding and pain.

The benefits of using Melaleuca alternifolia creams to treat periodontal disease include reduced inflammation, improved gum health, and a lower risk of tooth loss.

2. Curcuma longa creams

Curcuma longa creams, derived from the rhizomes of Curcuma longa, have been traditionally used to treat periodontal disease due to their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The bioactive constituents, including curcumin, demethoxycurcumin, and bisdemethoxycurcumin, in these creams help to reduce inflammation, prevent the progression of periodontal disease, and promote wound healing.

By reducing the growth of bacteria and inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, Curcuma longa creams help to treat periodontal disease by reducing inflammation and preventing further damage to the gums and surrounding tissues.

The benefits of using Curcuma longa creams to treat periodontal disease include reduced inflammation, improved wound healing, and prevention of the progression of the disease, making it a promising natural remedy for periodontal disease treatment.
