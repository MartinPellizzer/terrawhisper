10 Best Herbal Decoctions For Irritable Bowel Syndrome

These decoctions have been found to provide numerous benefits in treating IBS, including reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and alleviating symptoms such as bloating, abdominal pain, and changes in bowel movements.

Examples of herbal decoctions that have been used to treat IBS include peppermint decoction, which relaxes the muscles in the digestive tract and eases symptoms; ginger decoction, which reduces inflammation and nausea; chamomile decoction, which soothes the digestive system and promotes relaxation; and fennel decoction, which eases bloating and gas.

Other herbal decoctions that have been found to be effective in treating IBS include licorice root decoction, dandelion root decoction, and slippery elm decoction, all of which work to calm the digestive system and promote regular bowel movements.
