10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Eye Strain

Herbal lozenges are increasingly being used to alleviate symptoms of eye strain, offering a natural and soothing alternative to conventional eye drops.

These lozenges typically contain ingredients like chamomile, peppermint, and echinacea, which are known for their calming and anti-inflammatory properties. When dissolved in the mouth, they help promote relaxation and reduce the tension that often accompanies prolonged screen use. The menthol and other essential oils in these lozenges can also stimulate saliva production, which may indirectly support overall eye health by keeping the mucous membranes moist.

While they are not a cure for eye strain, herbal lozenges can be a beneficial addition to a holistic approach for managing discomfort and promoting eye wellness.
