1. Mentha piperita

The essential oils in peppermint, particularly menthol, help soothe the digestive tract and reduce excess stomach acid production. When consumed as a fresh juice, peppermint can provide quick relief from heartburn and indigestion by relaxing the muscles of the gastrointestinal tract. It is often combined with other herbs like ginger or licorice to enhance its effectiveness and improve taste.

However, individuals with severe hyperacidity should consult a healthcare professional before incorporating peppermint juice into their regimen.
