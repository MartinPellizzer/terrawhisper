10 Best Herbal Linctuses For Hyperacidity

Herbal linctuses for hyperacidity are traditional remedies that combine soothing herbs with expectorant and antacid properties to alleviate symptoms of excess stomach acid.

Common ingredients include licorice root, which has mild antacid and demulcent effects, and marshmallow root, known for its ability to coat and protect the lining of the digestive tract. These formulations are often preferred by individuals seeking natural alternatives to conventional antacids and proton pump inhibitors. They can help reduce heartburn, indigestion, and throat irritation associated with hyperacidity.

However, they should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional, especially for long-term management or in cases of severe symptoms.

1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, commonly known as licorice root, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its soothing effects on the respiratory and digestive systems.

Herbal linctuses containing glycyrrhiza glabra are often formulated to alleviate symptoms of hyperacidity, such as heartburn and indigestion, due to its anti-inflammatory and demulcent properties. The active compounds in licorice, including glycyrrhizin and flavonoids, help to reduce gastric acid secretion and protect the mucous membranes of the stomach and esophagus. These linctuses are typically used as a natural alternative to conventional antacids, offering a gentler and longer-lasting relief.

However, prolonged use of licorice-based products should be monitored, as they may lead to side effects such as hypertension or water retention due to their mineralocorticoid-like effects.
