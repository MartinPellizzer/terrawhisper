10 Best Herbal Capsules For Hyperacidity

Herbal capsules for hyperacidity are natural remedies that aim to reduce excess stomach acid and soothe digestive discomfort.

These capsules often contain ingredients like ginger, aloe vera, licorice root, and fenugreek, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antacid properties. Many people prefer herbal capsules over synthetic medications due to their mild side effects and holistic approach to health.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare professional before using them, especially if symptoms persist or worsen.
