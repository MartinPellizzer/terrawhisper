10 Best Herbal Creams For Hyperacidity

Herbal creams for hyperacidity are natural topical treatments designed to alleviate symptoms associated with excessive stomach acid, such as heartburn and indigestion.

These creams often contain ingredients like aloe vera, licorice root, and chamomile, which are known for their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties. They work by forming a protective barrier over the skin and reducing irritation caused by acid reflux, though they are not a cure for the underlying condition. While they can provide relief for mild discomfort, they should be used in conjunction with lifestyle changes and, if necessary, medical treatment.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal creams, especially if symptoms persist or worsen.
