10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Menstrual Cramps

They work by targeting the discomfort associated with menstrual cramps through gentle, localized relief without the side effects of conventional pain medications. Many women find them beneficial for managing mild to moderate cramping, especially when used consistently during their menstrual cycle.

As a complementary therapy, herbal lozenges can be a safe and effective option for those seeking natural relief from menstrual pain.
