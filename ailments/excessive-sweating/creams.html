Herbal creams for excessive sweating are topical ointments made from natural ingredients, such as herbs and essential oils, that help to reduce excessive sweating or hyperhidrosis.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat excessive sweating include Aloe Vera, Tea Tree Oil, Witch Hazel, Eucalyptus, Lavender, Chamomile, and Geranium, which are known for their antiseptic, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties that help to soothe and calm the skin.
