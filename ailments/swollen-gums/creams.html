10 Best Herbal Creams For Swollen Gums

Herbal creams for swollen gums are natural remedies that often contain ingredients like aloe vera, tea tree oil, and calendula, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These creams can help reduce inflammation, soothe irritation, and promote healing in the gums. They are typically preferred by individuals seeking alternatives to conventional treatments due to their mild and soothing nature. Many herbal creams are formulated to be gentle on the sensitive gum tissue, making them suitable for daily use.

However, it is important to consult a dentist or healthcare professional before using any herbal remedy to ensure it is appropriate for your specific condition.

1. Salvia (Salvia officinalis)

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable ingredient in herbal creams designed for swollen gums.

These creams often combine sage extract with other natural components like chamomile or aloe vera to enhance their soothing and healing effects. The active compounds in salvia officinalis, such as rosmarinic acid and flavonoids, help reduce gum inflammation and combat bacterial growth that can contribute to periodontal issues. When applied topically, these creams can provide relief from discomfort and promote faster recovery of irritated or inflamed gum tissues.

As a natural alternative to conventional treatments, salvia officinalis herbal creams offer a gentle and effective option for managing swollen gums.

2. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.

When formulated into a cream, it can be applied topically to swollen gums to help reduce inflammation and promote healing. The active compounds in hypericum perforatum, such as hyperforin and hypericin, are believed to have antimicrobial and analgesic effects that may alleviate gum irritation. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using such creams, especially if you are on medications, as St. John's Wort can interact with certain drugs.

Despite its potential benefits, it should not replace professional dental care for severe or persistent gum issues.

The anti-inflammatory compounds in echinacea may help soothe gum inflammation and promote healing, though it is important to consult a dentist or healthcare provider before using such products for persistent or severe gum conditions.
