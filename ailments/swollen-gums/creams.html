10 Best Herbal Creams For Swollen Gums

Herbal creams for swollen gums are natural remedies that incorporate plant-based ingredients known for their anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.

These creams often contain ingredients such as aloe vera, chamomile, calendula, and tea tree oil, which can help reduce inflammation and promote healing. They are generally considered safe for most people, including those with sensitive gums, and may offer a gentler alternative to traditional pharmaceutical treatments. Regular application of these creams can help alleviate discomfort and support gum health.

However, it is important to consult a dentist or healthcare professional to ensure they are suitable for individual conditions and to complement proper oral hygiene practices.

1. Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable ingredient in herbal creams designed to soothe swollen gums.

These creams often contain extracts of sage leaves, which can help reduce gum inflammation and promote healing by inhibiting bacterial growth. The soothing effects of salvia officinalis may also help alleviate pain and discomfort associated with gingivitis or periodontal disease. When applied topically, these creams can provide a natural alternative for individuals seeking gentle, plant-based remedies for oral health issues.

However, it is important to consult with a dental professional before using such products, especially if symptoms persist or worsen.

2. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis, commonly known as aloe vera, is often used in herbal creams to soothe swollen gums due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These creams can help reduce gum inflammation and promote healing by providing a cooling and moisturizing effect. The gel-like substance in aloe vera contains vitamins, enzymes, and amino acids that support tissue regeneration and oral health. When applied topically, aloe-based creams can alleviate discomfort and prevent further irritation from conditions like gingivitis or periodontal disease.

However, it is important to consult a dentist before using such products, especially if the swelling is severe or persistent.

3. Hypericum perforatum

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herb that has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

When formulated into a cream, it can be applied topically to swollen gums to help reduce inflammation and promote healing. The active compounds in hypericum perforatum, such as hyperforin and flavonoids, may contribute to its effectiveness in soothing gum irritation. While some studies suggest its potential benefits for oral health, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using it, especially if you are on other medications, as it can interact with certain drugs.

Overall, hypericum perforatum herbal cream may offer a natural alternative for managing mild gum inflammation when used as part of a comprehensive oral care routine.

4. Zingiber officinale

5. Calendula officinalis
