10 Best Herbal Lotions For Swollen Gums

Herbal lotions can be an effective natural remedy for swollen gums due to their anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.

Ingredients like chamomile, calendula, and sage are commonly used in these lotions for their ability to reduce irritation and promote healing. Applying a cool herbal lotion to the affected area can help alleviate pain and reduce swelling, providing relief without the use of harsh chemicals. These lotions are often preferred by individuals seeking gentle, holistic treatments for gum issues.

However, it is important to consult a dentist to rule out more serious conditions such as periodontitis.

1. Salvia (Salvia officinalis)

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties, making it a valuable ingredient in herbal lotions for swollen gums.

These lotions often contain extracts or essential oils from the leaves of the plant, which can help reduce inflammation and soothe gum irritation. The natural antibacterial properties of sage may also contribute to preventing infections that can exacerbate gum swelling. When applied topically, these lotions can provide a cooling effect, offering relief from discomfort associated with gingivitis or periodontal issues.

However, it is important to consult with a dental professional before using any herbal remedy to ensure it is safe and appropriate for individual oral health conditions.

2. Marigold (Calendula officinalis)

Calendula officinalis herbal lotions are traditionally used to soothe and reduce inflammation in swollen gums due to their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These lotions contain extracts from the yellow flowers of the calendula plant, which have been shown to promote healing and reduce redness and irritation. The gentle nature of calendula makes it suitable for sensitive oral tissues, offering a natural alternative to conventional treatments. When applied topically, calendula lotions can help alleviate discomfort and support the healing process in cases of gingivitis or periodontal inflammation.

However, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before using calendula products, especially if you have known allergies or are undergoing dental treatment.

3. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herbal plant that has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

When formulated into a herbal lotion, it can provide relief for swollen gums by reducing inflammation and preventing infection. The active compounds in Hypericum perforatum, such as hypericin and flavonoids, help soothe gum irritation and promote healing. Applying this lotion directly to the affected areas can be a natural alternative to conventional treatments for gum swelling.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using it, especially if you are on other medications or have underlying health conditions.
