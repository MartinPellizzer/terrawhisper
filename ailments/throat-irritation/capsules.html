The active compounds in licorice root, such as glycyrrhizin and flavonoids, help reduce inflammation and suppress coughing, making it effective for sore throats and other respiratory discomforts. Glycyrrhiza glabra herbal capsules offer a convenient and concentrated form of this traditional remedy, allowing for easy consumption and consistent dosing. These capsules are often used as a natural alternative to conventional throat lozenges or medications, especially for those seeking holistic health solutions.
