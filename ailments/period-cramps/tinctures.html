1. Chaste tree (Vitex agnus-castus)

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chasteberry, is a herbal tincture often used to support hormonal balance and alleviate symptoms associated with menstrual cycles.

It is particularly popular for its potential to reduce the severity of period cramps by regulating prostaglandin levels and easing uterine contractions. Many women find relief from cramping and bloating when using vitex tinctures, especially during the premenstrual phase. The herb is believed to influence the pituitary gland, which can help in managing menstrual irregularities and associated discomfort.

As a natural alternative to pharmaceuticals, vitex tinctures are often preferred for their gentle, holistic approach to menstrual health.

Cimicifuga racemosa, also known as black cohosh, is a commonly used herbal remedy for alleviating period cramps and menstrual discomfort.
