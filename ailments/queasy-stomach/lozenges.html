1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale, commonly known as ginger, has been traditionally used to alleviate digestive discomfort and nausea.

Ginger herbal lozenges are a convenient and effective form of this natural remedy, offering a soothing effect on the stomach. These lozenges work by calming the gastrointestinal tract and reducing inflammation, which can help ease queasiness and indigestion. The active compounds in ginger, such as gingerol and shogaol, are known for their antiemetic properties, making them beneficial for individuals experiencing motion sickness or morning sickness.

When used regularly, ginger lozenges can provide a gentle, natural way to manage stomach discomfort without the side effects of pharmaceutical alternatives.
