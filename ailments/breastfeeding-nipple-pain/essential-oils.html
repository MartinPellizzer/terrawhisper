1. Matricaria chamomilla

Matricaria chamomilla, commonly known as German chamomile, is a popular herbal essential oil that has been traditionally used for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

When diluted properly, it can be safely applied to the nipples of breastfeeding mothers to alleviate pain and discomfort caused by cracked or sore nipples. The oil's soothing effects help reduce inflammation and promote healing, making it a natural alternative to commercial creams. However, it is important to ensure that the essential oil is properly diluted with a carrier oil to avoid skin irritation.

As with any complementary therapy, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare provider before using chamomile essential oil during breastfeeding.
