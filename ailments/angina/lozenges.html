10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Angina

Herbal lozenges for angina are natural remedies designed to alleviate symptoms associated with chest pain and discomfort caused by angina.

These lozenges often contain ingredients such as garlic, ginger, and eucalyptus, which are believed to improve circulation and reduce inflammation. They may help relax blood vessels, thereby increasing blood flow to the heart muscle and reducing the frequency and severity of angina attacks. While they are generally considered safe, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using them, especially if you are already on medication for heart conditions.

Herbal lozenges should not replace conventional medical treatments but can be used as a complementary therapy under proper guidance.

1. Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, and herbal lozenges containing sage are often recommended for individuals experiencing angina due to their potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

These lozenges may help soothe the throat and reduce irritation, which can be beneficial for those with angina who also suffer from respiratory discomfort. While sage is not a substitute for conventional treatments for angina, it may offer additional support in managing symptoms and improving overall respiratory comfort. The active compounds in sage, such as rosmarinic acid and flavonoids, contribute to its therapeutic effects.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using sage lozenges, especially for individuals with existing health conditions or those taking other medications.
