11 Best Herbal Creams For Jet Lag

Herbal creams for jet lag are topical preparations made from various plant extracts, such as herbs, essential oils, and botanicals, designed to alleviate the symptoms of jet lag.

These creams offer numerous benefits, including reducing fatigue, improving sleep quality, and regulating the body's circadian rhythms.

Examples of herbal creams used to treat jet lag include those containing ashwagandha, which helps to reduce stress and anxiety, as well as creams infused with lavender, peppermint, and chamomile, known for their calming and sleep-promoting properties.

Additionally, creams featuring ingredients such as ginkgo biloba, valerian root, and passionflower are also used to help alleviate jet lag symptoms, as they are believed to enhance sleep quality and reduce jet lag-related fatigue.
