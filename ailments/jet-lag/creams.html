10 Best Herbal Creams For Jet Lag

Herbal creams for jet lag are natural topical treatments that aim to alleviate the physical discomfort associated with crossing time zones.

These creams often contain ingredients like lavender, chamomile, and arnica, which are known for their calming and soothing properties. While they are not a cure for jet lag itself, they can help ease symptoms such as muscle tension, fatigue, and mild headaches. Some people use these creams as part of a broader self-care routine to support their body's adjustment to new time zones.

However, it's important to note that herbal creams should not replace good sleep hygiene or other recommended strategies for managing jet lag.

1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis, commonly known as valerian, is a traditional herbal remedy often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.

While it is typically consumed as a tea or supplement, some formulations include valerian in topical creams, which may be used to alleviate the physical stress and tension associated with jet lag. These creams are believed to work by calming the nervous system and reducing muscle tension, which can be exacerbated during long flights and time zone changes. However, it is important to note that scientific evidence supporting the effectiveness of valerian-containing creams specifically for jet lag is limited.

As with any herbal product, individuals should consult with a healthcare provider before using valerian creams, especially if they have existing health conditions or are taking other medications.
