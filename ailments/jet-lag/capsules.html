10 Best Herbal Capsules For Jet Lag

Herbal capsules are increasingly popular as a natural remedy for combating jet lag, offering a gentler alternative to traditional pharmaceuticals.

These capsules often contain adaptogenic herbs such as ashwagandha, rhodiola rosea, and holy basil, which are known for their ability to help the body adjust to stress and time zone changes. Some formulations include melatonin, a hormone that regulates sleep-wake cycles, to support better sleep patterns during travel. Unlike synthetic medications, herbal capsules are generally considered safer with fewer side effects, making them appealing to health-conscious travelers.

However, it's important to consult with a healthcare provider before use, especially for those with existing medical conditions or who are taking other medications.

1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis, commonly known as valerian, is a traditional herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to support relaxation and improve sleep quality.

When formulated into herbal capsules, valerian root is often taken to help alleviate the symptoms of jet lag by promoting better sleep patterns and reducing anxiety. The active compounds in valerian, such as valerenic acid, may interact with the central nervous system to enhance sleep onset and duration. While some studies suggest valerian may be effective for mild sleep disturbances, its role in specifically addressing jet lag remains inconclusive and requires further research.

As with any supplement, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare professional before using valerian capsules for travel-related sleep issues.
