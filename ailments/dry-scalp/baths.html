10 Best Herbal Baths For Dry Scalp

Herbal baths for dry scalp are a natural and holistic approach to treating scalp conditions characterized by itchiness, flakiness, and irritation.

These baths are rich in antioxidants, anti-inflammatory properties, and essential oils that help to soothe and calm the scalp, promoting healthy hair growth and reducing the symptoms of dry scalp.
