10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Cavities

Herbal lozenges are natural remedies designed to support oral health by targeting common causes of cavities, such as bacterial plaque and acid erosion.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like tea tree oil, neem, and clove, which have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce harmful bacteria in the mouth. By neutralizing acids and promoting saliva production, herbal lozenges can help remineralize enamel and prevent the progression of early cavities. They are generally considered safe for regular use and can be a complementary option to traditional dental care routines.

However, they should not replace professional dental treatment for existing cavities or severe oral health issues.
