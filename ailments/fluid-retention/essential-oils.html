10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Fluid Retention

Herbal essential oils have been traditionally used to support the body's natural processes, including managing fluid retention.

Oils such as juniper, fennel, and ginger are commonly recommended for their diuretic properties that can help reduce water retention. These essential oils work by promoting the kidneys' ability to eliminate excess fluids from the body. When used in diffusers or applied topically with a carrier oil, they can offer a natural and aromatic way to support fluid balance.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially for those with existing medical conditions or during pregnancy.
