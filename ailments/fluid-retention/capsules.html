10 Best Herbal Capsules For Fluid Retention

Herbal capsules are commonly used as a natural remedy for fluid retention, often containing ingredients like dandelion root, parsley, and horse chestnut, which are believed to support kidney function and promote urine production.

Unlike synthetic diuretics, herbal capsules are generally considered safer with fewer side effects, though their effectiveness can vary depending on the individual and the specific formulation.
