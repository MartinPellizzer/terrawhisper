10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Fluid Retention

Herbal tinctures are concentrated liquid extracts made from plant materials and are often used to support the body's natural processes, including managing fluid retention.

Certain herbs such as dandelion root, parsley, and garlic are commonly included in tinctures due to their diuretic properties that help the body eliminate excess fluids. These tinctures can be taken orally and are typically diluted in water or another liquid to reduce their potency and enhance absorption. While herbal tinctures are generally considered safe when used as directed, they should not replace medical treatment for severe or persistent fluid retention.

It is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using herbal tinctures, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
