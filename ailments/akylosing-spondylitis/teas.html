10 Best Herbal Teas For Ankylosing Spondylitis

Herbal teas may offer potential relief for individuals with ankylosing spondylitis due to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Herbs such as turmeric, ginger, and willow bark are commonly used in herbal teas and contain compounds like curcumin and salicylates that may help reduce joint pain and inflammation. While these teas are not a cure for ankylosing spondylitis, they can complement conventional treatments by supporting overall wellness and symptom management. It is important to consult a healthcare provider before incorporating herbal teas into a treatment plan, as some herbs may interact with medications or have side effects.

Overall, herbal teas can be a natural and soothing addition to a holistic approach for managing the symptoms of ankylosing spondylitis.

When brewed into a tea, urtica dioica is often consumed to support joint health and alleviate symptoms.
