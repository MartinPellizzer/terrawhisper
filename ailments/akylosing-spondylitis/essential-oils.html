10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Ankylosing Spondylitis

Herbal essential oils have gained attention as complementary therapies for managing symptoms of ankylosing spondylitis, a chronic inflammatory disease affecting the spine.

Oils such as eucalyptus, lavender, and peppermint are commonly used for their anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which may help reduce pain and stiffness. These essential oils can be applied topically through massage or diffusion, offering a natural alternative for some patients seeking non-pharmacological relief. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before using them, as some oils may cause skin irritation or interact with medications.

While research on their efficacy is ongoing, many individuals report improved comfort and mobility when incorporating these oils into their self-care routines.

1. Curcuma longa

These essential oils can be used in aromatherapy, topical applications, or diffusion to help alleviate pain and stiffness in affected joints.
