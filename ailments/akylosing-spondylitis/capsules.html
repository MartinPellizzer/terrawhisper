10 Best Herbal Capsules For Ankylosing Spondylitis

Herbal capsules have gained attention as a complementary therapy for managing symptoms of ankylosing spondylitis, a chronic inflammatory disease affecting the spine.

These capsules often contain natural ingredients such as turmeric, ginger, and boswellia, which are believed to possess anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties. While some studies suggest that these herbs may help reduce inflammation and improve joint mobility, more rigorous clinical research is needed to confirm their efficacy and safety. Herbal capsules are generally considered safer than synthetic medications, but they can interact with other drugs and may cause side effects in some individuals.

It is important for patients to consult with healthcare professionals before incorporating herbal supplements into their treatment regimen for ankylosing spondylitis.
