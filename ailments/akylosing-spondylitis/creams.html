10 Best Herbal Creams For Ankylosing Spondylitis

Herbal creams are increasingly being used as complementary treatments for ankylosing spondylitis, a chronic inflammatory disease that primarily affects the spine.

These creams often contain natural ingredients such as turmeric, ginger, and essential oils, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. While some studies suggest that certain herbal compounds may help reduce pain and stiffness, the effectiveness of these creams can vary, and they are not a substitute for conventional medical treatments. It is important for patients to consult with healthcare professionals before using herbal creams to ensure safety and avoid potential interactions with other medications.

Overall, herbal creams may offer some relief for symptom management but should be used under proper guidance as part of a comprehensive treatment plan.
