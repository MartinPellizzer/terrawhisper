13 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Nerve Pain

Herbal essential oils for nerve pain are plant-derived extracts used to alleviate symptoms of neuropathic pain, which is characterized by numbness, tingling, and burning sensations in the nerves.

These oils have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to reduce nerve inflammation and promote healing, providing relief from nerve pain.

Examples of herbal essential oils used to treat nerve pain include peppermint oil, which has a cooling effect that numbs the pain; eucalyptus oil, which has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and ease pain; ginger oil, which has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties that reduce pain and inflammation; frankincense oil, which has anti-inflammatory and anti-arthritic properties that reduce pain and inflammation; and lavender oil, which has a calming effect that reduces anxiety and promotes relaxation.

By incorporating these oils into a treatment plan, individuals can experience relief from nerve pain and improve their overall quality of life.

According to "Journal of ethnopharmacology", essential oils from Melissa officinalis L. have potential anti-nociceptive properties that can modulate opioidergic and serotonergic pathways to help manage nerve pain.
