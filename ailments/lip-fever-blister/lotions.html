10 Best Herbal Lotions For Fever Blister On Lip

Herbal lotions can be a natural and soothing option for treating fever blisters on the lips, often containing ingredients like calendula, chamomile, or echinacea, which have anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties.

These lotions can help reduce swelling, redness, and discomfort associated with cold sores, while also promoting healing. To use, apply a small amount of the lotion gently to the affected area several times a day, ensuring it is specifically formulated for sensitive skin. While herbal lotions may not eliminate the blister entirely, they can alleviate symptoms and support the body's natural recovery process.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider if the fever blister persists or worsens, as more severe cases may require antiviral medication.
