1. St. john's wort (Hypericum perforatum)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used for its potential anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties.

While it is more commonly associated with treating mild depression, some studies suggest it may help reduce the duration and severity of fever blisters on the lips due to its ability to inhibit viral replication. However, it is important to note that hypericum perforatum capsules are not a guaranteed cure for cold sores and should be used in conjunction with other treatments recommended by a healthcare professional. The herb may interact with certain medications, so it is crucial to consult a doctor before use, especially if you are taking anticoagulants or antidepressants.
