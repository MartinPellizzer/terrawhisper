10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Bruises

Herbal lozenges for bruises are traditional remedies that combine natural ingredients to promote healing and reduce inflammation.

These lozenges often contain herbs such as arnica, chamomile, and calendula, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. They are typically used to alleviate pain and swelling associated with minor bruises by improving circulation and reducing cellular damage. Unlike conventional pain relievers, herbal lozenges are generally considered safer for long-term use and may offer a more holistic approach to recovery.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using them, especially for severe or persistent bruising.
