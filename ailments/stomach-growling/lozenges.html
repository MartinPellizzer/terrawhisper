1. Foeniculum vulgare

These lozenges are typically made from the dried seeds of the fennel plant and are valued for their carminative properties, which help to reduce gas and soothe the digestive system. The active compounds in fennel, such as anethol and fenchone, are believed to relax the smooth muscles of the gastrointestinal tract, thereby alleviating symptoms like bloating and rumbling. Fennel lozenges are often recommended for individuals experiencing indigestion or irregular bowel movements, as they can promote a sense of calm and ease in the stomach.

When used as directed, these herbal lozenges can be a natural and effective complement to dietary and lifestyle adjustments for managing stomach growling.
