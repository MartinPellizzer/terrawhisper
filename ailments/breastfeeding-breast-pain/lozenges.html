10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Breastfeeding Breast Pain

Herbal lozenges can be a natural and soothing option for breastfeeding mothers experiencing breast pain, offering relief without the use of pharmaceuticals.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like chamomile, calendula, or sage, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that may help reduce swelling and prevent infection. They are generally considered safe for use during breastfeeding, as the active ingredients are typically localized in the mouth and not absorbed into the bloodstream in significant amounts. However, it is advisable to consult with a healthcare provider before using any herbal products to ensure they are appropriate for individual health needs.

When used as part of a comprehensive approach that includes proper latch techniques and hygiene, herbal lozenges can contribute to comfort and continued breastfeeding success.
