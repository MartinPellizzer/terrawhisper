Shingles Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Shingles is a painful skin rash caused by the reactivation of the varicella-zoster virus, which is the same virus that causes chickenpox.

Herbal remedies offer a natural and potentially effective approach to treating shingles, as they can help alleviate symptoms, reduce pain, and boost the immune system.

Certain herbs such as turmeric, ginger, and licorice root are commonly used to treat shingles, as they possess anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties that can help combat the virus and reduce inflammation.

Herbal preparations such as ointments, creams, and teas made from herbs like aloe vera, calendula, and echinacea can be applied topically or ingested to provide relief from shingles symptoms, and supplements like vitamin C and Echinacea may also be beneficial in supporting immune function.
