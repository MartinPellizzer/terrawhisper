1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba herbal capsules are commonly used to support cognitive function and alleviate symptoms of brain fog, which refers to a feeling of mental cloudiness or difficulty concentrating.

It is believed to enhance blood circulation and increase oxygen flow to the brain, potentially improving mental clarity and focus. Studies suggest that ginkgo biloba may contain bioactive compounds like flavonoids and terpenoids that contribute to its cognitive benefits.
