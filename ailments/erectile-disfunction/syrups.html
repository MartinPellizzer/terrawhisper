1. Mucuna pruriens

Mucuna pruriens, commonly known as velvet bean, is a traditional Ayurvedic herb that has been used for centuries to enhance libido and sexual performance.

Herbal syrups made from Mucuna pruriens are believed to support erectile function by increasing testosterone levels and improving blood flow to the genital area. These syrups are often rich in L-dopa, a precursor to dopamine, which can enhance mood and sexual desire. While some studies suggest potential benefits, more clinical research is needed to confirm their efficacy and safety for treating erectile dysfunction.

As with any supplement, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before use, especially for individuals with pre-existing medical conditions or those taking other medications.
