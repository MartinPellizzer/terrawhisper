10 Best Herbal Creams For Palpitation

Herbal creams for palpitation are topical treatments that incorporate natural ingredients such as lavender, chamomile, and arnica, which are believed to have calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

These creams are often used to alleviate the sensation of a racing or irregular heartbeat by promoting relaxation and reducing stress, a common trigger for palpitations. While they are generally considered safe, their effectiveness can vary depending on the individual and the underlying cause of the palpitations. Some people may find relief from using these creams as part of a holistic approach to managing heart-related symptoms.

1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis, commonly known as valerian, is a traditional herbal remedy often used in the form of creams to address symptoms such as palpitations, which are irregular or forceful heartbeats.

These creams typically contain valerian root extract, which is believed to have calming properties that may help reduce nervous system overactivity. The active compounds in valerian, such as valerenic acid, are thought to interact with the brain's GABA receptors, promoting relaxation and potentially lowering heart rate. While some studies suggest valerian may help with anxiety-related palpitations, more research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety for this specific use.
