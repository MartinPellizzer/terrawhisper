10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Eye Bags

Herbal lozenges for eye bags are natural remedies designed to reduce the appearance of dark circles and puffiness around the eyes.

These lozenges often contain ingredients like green tea, chamomile, calendula, and licorice root, which are known for their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties. When dissolved in the mouth, the active compounds in the lozenges can help improve circulation and reduce fluid retention around the eye area. They are typically used as part of a holistic approach to skincare, complementing other treatments such as cold compresses or eye creams.

While they may not provide immediate results, consistent use of herbal lozenges can contribute to a more refreshed and youthful appearance around the eyes.
