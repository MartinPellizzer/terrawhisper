They work by improving circulation, reducing fluid retention, and promoting the healing of damaged skin tissues around the eyes. Many people prefer herbal capsules over topical creams because they are convenient and can be taken daily for long-term benefits.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare professional before starting any new supplement regimen, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
