Low Sperm Count Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Low sperm count is a medical condition where a man has fewer than 15 million sperm per milliliter of semen, affecting fertility and making it difficult to conceive.

Herbal remedies offer a natural and non-invasive approach to treating low sperm count, promoting overall reproductive health and fertility.

Certain herbs such as Ashwagandha, Tribulus Terrestris, and Maca root help to increase sperm count and motility by reducing oxidative stress and inflammation, while others like Ginseng and Ginkgo Biloba improve blood flow to the reproductive organs.

Herbal preparations such as capsules, teas, and tinctures like Tribulus Terrestris extract, Maca root powder, and Ginseng tea can be used to treat low sperm count, as they contain concentrated amounts of these herbs' active compounds, allowing for targeted and effective treatment.
