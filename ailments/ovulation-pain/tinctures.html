1. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chasteberry, is a popular herbal tincture used to support hormonal balance and may help alleviate symptoms associated with ovulation pain.

This herb is believed to influence the pituitary gland, potentially regulating menstrual cycles and reducing discomfort during ovulation. When used as a tincture, Vitex agnus-castus is typically taken orally, often in small doses, and is most effective when started several months before the desired menstrual cycle. While it is generally considered safe for many women, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before use, especially if pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking other medications.

Some individuals may experience mild side effects such as gastrointestinal discomfort or changes in menstrual flow, making it essential to monitor one's response to the herb.
