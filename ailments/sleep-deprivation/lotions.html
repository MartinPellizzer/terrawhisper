5. Strychnos nux-vomica

Strychnos nux-vomica, a traditional herbal remedy, has been historically used in some cultures for its purported effects on sleep and nervous system function.

Some alternative practitioners may incorporate diluted forms of nux vomica in formulations, claiming they can help with insomnia and stress-related sleep issues. However, due to the significant risks of poisoning and adverse effects, its use in sleep-related products is strongly discouraged.

It is essential to consult a qualified healthcare provider before considering any use of this plant, as it is not safe for general or long-term consumption.
