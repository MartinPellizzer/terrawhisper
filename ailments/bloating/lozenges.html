10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Bloating

Herbal lozenges for bloating are natural remedies that combine soothing herbs with mint or ginger to help relieve gastrointestinal discomfort.

These lozenges work by easing gas buildup and reducing inflammation in the digestive tract, making them a gentle alternative to over-the-counter medications. Common ingredients include peppermint, fennel, and licorice, which are known for their carminative and anti-inflammatory properties. They are particularly beneficial for individuals seeking holistic approaches to digestive health without harsh side effects.

Regular use of these lozenges can support digestion and promote a sense of comfort in cases of occasional bloating.
