These essential oils offer numerous benefits, including reducing inflammation, calming digestive issues, and promoting relaxation, thereby providing relief from acid reflux symptoms.

Some examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat acid reflux include peppermint oil, which helps to relax the muscles in the digestive tract and reduce inflammation; ginger oil, which has anti-inflammatory properties and can help to soothe the stomach; chamomile oil, which calms the digestive system and promotes relaxation; lemon balm oil, which has antacid properties and can help to neutralize stomach acid; and fennel oil, which aids in digestion and reduces inflammation in the digestive tract.
