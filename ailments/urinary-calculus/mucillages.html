10 Best Herbal Mucillages For Urinary Calculus

Herbal mucillages, such as those derived from plants like psyllium, flaxseed, and marshmallow root, have been traditionally used to support urinary health and aid in the management of urinary calculus, commonly known as kidney stones.

These mucilaginous substances form a gel-like substance when mixed with water, which can help to coat and soothe the urinary tract lining, reducing irritation and inflammation. Some studies suggest that mucillages may assist in the prevention of stone formation by increasing urine volume and promoting the flushing of minerals from the urinary system. Additionally, certain mucillages possess mild diuretic properties that can enhance urinary flow and reduce the risk of stone recurrence.

However, while these natural remedies may offer supportive benefits, they should not replace professional medical treatment for existing urinary calculi.
