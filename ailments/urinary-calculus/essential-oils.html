10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Urinary Calculus

Herbal essential oils have been explored for their potential role in the management of urinary calculus, or kidney stones, due to their antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties.

Certain oils, such as juniper, parsley, and peppermint, are believed to support urinary tract health by promoting urine flow and reducing the risk of stone formation. These essential oils may help dissolve mineral deposits and prevent the aggregation of crystals in the urinary system. However, their use should be approached with caution, as some oils can be irritating to the urinary tract or interact with medications.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional before incorporating herbal essential oils into a treatment plan for urinary calculus.

Some studies suggest that the essential oils of Urtica dioica may help in the prevention and management of urinary calculus, or kidney stones, by promoting urinary flow and reducing the formation of mineral deposits.
