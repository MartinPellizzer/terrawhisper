11 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Urinary Calculus

Herbal essential oils for urinary calculus are a natural remedy used to treat kidney stones and alleviate associated symptoms such as pain, discomfort, and difficulty urinating.

These oils work by reducing inflammation, improving kidney function, and preventing the formation of new stones.

Examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat urinary calculus include juniper berry oil, which helps to reduce inflammation and promote urination, lavender oil, which calms the nervous system and reduces stress, and uva ursi oil, which has anti-inflammatory properties and helps to prevent the formation of new stones.

Additionally, other oils such as bergamot, cedarwood, and sandalwood can also be beneficial in treating urinary calculus due to their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to prevent infections and reduce swelling in the affected area.
