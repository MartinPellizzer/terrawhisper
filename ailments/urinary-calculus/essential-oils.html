10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Urinary Calculus

Herbal essential oils have been traditionally used to support urinary health and may aid in the management of urinary calculus, or kidney stones.

Oils such as juniper, parsley, and dandelion are often cited for their diuretic properties, which can help increase urine flow and potentially reduce the risk of stone formation. Some studies suggest that certain essential oils may have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects that can ease discomfort associated with urinary tract issues. However, it is important to note that while these oils may offer supportive benefits, they should not replace conventional medical treatment for urinary calculus.

Always consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially for individuals with existing health conditions or those undergoing treatment for kidney stones.
