10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Urinary Calculus

Herbal tinctures for urinary calculus, also known as kidney stones, are concentrated liquid preparations made by soaking plant materials in alcohol or another solvent.

These tinctures are often used as complementary therapies to support urinary health and aid in the dissolution or prevention of kidney stones. Common herbs used in these tinctures include dandelion, nettle, parsley, and juniper, which are believed to have diuretic and litholytic properties. They may help increase urine production and promote the flushing of minerals from the urinary tract.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using herbal tinctures, as they can interact with medications and may not be suitable for everyone.
