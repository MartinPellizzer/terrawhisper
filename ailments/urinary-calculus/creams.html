10 Best Herbal Creams For Urinary Calculus

Herbal creams for urinary calculus are topical treatments that incorporate natural ingredients believed to support kidney health and urinary function.

These creams often contain herbs such as nettle, dandelion, and parsley, which are traditionally used to promote diuresis and reduce inflammation. While they are generally considered safe, their effectiveness in dissolving or preventing urinary stones is not fully supported by modern scientific research. Some users report relief from symptoms like pain and swelling, but it is important to consult a healthcare professional before relying solely on herbal creams for urinary issues.

These products should be used as a complementary therapy rather than a substitute for medical treatment.
