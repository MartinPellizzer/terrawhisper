10 Best Herbal Lotions For Urinary Calculus

Herbal lotions for urinary calculus are natural remedies that aim to support kidney health and aid in the dissolution or prevention of urinary stones.

These lotions typically contain ingredients like nettle, dandelion, and cranberry, which are known for their diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties. They are often used as complementary therapy alongside conventional medical treatments to promote urinary flow and reduce stone formation. While they may help alleviate symptoms and support overall urinary function, they should not replace professional medical advice or treatment.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before using herbal lotions, especially for individuals with existing health conditions or those taking other medications.
