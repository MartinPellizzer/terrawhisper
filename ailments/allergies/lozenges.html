10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Allergies

Herbal lozenges for Allergies are small, medicinal tablets or wafers made from natural herbs and plants that are designed to provide quick relief from allergy symptoms.

Examples of herbal lozenges used to treat allergies include those made from nettle leaf, which reduces inflammation and eases congestion; sage, which soothes a sore throat and reduces coughing; and peppermint, which helps to ease congestion and sinus pressure; as well as licorice root, which has anti-inflammatory properties and can help to reduce allergy-related inflammation.
