9 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Gum Swelling

Herbal essential oils for gum swelling are a type of plant-based remedy used to treat inflammation and pain associated with gum swelling, also known as gingivitis or periodontitis.

Examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat gum swelling include peppermint oil, which helps to reduce inflammation and kill bacteria, eucalyptus oil, which has decongestant and anti-inflammatory properties, and tea tree oil, which has antimicrobial properties that help combat bacterial and fungal infections.

Other examples include lavender oil, which helps to reduce stress and anxiety, chamomile oil, which soothes and calms the gums, and clove oil, which has analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties that help alleviate pain and reduce swelling.
