9 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Over Bleeding During Periods

Herbal essential oils for over bleeding during periods are a natural and holistic approach to treating menstrual disorders, particularly those characterized by excessive bleeding.

These essential oils are derived from plants and have been used for centuries to promote menstrual health and alleviate symptoms of heavy bleeding.

Some examples of herbal essential oils that can help alleviate over bleeding during periods include Clary Sage, which has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties that can help to reduce bleeding and relax the uterine muscles, as well as Geranium, which has a balancing effect on hormonal imbalances that can contribute to heavy bleeding.

Other examples include Ylang-Ylang, which can help to reduce stress and anxiety that can exacerbate bleeding, and Rose, which has a soothing and calming effect on the body and can help to reduce inflammation and promote healing.
