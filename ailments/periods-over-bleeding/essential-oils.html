10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Over Bleeding During Periods

Herbal essential oils, such as lavender, chamomile, and clary sage, are often used to support menstrual health and alleviate symptoms of heavy bleeding during periods.

These oils are known for their calming and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce cramping and regulate menstrual flow. When properly diluted, they can be applied topically to the abdomen or used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and hormonal balance. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially if you have underlying medical conditions or are pregnant.

While they may offer natural relief, they should not replace medical advice or treatment for severe menstrual bleeding.
