10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Over Bleeding During Periods

Herbal essential oils, such as lavender, chamomile, and clary sage, are often used to support menstrual health and may help reduce heavy bleeding during periods.

These oils are believed to have calming and anti-inflammatory properties that can ease uterine contractions and hormonal imbalances. When diluted properly with a carrier oil, they can be applied topically or used in aromatherapy diffusers to promote relaxation and regulate menstrual flow. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially for those with underlying medical conditions or who are pregnant.

While some individuals find relief from these natural remedies, they should not replace medical treatment for severe or prolonged menstrual bleeding.
