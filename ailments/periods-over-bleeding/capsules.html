1. Chaste tree (Vitex agnus-castus)

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chasteberry, is a herbal remedy often used to support hormonal balance and may help regulate menstrual cycles.

Herbal capsules containing vitex are typically taken orally and are believed to influence the pituitary gland, potentially reducing excessive uterine bleeding during menstruation. Studies suggest that vitex may help reduce the severity and duration of menstrual bleeding by modulating estrogen and progesterone levels. It is often recommended for women experiencing irregular periods or heavy bleeding due to hormonal imbalances.

As with any supplement, it is advisable to consult a healthcare provider before starting vitex, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are on other medications.
