10 Best Herbal Lotions For Over Bleeding During Periods

Herbal lotions can be a natural and soothing remedy for managing over bleeding during periods, as they often contain calming and astringent herbs like chamomile, calendula, and witch hazel.

These ingredients help reduce inflammation and promote healing of the uterine lining, which may contribute to excessive bleeding. Applying a cool herbal lotion to the lower abdomen can provide relief from cramping and discomfort associated with heavy menstrual flow. Some herbal formulations also contain antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds that support overall reproductive health.

While herbal lotions are generally safe, it's advisable to consult a healthcare provider before use, especially if symptoms persist or worsen.
