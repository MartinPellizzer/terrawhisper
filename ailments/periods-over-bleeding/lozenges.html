10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Over Bleeding During Periods

Herbal lozenges can be a natural remedy for managing light bleeding or spotting during periods, often containing ingredients like raspberry leaf, chamomile, or sage, which are known for their soothing and astringent properties.

These lozenges work by helping to tone the uterine lining and reduce excessive menstrual flow, offering a gentler alternative to conventional medications. They are generally safe for most women, though it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before use, especially if there are underlying health conditions or if symptoms persist. Many herbal lozenges are free from synthetic additives, making them a preferable option for those seeking natural health solutions.

Overall, they can be an effective and comforting way to support menstrual health and minimize discomfort during menstruation.
