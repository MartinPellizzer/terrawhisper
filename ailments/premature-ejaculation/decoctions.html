1. Velvet bean (Mucuna pruriens)

Mucuna pruriens, commonly known as velvet bean, is a traditional herbal remedy that has been used for centuries in Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine to address various sexual health issues, including premature ejaculation.

The plant contains high levels of L-dopa, a precursor to dopamine, which may help enhance libido and delay ejaculation by improving nerve signaling in the brain. Herbal decoctions made from Mucuna pruriens are typically prepared by boiling the seeds in water, allowing the active compounds to be extracted for consumption. These decoctions are often combined with other herbs to enhance their efficacy and address underlying factors contributing to premature ejaculation.

While some studies suggest potential benefits, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using Mucuna pruriens, as it may interact with medications and have side effects.
