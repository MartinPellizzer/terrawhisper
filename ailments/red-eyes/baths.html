Herbal baths for Red eyes are a natural and holistic treatment that involves soaking the eyes in a warm bath infused with various herbs to alleviate the symptoms of red, itchy, and irritated eyes.

Examples of herbal baths used to treat red eyes include those made with chamomile, which has anti-inflammatory properties to calm the eyes; calendula, which has antiseptic and antibacterial properties to combat infections; and peppermint, which has cooling properties to reduce redness and itching.
