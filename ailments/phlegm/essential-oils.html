11 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Phlegm

Herbal essential oils for Phlegm are plant-based extracts used to alleviate respiratory issues and excess mucus production in the body.

These oils have been found to possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties that help to break down and clear phlegm, reducing congestion and promoting overall respiratory health.

Examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat phlegm include eucalyptus, peppermint, thyme, and rosemary, which are known for their decongestant and expectorant properties, while tea tree oil and oregano oil are used for their antimicrobial properties to combat underlying infections.
