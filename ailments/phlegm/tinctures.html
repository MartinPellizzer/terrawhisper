11 Best Herbal Tinctures For Phlegm

Herbal tinctures for Phlegm are concentrated liquid extracts made from various herbs, plants, and roots that are used to treat respiratory issues and excess mucus production.

These natural remedies have been used for centuries to alleviate symptoms of Phlegm, such as congestion, coughing, and difficulty breathing, due to their antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties.

Examples of herbal tinctures used to treat Phlegm include Echinacea, which boosts the immune system and fights off infections; Thyme, which has antimicrobial properties to combat bacterial infections; Licorice root, which soothes and protects the mucous membranes; Ginger, which reduces inflammation and stimulates digestion; and Hyssop, which helps to expel mucus and phlegm from the lungs.

By using these herbal tinctures, individuals can experience relief from Phlegm symptoms and promote overall respiratory health.
