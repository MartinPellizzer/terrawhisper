11 Best Herbal Creams For Cirrhosis

Herbal creams for Cirrhosis are topical treatments made from plant extracts that are used to alleviate the symptoms and slow down the progression of liver cirrhosis.

These creams often contain a blend of herbs such as milk thistle, turmeric, aloe vera, and licorice root, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The benefits of using herbal creams to treat cirrhosis include reducing liver inflammation, improving liver function, and promoting overall liver health, as seen in creams like LivMax, Liv 52, and Liver Care.

Examples of herbal creams that can be used to treat cirrhosis include Arjuna cream, which is believed to improve liver function, and Silymarin cream, which is known for its antioxidant properties in protecting the liver.

1. Silybum marianum creams

Silybum marianum creams, derived from the milk thistle plant, have been used to treat cirrhosis due to their hepatoprotective properties.

The silymarin complex, a bioactive constituent of Silybum marianum, helps to treat cirrhosis by reducing liver inflammation, preventing oxidative stress, and promoting the regeneration of liver cells.

This herbal preparation also helps to treat cirrhosis by enhancing the antioxidant defense system, reducing the levels of liver enzymes, and improving the overall liver function.

The benefits of Silybum marianum creams in treating cirrhosis include improved liver health, reduced symptoms of the disease, and enhanced quality of life for patients suffering from this condition.
