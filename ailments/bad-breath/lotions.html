10 Best Herbal Lotions For Bad Breath

Herbal lotions for bad breath are natural remedies that use essential oils and plant-based ingredients to freshen breath and improve oral health.

These lotions often contain ingredients like peppermint, tea tree oil, and clove, which have antimicrobial properties that help reduce bacteria causing bad breath. They can be applied directly to the mouth or used as a mouth rinse, providing a refreshing and long-lasting effect. Unlike conventional breath fresheners, herbal lotions are generally safer for daily use and may offer additional benefits such as soothing irritated gums.

However, they should be used as part of a comprehensive oral hygiene routine for best results.

4. Foeniculum vulgare
