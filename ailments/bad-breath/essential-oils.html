13 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Bad Breath

Herbal essential oils for bad breath are a type of natural remedy that involves the use of concentrated plant extracts to eliminate unpleasant mouth odors.

These oils offer several benefits, including their antimicrobial properties, which help to kill bacteria that cause bad breath, as well as their ability to reduce inflammation and freshen breath.

Examples of herbal essential oils that can be used to treat bad breath include peppermint oil, which has a refreshing and cooling effect, eucalyptus oil, which helps to reduce inflammation and fight bacteria, and tea tree oil, which has antiseptic properties that help to combat oral infections.

Additionally, other oils such as ginger, lemon, and lavender can also be used to treat bad breath, as they have antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties that help to freshen and soothe the mouth.

1. Cinnamomum verum essential oils

Cinnamomum verum essential oils, also known as Ceylon cinnamon oil, possess antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help to treat bad breath.

The oil's active compounds, including cinnamaldehyde and eugenol, inhibit the growth of bacteria that cause bad breath, thereby reducing its occurrence.

Cinnamomum verum essential oils help to treat bad breath by reducing inflammation in the mouth and throat, and by preventing the formation of volatile sulfur compounds that contribute to bad breath.

The benefits of using Cinnamomum verum essential oils to treat bad breath include its natural, non-invasive, and cost-effective approach to oral health, making it an attractive alternative to conventional mouthwashes and breath fresheners.
