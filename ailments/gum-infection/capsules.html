10 Best Herbal Capsules For Gum Infection

Herbal capsules have gained popularity as a natural remedy for gum infections due to their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Ingredients such as clove, neem, and green tea are commonly used in these capsules for their ability to reduce bacteria and soothe inflamed gums. These herbal formulations can help alleviate symptoms like swelling, bleeding, and bad breath associated with gingivitis and periodontal disease. Unlike conventional medications, herbal capsules are often perceived as safer with fewer side effects, though their effectiveness can vary based on the specific ingredients and quality of the product.

It is advisable to consult a dentist or healthcare professional before using herbal capsules as a primary treatment for gum infections.

1. Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a promising natural remedy for gum infections.

Herbal capsules containing salvia officinalis extract can help reduce bacterial growth in the mouth, thereby alleviating symptoms such as redness, swelling, and bleeding gums. These capsules are often used as a complementary therapy alongside conventional dental treatments to support oral health. The active compounds in sage, such as rosmarinic acid and flavonoids, contribute to its ability to combat oral pathogens and promote healing.

While generally safe, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before using sage capsules, especially for individuals with known allergies or existing medical conditions.
