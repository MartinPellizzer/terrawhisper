10 Best Herbal Baths For Gum Infection

Herbal baths for gum infection are a natural, alternative remedy used to treat gum disease and inflammation of the gums, also known as periodontal disease.

These baths involve soaking the affected area in a solution of herbs and other natural ingredients, which have anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties that help to reduce swelling, ease pain, and promote healing.

Some popular examples of herbal baths for gum infection include aloe vera and tea tree oil baths, which have antibacterial properties that help to combat the underlying infection, while chamomile and calendula baths provide anti-inflammatory relief, and eucalyptus and peppermint baths help to reduce swelling and ease pain.

Additionally, herbal baths made with ingredients like turmeric, garlic, and ginger have also been shown to have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, making them effective in treating gum infections.
