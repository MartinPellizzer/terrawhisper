10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Eczema

Herbal lozenges for eczema are natural remedies that aim to soothe skin irritation and reduce inflammation through the use of plant-based ingredients.

These lozenges often contain herbs such as calendula, chamomile, and licorice root, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. While they may provide temporary relief from symptoms like dryness and itching, they are typically used as complementary treatments rather than a primary cure for eczema. It is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using herbal lozenges, especially if you have allergies or are taking other medications.

Overall, herbal lozenges can be a gentle option for managing eczema symptoms, but they should be part of a broader skincare and treatment plan.
