Echinacea purpurea herbal capsules are commonly used to support the immune system and may help alleviate symptoms like chills associated with colds or mild viral infections.

While some studies suggest echinacea may reduce the duration and severity of cold symptoms, its effectiveness for specifically targeting chills remains inconclusive.
