1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale, commonly known as ginger, has been traditionally used for its warming and soothing properties, making it a popular ingredient in herbal lozenges for alleviating symptoms of chills.

These lozenges work by stimulating circulation and promoting a sense of warmth in the body, which can help reduce the feeling of coldness associated with chills. Herbal lozenges made from zingiber officinale are often preferred for their natural formulation, offering a gentle and effective remedy without the side effects of pharmaceutical drugs.

Regular use of these lozenges can provide relief from mild chills and support overall respiratory wellness during cold or flu-like symptoms.
