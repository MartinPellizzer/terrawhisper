10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Bleeding Gums

Herbal tinctures for bleeding gums are concentrated liquid extracts made from various plants known for their anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

These tinctures can be applied directly to the gums using a cotton swab or diluted with water for safer use. They are often used as a natural alternative to conventional mouthwashes or medications.

1. Salvia (Salvia officinalis)

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its potential to address bleeding gums.

Herbal tinctures made from salvia officinalis are often prepared by soaking the dried plant in alcohol to extract its active compounds, such as flavonoids and essential oils. These tinctures may help reduce inflammation and promote healing due to their antimicrobial and astringent properties. When used as a mouth rinse or applied directly to the gums, salvia tinctures can soothe irritation and potentially reduce bleeding.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using these tinctures, especially for persistent or severe gum issues.
