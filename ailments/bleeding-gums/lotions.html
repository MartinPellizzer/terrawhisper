10 Best Herbal Lotions For Bleeding Gums

Herbal lotions can be a natural and soothing remedy for bleeding gums, often containing ingredients like aloe vera, chamomile, or calendula, which have anti-inflammatory and healing properties.

These lotions help reduce irritation and promote gum tissue repair by providing a gentle, cooling effect that calms inflamed areas. They are typically applied directly to the gums using a clean applicator or cotton swab, making them easy to use at home. Unlike harsh chemical treatments, herbal lotions are generally safe for daily use and can be incorporated into a holistic oral care routine.

However, it is advisable to consult a dentist if bleeding gums persist, as they may indicate a more serious underlying condition such as gingivitis or periodontal disease.

1. Salvia (Salvia officinalis)

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used for its astringent and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable ingredient in herbal lotions for bleeding gums.

These lotions often contain extracts or essential oils of sage, which can help reduce inflammation and promote healing in the mouth. The astringent nature of sage helps to tighten the gum tissue, potentially reducing bleeding and improving overall gum health. When applied topically, sage-based lotions may also help combat oral bacteria that contribute to gum disease.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using such products, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking medications.
