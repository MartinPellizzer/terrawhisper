13 Best Herbal Teas For Gum Pain

Herbal teas for gum pain are natural remedies made from various plant extracts that are used to alleviate discomfort and inflammation associated with gum diseases, such as gingivitis and periodontitis.

These teas offer numerous benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties that help reduce swelling and prevent infection.

Popular herbal teas used to treat gum pain include peppermint tea, which eases digestion and reduces inflammation, ginger tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties and soothes pain, chamomile tea, which calms the mind and body, and eucalyptus tea, which reduces swelling and fights infection.

Additionally, other herbal teas like clove tea, licorice root tea, and sage tea are also used to treat gum pain due to their antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties, making them effective alternatives to traditional medications.
