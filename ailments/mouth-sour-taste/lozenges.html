10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Sour Taste In Mouth

Herbal lozenges are a natural remedy designed to alleviate a sour taste in the mouth, often caused by digestive issues, acid reflux, or bacterial imbalances.

These lozenges typically contain ingredients like licorice root, ginger, and mint, which have soothing and antimicrobial properties. They work by neutralizing acid, reducing inflammation in the throat, and promoting saliva production to cleanse the mouth. Many herbal lozenges are sugar-free and suitable for people with dietary restrictions, making them a safe and effective option for daily use.

Regular use of these lozenges can help restore a balanced oral environment and improve overall comfort.
