1. Hypericum perforatum capsules

Hypericum perforatum capsules, commonly known as St. John's Wort, have been traditionally used to treat mild to moderate depression due to its antidepressant properties.

The bioactive constituents, such as hyperforin and hypericin, help to modulate the neurotransmitters serotonin, dopamine, and norepinephrine, thereby alleviating symptoms of depression.

By enhancing the levels of these neurotransmitters, St. John's Wort capsules help to improve mood, reduce anxiety, and promote a sense of well-being.

The benefits of using Hypericum perforatum capsules for depression include a natural and non-addictive treatment option, with studies suggesting a rapid onset of action and improved quality of life.

The benefits of Valeriana officinalis capsules in treating depression include improved mood, reduced anxiety, and enhanced sleep quality, making it a promising natural remedy for managing this complex condition.
