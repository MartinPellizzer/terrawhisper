Hypericum perforatum herbal creams are topical formulations that may help alleviate depressive symptoms when applied to the skin, though they are typically used in conjunction with oral supplements. While some studies suggest potential benefits, the effectiveness of these creams can vary, and they may interact with other medications, necessitating consultation with a healthcare provider.

Overall, hypericum perforatum herbal creams offer a natural alternative for managing depression, but their use should be carefully considered and monitored.
