1. Puncture vine (Tribulus terrestris)

Tribulus terrestris, commonly known as puncture vine, is a herbal plant traditionally used to support male reproductive health.

Its essential oils, derived from the plant's seeds and leaves, contain bioactive compounds that may enhance libido and improve sperm production. Some studies suggest that Tribulus terrestris can help increase sperm count and motility by stimulating hormone production, particularly testosterone. However, it is important to note that while some anecdotal and preliminary research supports these benefits, more rigorous scientific studies are needed to confirm its efficacy.

As with any herbal supplement, it should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional to ensure safety and proper dosage.
