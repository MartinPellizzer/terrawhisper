10 Best Herbal Essential Oils For Low Sperm Count

Herbal essential oils have been explored for their potential to support reproductive health, including improving low sperm count.

1. Tribulus terrestris

Tribulus terrestris, commonly known as puncture vine, is a herbal remedy that has been traditionally used to support male reproductive health.

Its essential oils, derived from the plant's flowers and leaves, contain bioactive compounds such as saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which may enhance testosterone production and improve sperm quality. Some studies suggest that Tribulus terrestris can help increase sperm count and motility by stimulating the hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal axis. However, while anecdotal evidence and traditional use support its potential benefits, scientific research on its efficacy for low sperm count remains limited and inconclusive.

It is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before using Tribulus terrestris essential oils, especially for individuals with underlying health conditions or those taking other medications.
