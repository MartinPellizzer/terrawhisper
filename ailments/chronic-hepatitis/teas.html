11 Best Herbal Teas For Chronic Hepatitis

Herbal teas for chronic hepatitis are a natural and non-invasive approach to treating this liver disease, characterized by inflammation and scarring of the liver.

The benefits of using herbal teas to treat chronic hepatitis include reducing inflammation, improving liver function, and boosting the immune system.

Examples of herbal teas that have shown promise in treating chronic hepatitis include milk thistle, which contains silymarin that helps protect the liver from damage; dandelion root, which has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce liver inflammation; turmeric, which contains curcumin that has potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects; and licorice root, which has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce liver damage.

Additionally, other herbal teas such as peppermint, chamomile, and ginseng may also help alleviate symptoms associated with chronic hepatitis, such as fatigue and digestive issues.
