10 Best Herbal Lotions For Chronic Hepatitis

Herbal lotions have gained attention as potential complementary treatments for chronic hepatitis, offering a natural approach to support liver health and reduce inflammation.

These lotions typically contain herbs such as milk thistle, dandelion, and licorice root, which are believed to have hepatoprotective properties. While some studies suggest that certain herbal ingredients may help detoxify the liver and improve overall function, more rigorous clinical research is needed to confirm their efficacy and safety. Herbal lotions should not replace conventional medical treatments but may be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional as part of an integrative care plan.

It is important for individuals with chronic hepatitis to consult with their doctor before incorporating any herbal remedies into their regimen.
