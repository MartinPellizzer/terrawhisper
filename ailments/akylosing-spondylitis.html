Ankylosing Spondylitis Causes, Medicinal Herbs And Herbal Preparations

Ankylosing spondylitis is a chronic inflammatory disease that affects the spine and other joints, causing pain, stiffness, and eventual fusion of the vertebrae.

Herbal remedies have been used to treat ankylosing spondylitis due to their anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties, which can help alleviate symptoms and slow disease progression.

Herbs such as turmeric (due to its high curcumin content), ginger (for its anti-inflammatory properties), boswellia (for its ability to reduce joint inflammation), willow bark (containing salicylic acid, similar to aspirin), and devil's claw (for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects) have been traditionally used to treat ankylosing spondylitis.

Herbal preparations such as turmeric capsules, ginger tea, boswellia oil, willow bark tincture, and devil's claw supplements can be used to treat ankylosing spondylitis, with the latter often recommended in combination with conventional medications to enhance their effectiveness.
