10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Benign Prostatic Hypertrophy

Herbal lozenges for benign prostatic hypertrophy (BPH) are natural supplements designed to support urinary health and alleviate symptoms associated with an enlarged prostate.

These lozenges often contain ingredients such as saw palmetto, pygeum, and beta-sitosterol, which are known for their potential anti-inflammatory and anti-androgenic properties. They are typically used as a complementary therapy to conventional treatments, offering a gentler alternative for individuals seeking non-pharmacological options. While some studies suggest they may help reduce urinary frequency and improve flow, their efficacy can vary, and they should not replace professional medical advice.

It is important to consult a healthcare provider before starting any herbal supplement to ensure safety and appropriateness for individual health conditions.
