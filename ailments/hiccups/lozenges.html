10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Hiccups

Herbal lozenges for hiccups are natural remedies that combine soothing herbs with menthol or other aromatic compounds to help alleviate the persistent, involuntary spasms of the diaphragm.

These lozenges are often formulated with ingredients like fennel, ginger, peppermint, and licorice root, which are known for their ability to relax the muscles and ease irritation in the throat. By stimulating the vagus nerve or providing a cooling effect, these lozenges may help break the hiccup cycle and offer quick relief. They are generally considered safe for most adults and children, though it's advisable to consult a healthcare professional if hiccups persist for an extended period.

Overall, herbal lozenges offer a gentle, alternative approach to managing hiccups without the side effects of over-the-counter medications.
