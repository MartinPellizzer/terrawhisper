10 Best Herbal Lozenges For White Patches In Month

Herbal lozenges are popular natural remedies used to address white patches in the mouth, often associated with conditions like oral thrush or leukoplakia.

These lozenges typically contain ingredients such as licorice root, chamomile, and sage, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. They work by soothing the oral mucosa and reducing fungal or bacterial overgrowth that may cause the patches. Regular use of herbal lozenges can help alleviate symptoms and promote healing without the side effects of conventional medications.

However, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment plan.
