1. Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Curcuma longa, commonly known as turmeric, is a popular herbal remedy used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

When formulated into capsules, curcuma longa can be a convenient and effective way to support the treatment of white patches, such as those seen in conditions like vitiligo. These capsules typically contain curcumin, the active compound in turmeric, which has been studied for its potential to promote skin pigmentation and reduce oxidative stress. Some clinical studies suggest that curcumin may help in repigmenting depigmented areas, though results can vary among individuals.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional before starting any herbal supplement, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
