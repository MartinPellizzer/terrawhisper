10 Best Herbal Creams For White Patches In Month

Herbal creams are increasingly popular for treating white patches on the skin, often associated with conditions like vitiligo or eczema.

These creams typically contain natural ingredients such as neem, turmeric, aloe vera, and calendula, which are believed to have soothing and restorative properties. They are often preferred for their mild, non-irritating nature compared to chemical-based treatments. Many users report improvement in skin tone and texture after consistent use, though results can vary.

It is advisable to consult a dermatologist before starting any herbal treatment to ensure safety and effectiveness.

1. Aloe vera (Aloe barbadensis)

Aloe barbadensis, commonly known as aloe vera, is often used in herbal creams to address white patches on the skin, which may be associated with conditions like vitiligo or fungal infections.

These creams contain aloe vera gel, which is rich in anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that can help soothe and rejuvenate the skin. The hydrating and regenerative effects of aloe vera may promote skin cell renewal, potentially improving the appearance of white patches over time.
