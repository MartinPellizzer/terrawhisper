10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Bitter Taste In Mouth

Herbal lozenges are commonly used to alleviate a bitter taste in the mouth, often caused by digestive issues, oral infections, or side effects of medications.

These lozenges typically contain natural ingredients such as ginger, licorice root, and peppermint, which help to soothe the throat and neutralize unpleasant flavors. The soothing properties of these herbs can also stimulate saliva production, which further helps in washing away the bitter taste. Many herbal lozenges are free from artificial additives, making them a preferred choice for individuals seeking natural remedies.

Regular use of these lozenges can provide relief and improve overall oral comfort.
