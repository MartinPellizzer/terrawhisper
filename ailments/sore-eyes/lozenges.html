10 Best Herbal Lozenges For Sore Eyes

Herbal lozenges for sore eyes are traditional remedies that combine natural ingredients to soothe eye discomfort and promote healing.

These lozenges often contain herbs like chamomile, calendula, and echinacea, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. While primarily designed for sore throats, some herbal lozenges are formulated to provide relief for eye irritation by reducing redness and dryness. They are typically used as a complementary therapy alongside conventional treatments for conditions like conjunctivitis or dry eye syndrome.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal lozenges for eye issues to ensure safety and effectiveness.
