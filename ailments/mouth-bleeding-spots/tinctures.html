10 Best Herbal Tinctures For Bleeding Spots In Mouth

Salvia officinalis, commonly known as sage, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential benefits in addressing oral health issues, including bleeding spots in the mouth.

Sage tinctures, which are concentrated liquid extracts of the plant, are often prepared using alcohol as a solvent to preserve the active compounds. These tinctures may help reduce inflammation and promote healing due to the presence of antimicrobial and astringent properties in sage. When applied directly to bleeding spots, sage tinctures may help soothe irritation and potentially reduce bleeding by strengthening the delicate oral tissues.
