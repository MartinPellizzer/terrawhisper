1. Boosts immune system

tree, boosts the immune system by stimulating the body's natural defenses, allowing it to fight off infections and diseases more effectively. The main active constituents of Tabebuia spp. that contribute to this benefit are lapachol and beta-lapachone, which have been found to have antibacterial, antifungal, and antiviral properties.

The inner bark and heartwood of the Tabebuia spp. tree are the primary parts of the plant used for their medicinal benefits. The most common medicinal preparations include teas, tinctures, capsules, and powders made from the bark or wood of the tree. The benefit of a boosted immune system from Tabebuia spp.
