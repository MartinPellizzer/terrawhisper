10 best herbal teas for fatigue

If you're feeling fatigued, certain herbal teas can provide a natural energy boost.

Peppermint tea is known to improve mental alertness and increase energy levels. Ginseng tea has been shown to help fight fatigue and promote overall well-being.

Another great option is matcha green tea, which contains a higher concentration of caffeine than other teas, but also contains L-theanine, an amino acid that helps to slow the absorption of caffeine, providing a sustained energy boost without the crash. Finally, licorice root tea can help combat fatigue by regulating cortisol levels and supporting adrenal function.

Just be sure to consult with a healthcare provider if you're experiencing ongoing fatigue, as it could be a sign of an underlying medical condition.

1. Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha tea reduces fatigue by providing adaptogenic properties that help the body manage stress and anxiety.

This herbal tea, derived from the ashwagandha plant, contains withanolides that restore balance to the body's systems. By consuming ashwagandha tea, individuals improve their energy levels, boost their stamina, and foster mental clarity. Furthermore, this tea supports restorative sleep, which contributes to overall well-being and reduced fatigue.

Regularly drinking ashwagandha tea enhances the body's capability to handle physical and mental stress, thus diminishing feelings of weakness and tiredness.

  • Roots: The ashwagandha roots are used to make medicinal tea for fatigue due to their potent adaptogenic properties that combat stress-induced fatigue.
  • Leaves: The ashwagandha leaves have mild sedative effects that help alleviate fatigue by promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality.
  • Seeds: The ashwagandha seeds are rich in withanolides, which combat fatigue by providing the body with energy and enhancing physical performance.
