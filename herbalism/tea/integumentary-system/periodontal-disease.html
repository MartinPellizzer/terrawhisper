10 Best Herbal Teas For Periodontal Disease

Herbal teas for periodontal disease are a natural approach to preventing and treating this condition, which is caused by inflammation and infection of the gums.

These teas help by reducing inflammation, preventing bacterial growth, and promoting healthy gum tissue. The best herbal teas for periodontal disease include Salvia officinalis, Curcuma longa, Melaleuca alternifolia, Echinacea purpurea, Ginkgo biloba, Glycyrrhiza glabra, Eucalyptus globulus, Zingiber officinale, Syzygium aromaticum, and Melissa officinalis.

If left untreated, periodontal disease can lead to tooth loss, difficulty eating, and even systemic infections, significantly impacting one's quality of life and overall health.
