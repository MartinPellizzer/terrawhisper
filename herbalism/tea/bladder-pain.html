10 best herbal teas for bladder pain

When it comes to herbal teas for bladder pain, several varieties have been noted for their potential beneficial effects. Firstly, marshmallow root tea is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe and reduce irritation in the bladder. Secondly, corn silk tea has been traditionally used to treat bladder infections and symptoms of urinary tract issues. Lastly, buchu leaf tea is also recommended for bladder health due to its diuretic properties that can help flush out toxins and bacteria from the urinary tract. However, it's always important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new herbal remedies.

8. Uva Ursi

Uva ursi tea contains a compound called arbutin, which converts to hydroquinone in the body. This tea inhibits the growth of E. coli bacteria, a common cause of urinary tract infections and bladder discomfort. Uva ursi further soothes the bladder lining, reducing irritation and inflammation.
