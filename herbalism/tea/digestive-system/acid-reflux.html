The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic marshmallow tincture for acid reflux.

  1. Measure 2 cups of dried marshmallow root and add to a pot with 8 cups of water, then bring to a boil.

What are the most common causes of acid reflux that are treatable with herbal tincture?

The primary causes of acid reflux that are treatable with herbal tinctures are lifestyle-related factors.

These include poor diet choices, such as consuming spicy, fatty, or greasy foods, overeating, and lying down immediately after meals. Additionally, stress and obesity can contribute to acid reflux, as they increase abdominal pressure and weaken the lower esophageal sphincter.

By addressing these underlying causes with herbal tinctures and lifestyle modifications, acid reflux symptoms can be effectively managed.
