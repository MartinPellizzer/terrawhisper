10 best herbal teas for ear infection

Certain herbal teas may provide relief for symptoms associated with ear infections, such as inflammation, pain, and congestion.

Warm liquids like herbal teas can help soothe a sore throat and alleviate congestion. One of the best herbal teas for ear infections is mullein tea, which has anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties that can help reduce ear pain and inflammation.

Another good option is chamomile tea, which has natural calming and pain-relieving effects. Ginger tea is also beneficial for reducing inflammation and alleviating nausea caused by ear infections.

However, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before using herbal remedies, especially for children or people with underlying health conditions.

1. Chamomile

Chamomile tea provides relief from ear infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The hot tea, when used as a compress, helps to alleviate pain by reducing inflammation in the Eustachian tubes and outer ear. Chamomile's antimicrobial properties combat bacterial and viral infections causing the discomfort. Moreover, its soothing effects promote relaxation, which aids in managing the stress and discomfort associated with ear infections.

As a natural remedy, chamomile tea serves as a gentle and accessible alternative to over-the-counter medications.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in chamomile tea that help with ear infection.

  • Flowers: Chamomile flowers contain chamazulene and bisabolol, compounds with anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that help alleviate ear infection symptoms when consumed as a tea.
  • Leaves: The leaves of chamomile plants have phenolic compounds, which exhibit antimicrobial effects, making them useful for treating ear infections when brewed into a medicinal tea.
  • Seeds: Chamomile seeds are rich in flavonoids and essential oils that possess anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial qualities, making them suitable for medicinal tea aimed at managing ear infections.
