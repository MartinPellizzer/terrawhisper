10 Best Herbal Teas For Weight Loss

Herbal teas, often made from natural plants and fruits, can aid in weight loss by helping to increase metabolism, creating a feeling of fullness, and providing a low-calorie alternative to sugary drinks.

Some herbal teas, such as those containing green tea or pu-erh tea, have been shown to boost metabolism and fat oxidation, helping the body to burn calories more efficiently. Additionally, drinking herbal teas can help to reduce hunger and increase satiety, making it easier to consume fewer calories throughout the day. Furthermore, herbal teas are typically calorie-free or very low in calories, making them a smart choice for those looking to lose or maintain weight.

However, it's important to note that while herbal teas can support weight loss efforts, they are not a magic solution and should be used in conjunction with a balanced diet and regular exercise.

1. Green Tea

Green tea helps with weight loss because it contains bioactive compounds, such as catechins and caffeine, known to increase metabolism and fat burning.

Catechins are antioxidants that inhibit the formation of new fat cells and help break down existing ones. Caffeine in green tea acts as a stimulant, boosting energy levels and suppressing appetite, leading to reduced caloric intake. Additionally, green tea has been shown to increase thermogenesis, the process by which the body burns calories to produce heat, further contributing to weight loss efforts.

With these properties, regular consumption of green tea can aid in weight management and support overall health goals.

The following procedure explains how to make green tea tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 8 ounces of water and let it cool down to 160-170°F (71-77°C), then steep 1 teaspoon of green tea leaves or 1 green tea bag in it for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Remove and discard the tea leaves or bag, and add the juice of half a lemon (about 1-2 tablespoons) and 1-2 teaspoons of raw honey to the green tea.
  3. Mix well until the honey is fully dissolved, then add 1-2 inches of sliced ginger and 1 cinnamon stick to the tea.
  4. Let the ginger and cinnamon steep for another 5-10 minutes, then strain and discard the solids.
  5. Drink the green tea immediately, preferably between meals or 30-60 minutes before workouts, for optimal weight loss benefits. Enjoy!

2. Oolong Tea

Oolong tea helps with weight loss because it is a type of tea that is partially fermented and oxidized, which gives it a unique set of properties that can aid in weight loss.

First, oolong tea increases metabolism, allowing the body to burn fat faster and more efficiently. Additionally, oolong tea has been shown to improve digestion and reduce inflammation, both of which can contribute to weight gain if left unchecked.

Furthermore, oolong tea contains caffeine and antioxidants, which can help to suppress appetite and reduce cravings, making it easier to stick to a healthy diet. Finally, oolong tea has been shown to boost energy levels, which can help individuals stay active and burn more calories throughout the day.

Overall, oolong tea is a powerful tool for weight loss, offering a range of benefits that can support a healthy lifestyle.

The following procedure explains how to make oolong tea tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 16 ounces of fresh, cold water to a rolling boil (200-212°F/93-100°C).
  2. Steep 1-2 teaspoons of high-quality Oolong tea leaves in a tea infuser or teapot with a built-in strainer for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Remove the tea leaves or infuser to prevent over-steeping, which can make the tea bitter.
  4. For optimal weight loss benefits, drink 2-3 cups of Oolong tea daily, preferably between meals or 30-60 minutes before workouts.
  5. Store unused Oolong tea in an airtight container in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight, to maintain freshness and potency.

3. Yerba Mate

Yerba mate tea helps with weight loss because it is a natural appetite suppressant, reducing the desire to overeat and aiding in portion control.

Additionally, it contains compounds that stimulate the metabolism, allowing the body to burn more calories throughout the day. Yerba mate tea is also a natural diuretic, helping to eliminate excess fluid retention and bloating. Furthermore, it is rich in antioxidants that support overall health and well-being, which can contribute to a more effective weight loss journey.

Finally, yerba mate tea has a mild, pleasant taste, making it a more enjoyable and sustainable alternative to other weight loss drinks.

The following procedure explains how to make yerba mate tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 1 liter of water and let it cool down until it reaches a temperature of 80°C (176°F) - this is the ideal temperature for yerba mate tea to extract its weight loss benefits without releasing excess tannins that can cause bitterness.
  2. In a gourd or large mug, place 1 tablespoon (7-10 grams) of loose-leaf yerba mate and add 1-2 tablespoons of lemon juice (optional, to aid in nutrient absorption and add a refreshing taste).
  3. Pour 1/4 of the hot water into the gourd, let it sit for 1 minute to allow the tea to bloom, then gently stir the mixture with a bombilla or a spoon.
  4. Fill the gourd with the remaining hot water, place the bombilla (metal straw) in the gourd, making sure it reaches the bottom, and enjoy your yerba mate tea while it's still warm.
  5. Refill the gourd with hot water 2-3 more times, as the tea will release more antioxidants and caffeine with each infusion, making it a cost-effective and sustainable choice for weight loss.

4. Guar Gum

Guar gum tea helps with weight loss because Guar gum tea aids in weight loss because it creates a sense of fullness due to its ability to expand in the stomach, reducing the overall food intake and helping to control hunger.

This soluble fiber, derived from the guar plant, forms a gel-like substance when combined with liquids, slowing down the digestion process and prolonging the feeling of fullness. Moreover, guar gum has been shown to lower the absorption of dietary fat, as it binds to the fat molecules in the stomach, making it easier to excrete excess fat from the body. By promoting healthy gut bacteria, guar gum can also enhance nutrient absorption and overall digestion, further supporting weight loss efforts.

Ultimately, incorporating guar gum tea into a balanced diet and exercise routine can help individuals achieve their weight loss goals by curbing appetite, reducing fat absorption, and improving digestive health.

The following procedure explains how to make guar gum tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in 1/8 teaspoon of guar gum powder until fully dissolved.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer into a cup.
  5. Drink the guar gum tea once cooled, up to three times a day before meals for weight loss.

5. Bitterghaap

Bitterghaap tea helps with weight loss because it is rich in antioxidants and other beneficial compounds that support a healthy metabolism and promote fat oxidation.

Additionally, the tea has a natural appetite-suppressing effect, which can help reduce overall calorie intake. Furthermore, Bitterghaap tea contains caffeine, a stimulant that can increase energy expenditure and enhance physical performance during exercise, potentially aiding in weight management. Moreover, the tea's bitter taste may help stimulate the production of digestive enzymes, aiding in the breakdown and absorption of nutrients, which can support a healthy weight.

However, it's important to note that Bitterghaap tea is not a magic solution for weight loss and should be consumed as part of a balanced diet and exercise routine.

The following procedure explains how to make bitterghaap tea for weight loss.

  1. Gather your ingredients: 1 teaspoon of bittergourd powder, 1 teaspoon of green tea leaves, 1 teaspoon of honey, and 2 cups of water.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot, then add 1 teaspoon of bittergourd powder. Allow it to simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of green tea leaves to the pot, continue simmering for an additional 3 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, then mix in 1 teaspoon of honey for taste.
  5. Enjoy your warm and healthy bittergourd-green tea for weight loss.

6. Ginger

Ginger tea helps with weight loss because Ginger tea aids in weight loss because it has thermogenic properties, which boost metabolism and increase calorie burning.

Additionally, ginger has been shown to suppress appetite and reduce cravings, helping people consume fewer calories throughout the day. Furthermore, ginger may improve digestion and nutrient absorption, allowing the body to make better use of the fuel it takes in. The antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties of ginger tea may also help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress, which are associated with obesity and weight gain.

Finally, ginger tea is a natural and caffeine-free alternative to other weight loss drinks, making it a healthier and more sustainable option for long-term weight management.

The following procedure explains how to make ginger tea for weight loss.

  1. Peel and thinly slice 1-2 inches of fresh ginger root; add to a teapot or heatproof pitcher.
  2. Boil 4 cups of water in a separate pot, then pour over the ginger slices; let steep for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Optional: add 1 cinnamon stick, a few cloves, or a dash of turmeric for added flavor and weight loss benefits.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the ginger and spices; add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
  5. Sip the tea throughout the day, especially before meals, to aid in digestion and weight loss. Enjoy!

7. Licorice Root

Licorice root tea helps with weight loss because Licorice root tea aids in weight loss because it contains compounds that can help suppress appetite and reduce cravings for sweets and salty snacks, potentially leading to a decrease in overall calorie intake.

Additionally, licorice root may help boost metabolism by enhancing the function of the adrenal glands, which can increase the body's ability to burn fat. Furthermore, licorice root has diuretic properties, which can help reduce bloating and water retention, contributing to a slimmer appearance. However, it's important to note that licorice root tea should not be relied on as the sole method for weight loss and should be used in conjunction with a healthy diet and exercise regimen.

As with any supplement, it's always best to consult with a healthcare professional before incorporating licorice root tea into a weight loss plan.

The following procedure explains how to make licorice root tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of dried licorice root (Glycyrrhiza glabra) to the boiling water.
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover the saucepan, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the used licorice root.
  5. Sip and enjoy the tea up to three times a day, 30 minutes before meals to support weight loss.

8. Dandelion Root

Dandelion root tea helps with weight loss because Dandelion root tea aids in weight loss because it acts as a natural diuretic, promoting fluid loss and reducing bloating.

It also contains compounds that stimulate the production of bile in the liver, which can help improve digestion and metabolism. Furthermore, dandelion root tea is low in calories and can help curb appetite, making it easier to maintain a calorie deficit for weight loss. Additionally, it has been suggested that dandelion root may increase fat burning, although more research is needed in this area.

Overall, incorporating dandelion root tea into a balanced diet and exercise routine can support weight loss efforts by promoting a healthy metabolism and reducing water retention.

The following procedure explains how to make dandelion root tea for weight loss.

  1. Gather 2-3 dandelion roots (preferably from a pesticide-free source) and thoroughly wash them to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Chop the cleaned dandelion roots into small, thin slices and place them in a pot.
  3. Add 4 cups of water to the pot and bring it to a boil, allowing the dandelion root slices to simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Strain the liquid from the pot into a teapot or a large container, discarding the used dandelion root slices.
  5. Pour the dandelion root tea into a cup, optionally adding a small amount of honey or lemon for flavor, and enjoy it 2-3 times a day, preferably 30 minutes before meals.

9. Turmeric

Turmeric tea helps with weight loss because Turmeric tea aids in weight loss primarily due to the presence of curcumin, a bioactive substance that helps reduce fat tissue growth and promote fat loss.

Additionally, curcumin enhances the function of adiponectin, a hormone responsible for regulating metabolism and energy use, thereby promoting weight loss. Furthermore, turmeric tea has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce inflammation in fat cells, which is linked to obesity. Moreover, turmeric tea can help improve insulin sensitivity, preventing the storage of excess fat and promoting weight loss.

Lastly, turmeric tea can also act as a natural appetite suppressant, reducing overall calorie intake and assisting in weight management.

The following procedure explains how to make turmeric tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in 1 teaspoon of grated fresh turmeric or 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric.
  3. Allow the mixture to simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding any solids.
  5. Add a squeeze of lemon and a dollop of honey for flavor, if desired. Enjoy 1-2 cups of this tea daily before meals for weight loss.

10. Cinnamon

Cinnamon tea helps with weight loss because Cinnamon tea aids in weight loss because it is a natural metabolism booster, helping your body to burn calories at a faster rate.

Additionally, cinnamon has been shown to have appetite-suppressing qualities, reducing cravings and overall food intake. Furthermore, cinnamon tea is a zero-calorie beverage, making it an excellent alternative to high-calorie drinks. Moreover, it helps to balance blood sugar levels, which can contribute to maintaining a healthy weight.

Lastly, cinnamon is rich in antioxidants, which can help to reduce inflammation and improve overall health, further supporting weight loss efforts.

The following procedure explains how to make cinnamon tea for weight loss.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 cinnamon stick (or 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon) and 1 teaspoon of honey to the boiling water.
  3. Reduce heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the cinnamon stick and any solids.
  5. Sip slowly and enjoy up to 3 times a day, 30 minutes before meals for best results.
