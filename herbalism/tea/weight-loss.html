10 best herbal teas for weight loss

When it comes to herbal teas that may support weight loss, a few stand out.

Green tea is one of the most well-known for its weight loss benefits, as it contains a catechin called EGCG that can boost metabolism and fat oxidation. Pu-erh tea is another good option, as it is said to help break down fat cells and lower cholesterol.

Rooibos tea, meanwhile, is caffeine-free and rich in antioxidants, which can help reduce inflammation and improve digestion. Lastly, holy basil tea is known for its ability to regulate cortisol levels, which can help reduce stress-related eating and, in turn, support weight loss.

However, it's important to note that while these teas may support weight loss efforts, they are not a magic solution and should be combined with a healthy diet and exercise routine for best results.

1. Green Tea

Green tea assists with weight loss due to its natural contents of caffeine and EGCG, a type of catechin.

These compounds boost metabolism, causing the body to burn more calories. Furthermore, green tea inhibits the enzyme that breaks down the hormone norepinephrine, which signals fat cells to break down fat.

As a result, fat oxidation increases, leading to a higher rate of fat burn. Additionally, green tea increases the levels of hormones that signal fullness to the brain, reducing overall food intake.

Therefore, green tea's natural properties promote weight loss by increasing calorie burn and reducing food intake.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in green tea tea that help with weight loss.

  • Leaves: Contain high levels of catechins, a type of antioxidant that helps boost metabolism and increase fat burning.
  • Buds: Rich in antioxidants and have been shown to boost weight loss by increasing fat oxidation and improving insulin sensitivity.
  • Stems: Contain a variety of beneficial compounds, including caffeine and catechins, which work together to increase energy expenditure and support weight loss.
  • Seeds: High in fiber, healthy fats, and antioxidants, seeds from the green tea plant can help promote feelings of fullness, reduce appetite, and support overall weight management.

2. Oolong

Oolong tea assists with weight loss due to its unique polyphenol content, which includes epigallocatechin gallate, epicatechin gallate, and theaflavins.

These polyphenols boost fat oxidation and increase metabolism, helping the body burn up to 12% more energy. Additionally, oolong tea prevents the absorption of dietary fats and enhances the function of enzymes responsible for breaking down fats. Furthermore, oolong's caffeine content supports weight loss by promoting lipolysis or the release of fat from fat cells, while its ability to balance blood sugar levels aids in controlling cravings.

Lastly, consistent consumption of oolong tea encourages overall gut health, facilitating a favorable environment for weight management and a healthy metabolism.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in oolong tea that help with weight loss.

  • Leaves: The leaves of the oolong plant contain high levels of polyphenols that stimulate fat oxidation and enhance weight loss.
  • Buds: Rich in catechins, the buds of the oolong plant help increase metabolism and reduce abdominal fat.
  • Stems: Oolong stems contain unique compounds that regulate the absorption of dietary fat, supporting weight loss efforts.
  • Flowers: The fragrant flowers of the oolong plant provide antioxidants that improve digestion and metabolic function, aiding weight management.

4. Guar Gum

Guar gum, a fiber sourced from the guar bean, forms a gel in the stomach when combined with fluid, promoting feelings of fullness and reducing appetite.

This gel also delays stomach emptying, causing a prolonged feeling of satiety and reducing the desire to eat. Moreover, guar gum inhibits the enzymes responsible for digesting dietary fats, allowing the body to absorb fewer fats and calories. Additionally, guar gum ferments in the gut, producing short-chain fatty acids that enhance metabolism and aid in weight loss.

Therefore, guar gum tea facilitates weight loss by promoting satiety, reducing fat absorption, and stimulating fat burning.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in guar gum tea that help with weight loss.

  • Leaves: Guar gum leaves are a natural source of dietary fiber, supporting healthy digestion and weight loss by reducing hunger and calorie intake.
  • Rhyzomes: Guar gum rhyzomes are known to slow stomach emptying, leading to a decrease in appetite and promoting weight loss.
