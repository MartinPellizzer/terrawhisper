10 best herbal teas for mucus in throat

When you have mucus in your throat, sipping on warm herbal teas can provide relief and soothe irritation. Some of the best herbal teas for this purpose include licorice root tea, which can help reduce inflammation and thin mucus; slippery elm tea, which creates a slippery film that coats and soothes mucous membranes; and thyme tea, known for its antimicrobial and expectorant properties that can help break up and expel mucus. Additionally, green tea and chamomile tea have anti-inflammatory effects and can help alleviate throat discomfort. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice, especially if symptoms persist.
