10 best herbal teas for viral hepatitis

While there is no definitive scientific evidence that any specific herbal tea can cure viral hepatitis, some herbal teas are known to support liver health.

Milk thistle tea, for example, contains silymarin which is believed to have protective effects on the liver. Dandelion tea is also known for its potential benefits in supporting liver function. However, it's important to consult with a healthcare professional before consuming these teas if you have viral hepatitis, as they may interact with any ongoing medications.

Additionally, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, including a balanced diet and regular exercise, is crucial in managing viral hepatitis.

Finally, milk thistle tea bolsters the immune system, further reinforcing the body's defense against hepatitis viruses.
