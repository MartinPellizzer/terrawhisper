10 best herbal teas for flat tummy

Certain herbal teas are known to help with digestion and bloating, which can contribute to a flatter tummy. Peppermint tea is well-known for its ability to soothe the digestive tract and reduce bloating. Ginger tea can also help by speeding up digestion and reducing inflammation. Dandelion tea is a natural diuretic, which can help reduce water retention and bloating. Fennel tea is another excellent option, as it can help relax the gastrointestinal muscles, reducing gas and bloating. For best results, try sipping on these herbal teas throughout the day, but be sure to avoid any sweeteners, as they can contribute to bloating.
