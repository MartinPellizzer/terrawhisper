The following procedure explaing how to make damiana tea for female libido.

  1. Heat 1 cup of water to boiling point, then turn off heat; add 1 teaspoon of dried damiana leaves, let steep for 15 minutes.
  2. Strain the tea to remove the leaves, discard leaves.
  3. Optional: add honey or lemon to taste.
  4. Drink 1 cup of damiana tea before bedtime or as needed.

The following procedure explaing how to make yohimbe tea for female libido.

  5. Consume the tea, up to one cup per day, to potentially support female libido; however, keep in mind that yohimbe may have strong side effects and interactions, so consult with a healthcare professional before use.
