10 Best Herbal Teas For Clear Skin

Herbal teas can help promote clear skin due to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Certain herbs, like chamomile and mint, have been shown to reduce inflammation and irritation, which can help diminish the appearance of acne and blemishes. Additionally, some herbs, such as burdock and nettle, have blood purifying properties that can help eliminate toxins from the body, which can also lead to clearer skin. Furthermore, drinking herbal teas can help keep the body hydrated, which is essential for maintaining healthy skin.

Lastly, some herbal teas, like rooibos and hibiscus, contain high levels of vitamin C and other antioxidants that can help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, promoting a clearer and more radiant complexion.

1. Nettle

Nettle tea helps with clear skin because it has anti-inflammatory properties that can reduce redness and swelling associated with acne, while its rich nutrient content, including vitamin C, helps build collagen and promotes skin cell growth.

The tea's ability to support the body's detoxification processes can also help eliminate toxins, reducing the likelihood of skin breakouts. Furthermore, nettle tea's antihistamine properties can alleviate allergic reactions that may manifest as skin issues.

Lastly, its astringent qualities can help tighten pores and reduce excess oil production, contributing to an overall clearer complexion.

The following procedure explains how to make nettle tea for clear skin.

  1. Gather 2 cups of fresh nettle leaves (Urtica dioica) from a clean, pesticide-free location, rinse them thoroughly, and then submerge in 4 cups of boiling water for 5-7 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds and achieve a deep green color.
  2. After steepment, strain the tea through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a teapot or heat-resistant container, catching and discarding any residual plant material while preserving the nutrient-rich liquid.
  3. Optional: To enhance the tea's skin-clearing properties, consider adding 1 tablespoon of honey, a natural anti-inflammatory and humectant, or a squeeze of lemon juice for its vitamin C content and additional antibacterial benefits.
  4. Serve the tea hot or chilled, as desired, and enjoy daily for at least 2-4 weeks to allow the skin-enhancing nutrients to accumulate and promote a clearer, more radiant complexion.
  5. For optimal freshness and potency, store any leftover nettle tea in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, gently reheating or serving chilled as preferred.

2. Burdock

Burdock tea helps with clear skin because it has anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties that can help reduce redness and swelling, while also fighting acne-causing bacteria.

It also contains antioxidants that help protect skin cells from damage caused by free radicals, which can contribute to a clearer complexion. Additionally, burdock tea is known to promote blood circulation and improve liver function, both of which can support healthy skin. The tea's diuretic properties can also help eliminate toxins from the body, which can further contribute to clearer skin.

Finally, burdock tea has been used traditionally as a depurative, helping to cleanse the blood and eliminate impurities, which can help reduce the likelihood of skin breakouts and promote a clearer, healthier complexion.

The following procedure explains how to make burdock tea for clear skin.

  1. Rinse and chop 2-3 tablespoons of dried burdock root into small pieces, then add them to a pot with 4 cups of water; bring the water to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds.
  3. Strain the tea into a teapot or a heat-resistant container, discarding the spent burdock root pieces.
  4. For added flavor and potential skin benefits, consider adding optional ingredients such as a slice of lemon, a teaspoon of honey, or a small piece of grated ginger before serving.
  5. Drink the burdock tea while warm, ideally 1-2 times a day, consistently for at least a few weeks to see improvements in skin clarity and complexion.

3. Dandelion

Dandelion tea helps with clear skin because it has anti-inflammatory properties that can reduce skin redness and swelling.

It also contains high levels of antioxidants that help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, promoting skin cell regeneration and a more even skin tone. Furthermore, dandelion tea has natural diuretic properties that can help eliminate toxins from the body, reducing the likelihood of skin breakouts and promoting a clearer complexion. Additionally, it is rich in vitamins A, C, and E, which are essential for maintaining healthy skin and preventing premature aging.

Lastly, dandelion tea has been shown to improve digestion and liver function, which can also contribute to clearer skin by promoting the elimination of waste and toxins from the body.

The following procedure explains how to make dandelion tea for clear skin.

  1. Gather 1-2 handfuls of fresh dandelion flowers, ensuring they are free of chemicals and pollutants, then rinse them gently in cold water and let them dry.
  2. Chop the dried dandelion flowers coarsely, then place them in a pot and add 4 cups of water; bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds from the flowers.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup or teapot, discarding the spent flowers, and let the tea cool down to a drinkable temperature; add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
  5. Drink 2-3 cups of this dandelion tea daily for at least a month, as clearer skin may take time to appear, and maintain a consistent skincare routine for optimal results.

4. Red Clover

Red clover tea helps with clear skin because it contains isoflavones, which are a type of plant estrogen that can help to regulate hormonal imbalances in the body.

These imbalances can often be a major cause of skin problems such as acne. Additionally, red clover has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce redness and swelling in the skin, which can also contribute to a clearer complexion.

Furthermore, red clover is rich in antioxidants, which can help to protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals and other environmental stressors. This can lead to healthier, more youthful-looking skin. Finally, red clover has been shown to have diuretic properties, which can help to flush toxins out of the body and support overall liver function.

A healthy liver is essential for clear, radiant skin.

The following procedure explains how to make red clover tea for clear skin.

  1. To make red clover tea for clear skin, start by boiling 2 cups of water in a pot, then add 2 teaspoons of dried red clover flowers and leaves; reduce heat to a simmer.
  2. Allow the mixture to steep for 15-20 minutes, covered, for the beneficial compounds from the red clover to be fully extracted.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the solid plant material, then let it cool down to a drinkable temperature.
  4. For added skin-clearing benefits, consider adding the juice of half a lemon and a small piece of grated ginger to your tea.
  5. Drink 2-3 cups of this red clover tea daily, consistently, for at least a few weeks to see improvements in your skin's clarity and overall health.

5. Yellow Dock

Yellow dock tea helps with clear skin because it contains high levels of iron and other minerals that support liver function, which is essential for healthy skin.

The tea's natural anti-inflammatory properties can help reduce redness and swelling associated with acne and other skin conditions. Yellow dock tea also has blood-purifying qualities, which can help eliminate toxins from the body that can lead to skin issues. Additionally, the tea's high levels of vitamin C can boost collagen production, promoting skin elasticity and overall skin health.

Finally, yellow dock tea's astringent properties can help tighten pores and reduce oil production, leading to clearer, healthier-looking skin.

The following procedure explains how to make yellow dock tea for clear skin.

  1. To make yellow dock tea, start by boiling 2 cups of water in a pot, then add 2 teaspoons of dried yellow dock root, reducing heat to a simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Strain the tea to remove the root, then let it cool down to a drinkable temperature.
  3. For added flavor and skin benefits, squeeze half a lemon and add 1 tablespoon of raw honey to the tea.
  4. Stir well until the honey is fully dissolved, creating a soothing and slightly sweet beverage.
  5. Drink the yellow dock tea once or twice a day, consistently, to help promote clear skin and support overall skin health.

6. Sarsaparilla

Sarsaparilla tea helps with clear skin because it has anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties that can help reduce skin inflammation and fight acne-causing bacteria.

Additionally, it contains compounds called saponins, which have been shown to have cleansing and purifying effects on the body, helping to eliminate toxins that can lead to skin problems. Sarsaparilla tea is also rich in antioxidants, which can help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals and promote skin cell regeneration for a more even and radiant complexion. Furthermore, it has been used traditionally as a blood purifier, helping to remove impurities from the blood that can manifest as skin issues.

Drinking sarsaparilla tea regularly can contribute to an overall improvement in skin health and appearance.

The following procedure explains how to make sarsaparilla tea for clear skin.

  1. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil, then add 1 tablespoon of dried sarsaparilla root and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds.
  2. After simmering, remove the pot from heat and add 1 teaspoon of honey (or to taste) and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice to the sarsaparilla tea, stirring well to dissolve the honey and balance the flavors.
  3. Allow the tea to cool down to a drinkable temperature, then strain the liquid through a fine-mesh sieve into a mug or teacup to remove the sarsaparilla root pieces.
  4. To enhance the tea's skin-clearing properties, add 1/2 teaspoon of chlorella powder or spirulina powder (optional) to the tea and stir well until fully combined.
  5. Drink the sarsaparilla tea while it's still warm, once or twice a day, for at least two weeks to see improvements in your skin's clarity and overall health.

7. Cleavers

Cleavers tea helps with clear skin because it has potent anti-inflammatory and depurative properties that assist in cleansing the body of toxins.

This natural diuretic herb supports the lymphatic system, helping to eliminate waste and reduce swelling, which can lead to healthier skin. Cleavers also contain essential nutrients, like vitamins A, C, and E, that promote skin cell regeneration and help maintain collagen levels, contributing to a smoother, more radiant complexion. Furthermore, its antibacterial and antifungal properties can help combat skin conditions such as acne, eczema, and psoriasis.

Drinking cleavers tea regularly can help reduce skin irritations and blemishes, resulting in a clearer, revitalized appearance.

The following procedure explains how to make cleavers tea for clear skin.

  1. Begin by boiling 2 cups of water, then add 2 teaspoons of dried cleavers herb, reducing heat to a simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the nutrients to steep.
  2. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the used herbs, then squeeze half a lemon into the tea for its vitamin C content and additional skin-clearing benefits.
  3. Add honey to taste, if desired, for its antibacterial properties and natural sweetness.
  4. Stir in 1 teaspoon of apple cider vinegar, which can help balance the skin's pH and reduce inflammation.
  5. Drink the tea while still warm, up to three times a day for clearer skin in a few weeks.

8. Milk Thistle

Milk thistle tea helps with clear skin because it contains silymarin, an antioxidant that helps reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, which can lead to skin breakouts.

Additionally, milk thistle has anti-inflammatory properties that can help soothe irritated skin and reduce redness. Furthermore, it has been shown to have a positive effect on liver health, which is important for maintaining clear skin as the liver plays a crucial role in eliminating toxins from the body. Milk thistle tea may also help regulate hormonal imbalances, which can contribute to acne and other skin issues.

Lastly, milk thistle has been used for centuries as a natural remedy for various health conditions, including skin problems, and its benefits have been supported by numerous scientific studies.

The following procedure explains how to make milk thistle tea for clear skin.

  1. To make milk thistle tea for clear skin, start by bringing 1 cup of water to a boil, then add 1 teaspoon of dried milk thistle flowers or 1 milk thistle tea bag to the boiling water.
  2. Allow the milk thistle to steep in the hot water for 5-10 minutes to extract its beneficial compounds and achieve a strong tea flavor.
  3. While the tea is steeping, finely chop a small piece of fresh ginger root (about the size of your thumb) and squeeze the juice of half a lemon into a separate cup or mug.
  4. After the milk thistle has steeped, strain the tea (if using loose flowers) and pour the liquid into the cup with the ginger and lemon juice.
  5. Mix the ingredients well and enjoy your homemade milk thistle tea for clear skin, ideally drinking it 1-2 times a day, preferably before meals.

9. Chaparral

Chaparral tea helps with clear skin because it contains anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that can help reduce redness, swelling, and fight bacteria that can lead to acne and other skin irritations.

The tea is also rich in antioxidants, which can help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals and promote cell regeneration, resulting in a more even skin tone and texture. Additionally, chaparral tea has been shown to improve digestion and liver function, which can help eliminate toxins from the body that may contribute to skin problems. The tea's anti-inflammatory properties can also help soothe skin conditions such as eczema and psoriasis, reducing inflammation and promoting healing.

Finally, drinking chaparral tea regularly can help boost the immune system, making it easier for the body to fight off infections and other factors that can lead to skin issues.

The following procedure explains how to make chaparral tea for clear skin.

  1. To make chaparral tea for clear skin, start by boiling 1 cup of water in a small pot, then add 1-2 teaspoons of dried chaparral leaves and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  2. After simmering, remove the pot from heat and let the tea cool down for a few minutes before straining out the leaves using a fine mesh strainer.
  3. For added benefits, consider adding 1/4 teaspoon of licorice root powder and 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder to the tea before straining.
  4. Drink the tea while it's still warm, 1-2 times a day, preferably on an empty stomach for best results.
  5. Consistency is key, so make sure to drink chaparral tea regularly for at least a month to see improvements in your skin's clarity and overall health.

10. Echinacea

Echinacea tea helps with clear skin because it has anti-inflammatory properties that can reduce redness and swelling associated with skin conditions such as acne.

It also has antioxidant properties that can help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, which can lead to premature aging and skin blemishes. Furthermore, echinacea has been shown to boost the immune system, which can help the body fight off infections that can cause skin breakouts. Additionally, echinacea may help regulate sebum production, reducing the likelihood of clogged pores and acne.

However, it's important to note that while echinacea tea can be a helpful addition to a clear skin regimen, it should not be relied on as the sole solution, and a consistent skincare routine and healthy lifestyle are also crucial for maintaining clear and healthy skin.

The following procedure explains how to make echinacea tea for clear skin.

  1. Begin by boiling 2 cups of water in a pot, then add 2 teaspoons of dried echinacea flowers and leaves, allowing the mixture to steep for 15-20 minutes.
  2. While the tea is steeping, finely chop a small piece of fresh ginger root (about the size of your thumb) and add it to the pot.
  3. After steeping, strain the tea to remove the solid plant material, then squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the liquid and stir in 1-2 teaspoons of raw honey for added sweetness and immune support.
  4. For an extra boost to clear skin, grate a small amount of fresh turmeric root (about the size of a fingernail) into the tea and stir well.
  5. Serve the tea hot, sip slowly, and enjoy the soothing and rejuvenating effects of this natural echinacea tea for clear skin.
