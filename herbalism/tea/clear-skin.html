10 best herbal teas for clear skin

For clear skin, consider herbal teas that are rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties.

Green tea, known for its high antioxidant content, can help reduce inflammation and improve skin complexion. Another option is chamomile tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties that can help soothe irritated skin and reduce redness. Lastly, consider rooibos tea, a caffeine-free tea that is rich in antioxidants and has been shown to improve skin texture and tone.

Regularly drinking these herbal teas can help promote clear, healthy skin from within.

1. Nettle

Nettle tea provides a natural source of nutrients and antioxidants that support skin health.

Its anti-inflammatory properties reduce redness and swelling, promoting clearer skin. Nettle tea also boasts abundant vitamins A, C, and E, which are essential for maintaining skin's elasticity and overall appearance. Furthermore, it assists in cleansing the body of toxins, consequently decreasing the likelihood of skin impurities.

Lastly, nettle tea's high mineral content, including zinc and sulfur, regulates oil production and aids in preventing acne breakouts.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in nettle tea that help with clear skin.

  • Leaves: Rich in nutrients and chlorophyll, leverage nettle leaves to brew a skin-purifying tea.
  • Stems: Packed with silica and calcium, steep nettle stems for a mineral-rich, clarifying brew.
  • Flowers: Abundant in antioxidants, infuse nettle flowers for a soothing, detoxifying tea.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic nettle tea for clear skin.

  1. Gather 2 handfuls of fresh stinging nettle leaves, rinse and place in a pot.
  2. Add 4 cups of water to the pot, bring to a boil and then simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the nettle leaves, discard the leaves and keep the liquid.
  4. Let the tea cool down to a drinkable temperature, then optionally add honey to taste.
  5. Consume the nettle tea 1-2 times a day for clear skin, storing any leftovers in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.

2. Burdock

Burdock tea promotes clear skin through its proven ability to purify the blood.

This tea contains antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties that rid the body of toxins, resulting in improved skin health. Burdock's diuretic properties also help eliminate waste and reduce water retention, further contributing to a clearer complexion. Furthermore, burdock tea boasts antibacterial qualities that combat skin issues by destroying acne-causing bacteria.

Lastly, burdock's nutrient-rich composition, including vitamins and minerals, supports skin cell regeneration for a radiant, rejuvenated appearance.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in burdock tea that help with clear skin.

  • Roots: Burdock roots contain antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties that aid in skin detoxification and help maintain clear skin.
  • Rhizomes: Burdock rhizomes have natural blood purifying qualities that assist in eliminating toxins, promoting clear and healthy skin.
  • Leaves: Rich in vitamins and minerals, burdock leaves offer skin-friendly nutrients that support a clear complexion when used in medicinal teas.
  • Flowers: Burdock flowers contain flavonoids that possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, contributing to a clear skin appearance.
  • Seeds: Burdock seeds are a great source of essential fatty acids and phytosterols that support skin health and help maintain a clear complexion.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic burdock tea for clear skin.

  1. Chop 2 tablespoons of dried burdock root into small pieces and rinse under cold water.
  2. Place the chopped burdock root in a pot and add 4 cups of water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Strain the liquid to remove the burdock root pieces.
  5. Allow the tea to cool down to a drinkable temperature and enjoy 1-2 cups daily for clear skin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic dandelion tea for clear skin.

  1. Gather 2-3 dandelion heads and rinse them under cold water to remove dirt and insects.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot and add the dandelion heads, letting it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea into a teapot or a cup, discarding the dandelion heads.
  4. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired, for added flavor and skin benefits.
  5. Serve the tea hot and enjoy its detoxifying and skin-clarifying properties.

  5. Serve - Enjoy your red clover tea either hot or cold for clear skin.

  4. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, then strain the tea to remove the root pieces.
  5. Allow the tea to cool down and drink 1-2 cups daily for clear skin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sarsaparilla tea for clear skin.

  2. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the sarsaparilla root pieces.
  4. Add the juice of half a lemon and honey to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, drink 2-3 times a day for clear skin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic chaparral tea for clear skin.

  1. Gather 2-3 teaspoons of dried chaparral leaves and place them in a tea infuser or loose in a tea pot.

  5. Drink 1-2 cups of chaparral tea daily for clear skin.
