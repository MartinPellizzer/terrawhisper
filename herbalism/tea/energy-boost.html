10 best herbal teas for energy boost

Herbal teas can be a great natural option to give you a gentle energy boost.

Some of the best herbal teas for increasing energy include ginseng tea, known for its ability to fight fatigue and improve mental performance; matcha green tea, which contains a moderate amount of caffeine and L-theanine for sustained energy; and yerba mate, a popular South American tea that is high in antioxidants and provides a smooth, balanced energy lift. Additionally, peppermint tea can help enhance mental alertness and improve focus, making it a good choice for an afternoon perk-me-up.

Always consult with a healthcare professional if you have any concerns or medical conditions.

1. Ginseng

Ginseng tea energizes the body due to its content of ginsenosides, natural compounds that boost energy levels.

This tea stimulates the central nervous system, enhancing mental clarity and physical stamina. Moreover, ginseng tea improves circulation, delivering oxygen and nutrients to cells, thereby increasing overall energy. Additionally, this herbal tea supports adrenal gland function, helping the body effectively manage stress and maintain vitality.

Lastly, ginseng tea's adaptogenic properties enable it to regulate energy levels, preventing fatigue and sustaining focus throughout the day.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in ginseng tea that help with energy boost.

  • Roots: The roots of ginseng are a popular choice for making medicinal tea for energy boost due to their high concentration of active compounds, called ginsenosides, which help combat fatigue.
  • Rhizomes: Ginseng rhizomes are stems that grow underground and are rich in adaptogenic properties, helping the body adapt to stress and encouraging sustained energy.
  • Leaves: Ginseng leaves offer a valuable source of antioxidants and beneficial compounds, supporting mental clarity and focus, which can contribute to increased energy levels.
  • Flowers: Though less commonly used, ginseng flowers contain bioactive constituents that can support adrenal function and help maintain consistent energy levels.
  • Seeds: Ginseng seeds are a lesser-known powerhouse of energy-boosting compounds, as they provide a natural source of B-vitamins and essential fatty acids, crucial for cellular energy production.

2. Yerba Mate

Yerba mate tea provides an energy boost through its natural caffeine content, which stimulates the central nervous system, enhancing mental alertness and physical performance.

This tea contains a higher concentration of antioxidants than green tea, aiding in efficient energy production and reducing oxidative stress. Additionally, yerba mate's unique blend of nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, and amino acids, supports overall body function and contributes to sustained energy levels. The xanthines, such as theobromine and theophylline, in yerba mate work synergistically with caffeine to enhance its already invigorating effects.

Drinking yerba mate tea contributes to a stable energy boost, avoiding the typical caffeine crash experienced with other caffeinated beverages because of its balanced nutritional profile and L-theanine content, which promotes relaxation.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in yerba mate tea that help with energy boost.

  • Leaves: Rich in caffeine and antioxidants, leaves are used to make a stimulating tea that boosts energy and enhances mental focus.
  • Stems: Packed with nutrients, stems contribute to a balanced and invigorating infusion, providing a sustainable energy boost.
  • Buds: High in flavonoids and caffeine, buds produce a potent and invigorating tea, ideal for increasing energy levels and alertness.
  • Seeds: Abundant in vitamins and minerals, seeds create a nourishing and reinvigorating tea, supporting sustained energy and vitality.

3. Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha tea, derived from the Ashwagandha plant, contains adaptogenic properties that combat fatigue by supporting the body's response to physical and mental stress, thereby enhancing energy levels.

This herbal tea's active compounds, including withanolides, promote balance within the adrenal system, steadying the production of energy-regulating hormones such as cortisol and adrenaline. By strengthening the nervous system, ashwagandha tea increases vigor and endurance while fostering mental clarity and focus, making it an ideal natural solution for sustainable energy boosts. Moreover, it improves sleep quality, allowing for optimal daytime energy restoration, ultimately increasing overall vitality.

Lastly, ashwagandha tea fortifies the immune system and fosters overall well-being, bolstering energy levels during the day.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in ashwagandha tea that help with energy boost.

  • Roots: The roots of ashwagandha contain bioactive compounds that help combat fatigue and stress, making them an ideal addition to a revitalizing tea.
  • Rhyzomes: These underground stems are rich in medicinal properties, providing a natural energy boost.
  • Leaves: Ashwagandha leaves possess adaptogenic qualities, aiding in increasing energy levels and improving mental focus.
  • Berries/Seeds: The seeds of ashwagandha are a powerhouse of energy-enhancing nutrients, promoting overall well-being.
  • Buds: Buds from the ashwagandha plant hold potent agents that support the body's natural response to exhaustion and lethargy.

4. Guarana

Guarana tea contains guaranine, a stimulant similar to caffeine, that directly stimulates the central nervous system, leading to increased alertness and energy.

This natural stimulant discourages fatigue by enhancing the brain's ability to stay focused, making guarana tea an effective energy booster. Moreover, guarana tea's catechins boost metabolism and improve physical performance, further increasing energy levels. By promoting healthy digestion and nutrient absorption, guarana tea ensures optimal energy release from consumed foods, sustainably supporting energy demands.

Finally, guarana's natural antioxidants guard against oxidative stress, enhancing overall well-being and reinforcing the tea's invigorating benefits.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in guarana tea that help with energy boost.

  • Seeds: Guarana seeds are the primary part of the plant used for making medicinal tea for energy boost. They contain twice the amount of caffeine found in coffee beans, making them a strong natural stimulant.
  • Leaves: While less potent than seeds, guarana leaves still contain caffeine and are often used in tea blends for a milder energy boost with added benefits from the leaves' other natural compounds.
  • Stems: Guarana stems are used less frequently but still contribute to the overall energy-boosting effect of the tea, as they contain small amounts of caffeine and other stimulating compounds.

5. Goji Berry

Goji berry tea boosts energy due to its rich nutritional profile, containing vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

This tea's antioxidants, specifically, aid in fighting fatigue and increasing stamina by neutralizing free radicals in the body. Goji berries have high levels of iron, which supports red blood cell production, enhancing oxygen transportation and resulting in increased energy levels. Furthermore, goji berry tea contains moderate levels of caffeine and a significant amount of vitamin B, both of which contribute to increased energy and improved mental focus.

Finally, goji berry tea's adaptogenic properties help the body adapt to stress, promoting overall well-being and sustained energy throughout the day.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in goji berry tea that help with energy boost.

  • Leaves: Goji berry leaves contain high levels of antioxidants that help boost energy by supporting overall health and well-being.
  • Stems: The stems of goji berry plants are rich in polysaccharides, which are known to increase stamina and endurance, providing a natural energy boost.
  • Flowers: Goji berry flowers have adaptogenic properties that help the body adapt to stress and fatigue, promoting sustained energy levels.
  • Seeds: The seeds of goji berries are a natural source of essential amino acids and healthy fats, providing a long-lasting energy boost and supporting endurance.
  • Buds: Goji berry buds are rich in vitamins and minerals that help improve circulation and increase oxygen supply to cells, providing a natural energy boost.

6. Licorice

Licorice tea contains glycyrrhizin, a compound that metabolizes into a substance similar to the stress hormone cortisol.

This tea's consumption leads to increased energy levels, as it helps maintain steady blood sugar levels by slowing down the absorption of carbohydrates. Moreover, licorice tea provides a natural boost without the crash associated with caffeine or sugar. Its consumption also supports the adrenal glands, which play a crucial role in regulating energy and stress response, leading to overall increased vitality.

Finally, the hydration from drinking this tea contributes to improved focus and concentration.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in licorice tea that help with energy boost.

  • Roots: Rich in beneficial compounds, the roots of the licorice plant provide a strong energy boost due to their high concentration of glycyrrhizin.
  • Rhizomes: Packed with antioxidants, licorice rhizomes support adrenal function, helping to increase energy levels naturally.
  • Leaves: A natural source of essential oils, licorice leaves promote mental clarity and focus, contributing to increased energy.
  • Seeds: High in flavonoids, licorice seeds offer a sustained energy boost by supporting the body's overall health and well-being.
  • Flowers: Abounding in vitamins and minerals, licorice flowers improve stamina and endurance, making them an ideal addition to energy-boosting tea.

7. Eleuthero

Eleuthero tea, derived from the Eleutherococcus senticosus plant, offers a caffeine-free energy boost.

This tea contains adaptogens, which combat fatigue by enhancing the body's resistance to stress. These adaptogens achieve this by improving the efficiency of the adrenal glands, which control the body's hormonal response to physical and mental stress. As a result, eleuthero tea users experience increased endurance, focus, and vitality.

Additionally, eleuthero's antioxidant properties further contribute to elevated energy levels by protecting cells from damage, ensuring optimal cell function and overall well-being.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in eleuthero tea that help with energy boost.

  • Roots: The roots of eleuthero contain compounds that help increase energy levels by supporting the body's natural stress response.
  • Rhizomes: Eleuthero's rhizomes are rich in adaptogens, which enhance physical performance and endurance, making them ideal for energy boosts.
  • Leaves: The leaves of eleuthero contain antioxidants that aid in cellular respiration, providing natural energy without the crash associated with caffeine.
  • Bark: The bark of the eleuthero plant has beneficial compounds that improve mental clarity and focus, providing a sustainable energy boost.

8. Peppermint

Peppermint tea is a natural stimulant that increases energy levels.

This invigorating effect results from the presence of menthol, a compound that boosts mental clarity and alertness. Moreover, peppermint tea contains modest amounts of caffeine and antioxidants, which work synergistically to enhance physical performance and endurance. Additionally, the act of sipping hot tea promotes blood circulation, delivering oxygen and nutrients more efficiently to body cells.

Ultimately, peppermint tea's invigorating properties make it an excellent choice for combating daytime fatigue and fostering sustained concentration.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in peppermint tea that help with energy boost.

  • Leaves: Rich in menthol and menthone, peppermint leaves help stimulate the mind and body, providing a natural energy boost.
  • Flowers: High in antioxidants, peppermint flowers support digestion and circulation, contributing to increased energy levels.
  • Stems: Containing essential oils, peppermint stems aid in relieving mental fatigue and invigorating the senses.
  • Seeds: Packed with nutrients, peppermint seeds enhance mental clarity and physical vitality, promoting overall energy.

9. Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo biloba tea contains high levels of antioxidants, which support cellular health and boost energy production.

This tea enhances circulation, ensuring that oxygen and nutrients reach body tissues efficiently, promoting overall energy. Ginkgo biloba is known to improve brain function, increasing mental clarity and focus, and reducing mental fatigue. Additionally, it helps regulate the body's energy balance by optimizing mitochondria function, the powerhouse of our cells.

Regular consumption of ginkgo biloba tea contributes to sustained energy levels without the crash associated with caffeine.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in ginkgo biloba tea that help with energy boost.

  • Rhyzomes: The rhyzomes of Ginkgo biloba contain active compounds that enhance blood circulation, thereby providing a natural energy boost.

10. Matcha

Matcha tea, a superior variety of green tea, provides an energy boost due to its rich concentration of catechins and caffeine.

Each cup contains high levels of L-theanine, an amino acid that triggers gradual release of caffeine, ensuring sustainable energy without crashes. This slow release leads to improved alertness and focus, enhancing productivity. Additionally, matcha's nutrient-dense composition boosts metabolism and thermal genesis, contributing further to its invigorating effects.

Ultimately, matcha tea stands out as a healthier energy source, surpassing conventional caffeine-laden drinks.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in matcha tea that help with energy boost.

  • Leaves: The leaves of the matcha plant are used to make medicinal tea for energy boost due to their high concentrations of caffeine and L-theanine.
  • Buds: The buds of the matcha plant contain a high amount of antioxidants that help in increasing energy levels.
  • Stems: The stems of the matcha plant contain a good amount of nutrients that can help in boosting energy levels.
  • Seeds: The seeds of the matcha plant are not typically used in medicinal tea, as they do not contain as many nutrients as the leaves and buds.
  • Roots and Rhyzomes: These parts of the matcha plant are not typically used in medicinal tea, as they do not contain as many nutrients as the leaves and buds.
  • Flowers and Bark: These parts of the matcha plant are not typically used in medicinal tea, as they do not contain as many nutrients as the leaves and buds.
