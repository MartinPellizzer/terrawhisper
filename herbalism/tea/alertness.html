10 best herbal teas for alertness

Certain herbal teas are known to help promote alertness and improve concentration, making them an excellent alternative to traditional caffeinated drinks.

One such tea is green tea, which contains a moderate amount of caffeine along with an amino acid called L-theanine, known to enhance brain function and promote relaxation without drowsiness. Peppermint tea is another great option, as it can help increase mental alertness and improve memory. Ginkgo biloba tea, derived from the leaves of the Ginkgo biloba tree, is widely used in Traditional Chinese Medicine to boost memory and cognitive function.

Lastly, rosemary tea, made from the leaves of the rosemary plant, has been shown to improve cognitive performance and reduce fatigue, making it an ideal beverage for those seeking to enhance their alertness and focus.

1. Guayusa

Guayusa tea contains a high concentration of caffeine, offering a natural energy boost that promotes alertness.

This caffeinated beverage also includes L-theanine, an amino acid that enhances focus and mental clarity. Guayusa's unique combination of caffeine and L-theanine delivers sustained alertness without common side effects such as jitters or crashes. Furthermore, guayusa's antioxidants support brain health, optimizing cognitive function and overall alertness.

Notably, traditional Amazonian cultures rely on guayusa for its invigorating qualities during hunting and rituals, demonstrating its long-standing reputation as a potent stimulant.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in guayusa tea that help with alertness.

  • Leaves: The leaves of the guayusa plant are rich in caffeine and antioxidants, making them ideal for a stimulating and healthy tea.
  • Buds: The buds of the guayusa plant contain a high concentration of caffeine and L-theanine, promoting alertness and mental clarity.
  • Seeds: Guayusa seeds are a natural source of xanthines, which help to increase energy and alertness.
  • Stems: The stems of the guayusa plant contain caffeine and other stimulating compounds, making them a valuable addition to a tea for alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic guayusa tea for alertness.

  1. Measure: Scoop 1-2 teaspoons of dried guayusa leaves into a tea infuser or loose leaf teapot.
  2. Boil: Heat 8 ounces of water to boiling (212°F) then pour over the guayusa leaves.
  3. Steep: Allow the tea to steep for 5-7 minutes for optimal flavor and alertness benefits.
  4. Remove: Take out the tea infuser or strain the leaves from the teapot.
  5. Serve: Pour the guayusa tea into a cup and enjoy as is or with a desired amount of sweetener or milk.

2. Yerba Mate

Yerba mate tea contains xanthines, specifically caffeine, theophylline, and theobromine, which stimulate the central nervous system, leading to increased alertness.

This tea provides a smoother increase in energy levels compared to coffee, due to the balanced presence of these xanthines. Additionally, yerba mate contains mateine, a type of caffeine that releases slowly into the bloodstream, extending the duration of mental clarity and focus. Furthermore, this tea is rich in antioxidants and vitamins, such as Vitamin B complex, which support brain function and overall well-being.

The combination of these elements in yerba mate tea ensures a sustainable enhancement of alertness and cognitive performance.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in yerba mate tea that help with alertness.

  • Leaves: The leaves of the yerba mate plant are used to make medicinal tea for alertness due to their natural caffeine content.
  • Stems: The stems of the plant contain smaller amounts of caffeine and support the release of stimulating compounds.
  • Seeds: Yerba mate seeds provide a concentrated source of beneficial plant compounds, including those that promote alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yerba mate tea for alertness.

  1. Measure out 1-2 teaspoons of yerba mate tea leaves and place them in a tea infuser or French press.
  2. Boil 8 ounces of water and let it cool slightly to around 160-180°F (71-82°C) to avoid burning the tea leaves.
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves in the infuser or French press and let it steep for 5-7 minutes for optimum flavor and alertness benefits.
  4. Remove the tea infuser or press and discard the tea leaves.
  5. Pour the tea into a cup and enjoy your caffeine-rich and antioxidant-packed yerba mate tea for alertness.

3. Green Tea

Green tea contains a natural stimulant called caffeine, which blocks adenosine receptors in the brain, enhancing neural communication and increasing alertness.

Additionally, green tea provides an amino acid named L-theanine that stimulates the release of neurotransmitters, promoting calmness and focus. These two compounds, working together, produce a steady supply of energy without the common crash caused by other caffeinated beverages. Regular green tea consumption improves cognitive functions, as it supports the growth of neuronal cells and protects them from oxidative stress.

Consequently, green tea emerges as a valuable aid in maintaining alertness and mental performance.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in green tea tea that help with alertness.

  • Leaves: Green tea leaves are rich in caffeine and L-theanine, which promote alertness and mental focus.
  • Buds: The unopened buds of the green tea plant contain high amounts of catechins and antioxidants, which support cognitive function and alertness.
  • Stems: Green tea stems have a balanced ratio of caffeine and theanine, making them ideal for promoting alertness and reducing stress.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic green tea tea for alertness.

  1. Boil 8 ounces of water, taking 3-5 minutes to reach a rolling boil.
  2. Place 1 teaspoon of high-quality green tea leaves in a tea infuser or teapot.
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves and let steep for 2-3 minutes for optimal flavor and alertness benefits.
  4. Remove the tea leaves to prevent over-steeping and bitterness.
  5. Sip the green tea mindfully, enjoying its refreshing taste and the calming alertness it provides.

4. Gotu Kola

Gotu kola tea contains active compounds, notably triterpenoids, that studies prove enhance brain function.

This herbal tea promotes blood circulation to the brain, resulting in improved oxygen and nutrient supply. Gotu kola tea's ability to boost mental clarity and enhance memory is due to its protection and enhancement of axons and dendrites. Furthermore, this tea reduces oxidative stress and inflammation in brain cells, leading to increased neural activity and alertness.

Thus, gotu kola tea's unique properties make it a natural aid for enhanced alertness and cognitive performance.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in gotu kola tea that help with alertness.

  • Leaves: Gotu kola leaves are rich in triterpenoid compounds, which are believed to help improve mental clarity and alertness.
  • Stems: The stems of gotu kola contain adaptogenic properties that help reduce mental fatigue and improve concentration.
  • Rhizomes: Gotu kola rhizomes have been used in traditional medicine to enhance memory and cognitive function, promoting alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic gotu kola tea for alertness.

  1. Measure out 2 teaspoons of dried gotu kola leaves, crushing them gently to release their aroma.
  2. Place the crushed leaves in a tea infuser or teapot with a built-in strainer.
  3. Heat 1 cup of water to boiling point (212°F or 100°C), then pour it over the gotu kola leaves.
  4. Allow the tea to steep for 5-10 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds.
  5. Remove the infuser or strain the tea, then enjoy it while it's still warm for improved alertness and focus.

5. Maca

Maca tea, derived from the maca root, contains nutrients and compounds that enhance mental clarity and alertness.

This invigorating effect stems from maca's rich supply of minerals, vitamins, and unique alkaloids, which nourish the body and promote overall well-being. Additionally, maca tea's caffeine-free nature allows for sustained alertness, as it does not produce the energy crashes associated with caffeinated beverages. Furthermore, maca's adaptogenic properties enable it to balance the body's systems and support optimal adrenal function, leading to increased focus and mental stamina.

Ultimately, maca tea's array of beneficial compounds work synergistically to elevate cognitive performance, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a natural, sustainable energy boost.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in maca tea that help with alertness.

  • Roots: The maca root is rich in adaptogens that enhance the body's resilience to stress, thereby improving mental clarity and alertness.
  • Rhyzomes: Maca rhyzomes contain unique compounds that support energy metabolism, promoting sustained mental stamina and focus.
  • Leaves: Maca leaves are a natural source of B vitamins and minerals, which are essential for optimal brain function and alertness.
  • Flowers: Maca flowers are rich in antioxidants that protect the brain from oxidative stress, improving cognitive performance and alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maca tea for alertness.

  1. In a pot, bring 1 cup of water to a boil (3-5 minutes).
  2. Stir in 1 teaspoon of maca root powder, reducing heat to low.
  3. Simmer the maca root and water mixture for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the maca tea into a cup, discarding the solid remains.
  5. Add honey or lemon to taste, then enjoy your homemade maca tea for alertness.

6. Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha tea contains active compounds, primarily adaptogens, that possess recognized benefits for alertness.

These adaptogens, including withanolides, trigger responses in the human body that enhance focus and mental clarity. By reducing stress and anxiety, ashwagandha tea allows the mind to concentrate, leading to increased alertness. Additionally, this herbal tea stimulates the production of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter tied to heightened attentiveness.

Regular consumption of ashwagandha tea contributes to improved cognitive functions, resulting in sustained alertness throughout the day.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in ashwagandha tea that help with alertness.

  • Roots: The roots of ashwagandha are used in medicinal tea for alertness due to their adaptogenic properties that help the body cope with stress and fatigue.
  • Leaves: The leaves of ashwagandha are used in tea to promote wakefulness and alertness, as they contain triethylene glycol, which has been shown to induce sleep.
  • Seeds: The seeds of ashwagandha are used to make medicinal tea for alertness, as they contain active compounds that help to improve cognitive function and reduce anxiety.
  • Flowers: The flowers of ashwagandha are used in tea to promote alertness and reduce anxiety, due to the presence of flavonoids, which have calming and soothing effects.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ashwagandha tea for alertness.

  1. In a pot, boil 2 cups of water for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of ashwagandha root powder and reduce heat to a simmer.
  3. Let the tea simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the solids.
  5. Add honey or lemon to taste and enjoy.

7. Holy Basil

Holy basil tea contains compounds, notably adaptogenic compounds like rosmarinic acid and eugenol, that actively engage with the human body to promote mental clarity and focus.

By consuming this tea, individuals introduce these natural compounds into their systems, which then interact with the brain to enhance cognitive function, specifically alertness. These compounds work by influencing neurotransmitter activity, enhancing the release of acetylcholine, a key neurotransmitter associated with alertness and cognitive performance.

Furthermore, holy basil tea provides a steady source of caffeine, which, in moderate amounts, stimulates the central nervous system, promoting alertness and reducing drowsiness. Additionally, this tea's antioxidant properties contribute to overall brain health, protecting neurons from oxidative stress and maintaining optimal neural functioning, thereby supporting prolonged periods of alertness.

Overall, holy basil tea materially benefits alertness by promoting neurotransmission, delivering caffeine, and safeguarding neural health.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in holy basil tea that help with alertness.

  • Leaves: Rich in essential oils, the leaves of holy basil contain compounds that promote mental clarity and alertness.
  • Flowers: Packed with antioxidants, holy basil flowers support cognitive function and help enhance focus.
  • Seeds: High in fatty acids, holy basil seeds provide a stable energy source, supporting alertness and mental stamina.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic holy basil tea for alertness.

  1. In a small pot, boil 1 cup of water for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of dried holy basil leaves to the boiling water, then reduce heat to low.
  3. Let the tea simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the water to absorb the basil's properties.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the spent basil leaves.
  5. Sip the hot tea slowly, savoring its earthy flavor and aroma, to promote alertness and calmness.

8. Schisandra

Schisandra tea contains compounds called schisandrins that stimulate the central nervous system, enhancing mental clarity and alertness.

These schisandrins protect and improve the functioning of neurotransmitters, which carry messages in the brain, promoting sharpness and focus. Schisandra tea's adaptogenic properties help the body adapt to stress and promote homeostasis, ensuring optimal cognitive function. Additionally, schisandra tea improves blood flow to the brain, delivering vital oxygen and nutrients that support neurological activity, further contributing to heightened alertness.

Finally, schisandra tea's antioxidant properties protect brain cells from oxidative stress and damage, maintaining optimal brain health and promoting long-term cognitive well-being.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in schisandra tea that help with alertness.

  • Schisandra Berries/Seeds: The berries or seeds of the Schisandra plant are rich in antioxidants and have been shown to improve mental clarity and alertness.
  • Schisandra Leaves: The leaves of the Schisandra plant contain high levels of adaptogenic compounds, which help to combat fatigue and increase focus and concentration.
  • Schisandra Roots: The roots of the Schisandra plant are a rich source of lignans, which have been shown to improve cognitive function and boost energy levels.
  • Schisandra Bark: The bark of the Schisandra plant contains active constituents, including Schisandrins, which have been shown to improve memory and mental performance.
  • Schisandra Rhyzomes: The rhyzomes of the Schisandra plant are a valuable source of Schisantherins, compounds which have been shown to promote alertness and reduce mental fatigue.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic schisandra tea for alertness.

  1. In a teapot, combine 1 teaspoon of dried schisandra berries with 8 ounces of boiling water.
  2. Allow the mixture to steep for 10 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the berries, preventing over-steeping and bitterness.
  4. Optionally, add raw honey to taste for a natural sweetness.
  5. Drink the tea while warm to enjoy the invigorating effects of schisandra and promote alertness.

9. Bacopa Monnieri

Bacopa Monnieri tea contains active compounds known as bacosides, which possess adaptogenic and nootropic properties.

This results in an improvement in alertness, concentration, and overall cognitive performance. Furthermore, Bacopa Monnieri's anti-oxidative and anti-inflammatory effects protect the brain from oxidative stress, thereby maintaining optimal brain function.

Consequently, Bacopa Monnieri tea consumption supports alertness by promoting overall cognitive health.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in bacopa monnieri tea that help with alertness.

  • Roots: The roots of Bacopa Monnieri contain active compounds that support cognitive function, leading to increased alertness.
  • Leaves: The leaves of this plant are rich in antioxidants that help improve brain function and enhance alertness.
  • Stems: The stems of Bacopa Monnieri contain beneficial alkaloids that promote mental clarity and alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic bacopa monnieri tea for alertness.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water to a rolling boil, then pour over 1 teaspoon of dried bacopa monnieri leaves.
  2. Steep the tea for 5-10 minutes, depending on desired strength.
  3. Strain the tea into a cup to remove the leaves.
  4. Optional: Add honey or lemon to taste.
  5. Drink the tea while warm to promote alertness and focus.

10. Licorice

Licorice tea contains a compound called glycyrrhizin, which activates the adrenal glands, prompting the release of stress- fighting hormones like cortisol.

These hormones, in turn, enhance mental clarity and alertness. Moreover, glycyrrhizin also regulates blood sugar levels, preventing energy crashes that lead to fatigue and drowsiness. Simultaneously, licorice tea improves digestion, allowing for optimal nutrient absorption and ensuring a steady supply of energy.

Lastly, the tea's soothing warmth helps alleviate mental fog, facilitating focus and increased awareness.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in licorice tea that help with alertness.

  • Roots: Rich in beneficial compounds, the roots of the licorice plant make a potent tea that promotes alertness.
  • Leaves: Containing invigorating properties, licorice leaves are excellent for brewing a stimulating tea that enhances alertness.
  • Seeds: Packed with energizing nutrients, licorice seeds create a revitalizing tea that promotes sustained alertness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice tea for alertness.

  1. In a pot, combine 1 cup of water and 1 tablespoon of dried licorice root, then bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the solids.
  4. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
  5. Drink the tea while hot for alertness.
