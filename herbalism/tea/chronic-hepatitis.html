10 best herbal teas for chronic hepatitis

For individuals with chronic hepatitis, certain herbal teas may provide symptomatic relief and promote liver health.

Milk thistle tea is widely recognized for its potential to protect and repair the liver due to its active compound, silymarin. Another beneficial tea is dandelion root tea, which has been shown to support liver function and reduce inflammation. Turmeric tea, with its active ingredient curcumin, is known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, potentially aiding in liver healing.

However, it is crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before incorporating these herbal teas into a treatment plan, as they can interact with certain medications or exacerbate existing health conditions.
