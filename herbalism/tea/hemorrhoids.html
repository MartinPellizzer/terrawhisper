1. Witch Hazel

Witch hazel tea helps with hemorrhoids because it contains tannins and volatile oils, which have anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

These compounds can help reduce swelling, irritation, and pain in the affected area. Additionally, witch hazel may help to decrease bleeding and promote healing of broken blood vessels.

By drinking witch hazel tea, the active compounds are absorbed into the bloodstream and can provide relief for internal hemorrhoids. Furthermore, applying cooled witch hazel tea topically with a compress or pad can offer additional soothing benefits for external hemorrhoids.

However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before starting any new treatment for hemorrhoids, as individual responses to treatments may vary.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in witch hazel tea that help with hemorrhoids.

  1. Tannins: These compounds have astringent properties, which can help reduce swelling and inflammation in hemorrhoids.
  2. Gallic Acid: This compound has anti-inflammatory and anti-oxidant properties that can help soothe and heal hemorrhoids.
  3. Esculin: This compound also has anti-oxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that can help alleviate symptoms of hemorrhoids.
  4. Proanthocyanidins: These compounds have astringent and anti-oxidant properties that can help shrink and heal damaged tissue in hemorrhoids.

The following list reveals what parts of witch hazel are most commonly used to make medicinal tea for hemorrhoids.

  1. Bark: The bark of the witch hazel plant is used due to its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe hemorrhoids.
  2. Leaves: Witch hazel leaves are used in medicinal tea because they contain tannins that can help reduce swelling in hemorrhoids.
  3. Bark of the branches and twigs: The bark of the branches and twigs is rich in flavonoids that have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful for hemorrhoid relief.
  4. Flowers: Witch hazel flowers are used for their astringent properties, which can help shrink swollen tissue and provide relief for hemorrhoids.
  5. Seeds: Witch hazel seeds contain high levels of tannins and flavonoids, making them useful for reducing inflammation and providing relief for hemorrhoids.

The following procedure explains how to make witch hazel tea for hemorrhoids.

  1. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and 2 teaspoons of dried witch hazel leaves, then bring the mixture to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat, cover the saucepan, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the leaves, then allow it to cool down to a comfortable temperature.
  4. Drink 1 cup of the prepared tea, 2-3 times a day, before or after meals.
  5. Continue this regimen for several days to see improvements in hemorrhoid symptoms.
