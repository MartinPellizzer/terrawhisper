10 best herbal teas for cholecystitis

Cholecystitis, an inflammation of the gallbladder, can cause significant discomfort and pain.

While it's important to seek medical attention for this condition, certain herbal teas may help alleviate symptoms and support overall digestive health. Peppermint tea is one such option, as it can help relax the smooth muscles of the gastrointestinal tract and potentially reduce painful spasms. Additionally, dandelion root tea has been suggested to promote bile flow and may support gallbladder function.

However, it's crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before incorporating these or any other herbal remedies into a treatment plan for cholecystitis.
