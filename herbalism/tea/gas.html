10 best herbal teas for gas

If you're looking for herbal teas to help with gas, there are several options that can provide relief.

Peppermint tea is a popular choice, as it can help relax the gastrointestinal muscles and reduce bloating. Ginger tea is another good option, as it can help stimulate digestion and reduce inflammation in the digestive tract.

Chamomile tea is also known for its calming effects on the stomach, and can help soothe and relax the digestive muscles. Fennel tea is another herbal tea that can help relieve gas and bloating, as it can help stimulate the production of digestive enzymes.

Ultimately, the best herbal tea for you will depend on your personal preferences and any other digestive symptoms you may be experiencing.

1. Chamomile

Chamomile tea contains volatile oils, particularly one called bisabolol, which have antispasmodic properties that relax the smooth muscles in the gastrointestinal tract, thereby relieving gas.

This herbal tea also includes flavonoids that trigger a decrease in basal gut motility, reducing the intensity and frequency of gas pains. Furthermore, chamomile tea's anti-inflammatory agents, such as matricin and apigenin, help soothe inflamed tissues in the digestive tract, alleviating discomfort caused by gas. Its calming effects on the digestive system make chamomile tea an ideal beverage for promoting proper digestion and reducing gas build-up.

Additionally, chamomile's carminative properties, which promote the expulsion of intestinal gas, aid in the overall relief of gas symptoms.

Right below you will find a list of the most important active constituents in chamomile tea that help with gas.
