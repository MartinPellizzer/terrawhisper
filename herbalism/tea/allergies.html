10 best herbal teas for allergy

For those seeking relief from allergies, certain herbal teas can offer natural relief. NETTLE LEAF tea is well-known for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate allergic reactions. Meanwhile, GREEN TEA contains epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), a compound with antihistamine effects. Chamomile tea is another popular choice, as it has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that may help soothe irritated membranes and alleviate allergy symptoms. Lastly, LEMON BALM tea has been shown to have antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a potential ally in the fight against allergies. However, it's always best to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new herbal remedies.
