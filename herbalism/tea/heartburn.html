10 Best Herbal Teas For Heartburn

Herbal teas, especially those made from chamomile, licorice root, marshmallow root, and slippery elm, can help alleviate heartburn symptoms.

These herbs have anti-inflammatory properties that can soothe the irritated esophageal lining caused by acid reflux. They can also help reduce gastric acid production, making the stomach environment less acidic.

Additionally, some herbs, like licorice root, create a protective coating on the esophageal and stomach lining, preventing acid from causing further irritation. Furthermore, these teas can stimulate the production of mucus in the gastrointestinal tract, providing a barrier against stomach acid.

Lastly, herbal teas can promote digestion and relaxation, reducing stress-induced heartburn.

1. Chamomile

Chamomile tea helps with heartburn because it has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce inflammation in the esophagus and stomach, which can cause heartburn.

  4. Apigenin: Apigenin has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that can help protect the lining of the stomach and esophagus from damage, preventing heartburn symptoms.
  5. Flavonoids: Flavonoids in chamomile have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which can help reduce inflammation and prevent muscle spasms in the digestive tract, alleviating heartburn.

The following list reveals what parts of chamomile are most commonly used to make medicinal tea for heartburn.

  3. Seeds: Chamomile seeds are rich in calming oils and constituents, which can help relax the digestive muscles and reduce the discomfort of heartburn when consumed as a tea.

The following procedure explains how to make chamomile tea for heartburn.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a kettle or pot for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of dried chamomile flowers to a tea infuser or teapot.
  3. Pour the hot water over the chamomile flowers and let steep for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the tea infuser or strain the tea into a cup.
  5. Enjoy your soothing chamomile tea, which may help alleviate heartburn symptoms.
