10 best herbal teas for eyesight

Herbal teas have been used for centuries as natural remedies for various health issues, including promoting eye health.

Some of the best herbal teas for eyesight include chamomile, green tea, and gingko biloba tea. Chamomile tea is rich in antioxidants that can help reduce inflammation and eye strains.

Green tea contains high levels of epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), which has been shown to protect the eyes from damage caused by free radicals. Gingko biloba tea, on the other hand, improves blood circulation in the body, including the eyes, promoting better oxygen and nutrient supply to the eyes. However, it's important to note that herbal teas should not replace any prescribed medication for eye conditions.

It's best to consult with a healthcare provider or optometrist for personalized advice.
