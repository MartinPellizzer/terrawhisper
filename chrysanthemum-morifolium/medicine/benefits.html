1. Alleviates cold symptoms

"Ju Hua alleviates cold symptoms" refers to its ability to reduce or ease the discomfort caused by a common cold.

The following list includes the main medicinal constituents that give Chrysanthemum morifolium this benefit.

  • Flavonoids
  • Iridoids
  • Chlorogenic acid
  • Coumarins
  • Polyphenols
  • Volatile oils
  • Saponins

Flavonoids and iridoids are known for their antioxidant properties, which help to fight off free radicals. Free radicals can damage cells causing health problems such as the common cold. Thus, flavonoids and iridoids in Ju Hua may help alleviate cold symptoms by strengthening the immune system. Chlorogenic acid is a polyphenolic compound with antioxidant capabilities which helps protect our bodies against oxidative stress. This stress can contribute to cold symptoms. Coumarins are also known for their anti-inflammatory and antibacterial actions, helping reduce inflammation caused by bacterial infection that may lead to colds. Polyphenols are a group of compounds with antioxidant activities which help combat the free radicals responsible for cold symptoms and damage cell structure in our bodies. Volatile oils found in Ju Hua have decongestive properties, meaning they can help open up congested nasal passages alleviating symptoms associated with the common cold. Lastly, saponins are plant compounds that exhibit immunostimulatory effects, meaning they enhance our body's immune system, potentially leading to an improvement in one's ability to fight off cold-causing pathogens.
