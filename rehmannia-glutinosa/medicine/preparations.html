10 Best Medicinal Preparations of Rehmannia glutinosa (Rehmannia)

Rehmannia glutinosa, a plant native to Asia, has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties.

Its roots are often used to prepare various medicinal preparations, including tisanes and tinctures. A tisane, or herbal tea, can be made by steeping dried and chopped roots in hot water.

This preparation is believed to help treat a variety of ailments, such as anemia, constipation, and inflammation. On the other hand, a tincture is made by soaking the roots in alcohol, which extracts the active compounds. This preparation is typically taken orally and is used to treat conditions such as kidney disease, dizziness, and high blood pressure.

However, it is important to note that the effectiveness of these preparations is not always supported by scientific evidence, and it is crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before using them for medicinal purposes.
