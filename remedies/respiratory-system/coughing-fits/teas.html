Eucalyptus globulus teas are a popular herbal remedy used to treat persistent and severe coughing fits, particularly those associated with respiratory conditions such as bronchitis and asthma.

  1. Get 1 cup of fresh Eucalyptus globulus leaves and 1 cup of boiling water.

  5. Drink the tea while it's warm to help soothe your cough and relieve congestion.

  5. Consult a doctor for proper dosage and treatment plan.

Eucalyptus globulus teas can be used to help alleviate coughing fits, but it may cause side effects such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea in some individuals due to its high menthol content.

Precautions should be taken when using Eucalyptus globulus teas to treat coughing fits, including drinking plenty of water to avoid dehydration, avoiding excessive consumption, and being aware of any potential allergic reactions to the menthol or other ingredients.
