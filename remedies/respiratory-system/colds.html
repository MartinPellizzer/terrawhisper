What to know about colds before treating it with medicinal herbs

Colds are viral infections of the upper respiratory tract, characterized by symptoms such as a runny nose, sore throat, cough, and congestion.

They can negatively impact people's lives by causing discomfort, missed work or school, and in some cases, leading to more severe complications. Colds are caused by over 200 different viruses, with rhinoviruses being the most common.

What is colds and how it affects your life?

A cold is a viral infection that primarily affects the nose and throat.

It is a common illness that can cause various unpleasant symptoms, such as a runny or stuffy nose, sore throat, cough, and sneezing. In addition, people with colds may also experience fatigue, headache, and body aches.

These symptoms can significantly impact a person's daily life, making it difficult to concentrate, complete tasks, or participate in usual activities. For example, a stuffy nose can make it hard to breathe, sleep, or taste food, while a persistent cough can disrupt sleep and cause discomfort. Furthermore, colds can also lead to missed work or school days, and in some cases, can even progress to more severe illnesses such as pneumonia or bronchitis.

Overall, a cold can be a significant inconvenience and can negatively affect a person's physical, emotional, and social well-being.
