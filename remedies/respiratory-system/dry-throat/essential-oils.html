  • Antimicrobial properties: Peppermint oil's antimicrobial compounds help to combat bacterial and fungal infections that can contribute to a dry, sore throat, promoting healthy tissue and preventing further irritation.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint essential oil for dry throat.

  1. Harvest 20 grams of fresh peppermint leaves at peak growth stage in early morning for optimal oil content.
  2. Steam distill the harvested peppermint leaves using 200 grams of water for 3 hours at atmospheric pressure.

  4. Store the filtered essential oil in dark glass bottles with tight-fitting lids at room temperature for up to 6 months.
  5. Use the resulting mentha x piperita essential oil as directed for relief of dry throat discomfort in dosages of 10-15 drops per application.
