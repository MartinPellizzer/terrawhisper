Herbal teas, such as chamomile, slippery elm, and licorice root, have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties that help to relieve irritation and reduce inflammation in the throat. Peppermint tea can also help to alleviate congestion and ease breathing. Additionally, ginger and turmeric teas have natural antiviral and antibacterial properties that can help to fight off infections and boost the immune system.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before using herbal teas as a treatment for any ailment.
