Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tasmanian blue gum for cough.

  1. Harvest 250g of fresh or dried eucalyptus globulus leaves from a healthy plant.
  2. Dry the leaves in a low-temperature oven at 50c for 2 hours.
  3. Chop the dried leaves into small pieces and place them in a clean glass jar.
  4. Mix 50ml of high-proof vodka with 250g of chopped leaves in the jar.
  5. Steep the mixture for 2 weeks, shaking the jar daily, then strain and discard the solids.
