FAQ

Can drinking herbal tea prevent runny nose from forming?

Drinking herbal tea may help ease a runny nose.

Some teas, like peppermint and eucalyptus, have ingredients that can thin mucus and open airways. These teas can calm the nose and sinuses, reducing congestion.

They may not completely prevent a runny nose, but they can provide relief from symptoms.

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for runny nose every day?

Herbal teas can help soothe a runny nose, but it's not recommended to drink them every day.

Some herbs, like peppermint and eucalyptus, can be too strong and cause stomach problems or interact with medications.

It's best to limit your intake to when you need it, like during colds or allergies.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in runny nose?

Herbal teas can help with a runny nose, but it depends on the type of tea and your body's response.

Some people notice relief within a few hours, while others may take a day or two to feel better.

It's usually a gradual process, and you might need to try different teas to find what works best for you.
