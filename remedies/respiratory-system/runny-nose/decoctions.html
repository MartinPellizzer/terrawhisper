Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic purple coneflower for runny nose.

  1. Harvest echinacea purpurea roots and rhizomes in the fall after the first frost for medicinal purposes.
  2. Dry the harvested roots and rhizomes in a single layer at 150 degrees fahrenheit for eight hours.
  3. Grind the dried roots and rhizomes into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle.
  4. Combine one to two teaspoons of the powder with eight ounces of boiling water to make a decoction.
  5. Steep the mixture for 10 to 15 minutes and strain before drinking the liquid decoction.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for runny nose.
