The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for chronic bronchitis.

  1. Gather 10-15 dried glycyrrhiza glabra roots and grind them into a fine powder using a spice grinder or mortar and pestle.
  2. Measure one teaspoon of the powdered root and combine it with one cup of boiling water in a large teapot.
  3. Allow the mixture to steep for 5-7 minutes, then strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean teapot or cup.
  4. Add honey to taste, approximately one to two teaspoons, and stir well to dissolve the sweetness evenly throughout the tea.
  5. Let the tea cool to room temperature before refrigerating it for up to 24 hours, serving chilled as needed throughout the day.
