Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for chronic bronchitis.

  1. Harvest 500g of fresh or dried glycyrrhiza glabra roots and clean them thoroughly with water.

  5. Store the tincture in a cool dark place for at least 2 months before using it.
