  • Astringent: Yarrow tincture's astringent properties help tighten and soothe mucous membranes, reducing irritation and inflammation in the respiratory tract. This is attributed to the tannins found in yarrow.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for chronic bronchitis.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, or use 1 ounce of dried yarrow, and chop finely.
