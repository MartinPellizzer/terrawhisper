11 Herbal Creams For Post Nasal Drip

Thymus vulgaris, or thyme, has antimicrobial properties that can help combat bacterial or fungal infections that can exacerbate the issue. By incorporating these herbs into your daily routine, you can start to feel the benefits. Your sinuses will start to clear out, and you'll notice a reduction in mucus production.

This can lead to a significant improvement in your quality of life, making it easier to sleep, eat, and go about your daily activities without feeling drained or exhausted.

1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus creams contain eucalyptol, a natural compound that helps to break down mucus and ease congestion.

This makes it easier to breathe and reduces the symptoms of post nasal drip. When using Eucalyptus globulus creams for post nasal drip, apply a small amount to your chest or nose, and inhale the vapors. You can also add a few drops of eucalyptus oil to a bowl of hot water, and inhale the steam.

However, be sure to follow the instructions on the packaging and use the creams as directed. As you inhale the vapors, you may start to feel a decrease in congestion and a reduction in the amount of mucus flowing down the back of your throat. Your cough may also become less frequent and less severe. You may also notice that your sinuses feel clearer and your breathing becomes easier. However, it's also possible that you may experience some initial discomfort or stinging sensation from the eucalyptol.

This is usually temporary and will subside as your body adjusts to the treatment.

2. Echinacea purpurea

When using Echinacea purpurea creams for post nasal drip, apply a small amount to the affected area, usually the nasal passages and throat, 2-3 times a day.
