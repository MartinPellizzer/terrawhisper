  1. Gather 1 tablespoon of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root, a cup of water, and a tea strainer.
  2. Boil the cup of water in a pot.
  3. Add the 1 tablespoon of dried root to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into a cup and drink while warm.

To use Glycyrrhiza glabra teas safely, it is essential to consume them in moderation and limit the duration of treatment, as excessive consumption can lead to these adverse effects.
