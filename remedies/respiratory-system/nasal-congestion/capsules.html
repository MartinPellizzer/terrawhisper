Allergic rhinitis, also known as hay fever, is an allergic response to environmental triggers like pollen or dust mites, resulting in symptoms such as nasal congestion, sneezing, and itchy eyes. Herbal capsules containing ingredients like butterbur, boswellia, and turmeric may help alleviate these symptoms by reducing inflammation and supporting immune function.

However, it's important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.
