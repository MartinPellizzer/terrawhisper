  • Leaves: The leaves are rich in petasin, a key compound that helps to alleviate symptoms of hay fever, such as congestion and runny nose.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic butterbur for hay fever.

  1. Harvest the petasites hybridus leaves and roots in the spring when the plant is in flower.
  2. Dry the harvested petasites hybridus material in a warm dry place for 2 to 3 weeks.
  3. Chop the dried petasites hybridus into small pieces and use a ratio of 1 part petasites to 2 parts solvent such as ethanol.
  4. Steep the chopped petasites hybridus in the solvent for 2 to 6 weeks in a cool dark place.
  5. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or coffee filter and discard the solids to obtain the tincture.
