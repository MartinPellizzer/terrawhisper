1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus teas contains compounds like eucalyptol, a key oil that helps break down mucus and makes it easier to cough up.

This is especially helpful for people with bronchitis, a condition characterized by inflammation and congestion in the airways. The menthol in Eucalyptus globulus also helps thin mucus, making it less sticky and easier to expel. Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties of the tea may help reduce swelling in the airways, making breathing easier. If you're experiencing bronchitis symptoms, consider making a warm tea by steeping dried Eucalyptus globulus leaves in boiling water for 5-10 minutes. You can add honey for sweetness and a soothing effect on the throat.

Some people find it helpful to drink Eucalyptus globulus tea 3-4 times a day, especially before bed to help with nighttime coughing. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Eucalyptus globulus tea as a treatment for bronchitis, especially if you have a severe case or underlying health conditions. When using Eucalyptus globulus tea for bronchitis, you may experience some relief from congestion and coughing within a few hours. The tea may help you sleep better at night, as you'll be able to breathe more easily. However, it's crucial to continue taking medications prescribed by your doctor and not rely solely on Eucalyptus globulus tea for treatment.

With consistent use and proper medical care, you may find that your symptoms gradually improve, and your airways become less inflamed.
