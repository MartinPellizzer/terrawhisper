10 best herbal teas for asthma

Herbal teas for asthma are infusions made from various dried herbs, flowers, and spices that can help alleviate asthma symptoms.

These teas contain natural anti-inflammatory and bronchodilator properties, which help relax the airways and improve breathing. Some popular herbal teas for asthma include chamomile, ginger, licorice root, turmeric, and fenugreek.

Drinking these teas regularly can significantly improve the quality of life for asthma sufferers by reducing the frequency and severity of asthma attacks.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic european goldenrod tea for asthma.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water and let it cool down for a few minutes until it reaches a temperature of around 200 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of dried European goldenrod herb to a tea infuser or teapot.
  3. Pour the hot water over the herb and let it steep for 5-10 minutes to allow the active compounds to be released.
  4. Remove the tea infuser or strain the tea to remove the herb.
  5. Drink the tea while it is still warm, up to three times a day, to help manage asthma symptoms.
