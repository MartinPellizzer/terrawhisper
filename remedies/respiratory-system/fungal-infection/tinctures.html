The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic garlic tincture for fungal infection.

  1. Chop 2-3 garlic bulbs and place them in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add 2 cups of 80-proof vodka or rum, ensuring garlic is fully submerged.

  4. Shake the jar daily to help the garlic release its medicinal properties.
