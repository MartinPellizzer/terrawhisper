1. Aloe vera

Aloe vera creams contain properties like salicylic acid, amino acids, and vitamins that help combat fungal infections.

The salicylic acid has antifungal properties that kill the fungus causing the infection, while the amino acids and vitamins help soothe and calm the affected area. When using Aloe vera creams for fungal infections, first make sure to clean and dry the affected area thoroughly. Then, apply a thin layer of the cream directly to the infected area, ideally twice a day.

It's essential to follow the product instructions and not overuse the cream, as this can cause skin irritation. As you start using the Aloe vera cream, you may notice a reduction in itching and redness within a few days. The fungal infection may start to clear up, and you may see a decrease in the severity of symptoms like blistering or crusting.
