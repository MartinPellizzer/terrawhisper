1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus essential oils contains compounds like eucalyptol, limonene, and alpha-pinene, which have natural decongestant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These constituents help to break down mucus and reduce swelling in the nasal passages, making it easier to breathe. When you inhale Eucalyptus globulus essential oils, the molecules from these compounds travel up your nostrils and reach your nasal passages, where they work to loosen and clear out excess mucus and debris. To use Eucalyptus globulus essential oils for a blocked nose, try adding a few drops to a bowl of hot water, then inhaling the steam for 5-10 minutes.

You can also add a few drops to your diffuser or inhale it directly from a cotton swab. Be sure to dilute the essential oil with a carrier oil if you're applying it to your skin. When you inhale Eucalyptus globulus essential oils, you may start to feel a sense of relief within a few minutes, as your nasal passages begin to clear out and your breathing becomes easier.

You may also notice a reduction in congestion and a decrease in the amount of mucus you produce.
