What is runny nose and how it affects your life?

A runny nose, also known as rhinorrhea, is a common condition characterized by an excessive flow of nasal discharge, often clear and watery.

This can cause discomfort, congestion, and sinus pressure, making everyday activities challenging. A runny nose can affect various aspects of life, such as work or school performance, due to constant sniffles and congestion, making it difficult to focus and participate in activities.

Social interactions can also be impacted, as individuals may feel self-conscious about their appearance and avoid social gatherings or events. Even simple tasks like sleeping, eating, or exercising can be disrupted by the constant need to blow one's nose and adjust nasal passages.

Additionally, a runny nose can exacerbate allergies, asthma, and other respiratory conditions, making it essential to manage and treat the condition effectively to maintain overall quality of life.

What are the main causes of runny nose?

Overall, a runny nose can significantly disrupt daily life and cause distress, discomfort, and inconvenience.
