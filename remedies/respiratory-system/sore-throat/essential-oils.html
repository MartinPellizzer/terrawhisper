The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic thyme essential oil for sore throat.

  1. Gather dried thymus vulgaris leaves and stems in quantities of 50 grams for every 100 milliliters of carrier oil needed.

  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or coffee filter into another clean glass container discarding solids and reserve the infused oil.
  5. Transfer the infused oil to a dark glass bottle and store it in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight.
