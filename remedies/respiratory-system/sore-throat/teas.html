The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic thyme for sore throat.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried thymus vulgaris leaves measuring one tablespoon for every cup of boiling water.

  4. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth squeezing out any remaining liquid.
  5. Serve the soothing herbal tea warm or at room temperature with one to two teaspoons of honey for added comfort.
