The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic great mullein tea for sore throat.

  1. Gather 2-3 dried mullein leaves and place them in a tea infuser or loose leaf teapot.
  2. Boil 8 ounces of water and pour it over the mullein leaves, allowing them to steep for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Optional: Add honey or lemon to taste for added soothing and flavor.
  4. Let the tea cool down to a comfortable drinking temperature.
