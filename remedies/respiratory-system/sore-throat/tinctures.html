The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice tincture for sore throat.

  1. Harvest dried roots of glycyrrhiza glabra measure out one part root to two parts vodka by volume.

  4. Store the jar in a cool dark place for at least four weeks or six months shaking daily for 2-3 seconds.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass bottle discarding the solids and labeling.
