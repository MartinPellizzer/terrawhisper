Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for altitude sickness.

  1. Harvest 2-4 cups of ginkgo biloba leaves from mature trees in late summer or early fall.
  2. Clean 2-4 cups of ginkgo biloba leaves by rinsing them in cold running water for 5 minutes.
  3. Dry 2-4 cups of ginkgo biloba leaves in a single layer at room temperature for 1-2 weeks.
  4. Steep 1 teaspoon of dried ginkgo biloba leaves in 8 ounces of boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea and serve immediately, allowing it to cool for 2-3 minutes before consumption.
