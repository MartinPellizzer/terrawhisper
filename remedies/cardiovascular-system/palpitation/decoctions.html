The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic foxglove for palpitation.

  1. Harvest 10-15 grams of fresh digitalis purpurea leaves in the early morning after dew has dried.
  2. Rinse the harvested leaves with cold running water to remove any dirt or debris.
