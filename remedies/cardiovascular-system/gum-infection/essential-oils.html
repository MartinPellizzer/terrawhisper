1. Melaleuca alternifolia

Melaleuca alternifolia essential oils contains powerful properties that help combat gum infections.

It's rich in compounds like eucalyptol, cineole, and terpineol, which have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects. These constituents help reduce bacteria and swelling in the gums, making it an effective natural remedy for gum infections. When using Melaleuca alternifolia essential oils for gum infection, swish 2-3 drops of the oil in water around your mouth for 30 seconds to 1 minute, 2-3 times a day.

This helps the oil penetrate and target the infected area. After swishing, spit it out and do not swallow the oil. As you continue using the oil, you may notice a reduction in inflammation, pain, and bleeding in the gums.
