The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sage tea for gum infection.

  1. In a small pot, boil 1 cup of water for 3-5 minutes.

  5. Allow the tea to cool to a drinkable temperature before sipping.

How to best treat gum infection with herbal teas?

However, it's important to also maintain good oral hygiene and seek professional dental care if symptoms persist.
