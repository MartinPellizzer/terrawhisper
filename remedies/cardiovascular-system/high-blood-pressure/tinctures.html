The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic garlic tincture for high blood pressure.

  1. Chop 1 cup of fresh organic garlic cloves and place them in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add 2 cups of a menstruum, such as 50% distilled water and 50% vodka or rum, covering the garlic.
  3. Tightly seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking it daily.
  4. After the maceration period, strain the mixture using cheesecloth, squeezing out the liquid.
  5. Store the final tincture in a dark glass bottle, in a cool, dark place, and take 1-2 ml three times a day for high blood pressure.
