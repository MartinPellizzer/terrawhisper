Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic hawthorn for high blood pressure.

  1. Harvest 20-30g of fresh or dried leaves and branches of crataegus monogyna in late summer or early autumn.
  2. Dry the harvested material in a warm place for 2-3 hours or until completely dry.
