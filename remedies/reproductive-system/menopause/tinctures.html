The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sage for menopause.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried salvia officinalis leaves in the morning when the essential oils are most concentrated.

  4. Mix one part of the powdered salvia officinalis with two parts of high-proof vodka or grain alcohol in a glass jar for 2 weeks shaking daily.
