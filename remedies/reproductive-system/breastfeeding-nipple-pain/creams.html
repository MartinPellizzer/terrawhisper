1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams contain anti-inflammatory compounds like triterpenoids, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which help reduce swelling and ease pain in the nipples.

The cream's soothing effect is due to its ability to calm the skin, promoting faster healing and reducing discomfort. To use Calendula officinalis cream for nipple pain, gently apply a thin layer to the affected area after feeding, and before feeding if the pain persists.
