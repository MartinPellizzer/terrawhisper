FAQ

Can drinking herbal tea prevent uterine fibroids from forming?

Some herbal teas may help prevent uterine fibroids from forming, but the evidence is limited.

Tea like red clover and chasteberry are thought to reduce estrogen levels, which might slow fibroid growth.

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for uterine fibroids every day?

Some herbal teas, like red clover and raspberry leaf, are said to help with uterine fibroids.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in uterine fibroids?

Herbal teas may help with uterine fibroids, but it's hard to say exactly how long it takes to see results.
