Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ashwagandha for hormonal imbalance.

  1. Harvest withania somnifera roots and leaves from a trusted source in the morning to ensure optimal potency.
  2. Dry the harvested withania somnifera roots and leaves using a food dehydrator at 150 degrees fahrenheit for 2 hours.
  3. Grind 2 to 4 grams of dried withania somnifera roots and leaves into a fine powder using a spice grinder.
  4. Steep 1 teaspoon of the ground withania somnifera powder in a cup of boiling water for 5 to 7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea and serve it hot or chilled within 30 minutes of preparation to retain its benefits.
