The list below shows the primary medicinal constituents in Withania somnifera teas that help with adrenal fatigue.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ashwagandha for adrenal fatigue.

  1. Select 2 teaspoons of dried withania somnifera root powder or 3 tablespoons of fresh root for every 8 ounces of boiling water.
  2. Combine the withania somnifera powder or fresh root with 8 ounces of boiling water in a heat-resistant cup.
  3. Reduce heat to a simmer and let the mixture steep for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the liquid through a fine-mesh sieve into a cup to remove the solids.
  5. Serve the tea warm or at room temperature after allowing it to cool for 2 to 3 minutes.
