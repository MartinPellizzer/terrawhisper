The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ashwagandha for adrenal fatigue.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginseng for adrenal fatigue.

  1. Harvest dried panax ginseng roots and measure out one tablespoon for every eight ounces of water.
  2. Combine the measured ginseng roots with filtered boiling water in a medium-sized saucepan.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer the mixture for 15-20 minutes or until the liquid has reduced by half.
  4. Strain the tea into a separate container using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove any solids.
  5. Allow the tea to cool to room temperature before refrigerating it for at least four hours or overnight before consumption.
