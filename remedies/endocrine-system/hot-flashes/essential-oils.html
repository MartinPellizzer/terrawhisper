The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic angelica essential oil for hot flashes.

  1. Harvest 20 grams of fresh angelica archangelica roots and leaves in the early morning for optimal oil yield.

  5. Store the filtered angelica archangelica essential oil in an airtight glass bottle, away from direct sunlight and heat sources, for future use.
