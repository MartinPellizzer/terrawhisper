Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for hot flashes.

  4. Mix the powder with a natural capsule filler such as rice flour in a 1:1 ratio by weight.
  5. Fill empty vegetable capsules with 400-500mg of the valeriana officinalis powder mixture.
