10 best herbal teas for inflammation

Herbal teas for inflammation are caffeine-free infusions made from various dried herbs, flowers, and spices, known for their anti-inflammatory properties.

These teas help reduce inflammation by providing antioxidants and other beneficial compounds that help combat free radicals, which contribute to inflammation. Some popular herbal teas for inflammation include chamomile, ginger, turmeric, and green tea.

For example, chamomile tea is often used to soothe digestive inflammation, while ginger and turmeric teas help alleviate joint pain and inflammation. Green tea, rich in epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), has been shown to reduce inflammation and improve overall health.

By incorporating these herbal teas into daily routines, individuals can experience relief from inflammation-related symptoms, ultimately improving their quality of life.
