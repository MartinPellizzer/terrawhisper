The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for inflammation.

  1. Grind dried curcuma longa roots into a fine powder using a spice grinder for 2 minutes.
  2. Combine 10 grams of the powdered curcuma longa with 20 milliliters of coconut oil in a small saucepan.

  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture steep for 2 hours before straining it through a cheesecloth into a clean bowl.
