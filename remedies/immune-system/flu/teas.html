Is it safe to consume herbal teas for flu every day?

While herbal teas can help ease flu symptoms, drinking them every day isn't recommended.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in flu?

Herbal teas can help with flu symptoms, but it's hard to say exactly how long it takes to feel better.
