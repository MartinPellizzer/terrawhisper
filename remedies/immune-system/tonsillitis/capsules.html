Some herbs that may be used in herbal capsules for treating pharyngitis and laryngitis include licorice root, marshmallow root, and slippery elm. These herbs have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties that can help to alleviate irritation and promote healing in the throat.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before taking herbal capsules or any other supplements for the treatment of these or any other medical conditions.
