The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic japanese honeysuckle for tonsillitis.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried lonicera japonica flowers and leaves in accordance with traditional chinese medicine guidelines.
  2. Combine 10-15g of dried lonicera japonica with 6 cups of pure water in a large pot for infusion.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes to allow for optimal extraction.
  4. Strain the decoction through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a clean container discarding solids immediately.
  5. Allow the decoction to cool and store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or freeze for longer preservation.
