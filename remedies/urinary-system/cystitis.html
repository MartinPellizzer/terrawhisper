What is cystitis and how it affects your life?

Cystitis is a painful and common urinary tract infection (UTI) that affects millions of people worldwide.

It is characterized by inflammation of the bladder, leading to discomfort, pain, and difficulty urinating. The symptoms of cystitis can be debilitating, making everyday activities a challenge. For instance, even simple tasks like getting out of bed or going for a walk can become excruciating due to the constant need to urinate.

Sleep patterns are disrupted, making it hard to get a good night's rest. Work and social life suffer as people often need to take frequent breaks to urinate, leaving them feeling embarrassed and self-conscious. Physical activities like sports, exercise, or even playing with children are also compromised due to the pain and discomfort. Even simple chores like cooking, cleaning, or driving become difficult to manage, making life a struggle.

The emotional toll is also significant, with feelings of frustration, anxiety, and even depression affecting relationships and overall well-being.
