Salix alba, also known as white willow, teas helps with neck pain because of its anti-inflammatory properties.

  • Salicylic acid: Salicylic acid is a phenolic acid that has anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, helping to reduce pain and inflammation in the neck by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines.

Plant's Parts

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic white willow for neck pain.

  1. Gather 20g of dried salix alba bark and 1l of boiling water to prepare a soothing tea.

  3. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a large heat-resistant cup to remove solids.
  4. Add honey to taste to enhance the flavor and sweetness of the herbal tea.
  5. Drink the tea immediately after preparation to reap its full health benefits within 30 minutes.
