The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for arthritis.

  1. Gather 2 teaspoons of dried turmeric root powder or fresh turmeric root 3-4 inches long.

  4. Strain the tea using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a large mug or teapot while hot.
