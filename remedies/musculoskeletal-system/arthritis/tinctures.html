The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic wormwood tincture for arthritis.

  1. Chop 2 ounces of dried wormwood herb and place it in a clean glass jar.

Ailments similar to arthritis that are treated with herbal tinctures include osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and gout.

These tinctures often contain anti-inflammatory and analgesic herbs such as willow bark, turmeric, and ginger. For example, willow bark contains salicin, which is a natural pain reliever similar to aspirin.

Turmeric and ginger have potent anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce swelling and pain in the joints. Other herbs like devil's claw, boswellia, and nettle leaf are also used in tinctures to alleviate symptoms of arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal remedies.
