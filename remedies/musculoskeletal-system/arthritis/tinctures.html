Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic bell pepper for arthritis.

  1. Harvest ripe capsicum annuum fruits at the peak of their ripeness for optimal flavor and potency.
  2. Dry the harvested capsicum annuum fruits in a warm dry place for 7 to 10 days.
  3. Chop the dried capsicum annuum fruits into small pieces using a sharp knife or tool.
  4. Combine the chopped dried capsicum annuum pieces with 80 proof ethanol in a 1 to 5 ratio and steep for 2 weeks.
