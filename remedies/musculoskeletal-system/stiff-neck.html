What is stiff neck and how it affects your life?

A stiff neck, also known as cervical stiffness, is a condition where the muscles in the neck become tight and painful, making it difficult to move the head or neck.

This can cause discomfort, limited mobility, and even affect daily activities. For instance, a stiff neck can prevent you from enjoying your favorite activities, such as playing sports, dancing, or even simply playing with your children. It can also make it challenging to work or study, as simple tasks like typing or reading can become unbearable.

Moreover, a stiff neck can disrupt your sleep patterns, causing fatigue, irritability, and decreased productivity. Additionally, it can also affect your social life, making it difficult to participate in social events or activities that require neck movement, such as hiking, swimming, or even just walking. Furthermore, a stiff neck can also impact your mental well-being, leading to feelings of anxiety, depression, and decreased confidence.

Overall, a stiff neck can have a significant impact on one's quality of life, making it essential to seek medical attention if symptoms persist.
