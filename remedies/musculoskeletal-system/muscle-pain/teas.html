Turmeric teas helps with muscle pain because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, particularly curcumin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for muscle pain.

  1. Grind one teaspoon of dried turmeric root curcuma longa into a fine powder using a spice grinder or mortar pestle.
