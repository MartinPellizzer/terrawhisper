The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic black cohosh tea for muscle pain.

  1. Measure out 1 teaspoon of dried black cohosh root.
  2. Place the herb in a tea infuser or teapot with a strainer.

  5. Remove the infuser or strain the tea before drinking.

How to best treat muscle pain with herbal teas?

The best way to treat muscle pain with herbal teas is by choosing teas with natural anti-inflammatory properties, such as chamomile, ginger, turmeric, or willow bark.

For best results, drink 2-3 cups of herbal tea per day. Additionally, consider taking a warm bath with Epsom salts and a few drops of essential oils, like lavender or eucalyptus, to further soothe sore muscles.

Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new treatment regimen.
