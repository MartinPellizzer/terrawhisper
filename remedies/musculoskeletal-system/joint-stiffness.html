What is joint stiffness and how it affects your life?

Joint stiffness is a common condition characterized by reduced mobility and flexibility in the joints, making everyday activities increasingly challenging.

It can affect any joint in the body, but common areas include the knees, hips, hands, and wrists. This stiffness can make even simple tasks, like bending, twisting, or extending the joints, difficult and painful.

As a result, individuals with joint stiffness may struggle to perform daily routines such as dressing, grooming, and cooking, leading to a decreased quality of life. Additionally, joint stiffness can also hinder participation in recreational activities, forcing individuals to give up hobbies and interests they once enjoyed. It can also make traveling, especially long-distance trips, extremely uncomfortable, and even make it difficult to get in and out of bed.

Furthermore, joint stiffness can lead to reduced productivity at work, impacting one's career and earning potential.
