Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for osteoarthritis.

  1. Harvest 10-15 fresh turmeric roots and wash them thoroughly under running cold water for 2 minutes.
  2. Peel the turmeric roots using a vegetable peeler to remove the outer skin layer completely.

  4. Boil 2 cups of water in a medium-sized saucepan over high heat for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Steep 1 teaspoon of chopped turmeric in the boiling water for 5-7 minutes and strain to serve.
