What is ankylosing spondylitis and how it affects your life?

Ankylosing spondylitis (AS) is a chronic and progressive autoimmune disease that affects the spine and other joints, leading to severe pain, stiffness, and limited mobility.

It is characterized by the inflammation of the spinal joints, which can cause the vertebrae to fuse together, leading to a loss of flexibility and range of motion. People with AS often experience chronic back pain, stiffness, and swelling, which can make everyday activities extremely challenging.

For instance, simple tasks like walking, bending, or even getting out of bed can become excruciatingly painful. Additionally, AS can also affect other areas of the body, such as the hips, knees, and ankles, causing further mobility limitations. For example, a person with AS may struggle to climb stairs, participate in sports, or even engage in household chores, leading to significant restrictions on their daily life.

Furthermore, AS can also have a significant impact on mental health, leading to anxiety, depression, and feelings of isolation and frustration.

What are the main causes of ankylosing spondylitis?

Ankylosing spondylitis is a chronic and debilitating autoimmune disorder that primarily affects the spine, although other joints and organs can also be impacted.

The condition causes inflammation and pain in the spinal joints, leading to a gradual fusion of the vertebrae, which can result in a loss of flexibility and mobility. As a result, individuals with ankylosing spondylitis may experience severe back pain, stiffness, and limited range of motion, making everyday activities, such as getting dressed, bathing, or even walking, extremely challenging.

Additionally, the condition can also lead to fatigue, headaches, and gastrointestinal issues, further exacerbating the quality of life. Simple tasks like tying shoelaces, using a computer, or even getting out of bed can become Herculean efforts, as the constant pain and stiffness make it difficult to perform even the most mundane tasks.

In severe cases, ankylosing spondylitis can also lead to bowel obstruction, heart problems, and eye inflammation, making it a significant concern for those affected.

When considering herbal remedies for ankylosing spondylitis, it is essential to exercise caution and consult with a healthcare provider before use.
