Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic danshen for foot pain.

  1. Harvest 1 part of fresh salvia miltiorrhiza roots and dry them for 2 weeks in a well-ventilated area.
  2. Chop 1 part of dried salvia miltiorrhiza roots into smaller pieces and mix with 2 parts of 80 proof vodka.
  3. Steep the mixture in a glass jar for 2 weeks in a cool dark place, shaking daily.
  4. Strain the liquid from the jar and discard the solids, using a coffee filter to clarify the liquid.
  5. Transfer the tincture to a glass bottle and store it in a cool dark place, away from children and pets.
