  3. Grind 6 tablespoons of dried turmeric root into a fine powder using a coffee grinder.
  4. Mix the powdered turmeric with 1 cup of high-proof vodka and 1/2 cup of distilled water, stirring well.
  5. Store the tincture in dark glass bottles at room temperature for 7-10 days, shaking daily.
