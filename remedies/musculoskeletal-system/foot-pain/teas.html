The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic anise hyssop tea for foot pain.

  1. In a saucepan, combine 2 cups of water and 2 tablespoons of dried anise hyssop leaves.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and allow the tea to steep for an additional 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea to remove the leaves, then let it cool to a comfortable temperature.
  5. Soak your feet in the anise hyssop tea for 15-20 minutes, then pat them dry.

Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal remedy.
