The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic curry plant for knee pain.

  1. Harvest fresh helichrysum italicum flowers in the morning when they are fully bloomed, approximately 2 cups.
  2. Dry the harvested flowers using a warm air dryer for at least 48 hours or until moisture content is reduced.
