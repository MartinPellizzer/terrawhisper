Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic barberry for gout.

  1. Harvest 1-2 pounds of berberis vulgaris roots and wash them thoroughly to remove any dirt.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for gout.

  1. Harvest fresh curcuma longa rhizomes in the morning after the dew has evaporated and store them in a cool dry place.
