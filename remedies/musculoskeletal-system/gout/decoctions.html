Barberry decoctions helps with gout because of its unique ability to reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic barberry for gout.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried berberis vulgaris root bark measuring 10-15 grams per serving.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for gout.

  2. Rinse the ingredients thoroughly under cold running water then chop them into small pieces.
  3. Combine the chopped turmeric and ginger in a large pot with 4-6 cups of filtered water and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer the mixture for 15-20 minutes or until the liquid has reduced slightly.
  5. Strain the decoction through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean container and discard the solids.
