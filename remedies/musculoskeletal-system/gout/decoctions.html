The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic fennel for gout.

  1. Measure 2 tablespoons of dried fennel seeds and add them to a heat-resistant glass bowl.
  2. Heat 1 cup of water in a saucepan over low heat, then slowly pour it over the fennel seeds in the bowl.
  3. Steep the mixture for 10-15 minutes, allowing the fennel seeds to infuse their active compounds into the water.
  4. Strain the liquid using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the decoction in the refrigerator and consume it within 24 hours, taking 1/2 cup three times a day as needed for gout relief.
