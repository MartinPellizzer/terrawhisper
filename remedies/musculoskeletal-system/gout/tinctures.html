The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic barberry for gout.

  1. Harvest fresh berberis vulgaris roots and dry them in a warm place for at least seven days.
  2. Chop the dried roots into small pieces and soak them in high-proof vodka or grain alcohol for 14 days.

  4. Add more vodka or grain alcohol to the liquid if necessary to fill the bottle, leaving about an inch at the top.
