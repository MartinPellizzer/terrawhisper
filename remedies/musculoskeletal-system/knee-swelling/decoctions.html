Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for knee swelling.

  1. Gather 1 teaspoon of dried curcuma longa rhizome and 2 cups of water for the decoction.

  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
  5. Discard the solids and store the decoction in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.
