Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for knee swelling.

  1. Harvest fresh curcuma longa roots in the early morning after the dew has dried completely.
  2. Clean and dry the harvested roots in a low-temperature oven for 2 hours at 150 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. Chop the dried roots into small pieces using a sharp knife and weigh out 1 part roots to 2 parts 95 percent ethanol.
  4. Combine the chopped roots and ethanol in a clean glass jar and steep for 2-3 weeks in a cool dark place.
  5. Strain the liquid through cheesecloth and discard the solids then bottle the tincture and store in a cool dark place.
