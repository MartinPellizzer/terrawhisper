1. Zingiber officinale

To try Zingiber officinale teas for jaw pain, steep 1-2 teaspoons of fresh or dried ginger root in a cup of boiling water for 5-7 minutes, then strain and drink.

It's recommended to drink 2-3 cups a day for optimal effect. As you take action, you may experience a decrease in pain and discomfort, as well as improved mobility in your jaw. You may also notice a reduction in inflammation and swelling in the affected area.
