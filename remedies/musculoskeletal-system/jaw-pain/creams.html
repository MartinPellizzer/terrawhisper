Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic lemongrass for jaw pain.

  1. Harvest 20 grams of dried cymbopogon citratus leaves and store them in an airtight container for later use.

  4. Mix 100 grams of beeswax and 50 grams of shea butter with the infused liquid and stir until fully incorporated.
