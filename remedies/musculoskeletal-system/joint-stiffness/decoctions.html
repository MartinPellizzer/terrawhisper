Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for joint stiffness.

  1. Gather 30 grams of dried glycyrrhiza glabra roots and wash them in cold running water for 5 minutes.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the glycyrrhiza glabra mixture for 10 to 15 minutes.
