The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginger tea for joint stiffness.

  1. Rinse 2-3 inches of fresh ginger root under cold running water and gently scrub off any dirt.
  2. Peel the ginger using a vegetable peeler or a sharp knife to remove the outer layer.

  4. Combine 1 cup of boiling water with 1-2 teaspoons of grated ginger and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a mug, discarding solids and enjoy warm.
