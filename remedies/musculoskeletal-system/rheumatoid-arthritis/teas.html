10 best herbal teas for rheumatoid arthritis

Herbal teas for rheumatoid arthritis are infusions made from various plants and herbs that can help alleviate symptoms of this autoimmune disorder.

These teas can reduce inflammation, manage pain, and promote overall well-being. Examples of herbal teas that help with rheumatoid arthritis include ginger, turmeric, green tea, and willow bark tea.

Ginger tea, for instance, contains anti-inflammatory compounds that inhibit the production of inflammatory molecules, while turmeric tea is rich in curcumin, which has potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Green tea, on the other hand, contains epigallocatechin-3-gallate (EGCG), which has been shown to reduce inflammation and slow down the progression of rheumatoid arthritis. Willow bark tea contains salicin, a compound similar to aspirin, which can help manage pain and reduce inflammation.

By incorporating these herbal teas into their daily routine, individuals with rheumatoid arthritis can experience improved joint mobility, reduced pain and inflammation, and enhanced quality of life.
