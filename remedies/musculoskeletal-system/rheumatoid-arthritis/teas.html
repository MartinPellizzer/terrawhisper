The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric tea for rheumatoid arthritis.

  1. Gather high-quality dried turmeric powder 2-3 teaspoons or fresh turmeric root sliced into thin pieces.
  2. Boil filtered water in a pot and reduce the heat to a simmer for about 5 minutes.

  4. Steep the mixture for 10-15 minutes allowing the active compounds to infuse into the tea.
