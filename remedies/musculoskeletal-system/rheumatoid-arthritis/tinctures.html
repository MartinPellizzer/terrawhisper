9 Herbal Tinctures For Rheumatoid Arthritis

By incorporating herbal tinctures into their treatment plan, people with rheumatoid arthritis may be able to find relief from their symptoms and improve their overall well-being.

1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help to reduce inflammation and pain in the joints, making it a potential natural remedy for rheumatoid arthritis. When using Zingiber officinale tinctures for rheumatoid arthritis, it's recommended to start with a low dose, around 250-500 mg per day, and gradually increase as needed.

It's also essential to consult with a healthcare professional before taking ginger tinctures, especially if you're already taking medications or have any underlying health conditions. To get the most out of ginger tinctures, it's best to take them with warm water, ideally after a meal, to enhance absorption. Taking Zingiber officinale tinctures regularly may lead to noticeable improvements in joint pain and inflammation.
