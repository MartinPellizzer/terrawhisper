The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic wormwood tincture for rheumatoid arthritis.

  1. Chop 2 ounces of dried wormwood herb and place it in a clean glass jar.

Ginger has also been found to have anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects. These herbs can be taken in tincture form, which is a concentrated extract made by soaking the herbs in alcohol or glycerin.

However, it's important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new treatment regimen.
