The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric decoction for rheumatoid arthritis.

  1. Grind 2 teaspoons of dried turmeric roots into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle.

  4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the mixture for an additional 20 minutes, allowing the curcuma longa to infuse into the water.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a cup, discarding the solids and retaining the liquid for consumption.
