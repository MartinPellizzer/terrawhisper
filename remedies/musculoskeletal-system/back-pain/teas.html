10 best herbal teas for back pain

Herbal teas for back pain are infusions made from various plants and herbs, which have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties that help alleviate back pain.

These teas can be a natural and gentle alternative to over-the-counter or prescription pain medications. Some examples of herbal teas that are beneficial for back pain include chamomile, ginger, turmeric, and willow bark tea.

Drinking these herbal teas can improve the quality of life for those suffering from back pain by providing a natural, caffeine-free, and low-cost method to manage their pain and discomfort.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic large-leaved lime tea for back pain.
