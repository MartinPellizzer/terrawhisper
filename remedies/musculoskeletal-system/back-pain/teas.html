Curcuma longa teas, made from the turmeric plant, have been widely used to treat back pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

Recipe:

  1. Get 1 cup of water and boil it in a pot.
  2. Add 1/2 teaspoon of dried Turmeric root (Curcuma longa) to the boiling water.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup and add 1 tablespoon of honey to taste.
  5. Drink the tea 2-3 times a day to help alleviate back pain.

Catha edulis teas, also known as khat, have been traditionally used to treat back pain ailments due to their analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.
