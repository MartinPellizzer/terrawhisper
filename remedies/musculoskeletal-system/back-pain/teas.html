Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for back pain.

  4. Steep 1 teaspoon of curcuma longa powder in boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea through a fine-mesh sieve into a cup after steeping.
