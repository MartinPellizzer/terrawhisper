The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic arnica flower tincture for back pain.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh arnica flowers, carefully avoiding the toxic roots and stems, during their full bloom in late spring or early summer.
  2. Clean the flowers thoroughly, then let them wilt for several hours to reduce their moisture content.

It is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal remedies, as they can interact with other medications and have potential side effects.
