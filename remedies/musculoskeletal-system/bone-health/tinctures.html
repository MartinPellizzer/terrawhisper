Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic stinging nettle for bone health.

  1. Harvest 100 grams of fresh urtica dioica leaves and stems in the morning after dew has evaporated.
  2. Dry the harvested urtica dioica in a single layer at 35 degrees celsius for 24 hours.
  3. Combine 100 grams of dried urtica dioica with 500 milliliters of 80 proof vodka in a clean glass jar.
  4. Allow the urtica dioica and vodka mixture to steep for 2 to 6 weeks in a cool dark place.
  5. Strain the liquid from the urtica dioica through a cheesecloth or coffee filter into another clean glass container.
