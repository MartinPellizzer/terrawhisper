Stinging nettle decoctions helps with bone health because they are rich in nutrients that support bone mineralization, such as calcium, magnesium, and silica.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic stinging nettle for bone health.

  1. Harvest fresh urtica dioica leaves and stems in the morning after dew has evaporated.

  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-30 minutes allowing the flavors to meld together slowly.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass container discarding solids.
