Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic stinging nettle for bone health.

  1. Gather 30 grams of fresh urtica dioica leaves and stems or 15 grams of dried material for decoction.

  4. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean container to remove the solids.
