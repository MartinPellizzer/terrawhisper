Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for plantar fasciitis.

  1. Harvest 2-3 fresh turmeric rhizomes and wash them thoroughly under cold running water for 2 minutes.
  2. Peel the turmeric rhizomes using a vegetable peeler to remove the outer skin.
  3. Chop the peeled turmeric rhizomes into small pieces and measure out 1 teaspoon for each 8-ounce cup of water.
  4. Combine the turmeric pieces with 8 ounces of boiling water in a teapot or infuser and steep for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea mixture using a fine-mesh sieve and serve the warm turmeric tea immediately.
