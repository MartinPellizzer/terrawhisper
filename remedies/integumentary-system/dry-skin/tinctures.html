Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry skin.

  1. Harvest 100% organic aloe vera leaves with a thickness of 1 inch or more for optimal potency and freshness.
  2. Dry the harvested aloe vera leaves in a single layer at 105 degrees fahrenheit for 2 hours.
  3. Chop the dried aloe vera leaves into small pieces and add 80 proof vodka as a solvent at a 1:3 ratio.
  4. Steep the aloe vera mixture in a cool dark place for 2 to 4 weeks to allow for infusion.
  5. Strain the tincture through a cheesecloth and store it in dark glass bottles with tight-fitting lids at room temperature.
