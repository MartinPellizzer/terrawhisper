Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry skin.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves in the early morning or late afternoon for optimal potency and flavor.
  2. Wash the harvested leaves thoroughly with cold water to remove any dirt or debris.
  3. Chop the leaves into small pieces to increase their surface area for better extraction.
  4. Combine the chopped aloe vera with 1 cup of boiling water to create a decoction.
  5. Steep the mixture for 10-15 minutes before straining and serving as a soothing drink.
