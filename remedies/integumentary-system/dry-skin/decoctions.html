The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry skin.

  4. Add one teaspoon of dried chamomile flowers and one teaspoon of dried calendula petals to the aloe vera mixture.
  5. Steep the mixture for 20-25 minutes, then strain and discard solids before using the decoction as needed.
