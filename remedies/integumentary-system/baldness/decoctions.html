The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic guggul for baldness.

  1. Gather one teaspoon of dried commiphora mukul resin and half a liter of pure water.
  2. Combine the ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture using a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass container.
  4. Allow the decoction to cool at room temperature before refrigerating it for up to three days.
  5. Use one tablespoon of the cooled decoction as a topical treatment applied directly to the affected area twice daily.
