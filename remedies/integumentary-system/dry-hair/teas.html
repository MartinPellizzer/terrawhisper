Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sesame for dry hair.

  1. Harvest the sesamum indicum flowers at the peak of their bloom for maximum potency.
  2. Dry the flowers in a cool dark place for 7 to 10 days to preserve their properties.
