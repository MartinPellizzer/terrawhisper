Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic rosemary for dry hair.

  1. Harvest fresh rosmarinus officinalis leaves in the morning after dew has evaporated for maximum potency.
  2. Dry the leaves in a single layer at 35 degrees celsius for 24 to 48 hours to remove moisture.
  3. Measure 250 grams of the dried leaves and add to 750 milliliters of 60 to 80 percent ethanol for a 1:3 ratio.
  4. Steep the mixture in a clean glass jar with a lid for 2 to 6 weeks in a cool dark place.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or coffee filter into a clean glass bottle to discard solids.
