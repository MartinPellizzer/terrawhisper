Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic stinging nettle for dry hair.

  1. Harvest 100g of fresh urtica dioica leaves in the early morning to ensure optimal potency.

  4. Add 100g of coconut oil and 10ml of vitamin e oil to the mixture and stir well.
  5. Allow the mixture to cool and thicken for 30 minutes before transferring it to a jar.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry hair.

  1. Harvest 5-7 aloe vera leaves of 10-15 cm in length and cut them into small pieces to extract gel.
  2. Mix 1 cup of aloe vera gel with 1/4 cup of distilled water and 1 tablespoon of vitamin e oil in a bowl.
  3. Combine 2 tablespoons of coconut oil and 1 tablespoon of beeswax in a saucepan and heat gently over medium heat.
  4. Add the aloe vera mixture to the coconut oil mixture and stir well to combine the ingredients thoroughly.
  5. Pour the mixture into a glass jar and let it cool and solidify for 10-15 minutes before use.
