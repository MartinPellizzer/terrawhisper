Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic rosemary for dry hair.

  1. Harvest fresh rosmarinus officinalis leaves or stems from well-maintained plants in late spring or early summer.
  2. Chop 5-10 grams of the fresh rosmarinus officinalis material into small pieces to enhance extraction.
  3. Combine the chopped rosmarinus officinalis with 250 milliliters of boiling water in a heat-resistant container.
  4. Allow the mixture to steep for 5-10 minutes to facilitate the release of bioactive compounds.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container to discard solids.
