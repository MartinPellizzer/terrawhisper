Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for psoriasis.

  1. Harvest aloe vera leaves at dawn when they are most potent and store them in a cool dry place for up to 5 days.
  2. Chop the aloe vera leaves into small pieces and combine them with 80 proof vodka at a 1:2 ratio.
  3. Store the mixture in a dark glass container for 2 to 3 weeks to allow for extraction and infusion.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle and discard the solids.
  5. Store the herbal aloe vera tincture in a cool dark place for up to 2 years and use 20 to 30 drops as needed.
