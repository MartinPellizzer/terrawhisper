English lavender essential oils helps with psoriasis because of its anti-inflammatory and calming properties.

  • Immunomodulatory activity: English lavender oil has been shown to modulate the immune system, reducing the overactive immune response that can exacerbate psoriasis symptoms, such as redness, itching, and thickened skin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic english lavender essential oil for psoriasis.

  4. Filter the resulting essential oil through a coffee filter paper and store it in a dark glass bottle at room temperature.
