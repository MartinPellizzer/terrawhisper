The list below shows the primary medicinal constituents in Aloe vera decoctions that help with psoriasis.

  • Aloe-emodin: This anthraquinone compound has anti-inflammatory and antiproliferative properties, which help reduce inflammation and slow down the growth of skin cells that contribute to psoriasis lesions.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for psoriasis.

  1. Harvest a mature aloe vera leaf and carefully extract 30ml of fresh gel from it.
  2. Combine the extracted gel with 200ml of boiling water in a saucepan to prepare the decoction.
  3. Boil the mixture for 10 to 15 minutes over low heat to release the active compounds.
  4. Strain the decoction through a fine mesh sieve into a clean glass container to remove the solids.
  5. Store the herbal aloe vera decoction in the refrigerator for up to 2 days before consumption.
