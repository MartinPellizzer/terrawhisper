1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus essential oils contains compounds like eucalyptol, limonene, and alpha-pinene that help with athlete's foot due to their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

Eucalyptus globulus essential oils can also reduce redness and itching by soothing the skin. If you want to try using Eucalyptus globulus essential oils for athlete's foot, here's what you can do: dilute a few drops of the oil in a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, and apply it to the affected area 2-3 times a day.

You can also add a few drops to your bath water for a relaxing soak. Make sure to clean and dry your feet thoroughly before applying the oil, and wear socks made of breathable materials to keep the area dry. After using Eucalyptus globulus essential oils for athlete's foot, you may notice a reduction in the severity of symptoms, such as itching, redness, and scaling.
