1. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils contains compounds like camphor, bornyl acetate, and 1,8-cineole, which are known to stimulate blood flow to the scalp.

This increase in blood flow can help to nourish the hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth and potentially slowing down the greying process. The antioxidants present in the oil, such as carnosic acid, can also help to protect the hair follicles from damage caused by free radicals, further contributing to healthy hair growth. To use Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils for grey hair, mix a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, and massage it into your scalp. You can also add it to your shampoo or conditioner for a more convenient application.

For best results, use the oil regularly, ideally 2-3 times a week, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing your hair. By incorporating Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils into your hair care routine, you may start to notice an improvement in the colour and texture of your hair. As with any new hair care treatment, be patient and give the oil time to work its magic.
