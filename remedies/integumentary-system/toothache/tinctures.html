The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic white willow tincture for toothache.

  1. Chop 1 cup of white willow bark into small pieces and combine with 2 cups of vodka or rum in a clean jar.

In the case of sore throats, herbs such as sage, wild indigo, and echinacea can be used in tincture form to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation.
