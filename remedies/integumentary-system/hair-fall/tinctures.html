1. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis tinctures contains rosmarinic acid, a powerful antioxidant that helps to protect the scalp from damage.

It's recommended to do this 2-3 times a week for the best results. Take a few deep breaths and massage the tincture into your scalp for about 5 minutes before rinsing. When using Rosmarinus officinalis tinctures for hair fall, you may start to notice an improvement in the texture and strength of your hair.
