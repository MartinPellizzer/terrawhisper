The list below shows the primary parts of yarrow tincture used to make tinctures for hair fall.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for hair fall.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, or use 1 ounce of dried yarrow, and chop finely.
  2. Combine herbs with 1 pint of vodka or rum (80 proof) in a clean jar, ensuring herbs are fully submerged.
  3. Seal the jar and store in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking daily.

  5. Apply 10-20 drops of yarrow tincture directly to the scalp daily, massaging gently, or mix with water and use as a rinse after shampooing.
