Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic rosemary for hair fall.

  1. Gather 2-3 sprigs of fresh or dried rosmarinus officinalis leaves and 1 cup of boiling water.
  2. Steep the rosmarinus officinalis leaves in the boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Strain the liquid mixture through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a bowl.
  4. Discard the solids and store the decoction in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
  5. Use the decoction within 3 days or consume it immediately as a tea.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for hair fall.
