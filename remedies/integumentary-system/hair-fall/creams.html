1. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis creams contains compounds like aloin, aloe-emodin, and vitamins A, C, and E that help to nourish and protect your scalp.

The vitamins and minerals in Aloe barbadensis creams also promote hair growth by strengthening hair follicles and improving blood circulation to the scalp. To use Aloe barbadensis creams for hair fall, apply a generous amount to your scalp and massage it in for a few minutes.

As you regularly apply Aloe barbadensis creams to your scalp, you may start to notice a reduction in hair fall and an improvement in the overall health of your hair. Your scalp may become less itchy and inflamed, and your hair may become stronger and more resistant to breakage.
