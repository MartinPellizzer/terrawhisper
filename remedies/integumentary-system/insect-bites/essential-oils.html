Tea tree essential oils helps with insect bites because of its potent antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree essential oil for insect bites.

  1. Harvest fresh leaves from melaleuca alternifolia plants, ensuring at least 30 grams are collected.

  4. Steam distill the powdered leaves with 300 milliliters of distilled water at atmospheric pressure for one hour and forty-five minutes.
  5. Collect and filter the resulting essential oil through cheesecloth or a coffee filter, yielding approximately 15 milliliters of pure melaleuca alternifolia essential oil.
