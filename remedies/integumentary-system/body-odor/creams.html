Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree for body odor.

  1. Harvest 500g of fresh melaleuca alternifolia leaves, making sure they are clean and free of contaminants.
  2. Dry the fresh leaves in a warm place for 7-10 days, or using a dehydrator on 40°c for 24 hours.

  5. Mix 100g of the infused oil with 50g of beeswax and 20g of shea butter, melting the mixture in a double boiler at 60-70°c.
