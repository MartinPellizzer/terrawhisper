Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic pot marigold for itchy skin.

  1. Harvest 20-30 calendula flowers from mature plants at peak bloom and dry them for 2-3 weeks.
  2. Steep 10g of dried calendula flowers in 100ml of carrier oil at 70-80°c for 2 hours.
  3. Strain the infused oil through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
  4. Mix 10g of beeswax with 10g of shea butter and 20g of coconut oil in a double boiler.
  5. Blend the infused calendula oil with the beeswax mixture and pour into tin containers for cooling.
