The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic english lavender for itchy skin.

  1. Harvest 50g of fresh lavandula angustifolia flowers on a sunny morning, taking care not to bruise or damage the petals.
  2. Dry the harvested flowers in a warm, well-ventilated area for 7-10 days, ensuring even air circulation and minimal exposure to direct sunlight.
  3. Transfer the dried flowers to a clean glass jar and cover with 150ml of high-proof industrial ethanol at room temperature for 14 days, shaking the mixture daily.
  4. Filter the infusion through a coffee filter or cheesecloth into another glass container, discarding the solids and setting the filtered liquid aside for future use.
  5. Distill the filtered liquid using a water bath or steam distillation apparatus for 2 hours at 100°c, collecting the resulting essential oil in a separate container and storing it in an airtight glass bottle at room temperature.
