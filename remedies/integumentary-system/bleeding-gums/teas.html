  • Antibacterial property: Marshmallow tea contains antimicrobial agents like tannins, which help inhibit the growth of bacteria responsible for gum infections and bleeding.
  • Astringent property: The astringent properties of marshmallow tea, due to its tannins content, help constrict blood vessels and tighten gum tissues, which can help control bleeding.

The list below shows the primary parts of marshmallow tea used to make teas for bleeding gums.

  • Roots: The roots of the marshmallow plant contain high amounts of mucilage, making them effective in reducing inflammation and promoting healing of bleeding gums.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic marshmallow tea for bleeding gums.

  1. In a pot, combine 1 cup of dried marshmallow root and 4 cups of water.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Strain the liquid to remove marshmallow root pieces.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon of powdered marshmallow root to the liquid and stir well.
  5. Drink the tea 2-3 times a day, as needed, for bleeding gums.

What ailments similar to bleeding gums are treated with herbal teas?

Ailments similar to bleeding gums that are treated with herbal teas include gingivitis and periodontal disease.

However, it is important to consult with a dental professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
