Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sage for bleeding gums.

  4. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass container discarding the solids.
  5. Allow the decoction to cool to room temperature before transferring it to a glass bottle for storage.
