Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree for foot odor.

  1. Harvest 50g of fresh leaves from mature plants in the early morning when the dew is still present.
  2. Dry the leaves in a single layer at room temperature for 7-10 days to reduce moisture content.
  3. Infuse 20g of dried leaves in 100ml of carrier oil such as coconut oil for 2-3 weeks.
  4. Strain the infused oil through a cheesecloth and discard the solids then add 10g of beeswax.
  5. Heat the mixture in a double boiler until the beeswax has melted and the mixture has cooled to room temperature.
