The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for chapped lips.

  1. Collect pure aloe vera gel from the leaves of a mature aloe barbadensis plant.
  2. Weigh out 50 grams of beeswax and melt it in a double boiler over low heat.

  4. Gradually add the melted beeswax to the oil mixture, stirring until smooth and creamy.
  5. Allow the cream to cool and thicken for 30 minutes before transferring it to an airtight container.
