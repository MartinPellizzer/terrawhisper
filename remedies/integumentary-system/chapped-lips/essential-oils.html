English lavender essential oils helps with chapped lips because of its remarkable moisturizing properties.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic english lavender for chapped lips.

  1. Harvest fresh lavandula angustifolia leaves in the early morning when dew is still present on the plants.
  2. Dry the harvested leaves for 2 weeks by spreading them out in a single layer on paper towels.
  3. Steam distill the dried lavandula angustifolia leaves with water at a ratio of 1:5 for 30 minutes.
  4. Collect and filter the resulting essential oil using a coffee filter or cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle.
  5. Store the final lavender essential oil in an airtight container away from direct sunlight for up to 6 months.
