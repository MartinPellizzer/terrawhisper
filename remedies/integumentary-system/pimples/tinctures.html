The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for pimples.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, ensuring they are fully grown and free from pesticides.
  2. Chop the yarrow into small pieces and place them in a clean, dry glass jar.
