The list below shows the primary parts of garlic tincture used to make tinctures for nail fungus.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic garlic tincture for nail fungus.

  1. Chop 1 cup of fresh garlic cloves and place them in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add 2 cups of vodka or rum (80 proof) to the jar, ensuring the garlic is completely submerged.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for two to four weeks, shaking it daily.
  4. Strain the mixture using a cheesecloth, squeezing out the liquid and discarding the garlic solids.
  5. Store the resulting tincture in a clean, dark glass bottle and apply it topically to affected nails, using a cotton swab, twice daily.
