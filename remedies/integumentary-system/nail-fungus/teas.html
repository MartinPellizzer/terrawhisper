The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic narrow-leaved paperbark tea for nail fungus.

  1. Harvest 2-3 narrow-leaved paperbark (Melaleuca squamea) leaves, ensuring they are free from pesticides and other contaminants.
  2. Rinse the leaves thoroughly under cold running water, then air dry or pat gently with a clean towel.
