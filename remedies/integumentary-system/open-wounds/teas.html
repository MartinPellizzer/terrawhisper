How to best treat open wounds with herbal teas?

The best way to treat open wounds with herbal teas is to first clean the wound with cool water and mild soap, then rinse and pat dry.

Choose teas with antimicrobial properties, such as calendula or chamomile, and steep them according to package instructions. Allow the tea to cool down to a warm temperature, then soak a clean cloth or gauze in the tea and apply it to the wound for several minutes. Repeat this process several times a day, and change the dressing regularly to prevent infection.

However, it's important to consult a healthcare professional if the wound is deep, shows signs of infection, or doesn't heal properly.
