Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic stinging nettle for itchy scalp.

  1. Gather fresh or dried urtica dioica leaves and stems in quantities of 30-60 grams for a decoction.

  3. Bring the urtica dioica and water mixture to a boil then reduce heat to a simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the urtica dioica decoction through a cheesecloth or a fine mesh strainer into a bowl for separation.
