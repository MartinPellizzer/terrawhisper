Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree for itchy scalp.

  1. Harvest fresh leaves from mature plants with dark green and lance-shaped leaves about 4-6 inches long.
  2. Dry the leaves in a low-temperature oven at 100-150 degrees fahrenheit for 1-2 hours to prevent oil loss.
  3. Grind the dried leaves into a fine powder using a coffee grinder or mortar and pestle to enhance extraction.
  4. Steep 1 teaspoon of the powder in 8 ounces of boiling water for 5-7 minutes to release its active compounds.
  5. Strain the tea through a fine-mesh sieve and discard the solids to enjoy its medicinal properties.
