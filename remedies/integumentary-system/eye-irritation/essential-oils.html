1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis essential oils contains properties that help soothe and calm irritated eyes.

The main constituents in Calendula officinalis essential oils include triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. These properties help reduce redness and swelling in the eyes, making Calendula officinalis essential oils a popular natural remedy for eye irritation caused by exposure to chemicals, dust, or pollen. To use Calendula officinalis essential oils for eye irritation, gently dilute a few drops in a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and apply it to a cotton pad.

Then, place the cotton pad over your closed eyes for 5-10 minutes. When you take this action, you can expect the anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects of Calendula officinalis essential oils to reduce redness and swelling in your eyes. You may also notice a decrease in eye discharge or tearing.

The soothing and calming effects of Calendula officinalis essential oils can help provide relief from the discomfort and pain associated with eye irritation.
