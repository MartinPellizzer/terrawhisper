The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe tincture for eye irritation.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves from your garden or purchase them from a reputable supplier measuring approximately 15-20 leaves.
  2. Clean the aloe vera leaves thoroughly with distilled water to remove any dirt debris and impurities then pat dry gently.
  3. Slice the cleaned aloe vera leaves into thin strips removing the tough outer rind to release the gel inside weighing approximately 250 grams.
  4. Combine the sliced aloe vera gel with 80-proof vodka at a ratio of 1 part aloe gel to 2 parts vodka filling a clean glass jar and store in a cool dark place for 6-8 weeks.
  5. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or coffee filter into another clean glass bottle discarding the solids then label and store the finished tincture in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 years using as needed to soothe eye irritation.
