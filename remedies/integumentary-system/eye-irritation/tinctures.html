The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic eyebright tincture for eye irritation.

  1. Chop 2 ounces of dried eyebright herb into small pieces and place in a clean glass jar.
  2. Pour 10 ounces of vodka or rum (80-100 proof) over the herb, ensuring it is fully submerged.
  3. Seal the jar tightly and store in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking daily.
  4. Strain the mixture using cheesecloth or a fine mesh strainer, discarding the solid herb material.
  5. Store the resulting tincture in a clean, dark glass bottle with a tight-fitting lid, away from direct sunlight.
