  2. Place the lavender in a clean glass jar and pour 2 cups of vodka or grain alcohol (80-100 proof) over the herbs, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking the mixture gently every few days.
  4. Strain the mixture using cheesecloth or a fine mesh strainer, discarding the spent lavender and reserving the liquid.
  5. Store the finished lavender tincture in a clean, dark glass bottle, and apply it to dark lips as needed, using a cotton swab or small brush.
