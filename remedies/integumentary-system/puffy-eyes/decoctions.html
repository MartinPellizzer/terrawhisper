Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for puffy eyes.

  1. Cut a 150-200g piece of aloe vera leaf from a mature plant and remove the outer skin.
  2. Chop the aloe vera leaf into small pieces weighing about 50g and soak them in 500ml water for 2 hours.
  3. Boil the aloe vera-soaked water in a saucepan over medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
  5. Store the herbal aloe vera decoction in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or freeze for later use.
