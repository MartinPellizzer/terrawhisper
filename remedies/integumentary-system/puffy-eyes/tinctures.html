1. Arnica flower tincture

Arnica flower tincture helps with puffy eyes because it has anti-inflammatory properties that can reduce swelling and inflammation in the eye area.

Arnica contains helenalin and dihydrohelenalin, which are compounds that help to stimulate the flow of blood and reduce fluid buildup, thereby alleviating puffiness. Additionally, arnica has been shown to have a soothing effect on the skin, which can help to reduce redness and irritation. Its natural astringent properties can also help to tighten the skin around the eyes, making it look more refreshed and revitalized.

Overall, arnica flower tincture is a gentle and effective natural remedy for reducing puffy eyes and promoting healthy-looking skin.

The list below shows the primary active constituents in arnica flower tincture that aid with puffy eyes.

  • Anti-inflammatory: Arnica flower tincture reduces inflammation and swelling in the eye area, providing relief from puffiness. This property is due to the presence of helenalin and dihydrohelenalin, which inhibit the production of prostaglandins that cause inflammation.
  • Vasoconstrictor: Arnica flower tincture constricts blood vessels, reducing the appearance of bloodshot eyes and puffiness. This is due to the presence of lactones, which cause the constriction of blood vessels.
  • Analgesic: Arnica flower tincture helps alleviate pain and discomfort associated with puffy eyes. This property is attributed to the presence of sesquiterpene lactones, which have pain-relieving effects.
  • Diuretic: Arnica flower tincture promotes fluid drainage, reducing the accumulation of fluid in the eye area and alleviating puffiness. This is due to the presence of essential oils, flavonoids, and tannins, which have diuretic properties.
  • Antioxidant: Arnica flower tincture protects the skin from oxidative stress, preventing damage to the delicate skin around the eyes and reducing puffiness. This property is due to the presence of flavonoids and phenolic acids, which neutralize free radicals and protect the skin from damage.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic arnica flower tincture for puffy eyes.

  1. Gather 2 ounces of dried arnica flowers and place them in a clean glass jar.
  2. Pour 10 ounces of vodka or rum (80 proof) over the arnica flowers, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  3. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for two to four weeks, shaking it daily.
  4. After the designated time, strain the mixture using a cheesecloth, squeezing out the liquid from the flowers.
  5. Store the final tincture in a dark glass bottle, keeping it in a cool, dark place for up to a year.
