What is oral thrust and how it affects your life?

Oral thrush, also known as oral candidiasis, is a condition in which a fungus called Candida albicans accumulates on the lining of the mouth, leading to the formation of white or yellow patches that can be painful and uncomfortable.

This condition can negatively affect a person's life in several ways. For instance, eating and speaking may become difficult due to the pain and discomfort caused by the thrush.

Additionally, oral thrush can cause bad breath, which can affect a person's social interactions and self-esteem. In severe cases, oral thrush can spread to other parts of the body, leading to more serious health complications. Furthermore, oral thrush can also affect a person's ability to taste food, reducing their enjoyment of eating and affecting their overall nutritional intake.

Overall, oral thrush can significantly impact a person's quality of life, causing discomfort, pain, and social embarrassment.
