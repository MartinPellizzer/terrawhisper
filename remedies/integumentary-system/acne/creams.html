Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree for acne.

  1. Harvest 20-30 grams of melaleuca alternifolia leaves at dawn when the dew is still present on the leaves.
  2. Steam distill the harvested leaves for 2-3 hours to extract the essential oil from the plant material.
  3. Mix 10 grams of the distilled essential oil with 100 grams of a carrier oil such as sweet almond oil.
  4. Add 2 grams of beeswax and 5 grams of shea butter to the mixture and heat it gently for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean container and allow it to cool and solidify for 30 minutes before use.
