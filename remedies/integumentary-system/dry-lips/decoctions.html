Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic pot marigold for dry lips.

  1. Collect 20-30 dried calendula officinalis flowers and rinse them gently under cool running water.
  2. Combine the dried flowers with 2 cups of boiling water in a heat-resistant glass cup.

  5. Refrigerate the decoction at 40 degrees fahrenheit or below for up to 3 days.
