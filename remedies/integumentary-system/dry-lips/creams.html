Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry lips.

  1. Harvest 10 large aloe vera leaves from mature plants and cut them into small pieces to extract gel.
  2. Weigh out 200g of aloe vera gel and mix with 50g of distilled water in a heat-resistant bowl.
  3. Add 10g of beeswax and 10g of coconut oil to the mixture and heat it gently over low heat for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the bowl from heat and let it cool until the mixture reaches 40°c then whisk in 5g of vitamin e oil.
  5. Pour the mixture into sterilized containers and let it cool and set at room temperature for 2 hours.
