Recipe:

  1. Gather Euphrasia officinalis dried herb (about 2 tablespoons).
  2. Heat 1 cup of boiling water in a pot.
  3. Add the Euphrasia officinalis dried herb to the boiling water.
  4. Let it steep for 5-7 minutes, then strain the liquid.
  5. Drink the Euphrasia officinalis tea 2-3 times a day for red eye relief.

Usage:

  1. Measure 1-2 teaspoons of dried Euphrasia officinalis in a cup.
