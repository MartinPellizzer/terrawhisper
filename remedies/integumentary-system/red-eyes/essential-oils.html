The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic pot marigold essential oil for red eyes.

  1. Harvest 20 grams of fresh calendula officinalis flowers on a sunny morning for optimal potency.
  2. Dry the harvested flowers in a warm place or using a food dehydrator at 60 degrees celsius for four hours.
  3. Steam distill the dried flowers using a copper alembic still with 1:4 ratio of flowers to distilled water for two hours.

  5. Store the final product in dark glass bottles with tight-fitting lids at room temperature, allowing it to mature for several weeks before use.
