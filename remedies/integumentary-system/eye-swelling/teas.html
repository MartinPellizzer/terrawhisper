How to best treat eye swelling with herbal teas?

The best way to treat eye swelling with herbal teas is to apply warm compresses soaked in cooled tea.

Choose teas with anti-inflammatory properties, such as chamomile or green tea. Steep the tea in boiling water for 5-10 minutes, then let it cool.

Soak a clean cloth in the tea and place it over closed eyes for 10-15 minutes. Repeat this process 2-3 times a day. The tea's anti-inflammatory properties will help reduce swelling and soothe the area around the eyes.

However, if swelling persists or is accompanied by other symptoms, it is important to seek medical attention.
