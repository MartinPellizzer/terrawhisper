Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for oily face.

  1. Gather ingredients including 100g aloe vera gel 50g coconut oil 20g shea butter 10g beeswax 10g vitamin e oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil shea butter and beeswax in a double boiler over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Whisk the aloe vera gel until smooth then add it to the melted mixture and stir until well combined.
  4. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool and thicken for 20 minutes until it reaches a creamy consistency.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean glass jar and add a few drops of vitamin e oil to prevent oxidation.
