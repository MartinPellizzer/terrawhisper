What is eye swelling and how it affects your life?

Eye swelling, also known as periorbital edema, is a condition where the eyes become puffy and swollen, often accompanied by a sense of tightness and discomfort.

This condition can have a significant impact on daily life, affecting not only physical appearance but also overall well-being. For instance, eye swelling can make it difficult to wear makeup or contact lenses, and the resulting puffiness can be embarrassing in social situations.

Furthermore, it can also interfere with daily activities such as driving, reading, or even working on a computer, as the blurred vision and discomfort can be distracting and debilitating. Moreover, eye swelling can also lead to fatigue, headaches, and irritability, further impairing one's ability to function normally. Additionally, it can also cause difficulty in recognizing faces or objects, making it challenging to perform tasks that require visual acuity, such as cooking, cleaning, or even playing sports.

Overall, eye swelling can have a significant impact on one's quality of life, making it essential to address the issue promptly and effectively.

What precautions to take when using herbal remedies for eye swelling?
