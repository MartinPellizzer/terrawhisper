What ailments similar to eye infection are treated with herbal tinctures?

Ailments similar to eye infection that are treated with herbal tinctures include blepharitis and conjunctivitis.

Blepharitis is an inflammation of the eyelids, often caused by bacteria or a skin disorder, and can be treated with goldenseal or calendula tinctures. Conjunctivitis, or pink eye, is an inflammation of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the eyelid and eye surface, and can be treated with eyebright or chamomile tinctures. These herbs have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and astringent properties that can help soothe and heal irritated eye tissues.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new treatment.
