The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic white tea for dark circles.

  1. Boil one cup of water and let it cool down to 160-185°F (71-85°C), an ideal temperature for white tea.
  2. Steep one teaspoon of dried herbal white tea (with ingredients like chamomile, lavender, and calendula) in the hot water for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea and let it cool down to a comfortable temperature.
  4. Soak two cotton pads in the cooled tea and place them on your closed eyes for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Remove the cotton pads and gently rinse your eyes with cool water, then pat dry. Repeat this process 1-2 times a day for best results.
