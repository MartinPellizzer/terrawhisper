The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for excessive sweating.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera gel from mature leaves by carefully slicing off the thick skin.
  2. Combine 100 grams of aloe vera gel with 20 grams of powdered witch hazel and 10 grams of dried sage leaves in a blender.

  4. Heat the blend in a double boiler for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and reaches a temperature of 160°f.
  5. Pour the cooled mixture into sterilized containers and let it set at room temperature for 2 hours before using or storing.
