Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for frizzy hair.

  1. Extract 100 grams of aloe vera gel from the leaves of the plant using a sharp knife.
  2. Mix the aloe vera gel with 20 grams of beeswax and 20 grams of coconut oil in a heat-proof bowl.
  3. Melt the mixture of aloe vera gel and oils in a double boiler over low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Add 10 grams of shea butter and 5 grams of vitamin e oil to the mixture and stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean glass jar and let it cool and solidify at room temperature for 30 minutes.
