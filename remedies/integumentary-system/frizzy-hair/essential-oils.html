The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic rosemary for frizzy hair.

  1. Harvest high-quality fresh rosmarinus officinalis leaves in the morning for maximum oil content and potency.
  2. Clean and dry the harvested leaves by gently shaking off excess moisture and spreading them out in a single layer.
  3. Steam distill 20 grams of dried rosmarinus officinalis leaves with 100 ml of distilled water at 100°c for 1 hour to extract essential oils.
  4. Separate the extracted essential oil from the resulting distillation liquid using a separating funnel and store it in dark glass bottles.
  5. Mix 10% rosmarinus officinalis essential oil with jojoba or sweet almond oil carrier oil and apply topically to hair as needed for frizz reduction.
