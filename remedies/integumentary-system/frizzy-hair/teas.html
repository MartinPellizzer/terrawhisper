The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tea for frizzy hair.

  1. In a pot, boil 2 cups of water and add 2 tablespoons of dried yarrow flowers, simmering for 10 minutes.
  2. Turn off the heat, cover the pot, and let the mixture steep for an additional 20 minutes.
  3. Strain the liquid to remove the yarrow flowers, reserving the tea.
  4. Allow the tea to cool down to a comfortable temperature.
  5. After shampooing and conditioning, pour the yarrow tea over your hair, massaging it into your scalp, then let it sit for 10 minutes before rinsing.
