Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic english lavender for frizzy hair.

  1. Gather 1 tablespoon of dried lavandula angustifolia flowers and 1 cup of boiling water for infusion.
  2. Steep the dried flowers in the boiling water for 5-7 minutes to release essential oils.
  3. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine-mesh sieve to remove the flowers completely.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of honey or sugar to sweeten the tea according to personal taste.
  5. Serve the herbal tea immediately and enjoy its calming and relaxing properties.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic lemongrass for frizzy hair.
