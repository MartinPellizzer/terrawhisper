Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic pot marigold for itchy eyes.

  1. Infuse 30 grams of dried calendula officinalis flowers in 1 liter of carrier oil such as coconut oil.
  2. Steep the mixture in a double boiler for 2-3 hours at a low heat setting.
  3. Strain the infused oil through a cheesecloth into a clean glass container.
  4. Mix the infused oil with 10 grams of beeswax and 5 grams of shea butter in a saucepan.
  5. Heat the mixture over low heat and stir until the beeswax and shea butter are fully melted.
