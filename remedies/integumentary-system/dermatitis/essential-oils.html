The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic pot marigold for dermatitis.

  1. Harvest fresh calendula officinalis flowers at peak bloom and rinse with distilled water for optimal quality.
  2. Dry the harvested flowers in a warm place away from direct sunlight until they reach a crumbly texture taking approximately 7 days.
