The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic butcher's broom tincture for eye bags.

When used consistently, these tinctures can help minimize the appearance of dark circles, reduce puffiness, and promote healthier-looking eyes.

However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before starting any new herbal treatment to ensure safety and effectiveness.
