Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic st john's wort for burning feet.

  1. Gather 250 grams of dried hypericum perforatum flowers and 750 milliliters of 60 percent ethanol.
  2. Combine the dried flowers and ethanol in a clean glass container and seal the lid tightly.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool dark place for 2 to 6 weeks shaking the container every day.
  4. Strain the liquid through a coffee filter or cheesecloth into another clean glass container and discard the solids.
  5. Transfer the tincture to dark glass bottles and store them in a cool dark place for up to 2 years.
