Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for burning feet.

  1. Melt 1 cup of aloe vera gel in a double boiler over low heat for about 10 minutes.
  2. Combine 1/4 cup of beeswax and 1/4 cup of coconut oil in a separate double boiler.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of vitamin e oil to the melted aloe vera gel and mix well for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir the aloe vera gel mixture into the beeswax and coconut oil mixture until fully incorporated for 10 minutes.
  5. Pour the herbal aloe vera cream mixture into a clean glass jar and let it cool and solidify for about 30 minutes.
