The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tea for bad breath.

  1. Measure out one teaspoon of dried peppermint leaves per eight ounces of boiling water.
  2. Steep the peppermint leaves in the hot water for five to seven minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  3. Strain the tea mixture through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the solids.
  4. Add one tablespoon of honey to taste and stir until fully dissolved before serving.
  5. Allow the tea to cool slightly before drinking to help freshen breath.
