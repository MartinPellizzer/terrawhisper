The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for sunburn.

  1. Measure one cup of dried aloe vera leaves and rinse them under cool running water.
  2. Combine the rinsed aloe vera leaves with four cups of distilled water in a large pot.

  4. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
  5. Let the decoction cool and refrigerate it for up to 24 hours before using as a topical treatment.
