Aloe teas helps with sunburn because they contain powerful antioxidants that help soothe and calm the skin.

The list below shows the primary active constituents in aloe teas that aid with sunburn.

  • Antioxidant properties: The antioxidants in aloe vera tea, including vitamins C and E, help neutralize free radicals that can damage skin cells and accelerate the aging process, reducing the appearance of sun-damaged skin.

The list below shows the primary parts of aloe used to make teas for sunburn.

  • Leaves: Contain aloin, a natural anti-inflammatory compound that helps to calm sunburned skin and promote healing.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for sunburn.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves or purchase high-quality dried aloe vera powder for your recipe.
  2. Rinse the fresh aloe vera leaves thoroughly with cool water and remove the thick green skin from the outside.
  3. Dry the inner gel of the fresh aloe vera leaves in a low-temperature oven at 150°f for 1 hour to dehydrate the liquid.
  4. Combine 1 teaspoon of dried aloe vera powder or 2 tablespoons of fresh aloe vera gel with 1 cup of boiling water and steep for 10 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into a glass and add 1 tablespoon of honey and 1 slice of lemon, serving warm or chilled as needed.
