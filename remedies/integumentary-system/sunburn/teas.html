Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for sunburn.

  1. Gather 10-12 ounces of fresh aloe vera gel and 1 cup of water for brewing.
  2. Combine aloe vera gel with water in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Strain the tea through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a large bowl to remove solids.
  5. Allow the tea to steep for an additional 5-10 minutes before serving over ice or chilled.
