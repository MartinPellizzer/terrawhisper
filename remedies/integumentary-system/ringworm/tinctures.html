Is it safe to consume herbal teas for ringworm every day?

Herbal teas can be a helpful addition to treating ringworm, but consuming them daily may not be the best idea.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in ringworm?

Herbal teas can help with ringworm, but it may take some time to see results.

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for ringworm?

To drink herbal tea for ringworm, try having it in the morning.

The morning also gives you the rest of the day to let the tea work its way through your system and help clear up the infection.
