The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic wormwood tea for ringworm.

  1. In a mortar, crush 1 tablespoon of dried wormwood herb (Artemisia absinthium) to release its oils.
  2. Transfer the crushed wormwood to a tea infuser and place it in a cup.
  3. Pour 1 cup of boiling water over the wormwood in the infuser and let it steep for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Remove the infuser and discard the wormwood.
  5. Drink the warm wormwood tea up to three times a day, between meals, for no more than two weeks.
