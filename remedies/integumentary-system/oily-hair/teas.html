The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe tea for oily hair.

  1. Rinse aloe leaves gently under cold running water for 10 seconds to remove dirt and debris.

  4. Strain the tea through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into another clean glass container discarding the solids.
  5. Use the herbal aloe tea as a final rinse after shampooing and conditioning your hair by pouring it over your scalp.
