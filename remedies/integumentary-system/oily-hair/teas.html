Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for oily hair.

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly before handling aloe vera gel to avoid contamination.
  2. Cut an aloe vera leaf lengthwise to extract the gel inside with a spoon.
  3. Mix 3 tablespoons of aloe vera gel with 8 ounces of boiling water for 5 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a cup or teapot.
  5. Serve the aloe vera tea immediately and let it cool to room temperature within 30 minutes.
