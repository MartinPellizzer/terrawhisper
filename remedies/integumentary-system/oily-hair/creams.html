The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic sage for oily hair.

  1. Harvest fresh salvia officinalis leaves in early morning misty weather for maximum potency and essential oils.
  2. Dry the harvested leaves using a food dehydrator at 60°c for 6 hours or until crispy and brittle.
