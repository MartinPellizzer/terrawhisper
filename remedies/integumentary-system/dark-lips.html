What is dark lips and how it affects your life?

Dark lips, also known as hyperpigmentation of the lips, is a condition where the skin on the lips becomes discolored, typically appearing darker than the surrounding skin.

This condition can have a significant impact on a person's life, causing emotional distress and affecting their daily activities. For instance, dark lips can make a person feel self-conscious and embarrassed, leading to a decrease in self-esteem and confidence.

They may avoid social interactions, public speaking, or even everyday activities like eating or smiling in front of others. Dark lips can also affect a person's career, as they may feel uncomfortable in job interviews or meetings, and may even struggle to establish professional relationships. Moreover, dark lips can limit a person's participation in extracurricular activities, sports, or hobbies, as they may feel anxious about drawing attention to themselves.

Additionally, dark lips can affect a person's mental health, leading to anxiety, depression, and low mood, further exacerbating the condition.
