Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic tea tree for dry scalp.

  1. Infuse 250g of dried melaleuca alternifolia leaves in 1l of carrier oil for 2 weeks.
  2. Strain the infused oil through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean container.
  3. Combine 200g of the infused oil with 100g of beeswax and 50g of candelilla wax.
  4. Heat the mixture over low heat for 10-15 minutes until the beeswax and candelilla wax melt.
  5. Pour the mixture into a mold or a glass jar and let it cool and solidify for 30 minutes.
