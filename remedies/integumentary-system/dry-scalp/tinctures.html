The list below shows the primary parts of yarrow tincture used to make tinctures for dry scalp.

  • Flowers: Rich in anti-inflammatory properties, promoting scalp health and reducing dryness.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for dry scalp.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, or use 1 ounce of dried yarrow, and chop coarsely.
  2. Place the yarrow in a clean glass jar, and pour 10 ounces of vodka or rum with at least 40% alcohol content over the herbs.
  3. Allow the mixture to steep for 2-6 weeks in a cool, dark place, shaking it gently every few days.
  4. Strain the mixture using cheesecloth or a fine-mesh strainer, squeezing out the excess liquid.
  5. Store the finished yarrow tincture in a clean, dark glass bottle, and use it as needed to treat dry scalp by massaging a few drops onto the scalp and letting it absorb for at least 30 minutes before washing your hair.
