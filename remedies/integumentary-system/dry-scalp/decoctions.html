Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry scalp.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves from mature plants for optimal potency and effectiveness.
  2. Clean and peel the aloe vera leaves to remove any impurities and outer skin layers.
