The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for dry scalp.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves measure 2 inches long cut off the rind remove any yellow gel.
  2. Chop 3-4 tablespoons of aloe vera gel into small pieces add 2 cups boiling water stir well.

  5. Let the decoction cool to room temperature store it in an amber glass bottle refrigerate for up to 3 days use twice daily as shampoo substitute.
