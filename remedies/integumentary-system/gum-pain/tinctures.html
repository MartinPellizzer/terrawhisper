The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for gum pain.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, ensuring they are fully dried and free of moisture.
  2. Chop the dried yarrow into small pieces and place them in a clean, dry jar.
  3. Pour 10 ounces of 80-proof vodka or rum over the chopped yarrow, ensuring the plant material is fully submerged.
