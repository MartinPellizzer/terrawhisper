Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for glowing skin.

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water before handling aloe vera gel to ensure cleanliness.
  2. Cut 100 grams of aloe vera leaves and remove the thick outer skin to expose the gel inside.
  3. Blend the exposed aloe vera gel in a juicer or blender for 1 minute at 20000 revolutions per minute.
  4. Strain the aloe vera gel through a cheesecloth or a fine mesh sieve into a bowl to remove any remaining pulp.
  5. Store the aloe vera decoction in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
