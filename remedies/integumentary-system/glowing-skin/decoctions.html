The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for glowing skin.

  1. Measure one cup of dried aloe vera leaves and add four cups of distilled water in a stainless steel pot.
  2. Heat the mixture on low flame for 20 minutes or until the liquid reduces by half slowly.
  3. Strain the decoction through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass bottle discarding solids immediately.
  4. Add two tablespoons of dried turmeric powder and stir well to combine with the aloe vera liquid thoroughly.
  5. Allow the mixture to steep for 30 minutes before refrigerating it for up to seven days safely.
