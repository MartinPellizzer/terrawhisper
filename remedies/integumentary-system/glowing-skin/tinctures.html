Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for glowing skin.

  1. Harvest 100% organic aloe vera leaves, 5-7 leaves for a 1000ml tincture, and wash them thoroughly with filtered water.
  2. Chop the aloe vera leaves into small pieces, about 1-inch in size, using a sharp knife or a food chopper.
  3. Combine the chopped aloe vera leaves with 70% ethanol, 500ml, in a clean glass jar, and seal it tightly.
  4. Store the jar in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean glass bottle, discard the solids, and label the tincture.
