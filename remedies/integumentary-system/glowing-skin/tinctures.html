The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for glowing skin.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves and rinse them thoroughly under cold running water.
  2. Chop the aloe vera leaves into small pieces and transfer them to a clean glass jar.
  3. Add 90-proof vodka or brandy as a solvent in a ratio of 1 part aloe vera to 2 parts solvent.
  4. Seal the jar and store it in a cool dark place for 2-3 weeks allowing the mixture to steep.
  5. Strain the tincture through cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass container discarding solids.
