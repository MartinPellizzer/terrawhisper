10 best herbal tinctures for oily skin

By using herbal tinctures, individuals with oily skin can experience fewer breakouts, reduced shine, and an overall improvement in the health and appearance of their skin.

  • Antibacterial: Yarrow tincture aids in preventing acne and skin infections associated with oily skin, as it contains volatile oils like azulene and camphor.
  • Antiseborrheic: Yarrow tincture helps control sebum production and regulates the skin's natural oil balance, owing to its salicylic acid and polyacetylenes content.

The list below shows the primary parts of yarrow tincture used to make tinctures for oily skin.

  • Flowers: Rich in salicylic acid, which helps regulate sebum production in the skin.
  • Leaves: Contain azulene, known for its anti-inflammatory properties, reducing redness and soothing oily skin.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for oily skin.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, rinse, and let dry for 24 hours.
  2. Chop the dried herbs and place them in a clean glass jar, then add 8 ounces of 80-proof vodka.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking it daily.
  4. Strain the mixture using cheesecloth, squeezing out the liquid and discarding the plant material.
  5. Store the final tincture in a dark glass bottle, keeping it in a cool, dark place for up to a year.
