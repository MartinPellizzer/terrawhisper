Is it safe to consume herbal teas for oily skin every day?

Herbal teas for oily skin can be safe to consume daily, but it's essential to choose teas that specifically help regulate oil production.

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for oily skin?

Drinking herbal tea can help with oily skin, especially in the morning.

Some herbal teas, like peppermint and chamomile, are particularly good at balancing skin oil levels, so consider having a cup first thing.
