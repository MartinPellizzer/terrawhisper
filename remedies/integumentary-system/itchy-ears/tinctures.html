Mullein oil, which contains antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, is used to soothe earaches and reduce inflammation. Garlic oil, with its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, is used to treat ear infections and alleviate itching and pain.

However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using any herbal remedies.
