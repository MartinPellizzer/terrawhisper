Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe for burns.

  1. Harvest mature aloe vera leaves with a thick fleshy texture and no signs of wilting or damage.
  2. Chop the aloe vera leaves into small pieces weighing 1 pound and mix with 80% ethanol at ratio 1:5.
  3. Steep the mixture in a glass jar for 2-3 weeks in a cool dark place.
  4. Strain the liquid through cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass container discarding the solids.
  5. Store the herbal aloe vera tincture in a glass bottle with a tight-fitting lid in a cool dark place.
