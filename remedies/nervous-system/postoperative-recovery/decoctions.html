The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for postoperative recovery.

  1. Weigh 500mg of dried turmeric rhizome powder and 250mg of dried ginger rhizome powder.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginger for postoperative recovery.

  1. Gather 2-3 inches of fresh ginger rhizome and wash it thoroughly under cool running water.
  2. Slice the ginger into thin pieces and combine them in a saucepan with 8 cups of boiling water.
  3. Reduce heat to a simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the liquid reaches 100°f to 110°f.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the decoction in an airtight container in the refrigerator and consume within 24 hours after preparation.
