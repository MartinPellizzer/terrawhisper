The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic brahmi tincture for brain fog.

  1. Harvest fresh bacopa monnieri leaves and stems in the morning when dew is still present on the plants ensuring maximum bioavailability of active compounds.
  2. Clean the harvested plant material by rinsing it gently under cold running water then pat dry with a paper towel removing excess moisture.
  3. Combine 50 grams of clean bacopa monnieri leaves and stems with 250 milliliters of high-proof vodka in a clean glass jar sealing tightly and labeling properly.
  4. Allow the mixture to macerate at room temperature for four weeks shaking the jar daily to extract bioactive compounds from the plant material.
  5. Strain the liquid through a coffee filter or cheesecloth into another clean glass bottle discarding the solids then store the tincture in a cool dark place for up to two years.
