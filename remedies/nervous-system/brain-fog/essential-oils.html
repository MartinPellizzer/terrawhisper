1. Bacopa monnieri

Brahmi essential oils helps with brain fog because it has been traditionally used to improve memory, concentration, and mental clarity.

The oil contains compounds that have a calming effect on the mind, reducing stress and anxiety that can contribute to cognitive impairment. Additionally, brahmi's adaptogenic properties help to increase oxygen flow to the brain, promoting mental alertness and focus.

As a result, using brahmi essential oils can help to clear mental fog and improve overall cognitive function.

The list below shows the primary active constituents in brahmi essential oils that aid with brain fog.

  • Improves cognitive function: Brahmi essential oil has been shown to enhance cognitive function by increasing blood flow to the brain, improving focus, and boosting memory retention. This is because it contains compounds that stimulate the production of neurotransmitters such as dopamine and acetylcholine.
  • Enhances neuroplasticity: Brahmi oil's ability to increase neurotrophic factors (such as BDNF) helps promote neural growth and development, which can aid in improving mental clarity and reducing brain fog by strengthening the connections between neurons.
  • Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties: Brahmi essential oil contains potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds that help protect the brain from oxidative stress and inflammation, which can contribute to cognitive impairment and brain fog.

The list below shows the primary parts of brahmi essential oil used to make essential oils for brain fog.

  • Roots: The roots of the Brahmi plant contain a high concentration of bioactive compounds, which are believed to improve memory and cognitive function.
  • Leaves: The leaves of the Brahmi plant have been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various neurological disorders, including anxiety and depression.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic brahmi essential oil for brain fog.

  1. Harvest fresh bacopa monnieri leaves and stems at dawn or before the sun rises.
  2. Chop the harvested plant material into small pieces weighing approximately 1 kg for distillation.
  3. Steam-distill the chopped plant material in a double boiler setup using 5 liters of water at boiling point.
  4. Collect and filter the resulting essential oil through a coffee filter or paper towel after 30 minutes of distillation.
  5. Store the extracted bacopa monnieri essential oil in dark glass bottles with tight-fitting lids for up to 12 months at room temperature.
