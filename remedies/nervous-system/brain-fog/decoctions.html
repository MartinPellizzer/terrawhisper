The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for brain fog.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried ginkgo biloba leaves and rinse them thoroughly with clean water.
  2. Combine one teaspoon of dried ginkgo biloba leaves or two teaspoons of fresh leaves with 250 milliliters of boiling water in a heat-resistant cup.
  3. Steep the mixture for 10 to 15 minutes, then strain it using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into another cup.
  4. Discard the solids and store the decoction in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days.
  5. Consume one-quarter cup of the cooled decoction two times daily, ideally with meals, for optimal brain function benefits.
