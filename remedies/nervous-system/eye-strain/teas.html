Recipe:

  1. Gather 1 cup of boiling water and 1 teaspoon of dried Ginkgo biloba leaves.

  5. Drink the tea while it's still warm and enjoy the potential benefits for eye strain.

Usage:

  1. Drink 1 cup of Ginkgo biloba tea 2 times a day

  5. Monitor your progress and adjust dosage as needed

Ginkgo biloba teas can be a potential treatment for eye strain, but some possible side effects may include dizziness, nausea, and headaches due to its blood-thinning properties.

To use Ginkgo biloba teas safely for eye strain, it's essential to monitor your blood pressure and avoid taking it in large doses, especially if you have a history of bleeding disorders or are taking anticoagulant medications.
