The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic dandelion for sleep deprivation.

  1. Harvest fresh dandelion flowers at dawn or just before sunset when they are most potent.
  2. Rinse the harvested dandelion flowers thoroughly under cold running water to remove any dirt or debris.
  3. Combine one teaspoon of dried dandelion flowers or three teaspoons of fresh dandelion flowers with boiling water in a teapot.
  4. Steep the mixture for 5-7 minutes, allowing the herbal properties to infuse into the water and create a soothing brew.
  5. Strain the tea through a fine-mesh sieve into a cup, discarding the solids, and enjoy the calming effects within 30 minutes of consumption.
