1. Valeriana officinalis

Valerian tinctures helps with sleep deprivation because it contains compounds that interact with the brain's GABA receptors, promoting relaxation and calming the nervous system.

Medicinal Constituents

The list below shows the primary medicinal constituents in Valeriana officinalis tinctures that help with sleep deprivation.

  • Isovaleric acid: This organic compound is a GABA receptor agonist, which helps regulate the body's sleep-wake cycle and promotes relaxation and sleep.
  • Valerenic acid: As a GABA receptor antagonist, valerenic acid counteracts the action of excitatory neurotransmitters, leading to a calming effect and improved sleep quality.
  • Valeranone: This sesquiterpene acts as a GABA receptor modulator, enhancing the activity of GABA and promoting a state of relaxation and reduced anxiety, ultimately leading to improved sleep.

Parts Used

The list below shows the primary parts of valerian used to make tinctures for sleep deprivation.

  • Roots: They are the primary part used due to their high concentration of valerenic acid, a key compound responsible for sedative effects.
  • Stems: Stems contain some amount of valerenic acid but at lower concentrations compared to roots, yet they are still used in tinctures for sleep aid.
  • Leaves: Leaves of Valeriana officinalis also contain valerenic acid and are sometimes used to make tinctures, although their use is less common than roots.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for sleep deprivation.

  1. Harvest fresh roots of valeriana officinalis in the morning after the dew has dried for optimal potency.
  2. Clean and dry the harvested roots in a cool dark place for at least 24 hours to prevent contamination.
  3. Chop the dried roots into small pieces to increase the surface area for infusion and extraction.
  4. Combine the chopped roots with 80 proof vodka in a clean glass jar in a 1:5 ratio by weight.
  5. Store the jar in a cool dark place for 6 to 8 weeks shaking it daily to facilitate the extraction process.
