English lavender essential oils helps with stress because its calming scent has a profound impact on the mind and body.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic english lavender essential oil for stress.

  1. Harvest 10 pounds of fresh lavender flowers at peak bloom in the morning when dew is still present.
  2. Dry the harvested flowers for 3-4 days indoors away from direct sunlight and moisture.
  3. Crush the dried lavender flowers into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle or food processor.
  4. Steam distill 1 pound of crushed lavender powder in 10 pounds of water at 100°c for 2 hours.
  5. Collect and filter the resulting essential oil through a coffee filter or paper towels to remove impurities.
