Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for stress.

  1. Harvest fresh valeriana officinalis roots and clean them thoroughly with distilled water.
  2. Chop 1 part of the cleaned roots into small pieces and combine with 2 parts of 80 proof vodka.
  3. Store the mixture in a glass jar with a lid and let it steep for 2 weeks in a cool dark place.
  4. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth into another clean glass container discarding the solids.
  5. Label the final tincture and store it in a dark glass bottle with a dropper in the refrigerator to preserve potency.
