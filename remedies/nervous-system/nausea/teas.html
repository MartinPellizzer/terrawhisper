The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tea for nausea.

  1. In a teapot, combine 1 teaspoon of dried peppermint leaves with 8 ounces of boiling water.
  2. Allow the mixture to steep for 5-10 minutes to extract the herb's properties.
  3. Strain the tea to remove the leaves, pouring the liquid into a cup.
  4. Optionally, add honey or lemon to taste.
  5. Sip the tea slowly, allowing it to soothe your stomach and alleviate nausea.
