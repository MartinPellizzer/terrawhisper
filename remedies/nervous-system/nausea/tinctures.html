Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginger for nausea.

  1. Gather 250 grams of fresh zingiber officinale roots and 500 milliliters of 25 percent ethanol as ingredients.
  2. Chop the zingiber officinale roots into small pieces to increase their surface area for infusion.
  3. Combine the chopped zingiber officinale roots with the ethanol in a clean glass jar.
  4. Steep the mixture for 2 to 4 weeks in a cool dark place with occasional shaking.
  5. Strain the liquid through cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean glass bottle for storage.
