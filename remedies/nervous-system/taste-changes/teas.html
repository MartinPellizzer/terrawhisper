  • Menthone: Exhibits analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate pain and discomfort in the mouth and throat that may be contributing to taste changes.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint for taste changes.

  1. Harvest 20-30 fresh mentha x piperita leaves from a well-maintained herb garden in the morning.
  2. Rinse the harvested leaves under cold running water to remove dirt and debris thoroughly.
  3. Chop the rinsed mentha x piperita leaves into small pieces weighing about 2-3 grams per serving.
  4. Steep 1-2 teaspoons of chopped mentha x piperita leaves in 8 ounces of boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a cup and discard the solids immediately.
