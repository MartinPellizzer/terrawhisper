Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for taste changes.

  1. Harvest 2 pounds of ginkgo biloba leaves in late summer or early fall for optimal potency.

  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle, discarding the solids.
