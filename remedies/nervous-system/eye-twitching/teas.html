Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for eye twitching.

  1. Harvest fresh valeriana officinalis roots from a trusted supplier or your own garden in the early morning.

  5. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the solids completely.
