Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for grief.

  1. Harvest valeriana officinalis roots in late summer or early fall when the plant is at its peak potency level.
  2. Dry the harvested roots in a warm dry place for 1 to 2 weeks to remove excess moisture content.
