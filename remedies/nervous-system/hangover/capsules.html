Certain herbs, such as milk thistle, ginger, and turmeric, can help alleviate symptoms by supporting liver function, reducing inflammation, and soothing the stomach. These herbs can be found in various herbal supplements and capsules, providing a natural and holistic approach to treating hangover-like symptoms.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new supplement regimen.
