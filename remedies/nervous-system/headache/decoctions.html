Asafoetida decoctions helps with headaches because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, particularly the sulfur-containing molecules that have a natural analgesic and anti-inflammatory effect.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic asafoetida for headaches.

  1. Harvest 10-20 grams of dried ferula foetida roots with a sharp knife and store in an airtight container.
  2. Grind the roots into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle for 5 minutes.

  4. Steep the mixture for 10-15 minutes to allow the active compounds to infuse into the water.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean container.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for headaches.
