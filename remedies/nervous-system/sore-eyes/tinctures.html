Aloe tinctures helps with sore eyes because of its soothing and anti-inflammatory properties.

The list below shows the primary active constituents in aloe tinctures that aid with sore eyes.

The list below shows the primary parts of aloe tincture used to make tinctures for sore eyes.

  • Roots: Aloe roots are rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, making them effective in soothing and calming irritated eyes.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic aloe tincture for sore eyes.

  1. Harvest fresh aloe vera leaves from the plant weighing approximately one pound or more.
  2. Clean the harvested leaves thoroughly by rinsing them under cool running water and pat drying.
  3. Slice the cleaned leaves into thin pieces and soak them in a high-proof vodka at a ratio of 1 part aloe to 3 parts vodka for 14 days.
  4. After 14 days, strain the liquid mixture through cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean glass bottle discarding the solids and store it in a cool dark place.
  5. Allow the tincture to mature for an additional 6 weeks before using it as needed by gently massaging a few drops onto affected eyes.
