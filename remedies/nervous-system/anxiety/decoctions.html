Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maypop for anxiety.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic kava for anxiety.

  1. Gather 20-30 grams of dried piper methysticum roots and 1 liter of boiling water.

  3. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
  4. Store the decoction in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours or freeze for longer storage.
  5. Take 100-200 milliliters of the decoction 2-3 times a day as needed.
