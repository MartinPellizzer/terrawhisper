  • Muscle relaxant effect: Valerian's muscle relaxant properties can help to reduce muscle tension and spasms that may be contributing to difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for insomnia.

  1. Harvest fresh or dried roots of valeriana officinalis in the morning after dew has evaporated.
  2. Measure 20-30 grams of root material and rinse it under cold running water.
  3. Combine the cleaned roots with 500 milliliters of boiling water in a large glass jar.
  4. Steep for 10-15 minutes, then strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into another container.
  5. Store the decoction in an amber-colored glass bottle in the refrigerator for up to three days.
