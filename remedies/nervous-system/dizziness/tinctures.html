What ailments similar to dizziness are treated with herbal tinctures?

Ailments similar to dizziness that are treated with herbal tinctures include vertigo, motion sickness, and anxiety-induced dizziness.

Ginkgo Biloba is a popular herbal tincture known to improve circulation in the inner ear, reducing vertigo and dizziness. For motion sickness, options like ginger or peppermint can help alleviate symptoms. Anxiety-related dizziness can be treated with tinctures such as passionflower or valerian root, which have calming and relaxing properties.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before starting any new herbal treatment.
