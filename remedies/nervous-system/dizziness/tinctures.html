The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginkgo biloba tincture for dizziness.

  1. Gather 1 ounce of dried Ginkgo Biloba leaves and place them in a clean, dry glass jar.
  2. Pour 2 cups of vodka or rum (80 proof) over the leaves, ensuring they are fully submerged.
