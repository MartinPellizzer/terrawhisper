However, it's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new herbal supplement regimen.

What other herbal preparations helps with dizziness?

Other herbal preparations to use with capsules for dizziness are tinctures, teas, and essential oils.

Tinctures are concentrated liquid extracts that can be taken sublingually or added to drinks. Teas are infusions of herbs in hot water, providing a gentle and soothing remedy. Essential oils, derived from plants, can be used in diffusers, baths, or topically, offering aromatherapy benefits.

Always follow dosage instructions and consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal regimen.
