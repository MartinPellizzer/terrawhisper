What is sinus headache and how it affects your life?

A sinus headache is a type of headache that occurs due to inflammation and pressure in the sinus cavities, which are air-filled spaces in the skull.

It is characterized by a dull, aching pain in the face, particularly in the forehead, cheeks, and nose area. The pain can be constant or episodic and may worsen when bending or coughing. Sinus headaches can significantly impact daily life, making even simple tasks a struggle.

For example, a person with a sinus headache may have difficulty concentrating at work or school due to the constant discomfort and pain. They may also experience fatigue, irritability, and sensitivity to light and sound. Daily activities like grocery shopping or running errands can become exhausting tasks, and even routine tasks like cooking or cleaning can become painful and time-consuming. Social interactions and relationships may also suffer as a result of the constant pain and fatigue, leading to feelings of isolation and frustration.

Even simple pleasures like reading or watching a movie can be ruined by the debilitating pain, making everyday life a challenge to manage.
