  • Bilobalide: This terpenoid compound has neuroprotective properties, helping to reduce inflammation and prevent the formation of beta-amyloid plaques, which are associated with Alzheimer's disease.

Parts Used

The list below shows the primary parts of maidenhair tree used to make teas for alzheimer’s disease.

  • Leaves: Used for teas related to Alzheimer's disease due to their high content of flavonoids and terpenoids, which may help improve memory and cognitive function.
  • Seeds: Used for teas related to Alzheimer's disease due to their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation associated with the condition.
  • Barks: Used for teas related to Alzheimer's disease due to their potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation associated with the condition.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for alzheimer’s disease.

  1. Harvest 2-4 ginkgo biloba leaves from the plant and clean them thoroughly to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Dry the leaves in a low-temperature oven at 150 degrees fahrenheit for 1-2 hours to preserve their potency.
  3. Crush 1-2 teaspoons of dried ginkgo biloba leaves into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle.
  4. Steep the powder in 8 ounces of boiling water for 5-7 minutes to release its active compounds.
  5. Strain the tea and discard the solids to serve the herbal ginkgo biloba tea immediately.

Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic brahmi for alzheimer’s disease.

  1. Gather 1 tablespoon of dried bacopa monnieri leaves and flowers for each cup of tea needed.
  2. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan for 5 minutes or until it reaches a rolling boil.
  3. Steep the dried bacopa monnieri in boiling water for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove solids.
  5. Serve the tea warm or at room temperature after it has cooled for 2 minutes.
