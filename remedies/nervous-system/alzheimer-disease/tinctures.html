Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for alzheimer’s disease.

  1. Gather 1 part dried ginkgo biloba leaves and 2 parts vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Steep the dried ginkgo biloba leaves in the vodka for 2-6 weeks in a cool dark place.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a separate container discard solids.
  4. Transfer the filtered liquid to a clean glass dropper bottle and store it in a cool dark place.
