Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for alzheimer’s disease.

  1. Harvest ginkgo biloba leaves in late summer when they are fully ripe and have a yellowish-green color.
  2. Dry the leaves in a warm oven at 150 degrees fahrenheit for 2 hours to remove moisture.
  3. Grind 2 ounces of dried ginkgo biloba leaves into a fine powder using a coffee grinder.
  4. Mix the powder with 2 tablespoons of gelatin and 2 tablespoons of water to form a gel.
  5. Fill empty gelatin capsules with the gel mixture and seal the capsules to create ginkgo biloba capsules.
