Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for restless leg syndrome.

  1. Harvest fresh valeriana officinalis roots and stems at dawn when the plant is at its highest moisture content.
  2. Clean and dry the harvested plant material to reduce bacterial contamination and preserve potency.
  3. Combine 250 grams of the dried plant material with 750 milliliters of 95 percent ethanol in a clean glass container.
  4. Steep the mixture in a cool dark place for 2 to 6 weeks to allow for maximum extraction.
  5. Strain the liquid through cheesecloth and discard the solids then transfer the tincture to dark glass bottles for storage.
