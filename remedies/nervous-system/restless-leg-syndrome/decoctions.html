Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic valerian for restless leg syndrome.

  1. Harvest 1 to 2 bunches of valeriana officinalis roots and clean them thoroughly to remove dirt and debris.
  2. Chop the roots into smaller pieces to increase their surface area for better extraction.
  3. Combine 5 to 10 grams of the chopped valeriana roots with 1 liter of boiling water to create a decoction.
  4. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the mixture steep for 5 to 10 minutes to allow for extraction.
  5. Strain the liquid and discard the solids to obtain a clear herbal valeriana officinalis decoction.
