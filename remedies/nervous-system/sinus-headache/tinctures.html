The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tincture for sinus headache.

  1. Harvest 2 ounces of fresh yarrow flowers and leaves, or use 1 ounce of dried yarrow, and roughly chop them.
  2. Combine the chopped yarrow with 1 pint of 80-proof vodka or grain alcohol in a clean glass jar, ensuring the herbs are fully submerged.
