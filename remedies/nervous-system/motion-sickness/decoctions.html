Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint for motion sickness.

  1. Harvest fresh mentha x piperita leaves and flowers in the morning when they are at their highest oil content.

  3. Combine the cut mentha x piperita with 1 liter of water in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the mixture steep for 5-10 minutes to allow the active compounds to extract.
