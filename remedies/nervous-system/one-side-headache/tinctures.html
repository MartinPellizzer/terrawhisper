1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help reduce pain and swelling, making Zingiber officinale effective in treating one side headaches. When you take Zingiber officinale tincture for a one side headache, here's what to do: take 10-20 drops of the tincture in water or tea, 2-3 times a day. Start with a small dose and adjust as needed.

If your headache worsens, stop taking the tincture and consult a healthcare professional. If you take Zingiber officinale tincture correctly, you may experience a reduction in pain and inflammation, making your headache less severe. 2. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba tinctures contains flavonoids, terpenoids, and bilobalide, which have anti-inflammatory and vasodilating properties.

When you take Ginkgo biloba tinctures for a one-sided headache, it's essential to start with a small dose, such as 20-30 drops, mixed with water, 2-3 times a day. However, consult with a healthcare professional before combining different herbal remedies.
