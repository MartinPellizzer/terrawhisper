The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic feverfew for migraine.

  1. Harvest fresh tanacetum parthenium leaves measure one teaspoon per cup boiling water.
  2. Combine the measured tanacetum parthenium leaves with one cup of filtered water in a saucepan.
