Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic feverfew for migraine.

  1. Gather 1-2 teaspoons of dried tanacetum parthenium flowers from a reputable source.

  4. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove solids.
  5. Allow the tea to cool to a comfortable drinking temperature before consumption within 30 minutes.
