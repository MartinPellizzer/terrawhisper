Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for migraine.

  1. Harvest ginkgo biloba leaves and flowers in late summer or early fall when the plant is mature.
  2. Dry the harvested plant material in a low-temperature oven at 100°f for 1 hour.
  3. Chop the dried plant material into small pieces and measure out 1 part herb to 2 parts solvent.
  4. Combine the chopped herb with 80-proof vodka in a clean glass jar and allow it to steep for 2 weeks.
  5. Strain the tincture through a cheesecloth or coffee filter and store it in a dark glass bottle at room temperature.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic feverfew for migraine.
