1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba teas contains flavonoids and terpenoids, which are its key constituents that help alleviate numbness in hands.

The terpenoids, including ginkgolides, have antioxidant properties that protect the nerves from damage and promote their healing. When you experience numbness in your hands, try making a cup of Ginkgo biloba tea by steeping one teaspoon of dried leaves in a cup of boiling water for five to seven minutes.

As you take Ginkgo biloba tea regularly, you may notice an improvement in your hand's sensation and feeling within a few weeks.
