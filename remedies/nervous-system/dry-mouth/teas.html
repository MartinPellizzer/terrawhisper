9 Best Herbal Teas For Dry Mouth

Herbal teas for Dry mouth are a natural remedy that involves drinking infusions made from various herbs to alleviate symptoms of dry mouth, also known as xerostomia.

This condition occurs when the salivary glands do not produce enough saliva, leading to dryness and discomfort in the mouth.

Herbal teas can treat dry mouth due to their ability to stimulate saliva production, soothe irritated tissues, and provide anti-inflammatory properties.

Examples of herbal teas that can help alleviate dry mouth symptoms include peppermint tea, which stimulates saliva production and provides a cooling sensation; ginger tea, which has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and pain; licorice root tea, which soothes and protects the mucous membranes; and slippery elm tea, which forms a protective barrier on the mucous membranes to prevent further irritation.

  5. Drink the tea while warm, 2-3 times a day to soothe dry mouth.
