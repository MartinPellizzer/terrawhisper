What is sore eyes and how it affects your life?

Sore eyes, also known as eye strain, is a common condition characterized by discomfort, fatigue, and irritation in the eyes.

It can manifest in various ways, such as dryness, itchiness, burning, and even blurred vision. Sore eyes can significantly impact daily life, making it difficult to perform even the simplest tasks. For instance, it can hinder one's ability to work on a computer or watch TV, leading to decreased productivity and mood swings.

Even daily activities like cooking, reading, and driving can become challenging and painful. Furthermore, sore eyes can affect relationships and social interactions, as individuals may feel irritable and withdrawn. It can also impact physical health, leading to headaches, neck and shoulder pain, and even migraines. Moreover, sore eyes can disrupt sleep patterns, making it hard to relax and recharge.

Overall, sore eyes can have a ripple effect on various aspects of life, causing inconvenience, discomfort, and distress.
