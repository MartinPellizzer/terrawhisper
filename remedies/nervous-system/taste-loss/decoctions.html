Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic maidenhair tree for taste loss.

  1. Rinse 60-120 grams of dried ginkgo biloba leaves with cold water to remove impurities.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.

  4. Simmer the mixture for 30-45 minutes or until the liquid has reduced to 1 cup.
  5. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a clean container.
