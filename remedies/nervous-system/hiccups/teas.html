Chamomile tea is an excellent choice due to its calming properties, while peppermint tea can help relax the muscles in the throat and chest. Drinking a warm cup of dandelion tea can also stimulate digestion and potentially alleviate hiccups. To maximize the benefits, sip the tea slowly and take deep, steady breaths.

However, if hiccups persist for an extended period or are accompanied by other symptoms, it is important to seek medical attention.
