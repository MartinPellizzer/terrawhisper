Silybum marianum may help increase bile production, which can help dissolve gallstones. Glycyrrhiza glabra, also known as licorice root, has anti-inflammatory properties that may help soothe the gallbladder and reduce pain. When you use these herbal teas or creams, you may experience relief from gallstone pain and discomfort. You may also feel more relaxed and less anxious, as the anti-inflammatory properties of these herbs can help reduce overall inflammation in the body.

By using these herbal remedies, you can help your body heal and recover from gallstone issues.
