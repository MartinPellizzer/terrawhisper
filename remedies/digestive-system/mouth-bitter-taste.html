What is bitter taste in mouth and how it affects your life?

A bitter taste in the mouth can be an unpleasant and persistent experience that can significantly impact daily life.

It's characterized by an unappealing, sharp, and unpleasant sensation that lingers in the mouth, throat, and tongue. This bitter taste can leave a person feeling frustrated and irritable, affecting their appetite and eating habits, as well as their social interactions.

For instance, it can make meals taste unpleasant, leading to a decrease in food intake, and in some cases, weight loss. It can also make social gatherings, like dining out or attending parties, uncomfortable due to the constant awareness of the bitter taste. Additionally, this condition can interfere with personal hygiene, as it can lead to a decrease in oral care and maintenance, which can result in further oral health issues.

Moreover, the constant presence of a bitter taste can be a significant source of stress and anxiety, impacting work or school performance, relationships, and overall quality of life.
