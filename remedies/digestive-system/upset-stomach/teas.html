10 best herbal teas for upset stomach

Herbal teas for upset stomach are aromatic beverages prepared by steeping dried herbs in hot water.

They provide relief from stomach discomfort due to their anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and carminative properties. Some popular herbal teas include chamomile, ginger, peppermint, and fennel.

Chamomile tea soothes the digestive tract, reducing inflammation and promoting relaxation. Ginger tea is known for its effectiveness against nausea and vomiting, while peppermint and fennel teas alleviate abdominal cramps and gas.

By consuming these herbal teas, individuals can experience improved digestion and reduced symptoms of stomach discomfort, leading to enhanced quality of life and overall well-being.

The list below shows the primary parts of peppermint tea used to make teas for upset stomach.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tea for upset stomach.

  1. In a teapot, measure out 1 teaspoon of dried peppermint leaves for every 8 ounces of water.
  2. Pour boiling water over the peppermint leaves in the teapot.
  3. Allow the tea to steep for 5-10 minutes to extract the peppermint's beneficial compounds.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup to remove the spent peppermint leaves.
  5. Sip the hot tea slowly, allowing it to soothe and calm an upset stomach.
