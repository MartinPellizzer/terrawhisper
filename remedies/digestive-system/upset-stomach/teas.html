The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic chamomile for upset stomach.

  1. Measure one teaspoon of dried matricaria chamomilla flowers for every eight ounces of water.

  4. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the herbal solids.
  5. Allow the tea to cool slightly before consuming and enjoy within thirty minutes of preparation.
