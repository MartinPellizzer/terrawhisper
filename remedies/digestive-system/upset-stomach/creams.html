Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for upset stomach.

  1. Harvest 100g of dried glycyrrhiza glabra roots from a reputable supplier or cultivate them yourself.

  3. Strain the infused oil through cheesecloth into a clean container after 2 hours of preparation time.
  4. Mix 100g of beeswax with 50g of shea butter and 50g of coconut oil to create a smooth emulsion.
