Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yellow gentian for upset stomach.

  1. Harvest 1-2 cups of dried gentiana lutea roots in the fall season when the plant is mature.
  2. Chop the dried gentiana lutea roots into small pieces to increase their surface area for infusion.
  3. Steep 1 tablespoon of chopped gentiana lutea roots in 1 cup of boiling water for 5-7 minutes to release their bioactive compounds.
  4. Strain the decoction through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve to separate the solids from the liquid.
  5. Store the cooled decoction in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or freeze it for later use.
