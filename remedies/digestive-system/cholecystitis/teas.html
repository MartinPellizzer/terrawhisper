These include chamomile, lemon balm, and peppermint teas. Drinking warm infusions of these herbs can help alleviate pain, reduce inflammation in the gallbladder, and improve digestion. Another beneficial tea is dandelion root tea, which aids in bile production and can help prevent gallstones.

However, it's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any herbal tea treatment for cholecystitis.
