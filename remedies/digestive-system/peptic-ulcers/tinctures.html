Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic licorice for peptic ulcers.

  1. Harvest 500g of fresh or dried root of glycyrrhiza glabra and clean it thoroughly with a soft brush.
  2. Chop the root into small pieces and combine it with 75% vodka and 25% distilled water in a ratio of 1:5.
  3. Store the mixture in a dark glass container and steep it for 2-4 weeks in a cool dry place.
  4. Strain the mixture using a cheesecloth or a coffee filter and discard the solids.
  5. Transfer the tincture to a clean glass bottle and store it in a cool dark place for at least 6 months before use.
