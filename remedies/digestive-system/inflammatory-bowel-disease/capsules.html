Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic turmeric for inflammatory bowel disease.

  1. Harvest 20-30 grams of dried curcuma longa root powder from a reputable supplier or dried from fresh roots.
  2. Mix the powder with 2 tablespoons of a binding agent like gum acacia in a clean bowl.
  3. Add a small amount of water to the bowl to create a dough-like consistency and knead for 5 minutes.
  4. Use a capsule machine to fill empty capsules with the mixture and seal them according to manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Label the capsules with the name of the product curcuma longa extract and the dosage of 500 mg per capsule.
