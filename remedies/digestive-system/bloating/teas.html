Is it safe to consume herbal teas for bloating every day?

While herbal teas can be helpful for bloating, having them every day might not be the best idea.

Herbal teas can help with bloating, but it takes some time to notice results.

This is because herbal teas work slowly to calm your digestive system and reduce gas and inflammation.
