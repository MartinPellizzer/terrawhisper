What is hyperacidity and how it affects your life?

Hyperacidity, also known as acid reflux or acid indigestion, is a condition characterized by an excessive production of stomach acid, leading to a burning sensation in the chest and throat.

This condition can have a significant impact on one's daily life, causing discomfort and pain. For instance, a person suffering from hyperacidity may experience difficulty sleeping due to the constant burning sensation in the chest and throat, making it hard to get a good night's rest. Additionally, the symptoms can also disrupt daily activities such as work, socializing, and even eating, as the individual may need to frequently pause to manage the discomfort.

Moreover, the condition can lead to a loss of appetite, digestive problems, and even affect one's mental health, causing anxiety and stress. Furthermore, hyperacidity can also cause tooth erosion, bad breath, and a sour taste in the mouth, making social interactions and personal hygiene a challenge. The discomfort and pain can also be severe enough to limit physical activity, making everyday tasks a struggle.

Overall, hyperacidity can have a significant impact on one's quality of life, affecting not only their physical health but also their mental well-being.
