The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic lemon balm tea for gerd.

  1. Harvest or obtain 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon balm leaves, or 1 tablespoon of dried leaves.
  2. Rinse the lemon balm leaves under cold water to remove any dirt or impurities.

  5. Strain the tea into a cup, discarding the leaves, and enjoy it while it's still warm.
