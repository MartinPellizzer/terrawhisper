Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic milk thistle for viral hepatitis.

  1. Harvest 1-2 pounds of silybum marianum seeds in the fall when the seed pods are dry and brittle.
  2. Dry the harvested seeds in a warm place or using a food dehydrator at 95 degrees fahrenheit for 8 hours.
  3. Grind 1-2 ounces of dried silybum marianum seeds into a fine powder using a coffee grinder or spice grinder.
  4. Mix 1 part of the ground silybum marianum seeds with 2 parts of 80-100 proof vodka in a clean glass jar.
  5. Allow the mixture to steep in a cool dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
