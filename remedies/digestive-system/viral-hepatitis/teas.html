Glycyrrhiza glabra teas, derived from the roots of the licorice plant, have been studied for their potential to treat viral hepatitis due to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

  5. Drink the tea 2-3 times a day for 7-10 days as needed, under medical supervision.
