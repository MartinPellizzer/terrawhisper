What to know about stomach pain before treating it with medicinal herbs

Stomach pain, also known as abdominal pain, refers to discomfort or pain felt between the chest and pelvic regions.

It can have a significant negative impact on people's lives, causing discomfort, distress, and interfering with daily activities. Stomach pain can be caused by various factors, including indigestion, gastritis, menstrual cramps, food allergies, and gastrointestinal infections. What is stomach pain and how it affects your life?

Stomach pain refers to discomfort or pain in the abdominal area, specifically in the region of the stomach.

It can manifest in various forms, such as sharp, dull, cramping, or burning sensations, and its intensity can range from mild to severe. Stomach pain can significantly impact a person's daily life, causing them to miss work or school, avoid social activities, and experience reduced appetite, leading to malnutrition or weight loss. It can also disrupt sleep patterns, causing fatigue and irritability, and may result in increased anxiety or depression due to the fear of recurring pain.

Furthermore, stomach pain can hinder physical activities, making it challenging to perform routine tasks or engage in exercise, ultimately affecting a person's overall quality of life.
