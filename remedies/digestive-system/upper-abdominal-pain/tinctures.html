The list below shows the primary parts of ginger used to make tinctures for upper abdominal pain.

Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginger for upper abdominal pain.

  1. Harvest the zingiber officinale roots in early morning after the dew has dried completely.
  2. Wash the harvested roots thoroughly with distilled water and pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. Chop the dried roots into small pieces using a sharp knife and measure out 500 grams of them.
  4. Combine the chopped roots with 70% ethanol in a 1:5 ratio and let it steep for 2 weeks in a glass jar.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth and discard the solids then transfer the tincture to dark glass bottles.
