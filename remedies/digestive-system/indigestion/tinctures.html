  • Antioxidant properties: Wormwood tincture can help protect the gastrointestinal tract from oxidative stress and damage, promoting overall digestive health. This is due to the presence of various polyphenols and flavonoids found in wormwood.

  • Flowers: The flowers of wormwood tincture plant are useful in making medicinal tea for indigestion as they contain volatile oils that can help relax the gastrointestinal tract and alleviate digestive discomfort.
  • Seeds: The seeds of wormwood tincture plant are used in making medicinal tea for indigestion because they are rich in essential oils that can promote digestion and reduce bloating and gas.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic wormwood tincture for indigestion.

  1. Chop 2 ounces of dried wormwood herb into small pieces and place in a clean glass jar.
  2. Pour 1 pint of vodka or rum over the herbs, ensuring they are fully submerged.
