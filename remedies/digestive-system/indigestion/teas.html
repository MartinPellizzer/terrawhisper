1. Zingiber officinale teas

Related Study

Recipe:

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a pot.
  2. Take 1 teaspoon of dried Zingiber officinale root (or 1-inch piece of fresh root) and place it in a tea infuser.

  4. Strain the tea into a cup and discard the root.
  5. Drink the tea as needed for indigestion relief.

Zingiber officinale teas can be an effective natural remedy for indigestion, but it may cause side effects such as stomach upset, nausea, and dizziness in some individuals.
