  • Anti-inflammatory: Chamomile essential oil has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, which can contribute to stomach pain and discomfort.
  • Antispasmodic: The antispasmodic properties of chamomile essential oil can help relax the muscles in the digestive tract, reducing spasms and cramps that may be causing stomach pain.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic chamomile for stomach pain.

  4. Add 2 cups of carrier oil such as coconut or sweet almond oil and seal the jar.
  5. Place the jar in a warm dark place for 2 weeks shaking daily before straining.
