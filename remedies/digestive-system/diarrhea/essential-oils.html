The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint for diarrhea.

  1. Harvest fresh peppermint leaves from your garden or obtain them from a trusted supplier.
  2. Clean and dry the harvested peppermint leaves thoroughly for at least 3 hours.

  5. Store the filtered mentha x piperita essential oil in dark glass bottles with tight-fitting lids at room temperature for up to 2 years.
