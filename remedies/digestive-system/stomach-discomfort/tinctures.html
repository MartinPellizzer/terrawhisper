Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic ginger for stomach discomfort.

  1. Harvest 1-2 pounds of fresh zingiber officinale roots with thick skin intact.
  2. Clean the roots by gently scrubbing with a soft brush under cold running water.
  3. Chop the cleaned roots into small pieces about 1/4 inch in size weighing 1/2 pound.
  4. Steep the chopped roots in 2 cups of 80 proof vodka for 2-3 weeks shaking daily.
  5. Strain the liquid through cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle discarding the solids.
