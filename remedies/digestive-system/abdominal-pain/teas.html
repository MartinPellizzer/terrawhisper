The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tea for abdominal pain.

  1. Measure 1 teaspoon of dried peppermint leaves or 2 teaspoons of fresh peppermint leaves.
  2. Place the peppermint leaves in a tea infuser or teapot.
  3. Pour 8 ounces of boiling water over the peppermint leaves.
  4. Let the tea steep for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea infuser or strain the tea, then drink it while warm.
