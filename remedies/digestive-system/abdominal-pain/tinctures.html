For IBS, tinctures made from herbs such as peppermint, lemon balm, and licorice may help alleviate symptoms like bloating, gas, and abdominal pain. To relieve menstrual cramps, cramp bark, black haw, and wild yam are often used in herbal tinctures to help relax the smooth muscles of the uterus and reduce spasms.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare practitioner before starting any new herbal treatment.
