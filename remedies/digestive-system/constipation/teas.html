10 best herbal teas for constipation

Herbal teas for constipation are infusions made from various plants and herbs that are known to promote bowel movements and provide relief from constipation.

These teas typically contain natural laxatives, such as senna, licorice root, or ginger, which help to stimulate the digestive system and encourage regular bowel movements. By drinking herbal teas for constipation, individuals can alleviate the discomfort and pain associated with irregular bowel movements, improve their overall digestive health, and enhance their quality of life. For example, someone who has been experiencing constipation due to a change in diet or lifestyle may find relief by drinking a soothing cup of chamomile and ginger tea before bed.

Similarly, a person who has been struggling with chronic constipation due to a medical condition may benefit from consuming senna tea regularly, under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

  • Stimulation of gut bacteria: Senna tea can stimulate the growth of beneficial gut bacteria, which can help improve overall gut health and alleviate constipation caused by gut dysbiosis.
