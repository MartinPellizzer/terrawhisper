Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic milk thistle for jaundice.

  1. Harvest silybum marianum flowers and seeds in full bloom and dry them thoroughly for 24 hours.
  2. Combine 1 part dried silybum marianum with 2 parts 80 proof vodka in a clean glass jar.

  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth into another clean glass jar and discard the solids.
