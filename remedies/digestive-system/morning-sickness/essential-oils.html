9 Herbal Essential Oils For Morning Sickness

Herbal essential oils can be a great help for morning sickness.

1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale essential oils contains gingerol and shogaol, compounds with anti-inflammatory properties that help alleviate morning sickness symptoms.

These natural compounds work by blocking the production of prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause stomach contractions and nausea. When inhaled or ingested, Zingiber officinale essential oils can help reduce the severity of morning sickness symptoms, including vomiting and dizziness. To use Zingiber officinale essential oils for morning sickness, add a few drops to a diffuser, inhale it directly from a cloth or handkerchief, or mix it with a carrier oil for a massage.

You can also try drinking ginger tea or taking ginger supplements, but always consult with a healthcare professional before ingesting any new substances. When using Zingiber officinale essential oils for morning sickness, you may notice a decrease in nausea and vomiting within 15-30 minutes. Some people may experience relief within 5-10 minutes after inhaling the oil. As your body adjusts to the new compound, you may also notice an improvement in appetite and energy levels.

However, it's essential to note that Zingiber officinale essential oils may not completely eliminate morning sickness symptoms, but it can help alleviate them.

2. Foeniculum vulgare

Foeniculum vulgare essential oils contains compounds like anethole, fenchone, and limonene that may help alleviate morning sickness symptoms.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which can help ease the digestive discomfort associated with nausea and vomiting. Foeniculum vulgare essential oils also have a calming effect on the nervous system, reducing stress and anxiety that can exacerbate morning sickness. To try using Foeniculum vulgare essential oils for morning sickness, add a few drops to a carrier oil like coconut or sweet almond oil and gently massage it onto your abdomen. You can also inhale the oil by adding it to a diffuser or by directly inhaling it from the bottle.

Start with small amounts and gradually increase as needed. It's essential to dilute the essential oil with a carrier oil before applying it to the skin, as undiluted essential oils can be irritating. Taking action with Foeniculum vulgare essential oils may lead to a reduction in nausea and vomiting, improved digestion, and a decrease in stress and anxiety. Some women may find relief from morning sickness symptoms, allowing them to feel more comfortable during the day. However, it's crucial to note that individual results may vary, and Foeniculum vulgare essential oils should not be relied upon as the sole treatment for morning sickness.

Always consult with a healthcare professional before using essential oils, especially if you have any underlying medical conditions or are pregnant.
