The volatile oils present in Zingiber officinale, such as zingiberene and bisabolol, can also help calm the stomach and prevent vomiting. If you're experiencing gastroenteritis, consider taking a Zingiber officinale tincture.

Start by mixing 2-3 drops of the tincture with water, and then drink it 2-3 times a day. You can also mix it with other herbal remedies like peppermint or chamomile to enhance its effectiveness. To see the best results, it's essential to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids, such as water, clear broths, or electrolyte-rich beverages like coconut water or sports drinks.
