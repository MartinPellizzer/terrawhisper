What is gastritis and how it affects your life?

Gastritis is a condition characterized by inflammation of the lining of the stomach, which can result in various unpleasant symptoms and significantly impact an individual's quality of life.

Common symptoms of gastritis include abdominal pain or discomfort, nausea, vomiting, bloating, loss of appetite, and acid reflux. These symptoms can lead to decreased productivity and absenteeism from work or school.

Additionally, gastritis can cause difficulty sleeping due to discomfort or pain in the stomach, leading to fatigue and irritability during the day. In severe cases, gastritis can also increase the risk of developing stomach ulcers or even stomach cancer.

Overall, gastritis can negatively affect an individual's physical, emotional, and social well-being, making it a significant health concern.
