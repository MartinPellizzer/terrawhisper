The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic fennel for bitter taste in mouth.

  2. Clean and chop the plant material into small pieces to facilitate drying and tincture-making.
  3. Combine 250 grams of chopped foeniculum vulgare with 500 milliliters of 45% ethanol in a large glass jar for a minimum of 14 days.
