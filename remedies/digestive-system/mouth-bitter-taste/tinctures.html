Quick Recipe

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yellow gentian for bitter taste in mouth.

  1. Harvest 50g of dried gentiana lutea roots in late summer or early fall when roots are mature.
  2. Chop the dried roots into small pieces and weigh out 50g for tincture making process.
  3. Combine the chopped gentiana lutea roots with 250ml of 60% ethanol in a clean glass jar.
  4. Steep the mixture for 2-6 weeks in a cool dark place with occasional shaking of the jar.
  5. Strain the tincture through cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean glass bottle and store.
