For IBS, herbs like chamomile, lemon balm, and peppermint may be used to alleviate symptoms such as bloating, cramping, and diarrhea. For IBD, anti-inflammatory herbs like turmeric, slippery elm, and marshmallow root can help reduce inflammation and soothe the digestive tract.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal treatment.
