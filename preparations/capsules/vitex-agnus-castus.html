Vitex agnus-castus capsule for medicinal use

Vitex agnus-castus capsule is an herbal preparation made from the dried fruits of the chaste tree, also known as Vitex agnus-castus.

It is commonly used in herbalism to support hormonal balance, particularly in women, by regulating menstrual cycles and alleviating symptoms of premenstrual syndrome. This herb is also believed to help with conditions such as infertility, menopausal symptoms, and thyroid disorders. The active compounds in Vitex agnus-castus are thought to influence the pituitary gland and reduce prolactin levels.

It is typically taken in capsule form as a dietary supplement under the guidance of a qualified herbalist or healthcare provider.

Preparation

To make Vitex agnus-castus capsule, first gather dried Vitex agnus-castus berries, which are typically available from herbal suppliers or health stores.

Next, clean the berries thoroughly and grind them into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle or a spice grinder. Then, measure the desired amount of powder and place it into a capsule filler or use empty gelatin capsules. Ensure the powder is evenly distributed to maintain potency.

Finally, seal the capsules and store them in an airtight container away from moisture and direct sunlight.
