Herbal Steam Inhalations: How to Prepare It Correctly

Herbal steam inhalation is a therapeutic technique used in herbal medicine to extract volatile medicinal compounds from specific plant parts by passing hot water vapor through a controlled process.

This method relies on the principle of distillation to release essential oils and other active constituents that would otherwise remain trapped in the plant tissue. Practitioners commonly use aromatic herbs such as eucalyptus or peppermint to treat respiratory congestion and clear the airways. As one of the most effective and accessible approaches among various herbal preparation methods, steam inhalation allows for rapid delivery of medication directly to the respiratory system.

It is frequently employed for relief from cold symptoms, sinus pressure, and mild asthma attacks.
