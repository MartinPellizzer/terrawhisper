Herbal Steam Inhalation: How to Prepare It Correctly

Herbal steam inhalation is a therapeutic technique used in herbal medicine to extract volatile medicinal compounds from aromatic plant parts by passing hot vapor through a controlled process.

This method relies on the principle of heat volatility, where steam carries active essential oils upward to reach mucous membranes directly. Practitioners commonly utilize plant materials such as eucalyptus leaves or peppermint to treat respiratory congestion and clear airways. By delivering these bioactive agents locally, this approach offers a rapid and gentle treatment for various upper respiratory conditions.

It serves as one of the most accessible and effective techniques among various herbal preparation methods for addressing acute respiratory discomfort.
