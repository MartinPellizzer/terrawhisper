Herbal Salve: How to Prepare It Correctly

A herbal salve is a topical preparation method in herbal medicine that creates a semi-solid ointment by infusing medicinal compounds from plant parts into a fatty base.

This process typically involves extracting active constituents from materials like comfrey leaf or calendula petals using heat and gentle agitation. The resulting formulation serves to soothe skin irritation, heal minor wounds, or reduce inflammation directly at the site of application. Understanding this technique is essential when exploring various herbal preparation methods, as it differs significantly from teas or tinctures.

By combining potent botanicals with carriers like beeswax and oil, practitioners can deliver long-lasting therapeutic benefits to the surface layers of the skin.
