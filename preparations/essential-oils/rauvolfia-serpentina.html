Rauvolfia serpentina essential oil for medicinal use

Rauvolfia serpentina essential oil is a concentrated extract derived from the roots of the Rauvolfia serpentina plant, commonly known as Indian snakeroot.

This oil is traditionally used in herbal medicine for its calming and sedative properties, particularly for managing conditions like hypertension and anxiety. It contains alkaloids such as reserpine, which have been historically used to lower blood pressure. In herbalism, it is often diluted and applied topically or used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and reduce stress.

However, due to its potency, it should be used with caution and under the guidance of a qualified practitioner.
