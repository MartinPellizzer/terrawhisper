100 best essential oils for medicinal use

Essential oils are concentrated extracts derived from various parts of plants, such as leaves, flowers, bark, and roots, through methods like distillation or cold pressing.

These oils contain the essential aromatic compounds of the plant and are highly potent due to their strong concentration. In herbalism, essential oils are used for their therapeutic properties, including their ability to promote relaxation, support respiratory health, and alleviate skin conditions. They are commonly applied through diffusion, topical application, or inhalation.

Due to their potency, they are often diluted with carrier oils before use to ensure safety and effectiveness.
