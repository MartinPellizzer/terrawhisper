Herbal Compress: How to Prepare It Correctly

An herbal compress is a therapeutic technique used in herbal medicine to extract medicinal compounds from plant parts by steeping or soaking them in a controlled liquid bath.

This method relies on the principle of diffusion, where active ingredients transfer from the plant material into the carrier liquid to create a potent treatment. Commonly prepared with anti-inflammatory herbs like comfrey or calendula, the resulting cloth or bundle is applied directly to the skin to deliver relief. Unlike other herbal preparation methods that focus on ingestion or tinctures, this approach targets localized pain, swelling, and muscle tension.

It serves as an accessible and effective modality within the broader category of herbal preparation methods for external care.
