Herbal Infusion: How to Prepare It Correctly

Herbal infusion is a gentle extraction process used in herbal medicine to draw out medicinal compounds from delicate plant materials like leaves and flowers by steeping them in hot water.

This method relies on the principle that hot water effectively dissolves water-soluble active ingredients without damaging heat-sensitive compounds found in many herbs. It is particularly suited for soothing teas made with plants such as chamomile or peppermint to treat minor ailments. As one of the most common and accessible herbal preparation methods, infusion allows beginners to harness nature's healing properties safely.

By controlling the steeping time and temperature, practitioners can optimize the potency of the final beverage for therapeutic use.
