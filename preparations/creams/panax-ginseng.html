Panax ginseng cream for medicinal use

Panax ginseng cream is a topical preparation made from the root of the Panax ginseng plant, often combined with a base of oils, beeswax, and other herbal ingredients.

It is used in herbalism to support energy levels, reduce fatigue, and enhance mental clarity by promoting circulation and stimulating the body's natural vitality. This cream is applied directly to the skin, typically on the wrists, temples, or feet, to allow the active compounds to be absorbed through the skin. It is valued for its ability to balance the body's systems and is often used during periods of stress or physical exertion.

In traditional herbal practices, it is believed to strengthen the immune system and improve overall well-being.
