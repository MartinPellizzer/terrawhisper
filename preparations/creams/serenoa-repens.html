Serenoa repens cream for medicinal use

Serenoa repens cream is a topical preparation made from the extract of the fruit of the Serenoa repens plant, commonly known as saw palmetto.

It is widely used in herbal medicine for its purported benefits in supporting prostate health, particularly in managing symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia. The cream is typically applied directly to the skin, often in the area of the lower abdomen or genital region, to allow for localized absorption. Herbalists recommend it as a natural alternative to conventional treatments for prostate-related issues.

Its use is based on traditional knowledge and some scientific studies suggesting its potential anti-inflammatory and anti-androgenic properties.

Health Benefits

Serenoa repens cream relieves prostate issues by reducing inflammation and supporting overall prostate health.

It is effective in treating benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) symptoms, such as frequent urination and difficulty in starting urination. The cream helps improve urine flow, making it easier for individuals to empty their bladder completely. Regular use of Serenoa repens cream may reduce the risk of prostate enlargement and related complications.

This natural remedy offers a safe and effective option for maintaining prostate wellness.

Bioactive Constituents

Serenoa repens cream contains polysaccharides, which contribute to its moisturizing and skin-protective properties by enhancing hydration and promoting skin barrier function.

Saponins in the cream exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, supporting skin health and reducing irritation. Tocopherols, a form of vitamin E, act as powerful antioxidants that neutralize free radicals and protect the skin from oxidative stress. Fatty acids help to nourish and soften the skin, improving its texture and elasticity.

Terpenes provide additional anti-inflammatory and soothing benefits, enhancing the overall therapeutic profile of the cream.

Plant Parts

Serenoa repens cream is primarily made using the extracted oil from the fruit of the plant, which is rich in beneficial compounds.

The fruits, known as berries, are the most commonly used part due to their high concentration of fatty acids and antioxidants. The leaves of Serenoa repens are also occasionally used, though less frequently than the fruit. The roots and stems are generally not used in the preparation of the cream.

This focus on the fruit ensures the cream's effectiveness in treating skin conditions and promoting overall skin health.
