Santalum album cream for medicinal use

Santalum album cream is a topical herbal preparation made from the oil extracted from the wood of the sandalwood tree, *Santalum album*.

This cream is traditionally used in herbalism for its calming and soothing properties. It is often applied to the skin to alleviate stress, anxiety, and skin irritations. The fragrance of the cream is believed to have a grounding and mood-enhancing effect.

It is also used in aromatherapy and as a natural remedy for promoting relaxation and emotional balance.

Preparation

To make Santalum album cream, first gather the necessary ingredients, including Santalum album (sandalwood) oil, a base cream, and optional additives like beeswax or vitamin E for stability.

Next, melt the base cream in a double boiler or a heat-safe container over low heat. Then, add a few drops of Santalum album oil and stir gently to ensure even distribution. Allow the mixture to cool slightly before pouring it into a clean, airtight container.

Finally, store the cream in a cool, dark place and use it as needed for its soothing and aromatic properties.
