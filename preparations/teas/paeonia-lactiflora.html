Paeonia lactiflora tea for medicinal use

Paeonia lactiflora tea is a herbal preparation made from the dried roots of the peony plant, known for its traditional use in Chinese medicine.

It is often used to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia. The tea is also believed to support cardiovascular health and may help regulate menstrual cycles in women. In herbalism, it is typically prepared by steeping the dried root in hot water for several minutes.

This preparation is valued for its calming properties and is commonly consumed as a soothing remedy during times of emotional or physical tension.
