Herbal Oil: How to Prepare It Correctly

Herbal oil infusion is a technique used in herbal medicine to extract medicinal compounds from specific plant parts by steeping them in a carrier oil under controlled conditions.

This method relies on the principle of maceration, where oil penetrates plant tissues over time to dissolve essential oils and soluble actives. Practitioners typically use dried flowers, leaves, or roots, such as calendula, as the source material for these preparations. Herbal oils serve primarily to deliver concentrated healing agents directly to the skin for topical relief.

Alongside other herbal preparation methods, this approach is a foundational tool for creating soothing balms and restorative liniments.
