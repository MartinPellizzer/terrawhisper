Satureja montana bath for medicinal use

Satureja montana bath is a traditional herbal preparation made by infusing the leaves of the mountain thyme plant in water.

It is often recommended for its calming effects and as a natural remedy for stress-related ailments.

Preparation

To make Satureja montana bath, begin by gathering fresh or dried Satureja montana leaves, also known as mountain thyme.

Place the leaves in a heatproof container and add enough water to cover them by about an inch. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 to 15 minutes to extract the beneficial compounds. Strain the liquid and allow it to cool to a comfortable temperature before adding it to a bathtub.

This soothing bath can help relieve muscle tension and promote a sense of calm.
