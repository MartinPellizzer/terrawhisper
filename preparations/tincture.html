Herbal Tincture: How to Prepare It Correctly

A herbal tincture is a liquid extraction technique used in herbal medicine to draw medicinal compounds from specific plant parts using alcohol or water-based solvents.

This method is particularly effective for preserving the potency of herbs like echinacea by utilizing the principle of solvent extraction to dissolve active ingredients efficiently. Practitioners typically select fresh or dried plant materials, such as leaves, roots, or flowers, to create these long-lasting remedies. Tinctures serve as a versatile base for various herbal preparation methods because they offer rapid onset and easy dosage compared to other forms.

Ultimately, this approach allows beginners to access concentrated therapeutic benefits with simple home preparation.
