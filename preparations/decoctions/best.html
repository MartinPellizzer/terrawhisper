100 best decoctions for medicinal use

Decoctions are a traditional method of preparing herbal remedies by boiling plant materials in water to extract their active compounds.

This process is commonly used in herbalism to make use of roots, bark, and other tough plant parts that cannot be easily dissolved in water. The resulting liquid is then strained and consumed as a tea or taken in other forms for its therapeutic effects. Decoctions are valued for their ability to deliver potent medicinal properties from the plant.

They are often used to treat conditions such as digestive issues, respiratory ailments, and inflammatory disorders.
