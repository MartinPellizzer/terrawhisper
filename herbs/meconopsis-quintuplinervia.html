What's the taxonomical classification of Meconopsis quintuplinervia?

Meconopsis quintuplinervia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Papaveraceae, specifically falling under the genus Meconopsis, with its precise biological identity defined by the species quintuplinervia.
