Cyperus minutus

Cyperus minutus, also known as small-flower umbrella sedge or small-flowered umbrella sedge, is a type of perennial sedge found in wetlands and moist areas, characterized by its long, flat stems and umbrella-like inflorescence.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its ability to treat digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and lower blood pressure.

The health benefits of Cyperus minutus include its potential to aid in the treatment of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its use as a diuretic to help manage fluid retention.
