Lindernia crustacea

What's the taxonomical classification of Lindernia crustacea?

Lindernia crustacea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Linderniaceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Lindernia, with its unique designation being the species crustacea.
