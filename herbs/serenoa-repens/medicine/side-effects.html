10 Possible Side Effects Of Serenoa Repens (Saw Palmetto)

Serenoa repens has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive problems, diarrhea, and stomach upset.

These side effects can occur due to allergic reactions, interactions with medications, or taking excessive doses.

For individuals with pre-existing gastrointestinal conditions, these symptoms can worsen their lives, leading to discomfort, fatigue, and decreased quality of life.
