1. Relieves prostate issues

This condition often leads to urinary symptoms such as frequent urination, difficulty starting urination, and a weak urine stream.

The serenoa repens plant, commonly known as saw palmetto, has been studied for its potential to alleviate symptoms associated with this condition by inhibiting the enzyme 5-alpha reductase, which plays a key role in prostate enlargement.

As a result, Serenoa repens is commonly used as a complementary therapy for prostate health.
