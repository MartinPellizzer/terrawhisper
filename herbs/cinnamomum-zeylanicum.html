Cinnamomum Zeylanicum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cinnamomum zeylanicum, commonly known as cinnamon, is a spice derived from the inner bark of the tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is widely used for its aromatic and medicinal properties.

The herb offers various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which may support digestive health and blood sugar regulation.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as cinnamaldehyde, eugenol, and polyphenols.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and powders can be made from cinnamon to harness its beneficial properties.
