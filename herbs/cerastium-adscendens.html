What is Cerastium adscendens?

Cerastium adscendens is a species of flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is an annual or biennial herb, with a compact, bushy growth habit and a height of 10-20 cm. The leaves are ovate, 1-2 cm long, and have a ciliate margin. The inflorescence is a dense, terminal corymb of 5-10 white or pink flowers, each 1-2 cm in diameter, with 5 petals and 10 stamens.

The fruit is a schizocarp, a type of dehiscent fruit that splits into 5-10 mericarps at maturity.
