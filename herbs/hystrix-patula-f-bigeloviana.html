Hystrix patula f. bigeloviana

Hystrix patula f. bigeloviana, also known as Bigelow's burdock or burdock root, is a plant species used in herbal medicine, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties, which can aid in the treatment of various health conditions.

The health benefits of burdock root include its ability to support digestive health, lower blood pressure, and improve immune function.

The plant is often prepared in herbal teas, tinctures, and infusions, as well as in supplements, to harness its medicinal properties and provide various health benefits.
