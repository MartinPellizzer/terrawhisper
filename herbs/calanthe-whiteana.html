Calanthe whiteana

What's the taxonomical classification of Calanthe whiteana?

Calanthe whiteana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Calanthe, ultimately identifying it as the specific species whiteana.
