    What is Trichilia parviflora?

    Trichilia parviflora is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Meliaceae.

    It is an evergreen tree or shrub that grows up to 20 meters tall, with a smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves that are 2-6 cm long and 1-2 cm wide. The leaves are elliptical in shape, with a rounded base and a pointed apex, and are covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are 1-2 cm in diameter and have 5 petals.

    The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release 2-4 brown seeds.
