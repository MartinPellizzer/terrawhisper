Senna neglecta

Senna neglecta, also known as Country Mallow or Sicklepod, is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Fabaceae.

The plant's leaves and pods have been used in traditional medicine for their medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Senna neglecta include treating skin conditions such as eczema and acne, as well as relieving digestive issues like constipation.

Senna neglecta is often prepared as a tea by infusing the leaves and pods in hot water, or as a tincture by soaking the plant material in a solvent like ethanol or glycerin.
