Celastrus paniculatus

What's the taxonomical classification of Celastrus paniculatus?

Celastrus paniculatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Celastrales and is a member of the family Celastraceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Celastrus, specifically identified by its species name, paniculatus.

Celastrus paniculatus has medicinal properties characterized by its ability to act as a tonic, an anti-inflammatory agent, and a treatment for various skin ailments.
