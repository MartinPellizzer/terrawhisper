Ligusticum Chuanxiong Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ligusticum chuanxiong, commonly known as Szechuan lovage, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its ability to improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pain.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as ligustilide, butylphthalide, and other essential oils that contribute to its therapeutic effects.
