The herb contains bioactive constituents such as ligustilide, butylphthalide, and other essential oils that contribute to its therapeutic effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its efficacy in treating conditions like headaches, menstrual disorders, and cardiovascular issues.
