Stevia yaconensis var. typica

It is prized for its sweet-tasting leaves and roots, which have been used for centuries in traditional medicine to support digestive health and weight management.

The health benefits of Stevia yaconensis var. typica include its potential to act as a prebiotic, promoting the growth of beneficial gut bacteria, as well as its ability to help regulate blood sugar levels and support healthy weight loss.
