10 Medicinal Constituents Of Terminalia Chebula (Chebulic Myrobalan)

Terminalia chebula has active constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and alkaloids that provide its medicinal properties.

The tannins confer antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, while the flavonoids exhibit antimicrobial and anticancer activities. These constituents enable Chebulic Myrobalan to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, respiratory infections, and skin conditions.

For instance, its antidiarrheal properties can alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, improving quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic digestive issues.
