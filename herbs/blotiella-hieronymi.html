What is Blotiella hieronymi?

Blotiella hieronymi is a species of fern in the family Dennstaedtiaceae.

It is characterized by its fronds with 3-5 pinnate segments, each 20-50 cm long, and pinnae with 2-4 pairs of lobes, 2-4 cm long. The rhizome is short, with scales up to 1 cm long, and the stipe is up to 30 cm long. Blotiella hieronymi is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, and typically grows in humid, shaded areas with rocky or sandy soils.

The plant reproduces via spores produced in sori on the underside of the pinnae.
