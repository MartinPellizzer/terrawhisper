Microglossa pyrifolia has small, fleshy, pyriform leaves that are typically arranged in an opposite pattern along its slender stems. Its stems are relatively thin and provide support for the compact grouping of leaves. The plant often displays a low-growing or creeping habit depending on its immediate environmental conditions.
