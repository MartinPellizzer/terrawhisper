Eupatorium urubambense var. typicum

Eupatorium urubambense var. typicum is a plant species, also known as the Urubamba Joe Pye Weed, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant has been found to possess medicinal properties, particularly in its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds, which contribute to its various health benefits.

Traditionally, the leaves and flowers of the plant are used in herbal preparations to alleviate symptoms of fever, colds, and respiratory issues, as well as to soothe digestive problems and ease menstrual cramps.

The plant is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is also used in topical applications to promote wound healing and reduce pain.
