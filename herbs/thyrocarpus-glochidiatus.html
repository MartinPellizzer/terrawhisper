        What side effects this plant can have?

        This plant can cause severe skin irritation, localized swelling, and painful dermatological reactions due to the presence of irritating glochids or tiny spines. Contact with these microscopic structures often leads to intense itching and redness as the hairs embed themselves in the epidermis. If the spines are not carefully removed, they can trigger a persistent inflammatory response or even secondary infections from bacteria. Some individuals may also experience allergic reactions characterized by hives or increased sensitivity in the affected area. Careful handling is necessary to avoid these uncomfortable physical symptoms.
