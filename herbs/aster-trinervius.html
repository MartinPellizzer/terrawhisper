What's the taxonomical classification of Aster trinervius?

Aster trinervius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is situated in Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized within the order Asterales and is a member of the widely known family Asteraceae. Finally, its taxonomic hierarchy concludes with its placement in the genus Aster, specifically identifying it as the species trinervius.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Aster trinervius has linear to lanceolate leaves characterized by three prominent longitudinal veins running from the base to the apex. The flower heads consist of numerous tiny ray florets surrounding a central cluster of disc florets.
