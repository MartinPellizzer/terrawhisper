What's the taxonomical classification of Crataegus oresbia?

Crataegus oresbia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Rosales and falls within the family Rosaceae. Finally, it is defined by the genus Crataegus and is identified by its specific species name, oresbia.
