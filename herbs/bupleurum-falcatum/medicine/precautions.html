10 Precautions To Take When Using Bupleurum Falcatum (Chinese Thoroughwort)

Bupleurum falcatum, also known as Chinese thoroughwort, has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as taking it under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.

It's crucial to monitor blood pressure, as the herb can lower blood pressure significantly, potentially leading to dizziness, fainting, or other circulatory issues.

Not taking this precaution may result in over-medication, especially when combined with other blood pressure-lowering medications, potentially causing severe health consequences.

1. Store properly to maintain potency

When using Bupleurum falcatum, also known as Chinese Thoroughwort, medicinally, it's important to be aware of pregnancy contraindication.

As this herb can stimulate uterine contractions and potentially induce abortion or premature labor, it's crucial for pregnant women to avoid using it, especially during the first trimester.

Additionally, breastfeeding mothers should exercise caution when using Bupleurum falcatum, as it may pass into breastmilk and affect infant development.
