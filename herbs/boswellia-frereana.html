Olibanum

Boswellia frereana

Ashwagandha is a prominent medicinal herb in Ayurvedic medicine, classified as a rasayana (rejuvenator). It is primarily utilized as an adaptogen to help the body manage physical and chemical stress.

Family
Solanaceae (Nightshade)

Native Region
India, Middle East, Africa

Part Used
Root, Leaf, Berry

Botanical Identification

Olibanum, scientifically known as Boswellia frereana, is a species of the Burseraceae family. Commonly called frankincense in some regions, it is a small, thorny shrub native to the dry forests of East Africa. Its resinous sap, used in herbal medicine, is harvested from its bark and has a distinctive aromatic profile. Synonyms include Olibanum resin and boswellia resin.

Active Compounds

Olibanum contains key active compounds such as terpenoids, flavonoids, and essential oils. These compounds work together to provide its therapeutic effects, with terpenoids contributing to anti-inflammatory properties and flavonoids offering antioxidant support. The essential oils enhance the overall bioavailability and efficacy of the herb.

  • Terpenoid
  • Flavonoid
  • Essential oil

Therapeutic Indications

System Condidtion Action
Musculoskeletal Rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, gout Anti-inflammatory
Digestive Inflammatory bowel disease, colitis, gastroesophageal reflux disease Anti-inflammatory
Respiratory Bronchitis, allergic rhinitis, lung fibrosis Anti-inflammatory, expectorant, antimicrobial

Preparation Methods

Extract: Used for inflammation and pain relief due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

Essential oil: Used for respiratory issues and skin conditions due to its aromatic and antimicrobial properties.

Decoction: Used for digestive issues and respiratory ailments due to its warming and soothing properties.

Safety Profile

Olibanum (Boswellia frereana) is generally safe when used as directed. However, it should be avoided during pregnancy and breastfeeding due to limited safety data. It may interact with blood-thinning medications, so consult a healthcare provider before use.
