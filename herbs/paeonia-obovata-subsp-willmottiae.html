Paeonia obovata subsp. willmottiae

What's the taxonomical classification of Paeonia obovata subsp. willmottiae?

Paeonia obovata subsp. willmottiae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the order Saxifragales. It is a member of the family Paeoniaceae and is placed within the genus Paeoonia. Ultimately, this specific botanical entity is identified by its formal scientific name, the species Paeonia obovata subsp. willmottiae.
