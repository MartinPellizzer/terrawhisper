Rubus macrophyllus var. bulgaricus

Rubus macrophyllus var. bulgaricus, also known as the Bulgarian blackberry, is a variant of the large-leaved blackberry plant, belonging to the Rosaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of Rubus macrophyllus var. bulgaricus include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and provide antioxidant protection against cell damage.

Herbal preparations of the plant can be made into teas, tinctures, and infusions, and are often used in traditional medicine to support overall health and well-being.
