What's the taxonomical classification of Rinorea subintegrifolia?

Rinorea subintegrifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is positioned in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Violaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Rinorea and its unique species designation, subintegrifolia.
