Castanopsis ferox

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Castanopsis ferox has large, leathery, elliptic leaves that measure up to 40 centimeters in length and possess distinctively wavy margins. These evergreen leaves are characterized by an entire margin and a prominent midrib that extends to the apex. The foliage is typically a deep, glossy green on the upper surface, providing a dense canopy structure. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, eventually giving rise to woody, globose fruits. This morphological profile distinguishes the species within its tropical forest habitat.
