What is Ajania remotipinna?

Ajania remotipinna is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It has a taproot and a woody base, with leafy branches that grow up to 50 cm in height. The leaves are alternate, lanceolate to ovate in shape, 5-10 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, with a ciliate margin and a glabrous to pubescent surface. The inflorescence is a capitulum with white or pink flowers, 1-2 cm in diameter, and has achenes that are 3-4 mm long and 1-2 mm wide, with a pappus of 10-20 bristles.

The plant is native to the mountainous regions of China and is often found in rocky, gravelly or sandy soils.
