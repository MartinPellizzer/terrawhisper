What's the taxonomical classification of Setaria faberi?

Setaria faberi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Poales and within the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Setaria and the species faberi.

Each spikelet is enclosed by several fine, spreading bristles that give the seed head a fuzzy appearance.
