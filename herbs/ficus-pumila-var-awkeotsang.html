Ficus pumila var. awkeotsang

What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus pumila var. awkeotsang?

Ficus pumila var. awkeotsang belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Moraceae and the genus Ficus, ultimately identifying it as the specific variety Ficus pumila var. awkeotsang.
