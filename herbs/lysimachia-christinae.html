Lysimachia Christinae: What To Know Before Using It For Medicinal Purposes

It has been traditionally employed to treat respiratory conditions such as coughs and bronchitis, as well as for its mild sedative and anti-inflammatory effects. Recent studies suggest that Lysimachia christinae may support immune function and aid in the treatment of skin conditions due to its antioxidant properties.
