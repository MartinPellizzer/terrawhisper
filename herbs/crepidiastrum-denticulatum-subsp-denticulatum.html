Crepidiastrum denticulatum subsp. denticulatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Crepidiastrum denticulatum subsp. denticulatum?

Crepidiastrum denticulatum subsp. denticulatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Asterales and is a representative of the family Asteraceae. Taxonomically, it is categorized under the genus Crepidiastrum and specifically belongs to the species Crepidiastrum denticulatum.
