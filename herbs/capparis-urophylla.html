What's the taxonomical classification of Capparis urophylla?

Capparis urophyllia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Brassicales. The plant is a member of the family Capparaceae, specifically falling within the genus Capparis, with its unique identity defined by its specific epithet, urophylla.
