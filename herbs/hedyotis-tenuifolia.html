What is Hedyotis tenuifolia?

Hedyotis tenuifolia is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Rubiaceae.

The plant's leaves are opposite, elliptical, and 1.5-4 cm long, with a pointed apex and an entire margin. The stem is slender, hairy, and 10-40 cm tall, rooting at the nodes.

Hedyotis tenuifolia is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is found in woodland and grassland habitats.
