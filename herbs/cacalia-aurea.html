Cacalia aurea

Cacalia aurea, also known as golden groundsel, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Cacalia aurea include reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and supporting immune function.

The plant can be prepared in various herbal forms, including infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which can be used to harness its medicinal properties and promote overall well-being.
