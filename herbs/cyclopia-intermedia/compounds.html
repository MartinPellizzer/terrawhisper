Cyclopia (Cyclopia intermedia) Compounds

The primary compounds in Cyclopia intermedia include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and glycosides contribute to its astringent taste and potential health benefits. Terpenoids and essential oils provide aromatic qualities, while saponins and alkaloids may support immune function. Coumarin and anthocyanin add to its medicinal profile with antimicrobial and antioxidant activities.
