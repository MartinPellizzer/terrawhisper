10 Possible Side Effects Of Cyclopia Intermedia (Suba)

Cyclopia intermedia has some side effects when used improperly, such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

These side effects are often caused by consuming excessive amounts or mixing it with other medications.

For instance, a person who relies heavily on the herb for anxiety relief may experience worsened symptoms if they neglect to eat, leading to malnutrition, fatigue, and impaired daily functioning.
