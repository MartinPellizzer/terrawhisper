Rosa spinosissima var. tuschetica

Rosa spinosissima var. tuschetica, also known as the Tusche Rose, is a variety of wild rose with densely thorny stems and pinkish-white flowers.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it effective in treating wounds, skin irritations, and infections.

The health benefits of Rosa spinosissima var. tuschetica include its ability to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial activity, making it a valuable natural remedy for various health issues.

Herbal preparations of the plant can be made into teas, infusions, and salves, allowing for a range of applications in traditional and modern herbal medicine.
