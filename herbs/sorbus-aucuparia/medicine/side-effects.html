10 Possible Side Effects Of Sorbus Aucuparia (Rowan)

These side effects can worsen lives by causing debilitating stomach cramps, diarrhea, and respiratory distress.

Additionally, rowan's anti-inflammatory properties may exacerbate existing conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, making everyday activities like walking and climbing stairs extremely painful.
