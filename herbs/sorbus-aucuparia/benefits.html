10 Best Sorbus Aucuparia Health Benefits

Sorbus aucuparia, commonly known as the rowan tree or mountain ash, offers several health benefits due to its rich content of bioactive compounds.

The berries of the rowan tree are high in antioxidants, including flavonoids and vitamin C, which help protect the body against oxidative stress and support immune function. These antioxidants may also contribute to reducing inflammation and lowering the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer. In traditional medicine, rowan berries have been used to treat ailments like scurvy and digestive issues, although they should be consumed in moderation due to their potential toxicity in large quantities.

Additionally, the tree's leaves and bark contain compounds that have shown antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, further supporting its role in natural health practices.
