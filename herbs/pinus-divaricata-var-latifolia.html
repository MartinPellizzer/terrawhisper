Pinus divaricata var. latifolia

Pinus divaricata var. latifolia, also known as the Japanese white pine, is a variety of evergreen coniferous tree native to Japan.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant activities.

The health benefits of Pinus divaricata var. latifolia include reducing inflammation, improving respiratory issues, and boosting the immune system.

Herbal preparations of the plant are typically made into teas, infusions, or extracts, and are used to treat various ailments, including coughs, colds, and skin conditions.
