Schefflera leucantha

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Schefflera leucantha has large, elliptical to oblong leaves that typically measure between 10 and 25 centimeters in length and feature a glossy, dark green surface with prominent midribs. The leaf margins are entire and smooth, while the texture feels somewhat leathery to the touch. These leaves are arranged alternately along woody, upright stems that can reach significant heights in their natural habitat. The plant is characterized by its dense, bushy foliage which provides a lush appearance. Small, white flowers emerge in terminal clusters, adding a delicate contrast to the heavy greenery.
