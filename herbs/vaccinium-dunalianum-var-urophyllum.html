Vaccinium dunalianum var. urophyllum

What's the taxonomical classification of Vaccinium dunalianum var. urophyllum?

Vaccinium dunalianum var. urophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the order Ericales and falls under the family Ericaceae. Specifically, this plant is a variety of the genus Vaccinium, identified by its full scientific name, Vaccinium dunalianum var. urophyllum.
