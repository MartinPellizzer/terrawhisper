Tagetes (Tagetes erecta) Compounds

The primary compounds in Tagetes erecta include essential oil, flavonoids, phenolic acids, and coumarin. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as antimicrobial and antioxidant effects. Terpenoids, tannins, and saponins also play roles in its therapeutic uses. Glycosides, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further support its traditional applications in herbal medicine.
