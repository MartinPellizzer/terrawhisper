10 Medicinal Constituents Of Arctostaphylos Uva-Ursi (Bearberry)

Arctostaphylos uva-ursi has active constituents such as arbutin, hyperoside, and uvaol.

These constituents possess diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and astringent properties, which can help alleviate urinary tract infections, digestive issues, and skin conditions. For instance, arbutin can inhibit the growth of certain bacteria, while hyperoside can soothe irritated tissues, promoting overall health and well-being.

By utilizing these natural constituents, people can benefit from improved urinary function, reduced inflammation, and enhanced skin resilience.
