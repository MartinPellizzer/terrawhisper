Marantochloa mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Marantochlloa, with its specific classification being the species mannii.
