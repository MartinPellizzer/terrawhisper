10 Possible Side Effects Of Vinca Rosea (Periwinkle)

Vinca rosea has some side effects when used improperly, such as headaches, nausea, and vomiting.

These side effects are often caused by the herb's high alkaloid content, particularly vincristine and vinblastine.

If left untreated, these symptoms can worsen, leading to decreased appetite, fatigue, and difficulty concentrating, ultimately affecting daily activities and relationships.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Vinca rosea if used imporperly.
