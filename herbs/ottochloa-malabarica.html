Ottochloa malabarica

Ottochloa malabarica, commonly known as Indian grass, is a perennial grass species found in the tropical regions of the world.

The therapeutic actions of Ottochloa malabarica include its ability to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and exhibit antimicrobial activity.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of Ottochloa malabarica
