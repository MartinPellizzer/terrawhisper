Morus Alba Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Morus alba, commonly known as the white mulberry, is a deciduous tree native to Asia and widely cultivated for its leaves, fruits, and medicinal properties.

This herb is valued for its rich nutritional profile and traditional use in supporting health and vitality.

The health benefits of Morus alba include improved digestion, enhanced immune function, and potential anti-inflammatory effects.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and polysaccharides.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and bark to harness its medicinal properties.
