What's the taxonomical classification of Premna interrupta?

Premna interrupta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent, it falls into the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Premna and is specifically designated as the species interrupta.
