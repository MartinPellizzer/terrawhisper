Salix longifolia f. opaca

Salix longifolia f. opaca, also known as Willow-leaved Willow or Salix opaca, is a subspecies of the Salix longifolia plant, which belongs to the willow family (Salicaceae).

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, due to the presence of salicin and other compounds.

The health benefits of Salix longifolia f. opaca include relief from pain and inflammation, as well as its potential to reduce fever and improve digestive health.

In traditional herbal medicine, this plant is often prepared in the form of infusions, decoctions, or tinctures, which are used to treat various health conditions.
