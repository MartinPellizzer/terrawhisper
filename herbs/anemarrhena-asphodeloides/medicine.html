What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Anemarrhena Asphodeloides (Rhizoma)?

Anemarrhena asphodeloides, also known as rhizoma, has health benefits such as lowering blood pressure and blood sugar levels, as well as reducing inflammation and fever.

Its medicinal constituents include iridoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its pharmacological effects. In traditional Chinese medicine, Anemarrhena asphodeloides is often used in decoctions or dried powder form.

However, excessive use may lead to nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea, and patients should avoid using it with other diuretics or sedatives.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Anemarrhena asphodeloides.
