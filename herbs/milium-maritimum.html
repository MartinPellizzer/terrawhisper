Milium maritimum

Milium maritimum, also known as sea milkwort, is a perennial plant that grows in coastal areas, particularly in salt marshes and sandy beaches.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which are believed to help soothe skin irritations and wounds.

The plant is also known to have various health benefits, such as reducing swelling and promoting wound healing, making it a valuable resource in natural remedies.

Herbal preparations of Milium maritimum can be made into teas, infusions, or topical creams, and are often used to treat skin conditions, such as eczema and acne.
