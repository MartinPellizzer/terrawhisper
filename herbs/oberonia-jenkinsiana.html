What's the taxonomical classification of Oberonia jenkinsiana?

Oberonia jenkinsiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling within the genus Oberonia, and is specifically identified as the species jenkinsiana.
