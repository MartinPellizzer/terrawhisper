What's the taxonomical classification of Oberonia jenkinsiana?

Oberonia jenkinsiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling within the genus Oberonia, and is specifically identified as the species jenkinsiana.
