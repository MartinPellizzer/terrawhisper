What's the taxonomical classification of Lepisorus waltonii?

Lepisorus waltonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. The plant is situated within the family Polypodiaceae, specifically falling under the genus Lepisorus, with its unique biological identity defined by its specific designation as the species waltonii.
