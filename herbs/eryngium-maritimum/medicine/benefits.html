10 Health Benefits Of Eryngium Maritimum (Sea Holly)

Eryngium maritimum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can aid in alleviating joint pain and arthritis symptoms.

Its anthelmintic properties help eliminate parasites and worms from the body, improving digestion and overall gut health.

By incorporating sea holly into one's regimen, individuals may experience reduced inflammation, improved mobility, and enhanced overall well-being, allowing them to enjoy activities without discomfort or restriction.
