What's the taxonomical classification of Craterispermum laurinum?

Craterispermum laurinum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical descent, it is placed in the order Gentianaales and falls within the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Craterispermum, and its unique species designation, laurinum.
