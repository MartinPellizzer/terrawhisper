What's the taxonomical classification of Lecaniodiscus cupanioides?

Lecaniodiscus cupanioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the taxonomic lineage of the order Sapindales. The species is further nested within the family Sapindaceae, falling under the genus Lecaniodiscus, and is ultimately identified by its specific epithet, cupanioides.
