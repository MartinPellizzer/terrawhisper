Calopogonium mucunoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Calopogonium mucunoides?

Calopogonium mucunoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Calopogonium and the species mucunoides.
