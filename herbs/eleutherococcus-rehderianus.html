What's the taxonomical classification of Eleutherococcus rehderianus?

Eleutherococcus rehderianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated in the order Apiales. It is a member of the family Araliaceae, falling under the genus Eleutherococcus, and is specifically identified by the species epithet rehderianus.
