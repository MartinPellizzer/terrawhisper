Rosa graveolens f. cheriensis

Rosa graveolens f. cheriensis, also known as the Rosa Damascena, is a fragrant and delicate species of rose plant native to the Middle East.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its high concentration of essential oils, including geraniol, linalool, and citronellol, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Rosa graveolens f. cheriensis include reducing stress and anxiety, promoting relaxation, and improving sleep quality, as well as its use in treating digestive issues and skin conditions.
