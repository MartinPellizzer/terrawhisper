Salvia Miltiorrhiza Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as tanshinone, salvianolic acid, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, and is often used to support heart health and improve circulation.
