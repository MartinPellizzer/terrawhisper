Possible Side Effects of salvia miltiorrhiza

Salvia miltiorrhiza can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Additionally, high doses of salvia miltiorrhiza can lead to interactions with other medications, potentially causing cardiovascular problems and changes in blood pressure.

If not used under the guidance of a healthcare professional, salvia miltiorrhiza may also cause liver damage and other serious health complications.
