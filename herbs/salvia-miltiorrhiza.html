What to know about Salvia miltiorrhiza before using it medicinally

Salvia miltiorrhiza, commonly known as red sage, is a herb that has been extensively used in traditional medicine to treat cardiovascular diseases, such as high blood pressure and coronary heart disease.

Its main medicinal uses also include the treatment of cerebrovascular diseases, such as stroke and dementia. The herb has been found to have several health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, anti-oxidative, and anti-apoptotic effects. The main therapeutic properties of Salvia miltiorrhiza include its ability to improve blood circulation, reduce blood pressure, and prevent platelet aggregation.

The herb contains bioactive compounds such as tanshinones, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties, and is often prepared into a traditional Chinese medicine called Danshen tea.
