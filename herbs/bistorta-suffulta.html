What is Bistorta suffulta?

Bistorta suffulta is an herbaceous perennial plant, belonging to the Polygonaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It grows 20-60 cm tall, with rhizomatous stems and elliptical-lanceolate leaves 3-10 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, having 5-7 pairs of veins and a serrated margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in a corymb, with 5 white or pink petals, 5-8 mm in diameter, and 5 stamens. The fruit is an achene, 2-3 mm long and 1-2 mm wide, with 10-20 seeds.
