What's the taxonomical classification of Urceola linearicarpa?

Urceola linearicarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is categorized under the order Gentianales and the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Urceola, and its specific epithet, linearicarpa.

      What are the herbal preparations of Urceola linearicarpa?

      Urceola linearicarpa is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical poultice depending on the specific medicinal application required. The leaves and stems are typically boiled in water to create a strong liquid extract used for internal consumption.
