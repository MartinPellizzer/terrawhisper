Michelia baillonii

What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia baillonii?

Michelia baillonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is placed in the order Magnoliales and belongs to the family Magnoliaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Michelia, and its unique species designation, baillonii.
