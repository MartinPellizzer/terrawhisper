What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Michelia baillonii has elliptic to oblong leaves that are coriaceous in texture, featuring entire margins and an acute apex. The petals are often creamy white or pale yellow, contributing to a distinct floral profile. This species exhibits a robust growth habit characteristic of many members within the Magnoliaceae family.
