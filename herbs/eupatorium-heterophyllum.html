What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eupatorium heterophyllum has leaves that vary significantly in shape and size along its stem, a characteristic known as heterophylly. This morphological diversity allows the plant to adapt to changing light levels and water availability within its environment. The stems are sturdy enough to support the weight of these varying foliage types as they grow.
