Viola erythraea

Viola erythraea, also known as the Red Pansy or Alpine Pansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the violet family (Violaceae).

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Viola erythraea include its ability to treat digestive issues, skin problems, and respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs.
