Gesneria vernicosa

Gesneria vernicosa, commonly known as the "Varnished Leaf Plant" or "Pig's Ear", is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

The therapeutic actions of Gesneria vernicosa are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include alkaloids and flavonoids, that possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Although more research is needed to confirm its efficacy, Gesneria vernicosa has been used in traditional medicine to promote wound healing and relieve symptoms of fever and respiratory infections.
