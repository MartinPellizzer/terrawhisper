Linum alpinum f. laeve

Linum alpinum f. laeve, also known as alpine flax or smooth-flowered meadow flax, is a flowering plant belonging to the Linaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can aid in wound healing and reduce inflammation.

The plant is rich in mucilage, which can help soothe digestive issues and provide relief from coughs and colds, making it a valuable remedy for various health benefits, including digestive health and respiratory issues.

Linum alpinum f. laeve can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is often used in herbal medicine to promote overall health and well-being.
