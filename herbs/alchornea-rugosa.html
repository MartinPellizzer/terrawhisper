Alchornea rugosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Alchornea rugosa?

Alchornea rugosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Malpighiales. The plant is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae, falling under the genus Alchornea, and is specifically identified by its species name, rugosa.
