What's the taxonomical classification of Diploclisia affinis?

Diploclisia affinis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the taxonomic order Ranunculales. Further classification places this plant in the family Menispermaceae, specifically within the genus Diploclisia, which ultimately identifies it as the species affinis.
