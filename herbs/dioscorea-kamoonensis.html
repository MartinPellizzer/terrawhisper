Dioscorea kamoonensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Dioscoreaceae and is categorized within the genus Dioscorea, ultimately reaching its specific classification as the species kamoonensis.
