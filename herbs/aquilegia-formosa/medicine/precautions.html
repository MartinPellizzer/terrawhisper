10 Precautions To Take When Using Aquilegia Formosa (Columbine)

1. Use fresh or dried only

When using Aquilegia formosa medicinally, it's important to take in controlled doses due to its potential toxicity.

Consuming excessive amounts can lead to nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain. Additionally, the plant contains anthraquinone glycosides, which can cause gastrointestinal irritation if not properly prepared.

To avoid adverse effects, it is crucial to follow established dosing guidelines and consult with a qualified healthcare practitioner before using Aquilegia formosa medicinally.

Regular monitoring of blood pressure helps prevent unexpected dips and ensures safe treatment outcomes.
