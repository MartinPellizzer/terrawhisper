10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aquilegia Formosa (Columbine)

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiseptic properties, which can soothe skin irritations, reduce fever, and protect against infections.

Aquilegia formosa rutin is a bioflavonoid compound found in its flowers.
