10 Possible Side Effects Of Melaleuca Leucadendron (Cajuput)

Melaleuca leucadendron has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory problems.

These side effects are often caused by the essential oils being too concentrated, exceeding the recommended dosages, or applying it to sensitive areas.

In severe cases, these reactions can lead to life-threatening anaphylaxis, organ damage, or even long-term skin scarring.
