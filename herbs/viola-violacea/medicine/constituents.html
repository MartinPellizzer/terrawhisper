10 Medicinal Constituents Of Viola Violacea (Heartsease)

Viola violacea has active constituents such as iridoids, flavonoids, and anthocyanins.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The iridoids, in particular, have been shown to reduce anxiety and stress, while the flavonoids have been found to improve cardiovascular health.

The anthocyanins, responsible for the plant's purple color, have been demonstrated to have anti-inflammatory effects, which can lead to improved symptoms for people suffering from conditions such as arthritis.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Viola violacea.

2. Flavonoids

Viola violacea flavonoids is a type of bioactive compound extracted from the plant's flowers and leaves.

Research has shown that Viola violacea flavonoids can help protect against oxidative stress, cancer, and cardiovascular disease, making them a promising area of study in the development of novel therapeutic agents.

Viola violacea phenolic acids is a type of bioactive compound found in its flowers and leaves.

9. Kaempferol

Viola violacea kaempferol is a flavonoid compound found in high concentrations within its leaves and flowers.
