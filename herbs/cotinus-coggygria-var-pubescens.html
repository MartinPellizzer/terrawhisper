Cotinus coggygria var. pubescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Cotinus coggygria var. pubescens?

Cotinus coggygriia var. pube sceas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Sapindales. The plant is a member of the family Anacardiaceae and falls under the genus Cotinus, specifically identified by its varietal designation, Cotinus coggygriia var. pube sceas.
