California Yerba Santa (Eriodictyon californicum) Compounds

California Yerba Santa contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and essential oils that contribute to its aromatic profile. Coumarin and phenolic acids provide anti-inflammatory effects, while terpenoids and tannins support its traditional use in respiratory support. Saponins, glycosides, and alkaloids also play roles in its medicinal properties, and anthocyanins contribute to its vibrant color and potential health benefits.
