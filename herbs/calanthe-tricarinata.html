Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific classification within the genus Calanthe, with its unique designation being the species tricarinaata.
