Rosa rubiginosa subsp. elliptica

Rosa rubiginosa subsp.

elliptica, also known as the dwarf sweet briar, is a subspecies of the fragrant rose species, Rosa rubiginosa.

This plant is rich in flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Rosa rubiginosa subsp.

elliptica has been used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, diarrhea, and menstrual cramps, due to its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, making it a versatile remedy in traditional medicine.
