What's the taxonomical classification of Caryopteris forrestii var. minor?

Caryopteris forrestii var. minor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales.
