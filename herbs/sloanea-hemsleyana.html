Sloanea hemsleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its lineage further, it is placed in the order Oxalidales and the family Elaeocarpaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Sloanea and its specific species designation, hemsleyana.
