Pygeum (Pygeum africanum) Uses

Pygeum africanum is used to treat benign prostatic hyperplasia, urinary tract infections, and kidney stones. It also supports kidney health in chronic kidney disease and reduces inflammation in joints and muscles, helping with conditions like osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.

Pygeum (Pygeum africanum) Monograph

1. Benign prostatic hyperplasia

Pygeum africanum has been traditionally used to support urinary health, particularly in managing symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia. Its active compounds, including sterols and tannins, may help reduce prostate size and improve urine flow. Clinical studies suggest it can alleviate frequent urination and nighttime voiding, offering a natural alternative for prostate support.

2. Urinary tract infections

Pygeum africanum has been traditionally used to support urinary tract health. It contains compounds that may reduce inflammation and improve bladder function. Studies suggest it can help alleviate symptoms of urinary tract infections by promoting diuresis and reducing bacterial adherence. It is often used as a complementary therapy in herbal medicine for urinary disorders.

3. Kidney stones

Pygeum africanum may support kidney stone prevention by reducing urinary calcium levels and promoting diuresis. Its compounds help dissolve calcium oxalate stones and prevent new formations, making it a valuable herb in urinary tract health.

4. Bladder infections

Pygeum africanum has been traditionally used to support urinary health. It contains compounds that may reduce inflammation and bacterial growth in the urinary tract. This makes it a valuable herb for managing symptoms of bladder infections. Its diuretic properties also help flush out harmful bacteria from the system.

5. Chronic kidney disease

Pygeum africanum has been studied for its potential benefits in managing chronic kidney disease. It contains compounds that may support kidney function by reducing inflammation and oxidative stress. Some research suggests it could help in slowing the progression of kidney damage. However, more clinical trials are needed to confirm its efficacy in this context.

6. Osteoarthritis

Pygeum africanum is used in herbal medicine to support joint health, particularly in managing osteoarthritis. It contains compounds that may reduce inflammation and improve joint mobility. Studies suggest it can alleviate pain and stiffness associated with the condition. Its traditional use aligns with its modern application in supportive care for arthritis.

7. Joint pain

Pygeum africanum is traditionally used to alleviate joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and stiffness in joints, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for conditions like arthritis. Its active compounds support joint health by improving circulation and reducing oxidative stress.

8. Rheumatoid arthritis

Pygeum africanum has been traditionally used to support joint health and reduce inflammation associated with rheumatoid arthritis. Its active compounds, including triterpenes and flavonoids, may help alleviate pain and stiffness by modulating inflammatory responses. It is often used as a complementary therapy to support conventional treatments.

9. Muscle inflammation

Pygeum africanum is used to reduce muscle inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps alleviate pain and swelling associated with conditions like arthritis and muscle strains. The herb supports joint health and may improve mobility in inflammatory disorders. Its active compounds work to modulate inflammatory responses in the body.

10. Joint inflammation

Pygeum africanum is used to reduce joint inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps alleviate symptoms like swelling and pain associated with conditions such as arthritis. The active compounds in Pygeum support cartilage health and reduce oxidative stress in joints. It is often recommended as a complementary therapy for inflammatory joint disorders.
