10 Best Pygeum Africanum Health Benefits

Pygeum africanum, also known as African wild almond, is a tree native to Africa and has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

It is particularly renowned for its potential role in supporting prostate health, with studies suggesting it may help reduce symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) by inhibiting the enzyme 5-alpha reductase. The bark of the tree contains bioactive compounds such as triterpenes and flavonoids, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. These properties may also support cardiovascular health by improving blood flow and reducing oxidative stress.

Additionally, Pygeum africanum has been studied for its potential to enhance urinary function and reduce fluid retention, making it a valuable natural remedy for men's health.

1. Reduces prostate size

Pygeum africanum reduces prostate size by inhibiting the conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a hormone that contributes to prostate enlargement.

This natural effect helps alleviate symptoms associated with benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), such as frequent urination and urinary retention. Clinical studies have shown that regular intake of Pygeum africanum can lead to a significant reduction in prostate volume over time. Its active compounds, including triterpenes and phytosterols, play a key role in this process by reducing inflammation and promoting hormonal balance.

As a result, Pygeum africanum is increasingly recognized as a complementary therapy for managing prostate-related health issues.

Regular use of Pygeum africanum may contribute to better urinary control and overall prostate health.
