What's the taxonomical classification of Toxicodendron delavayi var. delavayi?

Toxicodendron delavayi var. delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the order Sapindales. It is a member of the family Anacardiaceae and is specifically situated within the genus Toxicodendron.
