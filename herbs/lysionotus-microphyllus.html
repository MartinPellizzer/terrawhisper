Lysionotus microphyllus

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysionotus microphyllus?

Lysionotus microphyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Lysionotus, and its unique species designation, microphyllus.
