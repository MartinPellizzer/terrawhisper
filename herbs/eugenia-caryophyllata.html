Eugenia Caryophyllata: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Eugenia caryophyllata, commonly known as clove, is a spice derived from the dried flower buds of the clove tree.

It is rich in bioactive constituents such as eugenol, which contributes to its strong aromatic properties.

The herb offers various health benefits, including antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, and is often used to alleviate pain and reduce dental issues.

Its therapeutic actions include promoting oral health, reducing nausea, and supporting digestive wellness.
