Candle Bush (Cassia alata) Actions

Candle Bush exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and tonic properties. It also acts as a stimulant and carminative, aiding digestion and relieving gas. Its astringent and expectorant qualities help with respiratory issues, while antispasmodic and sedative effects provide relief from muscle spasms and anxiety.
