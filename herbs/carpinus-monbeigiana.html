What's the taxonomical classification of Carpinus monbeigiana?

Carpinus monbeigiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fagales. This plant is a member of the family Betulaceae, which is further narrowed down to the genus Carpinus, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species monbeigiana.
