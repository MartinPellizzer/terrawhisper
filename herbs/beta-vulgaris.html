Beta Vulgaris Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Beta vulgaris, commonly known as beetroot, is a biennial plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family, widely cultivated for its edible roots and leaves.

This herb is rich in nutrients and bioactive compounds, offering various health benefits such as supporting cardiovascular health, improving digestion, and enhancing detoxification processes.

The therapeutic actions of Beta vulgaris are attributed to its constituents like betalains, nitrates, fiber, and antioxidants.

It can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or juice, and is also used in culinary applications to support overall well-being.
