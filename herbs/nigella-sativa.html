Possible Side Effects of nigella sativa

Nigella sativa can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its strong emetic properties.

Improper use of nigella sativa may also cause interactions with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse effects.

Furthermore, high doses of nigella sativa may lower blood pressure, which can be problematic for individuals with hypotension or those taking medications for hypertension.
