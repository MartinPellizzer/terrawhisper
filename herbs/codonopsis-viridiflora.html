Codonopsis viridiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis viridiflora?

Codonopsis viridiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Campanulaceae, falling under the genus Codonoopsis, with its specific identification as the species viridiflora.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Codonopsis viridiflora has long, lanceolate leaves that grow in a basal rosette and produce upright, branched inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers.

These leaves are somewhat fleshy in texture and can reach significant lengths depending on the maturity of the plant. The flowering stalks emerge from the center of the rosette to display multiple small blooms arranged in a loose panicle.
