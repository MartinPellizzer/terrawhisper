Codonopsis viridiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis viridiflora?

Codonopsis viridiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Campanulaceae, falling under the genus Codonoopsis, with its specific identification as the species viridiflora.
