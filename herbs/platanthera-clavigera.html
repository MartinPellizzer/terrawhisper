Platanthera clavigera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific placement within the genus Platanthera and is identified by the species name clavigera.
