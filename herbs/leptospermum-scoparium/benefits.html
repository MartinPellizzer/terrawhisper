10 Best Leptospermum Scoparium Health Benefits

Manuka honey, derived from the nectar of this plant, is known for its ability to promote wound healing and combat antibiotic-resistant infections. Additionally, the plant itself contains antioxidants and anti-inflammatory agents that support immune function and overall well-being.

Its use in traditional Māori medicine highlights its historical significance and continued relevance in modern holistic health practices.
