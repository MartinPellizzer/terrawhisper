New Zealand Tea Tree (Leptospermum scoparium) Actions

New Zealand Tea Tree exhibits several therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and astringent properties. It also acts as an expectorant, tonic, and carminative. Its bitter and stimulant qualities support digestion, while its sedative and antispasmodic effects help calm the nervous system and relieve muscle spasms.
