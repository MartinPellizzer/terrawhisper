Possible Side Effects of aegle marmelos

Aegle marmelos can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its bitter and astringent properties.

Ingesting large amounts of the herb can also lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and swelling.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption of Aegle marmelos may cause kidney damage and liver dysfunction, especially in individuals with pre-existing liver or kidney conditions.

Additionally, interacting with other medications can exacerbate these side effects, highlighting the importance of consulting a healthcare professional before using this herb.
