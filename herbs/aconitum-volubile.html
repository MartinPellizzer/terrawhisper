What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum volubile?

Aconitum volubile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Ranunculales. The plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, which is further narrowed down to the genus Aconitum, with its specific classification being the species volubile.
