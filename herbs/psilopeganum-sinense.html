Psilopeganum sinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the taxonomic lineage of the order Sapindales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Rutaceae. Finally, its specific biological identification is defined by the genus Psilopeganum and the species sinese.
