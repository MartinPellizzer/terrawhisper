Garden Horseradish (Armoracia armoracia) Compounds

The primary compounds in Garden Horseradish include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins contribute to astringency, while coumarin has antispasmodic qualities. Saponins and glycosides support digestive health, and essential oils provide antimicrobial benefits. Terpenoids, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic potential.
