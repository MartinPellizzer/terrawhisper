Orthosiphon marmoritis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Lamiaceae, which contains the genus Orthosiphon. Finally, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, marmoritis.
