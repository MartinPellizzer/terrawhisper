Eurya brevistyla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Eurya, culminating in its unique designation as the species brevistyla.
