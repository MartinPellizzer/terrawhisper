Crataegus songarica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its higher taxonomic ranks, the plant is ordered within the Rosales and falls under the family Rosaecae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Crataegus and the species songarica.
