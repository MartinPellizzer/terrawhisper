Crepis blavii

Crepis blavii, also known as Blue Lettuce or Crepis, is a perennial herb that belongs to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can be attributed to the presence of flavonoids and sesquiterpenes.

The health benefits of Crepis blavii include its ability to soothe digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and act as an antiseptic.

Crepis blavii can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in tinctures and salves, to harness its medicinal properties.
