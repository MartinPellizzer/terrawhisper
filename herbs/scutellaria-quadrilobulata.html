Scutellaria quadrilobulata

What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria quadrilobulata?

Scutelariia quadrilobulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Lamiales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Lamiaceae, where it is placed in the genus Scutellaria, and it is specifically identified by the species epithet quadrilobulata.
