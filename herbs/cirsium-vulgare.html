From a botanical standpoint, thistle's purple flowers are adorned with spiny leaves and stems, providing a striking visual display in late summer.

With ancient references dating back to Greek mythology, where the thistle was said to have been used as a symbol of courage and resilience, it's no wonder this remarkable herb has stood the test of time.
