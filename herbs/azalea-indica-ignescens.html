Possible Side Effects of Azalea indica ignescens

Azalea indica ignescens can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing nausea, dizziness, and allergic reactions in some individuals.

Furthermore, improper use of the plant can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, exacerbating pre-existing health conditions.
