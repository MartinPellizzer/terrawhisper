Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) Compounds

The primary compounds of Yellow Sophora include alkaloids, which have various pharmacological effects, flavonoids with antioxidant properties, and tannins that contribute to its astringent qualities. Coumarin and phenolic acids provide additional therapeutic benefits, while glycosides, saponins, and terpenoids support its traditional use in herbal medicine. Essential oils and anthocyanins further enhance its medicinal profile.

Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) Monograph

1. Alkaloid

The alkaloid obacine is found in Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) and contributes to its traditional use in treating parasitic infections. It exhibits antiparasitic properties by disrupting the nervous system of parasites, making it a key component in herbal formulations for such conditions.
