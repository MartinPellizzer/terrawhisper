10 Possible Side Effects Of Sophora Flavescens (Ku Shen)

Sophora flavescens has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, skin rashes, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption or sensitivity to the herb.

In severe cases, they can lead to anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that worsens daily life by causing respiratory distress, cardiovascular issues, and even organ failure.
