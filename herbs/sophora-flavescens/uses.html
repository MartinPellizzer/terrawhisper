Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) Uses

Yellow Sophora is used to treat various skin conditions such as infections, inflammation, allergies, and fungal issues. It helps with mouth ulcers, psoriasis, and wound healing. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties make it effective for skin irritation and infectious dermatitis.

Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) Monograph

1. Skin infections

Yellow Sophora is used to treat skin infections due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce inflammation and combat bacterial growth on the skin. Traditionally applied as a poultice or decoction, it is effective for conditions like eczema and boils. Its active compounds support wound healing and prevent secondary infections.

2. Inflammatory skin conditions

Yellow Sophora is used to treat inflammatory skin conditions such as eczema and psoriasis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and irritation, promoting skin healing. Its active compounds, including flavonoids and alkaloids, modulate immune responses and inhibit inflammatory pathways.

3. Mouth ulcers

Sophora flavescens is used to treat mouth ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce pain and inflammation while promoting healing. The plant's active compounds, such as matrine and oxymatrine, inhibit harmful bacteria and soothe oral tissues. It is often prepared as a decoction or topical application for quick relief.

4. Psoriasis

Yellow Sophora is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat psoriasis due to its anti-inflammatory and detoxifying properties. It helps reduce skin inflammation and clear internal heat, which are believed to contribute to psoriasis flare-ups. The active compounds in Yellow Sophora support liver function and immune balance, aiding in managing the condition.

5. Skin irritation

Yellow Sophora is used to treat skin irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and inflammation associated with conditions like eczema and dermatitis. The herb is often prepared as a topical ointment or poultice for localized application. Its active compounds promote skin healing and soothe irritated tissues.

6. Skin allergies

Yellow Sophora is used to treat skin allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and swelling associated with allergic reactions. The active compounds in the plant modulate immune responses, making it effective for conditions like eczema and hives. It is often applied topically or taken internally as a herbal remedy.

7. Wound healing

Yellow Sophora is used in traditional medicine to promote wound healing due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce infection risk and accelerates tissue repair. The active compounds in its root and seed aid in skin regeneration and preventing scarring. It is commonly applied topically as a poultice or in herbal formulations.

8. Skin allergy

Yellow Sophora is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat skin allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and swelling associated with allergic reactions. The active compounds in the plant modulate immune responses, making it an effective remedy for eczema and other skin hypersensitivity conditions.

9. Fungal infection

Yellow Sophora is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat fungal infections due to its antimicrobial compounds, particularly sophorae flavescens extract, which inhibits fungal growth. It is often applied topically for conditions like athlete's foot and ringworm, and may be used internally in formulations to support systemic fungal management.

10. Infectious dermatitis

Yellow Sophora is used to treat infectious dermatitis due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce skin infections and inflammation caused by bacterial or fungal pathogens. The herb is often prepared as a decoction or topical application for localized skin conditions. It is valued for its effectiveness in clearing up redness, swelling, and pus in affected areas.
