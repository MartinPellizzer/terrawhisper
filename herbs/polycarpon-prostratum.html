What's the taxonomical classification of Polycarpon prostratum?

Polycarpon prostratum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Caryophyllales and the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Polycarpon, and its unique species designation, prostratum.
