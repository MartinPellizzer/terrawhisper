Ranunculus japonicus var. japonicus

What's the taxonomical classification of Ranunculus japonicus var. japonicus?

Ranunculus japonicus var. japonicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Ranunculales. Further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and the genus Ranunculus, specifically identified as the variety Ranunculus japonicus var. japonicus.
