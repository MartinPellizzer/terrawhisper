Medicinal Parts of sarracenia purpurea

Sarracenia purpurea, commonly known as the purple pitcher plant, contains various parts that have been traditionally used for medicinal purposes.

Herbal Preparations of sarracenia purpurea

Sarracenia purpurea teas are commonly prepared by steeping the dried leaves in hot water, and they are often used for their purported diuretic and detoxifying properties, though scientific evidence supporting these claims is limited.

Sarracenia purpurea tinctures, made by soaking the plant in alcohol, are valued for their ability to preserve active compounds and are often used in tincture form for internal and external applications in alternative medicine.
