Possible Side Effects of Psyllium sempervirens

Psyllium sempervirens can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal discomfort, nausea, and vomiting, particularly if taken in large doses or without adequate water intake.

Furthermore, psyllium can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, which may worsen underlying health conditions.
