Psyllium sempervirens

Psyllium sempervirens, also known as Indian senna or country mallow, is a plant species native to various parts of the world, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, which include its antidiarrheal, anti-inflammatory, and laxative effects, making it a valuable treatment for digestive issues such as constipation and diarrhea.

The health benefits of Psyllium sempervirens include its ability to lower cholesterol levels, regulate blood sugar levels, and support healthy gut bacteria.

The plant is often prepared as a herbal tea, capsule, or powder, and is also used in traditional medicine as an infusion or decoction.
