Radish (Raphanus sativus) Actions

Radish exhibits diuretic properties, helping to increase urine production. It also acts as an expectorant, aiding in the removal of mucus from the respiratory tract. Additionally, it has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects, reducing inflammation and muscle spasms. As a carminative, it helps relieve gas and bloating, while its tonic and stimulant qualities support overall vitality.
