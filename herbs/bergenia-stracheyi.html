What is Bergenia stracheyi?

Bergenia stracheyi is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Saxifragaceae.

It is characterized by its large, ovate to heart-shaped leaves, 10-30 cm in length, with a crenate margin and a petiole 5-10 cm long. The leaves are glabrous, dark green on the upper surface, and pale green on the lower surface. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers in racemes, 20-50 cm tall, in late spring, with six petals and six stamens.

Bergenia stracheyi grows to 30-60 cm in height and 30-60 cm in width, with a rhizomatous habit.
