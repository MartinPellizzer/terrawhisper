Anthyllis gandogeri

Anthyllis gandogeri is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, belonging to the legume family.

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, the plant is valued for its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties, which can be attributed to its high concentration of flavonoids and saponins.

The health benefits of Anthyllis gandogeri include soothing digestive issues, reducing fever, and alleviating respiratory problems, making it a popular remedy for colds and flu.

Anthyllis gandogeri can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal effects.
