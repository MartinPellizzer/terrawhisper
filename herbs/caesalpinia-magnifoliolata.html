      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Caesalpinia magnifoliolata can cause skin irritation, gastrointestinal distress, and potential allergic reactions if ingested or applied topically without proper processing. There is currently a lack of comprehensive human clinical trials to establish safe dosage levels or long-term toxicity profiles.
