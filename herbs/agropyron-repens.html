Agropyron Repens: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Agropyron repens, commonly known as quackgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe, Asia, and North America, often found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields.

This herb is known for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and wound-healing benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Agropyron repens include reducing inflammation, promoting tissue repair, and supporting digestive health.

Bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins contribute to its antioxidant and antimicrobial effects.
