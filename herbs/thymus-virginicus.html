Thymus Virginicus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Thymus virginicus, commonly known as narrow-leafed thyme, is a small shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, which contribute to its use in supporting respiratory and immune health.

The bioactive constituents in Thymus virginicus include thymol, carvacrol, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions.
