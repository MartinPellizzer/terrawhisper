Polygonum chinense var. paradoxum

What's the taxonomical classification of Polygonum chinense var. paradoxum?

Polygonum chinense var. paradoxum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Caryophyllales. The plant further falls into the family Polygonaceae and is situated within the genus Polygonum. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is identified as the species Polygonum chinense var. paradoxum.
