10 Medicinal Constituents Of Vinca Major (Periwinkle)

Vinca major has active constituents such as alkaloids like vinblastine and vincristine, which possess significant medicinal properties.

These alkaloids exhibit antitumor, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory activities, making Vinca major a valuable resource in traditional medicine. For instance, the plant's extracts have been used to treat various conditions, including cancer, arthritis, and wounds.

The constituents of Vinca major can improve people's lives by providing an effective treatment option for serious illnesses, reducing pain and inflammation, and promoting faster wound healing.
