10 Health Benefits Of Vinca Major (Periwinkle)

Vinca major, also known as periwinkle, has health benefits such as treating various conditions including anxiety, insomnia, and epilepsy.

The plant contains vincamine, vinpocetine, and alkaloids that have a calming effect on the nervous system. These properties can improve cognitive function, reduce anxiety and stress, and promote relaxation, allowing individuals to get better sleep and live more productive lives.

Its medicinal benefits have also been used to treat depression and memory disorders.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Vinca major.

1. Reduces inflammation

Vinca major reduces inflammation because it contains a number of bioactive compounds, including rosmarinic acid and flavonoids.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities, which can help to reduce the production of pro-inflammatory molecules and alleviate symptoms associated with inflammation.

Additionally, the alkaloids present in Vinca major may also contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects by inhibiting the activity of enzymes involved in the inflammatory response.

2. Alleviates pain

Vinca major alleviates pain because it contains a number of bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and phenolic acids, which have been shown to possess analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's leaves and stems contain a high concentration of these compounds, which are believed to work by blocking the production of pain-causing chemicals in the body and reducing inflammation.

3. Slows tumor growth

Vinca major slows tumor growth because of its unique chemical composition.

The plant contains a group of alkaloids called vinblastine and vincristine, which have been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells by interfering with the formation of microtubules, essential structures for cell division.

This disruption prevents the rapid proliferation of cancer cells, ultimately slowing down tumor growth and progression.

4. Inhibits cancer cell proliferation

Vinca major inhibits cancer cell proliferation because its alkaloids, particularly vinblastine and vincristine, bind to tubulin in microtubules, disrupting their dynamics and preventing the formation of a stable spindle apparatus during mitosis.

This leads to apoptosis or cell cycle arrest, ultimately resulting in inhibition of cancer cell growth and proliferation.

This mechanism has been exploited in chemotherapy to treat various types of cancer, including lymphoma and breast cancer.

5. Enhances immune function

Vinca major enhances immune function because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's bioactive compounds, such as rosmarinic acid and flavonoids, have been shown to modulate the immune response by increasing the production of cytokines, activating natural killer cells, and enhancing phagocytic activity.

This helps to protect against oxidative stress, infections, and chronic diseases, ultimately promoting overall immune health and resilience.

6. Improves cardiovascular health

Vinca major improves cardiovascular health because of its potent flavonoids and alkaloids.

The antioxidants in this plant have been shown to help lower blood pressure, improve circulation, and reduce inflammation, all of which contribute to a reduced risk of heart disease.

Additionally, the active compounds in Vinca major may help to relax blood vessels, improving overall cardiovascular function.

7. Regulates blood sugar levels

Vinca major regulates blood sugar levels because of its unique composition of bioactive compounds, particularly alkaloids.

These compounds have been shown to possess potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to improve insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake in the body. This, in turn, reduces blood sugar levels and helps to manage type 2 diabetes.

Additionally, Vinca major's alkaloids have been found to inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in carbohydrate metabolism, further contributing to its hypoglycemic effects.

8. Promotes respiratory health

Vinca major promotes respiratory health because of its potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The leaves and stems of this plant contain vinblastine and vincristine, which have been shown to reduce inflammation in the lungs and airways, alleviating symptoms such as bronchitis, asthma, and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).

Additionally, periwinkle's antimicrobial properties help combat respiratory infections, further supporting its role in maintaining overall respiratory health.

9. Supports digestive system

Vinca major supports digestive system because of its unique composition of alkaloids and saponins.

These compounds have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and reduce inflammation in the gut.

Additionally, Vinca major's saponins may also improve digestion by increasing the production of bile and pancreatic enzymes, promoting healthy absorption of nutrients.

10. Eases menstrual cramps

Vinca major eases menstrual cramps because of its unique combination of flavonoids and alkaloids.

The flavonoids in periwinkle have anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause uterine contractions and painful cramps. Additionally, the alkaloids in periwinkle relax smooth muscle cells, further alleviating cramping and discomfort.

This natural remedy has been traditionally used to soothe menstrual cramp symptoms and promote overall reproductive health.
