Smooth Yam (Dioscorea Convolvulacea Var. Glabra)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Dioscorea convolvulacea var. glabra.

Smooth yam, scientifically known as Dioscorea convolvulacea var. glabra, is a medicinal herb widely recognized for its adaptogenic properties and nutritional value. It is traditionally used in African and Asian cultures for its ability to support energy levels, enhance immune function, and promote overall vitality. The primary benefits of Smooth yam include its high content of starch, potassium, and various bioactive compounds that aid in digestion, reduce inflammation, and support metabolic health. In modern wellness practices, it is increasingly incorporated into dietary supplements and functional foods to boost stamina and manage stress. One of its unique features is its distinct earthy flavor and the presence of diosgenin, a rare active compound with potential hormonal and therapeutic applications.
