Possible Side Effects of galega officinalis

Galega officinalis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high content of glycosides and alkaloids.

Improper use of the herb can also lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and respiratory issues like bronchospasm.

Additionally, high doses of galega officinalis can cause cardiovascular problems, including hypertension and tachycardia, as well as interact with medications like blood thinners and diabetes medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using galega officinalis to avoid these potential side effects and ensure safe and effective use.
