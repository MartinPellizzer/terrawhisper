Rehmannia glutinosa, commonly known as rehmannia, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb used to treat various health conditions, including anemia, fatigue, and kidney diseases.

The main health benefits of rehmannia include its ability to promote blood circulation, reduce inflammation, and improve immune function. The herb's main therapeutic properties are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory activities. In traditional Chinese medicine, rehmannia is often prepared as a decoction or powder and used to make herbal formulations such as Huang Qi Jian Zhong Tang, a classic formula used to treat anemia and fatigue.
