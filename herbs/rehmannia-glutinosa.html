Rehmannia glutinosa

Rehmannia glutinosa, commonly known as Chinese foxglove or di Huang, is a perennial herb native to East Asia, belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

The therapeutic actions of Rehmannia glutinosa include tonifying the liver and kidney, nourishing the blood, and soothing the heart, making it a popular ingredient in traditional Chinese medicine.
