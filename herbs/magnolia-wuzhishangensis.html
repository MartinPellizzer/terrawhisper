What is Magnolia wuzhishangensis?

Magnolia wuzhishangensis is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae.

It is an evergreen tree, typically growing to 10-15 meters in height, with a trunk diameter of up to 30 centimeters. The leaves are elliptical, 8-20 centimeters long, and 2-4 centimeters wide, with a leathery texture and a crenate margin. The flowers are solitary, 20-30 centimeters in diameter, with 6-9 white petals and a yellow center.

The fruit is a red, oval-shaped capsule, 10-15 centimeters long, containing 6-12 seeds.
