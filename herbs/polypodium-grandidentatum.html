Polypodium Grandidentatum

Polypodium grandidentatum, commonly known as the giant shield fern, has been traditionally used in various medicinal practices for its potential therapeutic properties.

It contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and polyphenols, which may contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. In traditional medicine, it has been employed to treat ailments such as respiratory infections, skin conditions, and digestive issues. Modern research suggests that extracts from this fern may support immune function and have antimicrobial properties.

However, further scientific studies are needed to fully validate its medicinal applications and ensure its safe and effective use.
