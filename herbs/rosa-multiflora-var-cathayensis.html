Rosa multiflora var. cathayensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Rosa multiflora var. cathayensis?

Rosa multiflora var. cathayensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is a representative of the family Rosaceae. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Rosa, specifically identified by the botanical designation of the species Rosa multiflora var. cathayensis.
