      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Ormosia balansae can cause severe poisoning through the ingestion of its seeds, which contain toxic alkaloids known as toxalbumins. These chemical compounds act similarly to ricin by inhibiting protein synthesis within human cells. Once consumed, the toxins can lead to gastrointestinal distress, including intense vomiting and abdominal pain. In more extreme cases, the systemic toxicity may result in organ failure or death. Because of these potent effects, the seeds should never be handled or eaten by humans or animals.
