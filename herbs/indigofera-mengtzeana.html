What's the taxonomical classification of Indigofera mengtzeana?

Indigofera mengtzeana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Indigofera and is specifically distinguished as the species mengtzeana.
