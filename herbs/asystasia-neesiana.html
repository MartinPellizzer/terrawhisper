Asystasia neesiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Asystasia neesiana?

Asystasia neesiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Acanthaceae. Ultimately, its specific classification is defined by the genus Asystaisia and the species neesiaana.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Asystasia neeisiaana has spreading, herbaceous stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and feature opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins. These blossoms are clustered in dense, terminal cymes that create a colorful effect during the blooming season. The plant's overall structure is low-growing and mounding, making it well-suited for groundcover applications.
