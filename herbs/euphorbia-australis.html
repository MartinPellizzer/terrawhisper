Side Effects

Euphorbia australis can have some side effects, such as skin irritation and allergic reactions due to its milky sap, which contains irritants and toxic compounds.

Contact with the plant's sap may cause redness, itching, blisters, or even chemical burns on the skin. Ingestion of any part of the plant can lead to severe gastrointestinal distress, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. The plant is also known to cause respiratory issues if its airborne particles are inhaled, leading to coughing or difficulty breathing.

Due to these potential hazards, it is important to handle Euphorbia australis with care and avoid direct contact with its sap.
