What is Trichotosia hispidissima?

Trichotosia hispidissima is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, hairy stem and two to three ovate leaves that are often hairy and measure up to 7 cm long. The plant produces a single, hairy flower spike that bears several small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive shape and a short, hairy labellum. The flowers are about 1 cm in diameter and are arranged in a raceme that is up to 10 cm long.

The plant is native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia.
