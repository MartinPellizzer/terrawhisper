Berchemiella wilsonii

What's the taxonomical classification of Berchemiella wilsonii?

Berchemiella wilsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Rosales, the plant is placed within the family Rhamnaceae. Ultimately, it is identified by its specific genus, Berchemiella, and its unique species designation, wilsonii.
