10 Health Benefits Of Boerhavia Diffusa (Punarnava)

Boerhavia diffusa has health benefits such as relieving inflammation and swelling, treating kidney stones and urinary tract infections, and improving liver function.

Its medicinal properties include its ability to reduce oxidative stress and inhibit pro-inflammatory enzymes. For example, punarnava can help alleviate symptoms of cystitis, reducing frequency and urgency of urination, and improve overall quality of life for individuals suffering from kidney stones.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory effects can provide relief from joint pain and swelling, enhancing daily activities and mobility for those affected.

1. Reduces inflammation

Boerhavia diffusa reduces inflammation because of its rich composition of flavonoids and saponins.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to possess potent anti-inflammatory properties, which inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines. Additionally, punarnava's flavonoids exhibit antioxidant activity, scavenging free radicals that can contribute to inflammatory processes.

Its anti-inflammatory effects make it a valuable natural remedy for various inflammatory conditions, including arthritis, gout, and skin disorders.

Its antioxidant activity also helps to protect against oxidative stress, which can further exacerbate anemia.

3. Supports kidney function

Boerhavia diffusa supports kidney function because of its unique composition of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Boerhavia diffusa lowers blood pressure because of its ability to relax and dilate blood vessels, thereby reducing peripheral resistance.

The herb's vasodilatory properties are attributed to the presence of flavonoids and saponins, which help to reduce sympathetic nervous system activity, leading to decreased vasoconstriction and increased diastolic blood pressure.

This natural mechanism makes punarnava a potential adjunct therapy for hypertension management.
