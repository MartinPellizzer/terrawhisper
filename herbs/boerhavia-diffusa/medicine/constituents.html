10 Medicinal Constituents Of Boerhavia Diffusa (Punarnava)

Boerhavia diffusa has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides that contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant's anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties help alleviate symptoms of various health conditions. Additionally, its flavonoids have been shown to possess antiviral activity, providing relief from fever, cough, and respiratory issues.

These medicinal properties of punarnava make it a valuable natural remedy for improving overall well-being and quality of life.
