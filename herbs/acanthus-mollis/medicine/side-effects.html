10 Possible Side Effects Of Acanthus Mollis (Bear'S Breeches)

Acanthus mollis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects can be caused by excessive use, sensitivity, or interacting with other medications. In severe cases, they can lead to difficulty breathing, swelling, and stomach problems, significantly impairing a person's daily activities and overall well-being.

For example, severe skin irritation may cause discomfort and itching, making it challenging to perform even simple tasks.
