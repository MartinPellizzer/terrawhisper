Picea abies var. barryi

Picea abies var. barryi, commonly known as Rocky Mountain spruce, is a variant of the white spruce tree native to North America.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used to support respiratory health, with benefits including relief from congestion, coughs, and bronchitis.

The therapeutic actions of Picea abies var. barryi include expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in treating respiratory issues.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include alpha-pinene, beta-pinene, and limonene, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Possible Side Effects of Picea abies var. barryi
