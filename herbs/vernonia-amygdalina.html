Bitterleaf (Vernonia amygdalina)

Bitterleaf (Vernonia amygdalina) is a member of the Compositae family, native to West Africa, East Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
