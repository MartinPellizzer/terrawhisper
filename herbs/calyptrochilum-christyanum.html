What's the taxonomical classification of Calyptrochilum christyanum?

Calyptrochilum christyanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Calyptrochilum, and is identified by the species name christyanum.
