Pittosporum trigonocarpum

What's the taxonomical classification of Pittosporum trigonocarpum?

Pittosporum trigonocarpum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent, the plant falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Pittosporaceae. It is specifically categorized within the genus Pittosporum, ultimately reaching its unique identification as the species trigonocarpum.
