Adenophora petiolata subsp. huadungensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Adenophora petiolata subsp. huadungensis?

Adenophora petiolata subsp. huadungensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Asterales and falls into the family Campanulaceae. As a member of the genus Adenophora, this specific plant is identified by its formal taxonomic designation as the species Adenophora petiolata subsp. huadungensis.
