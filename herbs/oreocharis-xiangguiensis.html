Oreocharis xiangguiensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Oreocharis xiangguiensis?

Oreocharis xiangguiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Lamiales and belongs to the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Oreocharis and is specifically designated as the species xiangguiensis.
