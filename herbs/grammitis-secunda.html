Grammitis secunda

Grammitis secunda, commonly known as the Two-Flowered Polypody, is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

The therapeutic actions of Grammitis secunda include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antipyretic properties, which contribute to its use in treating various health conditions.
