Microtoena insuavis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It further belongs to the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Microtoena, with its unique biological identity being defined by its species name, insuavis.
