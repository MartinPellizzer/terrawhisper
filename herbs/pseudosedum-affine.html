Pseudosedum affine

What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudosedum affine?

Pseudosedum affine belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Saxifrages. This plant is a member of the family Crassulaceae, specifically falling under the genus Pseudosedum, and is identified by the species name affine.

Pseudosedum affine has antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties that support the healing of skin lesions and the reduction of oxidative stress.
