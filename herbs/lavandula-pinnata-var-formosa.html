Lavandula pinnata var. formosa

Lavandula pinnata var. formosa, also known as the Dwarf Portuguese Lavender, is a compact and fragrant shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

The health benefits of Lavandula pinnata var. formosa include its ability to promote relaxation, improve sleep quality, and reduce stress levels.
