Chelone Glabra Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Chelone glabra, commonly known as white hellebore, is a flowering plant native to North America, characterized by its white or pale pink flowers and medicinal leaves.

This herb has been traditionally used for its detoxifying and anti-inflammatory properties, offering potential health benefits such as supporting liver function and reducing inflammation.

The bioactive constituents in Chelone glabra include alkaloids, saponins, and glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from its leaves and roots to harness its medicinal properties.
