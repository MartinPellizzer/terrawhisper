Lindsaea heterophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Lindsaea heterophylla?

Lindsaea heterophyllla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, its taxonomic progression continues through the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This fern is a representative of the family Lindsaeaeceae, falling under the genus Lindsaea, and is specifically identified by its species name, heterophylla.
