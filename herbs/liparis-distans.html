What's the taxonomical classification of Liparis distans?

Liparis distans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is a member of the order Asparagales and is situated within the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Liparis and is specifically known as the species distans.
