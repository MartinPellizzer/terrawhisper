11 Best Crataegus Laevigata Benefits

1. Lowers cholesterol levels

Crataegus laevigata lowers cholesterol levels because it contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, proanthocyanidins, and ursolic acid, which help reduce low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol.

These compounds work by improving lipid metabolism and inhibiting the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the intestines.

This health benefit is particularly useful for individuals suffering from hyperlipidemia and atherosclerosis.

Regular consumption of Crataegus laevigata may also support cardiovascular health by reducing the risk of heart disease.
