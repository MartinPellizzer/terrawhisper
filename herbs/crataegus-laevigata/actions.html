Common Hawthorn (Crataegus laevigata) Actions

Common Hawthorn is a tonic and sedative, helping to strengthen the heart and calm the nervous system. It also acts as an astringent and anti-inflammatory, reducing swelling and supporting digestive health. Its bitter and carminative properties aid digestion, while its diuretic and antispasmodic effects support urinary function and relieve cramps. It is also a stimulant and expectorant, promoting respiratory health and clearing mucus.
