10 Possible Side Effects Of Crataegus Laevigata (Hawthorn)

Crataegus laevigata has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive problems, and interactions with medications.

These issues can be caused by high doses, prolonged use, or combining with other substances.

For instance, allergic reactions can cause skin rashes and respiratory issues, which can worsen someone's quality of life by limiting their physical activities and social interactions.
