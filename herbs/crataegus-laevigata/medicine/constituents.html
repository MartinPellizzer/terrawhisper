Crataegus laevigata has active constituents such as flavonoids, anthocyanins, and triterpenoid saponins.

Additionally, the antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities of hawthorn's constituents may help alleviate mild hypertension and support overall well-being, ultimately enhancing quality of life for individuals with chronic heart conditions.
