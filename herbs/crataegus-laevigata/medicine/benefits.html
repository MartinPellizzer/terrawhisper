10 Health Benefits Of Crataegus Laevigata (Hawthorn)

Crataegus laevigata, also known as hawthorn, has health benefits such as reducing high blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, oligomeric proanthocyanidins, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its cardiovascular effects. This can improve lives by reducing the risk of heart disease, improving exercise performance, and enhancing overall cardiovascular well-being.

Regular consumption may also alleviate symptoms of angina and palpitations, promoting a healthier and more active lifestyle.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Crataegus laevigata.

1. Reduces blood pressure

Crataegus laevigata reduces blood pressure because of its unique combination of flavonoids and anthraquinones.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to relax blood vessels and improve cardiovascular function by inhibiting the release of certain hormones that constrict blood vessels, thereby reducing peripheral resistance.

Additionally, hawthorn extract has been found to decrease blood vessel stiffness and enhance nitric oxide production, leading to improved blood flow and decreased blood pressure.

2. Improves blood circulation

Crataegus laevigata improves blood circulation because of its unique combination of flavonoids, anthocyanins, and procyanidins.

These bioactive compounds help to relax blood vessels, reducing peripheral resistance and increasing blood flow to the heart, brain, and other vital organs.

As a result, hawthorn extracts have been shown to lower blood pressure, reduce symptoms of angina, and improve overall cardiovascular health by promoting healthy circulation and preventing conditions such as varicose veins and Raynaud's disease.

3. Enhances cardiovascular health

Crataegus laevigata enhances cardiovascular health because of its unique combination of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and procyanidins.

These bioactive compounds help to relax blood vessels, reduce blood pressure, and improve blood lipid profiles, all of which contribute to a reduced risk of heart disease and stroke.

Additionally, hawthorn's flavonoids have been shown to inhibit platelet aggregation, reducing the formation of blood clots that can lead to cardiovascular events.

4. Lowers cholesterol levels

Crataegus laevigata lowers cholesterol levels because of its rich flavonoid and phenolic content.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol, improve blood lipid profiles, and enhance LDL receptor activity, leading to a decrease in serum cholesterol levels.

Additionally, hawthorn's polyphenols exhibit antioxidant properties, which help protect against oxidative stress-induced inflammation and cardiovascular damage, further supporting its potential as a natural adjunct therapy for hypercholesterolemia management.

5. Treats irregular heartbeat

Crataegus laevigata treats irregular heartbeat because of its ability to dilate blood vessels and improve cardiac function.

The flavonoids present in hawthorn extract help to reduce stress on the heart, increasing its pumping efficiency and regularity. Additionally, hawthorn's anticoagulant properties prevent blood clots from forming, which can contribute to irregular heartbeat.

By addressing these underlying issues, hawthorn helps to restore a normal cardiac rhythm and alleviate symptoms of arrhythmia.

6. Prevents heart disease

Crataegus laevigata prevents heart disease because it has been shown to improve cardiovascular function by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

The plant's bioactive compounds, such as oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs), have antioxidant properties that help protect against oxidative stress and inflammation in the cardiovascular system. This can lead to a reduced risk of heart attacks, strokes, and other cardiac complications.

Additionally, hawthorn extract has been found to dilate blood vessels, improving blood flow and reducing peripheral resistance.

7. Alleviates symptoms of angina

Crataegus laevigata alleviates symptoms of angina because it has a positive effect on the cardiovascular system.

The plant's flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins help to improve blood flow to the heart, reduce blood pressure, and strengthen cardiac muscle. Additionally, hawthorn extract has been shown to inhibit the formation of platelet aggregates, which can contribute to blood clotting and exacerbate anginal symptoms.

This combination of effects makes hawthorn a potential natural remedy for managing the discomfort associated with angina.

8. Increases exercise tolerance

Crataegus laevigata increases exercise tolerance because of its ability to enhance cardiac function and vasodilation.

The extract from the fruit and flowers of this plant has been shown to improve blood flow to the muscles, reduce blood pressure, and increase nitric oxide production, which can help to delay fatigue and improve overall physical performance during exercise.

9. Relieves stress and anxiety

Crataegus laevigata relieves stress and anxiety because its bioflavonoids and procyanidins have a calming effect on the nervous system.

The flavonoids in hawthorn berries interact with neurotransmitters like GABA and serotonin to promote relaxation, reducing feelings of anxiety and tension. Additionally, the antioxidant properties of hawthorn help protect against oxidative stress, which can contribute to anxiety and depression.

Overall, the gentle and soothing effects of hawthorn make it a natural remedy for managing stress and anxiety.

10. Boosts immune system

Crataegus laevigata boosts immune system because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

These compounds work together to stimulate the production of white blood cells, which are essential for fighting off infections and diseases. Hawthorn's immunomodulatory effects have been shown to increase natural killer cell activity, enhancing the body's ability to recognize and destroy infected cells, while also reducing inflammation and oxidative stress.

This results in a strengthened immune system that can better defend against pathogens and maintain overall health.
