Peristylus bulleyi

What's the taxonomical classification of Peristylus bulleyi?

Peristylus bulleyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. As a member of the Orchidaceae family, this plant is specifically identified by its genus, Peristylus, and its species designation, bulleyi.
