Cyclea gracillima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Menispermaceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Cyclea, with the binomial designation of the species gracillima.
