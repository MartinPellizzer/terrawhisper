Oxytropis anertii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its legume-bearing plants, and specifically belongs to the genus Oxytropis. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, anertii.
