Berberis reticulata

What's the taxonomical classification of Berberis reticulata?

Berberis reticulaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Berberidaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Berberis, with its specific designation being the species reticulata.
