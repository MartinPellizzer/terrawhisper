Torresia latifolia

Torresia latifolia, commonly known as the "Starfruit Tree" or "Torreya", is a species of coniferous tree in the family Podocarpaceae.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which offer various health benefits such as its potential to improve digestion, reduce inflammation, and aid in the management of fever.

The therapeutic actions of Torresia latifolia include its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenoids.

Research on the plant's chemical composition has identified the presence of bioactive compounds like torreynine, torresine, and podocarpic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties and potential health benefits.
