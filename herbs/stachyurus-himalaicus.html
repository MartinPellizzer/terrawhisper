Stachyurus himalaicus

What's the taxonomical classification of Stachyurus himalaicus?

Stachyurus himalaicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Crossosomatales. It is specifically placed within the family Stachyuraceae, falling under the genus Stachyurus, and is identified by its unique species designation, himalicus.
