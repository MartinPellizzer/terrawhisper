      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Calamus rhabdocladus can cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, or digestive distress if consumed or touched without precaution. There is also a potential for systemic allergic responses if the plant's essential oils enter the bloodstream. Due to these risks, careful handling and professional guidance are necessary when interacting with the species.
