Rhodiola lobulata

Rhodiola lobulata, also known as the Tibetan rhodiola or golden root, is a flowering plant species belonging to the rhodiola genus.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and is valued for its medicinal properties, which include adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant is rich in bioactive compounds such as rosavin and salidroside, which are believed to provide various health benefits, including reducing stress, improving mental performance, and enhancing mood.
