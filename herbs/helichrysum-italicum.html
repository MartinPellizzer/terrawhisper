Everlasting

Helichrysum italicum

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its vibrant yellow flowers and strong, aromatic scent, and it has been traditionally used for its various health benefits, including improving respiratory conditions and reducing inflammation.

Helichrysum italicum, commonly known as everlasting, belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Magnoliopsida, order Asterales, family Asteraceae, genus Helichrysum, and species Helichrysum italicum.

Morphology

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and dense clusters of yellow flowers that retain their color and shape even after drying.

Distribution

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in countries such as Italy, Greece, France, and Spain, where it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

Traditional Uses

Helichrysum italicum has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and wound-healing properties, often applied topically to treat skin conditions, bruises, and respiratory ailments.

Medicinal Systems

In traditional medicinal systems such as Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine, and European herbalism, Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) has been historically used for its anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and wound-healing properties.

Pharmacology

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) exhibits potent anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and wound-healing properties, making it effective in treating skin conditions, reducing pain and swelling, and supporting respiratory and digestive health.

Medicinal Uses

Everlasting is used to treat digestive issues, skin conditions, respiratory infections, anxiety, insomnia, menstrual cramps, and inflammation.
