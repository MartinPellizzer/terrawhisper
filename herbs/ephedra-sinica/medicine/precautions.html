When using Ephedra sinica medicinally, it's important to take consult your doctor first due to its potential risks and interactions with other medications.

As a stimulant, Ephedra can increase heart rate and blood pressure, making it crucial to monitor any pre-existing conditions or medication use. Moreover, Ephedra may interact with antidepressants, antihypertensives, and thyroid medications, which can lead to adverse reactions if not properly managed.

Consulting a doctor ensures safe use and prevents unintended consequences.
