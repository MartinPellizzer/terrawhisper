1. Treats asthma symptoms

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition characterized by inflammation and narrowing of the airways, leading to difficulty in breathing.

This condition, also known as bronchial asthma or obstructive airway disease, is often triggered by allergens, pollutants, or physical exertion.

It commonly affects individuals with a family history of allergies or respiratory issues, particularly children and adults with compromised immune systems.

Ephedra sinica has been studied for its potential to alleviate asthma symptoms by reducing bronchial spasms and improving airflow.

However, its use requires caution due to potential side effects and regulatory restrictions.

How this herb helps with treats asthma symptoms?

Ephedra sinica treats asthma symptoms because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds have bronchodilatory effects, helping to relax and widen the airways. The alkaloid ephedrine is particularly noted for its ability to stimulate the nervous system and reduce bronchial constriction. Flavonoids contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, further supporting respiratory health.

Together, these constituents work synergistically to alleviate asthma-related breathing difficulties.

Scientific Research

According to "Zhongguo Zhong yao za zhi = Zhongguo zhongyao zazhi = China journal of Chinese materia medica", Ephedra sinica, when combined with Pheretima in a 1:3 ratio, has been found to dose-dependently relax tracheal rings pre-contracted by carbachol and histamine, indicating its potential in relieving asthma symptoms.
