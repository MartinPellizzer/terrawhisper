The therapeutic actions of Alliaria parvifolia include its ability to reduce stress, improve cognitive function, and support respiratory health.

Possible Side Effects of Alliaria parvifolia

Alliaria parvifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal irritation, nausea, and vomiting, due to the presence of volatile compounds and saponins.

Prolonged consumption of the plant may also cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory issues.

In rare cases, excessive consumption can lead to more severe complications, including liver and kidney damage.

It is essential to consult with a medical professional before using Alliaria parvifolia for medicinal purposes to minimize the risk of adverse effects.
