Ononis spinosa var. diacantha

Ononis spinosa var. diacantha, also known as the spiny restharrow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

Traditionally, it has been used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating kidney and urinary issues due to its diuretic and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant's health benefits include alleviating symptoms of urinary tract infections, reducing blood pressure, and acting as a natural remedy for rheumatism and arthritis.
