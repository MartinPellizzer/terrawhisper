Hygrophila phlomoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Hygrophila phlomoides?

Hygrophila phlomoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Lamiales, it falls under the family Acanthaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Hygrophila, with its specific designation being the species phlomoides.
