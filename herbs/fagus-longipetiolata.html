What's the taxonomical classification of Fagus longipetiolata?

Fagus longipetiolata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Fagales and the family Fagaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Fagus, and its specific species epithet, longipetiolatata.
