Possible Side Effects of ceratonia siliqua

Ceratonia siliqua can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high content of phytic acid and tannins.

Excessive consumption can also lead to allergic reactions, headaches, and dizziness in some individuals.

Additionally, using Ceratonia siliqua for an extended period may cause kidney damage and interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Ceratonia siliqua for medicinal purposes to minimize the risk of side effects.
