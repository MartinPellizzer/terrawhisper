Urceola huaitingii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Urceola huaitingii has woody, scrambling stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that possess entire margins and acute apices. The foliage is typically characterized by a coriaceous texture and prominent pinnate venation. These inflorescences eventually give rise to small, fleshy capsules that contain the plant's seeds.
