10 Possible Side Effects Of Ligularia Przewalskii (Chinese Marsh Marigold)

These side effects can occur due to an allergic reaction to its active compounds, excessive consumption, or interaction with other medications.

If left untreated, they can lead to severe consequences, such as anaphylaxis, infections, or exacerbation of pre-existing conditions, significantly impairing an individual's quality of life.
