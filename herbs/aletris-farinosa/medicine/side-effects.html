These side effects may be caused by excessive consumption, sensitivities, or concurrent use with other herbs or medications. For instance, digestive issues can lead to nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain, impairing daily activities and productivity.

Allergic reactions can cause itching, rashes, and difficulty breathing, further complicating one's life.
