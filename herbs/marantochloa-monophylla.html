Marantochloa monophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Zingiberales and falls under the family Marantaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Marantochlloa and the species epithet monophylla.
