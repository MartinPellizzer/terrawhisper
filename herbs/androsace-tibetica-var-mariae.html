Androsace tibetica var. mariae

What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace tibetica var. mariae?

Androsace tibetica var. mariae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Primulaceae and falls under the genus Androsace, ultimately identified by its specific taxonomic designation as the variety mariae of the species Androsace tibetica.
