What's the taxonomical classification of Elaeagnus argyi?

Elaeagnus argyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Elaeagnaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Elaeagnus, reaching its specific identification as the species argyi.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Elaeagnus argyi can cause skin irritation, allergic dermatitis, and gastrointestinal distress if ingested.
