What's the taxonomical classification of Schoenoplectus juncoides?

Schoenoplectus juncoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Poales. Its taxonomic placement continues through the family Cyperaceae, which houses the genus Schoenoplectus. Ultimately, this specific plant is identified by its unique species designation, Schoenoplectus juncoides.
