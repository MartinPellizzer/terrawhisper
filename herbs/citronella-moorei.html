Citronella moorei

Citronella moorei, also known as the Indonesian or Java citronella, is a tropical grass species native to Indonesia, known for its distinct citrusy scent and medicinal properties.

The leaves of the citronella moorei plant have been traditionally used in herbal remedies for their anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, making them effective in treating various health issues.
