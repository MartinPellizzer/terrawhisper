Diarrhena mandshurica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Diarrhrena, and its specific species designation, mandshurica.
