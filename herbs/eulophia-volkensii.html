What is Eulophia volkensii?

Eulophia volkensii is a terrestrial orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by a short stem, up to 30 cm in height, with 2-5 linear to lanceolate leaves, and a racemose inflorescence bearing 10-30 yellowish-green flowers with a yellow labellum. The flowers are 3-4 cm in diameter, with a long, thin spur, and a cylindrical column bearing a single pollinium. The plant grows in shaded areas of tropical Africa, particularly in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda, at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 2,500 meters above sea level.

Eulophia volkensii flowers from June to September, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths and bees.
