What is Astragalus bilobatoalatus?

Astragalus bilobatoalatus is a species of legume in the family Fabaceae.

It is an annual herb with a height of 5-15 cm, bearing 2-4 pairs of leaflets that are 1-3 mm long and linear to lanceolate in shape, with a 1-2 mm long petiole. The inflorescence is a spike, 2-6 cm long, with 2-4 pairs of 5-8 mm long, pink to purple flowers. The fruit is a legume, 5-10 mm long, dehiscent and containing 2-4 seeds, each 2-3 mm long and kidney-shaped.

The species is endemic to the mountainous regions of China.
