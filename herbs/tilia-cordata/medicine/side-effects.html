10 Possible Side Effects Of Tilia Cordata (Littleleaf Linden)

Tilia cordata has some side effects when used improperly, such as dizziness, headaches, and digestive issues.

These side effects can occur due to an overdose or sensitivity to the herb.

For instance, a person suffering from dizziness may experience a road accident, while a person experiencing digestive issues may lead a life of discomfort and decreased productivity.

The tree's pollen is particularly notorious for triggering allergic reactions, especially among those with pre-existing respiratory issues or sensitivities to similar plant species.

As a result, exposure to Tilia cordata can be hazardous for those susceptible to its allergenic properties.
