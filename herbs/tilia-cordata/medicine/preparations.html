10 Medicinal Preparations Of Tilia Cordata (Littleleaf Linden)

Tilia cordata has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures.

These preparations are useful for treating anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness due to its sedative properties. The plant's flowers and leaves can also be used to make a calming tea that eases headaches, soothes nervous irritability, and reduces fever.

Additionally, its bark can be prepared as a decoction to relieve pain and inflammation.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Tilia cordata.
