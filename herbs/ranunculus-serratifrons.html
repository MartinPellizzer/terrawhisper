Ranunculus serratifrons

Ranunculus serratifrons is a species of flowering plant in the buttercup family, characterized by its distinctive serrated leaves and bright yellow flowers.

Traditionally, the plant has been used in medicine due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help soothe skin irritations and wounds.

The health benefits of Ranunculus serratifrons include its ability to reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a popular choice for topical applications.
