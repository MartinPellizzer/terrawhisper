Bulbophyllum ambrosia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its genus, Bulbophyllum, and its specific species designation, ambrosia.
