10 Medicinal Constituents Of Berberis Thunbergii (Japanese Barberry)

Berberis thunbergii, also known as Japanese barberry, has active constituents such as berberine, epiberberine, and magnoflorine.

These alkaloids are responsible for the plant's antimicrobial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues. Berberine has been shown to lower blood sugar levels, improve heart health, and reduce symptoms of depression.

Additionally, its antimicrobial properties can help combat infections and promote wound healing, improving overall well-being and quality of life.
