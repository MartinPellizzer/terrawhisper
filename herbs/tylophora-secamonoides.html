    What side effects this plant can have?

    This plant can cause severe toxicity, including cardiac dysfunction, gastrointestinal distress, and potential neurological impairment due to its high concentration of toxic cardiac glycosides. These compounds interfere with the sodium-potassium pump in heart cells, which can lead to life-threatening arrhythmias or sudden cardiac arrest. Ingesting even small amounts may trigger intense nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain as the body attempts to reject the toxins. Some individuals might also experience dizziness, confusion, or tremors if the toxins affect the central nervous system. Because the margin between a therapeutic dose and a lethal dose is extremely narrow, accidental poisoning is a significant risk.
