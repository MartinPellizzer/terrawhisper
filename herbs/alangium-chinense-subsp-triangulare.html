Alangium chinense subsp. triangulare

What's the taxonomical classification of Alangium chinense subsp. triangulare?

Aliangium chinense subsp. triangulare belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Cornales. As a member of the family Cornaceae, it is placed within the genus Aliangium, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Aliangium chinense subsp. triangulare.
