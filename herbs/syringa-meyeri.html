What's the taxonomical classification of Syringa meyeri?

Syringa meyeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Lamiales. It is further categorized into the family Oleaceae, which places it within the genus Syringa. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific classification as the species meyeri.
