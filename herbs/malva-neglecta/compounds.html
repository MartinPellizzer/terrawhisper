Little Mallow (Malva neglecta) Compounds

The primary compounds in Little Mallow include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory qualities, while coumarin may have antispasmodic benefits. Glycosides, saponins, and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal properties, along with terpenoids, essential oils, and trace alkaloids.
