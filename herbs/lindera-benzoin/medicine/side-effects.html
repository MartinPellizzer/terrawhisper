10 Possible Side Effects Of Lindera Benzoin (Spicebush)

Lindera benzoin has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These can be caused by ingesting large amounts, using essential oils undiluted, or applying topical preparations without proper skin preparation.

Severe cases can lead to nausea, vomiting, and anaphylaxis, significantly impacting daily life and potentially requiring medical attention.
