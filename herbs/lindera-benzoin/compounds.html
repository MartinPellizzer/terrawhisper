Lindera Benzoin (Lindera benzoin) Compounds

The primary compounds in Lindera Benzoin include essential oil, coumarin, and flavonoids. These compounds contribute to its aromatic properties and potential medicinal effects. Terpenoids and phenolic acids also play roles in its therapeutic applications. Tannins, glycosides, and saponins further support its use in traditional herbal medicine.

The essential oil of Lindera benzoin contains aromatic compounds like methylcinnamate and benzoin. It is used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
