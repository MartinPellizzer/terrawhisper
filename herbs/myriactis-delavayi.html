What's the taxonomical classification of Myriactis delavayi?

Myriactis delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, placing it among the composite flowers, within the genus Myriactis. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, delavayi.
