Crataegus lutea

The therapeutic actions of Crataegus lutea are attributed to its ability to act as a vasodilator, improving blood flow and reducing blood pressure, as well as its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Possible Side Effects of Crataegus lutea

Overuse of Crataegus lutea may also cause kidney and liver damage due to the presence of compounds like flavonoids and polyphenols.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Crataegus lutea for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
