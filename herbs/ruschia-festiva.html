Possible Side Effects of Ruschia festiva

Ruschia festiva can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues due to its high concentration of saponins and other compounds.

Improper use of Ruschia festiva may also trigger respiratory problems, including coughing and shortness of breath, in individuals with pre-existing conditions.
