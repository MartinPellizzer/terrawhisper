Ligularia dictyoneura

What's the taxonomical classification of Ligularia dictyoneura?

Ligularia dictyoneura belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is categorized under the genus Ligularia, with its specific designation being the species dictyoneura.
