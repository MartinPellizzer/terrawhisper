Flemingia fluminaalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Flemingia, and its unique species designation, fluinaalis.
