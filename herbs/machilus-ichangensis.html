Machilus ichangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Machilus ichangensis?

Machilus ichangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Laurales and is a member of the family Lauraceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified by the genus Machilus and the species ichangensis.
