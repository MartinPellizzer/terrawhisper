What's the taxonomical classification of Phlomis megalantha?

Phlomis megalantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. More specifically, it is situated within the genus Phlomis, and its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species megalantha.

Its most striking feature is the terminal verticillasters that hold massive, tubular, bright yellow flowers. These large corollas are arranged in distinct whorls along a sturdy, upright stem.
