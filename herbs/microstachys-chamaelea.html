What's the taxonomical classification of Microstachys chamaelea?

Microstachys chamaelea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is situated in the order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae. Finally, its specific scientific designation is defined by the genus Microstachys and the species name chamaelea.
