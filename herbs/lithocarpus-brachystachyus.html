Lithocarpus brachystachyus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Its evolutionary lineage continues through the order Fagales and the family Fagaceae, placing it within the genus Lithocarpus. Ultimately, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, brachystachyus.
