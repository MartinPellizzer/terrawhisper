What is Cattleya tereticaulis?

Cattleya tereticaulis is a species of orchid within the family Orchidaceae.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, characterized by a thick, fleshy rhizome and pseudo-bulbs with a diameter of 10-15 cm, and 2-3 leaves. The leaves are coriaceous, lanceolate, and 50-60 cm long, with a petiole of 10-15 cm. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing 5-7 flowers with sepals and petals that are yellow with green markings, and a labellum that is yellow with a red center, 10-12 cm long, and 8-10 cm wide.

The flowers are fragrant and bloom from summer to fall.
