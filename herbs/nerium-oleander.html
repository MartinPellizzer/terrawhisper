Nerium Oleander Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Nerium oleander, commonly known as the flowering shrub, is a plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its showy flowers and medicinal properties.

It contains bioactive constituents such as cardiac glycosides, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

The herb is traditionally used for its potential health benefits, including supporting heart health and reducing inflammation.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical applications can be made from Nerium oleander, though its use should be approached with caution due to its toxicity.
