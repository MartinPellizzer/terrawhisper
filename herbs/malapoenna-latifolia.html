Possible Side Effects of Malapoenna latifolia

Malapoenna latifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing dizziness, nausea, and stomach cramps due to its high levels of toxic alkaloids.

Prolonged consumption can also lead to kidney damage and an increased risk of heart arrhythmias.

In severe cases, malapoenna latifolia poisoning can cause seizures, coma, and even death.

It is essential to consult with a medical professional before using this plant for medicinal purposes to minimize the risk of these adverse side effects.
