What's the taxonomical classification of Davallia formosana?

Davalliia formosana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae, eventually placing it within the genus Davallia. Finally, the specific identification of this plant is completed by its species name, formosana.

It is generally advisable to wash your hands thoroughly after handling the foliage to prevent direct contact with any potential allergens.
