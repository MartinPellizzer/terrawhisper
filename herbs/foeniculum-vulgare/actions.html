Fennel (Foeniculum vulgare) Actions

Fennel exhibits several therapeutic actions, including carminative, which aids in expelling gas from the digestive tract. It also acts as an antispasmodic, relieving muscle spasms, and as an expectorant, helping to loosen mucus. Additionally, it has diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic properties, supporting overall health and digestion.
