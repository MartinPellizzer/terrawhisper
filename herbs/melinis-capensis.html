Melinis capensis

Melinis capensis, also known as Lion's Tail, is a plant species native to southern Africa, characterized by its reddish-brown or yellowish flowers and long, drooping leaves.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, which have been used to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of Melinis capensis include reducing inflammation and pain, as well as treating digestive issues and fever.

Melinis capensis can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used in traditional medicine, particularly in South African herbalism.
