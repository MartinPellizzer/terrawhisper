Ononis Spinosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ononis spinosa, commonly known as rock pepper or spring beauty, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its delicate pink or white flowers and hairy leaves.

This herb is valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and mild laxative properties, making it beneficial for urinary tract health, digestive support, and skin conditions.

The bioactive constituents in Ononis spinosa include saponins, flavonoids, and mucilage, which contribute to its therapeutic effects by reducing inflammation and promoting detoxification.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures can be made from the dried plant to harness its medicinal benefits.

The leaves of Ononis spinosa are also utilized, typically in the form of teas or infusions, to promote digestion and alleviate respiratory issues.
