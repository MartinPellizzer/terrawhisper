Ononis Spinosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ononis spinosa, commonly known as stonecrop or rock pepper, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and succulent leaves.

This herb is valued for its adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory properties, which support immune function and help the body manage stress.

The bioactive constituents in Ononis spinosa include flavonoids, tannins, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from the leaves and flowers to harness its medicinal benefits.
