Sclerosperma walkeri

What's the taxonomical classification of Sclerosperma walkeri?

Sclerosperma walkeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Arecales and the family Arecaceae, ultimately placing it within the genus Sclerosperma, with the specific designation of the species walkeri.
