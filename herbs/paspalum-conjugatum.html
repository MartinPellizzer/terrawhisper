What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Paspalum conjugatum has stoloniferous growth habits with slender, creeping stems that produce dense, low-growing mats of spreading foliage. These spikelets are small and oblong, often appearing in clusters that give the inflorescence a loose, airy appearance.
