Polystichum hancockii

What's the taxonomical classification of Polystichum hancockii?

Polystichum hancockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Polypodiaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Polystichum and is designated as the species hancockii.
