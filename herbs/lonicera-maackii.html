Lonicera maackii

What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera maackii?

Lonicera maackii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Dipsacales. Further taxonomic refinement places it in the family Caprifoliaceae, specifically within the genus Lonicera, and it is identified at the species level as maackii.
