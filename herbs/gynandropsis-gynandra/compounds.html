African Leafy Vegetable (Gynandropsis gynandra) Compounds

The primary compounds in Gynandropsis gynandra include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and cleansing properties. Glycosides and alkaloids may support cardiovascular health, while coumarin and terpenoids offer antispasmodic and antimicrobial benefits. Essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its medicinal and aromatic qualities.
