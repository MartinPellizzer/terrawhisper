Cynanchum forrestii

What's the taxonomical classification of Cynanchum forrestii?

Cynanchum forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Gentianales. This plant is further categorized under the family Apocynaceae, placing it within a group of flowering plants often known for their milky sap. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Cynanchum, specifically identified by its unique species designation, forrestii.
