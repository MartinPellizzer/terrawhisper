Premna henryana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It further falls into the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by its distinct botanical traits. Ultimately, the taxonomic hierarchy identifies this specific organism within the genus Premna, with its unique identity defined by the species name henryana.
