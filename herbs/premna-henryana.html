What's the taxonomical classification of Premna henryana?

Premna henryana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It further falls into the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by its distinct botanical traits. Ultimately, the taxonomic hierarchy identifies this specific organism within the genus Premna, with its unique identity defined by the species name henryana.
