Centella asiatica f. crispata

It is renowned for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, making it a valuable ingredient in traditional medicine.

The health benefits of Centella asiatica f. crispata include improved skin health, enhanced cognitive function, and increased wound healing, as well as potential benefits for anxiety and stress relief.
