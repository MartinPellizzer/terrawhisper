Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) Compounds

The primary compounds of Yanhusuo Corydalis include alkaloids, which have analgesic and sedative effects. Glycosides contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties. Flavonoids and terpenoids provide antioxidant benefits. Coumarins and phenolic acids support its antispasmodic and antimicrobial activities.
