Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) Uses

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to treat conditions such as migraine, headache, and neuralgia due to its analgesic properties. It also addresses mental stress, depression, and anxiety by regulating mood and reducing nervous system overactivity. Insomnia and neuropathic pain are commonly managed with its calming and pain-relieving effects. Its applications extend to various neurological disorders and pain-related conditions.

Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) Monograph

1. Migraine

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to treat migraines due to its ability to relieve pain and improve blood circulation. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness. Its active compounds help reduce inflammation and ease tension in the head and neck areas. This makes it a valuable component in traditional herbal formulations for migraine relief.

2. Depression

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate depressive symptoms by regulating qi and blood flow. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance mood stabilization and emotional balance. Its effects are attributed to compounds that influence neurotransmitter activity and reduce stress-related inflammation.

3. Anxiety

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate anxiety by regulating qi and calming the mind. It is often combined with other herbs to address emotional imbalances and promote mental clarity. Its effects are attributed to bioactive compounds that influence the nervous system. This application supports its role in managing stress-related conditions.

4. Insomnia

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat insomnia due to its sedative and calming properties. It helps soothe the nervous system and promote restful sleep. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in managing sleep disorders.

5. Headache

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to treat headache by promoting blood circulation and relieving pain. It is often combined with other herbs to address headaches caused by blood stasis or qi stagnation. Its analgesic properties make it effective for both acute and chronic headache symptoms.

6. Neuralgia

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to treat neuralgia due to its analgesic and antispasmodic properties. It helps alleviate pain along nerve pathways by reducing inflammation and improving blood circulation. Traditionally, it is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in treating conditions like trigeminal neuralgia and postherpetic neuralgia.

7. Neuropathic pain

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to treat neuropathic pain due to its analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate pain from conditions like diabetic neuropathy and post-herpetic neuralgia. Its active compounds, including tetrahydroprotoberberine alkaloids, modulate nerve signaling and reduce pain perception. Clinical studies support its efficacy in managing chronic neuropathic pain.

8. Neurological pain

Corydalis yanhusuo is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate neurological pain, such as headaches, neuralgia, and joint pain. Its active compounds, including tetrahydroberberine and tetrahydrocorydalisine, have analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties that target nerve-related discomfort. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its pain-relieving effects in formulations.

9. Mental stress

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used to alleviate mental stress by promoting emotional balance and calming the mind. Its active compounds help reduce anxiety and improve mood, making it a valuable herb in traditional herbal medicine for managing stress-related conditions.

10. Neurological disorders

Yanhusuo Corydalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to address neurological disorders such as neuropathic pain and epilepsy. Its active compounds, including tetrahydrocannabinol and tetrahydrocannabivarin, influence neurotransmitter activity and may reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the nervous system.
