Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) Actions

Yanhusuo Corydalis exhibits antispasmodic and analgesic properties, helping to relieve pain and muscle spasms. It also has anti-inflammatory and sedative effects, reducing inflammation and promoting calm. Additionally, it acts as a carminative and tonic, aiding digestion and strengthening the body.

Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) Monograph

1. Antispasmodic

Yanhusuo Corydalis has an antispasmodic action, effectively relieving muscle spasms and cramps. It works by relaxing smooth muscle tissue, making it useful for conditions like menstrual pain and gastrointestinal issues. Its active compounds, such as tetrahydropalmatine, contribute to its ability to reduce spasms and tension.

2. Anti-inflammatory

Yanhusuo Corydalis has an anti-inflammatory action, primarily due to its active alkaloids such as tetrahydrocannabinol and tetrahydropalmatine. These compounds inhibit pro-inflammatory cytokines and reduce oxidative stress, making it effective in treating inflammatory conditions like arthritis and skin inflammation.

3. Sedative

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a sedative action, which helps calm the nervous system and reduce anxiety. It is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality. Its calming effects are attributed to its active alkaloids, which influence the central nervous system.

4. Carminative

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a carminative action, helping to relieve gas and bloating in the digestive system. It promotes smooth muscle relaxation in the gastrointestinal tract, reducing discomfort caused by trapped air. This effect is due to its active alkaloids, which enhance gut motility and reduce spasms. It is commonly used in traditional formulas for digestive disorders.

5. Tonic

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a tonic action, enhancing vitality and energy by supporting the body's natural functions. It is used to strengthen the body, improve endurance, and promote overall health. This action helps in restoring balance and improving physical performance in individuals with weakness or fatigue.

6. Stimulant

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a stimulant action, enhancing neural activity and improving circulation. It is used to invigorate the mind and body, often in traditional formulations for fatigue and low energy. This effect is attributed to its alkaloid content, which influences the central nervous system.

7. Diuretic

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a diuretic action, promoting the elimination of excess water and toxins from the body. It is used to alleviate edema and fluid retention by enhancing urinary output. This effect is attributed to its active compounds that support kidney function and fluid balance.

8. Expectorant

Yanhusuo Corydalis has an expectorant action, aiding in the removal of mucus and phlegm from the respiratory tract. It stimulates the secretion of bronchial fluids, making it easier to expel congestion. This property makes it useful in treating conditions like coughs and bronchitis. Its expectorant effect is supported by traditional use and some modern pharmacological studies.

9. Analgesic

Yanhusuo Corydalis has an analgesic action, primarily due to the presence of alkaloids like tetrahydrocannabinol and protopine. These compounds interact with the central nervous system to reduce pain perception. It is traditionally used for relieving various types of pain, including menstrual cramps and postoperative discomfort.

10. Astringent

Yanhusuo Corydalis has an astringent action, which helps reduce secretions and tighten tissues. It is used to treat conditions like diarrhea and excessive vaginal discharge. This property also aids in wound healing by promoting tissue repair. Its astringent effect is attributed to its alkaloid content.

11. Bitter

Yanhusuo Corydalis has a bitter action, which enhances its ability to clear heat and resolve toxins. This property supports its use in treating conditions involving inflammation and excessive internal heat. The bitter taste also aids in promoting digestion and clearing dampness from the body. It is particularly effective in formulas addressing heat-related disorders.
