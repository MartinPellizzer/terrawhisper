1. Relieves pain

Yanhusuo relieves pain because it contains alkaloids, such as tetrahydropalmatine, that act as natural painkillers.

These alkaloids work by inhibiting the reuptake of neurotransmitters like norepinephrine and serotonin, which helps to reduce the perception of pain in the brain. Additionally, yanhusuo has been found to suppress the activity of certain enzymes that contribute to inflammation, further alleviating pain and discomfort. Its analgesic effects have been compared to those of opioid medications, but without the risk of addiction or dependence.

As a result, yanhusuo has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries as a natural remedy for various types of pain, including headaches, menstrual cramps, and muscle aches.

2. Reduces inflammation

Yanhusuo reduces inflammation because it contains alkaloids, such as tetrahydropalmatine and corydaline, which have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory properties.

These alkaloids work by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, such as tumor necrosis factor-alpha (TNF-α) and interleukin-6 (IL-6), which are involved in the inflammatory response. Additionally, yanhusuo has been found to suppress the activation of nuclear factor-kappa B (NF-κB), a transcription factor that plays a crucial role in the regulation of inflammation. By modulating these inflammatory pathways, yanhusuo may help alleviate inflammation and reduce associated pain and swelling.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the mechanisms underlying the anti-inflammatory effects of yanhusuo.

This dual action of relaxing uterine muscles and increasing pain relief makes yanhusuo an effective herbal remedy for menstrual cramps.

Its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties may also contribute to its ability to improve blood flow and reduce pain. Yanhusuo has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various conditions related to poor circulation, such as chest pain, cold hands and feet, and high blood pressure.

Additionally, yanhusuo has been found to inhibit the activity of angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE), an enzyme that constricts blood vessels and increases blood pressure. By inhibiting ACE, yanhusuo can help lower blood pressure and promote overall cardiovascular health.
