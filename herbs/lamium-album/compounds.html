White Dead-Nettle (Lamium album) Compounds

White Dead-Nettle contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and coumarin, known for its anti-inflammatory effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent qualities, while glycosides and terpenoids may support various therapeutic actions. Saponins, essential oils, and alkaloids also play roles in its medicinal profile, and anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant benefits.
