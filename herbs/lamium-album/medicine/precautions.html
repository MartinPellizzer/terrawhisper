1. Read instructions carefully beforehand

When using Lamium album medicinally, it's important to take correct dosage carefully.

This is crucial because the plant contains alkaloids that can cause adverse reactions if taken in excessive amounts. Taking an incorrect dosage can lead to symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, and even allergic reactions.
