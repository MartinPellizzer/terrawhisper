Crithmum (Crithmum maritimum) Compounds

The primary compounds of Crithmum maritimum include essential oil, flavonoids, coumarin, phenolic acids, terpenoids, tannins, glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins. These compounds contribute to its aromatic properties and potential medicinal effects.

Crithmum (Crithmum maritimum) Monograph

1. Essential oil

The essential oil of Crithmum maritimum contains bioactive compounds like terpenoids and phenolic acids. It is known for its antifungal and antioxidant properties, making it valuable in herbal medicine for skin conditions and infections. The oil is typically extracted through steam distillation from the plant's leaves and roots.

Flavonoids in Crithmum maritimum are bioactive compounds known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
