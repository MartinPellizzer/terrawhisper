10 Medicinal Constituents Of Crithmum Maritimum (Samphire)

Crithmum maritimum has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help alleviate digestive issues and prevent infections.

For instance, the flavonoids in samphire can help soothe irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) symptoms, while the antimicrobial properties can aid in wound healing, promoting the recovery of people with compromised immune systems.
