Ash, scientifically known as Fraxinus excelsior, is a herb that has been revered for its numerous health properties, which include antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compounds that have been shown to improve overall well-being by reducing stress and promoting relaxation.

Horticulturally, ash is a significant component of woodland ecosystems, providing vital shelter and food for various wildlife species. From a botanical standpoint, ash is a deciduous tree with distinctive compound leaves and small, winged seeds that are highly sought after by birds and other animals.
