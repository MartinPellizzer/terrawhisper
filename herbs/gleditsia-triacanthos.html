What's the taxonomical classification of Gleditsia triacanthos?

Gleditsia triacanthos belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Fabales, it falls under the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Gleditsia, and its specific species designation, triacanthos.
