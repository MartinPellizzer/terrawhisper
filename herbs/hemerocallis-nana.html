Hemerocallis nana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Asparagales and is a member of the family Asphodelaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Hemerocallis, with its specific designation being the species nana.
