Yellow Hawkweed (Hypochaeris radicata)

Yellow Hawkweed (Hypochaeris radicata) is a member of the Brassicaceae family, native to Europe, Asia, and North America. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and seeds have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and native american herbal medicine.
