Capsella belgica

Capsella belgica, also known as Shepherds Cress or Small Cress, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the mustard family (Brassicaceae) and is native to Europe and Asia.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve respiratory issues, and act as a diuretic.

The health benefits of Capsella belgica include its potential to reduce inflammation, alleviate symptoms of menopause, and support kidney function.

In herbal preparations, Capsella belgica is often combined with other herbs and made into teas, infusions, or tinctures to harness its medicinal properties.
