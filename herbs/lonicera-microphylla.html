Lonicera microphylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera microphylla?

Lonicera microphylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Dipsacales. This plant is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae, falling under the genus Lonicera, with its specific biological identity defined by the species name microphylla.
