Myrcia Laxa (Myrcia laxa) Compounds

The primary compounds of Myrcia Laxa include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory qualities, while essential oils provide aromatic and antimicrobial benefits. Other compounds like coumarin and terpenoids support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
