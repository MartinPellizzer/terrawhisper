10 Possible Side Effects Of Averrhoa Carambola (Starfruit)

Averrhoa carambola has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal problems, and neurotoxicity.

Causes of these side effects include eating excessive amounts, having kidney or liver issues, and consuming the fruit with certain medications.

For instance, gastrointestinal problems can lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, making daily activities, such as work or school, more challenging.
