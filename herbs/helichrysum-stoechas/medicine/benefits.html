10 Health Benefits Of Helichrysum Stoechas (Torch Flower)

Helichrysum stoechas has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain, thanks to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

Its essential oils have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including arthritis, menstrual cramps, and skin conditions like eczema and acne. By alleviating these symptoms, people can improve their quality of life, regain mobility, and enjoy better overall health.

Additionally, the plant's antioxidant properties help protect against oxidative stress, which can contribute to chronic diseases like cancer and cardiovascular disease.
