Stoechas Helichrysum (Helichrysum stoechas) Uses

Helichrysum stoechas is traditionally used for respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and cough. It supports the treatment of respiratory tract infections and may alleviate symptoms of influenza and chronic bronchitis. Its properties help reduce inflammation and ease breathing in individuals with asthmatic or respiratory issues.

Stoechas Helichrysum (Helichrysum stoechas) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Helichrysum stoechas is used traditionally to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help reduce bronchial irritation and ease coughing. It may also help loosen mucus, making it easier to expel. This makes it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for managing symptoms of bronchial inflammation.
