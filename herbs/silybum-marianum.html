Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum, commonly known as milk thistle, is a biennial plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its distinctive white and purple flowers.

The health benefits of milk thistle are attributed to its ability to protect the liver from damage, support the removal of toxins, and promote overall liver health.

The therapeutic actions of milk thistle include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and cytoprotective properties, which help to reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the liver.

The bioactive constituents of milk thistle include silymarin, a complex mixture of flavonolignans, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
