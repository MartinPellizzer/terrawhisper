Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum, also known as the milk thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region and widely cultivated for its medicinal properties.

The plant's seeds contain silymarin, a flavonoid complex that has been traditionally used to protect the liver and promote overall health.

The medicinal properties of milk thistle have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and hepatoprotective effects, making it beneficial for liver health, digestion, and reducing oxidative stress.

Herbal preparations of milk thistle, such as capsules, tablets, or tea, are commonly used to support liver function and overall well-being, and can be found in health food stores or online.
