Possible Side Effects of artemisia dracunculus

Artemisia dracunculus can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, allergic reactions, and interactions with other medications.

Some individuals may experience nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, or stomach pain after consuming the herb.

There is also a risk of anaphylaxis, a severe and potentially life-threatening allergic reaction, in people with sensitivities to artemisinin or other compounds found in the herb.

Additionally, people taking certain medications, such as blood thinners, may experience adverse interactions when consuming artemisia dracunculus.
