Artemisia Dracunculus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Artemisia dracunculus, commonly known as tarragon, is an aromatic herb native to Europe and Asia, widely used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

It is valued for its digestive benefits, including the relief of indigestion, bloating, and nausea, as well as its ability to stimulate appetite.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as essential oils (like estragole and methyl chavicol), flavonoids, and terpenes, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it versatile for use in herbal remedies and natural health practices.
