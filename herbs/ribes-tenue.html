Ribes tenue

What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes tenue?

Ribes tenue belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Saxifragales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Grossulariaceae, which characterizes its botanical relationships. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Ribes and the specific epithet tenue, making its full scientific designation Ribes tenue.
