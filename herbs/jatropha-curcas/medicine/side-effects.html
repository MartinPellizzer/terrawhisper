10 Possible Side Effects Of Jatropha Curcas (Physic Nut)

Jatropha curcas has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting.

These side effects can be caused by consuming large doses of the herb, which contains toxic compounds like phorbol esters.

Prolonged consumption can lead to liver and kidney damage, worsening an individual's quality of life by making everyday activities extremely difficult due to debilitating health conditions.
