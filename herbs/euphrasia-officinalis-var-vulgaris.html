Eyebright (Euphrasia Officinalis Var. Vulgaris)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Euphrasia officinalis var. vulgaris.

Eyebright, scientifically known as Euphrasia officinalis var. vulgaris, is a small, flowering medicinal herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, traditionally used for its therapeutic properties. It is renowned for its primary benefits in supporting eye health, reducing inflammation, and enhancing vision, due to its high concentration of flavonoids and antioxidants. Historically, it has been a key component in traditional European herbal medicine, used to treat eye ailments such as conjunctivitis, cataracts, and redness, as well as for its calming effects on the nervous system. In modern wellness practices, eyebright is often used in eye drops, teas, and supplements to promote overall ocular health and reduce stress-related fatigue. Its unique feature is its distinctive bitter taste and the presence of the rare compound aucubin, which contributes to its anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting properties.
