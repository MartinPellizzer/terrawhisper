10 Health Benefits Of Glycyrrhiza Lepidota (Licorice)

Glycyrrhiza lepidota has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and soothing digestive issues due to its medicinal properties like glycyrrhetic acid and triterpenoids.

These properties can help alleviate symptoms of stomach ulcers, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), and gastritis, improving digestion and overall gut health.

By reducing inflammation, licorice may also provide relief from respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, allowing individuals to breathe easier and enjoy improved lung function.
