10 Medicinal Constituents Of Glycyrrhiza Lepidota (Licorice)

Glycyrrhiza lepidota has active constituents such as glycyrrhizin, flavonoids, and triterpenoids.

These constituents are responsible for the plant's anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antimicrobial properties. The medicinal properties of Glycyrrhiza lepidota can help alleviate digestive issues, soothe respiratory problems, and boost the immune system.

For example, glycyrrhizin can help reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms of IBS, while flavonoids can help protect against viral infections such as the flu.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Glycyrrhiza lepidota.
