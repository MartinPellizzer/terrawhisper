Cephalantheropsis obcordata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Cephalantheropsis, and its unique species name, obcordata.
