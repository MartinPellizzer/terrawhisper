What's the taxonomical classification of Cephalantheropsis obcordata?

Cephalantheropsis obcordata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Cephalantheropsis, and its unique species name, obcordata.
