Scottellia klaineana

What's the taxonomical classification of Scottellia klaineana?

Scottellia klaineana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is positioned under the order Malpighiales. It is further categorized into the family Achariaceae, which defines its closer biological relationships. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Scottellia, with the specific epithet klaineaana designating its unique species identity.
