1. Fights oxidative stress

Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and the body's ability to neutralize them, leading to cellular damage.

This health issue, also known as reactive oxygen species (ROS) overload or oxidative damage, arises when there is an excess of harmful molecules that can harm DNA, proteins, and lipids.

It is commonly experienced by individuals exposed to environmental pollutants, smokers, and those with poor dietary habits or chronic illnesses.

The daucus carota plant, or carrot, contains antioxidants like beta-carotene and vitamin C, which help combat oxidative stress by neutralizing free radicals and supporting the body's natural defenses.

How this herb helps with fights oxidative stress?

Daucus carota fights oxidative stress because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds act as powerful antioxidants, neutralizing free radicals in the body. Alkaloids help in reducing inflammation and supporting immune function. Flavonoids contribute to the plant's ability to protect cells from oxidative damage.

Together, these constituents enhance the body's defense against oxidative stress and promote overall health.
