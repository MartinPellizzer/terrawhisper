Zinnia schiedeana

Zinnia schiedeana, also known as Golden Flower or Golden Zinnia, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, characterized by its bright yellow or golden flowers with delicate petals.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, and is also valued for its health benefits, including reducing fever and soothing digestive issues.

In herbal preparations, the flowers and leaves of the Zinnia schiedeana plant are typically used to make infusions, decoctions, and topical creams, which can be applied to the skin to treat wounds, burns, and other skin conditions.

Additionally, the plant's roots are sometimes used to make a tea that can help to calm the nervous system and promote relaxation.
