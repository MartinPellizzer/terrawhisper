What's the taxonomical classification of Pittosporum heterophyllum?

Pittosporum heterophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Pittosporaceae and is further categorized under the genus Pittosporum, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species heterophyllum.
