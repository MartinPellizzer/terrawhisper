What is Atropanthe sinensis?

Atropanthe sinensis is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Solanaceae.

It is characterized by large, ovate leaves with wavy margins and a cordate base, measuring up to 60 cm in length and 30 cm in width. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers with a yellow or greenish-yellow color and a white interior, which are arranged in clusters at the top of the stem. The flowers are followed by greenish-yellow berries containing numerous seeds.

Atropanthe sinensis is native to China and is found in woodland and mountainous regions, typically growing in shaded areas at high elevations.
