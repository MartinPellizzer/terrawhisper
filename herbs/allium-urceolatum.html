Purple Onion (Allium Urceolatum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Allium urceolatum.

Purple onion, scientifically known as Allium urceolatum, is a medicinal herb native to the Mediterranean region, valued for its unique purple bulbs and adaptogenic properties. This plant is traditionally used for its potential to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and enhance respiratory health due to its high concentration of sulfur compounds and antioxidants. Historically, it has been employed in various cultures for treating respiratory ailments, digestive issues, and as a natural remedy for colds and coughs. In modern wellness practices, it is gaining attention for its potential role in supporting cardiovascular health and as a dietary supplement for its adaptogenic effects. Notably, its distinctive violet hue and rare active compounds make it a unique ingredient in both culinary and medicinal applications.
