What's the taxonomical classification of Pseuderanthemum coudercii?

Pseuderanthemum coudercii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales. It is a representative of the family Acanthaceae, specifically falling under the genus Pseuderanthemum, with the distinct species designation coudercii.
