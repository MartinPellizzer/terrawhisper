Urariopsis cordifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Urariopsis cordifolia?

Urariopsis cordifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Fabales. This plant is a part of the family Fabaceae, specifically positioned within the genus Urariopsis, and is identified by its unique species name, cordifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Urariopsis cordifolia has cordate to ovate leaves with entire margins and an acute apex, supported by woody stems and a branching habit. Its flowers are small and arranged in compact inflorescences characteristic of the Asteraceae family.
