Elaeagnus gonyanthes

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Elaeagnus gonyanthes has lanceolate to elliptic leaves that are typically 3 to 7 centimeters long, featuring entire margins and a coriaceous texture. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, eventually giving rise to fleshy, ovoid drupes. These fruits change color during ripening, transitioning from green to a more vibrant shade.
