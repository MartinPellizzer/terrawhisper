What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Cyperus eleusinoides has a perennial rhizomatous growth habit characterized by triangular stems that reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged tightly within these spikelets, which are supported by bracts that often resemble continuing leafy stems.
