Deparia okuboaana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following the order Polypodiales, the plant is placed within the family Aspleniaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy concludes with its designation in the genus Deparia, specifically as the species Deparia okuboaana.
