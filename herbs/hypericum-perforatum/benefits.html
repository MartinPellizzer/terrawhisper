Studies suggest that St. John's wort may be as effective as some prescription antidepressants, with fewer side effects, though it can interact with other medications. It is also used to alleviate symptoms of seasonal affective disorder and may have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

However, due to its potential for drug interactions, it should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
