1. Alleviates symptoms of depression

St. John's wort alleviates symptoms of depression because it contains several chemical compounds, including hypericin and hyperforin, that may influence neurotransmitter levels in the brain.

These compounds may increase the availability of certain neurotransmitters, such as serotonin, norepinephrine, and dopamine, which play a crucial role in regulating mood. Additionally, St. John's wort has been found to inhibit the enzyme that breaks down serotonin, norepinephrine, and dopamine, resulting in higher levels of these neurotransmitters in the brain. This mechanism of action is similar to that of many conventional antidepressant drugs, making St. John's wort a viable alternative for individuals seeking natural remedies for depression.

However, it is important to note that St. John's wort can interact with several medications, so it is essential to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.
