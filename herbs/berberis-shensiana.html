Berberis shensiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is a member of the family Berberidaceae and is categorized under the genus Berberis, with its specific identification being the species shensiana.

What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

Berberis shensiana contains high concentrations of isoquinoline alkaloids, specifically berberine, palmatine, and jatrorrhizine.
