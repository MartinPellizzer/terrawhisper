What is Selliguea nigrovenia?

Selliguea nigrovenia is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae, native to tropical Africa and Asia.

It is a terrestrial fern with a rhizome that is short and compact, bearing 2-pinnate leaves that are 10-20 cm long and 6-12 cm wide, with the lower pinnae being larger than the upper ones. The leaves have a dark green color with a lighter green or yellowish midrib, and the veins are pale yellow or white. The fronds are borne on a long stalk, and the plant reaches a height of 30-60 cm.

The sporangia are borne on the lower surface of the fronds, and the plant reproduces via spores.
