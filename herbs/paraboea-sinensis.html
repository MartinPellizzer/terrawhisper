Paraboea sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Paraboea sinensis?

Paraboea sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Lamiales and the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Paraboea, with its specific designation being the species sinensis.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various inflammatory conditions, respiratory ailments, and skin disorders. It is commonly applied in traditional practices to alleviate symptoms of asthma and chronic coughs. The plant also serves as a remedy for skin infections and topical inflammations due to its medicinal properties. Furthermore, it is sometimes utilized to address digestive issues and general bodily heat. Its diverse applications make it a significant component in herbal medicine.
