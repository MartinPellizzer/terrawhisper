Vicia Faba: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Vicia faba, commonly known as the broad bean or fava bean, is a leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region, widely cultivated for its edible seeds.

This herb is rich in nutrients such as protein, fiber, folate, and various minerals, offering numerous health benefits including improved digestion and support for blood health.

Therapeutically, it may help in managing anemia, lowering cholesterol, and supporting liver function.

The bioactive constituents of Vicia faba include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
