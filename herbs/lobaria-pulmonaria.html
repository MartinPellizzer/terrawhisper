Lobaria pulmonaria has many herbal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, that are used for medicinal purposes.

The tea made from Lobaria pulmonaria is used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, due to its expectorant properties.

Possible Side Effects of lobaria pulmonaria

Lobaria pulmonaria can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems.

Additionally, improper use of Lobaria pulmonaria may cause interactions with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using this herb to avoid potential side effects and ensure safe and effective treatment.
