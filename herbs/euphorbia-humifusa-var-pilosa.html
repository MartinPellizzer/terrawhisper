Euphorbia humifusa var. pilosa

Euphorbia humifusa var. pilosa, also known as Creeping Euphorbia, is a perennial plant in the Euphorbiaceae family, native to East Asia, particularly in China, Japan, and Korea.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal effects.

The health benefits of Euphorbia humifusa var. pilosa include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate pain, and improve immune function, making it a valuable treatment for various conditions such as arthritis, eczema, and respiratory infections.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be used to treat a range of health issues, although its use should be under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
