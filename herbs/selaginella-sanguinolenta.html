What's the taxonomical classification of Selaginella sanguinolenta?

Selaginella sanguinolenta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Lycopodiidae. The plant's taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Selaginellales and the family Selaginellaceae. Ultimately, it is placed within the genus Selaginella, with its specific identification being the species sanguinolenta.
