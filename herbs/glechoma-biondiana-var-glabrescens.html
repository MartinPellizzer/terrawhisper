Glechoma biondiana var. glabrescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Glechoma biondiana var. glabrescens?

Glechoma biondiana var. glabrescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and is a member of the order Lamiales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Lamiaceae, specifically within the genus Glechoma, ultimately identifying it as the variety glabrescens of the species Glechoma biondiana.
