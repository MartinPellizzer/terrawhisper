What is Botschantzevia karatavica?

Botschantzevia karatavica is a perennial plant species in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are arranged in racemose corymbose inflorescences. The plant has ovate-lanceolate leaves, which are 2-3 times longer than the petiole, with a cordate base and an acute apex. Botschantzevia karatavica has a taproot system, with the stem being glabrous and herbaceous.

The plant is native to Kazakhstan and has a height of 20-40 cm.
