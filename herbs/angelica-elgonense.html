Angelica elgonense

Angelica elgonense, also known as African Angelica, is a perennial herb belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to tropical Africa.

The medicinal properties of Angelica elgonense lie in its roots and stems, which contain essential oils and compounds with anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to treat fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, as well as its use as a diuretic to aid in digestion and reduce blood pressure.

In traditional herbal medicine, Angelica elgonense can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or decoction, and is also used in the form of essential oils and tinctures to harness its medicinal properties.
