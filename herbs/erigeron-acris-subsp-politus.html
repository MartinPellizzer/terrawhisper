Erigeron acris subsp. politus

What's the taxonomical classification of Erigeron acris subsp. politus?

Erigeron acris subsp. politus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and is part of the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is categorized under the genus Erigeron, specifically identified by its full taxonomic designation as the species Erigeron acris subsp. politus.
