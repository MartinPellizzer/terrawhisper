What is Mapania purpuriceps?

Mapania purpuriceps is a species of plant that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb with rhizomatous growth habit, characterized by a short stem (0.5-1.5 cm) and linear, flat, and glabrous leaves (1.5-3.5 mm wide, 5-30 cm long). The leaves are distichous, with a ligule (0.2-0.3 mm long) and a glabrous margin. The inflorescence is a spike (2-10 cm long) with 4-8 racemes, bearing 2-10 spikelets per raceme, each containing 2-6 flowers.

The spikelets are 3.5-6.5 mm long, with a 1-2 mm long, green or purple glume and a 1-2 mm long, green or purple lemma.
