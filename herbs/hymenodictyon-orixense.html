Hymenodictyon orixense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. These identified compounds consist of one iridoid glycoside, loganin (1), and four coumarins, which are scopoletin (2), scopolin (3), hymexealsin (4), and scopoletin 7-O-β-D-xylopyranosyl-(1→6)-β-D-glucopyranoside (5). Compounds 1-5 demonstrated acetylcholinesterase (AChE) inhibitory activity within the range of 13.92-34.18% when tested at a concentration of 100 μg/mL.
