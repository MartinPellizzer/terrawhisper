Bidens tripartita may cause nausea, which is one of the most commonly reported side effects among individuals who consume this herb.

Additionally, Bidens tripartita can result in stomach upset, characterized by bloating, cramping, and discomfort, further exacerbating digestive issues.

Some people may also experience allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, or more severe symptoms like anaphylaxis, especially if they have a known sensitivity to plants in the Asteraceae family. Furthermore, the herb may trigger respiratory issues in individuals with asthma or other respiratory conditions, as it can act as an irritant when inhaled or ingested.

It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using Bidens tripartita, especially for those with pre-existing medical conditions or who are taking other medications.
