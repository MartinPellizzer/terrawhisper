Distichlis scoparia

Distichlis scoparia, also known as saltwort or glasswort, is a perennial halophyte plant that thrives in salt marshes and coastal areas.

Traditionally, it has been used for its medicinal properties, including its ability to reduce inflammation, treat digestive issues, and act as a diuretic.

The plant is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, offering various health benefits such as improving cardiovascular health, reducing blood pressure, and aiding in the treatment of respiratory issues.

Herbal preparations of Distichlis scoparia can be made into teas, infusions, or salves, and are often combined with other herbs to enhance their medicinal properties.
