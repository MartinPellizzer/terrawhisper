What is the geographical distribution of this plant?

This plant is endemic to the mountains of eastern California, specifically occurring within the high elevations of the Inyo and Mono counties. It is most frequently identified in the rugged terrain surrounding the White Mountains and the Eastern Sierra range. These populations typically occupy rocky slopes and alpine environments characterized by granitic soils. Because it is restricted to these specific montane habitats, the species has a very narrow and localized range.
