10 Possible Side Effects Of Aethusa Cynapium (Fool'S Parsley)

These symptoms can occur due to the herb's emetic and purgative properties, which can cause irritation to the stomach and intestines.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen people's lives by leading to dehydration, electrolyte imbalances, and even kidney damage.
