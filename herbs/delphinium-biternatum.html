What's the taxonomical classification of Delphinium biternatum?

Delphinium biternatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by its membership in the genus Delphinium and its designation as the species biternatum.
