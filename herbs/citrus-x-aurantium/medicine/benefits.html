10 Health Benefits Of Citrus × Aurantium (Bitter Orange)

Citrus × aurantium, also known as bitter orange, has health benefits such as aiding in weight loss, improving digestion, and reducing anxiety due to its medicinal properties of being a natural stimulant and antioxidant.

These properties can help improve people's lives by enhancing energy levels and reducing symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, allowing them to be more productive and comfortable in their daily activities.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Citrus × aurantium.

1. Reduces anxiety

Citrus × aurantium reduces anxiety because it has a unique combination of compounds that interact with the brain and nervous system.

The primary active ingredient, synephrine, has been shown to stimulate the release of neurotransmitters such as serotonin, which can help alleviate symptoms of anxiety.

Additionally, the essential oils present in bitter orange, including limonene and linalool, have a calming and relaxing effect, further contributing to its anxiolytic properties.

2. Lowers blood pressure

Citrus × aurantium lowers blood pressure because of its unique combination of flavonoids and terpenes, which have potent antioxidant and vasodilatory properties.

These compounds help to relax blood vessels and improve circulation, reducing blood pressure in individuals with hypertension.

The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in blood pressure regulation, contributing to a decrease in blood pressure.

3. Relieves insomnia

Citrus × aurantium relieves insomnia because it contains flavonoids and phenolic acids that have a sedative effect on the body.

These compounds help to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, making it easier to fall asleep.

The essential oils present in bitter orange, such as limonene and geraniol, also contribute to its insomnia-relieving properties, promoting a restful and rejuvenating sleep.

4. Stimulates appetite

Citrus × aurantium stimulates appetite because it contains a high concentration of limonoids, a class of compounds that have been shown to increase the release of ghrelin, the "hunger hormone".

These compounds also have a direct effect on the brain's appetite centers, enhancing the sensation of hunger and promoting food intake.

This makes bitter orange a popular ingredient in diet suppressants and weight loss supplements.

5. Increases metabolism

Citrus × aurantium increases metabolism because it contains norepinephrine, a hormone that stimulates the breakdown of fat cells.

This thermogenic effect enhances the body's ability to burn fat for energy, leading to increased caloric expenditure.

As a result, individuals who consume bitter orange supplements may experience weight loss and improved weight management, making it a popular ingredient in weight loss products.

6. Supports weight loss

Citrus × aurantium supports weight loss because it contains synephrine, a compound that stimulates fat burning and boosts metabolism.

Synephrine increases the activity of lipolysis, the breakdown of fat cells, allowing the body to burn stored fat more efficiently.

This natural stimulant also enhances thermogenesis, the production of heat, which further accelerates fat loss and improves overall weight management.

7. Enhances mood

Citrus × aurantium enhances mood because of its unique combination of compounds that interact with the brain and nervous system.

The essential oils present in bitter orange, such as limonene and linalool, have been shown to stimulate neurotransmitters like serotonin and dopamine, which play a crucial role in regulating mood and emotional responses.

This synergy can lead to improved mental well-being and a reduction in symptoms of anxiety and depression.

8. Reduces inflammation

Citrus × aurantium reduces inflammation because of its potent flavonoid and limonoid content.

These bioactive compounds possess strong antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes.

Studies have shown that bitter orange extracts exhibit significant anti-inflammatory activity, making it a potential natural remedy for various inflammatory conditions, including arthritis and allergies.

9. Improves digestion

Citrus × aurantium improves digestion because it stimulates digestive enzymes and enhances gut motility.

The peel and fruit of this plant contain a high concentration of citral, which helps to ease nausea and reduce inflammation in the digestive tract. As a result, bitter orange is often used as a natural remedy to alleviate symptoms of indigestion, bloating, and gas.

It can also help to regulate bowel movements.

10. Boosts immune system

Citrus × aurantium boosts immune system because it contains high levels of vitamin C, a potent antioxidant that combats oxidative stress and inflammation.

The flavonoids and limonoids present in bitter orange also exhibit immunomodulatory effects, stimulating the production of white blood cells and activating the body's natural killer cells to fight off infections and diseases.
