10 Precautions To Take When Using Citrus Aurantifolia (Lime)

Citrus aurantifolia has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious with its high citrus oil content.

This can cause allergic reactions, such as skin irritation or digestive issues, in some individuals. For example, using lime essential oil without dilution may lead to skin contact dermatitis, while consuming large quantities of lime juice may cause stomach upset or interact with medications.

Taking these precautions is crucial to avoid adverse effects.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Citrus aurantifolia medicinally.
