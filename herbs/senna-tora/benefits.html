1. Treat constipation

Constipation is a common digestive disorder characterized by infrequent bowel movements, difficulty passing stool, or a sensation of incomplete evacuation.

It often results from slow movement of stool through the intestines, leading to hardened, dry feces that are challenging to expel.

This condition can affect individuals of all ages, though it is more prevalent in older adults, pregnant women, and those with a sedentary lifestyle or poor dietary habits.

Senna tora, a medicinal plant, is known for its ability to alleviate constipation by promoting bowel movements through its natural laxative properties.

Its use is particularly beneficial for those seeking a natural remedy to ease digestive discomfort and restore regularity to their bowel habits.

How this herb helps with treat constipation?

Senna tora treat constipation because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds have mild laxative properties that stimulate bowel movements. The alkaloids, particularly sennosides, act as contact laxatives by increasing intestinal motility. Flavonoids contribute to the plant's overall digestive benefits by reducing inflammation in the gastrointestinal tract.

Together, these compounds help relieve constipation and promote regular bowel function.
