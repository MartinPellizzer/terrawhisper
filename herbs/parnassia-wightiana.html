Parnassia wightiana

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Parnassia wightiana has a basal rosette of ovate to lanceolate leaves that are relatively thin and lack prominent venation. The reproductive structures include a central gynoecium surrounded by several stamens that facilitate pollination.
