Serrate Saussurea (Saussurea Maximowiczii F. Serrata)

Saussurea maximowiczii f. serrata, commonly known as the Tibetan or Chinese snow lotus, is a traditional medicinal plant widely used in Tibetan and Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits. It is valued for its high concentration of active compounds, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and essential oils, which are believed to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-boosting properties. This plant is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as to enhance physical endurance and alleviate fatigue. Its roots and rhizomes are often prepared as decoctions or powders to support overall wellness and vitality. Due to its growing popularity, there is ongoing research to validate its therapeutic potential and explore its applications in modern pharmacology.
