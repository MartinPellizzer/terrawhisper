Trollius buddae

What's the taxonomical classification of Trollius buddae?

Trollius buddae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically situated within the genus Trollius, and is identified by the species epithet buddae.
