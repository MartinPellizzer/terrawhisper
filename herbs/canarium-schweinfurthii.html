Furthermore, the oil significantly increased HDL levels (38.61 ± 1.63 mg/dL; 27.33 ± 3.28 mg/dL), SOD enzymatic activity (85.00% ± 17.32%; 91.66% ± 2.88%), and catalase activity (10.67 ± 0.36 μM/min/mL; 10.85 ± 0.47 μM/min/mL).
