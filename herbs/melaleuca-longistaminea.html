Melaleuca longistaminea

Melaleuca longistaminea, commonly known as the yellow paperbark, is a plant species belonging to the myrtle family (Myrtaceae).

The therapeutic actions of the plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic compounds, which have been shown to exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anti-cancer properties.

The plant's extracts have been used to treat various health conditions, including wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues, showcasing its potential as a valuable natural remedy.
