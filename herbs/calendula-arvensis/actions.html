Field Marigold (Calendula arvensis) Actions

Field Marigold exhibits several key therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and carminative properties. It also acts as a diuretic and bitter tonic, aiding digestion and reducing spasms. Its antispasmodic and astringent qualities help soothe irritation, while its sedative and stimulant effects support overall balance. Additionally, it functions as an expectorant to clear respiratory congestion.
