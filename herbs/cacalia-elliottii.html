Cacalia elliottii

Cacalia elliottii, also known as Elliott's cacalia, is a perennial plant species native to North America, belonging to the family Compositae (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, which are attributed to its saponins, flavonoids, and other bioactive compounds.

The health benefits of Cacalia elliottii include its potential to alleviate pain, reduce fever, and support the immune system, as well as its use in treating respiratory issues and wounds.
