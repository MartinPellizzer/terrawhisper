Filipendula Ulmaria Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Filipendula ulmaria, commonly known as meadow sweet, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as salicylic acid, tannins, flavonoids, and mucilage, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
