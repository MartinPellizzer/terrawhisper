Paulownia kawakamii

What's the taxonomical classification of Paulownia kawakamii?

Paulownia kawakamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is classified under the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Paulowniaceae, falling within the genus Paulownia, and is specifically identified by the species name kawakamii.

While scientific studies on human toxicity are limited, many members of the Paulownia genus contain compounds that may trigger sensitivities in susceptible individuals. Direct contact with the plant's fluids might lead to redness, itching, or localized swelling on the skin. Additionally, inhaling airborne particles from the flowers could potentially provoke sneezing or asthma-like symptoms. It is advisable to use protective gear when handling the plant to minimize these potential reactions.
