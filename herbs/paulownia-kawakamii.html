Paulownia kawakamii

What's the taxonomical classification of Paulownia kawakamii?

Paulownia kawakamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is classified under the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Paulowniaceae, falling within the genus Paulownia, and is specifically identified by the species name kawakamii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Paulownia kawakamii has large, simple, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically 10 to 25 centimeters long and characterized by a dull green surface with serrated margins.

Its inflorescences consist of clusters of bell-shaped flowers that transition from light pink to a deeper rose hue. These blossoms are supported by long pedicels and emerge prominently from the axils of the foliage.
