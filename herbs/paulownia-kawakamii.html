Paulownia kawakamii has large, simple, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically 10 to 25 centimeters long and characterized by a dull green surface with serrated margins. Its inflorescences consist of clusters of bell-shaped flowers that transition from light pink to a deeper rose hue. These blossoms are supported by long pedicels and emerge prominently from the axils of the foliage.
