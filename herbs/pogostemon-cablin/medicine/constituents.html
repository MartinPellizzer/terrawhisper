10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pogostemon Cablin (Patchouli)

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. They can improve skin health by reducing acne and inflammation, as well as promote relaxation and reduce stress levels.

For example, people with skin conditions such as eczema may benefit from patchouli oil's soothing and anti-inflammatory effects, leading to improved skin comfort and reduced symptoms.

Cablinic acid has also been shown to exhibit anti-cancer activity and may help protect against neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.

Its unique structure and bioactive properties make it a promising area of research in the development of new therapeutic agents.
