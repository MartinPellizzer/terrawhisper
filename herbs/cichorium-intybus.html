Cichorium Intybus: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cichorium intybus, commonly known as chicory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia, widely used for its leaves, roots, and flowers.

This herb is valued for its mild bitterness and is often consumed as a coffee substitute or in salads.

Health benefits of chicory include supporting digestive health, promoting liver function, and potentially aiding in weight management.

Its therapeutic actions involve detoxification, anti-inflammatory effects, and antioxidant properties, attributed to bioactive constituents such as inulin, sesquiterpene lactones, and flavonoids.
