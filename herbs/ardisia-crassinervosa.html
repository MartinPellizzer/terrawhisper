Ardisia crassinervosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the taxonomic order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Ardisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, crassinervosa.
