Rubus swinhoei

What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus swinhoei?

Rubus swinhoei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Rubus, and its specific species designation, swinhoei.

Rubus swinhoei is prepared primarily as a decoction through boiling the dried leaves, stems, or fruits in water for medicinal use. These various forms ensure that the plant's bioactive constituents are delivered effectively to the body.

It is essential to consult a healthcare provider before incorporating this species into a medicinal regimen.
