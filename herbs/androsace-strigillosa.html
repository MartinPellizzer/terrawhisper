What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace strigillosa?

Androsace strigillosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is specifically situated within the family Primulaceae, a group known for its diverse flowering members. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Androsace, with its specific identification being the species strigillosa.
