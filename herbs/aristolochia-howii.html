What side effects this plant can have?

This plant can cause severe nephrotoxicity, carcinogenicities, and uropathy, along with gastrointestinal issues like nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, and diarrhea. The presence of aristolochic acids in the plant leads to progressive kidney damage and the development of malignant tumors. These toxic compounds are also known to cause permanent damage to the urinary tract through uropathy. Beyond these serious systemic risks, individuals consuming the plant often experience acute digestive distress.
