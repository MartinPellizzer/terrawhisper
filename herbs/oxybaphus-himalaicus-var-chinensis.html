What's the taxonomical classification of Oxybaphus himalaicus var. chinensis?

Oxybaphus himalaicus var. chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae, placing it within the vast group of multicellular, photosynthetic organisms. It is classified under the phylum Streptophyta and falls within the class Equisetopsida. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it is situated in the order Caryophyllales and belongs to the family Nyctaginaea. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Oxybaphus, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Oxybaphus himalaicus var. chinensis.
