10 Possible Side Effects Of Copaifera Officinalis (Balsam Fir)

Copaifera officinalis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects are often caused by excessive consumption or sensitivity to the herb's volatile oils. For instance, individuals with sensitive skin may experience redness, itching, and swelling, leading to discomfort during daily activities.

Furthermore, gastrointestinal issues may worsen conditions like irritable bowel syndrome, affecting daily life and overall well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Copaifera officinalis if used imporperly.
