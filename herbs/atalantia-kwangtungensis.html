Atalantia kwangtungensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Atalantia kwangtungensis has small, evergreen leaves that are elliptical to oblong in shape with entire margins and an acute apex. The plant typically exhibits a shrubby growth habit with slender, woody branches. Its foliage often possesses a slightly leathery texture and a dark green coloration on the upper surface. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, eventually developing into small, globose fruits. These fruits are characterized by a relatively thin rind and a fleshy interior containing multiple seeds.
