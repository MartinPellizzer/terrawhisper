Antenoron filiforme var. neofiliforme

What's the taxonomical classification of Antenoron filiforme var. neofiliforme?

Antenoron filiforme var. neofiliforme belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This specific variety is situated within the family Polygonaceae and is identified under the genus Antenoron, representing a distinct taxonomic variation of the species Antenoron filiforme.
