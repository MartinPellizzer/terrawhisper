What's the taxonomical classification of Gaultheria leucocarpa var. crenulata?

Gaultheria leucocarpa var. crenulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ericales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Ericaceae, placing it within the genus Gaultheria.
