Common Honeysuckle (Lonicera caprifolium) Compounds

Common Honeysuckle contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Coumarin and glycosides support its antimicrobial effects, while saponins and terpenoids may enhance immune function. Anthocyanins, essential oils, and alkaloids further contribute to its therapeutic and medicinal value.
