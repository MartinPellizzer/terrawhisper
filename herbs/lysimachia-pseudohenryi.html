What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia pseudohenryi?

Lysimachia pseudohenryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Ericales, this plant is situated in the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Lysimachia, specifically identified by its species name, pseudohenryi.
