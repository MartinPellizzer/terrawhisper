What is Rinorea lindeniana?

Rinorea lindeniana is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is an evergreen climber with woody stems and ovate-lanceolate leaves that are 2-6 cm long and have 3-5 pairs of lateral nerves. The leaves are coriaceous, glabrous, and have a cuneate base. The inflorescence is a terminal corymb, bearing small, greenish-white flowers with 5 petals and 10 stamens.

The plant produces a capsule with 2-3 seeds, each seed being 2.5-3 mm long and having a black testa.
