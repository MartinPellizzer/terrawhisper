What's the taxonomical classification of Liparis inaperta?

Liparis inaperta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asparagales, it falls under the family Orchidaceae, which is the distinctive group of orchids. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Liparis and the species epithet inaperta.
