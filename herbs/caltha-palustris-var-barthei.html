Caltha palustris var. barthei

What's the taxonomical classification of Caltha palustris var. barthei?

Caltha palustris var. barthei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified as a variety within the genus Caltha, where its full taxonomic designation is Caltha palustris var. barthei.
