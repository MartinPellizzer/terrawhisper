What's the taxonomical classification of Teucrium anlungense?

Teucrium anlungense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Teucrium and is specifically designated as the species anlungense.
