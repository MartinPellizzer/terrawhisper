Eremospatha cabrae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Arecaceae, which places it among the palms. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Eremospatha and the species cabrae.
