10 Possible Side Effects Of Lupinus Albus (White Lupin)

Lupinus albus has some side effects when used improperly, such as kidney damage, due to the high levels of isoflavones and saponins present in the herb.

Consuming excessive amounts can cause gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, diarrhea, and abdominal pain.

If left unchecked, these side effects can worsen and lead to long-term health complications, affecting daily life and overall well-being.
