Campylandra wattii

What's the taxonomical classification of Campylandra wattii?

Campylandra wattii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Asparagales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Asparagaceae, specifically falling under the genus Campylandra, with its unique designation being the species wattii.
