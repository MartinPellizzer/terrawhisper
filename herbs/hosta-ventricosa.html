What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hosta ventricosa has thick, cordate leaves that measure up to 15 centimeters in length, characterized by a distinctively puckered or rugose texture and a deep blue-green coloration. As the foliage matures, the prominent venation creates a deeply textured surface that distinguishes it from other hosta varieties.
