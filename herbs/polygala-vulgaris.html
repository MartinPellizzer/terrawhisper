Polygala Vulgaris

Polygala vulgaris, commonly known as milkwort, is a perennial herb belonging to the Polygalaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma.

The therapeutic actions of Polygala vulgaris include expectorant, demulcent, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it a popular remedy for soothing irritated mucous membranes and reducing inflammation.

The bioactive constituents of Polygala vulgaris include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties and potential health benefits.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Polygala vulgaris, especially if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking medications.
