Polygala Vulgaris

Polygala vulgaris, commonly known as the common milkvetch, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

It is often employed in Chinese and other traditional medicinal systems to support respiratory and digestive functions. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and flavonoids, which may contribute to its therapeutic effects. In modern herbal practices, it is sometimes used to alleviate symptoms of coughs, bronchitis, and digestive discomfort.

However, more scientific research is needed to fully validate its medicinal properties and ensure safe usage.
