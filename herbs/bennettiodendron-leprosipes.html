What's the taxonomical classification of Bennettiodendron leprosipes?

Bennettiodendron leprosipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, which falls under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Salicaceae. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Bennettiodendron, and its specific biological designation is completed by its species name, leprosipes.
