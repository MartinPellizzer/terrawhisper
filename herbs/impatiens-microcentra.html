What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Impatiens microcentra has small, succulent, elliptic to lanceolate leaves with entire margins and a slightly fleshy texture. Its flowers are relatively diminutive compared to other species in the genus, often displaying delicate hues. The reproductive structures include a characteristic nectar spur located at the rear of the corolla. These morphological traits allow the species to thrive in specific shaded, moist microhabitats.
