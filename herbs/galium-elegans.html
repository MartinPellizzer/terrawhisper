What's the taxonomical classification of Galium elegans?

Galium elegans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the Rubiaceae family, falling under the genus Galium, with its specific designation being the species elegans.

      What are the herbal preparations of Galium elegans?

      Galium elegans is prepared primarily as an aqueous infusion or a decoction using the dried aerial parts of the plant to extract its bioactive compounds. These liquid preparations are often consumed as teas to utilize the plant's specific chemical properties in traditional herbalism.
