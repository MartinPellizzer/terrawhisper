What's the taxonomical classification of Loxostigma griffithii?

Loxostigma griffithii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Gesneriaceae, which places it within the genus Loxostigma. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, griffithii.
