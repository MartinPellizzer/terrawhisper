Cyanotis vaga belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Commelinales. This plant is a member of the family Commelinaceae, specifically belonging to the genus Cyanotis, and is identified by its unique species designation, vaga.
