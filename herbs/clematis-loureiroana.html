What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis loureiroana?

Clematis loureiroana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically situated within the genus Clematis, and is identified by its unique specific epithet, loureiroana.
