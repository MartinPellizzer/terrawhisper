Neopallasia pectinata

What's the taxonomical classification of Neopallasia pectinata?

Nepallasia pectinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Ultimately, this specific plant is identified by the genus Neopallasia and is designated as the species pectinata.
