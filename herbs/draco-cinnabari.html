Draco cinnabari

Draco cinnabari, also known as the dragon's blood tree, is a unique tree species native to the islands of Socotra in Yemen, characterized by its bright red sap and umbrella-like canopy.

The medicinal properties of draco cinnabari have been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including wounds, fever, and rheumatism, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of draco cinnabari include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and support immune function.

Draco cinnabari is typically harvested for its sap, which can be used to make herbal preparations such as tinctures, capsules, and infusions.
