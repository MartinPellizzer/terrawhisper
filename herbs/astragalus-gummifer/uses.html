Gum Arabic Tree is used to treat chronic fatigue by supporting energy levels and immune function. It helps manage autoimmune diseases and disorders by modulating the immune system. It also alleviates allergic reactions and respiratory conditions like asthma and bronchitis.

Gum Arabic Tree (Astragalus gummifer) is used in herbal medicine to address chronic fatigue due to its high nutrient content, including vitamins and minerals. Gum Arabic Tree (Astragalus gummifer) is used in herbal medicine to support immune function and may help modulate autoimmune responses.
