Gum Arabic Tree (Astragalus gummifer) Compounds

The primary compounds of Gum Arabic Tree include flavonoids, which act as antioxidants, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory properties. Saponins and glycosides contribute to its emulsifying and detoxifying effects. Tannins and terpenoids provide astringent and antimicrobial actions, while coumarin and essential oils add to its aromatic and antispasmodic qualities.
