Angelica Sinensis: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Angelica sinensis, commonly known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb widely used for its numerous health benefits.

It is believed to support women's health, particularly in regulating menstrual cycles and alleviating symptoms of premenstrual syndrome.

The therapeutic actions of dong quai include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and circulatory stimulating properties.

Its bioactive constituents include compounds such as ligustilide,藁本内酯, and ferulic acid, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
