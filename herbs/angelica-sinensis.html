Angelica Sinensis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Angelica sinensis, commonly known as Dong Quai, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb widely used for its purported health benefits.

It is believed to support hormonal balance, alleviate menstrual discomfort, and enhance overall vitality.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as coumarins, flavonoids, and volatile oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Angelica sinensis can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it versatile for various herbal formulations.
