Hedychium villosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a prominent member of the family Zingiberaceae, commonly known as the ginger family, and is specifically classified under the genus Hedychium. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific classification as the species villosum.
