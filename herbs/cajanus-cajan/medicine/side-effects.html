10 Possible Side Effects Of Cajanus Cajan (Pigeon Pea)

Cajanus cajan has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal discomfort, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, individual allergies, and concurrent use of other medications.

These side effects can worsen people's lives by causing digestive issues, respiratory problems, and even increased risk of bleeding or organ damage.
