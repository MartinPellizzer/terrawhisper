Oreocharis forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically situated within the genus Oreocharis, with the distinct species designation forrestii.
