10 Possible Side Effects Of Tanacetum Vulgare (Tansy)

Tanacetum vulgare has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, dizziness, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by ingesting large quantities or using it in combination with other medications. For example, individuals with sensitive stomachs may experience severe abdominal pain, diarrhea, or vomiting, which can lead to dehydration and impact daily activities.

Additionally, allergic reactions can range from mild itching to life-threatening anaphylaxis, causing people to miss work, school, or important events.
