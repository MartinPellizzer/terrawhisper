What's the taxonomical classification of Orobanche megalantha?

Orobanche megalantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its evolutionary path continues through the order Lamiales, placing it within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Orobanche, and its distinct species designation, megalantha.
