1. Treats diabetes

Diabetes mellitus, a chronic metabolic disorder, is characterized by elevated blood glucose levels due to insufficient insulin production or impaired insulin function.

This condition arises when the body either doesn't produce enough insulin or cannot effectively utilize the insulin it produces, leading to hyperglycemia.

It commonly affects individuals with genetic predispositions, sedentary lifestyles, or poor dietary habits, particularly in older adults and those with obesity.

Garcinia mangostana, also known as mangosteen, has been studied for its potential to manage diabetes by improving insulin sensitivity and reducing glucose absorption.

Its bioactive compounds may support metabolic regulation, offering a natural complement to conventional diabetes management strategies.

How this herb helps with treats diabetes?

Garcinia mangostana treats diabetes because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which have been shown to improve insulin sensitivity and regulate blood glucose levels.

These compounds help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation, common complications in diabetes. The presence of xanthones, a type of flavonoid, contributes to the plant's ability to enhance glucose uptake by cells. Additionally, Garcinia mangostana supports pancreatic function, aiding in the production and release of insulin.

Research suggests that regular consumption of this plant may help manage blood sugar levels effectively.
