Coptosapelta diffusa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is categorized within the genus Coptosapelta, with its specific designation being the species diffusa.
