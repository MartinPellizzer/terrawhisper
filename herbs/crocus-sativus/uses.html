Saffron Crocus (Crocus sativus) Uses

Saffron Crocus is used to treat respiratory infections, inflammatory conditions, and asthma. It helps reduce inflammation and soothe symptoms of coughs and colds. It also addresses digestive discomfort, gastrointestinal disorders, and gastric ulcers by supporting digestive health and reducing irritation.

Saffron Crocus (Crocus sativus) Monograph
