What's the taxonomical classification of Notoseris melanantha?

Notoseris melanantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asterales and into the family Asteraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Notoseris, and its unique species designation, melanantha.
