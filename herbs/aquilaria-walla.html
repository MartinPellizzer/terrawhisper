Aquilaria walla

Aquilaria walla, commonly known as the Agarwood plant, is a type of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, known for its unique properties and uses.

The health benefits of Aquilaria walla include its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties, which may help to promote wound healing, reduce pain and inflammation, and protect against infection.
