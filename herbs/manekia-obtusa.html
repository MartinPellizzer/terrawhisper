Possible Side Effects of Manekia obtusa

Manekia obtusa can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

Ingesting large quantities of the plant's extracts can lead to headaches, nausea, and vomiting, while prolonged skin contact may cause rashes and inflammation.

Additionally, using Manekia obtusa for medicinal purposes without proper guidance can interact with other medications and worsen underlying health conditions, such as kidney or liver disease.

In severe cases, improper use of the plant can even trigger anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction.
