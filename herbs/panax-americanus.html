American Ginseng (Panax americanus)

American Ginseng (Panax americanus) is a member of the Araliaceae family, native to Eastern United States, Southeastern United States, and Eastern Canada. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and rhizomes have been used for decoctions, infusions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
