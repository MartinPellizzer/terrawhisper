What's the taxonomical classification of Rhodiola quadrifida?

Rhodiola quadrifidida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Saxifrages (Saxifragales). This plant is a member of the family Crassulaceae and is further categorized into the genus Rhodiola, ultimately reaching its specific classification as the species quadrifida.
