This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, which include the treatment of digestive issues, such as constipation and diarrhea, due to its purgative properties.

The therapeutic actions of Senna bicapsularis are attributed to its ability to stimulate bowel movements and relieve symptoms associated with irritable bowel syndrome.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include anthraquinones, which are responsible for its laxative effects, and flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties.
