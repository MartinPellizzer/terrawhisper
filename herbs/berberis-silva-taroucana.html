What's the taxonomical classification of Berberis silva-taroucana?

Berberis silva-taroucaana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a specific representative of the family Berberidaceae and falls under the genus Berberis, ultimately reaching its unique taxonomic designation at the species level as Berberis silva-taroucaana.
