What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium stenoglossum?

Cremanthodium stenoglossum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Asterales and falls under the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Cremanthodium and the species name stenoglossum.
