Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. formosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. formosa?

Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. formosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its classification through the class Equisetopsida, it is placed under the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Within this botanical hierarchy, the plant is situated in the genus Lespedeza, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Lespedeza thunbergii subsp. formosa.
