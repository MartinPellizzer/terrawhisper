Purple Thoroughwort (Eupatorium purpureum) Uses

Purple Thoroughwort is used to treat respiratory infections, allergic rhinitis, and cold symptoms. It helps reduce inflammation in the throat, alleviating inflamed tonsils and cough. The herb also supports relief from bronchitis and common cold symptoms. It may assist with arthritis, swelling, and joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

Purple Thoroughwort (Eupatorium purpureum) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Purple Thoroughwort is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and soothing symptoms of colds, bronchitis, and other respiratory infections. Its antiseptic and expectorant properties help clear mucus and ease breathing, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for respiratory support.

2. Allergic rhinitis

Purple Thoroughwort is used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by modulating immune responses. Traditionally, it has been prepared as a tea or tincture to support respiratory health during seasonal allergies.

3. Cold symptoms

Purple Thoroughwort is traditionally used to alleviate cold symptoms such as congestion, coughing, and fever. It is believed to support respiratory health and reduce inflammation. Its active compounds may help clear mucus and ease breathing during colds. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture for internal use.

4. Inflamed tonsils

Purple Thoroughwort is used to soothe inflamed tonsils due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce swelling and pain associated with throat infections. Traditionally, it is prepared as a gargle or infusion to alleviate symptoms. Its active compounds support immune function and tissue healing.

5. Cough

Purple Thoroughwort is used to alleviate coughs, particularly those associated with respiratory infections. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help reduce mucus buildup and soothe irritated airways. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to support respiratory health. Traditionally, it is combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness.

6. Common cold

Purple Thoroughwort is used to support the body's natural defenses during a common cold. It helps reduce symptoms like fever and congestion through its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. Traditionally, it is prepared as a tea or tincture to promote respiratory health and ease discomfort. Its action is supportive rather than curative, aiding in the body's recovery process.

7. Bronchitis

Purple Thoroughwort is used to support respiratory health in cases of bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce mucus buildup and soothe irritated airways, making it a valuable herb in herbal treatments for bronchial inflammation and coughing. Its use is often combined with other respiratory-supporting herbs for enhanced effect.

8. Arthritis

Purple Thoroughwort is used to alleviate arthritis symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce joint pain and swelling, making it a valued herb in herbal medicine for conditions like rheumatism and osteoarthritis. Its active compounds support overall joint health and mobility.

9. Swelling

Purple Thoroughwort is used to reduce swelling due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis and injuries by decreasing inflammation and supporting tissue repair. Its active compounds work to ease pain and promote healing in inflamed areas of the body.

10. Joint pain

Purple Thoroughwort is used to alleviate joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce swelling and stiffness, making it beneficial for conditions like arthritis. Traditionally, it has been applied topically or consumed as a tea to support joint health. Its active compounds may help ease discomfort associated with inflammatory joint disorders.
