Purple Thoroughwort (Eupatorium purpureum) Compounds

Purple Thoroughwort contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which have anti-inflammatory properties, and coumarin, known for its anticoagulant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its antioxidant and astringent qualities. Terpenoids and glycosides may support immune function, while saponins and essential oils provide antimicrobial benefits. Alkaloids and anthocyanins add to its broad pharmacological profile.
