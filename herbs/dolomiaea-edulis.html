Dolomiaea eduleds belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Dolomiaea, culminating in its unique species designation, Dolomiaea eduleds.
