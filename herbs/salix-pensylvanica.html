Salix pensylvanica

This plant is valued for its health benefits, which include its ability to reduce inflammation and alleviate pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The therapeutic actions of Salix pensylvanica are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include salicin, salicylic acid, and flavonoids, that exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities.
