What's the taxonomical classification of Malva crispa?

Malva crispa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Malvales and the family Malvaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Malva and the specific epithet crispa, forming the complete scientific name of the species.
