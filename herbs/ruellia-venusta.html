What's the taxonomical classification of Ruellia venusta?

Ruellia venusta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down its taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Lamiales and the family Acanthaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its membership in the genus Ruelliia, with the specific epithet venusta identifying it as the species Ruelliia venusta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The corolla of the flower is elongated and bell-shaped, specifically adapted to attract long-tongued pollinators.
