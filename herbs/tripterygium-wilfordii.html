Fleeceflower (Tripterygium wilfordii)

Fleeceflower (Tripterygium wilfordii) is a member of the Celastraceae family, native to China, East Asia, and Sichuan Province. Traditionally, its roots, stems, and leaves have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
