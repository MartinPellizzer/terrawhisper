What's the taxonomical classification of Polygala koi?

Polygala koi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Polygalaceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Polygala, specifically identified as the species koi.
