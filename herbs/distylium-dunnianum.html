Distylium dunnianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Distylium dunnianum?

Distylium dunnianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Saxifragales. The plant is further categorized into the family Hamamelidaceae, which contains the genus Distylium, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Distylium dunnianum.
