What to know about Ficus carica before using it medicinally

Ficus carica, commonly known as common fig, is a medicinal herb that has been used to treat various health conditions, including diabetes, high blood pressure, and cardiovascular disease.

The herb is rich in main health benefits, such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anti-diabetic properties, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. The main therapeutic properties of Ficus carica include its ability to inhibit the activity of alpha-glucosidase, an enzyme that breaks down carbohydrates, and its potential to lower lipid profiles and blood pressure. The bioactive compounds present in Ficus carica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and ascorbic acid, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Ficus carica is commonly used to make a herbal preparation called fig syrup, which is often used to treat digestive issues and as a natural sweetener substitute.
