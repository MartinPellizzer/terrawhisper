Sapindus rarak var. velutinus

What's the taxonomical classification of Sapindus rarak var. velutinus?

Sapindus rarak var. velutinus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and follows the taxonomic order Sapindales. It is a member of the family Sapindaceae, falling under the genus Sapindus, and is specifically identified by its botanical designation as the species Sapindus rarak var. velutinus.
