What is Phyllorkis aurea?

Phyllorkis aurea is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, producing a short stem with a few leaves. The plant has ovate to elliptic leaves, 10-20 cm long, with a leathery texture and a green color. The inflorescence is a raceme, 15-30 cm long, bearing 2-5 yellow flowers with a greenish-yellow labellum.

The flowers have a 3-lobed labellum and a sepals and petals with a similar shape and size.
