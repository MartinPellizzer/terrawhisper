What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes chinensis?

Strobilanthes chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is classified under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Acanthaceae, falling under the genus Strobilanthes, with its specific designation being the species chinensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Strobilanthes chinensis has an erect, perennial herbaceous habit characterized by quadrangular stems and opposite, ovate leaves with entire margins and acuminate tips. These spikes bear small, tubular flowers that are often pale blue or lavender in color. The plant's overall structure is defined by its woody base and the arrangement of its leaf veins.
