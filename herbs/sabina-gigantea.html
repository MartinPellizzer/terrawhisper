Sabina gigantea

Sabina gigantea, commonly known as Juniperus sabina, is a species of coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and the Himalayas.

It has been traditionally used in various cultures for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, which can aid in the treatment of various diseases.

The therapeutic actions of Sabina gigantea include its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve respiratory issues, and exert a sedative effect on the nervous system.
