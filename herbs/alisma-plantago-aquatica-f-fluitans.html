Water Plantain (Alisma Plantago-Aquatica F. Fluitans)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Alisma plantago-aquatica f. fluitans.

Water plantain, scientifically known as Alisma plantago-aquatica f. fluitans, is a water-loving medicinal herb native to wetlands and marshes across Asia and Europe. It is revered for its adaptogenic properties and has been traditionally used to support kidney function, reduce swelling, and promote urinary health. In traditional Chinese medicine and Ayurveda, water plantain has been employed to detoxify the body, alleviate fluid retention, and support digestive wellness. Modern wellness applications include its use in herbal formulations for inflammation management and as a natural diuretic. This unique plant is noted for its distinctive mild, slightly sweet flavor and contains compounds like alkaloids and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic potential.
