What's the taxonomical classification of Corylopsis willmottiae?

Corylopsis willmottiae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Saxifragales and falls under the family Hamamelidaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by its placement within the genus Corylopsis, resulting in its unique species designation, Corylopsis willmottiae.
