Aconitum jeholense var. jeholense

What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum jeholense var. jeholense?

Aconitum jeholense var. jeholense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae. Specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Aconitum, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the variety jeholense of the species Aconitum jeholense.
