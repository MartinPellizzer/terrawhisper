Nepeta yazdiana

Nepeta yazdiana, commonly known as Persian catmint, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, and its health benefits include relieving digestive issues, soothing respiratory problems, and acting as an antiseptic.

The therapeutic actions of Nepeta yazdiana include its antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its calming and relaxing effects.

The bioactive constituents of the plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, such as nepetalactone, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions.

Medicinal Parts of Nepeta yazdiana

Nepeta yazdiana has many medicinal parts, such as its leaves, flowers, and roots, which are utilized for various health benefits.

The roots of Nepeta yazdiana are known to contain iridoid glycosides like geniposide and aucubin, which have been used to treat digestive issues and as a diuretic.
