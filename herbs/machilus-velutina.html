Machilus velutina

What's the taxonomical classification of Machilus velutina?

Machilus velutina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Laurales and falls into the family Lauraceae. Furthermore, this plant is part of the genus Machilus, specifically identified by the binomial name species Machilus velutinina.
