What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium tripteropus?

Asplenium tripteropus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, its lineage progresses through the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This specific fern is a member of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, and is ultimately identified by its unique species designation, tripteropus.
