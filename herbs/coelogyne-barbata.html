What's the taxonomical classification of Coelogyne barbata?

Coelogyne barbaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Asparagales. It is a specialized member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Coelogyne, and is identified by the species name barbata.
