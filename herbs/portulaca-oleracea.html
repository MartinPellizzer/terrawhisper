Portulaca (Portulaca oleracea)

Portulaca (Portulaca oleracea) is a member of the Portulacaceae family, native to Southern Africa, Southern United States, and Australia. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and stems have been used for culinary uses, decoctions, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
