Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia) Uses

Scorodonia is primarily used for respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and sinusitis. It helps alleviate symptoms of the common cold, allergic rhinitis, and sore throat. It is also used for tonsillitis and influenza, supporting the body's recovery from respiratory tract infections.

Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia) Monograph
