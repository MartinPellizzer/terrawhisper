Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia) Actions

Scorodonia exhibits several key therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and carminative effects. It also acts as a bitter, astringent, and diuretic, helping to support digestion and urinary health. Its stimulating and antispasmodic properties aid in relieving muscle tension, while its sedative and expectorant actions can calm the nervous system and assist in respiratory conditions.
