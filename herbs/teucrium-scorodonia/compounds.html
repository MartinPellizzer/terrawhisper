Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia) Compounds

The primary compounds in Scorodonia include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile, while tannins provide astringent properties. Terpenoids and coumarin are also present, offering antimicrobial and antispasmodic benefits. Glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further support its therapeutic applications.
