Damnacanthus labordei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Rubiaceae, which defines its botanical relationships. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Damnacanthus, and its specific identification is completed by its species name, labordei.
