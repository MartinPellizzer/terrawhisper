What is Pilosocereus colombianus?

Pilosocereus colombianus is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is a columnar cactus that grows up to 4 meters in height and 15 centimeters in diameter, with 8-12 ribs that are densely covered with fine, yellowish spines. The stems are green, cylindrical and have a slightly waxy texture. The flowers are funnel-shaped, 5-6 centimeters in length and have a red or yellow color, while the fruits are red or yellow, globose and 2-4 centimeters in diameter.

Pilosocereus colombianus is native to the Andean region of Colombia.
