Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pterospermum, and its unique species designation, acerifolium.

    What are the herbal preparations of Pterospermum acerifolium?

    Pterospermum acerifolium is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical paste depending on the specific medicinal requirement. The dried leaves and bark are frequently boiled in water to create a liquid extract used for internal consumption. Users should exercise caution and avoid direct skin contact until the safety profile is more thoroughly established.
