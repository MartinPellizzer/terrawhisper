What's the taxonomical classification of Saurauia thyrsiflora?

Saurauia thyrsiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Ericales and the family Actinidiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Saurauia and is specifically designated as the species thyrsiflora.
