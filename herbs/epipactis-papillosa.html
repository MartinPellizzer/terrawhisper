Epipactis papillosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Further down the lineage, it is placed in the order Asparagales and belongs to the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Epipactis, and its specific species name, papillosa.
