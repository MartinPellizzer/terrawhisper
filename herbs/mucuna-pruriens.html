Mucuna pruriens

Mucuna pruriens, commonly known as Velvet Bean or Kapikacchu, is a tropical legume native to Africa and Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries, offering numerous health benefits, including improved cognitive function, enhanced libido, and stress relief.

The therapeutic actions of Mucuna pruriens are attributed to its ability to increase dopamine levels, reduce anxiety and depression, and improve sleep quality.
