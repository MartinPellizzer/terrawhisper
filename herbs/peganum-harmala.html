What precautions to take before using Peganum harmala medicinally?

The precautions to take before using Peganum harmala medicinally are multifaceted and crucial to ensure safe and effective treatment.

Firstly, it is essential to consult with a qualified healthcare practitioner or herbalist before using Peganum harmala, as its active compounds can interact with certain medications, including MAOIs, SSRIs, and blood thinners. Finally, it is crucial to follow proper dosage guidelines and monitor patients for signs of adverse reactions, such as nausea, vomiting, dizziness, or insomnia, which may occur at higher doses or in sensitive individuals.
