What's the taxonomical classification of Cladrastis platycarpa?

Cladraustis platycarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it within the genus Cladraustis, and it is specifically identified by its species name, platycarpa.
