What is Echeveria hallii?

It has rosettes of fleshy, glaucous leaves, typically 5-8 cm in diameter, with entire margins and a crenate or slightly lobed apex. Leaves are green to blue-green in color, often with a powdery coating, and have a short, stout petiole. The plant produces yellow flowers, 2-3 cm in diameter, on a short peduncle in late summer.

Echeveria hallii is a compact, low-growing species, typically forming a dense rosette of leaves.
