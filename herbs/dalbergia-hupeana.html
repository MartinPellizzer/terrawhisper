What's the taxonomical classification of Dalbergia hupeana?

Dalbergia hupeana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Dalbergia, and its unique species designation, hupeana.
