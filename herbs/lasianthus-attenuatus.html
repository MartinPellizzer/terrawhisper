What's the taxonomical classification of Lasianthus attenuatus?

Lasianthus attenuatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a specific representative of the family Rubiaceae, placing it among the diverse group of coffee-related plants. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Lasianthus, and its unique botanical identity is defined by its specific designation as the species attenuatus.
