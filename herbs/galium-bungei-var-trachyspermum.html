Galium bungei var. trachyspermum

What's the taxonomical classification of Galium bungei var. trachyspermum?

Galium bungei var. trachyspermum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Gentianales. Further taxonomic refinement places it within the family Rubiaceae and the genus Galium, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety trachyspermum of the species Galium bungei.
