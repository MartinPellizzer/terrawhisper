Manglietia conifera

What's the taxonomical classification of Manglietia conifera?

Manglietia conifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Magnoliales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Magnoliaceae, specifically within the genus Manglietia, and it is identified by the species name conifera.
