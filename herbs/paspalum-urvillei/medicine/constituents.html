10 Medicinal Constituents Of Paspalum Urvillei (Urvillea Grass)

Paspalum urvillei has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can alleviate symptoms of various health conditions. For instance, the flavonoids may help reduce inflammation in respiratory tract disorders, while the saponins could improve digestion and gut health.

Moreover, the plant's constituents have been found to possess antibacterial and antifungal activities, making it a potential natural remedy for wound infections and skin irritations, ultimately improving the overall well-being of individuals.
