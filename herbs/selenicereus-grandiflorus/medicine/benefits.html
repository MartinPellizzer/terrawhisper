10 Health Benefits Of Selenicereus Grandiflorus (Queen Of The Night)

3. Kills bacteria

Selenicereus grandiflorus kills bacteria because of its high concentration of organic acids, particularly citric acid and malic acid.

These acids disrupt the bacterial cell membrane, leading to its breakdown and death.

Additionally, the plant's saponin and flavonoid compounds have been shown to exhibit antimicrobial properties, further contributing to its ability to effectively kill a range of bacteria.

Selenicereus grandiflorus eases insomnia because its sweet, intoxicating fragrance helps to calm the mind and promote relaxation.
