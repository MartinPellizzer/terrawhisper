Keteleeria evelyniana

What's the taxonomical classification of Keteleeria evelyniana?

Keteleeria evelyniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Pinidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Pinales and is a member of the family Pinaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Keteleeria and is specifically designated as the species evelyniana.

What are the therapeutic actions of Keteleeria evelyniana?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, wounds, and various inflammatory conditions of the body. Local communities often apply its crushed leaves directly to the affected area to promote healing and reduce swelling. The medicinal properties are believed to help combat bacterial or fungal pathogens that cause surface ailments. Additionally, some practitioners utilize the plant to soothe minor irritations and accelerate the recovery of damaged tissue. This traditional application relies on the chemical compounds found within its foliage to provide relief.
