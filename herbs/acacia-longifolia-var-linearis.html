Acacia longifolia var. linearis

Acacia longifolia var. linearis, commonly known as the prickly acacia, is a plant species belonging to the Acacia genus, native to Australia and parts of the Pacific.

The therapeutic actions of Acacia longifolia var. linearis are attributed to its ability to modulate the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antiviral activity, which has been supported by scientific studies.
