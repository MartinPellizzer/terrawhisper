Carduus acaulis var. rigens

Carduus acaulis var. rigens, commonly known as the stemless thistle, is a plant species in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The plant's therapeutic actions include its ability to stimulate liver function, improve bile production, and enhance the removal of toxins from the body.

The bioactive constituents of Carduus acaulis var. rigens include cardenolides, which are cardiac glycosides with potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
