Huperzia serrata var. dentata

Huperzia serrata var. dentata, also known as Toothed Fir Club Moss or Black Fir Club Moss, is a variety of the Huperzia serrata plant, a type of club moss that belongs to the Huperziaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compounds, which are believed to have a neuroprotective effect and may help improve cognitive function and memory.

The health benefits of Huperzia serrata var. dentata include potential treatment for conditions such as Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, and age-related cognitive decline, as well as its ability to improve blood circulation and lower blood pressure.

Huperzia serrata var. dentata can be prepared into various herbal preparations, including capsules, tablets, and extracts, which can be taken orally or applied topically to the skin.
