Capparis masaikai

What's the taxonomical classification of Capparis masaikai?

Capparis masaikai belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Brassicales. Further taxonomic refinement places it in the family Capparaceae, specifically within the genus Capparis, where it is identified by the unique species name masaikai.

The inflorescence emerges from the leaf axils, often appearing solitary or in small clusters.
