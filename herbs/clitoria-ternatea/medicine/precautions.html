10 Precautions To Take When Using Clitoria Ternatea (Butterfly Pea)

Clitoria ternatea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels and potassium levels.

Taking this herb without proper management can cause hypoglycemia or hypokalemia, leading to symptoms like dizziness, fatigue, and muscle weakness.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Clitoria ternatea, especially for individuals with diabetes or kidney problems, to avoid severe complications.
