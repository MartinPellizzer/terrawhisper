The plant features a fine, pubescent texture across its vegetative surfaces, which aids in moisture retention.     What are the herbal preparations of Chamaesium paradoxum?

    Chamaesium paradoxum is prepared primarily as a decoction, a concentrated liquid infusion made by boiling the dried leaves and stems in water to extract its active constituents.
