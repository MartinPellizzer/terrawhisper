Crucianella pontica

Crucianella pontica, commonly known as Pontic rue or Pontic knotgrass, is a plant species in the family Lamiaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects.

The therapeutic actions of Crucianella pontica include wound healing, gastrointestinal issues, and respiratory problems, attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils.
