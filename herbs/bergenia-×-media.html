What is Bergenia × media?

Bergenia × media is a hybrid plant of Bergenia cordifolia and Bergenia stracheyi, belonging to the family Saxifragaceae.

It is a rhizomatous perennial with a stout stem, reaching heights of 60-120 cm, and has large, rounded, leathery leaves that are 20-30 cm long and 15-25 cm wide, with entire margins and a wavy base. The leaves are a deep green color on the upper surface and a lighter green on the lower surface, with a prominent midrib and primary veins. The plant produces spikes of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in late spring, which are followed by reddish-purple berries.

Bergenia × media prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.
