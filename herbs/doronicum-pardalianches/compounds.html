Alpine Buttercup (Doronicum pardalianches) Compounds

The primary compounds in Alpine Buttercup include flavonoids, which have anti-inflammatory properties, and phenolic acids, known for their antioxidant effects. Coumarin contributes to its mild sedative qualities, while tannins provide astringent properties. Terpenoids and glycosides also play roles in its medicinal applications.
