10 Possible Side Effects Of Carya Ovata (Shagbark Hickory)

Carya ovata has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, stomach cramps, and nausea.

These symptoms are caused by excessive consumption of the herb, which can stimulate the digestive system and lead to irritation.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen lives by causing dehydration, weight loss, and decreased productivity, ultimately affecting one's quality of life and daily activities.
