What is Ayenia abutilifolia?

Ayenia abutilifolia is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 3 meters in height, with a woody stem and a dense, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and slightly toothed, measuring 5-12 centimeters in length and 2-5 centimeters in width, with a petiole of 1-2 centimeters. The flowers are small, yellow or greenish-yellow in color, and are arranged in axillary clusters, with five petals and a prominent calyx of five lobes.

The fruits are small, spherical capsules, 1-2 centimeters in diameter, containing numerous small seeds.
