Sceletium (Sceletium Strictum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Sceletium strictum.

Sceletium, scientifically known as Sceletium strictum, is a medicinal herb native to southern Africa, traditionally used for its adaptogenic and mood-enhancing properties. It is also known as "Kanna" and has been historically valued for its ability to reduce anxiety, enhance focus, and promote a sense of well-being. The plant contains unique compounds such as mesembrine and mesembrenol, which are believed to contribute to its calming and uplifting effects. In traditional African cultures, Sceletium was used by warriors and shamans to increase endurance, courage, and mental clarity during rituals and combat. Today, it is gaining popularity in modern wellness practices as a natural alternative for stress relief, cognitive support, and mood regulation, often consumed as a tea, capsule, or tincture.
