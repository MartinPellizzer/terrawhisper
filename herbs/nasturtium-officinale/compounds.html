Garden Nasturtium (Nasturtium officinale) Compounds

The primary compounds in Garden Nasturtium include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and essential oils, known for their antimicrobial effects. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent qualities and anti-inflammatory benefits. Coumarin and terpenoids support its anti-spasmodic and antifungal actions. Glycosides, alkaloids, saponins, and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal effects.
