10 Possible Side Effects Of Sargassum Fusiforme (Welshman'S Beard)

For example, excessive consumption can lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, causing people to miss work or school, while thyroid issues can lead to fatigue, weight changes, and mood disturbances.
