10 Medicinal Preparations Of Rhus Coriaria

Rhus coriaria has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and ointments.

These preparations are often used to treat skin conditions like eczema, dermatitis, and acne. The tannins present in the plant have antiseptic and astringent properties, making them useful for wound healing and reducing inflammation.

The plant's leaves and berries can also be used to make a poultice or salve for treating skin irritations and rashes.
