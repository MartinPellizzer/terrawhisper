Pseudostellaria davidii

What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudostellaria davidii?

Pseudostellaria davidii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Caryophyllales. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae and is placed within the genus Pseudostellaria. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, Pseudostellaria davidii.
