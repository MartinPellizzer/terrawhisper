Crocus Sativus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Crocus sativus, commonly known as saffron, is a flowering plant whose dried stigmas are used as a spice and herbal remedy.

It is renowned for its vibrant color and distinct aroma, and it has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits.

Saffron is believed to improve mood, enhance cognitive function, and support cardiovascular health due to its bioactive constituents such as crocins, crocetin, and safranal.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it versatile for use in both culinary and herbal applications.
