Possible Side Effects of leptandra virginica

Overuse or misuse of leptandra virginica can also result in interactions with other medications, potentially exacerbating conditions such as diabetes or blood-thinning disorders.

Pregnant or breastfeeding women should exercise extreme caution when using this herb, as it may stimulate uterine contractions or affect milk production.
