Galactia crassifolia

Galactia crassifolia, commonly known as "Spanish flag" or "Climbing milk pea", is a plant species in the pea family (Fabaceae).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The therapeutic actions of Galactia crassifolia include antioxidant, anti-diabetic, and antiviral activities, which may help in managing conditions such as diabetes and certain infections.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
