Lindera Benzoin (Lindera benzoin)

Lindera Benzoin (Lindera benzoin) is a member of the Monimiaceae family, native to China, Japan, and Korea. Traditionally, its resin, bark, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in japanese kampo medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and native american herbal medicine.
