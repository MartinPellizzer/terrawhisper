What is Anginon paniculatum?

Anginon paniculatum is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It has a rhizomatous habit, with an underground stem that produces new shoots and roots. Anginon paniculatum produces small, white or pinkish flowers in terminal, flat-topped clusters, which are arranged in a panicle inflorescence, and are surrounded by a bract of a similar size and shape to the flower.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region.
