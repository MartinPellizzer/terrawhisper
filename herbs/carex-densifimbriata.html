What's the taxonomical classification of Carex densifimbriata?

Carex densifimbriata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Poales and falls within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Carex, and its unique species designation, densifimbriata.
