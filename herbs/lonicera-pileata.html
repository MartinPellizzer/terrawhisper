Lonicera pileata

What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera pileata?

Lonicera pileata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Dipsacales and the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Lonicera, and its species designation, pileata.
