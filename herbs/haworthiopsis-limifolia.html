Haworthiopsis limifolia

Haworthiopsis limifolia, also known as the 'Zebra Plant', is a low-growing, succulent plant belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is known for its striking, horizontal stripes on the leaves, which give it a unique appearance.
