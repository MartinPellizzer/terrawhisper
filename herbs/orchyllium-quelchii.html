Orchyllium quelchii

Orchyllum quelchii, also known as Barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe, North Africa, and Western Asia, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and red berries.

The medicinal properties of Orchyllium quelchii lie in its alkaloids, primarily berberine, which have antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of this plant include the treatment of digestive issues, such as diarrhea and constipation, as well as its ability to lower cholesterol and blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for heart health and weight management.

Herbal preparations of Orchyllium quelchii include teas, capsules, and tinctures, often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
