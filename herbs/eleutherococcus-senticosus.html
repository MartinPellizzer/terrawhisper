What to know about Eleutherococcus senticosus before using it medicinally

Eleutherococcus senticosus, commonly known as eleuthero, is a medicinal herb traditionally used to treat a range of health conditions, including fatigue, stress, and anxiety disorders.

The herb is also employed to support the immune system, improve cardiovascular health, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory diseases such as bronchitis and asthma. Eleuthero is prized for its adaptogenic properties, which enable the body to adapt to and withstand stress, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. The bioactive compounds present in eleuthero, including eleutheroside E, eleutheroside B, and scopoletin, are responsible for its therapeutic activities.

In traditional herbal medicine, eleuthero is often prepared as a tea or tincture by infusing the dried roots and rhizomes of the plant in hot water or a solvent, allowing for the extraction of its medicinal compounds.
