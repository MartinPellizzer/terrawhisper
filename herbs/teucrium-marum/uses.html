Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) Uses

Common Teucrium is used to treat respiratory infections, chronic cough, and bronchitis by reducing inflammation and clearing mucus. It also helps with cold symptoms, allergic rhinitis, and inflamed tonsils. The herb can alleviate mucous congestion and is applied topically for skin infections and inflammatory skin conditions.

Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of coughs, bronchitis, and other respiratory infections. Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties help reduce symptoms and promote healing. It is often prepared as a tea to soothe the throat and ease breathing.

2. Chronic cough

Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) is used to support respiratory health, particularly for chronic cough. Its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties help loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the airways. 3. Respiratory congestion

Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) is used to alleviate respiratory congestion due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.
