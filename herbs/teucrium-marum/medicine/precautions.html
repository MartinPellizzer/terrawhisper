1. Take under medical supervision

When using Teucrium marum medicinally, it's important to take under medical supervision because of its potential interactions with certain medications and its ability to lower blood pressure.

This herb can exacerbate bleeding disorders and increase the risk of adverse effects when combined with blood thinners or diabetes medications. Additionally, Teucrium marum can cause dizziness, nausea, and headaches in some individuals, especially at high doses.

Therefore, consulting a healthcare professional is crucial to ensure safe and effective use.

2. Consult doctor before using

When using Teucrium marum medicinally, it's important to consult a doctor before using.

3. Avoid during pregnancy and lactation

When using Teucrium marum medicinally, it's important to avoid during pregnancy and lactation.

This is crucial because the herb has been shown to stimulate uterine contractions, which can induce premature labor or even abortion. Moreover, its potential to affect milk production and alter the composition of breastmilk poses a risk for infants.

As such, pregnant and breastfeeding women should exercise caution when considering the use of Teucrium marum medicinally, opting instead for alternative therapies that are safer for both mother and child.
