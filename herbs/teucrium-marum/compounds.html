Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) Compounds

Common Teucrium contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids. These compounds contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and coumarin add astringent and antispasmodic effects, while essential oils provide aromatic and antimicrobial benefits. Glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
