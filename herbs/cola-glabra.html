Kola Nut (Cola glabra)

Kola Nut (Cola glabra) is a member of the Sterculiaceae family, native to West Africa, Central Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its seeds, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
