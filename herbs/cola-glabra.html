What are the side effects of Cola glabra when used improperly?

When used improperly, Cola glabra increases the chances of experiencing side effects, such as gastrointestinal upset, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Improper use can also lead to allergic reactions, characterized by skin rashes, itching, and swelling. Furthermore, consuming excessive amounts or using Cola glabra for extended periods may result in kidney damage or exacerbate existing kidney conditions.

In rare cases, high doses of the herb may cause an imbalance of potassium levels in the blood, leading to cardiovascular complications such as arrhythmias, palpitations, or even heart failure. Additionally, some individuals may experience central nervous system disturbances, including headaches, dizziness, and anxiety, which can be exacerbated by pre-existing conditions like epilepsy or other seizure disorders.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Cola glabra for medicinal purposes to ensure proper dosage and usage guidelines are followed to minimize the risk of adverse reactions.
