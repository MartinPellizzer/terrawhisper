What is Hypericum olivieri?

Hypericum olivieri is a shrub-like perennial plant that belongs to the family Hypericaceae.

It has opposite, lance-shaped leaves, 1-2 cm long, with 3-5 pairs of lateral veins, and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, 2-4 cm long, with 5 petals and 5 stamens. The flowers are followed by small, greenish-yellow fruit, 1-2 cm long, that turns brown at maturity. The plant grows up to 30-50 cm tall and has a woody base.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is often found in rocky, dry areas.
