Peristylus jinchuanicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the Orchidaceae family. Specifically, this plant is identified by its genus, Peristylus, and is distinguished as the species jinchuanicus.
