10 Precautions To Take When Using Chrysanthemum Morifolium (Chrysanth)

Chrysanthemum morifolium has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels and adjusting medication as necessary.

This is crucial because high doses of the herb can lower blood sugar, potentially causing hypoglycemia. For example, if a diabetic takes chrysanth without adjusting their insulin dose, they may experience symptoms like dizziness, shakiness, and rapid heartbeat.

Regular monitoring can help prevent these complications and ensure safe use.
