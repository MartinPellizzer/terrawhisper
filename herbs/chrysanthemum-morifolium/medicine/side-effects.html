10 Possible Side Effects Of Chrysanthemum Morifolium (Chrysanth)

Chrysanthemum morifolium has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, stomach upset, and liver damage.

These side effects can be caused by consuming high doses or prolonged use of the herb, or by people who are sensitive to its active compounds, such as pyrethrins and luteolin.

In severe cases, these side effects can lead to life-threatening conditions, such as liver failure, and worsen people's lives by causing chronic health issues and economic burdens due to medical treatment and lost productivity.
