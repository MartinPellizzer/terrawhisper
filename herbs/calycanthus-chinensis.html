Calycanthus chinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Calycanthus chinensis?

Calycanthus chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Laurales and the family Calycanthaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Calycanthus, and its specific species, chinensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
