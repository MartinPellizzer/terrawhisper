Plantago subulata var. pungens

Plantago subulata var. pungens, commonly known as needle-leaved plantain, is a type of flowering plant in the plantain family (Plantaginaceae).

This plant is used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, which include aiding digestion, reducing inflammation, and acting as an expectorant to relieve respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs.

The therapeutic actions of Plantago subulata var. pungens include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic properties, making it a valuable natural remedy for treating various health conditions.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
