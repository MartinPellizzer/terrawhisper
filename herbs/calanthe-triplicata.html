Calanthe triplicaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its lineage, it falls under the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Calanthe, under the specific species name triplicaata.
