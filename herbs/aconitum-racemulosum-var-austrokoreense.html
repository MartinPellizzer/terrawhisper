Aconitum racemulosum var. austrokoreense

Aconitum racemulosum var. austrokoreense, also known as Korean monk's hood, is a type of perennial plant belonging to the Aconitum genus, native to Korea.

The plant contains aconitine, a toxic compound with medicinal properties, and has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of Aconitum racemulosum var. austrokoreense include its anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving effects, making it a potential remedy for conditions such as arthritis and muscle soreness.
