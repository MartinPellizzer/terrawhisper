What is Borinda ferax?

Borinda ferax is a species of bamboo in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial, evergreen bamboo with a clumping growth habit. The plant typically grows to a height of 1.5 to 3 meters and has a culm diameter of 5 to 15 centimeters. The culms are greenish-yellow at the base and have a smooth, greyish-brown surface above.

The leaves are bright green, lanceolate in shape and measure 15 to 30 centimeters in length with a 1 to 2 centimeter long ligule.
