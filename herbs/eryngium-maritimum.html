Eryngium Maritimum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Eryngium maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and flowers to support digestive and respiratory health.
