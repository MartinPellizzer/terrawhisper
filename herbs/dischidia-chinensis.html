What's the taxonomical classification of Dischidia chinensis?

Dischidia chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae, specifically within the genus Dischidia, and is identified by its specific species name, chinensis.
