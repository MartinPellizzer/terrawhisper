Castanopsis eyrei

What's the taxonomical classification of Castanopsis eyrei?

Castanopsis eyrei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, the species is part of the order Fagales and falls under the family Fagaceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Castanopsis, with its specific designation being eyrei.
