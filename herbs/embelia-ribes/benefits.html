Infections are harmful conditions caused by pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites invading the body and disrupting normal bodily functions.

They are commonly experienced by individuals with weakened immune systems, such as the elderly, young children, or those with chronic illnesses.

The embelia ribes plant is known to fight infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which help in reducing the severity and duration of infectious diseases.
