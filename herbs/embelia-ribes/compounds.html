Embelia (Embelia ribes) Compounds

The primary compounds of Embelia ribes include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while saponins exhibit antimicrobial properties. Glycosides, terpenoids, and essential oils also play roles in its medicinal benefits. Coumarin, alkaloids, and anthocyanins add to its diverse therapeutic profile.
