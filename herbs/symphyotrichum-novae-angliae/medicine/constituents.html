10 Medicinal Constituents Of Symphyotrichum Novae-Angliae (New England Aster)

Symphyotrichum novae-angliae has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable plant for various medicinal applications.

The constituents of New England Aster can help alleviate symptoms of allergies and respiratory issues, while also providing protection against chronic diseases like cardiovascular disease and cancer.
