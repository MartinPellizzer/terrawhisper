New England Aster (Symphyotrichum novae-angliae) Compounds

The New England Aster contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent qualities, while coumarin and essential oils provide aromatic and potential antispasmodic benefits. Glycosides and terpenoids support its medicinal uses, and anthocyanins add color and additional antioxidant activity.
