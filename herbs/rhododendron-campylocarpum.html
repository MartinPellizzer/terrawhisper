What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron campylocarpum?

Rhododendron campylocarpum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Ericaceae, which places it within the genus Rhododendron, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species campylocarpum.
