10 Possible Side Effects Of Caulophyllum Thalictroides (Blue Cohosh)

Caulophyllum thalictroides has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

These symptoms occur due to its emetic and purgative properties.

If left untreated or worsened, these side effects can lead to dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, and kidney damage, causing individuals to miss work, experience fatigue, and have decreased productivity.
