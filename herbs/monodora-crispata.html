Monodora crispata

What's the taxonomical classification of Monodora crispata?

Monodora crispata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Magnoliales and is a member of the family Annonaceae. More specifically, it is identified by the genus Monodora, with its distinct biological identity defined by the species crispata.
