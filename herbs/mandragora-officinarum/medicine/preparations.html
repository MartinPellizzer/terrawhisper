10 Medicinal Preparations Of Mandragora Officinarum (Mandrake)

Mandragora officinarum has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and salves, that have been used to treat various ailments.

These preparations are commonly used to alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation. The root of the plant is also used to make a bitter tonic, useful for treating digestive issues and anxiety.

Additionally, mandrake is sometimes used in combination with other herbs to create remedies for skin conditions and respiratory problems.
