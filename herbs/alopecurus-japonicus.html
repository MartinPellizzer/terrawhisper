Alopecurus japonicus

What's the taxonomical classification of Alopecurus japonicus?

Alopecucus japonicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls within the order Poales and the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Alopecurus, and its specific species, japonicus.
