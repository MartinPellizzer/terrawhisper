Spartium (Spartium junceum)

Spartium (Spartium junceum) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and Middle East. Traditionally, its stems, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, astringent, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and native american herbal medicine.
