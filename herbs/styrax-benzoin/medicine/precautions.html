10 Precautions To Take When Using Styrax Benzoin (Benzoin)

Styrax benzoin has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use in pregnancy and breastfeeding due to potential hormonal interactions.

It's also essential to use benzoin in moderation, as excessive consumption can cause digestive issues and interact with blood thinners, antacids, and certain medications.

Failure to follow these guidelines may lead to side effects like diarrhea, nausea, and dizziness.
