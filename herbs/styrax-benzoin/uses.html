Benzoin (Styrax benzoin) Uses

Benzoin is used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, cough, and sore throat. It helps reduce inflammation and mucus production. It is also applied for skin infections and may alleviate symptoms of sinusitis and allergic rhinitis. Its antiseptic properties support healing in minor skin wounds and infections.

Benzoin (Styrax benzoin) Monograph

Benzoin is used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health, particularly for bronchitis. It has expectorant properties that help loosen mucus and ease coughing. Its aromatic compounds may also have mild antiseptic effects, reducing inflammation in the airways. It is often used in inhalation therapies or as a component in respiratory blends.
