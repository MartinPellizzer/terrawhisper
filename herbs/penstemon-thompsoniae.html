Penstemon Thompsoniae

Preliminary studies suggest that extracts from Penstemon thompsoniae could support immune system function and aid in the treatment of respiratory infections. However, due to its limited availability and the need for further scientific validation, it remains a subject of ongoing botanical and medical investigation.
