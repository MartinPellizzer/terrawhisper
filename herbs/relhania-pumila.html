    What is Relhania pumila?

    Relhania pumila is a shrubby perennial plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

    It has a woody base and slender branches, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant has linear, pubescent leaves that are 10-20 cm long and 1-5 mm wide, with entire margins. The flowers are yellow, solitary, and tubular, with 5-7 mm long corolla lobes, arranged in a corymbiform inflorescence.

    The plant produces achenes, 2-3 mm long, that are glabrous and have a pappus of 3-5 mm long, barbellate bristles.
