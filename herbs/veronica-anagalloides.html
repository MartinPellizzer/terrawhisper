What's the taxonomical classification of Veronica anagalloides?

Veronica anagalloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Lamiales and the family Plantaginaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Veronica, and its unique species designation, anagalloides.
