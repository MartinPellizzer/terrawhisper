Sorbus discolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized into the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Further taxonomic refinement places it within the genus Sorbus, specifically identified by its species name, discolor.
