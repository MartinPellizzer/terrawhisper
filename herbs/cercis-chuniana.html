Cercis chuniana

What's the taxonomical classification of Cercis chuniana?

Cercis chuniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it in the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, it is further categorized under the genus Cercis, with its specific identity defined by the species name chuniana.
