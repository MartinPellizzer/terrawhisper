Cercis chuniana has small, cordate leaves with entire margins and a base that is distinctly heart-shaped. During the flowering season, small, pea-like blossoms emerge directly from the branches, often appearing before the leaves fully develop. The plant produces elongated, flat seed pods that persist on the branches well into the growing season.
