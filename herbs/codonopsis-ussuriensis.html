Codonopsis ussuriensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Codonopsis ussuriensis has lanceolate, dark green leaves with prominent light-colored veins and small, white, tubular flowers that emerge from a central spike. The foliage grows in a dense rosette pattern, often displaying a slightly glossy texture on the leaf surfaces. Each flower is characterized by a slender corolla and is typically arranged in a terminal raceme. The plant's stems are relatively short, keeping the leaves close to the soil surface. These morphological traits allow the species to thrive in moist, shaded forest environments.
