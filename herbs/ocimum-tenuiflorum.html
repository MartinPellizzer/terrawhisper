Ocimum Tenuiflorum

Ocimum tenuiflorum, commonly known as Holy Basil, is a sacred herb in Ayurvedic medicine.

It is known for its numerous health benefits, including its ability to reduce stress and anxiety, improve cognitive function, and boost the immune system.

The therapeutic actions of Ocimum tenuiflorum include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and adaptogenic properties, which help to protect against cell damage and promote overall well-being.

The bioactive constituents of this herb include eugenol, rosmarinic acid, and ursolic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of ocimum tenuiflorum

Ocimum tenuiflorum can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal issues.

Consuming large amounts of the herb may lead to stomach upset, diarrhea, and vomiting.

In some cases, it may also interact with medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, potentially causing adverse effects.

Furthermore, using Ocimum tenuiflorum without proper guidance may exacerbate conditions like high blood pressure, as it can lower blood pressure in some individuals.
