What is Aquilaria cumingiana?

Aquilaria cumingiana is a species of flowering plant in the family Thymelaeaceae.

It is a deciduous tree, typically growing up to 20 meters tall, with a trunk diameter of up to 60 cm. The bark is grayish-brown, and the leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture, measuring 10-25 cm in length and 4-8 cm in width. The leaves have a crenulate margin and are arranged in opposite pairs on the stem.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in axillary panicles, each containing 5-7 petals.
