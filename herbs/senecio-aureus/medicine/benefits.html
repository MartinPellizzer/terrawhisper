10 Health Benefits Of Senecio Aureus (Golden Ragwort)

Senecio aureus has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments.

Its medicinal properties include the presence of sesquiterpenoids, flavonoids, and saponins, which help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

For example, the plant's anti-inflammatory effects can alleviate arthritis pain and swelling, while its antioxidant properties can protect against age-related diseases such as Alzheimer's and cancer, improving overall quality of life and health.
