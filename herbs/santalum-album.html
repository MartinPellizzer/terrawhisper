Santalum album phenylethanoid glycosides are a class of bioactive compounds that exhibit neuroprotective effects, enhance memory, and have been studied for their potential in treating neurodegenerative disorders such as Alzheimer's disease.

Santalum album α-santalol, another key sesquiterpene alcohol, is noted for its calming and sedative effects, often used in aromatherapy and for its ability to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation.

Santalum album santalbic acid is a unique fatty acid derivative that has shown antimicrobial and antifungal activities, making it valuable in the development of natural preservatives and therapeutic agents.
