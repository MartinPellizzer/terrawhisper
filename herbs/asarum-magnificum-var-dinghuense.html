Asarum magnificum var. dinghuense

What's the taxonomical classification of Asarum magnificum var. dinghuense?

Asarum magnificum var. dinghuense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is placed under the order Piperales, which connects it to a diverse group of flowering plants. Within this order, it is situated in the family Aristolochiaceae. Specifically, the plant is identified under the genus Asarum, ultimately falling under the specific taxonomic designation of the variety dinghuense within the species Asarum magnificum.
