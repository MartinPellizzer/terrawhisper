    What is Baccharis venosa?

    Baccharis venosa is a perennial shrub native to South America.

    It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is a member of the Baccharis genus. The plant has a woody base and branches that are 1-3 meters long, covered in small, grayish leaves that are lanceolate in shape and 1-5 cm long. The leaves are alternate and have a serrated margin.

    Baccharis venosa produces small, white flowers in a dense, cylindrical inflorescence that is 3-6 cm long.
