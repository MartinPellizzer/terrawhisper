The bioactive constituents of Satureja vulgaris include thymol, carvacrol, and rosmarinic acid, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions, such as anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and anti-anxiety effects.

The herb's therapeutic actions also include its ability to relieve symptoms of colds and flu, improve respiratory health, and support immune function.
