10 Health Benefits Of Canarium Strictum (Galip Nut)

Canarium strictum, also known as galip nut, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Its high content of galipine, a unique compound, helps to lower cholesterol and blood pressure levels.

For example, incorporating galip nut into a diet can help prevent heart disease and stroke, allowing people to enjoy an active and healthy lifestyle well into old age.

1. Reduces inflammation

Canarium strictum reduces inflammation because of its rich profile of polyphenolic compounds, including gallic acid, ellagic acid, and quercetin.

These bioactive molecules have been shown to possess potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, effectively scavenging free radicals and modulating the activity of pro-inflammatory enzymes.

By reducing oxidative stress and inhibiting inflammatory signaling pathways, galip nut may help mitigate chronic inflammation and promote overall health and well-being.

2. Relieves stress

Canarium strictum relieves stress because it contains a unique compound called galipn, which has been shown to have a calming effect on the nervous system.

When consumed, galipn helps to reduce cortisol levels, alleviating feelings of anxiety and tension.

Additionally, the nut's rich source of magnesium and potassium helps to regulate blood pressure and promote relaxation, further contributing to its stress-relieving properties.

3. Lowers cholesterol

Canarium strictum lowers cholesterol because it is rich in unsaturated fatty acids, particularly oleic acid.

This type of fatty acid has been shown to decrease the absorption of dietary cholesterol into the bloodstream, thus reducing overall cholesterol levels.

Additionally, galip nut is a good source of sterols, which also help to lower cholesterol by inhibiting the absorption of dietary cholesterol.

4. Increases antioxidant

Canarium strictum increases antioxidant because it is rich in polyphenolic compounds, particularly flavonoids, phenolic acids, and lignans.

These compounds have been shown to have potent antioxidant properties, effectively neutralizing free radicals and reducing oxidative stress. Additionally, galip nut's high content of unsaturated fatty acids, such as oleic acid, also contributes to its antioxidant potential by inhibiting lipid peroxidation.

Overall, galip nut's unique combination of polyphenols and fatty acids makes it a valuable source of antioxidants in the diet.

5. Kills parasites

Canarium strictum kills parasites because of its unique compound called galipol.

This compound has been found to exhibit potent anthelmintic properties, effectively destroying parasitic worms such as hookworms, roundworms, and tapeworms.

When consumed, galipol binds to the parasites, disrupting their nutrient absorption and eventually killing them.

6. Supports weight loss

Canarium strictum supports weight loss because it is a rich source of healthy fats and protein, making it a filling and satisfying snack.

The galip nut's high fiber content also helps to keep hunger pangs at bay, while its omega-9 fatty acid composition has been shown to increase feelings of fullness and reduce cravings for unhealthy snacks.

This combination of nutrients makes galip nut a valuable addition to a weight loss diet.

7. Boosts immunity

Canarium strictum boosts immunity because it is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.

The nut's high concentration of vitamin E, in particular, has been shown to enhance the body's natural defense mechanisms by scavenging free radicals and reducing oxidative stress.

Additionally, galip nut's fiber content helps regulate the gut microbiome, supporting the immune system's response to pathogens and promoting overall well-being.

8. Heals wounds

Canarium strictum heals wounds because of its remarkable anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The nut's extract contains compounds that inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, thereby reducing swelling and pain. Additionally, its antimicrobial properties help combat bacterial infections, promoting a healthy environment for wound healing.

This unique combination of properties makes galip nut a valuable natural remedy for treating wounds and promoting tissue regeneration.

9. Reduces fever

Canarium strictum reduces fever because it possesses remarkable therapeutic properties.

The nut's high content of polyphenolic compounds, particularly gallic acid, has been shown to possess potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant activities.

These properties help to reduce the production of pro-inflammatory mediators, thereby decreasing the body's temperature and alleviating symptoms associated with fever.

10. Treats arthritis

Canarium strictum treats arthritis because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The nut's fatty acid profile, rich in polyunsaturated fatty acids, has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce joint swelling and pain.

Additionally, galip nut's flavonoid content, particularly quercetin, inhibits the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, which contribute to the development of arthritis.
