10 Health Benefits Of Canarium Strictum (Galip Nut)

Canarium strictum has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health due to its rich content of antioxidants and polyphenols.

The seeds of this plant have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and respiratory issues.

By consuming galip nut oil, people can experience improved heart function, reduced blood pressure, and enhanced cognitive abilities, leading to a healthier and more productive lifestyle.
