Angelica Archangelica: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Angelica archangelica, commonly known as garden angelica, is a tall biennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped leaves and clusters of small white flowers.

This herb is valued for its potent medicinal properties and has been used historically for its digestive, respiratory, and circulatory benefits.

The health benefits of angelica archangelica include improving digestion, reducing inflammation, and supporting respiratory health, while its therapeutic actions encompass antispasmodic, carminative, and expectorant effects.

The bioactive constituents of this herb include essential oils, alkaloids, flavonoids, and volatile compounds that contribute to its wide range of healing properties.
