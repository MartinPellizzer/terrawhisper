What's the taxonomical classification of Uvaria tonkinensis?

Uvaria tonkinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Magnoliales, the plant is further organized into the family Annonaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Uvaria, resulting in its scientific designation as the species Uvaria tonkinensis.
