Possible Side Effects of glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra can have side effects if used improperly, such as excessive consumption leading to potassium depletion, which may cause muscle weakness, fatigue, and heart palpitations.

Long-term use of glycyrrhiza glabra can also lead to high blood pressure, edema, and an increased risk of cardiovascular disease.

Furthermore, it may interact with certain medications, such as diuretics, and exacerbate conditions like low blood pressure, kidney disease, and osteoporosis.

In rare cases, glycyrrhiza glabra can cause headaches, dizziness, and nausea due to its stimulatory effects on the nervous system.
