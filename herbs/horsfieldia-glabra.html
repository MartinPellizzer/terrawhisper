Horsfieldia glabra

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Horsfieldia glabra has simple, alternate, elliptical leaves that are glabrous on both surfaces and possess entire margins. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that transition in color as they reach maturity. These morphological traits distinguish it from other species within the Lauraceae family.
