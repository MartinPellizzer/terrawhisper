Phyllorkis Cornuta

Phyllorkis cornuta, commonly known as the horned lichen, is a species of lichen that has been explored for its potential medicinal properties due to its rich chemical composition.

It contains various bioactive compounds, including polyketides and terpenoids, which have shown antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant activities in preliminary studies. Traditional uses in some indigenous cultures suggest its application in treating skin conditions and respiratory ailments, although scientific validation is still ongoing. Researchers are particularly interested in its potential as a source of new natural products for pharmaceutical development.

Further studies are needed to fully understand its therapeutic applications and ensure its safe and effective use in modern medicine.
