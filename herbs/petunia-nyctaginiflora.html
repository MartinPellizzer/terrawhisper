      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Petunia nyctaginiflora can cause skin irritation, gastrointestinal distress, and mild toxicity if ingested by humans or pets. If the plant material is swallowed, symptoms often include nausea, vomiting, and stomach discomfort. While not considered highly lethal, these reactions can be significant for individuals with sensitive skin or small animals.
