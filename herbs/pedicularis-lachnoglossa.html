What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis lachnoglossa?

Pedicularis lachnoglossa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized within the genus Pedicularis, with its specific identification being the species lachnoglossa.
