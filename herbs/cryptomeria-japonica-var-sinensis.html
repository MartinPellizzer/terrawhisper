Cryptomeria japonica var. sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cryptomeria japonica var. sinensis?

Cryptomeria japonica var. sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the order Pinales and falls under the family Cupressaceae. The plant is further categorized into the genus Cryptomeria, with its specific designation being the species Cryptomeria japonica var. sinensis.
