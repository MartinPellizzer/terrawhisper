Selliguea engleri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Selliguea, and its unique species designation, engleri.
