What's the taxonomical classification of Adenophora jasionifolia?

Adenophora jasionifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Asterales and the family Campanulaceae. Finally, its specific identification is defined by the genus Adenophora and the species jasionifolia.
