Anemone geum subsp. ovalifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone geum subsp. ovalifolia?

Anemone geum subsp. ovalifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. As a member of the family Ranunculaceae, this plant is further organized into the genus Anemone, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species Anemone geum subsp. ovalifolia.
