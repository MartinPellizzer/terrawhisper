Galium Odoratum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Galium odoratum, commonly known as sweet woodruff, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, prized for its aromatic leaves and historical use in herbal medicine.

This herb is known for its mild sedative and calming effects, making it beneficial for reducing stress, anxiety, and insomnia.

The bioactive constituents in Galium odoratum include volatile oils, tannins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or poultice, often used to support respiratory health and as a mild sedative in herbal formulations.
