What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia mamillata?

Ardisia mamillaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through the higher taxonomic ranks, it falls under the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Ardisia, and its specific species designation, mamillata.
