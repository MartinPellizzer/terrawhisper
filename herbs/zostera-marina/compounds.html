Zostera Grass (Zostera marina) Compounds

Zostera marina contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, which act as antioxidants. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and detoxifying effects. Glycosides and terpenoids support cardiovascular health, while coumarins and alkaloids have anticoagulant and neuroprotective roles. Essential oils and anthocyanins add to its antioxidant and anti-cancer potentials.
