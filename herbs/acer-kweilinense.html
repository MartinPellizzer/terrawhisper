Acer kweilinense

What's the taxonomical classification of Acer kweilinense?

Acer kweilinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is a member of the order Sapindales and the family Sapindaceae. Finally, this plant is categorized under the genus Acer, with its specific designation being the species kweilinense.

Acer kweilinense is prepared primarily as a decoction made from the dried bark or leaves, though it can also be processed into extracts or tinctures for more concentrated use. Such preparation methods ensure that the plant's bioactive constituents are effectively released for consumption.
