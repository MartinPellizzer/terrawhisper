Epilobium wallichianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Myrtales and is a member of the family Onagraceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Epilobium, specifically identified by its unique species designation, wallichianum.
