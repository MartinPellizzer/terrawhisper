Tripterospermum coeruleum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is organized under the order Gentianaales and falls within the family Gentianaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Tripterospermum, and its unique species designation, coeruleum.
