Geranium divaricatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Geranium divaricatum?

Geranium divaricatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Geraniales. This plant is a member of the family Geraniaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Geranium, and is identified by its unique species name, divaricatum.
