Plantago lanceolata, commonly known as long-leaved plantain, is a medicinal herb that has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including inflammation, wounds, and respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.

Plantago lanceolata is also known for its therapeutic properties, including its ability to reduce inflammation, stop bleeding, and calm coughs. In herbal medicine, Plantago lanceolata is often prepared as a tincture or an infusion, which is used to treat a range of health issues, particularly those related to the respiratory and digestive systems.
