Liparis cespitosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is specifically identified within the genus Liparis. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species cespitosa.
