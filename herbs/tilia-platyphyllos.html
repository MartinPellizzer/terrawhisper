Small-Leaf Lime (Tilia platyphyllos)

Small-Leaf Lime (Tilia platyphyllos) is a member of the Tiliaceae family, native to Eastern Europe, Southeastern Europe, and Western Asia. Traditionally, its bark, leaves, and flowers have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and japanese kampo medicine.
