Yellow Gentian (Gentiana lutea) Actions

Yellow Gentian is known for its bitter, tonic, and stimulant properties. It acts as an anti-inflammatory and carminative, aiding digestion and reducing bloating. It also has diuretic and expectorant effects, supporting urinary health and respiratory function. Its astringent, sedative, antispasmodic, and mild antiseptic actions make it a versatile herb in herbal medicine.
