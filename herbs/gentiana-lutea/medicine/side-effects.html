10 Possible Side Effects Of Gentiana Lutea (Yellow Gentian)

Gentiana lutea has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and vomiting.

These problems may occur when the herb is consumed in excessive amounts or combined with other medications. If left unchecked, these side effects can worsen lives by causing malnutrition, dehydration, and strain on the digestive system.

In severe cases, it may even lead to electrolyte imbalances and organ damage.
