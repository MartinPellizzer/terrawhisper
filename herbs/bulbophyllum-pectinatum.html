Bulbophyllum pectinatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Bulbophyllum pectinatum?

Bulbophyllum pectinatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling under the specific genus Bulbophyllum, and is identified by its unique species name, pectinatum.
