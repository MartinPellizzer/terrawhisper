Prunus Avium

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in traditional medicine for treating various health conditions.

The therapeutic actions of Prunus avium include soothing digestive issues, reducing fever, and aiding in the relief of respiratory problems, while its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and anthocyanins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of prunus avium

Prunus avium can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Additionally, high doses of Prunus avium may lead to interactions with medications, potentially increasing the risk of bleeding or affecting blood pressure.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Prunus avium to avoid these potential side effects.
