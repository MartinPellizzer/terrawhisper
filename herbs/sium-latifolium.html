Sium latifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Sium latifolium?

Sium latifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is a member of the order Apiales and is situated within the family Apiaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its placement in the genus Sium, with the specific epithet latifolium denoting its species name.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Sium latifolium has a hollow stem that typically reaches heights of 30 to 100 centimeters and features compound, pinnate leaves with broad, ovate-shaped leaflets. Small, white flowers are arranged in dense, flat-topped umbels that emerge from the terminal ends of the stalks. The plant produces small, oblong fruits that are typically ribbed and contain several seeds.
