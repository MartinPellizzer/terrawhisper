Kleinia amaniensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high levels of saponins and iridoids.

Additionally, Kleinia amaniensis has been known to interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, which can exacerbate underlying health conditions.

In severe cases, improper use of Kleinia amaniensis can cause allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.
