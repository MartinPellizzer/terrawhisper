Spiraea japonica var. angulata

Spiraea japonica var. angulata, also known as Japanese Spirea or Delavay Spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Japan, characterized by its vibrant pink flowers and oval-shaped leaves.

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, the plant is believed to possess anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of various health conditions.

The health benefits of Spiraea japonica var. angulata are thought to include relief from fever, colds, and digestive issues, as well as its potential to lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be consumed to harness its medicinal properties.
