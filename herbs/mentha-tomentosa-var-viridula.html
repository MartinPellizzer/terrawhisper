Green Hairy Mint (Mentha Tomentosa Var. Viridula)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Mentha tomentosa var. viridula.

Green hairy mint, scientifically known as Mentha tomentosa var. viridula, is a medicinal herb valued for its adaptogenic properties and aromatic qualities. This unique plant is characterized by its distinctive minty flavor and dense, hairy foliage, which gives it its common name. Traditionally used in various cultures for its calming and digestive benefits, green hairy mint has been employed in herbal remedies to soothe gastrointestinal issues and reduce stress. In modern wellness practices, it is appreciated for its potential to support mental clarity and promote relaxation, often used in teas and aromatherapy. Its rare active compounds, such as menthol and other volatile oils, contribute to its efficacy, making it a sought-after ingredient in natural health products.
