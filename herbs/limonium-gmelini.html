What's the taxonomical classification of Limonium gmelini?

Limonium gmelini belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized under the order Caryophyllales. It is a constituent of the family Plumbaginaceae, falling under the specific genus Limonium, and is ultimately identified by its unique species designation, gmelini.
