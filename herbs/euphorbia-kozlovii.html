What's the taxonomical classification of Euphorbia kozlovii?

Euphorbia kozlovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Malpighiales. This plant is a representative of the family Euphorbiaceae, specifically nested within the genus Euphorbia, and is identified by its unique specific epithet, kozlovii.
