What is Elymus buschianus?

Elymus buschianus is a perennial grass species in the family Poaceae.

It has a slender culm, typically 50-100 cm tall, with leaf sheaths pubescent and leaf blades 5-15 cm long, 3-5 mm wide, flat, and glabrous. The panicle is linear, 20-40 cm long, with spikelets 10-15 mm long, having 3-5 florets. The glumes are 6-8 mm long, the lower glume with a distinct keel, and the upper glume 2-nerved.

The species is native to mountainous regions in Central Asia, specifically in China, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, and Tajikistan.
