Acanthopanax Senticosus (Acanthopanax senticosus) Compounds

The primary compounds of Acanthopanax Senticosus include saponins, which have anti-inflammatory properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Glycosides and alkaloids contribute to its adaptogenic and stimulant properties. Terpenoids and phenolic acids support immune function, while tannins and coumarins provide astringent and antimicrobial benefits. Essential oils and anthocyanins add to its diverse therapeutic profile.
