Acanthopanax Senticosus (Acanthopanax senticosus) Uses

Acanthopanax Senticosus is used to treat fatigue, chronic stress, anxiety, and depression. It also helps with insomnia and sleep disorders, as well as chronic pain and immune dysfunction. Its adaptogenic properties support overall vitality and resilience against stress-related conditions.
