Phoenix canariensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Phoenix canariensis?

Phoenix canariensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage that leads to the order Arecales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Arecaceae and is further categorized under the genus Phoenix, specifically identifying as the species canariensis.
