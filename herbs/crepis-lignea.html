Crepis lignea

What's the taxonomical classification of Crepis lignea?

Crepis lignea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its hierarchical descent, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asterales, it falls into the widely recognized family Asteraceae. Within this family, the plant is situated in the genus Crepis, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species linea.
