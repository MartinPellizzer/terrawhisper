What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Reevesia pubescens has small, elliptical to oblong leaves that are characterized by a dense covering of fine, soft hairs on both their upper and lower surfaces. During the flowering stage, the plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that emerge from the leaf axils.
