What's the taxonomical classification of Barteria fistulosa?

Barteria fistulosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Malpighiales, this plant is a member of the family Passifloraceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Barteria, with its unique designation being the species fistulosa.
