Adenanthera pavonina var. microsperma

What's the taxonomical classification of Adenanthera pavonina var. microsperma?

Adenanthera pavonina var. microsperma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is positioned in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Fabales. Further taxonomic refinement places it in the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of legumes. Specifically, this plant is a member of the genus Adenanthera, and its precise scientific designation is the variety microsperma of the species Adenanthera pavonina.
