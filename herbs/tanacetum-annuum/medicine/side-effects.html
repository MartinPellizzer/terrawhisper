10 Possible Side Effects Of Tanacetum Annuum (Feverfew)

Tanacetum annuum has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and increased risk of bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties.

Causes of these side effects include taking excessive doses, combining it with other medications, or having pre-existing conditions like ulcers or bleeding disorders.

Severe side effects can worsen lives by causing anaphylaxis, stomach ulcers, or uncontrolled bleeding.
