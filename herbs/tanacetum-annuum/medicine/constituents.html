10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tanacetum Annuum (Feverfew)

Tanacetum annuum has active constituents such as parthenolide, flavonoids, and terpenes that contribute to its medicinal properties.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and analgesic effects, which can help alleviate headaches, reduce fever, and relieve pain associated with migraines.

Additionally, the antioxidants in feverfew may improve overall health by protecting against free radicals and oxidative stress, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and cardiovascular disease.
