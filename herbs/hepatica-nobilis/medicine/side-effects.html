10 Possible Side Effects Of Hepatica Nobilis (Liverleaf)

These side effects can be caused by excessive dosage, prolonged use, or individual sensitivity.

For instance, stomach pain and diarrhea can disrupt daily life, preventing individuals from maintaining a healthy diet, while allergic reactions can cause life-threatening anaphylaxis.
