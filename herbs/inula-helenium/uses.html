Helenium (Inula helenium) Uses

Helenium is primarily used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, sinusitis, and asthma. It supports the immune system against respiratory infections, including the common cold, cough, and influenza. It may also help alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis and tonsillitis by reducing inflammation and congestion.

Helenium (Inula helenium) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Helenium, or Inula helenium, has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. It is valued for its expectorant properties, helping to loosen mucus and ease breathing. Its anti-inflammatory effects may reduce bronchial irritation, making it a useful herb in herbal medicine for managing symptoms of chronic bronchitis.
