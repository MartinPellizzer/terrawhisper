10 Medicinal Constituents Of Inula Helenium (Elf Dock)

Inula helenium has active constituents such as inulin, sesquiterpene lactones (helenin and alantolactone), and polyacetylenes.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, while the antimicrobial properties can aid in wound healing and the treatment of infections.

Furthermore, the antioxidant properties can protect against cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease.
