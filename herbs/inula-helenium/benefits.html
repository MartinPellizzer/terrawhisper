10 Best Inula Helenium Health Benefits

Inula helenium, also known as elecampane, is a herb that has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

It is known to support respiratory health by helping to alleviate symptoms of coughs, bronchitis, and asthma due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. The plant contains compounds such as inulin and alkaloids, which contribute to its immune-boosting effects and may help in reducing inflammation in the body. Inula helenium is also believed to aid in digestion by promoting the production of digestive enzymes and supporting gut health.

Additionally, it has shown potential in supporting liver function and may have antimicrobial properties that contribute to overall wellness.

Inula helenium supports digestion by promoting the healthy function of the gastrointestinal tract.

It contains bioactive compounds that may help stimulate the production of digestive enzymes, aiding in the breakdown of food and nutrient absorption. Regular use of Inula helenium may contribute to improved immune function and overall wellness.
