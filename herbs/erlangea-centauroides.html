What is Erlangea centauroides?

The leaves are glandular and have a dense pubescence on the underside. The inflorescence is a capitulum, 1-2 cm in diameter, composed of 3-5 florets, with white to yellowish flowers and a tubular corolla, 8-10 mm long.
