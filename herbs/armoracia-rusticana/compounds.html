Garden Horseradish (Armoracia rusticana) Compounds

Garden Horseradish contains several primary compounds, including saponin, which has surfactant properties, and phenolic acid, known for its antioxidant effects. Flavonoids and glycosides contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities. Tannins and coumarin provide astringent and antispasmodic properties, while essential oils and terpenoids offer aromatic and digestive benefits. Alkaloids and anthocyanins add additional therapeutic potential.
