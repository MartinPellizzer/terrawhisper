    What is Calliandra umbrosa?

    Calliandra umbrosa is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

    It is an evergreen shrub with a height range of 2-6 meters and a diameter of 1-2 meters. The plant has compound leaves with 3-5 pairs of pinnae, each with 5-7 pairs of leaflets. The leaves are dark green and elliptical in shape, measuring 5-10 cm in length.

    The plant produces clusters of red or pink flowers in the summer months, with each flower measuring 1-2 cm in diameter.
