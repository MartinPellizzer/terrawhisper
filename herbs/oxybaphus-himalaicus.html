What's the taxonomical classification of Oxybaphus himalaicus?

Oxybaphus himalaicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression through the order Caryophyllales, it is placed into the family Nyctaginaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Oxybaphus, and its unique species designation, himalaicus.
