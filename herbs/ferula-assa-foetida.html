Side Effects of ferula assa-foetida

Ferula assa-foetida leads to nausea, which is one of the most commonly reported gastrointestinal side effects when consumed in excessive amounts.

It can also cause stomach upset, resulting in discomfort and a feeling of fullness in the abdomen. The herb may trigger diarrhea due to its irritant properties on the digestive tract, and in some cases, it may cause vomiting, especially when ingested in large quantities.

Additionally, it can lead to bloating and gas pain, as it affects the normal functioning of the digestive system. Some individuals may experience heartburn or a burning sensation in the chest, which is often linked to its impact on stomach acid production. Furthermore, Ferula assa-foetida may induce skin rash or allergic reactions in sensitive individuals, and in rare cases, it can cause eye irritation if it comes into contact with the eyes.

These side effects highlight the importance of using the herb in moderation and consulting a healthcare professional before incorporating it into one's diet or supplement regimen.
