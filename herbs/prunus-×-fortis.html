Possible Side Effects of Prunus × fortis

Additionally, the seeds of the plant contain a toxic compound called prunasin, which can cause vomiting and abdominal pain if ingested.

If not handled and prepared correctly, Prunus × fortis can also cause skin irritation and allergic reactions in some individuals.
