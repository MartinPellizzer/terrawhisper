Larix × pendula

Larix × pendula, commonly known as Weeping Larch, is a hybrid deciduous coniferous tree that is a cross between Larix decidua and Larix laricina.

It is valued for its potential health benefits, which include reducing inflammation, improving respiratory function, and supporting immune system function.

The therapeutic actions of Larix × pendula include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which may be attributed to its bioactive constituents, such as terpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.
