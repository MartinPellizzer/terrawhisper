Hua gabonii

What's the taxonomical classification of Hua gabonii?

Hua gabonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Oxalidales and within the family Huaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Hua, and its specific species name, gabonii.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, stomach aches, digestive issues, and various types of inflammation. The leaves and roots are often prepared as decoctions or poultices to address these specific ailments. Local healers frequently apply the plant to wounds or fungal rashes to promote healing. Additionally, it is sometimes ingested in controlled amounts to alleviate internal discomfort or intestinal parasites. These traditional medicinal applications vary depending on the specific part of the plant utilized.
