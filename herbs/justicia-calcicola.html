Justicia calcicola

Justicia calcicola, also known as the salt marsh Justicia, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of South America.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include its ability to reduce inflammation and fight off bacterial infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory compounds.

The health benefits of Justicia calcicola include its potential to treat respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its use as a natural remedy for fever reduction and digestive issues.
