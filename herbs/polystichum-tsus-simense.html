Polystichum tsus-simense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Finally, this specific plant is identified by its genus, Polystichum, and its unique species designation, tsus-simense.
