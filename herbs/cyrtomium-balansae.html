What's the taxonomical classification of Cyrtomium balansae?

Cyrtomium balansae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Polypodiaceae. Ultimately, this fern is identified by its specific genus, Cyrtomium, and its unique species designation, balansae.
