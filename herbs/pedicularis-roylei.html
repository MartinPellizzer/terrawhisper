What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis roylei?

Pedicularis roylei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Lamiales and falls under the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Pedicularis, with the unique species designation roylei.

What are the therapeutic actions of Pedicularis roylei?

Pedicularis roylei has potent anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antipyretic properties used primarily to treat fever, pain, and various inflammatory conditions. The plant is frequently employed in traditional medicine to alleviate symptoms of respiratory ailments and digestive disturbances. Practitioners often utilize decoctions of the plant to stabilize body temperature during febrile episodes. These therapeutic actions make it a significant component in regional herbal remedies for managing acute physical distress.
