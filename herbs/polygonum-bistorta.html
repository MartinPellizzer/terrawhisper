Polygonum Bistorta Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Polygonum bistorta, commonly known as great bistort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

It is characterized by its thick, rhizomatous root system and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.

The herb is known for its potential health benefits, including supporting digestive health, reducing inflammation, and promoting wound healing.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and alkaloids.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from the root to harness its healing properties.
