10 Medicinal Constituents Of Populus X Candicans (Bastard Cottonwood)

Populus × candicans has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. They can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by soothing irritated airways.

Furthermore, the antioxidants in hybrid black cottonwood can also protect against cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of certain diseases, and promote overall cardiovascular health.
