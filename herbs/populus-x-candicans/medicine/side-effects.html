10 Possible Side Effects Of Populus X Candicans (Bastard Cottonwood)

Populus × candicans has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects can be caused by the herb's chemical compounds, particularly salicin, which can interact with other medications or worsen underlying conditions.

For example, excessive consumption can lead to stomach ulcers, kidney damage, or even anaphylaxis, significantly impacting daily life, productivity, and overall well-being.
