10 Health Benefits Of Populus X Candicans (Bastard Cottonwood)

Populus × candicans has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids, which help reduce inflammation and protect against oxidative stress.

These benefits can improve people's lives by alleviating symptoms of respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma, as well as reducing the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and cancer.
