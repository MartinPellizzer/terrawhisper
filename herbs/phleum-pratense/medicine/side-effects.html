10 Possible Side Effects Of Phleum Pratense (Timothy)

Phleum pratense has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects can occur due to overconsumption, cross-reactivity with other allergens, or poor preparation of the herb. For instance, severe allergic reactions can lead to anaphylaxis, a life-threatening condition that requires immediate medical attention.

Repeated exposure to skin irritation can cause chronic inflammation and skin conditions, affecting a person's quality of life and daily activities.
