10 Best Phleum Pratense Health Benefits

Phleum pratense, commonly known as timothy grass, offers several health benefits due to its rich nutritional profile.

Timothy grass is also known for its potential anti-inflammatory properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation-related conditions.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its calming effects, possibly due to its high content of bioactive compounds.
