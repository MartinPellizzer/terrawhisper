What's the taxonomical classification of Nymphaea tetragona?

Nymphaea tetragona belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Nymphaeales. This aquatic plant is a member of the family Nymphaeaceae and is situated within the genus Nymphaea, specifically identified as the species tetragona.
