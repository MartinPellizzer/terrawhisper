10 Medicinal Constituents Of Phyllanthus Emblica (Amla)

Phyllanthus emblica has active constituents such as gallic acid, ellagic acid, and tannins.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties. They help protect against oxidative stress and cell damage, potentially preventing chronic diseases. Amla's constituents can improve heart health by lowering cholesterol and blood pressure.

Additionally, they may also improve cognitive function and enhance the immune system, helping people live healthier lives.
