Indian Gooseberry (Phyllanthus emblica) Compounds

The primary compounds in Indian Gooseberry include flavonoids, which are antioxidants that support immune function. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties, while tannins provide astringent effects. Glycosides, saponins, and coumarin also play roles in its medicinal benefits, alongside terpenoids, anthocyanins, alkaloids, and essential oils.

Flavonoids are a group of bioactive compounds found in Indian Gooseberry (Phyllanthus emblica). These compounds contribute to the plant's antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, supporting its traditional use in promoting overall health and immune function.
