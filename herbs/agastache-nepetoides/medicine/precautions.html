10 Precautions To Take When Using Agastache Nepetoides (Giant Hyssop)

Agastache nepetoides has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use by pregnant women and people with bleeding disorders.

This is due to its potential to stimulate uterine contractions and increase bleeding risk. For instance, if not taken with caution, pregnant women may experience premature labor, while those with bleeding disorders may experience exacerbated symptoms.

It's also essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using this herb to minimize potential side effects.
