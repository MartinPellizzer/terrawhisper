Scirpus karuisawensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its hierarchical progression, the plant is placed in the order Poales and the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Scirpus, and its unique species designation, karuisawensis.
