Sudanese Spinach (Corchorus olitorius) Compounds

Sudanese spinach contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and antimicrobial actions. Coumarin and glycosides may support digestive health. Terpenoids, alkaloids, and essential oils offer additional therapeutic benefits, while anthocyanins contribute to its vibrant color and antioxidant activity.
