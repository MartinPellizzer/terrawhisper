Potentilla Erecta Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Potentilla erecta, commonly known as shrub cinquefoil, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used to support digestive and urinary health.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and astringent actions.

Potentilla erecta can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or decoction, making it a versatile herb for various herbal remedies.
