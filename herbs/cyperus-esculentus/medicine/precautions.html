10 Precautions To Take When Using Cyperus Esculentus (Chufa)

Cyperus esculentus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional, particularly when using it in large quantities or for extended periods.

This is important because consuming high amounts of the plant can cause digestive issues, such as diarrhea, stomach cramps, and vomiting.

Failing to take this precaution may lead to side effects like these, especially for individuals with sensitive stomachs or pre-existing digestive conditions.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Cyperus esculentus medicinally.
