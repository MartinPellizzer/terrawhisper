Eurya ciliata

What's the taxonomical classification of Eurya ciliata?

Eurya ciliata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ericales and placed within the family Pentaphylacaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Eurya and the specific species epithet ciliata.
