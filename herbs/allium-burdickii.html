What is Allium burdickii?

Allium burdickii is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaryllidaceae.

It is a perennial bulbous geophyte with linear, flat leaves and a scape that supports a spherical umbel of white flowers. Each flower has a 6-lobed corolla, 8-12 mm in diameter, with a red or purple center and a prominent greenish-white or greenish-yellow anther. The plant grows 20-40 cm tall and 10-15 cm wide.

It is native to eastern and central North America.
