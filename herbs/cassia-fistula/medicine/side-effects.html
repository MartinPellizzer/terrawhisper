10 Possible Side Effects Of Cassia Fistula (Golden Shower)

Cassia fistula has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, diarrhea, and stomach pain.

These side effects occur when the herb is taken in excess or combined with other medications, causing an imbalance of body chemistry.

In severe cases, these side effects can lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalance, worsening the quality of life for individuals, particularly the elderly and those with pre-existing medical conditions.

These compounds can increase the amount of calcium in urine, leading to the formation of kidney stones.

Additionally, Cassia fistula's saponins may interfere with the body's natural ability to dissolve and eliminate stone-forming minerals, further increasing the risk of developing kidney stones.
