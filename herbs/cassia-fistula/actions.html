Golden Shower Tree (Cassia fistula) Actions

The Golden Shower Tree exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and antispasmodic properties. It also acts as a tonic and stimulant, aiding digestion and reducing flatulence. Its bitter and astringent qualities help in clearing mucus and promoting healthy bodily functions. Additionally, it has sedative and expectorant effects, supporting respiratory and nervous system health.
