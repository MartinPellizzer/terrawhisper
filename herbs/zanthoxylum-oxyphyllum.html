Zanthoxylum oxyphyllum has a woody structure characterized by pinnately compound leaves with numerous narrow, lanceolate leaflets and small, inconspicuous greenish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy drupes that transition in color as they mature. This species also features a robust stem system capable of supporting significant height in its natural habitat.
