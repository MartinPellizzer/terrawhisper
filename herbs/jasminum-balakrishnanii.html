Jasminum balakrishnanii

Jasminum balakrishnanii, commonly known as Balakrishnan's jasmine, is an evergreen climbing shrub native to Western Ghats of India.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health issues, including anxiety, stress, and insomnia due to its health benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Jasminum balakrishnanii include sedative, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects, which help in reducing anxiety and promoting relaxation.

The bioactive constituents present in this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
