Jasminum balakrishnanii

Jasminum balakrishnanii is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae, commonly known as the Balakrishnan's Jasmine.

This plant is used in traditional medicine, particularly in Ayurveda, and is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Jasminum balakrishnanii include its potential to reduce stress and anxiety, improve sleep quality, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.
