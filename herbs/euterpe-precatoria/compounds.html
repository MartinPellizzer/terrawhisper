Red Cabbage Palm (Euterpe precatoria) Compounds

The Red Cabbage Palm contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and detoxifying actions. Coumarin and anthocyanins support skin health and act as natural preservatives.
