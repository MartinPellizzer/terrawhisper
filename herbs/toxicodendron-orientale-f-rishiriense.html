Possible Side Effects of Toxicodendron orientale f. rishiriense

Toxicodendron orientale f. rishiriense can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic contact dermatitis, and blistering due to the presence of urushiol oil.

Ingestion of the plant can lead to oral and gastrointestinal symptoms, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Exposure to the plant's oils can also cause eye irritation and even temporary blindness in severe cases.
