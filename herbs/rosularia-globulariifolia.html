What is Rosularia globulariifolia?

Rosularia globulariifolia is a perennial succulent plant that belongs to the family Crassulaceae.

It has thick, fleshy, and green leaves that are rounded and lobed, resembling those of the genus Globularia, from which it gets its specific epithet. The leaves are 2-5 cm long and arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant produces small, white flowers in late spring, which are clustered in a terminal inflorescence.

Rosularia globulariifolia is native to the Himalayan region and is adapted to high-altitude environments, where it grows in rocky areas with full sun and well-drained soil.
