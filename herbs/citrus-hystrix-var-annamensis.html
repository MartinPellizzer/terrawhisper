The therapeutic actions of kaffir lime include digestive relief, anti-diarrheal, and anti-septic properties, and its bioactive constituents include limonoids, flavonoids, and essential oils, particularly citral and limonene.

In rare cases, citrus hystrix var. annamensis can also cause cardiovascular problems, such as hypertension and arrhythmias, particularly in people with pre-existing heart conditions.

Furthermore, its interaction with certain medications, like blood thinners, may exacerbate bleeding disorders and other adverse health effects.
