What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllostachys nidularia?

Phyllostachys nidularia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Poales. As a member of the family Poaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Phyllostachys, with the specific designation of the species nidularia.
