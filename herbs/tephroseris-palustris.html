Tephroseris palustris

What's the taxonomical classification of Tephroseris palustris?

Tephroseris palustris belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is specifically categorized within the genus Tephroseris, with the species designation palustris.
