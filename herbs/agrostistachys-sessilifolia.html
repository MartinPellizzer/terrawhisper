What is Agrostistachys sessilifolia?

Agrostistachys sessilifolia is a perennial shrub that belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, where it is commonly found in tropical forests and grasslands. The plant has a woody stem, 1-2 meters in height, with simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, measuring 3-10 cm long and 1-4 cm wide, and have a sessile base. The leaves are dark green and glabrous on the upper surface, while the lower surface is lighter green and pubescent.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in a terminal corymb, which are followed by a capsule containing 3-4 seeds.
