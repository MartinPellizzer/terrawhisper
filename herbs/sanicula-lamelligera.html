What's the taxonomical classification of Sanicula lamelligera?

Sanicula lamelligera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, falling under the genus Sanicula, and is specifically identified by the species name lamelligera.
