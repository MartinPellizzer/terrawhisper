Drypetes klainei

What's the taxonomical classification of Drypetes klainei?

Drypetes klainei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Malpigiales. The plant is further categorized under the family Putranjivaceae, falling into the genus Drypetes, and is specifically identified by its species name, klainei.
