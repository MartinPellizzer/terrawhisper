10 Possible Side Effects Of Eriocaulon Decangulare (Water Celery)

Eriocaulon decangulare has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, dizziness, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption, interactions with other medications, or individual sensitivities. For instance, severe gastrointestinal issues can lead to chronic abdominal pain, digestive problems, and even malnutrition.

Allergic reactions, on the other hand, can cause anaphylaxis, which can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.
