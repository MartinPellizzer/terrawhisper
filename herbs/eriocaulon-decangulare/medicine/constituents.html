10 Medicinal Constituents Of Eriocaulon Decangulare (Water Celery)

Eriocaulon decangulare has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. As a result, water celery has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive disorders. The antioxidants present in the plant may help protect against cell damage and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease.

Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties can provide relief from joint pain and swelling, improving overall quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.
