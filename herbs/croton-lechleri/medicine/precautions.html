10 Precautions To Take When Using Croton Lechleri (Dragon'S Blood)

Croton lechleri has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood pressure and bleeding disorders.

Taking this herb can interact with anticoagulants and cause excessive bleeding, while also lowering blood pressure in individuals with hypotension.

Ignoring these precautions can result in complications, such as hemorrhages or hypotensive episodes, which may necessitate medical attention to prevent serious health issues.

1. Discard old or spoiled form

When using Croton lechleri medicinally, it's important to take fresh herb carefully because the plant can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions when dried or old.

The sap of Croton lechleri contains anthraquinones, which can be highly allergenic and irritate the skin, causing redness, itching, and rashes.

Therefore, it's crucial to ensure that the herb is freshly harvested and handled properly to minimize the risk of adverse reactions.
