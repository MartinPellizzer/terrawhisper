1. Treats peptic ulcers

Peptic Ulcers are chronic digestive tract sores that develop in the stomach or small intestine, often causing pain and discomfort.

Also known as gastric or duodenal ulcers, these conditions result from the erosion of the protective lining of the gastrointestinal tract.

Common causes include the bacterium *Helicobacter pylori* infection and prolonged use of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).

Individuals with a history of digestive issues, those over 50, and people with a family history of ulcers are more likely to experience this condition.

The croton lechleri plant has shown potential in treating peptic ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

How this herb helps with treats peptic ulcers?

Croton lechleri treats peptic ulcers because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids that have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These compounds help reduce gastric acid secretion and protect the stomach lining from damage. Alkaloids like mitragynine and isomitragynine contribute to the plant's ability to heal ulcers by promoting tissue repair. Flavonoids in the plant also exhibit antioxidant effects, which further support the healing process.

Overall, the combination of these bioactive compounds makes Croton lechleri an effective natural remedy for peptic ulcers.
