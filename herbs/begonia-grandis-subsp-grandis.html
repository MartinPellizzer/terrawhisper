Begonia grandis subsp. grandis

What's the taxonomical classification of Begonia grandis subsp. grandis?

Begonia grandis subsp. grandis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Cucurbitales. This specific plant is a member of the family Begoniaceae and the genus Begonia, ultimately reaching its precise taxonomic designation at the species level as Begonia grandis subsp. grandiis.
