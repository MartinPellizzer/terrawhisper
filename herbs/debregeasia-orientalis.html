What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Debregeasia orientalis has large, simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green with prominent pinnate venation and a slightly coriaceous texture. Its flowers are characterized by numerous long, white stamens that create a delicate, brush-like appearance. These blooms are often accompanied by small, inconspicuous petals and emerge from axillary positions. The fruit produced by this species is a fleshy capsule that contains the developing seeds.
