Lindera thomsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through the hierarchical ranks, it is placed in the order Laurales and the family Lauraceae.
