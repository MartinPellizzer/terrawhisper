What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia halodendron?

Artemisia halodendroon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the biological order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, which places it within the widely recognized genus Artemisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, halodendroon.
