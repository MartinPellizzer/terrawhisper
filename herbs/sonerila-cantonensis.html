What's the taxonomical classification of Sonerila cantonensis?

Sonerila cantonensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Melastommataceae, falling into the genus Sonerila, and is specifically identified by the species epithet cantonensis.
