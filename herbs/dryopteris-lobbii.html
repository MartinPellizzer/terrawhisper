What is Dryopteris lobbii?

Dryopteris lobbii is an evergreen, terrestrial fern species native to Taiwan, belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It has sterile pinnatifid fronds, typically 2-3 times pinnate, with linear, lanceolate, or ovate pinnae, and a rachis covered with brown or dark brown scales. The fronds grow up to 60-100 cm in length, and are dark green on the upper surface, with lighter-colored scales on the rachis. The fertile fronds are smaller, with 1-3 times pinnate pinnae, and a rachis covered with brown or dark brown scales.

The plant is typically found in shaded, humid areas with rich soil, and is often associated with other fern species.
