10 Precautions To Take When Using Cleome Viscosa (Spider Flower)

Cleome viscosa has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as exercising caution when using it on pregnant or breastfeeding women.

This is because the herb may stimulate uterine contractions, which could lead to miscarriage or premature labor.

Similarly, the herb's stimulant properties can cause adverse effects, such as increased heart rate and blood pressure, if taken in excessive amounts.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Cleome viscosa medicinally.
