10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cleome Viscosa (Spider Flower)

Cleome viscosa has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal activities. The flavonoids possess antioxidant properties, which can help protect against diseases and improve overall health. For instance, the plant's extracts have been used to treat skin infections, wounds, and burns, promoting faster healing and reducing scarring.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties can alleviate pain and discomfort associated with various conditions, such as arthritis, improving the quality of life for many individuals.
