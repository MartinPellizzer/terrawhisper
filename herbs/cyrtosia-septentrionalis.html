What's the taxonomical classification of Cyrtosia septentrionalis?

Cyrtosia septentrionalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further nested within the family Orchidaceae, which characterizes its botanical relationship to other orchids. Specifically, it is identified under the genus Cyrtosia, with its unique designation being the species septentrionalis.
