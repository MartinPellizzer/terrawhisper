What's the taxonomical classification of Elatostema schizocephalum?

Elatostema schizocephalum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Rosales and the family Urticaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Elatostema, with the specific epithet schizocephalum defining its unique species status.
