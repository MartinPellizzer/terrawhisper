Cinnamomum zeylanicum, or cinnamon, has been studied for its potential to reduce blood pressure by improving vascular function and reducing oxidative stress.

Flavonoids in cinnamon also exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects that support cardiovascular health.

Together, these compounds make Cinnamomum zeylanicum a valuable natural remedy for managing hypertension.
