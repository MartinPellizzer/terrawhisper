What's the taxonomical classification of Spathoglottis pubescens?

Spathoglotis pubescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, which places it among the diverse group of orchids. Specifically, its taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Spathoglotis and the species pubescens.
