Carex pisiformis var. mayebarana

Carex pisiformis var. mayebarana, also known as Japanese mayebara sedge, is a type of plant belonging to the Carex genus of sedges.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, primarily its roots, which contain compounds with anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The plant is believed to have health benefits, such as reducing fever and alleviating rheumatism, due to its bioactive compounds.
