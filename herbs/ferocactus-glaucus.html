What is Ferocactus glaucus?

Ferocactus glaucus is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is a globular to cylindrical cactus, typically growing 30-60 cm tall and 20-40 cm wide, with 20-30 ribs, each bearing 8-15 areoles. The plant has short, white, or yellow flowers, 3-4 cm long, appearing in late spring. Ferocactus glaucus is native to Mexico, and its stems are covered with short, fine, grayish or brownish spines.

The fruits are red to purple, 3-4 cm long, and contain numerous seeds.
