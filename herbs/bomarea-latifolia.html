What is Bomarea latifolia?

Bomarea latifolia is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Alstroemeriaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, with a distribution that includes countries such as Mexico, Costa Rica, and Ecuador. The plant grows up to 2 meters in height, with long, narrow leaves that are typically 10-30 cm in length and 2-5 cm in width. The flowers of Bomarea latifolia are white to purple, bell-shaped, and arranged in a terminal inflorescence, with three sepals and three petals.

The flowers are 4-6 cm in length and 2-3 cm in width, with a sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators.
