Eurya groffii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eurya groffii has small, simple, evergreen leaves that are typically elliptic to ovate in shape with entire margins and a glossy, dark green upper surface. The leaf texture is somewhat coriaceous, providing a leathery feel that helps the plant retain moisture. Its stems are woody and branch out to form a compact, shrubby growth habit. Tiny, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in small clusters. The plant eventually produces small, fleshy fruits that serve as a key identifying feature of the species.
