What are the side effects of Corydalis ambigua when used improperly?

Additionally, excessive consumption may result in an adverse reaction with other medications, leading to serotonin syndrome, a life-threatening condition characterized by agitation, confusion, and altered consciousness.

Furthermore, improper use of Corydalis ambigua may also lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rash or itching, which can range from mild to severe in severity. Moreover, the herb's interaction with certain medications, including blood thinners, antihypertensives, and anti-depressants, can increase the risk of bleeding, hypotension, and serotonin toxicity, respectively.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Corydalis ambigua for medicinal purposes to ensure proper dosing, preparation, and monitoring to minimize the risk of these potential side effects.
