What is Taenitis trilobata?

Taenitis trilobata is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and Madagascar. The plant has a rhizome with long, slender, and hairy stipes, supporting bipinnate to tripinnate fronds that are 10-40 cm in length. The fronds have lanceolate to elliptical pinnae, with the lower pinnae being larger and having a longer stalk than the upper ones.

The plant's sporangia are borne on the underside of the fronds, in two rows along the midrib.
