What's the taxonomical classification of Circaea alpina?

Circaea alpina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is a member of the family Onagraceae, which contains the genus Circaea, and is specifically identified by the species name alpinas.
