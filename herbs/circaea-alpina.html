What's the taxonomical classification of Circaea alpina?

Circaea alpina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Myrtales. The plant is a member of the family Onagraceae, which contains the genus Circaea, and is specifically identified by the species name alpinas.
