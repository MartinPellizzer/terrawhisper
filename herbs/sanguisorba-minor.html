Sanguisorba Minor: What To Know Before Using It For Medicinal Purposes

Sanguisorba minor, commonly known as lesser dropwort, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties due to its high content of tannins and other bioactive compounds.

It is often employed in herbal medicine to treat digestive issues such as diarrhea and dysentery, as the tannins help to reduce inflammation and firm stools. The plant also contains mucilage, which can soothe irritated mucous membranes in the digestive tract. In traditional Chinese medicine, Sanguisorba minor is valued for its ability to clear heat and detoxify the body.

However, it should be used with caution, as excessive consumption may lead to gastrointestinal discomfort or other adverse effects.

Side Effects

While there is limited research on long-term effects, it is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before using it for medicinal purposes.
