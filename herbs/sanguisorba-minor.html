Sanguisorba (Sanguisorba minor)

Sanguisorba (Sanguisorba minor) is a member of the Rosaceae family, native to Asia, Eastern Asia, and China. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, diuretic, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
