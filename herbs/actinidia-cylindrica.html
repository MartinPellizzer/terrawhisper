Actinidia cylindrica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Actinidiaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Actinidia, with its specific identification being the species cylindrica.
