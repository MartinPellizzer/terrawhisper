Sarcococca longipetiolata

What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcococca longipetiolata?

Sarcococca longipetiolatata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows a distinct evolutionary lineage that places it in the order Buxales. Within this order, it is situated in the family Buxaceae, which is the group to which its genus, Sarcococca, pertains. Ultimately, the plant is identified at the species level as Sarcococca longipetiolatata.
