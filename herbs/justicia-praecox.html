Justicia praecox

Justicia praecox, also known as the early justicia or Mexican honeysuckle, is a perennial herb native to Mexico and Central America.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities, making it effective against infections and wounds.

The health benefits of Justicia praecox include reducing fever, alleviating respiratory issues, and promoting digestive health, as well as its potential to lower blood sugar levels and act as an antioxidant.

Herbal preparations of Justicia praecox include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which can be made by steeping the leaves in hot water or using a solvent like ethanol to extract the plant's active compounds.
