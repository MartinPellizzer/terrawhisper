Pedicularis muscicola belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further organized under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Pedicularis and the specific species epithet muscicola.
