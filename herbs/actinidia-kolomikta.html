What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia kolomikta?

Actinidia kolomikta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It follows the order Ericales and is a member of the family Actinidiaceae. More specifically, the plant is categorized under the genus Actinidia, with its specific designation being the species kolomikta.
