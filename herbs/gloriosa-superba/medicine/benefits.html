10 Health Benefits Of Gloriosa Superba (Flame Lily)

Gloriosa superba has health benefits such as treating fever, rheumatism, and skin diseases.

Its medicinal properties include anthraquinones, alkaloids, and flavonoids which exhibit anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal activities. For instance, the plant's extracts can be used to reduce pain and swelling in joints, improving mobility and quality of life for individuals with arthritis.

Additionally, its topical application can help treat skin conditions like eczema and acne, promoting healthy and clear skin.
