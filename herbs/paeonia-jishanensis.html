What's the taxonomical classification of Paeonia jishanensis?

Paeonia jishanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Paeoniaceae and the genus Paeonia, ultimately reaching its specific designation at the species level as Paeonia jishanensis.
