What's the taxonomical classification of Metastachydium sagittatum?

Metastachydium sagittatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is a member of the order Lamiales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Lamiaceae, specifically within the genus Metastachydium, with its unique designation as the species sagittatum.
