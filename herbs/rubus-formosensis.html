Rubus formosenis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is placed in the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. It is further categorized under the genus Rubus, specifically identified by the species name formosenis.
