What is Stoechas Helichrysum (Helichrysum Stoechas)?

Stoechas Helichrysum, also known as Helichrysum stoechas, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and distinctive aromatic leaves.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Stoechas Helichrysum is Helichrysum stoechas. This plant belongs to the family Asteraceae, order Asterales, class Magnoliopsida, division Magnoliophyta, and kingdom Plantae. It is commonly known as the Cape gold in South Africa, and as the golden everlasting in regions of Europe and the Mediterranean. The synonyms for this plant include Helichrysum microphyllum and Helichrysum stoechas var. microphyllum.
