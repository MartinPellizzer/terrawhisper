Stoechas Helichrysum (Helichrysum stoechas)

Stoechas Helichrysum (Helichrysum stoechas) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Southern Africa, Madagascar, and Comoros. Traditionally, its flowers, leaves, and resin have been used for infusions, decoctions, and oil infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
