10 Possible Side Effects Of Hypoxis Hemerocallidea (Star Grass)

These side effects can occur due to improper dosages or prolonged use. For instance, gastrointestinal issues can lead to dehydration, nausea, and abdominal pain, making everyday activities challenging.
