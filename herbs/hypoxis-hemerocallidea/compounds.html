Hypoxis (Hypoxis hemerocallidea) Compounds

The primary compounds in Hypoxis include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and terpenoids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Glycosides and phenolic acids contribute to the plant's medicinal value by supporting immune function and reducing oxidative stress. Saponins and tannins provide antimicrobial and astringent properties, while coumarins and alkaloids offer additional therapeutic benefits. Essential oils and anthocyanins further enhance the plant's pharmacological profile.
