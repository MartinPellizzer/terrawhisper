What's the taxonomical classification of Saurauia napaulensis?

Saurauia napaulensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Ericales. This specific plant is a member of the family Actinidiaceae and falls under the genus Saurauia, ultimately identified by its unique species name, napaulensis.
