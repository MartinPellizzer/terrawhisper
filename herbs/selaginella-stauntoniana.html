Selaginella stauntoniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Selaginellaceae and is situated within the genus Selaginella, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation at the species level as stauntoniana.
