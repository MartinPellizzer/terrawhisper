Cicuta Virosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cicuta virosa, commonly known as water hemlock, is a highly toxic herb native to North America, belonging to the parsley family.

Despite its dangers, it has been historically used in small, controlled doses for its potent medicinal properties.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as cicutoxin, which contribute to its therapeutic effects, though its use is extremely limited due to severe toxicity.

Herbal preparations such as infusions or tinctures have been traditionally made, but they require extreme caution and professional supervision due to the risk of poisoning.
