Potentilla glabra var. mandshurica

What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla glabra var. mandshurica?

Potentiella glabra var. mandshurica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is situated within the family Rosaceae. At the most specific level of its biological classification, it is identified as a variety of the species Potentiella glabra, specifically designated as the variety mandshurica.
