Myrianthus cuneliifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression through the order Rosales, it is placed in the family Urticaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Myrianthus, and its species designation, cuneliifolius.
