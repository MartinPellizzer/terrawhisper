Senna obtusifolia, a medicinal plant, is known to relieve constipation by stimulating bowel movements through its natural laxative properties.

Senna obtusifolia relieves constipation because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

Flavonoids contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which support digestive health.

Together, these constituents make Senna obtusifolia an effective herbal remedy for constipation.
