10 Medicinal Preparations Of Senna Obtusifolia (Cassia)

Senna obtusifolia has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, decoctions, and infusions, which are traditionally used for their purgative properties.

These preparations are useful for treating constipation, diarrhea, and gastrointestinal disorders. The plant's leaves and pods are also used to make tinctures and ointments, which are applied topically to treat skin conditions like eczema and dermatitis.

Its medicinal preparations are also used in Ayurvedic and traditional Chinese medicine.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Senna obtusifolia.

1. Teas

Senna obtusifolia teas is used to alleviate various health issues.

The tea has been traditionally employed to treat constipation, diarrhea, and irritable bowel syndrome due to its anthraquinone compounds that stimulate the intestines and promote bowel movements. Additionally, it has anti-inflammatory properties, making it effective in reducing menstrual cramps, fever, and skin conditions such as eczema and acne.

Its antioxidant properties also contribute to its potential use in preventing cardiovascular diseases and cancer.
