What's the taxonomical classification of Buddleja crispa?

Buddlejia crispa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Lamiales and the family Scrophulariaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Buddleja, and its specific species name, crispa.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
