Aquilegia yabeana

What's the taxonomical classification of Aquilegia yabeana?

Aquilegia yabeana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Aquilegia, ultimately reaching its specific classification at the species level as Aquilegia yabeana.

These molecules also show affinity for modulating nitric oxide synthase (NOS) activity, thereby reducing oxidative stress in cellular environments. Additionally, certain constituents may influence the expression of various transcription factors involved in the inflammatory cascade. This multi-target approach allows the plant to exert complex pharmacological effects on the immune and nervous systems.

Because the toxicological profile is not fully established, any consumption carries a high risk of unpredictable systemic reactions.
