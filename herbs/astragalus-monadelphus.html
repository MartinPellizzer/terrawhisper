Astragalus monadelphus

What's the taxonomical classification of Astragalus monadelphus?

Astragalus monadelphus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Fabales. It is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of nitrogen-fixing legumes. Within this family, the plant is placed in the genus Astragalus, and its specific identity is defined by its species name, monadelphus.
