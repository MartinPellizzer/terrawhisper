Smooth Hydrangea (Hydrangea arborescens) Compounds

Smooth Hydrangea contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which act as antioxidants. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and anthocyanins provide astringent and coloring effects. Coumarin and glycosides may support digestive health. Saponins, terpenoids, and essential oils have mild detoxifying and antimicrobial actions. Alkaloids contribute to its overall medicinal profile.
