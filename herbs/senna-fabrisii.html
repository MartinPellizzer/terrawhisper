Senna fabrisii

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include treating various health issues such as fever, cough, and skin conditions.

Medicinal Parts of Senna fabrisii

Senna fabrisii has many medicinal parts, such as the leaves, flowers, roots, and pods, which are used in traditional medicine to treat various health issues.

The leaves of the senna fabrisii plant contain anthraquinone glycosides like aloe-emodin-8-O-β-D-glucopyranoside and emodin-8-O-β-D-glucopyranoside, which have laxative properties.

The roots, on the other hand, contain sennosides, which are also used as laxatives due to their anthraquinone content.

The pods of the senna fabrisii plant contain a variety of alkaloids, including fabrissin and fabriquinone, which have been shown to exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
