What's the taxonomical classification of Toxocarpus aurantiacus?

Toxocarpus aurantiacus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae, which places it within the genus Toxocarpus, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species aurantiacus.
