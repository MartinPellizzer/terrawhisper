10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rhus Chinensis (Chinese Sumac)

Rhus chinensis, also known as Chinese sumac, has active constituents such as gallic acid, flavonoids, and tannins.

These constituents are known for their anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties. The medicinal properties of Chinese sumac include pain relief, wound healing, and anti-aging.

For example, its anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate arthritis symptoms, while its antioxidant properties can protect against cell damage and promote overall health and well-being.
