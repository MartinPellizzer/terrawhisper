Iris chrysographes

What's the taxonomical classification of Iris chrysographes?

Iris chrysographes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As part of the order Asparagales, it is situated within the family Iridaeceae. Finally, its taxonomic position is completed by its placement in the genus Iris, identifying it specifically as the species Iris chrysographes.

What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

Iris chrysographes contains diverse secondary metabolites including iridoids, flavonoids, and various phenolic compounds that contribute to its bioactive potential.
