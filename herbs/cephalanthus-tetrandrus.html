Cephalanthus tetrandrus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Rubiaceae, which is a prominent group of flowering plants. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Cephalanthus, and its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, tetrandrus.
