laevigata, also known as the English hawthorn or smooth hawthorn, is a subspecies of hawthorn tree native to the United Kingdom and Europe.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, as well as its ability to help regulate blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health.

The health benefits of Crataegus oxyacantha subsp.

laevigata include reducing the risk of heart disease, improving blood lipid profiles, and acting as a natural anti-arrhythmic agent.
