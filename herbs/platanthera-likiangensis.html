What's the taxonomical classification of Platanthera likiangensis?

Platanthera likiangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Platanthera, specifically identified as the species likiangensis.
