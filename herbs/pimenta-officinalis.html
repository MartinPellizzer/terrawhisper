Pimento (Pimenta officinalis)

Pimento (Pimenta officinalis) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to South America, Central America, and Caribbean. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, carminative, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and ayurvedic medicine.
