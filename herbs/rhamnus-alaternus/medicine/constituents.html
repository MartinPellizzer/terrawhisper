10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rhamnus Alaternus (Italian Buckthorn)

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, has active constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and iridoids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it useful for treating digestive issues and skin conditions. The iridoids, in particular, have been shown to have hepatoprotective effects, improving liver function and overall health.

By utilizing these constituents, individuals can potentially alleviate symptoms of digestive disorders and maintain healthy skin, ultimately improving their quality of life.
