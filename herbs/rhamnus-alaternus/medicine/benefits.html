10 Health Benefits Of Rhamnus Alaternus (Italian Buckthorn)

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, due to its rich content of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These properties can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis and reduce the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and cancer.

For example, its antioxidant properties can improve cardiovascular health by protecting against oxidative stress, while its anti-inflammatory properties can alleviate joint pain and inflammation, allowing people to live more comfortably and maintain an active lifestyle.

1. Reduces inflammation

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, reduces inflammation because of its high content of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds have potent antioxidant properties that help to neutralize free radicals, which can cause inflammation and tissue damage. The flavonoids present in Italian buckthorn also inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, thus contributing to the reduction of inflammation and promotion of healing.

Its anti-inflammatory effects have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

3. Kills cancer cells

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, kills cancer cells because of its high content of flavonoids and terpenoids, particularly ursolic acid and oleanolic acid.

These compounds have been shown to induce apoptosis (programmed cell death) in cancer cells, while sparing healthy cells.

Studies have demonstrated that Italian buckthorn extract can inhibit the growth of various cancer types, including breast, lung, and colon cancer, making it a promising candidate for cancer therapy.

4. Heals skin ulcers

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, heals skin ulcers because of its astringent and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's extracts have been shown to stimulate the formation of new tissue, reducing the size of ulcers and promoting wound healing.

Its antimicrobial properties also help prevent infections, allowing the body to focus on repairing damaged tissue, making it an effective natural remedy for skin ulcers.

5. Treats varicose veins

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, treats varicose veins because it contains flavonoids and tannins, which have been shown to improve blood vessel function and reduce inflammation.

The plant's extract has been traditionally used in Mediterranean herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins, such as swelling and discomfort.

Its bioactive compounds may help to strengthen blood vessel walls and improve circulation, thereby reducing the appearance and discomfort of varicose veins.

6. Relieves menstrual cramps

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, relieves menstrual cramps because of its rich flavonoid and lignan content.

The plant's lignans, specifically, have been shown to mimic the effects of the hormone progesterone, helping to regulate uterine contractions and reduce spasmodic pain.

Additionally, Italian buckthorn's flavonoids have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may also contribute to its analgesic effects and help to alleviate menstrual cramp symptoms.

7. Helps lower blood pressure

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, helps lower blood pressure because it is rich in flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs), which have potent antioxidant and vasoprotective effects.

These compounds help to relax and dilate blood vessels, reducing blood pressure and improving cardiovascular health.

The OPCs in Rhamnus alaternus also help to inhibit the activity of enzymes that contribute to high blood pressure, making it a valuable natural remedy for individuals with hypertension.

8. Improves digestion issues

Rhamnus alaternus improves digestion issues because of its anti-inflammatory properties and ability to regulate bowel movements.

The plant contains anthraquinones, which stimulate the digestive system and increase the frequency and ease of bowel movements. This helps to alleviate symptoms of constipation, irritable bowel syndrome, and other digestive disorders, promoting a healthier gut and overall digestive function.

Regular consumption can also improve nutrient absorption.

9. Reduces blood sugar levels

Rhamnus alaternus, also known as Italian buckthorn, reduces blood sugar levels because it is rich in flavonoids and anthocyanins, which are powerful antioxidants that help regulate glucose metabolism.

These compounds have been shown to improve insulin sensitivity, allowing cells to absorb glucose more efficiently, thus lowering blood sugar levels.

Regular consumption of Rhamnus alaternus may also help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress, further contributing to its blood sugar-lowering effects.
