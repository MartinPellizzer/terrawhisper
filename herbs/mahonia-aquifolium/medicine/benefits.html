10 Health Benefits Of Mahonia Aquifolium (Oregon Grape)

Mahonia aquifolium, also known as Oregon grape, has health benefits such as treating skin conditions like eczema and acne due to its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

Its berberine content also exhibits antimicrobial and antiviral activities, making it effective against infections. Additionally, Oregon grape's antioxidant-rich extracts can help alleviate digestive issues like constipation and bloating.

1. Strengthens the immune system

Mahonia aquifolium, also known as Oregon Grape, strengthens the immune system because of its rich concentration of bioactive compounds, particularly berberine and polyphenols.

Additionally, Oregon Grape has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat a range of health issues, including respiratory problems and skin conditions, further supporting its immune-boosting effects.

2. Acts as a natural antibiotic

Berberine has been shown to inhibit the growth of various microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, and fungi, by interfering with their cell membrane structure and disrupting their metabolic processes.

As a result, Oregon grape extract has been used traditionally to treat a range of infections, from minor wounds to more severe conditions like pneumonia and skin diseases.
