Carex distans var. hostiana

Carex distans var. hostiana, also known as the Northern Sedge or Host's Sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

Traditionally, Carex distans var. hostiana has been used for its medicinal properties, particularly its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant is believed to have various health benefits, including reducing inflammation, promoting wound healing, and supporting digestive health.

Herbal preparations of Carex distans var. hostiana can be made by infusing the leaves and roots in hot water to create a tea or decoction, although its use should be consulted with a healthcare professional or herbalist due to limited research on its efficacy and potential interactions.
