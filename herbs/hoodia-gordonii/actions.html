Hoodia (Hoodia gordonii) Actions

Hoodia gordonii exhibits several therapeutic actions including stimulant, anti-inflammatory, and tonic properties. It also acts as a bitter and diuretic, aiding digestion and fluid balance. Additionally, it has carminative, sedative, and expectorant effects, while suppressing appetite and providing antispasmodic and astringent benefits.
