Hoodia (Hoodia gordonii) Uses

Hoodia gordonii is primarily used to treat obesity and diabetes by regulating appetite and improving insulin sensitivity. It also helps with hormonal imbalance and insulin resistance, while addressing symptoms like fatigue, insomnia, anxiety, depression, and stress through its appetite-suppressing and mood-stabilizing effects.

Hoodia (Hoodia gordonii) Monograph

Hoodia gordonii is traditionally used for its appetite-suppressing properties, making it a focus in obesity research.
