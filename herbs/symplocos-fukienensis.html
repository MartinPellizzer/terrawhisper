What's the taxonomical classification of Symplocos fukienensis?

Symplocos fukienensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and placed in the order Ericales. The taxonomic hierarchy continues into the family Symplocaceae, which contains the genus Symplocos, and it is specifically identified as the species fukienensis.
