What's the taxonomical classification of Axyris amaranthoides?

Axyris amaranthoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a representative of the family Amaranthaceae, specifically situated within the genus Axyris, and is identified by its unique species designation, amaranthoides.
