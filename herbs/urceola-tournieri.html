Urceola tournieri

What's the taxonomical classification of Urceola tournieri?

Urceola tournieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Gentianales. This specific plant is a representative of the family Apocynaceae, situated within the genus Urceola, and is identified by its unique species designation, tournieri.

What are the therapeutic actions of Urceola tournieri?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, wounds, stomach aches, and various inflammatory conditions. It is frequently employed in traditional medicine to address localized swelling and minor cuts. The plant's properties are often utilized to soothe digestive discomfort and abdominal pain. Some practitioners also apply it topically to help manage dermatological issues. These therapeutic applications are central to its use in regional herbal practices.
