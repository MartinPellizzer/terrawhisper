What are the medicinal properties of Prunus africana?

The antimicrobial properties of P. africana are also significant, with studies demonstrating its ability to inhibit the growth of a range of microorganisms, including bacteria, fungi, and viruses, making it a potential treatment for infections and diseases caused by microbial pathogens.
