Lonicera Periclymenum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Lonicera periclymenum, commonly known as honeysuckle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family, widely used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties.

This herb is valued for its ability to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of colds and fevers.

The bioactive constituents in honeysuckle include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures can be made from honeysuckle to harness its therapeutic benefits.
