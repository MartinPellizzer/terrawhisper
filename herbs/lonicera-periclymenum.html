Lonicera Periclymenum

Lonicera periclymenum, commonly known as honeysuckle, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties for centuries.

It contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects. In traditional Chinese medicine, honeysuckle is often used to treat fever, sore throat, and skin infections due to its cooling properties. Modern research has also shown its potential in supporting immune function and reducing oxidative stress.

However, it is important to use honeysuckle under the guidance of a healthcare professional to ensure safe and effective application.
