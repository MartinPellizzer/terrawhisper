Spiraea japonica var. glabra

What's the taxonomical classification of Spiraea japonica var. glabra?

Spiraea japonica var. glabra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic progression continues through the family Rosaceae, leading to its placement in the genus Spiraea. Ultimately, the specific botanical identity of this plant is defined by its unique variety, Spiraea japonica var. glabra.
