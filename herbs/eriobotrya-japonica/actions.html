Loquat (Eriobotrya japonica) Actions

Loquat is a tonic that strengthens the body and supports overall health. It has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and irritation. As a carminative, it helps relieve gas and bloating. It also acts as an expectorant, aiding in the removal of mucus from the respiratory tract.
