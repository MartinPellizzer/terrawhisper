10 Medicinal Constituents Of Eriobotrya Japonica (Loquat)

Eriobotrya japonica has active constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and triterpenoids.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Furthermore, the triterpenoids have been found to possess anti-cancer properties, making loquat a valuable plant for natural health remedies and potentially improving the lives of individuals suffering from chronic diseases.
