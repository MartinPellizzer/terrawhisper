Stuckenia pectinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its evolutionary path further, it falls under the order Alismatales and is a member of the family Potamogetonaceae. Within this botanical framework, the plant is identified by the genus Stuckenia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species pectinata.
