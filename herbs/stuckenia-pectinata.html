Stuckenia pectinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its evolutionary path further, it falls under the order Alismatales and is a member of the family Potamogetonaceae. Within this botanical framework, the plant is identified by the genus Stuckenia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species pectinata.
