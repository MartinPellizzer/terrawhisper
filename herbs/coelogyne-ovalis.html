What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Coelogyne ovalis has sympodial rhizomes supporting numerous small, elliptical to ovate leaves that are fleshy and somewhat leathery in texture. These blossoms feature yellowish or greenish petals and a prominent, somewhat fleshy lip that is often marked with subtle patterns.
