10 Possible Side Effects Of Lobaria Pulmonaria (Lungwort)

Lobaria pulmonaria has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory problems.

These side effects can occur when the herb is not properly identified, and its potency is not measured accurately. For instance, individuals with pre-existing respiratory conditions may experience worsened symptoms, leading to reduced lung function and increased difficulty in performing daily activities.

In severe cases, anaphylaxis can occur, posing a life-threatening risk.
