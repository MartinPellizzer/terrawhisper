10 Precautions To Take When Using Lobaria Pulmonaria (Lungwort)

Lobaria pulmonaria has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding individuals.

It's essential to take these precautions to avoid potential side effects like interactions with medications, allergic reactions, or excessive bleeding.

For instance, taking Lobaria pulmonaria without consulting a healthcare professional can lead to unwanted interactions with blood thinners or exacerbate underlying medical conditions, resulting in complications.
