Hoyas lyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Hoya, and its unique species designation, lyi.

What side-effects this plant can have?
