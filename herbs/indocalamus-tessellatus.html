What's the taxonomical classification of Indocalamus tessellatus?

Indocalamus tessellatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Poales. This plant is further categorized into the family Poaceae, specifically within the genus Indocalamus, and is identified by the species name tessellatus.
