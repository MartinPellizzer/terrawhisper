What's the taxonomical classification of Asarum geophilum?

Asarum geophilum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae, following the evolutionary lineage of the order Piperales. This plant is a member of the family Aristolochiaceae, which places it within the genus Asarum, and is specifically identified by its unique species designation, geophilum.
