What's the taxonomical classification of Cryptolepis buchananii?

Cryptolepis buchananii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Cryptolepis and the species buchananii.
