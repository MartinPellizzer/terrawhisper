10 Health Benefits Of Tropaeolum Majus (Nasturtium)

Tropaeolum majus has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain, treating skin conditions like eczema and acne, and boosting the immune system.

Its medicinal properties include antioxidants, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory compounds, which help to soothe irritated skin, calm digestive issues, and combat infections.

For example, a topical application of nasturtium can alleviate symptoms of rosacea and reduce redness, improving one's confidence and overall well-being.
