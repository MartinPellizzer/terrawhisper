10 Precautions To Take When Using Tropaeolum Majus (Nasturtium)

Tropaeolum majus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional due to its potential allergenic and phototoxic properties.

This is especially important for individuals with sensitive skin or allergies. If not taken seriously, using Tropaeolum majus may result in skin irritation, increased photosensitivity, and even anaphylaxis in severe cases.

Proper precautions help ensure safe and effective use.
