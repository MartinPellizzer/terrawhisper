Deutzia parviflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Deutzia parviflora?

Deutzia parviflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it resides in the order Cornales and the family Hydrangeaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Deutzia, and its specific species designation, parviflora.
