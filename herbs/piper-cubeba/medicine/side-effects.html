10 Possible Side Effects Of Piper Cubeba (Cubeb)

Piper cubeba has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and vomiting, which may be caused by excessive consumption or poor digestion.

These side effects can worsen the lives of people with pre-existing gastrointestinal conditions, leading to malnutrition, weight loss, and fatigue.

In severe cases, it may cause allergic reactions, such as hives and itching, which can impact daily activities and overall quality of life.
