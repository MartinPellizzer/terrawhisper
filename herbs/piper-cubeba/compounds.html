Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba) Compounds

The primary compounds of Cubeb Pepper include essential oil, flavonoids, coumarin, alkaloids, tannins, phenolic acids, terpenoids, glycosides, saponins, and anthocyanins. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects. Each plays a role in the plant's therapeutic potential in herbal medicine.

Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba) Monograph

1. Essential oil

The essential oil of Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba) contains bioactive compounds like cubebin and piperine. It is valued for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, commonly used in traditional herbal medicine to treat skin infections and digestive issues.

Flavonoids in Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba) contribute to its medicinal properties by exhibiting antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin is a key active compound in Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine for its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties.
