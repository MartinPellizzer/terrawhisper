Possible Side Effects of Ruellia multifolia

Ruellia multifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its saponin and alkaloid content.

In severe cases, improper use of Ruellia multifolia can cause allergic reactions, such as skin irritation, itching, and respiratory distress.

Additionally, pregnant or breastfeeding women should exercise caution when using the plant, as it may trigger uterine contractions or affect milk production.
