What's the taxonomical classification of Arisaema decipiens?

Arisaema decipiens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through the hierarchical levels of its classification, it falls under the order Alismatales and is a member of the family Araceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Arisaema, and its specific species designation, decipiens.
