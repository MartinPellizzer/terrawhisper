Hodgsonia macrocarpa var. capniocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Hodgsonia macrocarpa var. capniocarpa?

Hodgsonia macrocarpa var. capniocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, this plant is further organized into the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified as part of the genus Hodgsonia, specifically categorized as the variety capniocarpa of the species Hodgsonia macrocarpa.
