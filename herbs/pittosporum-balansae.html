Pittosporum balansae

What's the taxonomical classification of Pittosporum balansae?

Pittosporum balansae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Apiales. It is further categorized into the family Pittosporaceae and the genus Pittosporum, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species balansae.
