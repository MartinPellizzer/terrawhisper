Water Pennywort (Hydrocotyle asiatica) Compounds

Water Pennywort contains several active compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Coumarin and saponins may support liver function and digestive health, while glycosides and terpenoids have potential cardiovascular and antimicrobial benefits. Essential oils and alkaloids add to its diverse therapeutic profile. Anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant support.
