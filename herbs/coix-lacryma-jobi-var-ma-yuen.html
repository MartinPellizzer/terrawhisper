Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen

What's the taxonomical classification of Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen?

Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the order Poales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Poaceae, situated within the genus Coix and identified by the species Coix.
