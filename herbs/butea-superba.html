In the culinary world, red Kwao Krua is often used to add flavor and color to traditional Thai dishes such as curries and soups. Horticulturally speaking, this herb is a tropical perennial that thrives in warm climates and requires minimal maintenance. From a botanical perspective, Red Kwao Krua belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red flowers and slender leaves.

Historically, this herb has been an integral part of traditional Thai medicine for centuries, with references dating back to ancient Ayurvedic texts.
