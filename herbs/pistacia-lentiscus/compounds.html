Lentisk (Pistacia lentiscus) Compounds

The primary compounds in Lentisk include tannins, which have astringent properties, and phenolic acids, known for their antioxidant effects. Flavonoids and terpenoids contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities. Coumarin and essential oils provide aromatic and potential antispasmodic benefits. Glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins also play roles in its therapeutic effects.

Lentisk (Pistacia lentiscus) Monograph

Tannin is a key active compound in Lentisk (Pistacia lentiscus). Phenolic acid is a key active compound in Lentisk (Pistacia lentiscus), contributing to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
