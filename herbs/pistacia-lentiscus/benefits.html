How this herb helps with reduces inflammation?

Pistacia lentiscus reduces inflammation because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds possess potent anti-inflammatory properties that inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines. The synergistic effects of these compounds help suppress inflammatory pathways in the body.

As a result, Pistacia lentiscus is valued for its therapeutic potential in managing inflammatory conditions.
