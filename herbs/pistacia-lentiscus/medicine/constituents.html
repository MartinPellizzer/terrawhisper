10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pistacia Lentiscus (Mastich)

Pistacia lentiscus has active constituents such as terpenes, flavonoids, and polyphenols.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The constituents in mastich can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma by reducing inflammation and opening airways.

Additionally, the plant's antioxidant properties can improve cardiovascular health by protecting against oxidative stress and cholesterol buildup, ultimately reducing the risk of heart disease and improving overall well-being.
