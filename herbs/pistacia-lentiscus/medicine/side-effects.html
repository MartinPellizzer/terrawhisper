10 Possible Side Effects Of Pistacia Lentiscus (Mastich)

These side effects are often caused by excessive consumption, individual sensitivity, or combining with other substances.

For instance, mastich oil can cause nausea and vomiting, making it difficult for people to manage chronic conditions like irritable bowel syndrome.
