Gentianella dichotoma

Gentianella dichotoma, also known as yellow gentian, is a perennial plant species belonging to the gentian family.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its bitter compounds, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of Gentianella dichotoma include its potential to reduce fever, alleviate digestive issues, and support the immune system.

Gentianella dichotoma can be prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or infusion, and is also used in traditional folk medicine to treat various ailments.
