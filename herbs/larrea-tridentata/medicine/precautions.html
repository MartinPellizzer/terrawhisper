10 Precautions To Take When Using Larrea Tridentata (Creosote Bush)

Larrea tridentata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring liver and kidney function, as excessive consumption can lead to toxicity.

These precautions are crucial to avoid adverse effects.
