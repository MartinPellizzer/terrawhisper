10 Medicinal Constituents Of Larrea Tridentata (Creosote Bush)

Larrea tridentata has active constituents such as nordihydroguaiaretic acid (NDGA) and diterpenoids that exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

These constituents can improve lives by reducing inflammation, oxidative stress, and infection in various health conditions, such as arthritis, skin irritations, and respiratory issues.

For example, NDGA has been shown to reduce pain and inflammation in patients with rheumatoid arthritis, while its antioxidant properties may help protect against cell damage and promote overall well-being.

Its unique composition and therapeutic benefits have sparked ongoing research into its applications in modern medicine.
