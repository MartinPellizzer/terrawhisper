10 Medicinal Constituents Of Bidens Pilosa (Spanish Needle)

Bidens pilosa, also known as Spanish Needle, has active constituents such as flavonoids, lignans, and saponins.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The flavonoids and lignans in Spanish Needle have been shown to reduce inflammation and improve respiratory health, making it a potential treatment for asthma and bronchitis.

Additionally, the saponins can help lower cholesterol levels and regulate blood pressure, improving cardiovascular health.

1. Beta-amyrin

Bidens pilosa, also known as Spanish needle, beta-amyrin is a bioactive triterpenoid compound extracted from the plant's aerial parts.

This saponin has been extensively studied for its potential medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal activities.

Beta-amyrin has been shown to exhibit potent inhibitory effects on certain enzymes and may be useful in the treatment of various diseases, such as cancer, diabetes, and cardiovascular disorders.
