Beggarticks (Bidens pilosa) Compounds

The primary compounds in Beggarticks include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to its astringent effects. Phenolic acids and coumarin also play roles in its medicinal properties, while saponins and glycosides may support anti-inflammatory actions. Terpenoids, essential oils, and alkaloids further contribute to its diverse therapeutic potential. Anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant benefits.
