Banisteriopsis Caapi Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

This herb contains bioactive constituents such as harmine, harmaline, and tetrahydrobeta-carboline alkaloids, which are responsible for its psychoactive and therapeutic effects.

It is believed to offer health benefits including emotional healing, stress reduction, and enhanced mental clarity.

Herbal preparations of Banisteriopsis caapi can be made into infusions, tinctures, or teas, though its use is often guided by experienced practitioners due to its potent nature.
