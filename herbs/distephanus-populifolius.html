What is Distephanus populifolius?

Distephanus populifolius is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual herb, characterized by a branched stem and leafy rosettes with linear, serrated leaves that are 5-20 cm long. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers in a capitulum, consisting of 15-20 florets with an outer ring of 2-4 larger florets and an inner ring of 10-16 smaller florets. The florets are heterogamous, with the larger florets being female and the smaller florets being male.

Distephanus populifolius is native to tropical Africa.
