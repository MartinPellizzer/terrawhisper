Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Annual Seseli include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin contributes to its mild sedative qualities, while tannins provide astringent properties. Terpenoids and essential oils are responsible for its aromatic profile and potential antimicrobial activity.
