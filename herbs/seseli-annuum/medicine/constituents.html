10 Medicinal Constituents Of Seseli Annuum (Fool'S Parsley)

Seseli annuum has active constituents such as seselin and apiol, which possess analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties.

These compounds help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, such as cramps and flatulence, making Seseli annuum a natural remedy for irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) sufferers.

Additionally, seselin has been shown to exhibit antimicrobial activities, which can aid in wound healing and infection prevention, potentially improving overall health and wellbeing.
