Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) Uses

Annual Seseli is used to treat various respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and asthma. It helps reduce inflammation and mucous buildup in the airways. It is also applied for respiratory tract congestion and infections, including tuberculosis. Its properties support clearing the respiratory system and easing breathing.

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) Monograph

1. Cough

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) is used to alleviate cough symptoms due to its expectorant properties. It helps loosen mucus in the respiratory tract, making it easier to expel. The herb is often prepared as a tea or tincture for its soothing and anti-inflammatory effects on the throat. It is particularly effective for dry or productive coughs associated with respiratory conditions.

2. Inflammation

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) has been traditionally used to reduce inflammation due to its bioactive compounds, which exhibit anti-inflammatory properties. It is often applied topically for skin irritations and internally for conditions like arthritis. Its effectiveness is supported by historical use in herbal medicine practices.

3. Bronchitis

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in treating bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help reduce bronchial irritation and loosen mucus, easing breathing. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to alleviate symptoms associated with chronic bronchitis.

4. Respiratory tract congestion

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) is used to alleviate respiratory tract congestion due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce swelling in the airways, making it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis and asthma. Its traditional use supports clearing congestion and improving breathing in respiratory ailments.

5. Respiratory infections

6. Tuberculosis
