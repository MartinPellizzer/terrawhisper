Hemiphragma heterophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the family Plantaginaceae, which contains the specific genus Hemiphragma. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, heterophyllum.
