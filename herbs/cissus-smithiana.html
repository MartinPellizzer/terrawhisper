Cissus smithiana

Cissus smithiana, also known as the grape ivy or veld grape, is a type of climbing plant that belongs to the grape family (Vitaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating arthritis, rheumatism, and other inflammatory conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The health benefits of Cissus smithiana include its ability to improve bone density, reduce pain and inflammation, and promote wound healing.

Herbal preparations of Cissus smithiana can be found in various forms, including capsules, tablets, and teas, which are often used to support joint health and overall well-being.
