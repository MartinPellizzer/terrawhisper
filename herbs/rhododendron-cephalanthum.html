What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Rhododendron cephalanthum has small, lanceolate leaves that are dark green and leathery with a slightly revolute margin. Each blossom typically displays a pale pink to white coloration with subtle nectar guides. The overall structure of the plant is compact, making it well-suited for understory environments.
