Gilia latiflora var. caruifolia

Gilia latiflora var. caruifolia, commonly known as Mexican Gilia, is a perennial plant native to Mexico that belongs to the Plantaginaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including inflammation, fever, and respiratory issues, due to its potential health benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Gilia latiflora var. caruifolia are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antiviral properties, which help to soothe and protect the body from infections and diseases.
