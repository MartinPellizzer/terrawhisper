What's the taxonomical classification of Lepisorus contortus?

Lepisorus contortus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and is organized within the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic placement continues through the family Polypodiaceae, specifically within the genus Lepisorus, ultimately reaching its unique designation at the species level as L. contortus.
