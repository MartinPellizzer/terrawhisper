Barrenwort (Epimedium brevicornu) Compounds

The primary compounds in Barrenwort include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal effects. Saponins and terpenoids also play roles in its therapeutic actions, while phenolic acids and coumarins provide additional health benefits. Alkaloids, tannins, essential oils, and anthocyanins further support its use in traditional herbal medicine.
