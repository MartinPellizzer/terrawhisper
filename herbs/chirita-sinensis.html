Chirita sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita sinensis?

Chirita sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Lamiales and falls under the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Chirita and the species name sinensis.

Its inflorescences consist of tubular, zygomorphic flowers that are often purplish or pinkish in hue. These blossoms emerge from the leaf axils and possess a distinct bilabiate structure characteristic of the Gesneriaceae family.
