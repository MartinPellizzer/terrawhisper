What's the taxonomical classification of Bolbitis heteroclita?

Bolbitis heteroclita belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is organized under the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Bolbitis, and its unique species designation, heteroclita.
