Lonicera tatarinowii

What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera tatarinowii?

Lonicera tatarinowii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Dipsacales. As a member of the family Caprifoliaceae, it is situated within the genus Lonicera, with the specific designation of the species tatarinowii.
