What's the taxonomical classification of Artocarpus hypargyreus?

Artocarpus hypargyreus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within its taxonomic hierarchy, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Rosales and placed within the family Moraeaecae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Artocarpus, and its specific species designation, hypargyreuus.

    What are the herbal preparations of Artocarpus hypargyreus?

    Artocarpus hypargyreus is prepared as a decoction, a tea, or a topical poultice using the plant's various parts like leaves, bark, or fruit. There is also limited clinical data regarding long-term systemic effects or interactions with pharmaceutical medications. Therefore, caution is necessary when utilizing this plant for medicinal purposes.
