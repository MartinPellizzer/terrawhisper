White Mulberry (Morus Alba Subvar. Colombassa)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Morus alba subvar. colombassa.

*Morus alba* subvar. *colombassa*, commonly known as the Colombian white mulberry, has been traditionally used in medicinal practices for its various health benefits. This variant is particularly valued for its leaves, which contain high levels of nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support immune function and overall well-being. The leaves are often consumed raw or brewed into teas to treat ailments like inflammation, digestive issues, and respiratory conditions. In traditional medicine, it has also been used to manage diabetes due to its potential hypoglycemic properties. Research continues to explore its bioactive compounds for their therapeutic applications in modern pharmacology.
