What is Phaius blumei?

Phaius blumei is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial, sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs, which are cylindrical or conical in shape and measure up to 10 cm in height and 2 cm in width. The leaves are elliptical, measuring up to 60 cm in length and 10 cm in width, with a leathery texture and a waxy sheen. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing 10-20 flowers, each measuring up to 4 cm in length and 2.5 cm in width, with a yellow and brown coloration and a sweet fragrance.
