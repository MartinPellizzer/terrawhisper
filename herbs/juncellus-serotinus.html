Juncellus serotinus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Poales and the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Juncellus and the specific species epithet serotinus.
