What's the taxonomical classification of Elatostema obtusum?

Elatostema obtusum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Rosales, the plant is a member of the family Urticaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Elatostema and is specifically designated as the species obtusum.
