Amur Corktree (Phellodendron amurense) Compounds

The primary compounds of Amur Corktree include alkaloids, which have antimicrobial properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and anti-inflammatory actions. Terpenoids and phenolic acids support its overall therapeutic profile, while coumarin and anthocyanin add to its medicinal value.
