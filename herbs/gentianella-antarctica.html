Gentianella antarctica

Gentianella antarctica, also known as Antarctic gentian, is a small, alpine perennial plant species native to the southern parts of South America and the Falkland Islands.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its high concentration of gentiopicroside and amarogentin, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.
