What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace bulleyana?

Androsace bulleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated under the order Ericales. This plant is a part of the family Primulaceae, specifically falling under the genus Androsace, and is identified by its unique species designation, bulleyaana.
