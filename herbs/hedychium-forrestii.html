What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hedychium forrestii has erect, rhizomatous stems that reach heights of up to 1.5 meters and bear lanceolate, bright green leaves arranged in an alternate pattern. These blossoms feature long, slender petals and prominent stamens that emerge from the floral tube. The plant's root system consists of thick, fleshy rhizomes that facilitate underground spreading.
