Circaea alpina subsp. angustifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Circaea alpina subsp. angustifolia?

Circaea alpina subsp. angustifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Myrtales. The plant is further categorized into the family Onagraceae and the genus Circaea, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Circaea alpinia subsp. angustifolia.
