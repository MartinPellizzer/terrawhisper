Cedrus Atlantica

Cedrus atlantica, commonly known as the Atlas cedar, has been traditionally used in various medicinal practices for its therapeutic properties.

The essential oils extracted from its wood and leaves contain compounds such as limonene and pinene, which are known for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects. In traditional medicine, it has been employed to treat respiratory conditions like coughs and bronchitis due to its ability to soothe the respiratory tract. Additionally, its extracts are believed to have antimicrobial properties that can help in fighting infections.

Overall, Cedrus atlantica continues to be valued in both traditional and complementary medicine for its potential health benefits.
