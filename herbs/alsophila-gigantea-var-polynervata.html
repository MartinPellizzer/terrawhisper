What's the taxonomical classification of Alsophila gigantea var. polynervata?

Alsophila gigantea var. polynervaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Cyatheales and falls within the family Cyatheaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is categorized under the genus Alsophila, with its precise taxonomic designation being the species Alsophila gigantea var. polynervaata.
