Tetrastigma serrulatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma serrulatum?

Tetrastigma serrulatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Tetrastigma, with its specific designation being the species serrulatum.
