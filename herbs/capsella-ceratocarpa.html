What is Capsella ceratocarpa?

Capsella ceratocarpa is an annual or biennial plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its glabrous or pubescent stems, 10-30 cm tall, with 2-4 foliate leaves, each leaf divided into 2-4 pairs of leaflets. The flowers are white or pale yellow, 5-7 mm in diameter, with 4 sepals and 4 petals, arranged in a racemose inflorescence. The fruit is a siliculose capsule, 2-3 cm long, containing 4-6 seeds.

The plant has a native distribution in the Mediterranean region and parts of Africa, Asia, and Europe.
