Phyllanthodendron anthopotamicum

What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllanthodendron anthopotamicum?

Phyllanthodendron anthopotamicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Malpighiales and resides within the family Phyllanthaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement in the genus Phyllanthodendron, culminating in its unique species designation, anthopotamicum.
