Phyllostachys sulphurea

What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllostachys sulphurea?

Phyllostachys sulphurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is organized under the order Poales and falls within the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Phyllostachys, and its unique species designation, sulphurea.

Phyllostachys sulphurea has anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties that are utilized to treat skin infections and reduce localized swelling.
