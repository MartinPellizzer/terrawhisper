Adiantum Capillus-Veneris: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Adiantum capillus-veneris, commonly known as the "Maidenhair Fern," is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions, valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.

This herb is known for its potential health benefits, including antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties.

Therapeutically, it is used to support respiratory health, reduce fever, and alleviate symptoms of cough and cold.

The bioactive constituents of Adiantum capillus-veneris include flavonoids, tannins, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
