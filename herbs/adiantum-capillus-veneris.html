Adiantum Capillus-Veneris Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Adiantum capillus-veneris, commonly known as the "Maidenhair Fern," is a delicate fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its diuretic, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory effects, which support urinary tract health and alleviate menstrual discomfort.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from the dried fronds to harness its beneficial properties.
