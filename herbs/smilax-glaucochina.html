What's the taxonomical classification of Smilax glaucochina?

Smilax glaucochina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Liliales. This plant is a member of the family Smilacaceae, situated within the genus Smilax, and is specifically identified by its species name, glaucochinas.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These plants feature prominent three-veined leaf patterns characteristic of the genus, with veins originating from the base.
