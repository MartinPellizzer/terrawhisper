10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pseudotsuga Menziesii (Douglas Fir)

Pseudotsuga menziesii has active constituents such as terpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making them beneficial for respiratory and cardiovascular health. These medicinal properties can significantly improve quality of life for individuals suffering from respiratory or cardiovascular conditions.
