What's the taxonomical classification of Cerasus tomentosa?

Cerasus tomentosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Cerasus. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, tomentosa.
