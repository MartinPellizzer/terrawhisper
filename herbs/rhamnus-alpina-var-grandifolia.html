Rhamnus alpina var. grandifolia

Rhamnus alpina var. grandifolia, also known as alpine buckthorn, is a plant species belonging to the Rhamnaceae family, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and dark green, oval-shaped leaves.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional European herbal medicine, where it is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant effects.
