This perennial plant is native to Japan and has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of Hypericum oshimaense include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions of sedation, pain relief, and neuroprotection.
