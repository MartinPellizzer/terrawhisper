What to know about Matricaria chamomilla before using it medicinally

Matricaria chamomilla, commonly known as German chamomile, is a medicinal herb that has been used to help alleviate various health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome.

The herb is also known for its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which make it effective in treating conditions like menstrual cramps and muscle spasms. Some of the main health benefits of using Matricaria chamomilla include reducing stress and anxiety, promoting relaxation, and improving sleep quality. The herb's therapeutic properties are attributed to its flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpenes, which are the main bioactive compounds responsible for its medicinal effects.

In herbal medicine, German chamomile is often used to make a calming tea by infusing the dried flowers in hot water, which is a popular preparation method for its medicinal properties.
