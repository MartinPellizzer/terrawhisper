Matricaria Chamomilla Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Matricaria chamomilla, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant in the daisy family, widely used for its calming and therapeutic properties.

This herb is known for its ability to reduce anxiety, promote relaxation, and aid in digestion due to its mild sedative and antispasmodic effects.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils such as bisabolol and chamazulene.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from Matricaria chamomilla to harness its beneficial properties.

Side Effects of matricaria chamomilla

Matricaria chamomilla causes skin irritation due to its potential to trigger contact dermatitis, which occurs when the skin comes into direct contact with the essential oils present in the herb.

Matricaria chamomilla leads to allergic reactions because it contains allergens such as chamazulene and other compounds that can provoke an immune response in sensitive individuals.

Matricaria chamomilla can trigger respiratory issues as its pollen and volatile oils may irritate the airways, especially in people with asthma or allergies.

Matricaria chamomilla can cause eye irritation when its fine pollen or oils come into contact with the eyes, leading to redness, itching, and discomfort.

Below you find a list of the 7 most common health side effects of Matricaria chamomilla.

  1. Causes Skin Irritation: Matricaria chamomilla may cause skin irritation in some individuals, leading to redness, itching, or a burning sensation upon contact with the skin.
  2. Leads To Allergic Reaction: Some people may experience an allergic reaction to Matricaria chamomilla, which can manifest as hives, swelling, or difficulty breathing.
  3. Can Trigger Respiratory Issues: Inhaling Matricaria chamomilla particles or using it in aromatherapy may trigger respiratory issues such as coughing, wheezing, or asthma attacks in sensitive individuals.
  4. Can Cause Eye Irritation: Contact with Matricaria chamomilla may cause eye irritation, including redness, itching, or excessive tearing.
  5. Could Result In Skin Rash: Matricaria chamomilla may lead to a skin rash, characterized by red, itchy patches or bumps on the skin in some users.
  6. May Induce Drowsiness: Matricaria chamomilla may have mild sedative properties that can induce drowsiness or relaxation in some individuals.
  7. Might Cause Gastrointestinal Upset: Consuming Matricaria chamomilla in large amounts or in an incorrect form may cause gastrointestinal upset, including nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea.
