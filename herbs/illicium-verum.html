Possible Side Effects of illicium verum

Illicium verum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues like nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain due to its high anethole content.

In rare cases, excessive use of illicium verum can interact with certain medications and worsen conditions like epilepsy, high blood pressure, and bleeding disorders.
