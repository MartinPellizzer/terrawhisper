Eriosema glomeratum

What's the taxonomical classification of Eriosema glomeratum?

Eriosema glomeratum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it among the legumes, and is specifically categorized under the genus Eriosema, with the distinct species designation glomeratum.
