Aeschynanthus bracteatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Aeschynanthus, with the unique designation of the species bracteatus.
