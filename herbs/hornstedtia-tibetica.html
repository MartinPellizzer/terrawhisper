What's the taxonomical classification of Hornstedtia tibetica?

Hornstedtia tibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Zingiberales and is a member of the Zingiberaceae family. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Hornstedtia and is distinguished as the species tibetica.
