Calanthe davidii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Calanthe, with its unique species designation being davidii.
