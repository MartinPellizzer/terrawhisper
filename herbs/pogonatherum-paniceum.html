Pogonatherum paniceum

What's the taxonomical classification of Pogonatherum paniceum?

Pogonatherum paniceum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Pogonatherum and the species paniceum.
