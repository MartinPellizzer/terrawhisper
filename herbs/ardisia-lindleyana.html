What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia lindleyana?

Ardisia lindleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Ardisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, lindleyana.
