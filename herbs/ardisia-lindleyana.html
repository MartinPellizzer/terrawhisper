What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ardisia lindleyana has elliptical to oblong leaves that are dark green, glossy, and arranged oppositely on woody stems. The leaf margins are entire and the texture is typically coriaceous, providing a durable surface for the foliage. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, often appearing in clusters. These flowers give way to fleshy drupes that are essential for the plant's reproductive cycle. The overall growth habit is that of a shrub or small tree with a dense canopy.
