Psilanthus mannii

What's the taxonomical classification of Psilanthus mannii?

Psilanthus mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Gentianaales and the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Psilanthus, and its species designation, mannii.
