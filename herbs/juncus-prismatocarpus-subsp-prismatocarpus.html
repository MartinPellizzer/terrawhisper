Juncus prismatocarpus subsp. prismatocarpus

What's the taxonomical classification of Juncus prismatocarpus subsp. prismatocarpus?

Juncus prismatocarpus subsp. prismatocarpus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and is a member of the order Poales. Within the family Juncaceae, it is categorized under the genus Juncus, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Juncus prismatocarpus subsp. prismatocarpus.
