What is Olearia passerinoides?

Olearia passerinoides is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub, typically growing up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has a woody stem with a greyish-brown bark and lanceolate leaves that are 1-2.5 centimeters long and 1-2 millimeters wide. The leaves are oppositely arranged and have a pointed tip, while the inflorescence is a corymb of small white flowers that are 3-4 millimeters in diameter.

The flowers are borne in May and June, and are followed by small brown achenes that are 2-3 millimeters long.
