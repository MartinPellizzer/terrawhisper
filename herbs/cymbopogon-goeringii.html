What's the taxonomical classification of Cymbopogon goeringii?

Cymbopogon goeringii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Poales. This plant is a member of the family Poaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Cymbopogon, and is identified by the species name goeringii.

During the flowering season, it produces small, inconspicuous panicles that rise above the foliage. These inflorescences consist of numerous tiny spikelets arranged in a branched manner.
