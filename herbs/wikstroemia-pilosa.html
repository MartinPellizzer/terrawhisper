Wikstroemia pilosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Wikstroemia pilosa?

Wikstroemia pilosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Malvales, this plant is a member of the family Thymelaeaceae. Finally, its specific biological classification is defined by its placement in the genus Wikstroemia, with the species designation pilosa.
