Dendrobium hancockii

What's the taxonomical classification of Dendrobium hancockii?

Dendrobium hancockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling within the genus Dendrobium, and is specifically identified by its species name, hancockii.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin diseases, respiratory ailments, and various types of inflammation. Practitioners often apply the plant to soothe irritations and heal wounds on the skin. It is also utilized in traditional remedies to address issues such as coughs and congestion in the lungs. The botanical components are believed to possess properties that help reduce swelling throughout the body. These medicinal applications highlight the plant's role in addressing both external and internal health concerns.
