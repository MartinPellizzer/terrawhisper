What's the taxonomical classification of Tylophora arenicola?

Tylophora arenicola belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic lineage further, it falls under the order Gentianales and the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Tylophora, and its unique species designation, arenicola.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
