Strobilanthes tonkinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes tonkinensis?

Strobilanthes tonkinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Lamiales, it is further nested within the family Acanthaceae. This specific plant is identified by its genus, Strobilanthes, and is uniquely distinguished by its species name, tonkinensis.
