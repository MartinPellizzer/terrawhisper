Ophiorrhiza cantonensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Ophiorrhiza, with its unique designation being the species cantonensis.
