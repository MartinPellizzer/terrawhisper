What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla betonicifolia?

Potentiella betonicifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression through the order Rosales, it is placed in the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Potentiella, with its specific designation being the species betonicifolia.
