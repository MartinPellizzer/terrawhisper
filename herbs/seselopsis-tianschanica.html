What's the taxonomical classification of Seselopsis tianschanica?

Seselopsis tianschanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its descent through the order Apiales, it is placed within the family Apiaceae. The plant is specifically identified by its genus, Seselopsis, and its species designation, tianschanica.

Seselopsis tianschanica is prepared primarily as a decoction, a concentrated liquid made by boiling the dried roots or stems in water. This method allows the medicinal compounds to be thoroughly extracted into a potent tea for consumption. In certain traditional applications, the plant material is crushed into a fine powder to be used in topical poultices. These various forms ensure that the active constituents can be administered for both internal and external therapeutic purposes.
