10 Medicinal Constituents Of Arenaria Rubra (Red Sandwort)

Arenaria rubra has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These bioactive compounds are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The constituents in red sandwort can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation and opening airways.

Additionally, its antioxidants can protect against oxidative stress and cellular damage, potentially improving overall health and well-being.
