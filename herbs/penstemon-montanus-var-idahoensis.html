Penstemon montanus var. idahoensis

Penstemon montanus var. idahoensis, also known as Idaho beardtongue, is a perennial plant species within the penstemon genus, characterized by its unique appearance and medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Idaho beardtongue has been used to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and respiratory issues, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's health benefits include its ability to reduce inflammation, fight off infections, and alleviate respiratory problems, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine.
