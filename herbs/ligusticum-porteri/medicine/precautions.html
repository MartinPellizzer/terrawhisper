10 Precautions To Take When Using Ligusticum Porteri (Porter'S Lovage)

Ligusticum porteri has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as pregnancy and breastfeeding cautions.

It's essential to exercise caution due to its potential to stimulate the uterus and induce labor. If not taken during these periods, it may cause contractions, premature birth, or fetal distress.

Additionally, individuals with bleeding disorders or those taking anticoagulant medications should consult a healthcare professional before using Ligusticum porteri.
