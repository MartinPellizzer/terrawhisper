Mexican Angelica (Ligusticum porteri) Uses

Mexican Angelica is used to treat respiratory conditions like asthma, colds, and chest congestion. It helps alleviate nasal and chest congestion and is applied for allergic rhinitis. It also addresses muscle pain, gout, and rheumatoid arthritis by reducing inflammation and supporting immune function.

Mexican Angelica (Ligusticum porteri) Monograph

1. Asthma

Mexican Angelica has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in managing asthma symptoms. Its anti-inflammatory and bronchodilating properties may help reduce airway constriction and ease breathing. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture and is valued for its gentle yet effective action on the respiratory system.

2. Common cold

Mexican Angelica is traditionally used to support respiratory health and may help alleviate symptoms of the common cold. It is believed to have antispasmodic and expectorant properties that can ease congestion and reduce coughing. Its use is often combined with other herbs in herbal formulas to promote healing and immunity.

3. Respiratory infections

Mexican Angelica is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and easing congestion. It may help alleviate symptoms of colds, bronchitis, and other respiratory infections. Its expectorant properties aid in clearing mucus, while its antimicrobial effects may combat infections. Traditionally, it is prepared as a tea or tincture for respiratory support.

4. Colds

Mexican Angelica is used to support respiratory health and may help alleviate symptoms of colds due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It can aid in reducing congestion and promoting mucus clearance, making it a useful herb in traditional herbal medicine for managing cold-related discomfort.

5. Allergic rhinitis

Mexican Angelica has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by supporting respiratory health and modulating immune responses. Its essential oils and root extracts are often prepared as tinctures or teas for regular use during allergy seasons.

6. Chest congestion

Mexican Angelica is used to alleviate chest congestion due to its expectorant properties. It helps loosen mucus and ease breathing in conditions like bronchitis or colds. Its aromatic compounds stimulate respiratory function and reduce inflammation in the airways. It is typically prepared as a tea or tincture for respiratory support.

7. Nasal congestion

Mexican Angelica is used to alleviate nasal congestion due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce mucus buildup and soothe irritated nasal passages. Often prepared as a steam inhalation or nasal spray, it provides relief for colds and allergies. Its active compounds support respiratory health and ease breathing.

8. Rheumatoid arthritis

Mexican Angelica has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of rheumatoid arthritis due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It is believed to help reduce joint pain and swelling, though more research is needed to confirm its efficacy in modern medical settings.

9. Muscle pain

Mexican Angelica is used to alleviate muscle pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and discomfort in muscles, making it useful for conditions like sprains or overuse injuries. Its active compounds support muscle recovery and ease tension, often applied topically or consumed as a tea.

10. Gout

Mexican Angelica is used to alleviate gout symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce uric acid levels and inflammation in joints, providing relief from pain and swelling. Traditionally, it is prepared as a tea or tincture to support joint health and manage acute gout attacks.
