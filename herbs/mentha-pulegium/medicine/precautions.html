When using Mentha pulegium medicinally, it's important to take consult your healthcare provider before doing so.

This is because menthol, a primary compound in the herb, can cause kidney and liver damage when taken in large doses or for extended periods. Additionally, pennyroyal essential oil may interact with certain medications, such as antidepressants, blood thinners, and anticoagulants.

Consulting your healthcare provider ensures a safe and effective treatment plan tailored to your individual needs.
