10 Best Mentha Pulegium Health Benefits

Mentha pulegium, also known as pennyroyal, is a herb that has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

It contains compounds such as pulegone and menthone, which may help in reducing inflammation and aiding digestion. Pennyroyal is often used in aromatherapy to relieve stress and promote relaxation due to its refreshing and invigorating scent. Additionally, it has been studied for its potential antiparasitic properties, making it useful in treating certain types of infections.

However, it should be used with caution, as high doses can be toxic, especially during pregnancy.
