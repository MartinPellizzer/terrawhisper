What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis henryi?

Codonopsis henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Asterales. The plant is a member of the family Campanulaceae, situated within the genus Codonoopsis, and is specifically identified by its species name, henryi.
