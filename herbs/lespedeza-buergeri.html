What's the taxonomical classification of Lespedeza buergeri?

Lespedeza buergeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, the plant is ordered within Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Ultimately, it is identified by its specific genus, Lespedeza, and its unique species designation, buergeri.
