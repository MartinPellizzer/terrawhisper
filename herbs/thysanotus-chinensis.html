What's the taxonomical classification of Thysanotus chinensis?

Thysanotus chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asparagales and the family Asparagaceae, eventually placing the plant within the genus Thysanotus. Finally, the specific identity of this organism is defined by its species name, chinensis.
