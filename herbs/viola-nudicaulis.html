Viola nudicaulis

Viola nudicaulis, commonly known as yellow violet, is a perennial plant species within the violet family (Violaceae).

It is traditionally used for its medicinal properties and is valued for its ability to reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms associated with colds and flu, such as sore throats and coughs.

The plant contains bioactive constituents including flavonoids, glycosides, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Viola nudicaulis include reducing inflammation, improving respiratory health, and potentially lowering blood pressure due to its vasodilatory properties.
