Lunu-Ankenda Melicope (Melicope Lunu-Ankenda)

Lunu-ankenda melicope, scientifically known as Melicope lunu-ankenda, is a rare medicinal herb native to the island of Madagascar, valued for its unique properties and traditional use in local healing practices. This plant is recognized for its adaptogenic qualities, helping the body manage stress and promote overall vitality, while also containing bioactive compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Traditionally, it has been used by the Malagasy people to treat digestive issues, skin conditions, and respiratory ailments, often prepared as a decoction or poultice. In modern wellness contexts, it is gaining attention for its potential role in supporting immune function and metabolic health, with some studies exploring its impact on oxidative stress. Its distinctive, aromatic flavor and the presence of rare compounds like lupeol and ursolic acid make it a unique and culturally significant plant in both traditional and contemporary medicinal practices.
