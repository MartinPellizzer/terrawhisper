Brassica Alba Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Brassica alba, commonly known as white mustard, is an annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb contains bioactive constituents such as glucosinolates, alkaloids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Health benefits include supporting digestion, reducing inflammation, and enhancing respiratory function.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from its leaves and seeds to harness its medicinal properties.
