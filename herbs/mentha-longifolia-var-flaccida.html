Mentha longifolia var. flaccida

Mentha longifolia var. flaccida, commonly known as Hoary Pignut, is a variety of the large leaved mint plant native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa.

This herb is valued for its health benefits, including its ability to act as an anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial agent, which can help to soothe digestive issues and alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections.

The therapeutic actions of Hoary Pignut include its ability to relax muscles, reduce spasms, and calm the nervous system, making it a popular ingredient in herbal remedies for stress and anxiety relief.

The bioactive constituents of Mentha longifolia var. flaccida include menthone, menthol, and limonene, which contribute to its medicinal properties and potential health benefits.
