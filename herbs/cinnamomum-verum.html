Cinnamomum Verum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cinnamomum verum, commonly known as true cinnamon, is a spice derived from the inner bark of the Cinnamomum verum tree, native to Sri Lanka.

It is rich in bioactive constituents such as cinnamaldehyde, eugenol, and polyphenols, which contribute to its distinct flavor and medicinal properties.

The herb offers numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, which may support cardiovascular health and blood sugar regulation.

Therapeutically, it is used to alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, respiratory infections, and skin conditions, making it a valuable natural remedy in traditional medicine.
