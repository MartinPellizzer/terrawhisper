Cinnamomum verum

Cinnamomum verum, also known as True Cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka, belonging to the Lauraceae family.

The medicinal properties of Cinnamomum verum include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which have been used to treat various health issues.

The health benefits of consuming Cinnamomum verum include lowering cholesterol levels, reducing blood sugar levels, and improving digestion, as well as its potential to reduce the risk of heart disease and certain types of cancer.

Cinnamomum verum is commonly prepared as a powder or oil, and can also be used in tea infusions, making it a versatile and valuable herb in traditional medicine.
