Himalaiella peguensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a representative of the family Asteraceae, which places it among a diverse group of flowering plants. Specifically, the plant is identified by the genus Himalaiella and is distinguished by its unique specific epithet, peguensis.
