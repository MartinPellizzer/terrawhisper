Heteropanax brevipedicellatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Araliaceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Heteropanax, with its specific designation being the species brevipedicellatus.
