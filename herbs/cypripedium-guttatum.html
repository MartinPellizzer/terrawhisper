What's the taxonomical classification of Cypripedium guttatum?

Cypripedium guttatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its higher taxonomic ranks, the plant is ordered within the Asparagales and falls under the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Cypripedium and is specifically designated as the species guttatum.
