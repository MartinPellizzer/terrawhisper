Colocasia esculenta var. stolonifera

Colocasia esculenta var. stolonifera, commonly known as elephant ear, is a type of rhizomatous perennial plant that belongs to the taro family.

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, it is known for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The plant has been used to treat various health issues, such as skin conditions, wounds, and digestive problems, offering benefits such as reduced inflammation, improved wound healing, and enhanced gut health.
