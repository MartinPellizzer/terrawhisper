Phyllanthus Amarus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Phyllanthus amarus, commonly known as stonebreaker or cheeseweed, is a medicinal herb native to Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean, valued for its diverse therapeutic properties.

This herb is traditionally used to support kidney health, reduce inflammation, and promote urinary tract wellness.
