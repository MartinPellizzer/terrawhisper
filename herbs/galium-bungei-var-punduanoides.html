Galium bungei var. punduanoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Galium bungei var. punduanoides?

Galium bungei var. punduanoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Gentianaales. As a member of the family Rubiaceae, it is further nested within the genus Galium, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Galium bungei var. punduanoides.
