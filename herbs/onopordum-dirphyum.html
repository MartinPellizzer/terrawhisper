Meadow Thistle (Onopordum Dirphyum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Onopordum dirphyum.

Meadow thistle, scientifically known as Onopordum dirphyum, is a medicinal herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its potential health benefits and historical use in traditional medicine. It is often considered an adaptogen, helping the body manage stress and promote overall well-being. The plant is traditionally used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions, with its leaves and flowers being the primary parts used. In modern wellness practices, it is incorporated into herbal teas and supplements to boost immunity and aid in detoxification. One of its unique features is the presence of a rare compound called taraxacin, which contributes to its distinctive bitter flavor and potential therapeutic properties.
