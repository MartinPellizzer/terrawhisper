Tephrosia uliginosa

Tephrosia uliginosa, also known as Marsh Tephrosia or Marsh Hoarypea, is a species of flowering plant in the pea family, Fabaceae.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

The plant has various health benefits, such as reducing fever, treating skin conditions, and alleviating respiratory issues, due to its ability to act as an expectorant.

Herbal preparations of Tephrosia uliginosa can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, and are often used in Ayurvedic and traditional African medicine.
