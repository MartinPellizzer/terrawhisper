Epilobium pyrricholophum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Myrtales. This plant is a member of the family Onagraceae, which places it within the genus Epilobium. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, pyrricholophum.
