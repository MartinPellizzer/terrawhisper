10 Medicinal Constituents Of Euphorbia Peplus (Radium Weed)

Euphorbia peplus has active constituents such as isorhapontigenin and euphol, which possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

These medicinal properties can help alleviate skin conditions like acne, eczema, and psoriasis by reducing inflammation and promoting healing. Additionally, the plant's antibacterial agents can aid in wound care by preventing infection.

The constituents of Euphorbia peplus have also been shown to exhibit anti-tumor activities, potentially improving cancer treatment outcomes.
