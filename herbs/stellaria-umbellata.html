Stellaria umbellata

What's the taxonomical classification of Stellaria umbellata?

Stellaria umbellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Caryophyllales. As a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Stellaria, with the specific epithet umbellata identifying it as the species Stellaria umbellata.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Stellaria umbellata has a low-growing, spreading growth habit characterized by slender, reddish stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that grow in opposite pairs. Each flower features five distinct petals that are slightly notched at the tips. The root system is relatively shallow, allowing the plant to colonize moist soil surfaces effectively.
