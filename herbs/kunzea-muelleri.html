Kunzea muelleri

Kunzea muelleri, also known as Tasmanian kunzea or Tasmanian paperbark, is a shrub native to Tasmania and southeastern Australia, recognized for its distinctive papery bark and small white flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful in treating various health issues.
