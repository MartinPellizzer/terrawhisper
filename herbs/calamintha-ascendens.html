Calamintha Ascendens: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Calamintha ascendens, commonly known as mountain thyme, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and flowers, which contain a variety of bioactive constituents such as thymol, carvacrol, and various essential oils.

The herb offers several health benefits, including antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which may support respiratory and digestive health.
