Linum bienne var. nanum

Linum bienne var. nanum, also known as Dwarf Flax or Dwarf Blue Flax, is a perennial plant belonging to the Linaceae family.

It is a compact, low-growing variety of Linum bienne, native to Europe and Asia, with blue or purple flowers that bloom in mid-summer.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties.
