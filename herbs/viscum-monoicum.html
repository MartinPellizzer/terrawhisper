Viscum monoicum

What's the taxonomical classification of Viscum monoicum?

Viscum monoicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Santalales and the family Santalaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Viscum and the specific species epithet monoicum.

Its stems are woody and can form dense, shrub-like clumps that grow directly from the branches of its host tree.
