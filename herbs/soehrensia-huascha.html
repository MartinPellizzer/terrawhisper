What is Soehrensia huascha?

Soehrensia huascha is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is a globular to cylindrical plant with a short, stout stem, typically 5-10 cm in height, and 8-15 cm in diameter. The plant has 22-30 radial and 10-15 central spines, with the latter being white and the former being yellow. The flowers are funnel-shaped, white, and 3-4 cm in length, and are borne at the plant's apex.

Soehrensia huascha produces red, berry-like fruits that are 6-7 mm in diameter.
