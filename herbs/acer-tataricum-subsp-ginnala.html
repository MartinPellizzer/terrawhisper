Acer tataricum subsp. ginnala

What's the taxonomical classification of Acer tataricum subsp. ginnala?

Acer tataricum subsp. ginnala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Sapindales and falls into the family Sapindaceae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Acer, ultimately identifying this specific plant as a subspecies of the species Acer tataryicum.
