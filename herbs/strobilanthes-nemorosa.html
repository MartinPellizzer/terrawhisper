Strobilanthes nemorosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes nemorosa?

Strobilanthes nemorosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent, the plant is categorized under the order Lamiales and falls within the family Acanthaceae. Ultimately, it is identified by the genus Strobilanthes and is specifically designated as the species nemorosa.

Traditional practitioners also utilize its properties to help manage the discomfort associated with joint swelling and rheumatic conditions.
