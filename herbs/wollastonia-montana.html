What's the taxonomical classification of Wollastonia montana?

Wollastonia montana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is specifically identified within the genus Wollastonia, with the unique species designation montana.

These blossoms are arranged in terminal racemes that emerge from the upper portions of the stem.
