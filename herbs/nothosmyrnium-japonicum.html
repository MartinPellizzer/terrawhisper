What's the taxonomical classification of Nothosmyrnium japonicum?

Nothosmyrnium japonicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Apiaceae, which defines its botanical relationships. Within this group, it is identified by the genus Nothosmyrnium and is specifically designated by the species epithet japonicum.
