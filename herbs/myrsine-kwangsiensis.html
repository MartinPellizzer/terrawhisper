Myrsine kwangsiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Myrsine, with its specific identity defined by the species epithet kwangsiensis.
