Cnidium alsaticum

Cnidium alsaticum, also known as Chinese burnet or burnet saxifrage, is a perennial plant species that belongs to the family Apiaceae and is native to China and other parts of East Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties, which include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic effects.

The health benefits of Cnidium alsaticum include relief from pain, improved digestion, and reduced menstrual cramps, making it a popular herbal remedy for women's health issues.

Cnidium alsaticum can be prepared as a tea, capsule, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
