    What is Cynodendron ferrugineum?

    Cynodendron ferrugineum is a species of flowering plant in the family Sapotaceae.

    It is an evergreen shrub or small tree that grows up to 10 meters in height, with a trunk diameter of up to 30 centimeters. The leaves are alternate, elliptical to lanceolate in shape, 10-20 centimeters long, and 2-5 centimeters wide, with a leathery texture and a rusty-colored pubescence on the underside. The inflorescences are axillary, with 2-4 white or pinkish flowers, 2-3 centimeters in diameter, and a globose, rusty-colored calyx.

    The fruit is a subglobose, greenish-yellow berry, 1-2 centimeters in diameter, with a persistent calyx.
