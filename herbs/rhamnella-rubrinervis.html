Rhamnella rubrinervis

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhamnella rubrinervis?

Rhamnella rubrinervis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Rosales and the family Rhamnaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Rhamnella, and its species designation, rubrinervis.
