Mentha × piperita var. citrata

Mentha × piperita var. citrata, commonly known as Lemon Mint, is a natural hybrid of peppermint (Mentha piperita) and lemon balm (Melissa officinalis), which gives it a unique flavor and aroma profile.

This plant is rich in bioactive constituents, including menthone, limonene, and beta-pinene, which contribute to its therapeutic actions and numerous health benefits.

The health benefits of Lemon Mint include aiding digestion, reducing stress and anxiety, and providing relief from headaches and fever, while its therapeutic actions include antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties.

The bioactive constituents of Lemon Mint work synergistically to provide a range of benefits, making it a popular ingredient in herbal remedies and natural products.
