What's the taxonomical classification of Physochlaina physaloides?

Physochlaina physaloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Solanales and the family Solanaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its membership in the genus Physochlaina, with the specific epithet physaloides.
