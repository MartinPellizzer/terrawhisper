Haplormosia monophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Haplormosia monophylla?

Haplormosia monophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, specifically within the genus Haplormosia, and is identified by its unique species name, monophylla.
