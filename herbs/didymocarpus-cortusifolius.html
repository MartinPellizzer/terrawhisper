Didymocarpus cortusifolius

What's the taxonomical classification of Didymocarpus cortusifolius?

Didymocarpus cortusifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Lamiales, placing it within the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Didymocarpus, and its unique species designation, cortusifolius.

What are the therapeutic actions of Didymocarpus cortusifolius?

Its medicinal properties are often utilized to soothe skin irritation caused by eczema or minor abrasions. When applied topically, the extracts help reduce swelling and promote faster healing of the epidermis.
