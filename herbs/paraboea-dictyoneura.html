Paraboea dictyoneura

What's the taxonomical classification of Paraboea dictyoneura?

Paraboea dictyoneura belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it in the order Lamiales. As a member of the family Gesneriaceae, this plant is categorized under the genus Paraboea, with its specific identity defined by the species epithet dictyoneura.
