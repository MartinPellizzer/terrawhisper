What's the taxonomical classification of Paraboea dictyoneura?

Paraboea dictyoneura belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Paraboea dictyoneura has a perennial herbaceous habit characterized by leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern and small, tubular flowers.

Its inflorescence consists of several small flowers arranged along a slender scape. Each flower possesses a corolla that is fused into a tube, often displaying subtle color variations.
