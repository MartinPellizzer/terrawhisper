Rubus hallandicus, commonly known as Halland Thorn, is a species of thorny shrub in the rose family.

The bioactive constituents of Rubus hallandicus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and anthocyanins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions of antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties.
