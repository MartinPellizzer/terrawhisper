10 Medicinal Constituents Of Solidago Virgaurea (Goldenrod)

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can alleviate symptoms of allergies, colds, and respiratory issues. They can also improve cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of cancer.

For instance, the flavonoids may help alleviate symptoms of menopause, while the sesquiterpenes may reduce inflammation in arthritic conditions.
