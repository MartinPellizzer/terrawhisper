10 Possible Side Effects Of Oryza Sativa (Rice)

Oryza sativa has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive problems, nausea, and vomiting.

These side effects can be caused by consuming large amounts of rice, especially in individuals with gluten intolerance or sensitivity. Prolonged consumption can lead to malnutrition, fatigue, and weakened immunity, ultimately worsening the quality of life.

In severe cases, it can even cause life-threatening conditions like arsenic poisoning.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Oryza sativa if used imporperly.
