2. Neutralize free radicals

Oxidative stress is a health condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and the body's ability to neutralize them.

Oxidative stress is commonly experienced by individuals with poor diets, smokers, and those exposed to environmental pollutants.

Oryza sativa, or rice, contains compounds that help neutralize free radicals, thereby reducing oxidative stress and supporting overall health.
