Alpinia globosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Alpinia globosa?

Alpinia globosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Zingiberales. This plant is a representative of the family Zingiberaceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Alpinia, with the specific epithet globosa.

These inflorescences typically rise above the foliage on sturdy, upright stalks to attract specific pollinators.
