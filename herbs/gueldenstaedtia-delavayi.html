Gueldenstaedtia delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Fabaceae, commonly known as the legume family, and is specifically categorized under the genus Gueldenstaedtia, with the species designation delavayi.
