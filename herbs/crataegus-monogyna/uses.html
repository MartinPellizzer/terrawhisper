Common Hawthorn (Crataegus monogyna) Uses

Common Hawthorn is used to treat high blood pressure, heart failure, and circulatory disorders. It supports cardiovascular health by improving blood flow and reducing strain on the heart. It may also help with high cholesterol, arrhythmia, and edema, making it a valued herb in herbal medicine for heart-related conditions.

Common Hawthorn (Crataegus monogyna) Monograph

1. High blood pressure

Common Hawthorn is used to support cardiovascular health and may help manage high blood pressure. It contains flavonoids and antioxidants that promote healthy blood vessel function. Traditionally, it has been used to ease symptoms of hypertension when combined with a balanced diet and lifestyle. Its mild action makes it a suitable complementary therapy under professional guidance.

2. Heart failure

Common Hawthorn has been traditionally used to support heart function in cases of heart failure. It contains bioflavonoids and antioxidants that may improve circulation and strengthen cardiac muscle. Often used in herbal formulations, it is believed to help reduce symptoms like fatigue and shortness of breath. Its effects are typically supportive and should be used under professional guidance.

3. High cholesterol

Common Hawthorn may help lower high cholesterol by supporting cardiovascular health. It contains flavonoids that can reduce LDL cholesterol levels. Studies suggest it improves lipid metabolism and reduces arterial plaque buildup. Regular use may aid in managing cholesterol-related conditions.

4. Hypertension

Common Hawthorn is used in herbal medicine to support cardiovascular health, particularly for hypertension. It contains flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins that may help dilate blood vessels and improve circulation. Regular use may aid in lowering blood pressure, though it should complement, not replace, conventional treatment.

5. Arrhythmia

Common Hawthorn supports heart health by regulating heart rhythm. It contains flavonoids and antioxidants that may stabilize cardiac electrical activity. Traditionally used for arrhythmia, it helps ease irregular heartbeats and improve overall cardiovascular function. Its effects are often combined with other herbs in herbal formulations for heart support.

6. Circulatory insufficiency

Common Hawthorn supports circulatory insufficiency by improving blood flow and reducing venous pressure. It strengthens heart function and may alleviate symptoms like leg swelling and fatigue. Traditionally used for chronic venous insufficiency, it is often combined with other herbs for enhanced effects. Its active compounds, including flavonoids and proanthocyanidins, contribute to its cardiovascular benefits.

7. Edema

Common Hawthorn is used in herbal medicine to support the treatment of edema, particularly when it is related to poor circulation or heart conditions. It helps improve blood flow and reduce fluid retention by strengthening the cardiovascular system. Its mild diuretic properties aid in eliminating excess fluid from the body.

8. Atherosclerosis

Common Hawthorn supports cardiovascular health by improving blood flow and reducing arterial stiffness. It contains antioxidants and flavonoids that may help lower cholesterol and prevent plaque buildup in arteries, making it a traditional support for managing atherosclerosis.

9. Circulatory disorders

Common Hawthorn supports circulatory health by improving blood flow and reducing arterial stiffness. It contains flavonoids that strengthen blood vessel walls and may lower blood pressure. Traditionally used for heart conditions, it is often combined with other herbs in circulatory formulas. Its mild action makes it suitable for long-term use in managing cardiovascular symptoms.

10. Angina

Common Hawthorn is used to support heart health and may help manage angina by improving blood flow and reducing cardiac strain. It contains antioxidants and flavonoids that enhance circulation and support vascular function, making it a traditional remedy for cardiovascular support in herbal medicine.
