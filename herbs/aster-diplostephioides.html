Aster diplostephioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Asterales and falls within the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Aster and the species diplostephioides.
