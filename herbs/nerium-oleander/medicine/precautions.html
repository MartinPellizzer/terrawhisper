10 Precautions To Take When Using Nerium Oleander (Oleander)

Nerium oleander has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as taking it under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.

This is crucial because oleander contains toxic compounds that can be fatal in large doses. Not taking precautions can lead to serious side effects, including cardiac arrest, seizures, and even death, especially in children and pets who may accidentally ingest it.

It's essential to follow strict dosing and avoid self-medication.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Nerium oleander medicinally.
