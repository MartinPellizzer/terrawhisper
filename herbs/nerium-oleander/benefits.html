10 Best Nerium Oleander Health Benefits

Nerium oleander, a flowering plant known for its showy blooms, offers several health benefits due to its rich content of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.

Studies suggest that extracts from Nerium oleander may support cardiovascular health by helping to regulate blood pressure and improve circulation. It has also been traditionally used in herbal medicine to aid in respiratory conditions and reduce symptoms of inflammation. The plant's ability to detoxify the body and support liver function further enhances its therapeutic potential.

However, it is important to note that Nerium oleander is toxic if ingested, and its use should be approached with caution under professional guidance.
