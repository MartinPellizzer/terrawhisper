Poa frankii

Poa frankii, also known as Blue Grass, is a type of perennial grass native to New Zealand, characterized by its blue-green leaves and purple flowers.

Traditionally, this plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, derived from its essential oils and flavonoids.

The health benefits of Poa frankii include its ability to soothe respiratory issues, reduce inflammation, and support immune function.

Herbal preparations of Poa frankii can be found in the form of teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to aid in relaxation and promote overall well-being.
