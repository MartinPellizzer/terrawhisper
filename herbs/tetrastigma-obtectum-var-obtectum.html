What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma obtectum var. obtectum?

Tetrastigma obtectum var. obtectum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated under the order Vitales and falls within the family Vitaeaceae. At the more specific taxonomic levels, it is identified by the genus Tetrastigma, ultimately reaching its precise classification as the variety Tetrastigma obtectum var. obtectum.
