Radermachera yunnanensis has evergreen, opposite leaves that are elliptical to oblong in shape, measuring approximately 5 to 15 centimeters in length with finely serrated margins and a glossy, dark green surface. The leaf texture is somewhat leathery, providing a robust structure to the foliage. These leaves are supported by slender, woody stems that exhibit a branching growth habit typical of woody shrubs. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils of the leaves, often appearing in clusters.
