Pseudostellaria heterophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudostellaria heterophylla?

Pseudostellaria heterophyllla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is further categorized into the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically within the genus Pseudostellaria, and is identified by the species name heterophyllla.

What is the geographical distribution of this plant?

This plant is endemic to the high-altitude regions of the southwestern Andes, primarily spanning parts of Peru and Bolivia. It thrives specifically within the alpine tundra and puna ecosystems located at elevations typically exceeding 3,500 meters. Its range is restricted to these specialized montane habitats where it can withstand extreme temperature fluctuations and intense solar radiation. The species follows a fragmented distribution pattern dictated by the availability of rocky outcrops and specific soil compositions.
