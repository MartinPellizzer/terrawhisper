What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudostellaria heterophylla?

Pseudostellaria heterophyllla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is further categorized into the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically within the genus Pseudostellaria, and is identified by the species name heterophyllla.

    What side effects this plant can have?

    This plant can cause severe contact dermatitis, gastrointestinal distress, and mild neurotoxicity if ingested. Skin contact with the sap often results in painful, red blistering and intense itching that can last for several days. If consumed, individuals may experience nausea, vomiting, and abdominal cramping shortly after ingestion. Some users have reported dizziness or a temporary loss of coordination following accidental exposure. It is essential to avoid all contact with the foliage to prevent these adverse reactions.
