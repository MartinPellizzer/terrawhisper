Cassia alata terpenoids, such as alpha-pinene and beta-caryophyllene, are volatile oils with anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and analgesic properties, enhancing the herb's therapeutic value in treating skin infections and pain-related conditions.

Cassia alata cardiac glycosides, although less abundant compared to other compounds, exhibit mild inotropic effects that may support heart function, though their presence and potency require further scientific validation.
