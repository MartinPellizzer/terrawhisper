What's the taxonomical classification of Mussaenda divaricata?

Mussaenda divaricata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Mussaenda and the specific species name divaricata.
