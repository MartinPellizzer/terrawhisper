What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes hylonoma?

Trichosanthes hylonoma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Cucurbitales. As a member of the family Cucurbitaceae, it is categorized within the genus Trichosanthes, with its specific identification being the species hylonoma.

      What are the herbal preparations of Trichosanthes hylonoma?

      Trichosanthes hylonoma is prepared as a decoction, a powder, or a topical paste depending on the specific medicinal application required. The dried leaves and stems are often boiled in water to create a liquid infusion used for internal consumption. When treating skin conditions, the plant material is ground into a fine powder and mixed with a liquid to form a thick paste. Some practitioners also utilize a concentrated extract produced through maceration in oil or alcohol. It is essential to consult medical professionals before attempting any form of internal use.
