What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Trichosanthes hylonoma has a climbing or trailing habit characterized by slender, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with entire margins. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in axillary clusters, often exhibiting a pale or greenish coloration.
