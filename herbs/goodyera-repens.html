What's the taxonomical classification of Goodyera repens?

Goodyera repens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the Orchidaceae family, falling under the genus Goodyera, and is specifically identified by the species epithet repens.
