Possible Side Effects of cucurbita pepo

Cucurbita pepo can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues in some individuals.

Prolonged consumption of the herb may also cause stomach ulcers, diarrhea, and vomiting in sensitive users.

Furthermore, excessive use of cucurbita pepo can lead to interactions with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse health consequences.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using cucurbita pepo, especially if you have a pre-existing medical condition or are taking prescription medications.
