Cucurbita Pepo Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cucurbita pepo, commonly known as the common squash or marrow, is a flowering plant in the gourd family, Cucurbitaceae, widely cultivated for its edible fruits.

This herb is valued for its high nutritional content, offering health benefits such as supporting digestion, reducing inflammation, and boosting the immune system.

The bioactive constituents in Cucurbita pepo include vitamins A and C, fiber, antioxidants, and minerals like potassium and magnesium, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, or poultices can be made from the seeds, leaves, or flowers to harness its medicinal properties.
