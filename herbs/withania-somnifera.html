Withania somnifera has many medicinal parts, such as roots, leaves, and fruits, which possess distinct medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of withania somnifera

Withania somnifera can have side effects if used improperly, such as interactions with certain medications, including blood thinners, diabetes medications, and high blood pressure medications.

Improper use of withania somnifera may also lead to allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, excessive consumption of the herb can cause stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

In rare cases, withania somnifera may also lower blood sugar levels too much, causing hypoglycemia.
