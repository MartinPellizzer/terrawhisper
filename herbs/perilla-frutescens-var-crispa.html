Perilla frutescens var. crispa

What's the taxonomical classification of Perilla frutescens var. crispa?

Perilla frutescens var. crispa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Lamiaceae, which is further defined by its placement in the genus Perilla and the species Perilla.
