10 Medicinal Constituents Of Lycopus Europaeus (European Bugleweed)

Lycopus europaeus, also known as European bugleweed, has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoids.

These constituents confer anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and antioxidant properties. European bugleweed is traditionally used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and urinary issues.

For instance, flavonoids like euparin can alleviate stress and anxiety by promoting relaxation, while iridoids like aucubin can soothe urinary tract discomfort, improving overall quality of life.
