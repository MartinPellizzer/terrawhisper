10 Possible Side Effects Of Lycopus Europaeus (European Bugleweed)

Lycopus europaeus, also known as European bugleweed, has some side effects when used improperly, such as thyroid problems, skin rashes, and digestive issues.

These side effects can occur due to the herb's high concentration of thujone, a compound that can interfere with thyroid hormone production and cause allergic reactions.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen quality of life, leading to fatigue, weight gain, and difficulty swallowing, making everyday activities a significant challenge.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Lycopus europaeus if used imporperly.
