European Bugleweed (Lycopus europaeus) Compounds

The primary compounds in European Bugleweed include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Glycosides and saponins contribute to its medicinal uses, while coumarins and tannins support its antispasmodic and astringent actions. Essential oils and alkaloids further enhance its therapeutic profile.
