What's the taxonomical classification of Euchresta tubulosa?

Euchresta tubulosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae, which is commonly known as the legume family. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Euchresta, and its unique species designation, tubulosa.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Euchresta tubulosa has woody, perennial stems that produce pinnate leaves consisting of several pairs of leaflets and small, tubular, yellowish-white flowers.

Its leaflets are generally elliptic or oblong in shape with entire margins and a smooth texture. The inflorescence is often characterized by clusters of flowers emerging from the leaf axils.
