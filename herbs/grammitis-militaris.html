Grammitis militaris, commonly known as the soldier's shield fern, is a type of epiphytic fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, and is traditionally used to treat various health conditions.

The therapeutic actions of Grammitis militaris include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve wound healing, and act as an antiseptic.
