What is Madhuca curtisii?

Madhuca curtisii is a species of tree native to Sri Lanka, belonging to the family Sapotaceae.

It is a medium-sized tree that grows up to 15 meters in height, with a straight trunk and a rounded crown. The bark is smooth and grey, while the leaves are elliptical, 10-20 cm long, and 3-6 cm wide, with a leathery texture and a prominent midrib. The flowers are white, 4-6 cm long, and 3-4 cm wide, with a prominent calyx and a fragrant odor.

The fruit is a yellow, ellipsoidal capsule, 5-7 cm long, containing 2-3 seeds per locule.
