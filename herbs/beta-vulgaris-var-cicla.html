Beta vulgaris var. cicla

What's the taxonomical classification of Beta vulgaris var. cicla?

Beta vulgaris var. cicla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the order Caryophyllales, placing it under the family Amaranthaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Beta, specifically identified as the variety cicla.
