What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita subulatisepala?

Chirita subulatisepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically falling within the genus Chirita, and is identified at the species level as subulatisepala.
