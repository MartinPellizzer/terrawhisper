Common Goldenrod (Solidago occidentalis) Compounds

The primary compounds in Common Goldenrod include flavonoids, which act as antioxidants, and phenolic acids, which have anti-inflammatory properties. Terpenoids and tannins contribute to its bitter taste and potential antimicrobial effects. Glycosides and saponins may support digestive health, while coumarin and essential oils provide aromatic and potential antispasmodic benefits.
