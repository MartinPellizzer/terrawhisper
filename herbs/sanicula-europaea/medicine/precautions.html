10 Precautions To Take When Using Sanicula Europaea (Sanicle)

Sanicula europaea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as taking it in moderation and in consultation with a healthcare professional.

This is particularly important due to its potential interactions with blood-thinning medications and blood pressure medications.

For example, taking excessive amounts of sanicle may increase the risk of bleeding complications or hypotension, which can be life-threatening if not monitored and managed properly.
