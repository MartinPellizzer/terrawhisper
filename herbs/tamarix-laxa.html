What's the taxonomical classification of Tamarix laxa?

Tamarix laxa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This specific plant is a representative of the family Tamaricaceae, situated within the genus Tamarix, and is identified by its unique species designation, laxa.

Its woody structure often forms large, spreading shrubs or small trees with a somewhat irregular canopy.
