Cephaelis Acuminata Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cephaelis acuminata, commonly known as the "Brahma Kumaris" or "Kumari," is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used in Ayurveda for its ability to support respiratory and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents in Cephaelis acuminata include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic actions such as bronchodilation and anti-inflammatory effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures can be made from the leaves and flowers to harness its medicinal benefits.
