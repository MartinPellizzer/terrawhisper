Crataegus × gillotii

Crataegus × gillotii, also known as the hawthorn hybrid, is a plant species that belongs to the Rosaceae family, resulting from the cross-breeding of Crataegus monogyna and Crataegus laevigata.

The medicinal properties of Crataegus × gillotii lie in its berries, leaves, and flowers, which contain flavonoids, tannins, and other bioactive compounds that have been traditionally used to support cardiovascular health.
