Erythrina hederifolia

Erythrina hederifolia, also known as the Coral Tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, native to South Africa.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Erythrina hederifolia include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve immune function, and exhibit antimicrobial activity.

The plant is often prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be used to harness its medicinal properties and provide relief from various health issues.
