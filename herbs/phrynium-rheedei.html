Phrynium rheedei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Zingiberales and is a member of the family Marantaceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Phrynium, with its unique identity defined by the species name rheedei.
