Corydalis turtschaninovii is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system. It has slender, branched stems that grow upright. Corydalis turtschaninovii is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Russia. It thrives in temperate climates with cool summers and cold winters.
