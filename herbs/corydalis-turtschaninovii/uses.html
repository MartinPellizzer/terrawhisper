Corydalis Turtschaninovii (Corydalis turtschaninovii) Uses

Corydalis turtschaninovii is used to treat rheumatism, arthritis, and gout due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It also addresses neurological disorders and neuropathic pain, helping with migraines, depression, insomnia, and chronic fatigue. Its active compounds support pain relief and mood regulation.
