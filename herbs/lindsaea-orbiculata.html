What's the taxonomical classification of Lindsaea orbiculata?

Lindsaea orbiculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Lindsaeaceae. Specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Lindsaea, with its unique identification completed by the species name orbiculata.
