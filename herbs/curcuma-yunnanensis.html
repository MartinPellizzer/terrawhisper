Curcuma yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Zingiberaceae, a group known for its diverse aromatic plants, and falls specifically under the genus Curcumma, with its unique designation as the species yunnanensis.
