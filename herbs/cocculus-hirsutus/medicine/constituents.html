10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cocculus Hirsutus (Angelden)

Cocculus hirsutus has active constituents such as alkaloids, saponins, and flavonoids.

These compounds possess medicinal properties that can benefit human health. The alkaloids exhibit analgesic and anti-inflammatory activities, which can alleviate pain and reduce swelling in various parts of the body.

The saponins demonstrate antimicrobial properties, capable of fighting off bacteria, viruses, and fungi, thus preventing infections and promoting wound healing. Flavonoids, on the other hand, act as antioxidants, scavenging free radicals to protect cells from damage and oxidative stress.

These medicinal properties make Cocculus hirsutus a valuable herb in traditional medicine, offering relief from various health issues and improving overall well-being.
