Long-term use of Pterocarpus marsupium can also lead to liver damage and kidney problems, as the herb can interact with certain medications and exacerbate underlying health conditions.

Additionally, improper use of Pterocarpus marsupium can cause birth defects and reproductive issues in pregnant women or individuals with fertility problems.
