10 Medicinal Preparations Of Verbascum Thapsus (Mullein)

Verbascum thapsus has several medicinal preparations, such as dried leaves for teas, infusions, and poultices.

Mullein extracts are also used in tinctures and ointments. These preparations are useful for respiratory issues like bronchitis, coughs, and asthma, as well as skin conditions like eczema, burns, and wounds.

The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties make it an effective treatment for ear infections and other bacterial infections.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Verbascum thapsus.

1. Teas

Verbascum thapsus teas is used to soothe respiratory issues such as bronchitis, asthma, and coughs.

It also possesses antimicrobial and antiviral properties, making it effective against infections and wounds.
