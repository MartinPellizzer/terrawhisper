Scleria pergracilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Poales and the family Cyperaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Scleria, where it is identified as the species pergracilis.
