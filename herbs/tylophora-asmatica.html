Tylophora Asmatica: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Tylophora asmatica, commonly known as Indian Bellflower, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia, widely used in traditional Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine.

This herb is valued for its diverse health benefits, including its ability to support cognitive function, reduce inflammation, and enhance immune response.

Therapeutically, it is known to act as a nervine tonic, antioxidant, and adaptogen, helping the body manage stress and promote overall well-being.

The bioactive constituents of Tylophora asmatica include alkaloids, saponins, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
