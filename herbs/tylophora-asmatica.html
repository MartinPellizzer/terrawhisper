Asiatic Tylophora (Tylophora asmatica)

Asiatic Tylophora (Tylophora asmatica) is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, native to India, Southeast Asia, and China. Traditionally, its leaves, roots, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
