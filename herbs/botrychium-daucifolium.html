What's the taxonomical classification of Botrychium daucifolium?

Botrychium daucifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is part of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Ophioglossidae, falling under the order Ophioglossales. Within the family Ophioglossaceae, it is categorized under the genus Botrychium, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Botrychium daucifolium.
