Trapa astrachanica var. caspica

Trapa astrachanica var. caspica, also known as the Caspian water chestnut or Caspian burr, is a variety of water chestnut native to the Caspian Sea region.

This aquatic plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.
