Oresitrophe rupifraga belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Saxifragaceae. Finally, it is defined by its specific taxonomic placement within the genus Oresitrophe and the species rupifraga.

Some individuals may also experience swelling of the throat or coughing if microscopic particles enter the airways.
