Rhaphidophora hongkongensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Alismatales and the family Araceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Rhaphidophorha, with its specific identification being the species hongkongensis.

It is advisable to wash your hands thoroughly after handling the plant to prevent any potential dermal reactions. Monitoring for these minor symptoms is recommended for those with known plant allergies.
