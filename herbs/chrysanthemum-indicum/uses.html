Chrysanthemum (Chrysanthemum indicum) Uses

Chrysanthemum indicum is used to treat common cold, bronchitis, and respiratory infections due to its antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis and sinusitis by reducing nasal congestion. The herb also supports respiratory health, aiding in conditions like asthma and bronchitis. It is commonly used for tonsillitis and otitis media to reduce inflammation and boost immune response.

Chrysanthemum (Chrysanthemum indicum) Monograph

1. Common cold

Chrysanthemum indicum is commonly used in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of the common cold. It helps reduce fever, soothe sore throats, and relieve nasal congestion. Its antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties support the body's natural defenses during colds. It is often prepared as a tea or decoction for internal use.

2. Bronchitis

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional medicine to alleviate symptoms of bronchitis. It helps reduce inflammation and soothe respiratory tract irritation. Often prepared as a tea, it promotes easier breathing and supports the body's natural healing processes. Its antiseptic and expectorant properties make it a valuable remedy for chronic bronchial conditions.

3. Allergic rhinitis

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis. It contains anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic compounds that help reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose. Its soothing properties make it a valued remedy for seasonal allergies and hay fever.

4. Influenza

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of influenza. It possesses antipyretic and antiviral properties that help reduce fever and combat viral infections. Often prepared as a tea, it supports respiratory health and immune function during flu outbreaks.

5. Sinusitis

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional medicine to treat sinusitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and inflammation, alleviating symptoms associated with sinus infections. It is often prepared as a decoction or tea to support respiratory health.

6. Respiratory infection

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat respiratory infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion by supporting immune function and clearing mucus. Often prepared as a tea, it is valued for its soothing and therapeutic effects on the respiratory system.

7. Respiratory tract infections

Chrysanthemum indicum is used to treat respiratory tract infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion. Typically prepared as a tea, it supports immune function and soothes irritated airways. It is commonly recommended in traditional herbal medicine for colds and flu.

8. Asthma

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional medicine to help alleviate asthma symptoms. It is believed to reduce inflammation and ease bronchial constriction. Often prepared as a tea, it supports respiratory health and may help manage breathing difficulties associated with asthma. Its mild antispasmodic properties contribute to its therapeutic role in respiratory conditions.

9. Tonsillitis

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat tonsillitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce swelling and soothe throat irritation. Typically prepared as a tea, it is valued for its cooling effect and ability to alleviate symptoms associated with throat infections.

10. Otitis media

Chrysanthemum indicum is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat otitis media due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce ear inflammation and combat bacterial infections. Typically, it is prepared as a decoction or combined with other herbs in formulas. It is valued for its role in supporting respiratory and ear health.
