Ribes pulchellum

What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes pulchellum?

Ribes pulchellum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Grossulariaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Ribes and the species pulchellum.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat minor skin irritations, digestive issues, and symptoms of the common cold. Traditional indigenous knowledge often suggests using preparations of the berries or leaves to soothe stomach discomfort. Some practitioners also utilize the plant's properties to address topical skin conditions through poultices or washes.
