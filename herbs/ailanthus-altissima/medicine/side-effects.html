10 Possible Side Effects Of Ailanthus Altissima (Tree Of Heaven)

Ailanthus altissima has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation and allergic reactions.

These reactions are often caused by the plant's toxic compounds, such as ailanthone and flavonoids. Prolonged exposure can worsen skin conditions like eczema and dermatitis, and in severe cases, may cause respiratory problems like asthma.

In extreme cases, misuse can lead to liver damage, causing fatigue, jaundice, and other complications that significantly impair one's quality of life.
