10 Health Benefits Of Ailanthus Altissima (Tree Of Heaven)

Ailanthus altissima, also known as tree of heaven, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, thanks to its high content of flavonoids and saponins.

These medicinal properties can help to soothe digestive issues, reduce inflammation and pain, and even alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress. For example, traditional Chinese medicine uses the plant's bark and leaves to treat various ailments, including fever and rheumatism.

This can improve people's lives by providing natural relief from chronic conditions, promoting overall well-being, and enhancing their quality of life.

1. Reduces blood pressure

Ailanthus altissima reduces blood pressure because it contains a unique combination of flavonoids and phenolic acids that have vasodilatory effects.

The flavonoids, particularly quercetin and kaempferol, relax the walls of blood vessels, improving blood flow and reducing peripheral resistance.

This vasodilation leads to a decrease in blood pressure and is beneficial for individuals with hypertension and cardiovascular disease.

2. Lowers cholesterol levels

Ailanthus altissima lowers cholesterol levels because it contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the gut, leading to a decrease in serum cholesterol levels.

Additionally, the tree's extracts have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which may further contribute to its cholesterol-lowering effects.

4. Treats bronchitis symptoms

Ailanthus altissima treats bronchitis symptoms because it is rich in compounds with anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

The leaves and twigs of the tree contain flavonoids, saponins, and tannins that help to soothe irritated airways, reduce inflammation, and facilitate the removal of mucus, thereby alleviating the symptoms of bronchitis.

Traditional Chinese medicine has long used the tree for its medicinal properties.

5. Relieves respiratory issues

Ailanthus altissima relieves respiratory issues because its leaves contain compounds that exhibit anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, helping to ease congestion and reduce inflammation in the airways.

The leaves are rich in flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to their therapeutic effects in soothing respiratory irritations and promoting overall lung health, providing relief for individuals with bronchitis, asthma, and other respiratory conditions.

6. Stimulates appetite naturally

Ailanthus altissima stimulates appetite naturally because it contains unique compounds that increase gastric secretion and enhance digestive enzymes.

The leaves and bark of the tree have been used in traditional medicine for centuries to aid in digestion and increase appetite.

Research suggests that the tree's bioactive compounds can help regulate appetite hormones, making it a natural and effective way to stimulate appetite and improve overall digestion.

8. Reduces anxiety levels

Ailanthus altissima reduces anxiety levels because it contains flavonoids and alkaloids that promote relaxation and calmness.

The tree's leaves, bark, and roots have been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various ailments, including anxiety and stress-related disorders.

Studies have shown that the tree's extracts have a positive impact on the central nervous system, leading to a decrease in anxiety levels and an improvement in overall mental well-being.

9. Eliminates stress hormones

Ailanthus altissima eliminates stress hormones because of its unique bioactive compounds.

Research suggests that the plant's leaves and bark contain flavonoids and saponins that interact with the body's endocrine system to regulate stress hormones. Specifically, the compounds reduce cortisol and adrenaline levels, promoting relaxation and calming effects.

As a result, individuals exposed to the plant have reported reduced anxiety and improved mood.

10. Improves sleep quality

Ailanthus altissima improves sleep quality because it has been found to reduce stress and anxiety by releasing calming phytoremediated chemicals into the air.

These natural pollutants are broken down by the tree's roots, creating a cleaner environment that can help promote restful sleep.

By reducing stress and anxiety, the Tree of Heaven helps individuals achieve a deeper and more refreshing sleep, essential for physical and mental well-being.
