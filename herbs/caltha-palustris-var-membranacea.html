Caltha palustris var. membranacea

What's the taxonomical classification of Caltha palustris var. membranacea?

Caltha palustris var. membrancea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae. Within this botanical hierarchy, the plant is identified by the genus Caltha, specifically designated as the variety membrancea of the species Caltha palustris.
