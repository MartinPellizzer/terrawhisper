Anomalous Peony (Paeonia anomala) Compounds

The primary compounds of Anomalous Peony include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and glycosides, known for their cardiac and anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins contribute to astringency and may aid in wound healing, while coumarins are associated with anticoagulant and anti-inflammatory actions.
