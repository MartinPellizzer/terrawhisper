Anomalous Peony (Paeonia anomala) Uses

Anomalous Peony is used to treat inflammatory bowel disease, gastritis, and various gastrointestinal issues such as spasms, pain, and discomfort. It helps alleviate digestive disorders, diarrhea, and upset by reducing inflammation and soothing the digestive tract. Its properties make it valuable for managing chronic and acute digestive conditions.

Anomalous Peony (Paeonia anomala) Monograph

1. Inflammatory bowel disease

Paeonia anomala has shown potential in reducing inflammation associated with inflammatory bowel disease due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds. Studies suggest it may help regulate intestinal immunity and reduce gut inflammation. It is used in traditional medicine to support digestive health and manage symptoms of chronic inflammatory conditions.

2. Gastritis

Anomalous Peony (Paeonia anomala) is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat gastritis due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. It helps reduce stomach lining irritation and promotes healing. Often prepared as a decoction, it is valued for its ability to alleviate symptoms such as bloating and indigestion.

3. Gastrointestinal spasms

Paeonia anomala is used to alleviate gastrointestinal spasms due to its antispasmodic properties. It helps relax smooth muscle tissue in the digestive tract, reducing cramping and discomfort. Traditionally, it has been applied in herbal formulations to ease symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome and other digestive disorders. Its effectiveness is attributed to bioactive compounds that modulate muscle contractions.

4. Stomach pain

Paeonia anomala is used to alleviate stomach pain by soothing inflammation and promoting digestion. It is particularly effective for spasmodic pain and cramping. The root is typically decocted or made into a tincture for internal use. It helps regulate gastrointestinal function and reduce discomfort.

5. Gastrointestinal disorders

Paeonia anomala is used to treat gastrointestinal disorders due to its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties. It helps alleviate symptoms such as bloating, indigestion, and irritable bowel syndrome. The root is often prepared as a decoction or tincture to support digestive health. Its effects are attributed to compounds like paeoniflorin and essential oils.

6. Digestive discomfort

Anomalous Peony is used to alleviate digestive discomfort by soothing the stomach lining and reducing inflammation. It aids in easing symptoms like bloating, indigestion, and nausea. Its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties help regulate gastrointestinal function. It is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture for digestive support.

7. Diarrhea

Paeonia anomala is used to treat diarrhea due to its astringent properties that help firm stools. It is often prepared as a decoction or powder and is particularly effective for cases involving dampness and heat. This herb is valued for its ability to harmonize the spleen and stomach, improving digestion and reducing loose stools.

8. Digestive disorders

Paeonia anomala is used to treat digestive disorders such as indigestion and bloating. Its anti-inflammatory and carminative properties help soothe the gastrointestinal tract. It is often prepared as a tincture or decoction to support healthy digestion and reduce discomfort.

9. Gastrointestinal discomfort

Anomalous Peony is used to alleviate gastrointestinal discomfort by soothing inflammation and reducing spasms in the digestive tract. Its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties help ease symptoms like bloating, cramping, and indigestion. It is often prepared as a decoction or tincture for internal use.

10. Digestive upset
