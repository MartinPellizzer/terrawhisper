These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties, which can alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, improve cardiovascular health, and enhance skin health.

By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, these constituents can improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic diseases, promoting overall well-being and resilience.
