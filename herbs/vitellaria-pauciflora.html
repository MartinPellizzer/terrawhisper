Vitellaria pauciflora

Vitellaria pauciflora, commonly known as the Bongossi tree, is a species of plant native to the tropical forests of Central Africa.

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including wounds, fever, and skin conditions, due to its reported health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Vitellaria pauciflora include wound healing, pain relief, and antiseptic properties, which contribute to its traditional use as a topical treatment.
