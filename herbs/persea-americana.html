Side Effects of persea americana

Persea americana triggers allergic reaction due to the presence of allergenic proteins in its fruit and leaves, which can provoke immune responses in sensitive individuals.

Persea americana causes skin irritation because the oils and compounds in the fruit, such as urushiol, can lead to inflammation and redness upon contact. Persea americana causes eye irritation as the same allergenic compounds can cause conjunctival inflammation and redness when they come into contact with the eyes.

Persea americana triggers skin rash due to the release of histamines and other inflammatory mediators in response to the plant's irritants.
