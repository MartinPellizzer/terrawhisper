What's the taxonomical classification of Neolepisorus ovatus?

Neolepisorus ovatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within the family Polypodiaceae, it is placed in the genus Neolepisorus, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species ovatus.
