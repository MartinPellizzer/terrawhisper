10 Health Benefits Of Clerodendrum Trichotomum (Harlequin Glory Bower)

Clerodendrum trichotomum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate pain and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

The plant's medicinal properties include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have been shown to exhibit antimicrobial and antifungal activities.

By using harlequin glory bower extracts or teas, people may experience relief from arthritis and other inflammatory conditions, as well as improved immune function, ultimately improving their overall quality of life.
