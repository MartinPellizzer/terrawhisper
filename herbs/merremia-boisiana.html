What's the taxonomical classification of Merremia boisiana?

Merremia boisiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Convolvulaceae, falling under the genus Merremia, and is specifically identified by its species name, boisiana.
