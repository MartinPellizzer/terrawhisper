10 Medicinal Constituents Of Justicia Adhatoda (Adhatoda)

Justicia adhatoda has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenes, and alkaloids, particularly vasicoline and vasicinone.

These constituents possess expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and bronchodilator properties, making it a traditional remedy for respiratory issues, including bronchitis and asthma.

The constituents of adhatoda can help alleviate cough and congestion, improving breathing and overall quality of life.
