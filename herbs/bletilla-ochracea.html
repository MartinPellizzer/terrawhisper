What's the taxonomical classification of Bletilla ochracea?

Blettilla ochracea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic path further, it is placed in the order Asparagales and belongs to the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Blettilla, with its specific designation being the species ochracea.
