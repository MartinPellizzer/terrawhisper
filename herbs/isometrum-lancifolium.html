Isometrum lancifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Isometrum lancifolium?

Isometrum lancifoliuom belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Lamiales and into the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Isometrum, and its unique species designation, lancifolium.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, stomach aches, respiratory issues, and inflammatory conditions. Extracts from the leaves and roots are frequently applied topically to soothe skin irritations or wounds. When ingested in traditional preparations, it serves as a remedy for digestive discomfort and gastrointestinal distress. Some practitioners also utilize the plant to alleviate symptoms of coughs and other breathing difficulties. Its medicinal properties are primarily attributed to the specific phytochemical compounds found within its tissues.
