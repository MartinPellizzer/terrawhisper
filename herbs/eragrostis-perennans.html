Eragrostis perennans

What's the taxonomical classification of Eragrostis perennans?

Eragrostis perennans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Poales and the family Poaceae, which places it among the true grasses. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Eragrostis, and its unique species designation, perennans.
