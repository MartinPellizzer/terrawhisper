What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eragrostis perennans has an annual life cycle characterized by slender, tufted stems that typically grow between 10 and 40 centimeters in height. Its inflorescence consists of several dense, spike-like panicles that are arranged in a way that makes them appear somewhat compact. Each spikelet contains a single floret protected by lemma and palea structures that are often slightly hairy.
