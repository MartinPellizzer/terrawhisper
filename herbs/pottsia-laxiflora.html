What's the taxonomical classification of Pottsia laxiflora?

Pottsia laxiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its systematic position continues through the order Gentianales and the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific taxonomic designation within the genus Pottsia, with the species name laxiflora.
