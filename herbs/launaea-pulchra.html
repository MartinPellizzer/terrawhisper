Launaea pulchra

Launaea pulchra, also known as the common spiny chervil or Launaea, is a species of plant in the dandelion tribe within the sunflower family.

This plant is rich in medicinal properties, particularly in its leaves and roots, which have been used to treat various health issues.

Launaea pulchra is known to have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making it beneficial for treating conditions such as fever, cough, and digestive issues.
