1. Reduces inflammation

Phlomis russeliana reduces inflammation because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's leaves and stems contain a rich source of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have been shown to exhibit potent anti-inflammatory properties.
