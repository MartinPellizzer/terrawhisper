10 Medicinal Constituents Of Phlomis Russeliana (Russell'S Lambs' Ears)

Phlomis russeliana has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The medicinal properties of Phlomis russeliana can improve respiratory health, reduce fever, and alleviate skin irritations.

For example, the flavonoids in this plant may help alleviate symptoms of asthma and allergies, while the terpenoids can reduce inflammation and promote wound healing.
