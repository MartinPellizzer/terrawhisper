Aconitum cannabifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum cannabifolium?

Aconitum cannabifoliuum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ranunculales. As a member of the family Ranunculaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Aconitum, with its specific classification being the species cannabifoliuum.
