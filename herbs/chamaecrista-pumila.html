Chamaecrista pumila belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized under the order Fabales. The plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it within the genus Chamaecrista, and is specifically identified by the species epithet pumila.
