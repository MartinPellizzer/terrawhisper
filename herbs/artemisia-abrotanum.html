Possible Side Effects of artemisia abrotanum

Artemisia abrotanum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal issues.

Additionally, artemisia abrotanum can interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse reactions.

Misuse of the herb may also exacerbate underlying health conditions, such as kidney disease and heart conditions.
