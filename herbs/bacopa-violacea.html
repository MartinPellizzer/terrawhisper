Bacopa violacea

Bacopa violacea, commonly known as "Violet Bacopa" or "Blue Brahmi", is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Bacopaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia and has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Bacopa violacea include improving cognitive function, reducing anxiety and stress, and enhancing memory and learning ability.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, saponins, and glycosides, such as bacosides, which are believed to be responsible for its medicinal properties.
