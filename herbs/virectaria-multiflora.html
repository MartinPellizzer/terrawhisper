What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Each inflorescence consists of numerous individual florets arranged in compact cymes to maximize pollination potential. The root system is fibrous and deep, providing stability in its native soil types. Small, globular fruit develops following successful fertilization of the many flowers.
