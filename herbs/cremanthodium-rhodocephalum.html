Cremanthodium rhodocephalum

What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium rhodocephalum?

Cremanthodium rhodocephalum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Cremanthodium, with its unique biological identity defined by its specific epithet, species rhodocephalum.
