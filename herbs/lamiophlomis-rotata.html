Lamiophlomis rotaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae, eventually narrowing down to its specific designation within the genus Lamiophlomis and the species rotaata.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
