Possible Side Effects of Potentilla glabriuscula

Potentilla glabriuscula can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including nausea and diarrhea, due to its high tannin content.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to kidney damage and increased risk of bleeding, particularly in individuals taking anticoagulant medications.

Additionally, the plant's emmenagogue properties may cause uterine contractions, which can be problematic for pregnant women.

Overuse of Potentilla glabriuscula may also interact with certain medications and exacerbate underlying health conditions, such as hypertension and diabetes.
