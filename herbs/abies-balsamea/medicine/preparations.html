10 Medicinal Preparations Of Abies Balsamea (Balsam Fir)

Abies balsamea has several medicinal preparations, such as decoctions, infusions, and tinctures.

These preparations are often used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, due to its expectorant properties.

The plant's resin is also used to create topical ointments and salves for skin irritations, wounds, and inflammatory conditions.
