10 Precautions To Take When Using Bauhinia Variegata (Orchid Tree)

Bauhinia variegata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and dosage.

Overuse or improper use can lead to side effects like allergic reactions, diarrhea, and abdominal pain. For instance, not considering the potential allergic reactions may cause a skin rash or anaphylaxis, which can be life-threatening.

It's also crucial to be aware of interactions with other medications.
