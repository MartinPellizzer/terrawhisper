Pilea cavaleriei subsp. cavaleriei

What's the taxonomical classification of Pilea cavaleriei subsp. cavaleriei?

Pilea cavaleriei subsp. cavaleriei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. It is a member of the family Urticaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Pilea. Ultimately, this plant is recognized by its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Pilea cavaleriei subsp. cavaleriei.
