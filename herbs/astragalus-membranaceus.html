Yellow Milkvetch (Astragalus membranaceus)

Yellow Milkvetch (Astragalus membranaceus) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to China, Mongolia, and Russia. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
