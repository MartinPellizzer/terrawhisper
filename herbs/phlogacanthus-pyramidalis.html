What's the taxonomical classification of Phlogacanthus pyramidalis?

Phlogacanthus pyramidalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Acanthaceae, falling specifically within the genus Phlogacanthus, and is identified by the species name pyramidalis.
