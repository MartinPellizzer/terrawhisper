Ostodes katharinae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is placed in the order Malpighiales and falls under the family Euphorbiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Ostodes and is specifically designated as the species katharinae.
