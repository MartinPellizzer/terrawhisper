Premna angolensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Premna angolensis?

Premna angolensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Premna, with its specific designation being the species angolenis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Premna angolensis has opposite, decussate leaves that are elliptic to oblong in shape, featuring entire margins and an acute or acuminate apex. The inflorescence consists of small, inconspicuous flowers that are often arranged in axillary cymes.
