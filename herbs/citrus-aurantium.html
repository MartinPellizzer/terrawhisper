Possible Side Effects of citrus aurantium

Citrus aurantium can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing rapid heartbeat, dizziness, and nausea due to its high levels of synephrine.

Ingesting large amounts of the herb can also lead to increased blood pressure, anxiety, and insomnia in some individuals.

Furthermore, combining citrus aurantium with certain medications, such as blood thinners and stimulants, can result in serious health complications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using citrus aurantium to minimize the risk of adverse side effects.
