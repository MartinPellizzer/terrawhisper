Alcea acaulis

Alcea acaulis, also known as mountain hollyhock, is a perennial plant that belongs to the mallow family.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, making it effective in treating various health conditions.

The medicinal properties of Alcea acaulis can be attributed to its ability to reduce swelling, fight infections, and soothe digestive issues, offering benefits such as improved gut health and reduced inflammation.

Herbal preparations of Alcea acaulis can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, which can be consumed to harness its medicinal properties.
