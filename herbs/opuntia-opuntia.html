Prickly Pear (Opuntia opuntia)

Prickly Pear (Opuntia opuntia) is a member of the Cactaceae family, native to North America, Southwest United States, and Mexico. Traditionally, its fruits, stems, and leaves have been used for decoctions, poultices, and culinary uses.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and european herbal medicine.
