What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Zanthoxylum dissitum has pinnately compound leaves characterized by numerous small, elliptical leaflets arranged in an opposite or sub-opposite pattern along the rachis. The leaflets possess entire margins and acute to obtuse apices, often displaying a smooth or slightly coriaceous texture. The petioles are slender and support the leaf structure, while the overall foliage presents a light to medium green coloration.
