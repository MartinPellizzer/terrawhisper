The therapeutic actions of pennyroyal herb include its spasmolytic, carminative, and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help to relax muscles, reduce gas and bloating, and soothe irritated tissues.

Additionally, excessive use of mentha pulegium may cause interactions with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, and may worsen certain health conditions, like high blood pressure and epilepsy.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using mentha pulegium for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
