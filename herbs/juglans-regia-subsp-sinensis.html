Juglans regia subsp. sinensis

Juglans regia subsp.

sinensis, also known as Chinese walnut or Oriental English walnut, is a subspecies of the walnut tree native to China.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities.

The health benefits of Juglans regia subsp.

sinensis include reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and supporting immune function, making it a popular ingredient in traditional Chinese medicine.

Herbal preparations of this plant include extracts, tinctures, and teas, which can be used to treat various ailments, such as fever, cough, and digestive issues.
