Siphonostegia chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, specifically categorized under the genus Siphonostegia, with the specific epithet chinensis identifying its species.

As discussed in a study, the bioactive constituents of Siphonostegia chinensis include quinic acid esters. Three specific quinic acid esters were isolated and identified through spectroscopic methods and column chromatography. These compounds are 3,4-di-O-caffeoylquinic acid(I), macranthoin F(II), and the methyl ester of 3,4,5-tri-O-caffeoylquinic acid(III). All 3 of these compounds were isolated from Siphonostegia for the first time.
