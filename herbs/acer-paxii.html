Acer paxii

What's the taxonomical classification of Acer paxii?

Acer paxii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Sapindales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Sapindaceae, which contains the genus Acer. Ultimately, the plant is identified at the species level as Acer paxii.
