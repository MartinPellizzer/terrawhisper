Possible Side Effects of scutellaria barbata

Scutellaria barbata can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea and diarrhea, as well as liver damage due to its high saponin content.

In rare cases, it may also cause allergic reactions, such as hives and itching, in sensitive individuals.

Long-term use or high doses of scutellaria barbata can lead to kidney damage and may interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Additionally, pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult with a healthcare professional before using scutellaria barbata due to its potential risks.
