Scutellaria barbata

Scutellaria Barbata (Scutellaria barbata) is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands, known for its traditional use in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.

Botanical Identification

Scutellaria barbata, commonly known as bearded scutellaria, is a perennial herb in the Lamiaceae family. Native to parts of Asia, it features square stems, lance-shaped leaves, and violet-blue flowers. It is also referred to as barbed scutellaria in some herbal texts.

The following list summarize the taxonomy of plant:

  • Kingdom: Plantae
  • Division: Magnoliophyta
  • Class: Lamiales
  • Order: Lamiales
  • Family: Lamiaceae
  • Genus: Scutellaria
  • Species: Scutellaria barbata

Safety Profile

Scutellaria barbata is generally considered safe when used in recommended doses. However, it may cause gastrointestinal discomfort in some individuals. There are no well-documented contraindications, but caution is advised during pregnancy and lactation due to limited safety data.
