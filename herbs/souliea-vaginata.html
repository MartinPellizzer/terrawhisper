What's the taxonomical classification of Souliea vaginata?

Souliea vaginata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically situated within the genus Souliea, with its unique designation as the species vaginata.
