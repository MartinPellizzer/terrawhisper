Galium paradoxum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic ranks, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific classification within the genus Galium, with its unique identity established by the species name paradoxum.
