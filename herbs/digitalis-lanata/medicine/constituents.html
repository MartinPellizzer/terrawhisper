10 Medicinal Constituents Of Digitalis Lanata (Foxglove)

Digitalis lanata has active constituents such as digoxin, digoxigenin, and digitoxin, which possess medicinal properties that treat heart conditions, particularly atrial fibrillation and congestive heart failure.

These constituents help regulate heartbeat, increase cardiac contractility, and reduce cardiac workload. As a result, patients with heart failure can experience improved symptoms and enhanced quality of life.

Furthermore, these constituents can also be used to treat certain arrhythmias and reduce the risk of sudden cardiac death.

It has been used for centuries to treat various heart conditions, including atrial fibrillation and congestive heart failure. Digoxin works by slowing down the heart rate and increasing the contraction force, allowing the heart to pump more efficiently.

As an oral medication, it is often prescribed in combination with other treatments to manage cardiac arrhythmias and improve overall heart function.
