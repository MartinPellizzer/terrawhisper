What's the taxonomical classification of Trigonostemon fragilis?

Trigonostemon fragilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Malpighiales. This plant is further categorized under the family Euphorbiaceae, which places it within the genus Trigonostemon, specifically identified by the species epithet fragilis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Trigonostemon fragilis has a slender, herbaceous habit characterized by thin, delicate stems and small, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or lanceolate in shape.

The foliage often exhibits a smooth texture with entire or slightly serrated margins. Its reproductive structures consist of inconspicuous flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves. The plant is distinguished by its fragile architecture, which reflects its specific epithet.
