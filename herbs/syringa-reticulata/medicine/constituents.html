10 Medicinal Constituents Of Syringa Reticulata (Japanese Lilac)

Syringa reticulata, also known as Japanese lilac, has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making the plant a valuable asset in traditional Chinese medicine. The constituents can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation and opening airways.

Additionally, they may also aid in wound healing and skin disorders by promoting collagen synthesis and tissue regeneration.
