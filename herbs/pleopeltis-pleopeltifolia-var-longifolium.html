Pleopeltis pleopeltifolia var. longifolium

Pleopeltis pleopeltifolia var. longifolium, also known as the long-leaved polypody, is a type of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is traditionally used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antimicrobial activities.

The health benefits of Pleopeltis pleopeltifolia var. longifolium include its potential to treat wounds, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as in topical applications such as salves and creams, to take advantage of its medicinal properties.
