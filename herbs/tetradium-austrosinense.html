What's the taxonomical classification of Tetradium austrosinense?

Tetradium austrosinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Sapindales and the family Rutaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Tetradium, and its species name, austrosinense.
