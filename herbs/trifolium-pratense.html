Trifolium pratense

Trifolium pratense, also known as red clover, is a perennial flowering plant in the legume family that is native to Europe, Western Asia, and Northwest Africa.

The plant is rich in isoflavones, particularly formononetin and biochanin A, which have been shown to have medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Red clover has been used to help alleviate symptoms of menopause, such as hot flashes and night sweats, and to support cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol levels and improving blood vessel function.

Herbal preparations of red clover, including teas, tinctures, and capsules, are available and can be used to support overall health and well-being.
