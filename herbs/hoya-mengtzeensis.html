Hoya mengtzeensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hoya mengtzeensis has thick, succulent, elliptical leaves that are dark green and reach up to 10 centimeters in length. These leaves are arranged oppositely along trailing or climbing woody stems that allow the plant to scramble over surfaces. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers that feature a fleshy corona and a central ring of nectar. Each blossom is typically creamy white with subtle markings and emits a sweet fragrance. Small, rounded peduncles support these floral groups to facilitate pollination in its natural habitat.
