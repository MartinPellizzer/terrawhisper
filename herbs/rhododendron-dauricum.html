Rhododendron dauricum

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron dauricum?

Rhododendron dauricum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Ericales. Within the family Ericaceae, it is categorized under the genus Rhododendron, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species dauricum.
