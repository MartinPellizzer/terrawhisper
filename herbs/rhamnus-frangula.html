Possible Side Effects of rhamnus frangula

Rhamnus frangula can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high anthraquinone content.

Overuse of the herb can also lead to electrolyte imbalances, potentially causing muscle weakness, fatigue, and cardiac arrhythmias.

Additionally, improper use of rhamnus frangula may cause allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and difficulty breathing in some individuals.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of the herb may exacerbate underlying kidney problems, particularly in individuals with pre-existing kidney disease.
