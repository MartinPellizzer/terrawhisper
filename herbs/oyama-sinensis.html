Possible Side Effects of Oyama sinensis

Oyama sinensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Oyama sinensis for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
