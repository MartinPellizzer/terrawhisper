What's the taxonomical classification of Asparagus brachyphyllus?

Asparagus brachyphyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Asparagales. The plant is a member of the family Asparagaceae and falls under the genus Asparagus, specifically identified as the species brachyphyllus.
