Yuplania liliiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its lineage, it falls under the order Magnoliales and is a member of the family Magnoliaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Yuplania, specifically as the species liliiflora.
