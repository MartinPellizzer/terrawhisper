Utricularia vulgaris var. tenuicaulis

Utricularia vulgaris var. tenuicaulis, also known as the slender bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant species that belongs to the bladderwort family (Lentibulariaceae).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral activities.

The health benefits of Utricularia vulgaris var. tenuicaulis include its ability to help reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and support the immune system.
