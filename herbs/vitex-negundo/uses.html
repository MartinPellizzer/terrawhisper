Negundo Vitex (Vitex negundo) Uses

Vitex negundo is used to treat respiratory infections, cough, and asthma due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It also addresses skin infections, eczema, and inflammatory conditions by promoting healing and reducing irritation. The plant supports wound recovery and alleviates skin inflammation and irritation effectively.

Negundo Vitex (Vitex negundo) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Vitex negundo is used traditionally to support respiratory health. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help alleviate symptoms of coughs, colds, and bronchitis. It is often prepared as a decoction or inhalation to clear congestion and soothe irritated airways.

2. Cough

Vitex negundo is used to treat coughs by reducing inflammation in the respiratory tract and easing mucus production. It acts as an expectorant, helping to clear the airways. Traditionally, it is prepared as a decoction or infused oil to soothe persistent coughs and improve breathing.

3. Asthma

Vitex negundo is used in traditional medicine to alleviate asthma symptoms. Its leaves and flowers contain compounds that help reduce bronchial inflammation and relax airway muscles. It is often prepared as a decoction or infusion to ease breathing and manage respiratory distress.

4. Skin infections

Vitex negundo is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce bacterial growth and soothe inflamed skin. Often applied as a poultice or infused oil, it is effective for conditions like eczema and fungal infections. Its natural compounds support healing and prevent further infection.

5. Eczema

Vitex negundo is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat eczema due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce skin irritation and promote healing. The leaves and flowers are typically prepared as a poultice or infusion. It is valued for its soothing effect on inflamed skin.

6. Skin inflammation

Vitex negundo is used topically to treat skin inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and irritation, making it effective for conditions like eczema and psoriasis. The leaves and flowers are commonly applied as poultices or infused oils. It is a key herb in traditional remedies for soothing and healing inflamed skin.

7. Wound healing

Vitex negundo is used topically to promote wound healing due to its antimicrobial and astringent properties. It helps reduce inflammation, prevent infection, and accelerate tissue repair. Traditionally applied as a poultice or decoction, it is effective for minor cuts, abrasions, and skin irritations. Its ability to stimulate cellular regeneration supports faster recovery.

8. Skin irritation

Vitex negundo is used to treat skin irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and inflammation associated with rashes, eczema, and minor burns. External application of its leaves or extracts can provide relief and promote skin healing. It is often prepared as a poultice or infused oil for topical use.

9. Inflammatory skin conditions

Vitex negundo is used topically to treat inflammatory skin conditions due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and infection in conditions like eczema and psoriasis. The leaves and bark are commonly prepared as poultices or infusions for direct application. Its soothing effects promote skin healing and relief from irritation.

10. Wounds

Vitex negundo has been traditionally used to treat wounds due to its astringent and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps in reducing swelling, preventing infection, and promoting tissue repair. The leaves and bark are often applied topically to clean and heal cuts, abrasions, and minor burns. Its ability to stimulate healing makes it a valued remedy in herbal medicine for wound care.
