10 Medicinal Constituents Of Vitex Negundo (Chinese Chaste Tree)

Vitex negundo, also known as Chinese Chaste Tree, has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and essential oils.

These constituents exhibit medicinal properties that include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The flavonoids and iridoid glycosides can help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, such as diarrhea and dyspepsia, while the essential oils may aid in relieving stress and anxiety.

Additionally, these constituents may also contribute to the plant's potential benefits for skin health, wound healing, and respiratory issues, ultimately improving overall quality of life by promoting well-being and reducing discomfort.
