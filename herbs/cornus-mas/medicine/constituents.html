10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cornus Mas (Osage Orange)

Cornus mas, also known as Cornelian cherry, has active constituents such as alkaloids (cornine, berberine), flavonoids (rutin, quercetin), and terpenoids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The flavonoids in Cornus mas can improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Additionally, the antioxidants may protect against age-related diseases, such as Alzheimer's and cancer, by neutralizing free radicals.
