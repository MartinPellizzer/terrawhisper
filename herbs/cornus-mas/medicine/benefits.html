1. Reduces inflammation

Cornus mas reduces inflammation because it is rich in flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes, which possess potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, suppress the activation of NF-κB signaling pathways, and modulate the activity of enzymes involved in inflammatory responses.

This makes Cornus mas a valuable natural remedy for alleviating chronic inflammation and associated diseases.

2. Improves heart health

Cornus mas improves heart health because it is rich in flavonoids and anthocyanins, powerful antioxidants that help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

Its extracts have been shown to lower cholesterol levels, improve blood flow, and prevent platelet aggregation, all of which can contribute to a reduced risk of cardiovascular disease.

Additionally, its bioactive compounds may also help to stabilize blood pressure and improve overall cardiac function.
