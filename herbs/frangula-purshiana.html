Pursh'S Buckthorn (Frangula purshiana)

Pursh'S Buckthorn (Frangula purshiana) is a member of the Rhamnaceae family, native to North America, United States, and Rocky Mountains. Traditionally, its roots, bark, and seeds have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
