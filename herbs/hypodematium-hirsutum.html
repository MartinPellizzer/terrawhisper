What's the taxonomical classification of Hypodematium hirsutum?

Hypodematium hirsutum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. It further falls under the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Hypodematiaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Hypodematium, with its specific designation being the species hirsutum.
