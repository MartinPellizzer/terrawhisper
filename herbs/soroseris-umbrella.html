What's the taxonomical classification of Soroseris umbrella?

Soroseris umbrella belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, specifically situated within the genus Soroseris, and is identified by its unique species name, umbrella.

      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Soroseris umbrella can cause localized skin irritation, allergic dermatitis, and gastrointestinal distress if ingested. Contact with the plant's sap or foliage may trigger redness, itching, or swelling on the skin of sensitive individuals. If the plant is accidentally consumed, it can lead to nausea, stomach cramps, or vomiting. Some people might also experience respiratory irritation if they inhale any pollen or dust from the plant. Always exercise caution and consult a professional before handling or using this species.
