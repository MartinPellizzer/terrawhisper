1. Morphine

Papaver somniferum morphine is a naturally occurring alkaloid extracted from the opium poppy plant.

It is a powerful opioid analgesic, widely used to treat moderate to severe pain, especially in medical settings.

Morphine works by binding to opioid receptors in the brain and spinal cord, blocking the transmission of pain signals, providing relief from acute or chronic pain, including postoperative pain, cancer pain, and neuropathic pain.

2. Codeine

Papaver somniferum codeine is an alkaloid compound extracted from the opium poppy plant.

It is a naturally occurring opioid that is used to treat mild to moderate pain, coughs, and diarrhea. Codeine is often prescribed in combination with other medications or in small doses for short-term relief.

However, it can also be highly addictive and has a high potential for abuse, leading to dependence and withdrawal symptoms when use is stopped.
