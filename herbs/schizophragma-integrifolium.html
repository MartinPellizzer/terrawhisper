Schizophragma integrifolium

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Schizophragma integrifolium has pinnately compound leaves characterized by several pairs of entire, lanceolate leaflets that lack serrations along their margins. The plant's overall structure is defined by these spreading leaf patterns which help maximize light absorption.
