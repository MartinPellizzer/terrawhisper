Carduus armenus

Carduus armenus, also known as Armenian thistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family native to the mountainous regions of Asia Minor and the Caucasus.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can help to reduce fever, alleviate digestive issues, and support the immune system.

The health benefits of Carduus armenus include its ability to protect against oxidative stress, improve cardiovascular health, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in the form of tinctures, infusions, and decoctions, and can be combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
