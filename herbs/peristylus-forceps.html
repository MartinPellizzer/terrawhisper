What's the taxonomical classification of Peristylus forceps?

Peristylus forceps belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized under Equisetopsida, specifically falling into the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical progression, the plant is placed in the order Asparagales and is a member of the Orchidaceae family. Within this botanical framework, it is identified by the genus Peristylus and is specifically known by its species name, forceps.
