10 Best Brassica Juncea Preparations

Teas made from the leaves or seeds are commonly used to support digestion and respiratory health.

Decoctions involve simmering the root or stem to extract its medicinal compounds, often used for their anti-inflammatory properties.

Mucillages, derived from the seeds, are valued for their soothing effects on the digestive tract.

Capsules provide a convenient and standardized form for daily supplementation, making Brassica juncea a versatile herb in traditional and modern medicine.
