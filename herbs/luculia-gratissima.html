Luculia gratissima

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Luculia gratissima has an evergreen shrub or small tree structure characterized by elliptical, finely serrated leaves and fragrant, solitary white flowers. The foliage is typically glossy and dark green, providing a dense backdrop for its seasonal blooms. Each flower features delicate, thin petals that create a soft, papery texture. These blossoms emerge from the axils of the leaves and are often accompanied by small, rounded fruit. The overall growth habit is compact yet elegant, making it a distinct ornamental species.
