Tilia Argentea Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Tilia argentea, commonly known as the silver lime tree, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Tiliaceae family, known for its fragrant flowers and medicinal leaves.

This herb is valued for its calming and soothing properties, offering benefits such as reducing anxiety, improving sleep quality, and supporting respiratory health.

The bioactive constituents in Tilia argentea include flavonoids, tannins, mucilage, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
