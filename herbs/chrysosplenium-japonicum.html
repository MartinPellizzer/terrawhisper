Chrysospeltiun japonicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Saxifragales. It is further categorized into the family Saxifragaceae, which contains its specific genus, Chrysospeltiun, and its unique species designation, japonicum.

The plant possesses properties believed to help regulate digestive functions and reduce inflammation within the gut. Practitioners often prepare decoctions from its components to address these specific internal ailments. Its application is primarily focused on restoring balance to the digestive system.
