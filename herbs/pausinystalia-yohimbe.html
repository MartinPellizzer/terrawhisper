Yohimbe (Pausinystalia yohimbe)

Yohimbe (Pausinystalia yohimbe) is a member of the Rubiaceae family, native to West Africa, Central Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its bark, leaves, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and native american herbal medicine.
