Litsea rotundifolia var. oblongifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Litsea rotundifolia var. oblongifolia?

Litsea rotundifolia var. oblongifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Laurales. It is a member of the family Lauraceae, falling under the specific genus Litsea. Ultimately, its precise taxonomic designation is the species Litsea rotundifolia var. oblongifolia.
