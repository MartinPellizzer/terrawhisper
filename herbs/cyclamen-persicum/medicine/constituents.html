10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cyclamen Persicum (Persian Violet)

Cyclamen persicum, also known as Persian Violet, has active constituents such as alkaloids, saponins, and glycosides.

These compounds possess medicinal properties that can improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from various health issues. The alkaloids in Cyclamen persicum have antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of conditions like bronchitis and asthma.

Meanwhile, the saponins have antioxidant properties, making them useful in protecting against oxidative stress and potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
