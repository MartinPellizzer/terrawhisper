Dioscorea martiana var. genuina

Dioscorea martiana var. genuina, also known as the Philippine yam or white yam, is a type of plant that belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is a species of yam that is native to the Philippines and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including arthritis, rheumatism, and high blood pressure, due to its ability to reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health.

Dioscorea martiana var. genuina can be prepared in various herbal forms, including tea, extract, and capsule, making it accessible for consumption and utilization in traditional medicine.
