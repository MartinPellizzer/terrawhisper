What's the taxonomical classification of Celtis philippensis?

Celtis philippensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Rosales and belongs to the family Cannabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Celtis, and its specific species designation, philippensis.
