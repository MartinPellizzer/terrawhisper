Common Agrimony (Agrimonia pilosa) Uses

Common Agrimony is primarily used to treat gastrointestinal issues such as diarrhea, inflammation, and discomfort. It helps alleviate symptoms of gastric ulcers, stomach pain, and gas. Its anti-inflammatory properties support healing in conditions like inflammatory bowel disease and gastrointestinal spasms. It is commonly used for general digestive support and relief from upset stomach.

Common Agrimony (Agrimonia pilosa) Monograph

1. Diarrhea

Common Agrimony is used to treat diarrhea due to its astringent properties, which help firm up stools. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture and is effective for acute cases. Its ability to reduce intestinal secretions makes it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for digestive ailments.

2. Gastrointestinal inflammation

Common Agrimony has been used traditionally to support gastrointestinal health, particularly in cases of inflammation. Its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties may help soothe irritated mucous membranes in the digestive tract. It is often used to alleviate symptoms of conditions like gastritis and colitis. Its mild nature makes it suitable for long-term use under professional guidance.
