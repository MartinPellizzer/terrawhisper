Przewalskia tangutica

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Przewalskia tangutica has perennial, rhizomatous stems that support lanceolate to ovate leaves with entire or slightly serrated margins. These flowers feature five petals and are often arranged in compact clusters. The plant's overall structure is relatively low-growing, adapted to its specific high-altitude habitat.
