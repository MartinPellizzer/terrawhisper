Hylotelephium spectabile

What's the taxonomical classification of Hylotelephium spectabile?

Hylotelephium spectabile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Saxifragales. As a member of the family Crassulaceae, this plant is categorized under the genus Hylotelephium, specifically identified by the species name spectabile.
