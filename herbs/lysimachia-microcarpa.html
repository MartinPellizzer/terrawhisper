Lysimachia microcarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia microcarpa?

Lysimachia microcarpra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is specifically situated within the family Primulaceae, placed in the genus Lysimachia, and is identified by the species name microcarpra.
