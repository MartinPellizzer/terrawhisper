Triticum dicoccum var. caucasicum

Triticum dicoccum var. caucasicum, also known as Caucasian emmer wheat, is an ancient type of wheat that is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, and is used to treat various health conditions such as digestive issues and respiratory problems.

The health benefits of Triticum dicoccum var. caucasicum include reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and boosting the immune system.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in traditional cooking and baking, showcasing its versatility in both culinary and medicinal applications.
