Camellia sinensis var. sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Camellia sinensis var. sinensis?

Camellia sinensis var. sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Ericales and is a prominent member of the family Theaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Camellia, ultimately reaching the specific designation of Camellia sinensis.
