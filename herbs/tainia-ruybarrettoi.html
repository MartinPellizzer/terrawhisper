What's the taxonomical classification of Tainia ruybarrettoi?

Tainia ruybarrettoi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This specific plant is a part of the family Orchidaceae, placing it among the diverse orchids. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Tainia, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name ruybarrettoi.
