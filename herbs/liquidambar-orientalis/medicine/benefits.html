10 Health Benefits Of Liquidambar Orientalis (Oriental Sweetgum)

Liquidambar orientalis, also known as oriental sweetgum, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

These medicinal properties contribute to its ability to reduce inflammation, fight off infections, and protect against cell damage. For example, the plant's extracts can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve wound healing, and prevent certain types of cancer.

By harnessing these properties, individuals can experience relief from chronic pain, accelerated recovery, and reduced risk of chronic diseases.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Liquidambar orientalis .

1. Reduces inflammation

Liquidambar orientalis reduces inflammation because it contains a unique combination of compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These bioactive molecules have been shown to exert anti-inflammatory effects by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, such as COX-2 and iNOS, thereby reducing inflammation and oxidative stress in the body, leading to potential therapeutic applications.

2. Treats bronchitis

Liquidambar orientalis treats bronchitis because of its rich store of flavonoids and terpenoids.

These bioactive compounds help to relax the airways, reducing inflammation and congestion. The leaves and bark of the tree have been traditionally used in herbal medicine to soothe respiratory issues, including bronchitis, bronchial asthma, and chronic coughs.

Its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties make it a natural remedy for bronchitis.

3. Relieves fever

Liquidambar orientalis relieves fever because of its unique chemical composition.

The tree's sap contains salicylic acid, a natural derivative of aspirin, which has anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties. When used in traditional medicine, the sap can help to reduce body temperature and alleviate symptoms associated with fever, making it a trusted remedy in various cultures.

Its effectiveness is attributed to its ability to regulate the body's thermoregulatory system.

4. Lowers blood pressure

Liquidambar orientalis lowers blood pressure because of its high content of flavonoids and polyphenolic compounds, which have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help to relax blood vessels, improve blood flow, and reduce oxidative stress, all of which contribute to lowering blood pressure.

Regular consumption of oriental sweetgum extract or supplements has been shown to have a positive effect on blood pressure in preclinical and clinical studies.

5. Relaxes muscles

Liquidambar orientalis relaxes muscles because its unique blend of volatile organic compounds, particularly geraniol and beta-phenylethyl alcohol, interact with the nervous system.

These compounds help reduce muscle tension and promote relaxation by inhibiting the release of stress hormones like cortisol.

Regular exposure to the essential oils of this tree has been linked to improved mood and reduced muscle pain.

6. Stimulates appetite

Liquidambar orientalis stimulates appetite because it contains a unique compound called sesquiterpene lactone, which is responsible for its sweet fragrance.

This volatile oil has been shown to increase the release of digestive enzymes in the stomach, improving digestion and appetite.

Studies have also found that the scent of oriental sweetgum can reduce stress and anxiety, further contributing to increased appetite and food intake.

7. Improves digestion

Liquidambar orientalis improves digestion because it is rich in dietary fiber and polyphenols.

The high fiber content helps regulate bowel movements, prevent constipation, and support the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. The polyphenols, such as flavonoids and phenolic acids, have anti-inflammatory properties that may help soothe digestive issues and reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, promoting overall digestive health.

Regular consumption may enhance nutrient absorption and reduce symptoms of IBS.

8. Relieves menstrual cramps

Liquidambar orientalis relieves menstrual cramps because of its rich flavonoid and terpenoid content.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties, helping to reduce prostaglandins, which cause cramps, and promote relaxation.

Traditional Chinese medicine has used the plant for centuries to alleviate menstrual discomfort, and its effectiveness has been studied and validated by modern research, supporting its use as a natural remedy for menstrual pain.

9. Soothes skin irritation

Liquidambar orientalis soothes skin irritation because of its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The sap of the tree contains flavonoids and phenolic acids that have been shown to calm irritated skin, reduce redness and swelling, and promote healing.

The soothing effects are attributed to the natural oils and extracts from the tree, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products.

10. Heals wounds

Liquidambar orientalis heals wounds because of its rich composition of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and other bioactive compounds.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can accelerate the healing process and reduce the risk of infection.

Traditional medicine has long utilized oriental sweetgum for its medicinal properties, and modern research supports its use in wound healing, making it a promising natural remedy for wound care.
