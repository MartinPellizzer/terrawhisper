Possible Side Effects of terminalia belerica

Terminalia belerica can have side effects if used improperly, such as excessive stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea due to its high tannin content.

In rare cases, Terminalia belerica can cause allergic reactions, such as skin rashes or itching, in people sensitive to the herb.

Additionally, it may exacerbate certain health conditions, such as kidney or liver problems, if not used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
