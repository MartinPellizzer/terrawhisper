What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ceropegia mairei has slender, cylindrical, succulent stems that grow in a sprawling or climbing habit and are covered in small, pointed tubercles. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that typically emerge from the stem axils. These blossoms are often pale or white and possess a delicate, waxy texture.
