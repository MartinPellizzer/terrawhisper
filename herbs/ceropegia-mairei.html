Ceropegia mairei

What's the taxonomical classification of Ceropegia mairei?

Ceropegia mairei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Gentianales, the plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Ceropegia, with its unique designation being the species mairei.
