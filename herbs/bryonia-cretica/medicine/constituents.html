These compounds contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and expectorant properties. The constituents can help alleviate respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation and easing congestion.

Regular use of white bryony may also improve digestive health by regulating bowel movements and relieving symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.
