What's the taxonomical classification of Triglochin maritima?

Triglochin maritima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Alismatales and the family Juncaginaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Triglochin, and its unique species designation, maritima.
