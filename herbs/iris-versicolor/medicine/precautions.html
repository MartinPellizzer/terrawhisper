10 Precautions To Take When Using Iris Versicolor (Blue Flag)

Iris versicolor has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential liver damage.

Taking high doses of Iris versicolor may cause liver toxicity, which can lead to symptoms like nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain. This risk increases when combined with other herbs or medications that also affect the liver.

Not taking these precautions may result in liver damage or other complications, emphasizing the importance of consulting with a healthcare professional before using Iris versicolor medicinally.
