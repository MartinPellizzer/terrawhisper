Variable Iris (Iris versicolor) Compounds

The primary compounds in Variable Iris include flavonoids, which act as antioxidants, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins contribute to astringency, while coumarin has anticoagulant effects. Glycosides and essential oils provide additional therapeutic benefits, and alkaloids and terpenoids support various medicinal functions. Saponins and anthocyanins also play roles in healing and protection.
