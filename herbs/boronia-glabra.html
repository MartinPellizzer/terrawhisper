Boronia glabra

Boronia glabra, also known as the brown boronia or brown boron, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

This plant is native to Australia and has been used by indigenous Australians for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of Boronia glabra include its ability to soothe skin irritations, reduce fever, and aid in wound healing, making it a valuable resource in traditional medicine.

Boronia glabra can be prepared as a herbal tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used in combination with other herbs to enhance its medicinal effects.
