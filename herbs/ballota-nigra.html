Ballota Nigra Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ballota nigra, commonly known as black horehound, is a medicinal herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and flowers, which contain various bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, contributing to its therapeutic properties.

Health benefits of Ballota nigra include its potential to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and aid in digestive wellness.

This herb can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or herbal tea to harness its medicinal properties.
