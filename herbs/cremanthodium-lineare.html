Cremanthodium lineare belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, which is the characteristic group for many composite flowers. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Cremanthodium, with its specific classification being the species lineare.
