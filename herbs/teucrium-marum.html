Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum)

Common Teucrium (Teucrium marum) is a member of the Labiatae family, native to Southern Europe, North Africa, and Western Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and native american herbal medicine.
