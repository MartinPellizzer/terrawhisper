Nutgrass (Cyperus rotundus) Compounds

The primary active compounds of Nutgrass include flavonoids, tannins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Saponins and coumarins enhance its detoxifying and antimicrobial effects. Glycosides and essential oils provide additional therapeutic benefits, while terpenoids and alkaloids support its broad pharmacological profile. Anthocyanins add to its role in protecting cellular structures.
