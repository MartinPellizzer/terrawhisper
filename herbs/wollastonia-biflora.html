Wollastonia biflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Wollastonia biflora?

Wollastonia biflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asterales. It is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, falling under the genus Wollastonia, and is specifically identified by its species name, biflora.

Wollastonia biflora has slender, erect stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and bear small, blue to violet flowers with four petals.

The root system is relatively shallow, anchoring the herbaceous plant within moist or aquatic soil environments.
