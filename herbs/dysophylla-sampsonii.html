Dysphyllia sampsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Lamiales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its specific placement within the genus Dysophyllia, ultimately identifying it as the species sampsonii.
