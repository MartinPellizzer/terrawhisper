Libanotis laticalycina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Apiales, it falls under the family Apiaceae, which is characterized by its distinctive flowering structures. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Libanotis, and its specific identification is given by the species epithet laticalycina.
