Acer sterculiaceum subsp. franchetii

What's the taxonomical classification of Acer sterculiaceum subsp. franchetii?

Acer sterculiaceum subsp. franchetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Sapindales. Further taxonomic refinement places it in the family Sapindaceae, within the genus Acer. Ultimately, this specific plant is identified as the species Acer sterculiaceuum subsp. franchetii.
