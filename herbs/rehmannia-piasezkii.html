Rehmannia piasezkii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is specifically situated within the family Orobanchaceae, which defines its closer evolutionary relationships. Taxonomically, it is identified by its genus, Rehmannia, and its specific epithet, piasezkii, forming the complete scientific name of the species.
