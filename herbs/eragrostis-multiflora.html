Eragrostis multiflora

Eragrostis multiflora, also known as the many-flowered love grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

The health benefits of Eragrostis multiflora include reducing inflammation, relieving pain, and improving digestion, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine.
