Piscidia Erythrina Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Piscidia erythrina, commonly known as the "false hibiscus" or "rhubarb tree," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, known for its medicinal properties.

This herb is used traditionally for its calming effects and is believed to support mental health and emotional well-being.

The bioactive constituents in Piscidia erythrina include alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, such as reducing anxiety and promoting relaxation.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from its leaves and bark to harness its beneficial properties.
