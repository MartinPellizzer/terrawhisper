Prunus avium subsp. sylvestris

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and astringent properties, which are attributed to its rich content of anthocyanins and other bioactive compounds.

The health benefits of Prunus avium subsp.

sylvestris include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and exhibit antimicrobial activity, making it a popular ingredient in natural remedies for various ailments.
