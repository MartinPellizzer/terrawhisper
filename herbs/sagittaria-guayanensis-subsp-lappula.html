What's the taxonomical classification of Sagittaria guayanensis subsp. lappula?

Sagittaria guayanensis subsp. lappula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Alismatales. It is a member of the family Alismataceae, falling under the genus Sagittaria. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is identified as the species Sagittaria guauyanensis subsp. lappula.
