10 Possible Side Effects Of Citrus Limon (Lemon)

Citrus limon has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive issues, kidney stones, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption, sensitivity to citric acid, or pre-existing kidney problems.

For instance, a person with kidney disease may experience worsened symptoms, such as more frequent urination and painful kidney stones, which can significantly affect their daily life and quality of time.
