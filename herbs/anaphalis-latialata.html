Anaphalis latialata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, falling into the genus Anaphalis, and is specifically identified by its species name, latialata.
