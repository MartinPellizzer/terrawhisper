Helia glabra

Helia glabra, also known as Helichrysum glabrum, is a flowering plant species native to South Africa, belonging to the daisy family.

The medicinal properties of Helia glabra include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which have been utilized in traditional medicine.

This plant is valued for its health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and alleviate respiratory issues such as bronchitis.

Helia glabra can be prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or infused oil, and is often used in aromatherapy and topical applications.
