Medicago altissima

Medicago altissima, commonly known as tall medic or big burclover, is a perennial legume plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa and has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

The plant has been found to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease and cancer.

The bioactive constituents of Medicago altissima include isoflavones, saponins, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions of reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.

Possible Side Effects of Medicago altissima

Furthermore, excessive consumption of the plant's extracts can interact with certain medications, exacerbating health conditions and requiring medical attention.
