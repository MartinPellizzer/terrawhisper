Neolitsea aurata var. glauca

What's the taxonomical classification of Neolitsea aurata var. glauca?

Neolitsea auraata var. glauca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Laurales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Lauraceae, situated within the genus Neolitsea, and is specifically identified by its botanical designation as the species Neolitsea auraata var. glauca.
