What's the taxonomical classification of Holcoglossum kimballianum?

Holcoglossum kimballianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, situated within the genus Holcoglossum, and is specifically identified by the species name kimballianum.
