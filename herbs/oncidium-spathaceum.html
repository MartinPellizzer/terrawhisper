    What is Oncidium spathaceum?

    Oncidium spathaceum is an epiphytic orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

    It has pseudobulbs with 2-4 leathery, elliptical leaves and a single, terminal, racemose inflorescence bearing up to 10 small, yellowish-green flowers with a reddish-purple lip. The flowers have a short, peduncle and a cylindrical, greenish-yellow ovary. The plant typically grows 60-100 cm tall and has a spread of 30-60 cm.

    Oncidium spathaceum is native to the tropical forests of Central and South America.
