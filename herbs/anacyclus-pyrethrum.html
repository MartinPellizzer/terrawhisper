Pyrethrum (Anacyclus pyrethrum)

Pyrethrum (Anacyclus pyrethrum) is a member of the Compositae family, native to Southwest Asia, North Africa, and East Africa. Traditionally, its flowers, roots, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
