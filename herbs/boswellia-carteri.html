Olibanum (Boswellia carteri)

Olibanum (Boswellia carteri) is a member of the Burseraceae family, native to Eastern Africa, Sahelian Africa, and Arabian Peninsula. Traditionally, its resin, bark, and oleoresin have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, african traditional medicine, and european herbal medicine.
