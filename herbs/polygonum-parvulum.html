    What is Polygonum parvulum?

    Polygonum parvulum is an annual or perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Polygonaceae.

    It has alternate, simple leaves that are 1-5 cm long, lanceolate to linear in shape, and have entire margins. The plant produces small, greenish or purple flowers in axillary clusters, with the flowers being 3-5 mm long and having 5-8 stamens. The flowers are dioecious, with male and female flowers being found on separate plants.

    The plant grows 10-50 cm tall and has a rhizomatous habit.
