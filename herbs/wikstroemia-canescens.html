What's the taxonomical classification of Wikstroemia canescens?

Wikstroemia canescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Malvales. This plant is a member of the family Thymelaeaceae, specifically falling under the genus Wikstroemia, with its unique identity defined by the species name canescens.
