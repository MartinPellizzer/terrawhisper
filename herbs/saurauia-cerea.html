What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Saurauia cerea has large, ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins, woody stems, and small, actinomorphic flowers arranged in panicle inflorescences. Its woody architecture allows the plant to develop into a substantial shrub or small tree within its tropical habitat. The flowers are typically white or cream-colored and emerge from axillary positions along the branches.
