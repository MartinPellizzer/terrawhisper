Possible Side Effects of musa acuminata

Additionally, Musa acuminata can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse effects like increased bleeding risk and hypoglycemia.

Improper use of the herb may also cause kidney damage and liver toxicity due to its high levels of oxalates and other compounds.
