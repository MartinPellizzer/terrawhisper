Guaiacum Sanctum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Guaiacum sanctum, commonly known as the sacred wood or Guaiacum, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is characterized by its rich composition of bioactive constituents such as lignans, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Health benefits of Guaiacum sanctum include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which support immune function and skin health.

Therapeutically, it is used to treat conditions like arthritis, digestive disorders, and skin infections, making it a valuable natural remedy in traditional medicine.
