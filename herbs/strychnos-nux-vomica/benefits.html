10 Best Strychnos Nux Vomica Health Benefits

Strychnos nux vomica, a plant native to tropical regions, contains potent alkaloids such as strychnine and brucine, which are known for their neurotoxic properties and are generally considered highly poisonous.

Despite its toxic nature, historical and traditional use has occasionally explored its potential in medicinal contexts, though such applications are not supported by modern scientific evidence. The plant's compounds have been studied for their possible effects on the nervous system, but due to their extreme toxicity, they are not used in conventional medicine. Any use of S. nux vomica is extremely risky and can lead to severe poisoning, seizures, and even death.

As a result, it is strictly prohibited in most countries and should never be used without professional medical supervision.
