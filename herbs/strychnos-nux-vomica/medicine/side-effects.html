10 Possible Side Effects Of Strychnos Nux-Vomica (Nux Vomica)

Strychnos nux-vomica has some side effects when used improperly, such as muscle convulsions, confusion, and coma.

These side effects can occur if the herb is ingested in large quantities or taken without medical supervision. The risk of side effects is increased in people with pre-existing medical conditions, such as epilepsy or kidney disease.

For example, a person with epilepsy may experience increased seizure frequency or severity, while someone with kidney disease may experience further kidney damage or failure.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Strychnos nux-vomica if used imporperly.
