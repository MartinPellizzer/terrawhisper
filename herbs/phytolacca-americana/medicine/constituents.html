Phytolacca americana has active constituents such as phytolaccatoxin, phytolaccigenin, and phytolaccatine.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and anti-tumor properties. The medicinal properties of pokeweed constituents can help alleviate rheumatism, fever, and skin conditions.

For example, phytolaccatoxin has been shown to inhibit tumor growth and reduce inflammation, while phytolaccigenin has antimicrobial effects, making pokeweed a valuable plant for improving overall health and reducing the risk of various diseases.
