Chrysosplenium macrophyllum

What's the taxonomical classification of Chrysosplenium macrophyllum?

Chrysospeltiun macrophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized within the order Saxifragales. This plant is a representative of the family Saxifragaceae, which is the specific group to which its genus, Chrysospeltiun, is assigned. Finally, its unique biological identity is completed by its specific epithet, making the full taxonomic designation Chrysospeltiun macrophyllum.
