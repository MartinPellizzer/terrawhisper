What's the taxonomical classification of Lycopodiastrum casuarinoides?

Lycopodiastrum casuarinoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Lycopodiidae. The plant is further organized into the order Lycopodiales and falls under the family Lycopodiaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Lycopodiastrum and the species casuarinoides.
