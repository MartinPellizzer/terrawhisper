What's the taxonomical classification of Lappula redowskii?

Lappula redowskii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Boraginales. This specific plant is a representative of the family Boraginaceae, situated within the genus Lappula, and is identified by its unique species designation, redowskii.
