Parkia filicoidea

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Parkia filicoidea has bipinnate leaves with numerous small, oblong leaflets and woody stems that support a spreading canopy. Its flowers are typically small and clustered into dense, globose heads that often possess long, protruding stamens. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain the seeds within a protective fibrous structure.
