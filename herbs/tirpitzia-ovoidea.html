Tirpitzia ovoidea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Linaceae, specifically within the genus Tirpitzia, which culminates in its unique species designation, Tirpitzia ovoidea.
