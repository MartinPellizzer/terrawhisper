What's the taxonomical classification of Getonia floribunda?

Getonia floribunda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Myrtales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Combretaceae, within the genus Getonia, and is specifically identified as the species floribunda.
