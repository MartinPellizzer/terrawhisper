Rosa gallica f. delanata

Rosa gallica f. delanata, also known as the French rose or Rosa gallica var. officinalis, is a fragrant, fragrant and medicinal rose plant native to Europe and Western Asia.

The plant's flowers and leaves have been used in herbal medicine for centuries due to their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help with wound healing and skin conditions such as acne and eczema.

The health benefits of Rosa gallica f. delanata include reducing inflammation, promoting relaxation, and treating respiratory issues like colds and coughs.

Rosa gallica f. delanata can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures for medicinal purposes, as well as being used in skincare products and perfumes.
