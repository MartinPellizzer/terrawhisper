10 Health Benefits Of Rhus Glabra (Smooth Sumac)

Rhus glabra has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.

Its medicinal properties include anthraquinones, flavonoids, and terpenes, which have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and vasodilatory effects. This can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve blood circulation, and lower cholesterol levels.

For example, smooth sumac tea has been traditionally used to treat digestive issues and respiratory problems, while its extracts are used in skincare products to soothe skin irritations and reduce fine lines and wrinkles.
