        What side-effects this plant can have?

        Dendropanax proteus can cause severe gastrointestinal distress, including acute nausea, abdominal cramping, and violent vomiting upon ingestion. In extreme cases, individuals might experience neurological disturbances such as tremors or blurred vision. Medical intervention is typically required to manage these systemic reactions.
