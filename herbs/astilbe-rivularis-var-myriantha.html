Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha?

Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and is part of the order Saxifragales. It is further categorized into the family Saxifragaceae and belongs to the genus Astilbe, ultimately identified by its specific species designation.
