What's the taxonomical classification of Gentiana waltonii?

Gentiana waltonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls within the order Gentianales. As a member of the family Gentianaceae, it is placed in the genus Gentiana, with its specific identification being the species waltonii.
