Althaea acerifolia

Althaea acerifolia, also known as the maple leaf marshmallow, is a perennial plant species in the mallow family that is native to Europe and parts of Asia.

This plant contains mucilages, which are complex carbohydrates that have been used for their medicinal properties, including soothing and protecting mucous membranes.

The health benefits of Althaea acerifolia include its ability to reduce inflammation, calm digestive issues, and provide relief from coughs and sore throats.

Althaea acerifolia can be used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and topical creams, making it a versatile and valuable plant in traditional medicine.
