Caper (Capparis spinosa)

Caper (Capparis spinosa) is a member of the Capparaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Middle East, and North Africa. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
