Capparis Spinosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Capparis spinosa, commonly known as the common caper, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its small, edible buds and berries.

This herb is valued for its unique tart flavor and numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and digestive properties.

The therapeutic actions of Capparis spinosa are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from its buds and berries to harness its medicinal properties.
