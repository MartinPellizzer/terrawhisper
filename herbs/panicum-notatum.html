What's the taxonomical classification of Panicum notatum?

Panicum notatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Poales. This plant is a prominent representative of the family Poaceae, falling under the genus Panicum, and is specifically identified by the species name notatum.
