What's the taxonomical classification of Delphinium ceratophorum?

Delphinium ceratophorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further descends through the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Delphinium and the species ceratophorum.
