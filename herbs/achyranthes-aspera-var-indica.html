Achyranthes aspera var. indica

What's the taxonomical classification of Achyranthes aspera var. indica?

Achyranthes aspera var. indica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Caryophyllales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Amaranthaceae and is situated within the genus Achyranthus, specifically identified by the botanical name Achyranthus asperus var. indica.
