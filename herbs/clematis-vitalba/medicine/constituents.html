10 Medicinal Constituents Of Clematis Vitalba (Traveller'S Joy)

Clematis vitalba has active constituents such as lignans, flavonoids, and alkaloids.

These constituents are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help reduce inflammation and protect against cell damage. For example, the flavonoids in Clematis vitalba have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and preventing platelet aggregation.

This can lead to a reduced risk of heart disease and stroke.

Clematis vitalba flavonoids is a type of compound that has been isolated from its leaves and stems.

Clematis vitalba phenolic acids is a type of bioactive compound found in the plant.
