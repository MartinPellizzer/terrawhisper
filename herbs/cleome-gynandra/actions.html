Cleome Shrub (Cleome gynandra) Actions

Cleome gynandra exhibits several primary therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and antispasmodic properties. It acts as a tonic and stimulant, aiding digestion and energy levels. The plant also functions as a carminative, expectorant, and astringent, helping to relieve bloating and respiratory congestion. Its bitter nature enhances digestion and supports overall health.

Cleome Shrub (Cleome gynandra) Monograph

1. Anti-inflammatory

Cleome shrub has an anti-inflammatory action, which helps reduce swelling and redness caused by inflammation. Its active compounds inhibit inflammatory pathways, making it useful for conditions like arthritis and skin irritations. This effect supports its traditional use in treating inflammatory disorders.

2. Diuretic

Cleome shrub has a diuretic action, promoting the elimination of excess fluids from the body. This helps reduce swelling and supports kidney function. It is commonly used in herbal medicine to manage conditions like edema and urinary tract issues. Its natural diuretic properties make it a valuable component in traditional remedies.

3. Bitter

Cleome shrub has a bitter action, which stimulates digestive secretions and enhances nutrient absorption. This property helps to clear mucus and promote healthy digestion. It is particularly useful in cases of indigestion and respiratory congestion. The bitter taste also supports liver function and detoxification.

4. Tonic

Cleome shrub has a tonic action, stimulating the body's natural healing processes and enhancing overall vitality. It supports energy levels and strengthens the immune system, making it beneficial for individuals experiencing fatigue or weakness. Its tonic properties help restore balance and promote general well-being.

5. Carminative

Cleome shrub has a carminative action, helping to relieve gas and bloating in the digestive system. It promotes the expulsion of trapped air, reducing discomfort and cramping. This property makes it useful in treating gastrointestinal issues like indigestion and flatulence. Its mild nature supports gentle digestive support without harsh side effects.

6. Stimulant

Cleome shrub has a stimulant action, increasing energy and alertness by enhancing central nervous system activity. It is used to combat fatigue and improve mental clarity, making it beneficial for those experiencing low vitality or mental exhaustion.

7. Expectorant

Cleome shrub has an expectorant action, aiding in the removal of mucus from the respiratory tract. It stimulates the secretion of bronchial secretions, making it useful for relieving congestion and coughs associated with colds or bronchitis. This property supports respiratory health by promoting clearer airways.

8. Antispasmodic

Cleome shrub has an antispasmodic action, meaning it helps relieve muscle spasms and cramps. It works by relaxing smooth muscle tissues, making it useful for conditions like gastrointestinal spasms or menstrual cramps. Its active compounds reduce excessive nerve stimulation, promoting calmness in overactive muscles.

9. Astringent

Cleome shrub has an astringent action, which helps reduce inflammation and tighten tissues. It is commonly used to treat conditions like diarrhea and skin irritations. This property is due to the presence of tannins in the plant. Its astringent effect promotes healing and can soothe internal and external wounds.

10. Sedative

Cleome shrub has a sedative action, helping to calm the nervous system and reduce anxiety. It promotes relaxation and can aid in managing stress-related conditions. This effect is attributed to its bioactive compounds, which influence neurotransmitter activity. It is often used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.
