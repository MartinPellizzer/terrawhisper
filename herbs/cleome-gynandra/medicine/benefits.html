10 Health Benefits Of Cleome Gynandra (Spider Flower)

Cleome gynandra has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Its leaves and stems are used to treat various ailments, including fever, coughs, and skin infections. The plant's ability to reduce inflammation can alleviate symptoms of arthritis, while its antibacterial properties can help combat urinary tract infections. Furthermore, the spider flower's antifungal properties can soothe fungal infections like athlete's foot.

These benefits can significantly improve people's lives by providing natural relief from common illnesses.
