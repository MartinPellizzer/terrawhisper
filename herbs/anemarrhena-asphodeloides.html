Anemarrhena Lily (Anemarrhena asphodeloides)

Anemarrhena Lily (Anemarrhena asphodeloides) is a member of the Liliaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
