Boswellia Sacra Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Boswellia sacra, commonly known as frankincense, is a resin derived from the bark of the Boswellia sacra tree, native to regions of Africa and the Middle East.

This herb is renowned for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, making it beneficial for conditions such as arthritis, asthma, and digestive disorders.

The therapeutic actions of Boswellia sacra are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including boswellic acids, terpenes, and volatile oils.
