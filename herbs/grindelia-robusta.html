Grindelia robusta leaf is primarily used for its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties, making it valuable in respiratory treatments.

Grindelia robusta teas are commonly prepared by steeping the dried flower heads in hot water, resulting in a mild, slightly bitter beverage that is often consumed for its purported respiratory and anti-inflammatory benefits.
