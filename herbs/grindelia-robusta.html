Possible Side Effects of grindelia robusta

Grindelia robusta can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

Prolonged use without proper guidance may also cause liver toxicity and interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Grindelia robusta for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
