Piptanthus nepalensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Piptanthus nepalensis?

Piptanthus nepalenis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae, which characterizes its botanical relationship to other legumes. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Piptanthus and its unique species designation, nepalenis.
