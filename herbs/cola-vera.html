Coca-Cola Plant (Cola vera)

Coca-Cola Plant (Cola vera) is a member of the Sterculiaceae family, native to West Africa, Central Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and fruits have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
