Cola Vera Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cola vera, commonly known as the "tree of life," is a small evergreen tree native to West Africa, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is renowned for its ability to support cardiovascular health, reduce inflammation, and enhance cognitive function.

The bioactive constituents in Cola vera include alkaloids such as caffeine, theobromine, and theophylline, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from its leaves and seeds to harness its beneficial properties.
