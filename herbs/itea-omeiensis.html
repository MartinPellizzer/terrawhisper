What's the taxonomical classification of Itea omeiensis?

Itea omeiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Saxifrages, it is assigned to the family Iteaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Itea, with its specific designation being the species omeiensis.
