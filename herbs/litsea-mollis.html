What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Litsea mollis has simple, alternate, elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are characterized by a soft, pubescent texture on their surfaces. Small, inconspicuous flowers often appear in axillary clusters, eventually leading to the development of small drupe-like fruits. These morphological features collectively define the species within the Lauraceae family.
