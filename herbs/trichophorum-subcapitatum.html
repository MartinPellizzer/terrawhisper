Trichophorum subcapitatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Trichophorum subcapitatum?

Trichophorum subcapitatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the hierarchical lineage of the order Poales. It is further categorized under the family Cyperaceae, which contains the genus Trichophorum, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as subcapitatum.
