What is Gaultheria othmanii?

Gaultheria othmanii is an evergreen shrub that belongs to the family Ericaceae, native to the Himalayas.

It grows up to 1-2 meters in height, with a woody base and a slender stem covered with scale-like leaves that are 1-5 cm long and 0.5-2 cm wide, and arranged oppositely. The leaves are dark green on top and pale green on the underside, and have a leathery texture. Gaultheria othmanii produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters, which are followed by red or purple berries that are 5-7 mm in diameter.

The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments at high elevations.
