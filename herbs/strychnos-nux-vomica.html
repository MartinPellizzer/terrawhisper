Nux Vomica (Strychnos nux-vomica)

Nux Vomica (Strychnos nux-vomica) is a member of the Loganiaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, South China, and Eastern Himalayas. Traditionally, its seeds, bark, and leaves have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, stimulant, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
