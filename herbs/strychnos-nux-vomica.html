Strychnos Nux-Vomica Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Strychnos nux-vomica, commonly known as the Indian strychnos or devil's berry, is a plant native to Asia and Africa, known for its toxic seeds containing potent alkaloids.

Despite its toxicity, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits, including stimulating the nervous system and treating respiratory and digestive ailments.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include strychnine, brucine, and nux-vomica alkaloids, which act on the central nervous system.

Herbal preparations such as tinctures, powders, and infusions can be made, though caution is required due to its toxic nature.
