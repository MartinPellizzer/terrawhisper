What is Denseflower Lousewort (Pedicularis Densiflora)?

Denseflower Lousewort (Pedicularis densiflora) is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China, characterized by its dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers and its use in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported ability to clear heat, detoxify the body, and alleviate symptoms of inflammation and fever.

Native Habitat and Distribution
