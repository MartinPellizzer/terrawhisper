Bupleurum Chinense

Bupleurum chinense, commonly known as Chaihu in traditional Chinese medicine, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

This herb is valued for its health benefits, which include reducing inflammation, improving immune function, and exhibiting antimicrobial and antiviral properties.

The therapeutic actions of Bupleurum chinense include expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and immunomodulatory effects, making it useful in treating respiratory and gastrointestinal disorders.

Its bioactive constituents include alkaloids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, such as saikosaponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
