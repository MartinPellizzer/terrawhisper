Bacopa lanigera var. serpyllifolia

Bacopa lanigera var. serpyllifolia, also known as Bacopa serpyllifolia, is a variant of the Bacopa monnieri plant, a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective effects.

The health benefits of Bacopa lanigera var. serpyllifolia include improved cognitive function, enhanced memory, and reduced anxiety and stress levels.

Bacopa lanigera var. serpyllifolia can be prepared as an herbal supplement, tea, or tincture, often standardized to contain a specific percentage of bacosides, its active compounds.
