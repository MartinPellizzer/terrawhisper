What's the taxonomical classification of Pleurospermum lindleyanum?

Pleurospermum lindleyanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Pleurospermum, and is identified by the species name lindleyanum.
