What to know about Prunus mume (chinese plum) before using it medicinally

Chinese plum, scientifically known as Prunus mume, is a highly prized herb that has been revered for its numerous health benefits.

Rich in antioxidants and flavonoids, Chinese plum has been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties, improve cardiovascular health, and even aid in the prevention of certain cancers. In addition to its impressive health benefits, Chinese plum is also a staple ingredient in many Asian cuisines, often used to add flavor and aroma to stir-fries, sauces, and marinades. As an ornamental plant, Chinese plum is prized for its showy white or pink flowers and fragrant fruit, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

With roots dating back thousands of years to ancient Chinese medicine and Japanese cuisine, the Chinese plum has been a beloved and respected ingredient in many cultures throughout history.
