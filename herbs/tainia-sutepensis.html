Possible Side Effects of Tainia sutepensis

Tainia sutepensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, dizziness, and headaches due to its high saponin content.

Overconsumption of the plant may also lead to vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach cramps in some individuals.

In severe cases, excessive use of Tainia sutepensis can cause kidney damage and interact negatively with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Furthermore, pregnant and breastfeeding women should avoid using the plant altogether due to its potential toxicity and risk of birth defects or adverse effects on the infant.
