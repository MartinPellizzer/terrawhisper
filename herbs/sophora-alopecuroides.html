Its stems are woody and can develop a somewhat scrambling or climbing habit depending on the environment. The plant produces small, elongated legume pods that contain the seeds. Contact with the plant's surfaces can also trigger localized allergic reactions or dermatitis in sensitive individuals. In more serious cases of ingestion, the chemical compounds might interfere with neurological functions.
