What's the taxonomical classification of Cotoneaster frigidus?

Cotoneaster frigidus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its lineage through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed under the order Rosales. The plant is a member of the family Rosaceae, which is a diverse group of flowering plants, and is specifically categorized under the genus Cotoneaster. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species frigidus.
