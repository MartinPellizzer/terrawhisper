Crepis nana var. lyratifolia

Crepis nana var. lyratifolia, also known as Dwarf Hawksbeard, is a small, biennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant's medicinal properties are attributed to its roots and leaves, which contain compounds with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The health benefits of Crepis nana var. lyratifolia include its potential to support digestion, reduce inflammation, and aid in the treatment of various skin conditions.

Traditionally, the plant is prepared as a tincture, infusion, or decoction, often combined with other herbs, to create a holistic remedy for various health issues.
