10 Possible Side Effects Of Peucedanum Officinale (Cow Parsley)

Peucedanum officinale has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to its high concentration of furanocoumarins, which can cause stomach upset, diarrhea, and nausea.

In severe cases, these symptoms can worsen a person's quality of life, leading to weight loss, fatigue, and decreased productivity.
