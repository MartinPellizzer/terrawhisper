Ephedra likiangensis f. mairei

What's the taxonomical classification of Ephedra likiangensis f. mairei?

Ephedra likiangensis f. mairei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the order Ephedrales and the family Ephedraceae. This specific plant is a member of the genus Ephedra, where it is identified by its formal species designation, Ephedra likiangensis f. mairei.
