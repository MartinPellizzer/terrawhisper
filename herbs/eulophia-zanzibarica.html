What is Eulophia zanzibarica?

Eulophia zanzibarica is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, bearing pseudobulbs that are 10-20 cm long and 3-5 cm in diameter. The leaves are linear, 2-4 cm long and 0.5-1.5 cm wide, and are arranged in a basal rosette. The inflorescence is a raceme, 30-60 cm long, bearing 5-15 flowers that are 4-6 cm long and 2-3 cm wide, with a yellow-green labellum and a reddish-brown lip.

The flowers have a sweet, slightly spicy fragrance and are pollinated by bees and other insects.
