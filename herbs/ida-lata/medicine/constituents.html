10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ida Lata (Wild Ginger)

Ida lata has active constituents such as sesquiterpenes, phenolic acids, and flavonoids.

These compounds confer medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The sesquiterpenes may help alleviate respiratory issues, while the phenolic acids can aid in digestion and reduce inflammation. Additionally, the flavonoids exhibit antiviral and antibacterial effects, which could potentially treat infections and diseases.

By incorporating Ida lata into traditional medicine or modern supplements, individuals can potentially alleviate symptoms and improve overall well-being.
