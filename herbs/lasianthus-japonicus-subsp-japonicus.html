Lasianthus japonicus subsp. japonicus

What's the taxonomical classification of Lasianthus japonicus subsp. japonicus?

Lasianthus japonicus subsp. japonicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Lasianthus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the subspecies Lasianthus japonicus subsp. japonicus.
