Actinidia latifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia latifolia?

Actinidia latifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is part of the order Ericales and falls under the family Actinidiaceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Actinidia, specifically identified as the species latifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Actinidia latifolia has large, broadly ovate to elliptic leaves with obtuse or rounded apices and entire or slightly wavy margins. The plant exhibits a woody, climbing habit with slender stems that utilize tendrils for support. Its reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into fleshy, berry-like fruits.
