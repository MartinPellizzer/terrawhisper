What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Aleuritopteris rufa has pinnate fronds characterized by reddish-brown scales on the stipe and rachis, with pinnules that are oblong, slightly serrated, and possess distinctively colored undersides. The stipe is typically robust and covered in dense, ferruginous hairs that provide a rusty appearance. Each leaf structure consists of a central axis from which numerous smaller leaflets emerge in a regular pattern. The underside of the foliage features prominent sori located near the margins, which are often protected by a thin indusium.
