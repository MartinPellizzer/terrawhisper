Olgaea lomonossowii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its evolutionary lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Asterales, this plant is situated in the family Asteraceae, a group known for its diverse floral structures. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Olgaea, with its unique identity defined by the species epithet lomonossowii.
