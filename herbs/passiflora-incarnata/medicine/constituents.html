10 Medicinal Constituents Of Passiflora Incarnata (Maypop)

Passiflora incarnata has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids.

These compounds possess medicinal properties that have been traditionally used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness. The flavonoids, specifically kaempferol and quercetin, exhibit anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, while the terpenoids contribute to its sedative and anxiolytic properties.

As a result, maypop has been used to soothe nerves, calm the mind, and promote relaxation, ultimately improving sleep quality and reducing stress levels.

Passiflora incarnata phenolic acids is a group of bioactive compounds found in its fruit and leaves.
