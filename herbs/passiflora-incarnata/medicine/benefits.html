By increasing GABA activity, maypop can help decrease excessive neuronal excitation, promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety symptoms. Additionally, maypop has been shown to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which may contribute to its anxiolytic effects.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the mechanisms behind maypop's anxiety-reducing properties and to establish optimal dosages for therapeutic use.

Maypop lowers blood pressure because it contains several bioactive compounds, such as flavonoids and alkaloids, that have been shown to relax blood vessels and improve circulation.
