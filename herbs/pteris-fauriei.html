What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Pteris fauriei has a creeping rhizome that produces pinnate fronds characterized by narrow, oblong to lanceolate pinnae with entire or slightly wavy margins. Each leaf segment is arranged symmetrically along the central rachis, contributing to a delicate, airy appearance. The underside of the fronds features small, discrete sori located near the margins, which are a defining feature of this fern species.
