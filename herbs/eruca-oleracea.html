What is Eruca oleracea?

Eruca oleracea is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It has a taproot, erect stem, and simple or lobed, serrated leaves that are 2-10 cm long and 1-4 cm wide, with a petiole 1-10 cm long. The leaves are glabrous or sparsely hairy, with a distinctive pungent aroma and a peppery, slightly bitter taste. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing white or pale purple flowers with 4 petals, 1-1.5 cm in diameter, and 4 stamens.

The fruit is a silique, 2-6 cm long and 0.5-1 cm wide, containing 6-12 seeds.
