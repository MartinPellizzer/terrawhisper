Lepyrodiclis holosteoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the lineage, it is situated in the order Caryophyllales and the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Lepyrodiclis, with its specific designation being the species holosteoides.
