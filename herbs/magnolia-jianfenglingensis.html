What is Magnolia jianfenglingensis?

Magnolia jianfenglingensis is an evergreen tree species endemic to Yunnan Province in China, belonging to the family Magnoliaceae.

It grows up to 10 meters tall with an elliptical or ovate leaf shape, measuring 8-15 cm long and 4-7 cm wide, having 5-7 pairs of lateral veins and a cuneate base. The leaves are glabrous, dark green, and shiny on the upper surface, with a glabrous or sparsely pubescent lower surface. The flowers are white, 10-15 cm in diameter, and consist of 6-8 petals, 6-8 stamens, and numerous staminodes.

The fruit is a red, globose capsule, 5-7 cm in diameter.
