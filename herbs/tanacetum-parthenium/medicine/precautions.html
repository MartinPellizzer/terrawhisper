10 Precautions To Take When Using Tanacetum Parthenium (Feverfew)

Tanacetum parthenium has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional about potential interactions with blood thinners and other medications.

Taking feverfew without caution can lead to excessive bleeding, bruising, and strokes due to its effect on platelet aggregation.

Not considering these interactions may result in serious health consequences, making it crucial to prioritize caution when using this herb for medicinal purposes.
