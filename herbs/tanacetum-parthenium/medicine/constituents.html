10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tanacetum Parthenium (Feverfew)

Tanacetum parthenium has active constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and caffeic acid derivatives.

These constituents confer anti-inflammatory, anti-migraine, and antioxidant properties, which are beneficial in reducing pain and inflammation, as well as preventing and alleviating symptoms of migraines and other headaches.

For example, patients with frequent migraines have reported reduced frequency and severity of episodes after consuming feverfew tea or capsules, allowing them to enjoy improved quality of life and reduced reliance on medication.
