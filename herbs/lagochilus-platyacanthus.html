What's the taxonomical classification of Lagochilus platyacanthus?

Lagochilus platyacanthus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Lagochilus. Finally, the plant is identified at the species level as platyacanthus.
