10 Health Benefits Of Anamirta Cocculus (Cowhage)

Anamirta cocculus has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain relief.

Its medicinal properties include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which exhibit anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. Consuming cowhage can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, fibromyalgia, and menstrual cramps, improving daily quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic pain.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties may also contribute to its ability to combat cancer cells and promote overall well-being.
