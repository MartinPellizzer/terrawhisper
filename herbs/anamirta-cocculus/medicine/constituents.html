10 Medicinal Constituents Of Anamirta Cocculus (Cowhage)

Anamirta cocculus has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antispasmodic effects. The flavonoids help to reduce pain and inflammation, while the alkaloids exhibit analgesic and antispasmodic activity, making it effective in treating menstrual cramps and other related disorders.

As a result, people with menstrual cramps can experience relief from severe pain, improving their quality of life.
