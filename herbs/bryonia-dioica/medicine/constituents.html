10 Medicinal Constituents Of Bryonia Dioica (Wild Hops)

Bryonia dioica has active constituents such as bitter compounds like lupulone and isohumulone, flavonoids, and saponins.

These medicinal properties make it effective in alleviating digestive issues, such as dyspepsia and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), by reducing inflammation and relaxing the muscles in the gastrointestinal tract.

The constituents also possess antibacterial and antifungal properties, which can help combat infections and promote wound healing, improving overall health and well-being.
