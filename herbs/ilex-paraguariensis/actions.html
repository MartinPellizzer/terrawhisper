Mate (Ilex paraguariensis) Actions

Mate exhibits several therapeutic actions including stimulant, tonic, and anti-inflammatory properties. It acts as a mild stimulant to enhance alertness, serves as a tonic to strengthen the body, and has anti-inflammatory effects that reduce swelling and irritation. Additionally, it possesses antispasmodic and carminative qualities to ease digestive discomfort.
