10 Health Benefits Of Acmella Oleracea (Toothache Plant)

Acmella oleracea has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain relief.

Its medicinal properties include its ability to numb the affected area due to the presence of sesquiterpenes, a type of compound that interacts with nerve endings. This makes it an effective remedy for toothaches, headaches, and joint pain.

For example, chewing on the leaves or applying a poultice can provide quick relief from toothache discomfort, allowing individuals to resume their daily activities without distress.
