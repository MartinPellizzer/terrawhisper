Possible Side Effects of Buprestis gigantea

Buprestis gigantea can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems.

If not used correctly, buprestis gigantea may interact with certain medications and exacerbate pre-existing health conditions.
