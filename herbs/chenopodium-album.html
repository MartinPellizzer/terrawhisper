As an annual plant, lamb's quarters is easy to grow and can thrive in a variety of environments, making it a popular choice for home gardens and commercial agriculture.

Botanically speaking, lamb's quarters belongs to the amaranth family and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its high concentration of vitamins A, C, and K. With its ancient history dating back to Roman and Greek times, lamb's quarters has been revered for its medicinal properties and versatility in cooking, solidifying its place as a valuable herb in many cultures around the world.
