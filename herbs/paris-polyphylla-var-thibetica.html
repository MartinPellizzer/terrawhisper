Paris polyphylla var. thibetica

What's the taxonomical classification of Paris polyphylla var. thibetica?

Paris polyphylla var. thibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Liliales and placed in the family Melanthiaceae. This specific variety is a member of the genus Paris, ultimately falling under the taxonomic designation of the species Paris polyphylla var. thibetica.
