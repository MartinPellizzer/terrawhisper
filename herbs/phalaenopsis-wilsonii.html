What's the taxonomical classification of Phalaenopsis wilsonii?

Phalaenopsis wilsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. More specifically, it is identified by the genus Phalaenopsis and is designated as the species wilsonii.
