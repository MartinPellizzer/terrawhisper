Huperzia foliosa

Huperzia foliosa, commonly known as the Chinese club moss or tiger tail fern, is a species of plant in the family Huperziaceae.

It has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits, which include its ability to enhance cognitive function, boost memory, and reduce inflammation.

The therapeutic actions of Huperzia foliosa include acting as an antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective agent.

The bioactive constituents of the plant include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, which is responsible for breaking down acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter involved in memory and learning.
