Impatiens lasiophyton has hairy, ovate leaves and succulent, translucent stems that grow in a spreading or upright habit. Small, delicate flowers emerge from the leaf axils, typically displaying pale colors with subtle nectar guides. The plant's stems are relatively soft and moisture-rich, a common trait among many species in its genus.
