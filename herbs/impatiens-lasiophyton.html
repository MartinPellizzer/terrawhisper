What's the taxonomical classification of Impatiens lasiophyton?

Impatiens lasiophyton belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is positioned in the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Balsaminaceae, falling under the genus Impatiens, and is specifically identified by the species name lasiophyton.
