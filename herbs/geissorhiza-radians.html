What is Geissorhiza radians?

Geissorhiza radians is a species of flowering plant in the family Iridaceae.

It is a cormous perennial herb with linear, channeled leaves and a slender, simple stem that reaches up to 20 cm in height. The leaves are usually 2-5 mm wide and 5-15 cm long, while the inflorescence is a spike bearing 1-5 star-shaped flowers. Each flower has three outer and three inner tepals, which are yellowish-green in color with darker green midribs, and measures up to 1.5 cm in diameter.

The plant is native to South Africa and blooms from August to November.
