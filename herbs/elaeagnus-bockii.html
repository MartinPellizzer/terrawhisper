What's the taxonomical classification of Elaeagnus bockii?

Elaeagnus bockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Elaeagnaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic position is defined by the genus Elaeagnus and the species bockii.
