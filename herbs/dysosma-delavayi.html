Dysosmoma delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Berberidaceae. Specifically, it is identified by its genus, Dysosmoma, and its specific epithet, delaivayi.
