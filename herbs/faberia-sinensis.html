Faberia sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Faberia, and its unique species designation, sinensis.
