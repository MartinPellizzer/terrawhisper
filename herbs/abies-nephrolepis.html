What's the taxonomical classification of Abies nephrolepis?

Abies nephrolepis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Pinidae, placing it in the order Pinales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Pinaceae and falls under the genus Abies, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species nephrolepis.
