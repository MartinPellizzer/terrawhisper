What is Odina welwitschii?

Odina welwitschii is a plant species belonging to the family Anacardiaceae.

It is a deciduous tree that grows up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, measuring 10-25 centimeters long and 5-10 centimeters wide, with a cuneate base and a rounded apex. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in axillary panicles, while the fruit is a drupe, 1-2 centimeters in diameter, with a smooth, yellowish-green skin and a single seed.

The bark is grayish-brown, with vertical, longitudinal fissures, and the wood is hard and reddish-brown.
