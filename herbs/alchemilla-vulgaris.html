Possible Side Effects of alchemilla vulgaris

Alchemilla vulgaris can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal issues.

Excessive consumption of the herb may lead to dizziness, nausea, and stomach pain.

In rare cases, it can interact with certain medications and exacerbate conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, and kidney problems.

Moreover, improper preparation or dosage of the herb can result in an increased risk of bleeding, particularly in individuals with bleeding disorders or those taking anticoagulant medications.
