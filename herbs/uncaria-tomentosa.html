Possible Side Effects of uncaria tomentosa

Uncaria tomentosa can have side effects if used improperly, such as excessive bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties, which can increase the risk of bleeding disorders and bruising.

Additionally, high doses may cause stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea in some individuals.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to kidney damage or interact with other medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse effects.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Uncaria tomentosa to avoid these potential side effects.
