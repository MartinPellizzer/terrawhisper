Uncaria Tomentosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Uncaria tomentosa, commonly known as the "dragon's blood tree," is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is renowned for its rich content of bioactive compounds such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The herb is believed to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote overall wellness due to its antioxidant and antimicrobial effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it a versatile option for herbal remedies.
