Guangzhou Ophiopogon (Ophiopogon Guangzhouensis)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Ophiopogon guangzhouensis.

Guangzhou ophiopogon, scientifically known as Ophiopogon guangzhouensis, is a rare medicinal herb native to southern China, particularly Guangdong Province. It is valued for its adaptogenic properties and is traditionally used to nourish the lungs, moisten dryness, and enhance vitality. The primary benefits of this plant include improving respiratory health, reducing inflammation, and supporting overall energy levels, making it a key ingredient in traditional Chinese medicine. Historically, it has been used in herbal formulations to treat conditions such as dry cough, constipation, and heat-related illnesses, and it holds cultural significance in Chinese wellness practices. Modern wellness applications include its use in supplements and teas aimed at boosting immunity and promoting internal balance, with its unique, slightly sweet and cooling flavor attributed to its rare active compounds like saponins and polysaccharides.
