Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) Compounds

Sacred Lotus contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which act as antioxidants. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent properties. Saponins and glycosides may support immune function. Anthocyanins provide color and antioxidant benefits, while coumarin, terpenoids, and essential oils contribute to its aromatic and medicinal profile. Alkaloids add to its diverse pharmacological potential.

Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids are a class of bioactive compounds found in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera). They contribute to the plant's medicinal properties by exhibiting antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective effects. These compounds support the traditional use of Sacred Lotus in treating conditions like inflammation and cognitive decline.

2. Tannin

Tannin, found in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera), is a complex polyphenolic compound known for its astringent properties. It contributes to the plant's traditional use in treating inflammation and digestive issues. Tannins also act as antioxidants and may support wound healing in herbal medicine.

3. Phenolic acid

Phenolic acid is a key active compound found in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera). It contributes to the plant's antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, supporting its traditional use in herbal medicine for promoting health and treating various ailments.

4. Saponin

Saponin is a key active compound in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine due to its ability to reduce inflammation and support detoxification. Saponin is found in various parts of the plant, including the seeds and leaves, and is valued for its potential health benefits.

5. Glycoside

Glycoside is a key active compound in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera). It contributes to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Glycosides are often found in the flowers and seeds, supporting traditional uses in herbal medicine. They play a crucial role in the therapeutic applications of Sacred Lotus.

6. Anthocyanin

Anthocyanin, found in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera), is a powerful antioxidant that contributes to the flower's vibrant color. It supports cardiovascular health, reduces inflammation, and may improve cognitive function, making it a key active compound in traditional herbal medicine.

7. Coumarin

Coumarin is a bioactive compound found in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and anticoagulant properties. Coumarin is also valued for its role in supporting cardiovascular health and its potential therapeutic applications in various medicinal formulations.

8. Terpenoid

Terpenoids in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) contribute to its medicinal properties. These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, supporting the plant's traditional use in treating respiratory and skin conditions. They are also believed to aid in detoxification and immune support.

9. Essential oil

The essential oil of Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) contains bioactive components like aromatic compounds and flavonoids. It is used in traditional medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties, supporting respiratory and digestive health. The oil is often extracted through steam distillation from the flowers and leaves.

10. Alkaloid

Alkaloid compounds in Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) have been studied for their potential medicinal properties. These alkaloids may contribute to the plant's traditional use in treating inflammation and neurological conditions. Their presence supports the plant's role in herbal medicine. Further research is needed to fully understand their therapeutic effects.
