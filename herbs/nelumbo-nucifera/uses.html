Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) Uses

Sacred Lotus is used to treat respiratory infections, cough, asthma, and bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It also helps with skin inflammation, infections, and irritation, supporting wound healing and skin health. Its compounds reduce inflammation and promote tissue repair in various conditions.

Sacred Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) Monograph

1. Respiratory infection

Sacred Lotus is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion. The leaves and flowers are commonly prepared as decoctions or extracts. It is valued for its ability to support lung health and clear mucus.

2. Inflammatory conditions

Sacred Lotus is used to treat inflammatory conditions due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and pain associated with arthritis and other inflammatory disorders. The plant's compounds inhibit inflammatory pathways, supporting overall joint and tissue health. Its traditional use aligns with modern research on natural anti-inflammatory agents.
