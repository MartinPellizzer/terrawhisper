Plantago psyllium var. sicula

Plantago psyllium var. sicula, also known as Sicilian psyllium, is a type of flowering plant in the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) that is native to the Mediterranean region.

It has been used for centuries for its medicinal properties, including its ability to stimulate the digestive system, lower cholesterol levels, and help regulate bowel movements due to its high fiber content.
