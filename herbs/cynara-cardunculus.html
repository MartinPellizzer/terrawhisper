Cardoon (Cynara cardunculus)

Cardoon (Cynara cardunculus) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and Middle East. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and stems have been used for culinary uses, infusions, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
