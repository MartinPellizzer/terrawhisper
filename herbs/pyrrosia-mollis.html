Pyrrosia mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and further categorized into the subclass Polypodiinae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant falls under the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae. Finally, its specific identity is defined by its placement within the genus Pyrrosia, resulting in its scientific name, Pyrrosia mollis.
