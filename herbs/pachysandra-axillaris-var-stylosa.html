Pachysandra axillaris var. stylosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Pachysandra axillaris var. stylosa?

Pachysandra axillaris var. stylosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Buxales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Buxaceae and the genus Pachysandra, ultimately identifying it as the specific variety Pachysandra axiilaaris var. stylosa.
