Triticum scirpeum

Triticum scirpeum, also known as Wild Rye or Prairie Rye, is a perennial grass native to North America and parts of Asia.

It possesses medicinal properties, with its roots and seeds containing bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and alkaloids that exhibit anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.

The health benefits of Triticum scirpeum include its potential to alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and support immune function.

Traditionally, the plant is prepared as a tea or tincture, often used in herbal remedies to promote overall well-being and alleviate various health conditions.
