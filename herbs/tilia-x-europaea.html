What are the side effects of Tilia × europaea when used improperly?

When used improperly, Tilia × europaea increases the chances of experiencing side effects, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal disturbances, and cardiovascular complications.

Ingesting large quantities or taking the plant in combination with other herbal remedies can lead to anaphylaxis, a severe and potentially life-threatening allergic reaction characterized by symptoms like hives, swelling of the face and throat, and difficulty breathing. Furthermore, Tilia × europaea may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, antidiabetic drugs, and sedatives, resulting in adverse reactions like bleeding disorders, hypoglycemia, or increased drowsiness. Additionally, using Tilia × europaea without proper preparation or dosing can cause stomach upset, nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea due to its bitter taste and potential irritation of the digestive tract.

In some cases, improper use may also lead to cardiovascular issues, including changes in heart rate, blood pressure fluctuations, or even cardiac arrhythmias.

What are the planting tips of Tilia × europaea?

Gently remove the tree from its container and place it in the hole, backfilling with soil and firming it gently around the roots. Water thoroughly to settle the soil, and mulch around the base of the tree to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
