What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma henryi?

Tetrastigma henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It further falls under the family Vitaceae, within the genus Tetrastigma, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species henryi.
