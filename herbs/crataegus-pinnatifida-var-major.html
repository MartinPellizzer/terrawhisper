Crataegus pinnatifida var. major

What's the taxonomical classification of Crataegus pinnatifida var. major?

Crataegus pinnatifida var. major belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Within this order, it is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Crataegus, and is specifically designated by its species and variety, Crataegus pinnatifida var. major.
