Possible Side Effects of Tamuria deltoidea

Tamuria deltoidea can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and inflammation.

Additionally, improper use of Tamuria deltoidea can trigger anaphylaxis, a severe and potentially life-threatening allergic reaction, requiring immediate medical attention.
