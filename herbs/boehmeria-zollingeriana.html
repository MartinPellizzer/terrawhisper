Boehmeria zolliingeriana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Boehmeria zolliingeriana has an erect, perennial growth habit characterized by opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins and long, tapering petioles.
