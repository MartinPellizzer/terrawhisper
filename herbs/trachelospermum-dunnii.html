Trachelospermum dunnii

What's the taxonomical classification of Trachelospermum dunnii?

Trachelospermum dunnii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Gentianales. The plant is further categorized into the family Apocynaceae, which contains the genus Trachelospermum, and is specifically identified by its species name, dunnii.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, wounds, stomach aches, and various types of inflammation. The leaves are often prepared into a paste or decoction to soothe irritated or wounded skin surfaces. When ingested, the plant components help alleviate digestive discomfort and gastrointestinal distress. Local healers also rely on its properties to reduce swelling and general bodily aches. These medicinal applications highlight its significance in traditional herbal practices.
