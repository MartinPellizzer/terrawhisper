What's the taxonomical classification of Neillia ribesioides?

Neillia ribesioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Rosales, the plant is a member of the family Rosaceae. It is specifically identified under the genus Neillia, with its unique biological designation being the species ribesioides.
