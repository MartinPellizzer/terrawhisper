Ancylostemon saxatilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Ancylostemon saxatilis?

Ancylostemon saxatilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows the taxonomic lineage of the order Lamiales. It is further categorized under the family Gesneriaceae, falling within the genus Ancylostemon, and is specifically identified by its species name, saxatilis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, emerging from the leaf axils on short pedicels. The plant often forms dense, mat-like cushions that allow it to cling effectively to rocky substrates.
