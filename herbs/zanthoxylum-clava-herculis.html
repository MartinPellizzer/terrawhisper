Spineapple (Zanthoxylum clava-herculis)

Spineapple (Zanthoxylum clava-herculis) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to Eastern North America, Southeastern United States, and Eastern United States. Traditionally, its fruits, bark, and leaves have been used for decoctions, poultices, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, astringent, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
