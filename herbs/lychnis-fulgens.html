Lychnis fulgens

What's the taxonomical classification of Lychnis fulgens?

Lychnis fulgens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically placed within the genus Lychnis, and is identified by the species name fulgens.

It is important to keep this plant away from curious pets and small children to prevent accidental poisoning. Always wash your hands thoroughly after gardening with this species to minimize any risk of sensitivity.
