10 Medicinal Constituents Of Buddleja Asiatica (Chinese Orange)

Buddleja asiatica, also known as Chinese Orange, has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and triterpenoids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate respiratory issues, skin conditions, and digestive problems. The flavonoids can improve cardiovascular health, while the terpenoids may exhibit anti-cancer effects.

By harnessing these medicinal properties, people can experience relief from chronic conditions, leading to enhanced quality of life and overall well-being.
