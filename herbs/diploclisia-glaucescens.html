Diploclisia glaucescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Diploclisia glaucescens?

Diploclisia glaucescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the taxonomic order Ranunculales. This plant is further categorized into the family Menispermaceae, which contains the genus Diploclisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, glaucescens.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Diploclisia glaucescens has woody, climbing stems that produce small, opposite, elliptical leaves with a distinctive waxy, bluish-grey glaucous coating.

Its growth habit is characterized by a scrambling or twining mechanism that allows it to navigate through surrounding vegetation.
