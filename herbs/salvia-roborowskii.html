What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia roborowskii?

Salvia roborowskii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Lamiales, it is further nested within the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Salvia, and its specific species epithet, roborowskii.
