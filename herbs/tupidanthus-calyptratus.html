Tupidanthus calyptratus

What's the taxonomical classification of Tupidanthus calyptratus?

Tupidanthus calyptratus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. The plant is further categorized into the family Araliaceae, which places it within the genus Tupidanthus, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Tupidanthus calyptratus.
