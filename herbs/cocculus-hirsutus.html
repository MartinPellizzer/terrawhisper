Cocculus (Cocculus hirsutus)

Cocculus (Cocculus hirsutus) is a member of the Rhamnaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, Indo-Pacific, and Tropical Asia. Traditionally, its seeds, roots, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its antispasmodic, sedative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, african traditional medicine, and european herbal medicine.
