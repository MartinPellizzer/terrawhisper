Possible Side Effects of raphanus sativus

Raphanus sativus can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including nausea, diarrhea, and stomach cramps.

Improper use of Raphanus sativus can also lead to allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of Raphanus sativus may cause kidney damage or interact with blood thinners, resulting in bleeding complications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Raphanus sativus for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
