What's the taxonomical classification of Oxytropis psammocharis?

Oxytropis psammocharis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is a member of the order Fabales and falls within the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Oxytropis, and its unique species designation, psammocharis.

Constant monitoring of grazing areas is necessary to prevent accidental exposure to this species.
