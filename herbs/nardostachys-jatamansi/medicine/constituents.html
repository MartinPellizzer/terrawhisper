10 Medicinal Constituents Of Nardostachys Jatamansi (Spikenard)

Nardostachys jatamansi has active constituents such as sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and iridoid glycosides.

These constituents are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and sedative effects.

For example, the sesquiterpenes have been shown to reduce anxiety and improve sleep quality, while the flavonoids exhibit antioxidant and cardiovascular-protective properties, potentially improving heart health and reducing the risk of chronic diseases.

Asarone, the primary constituent of spikenard oil, is known for its sedative and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a sought-after ingredient in aromatherapy blends.
