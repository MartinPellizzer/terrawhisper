What's the taxonomical classification of Halogeton glomeratus?

Halogeton glomeratus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is ordered within the Caryophyllales and falls under the family Amaranthaecae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Halogeton and is identified as the specific species glomeratus.
