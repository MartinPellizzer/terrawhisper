Coptis Trifolia Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Coptis trifolia, commonly known as the lesser goldthread, is a perennial herb native to Asia and North America, belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

The health benefits of Coptis trifolia include antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and digestive support properties, making it useful for treating infections and gastrointestinal issues.

This herb contains bioactive constituents such as berberine, protoberberine alkaloids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from its rhizomes to harness its medicinal benefits.
