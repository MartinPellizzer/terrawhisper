Trifoliate Coptis (Coptis trifolia)

Trifoliate Coptis (Coptis trifolia) is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, native to North America, and Asia. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and european herbal medicine.
