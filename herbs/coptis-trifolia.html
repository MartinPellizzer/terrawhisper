Coptis Trifolia Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Coptis trifolia, commonly known as the lesser goldthread, is a perennial herb native to Asia, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and medicinal rhizomes.

This herb is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and digestive properties, making it a popular remedy in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include berberine, protoberberine alkaloids, and various flavonoids that contribute to its health benefits.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and powders can be made from Coptis trifolia to harness its medicinal properties.
