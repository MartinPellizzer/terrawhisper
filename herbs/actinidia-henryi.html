What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Actinidia henryi has woody, climbing vines that produce ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins and a smooth, somewhat leathery texture. These blossoms eventually give way to fleshy, oval-shaped fruits that transition from green to a deep purplish-black color upon ripening.
