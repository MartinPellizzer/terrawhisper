White Bryony (Bryonia alba) Compounds

The primary compounds in White Bryony include alkaloids, tannins, flavonoids, and saponins. These substances contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects. Other key components include phenolic acids, coumarins, and glycosides, which support its use in traditional herbal medicine.
