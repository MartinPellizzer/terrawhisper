What's the taxonomical classification of Streptolirion volubile?

Streptolirion volubiile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Commelinaales and the family Commelinaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Streptolirion.
