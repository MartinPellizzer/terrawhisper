What's the taxonomical classification of Streptolirion volubile?

Streptolirion volubiile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Commelinaales and the family Commelinaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Streptolirion. Ultimately, the plant is identified at the species level as Streptolirion volubiile.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Streptolirion volubile has slender, twining stems that reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters and support clusters of trumpet-shaped, cream-colored flowers.

These leaves possess smooth margins and a light green hue, providing a delicate texture to the plant's overall appearance. Small, inconspicuous buds develop at the nodes before opening into fragrant blossoms.
