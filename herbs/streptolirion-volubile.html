Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Commelinaales and the family Commelinaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Streptolirion. Ultimately, the plant is identified at the species level as Streptolirion volubiile.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Streptolirion volubiile can cause severe skin irritation, localized swelling, and potential allergic reactions upon direct contact with the human body. The plant contains chemical compounds that act as irritants to the epidermal layer. Some individuals may also experience dermatological symptoms such as redness or itching that persist for several days. Careful handling is necessary to avoid these adverse physical responses.
