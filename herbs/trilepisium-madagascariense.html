What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Trilepisium madagascariense has small, elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are arranged oppositely along a slender, herbaceous stem. These blooms are characterized by their delicate structure and minute size relative to the overall plant. The root system is adapted to its specific habitat, providing stability for the delicate aerial parts.
