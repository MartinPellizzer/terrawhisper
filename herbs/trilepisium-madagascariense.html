What's the taxonomical classification of Trilepisium madagascariense?

Trilepisium madagascariense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the biological hierarchy further, it is categorized under the order Rosales and falls within the family Moraceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Trilepisium, with its unique identity established by the species name madagascariense.
