Carex maubertiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Carex, with its specific designation being the species maubertiana.
