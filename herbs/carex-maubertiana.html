Carex maubertiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Carex maubertiana?

Carex maubertiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is part of the order Poales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Carex, with its specific designation being the species maubertiana.

Some people might experience redness or itching if they handle the plant without protection. These reactions are typically superficial and resolve quickly once the source of contact is removed.
