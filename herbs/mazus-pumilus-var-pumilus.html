Mazus pumilus var. pumilus

What's the taxonomical classification of Mazus pumilus var. pumilus?

Mazus pumilus var. pumilus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is situated within the family Mazaceae. Specifically, this plant is categorized under the genus Mazus, reaching its most precise taxonomic identification as the variety Mazus pumilus var. pumilus.
