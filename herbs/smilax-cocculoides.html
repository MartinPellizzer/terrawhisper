What's the taxonomical classification of Smilax cocculoides?

Smilax cocculoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling specifically into the order Liliales. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is a member of the family Smilacaceae and the genus Smilax, ultimately identified by its unique species designation, cocculoides.
