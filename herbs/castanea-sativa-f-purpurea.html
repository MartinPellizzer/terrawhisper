Castanea sativa f. purpurea

Castanea sativa f. purpurea, commonly known as Purple Chestnut, is a cultivar of the European Chestnut (Castanea sativa) tree, characterized by its deep purple foliage.

The Purple Chestnut tree is valued for its numerous health benefits, including its potential to lower cholesterol levels, reduce inflammation, and aid in the management of blood sugar levels.

The therapeutic actions of Castanea sativa f. purpurea are attributed to its ability to act as an antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and cardiovascular protective agent, making it a promising natural remedy for various health conditions.
