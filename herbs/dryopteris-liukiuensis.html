What is Dryopteris liukiuensis?

Dryopteris liukiuensis is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a perennial, herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous habit, typically growing to 60-100 cm in height. The fronds are lanceolate to elliptical in shape, measuring 10-40 cm in length and 2-5 cm in width, with a leathery texture and serrated margins. The fertile fronds are smaller and have a spore-bearing sori under the leaflets.

The plant is native to Taiwan.
