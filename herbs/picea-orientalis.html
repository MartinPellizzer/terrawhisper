Picea orientalis

Picea orientalis, commonly known as the Oriental or Bradshaw spruce, is a species of spruce tree native to the mountains of Turkey and the Caucasus.

This plant is valued for its essential oil, which is rich in bioactive constituents that provide a range of health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Picea orientalis essential oil include relaxation, reducing stress and anxiety, and improving sleep quality, making it a popular ingredient in aromatherapy.

Bioactive constituents of Picea orientalis include terpenes, specifically alpha-pinene, which contributes to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects.
