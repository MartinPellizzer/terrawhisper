What's the taxonomical classification of Anaphalis aureopunctata var. atrata?

Anaphalis aureopuncata var. atraata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Anaphalis, eventually reaching its precise taxonomic designation as the species Anaphalis aureopuncata var. atraata.
