What's the taxonomical classification of Cyrtogonone argentea?

Cyrtogonone argentea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Cyrtogonone, with its unique designation being the species argentea.
