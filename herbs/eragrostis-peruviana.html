What is Eragrostis peruviana?

Eragrostis peruviana is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is an annual or perennial plant with a tufted growth habit, typically 30-80 cm in height. The leaves are flat, linear, and 2-10 mm wide, with a ligule and a hairy sheath. The inflorescence is a panicle, 2-10 cm long, with spikelets that are 5-10 mm long and contain 2-5 florets.

The spikelets are lanceolate and have two sterile florets and a fertile floret with a 2-awned lemma and a 3-awned glume.
