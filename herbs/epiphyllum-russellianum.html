What is Epiphyllum russellianum?

Epiphyllum russellianum is a species of epiphytic cactus belonging to the family Cactaceae.

It is native to Mexico and Guatemala. This epiphyte has long, thin, and fleshy stems that grow horizontally, producing white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance and red or yellow stamens. The plant's stems can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, and its leaves are reduced to spines.

The stems are covered with fine, brown, and woolly areoles, which contain the plant's roots.
