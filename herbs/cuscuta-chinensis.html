What to know about Cuscuta chinensis before using it medicinally

Cuscuta chinensis, commonly known as Chinese dodder, is a medicinal herb used to treat various health conditions, including menopausal symptoms, menstrual irregularities, and infertility.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Cuscuta chinensis is often prepared as a decoction or a capsule, and is commonly used to support reproductive health and alleviate symptoms associated with menopause.
