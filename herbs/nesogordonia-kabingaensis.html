What's the taxonomical classification of Nesogordonia kabingaensis?

Nesogordonia kabingaensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Malvales. This plant is a member of the family Malvaceae, falling under the genus Nesogordonia, and is specifically identified by the species name kabingaensis.
