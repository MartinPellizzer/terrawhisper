Preparation Methods

Tincture: Used for respiratory issues and as an expectorant.

Infusion: Used for digestive issues and as a mild stimulant.

Safety Profile

Long-Leaved Sundew is generally safe when used in recommended doses. However, it may cause mild gastrointestinal upset in some individuals. Avoid use during pregnancy and by those with known allergies to plants in the Drosera genus.
