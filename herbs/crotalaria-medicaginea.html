Crotalaria medicaginea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is a large group of legumes, and is specifically identified by the genus Crotalaria. Ultimately, its unique taxonomic position is defined by its specific epithet, resulting in the scientific name Crotalaria medicaginea.
