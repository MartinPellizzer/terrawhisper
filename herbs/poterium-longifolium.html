Poterium longifolium

Poterium longifolium, also known as the burnet-saxifrage, is a perennial plant species that belongs to the Saxifragaceae family.

It has been traditionally used in medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to stimulate the digestive system, improve circulation, and act as a diuretic.

The plant's health benefits include reducing inflammation, relieving pain, and treating various gastrointestinal issues.

Poterium longifolium can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat conditions such as indigestion, diarrhea, and urinary tract infections.
