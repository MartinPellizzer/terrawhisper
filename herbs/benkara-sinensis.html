Benkara sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Benkara sinensis?

Benkara sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Gentianaales. Taxonomically, this plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Benkara, with the distinct species designation sinensis.
