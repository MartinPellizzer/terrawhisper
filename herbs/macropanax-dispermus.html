What's the taxonomical classification of Macropanax dispermus?

Macropanax dispermus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Apiales and falls within the family Araliaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Macropanax, with the specific epithet dispermus.
