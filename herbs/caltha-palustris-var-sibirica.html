Caltha palustris var. sibirica

What's the taxonomical classification of Caltha palustris var. sibirica?

Caltha palustris var. sibirica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Ranunculales and further categorized into the family Ranunculaceae. This specific botanical variety is identified by the genus Caltha, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation at the species level as Caltha palustris var. sibirica.
