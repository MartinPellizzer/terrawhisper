Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata)

Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) is a member of the Sarmentaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, East Asia, and China. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, expectorant, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
