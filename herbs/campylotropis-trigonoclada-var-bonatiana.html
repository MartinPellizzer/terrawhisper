Campylotropis trigonoclada var. bonatiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis trigonoclada var. bonatiana?

Campylotropis trigonoclada var. bonatiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Fabales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Fabaceae and is placed in the genus Campylotropis. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its designation as the variety bonatiana of the species Campylotropis trigonoclada.
