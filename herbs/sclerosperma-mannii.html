What's the taxonomical classification of Sclerosperma mannii?

Sclerosperma mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized under the order Arecales. It is situated within the family Arecaceae, specifically falling under the genus Sclerosperma, which culminates in its unique scientific designation as the species mannii.
