What's the taxonomical classification of Sclerosperma mannii?

Sclerosperma mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is situated within the family Arecaceae, specifically falling under the genus Sclerosperma, which culminates in its unique scientific designation as the species mannii.
