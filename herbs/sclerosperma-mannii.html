Sclerosperma mannii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Sclerosperma mannii has a woody, perennial habit characterized by erect stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves with entire margins. These reproductive structures eventually produce small, dry fruits that are central to its dispersal. The overall structure of the plant is adapted to its specific tropical forest environment.
