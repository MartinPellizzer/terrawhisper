What's the taxonomical classification of Phrynium placentarium?

Phrynium placentarium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Zingiberales. It is situated within the family Marantaceae, a group known for its diverse herbaceous plants, and is specifically identified under the genus Phrynium. The taxonomic hierarchy culminates in its unique species designation, Phrynium placentarium.
