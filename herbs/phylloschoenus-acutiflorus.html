Phylloschoenus acutiflorus

Phylloschoenus acutiflorus, commonly known as the Sharp-flowered Wire-grass or Sharp-flower Wire-grass, is a species of grass in the sedge family Cyperaceae.

The therapeutic actions of Phylloschoenus acutiflorus include expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities, which contribute to its effectiveness in alleviating respiratory problems and promoting healing.
