What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Nervilia aragoana has a single, broadly ovate to cordate leaf that typically measures between 5 and 15 centimeters in length and features prominent, reticulate venation. During its flowering stage, a small inflorescence emerges from the base of the leaf, bearing inconspicuous, brownish or yellowish flowers.
