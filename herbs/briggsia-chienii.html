What's the taxonomical classification of Briggsia chienii?

Briggsia chienii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, the plant is placed in the order Lamiales and belongs to the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Briggsia and is specifically known by its species name, chienii.
