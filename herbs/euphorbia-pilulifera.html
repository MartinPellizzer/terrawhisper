Euphorbia Pilulifera: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Euphorbia pilulifera, commonly known as the "pilule plant" or "milkweed plant," is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its latex-filled stems and yellow flowers.

This herb is valued for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used to treat various ailments.

The health benefits of Euphorbia pilulifera include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antidiabetic effects, making it useful in managing conditions like diabetes and skin infections.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its pharmacological profile.
