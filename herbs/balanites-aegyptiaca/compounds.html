Balanites (Balanites aegyptiaca) Compounds

Balanites aegyptiaca contains several primary active compounds, including tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Saponins and coumarin provide anti-inflammatory and antifungal effects, while terpenoids and glycosides support antioxidant activity. Alkaloids and essential oils may have analgesic and antimicrobial properties, and anthocyanins contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits.
