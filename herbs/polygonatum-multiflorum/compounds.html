Solomon'S Seal (Polygonatum multiflorum) Compounds

Solomon's Seal contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which act as antioxidants. Glycosides contribute to its medicinal properties, while saponins have anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and phenolic acids provide astringent and antimicrobial actions. Coumarin, terpenoids, and essential oils offer additional therapeutic benefits, and anthocyanins contribute to its antioxidant profile.
