10 Possible Side Effects Of Polygonatum Multiflorum (Chinese Angelica)

Polygonatum multiflorum, also known as Chinese Angelica, has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal upset, and interaction with medications.

These side effects can occur when the herb is taken in excessive amounts or combined with other substances.

In severe cases, these reactions can lead to anaphylaxis, digestive issues, and impaired medication efficacy, which can worsen quality of life by causing discomfort, anxiety, and decreased productivity.
