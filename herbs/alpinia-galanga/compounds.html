Java Ginger (Alpinia galanga) Compounds

Java Ginger contains several key compounds, including essential oil, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its aromatic and therapeutic properties. Coumarin and phenolic acids provide anti-inflammatory effects, while glycosides and tannins support digestive health. Saponins and alkaloids enhance its medicinal value, and anthocyanins add antioxidant benefits.
