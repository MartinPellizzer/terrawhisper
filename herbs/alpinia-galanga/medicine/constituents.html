10 Medicinal Constituents Of Alpinia Galanga (Galangal)

Alpinia galanga has active constituents such as galangin, alpinetin, and 1,8-cineole.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, contributing to its medicinal uses. The constituents in galangal may alleviate digestive issues, reduce fever, and soothe respiratory problems.

For example, its anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate arthritis pain and improve overall health, while its antimicrobial properties can prevent infections and promote wound healing, ultimately improving the quality of life for many individuals.
