Heteropappus sibiricus

Heteropappus sibiricus, also known as Siberian silverweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant is traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating wounds, inflammation, and skin conditions due to its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Heteropappus sibiricus include its ability to accelerate wound healing, reduce pain and swelling, and protect against infections.

Herbal preparations of Heteropappus sibiricus often take the form of tinctures, infusions, or salves, which can be used topically or internally to harness its medicinal properties.
