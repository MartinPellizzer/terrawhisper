What's the taxonomical classification of Vossia cuspidata?

Vossia cuspidata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Poales. This plant is a representative of the family Poaceae, situated within the genus Vossia, and is specifically identified by its species name, cuspidata.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The plant's root system is fibrous, helping it stabilize in the sandy or loamy soils it prefers.
