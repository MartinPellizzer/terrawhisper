What's the taxonomical classification of Paraboea martini?

Paraboea martini belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Lamiales and the family Gesneriaceae, ultimately placing the plant within the genus Paraboea, with its specific designation being the species martini.
