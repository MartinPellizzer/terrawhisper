Acacia oleifolia

The therapeutic actions of Acacia oleifolia are attributed to its antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties, which are thought to be triggered by its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.
