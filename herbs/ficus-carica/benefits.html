1. Reduce blood pressure

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a chronic condition characterized by elevated pressure in the arteries.

This condition occurs when the force of blood against the artery walls is consistently too high, potentially leading to serious health complications.

It is often caused by factors such as poor diet, lack of physical activity, and genetic predisposition.

Individuals over the age of 60 and those with a family history of hypertension are more commonly affected.

The ficus carica plant may help reduce blood pressure by promoting cardiovascular health and supporting natural regulatory mechanisms in the body.

How this herb helps with reduce blood pressure?

Ficus carica reduce blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids and alkaloids.

These compounds have been shown to possess vasodilatory properties that help relax blood vessels. The flavonoids in figs may improve endothelial function, promoting better blood flow. Alkaloids contribute to the plant's ability to modulate the cardiovascular system.

Together, these natural compounds support the management of hypertension through their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.
