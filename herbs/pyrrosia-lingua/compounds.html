Chinese Brake Fern (Pyrrosia lingua) Compounds

The primary compounds of Pyrrosia lingua include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while glycosides and saponins support its traditional use in digestive health. Coumarin and terpenoids add to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory profiles.
