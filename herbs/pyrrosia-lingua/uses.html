Chinese Brake Fern (Pyrrosia lingua) Uses

Chinese Brake Fern is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and asthma. It also addresses allergic rhinitis, sinusitis, and influenza by supporting immune function and reducing inflammation. Its applications extend to respiratory tract infections, tonsillitis, and respiratory allergies due to its antimicrobial and antihistamine properties.

Chinese Brake Fern (Pyrrosia lingua) Monograph

1. Cough

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat coughs, particularly those accompanied by phlegm. It helps to clear the lungs and reduce inflammation. Often combined with other herbs, it supports respiratory health and alleviates persistent coughing symptoms.

2. Bronchitis

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce mucus buildup and soothe respiratory irritation, making it a common ingredient in herbal formulations for coughs and bronchial inflammation.

3. Allergic rhinitis

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by modulating immune responses. Often combined with other herbs, it supports long-term management of seasonal allergies.

4. Influenza

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat influenza due to its antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce fever and alleviate symptoms such as coughing and sore throat. The herb is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in clearing heat and detoxifying the body.

5. Sinusitis

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat sinusitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and alleviate symptoms by promoting mucus drainage and soothing irritated nasal passages. Often combined with other herbs, it supports respiratory health and is valued for its efficacy in chronic sinus infections.

6. Asthma

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat asthma by promoting lung function and reducing inflammation. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its bronchodilating effects. The plant's anti-inflammatory properties help alleviate respiratory symptoms associated with asthma. It is typically prepared as a decoction or powder for internal use.

7. Respiratory tract infections

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat respiratory tract infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion by supporting lung function and clearing phlegm. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects in respiratory conditions.

8. Tonsillitis

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat tonsillitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce swelling and soothe throat irritation. Often prepared as a decoction, it is applied internally to alleviate symptoms and support recovery.

9. Respiratory allergies

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate respiratory allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. It helps reduce symptoms like sneezing, itching, and nasal congestion. Often combined with other herbs, it supports immune balance and soothes allergic reactions in the respiratory system.

10. Laryngitis

Pyrrosia lingua is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat laryngitis due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. It helps reduce throat irritation and inflammation, often prepared as a decoction or powder. It is commonly combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in clearing heat and resolving phlegm.
