Gastrodia (Gastrodia elata)

Gastrodia (Gastrodia elata) is a member of the Orchidaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its sedative, tonic, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
