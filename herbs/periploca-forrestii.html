What's the taxonomical classification of Periploca forrestii?

Periploca forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Gentianaales and the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Periploca, and its specific species epithet, forrestii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Periploca forrestii has opposite, elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are leathery in texture and possess entire margins.

The foliage is typically dark green on the upper surface and may feature a paler underside. Its woody stems exhibit a somewhat spreading growth habit characteristic of small shrubs or subshrubs. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in clusters.
