Otostegia Schimperi

Otostegia schimperi, a member of the Asteraceae family, is traditionally used in various African countries for its medicinal properties.

It is commonly employed to treat respiratory conditions such as coughs and asthma due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory effects. The plant's leaves and stems are often prepared as decoctions or infusions to alleviate symptoms of bronchitis and other lung-related ailments. Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine to address digestive issues and as a general tonic to boost overall health.

Preliminary scientific studies suggest that compounds in Otostegia schimperi may have potential for developing new treatments for respiratory and inflammatory diseases.
