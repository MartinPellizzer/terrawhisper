Trachyphrynium braunianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Ultimately, its taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Trachyphrynium, with its specific designation being the species braunianum.
