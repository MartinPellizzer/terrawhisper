10 Possible Side Effects Of Chamaelirium Lutea (Fairy Bells)

Chamaelirium lutea has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, dizziness, and headaches.

These effects occur due to its alkaloid content, which can be toxic in large doses. If not monitored, these symptoms can worsen, leading to decreased productivity and strained personal relationships.

In extreme cases, overconsumption can cause vomiting and diarrhea, further disrupting daily life and overall well-being.
