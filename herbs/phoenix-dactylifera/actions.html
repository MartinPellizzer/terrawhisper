Date Palm (Phoenix dactylifera) Actions

The Date Palm offers several therapeutic actions including tonic, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties. It also acts as an astringent, carminative, and stimulant, while providing antispasmodic and sedative effects. Additionally, it serves as an expectorant and has a bitter taste that supports digestive health.
