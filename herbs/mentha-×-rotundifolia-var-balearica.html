Mentha × rotundifolia var. balearica

Mentha × rotundifolia var. balearica, commonly known as Balearic mint, is a hybrid species of mint native to the Balearic Islands.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal and culinary properties, offering various health benefits such as soothing digestive issues, relieving stress and anxiety, and aiding in respiratory problems.

The therapeutic actions of Balearic mint include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its ability to calm and soothe the body.

The bioactive constituents of Balearic mint include menthol, menthone, and limonene, among other compounds that contribute to its medicinal properties.
