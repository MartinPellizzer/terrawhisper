What's the taxonomical classification of Lepisorus oligolepidus?

Lepisorus oligolepidus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae, placing it within the genus Lepisorus. Ultimately, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, oligolepidus.
