What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus fockeanus?

Rubus fockeanus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaceae. Ultimately, its specific classification is defined by the genus Rubus and the species fockeanus.
