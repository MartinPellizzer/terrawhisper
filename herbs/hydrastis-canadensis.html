Hydrastis Canadensis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Hydrastis canadensis, commonly known as goldenseal, is a perennial herb native to North America, known for its yellow roots and potent medicinal properties.

It has been traditionally used for its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and digestive support benefits.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as berberine, hydrastine, and canadine, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or powder for internal use, though it is often used in small doses due to its strong effects.
