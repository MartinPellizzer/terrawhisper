Goldenseal (Hydrastis canadensis)

Goldenseal (Hydrastis canadensis) is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, native to Eastern North America, Canada, and United States. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
