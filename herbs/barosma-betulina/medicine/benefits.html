1. Reduces inflammation

Barosma betulina reduces inflammation because it contains compounds that inhibit pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines.

The essential oil of buchu, in particular, has been shown to suppress the production of pro-inflammatory mediators, such as interleukin-1 beta and tumor necrosis factor-alpha.

This anti-inflammatory effect may contribute to its traditional use in treating various conditions, including arthritis and other inflammatory disorders, by providing relief from pain and swelling.

2. Kills bacteria naturally

Barosma betulina kills bacteria naturally because of its unique composition of essential oils, particularly borneol and limonene.

These oils have antimicrobial properties that disrupt the cell membranes of bacteria, ultimately leading to their death.

The potent combination of these compounds in buchu makes it an effective natural remedy for eliminating bacterial infections, such as urinary tract infections and digestive issues.

3. Treats urinary tract infections

Barosma betulina treats urinary tract infections because it has antimicrobial properties that target E. coli, the primary cause of UTIs.

The plant's essential oils and flavonoids reduce inflammation and kill bacteria that have entered the urinary tract, thereby alleviating symptoms and promoting healing.

Traditional medicine has long recognized buchu's efficacy in treating UTIs, and modern research supports its traditional use.

4. Relieves kidney stone symptoms

Barosma betulina relieves kidney stone symptoms because of its diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

It helps to increase urine production, flushing out small stones and reducing their size, making them easier to pass. The herb also reduces inflammation in the urinary tract, alleviating pain and discomfort associated with kidney stones.

This natural remedy has been used for centuries to provide relief from kidney stone symptoms without the harsh side effects of pharmaceuticals.

5. Lowers blood pressure slowly

Barosma betulina lowers blood pressure slowly because its bioactive compounds interact with the body's natural mechanisms to reduce blood vessel constriction.

As its flavonoids, phenolic acids, and other phytochemicals are absorbed, they trigger a response that helps dilate blood vessels, allowing for improved blood flow and reducing pressure.

This gentle effect, over time, leads to sustained blood pressure reduction and cardiovascular well-being.

6. Boosts immune system function

Barosma betulina boosts immune system function because of its high content of antioxidants and essential oils.

These compounds help to neutralize free radicals and reduce inflammation, which can compromise immune response. The plant's antimicrobial properties also aid in fighting off infections and supporting the body's natural defense mechanisms, promoting overall immune system health and well-being.

Regular use may support immune function and reduce the risk of illness.

7. Fights off fungal infections

Barosma betulina fights off fungal infections because of its antifungal properties.

The plant's essential oils, particularly borneol and limonene, have been shown to exhibit potent activity against various fungal species. These oils disrupt fungal cell membranes and inhibit growth, effectively preventing the spread of infections.

Traditional herbal medicine has long utilized buchu for its antimicrobial properties, providing a natural solution for fungal infections.

8. Relieves prostate issues

Barosma betulina relieves prostate issues because it has natural anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to soothe and calm the prostate gland, reducing inflammation and irritation that can cause discomfort and pain.

Its essential oils, particularly camphor and pinene, have been shown to have a relaxing effect on the prostate muscles, easing symptoms such as urinary retention and nocturia.

9. Soothes digestive issues quickly

Barosma betulina soothes digestive issues quickly because of its unique combination of essential oils, particularly borneol and alpha-pinene.

These compounds have potent anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which help to calm digestive irritations and reduce inflammation in the gut.

The natural compounds in buchu also ease cramping and spasms, making it an effective and fast-acting remedy for digestive issues.

10. Kills parasites and worms

Barosma betulina kills parasites and worms because of its unique essential oils and alkaloid compounds.

These compounds, particularly diosphenol and diospyrone, have been shown to have anthelmintic and anti-parasitic properties, capable of disrupting the life cycles of internal parasites and worms, such as tapeworms, hookworms, and pinworms, making it a popular natural remedy for intestinal health.
