Potentilla reptans var. sericophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla reptans var. sericophylla?

Potentiella reptans var. sericophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Rosaceae and the genus Potentiella, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety sericophylla within the species Potentiella reptans.
