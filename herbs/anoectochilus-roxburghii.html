Anoectochilus roxburghii

What's the taxonomical classification of Anoectochilus roxburghii?

Anoectochilus roxburghii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the Orchidaceae family, falling under the genus Anoectochilus, and is specifically identified by its species name, roxburghii.

Careful monitoring is necessary because the chemical constituents within the orchid may trigger unpredictable hypersensitivity in certain people. Always consult a medical professional before using such botanical specimens for therapeutic purposes.
