Duabanga grandiflora

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Duabanga grandiflora has large, woody stems that support broad, elliptical leaves with prominent pinnate venation and thick, waxy cuticles. The plant produces massive, bell-shaped flowers characterized by deep crimson petals and elongated stamen clusters. Its root system consists of a robust taproot that stabilizes the heavy structure in tropical soils. Each leaf margin is smooth, and the underside often exhibits a lighter green coloration compared to the dark dorsal surface. The overall growth habit is shrub-like, reaching significant heights through dense branching.

    What are the herbal preparations of Duabanga grandiflora?

    Duabanga grandiflora is prepared as a decoction, a concentrated liquid extract made by boiling the plant material in water to extract its active medicinal compounds. This traditional method is often used to create a potent tea that is ingested orally for its therapeutic properties. In addition to decoctions, the plant parts may be processed into infusions where the material is steeped in hot water rather than boiled. Some regional practices also involve crushing the fresh leaves or bark to create topical pastes applied directly to the skin. These various preparations allow for different methods of administration depending on the specific health condition being treated.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

    Duabanga grandiflora can cause severe gastrointestinal distress, skin irritation, and potential neurotoxicity if ingested or applied directly to the skin. These adverse reactions often manifest as intense nausea, abdominal cramping, and vomiting shortly after exposure. Contact with the plant's sap may also lead to localized redness, itching, or blistering on sensitive areas of the body. In high concentrations, the chemical compounds within the plant might interfere with nervous system functions, leading to dizziness or tremors. Caution is necessary when handling this species to avoid these unpredictable physiological responses.
