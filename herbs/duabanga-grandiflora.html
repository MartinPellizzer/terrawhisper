What's the taxonomical classification of Duabanga grandiflora?

Duabanga grandiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Lythraceae, specifically falling under the genus Duabanga, with the species name grandiflora.
