10 Medicinal Constituents Of Acacia Dealbata (Silver Wattle)

Acacia dealbata has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and condensed tannins.

These compounds are associated with antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties. These properties can improve lives by protecting against cell damage, reducing inflammation in chronic conditions, and preventing infections.

For example, flavonoids in silver wattle may help alleviate symptoms of arthritis and improve cardiovascular health by preventing the formation of free radicals in the body.
