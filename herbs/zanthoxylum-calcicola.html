What's the taxonomical classification of Zanthoxylum calcicola?

Zanthoxylum calcicola belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Sapindales and is a member of the family Rutaceae. Finally, its specific identification is defined by the genus Zanthoxylum and the species epithet calcicola.
