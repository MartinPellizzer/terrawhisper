Bistort (Polygonum bistortum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Bistort include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties, while glycosides may support cardiovascular health. Coumarin and saponins are also present, offering potential antimicrobial and digestive benefits.

Bistort (Polygonum bistortum) Monograph
