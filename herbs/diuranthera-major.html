What are the therapeutic actions of Diuranthera major?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat urinary tract infections, kidney stones, bladder ailments, and various inflammatory conditions of the renal system. Its diuretic properties help increase urine production to flush out toxins and bacteria from the body. It is often employed in traditional medicine to alleviate symptoms of edema and fluid retention. Furthermore, the plant is believed to assist in reducing pain associated with kidney-related complications. These therapeutic actions make it a significant resource in herbal remedies for reproductive and urinary health.
