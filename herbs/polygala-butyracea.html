Polygala butyracea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Fabales and the family Polygalae, where it is placed within the genus Polygala. Ultimately, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species name, butyracea.
