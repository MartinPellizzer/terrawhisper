10 Health Benefits Of Cymbopogon Martini (Lemongrass)

Cymbopogon martini, also known as lemongrass, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and relieving stress and anxiety.

Its medicinal properties, including citral and geranial, are responsible for these benefits. For example, lemongrass oil has been used to alleviate symptoms of IBS, while its anti-inflammatory properties can help reduce pain and swelling in individuals with arthritis.

This can improve quality of life for those who suffer from these conditions, allowing for increased mobility and comfort.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Cymbopogon martini.

1. Relieves digestive issues

Cymbopogon martini relieves digestive issues because of its natural antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oils present in its leaves help to ease cramps, bloating, and nausea associated with conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and indigestion. Its calming effects also promote digestion and reduce stress, contributing to improved overall gut health.

Regular consumption of lemongrass tea or supplements may provide relief from these common digestive problems.

2. Reduces inflammation

Cymbopogon martini reduces inflammation because of its high content of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.

The essential oils present in lemongrass, particularly geraniol and citral, have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, which contribute to the inflammatory response.

This makes lemongrass a valuable natural remedy for reducing inflammation and alleviating symptoms of conditions such as arthritis and other inflammatory diseases.

3. Lowers blood sugar

Cymbopogon martini lowers blood sugar because its essential oil, citral, has been shown to increase insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake in the body.

Studies have demonstrated that lemongrass extracts can inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in carbohydrate digestion and glucose absorption, leading to a decrease in blood sugar levels.

This makes lemongrass a potential natural remedy for managing blood sugar levels in individuals with diabetes.

4. Kills bacteria

Cymbopogon martini kills bacteria because of its essential oil content.

This oil contains citral, a natural compound with potent antimicrobial properties. When citral comes into contact with bacteria, it disrupts the cell membrane, leading to cellular death. The result is the destruction of the bacterial cell, effectively eliminating the infection.

This property makes lemongrass a popular ingredient in natural remedies and household cleaning products.

5. Soothes anxiety

Cymbopogon martini soothes anxiety because of its calming and relaxing properties.

The essential oil extracted from its leaves contains compounds like citral and geranial, which have a sedative effect on the nervous system, reducing stress and anxiety.

Inhaling lemongrass oil or consuming it in teas and infusions can promote a sense of calm and tranquility, making it an effective natural remedy for anxiety relief.

6. Relieves headaches

Cymbopogon martini relieves headaches because of its calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oils present in lemongrass, such as citral and geraniol, work together to reduce muscle tension and ease pain. The herb's natural cooling effect also helps to soothe the mind and body, providing quick relief from tension headaches and migraines.

Its aromatic scent further calms the senses, promoting a sense of relaxation and well-being.

7. Fights fungal infections

Cymbopogon martini fights fungal infections because of its high content of citral, a potent antifungal compound.

Studies have shown that citral extracted from lemongrass oil exhibits strong inhibitory activity against various fungal species, including Candida, Aspergillus, and Fusarium.

By suppressing fungal growth and proliferation, lemongrass oil provides a natural defense against fungal infections, making it a valuable ingredient in traditional medicine and natural remedies.

8. Reduces fever

Cymbopogon martini reduces fever because of its antimicrobial and antipyretic properties.

The essential oils present in lemongrass, particularly citral, exhibit strong anti-inflammatory activity, which helps to bring down the body temperature.

Additionally, the herb's diaphoretic properties help to induce sweating, which is a natural way for the body to release excess heat and toxins.

9. Improves circulation

Cymbopogon martini improves circulation because its essential oils help to dilate blood vessels and increase blood flow.

This can be attributed to the presence of compounds like citral, which has a vasodilatory effect, allowing for enhanced oxygenation and nutrient delivery to the tissues.

Improved circulation can help to reduce inflammation, promote healing, and alleviate symptoms of poor circulation such as fatigue and cold extremities.

10. Combats respiratory issues

Cymbopogon martini combats respiratory issues because of its anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oils present in lemongrass have been found to reduce congestion and ease coughs by thinning mucus. This helps to alleviate symptoms of colds and flu, as well as conditions such as bronchitis and asthma.

Additionally, its antimicrobial properties help prevent infections that can exacerbate respiratory issues.
