10 Health Benefits Of Cymbopogon Martini (Lemongrass)

Cymbopogon martini has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

These medicinal properties can help alleviate digestive issues, reduce fever, and soothe headaches and muscle pains. For example, a tea made from the leaves of this plant can be used to treat stomach ulcers and indigestion, improving digestion and reducing discomfort for individuals with gastrointestinal issues.

Additionally, lemongrass oil has been shown to have anti-anxiety effects, promoting relaxation and calmness in those who use it as aromatherapy or massage oil.
