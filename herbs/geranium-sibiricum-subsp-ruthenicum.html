Geranium sibiricum subsp. ruthenicum

Geranium sibiricum subsp.

ruthenicum, also known as Russian geranium or Siberian cranesbill, is a perennial plant species within the geranium family, characterized by its fragrant flowers and medicinal properties.

It has been traditionally used to treat various health issues due to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties, which can help reduce pain and inflammation, promote wound healing, and protect against oxidative stress.

The medicinal properties of Geranium sibiricum subsp.

ruthenicum can be utilized through herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and creams, allowing for topical and internal applications.

By leveraging its health benefits, this plant has been used to alleviate conditions like arthritis, eczema, and skin irritations, while also supporting overall well-being and immune function.
