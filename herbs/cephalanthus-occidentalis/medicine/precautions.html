10 Precautions To Take When Using Cephalanthus Occidentalis (Dogwood)

Cephalanthus occidentalis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious of allergic reactions.

Taking this herb without proper allergy testing can lead to skin irritation, itching, or difficulty breathing.

It's also crucial to note that dogwood may interact with blood thinners, diabetes medications, or other medications, which can cause adverse effects or reduce their efficacy.
