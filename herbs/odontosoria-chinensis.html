Odontosoria chinensis has opposite, decussate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape with serrated margins and a slightly pubescent underside. The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that transitions from green to a dark color upon ripening. This species is characterized by its woody stems and distinctively textured foliage.
