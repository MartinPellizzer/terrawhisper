Primula fasciculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Ericales and belongs to the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is situated within the genus Primula, with its specific designation being the species fasciulata.
