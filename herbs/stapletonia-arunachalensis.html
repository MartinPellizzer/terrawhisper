Stapletonia Arunachalensis

Stapletonia arunachalensis is a rare plant species native to the northeastern region of India, particularly in the state of Arunachal Pradesh, known for its rich biodiversity.

This plant has garnered attention in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Preliminary studies suggest that it may contain bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and alkaloids, which could contribute to its medicinal value. Researchers are exploring its use in treating various ailments, including skin infections and inflammatory conditions.

However, further scientific investigation is needed to validate its efficacy and safety for broader medicinal applications.
