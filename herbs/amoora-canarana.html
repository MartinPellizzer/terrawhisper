Possible Side Effects of Amoora canarana

Amoora canarana can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, diarrhea, and stomach cramps due to its high levels of tannins and saponins.

Ingesting large amounts of the plant's extracts can also lead to nausea, vomiting, and allergic reactions in some individuals.

Furthermore, improper use of Amoora canarana may cause an imbalance in blood sugar levels, which can be particularly problematic for people with pre-existing diabetes.

In severe cases, excessive consumption of the plant can lead to kidney damage and liver toxicity.
