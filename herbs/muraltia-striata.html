Possible Side Effects of Muraltia striata

Muraltia striata can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's sap can cause severe skin burns, blisters, and scarring.

Ingesting the plant's leaves or roots can lead to nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain, while excessive consumption may cause liver and kidney damage.

Improper use of Muraltia striata may also trigger anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction.
