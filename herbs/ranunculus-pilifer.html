Ranunculus pilifer

Ranunculus pilifer, commonly known as the wooly crowfoot, is a perennial plant species in the family Ranunculaceae.

This plant is traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its ability to treat various health issues such as fever, rheumatism, and respiratory problems.
