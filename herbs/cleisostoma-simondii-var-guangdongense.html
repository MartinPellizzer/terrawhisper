What's the taxonomical classification of Cleisostoma simondii var. guangdongense?

Cleisostoma simondii var. guangdongense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Asparagales and is a specialized representative of the family Orchidaceae. Within this botanical hierarchy, the plant is situated in the genus Cleisostoma, specifically categorized as the variety guangdongense of the species Cleisostoma simondii.
