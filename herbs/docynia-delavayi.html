Docynia delavayi has deciduous, simple, alternate leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape with serrated margins and acute tips. The fruit is a large, fleshy pome that becomes yellowish or reddish when fully mature. These botanical features are characteristic of members within the Rosaceae family.
