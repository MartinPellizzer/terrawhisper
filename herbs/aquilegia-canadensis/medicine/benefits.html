The plant's roots and leaves have been used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions. Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids that help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

By consuming eastern columbine tea or using its extracts topically, individuals can potentially alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve wound healing, and enhance overall well-being, leading to a better quality of life.
