What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Dorstenia Contrajerva (Contrajerva)?

Dorstenia contrajerva, also known as contrajerva, has health benefits such as relieving fever, pain, and inflammation, as well as acting as an antioxidant and anti-inflammatory agent.

The medicinal constituents of this herb include flavonoids, terpenoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties. Contrajerva is typically prepared as a tea or decoction and has been used in traditional medicine to treat various conditions, including rheumatism and skin disorders. However, high doses may cause gastrointestinal irritation and allergic reactions.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may interact with other medications, so caution is advised.
