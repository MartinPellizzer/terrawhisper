Peristylus densus

What's the taxonomical classification of Peristylus densus?

Peristylus densus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling under the genus Peristylus, and is specifically identified by its species name, densus.
