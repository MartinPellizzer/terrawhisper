Buxus arborescens var. angustifolia

Buxus arborescens var. angustifolia, commonly known as Japanese boxwood, is a type of evergreen shrub native to Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for treating wounds and reducing fever.

The therapeutic actions of Buxus arborescens var. angustifolia include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic effects, which can help alleviate pain and promote healing.
