What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its inflorescence features zygomorphic flowers where the uppermost sepal forms a prominent hood or galea. What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

This plant contains diterpene alkaloids, specifically aconitine, mesaconitine, hypaconitine, and jesaconitine, which serve as its primary medicinal compounds. In traditional medicine, controlled doses of these compounds are utilized for their analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties. However, the therapeutic window is extremely narrow because these substances can cause severe cardiac and respiratory distress if misused. Precise extraction and processing are essential to manage the high toxicity inherent in these chemical constituents.
