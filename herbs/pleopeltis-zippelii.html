Pleopeltis zippelii

Pleopeltis zippelii, also known as Zippel's polypody, is a type of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Pleopeltis zippelii include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and treat wounds due to its antiseptic properties.

Pleopeltis zippelii can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, or ointments, and is often used in folk medicine to support overall well-being.
