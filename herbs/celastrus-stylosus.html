What's the taxonomical classification of Celastrus stylosus?

Celastrus stylosus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Celastrales. This plant is further categorized under the family Celastraceae and falls within the genus Celastrus, ultimately reaching its specific designation at the species level as stylosus.
