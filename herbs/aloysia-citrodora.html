Aloysia Citrodora

Aloysia citrodora, commonly known as sweet verveine, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties due to its calming and antispasmodic effects.

It contains compounds such as linalool and geraniol, which contribute to its soothing properties and may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia. This herb is often used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies to promote relaxation and reduce stress. Its essential oil is also valued for its ability to ease muscle spasms and digestive discomfort.

Overall, Aloysia citrodora offers a natural approach to managing stress-related conditions and supporting overall well-being.
