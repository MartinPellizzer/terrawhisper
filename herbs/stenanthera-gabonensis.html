Stenanthera Gabonensis

The plant's roots and leaves are commonly prepared as decoctions or poultices to treat ailments such as inflammation, skin infections, and digestive disorders. Preliminary studies suggest that it contains bioactive compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, which may support its traditional use.
