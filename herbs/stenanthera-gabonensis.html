Stenanthera Gabonensis

Stenanthera gabonensis, a flowering plant native to Central Africa, has been traditionally used in local medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

The plant's roots and leaves are commonly prepared as decoctions or poultices to treat ailments such as inflammation, skin infections, and digestive disorders. Preliminary studies suggest that it contains bioactive compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, which may support its traditional use. However, further scientific research is needed to fully understand its pharmacological potential and ensure safe usage.

Despite limited modern validation, Stenanthera gabonensis remains an important resource in traditional healing practices across parts of Gabon and neighboring countries.
