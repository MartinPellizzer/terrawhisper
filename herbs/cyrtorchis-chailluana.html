Cyrtorchis chailluana

What's the taxonomical classification of Cyrtorchis chailluana?

Cyrtorchis chailluana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Cyrtorchis and is specifically designated as the species chailluana.
