Possible Side Effects of panax ginseng

Panax ginseng can have side effects if used improperly, such as insomnia, headaches, and anxiety due to its stimulatory properties.

High doses or prolonged use may also lead to stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea, as well as interactions with certain medications.

Additionally, ginseng can cause high blood pressure in some individuals, particularly those with pre-existing hypertension.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of Panax ginseng may lead to more severe health issues, including seizures and liver damage.
