What's the taxonomical classification of Aphanocalyx heitzii?

Aphanocalyx heitzii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it within the genus Aphanocalyx, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Aphanocalyx heitzii.
