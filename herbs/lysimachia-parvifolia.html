What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia parvifolia?

Lysimachia parvifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Lysimachia, specifically identifying as the species parvifolia.
