Lysimachia parvifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia parvifolia?

Lysimachia parvifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Ericales, it is a member of the family Primulaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Lysimachia, specifically identifying as the species parvifolia.
