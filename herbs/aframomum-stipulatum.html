What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Aframomum stipulatum has an erect, rhizomatous growth habit characterized by large, lanceolate leaves that emerge from prominent, sheath-like stipules. Its inflorescences are arranged in dense, spike-like structures that arise from the leaf axils or near the base of the stem. The individual flowers are small and often clustered closely together within these specialized bracts.
