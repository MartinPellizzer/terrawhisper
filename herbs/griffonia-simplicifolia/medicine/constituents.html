10 Medicinal Constituents Of Griffonia Simplicifolia (African Yams)

Griffonia simplicifolia, also known as African yams, has active constituents such as 5-HTP (5-hydroxytryptophan), flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents possess medicinal properties that include anti-anxiety, antidepressant, and appetite-suppressant effects. For example, 5-HTP can increase serotonin levels in the brain, improving mood and reducing stress. Regular consumption of Griffonia simplicifolia may also promote weight loss by regulating appetite and enhancing metabolism.

Additionally, its constituents have antioxidant properties, which can protect against oxidative stress and inflammation.
