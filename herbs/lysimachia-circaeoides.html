What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysimachia circaeoides has erect, glabrous stems that reach heights of 10 to 60 centimeters and bear opposite, lanceolate leaves with entire margins. Each bloom features a central cluster of stamens that provide a subtle contrast to the bright corolla.
