Stonebreaker (Phyllanthus niruri)

Stonebreaker (Phyllanthus niruri) is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, native to Africa, Asia, and Australia. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and fruits have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and african traditional medicine.
