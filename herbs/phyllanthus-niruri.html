What to know about Phyllanthus niruri before using it medicinally

Phyllanthus niruri, commonly known as chanca piedra, is a herb used to treat various health conditions, including hepatitis, kidney stones, and diabetes.

The main health benefits associated with this herb include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-diabetic properties. The herb's therapeutic properties are attributed to its ability to reduce inflammation, scavenge free radicals, and modulate blood sugar levels. The bioactive compounds present in Phyllanthus niruri, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

A popular herbal preparation made with this herb is the traditional Peruvian tea, which is often consumed to treat kidney stones and other urinary tract issues.
