1. Quercetin

Plumbago zeylanica quercetin is a flavonoid compound extracted from the plant.

It has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Quercetin has been shown to exhibit potent activity against various diseases, including cancer, cardiovascular disorders, and respiratory issues.

Its bioactive compounds have also been found to inhibit the growth of bacteria and viruses, making it a promising therapeutic agent for various medical applications.

2. Gallic acid

Plumbago zeylanica gallic acid is a bioactive compound extracted from the leaves and stems of this plant species.

It has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health conditions, including skin infections, wounds, and respiratory disorders. Gallic acid exhibits antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making it a potential agent against cancer, cardiovascular disease, and microbial infections.

Its unique bioactivity profile has sparked interest in its potential as a therapeutic agent in modern medicine.

3. Ellagic acid

Plumbago zeylanica ellagic acid is a phenolic compound that is abundant in the plant.

This antioxidant-rich acid has been extensively studied for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and anticancer properties. Ellagic acid has been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells, induce apoptosis (cell death), and protect against oxidative stress.

Its unique chemical structure makes it a promising candidate for the prevention and treatment of various diseases, including cancer, cardiovascular disease, and neurodegenerative disorders.

4. Kaempferol

Plumbago zeylanica kaempferol is a flavonoid compound extracted from the plant Plumbago zeylanica.

It has been found to possess various pharmacological activities, including antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. Kaempferol has been reported to exhibit potential therapeutic benefits in treating several diseases, including cardiovascular disorders, cancer, and neurological conditions.

5. Isorhapontigenin

Plumbago zeylanica isorhapontigenin is a bioactive compound extracted from the plant's leaves and stems.
