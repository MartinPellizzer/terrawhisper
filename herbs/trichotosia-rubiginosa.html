What is Trichotosia rubiginosa?

Trichotosia rubiginosa is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is an epiphytic orchid with a slender rhizome and 2-3 cm long pseudobulbs, each bearing a single ovate to elliptical leaf. The inflorescence is a raceme of 5-20 flowers, each measuring 1-2 cm in length, with a yellow-green sepals and petals, and a white and purple lip. The plant is native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia, specifically in Indonesia and Malaysia.

The species is characterized by its small size and delicate flowers, which bloom in the spring and summer months.
