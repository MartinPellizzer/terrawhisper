Alpinia Officinarum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Alpinia officinarum, commonly known as Chinese ginger or Kadsura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This herb is valued for its warming properties and is used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of colds and respiratory issues.

The health benefits of Alpinia officinarum include improving circulation, reducing inflammation, and enhancing immune function.

Its therapeutic actions involve antispasmodic, carminative, and analgesic effects, while its bioactive constituents include gingerols, shogaols, and essential oils that contribute to its medicinal properties.
