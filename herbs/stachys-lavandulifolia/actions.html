Lavender Mint (Stachys lavandulifolia) Actions

Lavender Mint exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and astringent properties. It also acts as a carminative, diuretic, and sedative, helping to relieve digestive discomfort and promote relaxation. Its bitter and tonic qualities support overall vitality, while its expectorant and stimulant effects aid respiratory and metabolic functions.
