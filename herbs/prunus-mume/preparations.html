10 Best Prunus Mume Preparations

The best medicinal preparations of Prunus mume are teas, decoctions, tinctures, mucillages, and lozenges, each offering unique therapeutic benefits.

Teas made from dried Prunus mume flowers or fruits are commonly used to soothe sore throats and aid digestion.

Decoctions involve simmering the herb to extract its active compounds, often used for respiratory support.

Mucillages, derived from the fruit's pulp, are valued for their demulcent properties to relieve irritation.

Lozenges made from Prunus mume are popular for their ability to ease coughs and promote oral health.
