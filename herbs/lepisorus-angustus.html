Lepisorus angustus

What's the taxonomical classification of Lepisorus angustus?

Lepisorus angustus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and is further categorized within the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the family Polypodiaceae, which contains the genus Lepisorus, eventually leading to its specific identification as the species angustus.
