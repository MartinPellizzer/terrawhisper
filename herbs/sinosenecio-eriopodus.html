Sinosenecio eriopodus

What's the taxonomical classification of Sinosenecio eriopodus?

Sinosenecio eriopodus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls into the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Sinosenecio and is distinguished as the species eriopodus.
