What's the taxonomical classification of Premna szemaoensis?

Premna szemaoensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized into the order Lamiales. As a member of the family Lamiaceae, this plant is situated within the genus Premna, where it is specifically identified by the species name szemaoensis.
