1. Heals heart issues

Olea europaea heals heart issues because it contains bioactive constituents such as oleic acid, polyphenols, and antioxidants that help reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular function.

These compounds support healthy cholesterol levels and reduce oxidative stress, which are key factors in heart disease.

Conditions like hypertension, atherosclerosis, and coronary artery disease can be alleviated through the consumption of olive products derived from this plant.

Additionally, Olea europaea has been linked to improved blood flow and reduced risk of heart attacks.

2. Lowers cholesterol levels

Olea europaea lowers cholesterol levels because it contains bioactive constituents such as oleic acid, polyphenols, and squalene, which help reduce low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol.

These compounds work by inhibiting the absorption of cholesterol in the intestines and promoting its excretion.

Conditions such as hypercholesterolemia and cardiovascular diseases are relieved with the cholesterol-lowering effects of Olea europaea.

Regular consumption of olive products derived from this plant can contribute to improved heart health.
