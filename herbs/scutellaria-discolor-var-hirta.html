Scutellaria discolor var. hirta

What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria discolor var. hirta?

Scutelariia discolor var. hirta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Lamiaceae, which places it within the genus Scutellaria. Ultimately, the specific botanical identity of this plant is defined by its unique designation as the species Scutellaria discolor var. hirta.
