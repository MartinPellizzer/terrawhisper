Laportea ovalifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Laportea ovalifolia?

Laportea ovalifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Rosales. This plant is a representative of the family Urticaceae, specifically situated within the genus Laportea, and is identified by its unique species designation, ovalifolia.

Its foliage is characterized by distinct venation patterns that extend from the central midrib toward the leaf edges.
