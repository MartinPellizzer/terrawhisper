What is Taenitis flabellivenia?

Taenitis flabellivenia is a fern species in the family Pteridaceae.

It is a perennial herb with fronds that are 0.5-1.5 meters long, bipinnate to tripinnate, and have a stipe that is 0.5-1 meter long. The rachis is covered with brown hairs and has 10-20 pairs of pinnae, which are 10-20 cm long and 2-4 cm wide. The laminae are pinnate with 2-4 pairs of pinnules, which are 1-3 cm long and 0.5-1 cm wide.

The sporangia are borne on the abaxial surface of the pinnules.
