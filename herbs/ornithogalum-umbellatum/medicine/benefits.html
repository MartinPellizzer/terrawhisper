10 Health Benefits Of Ornithogalum Umbellatum (Star-Of-Bethlehem)

Ornithogalum umbellatum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

These medicinal properties can be used to treat wounds, burns, and skin infections by reducing inflammation and preventing infection. Additionally, the plant's anti-irritant properties can soothe digestive issues and alleviate respiratory problems.

For example, a topical application of star-of-bethlehem can help speed up wound healing and reduce scarring, improving overall skin health and appearance.
