Bromus moellendorffianus

Bromus moellendorffianus, also known as Moellendorff's brome, is a species of grass native to China and parts of Asia, classified as a type of wild grass belonging to the family Poaceae.

The medicinal properties of Bromus moellendorffianus lie in its ability to reduce fever, alleviate coughs, and exhibit anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits associated with this plant include its potential to aid in respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its ability to lower blood pressure.

Bromus moellendorffianus is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and can be prepared through herbal infusions, decoctions, or as a tea, showcasing its versatility in various herbal preparations.
