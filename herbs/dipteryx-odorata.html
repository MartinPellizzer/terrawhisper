Dipteryx Odorata: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Dipteryx odorata, commonly known as copal, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America, known for its aromatic resin and leaves used in traditional medicine.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

Therapeutically, it may support respiratory health, aid digestion, and promote skin healing.

The bioactive constituents include compounds such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
