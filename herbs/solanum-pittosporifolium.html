What's the taxonomical classification of Solanum pittosporifolium?

Solanum pittosporifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the taxonomic order Solanales. It is a member of the family Solanaceae, falling under the genus Solanum, and is specifically identified by the species epithet pittosporifoliuum.
