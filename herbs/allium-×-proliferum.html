Allium × proliferum

Allium × proliferum, also known as the ornamental onion or proliferous onion, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Allium genus, which includes garlic, onions, and chives.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, making it effective in treating various health conditions, such as colds, fever, and respiratory issues.

The medicinal properties of Allium × proliferum are attributed to its active compounds, which have been found to have antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal effects, providing numerous health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and boosting the immune system.

The plant can be used in various herbal preparations, including teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be consumed internally or applied topically to alleviate symptoms and promote overall well-being.
