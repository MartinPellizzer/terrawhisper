Corypha umbraculifera

What's the taxonomical classification of Corypha umbraculifera?

Corypha umbraculifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Arecales. As a member of the family Arecaceae, it is placed in the genus Corypha, with the specific designation of the species umbraculifera.
