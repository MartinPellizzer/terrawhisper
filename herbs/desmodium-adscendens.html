Desmodium Adscendens: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Desmodium adscendens, commonly known as the climbing desmodium, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical regions, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is renowned for its ability to support digestive health and has been traditionally used to treat ailments such as dysentery and inflammation.

The therapeutic actions of Desmodium adscendens include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic effects, making it beneficial for gastrointestinal disorders.

It contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and tannins, which contribute to its pharmacological activities.
