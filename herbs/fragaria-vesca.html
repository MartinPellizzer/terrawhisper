Fragaria Vesca

Fragaria vesca, commonly known as the wild strawberry, has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties due to its rich content of antioxidants, vitamins, and bioactive compounds.

It contains high levels of flavonoids, ellagic acid, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. In folk medicine, it has been used to treat digestive issues, skin conditions, and as a diuretic. Modern research suggests that Fragaria vesca may support cardiovascular health and have potential anti-cancer properties.

Its use in herbal medicine continues to be explored for its therapeutic benefits in various health conditions.

Health Benefits

Fragaria vesca has several health benefits, such as being rich in antioxidants that help protect cells from damage caused by free radicals.

It is also a good source of vitamin C, which supports immune function and skin health. The plant contains various phytochemicals that may have anti-inflammatory and anti-cancer properties. Additionally, Fragaria vesca is low in calories and high in dietary fiber, making it a beneficial addition to a healthy diet.

Its natural compounds may also support digestive health and contribute to overall well-being.
