10 Possible Side Effects Of Swertia Chirata (Chiretta)

Swertia chirata has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and cardiovascular problems.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption, interaction with other medications, or individual sensitivity. For instance, nausea and diarrhea can lead to dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, and even hospitalization.

In severe cases, cardiovascular complications can cause heart attacks, strokes, and even death.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Swertia chirata if used imporperly.
