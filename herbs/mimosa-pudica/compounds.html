Sensitive Plant (Mimosa pudica) Compounds

The primary compounds in Mimosa pudica include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to its astringent effects. Phenolic acids and coumarin are also present, offering potential anti-inflammatory benefits. Saponins and glycosides may support digestive health, while terpenoids and essential oils provide aromatic and antimicrobial properties. Anthocyanins and alkaloids further contribute to its medicinal value.
