Sensitive Plant (Mimosa pudica) Actions

The Sensitive Plant, or Mimosa pudica, exhibits several therapeutic actions. It has anti-inflammatory and astringent properties, helping reduce swelling and tighten tissues. It also acts as a sedative and tonic, promoting calmness and overall vitality. Additionally, it functions as a diuretic and antispasmodic, aiding in fluid regulation and easing muscle spasms.
