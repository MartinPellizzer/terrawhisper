Gynostemma yixingense

What's the taxonomical classification of Gynostemma yixingense?

Gymnostemma yixingense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Gymnostemma, and its unique species designation, yixingense.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Gynostemma yixingense has climbing or creeping stems that produce opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with entire margins and acute apices.

The foliage typically features a smooth texture and a light green coloration on both surfaces. Small, white, tubular flowers emerge from the leaf axils, characterized by five fused petals. The inflorescence is often solitary or arranged in small clusters along the stem.

Its fruit develops into a small, globose capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.
