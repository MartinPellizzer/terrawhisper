10 Possible Side Effects Of Artemisia Tridentata (Sagebrush)

Artemisia tridentata has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and stomach upset.

These side effects are caused by the herb's high concentration of essential oils, particularly thujone.

If left untreated, skin irritation can lead to infections and scarring, while stomach upset can worsen existing digestive issues, making everyday activities and tasks more challenging for individuals.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Artemisia tridentata if used imporperly.
