Big Sagebrush (Artemisia tridentata) Compounds

The primary compounds in Big Sagebrush include terpenoids, which contribute to its aromatic properties, and essential oils, which are responsible for its distinctive scent. Flavonoids and phenolic acids provide antioxidant benefits, while tannins and saponins have astringent and anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin, glycosides, and alkaloids add to its complex pharmacological profile.
