What's the taxonomical classification of Roscoea tibetica?

Roscoea tibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further classified into the family Zingiberaceae, which places it among the ginger plants. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Roscoea and is specifically known by the species name tibetica.
