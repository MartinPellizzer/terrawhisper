10 Precautions To Take When Using Taraxacum Mongolicum (Mongolian Dandelion)

Taraxacum mongolicum, also known as Mongolian dandelion, has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional before using it, especially if you have liver or kidney issues.

It's crucial to be cautious as excessive consumption can lead to allergic reactions, such as itching, and increase the risk of interactions with medications.

For example, not taking these precautions may result in adverse side effects like increased bleeding when taking anticoagulant medications.
