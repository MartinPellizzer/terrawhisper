10 Medicinal Constituents Of Taraxacum Mongolicum (Mongolian Dandelion)

Taraxacum mongolicum has active constituents such as inulin, sesquiterpene lactones, and flavonoids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making the plant a potential remedy for various health issues. Inulin, for instance, can help regulate blood sugar levels and promote digestive health. Flavonoids may reduce oxidative stress and improve cardiovascular function.

These properties can lead to improved quality of life, particularly for individuals with diabetes, digestive disorders, or heart health concerns.
