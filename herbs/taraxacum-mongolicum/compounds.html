Taraxacum Dandelion (Taraxacum mongolicum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Taraxacum mongolicum include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarins contribute to its anti-inflammatory and anticoagulant actions, while terpenoids provide antimicrobial benefits. Saponins and tannins support digestive health, and glycosides, along with essential oils and anthocyanins, contribute to its overall therapeutic profile. Alkaloids may enhance its pharmacological activity.
