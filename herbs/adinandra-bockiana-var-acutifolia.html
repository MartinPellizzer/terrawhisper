Adinandra bockiana var. acutifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Adinandra bockiana var. acutifolia?

Adinandra bockiana var. acutifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Ericales and falls into the family Pentaphylacaceae. Specifically, this plant is a variety of the species Adinandra bockiana, which is situated within the genus Adinandra.
