1. Reduces blood pressure

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a chronic condition characterized by elevated force of blood against artery walls.

This condition can result from factors such as poor diet, lack of physical activity, genetic predisposition, or stress.

It commonly affects older adults, individuals with obesity, and those with a family history of cardiovascular diseases.

Cinnamomum verum, or true cinnamon, has been studied for its potential to reduce blood pressure due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Regular consumption of cinnamon may help lower systolic and diastolic readings, supporting overall cardiovascular health.

How this herb helps with reduces blood pressure?

Cinnamomum verum reduces blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds help relax blood vessels and improve blood flow. Alkaloids like cinnamic acid have been shown to have vasodilatory effects. Flavonoids in cinnamon possess antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that support cardiovascular health.

Together, these compounds contribute to the plant's ability to lower blood pressure naturally.
