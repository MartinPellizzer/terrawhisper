What is Phyllocactus russellianus?

Phyllocactus russellianus is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is a climbing epiphyte with long, cylindrical stems that are up to 30 cm in length and 5 cm in diameter. The stems are covered in 2-4 cm long, fleshy, linear to oblanceolate leaves that are pale green in color. The plant produces funnel-shaped flowers that are white with a yellow or pink tint on the inside and are up to 10 cm in diameter.

The flowers are nocturnal and are followed by red, berry-like fruits that are up to 6 cm in length.
