What's the taxonomical classification of Primula cicutariifolia?

Primula cicutariifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Primulaceae, specifically within the genus Primula, where it is identified by the unique species designation cicutariifolia.
