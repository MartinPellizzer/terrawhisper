10 Medicinal Preparations Of Hydrastis Canadensis (Goldenseal)

Hydrastis canadensis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and capsules.

These preparations are often used to treat respiratory and gastrointestinal issues, as well as to boost the immune system.

Goldenseal root is commonly made into a tea to treat sinus infections and digestive problems, while its tincture is used to treat wounds and skin conditions.
