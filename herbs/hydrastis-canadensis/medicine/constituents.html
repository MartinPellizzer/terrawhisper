10 Medicinal Constituents Of Hydrastis Canadensis (Goldenseal)

These compounds possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to the plant's medicinal value. Its constituents may also help to alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome and support skin health.
