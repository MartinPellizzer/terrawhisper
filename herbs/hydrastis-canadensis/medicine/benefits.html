Overall, goldenseal's immune-boosting properties make it a valuable herb for supporting overall health and well-being.

By inhibiting NF-kB, goldenseal helps to reduce inflammation, making it a valuable herb for treating conditions such as arthritis, skin irritations, and respiratory infections.

Additionally, goldenseal has been shown to have antimicrobial and immune-boosting properties, further enhancing its ability to reduce inflammation and promote overall health and well-being.
