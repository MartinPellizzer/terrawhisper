10 Possible Side Effects Of Momordica Charantia (Bitter Melon)

Momordica charantia has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and electrolyte imbalances.

These side effects can occur due to high doses, interactions with medications, or individual sensitivities.

For instance, gastrointestinal issues can lead to digestive problems, nausea, and vomiting, which can worsen the quality of life by interfering with daily activities and affecting overall health and well-being.
