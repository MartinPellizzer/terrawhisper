Sindora klaineana

What's the taxonomical classification of Sindora klaineana?

Sindora klaineana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Fabales. It is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it among the legumes, and is specifically categorized under the genus Sindora. Finally, its unique scientific identity is established by its specific epithet, making its full taxonomic designation Sindora klaineana.
