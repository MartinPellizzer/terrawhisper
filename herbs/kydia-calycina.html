What's the taxonomical classification of Kydia calycina?

Kydia calycina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Malvaceae, placing it within the same botanical group as many mallows. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Kydia and its unique species designation, calycina.
