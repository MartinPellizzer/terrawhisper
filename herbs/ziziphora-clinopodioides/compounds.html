Summer Savory (Ziziphora clinopodioides) Compounds

The primary compounds in Summer Savory include essential oil, which contributes to its aromatic profile, and flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Terpenoids and phenolic acids also play roles in its medicinal effects, while coumarin and tannins contribute to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. Glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further support its use in herbal medicine.

Summer Savory (Ziziphora clinopodioides) Monograph

1. Essential oil

The essential oil of Summer Savory (Ziziphora clinopodioides) contains compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. These components contribute to the plant's traditional use in treating digestive issues and respiratory infections.

Flavonoids in Ziziphora clinopodioides contribute to its medicinal properties by exhibiting antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. These compounds help protect cells from damage and may support respiratory and digestive health.
