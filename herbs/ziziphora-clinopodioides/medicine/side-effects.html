10 Possible Side Effects Of Ziziphora Clinopodioides (Persian Balm)

Ziziphora clinopodioides, also known as Persian balm, has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal irritation, nausea, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption, allergic sensitivity, or interaction with other medications. For instance, individuals who experience severe nausea may find it difficult to eat and maintain a healthy diet, leading to weight loss and fatigue.

Allergic reactions can also worsen respiratory conditions, making it challenging for people to perform daily activities.
