Green Tea (Thea sinensis) Actions

Green Tea (Thea sinensis) acts as a tonic to enhance vitality and support overall health. It serves as a mild stimulant, improving mental alertness. Its anti-inflammatory properties help reduce swelling and irritation. Additionally, it functions as a diuretic, aiding in fluid balance, and as an expectorant, easing respiratory congestion.
