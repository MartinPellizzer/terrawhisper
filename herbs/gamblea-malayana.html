What is Gamblea malayana?

Gamblea malayana is a species of plant in the family Araliaceae.

It is an evergreen shrub with a woody stem, growing up to 6 meters tall. The leaves are alternate, pinnate, and have 3-5 pairs of leaflets, with the terminal leaflet being smaller. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant is native to Southeast Asia, where it is found in tropical forests and woodland edges.
