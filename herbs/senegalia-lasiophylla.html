What is Senegalia lasiophylla?

Senegalia lasiophylla is a perennial shrub or small tree that belongs to the family Fabaceae.

It has a height of 2-6 meters, with a diametric spread of 1-3 meters. The plant has a straight or slightly curved stem, with a greyish-brown bark and a smooth, glabrous surface. The leaves are bipinnate, with 6-12 pairs of pinnae, each bearing 10-20 pairs of leaflets that are linear, lanceolate, and 6-15 mm long.

The leaves are also glabrous and arranged oppositely on the stem.
