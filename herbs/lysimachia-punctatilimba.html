What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia punctatilimba?

Lysimachia punctatilimba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of biological classification, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Lysimachia and is specifically distinguished by its species name, punctatilimba.
