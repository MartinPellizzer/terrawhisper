Sida alnifolia var. obovata

What's the taxonomical classification of Sida alnifolia var. obovata?

Sida alnifolia var. obovata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Malvales and falls into the family Malvaceae. Specifically, this plant is a variety of the genus Sida, identified by its scientific species name, Sida alnifolia var. obovata.
