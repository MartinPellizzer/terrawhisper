Salvia przewalskii var. mandarinorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia przewalskii var. mandarinorum?

Salvia przewalskii var. mandarinorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. This specific variety is a member of the genus Salvia, falling under the scientific name Salvia przewalskii var. mandarinorum.
