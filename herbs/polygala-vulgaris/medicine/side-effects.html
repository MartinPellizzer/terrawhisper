10 Possible Side Effects Of Polygala Vulgaris (Milkwort)

Polygala vulgaris has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

Causes of these side effects include taking excessive doses, combining with other herbs, and individual sensitivity.

For instance, severe gastrointestinal issues can lead to weight loss, fatigue, and decreased productivity, while allergic reactions can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening condition.

The plant contains polygalasaponins and alkaloids, which can trigger an immune response in sensitive people, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, the pollen of Polygala vulgaris is also known to exacerbate allergies in those who are already predisposed, further increasing the risk of adverse reactions.
