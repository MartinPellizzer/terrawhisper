10 Health Benefits Of Alhagi Maurorum (Persian Silk)

Alhagi maurorum, also known as persian silk, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These medicinal properties help to soothe digestive issues and protect against cell damage. For example, the plant's extracts have been used to treat gastrointestinal disorders, such as ulcers and colitis, improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.

Its benefits also contribute to its use in wound healing and skin care treatments.
