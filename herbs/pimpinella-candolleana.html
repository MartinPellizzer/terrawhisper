What's the taxonomical classification of Pimpinella candolleana?

Pimpinella candolleana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Apiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pimpinella, and its species designation, candolleana.
