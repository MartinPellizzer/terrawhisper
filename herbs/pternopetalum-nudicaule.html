What's the taxonomical classification of Pternopetalum nudicaule?

Pternopetalum nudicaule belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Apiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pternopetalum, and its unique species designation, nudicaule.
