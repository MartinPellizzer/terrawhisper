Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia

Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia, also known as the "Wild Cup," is a lesser-known variety of the Banisteriopsis caapi vine, a plant native to the Amazon rainforest in South America.

The medicinal properties of Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia lie in its unique combination of alkaloids, such as harmine and tetrahydroharmine, which have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions.

This plant has been associated with several health benefits, including its potential to reduce inflammation, improve blood circulation, and exhibit neuroprotective properties.

In herbal preparations, Banisteria stellaris var. intermedia is often combined with other plants, such as the Psychotria viridis leaf, to create ayahuasca, a traditional Amazonian brew used for spiritual and medicinal purposes.
