What to know about Chamomilla recutita before using it medicinally

Chamomilla recutita, commonly known as German chamomile, is a popular medicinal herb used to alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness, as well as soothe digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and upset stomachs.

This herb is also known to provide relief from menstrual cramps, headaches, and skin irritations. The main health benefits of German chamomile include its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and anti-anxiety properties, which contribute to its calming effects on the mind and body. The herb's therapeutic properties are primarily attributed to the presence of bioactive compounds such as apigenin, luteolin, and chamazulene, which possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant activities.

People often prepare German chamomile as a tea by infusing dried flowers in hot water, which is then consumed to reap its medicinal benefits.
