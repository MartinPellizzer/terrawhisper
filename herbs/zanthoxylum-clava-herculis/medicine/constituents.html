10 Medicinal Constituents Of Zanthoxylum Clava-Herculis (Prickly Ash)

Zanthoxylum clava-herculis has active constituents such as isorhapontin, isorhapontigenin, and ferulic acid.

These constituents exhibit analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties. They can help alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and prevent infections. For instance, people suffering from arthritis may experience relief from pain and improved joint mobility due to the anti-inflammatory effects of ferulic acid.

Additionally, the antimicrobial properties of isorhapontin and isorhapontigenin can help prevent infections and promote wound healing.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Zanthoxylum clava-herculis.
