Spineapple (Zanthoxylum clava-herculis) Compounds

The primary compounds in Spineapple include tannin, which has astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to anti-inflammatory actions, while coumarin may have anticoagulant properties. Alkaloids and essential oils add to its medicinal complexity, offering various therapeutic benefits.
