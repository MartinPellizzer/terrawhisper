Diphasiastrum complanatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic position is further refined within the family Lycopodiaceae, placing it specifically in the genus Diphasiastrum. Ultimately, the plant is identified at the species level as complanatum.
