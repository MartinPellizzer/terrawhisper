10 Best Capsella Bursa-Pastoris Health Benefits

Capsella bursa-pastoris, commonly known as shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant that has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

The plant contains compounds like flavonoids and tannins, which may help reduce inflammation and support the immune system. Historically, it has been used to ease menstrual discomfort and promote urinary tract health due to its diuretic properties.

Additionally, some studies suggest that shepherd's purse may have potential in supporting cardiovascular health and reducing oxidative stress in the body.
