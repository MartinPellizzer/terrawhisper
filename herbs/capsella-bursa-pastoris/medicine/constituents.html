10 Medicinal Constituents Of Capsella Bursa-Pastoris (Shepherd'S Purse)

Capsella bursa-pastoris has active constituents such as isothiocyanates, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic properties, making shepherd's purse a valuable remedy for menstrual cramps, bleeding, and respiratory issues. The isothiocyanates can help reduce inflammation and improve digestion, while the flavonoids can improve circulation and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

This plant has been used to alleviate symptoms of menopause, endometriosis, and heavy menstrual bleeding.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Capsella bursa-pastoris.
