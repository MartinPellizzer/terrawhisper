What's the taxonomical classification of Yulania campbellii?

Yulania campbellii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Magnoliales. It is further categorized under the family Magnoliaceae, which places it within the genus Yulania, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species campbellii.

      What are the herbal preparations of Yulania campbellii?

      Yulania campbellii is prepared primarily as a decoction made by boiling the dried leaves and stems in water to extract bioactive compounds. This liquid infusion is often consumed warm to facilitate the absorption of its medicinal properties. Some traditional practitioners also process the plant material into a fine powder for easier measurement and administration.
