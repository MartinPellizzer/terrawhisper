Possible Side Effects of Schefflera montana

Schefflera montana can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory issues, particularly when ingested or inhaled.

The sap of the plant can cause skin redness, itching, and even blisters in some individuals.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's volatile oils may also exacerbate conditions like asthma and other respiratory conditions.
