What's the taxonomical classification of Celosia laxa?

Celosia laxa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Amaranthaceae, which places it within the genus Celosia, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Celosia laxa.
