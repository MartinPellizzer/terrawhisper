Taxus wallichiana var. mairei

What's the taxonomical classification of Taxus wallichiana var. mairei?

Taxus wallichiana var. mairei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the order Pinales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Taxaceae and the genus Taxus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety Taxus wallichiana var. mairei.
