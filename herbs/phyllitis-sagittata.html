Phyllitis sagittata

Phyllitis sagittata, commonly known as the ladder-fronded spleenwort or the ladder spleenwort, is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Phyllitis sagittata are attributed to its ability to modulate the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antiviral activity.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
