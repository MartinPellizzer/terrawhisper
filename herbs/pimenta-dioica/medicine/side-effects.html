10 Possible Side Effects Of Pimenta Dioica (Allspice)

These side effects can worsen the lives of individuals with pre-existing stomach conditions, such as ulcers or irritable bowel syndrome, making everyday activities challenging and increasing the risk of dehydration and electrolyte imbalances.
