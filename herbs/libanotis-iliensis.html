Libanotis iliensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Libanotis iliensis?

Libanotis iliensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Apiales. It is a member of the family Apiaceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Libanotis and the species iliensis.
