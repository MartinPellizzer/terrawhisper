What's the taxonomical classification of Polycarpaea corymbosa?

Polycarpaea corymbosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Caryophyllales and belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae. The plant is further identified within the genus Polycarpaea, specifically designated as the species corymbosa.
