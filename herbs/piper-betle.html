Piper Betle: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Piper betle, commonly known as betel leaf, is a plant native to Southeast Asia, widely used in traditional medicine and cultural practices.

This herb is valued for its aromatic leaves, which contain various bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins.

The health benefits of Piper betle include aiding digestion, reducing oral bacteria, and promoting oral hygiene.

Its therapeutic actions encompass anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it a popular remedy in Ayurvedic and traditional healing systems.
