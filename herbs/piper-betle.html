Piper Betle Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Piper betle, commonly known as betel leaf, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia, widely used in traditional medicine and cultural practices.

This herb is valued for its aromatic leaves, which contain various bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and essential oils that contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The health benefits of betel leaf include aiding digestion, improving oral health, and providing antioxidant support.

It can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or chewed fresh, making it a versatile herbal remedy.
