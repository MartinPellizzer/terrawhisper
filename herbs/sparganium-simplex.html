What's the taxonomical classification of Sparganium simplex?

Sparganium simplex belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Poales and the family Typhaceae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Sparganium, and its species designation, simplex.
