Rhodoleia championii

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhodoleia championii?

Rhodoleia championii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Saxifrages (Saxifragales) and belongs to the family Hamamelidaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Rhodoleia, and its unique species designation, championii.
