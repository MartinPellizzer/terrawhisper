Damnacanthus tsaii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Damnacanthus tsaii has elongated, lanceolate leaves with serrated margins and a woody stem structure that supports its upright growth habit. The foliage typically exhibits a deep green color and a slightly leathery texture to aid in moisture retention. Small, clustered flowers emerge from the axillary nodes, displaying a delicate petal arrangement. The root system is fibrous and deeply embedded within the soil to provide stability for the plant. These morphological features collectively allow the species to thrive in its specific ecological niche.
