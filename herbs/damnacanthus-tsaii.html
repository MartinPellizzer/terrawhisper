What's the taxonomical classification of Damnacanthus tsaii?

Damnacanthus tsaii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Gentianales. This plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, specifically belonging to the genus Damnacanthus, with the species designation tsaii.
