Codonopsis cardiophylla

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Codonopsis cardiophylla has large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent, pale veins and a glossy texture. These foliage layers grow from a rhizomatous base, creating a lush and spreading habit. The leaf margins are typically smooth, contributing to the distinct cordate silhouette of the plant. During its flowering period, it produces small, delicate white flowers that emerge from the center of the foliage. The overall structure provides a dense, tropical appearance suitable for shaded environments.
