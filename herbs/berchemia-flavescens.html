What's the taxonomical classification of Berchemia flavescens?

Berchemia flavescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Rosales, it is categorized under the family Rhamnaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Berchemia, and its specific epithet, flavescens.
