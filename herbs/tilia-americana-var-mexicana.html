Tilia americana var. mexicana

Tilia americana var. mexicana, also known as the Mexican American Linden, is a deciduous tree native to the Americas, belonging to the Tilia genus of the family Malvaceae.

The medicinal properties of Tilia americana var. mexicana include its anti-inflammatory, sedative, and antispasmodic properties, which are primarily derived from its flowers and leaves.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to alleviate anxiety and insomnia, reduce stress and promote relaxation, as well as its use as a natural remedy for respiratory issues such as colds and bronchitis.
