Coleus triangularis

Coleus triangularis, also known as Painted Nettle, is a plant species that belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its unique leaf shape and vibrant colors.

The leaves of this plant have been used in traditional medicine due to their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

Coleus triangularis has been used to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, thanks to its health benefits, which include reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing.

The plant can be prepared in various herbal forms, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, allowing for a range of uses in herbal medicine.
