What's the taxonomical classification of Mitreola pedicellata?

Mitreola pedicellaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Gentianaales and the family Loganiaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Mitreola, and its unique species designation, pedicellaata.
