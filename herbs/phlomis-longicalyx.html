What's the taxonomical classification of Phlomis longicalyx?

Phlomis longicalyx belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Lamiales and falls within the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is identified under the genus Phlomis, with its specific designation being the species longicalyx.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its most distinguishing feature is the long, tubular calyx that supports the dense, verticillate clusters of flowers. The corolla is typically yellowish or pale, emerging from the protective, elongated calyx structure.
