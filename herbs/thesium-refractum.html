Thesium refractum

What's the taxonomical classification of Thesium refractum?

Thesium refractum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Santalales and is a member of the family Santalaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Thesium and the species refractum.
