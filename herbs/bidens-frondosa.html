What are the pharmacological activities of Bidens frondosa?

This plant has tyrosinase inhibitory activity. This specific pharmacological action allows the plant extracts to potentially reduce melanin production in skin cells. By inhibiting the tyrosinase enzyme, the plant may serve as a natural agent for treating hyperpigmentation or skin discoloration. Such biochemical properties suggest its potential utility in the development of dermatological treatments. This mechanism makes Bidens frondosa a subject of interest for cosmetic and medicinal research.
