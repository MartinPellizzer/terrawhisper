What is Wedelia jugata?

Wedelia jugata is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb with a woody rootstock and slender stems, typically growing up to 60 cm in height. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate to elliptic in shape, measuring 2-7 cm in length and 1-4 cm in width, with serrate margins and a cuneate base. The plant bears small, yellow flowerheads, 1-2 cm in diameter, with 10-15 ray florets and 20-30 disc florets, arranged in a capitulum.

The achenes are 2-3 mm long, with a short beak and a hairy pappus.
