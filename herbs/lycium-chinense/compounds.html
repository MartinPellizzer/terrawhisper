Chinese Wolfberry (Lycium chinense) Compounds

The primary compounds in Chinese Wolfberry include flavonoids, which act as antioxidants, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and glycosides contribute to its astringent taste and potential health benefits. Terpenoids and saponins may support immune function, while coumarin and anthocyanin provide additional antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. Alkaloids and essential oils contribute to its medicinal properties and aroma.

Flavonoids are a group of bioactive compounds found in Chinese Wolfberry (Lycium chinense).
