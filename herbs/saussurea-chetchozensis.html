Saussurea chetchozensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea chetchozensis?

Saussurea chetchozensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Saussurea and the species cretchozensis.

Scientific studies on its specific toxicological profile are limited, meaning any use should be approached with extreme caution.
