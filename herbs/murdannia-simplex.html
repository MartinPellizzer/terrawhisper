Murdannia simplex

What's the taxonomical classification of Murdannia simplex?

Murdannia simplex belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Commelinares and the family Commelinaeae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus, Murdannia, and its specific species name, simplex.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Some users may experience swelling or rashes if the sap or crushed leaves touch the skin. It is advisable to avoid handling the plant without gloves to prevent these potential dermatological issues. Monitoring for any signs of an adverse reaction is important for those working closely with this species.
