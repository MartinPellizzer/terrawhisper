What's the taxonomical classification of Heterostemma esquirolii?

Heterostemma esquirolii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Gentianaales. This specific plant is a part of the family Apocynaceae, which places it within the genus Heterostemma, ultimately identifying it as the species Heterostemma esquirolii.
