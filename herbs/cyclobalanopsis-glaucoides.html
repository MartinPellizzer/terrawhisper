What's the taxonomical classification of Cyclobalanopsis glaucoides?

Cyclobalanopsis glaucoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Fagales. It is further classified under the family Fagaceae, which places it among a group of diverse woody plants. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Cyclobalanopsis, with the specific epithet glaucoides denoting its unique species designation.
