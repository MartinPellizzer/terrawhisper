Asarum splendens

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Asarum splendens has heart-shaped, leathery leaves that are dark green with prominent, lighter veins and a smooth, entire margin. These perennial plants typically grow in dense, low-lying mats across the forest floor. The foliage features a slightly glossy texture that distinguishes it from other members of the genus. Small, brownish, bell-shaped flowers emerge from the soil near the base of the plant during the blooming season. The underground structure consists of creeping rhizomes that allow the plant to spread vegetatively.
