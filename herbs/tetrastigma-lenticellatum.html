What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma lenticellatum?

Tetrastigma lenticellatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Vitales. As a member of the family Vitaceae, it is placed within the genus Tetrastigma, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species lenticellatum.
