Mentzelia montana

Mentzelia montana, also known as big sagebrush or gray sage, is a perennial herb that grows in the southwestern United States and Mexico.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it useful for treating wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues.

The health benefits of Mentzelia montana include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve respiratory health, and boost the immune system.

Herbal preparations of Mentzelia montana can be made into teas, tinctures, or salves, and are often used to treat colds, coughs, and skin irritations.
