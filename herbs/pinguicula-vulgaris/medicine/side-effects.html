10 Possible Side Effects Of Pinguicula Vulgaris (Butterwort)

Pinguicula vulgaris has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive issues, skin irritation, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by ingesting the plant in large amounts or applying it to broken skin without proper preparation.

For instance, frequent digestive issues can lead to malnutrition and fatigue, causing individuals to struggle with everyday activities, while severe skin irritation can result in permanent scarring, significantly affecting one's self-esteem and confidence.
