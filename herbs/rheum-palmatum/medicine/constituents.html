10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rheum Palmatum (Chinese Rhubarb)

Rheum palmatum, also known as Chinese rhubarb, has active constituents such as emodin, rhein, and anthraquinone derivatives.

These compounds possess laxative and anti-inflammatory properties, which have been used to treat digestive issues and skin conditions.

The medicinal properties of Chinese rhubarb can improve people's lives by relieving constipation and hemorrhoids, promoting wound healing, and soothing skin irritations, ultimately enhancing overall health and well-being.
