It is widely used for its medicinal properties and is valued for its ability to treat various health issues.

Possible Side Effects of cassia angustifolia

Cassia angustifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea, due to its emetic properties that stimulate the vomiting center in the brain.

Prolonged use of the herb can also lead to kidney damage and electrolyte imbalances, which can be life-threatening if left untreated.

Additionally, Cassia angustifolia can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, causing adverse reactions like bleeding and hypoglycemia.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Cassia angustifolia to avoid these potential side effects.
