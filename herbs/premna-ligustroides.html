Premna ligustroides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Lamiales and falls within the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Premna, with its specific identification being the species ligustroides.
