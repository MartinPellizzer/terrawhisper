What's the taxonomical classification of Premna ligustroides?

Premna ligustroides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Lamiales and falls within the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Premna, with its specific identification being the species ligustroides.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Premna ligustroides can cause adverse reactions such as skin irritation, gastrointestinal distress, or allergic hypersensitivity depending on the method of administration and individual sensitivity. There is also a lack of extensive clinical data regarding long-term toxicity and potential interactions with pharmaceutical drugs. Consequently, cautious application and consultation with a healthcare professional are advised to mitigate these risks.
