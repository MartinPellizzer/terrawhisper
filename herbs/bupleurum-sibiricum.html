What's the taxonomical classification of Bupleurum sibiricum?

Bupleurum sibiricum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Apiales. It is a member of the family Apiaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Bupleurum, ultimately reaching its designation as the species sibiricum.

Its inflorescence consists of small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in compact, umbel-like clusters. Each individual flower possesses five petals and is supported by multiple fine rays.
