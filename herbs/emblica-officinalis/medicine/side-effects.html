10 Possible Side Effects Of Emblica Officinalis (Amla)

Embilica officinalis has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, nausea, and stomach cramps.

These side effects may be caused by consuming excessive amounts or taking it in combination with other medications. For instance, someone who already has stomach ulcers may experience worsening pain and discomfort if they take amla without medical supervision.

In severe cases, it may lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalance, further complicating their health condition.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Emblica officinalis if used imporperly.
