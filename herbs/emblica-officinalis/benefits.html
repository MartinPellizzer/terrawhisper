Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and antioxidants in the body, leading to cellular damage.

This imbalance can result from environmental toxins, poor diet, or chronic diseases.

It is often referred to as oxidative damage or reactive oxygen species overload.

People experiencing chronic inflammation, aging, or metabolic disorders are more prone to this condition.

The emblica officinalis plant helps reduce oxidative stress by boosting the body's antioxidant defenses.

How this herb helps with reduces oxidative stress?

Emblica officinalis reduces oxidative stress because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds act as powerful antioxidants, neutralizing free radicals in the body. The presence of these natural antioxidants helps protect cells from damage caused by oxidative stress. Alkaloids contribute to the plant's ability to modulate inflammatory responses.

Flavonoids enhance the body's antioxidant defense system, supporting overall cellular health.
