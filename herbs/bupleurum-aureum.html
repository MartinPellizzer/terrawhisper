What's the taxonomical classification of Bupleurum aureum?

Bupleurum aureum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, specifically falling within the genus Bupleurum, and is identified by its unique species name, aureum.
