What's the taxonomical classification of Heteropanax fragrans?

Heteropanax fragrans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Araliaceae, which is further divided into the genus Heteropanax, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species fragrans.
