Oxycedrus Juniper (Juniperus oxycedrus) Actions

Oxycedrus Juniper exhibits several key therapeutic actions. It acts as a diuretic to support kidney function and reduce fluid retention. Its antispasmodic and carminative properties help relieve gastrointestinal spasms and gas. Additionally, it has astringent and tonic effects, aiding in tissue repair and overall vitality. The plant also serves as an expectorant, stimulant, and anti-inflammatory agent, while offering mild sedative benefits.
