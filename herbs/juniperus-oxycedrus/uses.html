Oxycedrus Juniper (Juniperus oxycedrus) Uses

Oxycedrus Juniper is used to treat urinary tract infections and bladder inflammation due to its antimicrobial properties. It also helps with kidney stones and urinary tract infections by promoting urine flow. The plant is applied for arthritis, gout, and joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory effects. It may also support respiratory health by alleviating coughs and other respiratory infections.

Oxycedrus Juniper (Juniperus oxycedrus) Monograph

1. Urinary tract infection

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to support urinary tract health. Its antimicrobial properties help combat bacterial infections, while its diuretic effects promote urine flow. It is often used in herbal blends to alleviate symptoms of urinary tract infections and support overall renal function.

2. Kidney stones

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to support kidney health and aid in the treatment of kidney stones. Its diuretic properties help flush out toxins and reduce urinary tract obstruction, while its anti-inflammatory effects may prevent stone formation. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to promote urinary flow and alleviate discomfort associated with kidney stones.

3. Urinary tract infections

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to support urinary tract health. 5. Arthritis

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to alleviate arthritis symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. 6. Gout

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to support joint health and reduce inflammatory conditions like gout. Its anti-inflammatory and diuretic properties may help alleviate symptoms by reducing uric acid buildup and easing pain and swelling in affected joints.

7. Joint pain

Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to alleviate joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. Oxycedrus Juniper has been traditionally used to support respiratory health. Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties help alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections.
