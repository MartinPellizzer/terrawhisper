Syringa reticulata subsp. amurensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Syringa reticulata subsp. amurensis?

Syringa reticulata subsp. amurensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Oleaceae. Taxonomically, it is situated within the genus Syringa, specifically identified by the species name Syringa.
