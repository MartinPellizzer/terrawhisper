Thalictrum squarrosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Thalictrum, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species squarrosum.
