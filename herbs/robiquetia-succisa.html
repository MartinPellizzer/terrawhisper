What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Robiquetia succisa has an epiphytic growth habit characterized by slender, reed-like pseudobulbs that are compressed and often slightly curved.

The inflorescence is a terminal raceme that produces numerous small, fragrant flowers with delicate white or pale greenish petals. Each individual flower features a complex labellum that is often intricately shaped to facilitate pollination.
