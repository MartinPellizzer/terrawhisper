Cassia Senna

The health benefits of Cassia senna include its ability to stimulate bowel movements, relieve constipation, and support the digestive system.

The therapeutic actions of Cassia senna include its purgative, laxative, and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe digestive issues and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.

The bioactive constituents of Cassia senna include anthraquinone glycosides, such as sennosides, which are responsible for its laxative effects and have been shown to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Possible Side Effects of cassia senna

Cassia senna can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

In severe cases, cassia senna can also cause electrolyte imbalances, muscle weakness, and cardiovascular problems.
