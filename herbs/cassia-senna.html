Cassia Senna: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cassia senna, commonly known as Indian senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions, widely used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.

This herb is known for its laxative effects and is often used to treat constipation and promote digestive health.

The health benefits of Cassia senna include supporting bowel movements, reducing inflammation, and aiding in detoxification.

Its therapeutic actions involve mild laxation, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, while its bioactive constituents include alkaloids, flavonoids, and sennosides, which contribute to its medicinal value.
