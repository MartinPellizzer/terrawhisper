10 Possible Side Effects Of Hemidesmus Indicus (Indian Sarsaparilla)

Hemidesmus indicus, also known as Indian sarsaparilla, has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, dizziness, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur when the herb is taken in excessive amounts or combined with other medications.

For instance, excessive use can lead to stomach cramps, diarrhea, and vomiting, which can cause dehydration and further health complications, affecting one's daily activities and overall quality of life.
