How this herb helps with reduces eye pressure?

These compounds act as cholinergic agonists, stimulating the muscarinic receptors in the eye. This stimulation helps to increase the outflow of aqueous humor, thereby lowering intraocular pressure. The plant's alkaloid, pilocarpine, is particularly effective in this regard.

As a result, Pilocarpus jaborandi is often used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic effects on glaucoma and other eye conditions.
