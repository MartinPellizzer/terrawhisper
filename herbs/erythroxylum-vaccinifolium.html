Redleaf Tobacco (Erythroxylum vaccinifolium)

Redleaf Tobacco (Erythroxylum vaccinifolium) is a member of the Erythroxylaceae family, native to Central America, Northern South America, and Caribbean. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
