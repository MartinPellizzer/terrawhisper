Hyoscyamus niger subsp. auriculatus

Hyoscyamus niger subsp.

auriculatus, also known as Syrian henbane or auriculated henbane, is a subspecies of the herb Hyoscyamus niger, belonging to the nightshade family.

Traditionally, this plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include its sedative, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Hyoscyamus niger subsp.

auriculatus include its ability to alleviate anxiety, insomnia, and muscle spasms, as well as its potential to reduce pain and inflammation.

The plant can be prepared as a tincture, tea, or infusion, with caution advised due to its potential toxicity and side effects.
