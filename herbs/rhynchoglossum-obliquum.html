What's the taxonomical classification of Rhynchoglossum obliquum?

Rhynchoglossum obliquum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Rhynchoglossum, with its unique designation being the species obliquum.
