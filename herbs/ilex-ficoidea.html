What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex ficoidea?

Ilex ficoidea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Aquifoliales and the family Aquifoliaceae. Finally, this plant is identified by its genus, Ilex, and its specific species designation, ficoidea.
