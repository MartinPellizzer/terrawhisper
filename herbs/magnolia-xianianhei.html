What is Magnolia xianianhei?

Magnolia xianianhei is a deciduous tree species endemic to China, belonging to the family Magnoliaceae.

It grows up to 12 meters in height, with a broad, rounded crown and a straight, columnar trunk. The leaves are elliptical, 7-16 centimeters long, and 2-4 centimeters wide, with a leathery texture and a pointed tip. The flowers are white, 10-15 centimeters in diameter, with nine petals and 18-21 stamens, and appear in late spring.

The fruit is a red, cone-shaped structure, 10-15 centimeters long, with three carpels and containing many seeds.

What are the therapeutic properties this herb?

The main therapeutic properties of Magnolia xianianhei are its antioxidant activity, which helps protect cells from oxidative damage, as well as its antimicrobial properties, which inhibit the growth of microorganisms responsible for various infections.

What are the medicinal constituents this herb?

The most important healing constituents of Magnolia xianianhei are magnolol and honokiol, which are lignans with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, capable of inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory mediators and scavenging free radicals.

The combination of these constituents in Magnolia xianianhei is thought to contribute to its traditional use in treating a range of conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.

Research has demonstrated that the bioactive compounds in Magnolia xianianhei can interact with various cellular pathways to exert their therapeutic effects.

What are the main herbal preparations this herb?

The main herbal preparations of Magnolia xianianhei are magnolia tincture, a concentrated liquid extract typically made from the bark and flowers, used for its sedative and anti-inflammatory properties.

Magnolia bark tea, a decoction made by steeping the bark in hot water, has been traditionally used to treat fever, sore throat, and cough. Infused oil, a topical preparation made by steeping the bark in a carrier oil, is used to treat skin conditions such as eczema and acne due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Baihe decoction, a traditional Chinese medicine made by boiling the bark and flowers, has been used to treat respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Magnolia extract, a concentrated powder made from the bark and flowers, is used as a dietary supplement to support immune function and reduce inflammation.
