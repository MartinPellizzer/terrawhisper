Matucana roseiflora

Matucana roseiflora, commonly known as the "Pincushion Cactus" due to its unique, globular shape, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America.

The therapeutic actions of Matucana roseiflora are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antifungal properties, which may help to soothe skin irritations and promote tissue repair.
