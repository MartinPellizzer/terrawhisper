Eriophyton wallichii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. While the plant tissues are altered, the mite itself does not possess pharmacological properties used in human or animal medicine. Therefore, research regarding this species focuses on agricultural pest management rather than therapeutic applications.

Continuous infestations often lead to weakened plant immunity and increased susceptibility to other environmental stressors.
