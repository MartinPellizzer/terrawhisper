What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis harmsii?

Campylotropis harmsii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Fabales. It is a representative of the family Fabaceae, falling under the genus Campylotropis, and is specifically identified by its species name, harmsii.
