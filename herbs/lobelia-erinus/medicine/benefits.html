10 Health Benefits Of Lobelia Erinus (Sweet Alyssum)

Lobelia erinus has health benefits such as relieving anxiety and insomnia due to its sedative and calming properties.

Its flavonoids and phenolic acids exhibit antioxidant activity, which can help protect against oxidative stress and inflammation. This may improve overall well-being and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

For instance, individuals with sleep disorders or anxiety can benefit from sweet alyssum's soothing effects, promoting relaxation and improving quality of life.
