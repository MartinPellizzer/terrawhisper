10 Medicinal Constituents Of Lobelia Erinus (Sweet Alyssum)

Lobelia inflata, also known as Lobelia, has active constituents such as alkaloids, glycosides, and terpenoids.

These constituents possess medicinal properties like antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties. For example, the alkaloid lobeline can help improve respiratory conditions, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing spasms and inflammation, thus allowing patients to breathe more easily and comfortably.

Additionally, its expectorant properties can aid in the relief of coughs and congestion.
