Lysimachia dushanensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysimachia dushanei has a perennial herbaceous habit characterized by erect stems that reach heights of approximately 20 to 40 centimeters. The leaves are arranged in an opposite pattern and possess an elliptical to lanceolate shape with entire margins. Small, bright yellow flowers emerge from the leaf axils, featuring five distinct petals that form a star-like shape. These blossoms are typically borne in small clusters or as solitary units at the upper nodes of the plant. The reproductive structures include a central pistil surrounded by several stamens that support the pollination process.

      What are the herbal preparations of Lysimachia dushanensis?

      Lysimachia duscaneus is prepared as a decoction, a tincture, or a topical poultice to utilize its bioactive compounds.
