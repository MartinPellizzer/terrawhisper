10 Possible Side Effects Of Solidago Canadensis (Goldenrod)

Solidago canadensis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

These side effects can occur if the herb is taken in large doses, combined with other herbs or supplements, or used by individuals with sensitive stomachs or allergies.

In severe cases, improper use can lead to anaphylaxis, gastrointestinal bleeding, or exacerbate conditions like ulcers, IBS, or respiratory diseases, significantly impacting daily life.
