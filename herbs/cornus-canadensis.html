Cornus Canadensis: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cornus canadensis, commonly known as Canadian dogwood, is a perennial herb native to North America, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its ability to support urinary tract health and promote diuretic effects.

The therapeutic actions of Cornus canadensis include anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and antioxidant properties.

It contains bioactive constituents such as triterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its health benefits.
