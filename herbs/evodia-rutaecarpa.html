Evodia Fruit (Evodia rutaecarpa)

Evodia Fruit (Evodia rutaecarpa) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to China, Southeast Asia, and East Asia. Traditionally, its fruits, bark, and roots have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
