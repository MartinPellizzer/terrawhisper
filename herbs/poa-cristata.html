Poa cristata

Poa cristata, commonly known as Crested Hair Grass or Crested Brome, is a perennial grass species belonging to the family Poaceae.

The therapeutic actions of Poa cristata include treating respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its use in treating digestive problems and skin conditions.

The bioactive constituents present in Poa cristata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
