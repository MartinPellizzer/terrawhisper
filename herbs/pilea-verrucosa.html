What's the taxonomical classification of Pilea verrucosa?

Pilea verrucosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further classified under the family Urticaceae, which places it within the genus Pilea, and it is specifically identified by its species name, verrucosa.
