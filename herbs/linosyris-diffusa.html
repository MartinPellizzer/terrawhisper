What is Linosyris diffusa?

Linosyris diffusa is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa, belonging to the family Santalaceae.

It has a woody base, with a branching system that is stoloniferous, producing multiple stems and roots. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, measuring 2-7 cm in length and 0.5-2.5 cm in width, with a glandular-dotted upper surface and a hairy lower surface. The inflorescence is a corymbous panicle, consisting of small, yellowish-green flowers, each with five petals and five stamens.

The plant's fruit is a drupe, measuring 2-3 mm in diameter, containing a single seed.
