What is Lindsaea coriacea?

Lindsaea coriacea is a species of fern in the family Lindsaeaceae, native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a terrestrial fern with a rhizome that bears pinnatifid fronds up to 50 cm long, composed of linear-lanceolate pinnae. The pinnae are pinnatifid, with 2-4 pairs of secondary pinnae, and are often covered with scales. The frond stalks are yellowish-brown and have a distinctive brownish-brown base.

Lindsaea coriacea is a medium-sized fern with a distinctive appearance that is characteristic of the genus Lindsaea.
