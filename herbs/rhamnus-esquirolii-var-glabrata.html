Rhamnus esquirolii var. glabrata

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhamnus esquirolii var. glabrata?

Rhamnus esquirolii var. glabrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Rhamnaceae and the genus Rhamnus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety glabrata within the species Rhamnus esquirolii.
