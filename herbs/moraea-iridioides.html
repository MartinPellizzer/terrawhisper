What's the taxonomical classification of Moraea iridioides?

Moraea iriidioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Iridaceae, falling within the genus Moraea, and is specifically identified by its species name, iridioides.
