Gum Arabic Tree (Ferula gummosa) Compounds

The primary compounds of Ferula gummosa include essential oil, coumarin, terpenoids, phenolic acids, flavonoids, tannins, glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

Gum Arabic Tree (Ferula gummosa) Monograph

Essential oil is a key active compound of Ferula gummosa, a medicinal plant used in traditional herbal medicine.
