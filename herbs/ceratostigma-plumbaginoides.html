Ceratostigma plumbaginoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Further taxonomic refinement identifies it as a member of the family Plumbaginaceae, specifically within the genus Ceratostigma, and is ultimately identified by its species name, plumbaginoides.
