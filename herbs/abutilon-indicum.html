Possible Side Effects of abutilon indicum

Abutilon indicum can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and increased heart rate.

Some individuals may experience dizziness, nausea, and stomach discomfort after consuming the herb.

Prolonged use or excessive dosage can lead to more severe side effects, including liver damage and respiratory problems.

Furthermore, Abutilon indicum may interact with certain medications, exacerbating pre-existing medical conditions and potentially causing adverse reactions.
