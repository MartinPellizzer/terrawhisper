Sabia swinhoei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Proteales and is a member of the family Sabiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Sabia and is specifically designated as the species swinhoei.
