Goatweed (Tridax procumbens)

Goatweed (Tridax procumbens) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Tropical And Subtropical Regions Of The Americas, Southern United States, and Caribbean Islands. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
