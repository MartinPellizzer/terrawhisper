What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Codonacanthus pauciflorus has slender, woody stems that support small, lanceolate leaves arranged in an opposite pattern along the branches. Its inflorescence consists of sparse, tubular flowers that emerge from the leaf axils in limited quantities. These delicate blooms typically feature a pale pigmentation and a narrow corolla structure. The overall growth habit is that of a compact, spreading shrub with fine architectural details.
