What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis decorissima?

Pedicularis decorissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae and is specifically identified by its genus, Pedicularis, and its species designation, decorissima.
