10 Possible Side Effects Of Iberis Amara (Yellow Rocket)

Iberis amara has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These side effects can occur due to its high content of glucosinolates, which can cause digestive problems if ingested in large quantities or by individuals with pre-existing conditions.

For instance, stomach pain and diarrhea can hinder daily activities, while severe allergic reactions can lead to respiratory issues and anaphylaxis.
