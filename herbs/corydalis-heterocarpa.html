Corydalis heterocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Corydalis and the species name heterocarpa.

What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

Research indicates that these alkaloids possess significant sedative, analgesic, and antitussive properties.
