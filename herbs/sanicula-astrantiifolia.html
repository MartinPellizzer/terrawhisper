Sanicula astrantiifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Sanicula astrantiifolia?

Sanicula astrantiifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated in the order Apiales. It further falls under the family Apiaceae, a group known for its characteristic inflorescences. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Sanicula, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species name astrantiifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its flowering structure consists of dense, umbel-like clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers held atop sturdy stems. These inflorescences eventually produce small, nut-like fruits that are often equipped with hooked bristles to aid in seed dispersal.
