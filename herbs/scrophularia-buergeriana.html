Buerger'S Scrophularia (Scrophularia buergeriana)

Buerger'S Scrophularia (Scrophularia buergeriana) is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
