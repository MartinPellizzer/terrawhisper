What's the taxonomical classification of Scrophularia buergeriana?

Scrophularia buergerianana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Lamiales and is a member of the family Scrophulariaceae. Specifically, it is nested within the genus Scrophularia, with its unique identity defined by the species buergeriana.
