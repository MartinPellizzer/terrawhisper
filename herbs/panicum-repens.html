What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Panicum repens has a perennial growth habit characterized by creeping, stoloniferous rhizomes that allow the plant to spread horizontally through the soil. The inflorescence is a compact, terminal panicle composed of numerous small, spikelet-like branches.
