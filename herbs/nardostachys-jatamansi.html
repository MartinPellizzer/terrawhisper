Nardostachys jatamansi

Nardostachys Jatamansi (Nardostachys jatamansi) is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas, known for its aromatic rhizomes and traditional use in Ayurvedic medicine to treat anxiety, insomnia, and neurological disorders.

Botanical Identification

Nardostachys jatamansi, commonly known as Jatamansi, belongs to the Ranunculaceae family. It is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas. The plant has aromatic roots, trifoliate leaves, and yellow flowers. It is also known as Nepal saffron and has been referenced in Ayurvedic texts.
