Nardostachys Jatamansi (Nardostachys jatamansi)

Nardostachys Jatamansi (Nardostachys jatamansi) is a member of the Caprifoliaceae family, native to Himalayas, Central Asia, and South Asia. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its sedative, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, tibetan medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
