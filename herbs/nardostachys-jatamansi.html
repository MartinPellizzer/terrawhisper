What is Nardostachys Jatamansi (Nardostachys Jatamansi)?

Nardostachys jatamansi, commonly known as Jatamansi, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal, India, and China, and is widely used in traditional Ayurvedic and Tibetan medicine for its calming, sedative, and neuroprotective properties.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Nardostachys jatamansi is Nardostachys jatamansi. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Magnoliopsida, order Dipsacales, family Caprifoliaceae, genus Nardostachys, and species Nardostachys jatamansi. It is commonly known as Jatamansi in Nepal and Tibet, and as Himalayan valerian in India. The synonyms for this plant include Nardostachys jatamansi, Nardostachys glomerata, and Nardostachys himalayana.
