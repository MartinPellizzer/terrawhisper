Melissa officinalis subsp. altissima

Melissa officinalis subsp.

altissima, also known as tall lemon balm, is a subspecies of the lemon balm plant (Melissa officinalis), a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae).

This plant is valued for its calming and antiseptic properties, which can be attributed to its essential oils, particularly citral and geraniol.

The health benefits of Melissa officinalis subsp.

altissima include reducing anxiety and stress, improving sleep quality, and possessing antimicrobial properties that can aid in wound healing and immune system support.

Melissa officinalis subsp.

altissima can be used in various herbal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which can be consumed orally or applied topically to the skin.
