10 Best Khaya Senegalensis Health Benefits

Khaya senegalensis, also known as African shea tree, offers numerous health benefits due to its rich content of nutrients and bioactive compounds.

Khaya senegalensis reduces inflammation by containing bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and tannins, which possess anti-inflammatory properties.
