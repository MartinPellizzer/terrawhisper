Ranunculus multifidus var. repens

Ranunculus multifidus var. repens, commonly known as Lesser Spearwort, is a plant species in the buttercup family (Ranunculaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to soothe skin irritations and protect against cell damage.

The therapeutic actions of Lesser Spearwort are attributed to its ability to calm the skin and reduce inflammation, making it a potential remedy for conditions such as eczema and acne.

The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus multifidus var. repens include saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
