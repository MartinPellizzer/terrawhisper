Iris wattii

What's the taxonomical classification of Iris wattii?

Iris wattii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and the family Iridaeceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Iris, and is specifically designated as the species wattii.
