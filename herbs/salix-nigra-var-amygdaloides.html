Salix nigra var. amygdaloides

Salix nigra var. amygdaloides, also known as the black willow or amygdaloid black willow, is a variety of the black willow tree (Salix nigra) native to eastern North America, characterized by its slender branches and compound leaves.

The bark and leaves of this plant have been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
