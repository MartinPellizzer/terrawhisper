What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Dryopteris Crassirhizoma (Giant Chain Fern)?

Dryopteris crassirhizoma, also known as giant chain fern, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Its medicinal constituents include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects. Medicinal preparations of giant chain fern include teas, infusions, and decoctions that are used to treat various health conditions. However, it may cause allergic reactions or interact with certain medications, and users should be cautious when consuming it in large quantities.

Precautions should also be taken for pregnant or breastfeeding women.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Dryopteris crassirhizoma.

What are the medicinal preparations of Dryopteris crassirhizoma?

Dryopteris crassirhizoma has different medicinal preparations, such as dried rhizome, fresh rhizome, and leaf extracts.

The dried rhizome is used to treat fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal issues due to its anti-inflammatory properties. Fresh rhizome extract is employed to treat snakebites and other venomous wounds owing to its antiseptic and antibacterial effects.

Leaf extracts are used to alleviate respiratory symptoms like bronchitis and coughs due to their expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.
