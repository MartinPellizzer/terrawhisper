What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Dryopteris Crassirhizoma (Giant Chain Fern)?

Dryopteris crassirhizoma, also known as giant chain fern, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving digestion.

Medicinal preparations of giant chain fern include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various ailments. Potential side effects of using this herb include allergic reactions and interactions with certain medications.
