What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Dryopteris Crassirhizoma (Giant Chain Fern)?

Dryopteris crassirhizoma has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving respiratory issues.

The herb contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins that contribute to its medicinal properties. Its medicinal preparations include infusions, decoctions, and teas, which are used to treat coughs, bronchitis, and skin conditions. However, prolonged use or high doses of the herb may cause stomach upset and allergic reactions in some individuals.

It is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before using giant chain fern, especially for people with pre-existing medical conditions or taking medications.

What are the medicinal preparations of Dryopteris crassirhizoma?

Dryopteris crassirhizoma has different medicinal preparations, such as dried rhizome, fresh rhizome, and leaf extracts.

The dried rhizome is used to treat fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal issues due to its anti-inflammatory properties. Fresh rhizome extract is employed to treat snakebites and other venomous wounds owing to its antiseptic and antibacterial effects.

Leaf extracts are used to alleviate respiratory symptoms like bronchitis and coughs due to their expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.

What are the possible side effect of using Dryopteris crassirhizoma improperly?

Improper use of Dryopteris crassirhizoma increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as allergic reactions like hives, itching, and swelling, digestive issues like nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, respiratory problems like coughing and shortness of breath, cardiovascular issues like palpitations and hypertension, liver damage, kidney inflammation, increased risk of bleeding due to anticoagulant properties, skin rashes, and even anaphylactic shock in severe cases.
