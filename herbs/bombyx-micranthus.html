Possible Side Effects of Bombyx micranthus

Bombyx micranthus can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal issues.

Ingesting the plant's leaves or roots can lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's sap has been linked to respiratory problems, including bronchitis and asthma.

Improper use of Bombyx micranthus can also lead to liver damage and kidney failure in severe cases.
