Hippophae gyantsensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Elaeagnaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Hippophae, and its unique species designation, gyantsensis.
