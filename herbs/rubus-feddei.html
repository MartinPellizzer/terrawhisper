What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus feddei?

Rubus feddei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Rubus, with its specific designation being the species feddei.
