Connarus griffonianus

What's the taxonomical classification of Connarus griffonianus?

Connarus griffonianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is positioned within the order Oxalidales. It is specifically categorized under the family Connaraceae, falling into the genus Connarus, and is ultimately identified by its specific epithet, griffonianus.

While some studies explore its bioactive compounds, the specific physiological impact on humans remains insufficiently documented in clinical settings. Because the long-term safety profile is unknown, caution is necessary when handling this species.
