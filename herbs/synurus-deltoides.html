What's the taxonomical classification of Synurus deltoides?

Synurus deltoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Asterales, this plant is a member of the family Asteraceae. It is further distinguished by its specific placement in the genus Synurus, with its unique identity defined by the species name deltoides.
