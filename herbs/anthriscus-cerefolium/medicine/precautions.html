10 Precautions To Take When Using Anthriscus Cerefolium (Chervil)

Taking chervil without caution may lead to excessive bleeding or decreased blood pressure, which can be particularly problematic for individuals with bleeding disorders or those on anticoagulant medications.

Failure to consider these interactions can result in adverse side effects, such as prolonged bleeding or fainting.
