10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aerva Lanata (Lamb'S Tail)

Aerva lanata has active constituents such as alkaloids, saponins, and flavonoids.

These constituents are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities. The plant's extracts have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, such as wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues.

For example, the anti-inflammatory compounds can help alleviate pain and swelling in patients with arthritis or injuries, while the antimicrobial properties can prevent infections and promote wound healing.
