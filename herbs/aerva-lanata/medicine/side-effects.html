10 Possible Side Effects Of Aerva Lanata (Lamb'S Tail)

Aerva lanata has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts, allergic sensitivities, or improper preparation methods.

In severe cases, they can worsen lives by causing chronic digestive problems, skin rashes, or even respiratory distress, leading to discomfort, reduced productivity, and in some cases, hospitalization.

1. Leads to insomnia frequently

Aerva lanata causes allergic reactions in some individuals due to its potential to trigger an immune response.

The allergenic properties of this plant are thought to be caused by the presence of certain compounds, such as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which can bind to proteins on the surface of skin cells, leading to the activation of immune cells and the subsequent release of histamine, resulting in symptoms like itching, redness, and swelling.

These substances can cause an allergic reaction in some individuals, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, difficulty breathing, swelling of the face and throat, and even anaphylactic shock.

The sudden onset of anaphylaxis is a result of the rapid release of histamine from mast cells, causing a severe immune response.
