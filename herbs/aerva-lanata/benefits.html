10 Best Aerva Lanata Health Benefits

Aerva lanata, also known as the "Sahyadri nettle," is a medicinal plant widely used in Ayurveda for its numerous health benefits.

The plant is also rich in antioxidants, which help in neutralizing free radicals and reducing oxidative stress in the body. Aerva lanata is traditionally used to treat skin conditions such as eczema and psoriasis because of its soothing and healing properties.

Additionally, it is believed to enhance immunity and promote overall well-being when consumed as a tonic or in herbal formulations.
