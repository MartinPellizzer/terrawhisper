Blister Bean (Spilanthes Alba)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Spilanthes alba.

Blister bean, scientifically known as Spilanthes alba, is a medicinal herb native to the Americas, renowned for its pungent flavor and potent therapeutic properties. This plant is commonly used as a spice and a traditional remedy, valued for its ability to stimulate the body and enhance overall vitality. Its primary benefits include enhancing digestion, reducing inflammation, and supporting immune function due to its high concentration of alkaloids and other bioactive compounds. Historically, indigenous peoples of North and South America have used blister bean to treat respiratory issues, skin conditions, and as a stimulant during long journeys. In modern wellness practices, it is increasingly incorporated into natural supplements and topical treatments, and its unique ability to produce a tingling sensation when consumed has made it a popular ingredient in both culinary and medicinal applications.
