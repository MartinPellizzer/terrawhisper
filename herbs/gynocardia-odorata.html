Gynocardia odorata

What's the taxonomical classification of Gynocardia odorata?

Gynocardia odorata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Achariaceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified by the genus Gynocardia and the species odorata.
