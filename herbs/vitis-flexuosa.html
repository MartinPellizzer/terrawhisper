Vitis flexuosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Vitis flexuosa?

Vitis flexuosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the biological order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Vitis and the species flexuosa, representing a unique position within its taxonomic hierarchy.

Its leaf blades typically consist of three distinct lobes with prominent venation patterns.
