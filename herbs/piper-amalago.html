Piper Amalago Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Piper amalago, commonly known as African black pepper, is a flowering plant native to Africa, belonging to the Piperaceae family.

This herb is valued for its pungent aroma and various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and digestive properties.

It contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

You can prepare Piper amalago through infusions, decoctions, or as a spice in culinary dishes to harness its medicinal and aromatic qualities.
