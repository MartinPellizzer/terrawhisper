Doronicum calotum

Doronicum calotum, also known as Leopard's Bane, is a perennial plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae) native to Eastern Europe and Western Asia.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help alleviate various health issues such as skin conditions, wounds, and respiratory problems.

The health benefits of Doronicum calotum include reducing inflammation, fighting infections, and promoting wound healing.

The herbal preparations of this plant include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which can be used to treat a range of health conditions when taken under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.
