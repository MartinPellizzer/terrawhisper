What's the taxonomical classification of Sinosenecio oldhamianus?

Sinosenecio oldhamianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually reaching the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is placed within the genus Sinosenecio, where it is specifically identified by the species name oldhamianus.
