What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis atuntsuensis?

Corydalis atuntsuensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Ranunculales, it falls under the family Papaveraceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Corydalis and the species epithet atuntsuenis.
