Peucedanum medicum var. gracile

What's the taxonomical classification of Peucedanum medicum var. gracile?

Peucedanum medicum var. gracile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Peucedanum, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the species Peucedanum medicum var. gracile.
