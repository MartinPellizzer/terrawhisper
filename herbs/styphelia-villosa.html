What is Styphelia villosa?

Styphelia villosa is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae, native to Australia.

It is an evergreen shrub that grows up to 1 meter tall, with a woody stem and hairy leaves that are 2-5 mm long and elliptical in shape. The leaves are oppositely arranged and have a wavy margin. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in axillary racemes, which are 5-10 mm long and have 5 petals.

The fruit is a small, hairy capsule that contains 5 seeds.
