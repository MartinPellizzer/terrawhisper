What is Pleopeltis rotunda?

Pleopeltis rotunda is a species of epiphytic fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its round or ovate fronds that are typically 10-30 cm in length and 8-20 cm wide, with a pinnate or subpinnate venation pattern. The frond rachis is brown and scaly, while the leaflets are lanceolate or ovate, with serrate margins. The plant's indusium is rounded and has a short stalk, and the spores are brown and multiseriate.

The species is found in tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, including Central and South America, and the southern United States.
