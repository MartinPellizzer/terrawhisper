Dumasia forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Dumasia, and its species designation, forrestii.
