Scutellaria obtusifolia var. trinervata

What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria obtusifolia var. trinervata?

Scutellaria obtusifolia var. trinervata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Lamiales. It further belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which places it within the genus Scutellaria. Finally, this specific plant is identified at the varietal level as the species Scutellaria obtusifolia var. trinervata.
