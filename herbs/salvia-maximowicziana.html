Salvia maximowicziana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by its distinct morphological traits. Within this family, the plant is placed in the genus Salvia, and its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, maximowicziana.
