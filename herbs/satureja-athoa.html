Satureja athoa

Satureja athoa, also known as Moroccan savory, is a perennial herb native to the Atlas Mountains in Morocco.

It has been used for centuries in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties, including antiseptic, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant's essential oil and extracts have been found to possess various health benefits, including reducing stress, promoting digestion, and alleviating respiratory issues.

Satureja athoa can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and essential oils for medicinal use, making it a valuable addition to natural health remedies.
