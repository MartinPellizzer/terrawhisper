Goswamia indica

The health benefits of Goswamia indica include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its therapeutic actions such as reducing pain and inflammation, and enhancing immune function.

The bioactive constituents of Goswamia indica include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
