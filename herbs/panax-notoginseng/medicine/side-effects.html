10 Possible Side Effects Of Panax Notoginseng (Tienchi)

Panax notoginseng has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, bleeding, and dizziness.

These side effects may be caused by taking high doses or combining it with other medications that thin the blood. For instance, someone who already has a bleeding disorder may experience severe internal bleeding, leading to anemia, fatigue, and poor quality of life.

Furthermore, dizziness can cause accidents and injuries, affecting daily activities and work performance.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Panax notoginseng if used imporperly.

The saponin compounds present in Panax notoginseng can trigger an overactive response from the body's defenses, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, the herb's ability to increase white blood cell count may also contribute to allergic reactions by amplifying the body's natural defense mechanisms.
