10 Health Benefits Of Panax Notoginseng (Tienchi)

Panax notoginseng, also known as tienchi, has health benefits such as improving cardiovascular health, reducing inflammation, and enhancing cognitive function.

Its medicinal properties, including ginsenosides and saponins, work to relax blood vessels and reduce blood pressure, alleviating symptoms of hypertension and heart disease.

By promoting blood flow and oxygenation, tienchi can improve mental clarity, memory, and focus, benefiting individuals with chronic fatigue syndrome and Alzheimer's disease.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Panax notoginseng.

1. Reduces inflammation

Panax notoginseng reduces inflammation because it possesses a unique combination of bioactive compounds, including saponins and flavonoids.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thereby reducing the inflammatory response. Additionally, tienchi has been found to have antioxidant properties, which help to neutralize free radicals that can contribute to inflammation.

These properties make tienchi a promising natural remedy for the treatment of various inflammatory-related diseases.

2. Lowers blood pressure

Panax notoginseng lowers blood pressure because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, including saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds work synergistically to relax blood vessels, reduce peripheral resistance, and decrease the heart rate, thereby decreasing blood pressure.

Additionally, tienchi's flavonoids have been shown to inhibit the activity of angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE), a key enzyme involved in blood pressure regulation, further contributing to its hypotensive effects.

3. Improves cardiovascular health

Panax notoginseng improves cardiovascular health because it has been shown to lower blood pressure, improve blood lipid profiles, and enhance blood vessel function.

The saponins present in tienchi have been found to relax blood vessels, reducing the risk of cardiovascular events such as heart attacks and strokes.

Additionally, tienchi's antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties help to protect the cardiovascular system from damage caused by oxidative stress and chronic inflammation.

4. Enhances immune function

Panax notoginseng enhances immune function because of its rich content of saponins, polysaccharides, and other bioactive compounds.

These compounds stimulate the production of cytokines, such as interleukin-2 and interferon-gamma, which are essential for activating immune cells like natural killer cells and T-cells. Additionally, tienchi's polyphenolic compounds have been shown to inhibit the proliferation of cancer cells and induce apoptosis, further supporting its immunomodulatory effects.

Overall, tienchi's unique chemical profile makes it a valuable adjunct for supporting immune function and overall health.

5. Relieves anxiety symptoms

Panax notoginseng relieves anxiety symptoms because of its adaptogenic properties, which allow it to help the body adapt to stress and promote balance in the nervous system.

Its saponin compounds, particularly ginsenosides, have been shown to inhibit the production of stress hormones like cortisol and adrenaline, thereby reducing feelings of anxiety and unease.

Additionally, tienchi has a sedative effect on the nervous system, promoting relaxation and reducing muscle tension, which can contribute to the alleviation of anxiety symptoms.

6. Treats respiratory issues

Panax notoginseng treats respiratory issues because of its potent adaptogenic and antiviral properties.

The plant's saponins and polyacetylenes work together to modulate the immune response, reducing inflammation and congestion in the lungs.

Additionally, tienchi's expectorant properties help loosen and clear out mucus, making it an effective natural remedy for conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and chronic coughs.

7. Reduces pain perception

Panax notoginseng reduces pain perception because of its potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The compound notoginsenoside R1, found in tienchi, has been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thereby reducing pain and inflammation.

Additionally, tienchi's ability to modulate the activity of pain-sensing neurons in the spinal cord and brain further contributes to its analgesic effects, making it a valuable natural remedy for pain management.

8. Supports liver health

Panax notoginseng supports liver health because it exhibits remarkable liver-protective effects.

Studies have shown that tienchi's saponin compounds can significantly reduce liver damage caused by toxins, oxidative stress, and inflammation.

Additionally, tienchi has been found to increase the expression of liver detoxification enzymes, enhancing the liver's natural ability to remove toxins and promote overall liver health.

9. Helps regulate blood sugar

Panax notoginseng helps regulate blood sugar because it has been shown to improve insulin sensitivity and reduce glucose uptake in the intestines.

Tienchi's active compounds, such as saponins and flavonoids, have been found to inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in glucose metabolism, thereby decreasing blood glucose levels.

Additionally, tienchi has been demonstrated to stimulate the release of insulin, further enhancing glucose uptake and utilization by cells, thereby maintaining normal blood sugar levels.

10. Enhances cognitive function

Panax notoginseng enhances cognitive function because it promotes blood flow to the brain, increasing oxygenation and nutrient delivery.

Its saponin compounds, particularly notoginsenoside R1, have been shown to inhibit the breakdown of neurotransmitters, such as acetylcholine, dopamine, and serotonin, which are essential for attention, memory, and learning.

By maintaining optimal neurotransmitter levels, tienchi supports cognitive performance, improves focus, and may even protect against age-related cognitive decline.
