10 Possible Side Effects Of Aster Alpinus (Alpine Aster)

Aster alpinus has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects are usually caused by overconsumption or individual sensitivities to the herb. In severe cases, they can lead to anaphylaxis, nausea, and vomiting, significantly impacting daily life.

For example, an individual experiencing anaphylaxis may struggle to breathe, while excessive nausea may hinder their ability to maintain a healthy diet.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Aster alpinus if used imporperly.

These compounds can trigger an immune response in some individuals, leading to symptoms like hives, itching, and swelling.

The exact mechanisms behind this reaction are not yet fully understood, but it is believed that the allergens are released during the processing of the plant material or when it comes into contact with human skin.
