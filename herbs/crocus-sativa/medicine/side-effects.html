10 Possible Side Effects Of Crocus Sativa (Saffron)

Crocus sativa has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, diarrhea, and dizziness.

These side effects are often caused by excessive consumption, particularly when ingesting high doses of its active compound, crocin.

In severe cases, excessive saffron intake can worsen underlying conditions like acid reflux, causing persistent heartburn and discomfort during meals, significantly impacting daily activities and social interactions.
