Possible Side Effects of Euplassa organensis

Euplassa organensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea due to its high alkaloid content.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to headaches, dizziness, and fatigue, as the body struggles to process the toxic compounds.

Additionally, Euplassa organensis may interact with other medications, such as blood thinners and certain antidepressants, resulting in adverse reactions.

In severe cases, improper use of the plant can even cause respiratory problems, skin irritation, and allergic reactions, requiring medical attention.
