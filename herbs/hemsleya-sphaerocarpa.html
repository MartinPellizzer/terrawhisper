Hemsleya sphaerocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Cucurbitaceae, which places it within the genus Hemleysa, and it is specifically identified by the species epithet sphaerocarpa.
