What's the taxonomical classification of Parnassia bifolia?

Parnassia bifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Celastrales. It falls within the family Celastraceae and is specifically identified under the genus Parnassia, with its unique biological classification completed by its species designation, bifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its solitary white flower is characterized by five petals that feature distinct, dark, vein-like markings and a prominent yellowish center. The reproductive structures include a central cluster of numerous stamens and a single pistil surrounded by nectar-secreting glands.
