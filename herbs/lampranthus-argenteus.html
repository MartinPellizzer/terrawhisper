What is Lampranthus argenteus?

Lampranthus argenteus is a succulent plant that belongs to the family Aizoaceae.

The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing small, star-shaped flowers with five white petals that are 1.5-2 cm in diameter, and the fruit is a capsule containing numerous seeds.

This plant is native to the Western Cape Province in South Africa.
