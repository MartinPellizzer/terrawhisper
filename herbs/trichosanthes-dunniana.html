What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes dunniana?

Trichosanthes dunniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trichosanthes, and its unique species designation, dunniana.
