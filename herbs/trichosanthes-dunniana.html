Trichosanthes dunniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trichosanthes, and its unique species designation, dunniana.
