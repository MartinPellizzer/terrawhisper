Possible Side Effects of agave americana

Agave americana can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain due to its high saponin content.

Ingesting large quantities can also lead to headaches, dizziness, and fatigue, while long-term use may cause kidney damage and electrolyte imbalances.

Some individuals may experience allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and swelling, particularly those with pre-existing sensitivities.

Improper preparation or excessive consumption of agave americana can exacerbate these side effects, making it essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using it.
