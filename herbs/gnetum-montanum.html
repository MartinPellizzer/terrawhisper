What's the taxonomical classification of Gnetum montanum?

Gnetum montanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Gnetidae. Following the order Gnetales, this plant is a member of the family Gnetaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic position is defined by its classification within the genus Gnetum, with its unique identity designated as the species montanum.

What is the geographical distribution of this plant?

It typically occupies rocky outcrops and subalpine scrublands located at elevations between 2,000 and 3,500 meters above sea level. Its distribution is closely tied to specific microclimates characterized by cool temperatures and well-drained volcanic soils.
