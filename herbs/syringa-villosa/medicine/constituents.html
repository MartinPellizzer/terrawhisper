10 Medicinal Constituents Of Syringa Villosa (Lilac)

Syringa villosa has active constituents such as flavonoids, iridoids, and essential oils.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can alleviate symptoms of various conditions. For instance, the antioxidant properties of lilac can protect against cell damage and oxidative stress, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases.

Its anti-inflammatory effects can also soothe skin irritations and improve respiratory issues, improving the overall quality of life for individuals.
