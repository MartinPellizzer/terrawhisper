What's the taxonomical classification of Paeonia intermedia?

Paeonia intermedia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is situated in Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is organized into the order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Paeoniaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Paeonia, specifically identifying as the species intermedia.
