What is Huperzia polytrichoides?

Huperzia polytrichoides is a species of lycopod fern that belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small to medium-sized, perennial plant that grows up to 20 cm in height, with a cylindrical rhizome and a crown of sterile and fertile leaves. The sterile leaves are needle-like, dark green, and 2-5 cm long, while the fertile leaves are longer and have a distinctive brownish color. The plant produces yellowish-brown cones in late spring to early summer, which are 1-2 cm long and contain spores.

Huperzia polytrichoides is typically found in humid, shaded areas in tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia.
