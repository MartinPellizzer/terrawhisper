The therapeutic actions of Poa angustata include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it effective in treating various health issues.

Furthermore, improper use of Poa angustata may increase the risk of toxicity, particularly for individuals with pre-existing health conditions.
