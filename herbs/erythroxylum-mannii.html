Erythroxylum mannii

What's the taxonomical classification of Erythroxylum mannii?

Erythroxylum mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Malpighiales and the family Erythroxylaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Erythroxylum and is specifically designated as the species mannii.

The leaf surface can vary in texture, sometimes appearing glabrous or slightly pubescent depending on the specific specimen.
