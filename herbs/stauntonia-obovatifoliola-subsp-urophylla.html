Stauntonia obovatifoliola subsp. urophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Stauntonia obovatifoliola subsp. urophylla?

Stauntonia obovatifoliola subsp. urophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Lardizabalaeceae, eventually reaching the genus Stauntonia and the species Stauntonia.
