Dendrocalamus giganteus

What's the taxonomical classification of Dendrocalamus giganteus?

Dendrocalamus giganteus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is positioned under the order Poales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Poaceae, which places it within the genus Dendrocalamus, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species giganteus.
