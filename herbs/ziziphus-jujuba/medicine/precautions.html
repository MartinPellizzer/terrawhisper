1. Watch for gastrointestinal side effects

When using Ziziphus jujuba, also known as Chinese Date, medicinally, it's important to avoid high doses suddenly.

This precaution is crucial because sudden increases in dosage can lead to adverse effects such as dizziness, headaches, and digestive issues. Additionally, high doses may cause an imbalance of neurotransmitters, exacerbating anxiety and sleep disorders.

Gradual dose escalation helps to prevent these complications, ensuring a safe and effective treatment experience.
