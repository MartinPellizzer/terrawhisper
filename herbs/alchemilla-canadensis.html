Alchemilla Canadensis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Alchemilla canadensis, commonly known as Canadian lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to North America, characterized by its broad, shield-shaped leaves and yellow flowers.

This herb is known for its soothing and healing properties, often used in traditional medicine to support women's health and digestive wellness.

It contains bioactive constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and mucilage, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and protective effects.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from its leaves to harness its therapeutic benefits.
