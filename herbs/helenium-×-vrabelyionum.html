What is Helenium × vrabelyionum?

Helenium × vrabelyionum is a hybrid perennial plant in the family Asteraceae.

It has upright stems, typically 60-120 cm tall, with green, lance-shaped leaves that are 5-15 cm long and 1-3 cm wide. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with 5-9 petals, which are 3-5 cm in diameter and have a yellow center surrounded by reddish-brown or dark purple petals. The flowers are arranged in clusters at the top of the stem, blooming from June to August.

Helenium × vrabelyionum is native to North America, specifically in the United States and Canada.
