What's the taxonomical classification of Indigofera fortunei?

Indigofera fortunei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Fabales and belongs to the family Fabaceae, which is known for its significant role in nitrogen fixation. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed within the genus Indigofera, specifically identified as the species fortunei.
