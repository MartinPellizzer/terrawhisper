What's the taxonomical classification of Helicteres glabriuscula?

Helicteres glabriuscula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Malvaceae, specifically within the genus Helicteres, which defines its species designation as Helicteres glabriuscula.
