What's the taxonomical classification of Cassia nomame?

Cassia nomame belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical descent, it falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Cassia, and its specific species designation, nomame.
