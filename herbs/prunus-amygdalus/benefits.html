1. Fights oxidative stress

Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and antioxidants in the body, leading to cellular damage.

This health issue, also known as oxidative damage or reactive oxygen species overload, occurs when there is an excess of harmful molecules that the body cannot neutralize effectively.

It is commonly experienced by individuals exposed to environmental pollutants, smokers, and those with poor dietary habits or chronic illnesses.

Prunus amygdalus, or the almond plant, contains compounds that help combat oxidative stress by providing essential antioxidants.

These natural substances support the body's defense mechanisms, reducing the risk of chronic diseases and promoting overall cellular health.

How this herb helps with fights oxidative stress?

Prunus amygdalus fights oxidative stress because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds act as powerful antioxidants, neutralizing free radicals in the body. Alkaloids like amygdalin contribute to the plant's ability to reduce oxidative damage. Flavonoids, including quercetin and kaempferol, further enhance its antioxidant properties.

Together, these constituents support cellular health and may help prevent chronic diseases linked to oxidative stress.
