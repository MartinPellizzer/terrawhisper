Scardic Sideritis (Sideritis scardica)

Scardic Sideritis (Sideritis scardica) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Balkan Peninsula, Southeast Europe, and Eastern Mediterranean. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and tibetan medicine.
