Centranthera grandiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Centranthera grandiflora?

Centranthera grandiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, falling into the genus Centrantheera, and is specifically identified as the species grandiflora.
