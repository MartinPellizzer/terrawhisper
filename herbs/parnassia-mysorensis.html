Parnassia mysorensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Parnassia mysorensis?

Parnasssia mysorensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Celastrales and the family Celastraceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Parnasssia, with its specific designation being the species mysorensis.
