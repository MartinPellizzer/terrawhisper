Ervatamia coronaria lowers blood pressure because its bioactive compounds interact with the body's vascular system, causing relaxation of blood vessels.

Regular consumption may therefore help alleviate symptoms of high blood pressure.

Ervatamia coronaria regulates blood sugar because of its bioactive compounds that have been shown to inhibit the activity of alpha-glucosidase, an enzyme responsible for breaking down carbohydrates into glucose.

The plant's extracts also stimulate the secretion of insulin, making it a potential natural remedy for managing blood sugar levels.
