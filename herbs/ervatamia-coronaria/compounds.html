Crown Vetch (Ervatamia coronaria) Compounds

Crown Vetch contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and digestive benefits. Coumarin and terpenoids may support skin and respiratory health. Glycosides and alkaloids are also present, offering additional therapeutic potential.
