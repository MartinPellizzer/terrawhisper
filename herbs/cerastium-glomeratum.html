Cerastium glomeratum

What's the taxonomical classification of Cerastium glomeratum?

Cerastium glomeratum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Caryophyllales and the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Cerastium, specifically as the species glomeratum.
