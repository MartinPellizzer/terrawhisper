Aesculus Hippocastanum

The Horse Chestnut tree is a rich source of the herb Aesculus hippocastanum, which has been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

Possible Side Effects of aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach pain due to its saponin and glycoside content.

Ingesting large amounts of the herb can also lead to dizziness, headache, and even seizures in severe cases.

Prolonged use of aesculus hippocastanum can cause liver and kidney damage, as well as interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Additionally, it can also cause allergic reactions, including skin rashes and itching, in some individuals.
