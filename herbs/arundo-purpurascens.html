Arundo purpurascens

Arundo purpurascens, commonly known as purple cat's tail, is a plant species within the genus Arundo and the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

The purple cat's tail plant has been traditionally used for its health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and provide relief from respiratory issues.

The bioactive constituents present in the plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties.
