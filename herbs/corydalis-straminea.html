What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis straminea?

Corydalis straminea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Papaveraceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Corydalis and the specific species epithet stramineea.
