    What side effects this plant can have?

    This plant can cause severe oral irritation, swelling of the mouth and throat, a burning sensation, difficulty swallowing, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, dyspnea, and dizziness. Upon contact or ingestion, the calcium oxalate crystals typically trigger immediate localized reactions such as intense burning and swelling. These symptoms can rapidly progress to more dangerous systemic issues like difficulty swallowing and respiratory distress known as dyspnea. Digestive distress frequently follows, manifesting as nausea, vomiting, and intense abdominal pain. Furthermore, the physiological stress from these reactions may lead to dizziness in affected individuals.
