10 Possible Side Effects Of Pimenta Racemosa (Bay Rum)

Pimenta racemosa has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, dizziness, and headaches.

These side effects can occur due to the herb's high concentration of essential oils, which can cause irritation or interact with certain medications. For example, individuals with skin conditions or epilepsy may experience worsening symptoms or increased seizures.

This can significantly impact their quality of life, making everyday activities challenging and even debilitating.
