Sarcococca ruscifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Buxaceae. Finally, its precise biological identity is defined by its placement in the genus Sarcococca, with the specific epithet rusciifolia completing its scientific name.
