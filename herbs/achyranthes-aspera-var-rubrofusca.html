What's the taxonomical classification of Achyranthes aspera var. rubrofusca?

Achyrantheres aspera var. rubrofusca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Caryophyllales. It is a member of the family Amaranthaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Achyranthes. Ultimately, its precise taxonomic identity is defined by its designation as the variety rubrofusca of the species Achyranthes aspera.
