Sarcopyramis napalensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcopyramis napalensis?

Sarcopyramis napalensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Myrtales. More specifically, this plant is a part of the family Melastomataceae. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Sarcopyramis, with its specific identification being the species napalenis.
