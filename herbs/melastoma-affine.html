Melastoma affine

What's the taxonomical classification of Melastoma affine?

Melastoma affine belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic progression continues through the order Myrtales and the family Melastomataceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Melastoma, with its specific identification as the species affine.
