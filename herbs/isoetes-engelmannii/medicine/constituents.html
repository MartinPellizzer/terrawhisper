10 Medicinal Constituents Of Isoetes Engelmannii (Dwarf Lake Rush)

Isoetes engelmannii has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties that can benefit human health. For instance, the flavonoids may help reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis or respiratory disorders, while the phenolic acids may exhibit antibacterial effects against infections.

The saponins, meanwhile, could potentially improve cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol levels and reducing blood pressure, ultimately contributing to a better quality of life for individuals with these conditions.
