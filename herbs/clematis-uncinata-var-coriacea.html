Clematis uncinata var. coriacea

What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis uncinata var. coriacea?

Clematis uncinata var. coriacea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae. Within this family, the plant is situated in the genus Clematis, specifically identified by its botanical designation as the variety coriacea of the species Clematis uncinata.
