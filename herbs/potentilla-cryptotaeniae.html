What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Potentiella cryptotaeniae has a low-growing, caespitose habit characterized by pinnately compound leaves with five to seven narrow, serrated leaflets and small, five-petaled yellow flowers. The floral structures include multiple stamens and numerous carpels centered within a cup-like receptacle. Once pollination occurs, the plant develops small, aggregate dry fruits known as achenes.
