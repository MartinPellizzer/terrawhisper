What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla cryptotaeniae?

Potentiella cryptotaeniae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae, eventually reaching its specific classification within the genus Potentiella. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, cryptotaeniae.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

Because there is limited clinical research on this specific species, the full range of toxicological effects remains largely uncharacterized. It is advisable to exercise caution and avoid skin contact until further safety studies are completed.
