What's the taxonomical classification of Symplocos laurina?

Symplocos laurina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a representative of the family Symplocaceae, falling under the genus Symplocos and specifically identified by the species name laurina.
