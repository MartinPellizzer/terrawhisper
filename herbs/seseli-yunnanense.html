Seseli yunnanense

What's the taxonomical classification of Seseli yunnanense?

Seseli yunnanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Apiales. It is a member of the family Apiaceae, which places it within the genus Seseli, specifically identified by the species epithet yunnanense.
