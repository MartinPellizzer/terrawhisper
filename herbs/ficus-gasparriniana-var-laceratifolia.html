Ficus gasparriniana var. laceratifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus gasparriniana var. laceratifolia?

Ficus gasparriniana var. laceratifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the order Rosales and falls under the family Moraceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Ficus, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Ficus gasparriniana var. laceratifolia.
