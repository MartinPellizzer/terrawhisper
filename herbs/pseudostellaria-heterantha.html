Pseudostellaria heterantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These leaves are arranged oppositely along the stems, often appearing crowded or overlapping in certain sections. The reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that typically emerge from the leaf axils.
