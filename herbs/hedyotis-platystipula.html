Hedyotis platystipula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Gentianaales. Within this order, it is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Specifically, this plant is categorized under the genus Hedyotis, with its unique identification completed by its species name, platystipula.
