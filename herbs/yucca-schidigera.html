Socotra Aloe (Yucca schidigera)

Socotra Aloe (Yucca schidigera) is a member of the Asparagaceae family, native to Southwest United States, Northern Mexico, and Baja California. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
