What is Socotra Aloe (Yucca Schidigera)?

Socotra Aloe, also known as Yucca schidigera, is a perennial shrub native to the Socotra archipelago, characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves and its ability to thrive in arid and coastal environments.
