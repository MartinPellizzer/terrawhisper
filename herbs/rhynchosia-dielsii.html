What's the taxonomical classification of Rhynchosia dielsii?

Rhynchosia dielsii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the biological order Fabales. It is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, a group widely known for its diverse legumes. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Rhynchosia, with its specific identification being the species dielsii.
