Ipecac (Cephaelis ipecacuanha)

Ipecac (Cephaelis ipecacuanha) is a member of the Rubiaceae family, native to India, Madagascar, and South America. Traditionally, its roots, stems, and leaves have been used for syrups, decoctions, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its expectorant, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
