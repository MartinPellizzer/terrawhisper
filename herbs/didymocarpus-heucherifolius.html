What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Didymocarpus heucherifoletes has a basal rosette of thick, leathery, kidney-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent, lighter veins. These flowers are characterized by their delicate structure and are often held in loose clusters at the apex of the stem.
