Begonia cathayana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Ultimately, it is identified by the genus Begonia and is specifically designated as the species cathayana.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These blooms are monoecious, meaning individual plants produce both male and female flowers on the same stem.
