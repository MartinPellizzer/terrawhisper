Habenaria pectinata

What's the taxonomical classification of Habenaria pectinata?

Habeanaria pectinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Asparagales. As a member of the family Orchidaceae, this plant is further distinguished by its genus, Habeanaria, and is specifically identified by its species name, pectinata.
