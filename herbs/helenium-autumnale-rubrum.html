Red Helenium (Helenium Autumnale Rubrum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Helenium autumnale rubrum.

Red helenium, scientifically known as Helenium autumnale rubrum, is a vibrant medicinal herb native to North America, valued for its therapeutic properties and historical significance. This plant is traditionally used as an adaptogen to support the body's ability to cope with stress and promote overall vitality. Its primary benefits include anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and circulatory stimulation properties, making it useful for treating conditions like arthritis, headaches, and circulatory disorders. In traditional Native American medicine, red helenium was used to treat wounds, fevers, and digestive issues, while in modern wellness practices, it is incorporated into herbal remedies and supplements for stress relief and immune support. Notably, it contains a unique compound called helenalin, which contributes to its potent anti-inflammatory effects, and its striking red-orange flowers make it both a medicinal and ornamental plant.
