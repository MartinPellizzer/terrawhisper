Balanites (Balanites aegyptiaca)

Balanites (Balanites aegyptiaca) is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, native to Sahel Region Of Africa, Northern Africa, and Arabian Peninsula. Traditionally, its seeds, fruits, and bark have been used for decoctions, powders, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and european herbal medicine.
