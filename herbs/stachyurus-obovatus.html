Stachyurus obovatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized into the family Stachyuraceae, within which it resides in the genus Stachyurus. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species obovatus.
