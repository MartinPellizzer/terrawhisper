Phlogacanthus pubinervius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Lamiales and the family Acanthaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Phlogacanthus, with its unique designation being the species pubinervius.

It is essential to wash the affected area immediately with soap and water to minimize these adverse reactions.
