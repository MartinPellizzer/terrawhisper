Pedicularis nigra

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis nigra?

Pedicularis nigra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, falling into the genus Pedicularis, and is specifically identified by its species name, nigra.
