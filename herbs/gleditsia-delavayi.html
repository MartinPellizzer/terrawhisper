Gleditsia delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae.         Gleditsia delavayi is prepared as a decoction of its bark, a powder made from its seeds, or a topical paste derived from its resin. Some users have also reported mild itching or redness following direct exposure to the plant's components. Careful handling is advised to avoid these adverse physical responses.
