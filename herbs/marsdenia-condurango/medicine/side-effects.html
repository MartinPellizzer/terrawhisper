10 Possible Side Effects Of Marsdenia Condurango (Condurango)

Marsdenia condurango has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal disturbances, including diarrhea, nausea, and stomach pain.

These side effects can occur due to the herb's high concentration of cardiac glycosides and anthraquinone compounds.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen a person's life by leading to dehydration, malnutrition, and even electrolyte imbalances, which can be particularly problematic for individuals with pre-existing kidney or heart conditions.
