10 Possible Side Effects Of Senna Alexandrina (Cassia)

These issues arise from excessive consumption or prolonged use, causing dehydration and electrolyte imbalance. Severe cases can lead to life disruptions, such as missed workdays, cancelled social events, and compromised daily routines.

In addition, the strain on the digestive system may trigger more frequent bowel movements and exacerbate underlying health conditions.
