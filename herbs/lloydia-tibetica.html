What's the taxonomical classification of Lloydia tibetica?

Lloydia tibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Liliales and the family Liliaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Llloydia, and its unique species designation, tibetica.
