What's the taxonomical classification of Brandisia discolor?

Brandisia discolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Lamiales and places it within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Brandisia, and its specific species name, discolor.
