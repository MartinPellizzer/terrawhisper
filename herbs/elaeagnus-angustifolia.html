Elaeagnus Angustifolia

Elaeagnus angustifolia, commonly known as silverberry or Russian olive, has been utilized for its medicinal properties in traditional systems of medicine such as Ayurveda and Chinese medicine.

The plant contains bioactive compounds like flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. It has been traditionally used to treat ailments such as respiratory infections, skin conditions, and digestive disorders. The leaves and fruits are often prepared as decoctions or poultices to alleviate symptoms of inflammation and promote wound healing.

Modern research is exploring its potential in managing diabetes and cardiovascular diseases due to its hypoglycemic and cardioprotective properties.
