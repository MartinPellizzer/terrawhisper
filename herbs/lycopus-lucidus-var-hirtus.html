Lycopus lucidus var. hirtus

What's the taxonomical classification of Lycopus lucidus var. hirtus?

Lycopus lucidus var. hirtus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is a member of the family Lamiaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Lycopus. Ultimately, its precise scientific designation is the species Lycopus lucidus var. hirtus.
