What is Pyrrosia adnascens?

Pyrrosia adnascens is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial epiphyte with a rhizome that is stoloniferous, giving rise to several stems. The stems are erect to pendent, simple, and 1-2 mm in diameter, with deltoid scales. The leaves are lanceolate, 4-15 cm long, and 2-5 cm wide, with a wavy or lobed margin and a rachis that is glandular and hairy.

The fronds are borne in a distichous pattern on the stems.
