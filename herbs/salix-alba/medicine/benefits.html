1. Reduces inflammation

White willow reduces inflammation because it contains a compound called salicin.

When ingested, salicin is converted into salicylic acid in the body, which is a potent anti-inflammatory agent. This compound works by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause inflammation and pain in the body.

By reducing the production of prostaglandins, white willow can help alleviate symptoms of inflammation such as swelling, redness, and pain. Additionally, white willow has been found to have a slower onset but longer duration of action compared to other anti-inflammatory drugs, making it a popular natural alternative for managing inflammation.

However, it is important to note that white willow should not be used by individuals who are allergic to aspirin or have bleeding disorders, and it should be used with caution in children and pregnant women.
