10 Precautions To Take When Using Gerbera Jamesonii (Barberton Daisy)

This is because the herb contains saponins, which can irritate the digestive system if taken in large amounts.

For example, if you consume too much of Gerbera jamesonii, you may experience unpleasant side effects, making it essential to follow the recommended dosages.
