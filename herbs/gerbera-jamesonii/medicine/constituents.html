10 Medicinal Constituents Of Gerbera Jamesonii (Barberton Daisy)

Gerbera jamesonii has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate various health issues.

For example, the flavonoids can improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels, while the antimicrobial properties can aid in wound healing and prevent infections.
