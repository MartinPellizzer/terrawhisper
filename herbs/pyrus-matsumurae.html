Possible Side Effects of Pyrus matsumurae

Improper use of the plant can also lead to an increased risk of bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties, and may interact with blood thinners, such as warfarin.

Furthermore, Pyrus matsumurae can also cause dizziness, headaches, and fatigue in some individuals, especially when used in combination with other herbs or supplements.
