Hamamelis (Hamamelis hybr)

Hamamelis (Hamamelis hybr) is a member of the Hamamelidaceae family, native to Eastern North America, and Northern Mexico. Traditionally, its bark, resin, and leaves have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, anti-inflammatory, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, native american herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
