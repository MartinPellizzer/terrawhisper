Siberian Ginseng (Eleutherococcus senticosus) Actions

Siberian Ginseng is known for its tonic and stimulant properties, enhancing energy and endurance. It also has anti-inflammatory and bitter effects, aiding digestion and reducing inflammation. Additionally, it acts as a carminative and antispasmodic, relieving gas and muscle spasms. Its diuretic, expectorant, and astringent actions support urinary health, respiratory function, and tissue repair.
