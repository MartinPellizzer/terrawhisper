As a result, eleutherococcus senticosus has been used to relieve symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and fatigue, which are often associated with chronic stress.

Regular consumption of eleutherococcus senticosus may also help alleviate symptoms of conditions such as post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), fibromyalgia, and chronic fatigue syndrome.
