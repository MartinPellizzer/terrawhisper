10 Possible Side Effects Of Rosa Damascena (Damask Rose)

Rosa damascena has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by sensitivity to the herb's essential oils or due to individual allergies. In severe cases, skin irritation can lead to swelling, redness, and even scarring.

People who experience these side effects may find everyday activities, such as washing dishes or wearing certain fabrics, unbearable due to increased discomfort and pain.
