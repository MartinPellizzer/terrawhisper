Ginkgo (Ginkgo biloba)

Ginkgo (Ginkgo biloba) is a member of the Ginkgoaceae family, native to Eastern Asia, China, and Korea. Traditionally, its leaves, bark, and resin have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and european herbal medicine.
