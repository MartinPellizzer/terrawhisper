Tamarix ramosissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Caryophyllales, the plant is categorized under the family Tamaricaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Tamarix, with its unique biological designation being the species ramosissima.
