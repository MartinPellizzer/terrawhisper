10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aspalathus Linearis (Rooibos)

Aspalathus linearis has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and aspalthin that possess medicinal properties.

These compounds have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects, which can help improve overall health by reducing the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and cancer. The antioxidants in rooibos tea can also protect against cell damage and improve cognitive function, making it a potential natural remedy for age-related disorders.

By incorporating rooibos into one's diet, individuals may experience improved cardiovascular health, reduced anxiety, and enhanced well-being.
