What is Stelis simplex?

Stelis simplex is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial plant with a short stem, 1-2 cm long, and 2-3 linear, subulate leaves, 2-4 cm long, that arise from the base of the plant. The inflorescence is a raceme, 2-4 cm long, with 3-6 flowers. The flowers are resupinate, greenish-white to yellowish-green in color, with a long, narrow labellum that is almost as long as the sepals.

The plant typically blooms in the summer months and is native to Central America.
