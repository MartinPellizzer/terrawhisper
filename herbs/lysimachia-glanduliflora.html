Lysimachia glanduliflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia glanduliflora?

Lysimachia glanduliflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Lysimachia, and is specifically identified by its species name, glanduliflora.
