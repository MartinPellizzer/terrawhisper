Rhamnus esquirolii

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhamnus esquirolii?

Rhamnus esquirolii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Rosales and the family Rhamnaceae. Ultimately, the species is identified by its unique binomial name, which comprises the genus Rhamnus and the specific epithet esquirolii.
