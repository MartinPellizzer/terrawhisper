Neocinnamomum delavayi

What's the taxonomical classification of Neocinnamomum delavayi?

Neocinnamomum delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent, it is placed in the order Laurales and falls within the family Lauraceae. Finally, its specific identity is defined by its placement in the genus Neocinnamomum, culminating in its unique species designation, delaayi.

Neocinnamomum delavayi has potent antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties derived from its high concentration of essential oils and bioactive secondary metabolites.
