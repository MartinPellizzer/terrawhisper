Sorbus hemsleyi

What's the taxonomical classification of Sorbus hemsleyi?

Sorbus hemsleyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Rosales. It further belongs to the family Rosaceae, a group known for its diverse flowering plants. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Sorbus, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species name hemsleyi.
