10 Medicinal Preparations Of Sesamum Indicum

Sesamum indicum has several medicinal preparations, such as oil, capsules, and teas.

These preparations are useful for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, and are used to treat various health issues, including skin conditions, digestive problems, and cardiovascular diseases. They are also believed to have immune-boosting effects and are used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat respiratory problems.

Regular consumption of sesame oil is also known to have beneficial effects on bone health.
