What's the taxonomical classification of Ornithoboea wildeana?

Ornithoboea wildeana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. Finally, its specific identity is defined by its placement within the genus Ornithoboea, with the specific epithet wildeana.
