Medinilla assamica

What's the taxonomical classification of Medinilla assamica?

Medinilla assamica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the species is placed in the order Myrtales and falls under the family Melastomataceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Medinilla, with its specific designation being the species assamica.

These preparations are often used to address skin inflammations or to alleviate gastrointestinal discomfort. The choice of preparation method depends on whether the healer intends to utilize the plant's systemic or localized properties.
