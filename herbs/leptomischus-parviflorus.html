What's the taxonomical classification of Leptomischus parviflorus?

Leptomischus parviflorus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Leptomischus, and its unique species designation, parviflorus.
