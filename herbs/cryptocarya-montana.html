What is Cryptocarya montana?

Cryptocarya montana is a species of plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is a tree that typically grows to 20-30 meters in height, with a straight, cylindrical bole and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, measuring 10-20 centimeters in length and 4-8 centimeters in width, with a leathery texture and a glossy green color. The flowers are small, yellowish-green in color, and arranged in panicles; they are dioecious, with male and female flowers on separate trees.

The fruit is a drupe, measuring 2-3 centimeters in length, with a reddish-brown color when mature, containing a single seed.
