Cryptocarya montana

Cryptocarya montana, also known as Mountain Sassafras, is a plant species native to the mountainous regions of Australia and Southeast Asia, belonging to the Lauraceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Cryptocarya montana include reducing inflammation, relieving digestive issues, and supporting immune function, making it a popular herb in traditional medicine.

Herbal preparations of Cryptocarya montana typically involve making a tea from its leaves, or using the essential oil extracted from its bark, which can be used in aromatherapy and topical applications.
