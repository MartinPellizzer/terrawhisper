What's the taxonomical classification of Elaeocarpus hainanensis?

Elaeocarpus hainanenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Oxalidales and is a member of the family Elaeocarpaceae. Specifically, it is categorized within the genus Elaeocarpus, with its unique identity defined by the species name hainanensis.
