What is Centaurea × paredensis?

Centaurea × paredensis is a hybrid species of plant within the genus Centaurea, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows to a height of 60-120 cm, with a spread of 30-60 cm. The leaves are lance-shaped, 10-15 cm long, and 1-2 cm wide, with a smooth margin. The plant produces large, pink to purple flowers, 5-7 cm in diameter, with a prominent spine-like projection at the base of the petals.

The flowers are arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence at the top of a tall stem.
