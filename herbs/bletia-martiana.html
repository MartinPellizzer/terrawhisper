Bletia martiana

Bletia martiana, commonly known as the "Butterfly Orchid", is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it effective in treating various ailments such as fever, cough, and respiratory issues.

The bioactive constituents present in Bletia martiana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions, including analgesic, anti-spasmodic, and anti-inflammatory effects.

The plant's therapeutic actions are attributed to its ability to modulate the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant properties, making it a valuable resource in traditional medicine.
