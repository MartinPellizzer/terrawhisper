Campanula pallida

What's the taxonomical classification of Campanula pallida?

Campanula pallida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Campanulaceae, falling into the genus Campanula, and is specifically identified by its species name, pallida.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Campanula pallida has pale violet to white bell-shaped flowers supported by slender, upright stems that reach heights of 10 to 40 centimeters. Higher up the stem, the foliage becomes smaller and more sparse as it approaches the floral bracts. The plant features a perennial root system that allows it to establish itself in rocky or alpine environments.
