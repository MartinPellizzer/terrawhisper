What is Oberonia lunata?

Oberonia lunata is an orchid species in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender stems and elliptical-lanceolate leaves, typically measuring 5-10 cm in length. The flowers are white or yellowish, with a curved sepal and a relatively long, yellowish-green labellum, measuring 1-2 cm in length. The plant blooms in the summer months and is found in tropical forests, typically growing in shaded areas at elevations of 300-1,000 meters.

Oberonia lunata is native to the tropical regions of Asia, specifically in countries such as India, China, and Southeast Asia.
