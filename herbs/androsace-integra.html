What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace integra?

Androsace integra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Primulaceae, situated within the genus Androsace, and is specifically identified by its species name, integra.
