What's the taxonomical classification of Cyclobalanopsis delavayi?

Cyclobalanopsis delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it resides in the order Fagales and is a member of the family Fagaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Cyclobalanopsis, and its unique species designation, delavayi.
