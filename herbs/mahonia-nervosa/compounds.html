Mahonia (Mahonia nervosa) Compounds

Mahonia nervosa contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while glycosides and saponins may support digestive health. Other compounds like terpenoids and coumarin add to its therapeutic profile.
