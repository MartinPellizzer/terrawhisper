What is Athanasia minuta?

Athanasia minuta is a flowering plant endemic to the Western Cape Province in South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is a member of the subfamily Asteroideae. The plant grows to a height of up to 15 cm, bearing linear-lanceolate leaves that are 2-5 cm long and 1-2 mm wide. The leaves are densely hairy on the lower surface and have a distinct white or pinkish colouration on the midrib.

The plant produces daisy-like flowers with a yellow or white disc surrounded by white or pinkish ray florets.
