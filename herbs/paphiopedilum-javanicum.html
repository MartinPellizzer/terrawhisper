What is Paphiopedilum javanicum?

Paphiopedilum javanicum is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a thick, tuberous rhizome and 2-3, plicate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are 10-20 cm long. The inflorescence is 20-40 cm long, with 2-5, fragrant, white to greenish-yellow flowers with a purple-tinged lip and a greenish-yellow to brownish-purple column. The flowers have a unique, spurred shape, with a 3-5 cm long, greenish-yellow to brownish-purple labellum.

The plant is native to the tropical forests of Java, Indonesia, and typically blooms in the summer months.
