What's the taxonomical classification of Impatiens recurvicornis?

Impatiens recurvicornis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Balsaminaceae. Ultimately, its specific classification is defined by the genus Impatiens and the species recurvicornis.
