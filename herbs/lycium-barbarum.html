What to know about Lycium barbarum (goji berry) before using it medicinally

Goji berry, scientifically known as Lycium barbarum, is a nutrient-rich herb that has been prized for its numerous health benefits.

Its antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals work together to boost immunity, improve eye health, and even support healthy aging. In the culinary world, goji berries are often used in traditional Chinese medicine teas, desserts, and snacks, adding natural sweetness and chewiness to a variety of dishes. From a horticultural perspective, goji berries require specific growing conditions and are typically harvested by hand, making them a labor-intensive crop.

With its ancient history dating back over 2,000 years, the goji berry has been revered for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine, with written records of its use found in the famous text "Shennong Ben Cao Jing".
