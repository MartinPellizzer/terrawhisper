What's the taxonomical classification of Scurrula chingii?

Scurrula chingii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Santalales. This plant is a member of the family Loranthaceae and falls under the genus Scurrula, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Scurrula chingii.
