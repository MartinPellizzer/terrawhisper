10 Health Benefits Of Cassia Senna (Senna)

Cassia senna has health benefits such as promoting digestive regularity and reducing constipation due to its anthraquinone glycosides.

These medicinal properties help stimulate bowel movements, relieving symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and chronic constipation. By improving gut health, senna can alleviate discomfort, reduce bloating, and even increase energy levels.

For individuals with IBS, regular consumption of senna may provide relief from abdominal pain, gas, and diarrhea, significantly enhancing their quality of life.
