1. Treats diarrhea

Diarrhea is a gastrointestinal disorder characterized by frequent, loose, or watery stools.

It can be caused by infections, food intolerances, or digestive system disorders.

This condition often affects individuals with weakened immune systems, travelers in unfamiliar regions, or those consuming contaminated food.

Cassia senna plant has been traditionally used to treat diarrhea due to its laxative properties.

It helps regulate bowel movements and reduce the frequency of stools, offering relief to those suffering from this condition.

How this herb helps with treats diarrhea?

Cassia senna treats diarrhea because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds possess antimicrobial properties that help eliminate harmful pathogens in the gastrointestinal tract. The alkaloids, including sennosides, also have a mild laxative effect that can regulate bowel movements. Flavonoids contribute to reducing inflammation and improving gut health.

Together, these constituents support the body's natural processes to alleviate diarrhea symptoms.
