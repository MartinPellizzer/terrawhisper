What's the taxonomical classification of Rorippa indica?

Rorippa indica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Brassicales and the family Brassicaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Rorippa, and its species name, indica.
