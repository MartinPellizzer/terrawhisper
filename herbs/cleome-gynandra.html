Cleome Shrub (Cleome gynandra)

Cleome Shrub (Cleome gynandra) is a member of the Cleomaceae family, native to West Africa, Central Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
