Curculigo Orchid (Curculigo orchioides)

Curculigo Orchid (Curculigo orchioides) is a member of the Orchidaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, Indonesia, and Philippines. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and european herbal medicine.
