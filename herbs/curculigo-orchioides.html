What is Curculigo Orchid (Curculigo Orchioides)?

Curculigo Orchid, or Curculigo orchioides, is a small, tuberous plant native to Southeast Asia, commonly used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported ability to enhance vitality, treat sexual dysfunction, and support overall health.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Curculigo Orchid is Curculigo orchioides. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Liliopsida, order Asparagales, family Orchidaceae, genus Curculigo, and species Curculigo orchioides. It is commonly known as the Indian arrowroot in India and the African arrowroot in Africa. The plant is also referred to as the arrowhead orchid in some regions. Synonyms include Curculigo africana (botanical) and various regional names such as "Kudam" in Tamil and "Kudamathi" in Malayalam.
