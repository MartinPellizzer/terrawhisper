Curculigo orchioides is a small, herbaceous plant with a short, rhizomatous stem that gives rise to a single, upright flowering scape bearing a dense cluster of small, yellowish-green flowers.

Curculigo orchioides is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as India, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, and the Philippines, and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions with moist, shaded habitats.

Curculigo Orchid (Curculigo orchioides) has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to support kidney function, enhance sexual vitality, and treat conditions such as impotence and infertility.

Curculigo orchioides has been traditionally used in Ayurveda, Chinese medicine, and traditional African medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
