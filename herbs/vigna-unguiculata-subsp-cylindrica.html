Vigna unguiculata subsp. cylindrica

What's the taxonomical classification of Vigna unguiculata subsp. cylindrica?

Vigna unguiculata subsp. cylindrica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Vigna, ultimately identifying it as the species Vigna unguiculata subsp. cylindrica.
