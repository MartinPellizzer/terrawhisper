What is Pabstiella bacillaris?

Pabstiella bacillaris is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by a slender stem, 2-4 cm in height, bearing a single leaf and a raceme of flowers, typically 2-5 in number. The leaf is elliptic in shape, 2-5 cm in length, and 0.5-1.5 cm in width. The flowers are white or yellowish-green in color, with a labellum that is deeply lobed and has a rounded tip.

The plant is native to South America, specifically in the countries of Brazil and Colombia.
