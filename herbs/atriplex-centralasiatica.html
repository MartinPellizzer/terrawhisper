What's the taxonomical classification of Atriplex centralasiatica?

Atriplex centralasiatica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Amaranthaceae, which places it within the genus Atriplex, eventually reaching its specific identification at the species level as Atriplex centralasiatica.

What are the molecular targets of this plant?

These interactions also extend to the modulation of nitric oxide synthase (NOS), which helps regulate oxidative stress within cellular environments. Additionally, certain phytochemicals in the plant demonstrate affinity for various antioxidant enzymes, thereby neutralizing reactive oxygen species. Through these multi-target molecular interactions, the plant exerts its observed anti-inflammatory and cytoprotective medicinal effects.
