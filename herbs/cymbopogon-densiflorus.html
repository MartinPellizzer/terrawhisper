What's the taxonomical classification of Cymbopogon densiflorus?

Cymbopogon densiflorus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Poales, the plant is a member of the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Cymbopogon, with the specific epithet densiflorus.
