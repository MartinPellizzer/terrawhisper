Cancer Bush (Sutherlandia frutescens) Compounds

The primary compounds in Cancer Bush include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Terpenoids and glycosides contribute to its medicinal properties, while tannins and saponins support its astringent and detoxifying actions. Coumarin and alkaloids add to its therapeutic potential, and essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its overall bioactive profile.
