Cancer Bush (Sutherlandia frutescens) Uses

Cancer Bush is primarily used for cancer treatment, supporting immune function, and managing infections. It also addresses autoimmune diseases, HIV/AIDS, and respiratory conditions like asthma and lung inflammation. Additionally, it shows potential in managing diabetes and malaria.

Cancer Bush (Sutherlandia frutescens) Monograph

1. Cancer

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is traditionally used in South African herbal medicine to support cancer treatment. It is believed to help reduce tumor size and alleviate symptoms, though it is not a cure. Often combined with other herbs, it is used to boost energy and improve quality of life during chemotherapy or radiation. Always consult a healthcare professional before use.

2. Infections

Cancer Bush is used to support immune function and may help manage infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It is often included in herbal formulations to aid in the treatment of respiratory and urinary tract infections. Its active compounds may enhance the body's natural defenses against pathogens.

3. Autoimmune diseases

Cancer Bush has been studied for its potential to support immune system balance in autoimmune diseases. Its compounds may modulate immune responses, reducing inflammation and overactivity. Preliminary research suggests it could help manage symptoms, though more clinical trials are needed for confirmation. It is often used alongside conventional treatments under professional guidance.

4. Hiv/aids

Sutherlandia frutescens, known as Cancer Bush, is used in traditional herbal medicine to support individuals living with HIV/AIDS. It is believed to help boost the immune system and may alleviate some symptoms associated with the condition. However, it should not replace prescribed medical treatments and should be used under professional guidance.

5. Malaria

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, has been traditionally used in South Africa to support treatment for malaria. It is believed to enhance immune function and may help reduce fever symptoms. However, it is not a substitute for conventional antimalarial drugs and should be used under professional guidance.

6. Asthma

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is traditionally used in South African herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It is believed to help alleviate asthma symptoms by reducing inflammation and improving lung function. While not a cure, it may complement conventional treatments when used under professional guidance.

7. Lung inflammation

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is traditionally used in South African herbal medicine to support the body's response to lung inflammation. It is believed to have anti-inflammatory and immune-modulating properties that may help alleviate symptoms associated with respiratory conditions.

8. Respiratory infections

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is used in herbal medicine to support the body's response to respiratory infections. It is believed to enhance immune function and may help reduce symptoms associated with conditions like bronchitis and pneumonia. Traditionally, it is prepared as a tea or tincture to aid in respiratory health.

9. Lung infections

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is traditionally used in South African herbal medicine to support the treatment of lung infections. It is believed to enhance immune function and may help reduce inflammation in the respiratory system. Its antimicrobial properties are thought to aid in clearing pathogens from the lungs.

10. Diabetes

Sutherlandia frutescens, or Cancer Bush, is traditionally used in herbal medicine to support diabetes management. It may help regulate blood sugar levels by enhancing insulin sensitivity and improving glucose metabolism. Its bioactive compounds, such as alkaloids and flavonoids, contribute to its potential therapeutic effects in diabetes care.
