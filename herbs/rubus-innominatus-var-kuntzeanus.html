Rubus innominatus var. kuntzeanus

What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus innominatus var. kuntzeanus?

Rubus innominatus var. kuntzeanus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Rosaceae, eventually reaching the genus Rubus, which defines its specific group. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique scientific designation as the species Rubus innominatus var. kuntzeanus.
