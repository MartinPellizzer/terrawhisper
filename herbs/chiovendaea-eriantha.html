    What is Chiovendaea eriantha?

    Chiovendaea eriantha is a species of plant in the family Fabaceae, native to Ethiopia.

    It is a shrub or small tree that grows up to 3 meters in height, with a branching habit and brown, pubescent twigs. The leaves are alternate, elliptic to obovate in shape, 10-20 cm long, 3-6 cm wide, with 2-4 pairs of secondary veins and a glandular pubescent underside. The flowers are pink or white, in axillary racemes, 1-2 cm long, with 5 petals and 10 stamens.

    The pods are linear, 5-10 cm long, 1-2 cm wide, with 2-5 seeds per pod.
