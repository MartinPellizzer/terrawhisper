Crataegus monogyna, commonly known as hawthorn, is a medicinal herb that has been traditionally used to help alleviate conditions such as hypertension, heart failure, and angina pectoris.

In herbal medicine, Crataegus monogyna is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or capsules, and is commonly used to support cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.
