What is Erigeron abyssinicus?

Erigeron abyssinicus is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It has a stem that grows up to 20-30 cm tall, with linear leaves that are 2-5 cm long and 0.5-1.5 cm wide. The leaves are alternate, sessile, and have a crenate-serrate margin. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in a capitulum, with 10-20 florets, and the corolla is 3-5 mm long and tubular.

The fruit is an achene, 2-3 mm long, with a pappus of 30-40 bristles.
