What side-effects this plant can have?

Helixanthera parasitica can cause severe contact dermatitis, systemic nausea, and localized numbness upon skin exposure. Some individuals might also experience neurological symptoms like dizziness or tingling in the extremities. Careful avoidance of all plant parts is necessary to prevent these adverse reactions.
