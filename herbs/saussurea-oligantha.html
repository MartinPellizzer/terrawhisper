Saussurea oligantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea oligantha?

Saussurea oligantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Saussurea, specifically identified as the species oligantha.
