What is Abrotanella pusilla?

Abrotanella pusilla is a small, perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It has narrow, linear leaves that are 2-6 cm long and 1-3 mm wide, arranged oppositely along the stem. The plant produces small, yellow or purple flowers in a capitulum, a type of inflorescence, with 2-5 florets that are 5-7 mm in diameter. The capitulum is surrounded by 3-5 phyllaries that are lance-shaped and 1-2 mm long.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1-10 cm and is found in New Zealand and Australia, often in rocky or stony areas.
