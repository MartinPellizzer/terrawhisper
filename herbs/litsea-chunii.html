Litsea chunii

What's the taxonomical classification of Litsea chunii?

Litsea chunii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is a member of the order Laurales. This plant is further categorized into the family Lauraceae, placing it within the genus Litsea, and is specifically identified by its species name, chunii.

While specific clinical studies on human toxicity are limited, many plants in the Lauraceae family contain essential oils that act as potential irritants to sensitive tissues. Topical application might result in contact dermatitis, characterized by redness, itching, or localized swelling. If consumed, the chemical constituents could potentially trigger nausea or stomach discomfort in sensitive individuals. It is therefore advisable to exercise caution and avoid direct contact without prior testing.
