Lemmaphyllum drymoglossoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Polypodiaceae, falling specifically under the genus Lemmaphyllum, with its unique designation being the species drymoglossoides.
