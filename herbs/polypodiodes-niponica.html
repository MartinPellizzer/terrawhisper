What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Polypodioides niponica has creeping rhizomes that are scaly and produce pinnate fronds that typically measure between 10 and 30 centimeters in length. Each leaflet is oblong to lanceolate in shape and possesses a dull green color on both the upper and lower surfaces. The underside of the fronds features small, circular sori arranged in rows near the margins of the pinnules.
