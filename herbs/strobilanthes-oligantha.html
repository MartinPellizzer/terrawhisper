Strobilanthes oligantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes oligantha?

Strobilanthes oligantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Acanthaceae, falling under the genus Strobilanthes, and is specifically identified by its species name, oligantha.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, respiratory ailments, stomach aches, and inflammation. The leaves and stems are often prepared into traditional decoctions or poultices to soothe irritated skin. When ingested, the plant helps alleviate symptoms of indigestion and abdominal discomfort. It also serves as a remedy for coughs and other breathing difficulties in various folk medicine practices. These applications demonstrate the plant's diverse role in managing both localized and systemic health issues.
