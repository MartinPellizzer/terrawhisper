Polygonum bungeanum

What's the taxonomical classification of Polygonum bungeanum?

Polygonum bungeanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Caryophyllales. It is a further taxonomic division of the family Polygonaceae, falling under the genus Polygonum, and is specifically identified by its species name, bungeanum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Polygonum bungeanum has slender, erect stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and are characterized by distinct, swollen nodes. Tiny, inconspicuous flowers are produced in axillary clusters, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is distinguished by its delicate, wiry structure and the presence of small, ocreae sheath-like structures at each node.
