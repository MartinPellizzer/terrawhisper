What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Teucrium Chamaedrys (Germander)?

Teucrium chamaedrys, also known as germander, has health benefits such as soothing digestive issues and alleviating symptoms of anxiety and insomnia.

Its medicinal constituents include diterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Teucrium chamaedrys is commonly prepared as an infusion or tea, and in some cases, as a tincture or essential oil.

However, high doses of germander can cause liver damage, making it essential to use it in moderation and under the guidance of a qualified healthcare practitioner.
