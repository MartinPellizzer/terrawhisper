Germander (Teucrium chamaedrys) Actions

Germander exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic properties. It also acts as a carminative and astringent, helping to relieve digestive discomfort. Its stimulating and sedative effects make it useful for balancing nervous system function. Additionally, it has antispasmodic and expectorant qualities that support respiratory and muscular health.
