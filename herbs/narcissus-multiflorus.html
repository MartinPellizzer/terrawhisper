Narcissus multiflorus

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, and it is valued for its health benefits, including its ability to lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and act as an antioxidant.

The therapeutic actions of Narcissus multiflorus include its analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties, which make it effective in treating pain, reducing swelling, and relieving muscle spasms.

Possible Side Effects of Narcissus multiflorus
