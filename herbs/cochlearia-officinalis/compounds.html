Cochlearia (Cochlearia officinalis) Compounds

Cochlearia officinalis contains several active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and detoxifying qualities. Coumarin and terpenoids may support digestive health, while alkaloids and anthocyanins offer additional therapeutic benefits.
