10 Possible Side Effects Of Aloysia Citriodora (Lemon Verbena)

Aloysia citriodora has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects can be caused by ingesting large amounts of the herb, using essential oils in high concentrations, or having pre-existing sensitivities.

In severe cases, these reactions can worsen lives by causing respiratory distress, gastrointestinal problems, and emotional discomfort, potentially leading to missed work, lost income, and strained relationships.

This can cause respiratory issues like sneezing, congestion, and coughing in sensitive individuals.
