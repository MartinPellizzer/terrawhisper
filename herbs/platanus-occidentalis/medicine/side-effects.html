1. Irritates skin causing redness

This irritation is more likely to occur in individuals with sensitive skin or a history of allergies to certain plants.

3. Triggers asthma attacks happening

Platanus occidentalis triggers asthma attacks happening in some individuals due to its allergenic properties.

The plant's pollen, sap, and leaves contain potent allergens that can cause inflammation and constriction of the airways, leading to bronchospasm and respiratory distress.

Additionally, the plant's volatile organic compounds (VOCs) can exacerbate existing respiratory conditions, making it difficult for individuals with asthma to breathe freely.

Platanus occidentalis induces diarrhea appearing suddenly due to its high content of tannins and anthraquinones.
