Dog Rose (Rosa Canina Var. Ololeia)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Rosa canina var. ololeia.

Dog rose, scientifically known as Rosa canina var. ololeia, is a medicinal herb prized for its adaptogenic properties and rich nutritional profile. It is commonly used in traditional medicine to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote cardiovascular health due to its high content of vitamin C, flavonoids, and antioxidants. Historically, it has been valued in European herbalism for its ability to soothe respiratory ailments and aid in digestion, often brewed as a tea or used in tinctures. In modern wellness, dog rose is incorporated into skincare products and dietary supplements to enhance skin health and support metabolic function. Its unique combination of a subtle floral aroma and tart flavor, along with the presence of unique compounds like ellagic acid, makes it a distinctive and versatile plant in both traditional and contemporary health practices.
