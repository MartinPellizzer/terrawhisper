Rhododendron benomense

Rhododendron benomense is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, native to the Himalayas and southeastern Tibet.

The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine due to its medicinal properties, which include antibacterial and anti-inflammatory compounds.

The rhizomes of Rhododendron benomense have been reported to have health benefits, such as reducing fever and alleviating pain, and have been used to treat various ailments.

Herbal preparations of Rhododendron benomense include decoctions and infusions, which can be used to make teas or applied topically to the skin.
