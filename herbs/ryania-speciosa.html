Ryania Tree (Ryania speciosa)

Ryania Tree (Ryania speciosa) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to South America, Central America, and Caribbean. Traditionally, its leaves, bark, and stems have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and african traditional medicine.
