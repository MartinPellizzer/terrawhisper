Biondia insignis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Apocynaceae, which dictates its specific botanical characteristics. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Biondia, with the specific epithet insignis completing its unique scientific designation.
