What's the taxonomical classification of Gynostemma burmanicum?

Gymnostemma burmanicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Cucurbitaceae, which characterizes its botanical lineage. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Gymnostemma, with its unique designation being the species burmanicum.
