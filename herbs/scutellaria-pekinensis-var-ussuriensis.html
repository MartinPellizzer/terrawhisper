Scutellaria pekinensis var. ussuriensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria pekinensis var. ussuriensis?

Scutelariia pekinenensis var. ussuriensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues into the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by its distinct morphological features. At the more specific levels of classification, it is identified as a member of the genus Scutellaria, specifically categorized as the variety ussuriensis within the species Scutellaria pekinenensis.
