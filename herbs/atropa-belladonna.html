Possible Side Effects of atropa belladonna

Atropa belladonna can have side effects if used improperly, such as hallucinations, delirium, and altered mental status due to its potent anticholinergic properties.

Ingesting large amounts of the herb can lead to severe symptoms, including tachycardia, dry mouth, and urinary retention.

If used inappropriately, it can also cause respiratory depression, coma, and even death.

Additionally, long-term use of Atropa belladonna can lead to cognitive impairment, memory loss, and permanent neurological damage.
