10 Possible Side Effects Of Opuntia Opuntia (Prickly Pear)

Opuntia opuntia has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by consuming large amounts of the herb, using it for extended periods, or combining it with other medications. Diarrhea can lead to dehydration, nutrient deficiencies, and increased risk of infections, worsening one's quality of life.

Allergic reactions can cause difficulty breathing, itching, and swelling, potentially life-threatening in severe cases.

The sap of this cactus contains a high concentration of latex, which is a common allergen responsible for triggering adverse reactions in people with latex sensitivity.
