What's the taxonomical classification of Rosa sertata?

Rosa sertaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic lineage further, it falls under the order Rosales and the family Rosaecae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Rosa and is specifically designated as the species sertaata.
