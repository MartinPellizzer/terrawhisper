What's the taxonomical classification of Cyperus amuricus?

Cyperus amuricus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Poales. Its taxonomic classification continues through the family Cyperaceae and the genus Cyperus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species amuricus.
