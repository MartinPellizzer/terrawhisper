What's the taxonomical classification of Hedychium sinoaureum?

Hedychium sinoaureum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Zingiberales. This plant is a member of the family Zingiberaceae and falls within the genus Hedychium, specifically identified by its species name, sinoaureum.
