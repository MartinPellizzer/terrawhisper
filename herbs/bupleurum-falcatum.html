Bupleurum Falcatum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Bupleurum falcatum, commonly known as Chinese thornapple, is a flowering plant native to Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used in herbal medicine to support liver function and alleviate symptoms of digestive disorders.

The bioactive constituents in Bupleurum falcatum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from the roots and leaves to harness its health benefits.
