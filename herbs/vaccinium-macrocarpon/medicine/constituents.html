10 Medicinal Constituents Of Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Highbush Cranberry)

These compounds provide antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties that can benefit human health. For instance, the flavonoids in highbush cranberry may help reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health by lowering blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Additionally, its anthocyanins have been shown to exhibit antioxidant activity, which can protect against cell damage and oxidative stress, potentially improving cognitive function and reducing the risk of age-related diseases.
