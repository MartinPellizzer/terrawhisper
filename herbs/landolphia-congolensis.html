What's the taxonomical classification of Landolphia congolensis?

Landolphia congolensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Gentianaales. It is a member of the family Apocynaceae, which places it within the genus Landolphia, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species congolenis.
