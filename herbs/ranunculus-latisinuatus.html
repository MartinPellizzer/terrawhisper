Ranunculus latisinuatus

Ranunculus latisinuatus, also known as buttercup, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

The plant contains compounds like ranunculin and protoanemonin, which have been found to possess medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

Ranunculus latisinuatus has been used to treat various health issues, such as skin conditions, wounds, and gastrointestinal problems, due to its antiseptic and antispasmodic properties.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in the form of infusions, tinctures, or salves to take advantage of its medicinal and health benefits.
