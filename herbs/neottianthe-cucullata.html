Neottianthe cucullata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Neottianthe and is further distinguished at the species level as Neottianthe cucullaata.
