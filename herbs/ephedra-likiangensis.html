What are the pharmacological activities of Ephedra likiangensis?

This plant has potent pharmacological activities including sympathomimetic, bronchodilatory, and thermogenic properties primarily driven by its high concentration of ephedrine alkaloids. These alkaloids act as agonists on alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors to stimulate the central nervous system and increase heart rate. The substance also functions as a potent stimulant that can enhance metabolic rates and promote fat oxidation. Furthermore, its ability to relax smooth muscles in the airways makes it effective for treating respiratory conditions like asthma. What side effects this plant can have?

This plant can cause severe cardiovascular and neurological complications including tachycardia, hypertension, palpitations, nausea, dizziness, insomnia, anxiety, headache, and tremors.
