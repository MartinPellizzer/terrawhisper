What is Jasminum multiflorum?

Jasminum multiflorum is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Oleaceae.

It is an evergreen climbing shrub with woody stems, simple, alternate, and elliptical leaves that are 4-10 cm long and 1.5-4 cm wide, with entire margins and a petiole 1-2 cm long. The leaves are dark green, leathery, and have a glossy upper surface. Jasminum multiflorum produces fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers in clusters, 7-10 cm in diameter, with five petals and a calyx of five sepals, in the axils of the leaves from spring to summer.

The flowers are followed by red or purple berries that are 1-2 cm in diameter and contain several seeds.
