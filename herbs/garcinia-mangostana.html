Mangosteen (Garcinia mangostana)

Mangosteen (Garcinia mangostana) is a member of the Guttiferae family, native to Southeast Asia, Indonesia, and Philippines. Traditionally, its fruits, bark, and leaves have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
