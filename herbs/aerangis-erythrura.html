Aerangis erythrura

Aerangis erythrura, commonly known as the Red Orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to sub-Saharan Africa.

The health benefits of Aerangis erythrura include its potential to treat various ailments, such as fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
