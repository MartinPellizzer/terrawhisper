Spiraea thuunbergii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Spiraea, and its specific species name, thunbergii.

Ingestion of unknown plant species can lead to nausea, vomiting, or abdominal cramping in sensitive subjects. Scientific documentation regarding its specific toxicity in humans remains limited, necessitating caution during handling.
