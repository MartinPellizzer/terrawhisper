10 Health Benefits Of Helenium Amarum (Sneezeweed)

Helenium amarum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These properties make it effective in treating various ailments like arthritis, respiratory issues, and skin infections. The plant's compounds can help reduce pain and swelling, while its antimicrobial properties combat bacterial and fungal infections. For example, sneezeweed tea has been used to alleviate symptoms of bronchitis and asthma, improving lung function and overall breathing capacity.

Its topical application has also shown promise in treating acne, reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing.
