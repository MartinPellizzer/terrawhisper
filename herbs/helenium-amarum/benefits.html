10 Best Helenium Amarum Health Benefits

Helenium amarum, commonly known as the American coneflower, offers a range of health benefits due to its rich content of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.

The plant contains compounds such as flavonoids and sesquiterpene lactones, which may help boost the immune system and reduce oxidative stress in the body. Some studies suggest that Helenium amarum may have potential in supporting cardiovascular health by improving blood flow and reducing inflammation.

However, more research is needed to fully understand its therapeutic potential and ensure safe usage.
