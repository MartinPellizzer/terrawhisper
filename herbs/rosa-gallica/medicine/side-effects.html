10 Possible Side Effects Of Rosa Gallica (Damask Rose)

Rosa gallica has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin irritation.

These side effects are often caused by excessive consumption or skin exposure to the herb, which can lead to anaphylaxis, diarrhea, or rashes.

In severe cases, these reactions can worsen people's lives by restricting daily activities, causing chronic discomfort, and potentially triggering other health conditions.
