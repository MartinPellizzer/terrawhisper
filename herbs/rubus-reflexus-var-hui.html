Rubus reflexus var. hui

What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus reflexus var. hui?

Rubus reflexus var. hui belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is situated in Equisetopsida, followed by its placement in the order Rosales. The plant is a member of the family Rosaceae and falls under the genus Rubus. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is identified as the species Rubus reflexus var. hui.
