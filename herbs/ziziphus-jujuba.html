Planting tips for Chinese date involve selecting a location with adequate space for the tree's mature size, typically 10-15 meters (33-49 feet) in diameter. Common pests of Chinese date include scale insects, mealybugs, and spider mites, while common diseases include anthracnose, root rot, and powdery mildew, which can be managed through integrated pest and disease management practices, including pruning, crop rotation, and applying fungicides and insecticides as needed.
