Possible Side Effects of ziziphus jujuba

Ziziphus jujuba can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea and stomach cramps, due to its high levels of saponins and flavonoids.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of ziziphus jujuba may cause more severe side effects, including liver damage and kidney problems.
