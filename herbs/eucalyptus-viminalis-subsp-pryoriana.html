Eucalyptus viminalis subsp.

pryoriana, commonly known as the mallee blue gum, is a subspecies of Eucalyptus viminalis, a type of evergreen tree native to Australia.

This plant is valued for its health benefits, which include reducing inflammation and relieving respiratory issues due to its expectorant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Eucalyptus viminalis subsp.
