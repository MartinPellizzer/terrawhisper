What's the taxonomical classification of Elatostema involucratum?

Elatostema involucratum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Urticaceae, which places it among a diverse group of herbaceous plants. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Elatostema, specifically designated as the species involucratum.
