What is Podalyria retzii?

Podalyria retzii is a shrub or small tree species belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are 30-60 cm long and have 4-6 pairs of pinnae, each consisting of 6-10 pinnules. The plant produces white to pinkish flowers in a dense spike, 10-20 cm long, and is pollinated by bees and butterflies. The fruit is a pod, 4-6 cm long, containing 2-4 seeds.

Podalyria retzii is native to southern Africa, where it grows in well-drained sandy soils in savannas and grasslands.
