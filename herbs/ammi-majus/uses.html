Ammi Majus (Ammi majus) Uses

Ammi majus is used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and asthma by reducing inflammation and mucus production. It helps alleviate nasal congestion and sinusitis, and is also applied for allergic rhinitis and respiratory tract infections. Its properties support clearing lung congestion and easing breathing difficulties.

Ammi Majus (Ammi majus) Monograph

1. Cough

Ammi majus has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for coughs. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, making it easier to expel. It is often prepared as a tincture or tea to alleviate persistent or productive coughs. However, it should be used with caution due to its potential toxicity if misused.

2. Respiratory infections

Ammi majus is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and clearing mucus. It is commonly applied for conditions like bronchitis and coughs. Its expectorant properties help loosen congestion, making it a valuable herb in herbal treatments for respiratory infections.

3. Bronchitis

Ammi majus has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, easing coughing and improving airflow. It is often prepared as a tincture or infusion to reduce inflammation and promote healing in the respiratory tract.

4. Asthma

Ammi majus has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It is believed to help alleviate asthma symptoms by acting as a bronchodilator and reducing inflammation in the airways. Its essential oils may enhance lung function and ease breathing in individuals with asthma.

5. Nasal congestion

Ammi majus is used to alleviate nasal congestion due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps clear mucus and reduce swelling in the nasal passages. Traditionally prepared as a nasal spray or inhalation, it provides relief for sinusitis and common cold symptoms. Its effectiveness makes it a valuable remedy in herbal medicine for respiratory issues.

6. Allergic rhinitis

Ammi majus has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by inhibiting histamine release. Its anti-inflammatory effects further support respiratory comfort in allergic conditions. It is often used in herbal formulations for seasonal allergies.

7. Respiratory tract inflammation

Ammi Majus has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in reducing inflammation of the respiratory tract. Its anti-inflammatory properties may help alleviate symptoms associated with conditions like bronchitis and asthma. It is often prepared as a tincture or tea to promote expectoration and soothe irritated airways.

8. Lung congestion

Ammi majus is used to address lung congestion by promoting respiratory clearance and reducing mucus buildup. Its expectorant properties help loosen phlegm, making it easier to expel. It is often employed in herbal formulations for conditions like bronchitis and chronic cough.

9. Respiratory tract infections

Ammi majus has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for conditions like bronchitis and coughs. It contains compounds that may help reduce inflammation and loosen mucus, aiding in clearing the airways. Its expectorant properties make it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for managing respiratory tract infections.

10. Sinusitis

Ammi majus is used in herbal medicine to support the treatment of sinusitis due to its anti-inflammatory and mucolytic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and thin mucus, aiding in the drainage of the sinuses. Often combined with other herbs, it is valued for its ability to alleviate symptoms and promote respiratory health.
