10 Health Benefits Of Ammi Majus (Bishop'S Weed)

Ammi majus has health benefits such as relieving digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and exhibiting antimicrobial properties.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, terpenes, and volatile oils that have been shown to soothe stomach upset, alleviate joint pain, and combat infections.

For example, bishop's weed tea may help improve digestion for individuals with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), while its anti-inflammatory compounds can provide relief from arthritis symptoms, ultimately improving overall quality of life.
