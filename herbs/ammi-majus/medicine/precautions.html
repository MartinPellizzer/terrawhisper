1. Start with low dose initially

When using Ammi majus medicinally, it's important to take correct dosage form, as incorrect preparation or dosing can lead to adverse effects.

This is because the plant contains a compound called coumarin, which can be toxic in large quantities. Taking the wrong dosage form can cause an imbalance of this compound, resulting in symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.

Additionally, improper preparation can reduce the efficacy of the remedy, making it ineffective for treating intended health issues.

4. Start with low dose initially

When using Ammi majus medicinally, it's important to avoid exceeding recommended amounts.

This is crucial because excessive consumption can lead to adverse reactions such as allergic contact dermatitis and phototoxicity. Additionally, high doses may increase the risk of miscarriage and birth defects when used during pregnancy or breastfeeding.

By sticking to established guidelines, users can minimize the potential for harm and ensure safe and effective use of this herb.
