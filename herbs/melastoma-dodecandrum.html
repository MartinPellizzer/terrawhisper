Melastoma dodecandrum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Myrtales and the family Melastomataceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Melastoma. Finally, the plant is identified at the species level as dodecandrum.
