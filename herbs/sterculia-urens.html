Sterculia Urens Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Sterculia urens, commonly known as the African breadfruit, is a tropical plant native to Africa, valued for its edible fruit and medicinal properties.

This herb is used traditionally for its ability to support digestive health and manage diabetes due to its hypoglycemic effects.

The bioactive constituents include tannins, saponins, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and poultices can be made from the leaves and seeds to harness its medicinal benefits.
