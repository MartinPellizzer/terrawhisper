10 Best Calendula Officinalis Health Benefits

Calendula officinalis, commonly known as pot marigold, is renowned for its numerous health benefits, particularly due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

It has been traditionally used to support skin health, helping to reduce redness, irritation, and promote healing in conditions like eczema and psoriasis. The plant contains compounds such as flavonoids and triterpenoids, which contribute to its wound-healing and antimicrobial effects. Calendula is also used in herbal remedies to alleviate digestive issues and may support immune function.

Its versatility makes it a valuable natural ingredient in both topical applications and dietary supplements.
