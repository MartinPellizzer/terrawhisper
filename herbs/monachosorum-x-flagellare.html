What's the taxonomical classification of Monachosorum × flagellare?

Monachosorum × flagellare belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic classification continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, placing it under the order Polypodiales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Dennstaedtiaceae, specifically falling under the genus Monachosorum, with the species designation flagellare.
