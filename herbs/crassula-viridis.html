Crassula viridis

Crassula viridis, also known as the Green Jade Plant, is a succulent species in the Crassulaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine, where it is used to treat various health issues.

The leaves and stems of the Crassula viridis are rich in antioxidants and have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties, making it beneficial for reducing stress, improving sleep, and boosting the immune system.

The herbal preparation of the plant can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, which can be consumed to harness its health benefits.
