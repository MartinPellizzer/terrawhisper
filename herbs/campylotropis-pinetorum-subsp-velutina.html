Campylotropis pinetorum subsp. velutina

What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis pinetorum subsp. velutina?

Campylotropis pinetorum subsp. velutina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Fabales and is a constituent of the family Fabaceae. Within this botanical framework, it is situated in the genus Campylotropis, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies velutina of the species Campylotropis pinetorum.
