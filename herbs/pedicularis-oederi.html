Pedicularis oederi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is specifically situated within the family Orobanchaceae, which defines its close botanical relatives. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Pedicularis, and its specific biological identity is completed by its species designation, oederi.
