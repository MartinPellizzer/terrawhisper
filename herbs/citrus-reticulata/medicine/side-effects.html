10 Possible Side Effects Of Citrus Reticulata (Mandarin)

Citrus reticulata has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues.

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, skin contact with the oils, and sensitivity to certain compounds.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen lives, causing discomfort, inflammation, and even respiratory problems, leading to missed workdays, reduced productivity, and compromised overall well-being.
