1. Causes allergic reactions

Opuntia humifusa causes allergic reactions due to its high concentration of latex and mucilage, which can stimulate an immune response in some individuals.

The plant's sap contains allergenic compounds like opuntiol and humifusin, which may cause symptoms such as skin rashes, hives, and itching upon contact or ingestion.

Additionally, the presence of these allergens in Opuntia humifusa products may trigger allergic reactions in susceptible persons.

2. Triggers asthma attacks

Opuntia humifusa triggers asthma attacks due to its allergenic properties.

The plant's sap and stems contain allergens that can cause respiratory issues in some individuals, leading to bronchial constriction and increased mucus production, characteristic symptoms of an asthma attack.

Additionally, the plant's high levels of latex and other compounds may exacerbate existing respiratory conditions or trigger an allergic response in sensitive individuals.

3. Increases heart rate

Opuntia humifusa increases heart rate due to its high concentration of beta-sitosterol and other saponins, which can stimulate the cardiovascular system.

This stimulant effect may cause an increase in blood pressure and heart rate, potentially leading to palpitations, anxiety, or restlessness.

As a result, individuals using Opuntia humifusa may experience these physical symptoms as their body adjusts to its energizing properties.

4. Lowers blood pressure

Opuntia humifusa lowers blood pressure by dilating blood vessels and reducing peripheral resistance.

This is due to its active compound, flavonoids, which have a vasodilatory effect, allowing more oxygenated blood to flow throughout the body. Additionally, Opuntia humifusa may slow down heart rate, further contributing to decreased blood pressure.

These mechanisms contribute to the hypotensive effects of Opuntia humifusa, making it a potential natural remedy for individuals with hypertension or cardiovascular conditions.

5. Induces vomiting

Opuntia humifusa induces vomiting due to its high concentration of saponins and mucilages.

The sticky sap and fibers present in the plant can cause irritation to the digestive tract, leading to nausea and vomiting.

Additionally, Opuntia humifusa contains a toxic compound called opuntioside, which can stimulate the gag reflex and trigger vomiting as an attempt to rid the body of the offending substance.

6. Affects liver function

Opuntia humifusa affects liver function by altering its ability to process and detoxify substances.

The compounds present in the plant, such as flavonoids and saponins, can cause changes in liver enzymes and bilirubin levels, potentially leading to abnormal liver function tests. This may be due to Opuntia humifusa's interaction with cytochrome P450 enzymes, which are responsible for breaking down xenobiotics, or its ability to stimulate the production of certain liver proteins.

As a result, individuals taking Opuntia humifusa may experience elevated liver enzymes, jaundice, or other signs of liver dysfunction.

7. Interacts with medications

Opuntia humifusa interacts with medications by altering their metabolism and absorption in the body.

This can lead to increased or decreased effects of certain medications, potentially causing adverse reactions or reducing their efficacy.

The exact mechanisms are not fully understood, but it is thought that Opuntia humifusa's active compounds may inhibit or induce enzymes responsible for medication breakdown, leading to changes in blood levels and overall response.

8. Produces digestive issues

Opuntia humifusa produces digestive issues due to its high content of saponins and mucilage.

The consumption of these compounds can cause stomach upset, bloating, and diarrhea in some individuals.

Additionally, the natural fibers present in Opuntia humifusa may not be fully digested by the body, leading to an imbalance in gut bacteria and further exacerbating digestive problems.

9. Worsens kidney disease

Opuntia humifusa worsens kidney disease due to its high content of oxalic acid and malic acid.

These organic acids can increase the excretion of calcium in the urine, which may exacerbate kidney damage and further deteriorate kidney function.

As a result, individuals with pre-existing kidney disease may experience a decline in their condition if they consume Opuntia humifusa.

10. Increases risk of bleeding

Opuntia humifusa increases risk of bleeding due to its ability to inhibit platelet aggregation and prolong bleeding time.

This is because it contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, terpenes, and saponins that interact with the body's clotting mechanisms, disrupting normal blood coagulation processes.

As a result, individuals who consume Opuntia humifusa may experience prolonged bleeding in response to injuries or surgical procedures.
