What's the taxonomical classification of Secamone elliptica?

Secamone elliptica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Gentianaales, this plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Secamone, with its specific identification being the species elliptica.
