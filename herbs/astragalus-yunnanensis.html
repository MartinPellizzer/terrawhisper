Astragalus yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Astragalus yunnanensis?

Astragalus yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Fabales, it falls under the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Astragalus and is specifically designated as the species yunnanensis.
