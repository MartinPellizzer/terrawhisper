Phalaenopsis deliciosa

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Phalaenopsis deliciosa has monopodial growth characterized by thick, fleshy, elliptical leaves that are dark green and arranged in a distichous pattern along a central stem. The foliage is notably leathery and provides a sturdy base for the plant's epiphytic lifestyle. From the axils of these leaves, long, arching inflorescences emerge to bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. These blossoms typically feature delicate petals and a complex labellum, often displaying soft pastel hues. The root system consists of thick, silvery-green aerial roots designed to cling to tree bark and absorb moisture from the air.
