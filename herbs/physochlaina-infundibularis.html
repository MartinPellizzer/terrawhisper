What are the pharmacological activities of Physochlaina infundibularis?

These combined effects highlight the significant medicinal potential of Physochlaina infundibularis in herbal pharmacology.

According to a study published by "Analytical and bioanalytical chemistry", the plant Physochlaina infundibularis Kuang, known as Huashanshen (HSH) in China, is rich in tropane alkaloids (TAs). These TAs showed good effects on types of spasms. Through the use of UPLC-Q/TOF-MS, 29 TAs were efficiently profiled from the plant. Additionally, via virtual validation and molecular dynamic simulation, approximately a dozen alkaloids were found to have high selectivity for muscarinic receptors. One specific alkaloid, N-acetyl convolicine, was evaluated for its selectivity for M2 or M3 receptors.
