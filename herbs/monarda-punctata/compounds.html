Spotted Joe-Pye Weed (Monarda punctata) Compounds

The primary compounds in Spotted Joe-Pye Weed include essential oil, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its aromatic profile and antioxidant properties. Terpenoids and coumarin provide additional therapeutic benefits, while tannins and glycosides support anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial actions. Saponins and alkaloids further enhance its potential medicinal uses. Anthocyanins give the plant its distinctive color and may offer protective health benefits.
