10 Possible Side Effects Of Monarda Punctata (Dwarf Beebalm)

These side effects can be caused by individual sensitivities, excessive consumption, or interactions with other medications.

In severe cases, these reactions can lead to respiratory problems, anaphylaxis, or even organ damage, significantly impacting a person's quality of life and daily activities.
