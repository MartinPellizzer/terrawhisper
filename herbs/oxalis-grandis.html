What is Oxalis grandis?

Oxalis grandis is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Oxalidaceae.

It has a rhizomatous stem, typically growing up to 1-2 meters in height, with a spread of 1-1.5 meters. The leaves are compound, with 5-7 leaflets, each 5-10 cm long and 2-3 cm wide, having a cleft shape and a pointed tip. The flowers are pink, star-shaped, and arranged in a corymb, typically blooming from spring to autumn, and are 3-4 cm in diameter.

The plant produces small, red, berry-like fruits containing 3-4 seeds.
