Actinodaphne cupularis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Laurales and the family Lauraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Actinodaphne, and its species designation, cupularis.
