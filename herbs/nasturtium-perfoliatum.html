Nasturtium perfoliatum

Nasturtium perfoliatum, also known as Yellowcress, is a flowering plant that belongs to the Brassicaceae family.

The plant's medicinal properties are attributed to its high content of glucosinolates, which have been shown to have antibacterial and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Nasturtium perfoliatum include its ability to boost the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant properties.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, tinctures, and salves, and is also used as a natural remedy for skin conditions, wounds, and respiratory issues.
