Dendrobium trigonopus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its placement in the order Asparagales, it falls into the family Orchidaceae, a diverse group of flowering plants. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Dendrobium and its unique designation as the species trigonopus.
