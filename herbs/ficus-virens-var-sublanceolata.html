Ficus virens var. sublanceolata

What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus virens var. sublanceolata?

Ficus virens var. sublanceolata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Moraceae, placing it within the genus Ficus. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific botanical designation as the species Ficus virens var. sublanceolata.
