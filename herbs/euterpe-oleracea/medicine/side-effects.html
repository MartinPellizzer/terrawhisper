10 Possible Side Effects Of Euterpe Oleracea (Açaí)

Euterpe oleracea has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

Causes of these side effects include consuming large amounts, allergic sensitivities, and combining with certain medications, such as blood thinners.

Severe gastrointestinal issues can lead to weight loss, nutrient deficiencies, and malnutrition, while allergic reactions can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening condition.

1. Worsens depression symptoms

Euterpe oleracea causes allergic reactions due to its unique combination of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and anthocyanins.

The allergic response is thought to be triggered by the body's immune system overreacting to these bioactive compounds, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

In some cases, an allergic reaction can progress to a life-threatening condition known as anaphylaxis, characterized by swelling of the face, throat, and airways.
