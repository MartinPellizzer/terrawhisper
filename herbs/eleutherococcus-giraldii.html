Eleutherococcus giraldii has a woody, shrubby habit characterized by multiple stems and a complex branching structure often armed with small prickles or spines.     What side-effects this plant can have?

    Eleutherococcus giraldii can cause side effects such as insomnia, nervousness, anxiety, and increased blood pressure in sensitive individuals. People with hypertension or those taking medications for blood pressure should exercise caution to avoid adverse cardiovascular reactions. Consult a healthcare professional before use to mitigate the risk of these potential complications.
