Toxicodendron succedaneum var. succedaneum

What's the taxonomical classification of Toxicodendron succedaneum var. succedaneum?

Toxicodendron succedaneum var. succedaneum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Sapindales. The plant is further categorized into the family Anacardiaceae and the genus Toxicodendron, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Toxicodendron succedaneum var. succedaneum.
