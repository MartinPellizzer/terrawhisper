What's the taxonomical classification of Notopterygium franchetii?

Notopterygium franchetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. This plant is a representative of the family Apiaceae, situated within the genus Notopterygium, and is specifically identified by the species name franchetii.
