Halerpestes sarmentoosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Halerpestes and the species name sarmentoosa.
