Possible Side Effects of Toxicodendron succedaneum var. dumoutieri

Toxicodendron succedaneum var. dumoutieri can have side effects if used improperly, such as severe skin irritation, allergic reactions, and contact dermatitis, which can lead to blistering, swelling, and redness of the skin.

In some cases, using this plant without proper caution can also cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention.

Prolonged exposure to its sap can also lead to respiratory issues, including coughing, sneezing, and shortness of breath.
