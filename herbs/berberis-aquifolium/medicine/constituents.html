10 Medicinal Constituents Of Berberis Aquifolium (Oregon Grape)

Berberis aquifolium, also known as Oregon grape, has active constituents such as berberine, berbamine, and protopine.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties, which are thought to contribute to the plant's medicinal potential.

Berberine, in particular, has been shown to improve cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol levels and reducing blood pressure, potentially improving overall well-being and reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes.
