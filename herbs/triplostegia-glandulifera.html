Triplostegia glandulifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Caprifoliaceae, which contains the genus Triplostegia. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its classification as the species glandulifera.
