Medicinal Parts of clerodendrum trichotomum

Clerodendrum trichotomum leaf is commonly used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties.

The root of Clerodendrum trichotomum is valued for its potential analgesic and antispasmodic effects, and it is typically decocted in water to create a medicinal infusion.

The fruit, though less commonly used, can be dried and ground into a powder or made into a syrup, often employed to address digestive issues and respiratory ailments.
