Tetraberlinia polyphylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Fabaceae, specifically under the genus Tetraberlinia, with the specific epithet polyphylla defining its unique species identity.
