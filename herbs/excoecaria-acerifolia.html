Excoecaria acerifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Excoecaria acerifolia?

Excoecaria acerifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Malpighiales and the family Euphorbiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Excoecaria, and its unique species designation, acerifolia.
