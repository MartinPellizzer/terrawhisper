Excoecaria acerifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Malpighiales and the family Euphorbiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Excoecaria, and its unique species designation, acerifolia.
