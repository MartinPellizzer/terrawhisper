Microtoena omeiensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Microtoena omeiensis?

Microtoena omeiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae, which places it within the genus Microtoena. Ultimately, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, omeiensis.
