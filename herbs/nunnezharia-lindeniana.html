Nunnezharia lindeniana

Nunnezharia lindeniana, also known as the linden vine, is a perennial climber native to the southeastern United States, characterized by its fragrant white flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

The health benefits of Nunnezharia lindeniana include reducing anxiety and promoting relaxation, as well as its potential in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs.
