Possible Side Effects of Stauntonia chapaensis

Stauntonia chapaensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea due to its emetic properties.

Ingesting large quantities of the plant can also cause abdominal pain, dizziness, and headaches in some individuals.

Moreover, excessive use of Stauntonia chapaensis may lead to electrolyte imbalance, dehydration, and even cardiovascular problems in severe cases.
