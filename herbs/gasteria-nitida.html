What is Gasteria nitida?

Gasteria nitida is a succulent plant species belonging to the family Asphodelaceae.

It has thick, glossy leaves that are lance-shaped and pointed, often forming rosettes, typically 5-8 cm in diameter. The leaves are dark green with a distinctive silvery sheen and are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers on a tall, slender inflorescence, typically 30-60 cm high, during the late winter or early spring months.

Gasteria nitida is native to the Western Cape Province of South Africa, where it grows in a variety of habitats, including rocky outcrops and scrublands.
