Pinellia (Pinellia ternata)

Pinellia (Pinellia ternata) is a member of the Poaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and tubers have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
