10 Medicinal Constituents Of Galium Aparine (Cleavers)

Galium aparine has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antitumor activities. The saponins in particular are believed to be responsible for the plant's ability to stimulate lymphatic drainage, reducing swelling and improving circulation.

This property can be beneficial for people suffering from conditions such as edema or cellulite, allowing them to experience improved skin health and reduced discomfort.
