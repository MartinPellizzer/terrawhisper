What is Ayenia morifolia?

Ayenia morifolia is a plant species belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3-6 meters in height, with a woody stem and a rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, measuring 2-8 cm in length and 1-4 cm in width, with a leathery texture and a crenate margin. The flowers are small, yellowish-green in color, and arranged in axillary clusters, with five sepals and five petals.

The plant is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.
