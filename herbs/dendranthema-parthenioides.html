What is Dendranthema parthenioides?

Dendranthema parthenioides is a perennial plant native to China and Japan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is a member of the genus Dendranthema, now often included in the genus Chrysanthemum. This herbaceous plant has erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, bearing alternate, simple, and entire leaves with serrated margins. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, measuring 2-8 centimeters in length, and have a glabrous or pubescent surface.

Dendranthema parthenioides produces white or yellow flowers in late summer, arranged in small, daisy-like capitula.
