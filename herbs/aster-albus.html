Aster albus

Aster albus, also known as White Aster, is a flowering plant in the daisy family native to North America.

The roots and leaves of the plant have been used in traditional medicine for their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for treating wounds and infections.

The health benefits of Aster albus include reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and boosting the immune system.
