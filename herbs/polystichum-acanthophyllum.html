What's the taxonomical classification of Polystichum acanthophyllum?

Polystichum acanthophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. It is further organized into the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Polystichum, and its species designation, acanthophyllum.
