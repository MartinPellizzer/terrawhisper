What's the taxonomical classification of Ludwigia perennis?

Ludwigia perennis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Myrtales. Specifically, this plant is a member of the family Onagraceae, falling within the genus Ludwigia, and is identified by its specific epithet, perennis.
