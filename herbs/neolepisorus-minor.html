What's the taxonomical classification of Neolepisorus minor?

Neolepisorus minor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Polypodiaceae, which contains the genus Neolepisorus, ultimately identifying this specific organism as the species minor.
