Adenosma indianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Adenosma indianum?

Adenosma indianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Plantaginacea, which houses the genus Adenosma. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is completed at the species level as Adenosma indianum.
