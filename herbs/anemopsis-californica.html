Possible Side Effects of anemopsis californica

Anemopsis californica can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its saponin content.

Additionally, the herb may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and decrease their effectiveness.

Overconsumption of Anemopsis californica can also lead to liver toxicity, making it essential to consult a healthcare professional before using it for medicinal purposes.
