Guaiacum (Guaiacum sanctum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Guaiacum sanctum include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties, while coumarin may support circulatory health. These compounds work synergistically to provide the plant's therapeutic benefits.
