What's the taxonomical classification of Berberis hobsonii?

Berberis hobsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Berberidaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Berberis, and its specific species name, hobsonii.
