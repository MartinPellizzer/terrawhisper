European Plum (Prunus domestica) Uses

European plum is used to treat gastrointestinal inflammation, constipation, and diarrhea. It helps soothe gastritis and gastroesophageal reflux disease. It may also support those with inflammatory bowel disease and gastroenteritis. European plum can alleviate hemorrhoids and is used for skin infections and eczema.

European Plum (Prunus domestica) Monograph

1. Gastrointestinal inflammation

European plum has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce gastrointestinal inflammation. It supports the lining of the digestive tract and may alleviate symptoms of conditions like gastritis and irritable bowel syndrome. The fruit's high levels of polyphenols contribute to its soothing and healing effects on the gut.

2. Constipation

European plum, or Prunus domestica, is known for its mild laxative properties. The fruit contains natural sugars and fibers that help stimulate bowel movements. It is often used in herbal medicine to alleviate constipation when consumed as a juice or dried fruit. Regular intake can promote digestive health and ease discomfort associated with slow digestion.

3. Diarrhea

European plum is used to treat diarrhea due to its astringent properties, which help firm stools. It contains tannins that reduce intestinal inflammation and slow bowel movements. The fruit is often prepared as a decoction or syrup to alleviate symptoms. It is particularly effective for watery diarrhea caused by infections or dietary indiscretion.

4. Gastritis

European plum is used in herbal medicine to support digestive health, particularly for gastritis. It contains compounds that may reduce inflammation and soothe stomach lining. Its mild astringent properties help control excess acid and promote healing. Regular use can alleviate symptoms like bloating and indigestion.

5. Gastroesophageal reflux disease

European plum has been traditionally used to support digestive health, particularly in managing gastroesophageal reflux disease. Its high pectin content helps soothe the stomach lining and reduce acid reflux. It may also promote regular digestion and alleviate bloating, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for digestive disorders.

6. Inflammatory bowel disease

European Plum has shown potential in supporting inflammatory bowel disease due to its high content of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds. These properties may help reduce intestinal inflammation and support gut health. Preliminary studies suggest it may aid in symptom management, though more research is needed for definitive conclusions.

7. Gastroenteritis

European plum is used in herbal medicine to support digestion and soothe gastrointestinal inflammation. Its high pectin content helps firm stools, while its anti-inflammatory properties may reduce intestinal irritation. It is often recommended for mild cases of gastroenteritis to alleviate symptoms and promote recovery.

8. Hemorrhoids

European plum is used to treat hemorrhoids due to its high content of flavonoids and vitamin C, which strengthen blood vessels and reduce inflammation. It also contains fiber, aiding in digestive health and preventing constipation, a common contributor to hemorrhoid flare-ups. The fruit's astringent properties help shrink swollen veins and promote healing. Regular consumption of European plum can support overall rectal health and alleviate symptoms of hemorrhoids.

9. Skin infections

European plum has antimicrobial properties that help treat skin infections. Its compounds inhibit bacterial growth, making it useful for conditions like eczema and fungal infections. Topical applications can reduce inflammation and promote healing. It is often used in herbal remedies for its soothing and protective effects on the skin.

10. Eczema

European plum (Prunus domestica) has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat eczema due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. The fruit's high content of vitamins and antioxidants helps reduce skin irritation and promote healing. It is often prepared as a topical poultice or infused oil to alleviate symptoms associated with eczema.
