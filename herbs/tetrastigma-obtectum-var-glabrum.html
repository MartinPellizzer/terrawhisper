Tetrastigma obtectum var. glabrum

What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma obtectum var. glabrum?

Tetrastigma obtectum var. glabrum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Vitales and is a specific representative of the family Vitaeaceae. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Tetrastigma, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation at the species level as Tetrastigma obtectum var. glabrum.
