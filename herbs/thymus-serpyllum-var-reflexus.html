Thymus serpyllum var. reflexus

Thymus serpyllum var. reflexus, commonly known as creeping thyme, is a low-growing, spreading perennial herb native to Europe and Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic, antifungal, and antibacterial properties, making it effective in treating wounds, colds, and respiratory issues.

The health benefits of Thymus serpyllum var. reflexus include its ability to boost the immune system, reduce stress and anxiety, and exhibit anti-inflammatory properties.

In herbal preparations, the leaves of this plant can be made into teas, tinctures, and salves, and can also be used as a culinary herb in various recipes.
