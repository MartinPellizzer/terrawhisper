Jaborandi (Pilocarpus jaborandi)

Jaborandi (Pilocarpus jaborandi) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina. Traditionally, its bark, leaves, and stems have been used for infusions, decoctions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, expectorant, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
