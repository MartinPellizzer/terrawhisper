Clematis peterae var. trichocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis peterae var. trichocarpa?

Clematis peterae var. trichocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. The plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is further categorized within the genus Clematis. Specifically, its taxonomic identity is defined at the varietal level as the species Clematis peterae var. trichocarpa.
