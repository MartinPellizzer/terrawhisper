Pleurospermum uralense

What's the taxonomical classification of Pleurospermum uralense?

Pleurospermum uralense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. This plant is a representative of the family Apiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Pleurospermum, and is identified by the species name uralense.

Pleurospermum uralense has perennial, creeping rhizomes with small, opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically entire and lack petioles.
