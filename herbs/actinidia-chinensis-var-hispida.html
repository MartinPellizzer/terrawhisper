Actinidia chinensis var. hispida

What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia chinensis var. hispida?

Actinidia chinensis var. hispida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ericales and is classified within the family Actinidiaceae. This specific variety is situated under the genus Actinidia and is identified by the species name Actinidia.
