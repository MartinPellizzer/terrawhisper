Black Ballota (Ballota nigra) Compounds

Black Ballota contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, tannins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Coumarin and glycosides support its antispasmodic effects, while essential oils and saponins enhance its digestive and detoxifying actions. Terpenoids, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further contribute to its therapeutic profile.

Black Ballota (Ballota nigra) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids are key active compounds in Black Ballota (Ballota nigra). They contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. These compounds support the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine for digestive and respiratory ailments. Their presence enhances the therapeutic potential of Ballota nigra.

2. Tannin

Tannin is a key active compound in Black Ballota (Ballota nigra). It contributes to the plant's astringent properties and is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Tannins help in reducing swelling and supporting wound healing.

Phenolic acid is a key active compound in Black Ballota (Ballota nigra).
