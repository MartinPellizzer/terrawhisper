Ballota nigra fights bacterial growth because of its rich content of bioactive constituents such as iridoid glycosides, diterpenes, and phenolic compounds.

By fighting bacterial growth, Ballota nigra can help alleviate ailments such as urinary tract infections, skin infections, and respiratory tract infections, which are often caused by bacterial overgrowth.

The antimicrobial properties of Ballota nigra make it a potential natural remedy for these types of infections.
