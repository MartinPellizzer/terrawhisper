Butterfly Bush (Buddleja officinalis) Compounds

The primary active compounds of Butterfly Bush include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and digestive properties, while coumarin and terpenoids may have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory actions. Glycosides and anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant support, and alkaloids may influence nervous system function.
