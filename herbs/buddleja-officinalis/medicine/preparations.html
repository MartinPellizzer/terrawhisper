10 Medicinal Preparations Of Buddleja Officinalis (Butterfly Bush)

Buddleja officinalis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions.

These preparations are traditionally used to treat respiratory issues, including bronchitis and coughs, as well as inflammation and digestive problems. The leaves and flowers are dried and made into teas, while the roots are used to make tinctures.

The plant's antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties are also useful for wound care and skin conditions.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Buddleja officinalis.
