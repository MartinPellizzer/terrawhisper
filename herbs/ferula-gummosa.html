Ferula Gummosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ferula gummosa, commonly known as gum arabic plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its distinctive resinous sap.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including digestive support, anti-inflammatory properties, and antioxidant effects.

The bioactive constituents in Ferula gummosa include flavonoids, tannins, and gum arabic, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical applications can be made from the resin and leaves of this plant.
