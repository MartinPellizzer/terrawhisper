Scouring Rush (Equisetum hyemale) Compounds

The primary compounds in Scouring Rush include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties, while coumarin may have antispasmodic effects. Glycosides, saponins, and alkaloids also play roles in its medicinal actions.
