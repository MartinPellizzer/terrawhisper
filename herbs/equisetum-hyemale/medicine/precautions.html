10 Precautions To Take When Using Equisetum Hyemale (Scouring Rush)

Equisetum hyemale has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as ingesting it in moderation and avoiding prolonged use.

Overconsumption can cause gastrointestinal upset, diarrhea, and allergic reactions. Additionally, individuals with kidney or liver issues should exercise caution, as the herb's high silica content may exacerbate these conditions.

Neglecting these precautions can lead to unpleasant side effects, such as stomach cramps, nausea, and skin rashes.
