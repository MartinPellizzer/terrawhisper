Celastrus hookeri

What's the taxonomical classification of Celastrus hookeri?

Celastrus hookeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Celastrales and the family Celastraceae, eventually reaching its specific classification within the genus Celastrus, with the distinct species designation hookeri.
