In terms of horticulture, wintergreen is an evergreen perennial that thrives in shady areas with acidic soil, making it a low-maintenance addition to any garden. From a botanical perspective, wintergreen belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its small, white flowers and distinctive red berries.

With a history dating back thousands of years, wintergreen has been revered by many indigenous cultures for its medicinal and spiritual properties.
