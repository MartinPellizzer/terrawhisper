Genianthus bicoronatus

What's the taxonomical classification of Genianthus bicoronatus?

Genianthus bicoronatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchy through the order Gentianales, the plant is placed in the family Apocynaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Genianthus and the species bicoronatus.
