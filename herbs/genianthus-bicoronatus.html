Genianthus bicoronatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the hierarchy through the order Gentianales, the plant is placed in the family Apocynaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Genianthus and the species bicoronatus.
