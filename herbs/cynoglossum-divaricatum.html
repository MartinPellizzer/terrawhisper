What's the taxonomical classification of Cynoglossum divaricatum?

Cynoglossum divaricatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, the plant is organized under the order Boraginales and the family Boraginaceae. Ultimately, it is identified by the genus Cynoglossum and the specific species epithet divaricatum.
