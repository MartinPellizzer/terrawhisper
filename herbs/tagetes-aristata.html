Mexican Marigold (Tagetes Aristata)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Tagetes aristata.

Mexican marigold, scientifically known as Tagetes aristata, is a medicinal herb native to Mexico and widely used in traditional and modern wellness practices. It is valued for its adaptogenic properties, which help the body manage stress and enhance resilience. The plant is rich in bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and essential oils, offering primary benefits like anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. Traditionally, it has been used by indigenous communities for treating respiratory infections, digestive issues, and skin conditions, while modern wellness applications include its use in herbal supplements and topical treatments for inflammation and infections. Its unique features include a distinctive, slightly spicy aroma and a rare active compound called artemetin, which contributes to its therapeutic potential.
