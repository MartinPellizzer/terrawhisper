Agathosma (Agathosma betulina) Uses

Agathosma betulina is used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and allergic rhinitis. It supports the immune system and helps alleviate symptoms of the common cold, respiratory infections, and sinusitis. Its antimicrobial properties make it effective for skin infections and can ease coughing associated with influenza.

Agathosma (Agathosma betulina) Monograph

Agathosma betulina is used to support respiratory health, particularly in managing symptoms of bronchitis. Its expectorant properties aid in clearing the lungs, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for respiratory conditions.
