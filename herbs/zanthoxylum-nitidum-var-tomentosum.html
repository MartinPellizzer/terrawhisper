Zanthoxylum nitidum var. tomentosum

What's the taxonomical classification of Zanthoxylum nitidum var. tomentosum?

Zanthoxylum nitidum var. tomentosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Sapindales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Rutaceae and is situated in the genus Zanthoxylum, specifically identified as the variety tomentosum of the species Zanthoxylum nitidum.
