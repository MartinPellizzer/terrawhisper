Clerodendrum yunnanense

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Clerodendrum yunnanense has opposite, elliptical leaves with serrated margins and a woody, shrubby stem structure. The foliage typically exhibits a deep green color on the upper surface and may possess fine pubescence on the underside. Small, tubular flowers emerge from the branches, often arranged in dense terminal clusters or cymes. These blossoms are characterized by prominent, elongated stamens that extend beyond the corolla to facilitate pollination. The plant also produces small, fleshy fruits that develop following successful fertilization.
