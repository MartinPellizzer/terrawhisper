What's the taxonomical classification of Clerodendrum yunnanense?

Clerodendrum yunnanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Clerodendrum, with its unique designation being the species yunnanense.
