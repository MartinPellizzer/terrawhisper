What's the taxonomical classification of Sanicula orthacantha?

Sanicula orthacantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Apiales. It is further categorized into the family Apiaceae, a group known for its characteristic floral structures, and is situated within the genus Sanicula. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species orthacantha.
