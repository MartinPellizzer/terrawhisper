Scardic Sideritis (Sideritis scardica) Compounds

Scardic Sideritis contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Terpenoids and essential oils provide aromatic and antimicrobial benefits, while coumarin and glycosides support its traditional use in digestive and respiratory health. Saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its medicinal profile.
