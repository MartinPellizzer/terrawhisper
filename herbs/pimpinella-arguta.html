Pimpinella arguta

What's the taxonomical classification of Pimpinella arguta?

Pimpinella arguta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, falling under the genus Pimpinella, and is specifically identified by its species name, arguta.
