10 Health Benefits Of Aristolochia Sempervirens (Everlasting)

Aristolochia sempervirens has health benefits such as treating digestive issues and reducing inflammation due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's alkaloids have been shown to soothe digestive problems like diarrhea and constipation, while its flavonoids and phenolic acids combat oxidative stress and swelling. For example, people with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) may experience relief from symptoms after consuming everlasting tea or supplements.

Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties can aid in pain management, providing relief for those suffering from conditions like arthritis.
