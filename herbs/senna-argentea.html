Senna argentea

Senna argentea, commonly known as silver cassia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as treating constipation, diarrhea, and other gastrointestinal issues due to its purgative properties.

The therapeutic actions of Senna argentea include acting as a laxative, emetic, and expectorant, making it useful in treating respiratory issues like bronchitis and coughs.

The bioactive constituents of Senna argentea include anthraquinones, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties and are often used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments.
