What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace gmelinii?

Androsace gmelinii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Primulaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by the genus Androsace and is specifically designated as the species gmelinii.
