Rugosa Rose (Rosa rugosa)

Rugosa Rose (Rosa rugosa) is a member of the Rosaceae family, native to China, Japan, and Korea. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and flowers have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and european herbal medicine.

Woody, branched, thorny, with elliptic to ovate leaves and pinnately compound inflorescences.
