Acacia longifolia var. obtusifolia

Acacia longifolia var. obtusifolia, also known as the Green Mop Acacia, is a tree species native to the coastal regions of Australia.

The plant has been found to possess medicinal properties, particularly in its bark, leaves, and flowers, which have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions.

The bark contains compounds that have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, while the leaves have been used to treat digestive issues, fever, and skin conditions.

In traditional herbal preparations, the plant's extracts are often used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions for the treatment of various health issues.
