What's the taxonomical classification of Justicia kwangsiensis?

Justicia kwangsiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales. It is a prominent representative of the family Acanthaceae, falling under the genus Justicia, with its specific designation being the species kwangsiensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Justicia kwangsiensis has lanceolate to elliptic leaves that are arranged oppositely on woody stems and feature prominent longitudinal veins. These blossoms are arranged in dense terminal spikes that attract various pollinators. The plant structure is primarily shrub-like, reaching moderate heights in its natural forest habitat.
