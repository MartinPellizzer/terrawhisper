What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum hainanense?

Viburnum hainanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Dipsacales. This plant is a member of the family Viburnaceae and is specifically situated within the genus Viburnum, ultimately being identified by its unique species name, hainanense.
