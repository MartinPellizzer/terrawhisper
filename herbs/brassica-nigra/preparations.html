10 Best Brassica Nigra Preparations

The best medicinal preparations of Brassica nigra are teas, decoctions, tinctures, mucillages, and creams, each offering unique therapeutic benefits.

Teas made from the leaves or seeds are commonly used to support digestive health and reduce inflammation.

Decoctions, which involve simmering the plant material, are effective for extracting more potent compounds.

Mucillages, derived from the seeds, are used for their soothing and demulcent properties.

Creams infused with Brassica nigra are applied topically to alleviate skin irritations and promote healing.
