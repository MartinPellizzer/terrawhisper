Asparagus gobicus

What's the taxonomical classification of Asparagus gobicus?

Asparagus gobicus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls into the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Asparagaceae. At the most specific taxonomic levels, it is identified by the genus Asparagus and the species gobicus.

Furthermore, its medicinal qualities can help soothe internal inflammation within the digestive and reproductive systems.
