What's the taxonomical classification of Oreocharis auricula?

Oreocharis auricula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the diverse order Lamiales. As a member of the family Gesneriaceae, this plant is specifically categorized under the genus Oreocharis, with its unique identity defined by the species name auricula.
