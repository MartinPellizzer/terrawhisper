Rhododendron dendrocharis

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron dendrocharis?

Rhododendron dendrocharis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is part of the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Ericaceae, specifically situated within the genus Rhododendron, and is identified by the unique species name dendrocharis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its inflorescences consist of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to a deep, vibrant rose. These blossoms are produced in terminal clusters, emerging from the ends of the branches during the flowering season.
