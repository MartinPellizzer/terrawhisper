Wikstroemia dolichantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta.     As discussed in a study, the EtOH extract of Wikstroemia dolichantha (WDE) contains four flavonoids tentatively identified by HPLC-DAD/QTOF-MS.
