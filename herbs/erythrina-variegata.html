Horticulturally, coral tree is valued for its beautiful flowers and unique branching habit, making it a popular ornamental plant.

With a long history dating back to ancient China, where it was considered a symbol of good luck, coral tree has been revered for its medicinal, culinary, and ornamental uses throughout the ages.
