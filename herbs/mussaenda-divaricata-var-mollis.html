Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis

What's the taxonomical classification of Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis?

Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its hierarchical lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the order Gentianaales. The plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, which is further narrowed down to the genus Mussaenda. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis.
