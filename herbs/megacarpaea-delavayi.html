Megacarpaea delavayi

What's the taxonomical classification of Megacarpaea delavayi?

Megacarpaea delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Brassicales and the family Brassicaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Megacarpaea and the species delavayi.
