Coleus Diversus

Coleus diversus, a member of the Lamiaceae family, has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic and indigenous medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

The plant is known for its rich content of bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which may contribute to its medicinal value. In traditional practices, it has been utilized to treat ailments such as inflammation, skin conditions, and respiratory issues due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Recent scientific studies suggest that extracts from Coleus diversus may exhibit antioxidant and neuroprotective properties, supporting its use in managing oxidative stress-related disorders.

However, further research is needed to fully validate its efficacy and establish standardized medicinal applications.
