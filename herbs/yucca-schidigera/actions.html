Socotra Aloe (Yucca schidigera) Actions

Socotra Aloe exhibits anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and pain. It acts as a diuretic to support kidney function and aid in detoxification. As an expectorant, it helps clear mucus from the respiratory system. Its stimulating and tonic effects promote overall vitality and digestive health.
