What's the taxonomical classification of Hedyotis costata?

Hedyotis costata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Gentianales and into the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Hedyotis, and its species designation, costaata.

Some individuals might also experience allergic reactions such as itching or hives after exposure. Always use protective gear when handling this species to avoid these adverse physical reactions.
