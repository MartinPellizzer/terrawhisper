Eriocoma hibiscifolia

Eriocoma hibiscifolia, also known as the Desert Broom or Plume Grass, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to treat respiratory issues and skin conditions due to its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Eriocoma hibiscifolia include alleviating symptoms of bronchitis, asthma, and eczema, as well as reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing.

Herbal preparations of Eriocoma hibiscifolia can be made into teas, infusions, and salves, and are often used in conjunction with other herbs to enhance their medicinal effects.
