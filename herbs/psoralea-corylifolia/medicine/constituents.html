10 Medicinal Constituents Of Psoralea Corylifolia (Babchi)

Psoralea corylifolia has active constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, lignans, and terpenes.

These compounds provide the plant with medicinal properties that have been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including skin conditions, infections, and respiratory problems. The antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties of these constituents can improve lives by reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, thus alleviating symptoms such as itching, redness, and swelling associated with eczema and psoriasis.

Additionally, the antimicrobial properties can help combat bacterial and fungal infections, promoting overall health and well-being.
