10 Precautions To Take When Using Psoralea Corylifolia (Babchi)

Psoralea corylifolia has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding direct sunlight after consumption.

This is crucial because the herb can cause photosensitivity, leading to skin irritation, blistering, and even sunburn. Without proper care, individuals may experience adverse effects like increased risk of skin cancer and premature aging.

Therefore, it is essential to use protective clothing and apply sunscreen when venturing outdoors after taking the herb.
