10 Health Benefits Of Psoralea Corylifolia (Babchi)

Psoralea corylifolia has health benefits such as treating skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The plant's flavonoids and lignans have antioxidant and antimicrobial effects, which can help soothe and heal wounds, reduce inflammation, and prevent infection.

These benefits can greatly improve people's lives by providing relief from chronic skin conditions, reducing scarring, and promoting overall well-being.
