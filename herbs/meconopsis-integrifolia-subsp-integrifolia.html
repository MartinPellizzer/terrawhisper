Meconopsis integrifolia subsp. integrifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Meconopsis integrifolia subsp. integrifolia?

Meconopsis integrifolia subsp. integrifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Papaveraceae. The plant is categorized under the genus Meconopsis, specifically identified by its full scientific designation as the species Meconopsis integrifolia subsp. integrifolia.
