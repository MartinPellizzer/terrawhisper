Inula Helenium Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Inula helenium, commonly known as Huang Lian, is a perennial herb native to Asia, renowned for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

This herb is valued for its ability to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and enhance immune function.

The bioactive constituents in Inula helenium include alkaloids, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures can be made from the roots and rhizomes of this plant.

Health Benefits of inula helenium

Inula helenium has many health benefits, such as enhancing digestion by stimulating the production of digestive enzymes and improving the motility of the gastrointestinal tract, which helps in the efficient breakdown of food.

It supports digestive health by acting as a natural detoxifier, helping to remove toxins and reduce the risk of digestive disorders like bloating and indigestion.

Inula helenium reduces inflammation due to its high content of bioactive compounds, including sesquiterpene lactones, which have anti-inflammatory properties that can soothe the digestive tract and other inflammatory conditions.

It boosts the immune system by enhancing the activity of white blood cells and promoting the production of antioxidants, which help protect the body against infections and diseases.
