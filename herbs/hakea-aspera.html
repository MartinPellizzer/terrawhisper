Hakea aspera

Hakea aspera, also known as the rough leaf hakea, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to Australia, characterized by its rough, leathery leaves and clusters of white or cream-colored flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it effective in treating wounds, cuts, and skin irritations.

The health benefits of Hakea aspera include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate rheumatism, and soothe digestive issues.
