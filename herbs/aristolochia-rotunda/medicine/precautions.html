10 Precautions To Take When Using Aristolochia Rotunda (Birthwort)

Aristolochia rotunda has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding prolonged use and large doses, as this can lead to kidney damage.

Aristolochia contains aristolochic acid, a toxic compound that can cause kidney nephropathy if ingested excessively.

If not taken with caution, prolonged use can result in serious kidney problems and even kidney failure, leading to permanent damage or even death.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Aristolochia rotunda medicinally.
