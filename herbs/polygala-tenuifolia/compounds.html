Chinese Milkvetch (Polygala tenuifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds of Chinese Milkvetch include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and saponins, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Glycosides and alkaloids contribute to its therapeutic benefits, while coumarins and phenolic acids support its pharmacological activities. Terpenoids, tannins, essential oils, and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal properties.
