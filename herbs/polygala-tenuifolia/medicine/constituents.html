10 Medicinal Constituents Of Polygala Tenuifolia (Milkwort)

Polygala tenuifolia has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids.

These constituents have been shown to have anti-anxiety, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective properties. The flavonoids, in particular, have been found to improve cognitive function and reduce stress levels, while the alkaloids have been shown to enhance memory and learning abilities.

As a result, milkwort is used to alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, improving overall mental clarity and well-being.
