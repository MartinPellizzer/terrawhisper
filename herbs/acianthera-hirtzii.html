Acianthera hirtzii is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

The inflorescence is an axillary, racemose spike 1-2 cm long, bearing 2-3 flowers that are greenish-yellow or yellowish-green, with a labellum that is rectangular, concave, and 1-1.5 cm long. The sepals and petals are linear, acute, and 1-1.5 cm long, while the flowers are 1-2 cm in diameter.

It is found in Ecuador.
