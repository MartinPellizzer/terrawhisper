Arenaria tetraquetra var. cavanillesiana

Arenaria tetraquetra var. cavanillesiana, also known as Mountain Sandwort, is a plant species in the pink family (Caryophyllaceae) that is native to the Iberian Peninsula.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties.

The health benefits of Mountain Sandwort include its ability to reduce fever, treat respiratory issues, and soothe digestive problems.

It is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture, and in some regions, it is also used as a topical application for wounds and skin irritations.
