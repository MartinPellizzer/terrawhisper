Anise Hyssop (Agastache foeniculum) Compounds

The primary compounds of Anise Hyssop include essential oil, which gives it its aromatic properties, and flavonoids, which contribute to its antioxidant effects. Terpenoids and coumarin are also present, offering anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial benefits. Phenolic acids, glycosides, and tannins support its use in traditional herbal medicine for digestive and respiratory support.

Anise Hyssop (Agastache foeniculum) Monograph

The essential oil of Anise Hyssop (Agastache foeniculum) contains compounds like anethole and humulene. It is commonly used in herbal medicine for respiratory and digestive support.
