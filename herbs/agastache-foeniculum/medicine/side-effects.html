10 Possible Side Effects Of Agastache Foeniculum (Anise Hyssop)

Agastache foeniculum has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal issues, and interactions with medications.

Allergic reactions can be triggered by sensitivity to its essential oils, causing symptoms like hives, itching, and difficulty breathing. Gastrointestinal problems, such as bloating and nausea, may occur when consumed in large quantities or by individuals with digestive issues.

Furthermore, interactions with medications can exacerbate existing conditions, potentially leading to worsened health outcomes, impaired daily functioning, and a diminished quality of life.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Agastache foeniculum if used imporperly.
