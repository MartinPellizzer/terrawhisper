Blueweed (Ursinia anthemoides) Compounds

Blueweed contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Essential oils and coumarin provide aromatic and antispasmodic effects, while tannins and glycosides support anti-inflammatory and antioxidant actions. Saponins and alkaloids may enhance its therapeutic potential, and anthocyanins contribute to its vibrant color and additional health benefits.

Blueweed (Ursinia anthemoides) Monograph

Flavonoids in Blueweed (Ursinia anthemoides) are a group of plant compounds known for their antioxidant properties.
