10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ursinia Anthemoides (Silver Daisy)

Ursinia anthemoides has active constituents such as sesquiterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These bioactive compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, which can be beneficial in treating various health conditions. The constituents of Ursinia anthemoides may help alleviate symptoms of skin irritations, wounds, and respiratory infections, thereby improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from these afflictions.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties can contribute to protecting against oxidative stress and cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
