Pergularia Extensa (Pergularia extensa)

Pergularia Extensa (Pergularia extensa) is a member of the Acanthaceae family, native to Madagascar, Comoros, and Seychelles. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, diuretic, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
