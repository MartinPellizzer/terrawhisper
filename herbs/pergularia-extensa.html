Possible Side Effects of pergularia extensa

In some cases, Pergularia extensa may also lead to gastrointestinal problems, including stomach ulcers, diarrhea, and nausea, especially when consumed in large quantities or combined with other medications.

Furthermore, prolonged use or misuse of the herb can lead to kidney damage and liver dysfunction in susceptible individuals.
