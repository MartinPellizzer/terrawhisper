Stenosolenium saxatile

What's the taxonomical classification of Stenosolenium saxatile?

Stenosolenium saxatile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Boraginales and belongs to the family Boraginaceae. Finally, its specific identification is defined by the genus Stenosolenium and the species saxatile.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat respiratory ailments such as asthma and bronchitis, as well as digestive issues like gastritis and stomach cramps. The medicinal properties of its extracts are primarily utilized to soothe inflamed mucous membranes in the lungs. Additionally, some traditional practices employ the plant to alleviate various skin irritations and minor wounds.
