What's the taxonomical classification of Alnus ferdinandi-coburgii?

Alnus ferdinandi-coburgii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Fagales. It is a member of the family Betulaceae, falling under the genus Alnus, and is specifically identified by its species name, ferdinandi-coburgii.
