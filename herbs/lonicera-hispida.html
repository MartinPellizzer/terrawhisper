What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera hispida?

Lonicera hispida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is categorized under the order Dipsacales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae, falling into the genus Lonicera, and is specifically identified by the species name hispida.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These flowers develop into fleshy, oblong berries that transition from green to a deep purple or black color when ripe.
