What's the taxonomical classification of Acanthocalyx alba?

Acanthocalyx alba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Dipsacales and is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Acanthocalyx and the species alba.
