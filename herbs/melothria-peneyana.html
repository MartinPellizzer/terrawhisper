What is Melothria peneyana?

Melothria peneyana is a species of flowering plant in the family Cucurbitaceae, native to tropical America.

It is an annual or perennial climber with simple, alternate, and stipulate leaves that are 3-5-lobed, ovate, and 4-10 cm long. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers, 4-5-merous, with 4-5 sepals and 4-5 petals, that are arranged in a corymb. Melothria peneyana produces a globose, greenish-yellow, 10-12 cm long and 6-8 cm wide fruit with a 1-2 cm long, yellow, spiny beak.

The plant's fruit is edible and contains 3-4 seeds in a single locule.
