Vicia pseudo-orobus

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Vicia pseudoorobus has pinnate leaves composed of several pairs of leaflets and a terminal tendril used for climbing. The leaflets are typically elliptical or oblong in shape with smooth margins. Small, pea-like flowers emerge from the leaf axils in clusters. These flowers often display a range of colors from pale blue to purple. The plant structure is characterized by slender, flexible stems that allow it to scramble through surrounding vegetation.
