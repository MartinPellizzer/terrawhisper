Ribes Uva-Crispa: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Ribes uva-crispa, commonly known as red currant bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its small, tart berries and medicinal properties.

This herb is renowned for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, offering potential health benefits such as supporting immune function and digestive health.

Therapeutically, it is used to alleviate symptoms of respiratory and skin conditions, as well as to promote liver detoxification.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and polyphenols, which contribute to its medicinal value.
