10 Health Benefits Of Scutellaria Galericulata (Skullcap)

Scutellaria galericulata has health benefits such as calming anxiety and stress due to its flavonoid compounds and essential oils.

The plant's sedative properties help regulate sleep patterns, alleviating insomnia and restlessness, allowing individuals to wake up feeling refreshed and focused.

Additionally, skullcap's anti-inflammatory properties soothe digestive issues, reducing symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and providing relief from chronic pain, improving overall quality of life for those who suffer from these conditions.
