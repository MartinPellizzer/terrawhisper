Catigua (Trichilia catigua)

Catigua (Trichilia catigua) is a member of the Meliaceae family, native to Amazon Basin, South America, Brazil, and Central America. Traditionally, its leaves, bark, and fruits have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and native american herbal medicine.
