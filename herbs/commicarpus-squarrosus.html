What is Commicarpus squarrosus?

Commicarpus squarrosus produces a berry-like fruit, approximately 1.5 cm in diameter, with a dark brown color and containing 4-5 seeds.

The plant's growth habit and flowering characteristics allow it to thrive in full sun to partial shade environments.
