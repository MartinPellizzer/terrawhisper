10 Medicinal Constituents Of Crataegus Pinnatifida (Chinese Hawthorn)

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese hawthorn, has active constituents such as flavonoids, triterpenoids, and organic acids.

These compounds exhibit cardiotonic, diuretic, and antioxidant properties, contributing to its traditional use in cardiovascular and digestive health. For instance, flavonoids like vitexin and isovitexin may help regulate blood pressure and lipid profiles, while triterpenoids may improve liver function and reduce inflammation.

By enhancing cardiovascular health, Chinese hawthorn constituents may alleviate symptoms of hypertension and metabolic syndrome, ultimately improving overall well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Crataegus pinnatifida.
