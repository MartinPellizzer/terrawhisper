10 Possible Side Effects Of Crataegus Pinnatifida

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese hawthorn, has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, stomach upset, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by an overdose or interaction with other medications.

For example, a person with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) may experience worsened symptoms due to the herb's high fiber content, leading to discomfort and social isolation.

1. Causes allergic reactions

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, causes allergic reactions due to its high concentration of flavonoids and triterpenoid saponins.

These compounds can stimulate an immune response, leading to the release of histamine and other chemical mediators that cause allergic symptoms such as hives, itching, and swelling.

Additionally, some individuals may be sensitive to specific proteins or terpenes present in Crataegus pinnatifida, which can trigger an allergic response characterized by respiratory issues like wheezing, congestion, and difficulty breathing.

2. Produces digestive upset

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, produces digestive upset.

This side effect is often attributed to the herb's ability to alter the body's natural pH balance and disrupt the gut microbiome. The flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs) present in Crataegus pinnatifida may interfere with nutrient absorption, leading to symptoms such as bloating, gas, and stomach discomfort.

Additionally, the herb's bitter compounds can irritate the digestive tract, exacerbating existing conditions like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

3. Triggers respiratory issues

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, triggers respiratory issues in some individuals due to its ability to interact with certain medications and exacerbate existing conditions.

The herb contains flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs) that can cause constriction of airways, leading to symptoms such as asthma-like wheezing, coughing, and shortness of breath.

Additionally, the potential for allergic reactions or hypersensitivity responses may also contribute to respiratory issues in some individuals who use Crataegus pinnatifida.

4. Induces drowsiness suddenly

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, induces drowsiness suddenly due to its ability to increase the levels of serotonin and GABA in the brain.

These neurotransmitters are involved in regulating sleep and relaxation, causing feelings of sedation and drowsiness.

Additionally, Crataegus pinnatifida's flavonoids may interact with certain enzymes, further contributing to its sedative effects.

5. Causes skin rash quickly

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, causes skin rash quickly due to its high concentration of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds can stimulate the immune system, leading to an allergic reaction on the skin, manifesting as a rash or irritation.

Additionally, the herb's ability to alter blood flow and increase capillary permeability may also contribute to the development of a skin rash.

6. Increases heart rate rapidly

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, increases heart rate rapidly due to its inherent properties that stimulate the cardiovascular system.

The herb's flavonoids and glycosides have been found to exhibit sympathomimetic effects, which can directly affect the autonomic nervous system, causing an increase in heart rate and blood pressure.

This rapid tachycardia is thought to be a result of the herb's ability to enhance cardiac contractility and increase vasopressor activity.

7. Lowers blood pressure drastically

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese hawthorn, lowers blood pressure drastically, which is due to its ability to relax blood vessels and dilate them.

This allows for increased blood flow and reduced resistance in the circulatory system, resulting in a drop in blood pressure. Additionally, Chinese hawthorn may also slow down heart rate, further contributing to a decrease in blood pressure.

These effects are thought to be mediated by the plant's flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins, which have been shown to have vasodilatory properties.

8. Affects mental clarity sharply

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, affects mental clarity sharply due to its ability to significantly increase blood flow and oxygenation to the brain.

The flavonoids present in this herb can cause a stimulating effect on the nervous system, leading to increased alertness and improved cognitive function, but at times, it may also disrupt mental focus and lead to feelings of confusion or fogginess.

This is particularly noticeable when taking high doses or combining it with other stimulating herbs or supplements.

9. Disrupts sleep patterns seriously

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, disrupts sleep patterns seriously, possibly due to its ability to stimulate the nervous system and increase alertness.

The herb's bioactive compounds, such as flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins, may enhance mental clarity and energy levels, making it difficult for individuals to wind down and fall asleep.

Additionally, Chinese Hawthorn's effects on blood pressure and heart rate regulation could also contribute to restlessness and insomnia, further disrupting normal sleep patterns.

10. Provokes stomach pain intensely

Crataegus pinnatifida, also known as Chinese Hawthorn, provokes stomach pain intensely.

The flavonoids and oligomeric procyanidins present in the plant may cause stomach upset due to their ability to inhibit gastric acid secretion and reduce gut motility. Additionally, the plant's anthraquinone glycosides can irritate the mucous membranes of the digestive tract, leading to inflammation and pain in the stomach.

These compounds may also interfere with the absorption of nutrients, contributing to gastrointestinal discomfort.
