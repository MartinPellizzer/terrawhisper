Chinese Hawthorn (Crataegus pinnatifida) Uses

Chinese Hawthorn is used to treat hypertension, heart failure, and atherosclerosis. It helps manage arrhythmia, chest pain, and angina pectoris. It is also applied for cardiac ischemia and other cardiovascular diseases, supporting heart health through its circulatory benefits.

Chinese Hawthorn (Crataegus pinnatifida) Monograph

1. Hypertension

Chinese Hawthorn is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat hypertension due to its ability to improve blood circulation and reduce vascular resistance. It contains bioactive compounds like flavonoids and polyphenols that help lower blood pressure by dilating blood vessels and reducing oxidative stress. This herb is often combined with other herbs in formulas to enhance its hypotensive effects.

2. Heart failure

Chinese Hawthorn is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support heart function and treat heart failure. It improves blood circulation, reduces cardiac strain, and helps manage symptoms like shortness of breath and fatigue. Its active compounds, including flavonoids and triterpenes, enhance myocardial contractility and protect heart tissue.

3. Atherosclerosis

Chinese Hawthorn (Crataegus pinnatifida) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat atherosclerosis due to its ability to improve blood circulation and reduce cholesterol. It contains bioactive compounds that help lower LDL levels and prevent plaque buildup in arterial walls, supporting cardiovascular health.

4. Arrhythmia

Chinese Hawthorn is used to treat arrhythmia due to its ability to regulate heart function and improve blood circulation. It contains flavonoids and oligomeric procyanidins that help stabilize cardiac rhythms. Traditionally, it is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effects on heart health. This makes it a valuable component in herbal formulations for arrhythmia management.

5. Chest pain

Chinese Hawthorn is used to relieve chest pain caused by stagnation of qi and blood stasis. It promotes circulation, reduces inflammation, and alleviates cardiac discomfort. It is commonly combined with other herbs in formulas to treat angina and cardiovascular conditions. Its effects are supported by traditional use and modern pharmacological studies.

6. Cardiovascular disease

Chinese Hawthorn is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine to support cardiovascular health. It helps regulate blood pressure, improve circulation, and reduce cholesterol. Its active compounds, such as flavonoids and triterpenes, contribute to its cardioprotective effects. It is often prescribed for conditions like hypertension and coronary insufficiency.

7. Angina pectoris

Chinese Hawthorn is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat angina pectoris due to its ability to improve coronary blood flow and reduce cardiac workload. It contains bioactive compounds that help regulate blood pressure and enhance heart function, making it a valued herb in cardiovascular therapy.

8. Cardiac failure

Chinese Hawthorn is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat cardiac failure due to its ability to improve blood circulation and strengthen heart function. It contains flavonoids and polyphenols that help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation, supporting overall cardiovascular health.

9. High blood pressure

Chinese Hawthorn is used in traditional medicine to lower high blood pressure due to its ability to improve cardiovascular function and dilate blood vessels. It contains flavonoids and polyphenols that help reduce arterial stiffness and promote healthy blood flow. This makes it a valuable herb in managing hypertension naturally.

10. Cardiac ischemia

Chinese Hawthorn is used to treat cardiac ischemia due to its ability to improve coronary blood flow and reduce myocardial oxygen consumption. It contains flavonoids and phenolic acids that exhibit anti-platelet and antioxidant properties, enhancing cardiac function and protecting against ischemic injury.
