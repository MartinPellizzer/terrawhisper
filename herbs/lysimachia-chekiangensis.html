Lysimachia chekiangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia chekiangensis?

Lysimachia chekiangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This specific plant is a part of the family Primulaceae, situated within the genus Lysimachia, and is identified by its unique species designation, chekiangenensis.

Because the specific toxicological profile of this species is not extensively documented in clinical literature, potential users should exercise caution regarding unpredictable sensitivities. It is essential to consult a medical professional or toxicologist before any direct exposure occurs.
