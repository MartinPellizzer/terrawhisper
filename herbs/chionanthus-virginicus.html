Chionanthus Virginicus: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Chionanthus virginicus, commonly known as the fringe tree, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States, valued for its ornamental flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and diuretic effects, which support respiratory and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, contributing to its therapeutic actions.

It is believed to help alleviate symptoms of bronchitis, coughs, and digestive disorders, making it a valuable herb in traditional herbal medicine.
