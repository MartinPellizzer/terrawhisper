10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cucurbita Pepo (Zucchini)

Cucurbita pepo has active constituents such as cucurbitacins, flavonoids, and beta-carotene.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-cancer properties, which can help alleviate digestive issues, protect against chronic diseases, and promote overall health. The anti-inflammatory effects of cucurbitacins can also aid in reducing pain and inflammation associated with arthritis.

Regular consumption of zucchini may improve cardiovascular health, support immune function, and even help manage blood sugar levels.

1. Cucurbitin

Cucurbita pepo cucurbitin is a type of glycoside found in various species of the Cucurbitaceae family.
