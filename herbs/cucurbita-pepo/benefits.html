Cucurbita pepo lowers blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as potassium, magnesium, and flavonoids, which help relax blood vessels and reduce tension.

Cucurbita pepo lowers cholesterol levels because it contains bioactive constituents such as cucurbitacin, dietary fiber, and antioxidants like carotenoids.

Regular consumption of Cucurbita pepo may support heart health and reduce the risk of cardiovascular diseases.
