Kadsura renchangiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Kadsura renchangiana?

Kadsura renchangiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Austrobaileyales. Specifically, this plant is a member of the family Schisandraceae, falling under the genus Kadsura, and is identified as the species renchangiana.

What are the herbal preparations of Kadsura renchangiana?

Kadsura renchangiana is prepared primarily as a decoction through boiling the dried roots or stems in water to extract its medicinal compounds.
