What's the taxonomical classification of Craibiodendron yunnanense?

Craibiodendron yunnanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is organized under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Ericaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Craibiodendron, with its specific designation being the species yunnanense.
