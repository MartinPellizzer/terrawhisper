What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex viridis?

Ilex viridis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Aquifoliales and the family Aquifoliaceae, eventually reaching its specific classification within the genus Ilex, with the species epithet viridis.

The plant often develops small, dark berries during the fruiting season. Its overall structure remains dense and bushy throughout the year due to its persistent foliage.
