What's the taxonomical classification of Rhaphiolepis umbellata?

Rhaphiolepis umbellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Rosales. As a member of the family Rosaceae, it falls under the genus Rhaphiolepis, with its specific identification completed by the species name umbellata.
