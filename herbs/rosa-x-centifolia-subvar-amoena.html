Centifolia Rose (Rosa × Centifolia Subvar. Amoena)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Rosa × centifolia subvar. amoena.

Centifolia rose, scientifically known as Rosa × centifolia subvar. amoena, is a fragrant, ornamental shrub prized for its abundant, layered blooms and historical significance in both medicine and perfumery. This medicinal herb has been traditionally used for its calming and antiseptic properties, with its petals and essential oils offering a range of therapeutic benefits. Historically, it was valued in ancient Persian, Indian, and Chinese medicine for treating digestive ailments, respiratory conditions, and skin disorders. In modern wellness practices, centifolia rose is used in aromatherapy, herbal teas, and skincare products due to its soothing and anti-inflammatory effects. Its unique feature is the presence of a rare compound called damascenone, which contributes to its distinctive aroma and potential health benefits, making it a cherished ingredient in both traditional and contemporary holistic remedies.
