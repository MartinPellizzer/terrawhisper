Festuca durissima

Festuca durissima, commonly known as hard fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountains of Europe and Asia.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health benefits, including the treatment of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Festuca durissima include soothing coughs, reducing inflammation, and aiding in the relief of respiratory congestion.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
