Vitex Agnus-Castus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Vitex agnus-castus, commonly known as chaste tree, is a medicinal plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its aromatic berries and therapeutic properties.

This herb is traditionally used to support hormonal balance, particularly in women, and is often employed for menstrual regulation and menopausal symptoms.

The bioactive constituents of Vitex agnus-castus include lignans, flavonoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its adaptogenic and phytoestrogenic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from the dried berries to harness its beneficial properties.
