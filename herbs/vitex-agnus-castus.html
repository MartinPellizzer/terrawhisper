The therapeutic properties of Vitex agnus-castus include its adaptogenic, anti-anxiety, and anti-inflammatory effects, which contribute to its ability to regulate hormonal imbalances and alleviate symptoms of menopause. The bioactive compounds present in Vitex agnus-castus include iridoids, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

A common herbal preparation made with Vitex agnus-castus is the dried fruit, which is often used to make teas, tinctures, and capsules, and is also incorporated into supplements and women's health formulas.
