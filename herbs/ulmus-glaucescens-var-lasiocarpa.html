Ulmus glaucescens var. lasiocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Ulmus glaucescens var. lasiocarpa?

Ulmus glaucescens var. lasiocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is a representative of the family Ulmaceae. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Ulmus, ultimately categorized by its specific botanical designation as the variety lasiocarpa of the species Ulmus glaucescens.
