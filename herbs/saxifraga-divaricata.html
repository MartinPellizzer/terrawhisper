Saxifraga divaricata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Saxifragales and the family Saxifragaceae, eventually reaching the genus Saxifraga. Finally, the specific identity of this plant is established by its species designation, divaricata.
