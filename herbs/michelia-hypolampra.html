What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia hypolampra?

Michelia hypolampra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Magnoliales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Magnoliaceae and is categorized under the genus Michelia, specifically identified by the species name hypolampra.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Michelia hypolampra has large, elliptic to oblong leaves that are leathery in texture and possess an entire margin with an acute apex.
