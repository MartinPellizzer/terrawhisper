10 Medicinal Constituents Of Euphorbia Hirta (Tickseed)

Euphorbia hirta has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antimalarial activities. The flavonoids can help alleviate respiratory issues and improve immune function, while the phenolic acids may help reduce oxidative stress and prevent certain diseases.

These constituents can improve people's lives by providing relief from common health issues and reducing the risk of chronic diseases.

1. Caffeic acid

Euphorbia hirta caffeic acid is a bioactive compound that has been isolated from its leaves.
