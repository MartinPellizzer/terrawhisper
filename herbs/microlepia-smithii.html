Microlepia smithii, also known as the Renga Renga fern or New Zealand fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand and other parts of the Pacific region.

It is a popular medicinal plant used in traditional Maori medicine, valued for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.
