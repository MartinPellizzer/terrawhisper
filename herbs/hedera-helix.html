What are the pest and diseases tips of Hedera helix?

Hedera helix, also known as English ivy, is a popular ornamental plant that is susceptible to several pests and diseases.

Another pest that can infest Hedera helix is the aphid, which can transmit plant viruses like potato virus X. Fungal diseases such as root rot and leaf spot are also common issues for English ivy, often caused by overwatering or poor drainage. Other diseases that can affect Hedera helix include powdery mildew, which causes a white, powdery coating to form on the leaves, and botrytis blight, which results in grayish-white mold growth on flowers and leaves.
