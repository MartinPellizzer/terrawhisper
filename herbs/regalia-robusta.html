Regalia robusta has many medicinal parts, such as roots, leaves, and stems, which are used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments.

The roots of Regalia robusta are rich in alkaloids, including robusta alkaloid and robustamine, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Possible Side Effects of Regalia robusta

Regalia robusta can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, dizziness, and allergic reactions in some individuals.

If ingested in large quantities, it may cause stomach upset, diarrhea, and vomiting.

Excessive use of the plant's extract or oil may also lead to interactions with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

In severe cases, improper use of Regalia robusta can result in kidney or liver damage.
