Aceras lanceum

Aceras lanceum, also known as the Orchis lutea or the Butterfly Orchid, is a rare and endangered orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Aceras lanceum include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve breathing, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections.

Herbal preparations of this plant often involve drying and processing the roots and rhizomes to create teas, infusions, or tinctures that can be used to harness its medicinal properties.
