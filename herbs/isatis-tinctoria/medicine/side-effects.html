10 Possible Side Effects Of Isatis Tinctoria (Woad)

Isatis tinctoria has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption, individual sensitivities, or poor quality extracts. For example, an allergic reaction can lead to anaphylaxis, a severe condition that requires immediate medical attention.

Additionally, digestive issues can cause stomach pain, nausea, and diarrhea, which can lead to dehydration and fatigue, further exacerbating daily life.
