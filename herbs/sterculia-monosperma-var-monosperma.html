What's the taxonomical classification of Sterculia monosperma var. monosperma?

Sterculia monosperma var. monosperma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Malvales. Its taxonomic progression continues through the family Malvaceae and the genus Sterculia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety Sterculia monosperma var. monosperma.
