Dichondra repens

What's the taxonomical classification of Dichondra repens?

Dichondra repens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Solanales and into the family Convolvulaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Dichondra, and its unique species designation, repens.

Some traditional medicinal practices utilize extracts from the leaves to soothe skin irritations and promote faster healing of superficial abrasions.
