Urtica subinermis

Urtica subinermis, commonly known as Chinese nettle, is a plant species within the genus Urtica.

It is a perennial herb native to Asia, known for its medicinal properties and uses in traditional medicine.

The therapeutic actions of the plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory activities.

If not handled correctly, the plant's sap can also cause eye irritation and respiratory issues, especially for people with pre-existing allergies or sensitivities.
