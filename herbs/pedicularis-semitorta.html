Pedicularis semitorta

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis semitorta?

Pedicularis semitorta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Lamiales, the plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Pedicularis, with its unique designation being the species semitorta.
