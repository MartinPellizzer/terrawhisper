Rubus irenaeus var. innoxius

What's the taxonomical classification of Rubus irenaeus var. innoxius?

Rubus irenaeus var. innoxius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Rosales. Following its placement in the family Rosaceae, the plant is identified by the genus Rubus, eventually reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Rubus irenaeus var. innoxius.
