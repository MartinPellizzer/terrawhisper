Java Tea (Orthosiphon stamineus) Uses

Java Tea is used to treat kidney stones, urinary tract infections, and gout by supporting urinary health and reducing oxalate accumulation. It also helps with urinary discomfort, high blood pressure, and edema due to its diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties. It may assist in managing diabetes by improving kidney function and regulating fluid retention.

Java Tea (Orthosiphon stamineus) Monograph

1. Kidney stones

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used traditionally to support kidney health. It may help in the prevention and management of kidney stones by promoting urine production and reducing the concentration of minerals in urine. Its diuretic properties aid in flushing out stones and preventing their formation.

2. Urinary tract infections

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used to support urinary tract health by promoting diuresis and reducing inflammation. It contains compounds that may help alleviate symptoms of urinary tract infections by increasing urine flow and supporting kidney function. Its traditional use aligns with modern interest in natural remedies for urinary health.

3. Gout

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used traditionally to support kidney function and reduce uric acid levels, which can help manage gout symptoms. Its diuretic properties aid in flushing out toxins and excess uric acid from the body, potentially reducing inflammation and pain associated with gout attacks.

4. Oxalate accumulation

Java Tea, or Orthosiphon stamineus, contains oxalates that can accumulate in the body, potentially leading to kidney stones or other urinary tract issues. Prolonged use without proper hydration may increase this risk, so it is important to consume it in moderation and stay well-hydrated.

5. Urinary discomfort

Java Tea, or Orthosiphon stamineus, is traditionally used to alleviate urinary discomfort. It helps reduce inflammation and supports kidney function, making it beneficial for conditions like urinary tract infections and bladder irritation. Its diuretic properties promote the elimination of toxins, easing symptoms associated with urinary issues.

6. High blood pressure

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used traditionally to manage high blood pressure. It contains compounds that may help relax blood vessels and reduce fluid retention. Studies suggest it can lower blood pressure naturally, though it should be used under medical supervision.

7. Edema

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is traditionally used to treat edema due to its diuretic properties. It helps reduce fluid retention by increasing urine production. Studies suggest it may support kidney function and reduce swelling, particularly in the lower limbs. It is often used in herbal medicine for its gentle yet effective role in managing water retention.

8. Hypertension

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used in traditional medicine to support blood pressure regulation. It contains compounds that may help reduce hypertension by promoting diuresis and relaxing blood vessels. Studies suggest it can lower systolic and diastolic blood pressure in individuals with mild to moderate hypertension. Its use is often combined with lifestyle changes for optimal results.

9. Fluid retention

Java Tea, or Orthosiphon stamineus, is traditionally used to reduce fluid retention due to its diuretic properties. It helps eliminate excess water and salt from the body, supporting kidney function. This makes it beneficial for conditions like edema and hypertension. Its natural compounds promote urine production and balance fluid levels in the body.

10. Diabetes

Orthosiphon stamineus, or Java Tea, is used in traditional medicine to support diabetes management. It contains compounds that may help regulate blood sugar levels by enhancing insulin sensitivity and promoting glucose metabolism. Studies suggest it may reduce hyperglycemia and improve pancreatic function, making it a valuable complementary therapy under medical supervision.
