Java Tea (Orthosiphon stamineus) Compounds

The primary compounds in Java Tea include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Saponins and terpenoids contribute to its bitter taste and potential health benefits. Tannins and coumarin provide astringent properties, while glycosides and essential oils add to its medicinal profile. Alkaloids and anthocyanins further support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
