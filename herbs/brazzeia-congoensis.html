Brazzeia congoensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Brazzeia congoensis has a large woody structure characterized by a tall, straight trunk that can reach heights of up to 30 meters with a diameter of approximately 60 centimeters. The leaves are large, elliptical, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the branches. Its reproductive organs consist of dense, cylindrical inflorescences that emerge from the leaf axils. The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several small, hard seeds. The bark is typically smooth and dark, often providing a distinct textural contrast to the lush green foliage.
