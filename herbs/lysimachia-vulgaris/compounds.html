Yellow Loosestrife (Lysimachia vulgaris) Compounds

Yellow Loosestrife contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and glycosides contribute to its astringent and medicinal qualities. Coumarin and saponins support its use in traditional remedies, while anthocyanins, essential oils, and terpenoids provide additional therapeutic benefits. Alkaloids also play a role in its pharmacological activity.
