Common Cudweed (Tragopogon pratensis) Compounds

Common Cudweed contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin contributes to its mild sedative properties, while terpenoids and glycosides support its overall medicinal value. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and detoxifying effects, and essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile.
