Phyllostachys heteroclada belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Poaceae, which places it within a group of specialized grasses. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Phyllostachys, with its unique biological designation being the species heteroclada.
