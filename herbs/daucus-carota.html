Possible Side Effects of daucus carota

Daucus carota can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing excessive bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties.

Consuming high amounts of the herb may lead to allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, Daucus carota may interact with blood-thinning medications, further increasing the risk of bleeding.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption of the herb can also cause gastrointestinal issues, such as nausea, diarrhea, and stomach pain.
