What's the taxonomical classification of Peliosanthes ophiopogonoides?

Peliosanthes ophiopogonoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the family Asparagaceae, this plant is specifically categorized within the genus Peliosanthes, with its unique identity defined by the species name ophiopogonoides.
