What's the taxonomical classification of Peliosanthes ophiopogonoides?

Peliosanthes ophiopogonoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asparagales. As a member of the family Asparagaceae, this plant is specifically categorized within the genus Peliosanthes, with its unique identity defined by the species name ophiopogonoides.
