10 Precautions To Take When Using Punica Granatum (Pomegranate)

Punica granatum has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as taking it in moderation due to its high antioxidant content.

Consuming excessive amounts can lead to side effects like gastrointestinal upset, diarrhea, and allergic reactions. Moreover, its high tannin content can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and reduce their effectiveness. Ignoring these precautions may cause adverse effects like bleeding, nausea, and stomach cramps.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the safe dosage and potential interactions.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Punica granatum medicinally.
