Dracocephalum argunense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further nested within the family Lamiaceae, a group well-known for its aromatic qualities. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Dracocephalum, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Dracocephalum argunense.
