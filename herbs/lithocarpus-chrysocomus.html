What's the taxonomical classification of Lithocarpus chrysocomus?

Lithocarpus chrysocomus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical descent, it falls into the order Fagales and the family Fagaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus, Lithocarpus, and its specific species name, chrysocomus.
