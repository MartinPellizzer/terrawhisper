Ajuga (Ajuga reptans) Uses

Ajuga reptans is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and respiratory tract infections. It helps alleviate symptoms like cough, cold symptoms, and mucosal irritation by reducing inflammation and lung congestion. Its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties support respiratory health.

Ajuga (Ajuga reptans) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help reduce coughing and clear mucus from the airways. It may also soothe irritation in the bronchial passages, offering relief from symptoms associated with chronic bronchial inflammation.

2. Allergic rhinitis

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and sneezing by supporting the body's natural response to allergens. Its use is often combined with other herbs in herbal formulations for enhanced relief.

3. Cough

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to alleviate cough symptoms due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the respiratory tract. Often prepared as a tea, it supports clearing congestion and easing breathing in mild respiratory conditions.

4. Asthma

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It is believed to help alleviate asthma symptoms by acting as an expectorant and bronchodilator. Its anti-inflammatory properties may reduce airway swelling, while its mild sedative effects can ease breathing difficulties. However, it should be used under professional guidance.

5. Respiratory tract infections

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for conditions like coughs and bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help clear mucus and reduce irritation in the airways. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to ease symptoms of respiratory tract infections.

6. Respiratory infections

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for conditions like coughs and bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties may help reduce mucus production and combat pathogens. It is often prepared as a tea to soothe irritated airways and ease breathing.

7. Cold symptoms

Ajuga reptans is traditionally used to alleviate cold symptoms due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce congestion and soothe respiratory discomfort. Often prepared as a tea, it supports the body's natural healing processes during mild colds. Its use is common in herbal medicine for short-term relief of upper respiratory tract symptoms.

8. Respiratory inflammation

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to alleviate respiratory inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce swelling in the airways and promotes the clearance of mucus, making it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis and coughs. Its active compounds support respiratory health by soothing irritation and improving airflow.

9. Mucosal irritation

Ajuga reptans has been traditionally used to soothe mucosal irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. It helps reduce swelling and discomfort in the throat, mouth, and digestive tract. Its mild nature makes it suitable for internal and external applications in herbal remedies.

10. Lung congestion

Ajuga reptans is traditionally used to alleviate lung congestion due to its expectorant properties. It helps loosen mucus and promote its expulsion from the respiratory tract. Often prepared as a tea, it supports respiratory health by reducing inflammation and improving airflow. Its use is particularly valued in herbal medicine for treating coughs and bronchial issues.
