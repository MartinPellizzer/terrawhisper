10 Health Benefits Of Ajuga Reptans (Bugleweed)

Ajuga reptans has health benefits such as reducing anxiety and insomnia due to its antispasmodic and sedative properties.

Its ability to calm the nervous system can help improve sleep quality and alleviate stress-related symptoms.

Additionally, bugleweed's anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties make it effective in treating respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma, allowing individuals to breathe easier and reduce reliance on medication.
