10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ajuga Reptans (Bugleweed)

Ajuga reptans has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoid glycosides.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and sedative effects. The iridoids, in particular, have been shown to help regulate thyroid function and reduce symptoms of hyperthyroidism. For example, bugleweed has been used traditionally to alleviate anxiety and insomnia, allowing individuals to enjoy a more restful night's sleep and wake up feeling refreshed.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties can provide relief from joint pain and discomfort.
