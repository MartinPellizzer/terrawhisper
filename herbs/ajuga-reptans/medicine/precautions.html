10 Precautions To Take When Using Ajuga Reptans (Bugleweed)

Ajuga reptans has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use in pregnancy and breastfeeding due to potential uterine stimulation and hormonal effects.

Not taking these precautions can lead to issues like uterine contractions and hormonal imbalances.

For instance, consuming bugleweed during pregnancy can cause premature labor or birth, while in breastfeeding mothers, it can interfere with milk production.
