Centifolia Rose (Rosa × Centifolia Prolifera)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Rosa × centifolia prolifera.

Centifolia rose, scientifically known as Rosa × centifolia prolifera, is a fragrant, ornamental shrub prized for its medicinal and aromatic properties. This plant, often referred to as the hundred-petaled rose, has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant benefits. Its petals are rich in essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic effects, making it a valuable adaptogen and natural remedy. In traditional Persian, Indian, and Chinese medicine, Centifolia rose has been used to treat digestive issues, skin conditions, and emotional distress, while in modern wellness practices, it is utilized in aromatherapy, skincare products, and as a natural sweetener. Its unique, delicate flavor and rare active compounds like geraniol and citronellol make it a distinctive ingredient in both culinary and therapeutic applications.
