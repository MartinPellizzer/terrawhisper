Manglietia insignis

What's the taxonomical classification of Manglietia insignis?

Manglietia insignis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae, which falls under the order Magnoliales. The plant is a member of the family Magnoliaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Manglietia, with its unique identification as the species insignis.
