Chrysospeltiun uniflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Saxifragales and the family Saxifragaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Chrysospeltiun, and its species designation, uniflorum.
