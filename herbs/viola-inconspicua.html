What's the taxonomical classification of Viola inconspicua?

Viola inconspicua belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Malpighiales and is a member of the family Violaceae. At a more specific level, it is situated within the genus Viola, eventually reaching its unique designation as the species inconspicua.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
