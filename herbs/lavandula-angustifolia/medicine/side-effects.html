10 Possible Side Effects Of Lavandula Angustifolia (English Lavender)

Lavandula angustifolia, also known as English lavender, has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

Causes of these side effects include improper preparation or use of the herb, high dosages, or sensitivity to its active compounds.

For example, skin irritation can lead to discomfort and interfere with daily activities, while allergic reactions can cause life-threatening anaphylaxis, further exacerbating pre-existing health conditions.
