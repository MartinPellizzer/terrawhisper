Hymenophyllum polyanthos

What's the taxonomical classification of Hymenophyllum polyanthos?

Hymenophyllum polyanthos belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Hymenophyllales and the family Hymenophyllaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Hymenophyllum, specifically identifying as the species polyanthos.
