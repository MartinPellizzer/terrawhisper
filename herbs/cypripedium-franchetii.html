Cypripedium franchetii

What's the taxonomical classification of Cypripedium franchetii?

Cypripedium franchetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling under the genus Cypripedium, and is specifically identified by its species name, franchetii.
