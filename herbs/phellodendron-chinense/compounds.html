Chinese Mahogany (Phellodendron chinense) Compounds

The primary compounds of Chinese Mahogany include alkaloids, which have antimicrobial properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent qualities and anti-inflammatory actions. Saponins and glycosides support detoxification, while coumarin and terpenoids offer anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic benefits. Essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its therapeutic and protective properties.
