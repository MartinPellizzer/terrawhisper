Chinese Mahogany (Phellodendron chinense) Uses

Chinese Mahogany is used to treat various skin conditions such as infections, eczema, acne, fungal infections, and psoriasis. It helps reduce inflammation and itching, making it effective for dermatitis and skin allergies. Its antimicrobial properties support healing in inflammatory skin conditions.

Chinese Mahogany (Phellodendron chinense) Monograph

1. Skin infection

Chinese Mahogany is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce inflammation and combat bacterial growth. The bark is often prepared as a poultice or infused oil for direct application on infected areas. Its effectiveness makes it a valued remedy in traditional herbal medicine for skin conditions.

2. Eczema

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat eczema due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce skin irritation and promote healing. The bark is often decocted and applied topically for its soothing effects on affected areas.

3. Skin infections

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat skin infections due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and pus in conditions like boils and eczema. The bark is typically prepared as a decoction or ointment for external application. It is valued for its ability to clear heat and detoxify the skin.

4. Inflammatory skin conditions

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat inflammatory skin conditions such as eczema and psoriasis. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help reduce redness, itching, and infection. The bark is often prepared as a decoction or ointment for topical application. It is valued for its ability to clear heat and detoxify the body, promoting skin healing.

5. Skin inflammation

Phellodendron chinense is used to treat skin inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and infection in conditions like eczema and dermatitis. The bark and leaves are typically prepared as a topical ointment or infusion for localized application. Its active compounds inhibit inflammatory responses and promote skin healing.

6. Acne

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat acne due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce skin inflammation and regulate sebum production, making it effective for managing acne and preventing breakouts. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations tailored for skin health.

7. Fungal infections

Chinese Mahogany is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat fungal infections due to its antimicrobial and antifungal properties. It contains compounds like berberine and phellodendrin that inhibit fungal growth. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness against skin and nail infections.

8. Skin allergies

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat skin allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce redness, itching, and swelling associated with allergic reactions. The bark contains berberine, which modulates immune responses and supports skin health. It is often prepared as a decoction or topical application for localized relief.

9. Dermatitis

Chinese Mahogany is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat dermatitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce skin irritation and redness, often applied topically as a paste or wash. The bark contains compounds that soothe inflamed skin and promote healing in cases of eczema or fungal infections.

10. Psoriasis

Phellodendron chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat psoriasis due to its anti-inflammatory and detoxifying properties. It helps reduce skin inflammation and regulate immune responses. The bark is often prepared as a decoction and applied topically or ingested to manage symptoms. It is valued for its ability to clear heat and toxins from the body.
