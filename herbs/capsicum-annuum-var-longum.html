Capsicum annuum var. longum

Capsicum annuum var. longum is a variety of the capsicum plant, commonly known as the long pepper or Chinese pepper, characterized by its long, thin, and cylindrical shape, typically 5-10 cm in length.

The medicinal properties of this plant include its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of long pepper include its ability to aid digestion, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory issues, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine.

Long pepper is often used in herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as in traditional Chinese medicine to treat a range of health conditions.
