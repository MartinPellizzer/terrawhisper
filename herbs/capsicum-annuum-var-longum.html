Capsicum annuum var. longum

Capsicum annuum var. longum, commonly known as the long thin pepper or long red pepper, is a variety of pepper plant that belongs to the Capsicum genus.

This plant is known for its elongated, thin fruits, typically red in color, which have been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

The health benefits of Capsicum annuum var. longum include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can help alleviate various health issues such as pain, fever, and respiratory problems.

The therapeutic actions of this plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including capsaicin, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which can modulate the nervous system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial activity.
