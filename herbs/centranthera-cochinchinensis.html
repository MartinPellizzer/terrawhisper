Centranthera cochinchinensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Centranthera cochinchinensis has an erect, woody habit characterized by opposite, elliptical leaves with entire margins and a distinctively coriaceous texture. The stems are cylindrical and can develop a slightly quadrangular shape during certain growth stages. These structural features help the plant adapt to its specific tropical forest environment.
