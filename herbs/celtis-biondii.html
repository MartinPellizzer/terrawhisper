Celtis biondii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is ordered within the Rosales and placed in the family Cannabaceae. Ultimately, this plant is defined by its genus, Celtis, and its specific species designation, biondii.
