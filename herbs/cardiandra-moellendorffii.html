Cardiandra moellendorffii

What's the taxonomical classification of Cardiandra moellendorffii?

Cardiandra moellendorffii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it resides in the order Cornales and is a member of the family Hydrangeaceae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Cardiandra, and its unique species designation, moellendorffii.
