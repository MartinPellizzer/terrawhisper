Acacia dealbata

What's the taxonomical classification of Acacia dealbata?

Acacia dealbata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Acacia and the specific species epithet dealbatia.
