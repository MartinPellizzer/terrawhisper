Picea smithiana subsp. nepalensis

Picea smithiana subsp.

nepalensis, also known as Nepal spruce or Himalayan spruce, is a subspecies of evergreen coniferous tree native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antibacterial properties, making it a valuable resource for treating various health conditions.

The health benefits of Picea smithiana subsp.

nepalensis include reducing fever, relieving respiratory issues, and acting as an antiseptic to promote wound healing.

In herbal preparations, the needles and bark of the tree are often used in tea form or as essential oils to harness its medicinal properties.
