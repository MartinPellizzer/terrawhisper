Allium Chinense Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Allium chinense, commonly known as Chinese leek or garlic chives, is a flowering plant belonging to the Allium genus, renowned for its aromatic leaves and bulbous underground stem.

This herb is valued for its potential to support cardiovascular health, reduce inflammation, and enhance immune function due to its rich content of sulfur compounds and flavonoids.

The bioactive constituents in Allium chinense include allicin, quercetin, and other organosulfur compounds, which contribute to its antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from its leaves and bulbs to harness its therapeutic benefits.
