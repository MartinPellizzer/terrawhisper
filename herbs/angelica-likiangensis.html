What's the taxonomical classification of Angelica likiangensis?

Angelica likiangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Apiales and into the family Apiaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Angelica, with its specific identification being the species likiangensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These inflorescences are supported by sturdy peduncles that rise above the vegetative mass.
