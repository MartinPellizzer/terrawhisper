Cassiope selaginoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical descent through the order Ericales, it is a member of the family Ericaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Cassiope, and its unique species designation, selaginoides.
