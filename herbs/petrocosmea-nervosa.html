Petrocosmea nervosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Petrocosmea nervosa?

Petrocosmea nervosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the taxonomic order Lamiales. As a member of the family Gesneriaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Petrocosmea, with its specific designation being the species nervosa.
