What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllanthus tsarongensis?

Phyllanthus tsarongensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the hierarchical structure of its order, Malpighiales, it falls into the family Phyllanthaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Phyllanthus, with its specific identification being the species tsarongensis.
