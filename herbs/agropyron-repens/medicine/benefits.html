Agropyron repens helps in weight loss because it contains inulin, a prebiotic fiber that promotes satiety and aids digestion.

The fiber absorbs water and expands in the stomach, creating a feeling of fullness and reducing hunger pangs.

Regular consumption of couch grass tea or supplements can support a weight loss diet by increasing feelings of satisfaction and reducing cravings for unhealthy snacks.

10. Controls blood pressure

Agropyron repens controls blood pressure because its extracts exhibit vasodilatory and diuretic properties.

The herb contains flavonoids and glycosides that help relax blood vessel walls, reducing blood pressure.

Additionally, its diuretic effect aids in reducing fluid retention in the blood vessels, further contributing to its blood pressure-lowering properties, making it a potential natural remedy for hypertension.
