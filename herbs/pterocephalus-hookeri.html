Pterocephalus hookeri

What's the taxonomical classification of Pterocephalus hookeri?

Pterocephalus hookeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Dipsacales. Within this hierarchy, it is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae and is specifically identified by the genus Pterocephalus, with its unique species designation being hookeri.
