What's the taxonomical classification of Deparia boryana?

Deparia boryana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This fern is a member of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Deparia, and is specifically identified by its species name, boryana.
