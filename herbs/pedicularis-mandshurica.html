What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis mandshurica?

Pedicularis mandshurica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Lamiales. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the family Orobanchaceae, specifically within the genus Pedicularis, and is identified by the species epithet mandshurica.
