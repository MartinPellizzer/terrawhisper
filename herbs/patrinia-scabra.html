Patriina scabra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the therapeutic actions of Patrinia scabra?

Patrinia scabra has therapeutic properties that function as a bitter, diuretic, cholagogue, stomachic, and aperient agent. Its bitter components stimulate the appetite and improve digestion by acting as a reliable stomachic. The diuretic action helps the body eliminate excess fluids and waste through increased urine production. Finally, its aperient qualities provide a mild laxative effect to support regular bowel movements.
