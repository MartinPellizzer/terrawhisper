Urtica dioica var. gracilis

Urtica dioica var. gracilis, commonly known as stinging nettle var. gracilis, is a variant of the stinging nettle plant, characterized by its slightly smaller leaves and reduced sting.

This plant is valued for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, as well as its ability to support urinary tract health and alleviate symptoms of menopause.

The therapeutic actions of Urtica dioica var. gracilis include its analgesic, anti-allergic, and anti-cancer properties, making it a popular natural remedy for a range of health issues.
