Possible Side Effects of ailanthus altissima

Additionally, Ailanthus altissima may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse reactions.

In severe cases, improper use of the herb can cause allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.
