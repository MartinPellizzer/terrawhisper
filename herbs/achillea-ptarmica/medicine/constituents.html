10 Medicinal Constituents Of Achillea Ptarmica (Yarrow)

Achillea ptarmica has active constituents such as acetic acid, borneol, and sesquiterpene lactones.

These constituents contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and antiseptic effects. Examples of how these properties can improve people's lives include relieving menstrual cramp pain and promoting wound healing.

Additionally, the antiseptic properties can aid in preventing infection, promoting recovery, and improving overall health.
