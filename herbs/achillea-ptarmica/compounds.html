Common Yarrow (Achillea ptarmica) Compounds

Common Yarrow contains essential oil, which includes compounds like chamazulene and cineole. Flavonoids such as quercetin and kaempferol provide antioxidant benefits. Coumarin contributes to its anti-inflammatory properties, while phenolic acids like caffeic acid support its medicinal uses. These compounds work together to give Yarrow its therapeutic effects.

Common Yarrow (Achillea ptarmica) Monograph

The essential oil of Common Yarrow (Achillea ptarmica) contains compounds like chamazulene and bisabolol. These have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It is used in herbal medicine for treating skin conditions and respiratory issues. The oil is often applied topically or in steam inhalation.
