10 Medicinal Constituents Of Dipteryx Odorata (Tonka Bean)

Dipteryx odorata has active constituents such as coumarins, vanillin, and limonene.

These compounds exhibit analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties. The analgesic properties of tonka bean can help alleviate pain, while its anti-inflammatory properties can reduce swelling and inflammation.

Additionally, its antispasmodic properties can help relax muscles and improve sleep quality, enhancing overall well-being and quality of life.
