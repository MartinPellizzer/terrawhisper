10 Precautions To Take When Using Dipteryx Odorata (Tonka Bean)

Dipteryx odorata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use by pregnant women, as it can stimulate the uterus and cause contractions.

Additionally, it's essential to be cautious with its high coumarin content, as excessive consumption can lead to liver damage, nausea, and vomiting.

Consuming tonka bean in large quantities can also cause respiratory issues, headaches, and allergic reactions in some individuals.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Dipteryx odorata medicinally.
