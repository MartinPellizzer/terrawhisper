What's the taxonomical classification of Pittosporum brevicalyx?

Pittosporum brevicalyx belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized under the order Apiales. This specific plant is a part of the family Pittosporaceae, situated within the genus Pittosporum, and is identified by its unique species designation, brevicalyx.
