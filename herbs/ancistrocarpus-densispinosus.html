What's the taxonomical classification of Ancistrocarpus densispinosus?

Ancistrocarpus densispinosus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the taxonomic order Malvales. This plant is a member of the family Malvaceae and is further categorized under the genus Ancistrocarpus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species densispinosus.
