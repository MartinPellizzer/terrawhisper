What's the taxonomical classification of Gaultheria griffithiana?

Gaultheria griffithiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ericales and falls under the family Ericaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Gaultheria, with its unique designation being the species griffithiana.
