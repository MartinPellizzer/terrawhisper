Ipomoea aculeata

Ipomoea aculeata, commonly known as thorny morning glory, is a perennial climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The therapeutic actions of Ipomoea aculeata include its ability to act as an analgesic, antipyretic, and anti-ulcer agent, making it a valuable remedy in folk medicine.
