Fortunella margarita

What's the taxonomical classification of Fortunella margarita?

Fortunella margarita belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Sapindales and the family Rutaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Fortunella, and its species designation, margarita.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various skin conditions, respiratory ailments, and gastrointestinal issues. Its medicinal properties are frequently applied to soothe inflammatory skin rashes and minor wounds. Some traditional practices utilize its extracts to alleviate symptoms of common colds and persistent coughs. Additionally, certain preparations are ingested to help manage stomach discomfort or digestive irregularities. These applications rely on the unique bioactive compounds found within its leaves and stems.
