Yunjin Curcuma (Curcuma wenyujin) Compounds

Yunjin Curcuma contains several primary active compounds, including terpenoids, which have anti-inflammatory properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Glycosides and coumarins contribute to its antimicrobial and anti-cancer activities. Phenolic acids and saponins support its digestive and detoxifying functions.
