10 Medicinal Constituents Of Maianthemum Racemosum (False Lily)

Maianthemum racemosum has active constituents such as saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The saponins in false lily can improve cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol levels and blood pressure, while the flavonoids can boost immune function and alleviate symptoms of allergies and respiratory issues.
