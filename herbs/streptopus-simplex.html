Streptopus simplex

What's the taxonomical classification of Streptopus simplex?

Streptopus simplex belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its progression through the order Liliales, it is placed in the family Liliaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed within the genus Streptopus, specifically as the species simplex.
