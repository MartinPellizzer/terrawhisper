What's the taxonomical classification of Peristylus coeloceras?

Peristylus coeloceras belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Asparagales.
