Magnolia oblongifolia

Magnolia oblongifolia, commonly known as the Small White Magnolia, is a species of evergreen tree native to China and Taiwan.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with health benefits including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can help to reduce pain and improve overall well-being.

The therapeutic actions of Magnolia oblongifolia are attributed to its ability to modulate the immune system and exhibit sedative and anticonvulsant properties, making it useful in treating anxiety and insomnia.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects and make it a valuable resource in traditional Chinese medicine.
