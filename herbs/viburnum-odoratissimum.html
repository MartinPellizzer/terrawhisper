Possible Side Effects of viburnum odoratissimum

Viburnum odoratissimum can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing allergic reactions, particularly skin irritation and hives, in individuals sensitive to its compounds.

Overuse of Viburnum odoratissimum may cause interactions with certain medications, potentially exacerbating conditions like high blood pressure or altering the effectiveness of prescribed treatments.

Furthermore, improper use can result in kidney or liver damage in rare cases, making it essential to consult a healthcare professional before using this herb.
