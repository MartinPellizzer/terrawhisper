Ipomoea laevigata

Ipomoea laevigata, also known as the smooth morning glory, is a perennial vine plant native to the southeastern United States.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help to soothe skin irritations and wounds.

The health benefits of Ipomoea laevigata include its potential to lower blood pressure, reduce fever, and alleviate symptoms of insomnia and anxiety.

Ipomoea laevigata can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures for its medicinal use, although more research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety.
