Aconitum brachypodum var. laxiflorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum brachypodum var. laxiflorum?

Aconitum brachypoduum var. laxiflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae. More specifically, this plant is categorized under the genus Aconitum, with its precise taxonomic identity being the variety laxiflorum of the species Aconitum brachypoduum.
