What is Vincetoxicum mollissimum?

Vincetoxicum mollissimum is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It has a woody base and stems that are 30-100 cm long, with leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape, 2-10 cm long, and have entire margins. The flowers are white or pinkish, and are arranged in axillary cymes, with a corolla tube 10-20 mm long and a spur 5-10 mm long. The fruit is a pair of follicles that are 15-30 mm long, containing numerous seeds.

The plant is native to China, Japan, and Korea.
