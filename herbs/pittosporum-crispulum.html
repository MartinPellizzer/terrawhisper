Pittosporum crispulum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, its taxonomic hierarchy is completed by its placement within the genus Pittosporum, specifically identifying it as the species crispulum.

Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, often followed by fleshy, orange-colored drupes.
