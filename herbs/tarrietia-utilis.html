What's the taxonomical classification of Tarrietia utilis?

Tarrietia utilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further belongs to the order Malvales and is a member of the family Malvaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Tarrietia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species utilis.
