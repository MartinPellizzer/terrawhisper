    What is Bulbophyllum kenejiense?

    Bulbophyllum kenejiense is an epiphytic orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

    It is characterized by a slender stem, reaching up to 30 cm in length, and bearing 2-3 apical leaves, which are coriaceous and elliptic in shape, measuring 7-12 cm in length and 1.5-2.5 cm in width. The inflorescence is a short, racemose spike, typically producing 1-2 flowers per node, which are zygomorphic and have a labellum with a distinctive keel shape, measuring 1-1.5 cm in length and 0.5-0.8 cm in width. The plant's flowers are pale yellow to yellow-green in color, with a lip that is slightly curved, bearing a series of minute, glandular hairs.

    The species is endemic to the tropical forests of Sabah, Malaysia.
