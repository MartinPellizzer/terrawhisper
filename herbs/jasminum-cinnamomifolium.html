What's the taxonomical classification of Jasminum cinnamomifolium?

Jasminum cinnamomifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Oleaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Jasminum, with the species designation cinnamomifolium.
