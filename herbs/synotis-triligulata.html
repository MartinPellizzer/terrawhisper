Synotis triligulata

What's the taxonomical classification of Synotis triligulata?

Synotis triligulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a specific representative of the family Asteraceae, which is one of the most diverse families of flowering plants. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Synotis, and its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, triligulata.
