What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Veronica himalenensis has a perennial herbaceous habit characterized by erect, glandular-pubescent stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 40 centimeters. The reproductive structures include four stamens and a single style that protrude from the center of the floral tube.
