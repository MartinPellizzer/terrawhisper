Leersia hexandra

What's the taxonomical classification of Leersia hexandra?

Leersia hexandra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Poales. This plant is a representative of the family Poaceae, falling specifically under the genus Leersia, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name hexandra.

What are the herbal preparations of Leersia hexandra?

Leersia hexandra is prepared primarily as a decoction of the dried fruit or as a powder mixed with water to treat various ailments. Additionally, the plant components may be incorporated into alcoholic tinctures to enhance the solubility of its constituents. These various forms allow for different rates of absorption and therapeutic applications in folk medicine.
