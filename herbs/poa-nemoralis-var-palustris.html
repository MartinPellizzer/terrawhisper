Poa nemoralis var. palustris

Poa nemoralis var. palustris, also known as marsh meadow-grass, is a variant of the Poa nemoralis species, a type of perennial grass that thrives in wetland areas.

The medicinal properties of Poa nemoralis var. palustris include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.

The plant is believed to offer several health benefits, such as soothing digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and supporting the immune system.

Traditionally, the plant has been prepared as a tea or infused into oil for its medicinal properties, and it can also be dried and used in herbal remedies and supplements.
