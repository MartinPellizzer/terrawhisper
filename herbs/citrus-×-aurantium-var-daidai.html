Citrus × aurantium var. daidai

Citrus × aurantium var. daidai is a variety of bitter orange, a hybrid of the sour orange (Citrus aurantium) and the citron (Citrus medica).

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral effects.

The health benefits of Citrus × aurantium var. daidai include reducing stress and anxiety, improving digestion, and boosting the immune system, as well as providing relief from headaches and colds.

Citrus × aurantium var. daidai is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, and can also be consumed as a juice or added to food and beverages for its flavor and medicinal properties.
