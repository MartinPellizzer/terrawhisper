10 Medicinal Constituents Of Panax Quinquefolius (American Ginseng)

These compounds enhance cognitive function, reduce stress and anxiety, and alleviate fatigue. For example, ginsenosides have been shown to improve memory and concentration, while terpenoids can reduce symptoms of insomnia and promote relaxation.

These medicinal properties can improve quality of life by enhancing mental and physical well-being.
