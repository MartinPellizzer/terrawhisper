Medicago Sativa

Medicago sativa, commonly known as alfalfa, has been utilized for its medicinal properties for centuries.

It contains a variety of bioactive compounds, including vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which contribute to its therapeutic potential. Alfalfa is traditionally used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and boost the immune system. It has also been studied for its possible role in managing conditions such as diabetes and menopause symptoms.

However, due to its high nutrient content, it is often used as a dietary supplement rather than a conventional medication.
