Othera japonica

Othera japonica, commonly known as Japanese angelica tree, is a plant species native to East Asia, characterized by its unique, deciduous foliage and vibrant flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries, offering various health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may aid in reducing pain, improving immune function, and protecting against oxidative stress.

The therapeutic actions of Othera japonica include its ability to relax smooth muscles, reduce fever, and exert a mild sedative effect, making it a potential natural remedy for various conditions.
