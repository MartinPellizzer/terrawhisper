Lapsana (Lapsana communis) Uses

Lapsana communis is used to treat respiratory infections, cough, and mucus buildup. It helps alleviate symptoms of bronchitis and allergic rhinitis. It may support respiratory function in asthma and reduce mucus congestion. It is also used for sinusitis and other respiratory allergies.

Lapsana (Lapsana communis) Monograph

1. Respiratory infection

Lapsana communis has been traditionally used to support respiratory health. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties may help alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections. It is often prepared as a tea to soothe coughs and reduce congestion. Regular use may support the body's natural defenses against respiratory ailments.

2. Cough

Lapsana communis has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for alleviating coughs. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help reduce irritation and loosen mucus, making it easier to clear the airways. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to ease persistent or dry coughs.

3. Mucus buildup

Lapsana communis is used to help alleviate mucus buildup due to its expectorant properties. It stimulates the expulsion of respiratory secretions, making it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis or persistent coughs. This herb supports clear airways by reducing congestion and promoting healthy mucus flow.

4. Bronchitis

Lapsana communis has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its expectorant properties may aid in relieving congestion and supporting the healing process in the respiratory tract.

5. Allergic rhinitis

Lapsana communis has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and itching by modulating immune responses. Often prepared as a tincture or infusion, it supports respiratory health in those suffering from seasonal allergies.

6. Asthma

Lapsana communis has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in managing asthma symptoms. Its expectorant properties may also aid in clearing mucus from the airways, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for respiratory conditions.
