10 Precautions To Take When Using Lapsana Communis (Nipplewort)

Lapsana communis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring liver function and adjusting dosages accordingly.

This is crucial because excessive use can lead to liver damage and toxicity. For instance, using high doses without monitoring liver function may cause symptoms like nausea, fatigue, and yellowing of the skin.

Taking these precautions ensures safe and effective use of Lapsana communis for medicinal purposes.

When using Lapsana communis medicinally, it's important to take correct dosage only.
