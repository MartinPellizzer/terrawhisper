Coriaria nepalensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Coriaria nepalensis?

Coriaria nepalensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Cucurbitaleales, which places it in the family Coriariaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Coriaria, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species Coriaria nepalensis.
