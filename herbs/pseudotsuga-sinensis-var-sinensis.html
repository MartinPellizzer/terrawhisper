Pseudotsuga sinensis var. sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudotsuga sinensis var. sinensis?

Pseudotsuga sinensis var. sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the order Pinales, placing it within the family Pinaceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is categorized under the genus Pseudotsuga and the species Pseudotsuga.
