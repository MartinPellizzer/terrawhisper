What's the taxonomical classification of Juncus amplifolius?

Juncus amplifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Poales, it is categorized under the family Juncaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Juncus, and its unique species designation, amplifolius.
