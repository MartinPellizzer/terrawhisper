Aspidosperma Quebracho-Blanco: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Aspidosperma quebracho-blanco, commonly known as *Palo de Palo*, is a medicinal plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Brazil, known for its bark and leaves used in traditional medicine.

This herb is valued for its rich content of bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The health benefits of Aspidosperma quebracho-blanco include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, supporting immune function and digestive health.

Its therapeutic actions make it useful in treating conditions like inflammation, infections, and gastrointestinal disorders.
