Terminalia Arjuna Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Terminalia arjuna, commonly known as the Indian syzygyium, is a small evergreen tree native to India, prized for its medicinal bark.

This herb is widely used in Ayurvedic medicine for its cardiovascular and anti-inflammatory properties.

The bioactive constituents in Terminalia arjuna include tannins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and powders can be made from the bark to support heart health and reduce inflammation.
