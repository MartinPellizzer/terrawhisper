What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Pueraria alopecuroides has a climbing or trailing habit characterized by trifoliate leaves with leaflets that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and possess acute or obtuse apices. The legume pods are relatively small and oblong, containing a limited number of seeds. Fine pubescence often covers the stems and leaf surfaces, distinguishing it from closely related species.
