10 Precautions To Take When Using Prunus Serotina (Black Cherry)

Prunus serotina has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious of allergic reactions and interactions with medications.

Taking this herb without consideration of these risks can lead to side effects like dizziness, nausea, and respiratory issues. For instance, using black cherry with blood thinners may increase bleeding risk, while an allergic reaction could cause severe skin irritation.

Consult a healthcare professional before using Prunus serotina to minimize potential harm.
