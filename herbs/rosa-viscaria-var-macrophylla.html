Rosa viscaria var. macrophylla

Rosa viscaria var. macrophylla, also known as the alpine rose or viscaria, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

The medicinal properties of Rosa viscaria var. macrophylla are primarily attributed to its high content of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.

The health benefits of this plant include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can help to soothe digestive issues, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory problems.
