What's the taxonomical classification of Orostachys cartilaginea?

Orostachys cartilaginea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the taxonomic order Saxifragales. This plant is a member of the family Crassulaceae, which places it within the genus Orostachys, ultimately identifying it at the species level as Orostachys cartilaginea.
