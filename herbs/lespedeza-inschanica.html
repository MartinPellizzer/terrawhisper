Lespedeza inschanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Specifically, this plant is identified by its genus, Lespedeza, and is further distinguished by its unique species epithet, inschanica.
