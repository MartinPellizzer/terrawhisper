Populus nigra var. thevestina

What's the taxonomical classification of Populus nigra var. thevestina?

Populus nigra var. thevestina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Malpighiales and falls within the family Salicaceae. As a member of the genus Populus, this specific plant is taxonomically identified by its unique variety designation, Populus nigra var. thevestina.
