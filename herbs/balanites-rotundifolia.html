Balanites rotundifolia

Balanites rotundifolia, commonly known as Desert Date or Horned Melon, is a species of spiny shrub or small tree native to the deserts of the African continent.

It has been traditionally used in various cultures for its medicinal properties, including the prevention and treatment of various health conditions such as fever, diarrhea, and skin diseases.

The health benefits of Balanites rotundifolia are attributed to its therapeutic actions, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.
