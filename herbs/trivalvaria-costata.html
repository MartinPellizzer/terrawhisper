What's the taxonomical classification of Trivalvaria costata?

Trivalvaria costata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Magnoliales and the family Annonaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trivalvaria, and its species name, costata.
