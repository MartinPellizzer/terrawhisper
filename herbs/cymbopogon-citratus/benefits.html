10 Best Cymbopogon Citratus Health Benefits

Cymbopogon citratus, commonly known as lemon grass, offers a variety of health benefits due to its rich content of essential oils, antioxidants, and phytochemicals.

It is widely used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic properties, which can help in treating digestive issues and reducing stress. Lemon grass has been shown to support respiratory health by acting as a natural decongestant and may aid in alleviating symptoms of asthma and colds. Additionally, its calming effects make it useful in aromatherapy for promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality.

Regular consumption of lemon grass in teas or culinary applications may also contribute to boosting the immune system and lowering cholesterol levels.

Cymbopogon citratus reduces stress levels by promoting relaxation and calming the nervous system.
