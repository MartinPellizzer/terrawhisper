Ficus sarmentosa var. sarmentosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus sarmentosa var. sarmentosa?

Ficus sarmentosa var. sarmentosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. It is a member of the family Moraceae and is specifically identified by the genus Ficus, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the species Ficus sarmentosa var. sarmentosa.
