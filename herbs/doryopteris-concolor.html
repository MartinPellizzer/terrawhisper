What's the taxonomical classification of Doryopteris concolor?

Doryopteris concolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and follows the order Polypodiales. This fern is further categorized into the family Pteridaceae, within which it is placed in the genus Doryopteris. Ultimately, it is identified at the species level as Doryopteris concolor.
