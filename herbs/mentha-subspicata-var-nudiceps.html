Mentha subspicata var. nudiceps

Mentha subspicata var. nudiceps, also known as hairy leaf mint or hairy spearmint, is a variant of the spearmint plant that is native to Australia.

This plant has been used for its medicinal properties, including its ability to alleviate digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and act as a natural antiseptic.

The health benefits of hairy leaf mint include reducing symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), relieving nausea and headaches, and supporting oral health.

Hairy leaf mint can be prepared for use as a tea, added to cooking as a fresh herb, or used in herbal preparations such as essential oils and tinctures.
