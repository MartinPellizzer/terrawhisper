Possible Side Effects of vaccinium myrtillus

Vaccinium myrtillus can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and diarrhea due to its high tannin content.

Excessive consumption may cause allergic reactions, including skin rashes, itching, and difficulty breathing in sensitive individuals.

In rare cases, it may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse health effects.
