What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes komarovii?

Ribes komarovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Saxifragales, placing it within the family Grossulariaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Ribes and is specifically distinguished as the species komarovii.
