What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis polyantha?

Campylotropis polyantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Fabales, it is situated in the family Fabaceae, which is commonly known for its diverse group of legumes. Specifically, the plant is identified by the genus Campylotropis and is distinguished as the species polyantha.
