Fenugraecum officinale

Fenugraecum officinale, commonly known as Fenugreek, is a legume plant native to the Indian subcontinent and the Mediterranean region.

It has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, which include reducing blood sugar levels, improving digestion, and alleviating symptoms of menopause and anxiety.

The therapeutic actions of Fenugreek include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-diabetic properties, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids.
