Rhodiola rosea

Rhodiola rosea, also known as golden root or Arctic root, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Arctic regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties, including adaptogenic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects.

Rhodiola rosea is believed to have several health benefits, such as reducing stress and anxiety, improving mental performance and memory, and boosting mood and overall well-being.
