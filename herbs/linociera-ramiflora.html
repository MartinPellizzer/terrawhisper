Linociera ramiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Linociera ramiflora?

Linociera ramiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling under the order Lamiales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Oleaceae, which places it within the genus Linociera, specifically identified as the species ramiflora.
