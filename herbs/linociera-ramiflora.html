Linociera ramiflora

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Linociera ramiflora has woody stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that feature serrated margins and prominent pinnate venation. The foliage is typically dark green and possesses a slightly leathery texture. Its inflorescence consists of small, tubular flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters along the branches. These flowers display a range of colors including white, pink, or pale lilac. The plant also produces small, rounded fruits that follow the flowering stage.
