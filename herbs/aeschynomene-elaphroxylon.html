What's the taxonomical classification of Aeschynomene elaphroxylon?

Aeschynomene elaphroxylon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Fabaceae, a group known for its significant ecological importance. Within this family, the plant is situated in the genus Aeschynomene, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species elaphroxylon.
