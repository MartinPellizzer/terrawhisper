10 Precautions To Take When Using Vitellaria Paradoxa (Shea Butter Tree)

Vitellaria paradoxa has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential allergenic reactions.

It's essential to patch test the herb to avoid skin irritation, rashes, and itching. Failure to do so may result in skin problems, especially for individuals with sensitive skin.

Furthermore, pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a healthcare professional before using Vitellaria paradoxa due to limited research on its effects during these periods.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Vitellaria paradoxa medicinally.
