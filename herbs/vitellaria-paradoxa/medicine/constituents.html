10 Medicinal Constituents Of Vitellaria Paradoxa (Shea Butter Tree)

Vitellaria paradoxa has active constituents such as pentadecanoic, linoleic, and oleic acids, triterpenoids, and phytosterols.

These constituents are credited with anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and moisturizing properties. They can soothe dry skin conditions, accelerate wound healing, and protect against skin infections.

For instance, topical application of shea butter has improved skin elasticity and hydration in individuals with dry skin conditions, such as eczema and dermatitis, promoting healthier and more resilient skin.
