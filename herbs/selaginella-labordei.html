What's the taxonomical classification of Selaginella labordei?

Selaginella labordei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Lycopodiidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Selaginellales and the family Selaginellaceae. Ultimately, this plant is situated within the genus Selaginella, specifically identified as the species labordei.
