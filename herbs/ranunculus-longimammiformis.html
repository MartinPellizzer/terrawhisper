Ranunculus longimammiformis

This plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health issues, including inflammation and pain, due to its numerous health benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Ranunculus longimammiformis are attributed to its ability to reduce inflammation, exhibit antimicrobial and antifungal properties, and act as a natural antioxidant.
