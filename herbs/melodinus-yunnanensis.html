What's the taxonomical classification of Melodinus yunnanensis?

Melodinus yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling into the order Gentianales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae, which defines its broader biological grouping. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is assigned to the genus Melodinus, with the specific epithet yunnanensis identifying the unique species.
