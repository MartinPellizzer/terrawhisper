Oncorhiza esculentus

Oncorhiza esculentus, commonly known as Goldenseal, is a perennial herb native to North America, belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues and infections, due to its numerous health benefits.

The therapeutic actions of Goldenseal include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties, which help to combat infections and promote overall well-being.

The bioactive constituents of Goldenseal include berberine, a potent alkaloid responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as other compounds such as isoquinoline alkaloids and glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
