Cissampelos pareira var. hirsuta

What's the taxonomical classification of Cissampelos pareira var. hirsuta?

Cissampelos pareira var. hirsuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls within the order Ranunculales. At the family level, this plant is a member of the Menispermaceae, belonging to the genus Cissampelos and specifically identified as the species Cissampelos.
