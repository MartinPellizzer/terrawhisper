The health benefits of Argentine sage are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which have been shown to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties.

The therapeutic actions of Argentine sage are multifaceted, including its use as a calming agent, anxiolytic, and a natural remedy for digestive issues and skin irritations.
