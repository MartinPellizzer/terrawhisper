What are the pharmacological activities of Maytenus guangxiensis?

This plant has demonstrated significant antiproliferative, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant pharmacological activities through its various bioactive secondary metabolites. Research indicates that specific extracts from Maytenus guangxiensis can effectively inhibit the growth and multiplication of certain cancer cell lines. According to a study published by "Natural product research", Maytenus guangxiensis C. Y. Cheng et W. L. Sha yielded two new triterpene compounds, 3β, 7β-dihydroxyolean-12-en-11-one (1) and 3β-O-acetyl-7β-hydroxyolean-12-en-11-one (2). Additionally, four known triterpene compounds, 3β-hydroxyolean-12-en-11-one (3), β-amyrin (4), lup-20(29)-ene-1β, 3β-diol (5) and 1β, 3β-dihydroxy-urs-9(11)-12-diene (6), were isolated from its stems and leaves. These compounds (3-6) were isolated from Maytenus guangxiensis for the first time. The antiproliferative activities of compounds 1-6 were evaluated against Eca-109, PANC-1, EJ and HeLa cell lines. The results demonstrated that compounds 1-6 displayed a certain degree of inhibitory effects against the proliferation of these various human cancer cell lines.
