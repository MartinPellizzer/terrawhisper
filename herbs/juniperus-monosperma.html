Monosperma Juniper (Juniperus monosperma)

Monosperma Juniper (Juniperus monosperma) is a member of the Cupressaceae family, native to South Africa, Namibia, and Botswana. Traditionally, its fruits, bark, and resin have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, astringent, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and african traditional medicine.
