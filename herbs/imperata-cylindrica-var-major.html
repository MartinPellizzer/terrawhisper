Imperata cylindrica var. major

What's the taxonomical classification of Imperata cylindrica var. major?

Imperata cylindrica var. major belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Imperata, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety Imperata cylindrica var. major.
