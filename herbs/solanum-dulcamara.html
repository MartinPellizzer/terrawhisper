Solanum Dulcamara

Solanum dulcamara, commonly known as woody nightshade, has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids, glycoalkaloids, and flavonoids, which may contribute to its medicinal effects. Historically, it has been employed to treat ailments such as respiratory infections, skin conditions, and inflammatory disorders. However, due to its toxic components, its use is limited and requires careful preparation and administration.

Despite its risks, some studies suggest that controlled applications of Solanum dulcamara may have antispasmodic and analgesic properties.
