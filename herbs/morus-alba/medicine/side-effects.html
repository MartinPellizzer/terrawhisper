10 Possible Side Effects Of Morus Alba (Mulberry)

Morus alba has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, abdominal pain, and nausea.

These side effects can occur when the herb is consumed in large amounts or for extended periods. In severe cases, the gastrointestinal issues can lead to dehydration, fatigue, and weight loss, significantly impacting one's quality of life.

Additionally, the diuretic properties of Morus alba can cause frequent urination, which may disrupt work schedules and social activities.
