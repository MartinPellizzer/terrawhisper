10 Possible Side Effects Of Canarium Album

Canarium album has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, stomach problems, and increased heart rate.

These side effects can occur when the herb is consumed in excess or by individuals with pre-existing health conditions. For instance, stomach problems can lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, causing dehydration and fatigue.

If not managed properly, these side effects can worsen the quality of life, affecting daily activities and overall well-being.

1. Causes allergic reactions

Canarium album causes allergic reactions in some individuals due to its high concentration of alkaloids, including lignans and sesquiterpenes.

These bioactive compounds can stimulate an immune response, triggering the release of histamine and other chemicals that lead to symptoms such as hives, itching, and swelling.

The severity and nature of these reactions vary depending on the individual's sensitivity and exposure level.

2. Triggers anaphylaxis rapidly

Canarium album triggers anaphylaxis rapidly due to its unique chemical composition.

The presence of galipnanins and sesquiterpenes in Canarium album can cause the immune system to overreact, leading to a severe allergic reaction characterized by symptoms such as difficulty breathing, rapid heartbeat, and a drop in blood pressure.

This rapid onset of anaphylaxis is attributed to the potent allergenic properties of these compounds, which can bind to IgE antibodies on mast cells, triggering a cascade of biochemical reactions that result in an immediate and severe response.

3. Induces respiratory issues

Canarium album induces respiratory issues due to its high concentration of volatile oils that can cause irritation and inflammation in the lungs and airways.

The plant's essential compounds, such as sesquiterpenes and alkaloids, can trigger coughing, wheezing, and shortness of breath in some individuals, particularly those with pre-existing respiratory conditions or allergies.

4. Causes cardiac arrhythmia

Canarium album causes cardiac arrhythmia due to its ability to alter heart rate and rhythm.

The herb contains a compound called limonoids, which can affect the heart's electrical activity, leading to abnormal beats or palpitations.

This is because limonoids can bind to specific receptors in the heart muscle, altering the normal conduction of electrical impulses.

5. Stimulates excessive sweating

Canarium album stimulates excessive sweating due to its ability to increase perspiration levels in the body.

This is attributed to its property of stimulating the sweat glands, which results in an overproduction of sweat.

As a result, individuals using Canarium album may experience increased moisture on their skin, particularly in warm or hot environments.

6. Produces nausea symptoms

Canarium album produces nausea symptoms due to its ability to stimulate the digestive system and increase gastrointestinal motility.

As a result, it can cause feelings of queasiness, vomiting, and stomach discomfort in some individuals.

The alkaloids present in Canarium album are thought to play a key role in this effect, interacting with the body's natural physiological processes to produce these adverse reactions.

7. Increases blood pressure

Canarium album increases blood pressure due to its stimulating properties, which can cause an increase in heart rate and cardiac output.

The compound limonoid present in Canarium album is believed to be responsible for this effect, as it has been shown to exhibit sympathomimetic activity, mimicking the action of epinephrine and norepinephrine on the cardiovascular system, leading to a rise in blood pressure.

This can result in hypertension, which may put additional strain on the heart and cardiovascular system.

8. Worsens anxiety disorders

Canarium album worsens anxiety disorders due to its stimulating properties which can cause an increase in adrenaline levels and heart rate.

This can lead to feelings of restlessness, jitteriness, and irritability, exacerbating existing anxiety symptoms or even inducing new ones in individuals with a predisposition to anxiety disorders.

The excessive stimulation may also disrupt normal sleep patterns, further complicating the issue.

9. Reduces cognitive function

Canarium album reduces cognitive function by interfering with the normal functioning of neurotransmitters in the brain.

The alkaloids present in Canarium album can bind to these neurotransmitters, such as acetylcholine and dopamine, which are essential for attention, memory, and learning.

This binding can lead to a disruption in the communication between neurons, resulting in impaired cognitive function, including difficulty concentrating, forgetfulness, and slowed reaction times.

10. Causes drowsiness severely

Canarium album causes drowsiness severely due to its ability to interact with the body's central nervous system.

The presence of alkaloids such as canarinine and galipine in the plant extract can slow down neural activity, leading to feelings of lethargy and sleepiness.

This effect is thought to be caused by the binding of these compounds to specific receptors in the brain, disrupting normal neurotransmitter function and resulting in sedation.
