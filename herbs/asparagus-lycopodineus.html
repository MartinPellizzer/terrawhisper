Asparagus lycopodineus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asparagales and the family Asparagaceae, eventually narrowing down to its specific genus, Asparagus, and its unique species designation, lycopodineus.
