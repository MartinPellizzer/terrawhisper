Machilus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Machilus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis?

Machilus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Laurales and falls into the family Lauraceae. More specifically, this plant is a member of the genus Machilus, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the variety yunnanensis of the species Machilus yunnanensis.
