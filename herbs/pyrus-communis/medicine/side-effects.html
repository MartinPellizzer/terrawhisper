10 Possible Side Effects Of Pyrus Communis (Pear)

Pyrus communis has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

Causes of these side effects include consuming excessive amounts, having underlying health conditions, or taking the herb in combination with other substances.

For example, gastrointestinal issues can worsen conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome, while allergic reactions can lead to anaphylaxis in severe cases, impacting daily life and increasing healthcare costs.
