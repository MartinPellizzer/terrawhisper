Epipremnum pinnatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Epipremnum pinnatum?

Epipremnum pinnatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Aliasmaetales and is a member of the family Araceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Epipremnum, and its unique species designation, pinnatum.
