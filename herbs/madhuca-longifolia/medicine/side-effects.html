10 Possible Side Effects Of Madhuca Longifolia (Mahua)

Madhuca longifolia has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, stomach cramps, and vomiting.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption of the herb, leading to an imbalance of digestive enzymes. In severe cases, these symptoms can worsen the lives of individuals by disrupting their daily routines and leading to dehydration.

It can also exacerbate existing medical conditions like irritable bowel syndrome.
