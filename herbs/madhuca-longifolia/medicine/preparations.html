10 Medicinal Preparations Of Madhuca Longifolia (Mahua)

Madhuca longifolia has several medicinal preparations, such as oil, butter, and ashoka tablets.

These preparations are useful for treating various ailments, including skin conditions, ulcers, and rheumatism. The oil extracted from its seeds and bark is also used as a topical application for wounds, while the ashoka tablets are used to treat menstrual disorders and inflammation.

Its butter is consumed orally to boost energy and vitality.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Madhuca longifolia .
