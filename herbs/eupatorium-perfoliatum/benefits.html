Eupatorium perfoliatum, also known as boneset, has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms by reducing inflammation and easing discomfort in affected joints.

How this herb helps with relieves joint pain?

These compounds help reduce swelling and pain in the joints by inhibiting inflammatory pathways in the body. Alkaloids like eupatorin contribute to the plant's ability to modulate pain signals. Flavonoids act as antioxidants, protecting joint tissues from oxidative stress.

Together, these constituents make Eupatorium perfoliatum a traditional remedy for arthritis and other inflammatory joint conditions.
