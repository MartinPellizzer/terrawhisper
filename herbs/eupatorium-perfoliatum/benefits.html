10 Best Eupatorium Perfoliatum Health Benefits

Eupatorium perfoliatum, also known as boneset, is a herb that has been traditionally used for its various health benefits.

It is particularly valued for its ability to alleviate symptoms of the common cold and influenza due to its antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties. The plant contains compounds such as flavonoids and sesquiterpenes, which contribute to its immune-boosting effects. Boneset is also believed to help reduce fever and ease muscle aches, making it a popular remedy during seasonal illnesses.

However, it should be used with caution, as it may cause gastrointestinal discomfort in some individuals.
