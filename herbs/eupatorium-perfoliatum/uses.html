Boneset (Eupatorium perfoliatum) Uses

Boneset is primarily used for respiratory infections, including bronchitis, influenza, cough, and asthma. It helps alleviate cold symptoms and allergic rhinitis. It may also support treatment for sinusitis and skin conditions like dermatitis and infections.

Boneset (Eupatorium perfoliatum) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Boneset is used to support the body's response to respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It may help reduce fever and soothe symptoms like coughing and congestion. Traditionally, it was used to treat ailments such as influenza and bronchitis. Its effectiveness is often combined with other herbs for enhanced therapeutic results.

2. Bronchitis

Boneset is used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. It helps reduce inflammation and ease coughing by promoting expectoration. Its antipyretic properties may also help lower fever associated with bronchial infections. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in respiratory conditions.

3. Influenza

Boneset is used to support recovery from influenza by reducing fever and easing muscle aches. It may also help clear mucus and relieve congestion. Traditionally, it is taken in teas or tinctures during the early stages of flu symptoms. Its antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties are key to its role in treating influenza.

4. Cough

Boneset is used to treat coughs, particularly those accompanied by fever and body aches. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, while its anti-inflammatory effects reduce bronchial irritation. It is often combined with other herbs to support respiratory health during colds or flu.

5. Asthma

Boneset is used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health, particularly in managing asthma symptoms. It helps reduce inflammation and ease bronchial spasms, promoting easier breathing. Traditionally, it is combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in respiratory conditions. Its action is gentle yet supportive for chronic asthma management.

6. Cold symptoms

Boneset is traditionally used to alleviate cold symptoms such as fever, chills, and body aches. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture and is believed to support the body's natural healing process during respiratory infections. Its effectiveness is attributed to its anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties.

7. Allergic rhinitis

Boneset is used in herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis, such as sneezing and nasal congestion. Its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties help reduce nasal swelling and mucus production. It is often combined with other herbs to support respiratory health during seasonal allergies.

8. Sinusitis

Boneset is used to treat sinusitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce mucus production and ease congestion, making it effective for sinus infections. It is often combined with other herbs like echinacea and goldenseal for enhanced results. Its actions support clearing respiratory passages and fighting microbial infections.

9. Skin infections

Boneset is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and bacterial growth on the skin. Often applied as a poultice or infused oil, it supports healing in conditions like eczema and minor wounds. Its traditional use highlights its role in supporting skin health.

10. Dermatitis

Boneset is used externally for dermatitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce skin irritation and redness. A poultice made from the fresh leaves can be applied to affected areas. It is particularly effective for eczema and other inflammatory skin conditions.
