What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Illigera henryi has woody climbing vines characterized by opposite, ovate leaves with entire margins and distinctively long, tapering acuminate tips. These flowers eventually develop into fleshy, indehiscent drupes that serve as the plant's fruit.
