What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lespedeza dunnii has pinnately compound leaves with 5 to 11 leaflets, small yellow pea-like flowers, and narrow, oblong to lanceolate pods. The leaflets are typically elliptical or oblong in shape and feature entire margins. Its stems are woody at the base and often exhibit a decumbent or ascending growth habit. The inflorescence consists of small clusters that emerge from the leaf axils. The mature fruit is a legume that is slightly compressed and contains several small seeds.
