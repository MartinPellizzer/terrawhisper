10 Medicinal Preparations Of Syringa Vulgaris (Lilac)

Syringa vulgaris has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions.

These are often used to treat respiratory issues, such as coughs and colds, as well as to reduce inflammation and soothe digestive problems.

The flowers and leaves can be made into a warm tea to help relieve congestion, while the tincture is commonly used to calm nervousness and promote a restful sleep.
