Cirsium chlorolepis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cirsium chlorolepis?

Cirsium chlorolepis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Cirsium, and its specific species name, chlorolepis.
