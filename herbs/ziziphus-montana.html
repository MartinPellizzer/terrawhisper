Ziziphus montana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the evolutionary path of the order Rosales and is a member of the family Rhamnaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Ziziphus, specifically identified as the species montana.
