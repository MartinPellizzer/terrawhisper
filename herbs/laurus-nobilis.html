Laurus (Laurus nobilis)

Laurus (Laurus nobilis) is a member of the Lauraceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and Middle East. Traditionally, its leaves, fruits, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and oil infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, carminative, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and unani medicine.
