Symplocos pilosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Symplocos pilosa?

Symplocos pilosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is further categorized into the family Symplocaceae, specifically within the genus Symplocos, and is identified by its unique species name, pilosa.

Its bark is generally smooth or slightly fissured depending on the age of the specimen. The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that typically matures into a dark color.
