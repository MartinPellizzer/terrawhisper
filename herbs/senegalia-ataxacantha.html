What is Senegalia ataxacantha?

Senegalia ataxacantha is a species of plant in the family Fabaceae, native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, growing up to 10 meters tall, with a straight, spiny stem. Leaves are bipinnate, with 2-4 pairs of pinnae, each bearing 2-6 pairs of leaflets, which are elliptic in shape and 5-20 millimeters long. The inflorescence is a spicate raceme, bearing yellow flowers with 5 petals and 10 stamens.

The fruit is a pod, 6-12 centimeters long and 1-2 centimeters wide, containing 2-4 seeds.
