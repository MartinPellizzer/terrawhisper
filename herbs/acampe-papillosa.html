What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Acampe papillosa has pendulous, fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow several meters long and are covered in numerous small, thickened aerial roots. From the leaf axils, the plant produces fragrant, star-shaped flowers that are characterized by a distinctively fleshy, trilobed lip.
