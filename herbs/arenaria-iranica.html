Arenaria iranica

Arenaria iranica, commonly known as Iranian saxifrage, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Saxifragaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include reducing inflammation, pain relief, and antioxidant activity.

The health benefits of Arenaria iranica include its ability to protect against oxidative stress, improve cardiovascular health, and exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory actions.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities.

Possible Side Effects of Arenaria iranica

Arenaria iranica can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems in some individuals.

Ingesting the plant can lead to nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain due to its toxic compounds.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's sap can cause skin lesions, blisters, and even permanent scarring.

Additionally, using arenaria iranica as a medicinal treatment without proper guidance and caution can lead to interactions with other medications and exacerbate underlying health conditions.
