The therapeutic actions of Synnema africanum include reducing pain and inflammation, as well as exhibiting antioxidant and antiseptic activities.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include harpagoside, harpagide, and other iridoid glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
