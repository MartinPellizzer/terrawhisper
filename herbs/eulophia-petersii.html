What is Eulophia petersii?

Eulophia petersii is an epiphytic perennial orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa, specifically in the regions of Ethiopia, Kenya, Tanzania, and South Africa. Eulophia petersii has pseudobulbs and a terete stem, reaching heights of 30-60 cm, with 2-4 leaves that are lanceolate to elliptical in shape, measuring 15-30 cm in length. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing 5-10 flowers that are greenish-yellow or white in color, with a labellum that has a three-lobed shape and is 3-4 cm in length.

The flowers are hermaphroditic, with a pollinium that is bilobed and a rostellum that is unlobed.
