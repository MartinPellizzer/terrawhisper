What's the taxonomical classification of Erysimum amurense?

Erysimum amurense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Brassicales. As a member of the family Brassicaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Erysimum, with its specific designation being the species amurense.
