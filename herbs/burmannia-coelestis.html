Burmannia coelestis

What's the taxonomical classification of Burmannia coelestis?

Burmannia coelesteis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Dioscoreales. This plant is a member of the family Burmanniaceae, situated within the genus Burmannia, and is specifically identified by the species epithet coelestis.
