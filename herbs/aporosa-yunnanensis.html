What's the taxonomical classification of Aporosa yunnanensis?

Aporosa yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Phyllanthaceae, which places it within the genus Aporosa. The specific taxonomic designation for this particular species is Aporosa yunnanensis.
