Aporosa yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Aporosa yunnanensis?

Aporosa yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Malpighiales. It is further categorized under the family Phyllanthaceae, which places it within the genus Aporosa. The specific taxonomic designation for this particular species is Aporosa yunnanensis.

Detailed toxicological studies on this specific species are currently limited, making the exact physiological impact difficult to predict with certainty. Therefore, cautious handling is recommended to avoid accidental exposure to its chemical constituents.
