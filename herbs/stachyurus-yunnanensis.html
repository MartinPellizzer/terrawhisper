What's the taxonomical classification of Stachyurus yunnanensis?

Stachyurus yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the family Stachyuraceae, which contains its parent genus, Stachyurus. Finally, it is identified at the species level as yunnanensis, completing its formal taxonomic hierarchy.
