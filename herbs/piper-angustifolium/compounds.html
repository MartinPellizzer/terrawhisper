Piper'S Betony (Piper angustifolium) Compounds

Piper's Betony contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to its astringent effects. Alkaloids and phenolic acids also play roles in its medicinal properties. Essential oils and terpenoids provide aromatic and potential anti-inflammatory benefits, while coumarin, glycosides, saponins, and anthocyanins add to its complex chemical profile.
