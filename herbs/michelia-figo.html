What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia figo?

Michelia figo belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Magnoliales and is a member of the family Magnoliaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus, Michelia, and its specific species name, figo.
