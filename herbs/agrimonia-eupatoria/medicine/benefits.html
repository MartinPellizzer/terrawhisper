10 Health Benefits Of Agrimonia Eupatoria (Agrimony)

Its medicinal properties help to soothe digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing. For example, agrimony tea can alleviate symptoms of IBS and gastritis, while its topical application may accelerate the healing process for minor cuts and burns.
