Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) Compounds

The primary compounds in Dragon Herb include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and essential oils, known for their aromatic and antimicrobial effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while coumarin may support blood circulation. Terpenoids, glycosides, saponins, tannins, anthocyanins, and alkaloids also play roles in its medicinal benefits.
