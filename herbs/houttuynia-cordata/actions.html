Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) Actions

Dragon Herb exhibits several key therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, expectorant, and diuretic properties. It also acts as an antispasmodic and tonic, aiding digestion through carminative and stimulant effects. Its bitter and sedative qualities help balance the body, while its astringent nature supports tissue repair and reduction of excess secretions.

Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) Monograph

1. Anti-inflammatory

Dragon herb has an anti-inflammatory action, primarily due to its rich content of flavonoids and polyphenols. These compounds inhibit pro-inflammatory cytokines and reduce oxidative stress. It is commonly used to alleviate symptoms of inflammation in conditions like respiratory infections and skin disorders. Its effectiveness is supported by traditional use and preliminary scientific studies.

2. Expectorant

Dragon herb has an expectorant action, helping to loosen and expel mucus from the respiratory tract. It is commonly used to alleviate symptoms of coughs and bronchitis by reducing mucus viscosity and promoting its drainage. This property makes it effective in treating respiratory conditions characterized by excessive phlegm.

3. Diuretic

Dragon herb has a diuretic action, promoting the elimination of excess fluids from the body. This helps reduce swelling and supports kidney function. It is commonly used in traditional medicine to address urinary retention and fluid retention issues. Its diuretic properties are attributed to its bioactive compounds that stimulate urine production.

4. Antispasmodic

Dragon herb has an antispasmodic action, helping to relieve muscle spasms and cramps. It works by relaxing smooth muscle tissue, often used in treating conditions like gastrointestinal spasms or menstrual pain. Its effects are attributed to compounds that inhibit spasmodic contractions in the body.

5. Tonic

Dragon herb has a tonic action, enhancing overall vitality and supporting the body's natural healing processes. It is used to strengthen the immune system and improve energy levels, making it beneficial for individuals with chronic fatigue or weakened constitution. Its warming properties help balance internal energies, promoting general well-being and recovery.

6. Carminative

Dragon herb has a carminative action, helping to relieve gas and bloating by promoting the movement of trapped air in the digestive tract. It supports digestion and alleviates discomfort caused by excess gas, making it useful for digestive issues. This effect is attributed to its active compounds that stimulate intestinal motility.

7. Stimulant

Dragon herb has a stimulant action, enhancing metabolic processes and increasing energy levels. It is often used to invigorate the body and improve physical performance. This effect is attributed to its active compounds that stimulate the nervous system and promote circulation.

8. Bitter

Dragon herb has a bitter action, which helps to clear heat and detoxify the body. It promotes digestion by stimulating the production of digestive enzymes and enhances the body's ability to eliminate toxins. This action is particularly beneficial for conditions involving internal heat and dampness.

9. Sedative

Dragon herb has a sedative action, helping to calm the nervous system and reduce anxiety. It is often used in traditional medicine to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality. Its calming effects make it a valuable herb for managing stress and insomnia.

10. Astringent

Dragon herb has an astringent action, which helps to reduce secretions and tighten tissues. It is commonly used to treat diarrhea, excessive sweating, and respiratory conditions by constricting mucous membranes and reducing inflammation. This property makes it valuable in herbal formulations for digestive and respiratory health.
