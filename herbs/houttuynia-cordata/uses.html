Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) Uses

Dragon Herb is used to treat acne, skin and fungal infections, and inflamed or inflammatory skin conditions. It supports wound healing and can help with mouth ulcers and respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

Dragon Herb (Houttuynia cordata) Monograph

1. Acne

Houttuynia cordata is used in traditional medicine to treat acne due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce skin inflammation and combat bacterial infections that contribute to acne. The herb is often prepared as a topical application or infusion to support skin health. Its effectiveness is attributed to compounds like flavonoids and essential oils.

2. Skin infection

Houttuynia cordata is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce inflammation and prevent bacterial growth. The herb is often applied as a poultice or infused oil. It is effective against conditions like acne, boils, and fungal infections.

3. Fungal infection

Houttuynia cordata is used in traditional medicine to treat fungal infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It contains compounds that inhibit fungal growth, making it effective for skin infections and other fungal-related conditions. Its use is supported by both historical practice and modern research in herbal medicine.

4. Skin infections

Houttuynia cordata is used to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce inflammation and combat bacterial and fungal infections. Topical applications are common, often combined with other herbs for enhanced effectiveness. Its ability to promote healing makes it a valued remedy in traditional herbal medicine.

5. Wound healing

Houttuynia cordata promotes wound healing by reducing inflammation and preventing infection. Its antimicrobial properties help clean the wound, while its ability to stimulate cell regeneration accelerates tissue repair. Traditionally used in herbal medicine, it is applied topically to promote faster recovery and minimize scarring.

6. Inflamed skin conditions

Houttuynia cordata is used to treat inflamed skin conditions due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and infection in wounds, rashes, and boils. Traditionally applied as a poultice or decoction, it promotes skin healing and soothes irritations.

7. Inflammatory skin conditions

Houttuynia cordata is used in traditional medicine to treat inflammatory skin conditions such as eczema and acne. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help reduce redness, swelling, and infection. Topical applications are common, often in poultices or herbal preparations. 8. Fungal infections

Houttuynia cordata is used in traditional medicine to treat fungal infections due to its antimicrobial properties. It is often applied topically as a poultice or incorporated into herbal formulations to combat skin and systemic fungal infections.

9. Mouth ulcers

Houttuynia cordata is used to treat mouth ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce pain and swelling while promoting healing. The herb can be applied topically as a paste or used in herbal teas to soothe oral irritation. Its cooling effect provides relief and supports the body's natural healing process.

10. Respiratory infection

Houttuynia cordata is used in traditional medicine to treat respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and fever by supporting the body's immune response. Often prepared as a decoction, it is valued for its ability to clear phlegm and soothe the respiratory system.
