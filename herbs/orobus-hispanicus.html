Orobus hispanicus

Orobus hispanicus, commonly known as the Spanish vetchling, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its medicinal properties, offering several health benefits such as reducing inflammation and exhibiting antioxidant activities.
