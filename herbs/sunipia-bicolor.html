What's the taxonomical classification of Sunipia bicolor?

Sunipia bicolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Sunipia, with its specific designation being the species bicolor.
