What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron mucronatum?

Rhododendron mucronaatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Ericales. This plant is a representative of the family Ericaceae, falling under the well-known genus Rhododendron, and is specifically identified by its unique species designation, mucronatum.
