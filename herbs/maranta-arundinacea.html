Maranta Arundinacea: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Maranta arundinacea, commonly known as arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, valued for its starchy, edible roots.

This herb is widely used in cooking and traditional medicine for its soothing and nourishing properties.

Health benefits include aiding digestion, reducing inflammation, and supporting immune function.

Its therapeutic actions involve detoxification, skin healing, and relief from gastrointestinal discomfort.

Bioactive constituents include mucilage, starch, and various minerals that contribute to its medicinal value.
