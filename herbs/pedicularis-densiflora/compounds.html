Denseflower Lousewort (Pedicularis densiflora) Compounds

Denseflower Lousewort contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and glycosides contribute to its astringent and medicinal qualities, while terpenoids and coumarin offer antimicrobial benefits. Saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic potential, and essential oils provide aromatic and digestive support.
