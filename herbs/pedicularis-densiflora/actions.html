Denseflower Lousewort (Pedicularis densiflora) Actions

Denseflower Lousewort exhibits several therapeutic actions including being a tonic and stimulant to boost vitality. It also has anti-inflammatory and diuretic properties, helping reduce swelling and support kidney function. Additionally, it acts as a carminative, expectorant, and antispasmodic, aiding digestion and respiratory health. Its astringent and sedative effects can soothe inflammation and calm the nervous system.
