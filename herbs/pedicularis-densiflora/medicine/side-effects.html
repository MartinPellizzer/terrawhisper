10 Possible Side Effects Of Pedicularis Densiflora (Lousewort)

Pedicularis densiflora has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, dizziness, and vomiting.

These symptoms can be caused by consuming high doses of the herb, which can lead to stomach upset and dehydration.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen a person's quality of life, making it difficult to work, engage in physical activities, or even maintain daily routines.
