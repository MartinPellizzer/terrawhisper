These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions.

The flavonoids in particular may help improve cardiovascular health by reducing inflammation and improving blood vessel function, potentially decreasing the risk of heart disease and stroke.
