Copaifera Officinalis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Copaifera officinalis, commonly known as copaiba, is a tropical tree native to South America, renowned for its aromatic resinous sap used in traditional medicine.

This herb is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and analgesic properties, which support respiratory, digestive, and skin health.

The bioactive constituents include sesquiterpene hydrocarbons, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds that contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from copaiba to harness its medicinal benefits.
