Pleurospermum Rupestre

Pleurospermum rupestre, a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China, has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

It is believed to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects, which may contribute to its use in treating respiratory and skin conditions. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including flavonoids and sesquiterpenes, which are thought to underlie its medicinal benefits. In modern research, Pleurospermum rupestre is being studied for its possible applications in pharmaceuticals and natural remedies.

However, more clinical trials are needed to fully validate its efficacy and safety for medicinal use.
