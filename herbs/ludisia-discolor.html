What is Ludisia discolor?

Ludisia discolor is a species of terrestrial orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves, which are dark green in color with yellow-green veins, and its short, slender stems. The inflorescence is a short, racemose spike, with 2-5 flowers that are greenish-yellow with a yellow labellum.
