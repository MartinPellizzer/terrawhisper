Bracteocarpus compactus

Bracteocarpus compactus, also known as button weed, is a type of succulent plant native to Australia, characterized by its compact growth and small, round leaves.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating skin conditions such as eczema and acne due to its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

The health benefits of Bracteocarpus compactus include its ability to soothe and calm irritated skin, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, or topical creams, making it a popular choice for natural remedies and skincare products.
