What's the taxonomical classification of Onosma exserta?

Onosma exserta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Boraginales and the family Boraginaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its placement within the genus Onosma, specifically as the species exserta.

Because its chemical profile is not fully documented, any accidental consumption should be treated as a potential health risk.
