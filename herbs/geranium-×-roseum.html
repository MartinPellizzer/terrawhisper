Geranium × roseum

Geranium × roseum, commonly known as the Rose Geranium, is a hybrid plant species that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

This plant is known for its potential health benefits, including its ability to reduce stress and anxiety, and its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe skin irritations and wounds.

The therapeutic actions of Rose Geranium include its use as a natural antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and sedative, which can help to promote relaxation and reduce pain.

The bioactive constituents of Rose Geranium include geraniol, linalool, and beta-caryophyllene, which are responsible for its medicinal properties and aromatherapeutic benefits.
