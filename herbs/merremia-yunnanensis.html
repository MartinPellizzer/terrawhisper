Merremia yunnanensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Merremia yunnanensis has perennial herbaceous stems that reach heights of up to 50 centimeters and feature deeply lobed, serrated leaves. The foliage consists of alternate, ovate leaves with distinct venation patterns and slightly hairy surfaces. Its inflorescence displays small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. These blossoms are arranged in clusters and emerge from the leaf axils during the blooming season. The plant is characterized by a fine root system that helps it anchor in its native subtropical habitats.
