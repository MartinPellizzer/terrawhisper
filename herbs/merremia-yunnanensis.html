Merremia yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Merremia yunnanensis?

Merremia yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Solanales and is a member of the family Convolvulaceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Merremia, with its specific designation being the species yunnanensis.
