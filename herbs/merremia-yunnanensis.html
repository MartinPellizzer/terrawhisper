Merremia yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the order Solanales and is a member of the family Convolvulaceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Merremia, with its specific designation being the species yunnanensis.
