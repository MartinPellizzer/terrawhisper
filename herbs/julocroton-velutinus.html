What is Julocroton velutinus?

The inflorescence is a cyathium, a type of reduced flower, surrounded by small, leaf-like bracts, producing small, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant is native to tropical Africa, specifically in the countries of Cameroon, Democratic Republic of the Congo, and Republic of the Congo.
