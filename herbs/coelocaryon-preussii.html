What's the taxonomical classification of Coelocaryon preussii?

Coelocaryon preussii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, which falls under the subclass Magnoliidae. It is further organized into the order Magnoliales and is a member of the family Myristicaceae. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Coelocaryon, with the specific designation of the species preussii.
