Lespedeza cyrtobotrya belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of flowering plants. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Lespedeza, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species cyrtobotrya.
