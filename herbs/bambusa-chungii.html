Bambusa chungii

What's the taxonomical classification of Bambusa chungii?

Bambusa chungii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Bambusa, and its unique species designation, chungii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The branching pattern is moderately dense, creating a lush and voluminous canopy as the plant matures.
