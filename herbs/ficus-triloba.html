The therapeutic actions of Ficus triloba include its use in treating fever, headache, and gastrointestinal disorders, as well as its potential in wound healing and skin care.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

Possible Side Effects of Ficus triloba

Furthermore, the plant's sap can cause skin burns and blisters in some individuals, especially when not handled properly.
