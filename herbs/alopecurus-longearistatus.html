What's the taxonomical classification of Alopecurus longearistatus?

Alopecurus longearistatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Poales and the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Alopecurus, and its specific species designation, longearistatus.
