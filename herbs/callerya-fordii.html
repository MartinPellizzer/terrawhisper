What's the taxonomical classification of Callerya fordii?

Callerya fordii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, falling under the genus Callerya, with the specific species designation fordii.
