Ainsliaea glabra var. glabra

What's the taxonomical classification of Ainsliaea glabra var. glabra?

Ainsliaea glabra var. glabra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this biological hierarchy, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, it is further organized into the genus Ainsliaea, eventually reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Ainsliaea glabra var. glabra.
