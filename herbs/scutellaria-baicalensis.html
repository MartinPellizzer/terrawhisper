Scutellaria baicalensis, commonly known as Baikal skullcap, is a herb widely used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and viral infections, as well as to reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health.

The main health benefits of this herb include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and immunomodulatory effects, which contribute to its therapeutic potential. In traditional medicine, Scutellaria baicalensis is often prepared as a tea or capsule, and its extract is used to treat a range of health conditions, including viral hepatitis and type 2 diabetes.
