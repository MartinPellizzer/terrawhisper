What to know about Scutellaria baicalensis (skullcap) before using it medicinally

Skullcap, scientifically known as Scutellaria baicalensis, is a herb that has been prized for centuries for its impressive health benefits.

This plant is renowned for its ability to calm the mind and body, reducing stress and anxiety, while also possessing anti-inflammatory properties that can help alleviate pain and discomfort. In culinary traditions, skullcap is often used as a tea, adding a subtle flavor to soups, stews, and desserts. Horticulturally, skullcap is a relatively low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

With its rich history dating back to ancient Chinese medicine, where it was referred to as "Huang Qin," skullcap has been a staple herb for generations, earning its place as a trusted remedy for a variety of ailments.
