Scutellaria Baicalensis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Scutellaria baicalensis, commonly known as Huang Qin, is a perennial herb native to East Asia, particularly China, and is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It is renowned for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its various health benefits, including supporting immune function and reducing symptoms of inflammation.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, including baicalin and wogonin, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or powder, and is often used in herbal formulas to treat conditions like fever, digestive issues, and skin disorders.
