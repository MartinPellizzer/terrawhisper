Narcissus tazetta var. chinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Narcissus tazetta var. chinensis?

Narcissus tazetta var. chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Amaryllidaceae, falling under the genus Narcissus, and is specifically identified by its botanical name, Narcissus tazetta var. chinensis.
