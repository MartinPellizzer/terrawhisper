What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrorchidium oppositifolium?

Tetrorchidium oppositifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Tetrorchidium, with its unique identification defined by the species epithet oppositifolium.
