What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace umbellata?

Androsace umbellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is a part of the family Primulaceae, specifically positioned within the genus Androsace, and is identified by its unique species name, umbellata.
