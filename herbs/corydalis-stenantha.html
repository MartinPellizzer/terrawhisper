What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis stenantha?

Corydalis stenantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its lineage further, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Papaveraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Corydalis, and its unique species designation, stenantha.
