10 Health Benefits Of Solanum Melongena (Eggplant)

Solanum melongena, also known as eggplant, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, antioxidant properties, and antimicrobial effects.

Its anthocyanins and flavonoids are responsible for these medicinal properties, which have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by lowering blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

By incorporating eggplant into one's diet, individuals can experience reduced risk of heart disease and certain cancers, ultimately leading to a longer, healthier life.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Solanum melongena.

1. Helps lower cholesterol levels

Solanum melongena helps lower cholesterol levels because of its rich content of sterols and saponins.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol, thereby reducing serum cholesterol levels. Additionally, eggplant is a good source of fiber, which can also contribute to lowering cholesterol by binding to bile acids and eliminating them from the body.

This combination of sterols, saponins, and fiber makes eggplant a valuable addition to a heart-healthy diet.

2. Reduces inflammation risks

Solanum melongena reduces inflammation risks because of its rich antioxidant content.

The fruit is a rich source of polyphenolic compounds, particularly nasunin, which has been shown to have potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help to neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress, thereby lowering the risk of chronic inflammation and its associated health problems.

3. Prevents cancer growth

Solanum melongena prevents cancer growth because it is rich in antioxidants and phytochemicals that have been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.

The plant's high levels of flavonoids, anthocyanins, and saponins have been found to induce apoptosis (cell death) in cancer cells, while also preventing the formation of new blood vessels that feed tumors.

Additionally, the soluble fiber in eggplant has been shown to bind to cancer-causing compounds, reducing their absorption and toxicity.

4. Improves blood sugar control

Solanum melongena improves blood sugar control because it contains a unique compound called nasunin, which has been shown to have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Nasunin has been found to inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in the breakdown of starches, thereby slowing the release of glucose into the bloodstream.

This natural mechanism helps to regulate blood sugar levels, making eggplant a valuable addition to a diabetic-friendly diet.

5. Boosts immune system function

Solanum melongena boosts immune system function because of its high antioxidant and polyphenol content.

These bioactive compounds help to neutralize free radicals, reducing oxidative stress and inflammation that can weaken the immune system. Additionally, eggplant contains flavonoids, which have been shown to modulate immune cell function and enhance the body's natural defense against pathogens.

This can help to protect against diseases and promote overall health and well-being.

6. Kills helicobacter pylori bacteria

Solanum melongena kills helicobacter pylori bacteria because it contains a unique compound called nasunin.

Nasunin has been shown to possess potent antibacterial properties, effectively inhibiting the growth and reproduction of H. pylori. This bacterium is responsible for causing gastric ulcers and stomach cancer, making eggplant a potential natural remedy for preventing and treating these conditions.

The consumption of eggplant has been associated with a reduced risk of H. pylori-related illnesses, making it a valuable addition to a healthy diet.

7. Relieves allergy symptoms

Solanum melongena relieves allergy symptoms because of its rich content of antioxidants, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory effects, which can help reduce the severity of allergy symptoms such as itching, sneezing, and congestion.

Additionally, the anthocyanins present in eggplant have been found to have anti-allergic properties, further supporting its potential as a natural remedy for alleviating allergy symptoms.

8. Reduces anxiety levels

Solanum melongena reduces anxiety levels because it is a rich source of anxiolytic compounds such as withanolides.

These compounds have been shown to have a sedative effect, calming the nervous system and reducing stress hormones. Additionally, the antioxidants present in eggplant, including flavonoids and phenolic acids, help to protect the brain from oxidative damage, further contributing to its anxiolytic effects.

This makes eggplant a nutritious and delicious way to support mental well-being and reduce anxiety.

9. Improves digestion efficiency

Solanum melongena improves digestion efficiency because of its unique composition.

The fruit is rich in dietary fiber, which helps to stimulate the growth of beneficial gut bacteria, promoting a healthy gut microbiome. Additionally, eggplant contains a compound called nasunin, which has been shown to have anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, making it easier for the body to absorb nutrients.

As a result, eggplant consumption can lead to improved digestion and reduced symptoms of digestive disorders.

10. Supports healthy skin tone

Solanum melongena supports healthy skin tone because of its rich antioxidant properties.

The purple pigment in eggplant, known as nasunin, has been shown to have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help to protect the skin from environmental stressors and oxidative damage.

This can result in a more even and radiant complexion, as well as improved skin elasticity and a reduced risk of premature aging.
