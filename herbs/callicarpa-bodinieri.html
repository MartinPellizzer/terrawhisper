Callicarpa bodinieri

What's the taxonomical classification of Callicarpa bodinieri?

Callicarpa bodinieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. This specific plant is further categorized into the genus Callicarpa, ultimately reaching its unique designation at the species level as bodinieri.

According to a study published by "Journal of natural products", investigation of the leaves and twigs of Callicarpa bodinieri resulted in the isolation of nine new abietane diterpenoids, bodinieric acids A-I (1-9), alongside six known compounds (10-15). To explore potential therapeutic targets for immune-mediated disease, the inhibitory activities of the isolates were determined against 13 kinase enzymes. Eight compounds exhibited moderate inhibitory effects on spleen tyrosine kinase (SYK). Specifically, the IC50 values of compounds 2 and 6 were 7.2 and 10.7 μM, respectively.
