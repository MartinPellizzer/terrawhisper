What's the taxonomical classification of Asarum sagittarioides?

Asarum sagittarioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Piperales and the family Aristolochiaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized within the genus Asarum, specifically identified by the species epithet sagittarioides.
