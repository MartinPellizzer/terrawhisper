Medicinal Parts of Tetraria fragilis

Tetraria fragilis has many medicinal parts, such as leaves, roots, and rhizomes, that are used in traditional medicine.

Herbal Preparations of Tetraria fragilis

Tetraria fragilis has many herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used for various medicinal purposes.

The tea made from the leaves of Tetraria fragilis is used to treat digestive issues, such as nausea and indigestion, while the infusion is used to relieve respiratory problems, like bronchitis and asthma.

Possible Side Effects of Tetraria fragilis

Tetraria fragilis can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems due to its potential to release allergenic particles into the air.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of the plant's parts, especially the leaves, may cause gastrointestinal issues like nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

However, it is essential to note that the plant is often used in traditional medicine and is considered safe under proper guidance and usage.
