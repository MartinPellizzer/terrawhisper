What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis glauca?

Clematis glauca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, which falls under the genus Clematis, and is specifically identified by its species name, glauca.
