Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica) Compounds

Water Plantain contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and detoxifying actions. Glycosides and coumarin support its diuretic and antispasmodic qualities, while terpenoids, alkaloids, anthocyanins, and essential oils add to its diverse therapeutic profile.

Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids in Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica) contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. These compounds help support kidney function and may aid in urinary tract health. They are naturally present in the plant's rhizomes and are often extracted for medicinal use. Flavonoids play a key role in the plant's therapeutic applications in traditional herbal medicine.

Phenolic acid in Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica) contributes to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
