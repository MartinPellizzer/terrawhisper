Possible Side Effects of Tribulus splendens

Tribulus splendens can have side effects if used improperly, such as interactions with medications that may lead to increased bleeding or bruising.

Additionally, tribulus splendens may also cause allergic reactions, resulting in symptoms like hives, itching, and swelling.

Furthermore, it can also lower blood pressure, which may be problematic for individuals with pre-existing hypotension or those taking medications to regulate their blood pressure.
