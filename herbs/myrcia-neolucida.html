Myrcia neolucida

Myrcia neolucida, also known as the Lucid Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in the treatment of respiratory issues and fever.

It has been found to possess antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to alleviate symptoms of colds, coughs, and other respiratory infections, as well as providing relief from pain and inflammation.

The plant can be prepared as a herbal tea, infusion, or decoction, and is often used in folk medicine in its native regions to promote overall health and well-being.
