Guibourtia arnoldiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Guibourtia arnoldiana?

Guibourtia arnoldiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Fabales, it is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Guibourtia and the species arnoldiana.
