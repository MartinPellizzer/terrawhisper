10 Possible Side Effects Of Tamarindus Indica (Tamarind)

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, underlying gastrointestinal issues, or sensitivities to the herb's compounds.

In severe cases, these reactions can lead to malnutrition, electrolyte imbalances, and even life-threatening complications, significantly impacting individuals' daily lives.
