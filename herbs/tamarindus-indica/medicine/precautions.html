10 Precautions To Take When Using Tamarindus Indica (Tamarind)

Tamarindus indica has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels when using it to treat diabetes, as it can interact with medications and exacerbate low blood sugar.

Failure to do so may lead to dizziness, weakness, or even seizures.

Additionally, tamarind can interact with blood thinners, and users should consult with their doctor to ensure safe dosages and minimize the risk of bleeding complications.
