What's the taxonomical classification of Elatostema myrtillus?

Elatostema myrtillus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic placement continues through the order Rosales and the family Urticaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Elatostema, and its unique species designation, myrtillus.
