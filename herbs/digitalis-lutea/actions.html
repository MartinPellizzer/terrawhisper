Ladys' Mantle (Digitalis lutea) Actions

Ladys' Mantle exhibits several primary therapeutic actions, including tonic, which strengthens the body, and cardiotonic, which supports heart function. It also acts as a diuretic and anti-inflammatory, helping to reduce swelling and promote urine flow. Additionally, it has stimulant and sedative properties, balancing energy and calming the nervous system.
