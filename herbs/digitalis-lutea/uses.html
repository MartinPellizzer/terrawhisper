Ladys' Mantle (Digitalis lutea) Uses

Ladys' Mantle is primarily used to treat heart conditions such as heart failure, arrhythmia, and hypertension. It supports cardiac function by improving circulation and reducing fluid retention, making it beneficial for edema and congestive heart failure. Its effects also help manage tachycardia and ventricular dysfunction through its circulatory insufficiency support.
