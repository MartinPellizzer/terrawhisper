10 Medicinal Preparations Of Digitalis Lutea

Digitalis lutea has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions.

These preparations are useful for treating heart conditions, including arrhythmias and congestive heart failure. The plant's leaves contain digoxin, a glycoside that stimulates the heart and increases its efficiency. Tinctures are often used to treat edema and circulatory disorders.

Infusions are consumed for their cardiotonic and diuretic properties.
