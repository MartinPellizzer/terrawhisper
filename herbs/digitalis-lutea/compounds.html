Ladys' Mantle (Digitalis lutea) Compounds

The primary compounds in Ladys' Mantle include glycosides, such as digitoxin and digoxin, which are cardiac stimulants. Alkaloids like scopolamine contribute to its sedative properties. Flavonoids and anthocyanins provide antioxidant benefits, while coumarin and tannins support anti-inflammatory effects.
