Black Nightshade (Solanum nigrum) Actions

Black Nightshade exhibits anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, helping reduce inflammation and muscle spasms. It acts as a tonic and stimulant, supporting overall vitality and bodily functions. Its bitter and astringent qualities aid digestion, while diuretic effects promote fluid balance. The plant also has sedative, carminative, and expectorant actions, easing respiratory and digestive discomfort.
