Populus alba var. pyramidalis

What's the taxonomical classification of Populus alba var. pyramidalis?

Populus alba var. pyramidalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Malpighiales. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is a member of the family Salicaceae and is placed within the genus Populus. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its designation as the species Populus alba var. pyramidalis.
