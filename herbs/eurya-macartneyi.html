What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eurya macartniei has elliptical to oblong leaves that are typically 2 to 5 centimeters long, featuring an entire margin and a shiny, dark green upper surface. The leaf texture is often coriaceous, meaning it feels somewhat leathery to the touch. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in clusters. These flowers are characterized by their minute size and lack of prominent petals. The plant also develops small, fleshy fruits that serve as a key identifying feature.
