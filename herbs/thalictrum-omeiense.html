Thalictrum omeiense

What's the taxonomical classification of Thalictrum omeiense?

Thalictrum omeiense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ranunculales. The plant is a representative of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically situated within the genus Thalictrum, and is identified by the species epithet omeiense.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that emerge in terminal panicles or clusters. These flowers feature numerous stamens that typically surround a central group of carpels.
