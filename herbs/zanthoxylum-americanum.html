Zanthoxylum Americanum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Zanthoxylum americanum, commonly known as prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to North America, characterized by its spiny stems and small, red berries.

This herb is valued for its stimulating and warming properties, often used in herbal medicine to support circulation and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents in Zanthoxylum americanum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and circulatory-stimulating effects.
