Perrottetia racemosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Huerteales and the family Dipentodontaceae, which ultimately leads to its placement within the genus Perrottetia and its specific designation as the species racemosa.
