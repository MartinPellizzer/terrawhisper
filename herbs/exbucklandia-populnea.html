What's the taxonomical classification of Exbucklandia populnea?

Exbucklandia populnea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Saxifragales. This plant is further categorized under the family Hamamelidaceae, falling into the genus Exbucklandia, and is specifically identified by its species name, populnea.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Exbucklandia populnea has an upright, evergreen tree habit characterized by simple, alternate leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic with entire margins and acute tips.

The foliage typically displays a coriaceous texture and a glossy green upper surface. Its bark is generally smooth and greyish, providing a stark contrast to the lush canopy. Small, actinomorphic flowers emerge in terminal panicles, featuring five white petals and numerous stamens.

The resulting fruit is a small, dry, indehiscent capsule that contains tiny seeds.
