Hylodesmum oldhamii

What's the taxonomical classification of Hylodesmum oldhamii?

Hylodesmum oldhamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. It is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, this plant is a member of the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Hylodesmum and is specifically distinguished as the species oldhamii.
