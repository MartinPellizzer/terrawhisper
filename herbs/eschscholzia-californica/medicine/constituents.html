Eschscholzia californica, also known as California poppy, has active constituents such as isoquinoline alkaloids, terpenes, and flavonoids.

For example, its isoquinoline alkaloids can help regulate sleep patterns and reduce stress levels, while its terpenes may provide relief from migraines and muscle tension.
