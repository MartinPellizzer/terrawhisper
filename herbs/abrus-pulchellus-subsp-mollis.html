Abrus pulchellus subsp. mollis

What's the taxonomical classification of Abrus pulchellus subsp. mollis?

Abrus pulchellus subsp. mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the order Fabales and sits within the family Fabaceae. This specific plant is identified by the genus Abrus, and its full taxonomic designation is the species Abrus pulchellus subsp. mollis.
