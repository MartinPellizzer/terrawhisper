What's the taxonomical classification of Eberhardtia aurata?

Eberhardtia auraata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Ericales, it falls specifically within the family Sapotaeceae. The plant's precise taxonomic placement is defined by its membership in the genus Eberhardtia, with its specific designation being the species auraata.
