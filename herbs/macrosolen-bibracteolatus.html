What's the taxonomical classification of Macrosolen bibracteolatus?

Macrosolen bibracteolatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Santalales and the family Loranthaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Macrosolen, and its unique species designation, bibracteolatus.
