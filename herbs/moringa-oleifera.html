Moringa Oleifera

Moringa oleifera, commonly known as the drumstick tree, is a highly nutritious and versatile plant with a wide range of medicinal uses.

Its leaves, seeds, and bark contain bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, polyphenols, and vitamins that contribute to its therapeutic properties. Moringa has been traditionally used to treat ailments like inflammation, diabetes, and digestive issues due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. Modern research supports its potential in managing chronic diseases and enhancing immune function.

Additionally, it is valued for its ability to improve nutrient absorption and support overall health when incorporated into diets or herbal remedies.
