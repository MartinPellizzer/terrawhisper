By lowering blood pressure, Allium sativum can alleviate conditions such as hypertension, heart failure, and peripheral artery disease, which can also help to reduce the risk of stroke and kidney disease.

Regular consumption of Allium sativum has been associated with improved cardiovascular health and a reduced risk of these conditions.
