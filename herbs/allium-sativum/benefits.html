1. Lower blood pressure

This condition can lead to serious health complications if left unmanaged.

It is often caused by a combination of genetic predisposition, poor diet, lack of physical activity, and stress.

Hypertension commonly affects middle-aged and older adults, though it can occur in younger individuals as well.

The allium sativum plant, commonly known as garlic, has been shown to help lower blood pressure due to its bioactive compounds that promote vasodilation and improve cardiovascular health.

How this herb helps with lower blood pressure?

Allium sativum lower blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds help relax blood vessels and improve blood flow. Alkaloids like alliin and sulfur-containing compounds contribute to vasodilation. Flavonoids in garlic have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that support cardiovascular health.

Together, these components reduce oxidative stress and enhance nitric oxide production, which is essential for maintaining healthy blood pressure levels.
