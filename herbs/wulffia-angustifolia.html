Wulffia angustifolia

The therapeutic actions of Wulffia angustifolia are attributed to its ability to interact with the body's natural processes, exhibiting adaptogenic and immunomodulatory effects.

Possible Side Effects of Wulffia angustifolia

Wulffia angustifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach cramps due to its high saponin content.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption of the plant can also lead to liver and kidney damage, as well as electrolyte imbalances.

Additionally, Wulffia angustifolia may interact with certain medications and exacerbate conditions such as low blood pressure and thyroid disorders.

In severe cases, improper use of the plant can result in seizures, coma, or even death.
