What's the taxonomical classification of Dictyocline wilfordii?

Dictyocline wilfordii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following its taxonomic descent further, it falls under the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Aspleniaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Dictyocline, specifically as the species wilfordii.
