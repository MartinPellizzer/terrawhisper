What to know about Hyoscyamus niger (henbane) before using it medicinally

Henbane, scientifically known as Hyoscyamus niger, is a herb that has been used for centuries to improve overall health due to its sedative and anti-inflammatory properties.

In addition to its medicinal uses, henbane is also commonly used in culinary dishes, particularly in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine, where it adds a unique flavor and aroma to soups, stews, and salads. Horticulturally, henbane is a member of the nightshade family and is prized for its showy, white flowers that bloom in mid-summer. From a botanical standpoint, henbane is a perennial plant with hairy leaves and stems that are rich in alkaloids, giving it its distinctive properties.

With roots dating back to ancient Greece and Rome, henbane has a long history of use as a medicinal herb, ritualistic offering, and even as a means of inducing visions and divination.
