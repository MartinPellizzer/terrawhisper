Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) Uses

Chinese Privet is used to treat diabetes, high blood pressure, and high cholesterol due to its hypoglycemic and lipid-lowering properties. It also addresses skin infections, psoriasis, and dry eye syndrome, aiding in wound healing and reducing hair loss through its anti-inflammatory and regenerative effects.

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) Monograph

1. Diabetes

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support blood sugar regulation. It contains compounds that may enhance insulin sensitivity and reduce glucose absorption. Often combined with other herbs, it is prescribed to manage diabetes symptoms and improve metabolic function.

2. High blood pressure

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to help manage high blood pressure. It is believed to calm the liver and clear heat, which can reduce hypertension symptoms. The herb is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effect on lowering blood pressure. It is typically prepared as a decoction or powder.

3. Hypertension

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to help manage hypertension. It is believed to clear heat, calm the liver, and reduce internal wind, which are considered contributing factors to high blood pressure. Its extracts may support cardiovascular health by promoting blood circulation and lowering blood pressure naturally.

4. High cholesterol

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to help manage high cholesterol. It contains compounds that may support liver function and lipid metabolism. Regular use is believed to reduce LDL cholesterol levels and improve overall cardiovascular health.

5. Skin infections

Chinese Privet is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and bacterial growth on the skin. Applications include treating wounds, boils, and fungal infections. Its active compounds promote healing and prevent secondary infections.

6. Psoriasis

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat psoriasis due to its anti-inflammatory and skin-regenerating properties. It helps reduce redness, scaling, and itching associated with the condition. The herb is often combined with others to balance internal heat and promote skin health. It is typically prepared as a decoction or in pill form for internal use.

7. Dry eye syndrome

Ligustrum lucidum is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat dry eye syndrome due to its moistening and nourishing properties. It helps alleviate eye dryness, irritation, and fatigue by promoting fluid balance and improving ocular circulation. Often combined with other herbs, it supports long-term eye health and is particularly beneficial for those with chronic dryness.

8. Wound healing

Chinese Privet (Ligustrum lucidum) is used in traditional herbal medicine to promote wound healing. Its leaves and fruits contain compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. These properties help reduce infection risk and accelerate tissue repair. It is often applied topically in poultices or decoctions for minor cuts and skin injuries.

9. Dry eyes

Chinese Privet is used in traditional medicine to alleviate dry eyes by nourishing the liver and improving eye health. It is often prepared as a decoction and taken internally to enhance moisture and reduce eye dryness. Its properties are attributed to its ability to moisten tissues and balance internal heat.

10. Hair loss

Chinese Privet is used in traditional herbal medicine to address hair loss by promoting scalp health and stimulating hair follicles. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effects, supporting overall hair growth and thickness. Its properties are believed to nourish the scalp and improve circulation, making it a valuable component in formulations for alopecia.
