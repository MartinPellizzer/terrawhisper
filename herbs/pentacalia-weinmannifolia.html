What is Pentacalia weinmannifolia?

Pentacalia weinmannifolia is an evergreen shrub belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It has a height of 1-2 meters, with a spread of 1-1.5 meters, and a woody base with a few branches. The leaves are elliptical, opposite, and coriaceous, measuring 5-12 centimeters in length and 2-4 centimeters in width, with a glossy upper surface and a tomentose lower surface. The inflorescence is a capitulum, bearing 5-7 ligulate florets and 3-5 discoid florets, with a yellow or yellowish-brown corolla.

The plant is native to the Andes mountain range in South America.
