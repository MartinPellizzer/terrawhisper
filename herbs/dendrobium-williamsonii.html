Dendrobium williamsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically situated within the genus Dendrobium, and is identified by its unique species name, williamsonii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Each flower features a distinctive labellum that provides a landing platform for pollinators.
