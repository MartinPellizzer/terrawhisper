Dendrobium williamsonii

What's the taxonomical classification of Dendrobium williamsonii?

Dendrobium williamsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is classified under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically situated within the genus Dendrobium, and is identified by its unique species name, williamsonii.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat inflammatory conditions, fever, and various respiratory ailments. It is frequently applied in traditional practices to alleviate symptoms associated with coughs and bronchial issues. Some studies suggest its chemical constituents may help manage swelling and pain through natural anti-inflammatory properties. The plant is also utilized to help reduce high body temperatures during febrile illnesses. Local medicinal knowledge often incorporates its extracts to support the body's recovery from minor infections.
