What's the taxonomical classification of Dendrobium williamsonii?

Dendrobium williamsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is classified under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically situated within the genus Dendrobium, and is identified by its unique species name, williamsonii.
