Ambrosia magdalenae

Ambrosia magdalenae, also known as Magdalena's plumeless thistle or woolly ambrosia, is a plant species in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

This plant is traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Ambrosia magdalenae include alleviating coughs, reducing inflammation, and improving respiratory function.

Herbal preparations of Ambrosia magdalenae can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, which can be consumed to reap its medicinal benefits, although it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using it as a treatment.
