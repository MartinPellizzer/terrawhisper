Rehdereodendron macrocarpum has large, leathery, elliptic to oblong leaves that measure up to 25 centimeters in length and possess serrated margins. The plant produces striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, often followed by large, fleshy, globose fruits. These fruits transition from green to a deep red or purplish hue as they mature, providing a significant visual element to the foliage.
