What's the taxonomical classification of Elatostema monandrum?

Elatostema monandrum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Urticaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Elatostema and the species monandrum.
