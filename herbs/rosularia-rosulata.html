What is Rosularia rosulata?

Rosularia rosulata is a species of succulent plant that belongs to the family Crassulaceae.

It is characterized by its rosettes of fleshy, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are 5-10 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, with a leathery texture and a waxy appearance. The leaves are a deep green color with a red tint on the edges and are arranged in a dense, symmetrical pattern. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer on a short, upright stem that is 10-20 cm tall.

The flowers are small, cup-shaped and have a five-petaled structure.
