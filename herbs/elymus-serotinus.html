Elymus serotinus

What's the taxonomical classification of Elymus serotinus?

Elymus serotinus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Poales. As a member of the family Poaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Elymus, with its specific identity defined by the species epithet serotinus.
