What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Elymus serotinus has narrow, linear leaves that typically measure between 5 and 20 centimeters in length and are characterized by a slightly scabrous texture along their margins. Each spikelet is composed of several florets protected by long, pointed lemmas that often feature fine hairs.
