What's the taxonomical classification of Scrophularia kakudensis?

Scrophularia kakudensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling under the order Lamiales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Scrophulariaceae and is situated within the genus Scrophularia, specifically identified by the species epithet kakudensis.
