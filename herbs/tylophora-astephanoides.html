Tylophora astephanoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is ordered under Gentianales and placed within the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Tylophora, with its specific designation being the species astephanoides.
