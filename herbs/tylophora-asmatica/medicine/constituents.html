10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tylophora Asmatica (Asmatica)

These constituents are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. Asmatica's medicinal properties can alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions like asthma and bronchitis, improving lung function and reducing inflammation.

Regular consumption of asmatica can also boost the immune system, reducing the risk of infections and promoting overall well-being, thus enhancing the quality of life.
