What's the taxonomical classification of Ophiorrhiza succirubra?

Ophiorrhiza succirubra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, falling specifically into the genus Ophiorrhiza, with its unique identification as the species succirubra.
