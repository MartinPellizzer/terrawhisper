10 Health Benefits Of Ambrosia Arborescens (Golden Ragweed)

Ambrosia arborescens has health benefits such as relieving menstrual cramps, reducing inflammation, and acting as an antispasmodic.

Its medicinal properties include the presence of flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpenes, which work together to provide these benefits.

For example, women can use golden ragweed tea or tincture to alleviate menstrual discomfort, allowing them to resume daily activities without interruption.
