10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ambrosia Arborescens (Golden Ragweed)

Ambrosia arborescens has active constituents such as sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and lignans.

These compounds confer medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. The sesquiterpenes can alleviate respiratory issues by reducing inflammation in the lungs, while the flavonoids may help protect against oxidative stress and cardiovascular disease. Additionally, the lignans could have estrogen-like effects, making them useful for menopausal relief.

By harnessing these constituents, golden ragweed extracts may improve respiratory health, reduce the risk of chronic diseases, and provide natural support for women's hormonal balance.
