10 Possible Side Effects Of Ambrosia Arborescens (Golden Ragweed)

Ambrosia arborescens has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory issues.

These side effects can occur due to the herb's high content of sesquiterpene lactones, which can be released into the air when dried or crushed.

If not properly managed, these side effects can lead to discomfort, decreased quality of life, and even anaphylaxis in severe cases, causing people to avoid social gatherings and outdoor activities.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Ambrosia arborescens if used imporperly.
