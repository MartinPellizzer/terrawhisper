10 Precautions To Take When Using Ambrosia Arborescens (Golden Ragweed)

Ambrosia arborescens has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

Taking this herb can stimulate the uterus and cause contractions, leading to miscarriage or premature birth.

Additionally, it is essential to use it under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as excessive consumption can lead to allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and swelling.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Ambrosia arborescens medicinally.
