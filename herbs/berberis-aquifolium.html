Side Effects of berberis aquifolium

Berberis aquifolium triggers allergic reaction in some individuals, manifesting as skin rashes, itching, and even hives.

This herb can cause breathing difficulty in severe cases, leading to wheezing or shortness of breath, which may require immediate medical attention. The skin irritation and rash are common side effects, often appearing at the site of contact or ingestion.

Additionally, Berberis aquifolium may induce nausea, leading to stomach upset and vomiting in some users. It can also result in dizziness, confusion, and heart palpitations, which may be more pronounced in people with pre-existing health conditions. These side effects highlight the importance of consulting a healthcare professional before using this herb, especially for those with allergies or chronic illnesses.

While it is commonly used in traditional medicine, its potential to cause adverse reactions underscores the need for caution and proper guidance when incorporating it into one's health regimen.
