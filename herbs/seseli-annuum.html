Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum)

Annual Seseli (Seseli annuum) is a member of the Apiaceae family, native to North America, and Southwest United States. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and native american herbal medicine.
