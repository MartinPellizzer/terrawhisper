Lady'S Mantle (Alchemilla arvensis) Actions

Lady's Mantle exhibits several key therapeutic actions including diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and astringent properties. It also acts as a sedative, tonic, and carminative, helping to calm the nervous system and support digestive health. Its bitter and antispasmodic effects aid in stimulating digestion and relieving muscle spasms. Additionally, it functions as an expectorant and stimulant, promoting respiratory health and overall vitality.
