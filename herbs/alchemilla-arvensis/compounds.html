Lady'S Mantle (Alchemilla arvensis) Compounds

Lady's Mantle contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and glycosides contribute to its astringent qualities, while coumarin and terpenoids may support skin health. Saponins and essential oils provide cleansing and antimicrobial benefits, and anthocyanins offer additional antioxidant support.
