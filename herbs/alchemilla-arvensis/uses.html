Lady'S Mantle (Alchemilla arvensis) Uses

Lady's Mantle is used to treat various skin conditions such as irritation, eczema, allergies, and inflammation. It supports wound healing and can be applied to rashes, ulcers, and other inflammatory skin issues. Its astringent properties help soothe and repair damaged skin tissue.

Lady'S Mantle (Alchemilla arvensis) Monograph

1. Skin irritation

Lady's Mantle is used topically to soothe skin irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. It can help reduce redness, swelling, and discomfort associated with minor skin conditions. A poultice or infused oil made from the leaves is often applied directly to the affected area for relief. Its mild astringent action also helps to tighten and heal damaged skin tissue.
