What to know about Arctium lappa (burdock) before using it medicinally

Burdock, scientifically known as Arctium lappa, is a versatile herb that has been prized for its numerous health benefits.

Rich in antioxidants, burdock root and leaves have been shown to improve digestion, reduce inflammation, and even combat certain types of cancer. While it's commonly used in soups, stews, and teas, burdock is also a popular ingredient in Asian cuisine, where it's often pickled or sautéed as a side dish. As an ornamental plant, burdock has become a favorite among gardeners for its towering height and striking purple flowers.

With its ancient history dating back to the Middle Ages, burdock has been revered not only for its culinary and medicinal uses, but also for its symbolism in mythology and folklore.
