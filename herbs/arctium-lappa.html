Medicinal Parts of arctium lappa

Arctium lappa root is known for its strong, bitter taste and is primarily used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and detoxifying effects.

The root is typically harvested in the fall and dried for use in herbal preparations.

The leaf of Arctium lappa is also utilized, though less commonly, and is valued for its mild diuretic and expectorant qualities.

The main herbal preparations made from these parts include tinctures, decoctions, and poultices, with the root being more frequently used in formulations for digestive and skin-related ailments.
