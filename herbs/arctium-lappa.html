Arctium Lappa: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Arctium lappa, commonly known as common burdock, is a biennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is rich in bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, tannins, and mucilage, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Health benefits include supporting skin health, reducing inflammation, and aiding in detoxification processes.

Its therapeutic actions encompass antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, making it a popular remedy in traditional medicine.
