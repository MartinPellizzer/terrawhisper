What's the taxonomical classification of Pauldopia ghonta?

Pauldopia ghonta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized into the order Lamiales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Bignoniaceae. Its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Pauldopia, with its unique designation being the species ghonta.
