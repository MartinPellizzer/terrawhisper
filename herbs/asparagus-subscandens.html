What's the taxonomical classification of Asparagus subscandens?

Asparagus subscandens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a part of the family Asparagaceae and is nested within the genus Asparagus, ultimately being identified by its specific species name, subscandens.

Following flowering, it develops small, fleshy berries that typically transition from green to a deep red or black color when ripe.
