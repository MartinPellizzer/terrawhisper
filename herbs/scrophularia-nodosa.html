Scrophularia nodosa terpenoids, including labdane-type diterpenes, display anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antitumor activities, contributing to the herb's diverse therapeutic applications.

Scrophularia nodosa root is known for its cooling and detoxifying properties, often used in traditional medicine to support respiratory and skin health.
