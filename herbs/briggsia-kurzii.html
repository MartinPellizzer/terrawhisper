Briggsia kurzii

What's the taxonomical classification of Briggsia kurzii?

Briggsia kurzii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Lamiales. It is a representative of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically falling under the genus Briggsia, with the specific epithet kurzii defining its unique species identity.
