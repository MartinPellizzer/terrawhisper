What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia wellbyi?

Artemisia wellbyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Asterales and is a member of the prominent family Asteraceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Artemisia, with its unique designation being the species wellbyi.
