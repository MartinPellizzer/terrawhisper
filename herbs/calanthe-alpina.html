What's the taxonomical classification of Calanthe alpina?

Calanthe alpina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Calanthe, and is identified by its unique species name, alpina.
