What's the taxonomical classification of Liparis bootanensis?

Liparis bootanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling into the genus Liparis, and is specifically identified by the species name bootanensis.
