Toxocarpus wightianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. At the most specific taxonomic levels, this plant is identified by the genus Toxocarpus and the species wightianus.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
