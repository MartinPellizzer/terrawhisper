Goniothalamus donnaiensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Goniothalamus donnaiensis?

Goniothalamus donnaiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Magnoliales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Annonaceae, placing it within the genus Goniothalamus, with its specific identification being the species donnaiensis.

    What are the herbal preparations of Goniothalamus donnaiensis?
