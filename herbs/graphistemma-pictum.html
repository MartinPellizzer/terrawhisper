Graphistemma pictum

What's the taxonomical classification of Graphistemma pictum?

Graphistemma pictum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Graphistemma, and its species designation, pictum.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, inflammatory conditions, and various digestive ailments. The medicinal properties of the plant are primarily utilized to alleviate symptoms of topical rashes and fungal growths. In some traditional practices, it is also applied to soothe wounds and reduce localized swelling. Furthermore, certain preparations of the plant are consumed to manage stomach discomfort and gastrointestinal distress. These applications rely on the specific chemical compounds found within its leaves and bark.
