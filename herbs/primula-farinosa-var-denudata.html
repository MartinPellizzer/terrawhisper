Primula farinosa var. denudata

What's the taxonomical classification of Primula farinosa var. denudata?

Primula farinosa var. denudaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is positioned in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Primulaceae and is categorized under the genus Primula, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Primula farinosa var. denudata.
