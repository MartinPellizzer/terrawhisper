What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Aeluropus littoralis has slender, erect, caespitose culms that typically reach a height of 20 to 60 centimeters and possess narrow, linear leaves with scaberulous margins. The inflorescence is a compact, spike-like panicle that measures approximately 3 to 8 centimeters in length. Each spikelet is small and contains several florets enclosed by a lemma that is often somewhat compressed.
