What is Fragaria breviscapa?

Fragaria breviscapa is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It has simple leaves with serrated margins and 3-5 lobed leaflets that are 2-7 cm long, arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers with five petals, 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter, in clusters at the apex of the stem. The flowers have 20-30 stamens and a pistil with 3-5 styles, and are pollinated by bees and other insects.

Fragaria breviscapa is native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China and has been used in traditional medicine for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
