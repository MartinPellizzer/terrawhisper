10 Medicinal Constituents Of Quercus Robur (English Oak)

Quercus robur, also known as English oak, has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and protect against oxidative stress.

For example, flavonoids can improve symptoms of arthritis and reduce the risk of heart disease, while tannins may help to prevent certain cancers and infections, ultimately improving quality of life for those suffering from chronic conditions.
