Mentha Crispa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Mentha crispa, commonly known as wrinkled mint, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its serrated leaves and aromatic, cooling scent.

This herb is known for its refreshing menthol-like aroma and is valued for its digestive, respiratory, and calming properties.

Mentha crispa contains bioactive constituents such as menthol, menthone, and various flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects, including soothing muscle spasms and reducing inflammation.

It can be used in herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves to harness its beneficial properties.
