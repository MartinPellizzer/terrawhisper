Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia)

Scorodonia (Teucrium scorodonia) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Middle East, Southwest Asia, and North Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
