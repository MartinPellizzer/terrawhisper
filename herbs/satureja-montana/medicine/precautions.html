10 Precautions To Take When Using Satureja Montana (Summer Savory)

Satureja montana has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as pregnant or breastfeeding women avoiding its use due to potential uterotonic effects.

Excessive consumption may also cause gastrointestinal upset, such as diarrhea or stomach cramps.

Failure to consider these precautions may result in side effects, like premature birth or allergic reactions, emphasizing the importance of consulting a healthcare professional before using summer savory as a medicinal herb.

1. Follow proper preparation

When using Satureja montana medicinally, it's important to take it under guidance from a qualified healthcare professional.

This is because summer savory can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes drugs, and may exacerbate underlying medical conditions like hypertension and epilepsy. Additionally, its stimulating properties may not be suitable for individuals with anxiety or insomnia.

With proper guidance, the potential benefits of summer savory can be maximized while minimizing the risk of adverse effects.
