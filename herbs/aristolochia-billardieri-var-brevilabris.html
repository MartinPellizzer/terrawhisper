Native to Australia, this plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal activities.

The health benefits associated with Aristolochia billardieri var. brevilabris include its potential to treat various ailments such as skin conditions, wounds, and respiratory issues, as well as its ability to reduce fever and alleviate pain.
