What is Cleisostoma lendyanum?

Cleisostoma lendyanum is an epiphytic orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by a short to medium-sized rhizome with a few nodes, bearing two to three or more lanceolate to elliptic leaves that are usually 5-7 cm long and 1-2 cm wide. The inflorescence is an erect, 10-15 cm long raceme bearing 2-5 flowers, each 1.5-2 cm long and 1-1.5 cm wide, with a yellowish-green sepals and petals, and a white lip with a yellow or greenish-yellow callus. The flowers have a distinctive shape with the sepals and petals being similar in size and shape, and the lip being larger and more prominent.

Cleisostoma lendyanum is native to Borneo and has been found growing in shaded to partially shaded areas of montane forests.
