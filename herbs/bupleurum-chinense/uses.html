Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) Uses

Chinese Bupleurum is used to treat rheumatoid arthritis and joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It also helps with respiratory infections, influenza, and lung inflammation. Additionally, it is applied for skin infections and irritation, supporting immune function and reducing symptoms of the common cold.

Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) Monograph

1. Rheumatoid arthritis

Bupleurum chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate symptoms of rheumatoid arthritis by reducing inflammation and modulating immune response. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects. The root is typically processed and decocted for internal use. Its efficacy is supported by both traditional practice and modern pharmacological studies.

2. Joint pain

Bupleurum chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate joint pain by promoting circulation and reducing inflammation. It is often combined with other herbs to address rheumatic conditions and arthritic symptoms. Its action helps to relieve stiffness and pain in the joints, supporting overall musculoskeletal health.

3. Common cold

Bupleurum chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat common cold symptoms, particularly those involving fever and chills. It helps to疏散风热 (disperse wind-heat), supporting the body's defenses against external pathogens. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in clearing heat and relieving congestion.

4. Respiratory infection

Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat respiratory infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and fever by supporting immune function and clearing lung congestion. Often combined with other herbs, it is prescribed to alleviate acute and chronic respiratory conditions.

5. Tuberculosis

Bupleurum chinense is occasionally used in traditional Chinese medicine to support lung health and may be included in formulations for tuberculosis. It is believed to help clear heat and toxins, though its role is supplementary and not a standalone treatment for the disease.

6. Lung inflammation

Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat lung inflammation by reducing fever and clearing heat. It helps alleviate symptoms like coughing and chest discomfort associated with respiratory infections. Its anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties make it a key herb in addressing lung-related inflammatory conditions.

7. Respiratory infections

Bupleurum chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat respiratory infections by reducing inflammation and clearing heat. It supports the lungs and immune system, making it effective for conditions like bronchitis and common colds. Its active compounds help alleviate symptoms and promote recovery.

8. Influenza

Bupleurum chinense is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat influenza due to its antipyretic and antiviral properties. It helps reduce fever and alleviate symptoms by regulating the body's immune response. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects in respiratory infections.

9. Skin infections

Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat skin infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and bacterial growth on the skin. Often combined with other herbs, it supports the body's natural healing process and is applied topically or taken internally as a remedy.

10. Skin irritation

Chinese Bupleurum (Bupleurum chinense) is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate skin irritation by reducing inflammation and promoting healing. It is often applied topically in formulations to soothe conditions like eczema and rashes. Its active compounds help regulate skin responses and provide relief from redness and itching.
