Carpinus polyneura

What's the taxonomical classification of Carpinus polyneura?

Carpinus polyneura belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Fagales. It is further categorized under the family Betulaceae, which places it within the genus Carpinus. Ultimately, the specific identification of this plant is defined by its species name, polyneura.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that eventually develop into small nutlets.
