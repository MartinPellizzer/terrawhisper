Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus globulus) Actions

Eucalyptus globulus exhibits several key therapeutic actions. It acts as an expectorant to loosen mucus, an anti-inflammatory to reduce swelling, and an antispasmodic to ease muscle contractions. It also has diuretic, carminative, and stimulant properties, aiding digestion and promoting urine flow. Its astringent and tonic effects help tighten tissues and strengthen the body, while its sedative and bitter qualities can calm the nervous system and stimulate appetite.
