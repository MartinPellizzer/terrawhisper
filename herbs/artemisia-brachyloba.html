Artemisia brachyloba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Asteraceae, a large group of flowering plants, and is specifically nested within the genus Artemisia. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species brachyloba.
