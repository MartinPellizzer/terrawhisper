Eucalyptus camaldulensis var. brevirostris

Eucalyptus camaldulensis var. brevirostris, also known as the river red gum, is a variety of eucalyptus tree native to Australia, characterized by its short beak-like shape on the stamens.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and decongestant properties, derived from its essential oil rich in eucalyptol.
