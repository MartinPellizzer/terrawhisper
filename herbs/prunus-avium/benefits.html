1. Lowers blood pressure

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a chronic medical condition characterized by elevated pressure within the arteries.

This condition occurs when the force of blood against the artery walls is consistently too high, potentially leading to serious health complications.

It is commonly experienced by adults, particularly those with a family history of the condition, poor dietary habits, or a sedentary lifestyle.

Prunus avium, or the European cherry tree, has been studied for its potential to lower blood pressure due to its rich content of antioxidants and bioactive compounds.

These components may help relax blood vessels and improve cardiovascular health, offering a natural support for managing hypertension.

How this herb helps with lowers blood pressure?

Prunus avium lowers blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds have been shown to possess vasodilatory properties that help relax blood vessels. Alkaloids like cyanogenic glycosides may contribute to the plant's ability to reduce vascular resistance. Flavonoids present in peaches act as antioxidants, reducing oxidative stress and inflammation in the cardiovascular system.

Together, these constituents support improved circulation and help maintain healthy blood pressure levels.
