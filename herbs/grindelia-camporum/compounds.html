Grindelia (Grindelia camporum) Compounds

Grindelia camporum contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and saponins. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Phenolic acids and coumarin also play roles in its therapeutic applications. Tannins, glycosides, and essential oils further support its use in herbal medicine.
