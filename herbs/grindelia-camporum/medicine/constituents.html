10 Medicinal Constituents Of Grindelia Camporum (Gumplant)

Grindelia camporum has active constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, making gumplant an effective remedy for treating respiratory issues, wounds, and skin conditions.

The sesquiterpene lactones, in particular, have been shown to soothe irritated tissues and reduce inflammation, thereby improving respiratory function and promoting wound healing in individuals.
