Hernandia sonora

What's the taxonomical classification of Hernandia sonora?

Hernandia sonora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling into the order Laurales. This plant is a member of the family Hernandiaceae and is specifically identified by the genus Hernandia and the species sonora.

Therefore, extreme caution should be exercised when handling this plant to avoid adverse physiological responses.
