What is Paphiopedilum leucochilum?

Paphiopedilum leucochilum is a species of terrestrial orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a type of slipper orchid characterized by its epiphytic to hemiepiphytic growth habit, and its leaves are dark green and lanceolate with a waxy texture. The flowers of Paphiopedilum leucochilum are white and green, with a green lip and white petals that are slightly reflexed, and they are borne on a short inflorescence. The plant typically reaches a height of 20-30 cm, and its rhizome is thick and fleshy.

Paphiopedilum leucochilum is native to the tropical forests of the Philippines.
