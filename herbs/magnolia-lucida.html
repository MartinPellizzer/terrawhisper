What is Magnolia lucida?

Magnolia lucida is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Magnoliaceae.

It is a tree that typically grows up to 30 meters in height, with a trunk diameter of up to 1 meter. The leaves are dark green, elliptical in shape, and 10-15 centimeters long, with a leathery texture and entire margin. The flowers are solitary, white, and 10-15 centimeters in diameter, with 9-12 tepals and a 6-8 mm long stamen.

The fruit is a red, ovoid capsule 5-7 centimeters long, containing 6-8 seeds.
