What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis labordei?

Pedicularis labordei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Within this family, it is assigned to the genus Pedicularis, with its specific designation being the species labordei.

Detailed toxicological studies on this specific species are limited, which increases the risk of unknown systemic complications.
