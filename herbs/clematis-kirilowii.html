What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis kirilowii?

Clematis kirilowii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is situated within the genus Clematis, specifically identified by its species name, kirilowii.
