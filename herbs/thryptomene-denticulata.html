What is Thryptomene denticulata?

Thryptomene denticulata is an Australian shrub species belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is an erect shrub growing up to 1.5 meters in height, with a spread of 1 meter. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a wavy margin, measuring 2-5 mm in length and 0.5-1.5 mm in width. The flowers are white, produced in clusters at the leaf axils, and are 5-7 mm in diameter, with five petals and numerous stamens.

The species is found in Western Australia, typically growing in sandy soils and full sun.
