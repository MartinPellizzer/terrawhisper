Keteleeria fortunei

What's the taxonomical classification of Keteleeria fortunei?

Keteleeria fortunei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the subclass Pinidae. Following the hierarchy further, it is part of the order Pinales and the family Pinaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus Keteleeria and its specific species name, fortunei.
