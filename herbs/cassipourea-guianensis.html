Cassipourea guianensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cassipourea guianensis?

Cassipourea guianensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Malpighiales and belongs to the family Rhizophoraceae. Within this family, it is identified under the genus Cassipourea, specifically as the species guianenis.
