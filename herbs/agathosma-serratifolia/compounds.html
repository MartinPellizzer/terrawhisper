Serrated Agathosma (Agathosma serratifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds of Serrated Agathosma include terpenoids, which contribute to its aromatic properties, and essential oils, known for their antimicrobial effects. Flavonoids and phenolic acids provide antioxidant benefits, while coumarin and tannins support anti-inflammatory actions. Glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic potential.
