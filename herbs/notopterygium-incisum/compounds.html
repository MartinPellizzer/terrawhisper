Notopterygium Incisum (Notopterygium incisum) Compounds

Notopterygium incisum contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids. These compounds contribute to its therapeutic properties, such as anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects. Other notable components are tannins, glycosides, and coumarins, which support its use in traditional herbal medicine.
