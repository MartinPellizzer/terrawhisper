Cissus inundata

Cissus inundata, also known as the water vine or flood vine, is a type of climbing plant native to tropical Africa and Southeast Asia.

It has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Cissus inundata include reducing inflammation, improving joint health, and aiding in the management of diabetes and high blood pressure.

Cissus inundata can be prepared as an herbal supplement or tea, often in combination with other herbs, to take advantage of its medicinal properties.
