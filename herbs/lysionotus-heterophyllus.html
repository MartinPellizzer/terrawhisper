Lysionotus heterophyllus

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysionotus heterophyllus has heterophyllous leaves that vary in shape and size depending on their position along the stem. The lower leaves are typically larger and more elliptical, while the upper leaves transition into narrower, more lanceolate forms. The stem structure supports these diverse leaf arrangements through a woody base that matures as the plant grows. Each leaf features a distinct margin and a central vein that provides structural integrity.
