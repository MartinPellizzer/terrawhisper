10 Medicinal Constituents Of Agastache Rugosa (Giant Hyssop)

Agastache rugosa has active constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. The plant's constituents can help alleviate respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation and opening airways.

Regular consumption may also improve cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol levels and blood pressure, leading to a reduced risk of heart disease and stroke.
