What's the taxonomical classification of Clethra cavaleriei?

Clethra cavaleriei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its specific hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Ericales and is a member of the family Clethraceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed within the genus Clethrra, specifically as the species cavaleriei.

What are the therapeutic actions of Clethra cavaleriei?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various respiratory conditions including asthma, bronchitis, and chronic coughs. The medicinal properties of the plant help to soothe inflamed airways and reduce mucus production. Some traditional practices also utilize its extracts to alleviate symptoms of common colds and influenza. By acting as an expectorant, it assists the body in clearing congestion from the lungs. These therapeutic applications rely on the plant's natural bioactive compounds to provide relief.
