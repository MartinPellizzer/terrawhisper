Clethra cavaleriei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its specific hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Ericales and is a member of the family Clethraceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed within the genus Clethrra, specifically as the species cavaleriei.
