Chelonopsis subglabra

Chelonopsis subglabra, also known as Chinese bugleweed or subglabrous chelonopsis, is a perennial plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties.

Chelonopsis subglabra is valued for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which are believed to help with various health issues, including respiratory problems, fever, and digestive issues.
