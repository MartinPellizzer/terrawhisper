What is Arrowsmithia corymbosa?

Arrowsmithia corymbosa is a plant species endemic to South Africa, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual herb with a height range of 0.1-0.5 meters, possessing a single stem and a corymbosely arranged inflorescence. The leaves are simple, alternate, and lanceolate in shape, measuring 1-3 centimeters in length and 0.5-1.5 centimeters in width. The flowers are small and yellow in color, consisting of 5-10 florets per capitulum.

The plant produces small, brown achenes as fruit, which are 1-2 millimeters in length and equipped with a pappus.
