10 Medicinal Preparations Of Chamaemelum Nobile (Chamomile)

Chamaemelum nobile has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and essential oils.

These preparations are useful for alleviating digestive issues, insomnia, and anxiety. Chamomile tea is also used to soothe menstrual cramps and promote relaxation.

Topical applications of chamomile oil can help with skin irritations and minor burns, while its anti-inflammatory properties make it a popular remedy for eczema and other skin conditions.
