Dendrobium capillipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its classification through the order Asparagales, it is placed within the family Orchidaceae. The plant is further categorized under the genus Dendrobium, and its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, capillipes.
