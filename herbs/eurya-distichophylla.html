Eurya distichophyllla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Eurya distichophyllia has small, lanceolate to ovate leaves that are arranged in two distinct, opposite rows along the stem. The stems are woody and can become quite branched as the shrub matures. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, eventually producing tiny dark fruits.
