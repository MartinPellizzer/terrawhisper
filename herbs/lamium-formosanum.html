Lamium formosanum

Lamium formosanum, also known as Formosa lamium or Formosan Spurred Lavender, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Lamiaceae family.

Traditionally, Lamium formosanum has been used in folk medicine due to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to soothe digestive issues, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory problems.

Lamium formosanum can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat various ailments.
