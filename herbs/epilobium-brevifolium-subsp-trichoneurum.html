Epilobium brevifolium subsp. trichoneurum

What's the taxonomical classification of Epilobium brevifolium subsp. trichoneurum?

Epilobium brevifolium subsp. trichoneurum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Myrtales and is situated within the family Onagraceae. This specific plant is categorized under the genus Epilobium, and its precise taxonomic designation is the species Epilobium brevifolium subsp. trichoneurum.
