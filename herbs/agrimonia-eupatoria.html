Agrimonia Eupatoria Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Agrimonia eupatoria, commonly known as agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, valued for its medicinal properties.

It has been traditionally used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote urinary tract wellness.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its astringent, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.

Agrimony can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or decoction, making it versatile for various herbal remedies.
