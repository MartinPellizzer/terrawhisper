Possible Side Effects of Papaya platanifolia

Papaya platanifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal problems.

Consuming large amounts of papaya platanifolia can lead to diarrhea, vomiting, and stomach cramps due to its high levels of papain, an enzyme that can irritate the digestive system.

The latex of the papaya platanifolia plant can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions in some individuals, including redness, itching, and swelling.

Additionally, people taking blood-thinning medications should avoid consuming papaya platanifolia, as its high vitamin K content may interact with their medication and reduce its effectiveness.
