Corylus avellana var. laciniata

Corylus avellana var. laciniata, also known as the cut-leaved hazel, is a variety of the hazel tree (Corylus avellana) characterized by its distinctive, deeply cut or lobed leaves.

The plant's leaves, bark, and nuts have been used in traditional herbal medicine for their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Corylus avellana var. laciniata are said to include reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health, as well as providing antioxidant protection against cell damage.
