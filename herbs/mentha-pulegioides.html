Mentha Pulegioides Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Mentha pulegioides, commonly known as pennyroyal, is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family, known for its strong, minty aroma and medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its digestive and antiparasitic benefits, helping to alleviate symptoms of indigestion, bloating, and gastrointestinal discomfort.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include menthone, pulegone, and limonene, which have antispasmodic, antimicrobial, and carminative effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from pennyroyal to harness its medicinal properties.
