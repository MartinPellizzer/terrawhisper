Gleditsia japonica var. delavayi

What's the taxonomical classification of Gleditsia japonica var. delavayi?

Gleditsia japonica var. delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Fabales and further categorized under the family Fabaceae. Specifically, this plant is a variety of the species Gleditsia japonica, falling under the genus Gleditsia.
