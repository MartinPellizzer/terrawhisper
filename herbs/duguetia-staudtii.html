What's the taxonomical classification of Duguetia staudtii?

Duguetia staudtii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical classification, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Magnoliales, it falls under the family Annonaceae. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Duguetia, and its specific taxonomic designation is completed by the species name staudtii.

From the broth-culture of this fungus, two new tetracyclic polyketides named simplicilones A (1) and B (2) were isolated. These compounds demonstrated weak cytotoxic effects against the KB3.1 cell line. Specifically, the in vitro cytotoxicity IC50 was 25 µg/mL for simplicilone A (1) and 29 µg/mL for simplicilone B (2).
