Possible Side Effects of cocos nucifera

Cocos nucifera can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea, nausea, and stomach cramps, due to its high fiber and oil content.

Additionally, consuming excessive amounts of the herb can lead to allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing in sensitive individuals.

Overuse of coco nucifera may also cause an imbalance in blood sugar levels, particularly in people with diabetes, as it can lower blood sugar levels.

In severe cases, improper use of the herb can lead to interactions with medications, such as blood thinners, and worsen underlying health conditions.
