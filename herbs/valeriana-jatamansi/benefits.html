1. Improve sleep quality

How this herb helps with improve sleep quality?

Valeriana jatamansi improve sleep quality because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

Flavonoids contribute to the plant's calming properties by reducing oxidative stress and inflammation.

Together, these constituents support better sleep onset, duration, and overall sleep quality.
