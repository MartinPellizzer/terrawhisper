Codariocalyx motorius

What's the taxonomical classification of Codariocalyx motorius?

Codariocalyx motorius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated under the order Fabales. This plant is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is a diverse group of flowering plants. Within this family, it is specifically identified by the genus Codariocalyx and is designated as the species motorius.

    Codariocalyx motorius has potent neuroprotective and antispasmodic properties that act by modulating neurotransmitter pathways to reduce involuntary muscle contractions. Additionally, its chemical constituents exhibit mild sedative effects that assist in reducing systemic muscle tension and anxiety. This multifaceted approach makes it a significant subject of interest for treating movement-related pathologies.

      What are the herbal preparations of Codariocalyx motorius?

      Codariocalyx motorius is prepared as a concentrated aqueous decoction, a topical poultice made from macerated leaves, and a distilled essential oil extracted through steam. The decoction is typically simmered for extended periods to extract its active alkaloids for oral consumption. For external applications, the crushed fresh foliage is applied directly to the skin to soothe localized inflammation. The essential oil is often diluted in a carrier fat to facilitate rapid absorption through the dermal layers. These varied methods allow practitioners to tailor the plant's potency to specific medicinal needs.

      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Codariocalyx motorius can cause localized skin irritation, mild nausea, and unexpected involuntary muscle twitching if ingested or touched. The plant contains specific alkaloids that may trigger an allergic reaction in sensitive individuals. Users often report a tingling sensation on the skin following direct contact with the leaves. In some cases, excessive exposure leads to temporary dizziness or lightheadedness. It is crucial to handle this species with gloves to avoid these physiological disturbances.
