Salvia cavaleriei var. simplicifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia cavaleriei var. simplicifolia?

Salvia cavaleriei var. simplicifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Lamiaceae. The plant is a specific variety within the genus Salvia, specifically identified by the scientific name Salvia cavaleriei var. simplicifolia.
