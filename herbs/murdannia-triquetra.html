Murdannia triquetra

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Murdannia triquetra has triangular stems, lanceolate leaves, and small white flowers with three petals and yellow centers. The inflorescence consists of small flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. These blossoms are often accompanied by small, inconspicuous bracts that surround the base of the petals.
