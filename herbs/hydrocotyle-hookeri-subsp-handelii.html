Hydrocotyle hookeri subsp. handelii

What's the taxonomical classification of Hydrocotyle hookeri subsp. handelii?

Hydrocotyle hookeri subsp. handelii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Apiales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Araliaceae and is placed within the genus Hydrocotyle, specifically identified as the species Hydrocotyle hookeri subsp. handelii.
