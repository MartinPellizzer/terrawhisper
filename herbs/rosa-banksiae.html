Rosa banksiae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Rosa, and its specific species epithet, banksiae.
