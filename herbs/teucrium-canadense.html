Botanically speaking, wood sage belongs to the mint family and features small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink.

With its rich history dating back to ancient civilizations, wood sage has been mentioned in texts such as Pliny the Elder's Naturalis Historia and has been used for centuries as a natural remedy and flavoring agent.
