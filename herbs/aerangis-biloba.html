Aerangis biloba

What's the taxonomical classification of Aerangis biloba?

Aerangis biloba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it in the order Asparagales. As a member of the family Orchidaceae, this plant is situated within the genus Aerangiis, with its specific identity defined by the species name biloba.
