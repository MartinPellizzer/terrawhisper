What's the taxonomical classification of Celtis bungeana?

Celtis bungeanana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Rosalales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Cannabaceae, specifically within the genus Celtis. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific classification as the species bungeanana.

It is generally recommended to avoid direct contact with the plant to prevent unexpected dermatological issues.
