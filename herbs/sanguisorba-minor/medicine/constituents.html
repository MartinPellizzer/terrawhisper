10 Medicinal Constituents Of Sanguisorba Minor (Salad Burnet)

Sanguisorba minor has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These compounds confer medicinal properties to the plant, including antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol levels and blood pressure, while the phenolic acids have antibacterial effects that can help prevent infections.

These constituents can significantly improve people's lives by promoting overall well-being, supporting immune function, and enhancing the body's natural defenses against disease.

1. Quercetin

Sanguisorba minor quercetin is a flavonoid compound found in its leaves and flowers.

It also exhibits anti-allergic and anti-anaphylactic activities, making it a promising natural remedy for various diseases.
