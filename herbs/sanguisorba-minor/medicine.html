What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Sanguisorba Minor (Salad Burnet)?

Sanguisorba minor, also known as salad burnet, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing.

Its medicinal constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and salicylic acid, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Various preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and poultices have been used to treat skin conditions and injuries. However, excessive consumption may cause gastrointestinal irritation and allergic reactions.

To ensure safe use, individuals should consult with a healthcare professional before using Sanguisorba minor, particularly those with sensitive skin or allergies.
