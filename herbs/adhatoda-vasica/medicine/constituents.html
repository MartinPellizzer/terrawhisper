10 Medicinal Constituents Of Adhatoda Vasica (Malabar Nut)

Adhatoda vasica has active constituents such as vasicine, vasicol, and adenine.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, which include bronchodilatory, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects. For example, the vasicine content can help alleviate respiratory issues, such as asthma, by widening air passages.

This can significantly improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions, enabling them to breathe more easily and engage in daily activities with greater ease.
