Anadendrum latifolium

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Anadendrum latifolium has large, broadly ovate leaves with prominent venation and a distinctively wide lamina. The leaf margins are typically entire or slightly wavy, providing a lush appearance to the foliage. The stems are robust and fleshy, supporting the heavy weight of the expansive leaf blades. Small, inconspicuous flowers are often produced from the axils of the leaves. The overall structure of the plant emphasizes a spreading growth habit driven by its expansive leaf surface area.
