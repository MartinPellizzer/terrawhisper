Araiostegia pulchra

What's the taxonomical classification of Araiostegia pulchra?

Araiostegia pulchra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical classification continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, placing it under the order Polypodiales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Polypodiaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Araiostegia, with the specific epithet pulchra denoting its unique species designation.

Careful handling is recommended to avoid unexpected physiological responses.
