Ranunculus membranaceus var. stracheyanus

Ranunculus membranaceus var. stracheyanus, also known as Tibetan buttercup, is a plant species of the genus Ranunculus, commonly found in the Himalayan and Tibetan regions.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Tibetan buttercup include its potential to treat wounds, reduce inflammation, and improve cardiovascular health.

Tibetan buttercup can be prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or infusion, allowing for various applications in traditional medicine and modern herbalism.
