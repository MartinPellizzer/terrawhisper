Miconia elegantissima

Miconia elegantissima, also known as the "Velvet Tree," is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

This plant has been found to possess medicinal properties, particularly in its ability to exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory activities.

The health benefits of Miconia elegantissima include its potential to treat wounds, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory issues.
