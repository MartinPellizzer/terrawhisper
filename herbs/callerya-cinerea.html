What's the taxonomical classification of Callerya cinerea?

Callerya cinerea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Fabales, placing it within the family Fabaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by the genus Callerya and the specific epithet cinerea, forming its complete scientific name.
