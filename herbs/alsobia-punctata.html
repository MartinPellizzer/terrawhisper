What is Alsobia punctata?

Alsobia punctata is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is an epiphytic herb with ovate, pointed leaves that are 3-7 cm long and 2-5 cm wide, having a leathery texture and a pointed apex. The inflorescence is a raceme of white, bell-shaped flowers that are 5-6 cm long and 3-4 cm wide, with five lobes and a short pedicel. The plant is native to the tropical rainforests of Central America, specifically in Costa Rica and Panama.

Alsobia punctata has a sympodial growth habit, producing new growths from the base of the plant.
