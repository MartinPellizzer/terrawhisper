Ammannia baccifera

What's the taxonomical classification of Ammannia baccifera?

Ammannia baccifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the order Myrtales. Within this order, it is a member of the family Lythraceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement in the genus Ammannia, with its unique designation being the species baccifera.
