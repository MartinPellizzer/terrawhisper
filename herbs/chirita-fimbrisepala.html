What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita fimbrisepala?

Chirita fimbrisepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized into the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, falling under the genus Chirita, and is specifically identified by the species name fimbrisepala.

The leaves and other parts of the plant are often prepared into topical applications to soothe irritated skin. It is frequently utilized in traditional practices to promote the healing of open cuts and abrasions. Chirita fimbrisepala is prepared primarily as a decoction of its dried leaves and stems to extract active medicinal compounds. Depending on the specific traditional use, the plant material may also be infused into oils for massage purposes.
