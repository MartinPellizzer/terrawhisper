10 Health Benefits Of Backhousia Citriodora (Lemon Ironbark)

Backhousia citriodora has health benefits such as treating respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

Its essential oil contains citral, a compound that helps to clear mucus from the airways, relieving congestion and coughs.

By alleviating symptoms of respiratory conditions, lemon ironbark can improve sleep quality, increase energy levels, and enhance overall well-being, allowing individuals to enjoy daily activities with renewed vitality and confidence.
