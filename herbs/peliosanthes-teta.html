What's the taxonomical classification of Peliosanthes teta?

Peliosanthes teta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asparagales and the family Asparagaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Peliosanthes, and its unique species designation, teta.
