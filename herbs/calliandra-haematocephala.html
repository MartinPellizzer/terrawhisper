Calliandra haematocephala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it in the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Calliandra, with its specific identification being the species haematocephala.
