Viburnum sympodiale

What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum sympodiale?

Viburnum sympodiale belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Dipsacales. This plant is a representative of the family Viburnaceae, falling under the genus Viburnum, and is specifically identified by its species name, sympodiale.
