Corydalis ramosa var. nana

Corydalis ramosa var. nana, also known as dwarf corydalis or Chinese corydalis, is a small, low-growing perennial plant native to China and the Himalayan region.

It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties, which include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic effects.

The plant has been found to have various health benefits, including reducing pain and inflammation, improving sleep, and treating anxiety and depression.

In herbal preparations, the roots of Corydalis ramosa var. nana are commonly used to make teas, capsules, and tinctures, which can be used to support overall well-being and alleviate various health conditions.
