Dahlia bidentifolia

Dahlia bidentifolia, also known as the Mexican dahlia, is a flowering plant species native to Mexico and parts of Central America, characterized by its bright, vibrant flowers and slender stems.

The plant is used in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties, including its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help soothe skin irritations and wounds.

The health benefits of Dahlia bidentifolia include its ability to lower blood pressure, reduce fever, and act as an antioxidant to protect against cell damage.

Herbal preparations of Dahlia bidentifolia can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, which can be used to treat a variety of ailments, including digestive issues and respiratory problems.
