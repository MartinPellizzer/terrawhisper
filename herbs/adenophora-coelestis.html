What's the taxonomical classification of Adenophora coelestis?

Adenophora coelestis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls within the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Campanulaceae and is specifically identified by the genus Adenophora, with its unique biological identity defined by the species coelestis.
