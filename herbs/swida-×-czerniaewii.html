Swida × czerniaewii

Swida × czerniaewii, also known as the hybrid swida or alder-leaved honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant species resulting from the cross between the Swida alba and the Swida sanguinea.

The Swida × czerniaewii plant has been found to possess medicinal properties, particularly in its bark, which contains salicylic acid, a compound similar to aspirin.

The health benefits of this plant include its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can be used to treat various ailments such as fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions.

The Swida × czerniaewii plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures for medicinal purposes, and its bark can also be used to make salicylic acid-rich extracts.
