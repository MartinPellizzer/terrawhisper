Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) Uses

Wild Angelica is used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and cough by reducing inflammation and soothing airways. It also helps with respiratory allergies and infections, while its anti-inflammatory properties aid in healing skin infections and wounds.

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in the treatment of bronchitis. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, while its anti-inflammatory effects may reduce bronchial irritation. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to soothe coughing and ease breathing in bronchial inflammation.

2. Respiratory allergies

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) is used in herbal medicine to alleviate respiratory allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. It helps reduce symptoms like sneezing, itching, and congestion by supporting the body's natural response to allergens. Often prepared as a tea or tincture, it is valued for its gentle yet effective action on the respiratory system.

3. Respiratory infections

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and clearing mucus. It may help alleviate symptoms of bronchitis, coughs, and other respiratory infections. Its expectorant properties aid in loosening congestion, making it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for respiratory conditions.

4. Cough

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) is traditionally used to alleviate coughs, particularly in cases of respiratory irritation or inflammation. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, while its anti-inflammatory effects reduce throat discomfort. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to support respiratory health.

5. Respiratory inflammation

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) has been traditionally used to address respiratory inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce swelling in the airways and promotes the expulsion of mucus, making it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis and asthma. Its active compounds support lung function and may alleviate symptoms associated with chronic respiratory issues.

6. Inflamed airways

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) may support respiratory health by reducing inflammation in airways. Its compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that can ease symptoms of conditions like bronchitis or asthma. It is often used in herbal formulations to promote clearer breathing and soothe irritated airways.

7. Asthma

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in managing asthma symptoms. It is believed to help reduce bronchial inflammation and ease breathing through its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. However, it should be used under professional guidance due to potential interactions and dosage considerations.

8. Skin infections

Wild Angelica has antimicrobial properties that make it effective for treating skin infections. Its compounds help reduce bacterial growth and inflammation. It can be applied topically as a poultice or infused oil. It is particularly useful for minor infections like boils and fungal conditions.

9. Skin irritation

Wild Angelica is used to alleviate skin irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It can help reduce redness, itching, and swelling associated with rashes or minor burns. Topical application of a prepared tincture or salve is common. Its soothing effects make it a valuable remedy in herbal skin care.

10. Wound healing

Wild Angelica (Pimpinella major) has been traditionally used to promote wound healing due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. It helps reduce swelling, prevent infection, and accelerate tissue repair. Topical applications of its extracts or infusions are commonly used to treat minor cuts, bruises, and skin irritations. Its high content of essential oils and flavonoids contributes to its healing effects.
