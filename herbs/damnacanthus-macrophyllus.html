What's the taxonomical classification of Damnacanthus macrophyllus?

Damnacanthus macrophyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a representative of the family Rubiaceae, specifically falling within the genus Damnacanthus, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name macrophyllus.
