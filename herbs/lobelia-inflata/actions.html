Indian Tobacco (Lobelia inflata) Actions

Indian Tobacco exhibits several therapeutic actions including expectorant, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory properties. It acts as a bitter tonic and stimulant, aiding digestion and respiratory health. Additionally, it has carminative, diuretic, and astringent effects, supporting overall bodily function and reducing inflammation. Its sedative qualities also help in calming nervous system activity.
