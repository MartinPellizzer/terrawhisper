What's the taxonomical classification of Arundinella hirta?

Arundinella hirta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific biological identity as a member of the genus Arundinella, with its unique designation being the species hirta.
