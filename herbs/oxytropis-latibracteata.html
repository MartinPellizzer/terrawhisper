What's the taxonomical classification of Oxytropis latibracteata?

Oxytropis latibracteata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Fabales, the plant is a member of the family Fabaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Oxytropis and is distinguished as the species latibracteata.
