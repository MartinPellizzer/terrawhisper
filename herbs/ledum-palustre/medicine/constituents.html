10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ledum Palustre (Labrador Tea)

Ledum palustre, also known as Labrador tea, has active constituents such as triterpenoids, flavonoids, and caffeic acid.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues. For example, the flavonoids in Labrador tea may help alleviate symptoms of arthritis by reducing inflammation and pain. Additionally, its antioxidant properties can improve cardiovascular health by protecting against oxidative stress and cell damage.

By incorporating Labrador tea into their daily routine, individuals can potentially experience relief from chronic pain and inflammation, leading to improved overall well-being.
