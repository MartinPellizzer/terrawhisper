10 Possible Side Effects Of Ledum Palustre (Labrador Tea)

Ledum palustre has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

These side effects can occur when the herb is taken in large quantities or in combination with other medications. Excessive use can lead to kidney damage and exacerbate existing health conditions, such as liver disease or high blood pressure.

In severe cases, improper use of labrador tea can worsen quality of life and even lead to organ failure.
