What is Astragalus piscinus?

Astragalus piscinus is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It has a woody base and an unbranched stem that grows up to 10 cm in height. The plant bears a solitary leaf that is trifoliolate, with three leaflets that are elliptical in shape and 1-2 cm in length. The leaflets are sessile and the margins are entire.

The plant produces a single raceme of 5-10 pink flowers that are 2-3 mm in length and occur in June.
