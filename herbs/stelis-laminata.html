What is Stelis laminata?

Stelis laminata is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to South America, specifically in countries such as Brazil and Colombia. The plant features a monopodial stem, with 3-6 linear and leathery leaves that are 10-20 cm long, and a racemose inflorescence that bears 10-30 yellowish-green flowers, each 1.5-2.5 cm in length. The flowers are characterized by a cylindrical and slightly curved shape, with a flat and 3-lobed labellum.

The plant typically grows in humid and shaded environments at elevations between 600-1800 meters above sea level.
