Peperomia reflexa

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Peperomia reflexa has fleshy, succulent leaves that are elliptical or obovate in shape and grow in an alternating pattern along its stems. The foliage often displays a glossy texture and can range in color from deep green to variegated patterns of cream and light green. These leaves are typically arranged in a somewhat crowded manner, creating a dense and bushy appearance. The stems are relatively soft and can exhibit a slightly woody base as the plant matures. Small, inconspicuous flower spikes emerge from the leaf axils, contributing to its unique structural form.
