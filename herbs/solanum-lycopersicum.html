Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum) is a member of the Solanaceae family, native to Central And South America, Caribbean, and Southern United States. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and seeds have been used for culinary uses, decoctions, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and native american herbal medicine.
