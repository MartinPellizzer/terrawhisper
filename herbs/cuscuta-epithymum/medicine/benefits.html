10 Health Benefits Of Cuscuta Epithymum (Dodder)

Cuscuta epithymum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of various ailments.

Its medicinal properties include the presence of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have been shown to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

For example, dodder extract has been used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory conditions, improving joint mobility and reducing pain and discomfort, allowing individuals to maintain a more active lifestyle and enjoy improved overall well-being.
