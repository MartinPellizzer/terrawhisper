Garcina cowa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Malpighiales and the family Clusiaceae. Finally, its specific identification is established within the genus Garcinia, where it is designated as the species cowia.
