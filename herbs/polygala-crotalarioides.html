What's the taxonomical classification of Polygala crotalarioides?

Polygala crotalarioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. The plant is a member of the family Polygalaceae, which is further refined into the genus Polygala, with its specific designation being the species crotalarioides.
