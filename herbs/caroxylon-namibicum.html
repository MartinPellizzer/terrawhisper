What is Caroxylon namibicum?

Caroxylon namibicum is a succulent plant species belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its erect stems, which can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, and its leaves, which are linear, entire, and glabrous. The leaves are often crowded at the base of the stem and are up to 30 centimeters long. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in axillary clusters, which are followed by winged seeds.

Caroxylon namibicum is native to southern Africa and is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy areas.
