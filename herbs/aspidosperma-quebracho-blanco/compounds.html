Quebracho Blanco (Aspidosperma quebracho-blanco) Compounds

The primary compounds of Quebracho Blanco include alkaloids, which have medicinal properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Flavonoids and phenolic acids contribute to its antioxidant activity. Coumarin, saponins, and glycosides also play roles in its therapeutic potential. Terpenoids, essential oils, and anthocyanins add to its complex chemical profile.
