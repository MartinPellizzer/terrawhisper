Quebracho Blanco (Aspidosperma quebracho-blanco) Uses

Quebracho Blanco is used to treat inflammation, joint pain, and arthritis, including rheumatoid arthritis and inflammatory arthritis. It also supports skin wound healing and reduces skin infections and inflammatory skin conditions. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties aid in these therapeutic applications.

Quebracho Blanco (Aspidosperma quebracho-blanco) Monograph

1. Inflammation

Quebracho Blanco contains alkaloids with anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and pain. It is traditionally used to treat conditions like arthritis and inflammatory bowel disease. Its active compounds inhibit inflammatory pathways in the body. Studies suggest it may offer natural relief for chronic inflammation.

2. Joint pain

Quebracho Blanco has been traditionally used to alleviate joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. The bark contains alkaloids that help reduce swelling and discomfort in joints. It is often prepared as a decoction or tincture for internal use. Its effectiveness in managing arthritis and rheumatic conditions is well-documented in herbal medicine practices.
