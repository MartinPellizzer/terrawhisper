What is Bolbitis aspleniifolia?

Bolbitis aspleniifolia is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial, evergreen plant with a rhizome that produces a crown of fronds. The fronds are bipinnate, 50-80 cm long, and 20-40 cm wide, with pinnae and pinnules that have a wavy margin. The plant's leaves are leathery, dark green, and have a hairy rachis.

Bolbitis aspleniifolia is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia.
