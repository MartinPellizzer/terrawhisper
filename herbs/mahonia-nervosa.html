Possible Side Effects of mahonia nervosa

Prolonged use of the herb may interact with medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse health effects.

Furthermore, pregnant or breastfeeding women should avoid using Mahonia nervosa due to potential risks to the fetus or baby.
