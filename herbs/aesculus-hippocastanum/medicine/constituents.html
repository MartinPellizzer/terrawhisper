10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aesculus Hippocastanum (Horse Chestnut)

Aesculus hippocastanum has active constituents such as aescin, flavonoids, and saponins.

These constituents have anti-inflammatory, anti-edematous, and antioxidant properties. They can improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and promote healing of veins. As a result, horse chestnut is used to treat conditions such as chronic venous insufficiency, varicose veins, and edema.

By improving blood flow and reducing swelling, these constituents can alleviate discomfort and improve the quality of life for individuals affected by these conditions.

It is also used topically to treat skin irritations and wounds.
