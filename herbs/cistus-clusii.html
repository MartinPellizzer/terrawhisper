Cistus Clusii (Cistus clusii)

Cistus Clusii (Cistus clusii) is a member of the Cistaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and North Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, resin, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
