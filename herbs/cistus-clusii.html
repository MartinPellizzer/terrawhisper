Cistus Clusii: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cistus clusii, commonly known as the Algerian rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, renowned for its aromatic leaves and flowers.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

Therapeutically, it is used to support respiratory health, aid in detoxification, and promote skin health.

The bioactive constituents of Cistus clusii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal value.
