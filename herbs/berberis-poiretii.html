Berberis poiretii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Berberidaceae. Specifically, it is classified within the genus Berberis, with its unique identity defined by its species name, poiretii.
