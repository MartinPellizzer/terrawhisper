Bonduc Tree (Caesalpinia bonduc) Compounds

The Bonduc Tree contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent effects. Saponins and glycosides may support digestive health. Coumarin and terpenoids offer anti-inflammatory benefits, while essential oils and alkaloids provide additional therapeutic properties. Anthocyanins contribute to its vibrant color and antioxidant activity.

Bonduc Tree (Caesalpinia bonduc) Monograph
