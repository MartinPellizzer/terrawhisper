Bonduc Tree (Caesalpinia bonduc) Actions

The Bonduc Tree offers several therapeutic actions including astringent, anti-inflammatory, and tonic properties. It also acts as a diuretic and carminative, helping to reduce inflammation and promote digestion. Its bitter and stimulant qualities aid in enhancing metabolic functions, while its antispasmodic and sedative effects can relieve muscle spasms and calm the nervous system. Additionally, it serves as an expectorant to assist in clearing respiratory secretions.
