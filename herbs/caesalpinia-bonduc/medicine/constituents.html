10 Medicinal Constituents Of Caesalpinia Bonduc (Yellow Pencilwood)

Caesalpinia bonduc has active constituents such as flavonoids, triterpenoids, and alkaloids.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in traditional medicine for treating various ailments.

The plant's extracts have been shown to improve respiratory issues by reducing inflammation and improving lung function, while its antiseptic properties can aid in wound healing and reduce the risk of infection.
