10 Precautions To Take When Using Caesalpinia Bonduc (Yellow Pencilwood)

Caesalpinia bonduc has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as proper dosing and individual tolerance.

Taking excessive amounts can lead to stomach upset, vomiting, and diarrhea. Additionally, it may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, which can exacerbate side effects.

Failure to monitor doses and interactions can result in adverse effects, such as nausea, dizziness, and allergic reactions.
