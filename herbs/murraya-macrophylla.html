What is Murraya macrophylla?

Murraya macrophylla is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree that grows up to 10 meters in height, with a broad, rounded crown and a smooth, grayish-brown bark. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, measuring 4-12 cm in length and 2-6 cm in width, with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a lighter lower surface. The flowers are small, white or yellowish in color, with five petals and a sweet, fragrant scent, and are arranged in axillary panicles.

The plant is native to China and Southeast Asia, and is cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.
