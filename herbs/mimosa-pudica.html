Sensitive Plant (Mimosa pudica)

Sensitive Plant (Mimosa pudica) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to South America, Madagascar, and Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and roots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, native american herbal medicine, and european herbal medicine.
