Cow Parsnip (Peucedanum officinale)

Cow Parsnip (Peucedanum officinale) is a member of the Apiaceae family, native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, diuretic, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
