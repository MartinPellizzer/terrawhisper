What's the taxonomical classification of Elsholtzia communis?

Elsholtzia communis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Lamiaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Elsholtzia, with its designation completed by the species epithet communis.
