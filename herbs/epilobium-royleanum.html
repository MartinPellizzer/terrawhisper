What's the taxonomical classification of Epilobium royleanum?

Epilobium royleanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Myrtales and falls under the family Onagraceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Epilobium, with the specific epithet royleanum denoting its unique species designation.
