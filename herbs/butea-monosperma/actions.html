Butea (Butea monosperma) Actions

Butea monosperma exhibits several therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and astringent properties. It also acts as a diuretic and carminative, helping to reduce inflammation and support digestive health. Its bitter and stimulant qualities enhance digestion, while its expectorant and antispasmodic effects aid respiratory and muscular functions. The sedative action promotes calmness and relaxation.
