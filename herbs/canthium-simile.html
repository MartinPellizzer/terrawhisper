Canthium simile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Rubiaceae, which defines its broader botanical relationships. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Canthium, ultimately reaching its specific classification as the species Canthium simile.
