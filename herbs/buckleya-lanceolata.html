Buckleya lanceolata

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Buckleya lanceolata has lanceolate leaves that are typically 10 to 25 centimeters long, featuring entire margins and prominent pinnate venation. The foliage is characterized by a coriaceous texture and a deep green color on the upper surface. Its reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that emerge from the axils of the branches. The fruit develops into a woody capsule that eventually splits to release its seeds.
