Euonymus phellomanus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Celastrales and the family Celastraeaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Euonymus, specifically identified as the species phellomanus.
