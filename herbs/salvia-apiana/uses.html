White Sage (Salvia apiana) Uses

White Sage is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and sinusitis. It helps reduce inflammation and soothe an inflamed throat, making it effective for cold symptoms and respiratory allergies. It is also used for upper respiratory infections and sore throat, supporting overall respiratory health.

White Sage (Salvia apiana) Monograph

1. Cough

White Sage is traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for coughs. It is believed to help soothe irritated airways and reduce inflammation. Its antimicrobial properties may aid in clearing infections. It is often prepared as a steam inhalation or infused tea.

2. Inflammation

White Sage is traditionally used to reduce inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory compounds. It has been applied topically for skin irritations and internally for respiratory and digestive inflammation. Its use aligns with its role in traditional herbal medicine for soothing inflammatory conditions.

3. Bronchitis

White Sage (Salvia apiana) has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for bronchitis. It is believed to help reduce inflammation and clear mucus from the airways. Its antimicrobial properties may assist in fighting infections, while its calming effects can ease breathing and soothe irritation.

4. Sinusitis

White Sage is used to support respiratory health, particularly for sinusitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help reduce nasal congestion and combat infection. Traditionally, it is steam-distilled into oil or used in inhalation therapies to clear sinuses and promote breathing.

5. Respiratory allergy

White Sage is used to support respiratory health and alleviate allergy symptoms by promoting clear breathing and reducing inflammation. Its aromatic properties help detoxify the lungs and ease congestion, making it a valued remedy for seasonal and environmental allergies. Often used in steam inhalations or as a nasal mist, it supports the body's natural ability to manage allergic reactions.

6. Upper respiratory infections

White Sage is traditionally used to support the treatment of upper respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It may help alleviate symptoms such as congestion and sore throat when used in steam inhalations or as a herbal infusion. Its aromatic compounds are believed to clear nasal passages and reduce microbial load in the respiratory tract.

7. Inflamed throat

White Sage is used to soothe inflamed throats due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It can be brewed into a tea or used in steam inhalations to reduce swelling and ease discomfort. Traditionally, it is valued for its ability to support respiratory health and ease symptoms of soreness and irritation.

8. Cold symptoms

White Sage is traditionally used to support the body's natural defenses against cold symptoms. It is often prepared as a steam inhalation or herbal tea to help clear nasal congestion and soothe respiratory discomfort. Its antimicrobial properties may aid in reducing viral and bacterial infections associated with colds.

9. Respiratory tract infections

White Sage is traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly for infections like colds, bronchitis, and sinusitis. Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties help clear congestion and reduce inflammation in the airways. It is often used in steam inhalations or as a smudge to purify the respiratory environment.

10. Sore throat

White Sage is traditionally used to soothe sore throats due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It can be brewed into a tea or used in steam inhalations to reduce irritation and alleviate symptoms. Its aromatic compounds help clear respiratory passages and provide relief from throat discomfort.
