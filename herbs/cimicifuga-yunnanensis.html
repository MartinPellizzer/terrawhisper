What's the taxonomical classification of Cimicifuga yunnanensis?

Cimicifuga yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, which is further refined into its specific genus, Cimicifuga, and ultimately identified by its unique species designation, yunnanensis.
