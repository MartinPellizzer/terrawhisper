10 Medicinal Preparations Of Corydalis Ternata

Corydalis ternata has several medicinal preparations, such as dried roots used to make decoctions for treating rheumatic pain, arthritis, and neuralgia.

Teas and tinctures are also prepared from the dried roots and leaves to alleviate coughs, asthma, and bronchitis. Additionally, the plant is used in traditional Chinese medicine to calm anxiety and promote restful sleep.

Its medicinal properties make it a popular herbal remedy in Eastern cultures.
