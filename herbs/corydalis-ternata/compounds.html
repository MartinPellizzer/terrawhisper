Spring Corydalis (Corydalis ternata) Compounds

The primary compounds in Spring Corydalis include alkaloids, which have pain-relieving properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Glycosides and tannins contribute to its anti-inflammatory and astringent actions. Coumarin and essential oils provide aromatic and potential antispasmodic benefits.
