Cyphostemma pendulum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Vitales and the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific classification within the genus Cyphostemma and is identified by the species name pendulum.
