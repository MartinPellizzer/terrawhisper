Cyphostemma pendulum

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Cyphostemma pendulum has slender, pendulous stems that feature small, simple, opposite leaves and small, axillary, tubular flowers. The foliage typically consists of elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are arranged in pairs along the length of the drooping branches. These stems are characterized by their delicate, weeping habit which allows the plant to drape elegantly over surfaces. The inflorescences emerge from the leaf axils and produce blossoms that are often subtly colored. Overall, the plant's morphology is defined by its fine texture and graceful, hanging structure.
