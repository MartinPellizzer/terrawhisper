What's the taxonomical classification of Galium boreale?

Galium boreale belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling under the order Gentianales. This plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, which is a large group of flowering plants, and is specifically categorized under the genus Galium. Ultimately, the unique identity of this organism is defined by its specific epithet, making its full scientific name Galium boreale.
