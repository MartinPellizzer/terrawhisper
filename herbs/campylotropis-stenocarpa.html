What is Campylotropis stenocarpa?

Campylotropis stenocarpa is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree that grows up to 10 meters in height, with a woody stem and a spreading crown. The leaves are pinnate, with 5-7 pairs of leaflets, measuring 5-12 cm in length and 2-5 cm in width, with a lanceolate shape and an entire margin. The flowers are yellow, in a raceme, and contain 5 petals, with a calyx of 5 sepals.

The fruit is a pod, 4-6 cm in length, containing 2-4 seeds.
