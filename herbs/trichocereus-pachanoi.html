San Pedro (Trichocereus pachanoi)

San Pedro (Trichocereus pachanoi) is a member of the Cactaceae family, native to Peru, Bolivia, and Ecuador. Traditionally, its stems, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, tonic, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, tibetan medicine, and european herbal medicine.
