What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ribes glabricalycinum has a deciduous shrub habit characterized by smooth, hairless stems and palmately lobed leaves with serrated margins. During the flowering season, small, bell-shaped blossoms emerge from the axils of the leaves. These flowers are often held in clusters and serve as a primary feature of its seasonal morphology.
