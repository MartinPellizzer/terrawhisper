10 Possible Side Effects Of Anethum Graveolens (Dill)

Anethum graveolens has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

Allergic reactions can cause hives, itching, and swelling, which can be life-threatening in severe cases. Digestive issues, like nausea and diarrhea, can worsen with prolonged use and lead to malnutrition and dehydration.

Additionally, interactions with medications can reduce their effectiveness or increase the risk of bleeding.
