10 Health Benefits Of Pueraria Mirifica (Kwao Krua)

Pueraria mirifica has health benefits such as alleviating menopausal symptoms, boosting hormone production, and improving overall well-being.

Its medicinal properties include isoflavones, saponins, and coumarins, which possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and estrogenic activities. This plant can benefit women experiencing hot flashes, mood swings, and vaginal dryness by regulating hormonal imbalances and promoting natural lubrication.

For instance, kwao krua supplements have helped postmenopausal women regain their confidence and quality of life, allowing them to live more comfortably and actively throughout the day.
