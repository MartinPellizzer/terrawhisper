10 Precautions To Take When Using Curcuma Longa (Turmeric)

Curcuma longa has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional, especially if pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking blood thinners.

This is crucial because taking turmeric in excess can cause interactions with certain medications, leading to bleeding or allergic reactions.

For example, not consulting with a healthcare professional may result in bleeding complications if taken with warfarin, while an allergic reaction can occur without proper monitoring, causing symptoms like hives, itching, or difficulty breathing.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Curcuma longa medicinally.
