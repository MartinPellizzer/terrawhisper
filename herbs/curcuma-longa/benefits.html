1. Fights inflammation

Inflammation is a defensive response of the body to injury or infection, characterized by redness, swelling, and pain.

A synonym for this condition is chronic inflammation, which persists over a prolonged period.

It is often caused by prolonged exposure to irritants, autoimmune responses, or persistent infections.

This condition commonly affects individuals with conditions like arthritis, digestive disorders, or metabolic syndrome.

Curcuma longa, also known as turmeric, contains curcumin, which has been shown to reduce inflammatory markers in the body, offering a natural remedy for managing this condition.

How this herb helps with fights inflammation?

Curcuma longa fights inflammation because it contains bioactive constituents such as curcumin, alkaloids, and flavonoids.

These compounds work by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes like COX-2 and LOX. Curcumin, in particular, is known for its potent anti-inflammatory properties. Alkaloids in the plant also contribute to reducing inflammatory responses in the body.

Flavonoids further support this effect by scavenging free radicals and modulating immune system activity.
