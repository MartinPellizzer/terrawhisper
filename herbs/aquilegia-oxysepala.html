What's the taxonomical classification of Aquilegia oxysepala?

Aquilegia oxysepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and into the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Aquilegia, specifically identified as the species oxysepala.
