Nagalapuram Indian Bushbean (Leucas Indica Var. Nagalapuramiana)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Leucas indica var. nagalapuramiana.

Nagalapuram Indian bushbean, scientifically known as Leucas indica var. nagalapuramiana, is a traditional medicinal herb native to the Indian subcontinent, valued for its adaptogenic and therapeutic properties. This plant is renowned for its ability to enhance resilience to stress and promote overall well-being, making it a key ingredient in Ayurvedic formulations. Historically, it has been used in traditional medicine to treat digestive disorders, respiratory ailments, and skin conditions, reflecting its deep-rooted cultural significance in Indian herbal practices. In modern wellness, it is increasingly incorporated into supplements and natural remedies for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, supporting immune health and cognitive function. Unique to this variety is its rich content of bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and alkaloids, which contribute to its distinct medicinal profile and historical use in Ayurvedic healing.
