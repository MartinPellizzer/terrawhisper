What is Lepisorus rotundus?

Lepisorus rotundus is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded leaves, which are 5-8 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, with a leathery texture and a serrated margin. The rhizome is erect, short, and scaly, and the stipe is short, smooth, and green. The leaflets are pinnate, with 2-4 pairs of pinnae, and the sori are marginal and round.

Lepisorus rotundus is native to the Himalayas and adjacent regions.
