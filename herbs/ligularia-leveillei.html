What's the taxonomical classification of Ligularia leveillei?

Ligularia leveillei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, this plant is placed within the genus Ligularia, ultimately reaching its specific classification at the species level as Ligularia leveillei.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ligularia leveillei has large, heart-shaped leaves that are coarsely serrated along the margins and possess a distinctively rugose texture. These flower heads are surrounded by prominent involucral bracts which protect the developing seeds.
