Phryma leptostachya

What's the taxonomical classification of Phryma leptostachya?

Phrymma leptostachya belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further into its specific classification, the plant is a member of the order Lamiales and falls within the family Phrymaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed at the genus level as Phrymna and at the species level as leptostachya.
