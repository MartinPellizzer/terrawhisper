What's the taxonomical classification of Dichocarpum dalzielii?

Dichocarpum dalzielii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ranunculales. As a member of the family Ranunculaceae, this plant is specifically categorized under the genus Dichocarpum, with its unique identification being the species dalzielii.
