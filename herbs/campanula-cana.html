What's the taxonomical classification of Campanula cana?

Campanula cana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Asterales, the plant is a member of the family Campanulaceae. Its specific taxonomic hierarchy concludes with the genus Campanula and the species cana.

These blossoms emerge from the leaf axils on slender pedicels and feature five distinct lobes at the mouth of the corolla.
