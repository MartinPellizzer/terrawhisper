Symphytum (Symphytum orientale)

Symphytum (Symphytum orientale) is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, native to Southern Europe, Middle East, and Central Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, roots, and rhizomes have been used for poultices, decoctions, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and african traditional medicine.
