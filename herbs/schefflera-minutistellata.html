What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Schefflera minutistellata has small, elliptical to oblong leaves that are dark green, glossy, and arranged in an opposite pattern along its woody stems. The leaf margins are typically entire and smooth, while the texture remains somewhat coriaceous throughout the foliage. Its stems are relatively slender and serve as the structural base for the compact, shrub-like growth habit of the plant. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, contributing to its understated botanical appearance.
