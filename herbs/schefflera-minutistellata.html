Schefflera minutistellata

What's the taxonomical classification of Schefflera minutistellata?

Schefflera minutistellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Apiales and the family Araliaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Schefflera and is identified by its specific species name, minutistellata.
