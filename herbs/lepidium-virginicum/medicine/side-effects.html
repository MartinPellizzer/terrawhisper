10 Possible Side Effects Of Lepidium Virginicum (Wild Cress)

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, individual sensitivity, and concurrent use of other herbs or medications.

For example, severe gastrointestinal upset can lead to dehydration, electrolyte imbalances, and malnutrition, which can worsen lives by impairing daily functioning and overall well-being.
