Phlojodicarpus sibiricus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified by the genus Phlojodicarpus and the species name sibiricus.
