What is Acmella filipes?

Acmella filipes is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb with a height of 20-100 cm, having a branched stem, and leaves that are lanceolate to linear in shape, 1-12 cm long, and 0.5-5 mm wide. The leaves are usually hairy, with a petiole that is 1-10 mm long. The inflorescence is a capitulum, containing 5-30 yellow or white florets, and a discoid receptacle with 5-20 achenes, each 1-5 mm long.

Acmella filipes is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Central and South America.
