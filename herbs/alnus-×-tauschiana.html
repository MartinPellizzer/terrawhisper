Alnus × tauschiana

Alnus × tauschiana, commonly known as hybrid alder, is a plant species that is a natural hybrid of the black alder (Alnus glutinosa) and the green alder (Alnus viridis).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine to provide various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help to protect against infections and oxidative stress.

The therapeutic actions of Alnus × tauschiana include its ability to act as an anti-inflammatory agent, which can help to soothe and calm irritated tissues, and its antimicrobial properties, which can help to prevent the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties and have been shown to have potential therapeutic applications in the treatment of various diseases.
