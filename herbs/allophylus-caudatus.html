What's the taxonomical classification of Allophylus caudatus?

Allophylus caudatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage leading to the order Sapindales. This plant is further categorized under the family Sapindaceae, which places it within the genus Allophylus. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species caudatus.

The inflorescence consists of small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in axillary clusters.
