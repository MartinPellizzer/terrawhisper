Bitter Melon (Momordica charantia)

Bitter Melon (Momordica charantia) is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, native to Southern Asia, Eastern Africa, and Northern Australia. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and korean traditional medicine.

Erect, branching habit with opposite arrangement, smooth surface, and presence of prominent longitudinal ridges.
