Peregrine Marrubium (Marrubium peregrinum) Compounds

The primary compounds of Peregrine Marrubium include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile, while phenolic acids provide additional antioxidant benefits. Coumarin and terpenoids are also present, offering potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
