Peregrine Marrubium (Marrubium peregrinum) Uses

Peregrine Marrubium is primarily used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and respiratory infections. It helps alleviate symptoms of asthma, common cold, and influenza by reducing mucous congestion and respiratory irritation. Its anti-inflammatory properties make it effective for inflamed lungs and other respiratory tract infections.

Peregrine Marrubium (Marrubium peregrinum) Monograph

1. Cough

Marrubium peregrinum is used to soothe coughs due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the respiratory tract. Traditionally prepared as a tea, it is valued for its mild and effective action on persistent or dry coughs.
