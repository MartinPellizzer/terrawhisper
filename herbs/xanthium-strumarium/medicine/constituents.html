10 Medicinal Constituents Of Xanthium Strumarium (Cocklebur)

Xanthium strumarium has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antipyretic effects, making them useful in treating fever, pain, and inflammation. The flavonoids in cocklebur also have hepatoprotective properties, protecting the liver from damage.

By consuming cocklebur, individuals can benefit from these properties, potentially alleviating symptoms of fever and pain, and maintaining liver health.
