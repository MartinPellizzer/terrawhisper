Caltha scaposa

What's the taxonomical classification of Caltha scaposa?

Caltha scaposa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and the family Ranunculaceae. Ultimately, this plant is categorized under the genus Caltha, with its specific designation being the species scaposa.
