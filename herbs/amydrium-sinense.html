What's the taxonomical classification of Amydrium sinense?

Amydrium sinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, the plant is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Alismatales and is a member of the family Araceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Amydrium, and its specific biological designation is the species sinense.
