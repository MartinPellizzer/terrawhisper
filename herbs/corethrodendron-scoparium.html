What side-effects this plant can have?

While the plant is generally considered non-toxic to ingest in small amounts, consuming significant quantities may lead to gastrointestinal upset or stomach discomfort. Some individuals might also experience localized allergic reactions if the plant's oils touch sensitive areas of the body. It is always prudent to handle this species with care to avoid these dermatological issues. Always consult a medical professional if a severe skin reaction or illness occurs after exposure.
