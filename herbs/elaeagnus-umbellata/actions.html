Silver Bush (Elaeagnus umbellata) Actions

Silver Bush exhibits several therapeutic actions including tonic, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties. It also acts as a carminative, stimulant, and expectorant. Additionally, it has bitter, sedative, antispasmodic, and astringent effects. These actions support its use in various herbal formulations for health support.
