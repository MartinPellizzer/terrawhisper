Silver Bush (Elaeagnus umbellata) Compounds

Silver Bush contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its antioxidant properties. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and cleansing effects, while coumarin and glycosides support anti-inflammatory actions. Essential oils and alkaloids add to its therapeutic versatility, and anthocyanins enhance its color and potential health benefits.
