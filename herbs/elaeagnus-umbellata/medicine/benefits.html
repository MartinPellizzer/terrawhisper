Elaeagnus umbellata reduces inflammation because it is rich in bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes.

2. Boosts immune system

Elaeagnus umbellata boosts immune system because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids that help combat free radicals and oxidative stress.

The plant's extracts have been shown to stimulate the production of cytokines, which are proteins that aid in the fight against infections. Additionally, oleaster has anti-inflammatory properties that may reduce the severity of infections and promote a healthy immune response.

This natural defense system support makes oleaster an attractive option for those looking to strengthen their immunity without relying on pharmaceuticals.

3. Improves digestion

Elaeagnus umbellata improves digestion because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's fruit and leaves contain bioactive compounds like flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes that help reduce inflammation in the gut, alleviating symptoms such as bloating, cramps, and diarrhea.

Additionally, oleaster's fiber content promotes regular bowel movements, supports the growth of beneficial gut bacteria, and helps break down nutrients for better absorption.

4. Alleviates menstrual cramps

Elaeagnus umbellata alleviates menstrual cramps because of its unique combination of phytochemicals.

The plant's extracts have been shown to exhibit potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which help to reduce prostaglandins that cause uterine contractions and discomfort.

Elaeagnus umbellata reduces risk of cancer because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells, induce apoptosis (cell death), and modulate various signaling pathways involved in carcinogenesis. Additionally, oleaster has been found to possess anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce chronic inflammation, a known risk factor for cancer development.

Consuming oleaster extracts or supplements may therefore be a valuable adjunctive therapy for reducing the risk of cancer.

6. Slows aging process

Elaeagnus umbellata slows aging process because of its exceptional antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's extract has been shown to stimulate the production of telomerase, an enzyme that maintains telomere length, effectively preventing cellular senescence and promoting healthy aging.

Additionally, oleaster's flavonoids and polyphenols have potent antioxidant effects, neutralizing free radicals and oxidative stress that can damage cells and contribute to premature aging.

Elaeagnus umbellata enhances cognitive function because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's leaves and stems are rich in flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have been shown to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective properties.

8. Promotes heart health

Elaeagnus umbellata promotes heart health because of its rich nutritional profile and potent antioxidant properties.

The fruit of this plant is particularly notable for containing high levels of vitamins A, C, E, and K, as well as potassium, calcium, and magnesium.

These nutrients help to lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and improve lipid profiles, all of which can contribute to a reduced risk of heart disease and stroke.

9. Regulates blood sugar levels

Elaeagnus umbellata regulates blood sugar levels because of its rich composition of bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds have been shown to possess insulin-mimetic properties, which help to increase glucose uptake in cells and improve insulin sensitivity.

Additionally, oleaster extracts have been found to inhibit alpha-glucosidase activity, thereby reducing the absorption of excess sugar from digested food and contributing to the regulation of blood sugar levels.

10. Supports eye health

Elaeagnus umbellata supports eye health because it is rich in antioxidants and omega-3 fatty acids.

The berries of this plant are particularly effective at protecting the eyes from oxidative stress and inflammation, which can contribute to age-related macular degeneration and cataracts.

Additionally, the flavonoids present in oleaster have been shown to improve blood vessel function and reduce the risk of retinal damage, further supporting overall eye health and vision preservation.
