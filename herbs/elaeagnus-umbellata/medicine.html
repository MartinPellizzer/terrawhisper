Elaeagnus umbellata, also known as oleaster, has health benefits such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties.

Possible side effects include gastrointestinal upset and allergic reactions, requiring caution when using it, especially in pregnant women or individuals with sensitive skin.
