What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Elaeagnus Umbellata (Oleaster)?

Elaeagnus umbellata, also known as oleaster, has health benefits such as lowering cholesterol and blood pressure, reducing inflammation, and alleviating digestive issues.

The medicinal constituents of oleaster include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. Various preparations of oleaster, including infusions, decoctions, and capsules, are used to treat ailments such as hypertension and gastrointestinal problems. However, excessive consumption can lead to gastrointestinal upset and allergic reactions.

Precautions should be taken when using oleaster, especially in individuals with underlying health conditions or taking medications.
