What is Borinda yulongshanensis?

Borinda yulongshanensis is a perennial bamboo species in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its culms, which are 4-5 mm in diameter, green or yellowish at the base, and white or yellowish at the node, with nodes being prominent and internodes being green. The leaves are 10-15 cm long and 0.8-1.1 cm wide, with the blade being yellowish-green and white at the base, and the ligule being 0.5-1 mm long. This species is found in the Himalayas and southern Tibet, with its distribution limited to high-altitude areas.

Borinda yulongshanensis is a relatively small species, with culm height ranging from 1-3 m.
