Tangshen (Codonopsis tangshen) Compounds

The primary compounds of Tangshen include glycosides, which support immune function, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant properties. Saponins and phenolic acids contribute to anti-inflammatory effects, while terpenoids and tannins aid in digestive health. Coumarins and alkaloids have potential antispasmodic and sedative properties, and essential oils and anthocyanins enhance overall therapeutic benefits.
