Microlepia chinensis

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and aiding in digestion.

The plant's therapeutic actions are attributed to its ability to cool the blood, relieve heat, and promote the flow of qi, making it a treatment for conditions such as fever, sweating, and digestive disorders.

The bioactive constituents of Microlepia chinensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
