Chinese Tea Vine (Uncaria tomentosa) Uses

Chinese Tea Vine is used to treat arthritis and joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory properties. It also supports skin health by addressing infections, eczema, and fungal issues. The plant aids in wound healing and may help with psoriasis and gastrointestinal inflammation, including inflammatory bowel disease and diarrhea.

Chinese Tea Vine (Uncaria tomentosa) Monograph

1. Arthritis

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, has been traditionally used to support joint health. Its alkaloids may help reduce inflammation and pain associated with arthritis by modulating immune responses and inhibiting inflammatory pathways. It is often used as a complementary therapy in herbal medicine for managing symptoms of rheumatoid arthritis and osteoarthritis.

2. Joint pain

Chinese Tea Vine, or Uncaria tomentosa, is used in herbal medicine to alleviate joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and discomfort associated with conditions like arthritis by supporting the body's natural healing processes. Its active compounds may inhibit inflammatory pathways, offering relief for chronic joint inflammation.

3. Skin infections

Chinese Tea Vine, or Uncaria tomentosa, is used to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce bacterial and fungal growth on the skin, alleviating symptoms like redness, itching, and swelling. Its natural compounds support the body's ability to fight infections and promote skin healing.

4. Eczema

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, is used in herbal medicine to treat eczema due to its anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory properties. It helps reduce skin irritation and redness by regulating the immune response and decreasing histamine release. Often combined with other herbs, it supports long-term skin health and manages flare-ups effectively.

5. Wound healing

Chinese Tea Vine, or Uncaria tomentosa, is known for its ability to promote wound healing due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce infection risk and accelerates tissue regeneration, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine for treating cuts, burns, and other injuries.

6. Fungal infections

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, contains compounds like pentacyclic triterpenes that exhibit antifungal properties. It is used to support the body's defense against fungal infections by enhancing immune response and reducing inflammation. Studies suggest it may be effective against Candida and other fungal pathogens. Its anti-inflammatory effects also help alleviate symptoms associated with fungal infections.

7. Psoriasis

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, has been traditionally used to support skin health. It contains compounds that may reduce inflammation and modulate the immune system, which can help manage psoriasis symptoms. Some studies suggest it may inhibit excessive skin cell growth, though more research is needed to confirm its efficacy.

8. Gastrointestinal inflammation

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, has been used in traditional herbal medicine to address gastrointestinal inflammation. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help reduce irritation and infection in the digestive tract. It supports healing of the stomach and intestinal lining, often used in formulations for digestive disorders.

9. Inflammatory bowel disease

Uncaria tomentosa, or Chinese Tea Vine, contains compounds like pentacyclic triterpenes and alkaloids that exhibit anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory effects. These properties make it a potential support for managing inflammatory bowel disease by reducing gut inflammation and modulating immune responses. Clinical studies suggest it may alleviate symptoms and promote intestinal health in individuals with IBD.

10. Diarrhea

Chinese Tea Vine, or Uncaria tomentosa, has been traditionally used to treat diarrhea due to its astringent properties that help firm stools and reduce intestinal inflammation. It is often combined with other herbs to support digestive health and ease symptoms of gastrointestinal distress.
