10 Medicinal Constituents Of Uncaria Tomentosa (Cat'S Claw)

Uncaria tomentosa has active constituents such as alkaloids, glycosides, and polyphenols.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it a popular natural remedy for various health issues. The medicinal properties of cat's claw can improve people's lives by reducing inflammation and pain associated with arthritis, improving wound healing and tissue repair, and boosting the immune system to combat infections.

Additionally, its antioxidants may help protect against chronic diseases such as cancer and cardiovascular disease.
