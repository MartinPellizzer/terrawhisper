10 Best Prunus Virginiana Preparations

Capsules ensure consistent dosing, making it convenient for daily use.

Decoctions involve simmering the bark or leaves to extract potent compounds, often used for digestive support.

Syrups are commonly used to soothe respiratory issues and provide a palatable form for children or those with swallowing difficulties.
