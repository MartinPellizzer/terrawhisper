Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens)

Yellow Sophora (Sophora flavescens) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to East Asia, China, and Sichuan Province. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and bark have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
