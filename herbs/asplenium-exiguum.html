What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium exiguum?

Asplenium exiguum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This specific fern is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, with its unique identification completed by its species name, exiguum.
