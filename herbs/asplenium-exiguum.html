What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium exiguum?

Asplenium exiguum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This specific fern is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, with its unique identification completed by its species name, exiguum.
