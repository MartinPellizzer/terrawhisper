Stephania japonica var. timoriensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Stephania japonica var. timoriensis?

Stephania japonica var. timoriensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated under the order Ranunculales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Menispermaceae and the genus Stephania, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety timoriensis of the species Stephania japonica.
