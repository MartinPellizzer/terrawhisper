Silene songarica

What's the taxonomical classification of Silene songarica?

Silene songarica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The species is a member of the order Caryophyllales and the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Silene, with its specific designation being the species songarica.
