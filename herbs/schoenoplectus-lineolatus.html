Schoenoplectus lineolatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It further falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Schoenoplectus and the species lineolatus.
