Leonurus pseudomacranthus

What's the taxonomical classification of Leonurus pseudomacranthus?

Leonurus pseudomacranthus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it in the order Lamiales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Lamiaceae, falling under the genus Leonurus, and is specifically identified by its species name, pseudomacranthus.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including menstrual irregularities, postpartum complications, and digestive disturbances. It is often utilized in traditional medicine to regulate the reproductive cycle and manage painful periods. Some practitioners also apply it to alleviate symptoms of uterine contractions following childbirth. Beyond reproductive health, it may be employed to soothe stomach pains or assist with general gastrointestinal issues.
