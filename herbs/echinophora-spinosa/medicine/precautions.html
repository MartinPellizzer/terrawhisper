10 Precautions To Take When Using Echinophora Spinosa (Thorny Parsley)

Echinophora spinosa has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious with its allergic potential.

If not taken properly, Echinophora spinosa can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions. For example, if the skin comes into contact with the sap of the plant, it may cause redness, itching, or even blistering.

Therefore, it's essential to wear protective clothing and apply a barrier cream when handling the plant to avoid potential skin irritation.
