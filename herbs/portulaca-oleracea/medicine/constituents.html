10 Medicinal Constituents Of Portulaca Oleracea (Purslane)

Portulaca oleracea has active constituents such as betalains, flavonoids, and omega-3 fatty acids.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Purslane's constituents can help alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis, improve wound healing, and support heart health by reducing inflammation and improving lipid profiles.

As a result, purslane has been used to improve quality of life for individuals with chronic health conditions, enabling them to engage in daily activities with greater ease and comfort.

1. Betacyanin

Portulaca oleracea betacyanin is a naturally occurring pigment present in its leaves and stems.

Additionally, studies have shown that betacyanin exhibits antibacterial and antifungal activities, indicating its potential as a natural food preservative.

2. Beta-carotene
