10 Possible Side Effects Of Chelone Glabra (Turtlehead)

Chelone glabra has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, dizziness, and headaches.

These side effects may arise from excessive consumption of the herb, which can lead to an overexposure to its active compounds.

For example, an individual with a pre-existing condition such as gastritis may experience severe stomach discomfort, leading to missed workdays and strained relationships.
