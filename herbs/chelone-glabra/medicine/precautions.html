10 Precautions To Take When Using Chelone Glabra (Turtlehead)

Chelone glabra has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as its interaction with blood thinners.

Taking this herb with blood thinners can increase the risk of bleeding. For example, if Chelone glabra is taken with warfarin, it can lead to excessive bleeding and bruising.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using this herb, especially if you are already taking medications that affect blood clotting.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Chelone glabra medicinally.
