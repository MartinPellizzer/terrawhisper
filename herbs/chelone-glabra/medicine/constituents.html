10 Medicinal Constituents Of Chelone Glabra (Turtlehead)

Chelone glabra has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents are known to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The iridoid glycosides can help alleviate digestive issues and reduce inflammation, while the flavonoids can improve heart health and combat oxidative stress.

As a result, Chelone glabra may be beneficial in reducing the risk of chronic diseases and improving overall well-being.
