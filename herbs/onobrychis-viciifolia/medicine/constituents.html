10 Medicinal Constituents Of Onobrychis Viciifolia (Sainfoin)

Onobrychis viciifolia has active constituents such as coumarins, flavonoids, and saponins.

These compounds have demonstrated anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can aid in reducing symptoms of arthritis and other inflammatory diseases.

For example, the flavonoids present in sainfoin have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by preventing the formation of blood clots and improving blood vessel function, thereby reducing the risk of heart disease.
