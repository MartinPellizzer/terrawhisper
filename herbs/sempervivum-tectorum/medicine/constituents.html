10 Medicinal Constituents Of Sempervivum Tectorum (Houseleek)

Sempervivum tectorum has active constituents such as saponins, tannins, and flavonoids.

These compounds are known for their antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties. The constituents help to soothe wounds, reduce pain, and prevent infection.

For example, saponins can improve wound healing by stimulating collagen synthesis, while flavonoids can reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis.
