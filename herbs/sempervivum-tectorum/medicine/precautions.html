10 Precautions To Take When Using Sempervivum Tectorum (Houseleek)

These compounds can cause digestive issues, allergic reactions, and interact with other medications if not used properly. For instance, if ingested in excess, it may lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

It is also essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using it, especially if pregnant or breastfeeding, as it can stimulate uterine contractions.
