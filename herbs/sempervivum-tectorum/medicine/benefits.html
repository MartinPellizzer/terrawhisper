10 Health Benefits Of Sempervivum Tectorum (Houseleek)

Sempervivum tectorum has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving wound healing due to its antibacterial, antifungal, and antioxidant properties.

The plant's sap is traditionally used topically to treat minor cuts and scrapes, while its leaves are consumed as a tea or infusion to alleviate digestive issues and respiratory problems.

By promoting faster recovery from injuries and relieving discomfort, houseleek can improve the quality of life for individuals with chronic wounds or those recovering from surgery.
