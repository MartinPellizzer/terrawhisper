Desert Gumweed (Grindelia squarrosa) Compounds

The primary compounds in Desert Gumweed include flavonoids, terpenoids, and coumarins, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Saponins and phenolic acids provide anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Glycosides and tannins support wound healing, while essential oils and alkaloids may have antimicrobial properties. Anthocyanins add to its antioxidant profile and contribute to its vibrant color.
