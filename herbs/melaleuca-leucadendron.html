What are the side effects of Melaleuca leucadendron when used improperly?

When used improperly, Melaleuca leucadendron increases the chances of experiencing side effects, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and even serious complications.

Furthermore, applying undiluted Melaleuca leucadendron oil to the skin without proper dilution can cause irritation, redness, and inflammation. Moreover, using high concentrations of the essential oil may interact with medications, such as blood thinners, diabetes medications, and certain antidepressants, increasing the risk of adverse reactions like bleeding, low blood sugar, or serotonin syndrome.

In severe cases, improper use of Melaleuca leucadendron can lead to more serious complications, including kidney damage, liver toxicity, and even life-threatening conditions such as seizures or anaphylaxis.
