What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia hanceana?

Ardisia hanceana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is classified under the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling specifically within the genus Ardisia, with the unique specific epithet hanceana completing its scientific designation.
