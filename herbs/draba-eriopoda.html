Draba eriopoda

What's the taxonomical classification of Draba eriopoda?

Draba eriopoda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Brassicales and is a member of the family Brassicaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Draba and the species epithet eriopoda.
