This herb is valued for its calming effects and is often used to relieve anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness, as well as to soothe digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

The therapeutic actions of chamomile include sedative, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties, which contribute to its ability to calm the mind and body.

Possible Side Effects of anthemis nobilis

Additionally, using the herb in high concentrations or for extended periods may cause liver damage and other adverse effects.
