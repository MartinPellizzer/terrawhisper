Anthemis Nobilis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Anthemis nobilis, commonly known as Roman Chamomile, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, known for its calming and soothing properties.

This herb is widely used in traditional medicine for its ability to relieve stress, promote sleep, and reduce inflammation.

The bioactive constituents in Roman Chamomile include flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from Roman Chamomile to harness its health benefits.
