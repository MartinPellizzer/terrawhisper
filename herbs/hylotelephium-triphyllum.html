Hylotelephium triphyllum

What's the taxonomical classification of Hylotelephium triphyllum?

Hylotelephium triphyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it in the order Saxifragales. This plant is a member of the family Crassulaceae, falling under the genus Hylotelephium, and is specifically identified as the species triphyllum.
