Hylotelephium triphyllum has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a trifoliate pattern, meaning they grow in groups of three from a single point. The plant features a creeping growth habit, often forming dense mats via rhizomes that allow it to colonize rocky crevices.
