Matricaria alpina

Matricaria alpina, also known as mountain chamomile, is a perennial plant species in the daisy family native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It possesses calming and soothing properties, making it a popular herb in herbal medicine, with medicinal properties attributed to its essential oils, flavonoids, and sesquiterpenes.

The health benefits of Matricaria alpina include reducing inflammation, promoting relaxation, and alleviating symptoms of anxiety and insomnia, while also exhibiting antimicrobial and antispasmodic properties.

Matricaria alpina can be prepared in various herbal forms, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be used to promote a restful night's sleep and soothe digestive issues.
