Matricaria alpina

Matricaria alpina, commonly known as Alpine marigold or Alpine chamomile, is a perennial herb belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant is valued for its soothing and anti-inflammatory properties, offering various health benefits, including reducing stress and anxiety, promoting relaxation, and alleviating menstrual cramps and insomnia.

The therapeutic actions of Matricaria alpina include antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties, making it a popular natural remedy for digestive issues and skin irritations.

The bioactive constituents of Matricaria alpina include apigenin, luteolin, and chamazulene, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
