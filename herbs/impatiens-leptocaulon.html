Impatiens leptocaulon

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Impatiens leptocaulon has slender, succulent stems that are often translucent and exhibit a reddish or greenish tint. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate with serrated margins and a smooth, somewhat fleshy texture. Small, delicate flowers emerge from the axils, often displaying subtle colors that complement the plant's lightweight structure. The root system is relatively shallow, supporting the plant's tendency to thrive in moist, shaded environments. This combination of thin stems and soft foliage defines its fragile morphological profile.
