Leptomischus primuloides

What's the taxonomical classification of Leptomischus primuloides?

Leptomischus primuloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Gentianales, the plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified by the genus Leptomischus and is distinguished as the species primuloides.
