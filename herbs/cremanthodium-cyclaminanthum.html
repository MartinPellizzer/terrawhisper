Cremanthodium cyclaminanthum has an erect herbaceous habit characterized by lanceolate leaves with serrated margins and small, yellow, tubular flowers arranged in terminal clusters. Its leaves possess distinct venation patterns and can vary in size depending on environmental conditions. The inflorescence consists of many tiny blossoms that provide a bright visual display during the blooming season.
