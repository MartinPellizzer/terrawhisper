9 Best Viscum Album Benefits

1. Boosts immune system

Immune System Weakness is a condition characterized by the body's reduced ability to defend against pathogens and diseases.

This health issue often results from a combination of factors such as poor nutrition, chronic stress, lack of sleep, or underlying medical conditions.

It commonly affects individuals with weakened immunity, including the elderly, those with chronic illnesses, and people undergoing intense treatments like chemotherapy.

Viscum album, also known as mistletoe, has been studied for its potential to enhance immune function by stimulating the production of white blood cells.

Its active compounds may help improve the body's defenses, offering support to those struggling with immune system weakness.

How this herb helps with boosts immune system?

Viscum album boosts immune system because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help strengthen the body's defense mechanisms. Alkaloids like viscotoxins stimulate the production of white blood cells, enhancing the body's ability to fight infections. Flavonoids in the plant act as antioxidants, reducing oxidative stress and supporting immune cell function.

Together, these natural compounds contribute to a more robust and responsive immune system.

Scientific Research

According to "Biomedicine & pharmacotherapy = Biomedecine & pharmacotherapie", Viscum album has an immunomodulatory effect that boosts the immune system by increasing Th1 cytokine levels, particularly interleukin-12 (IL-12) and gamma interferon (IFN-gamma), in a positive way.
