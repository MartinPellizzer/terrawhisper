10 Medicinal Constituents Of Alchemilla Vulcanica (Lady'S Mantle)

Alchemilla vulcanica has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These compounds are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and astringent effects. The flavonoids can improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and lipid profiles, while the phenolic acids may help protect against oxidative stress and cell damage.

These constituents can improve people's lives by enhancing overall well-being and reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
