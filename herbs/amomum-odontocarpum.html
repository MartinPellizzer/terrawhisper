What's the taxonomical classification of Amomum odontocarpum?

Amomum odontocarpum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Zingiberales. It is a specific representative of the family Zingiberaceae, falling under the genus Amomum, and is identified by its unique species designation, odontocarpum.
