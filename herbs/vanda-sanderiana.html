Vanda Sanderiana

Vanda sanderiana, a rare and striking orchid native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and flavonoids, which may contribute to its medicinal properties. In traditional Chinese medicine, it has been employed to treat ailments such as respiratory infections and inflammation due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. However, scientific research on its efficacy and safety for medicinal use remains limited, highlighting the need for further studies.

Despite its limited availability and conservation status, Vanda sanderiana continues to be of interest in both traditional and modern pharmacological research.
