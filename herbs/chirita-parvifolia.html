Chirita parvifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita parvifolia?

Chirita parvifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Chirita, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species parvifolia.
