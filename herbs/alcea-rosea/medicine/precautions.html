10 Precautions To Take When Using Alcea Rosea

Alcea rosea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as proper dosing and careful consideration of its potential interactions with other medications.

Using high doses or taking the herb over an extended period may lead to gastrointestinal side effects like nausea and vomiting.

For example, not taking the recommended dose can cause stomach upset, which may be exacerbated by pre-existing conditions like ulcers or irritable bowel syndrome.
