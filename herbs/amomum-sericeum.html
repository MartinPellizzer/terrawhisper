What's the taxonomical classification of Amomum sericeum?

Amomum seriaceum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Zingiberaceae, which places it among the ginger plants. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Amomum, with its unique identity defined by the species name sericeum.
