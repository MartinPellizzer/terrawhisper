What side effects this plant can have?

This plant can cause severe liver damage, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, neurological symptoms, and convulsions due to the presence of pyrrolizidine alkaloids. Ingestion of these toxic compounds often leads to gastrointestinal distress including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. Prolonged or acute exposure can result in significant liver damage through a process known as veno-occlusive disease. The toxins also impact the central nervous system, potentially triggering neurological symptoms. In severe cases of poisoning, patients may experience life-threatening convulsions.
