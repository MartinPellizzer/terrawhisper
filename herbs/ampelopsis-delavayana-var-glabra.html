What's the taxonomical classification of Ampelopsis delavayana var. glabra?

Ampelopsis delavayana var. glabra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Vitales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Vitaceae, which places it within the genus Ampelopsis. Ultimately, this specific botanical variety is identified by its full scientific designation, Ampelopsis delavayana var. glabra.
