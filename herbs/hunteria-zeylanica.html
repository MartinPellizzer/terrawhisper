What is Hunteria zeylanica?

Hunteria zeylanica is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae, native to Sri Lanka.

It is a deciduous tree, typically growing to 10-15 meters in height, with a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, measuring 10-20 cm in length and 4-6 cm in width, with a rounded base and an acuminate apex. The flowers are small, white, and arranged in axillary cymes, while the fruit is a red or yellow drupe, measuring 5-10 cm in length.

The plant's bark is grayish-brown, smooth, and slightly fibrous, with horizontal lenticels.
