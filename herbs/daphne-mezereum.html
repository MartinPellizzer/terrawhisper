Daphne Mezereum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Daphne mezereum, commonly known as the spring snowbell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, known for its ornamental flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb has been traditionally used for its purported ability to support respiratory and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents in Daphne mezereum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from the plant, though caution is advised due to its potential toxicity.
