10 Health Benefits Of Cirsium Lanceolatum (Thorny Burdock)

1. Helps lower blood sugar

Cirsium lanceolatum helps lower blood sugar because its roots contain inulin, a type of prebiotic fiber.

Inulin is digested by beneficial gut bacteria, which helps regulate blood sugar levels by improving insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake. Regular consumption of Cirsium lanceolatum can lead to improved blood sugar control, making it a potential natural remedy for managing type 2 diabetes and other metabolic disorders.

Its fiber content also aids in satiety and weight management.

2. Relieves arthritis symptoms

Cirsium lanceolatum relieves arthritis symptoms because it contains anti-inflammatory compounds such as arctiin and sesquiterpene lactones.

These compounds reduce joint inflammation and alleviate pain, making it a natural remedy for arthritis sufferers.

The plant's extracts also inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, thereby reducing inflammation and improving joint mobility, leading to a reduction in arthritis symptoms.

3. Reduces inflammation

Cirsium lanceolatum reduces inflammation because it contains flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenoids that exhibit potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, which contribute to inflammation.

The flavonoids present in the plant, particularly kaempferol and quercetin, have been shown to suppress the activity of inflammatory mediators, providing relief from swelling and pain.

4. Aids digestive issues

Cirsium lanceolatum aids digestive issues because its roots and flowers contain inulin, a soluble fiber that helps regulate bowel movements and promotes the growth of beneficial gut bacteria.

This prebiotic effect supports the breakdown and absorption of nutrients, reducing symptoms of bloating, constipation, and other digestive discomforts.

Regular consumption of thorny burdock has also been associated with improved gut health.

5. Soothes skin irritation

Cirsium lanceolatum soothes skin irritation because it contains anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that calm and protect the skin.

The plant's root and leaves have been traditionally used to reduce redness and itching, and its saponins and flavonoids help to neutralize free radicals that can exacerbate skin irritation.

As a result, it can provide effective relief for skin irritations, such as eczema and acne, while promoting overall skin health.

6. Fights cancer cells

Cirsium lanceolatum fights cancer cells because it contains sesquiterpene lactones and polyacetylenes, compounds that exhibit anti-cancer properties.

Research suggests that these compounds can induce apoptosis (cell death) in cancer cells, while sparing healthy cells.

Additionally, thorny burdock's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects may also contribute to its anti-cancer properties, making it a potential candidate for cancer prevention and treatment.

7. Supports immune system

Cirsium lanceolatum supports immune system because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
