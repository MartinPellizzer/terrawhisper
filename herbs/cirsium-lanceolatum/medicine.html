What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Cirsium Lanceolatum (Thorny Burdock)?

Cirsium lanceolatum, also known as thorny burdock, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving digestion.

Its medicinal constituents include inulin, a soluble fiber, and polyphenols, which have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Medicinal preparations of thorny burdock include teas, infusions, and tinctures that can be used to treat digestive issues and skin conditions. However, long-term use can cause stomach upset and interact with blood thinners.

Caution is advised, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding women, as excessive consumption may affect blood sugar levels.
