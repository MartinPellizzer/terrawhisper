Braun'S Lime (Tilia × Braunii)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Tilia × braunii.

Braun's lime, scientifically known as Tilia × braunii, is a rare hybrid tree species belonging to the Tiliaceae family, valued for its medicinal properties and unique botanical characteristics. It is primarily regarded as a medicinal herb and adaptogen, known for its calming effects and ability to support stress resilience. Traditionally, it has been used in European herbal medicine to treat respiratory ailments, anxiety, and insomnia, with its leaves and flowers often prepared as teas or tinctures. In modern wellness practices, Braun's lime is appreciated for its potential to enhance mental clarity, reduce inflammation, and support cardiovascular health. Notably, it contains a rare compound called tiliroside, which contributes to its distinctive mild, citrusy flavor and may offer additional therapeutic benefits.
