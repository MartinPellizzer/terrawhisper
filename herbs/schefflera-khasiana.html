Schefflera khasiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the order Apiales and resides within the family Araliaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement in the genus Schefflerera and the species khasiana.
