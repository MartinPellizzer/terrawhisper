Bulbophyllum drymoglossum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and into the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Bulbophyllum, reaching its specific designation as the species drymoglossum.
