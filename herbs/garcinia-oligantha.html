      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Garcinia oligantha can cause gastrointestinal distress, including nausea, abdominal pain, and diarrhea, due to its high concentration of organic acids. These acidic compounds may irritate the lining of the stomach if consumed in large quantities or on an empty stomach. Some individuals might also experience allergic skin reactions or rashes after topical application of the plant extracts. There is also a risk of drug interactions if the plant's bioactive compounds interfere with how the liver processes other medications. Long-term consumption effects remain understudied, requiring cautious approach to avoid potential toxicity.
