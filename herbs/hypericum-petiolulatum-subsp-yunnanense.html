Hypericum petiolulatum subsp. yunnanense

What's the taxonomical classification of Hypericum petiolulatum subsp. yunnanense?

Hypericum petiolulatum subsp. yunnanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Malpighiales and falls into the family Hypericaceae. Specifically, it is a member of the genus Hypericum, where it is identified by its distinct taxonomic designation as the species Hypericum petiolulatum subsp. yunnanense.
