What's the taxonomical classification of Staudtia kamerunensis?

Staudtia kamerunenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Magnoliales and is a member of the family Myristicaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Staudtia, with its unique scientific designation being the species kamerunensis.
