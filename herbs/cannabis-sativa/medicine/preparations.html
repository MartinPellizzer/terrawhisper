10 Medicinal Preparations Of Cannabis Sativa (Marijuana)

Cannabis sativa has several medicinal preparations, such as tinctures, oils, and topical creams.

These preparations are useful for relieving pain, reducing inflammation, and alleviating symptoms of anxiety and insomnia.

Cannabidiol (CBD)-based products, for example, are often used to treat epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and chronic pain, while tetrahydrocannabinol (THC)-based products are commonly used for managing nausea and vomiting associated with chemotherapy.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Cannabis sativa.

1. Tinctures

Cannabis sativa tinctures is used to promote overall wellness and alleviate various health concerns.

The liquid extract of cannabis can be taken sublingually, under the tongue, or added to food and drinks to reap its benefits. It has been shown to reduce inflammation, anxiety, and stress levels, while also providing relief from chronic pain, insomnia, and gastrointestinal issues.

Additionally, it may help regulate appetite, improve mood, and enhance overall quality of life.
