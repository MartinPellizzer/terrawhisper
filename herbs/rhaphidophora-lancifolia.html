What's the taxonomical classification of Rhaphidophora lancifolia?

Rhaphidophora lancifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Alismatales and the family Araceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Rhaphidophora, and its unique species name, lancifolia.
