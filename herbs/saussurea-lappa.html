Gao Ben (Saussurea lappa)

Gao Ben (Saussurea lappa) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Central Asia, Eastern Himalayas, and Tibetan Plateau. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
