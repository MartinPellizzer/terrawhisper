Gerbera tanantii

What's the taxonomical classification of Gerbera tanantii?

Gerbera tanantii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Asteraceae, a group known for its diverse flowering members, and is further categorized into the genus Gerbera. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, tanantii.
