Arnica fulgens var. sororia

Arnica fulgens var. sororia, commonly known as the Western Mountain Arnica, is a variety of the Arnica fulgens plant that belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

This perennial herb is native to the western regions of North America and is used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Arnica fulgens var. sororia include reducing pain and inflammation, promoting wound healing, and supporting the immune system.

The therapeutic actions of this plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.
