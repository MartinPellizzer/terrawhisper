Campylotropis macrocarpa var. macrocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis macrocarpa var. macrocarpa?

Campylotropis macrocarpa var. macrocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. More specifically, it falls under the genus Campylotropis, where it is identified as the variety macrocarpa of the species Campylotropis macrocarpa.
