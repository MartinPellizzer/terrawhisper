What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace stenophylla?

Androsace stenophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated in the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Androsace, with its specific designation being the species stenophyllas.
