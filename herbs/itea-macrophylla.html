Itea macrophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Itea macrophylla?

Itea macrophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is part of the order Saxifragales and falls under the family Iteaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Itea and the species macrophylla.

Proper identification and avoidance are essential to prevent these adverse biological reactions.
