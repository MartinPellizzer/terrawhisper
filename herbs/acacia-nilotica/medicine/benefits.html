10 Health Benefits Of Acacia Nilotica (Prickly Acacia)

Acacia nilotica has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, asthma, and allergies.

The plant's flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids contribute to its medicinal properties, making it effective in soothing digestive issues, reducing fever, and supporting wound healing.

By using prickly acacia extracts, people with respiratory conditions may experience relief from congestion and coughing, while those with skin irritations can enjoy a reduction in redness and itching.
