What's the taxonomical classification of Meiocarpidium lepidotum?

Meiocarpidium lepidotum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Magnoliales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Annonaceae, specifically falling within the genus Meiocarpidium, and is identified by its unique species designation, lepidotum.
