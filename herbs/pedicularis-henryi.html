What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis henryi?

Pedicularis henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, which falls under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, its taxonomic hierarchy concludes with its placement in the genus Pedicularis, with its specific designation being the species henryi.
