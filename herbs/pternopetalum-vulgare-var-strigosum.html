What's the taxonomical classification of Pternopetalum vulgare var. strigosum?

Pternopetalum vulgare var. strigosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Apiales and is a specific representative of the family Apiaceae. At the more granular taxonomic levels, this plant is situated within the genus Pternopetalum, ultimately categorized under the specific variety Pternopetalum vulgare var. strigosum.
