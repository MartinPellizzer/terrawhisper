Pedicularis rhinanthoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis rhinanthoides?

Pedicularis rhinanthoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Pedicularis, specifically identified by its species name, rhinanthoides.
