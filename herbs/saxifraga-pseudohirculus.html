What's the taxonomical classification of Saxifraga pseudohirculus?

Saxifraga pseudohirculus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Saxifragales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Saxifragaceae, which contains the genus Saxifraga, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species Saxifraga pseudohirculus.

Saxifraga pseudohirculus has a low-growing, rosette-forming habit characterized by thick, fleshy leaves that are often rounded or spatulate in shape and possess slightly serrated or crenate margins. From the center of this basal rosette, slender, upright flowering stalks emerge to bear small, star-shaped blossoms.
