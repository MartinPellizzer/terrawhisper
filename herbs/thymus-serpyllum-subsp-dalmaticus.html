Thymus serpyllum subsp. dalmaticus

Thymus serpyllum subsp.

dalmaticus, also known as Dalmatian thyme, is a subspecies of the thyme plant that is native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its antiseptic, antibacterial, and antifungal properties, which can help to soothe skin irritations, reduce inflammation, and combat infections.

The health benefits of Dalmatian thyme include its ability to boost the immune system, relieve respiratory issues, and exhibit antioxidant properties that can help protect against cell damage.
