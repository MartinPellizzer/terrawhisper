What is American Angel'S Trumpets (Aralia Racemosa)?

American Angel's Trumpets (Aralia racemosa) is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its clusters of white, trumpet-shaped flowers and its use in traditional herbal medicine for its purported sedative and pain-relieving properties.
