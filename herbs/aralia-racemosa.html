American Angel'S Trumpets (Aralia racemosa)

American Angel'S Trumpets (Aralia racemosa) is a member of the Araliaceae family, native to Eastern United States, Eastern Canada, and Northern Midwest. Traditionally, its roots, stems, and rhizomes have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
