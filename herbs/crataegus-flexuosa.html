Crataegus flexuosa

Crataegus flexuosa, commonly known as flexible hawthorn, is a species of hawthorn tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, including cardiovascular diseases, due to its health benefits, such as lowering blood pressure and improving lipid profiles.

The therapeutic actions of Crataegus flexuosa include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and cardioprotective properties, which contribute to its ability to improve heart health and reduce the risk of cardiovascular events.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and oligomeric proanthocyanidins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
