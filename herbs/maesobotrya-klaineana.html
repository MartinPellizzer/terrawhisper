What's the taxonomical classification of Maesobotrya klaineana?

Maesobotrya klaineana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Malpighiales, the plant is further organized into the family Phyllanthaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is defined by its assignment to the genus Maesobotrya and its specific designation as the species klaineaana.
