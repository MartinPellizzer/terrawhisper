    What is Machaonia ottonis?

    Machaonia ottonis is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Rubiaceae.

    It is a shrub or small tree that grows up to 10 meters in height, with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a leathery texture, measuring 4-12 centimeters in length and 2-6 centimeters in width. The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, with four petals and a calyx of four sepals, arranged in axillary clusters.

    The plant is native to the tropical forests of Central and South America, particularly in Costa Rica and Colombia.
