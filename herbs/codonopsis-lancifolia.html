Codonopsis Lancifolia

Codonopsis lancifolia, also known as "Dang Shen," is a traditional Chinese medicinal plant widely used for its purported health benefits.

It is valued for its ability to tonify the spleen and lungs, enhance energy, and improve digestion, making it a popular remedy for fatigue and weakness. The roots of the plant are typically dried and used in herbal formulations to support immune function and reduce stress. In traditional medicine, it is often combined with other herbs to treat conditions such as anemia and respiratory disorders.

Modern research suggests that compounds in Codonopsis lancifolia may have anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory properties, further supporting its traditional use in health care.
