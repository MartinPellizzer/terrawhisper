What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Dicranopteris linearis has linear to lanceolate pinnules that are typically 1 to 3 millimeters wide and arranged along slender, spreading fronds. The underside of the fronds contains small sori, which are the reproductive structures necessary for spore production.
