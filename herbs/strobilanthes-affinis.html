Strobilanthes affinis

What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes affinis?

Strobilanthes affinis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the evolutionary hierarchy, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Acanthaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Strobilanthes and is designated as the species affinis.
