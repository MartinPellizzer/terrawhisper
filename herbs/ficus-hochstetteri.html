What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus hochstetteri?

Ficus hochsteetteri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Rosales. It is further categorized into the family Moraceae, where it resides in the genus Ficus, specifically identified as the species hochsteetteri.
