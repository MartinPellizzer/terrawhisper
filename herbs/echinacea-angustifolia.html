What to know about Echinacea angustifolia before using it medicinally

Echinacea angustifolia, commonly known as coneflower, has been traditionally used to help alleviate symptoms associated with various health conditions, including the common cold, flu, and respiratory tract infections.

The herb is valued for its ability to boost the immune system and reduce inflammation, providing relief from conditions such as sore throats, coughs, and fever. The main health benefits of Echinacea angustifolia include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. The herb's bioactive compounds, including alkylamides, glycoproteins, and phenolic acids, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Echinacea angustifolia is often prepared as a root tea or dried root supplement, which can be consumed to support immune function and overall health.
