10 Health Benefits Of Tetraclinis Articulata (Arborvitae)

Tetraclinis articulata, also known as arborvitae, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The essential oils present in the plant, including pinene and bornyl acetate, contribute to these medicinal properties. These benefits can improve respiratory issues by opening airways and reducing inflammation, as well as protect against oxidative stress.

For instance, people with asthma can benefit from the anti-inflammatory effects, experiencing easier breathing and improved quality of life.

1. Reduces anxiety levels

Tetraclinis articulata reduces anxiety levels because of its adaptogenic properties.

The plant's unique biochemistry allows it to interact with the nervous system, promoting balance and calmness. Research suggests that Tetraclinis articulata's active compounds, such as essential oils and terpenes, have anxiolytic effects, alleviating stress and anxiety symptoms.

This natural remedy provides a safe and effective way to manage anxiety without the risk of side effects associated with pharmaceuticals.

2. Lowers blood pressure

Tetraclinis articulata lowers blood pressure because it contains bioactive compounds such as sesquiterpenes and flavonoids.

These compounds have been shown to have vasodilatory properties, which help to relax blood vessels and improve blood flow. Regular consumption of Tetraclinis articulata extracts has been linked to reduced systolic and diastolic blood pressure in individuals with hypertension.

Its cardiovascular benefits make it a valuable natural remedy for managing high blood pressure.

3. Reduces inflammation

Tetraclinis articulata reduces inflammation because of its unique bioactive compounds.

Studies have shown that its essential oils, including bornyl acetate and bornyl alcohol, exhibit potent anti-inflammatory properties. These compounds inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thus alleviating symptoms of inflammation.

The plant's efficacy in reducing inflammation is also attributed to its antioxidant activity, which helps mitigate oxidative stress and promote healing.

4. Prevents cancer growth

Tetraclinis articulata prevents cancer growth because of its rich content of volatile oils, particularly thujaplicin.

This compound has been found to have potent anti-cancer properties, inhibiting the growth of cancer cells and inducing apoptosis. Studies have shown that thujaplicin can selectively target and kill cancer cells while leaving normal cells intact, providing a promising natural solution for cancer prevention and treatment.

Its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties also contribute to its anti-cancer effects.

5. Kills bacteria effectively

Tetraclinis articulata kills bacteria effectively because of its unique essential oil composition.

The oil contains terpenes and diterpenes that have antimicrobial properties, specifically targeting gram-positive bacteria. These compounds inhibit bacterial growth, disrupt cell membranes, and ultimately lead to cell death.

The efficacy of arborvitae against bacteria has been attributed to its ability to produce reactive oxygen species that damage bacterial DNA, effectively killing bacteria and inhibiting their proliferation.

6. Relieves respiratory issues

Tetraclinis articulata relieves respiratory issues because its essential oils contain alpha-pinene, limonene, and caryophyllene, which have been shown to have bronchodilatory properties.

These compounds help to relax airway muscles, making it easier to breathe.

Additionally, arborvitae's anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties also contribute to its ability to alleviate respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation and fighting off underlying infections.

Tetraclinis articulata supports immune system because it contains terpenes and polyphenols with antioxidant and antimicrobial properties.

Regular consumption or use of arborvitae may help boost the immune system and reduce the risk of illnesses, making it a valuable addition to a healthy lifestyle regimen.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, allowing them to relax muscle tissue and alleviate cramping.

The calming effect of arborvitae's essential oils also helps to reduce tension and soothe overactive muscles, making it a natural remedy for relieving muscle spasms and promoting overall relaxation.

9. Soothes digestive issues

Tetraclinis articulata soothes digestive issues because of its unique combination of essential oils and bioactive compounds.

The plant's extracts have been shown to reduce inflammation and promote gut health by enhancing the growth of beneficial gut bacteria and modulating the immune system's response to digestive stress.

Tetraclinis articulata reduces fever quickly because it contains compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
