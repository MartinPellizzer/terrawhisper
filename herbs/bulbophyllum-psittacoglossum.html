Bulbophyllum psittacoglossum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asparagales, it falls into the diverse family Orchidaceae. More specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Bulbophyllum, and its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species psittacoglossum.
