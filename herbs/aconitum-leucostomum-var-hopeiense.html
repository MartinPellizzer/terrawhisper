Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense

What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense?

Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is placed under the order Ranunculales and falls within the family Ranunculaceae. This specific variety is classified under the genus Aconitum, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the species Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense.
