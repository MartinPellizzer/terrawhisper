Rehmannia henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Lamiales and the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Rehmannia, with its specific designation being the species henryi.
