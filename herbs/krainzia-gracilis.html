What is Krainzia gracilis?

Krainzia gracilis is a species of plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is a succulent shrub with cylindrical stems, 10-30 cm long and 1-2 cm in diameter, bearing fine, white, woolly spines. The stems are slightly ribbed and branch from the base, producing yellow flowers that are 3-5 cm in diameter and have a 5-8 mm long tube. The flowers are funnel-shaped and have 5-8 elliptical petals that are 10-15 mm long.

The plant grows in rocky areas and is native to Argentina.
