Hypericum perforatum flower, commonly known as St. John's wort, is a significant medicinal part of the plant, renowned for its therapeutic properties.

These flowers contain bioactive compounds such as hypericin and hyperforin, which are believed to contribute to the plant's antidepressant and anti-inflammatory effects.
