Hypericum perforatum

Hypericum perforatum, also known as St. John's Wort, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and Asia.

The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties, which include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of St. John's Wort include relief from mild to moderate depression, anxiety, and insomnia, as well as wound healing and skin irritation relief.

St. John's Wort is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, and is also topically applied to the skin to treat wounds and skin conditions.
