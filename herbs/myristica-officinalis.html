Myristica (Myristica officinalis)

Myristica (Myristica officinalis) is a member of the Myristicaceae family, native to Malay Archipelago, Indo-Malayan Region, and Southeast Asia. Traditionally, its fruits, seeds, and bark have been used for powders, infusions, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, astringent, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
