What is Parablechnum vestitum?

Parablechnum vestitum is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern with a rhizome, producing sterile leaves and fertile leaves with sori in two rows along the underside of the blade. The sterile leaves are larger, with 20-40 pairs of pinnae, while the fertile leaves have 10-20 pairs of pinnae. The plant grows up to 60 cm tall, with leaves 20-40 cm long and 10-20 cm wide.

The fertile leaves have sporangia in the sori, with 64 spores per sporangium.
