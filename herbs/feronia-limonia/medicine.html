What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Feronia Limonia (Citron)?

Feronia limonia, also known as citron, has health benefits such as aiding digestion and reducing fever.

The herb is rich in flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties. Medicinal preparations of citron include extracts, oils, and teas, which can be used to treat various health conditions. However, excessive consumption of citron can lead to side effects such as nausea and stomach pain.

To use citron safely, it is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before using it for medicinal purposes.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Feronia limonia .

What are the health benefits of Feronia limonia ?

Feronia limonia, also known as citron, has health benefits such as aiding in digestion and treating nausea.

The fruit's peel and pulp contain high levels of vitamin C, antioxidants, and limonoids, which have been shown to have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

Additionally, citron's flavonoids may help lower blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health, while its high fiber content supports healthy bowel movements.

What are the active constituents of Feronia limonia ?

Feronia limonia, also known as citron, has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and coumarins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Flavonoids like naringenin and eriodictyol exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities, while alkaloids like limonoides and coumarins like scopoletin show antimicrobial and anticancer properties.

These constituents make Feronia limonia a valuable plant in traditional medicine, used to treat various ailments including fever, digestive disorders, and skin conditions.

What are the medicinal preparations of Feronia limonia ?

Feronia limonia, also known as citron, has medicinal preparations such as infusions and decoctions used to treat various ailments.

The fruit, leaves, and bark of the tree are used separately to prepare these medicines. Citron oil is obtained through distillation, which is used in aromatherapy and skincare products.

It is valued for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

What are the possible side effect of using Feronia limonia improperly?

Improper use of Feronia limonia, also known as citron, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as digestive issues, diarrhea, and stomach discomfort.

Excessive consumption can also cause allergic reactions, headaches, and skin irritation. Furthermore, citron seeds are said to be toxic and should be avoided altogether.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using citron for medicinal purposes to minimize the risk of adverse effects.

What precautions to take when using Feronia limonia medicinally?

Before using Feronia limonia, also known as citron, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting a healthcare professional, as its extracts can interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying conditions.

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should use it under medical supervision, and individuals with allergies or sensitivities should start with a small dose.

Additionally, be cautious when consuming large quantities, as excessive citron consumption can cause adverse effects.
