10 Health Benefits Of Feronia Limonia (Citron)

Feronia limonia has health benefits such as antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's essential oil is rich in limonene, a compound that has been shown to exhibit antiseptic and antioxidant effects. These medicinal properties make citron useful in treating various ailments, including digestive issues and skin problems. For example, drinking citron tea may help alleviate symptoms of indigestion and reduce the risk of certain diseases.

Additionally, applying citron oil topically can soothe acne and minor wounds, promoting overall well-being and improving quality of life.
