10 Health Benefits Of Feronia Limonia (Citron)

Feronia limonia, also known as citron, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and promoting digestion.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, limonoids, and ascorbic acid, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. These benefits can improve people's lives by alleviating symptoms of digestive disorders, such as constipation and diarrhea, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases, such as cardiovascular disease and certain cancers.

Regular consumption of citron juice or tea may also boost immunity and energy levels.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Feronia limonia.

1. Treats toothaches

Feronia limonia treats toothaches because of its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oil extracted from its peel has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to soothe and relieve dental pain.

The citron's bioactive compounds, such as limonene and citral, have analgesic and antibacterial effects that help combat infection and reduce inflammation in the affected area, providing quick relief from toothaches.

2. Relieves feverishness

Feronia limonia relieves feverishness because of its antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The peel and pulp of the citron contain bioactive compounds that help to reduce body temperature and alleviate symptoms associated with fever.

Additionally, the fruit's antimicrobial properties help to fight off infections, which are often the underlying cause of fever, thereby reducing the discomfort and distress associated with feverishness.

3. Inhibits nausea

Feronia limonia inhibits nausea because it has potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The fruit's essential oil, which contains limonene and citral, has been shown to have antiemetic effects, reducing inflammation and oxidative stress in the gastrointestinal tract. This makes it a potential natural remedy for nausea and vomiting, particularly in cases related to digestive issues and travel sickness.

Its efficacy has been studied in various traditional medicine systems.

4. Soothes digestive issues

Feronia limonia soothes digestive issues because of its unique combination of vitamins and minerals.

The citron's high concentration of potassium and vitamins C and B6 help to balance stomach acid and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating. Its natural anti-inflammatory properties also soothe irritated stomach linings, promoting healthy digestion and reducing discomfort.

This natural remedy has been used for centuries to calm digestive ailments.

5. Lowers blood pressure

Feronia limonia lowers blood pressure because it contains flavonoids and limonoids, which have been shown to have a beneficial effect on cardiovascular health.

These compounds have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, helping to relax blood vessels and reduce blood pressure.

Regular consumption of citron juice or extract may also help to improve lipid profiles and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

6. Fights fungal infections

Feronia limonia fights fungal infections because of its unique bioactive compounds, particularly limonoids and coumarins.

These compounds possess strong antifungal properties that inhibit the growth of fungi by disrupting their cell membrane and interfering with their metabolic pathways.

As a result, Feronia limonia extracts have been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various fungal infections, showcasing its potential as a natural antifungal agent.

7. Prevents scurvy

Feronia limonia prevents scurvy because it is an excellent source of vitamin C. In the past, citron was a staple in many sailors' diets to combat scurvy, a disease caused by a lack of vitamin C. The high concentration of ascorbic acid in citron made it an effective remedy for scurvy, reducing the risk of infection and inflammation.

8. Treats diarrhea

Feronia limonia treats diarrhea because of its antidiarrheal properties.

The fruit, leaves, and bark of the tree are used in traditional medicine to alleviate digestive issues. The citron's high tannin content helps to reduce inflammation in the gut, while its antioxidant properties help to soothe the digestive tract and prevent further irritation, ultimately alleviating diarrhea symptoms.

Its effectiveness makes it a valuable treatment for digestive problems.

9. Relieves stress

Feronia limonia relieves stress because of its unique blend of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

The high levels of vitamin C in citron help to reduce cortisol levels, while its flavonoids and limonoids have a calming effect on the nervous system, promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety.

Regular consumption of citron can also improve mood and cognitive function, further alleviating stress and promoting overall well-being.

10. Boosts immune system

Feronia limonia boosts immune system because of its high content of vitamin C and flavonoids.

These antioxidants help to fight off pathogens and diseases, protecting the body from infections and inflammation.

Regular consumption of citron can also increase the production of white blood cells, which play a crucial role in fighting off infections and diseases, thereby strengthening the immune system and promoting overall well-being.
