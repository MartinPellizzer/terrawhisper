Clematis apiifolia var. argentilucida

What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis apiifolia var. argentilucida?

Clematis apiifolia var. argentiluida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Ranunculales. Its taxonomic placement continues within the family Ranunculaceae and the genus Clematis, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety Clematis apiifolia var. argentiluida.
