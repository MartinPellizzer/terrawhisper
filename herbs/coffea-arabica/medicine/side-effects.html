10 Possible Side Effects Of Coffea Arabica

Coffea arabica has some side effects when used improperly, such as insomnia, rapid heartbeat, and anxiety.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption of arabica, leading to caffeine overload in the body.

For instance, a person who relies heavily on arabica to stay awake for work may experience difficulty sleeping at night, affecting their daily routines and productivity.

1. Increases anxiety levels

Coffea arabica increases anxiety levels due to its high concentration of caffeine, a stimulant that can affect the nervous system.

The body's response to caffeine is to release adrenaline, which can cause feelings of jitteriness and restlessness, leading to increased anxiety levels.

Additionally, the acidity of arabica coffee can irritate the stomach, further exacerbating anxiety symptoms.

2. Causes sleep disturbances

Coffea arabica causes sleep disturbances due to its high levels of caffeine, a stimulant that can interfere with an individual's ability to fall asleep and stay asleep.

The caffeine in arabica coffee can increase alertness, energy, and focus, making it difficult for the body to wind down and relax before bedtime.

This can lead to insomnia, restless sleep, or difficulty staying asleep throughout the night.

3. Produces tremors violently

Coffea arabica produces tremors violently due to its high caffeine content.

The stimulating effects of caffeine can overexcite the nervous system, leading to muscle contractions and uncontrollable shaking or trembling. This is particularly common in individuals who consume excessive amounts of arabica coffee or have a sensitivity to its effects.

As a result, users may experience violent tremors, which can be uncomfortable and potentially disrupt daily activities.

4. Enhances irritability strongly

Coffea arabica enhances irritability strongly due to its high caffeine content.

The stimulating effects of caffeine can cause an increase in adrenaline levels, leading to feelings of restlessness and agitation. Additionally, the acidity present in arabica beans can lead to an imbalance of neurotransmitters such as serotonin and dopamine, further contributing to heightened emotions and impatience.

This combination can result in a person becoming easily frustrated or annoyed, making it challenging for them to manage their emotions effectively.

5. Increases heart rate rapidly

Coffea arabica increases heart rate rapidly due to its high concentration of caffeine.

The stimulant properties of caffeine cause a surge in adrenaline levels, leading to increased heart contractions and cardiac output.

This rapid heartbeat can be attributed to the way caffeine binds to adenosine receptors in the brain, blocking the inhibitory effects of adenosine on the cardiovascular system, resulting in an increased sympathetic tone.

6. Decreases appetite noticeably

Coffea arabica decreases appetite noticeably due to its stimulatory effects on the body.

The high levels of caffeine present in arabica coffee can suppress appetite by increasing the release of stress hormones like cortisol and epinephrine, which can reduce hunger pangs.

Additionally, the stimulating effect of caffeine can lead to increased alertness and energy, making people less interested in consuming food.

7. Induces nausea frequently

Coffea arabica induces nausea frequently due to its high caffeine content.

The sudden surge of adrenaline and increased heart rate caused by caffeine can lead to stomach upset and queasiness. Additionally, the acidic nature of coffee beans, which are used to make arabica, may irritate the digestive system and exacerbate nausea.

These factors combined can result in unpleasant symptoms for some individuals who consume Coffea arabica.

8. Causes stomach upset often

Coffea arabica causes stomach upset often due to its high concentration of caffeine and polyphenols.

The caffeine in arabica can stimulate the digestive system, leading to symptoms such as heartburn, bloating, and nausea.

Additionally, the polyphenols can slow down digestion and increase the production of acid in the stomach, further contributing to discomfort and pain.

9. Promotes jitteriness intensely

Coffea arabica promotes jitteriness intensely due to its high levels of caffeine and other stimulants.

These substances can stimulate the central nervous system, leading to feelings of anxiety, restlessness, and an increased sense of alertness. As a result, individuals may experience jitters, tremors, or an overall sense of unease when consuming arabica.

This intense stimulation can be attributed to the unique chemical composition of the coffee beans, which contain higher concentrations of caffeine and other stimulants compared to other types of coffee.

10. Leads to headaches periodically

Coffea arabica leads to headaches periodically due to its high levels of caffeine and other stimulants.

These substances can cause blood vessels to constrict, leading to a decrease in blood flow to the brain, which may result in headaches. Additionally, caffeine can also cause increased sensitivity to light and sound, exacerbating headache symptoms.

As a result, some individuals may experience frequent or severe headaches after consuming Coffea arabica.
