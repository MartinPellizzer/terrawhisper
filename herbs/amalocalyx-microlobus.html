Amalocalyx microlobus

What's the taxonomical classification of Amalocalyx microlobus?

Amalocalyx microlobus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Gentianales and the family Apocynaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its membership in the genus Amalocalyx, with the distinct species designation being microlobus.

Amalocalyx microlobus is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical wash using the plant's leaves and bark. It is important to handle this species with caution to avoid unforeseen adverse biological responses.
