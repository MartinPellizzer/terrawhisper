Ajuga campylantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Ajuga campylantha?

Ajuga campylantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Ajuga, and its unique species designation, campylantha.
