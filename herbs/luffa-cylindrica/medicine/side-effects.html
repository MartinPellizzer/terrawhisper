10 Possible Side Effects Of Luffa Cylindrica (Loofah)

Luffa cylindrica has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts of the herb, using it without proper preparation, or having an underlying sensitivity. For example, gastrointestinal upset can lead to stomach cramps, nausea, and diarrhea, causing discomfort and disrupting daily activities.

Allergic reactions can also worsen respiratory problems like asthma, making it challenging to breathe and perform daily tasks.
