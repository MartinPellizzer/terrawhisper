What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Primula cavaleriei has a basal rosette of long-petioled, obovate to elliptical leaves that feature crenate or dentate margins and a slightly pubescent texture. From the center of this rosette, several upright flowering stalks emerge, bearing clusters of salverform flowers. These blossoms display a distinct color palette, often ranging from pale pink to deeper shades of lavender or violet.
