What's the taxonomical classification of Hydrocotyle benguetensis?

Hydrocotyle benguetensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Apiales and into the family Aralaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified within the genus Hydrocotyle, with its specific designation being the species benguetensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hydrocotyle benguetensis has creeping stolons that produce small, orbicular to reniform leaves with crenate margins and long petioles. The leaves are typically green, sometimes displaying a slightly yellowish hue, and can reach sizes of several millimeters in diameter. Its flowers are minute, white or greenish, and are arranged in small umbels atop slender peduncles. The plant develops a dense mat-like growth habit through its spreading rhizomatous structure.
