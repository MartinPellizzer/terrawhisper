Torenia concolor

What's the taxonomical classification of Torenia concolor?

Torenia concolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Linderniaceae, specifically falling within the genus Torenia, with its unique identity defined by its species name, concolor.

What are the herbal preparations of Torenia concolor?

Torenia colorata is prepared as a topical infusion or decoction applied to the skin rather than consumed internally. Because the plant is primarily used as an ornamental species, its medicinal preparation is limited to external applications for minor dermatological concerns. Some traditional practices involve crushing the fresh leaves to create a direct poultice for topical use. These preparations are strictly used to avoid the potential toxicity associated with ingestion.
