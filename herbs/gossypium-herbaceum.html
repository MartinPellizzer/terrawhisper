Gossypium Herbaceum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Gossypium herbaceum, commonly known as cottonweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas, characterized by its hairy stems and small white flowers.

This herb is known for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

It contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
