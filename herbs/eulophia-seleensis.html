What is Eulophia seleensis?

Eulophia seleensis is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial or lithophytic plant with a short stem and 1-2 leaves, each 15-30 cm long, and 5-10 cm wide, with a bright green color and a rounded or cordate base. The inflorescence is a raceme, 15-30 cm long, bearing 5-10 flowers, each 3-4 cm in diameter, with a greenish-yellow or yellowish-green color and a white or yellow labellum. The sepals and petals are lanceolate, while the labellum is trilobed with a callus and a spur.

The plant is native to Madagascar.
