Rorippa islandica var. pusilla

Rorippa islandica var. pusilla, also known as dwarf watercress or small land cress, is a small, annual herb belonging to the Brassicaceae family, similar to watercress but smaller in size.

This plant is rich in medicinal properties, containing compounds such as glucosinolates, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Rorippa islandica var. pusilla include its potential to reduce inflammation and improve immune function, as well as its use as a natural remedy for digestive issues and respiratory problems.

The plant can be consumed fresh in salads or as a cooked green, and it is also used to make herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, although more research is needed to fully understand its effects and optimal uses.
