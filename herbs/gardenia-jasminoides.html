Gardenia Jasminoides Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Gardenia jasminoides, commonly known as the gardenia or frangipani, is a flowering plant native to Asia, renowned for its fragrant white flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb is valued for its ability to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression.

The bioactive constituents in Gardenia jasminoides include alkaloids, flavonoids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from the plant to harness its beneficial properties.
