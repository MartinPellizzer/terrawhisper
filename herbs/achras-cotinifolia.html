Possible Side Effects of Achras cotinifolia

Achras cotinifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea and nausea, due to its high saponin content.

Prolonged consumption of the plant's extract can also lead to kidney damage and impaired liver function in some individuals.

Additionally, achras cotinifolia may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions like high blood pressure.
