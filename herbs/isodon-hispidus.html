What's the taxonomical classification of Isodon hispidus?

Isodon hispidus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic designation is defined by the genus Isodon and the species hispidus.
