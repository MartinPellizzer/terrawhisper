Salix phylicifolia var. cordifolia

Salix phylicifolia var. cordifolia, commonly known as Cordleaf Willow or Irish Weeping Willow, is a variety of the Salix phylicifolia plant, a type of willow tree native to Ireland and other parts of Europe.

The leaves of this plant have been used in traditional medicine for their health benefits, which include reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.

The therapeutic actions of Salix phylicifolia var. cordifolia are primarily attributed to its ability to act as a diuretic, and to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
