What's the taxonomical classification of Sedum celatum?

Sedum celatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This succulent plant is further organized into the order Saxifrages (Saxifragales) and falls under the family Crassulaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Sedum, with the specific epithet celatum, forming the full scientific name Sedum celatum.
