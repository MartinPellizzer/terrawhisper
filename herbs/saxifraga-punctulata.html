Saxifraga punctulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Saxifragales and the family Saxifragaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Saxifraga, with its specific identification being the species punctulata.
