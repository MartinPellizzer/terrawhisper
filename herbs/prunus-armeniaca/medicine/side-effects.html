10 Possible Side Effects Of Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot)

Prunus armeniaca has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

Causes of these side effects may include ingesting excessive amounts, having pre-existing allergies or sensitivities, or taking the herb with certain medications, such as blood thinners or diabetes medications.

For instance, gastrointestinal issues can worsen irritable bowel syndrome, while allergic reactions can exacerbate skin conditions like eczema.
