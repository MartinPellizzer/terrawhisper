Dendropanax chevalieri

What's the taxonomical classification of Dendropanax chevalieri?

Dendropanax chevalieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. The plant is further categorized into the family Araliaceae, which serves as the taxonomic home for the genus Dendropanax, and is specifically identified by its species name, chevalieri.
