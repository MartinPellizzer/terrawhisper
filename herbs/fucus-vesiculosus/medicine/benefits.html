Fucus vesiculosus supports immune system because it is rich in fucoidan, a polysaccharide with potent immunomodulatory properties.

Fucus vesiculosus fights cancer cells because of its rich content of bioactive compounds, including fucoidan and lignans.

Fucus vesiculosus lowers cholesterol levels because it contains fucoidan, a sulfated polysaccharide with antioxidant properties.
