10 Medicinal Constituents Of Salvia Miltiorrhiza (Danshen)

Salvia miltiorrhiza has active constituents such as diterpenoids, phenolic acids, and volatile oils.

These constituents are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and cardiovascular-protective effects. Danshen's constituents have been shown to improve blood circulation, reduce platelet aggregation, and lower blood pressure, which can help alleviate symptoms of cardiovascular disease.

For example, danshen has been used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat angina pectoris and myocardial infarction, potentially improving the quality of life for patients with these conditions.
