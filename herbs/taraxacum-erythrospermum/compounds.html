Red-Seed Dandelion (Taraxacum erythrospermum) Compounds

Red-Seed Dandelion contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin contributes to its bitter taste and may have anticoagulant properties. Terpenoids and glycosides also play roles in its medicinal effects, while saponins and tannins support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
