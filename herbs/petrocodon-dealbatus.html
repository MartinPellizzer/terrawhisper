What's the taxonomical classification of Petrocodon dealbatus?

Petrocodon dealbatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically falling within the genus Petrocodon, and is identified by its unique species name, dealbatus.
