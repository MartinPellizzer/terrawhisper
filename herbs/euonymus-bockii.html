What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus bockii?

Euonymus bockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Celastraceae, falling into the genus Euonymus, and is specifically identified by its species name, bockii.
