What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus bockii?

Euonymus bockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Celastrales. It is further categorized under the family Celastraceae, falling into the genus Euonymus, and is specifically identified by its species name, bockii.
