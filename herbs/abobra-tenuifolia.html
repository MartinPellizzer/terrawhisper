Abobra tenuifolia

Abobra tenuifolia, also known as the Brazilian passionflower, is a plant species native to the tropical regions of South America.

The medicinal properties of Abobra tenuifolia include its use in treating anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness due to its sedative and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to reduce stress, promote relaxation, and improve sleep quality.

Abobra tenuifolia can be prepared in various herbal forms, including teas, tinctures, and capsules, allowing for a convenient and accessible way to harness its medicinal properties.
