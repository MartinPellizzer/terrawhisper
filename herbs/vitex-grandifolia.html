What's the taxonomical classification of Vitex grandifolia?

Vitex grandifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Lamiaceae, which places it within the genus Vitex, specifically identified by its species name, grandiifolia.

The plant produces terminal panicles of small, tubular flowers that are usually violet or blue in color.
