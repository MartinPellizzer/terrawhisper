Satureja incana

Satureja incana, also known as winter savory, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

The plant's leaves and flowers have been used for centuries in traditional medicine, exhibiting anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties.

Winter savory is believed to provide various health benefits, including relief from digestive issues, reduction of stress and anxiety, and improvement of respiratory problems.

The leaves and flowers can be prepared into teas, infusions, or added to meals as a culinary herb, making it a versatile addition to herbal remedies.
