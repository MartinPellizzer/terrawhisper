Epiphyllum purpusii

Epiphyllum purpusii, also known as the Purpus' Epiphyllum, is a species of flowering epiphytic cactus native to Mexico.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to treat fever, pain, and inflammation due to its high concentration of flavonoids and terpenoids.

The health benefits of Epiphyllum purpusii include its potential to reduce oxidative stress, improve cardiovascular health, and exhibit antimicrobial properties.

Traditionally, the plant is prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures for its medicinal use, although further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
