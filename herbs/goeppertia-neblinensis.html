Bioactive Constituents of Goeppertia neblinensis

Goeppertia neblinensis has many active constituents, such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which exhibit medicinal properties that improve people's daily lives.

Possible Side Effects of Goeppertia neblinensis

Goeppertia neblinensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including diarrhea and nausea, due to its high levels of saponins.

Ingestion of large quantities can also lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rashes and itching.

Furthermore, improper use of the plant can cause respiratory issues, including difficulty breathing and coughing, especially in individuals with pre-existing conditions like asthma.

Additionally, goeppertia neblinensis may interact with certain medications, exacerbating conditions like high blood pressure and kidney disease.
