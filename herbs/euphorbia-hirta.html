Euphorbia Hirta Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Euphorbia hirta, commonly known as greater spurge or Indian spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia and parts of Africa, known for its medicinal properties.

This herb is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which support overall health and immune function.

The bioactive constituents in Euphorbia hirta include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and stems to harness its healing properties.
