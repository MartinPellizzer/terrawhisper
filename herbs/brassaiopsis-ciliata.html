What's the taxonomical classification of Brassaiopsis ciliata?

Brassaiopsis ciliata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Araliaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Brassaiopsis, and its species designation, ciliata.
