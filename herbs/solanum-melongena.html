Possible Side Effects of solanum melongena

Solanum melongena can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

Improper use of the herb can lead to symptoms like nausea, diarrhea, and stomach pain.

In severe cases, it may also cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention.

Additionally, consuming large quantities of Solanum melongena can lead to nicotinic acetylcholine receptor overstimulation, resulting in muscle weakness, fatigue, and tremors.
