Isachne globosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Isachne globosa?

Isachne globosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Poales, it is placed into the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Isachne, and its unique species designation, globosa.
