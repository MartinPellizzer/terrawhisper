Dolomiaea berardioidea

What's the taxonomical classification of Dolomiaea berardioidea?

Dolomiaea berardioidea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Asterales. It is further categorized under the family Asteraceae, which places it among the diverse group of composite flowers. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Dolomiaea, specifically representing the species berardioidea.
