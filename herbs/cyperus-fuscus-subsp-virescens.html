Cyperus fuscus subsp. virescens

Cyperus fuscus subsp.

virescens is a subspecies of the Cyperus fuscus sedge, a perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which are beneficial in treating skin conditions and wounds.

The health benefits of Cyperus fuscus subsp.

virescens include reducing inflammation, promoting wound healing, and aiding in the treatment of various skin conditions, such as eczema and acne.

The plant can be prepared as a herbal remedy by infusing its roots and rhizomes in water to make a decoction or by using it as a topical cream or paste.
