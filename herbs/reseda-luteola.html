Reseda Luteola Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Reseda luteola, commonly known as yellow reseda, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and mild diuretic effects, which contribute to its use in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents in Reseda luteola include flavonoids, tannins, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or decoction to support urinary health and skin conditions.
