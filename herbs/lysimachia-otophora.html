Lysimachia otophora

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia otophora?

Lysimachia otophora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ericales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant within the family Primulaceae, specifically belonging to the genus Lysimachia, with its unique designation being the species otophora.
