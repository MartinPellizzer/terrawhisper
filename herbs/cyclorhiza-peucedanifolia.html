What's the taxonomical classification of Cyclorhiza peucedanifolia?

Cyclorhiza peucedanifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Specifically, this plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, which is the characteristic group for this taxon. Its taxonomic hierarchy is completed by its designation within the genus Cyclorhiza, with the specific epithet peucedanifolia identifying the individual species.
