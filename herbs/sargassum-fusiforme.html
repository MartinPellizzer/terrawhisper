Sargassum Seaweed (Sargassum fusiforme)

Sargassum Seaweed (Sargassum fusiforme) is a member of the Sargassaceae family, native to East China Sea, South China Sea, and Northwest Pacific Ocean. Traditionally, its stems, leaves, and resin have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
