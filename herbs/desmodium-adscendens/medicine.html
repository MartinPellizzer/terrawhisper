What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Desmodium Adscendens (Sticktight)?

Desmodium adscendens, also known as sticktight, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and treating fever.

The herb is rich in flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties. It is used to prepare infusions and decoctions, which can be used to treat various ailments. However, excessive consumption of the herb can cause nausea and stomach discomfort.

Precautions should be taken when using Desmodium adscendens, especially for pregnant and breastfeeding women, as its effects on the fetus and infant are not well established.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Desmodium adscendens.

Desmodium adscendens has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and fever.

What are the medicinal preparations of Desmodium adscendens?

Desmodium adscendens has different medicinal preparations, such as leaf and root infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

The leaf infusion is used to treat fever, coughs, and respiratory issues, while the root decoction is utilized for its antiseptic properties to heal wounds and abscesses. The leaf poultice is applied topically to relieve skin irritations and itching, and the root poultice is used to treat joint pain and arthritis.

These preparations demonstrate the plant's versatility in treating various ailments and promoting overall well-being.

What are the possible side effect of using Desmodium adscendens improperly?

Improper use of Desmodium adscendens increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as digestive issues like nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, respiratory problems including asthma-like symptoms and bronchitis, allergic reactions that may lead to hives and anaphylaxis, cardiovascular concerns including palpitations and arrhythmias, kidney damage, liver toxicity, hormonal imbalances, increased risk of bleeding, and even neurological complications like headaches, tremors, and seizures.
