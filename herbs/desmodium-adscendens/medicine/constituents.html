10 Medicinal Constituents Of Desmodium Adscendens (Sticktight)

Desmodium adscendens has active constituents such as alkaloids, saponins, flavonoids, and terpenoids.

These bioactive compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antibacterial properties, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine. The plant's medicinal properties can alleviate respiratory issues, reduce fever, and soothe skin irritations.

For instance, the antioxidants in sticktight may help mitigate oxidative stress, while its antibacterial properties can aid in wound healing and prevent infections.
