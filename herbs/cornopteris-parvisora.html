What is Cornopteris parvisora?

Cornopteris parvisora is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a perennial plant with a rhizome and a leafy frond, which is lanceolate and 2-3 times pinnate, with linear to lanceolate pinnae and crenate or dentate margins. The plant has a length of 15-30 cm and a width of 5-10 cm, with a dark green color and a waxy texture. The frond has a distinct midrib and a petiole that is attached to the rhizome via a sheath.

The plant is native to the tropical regions of Asia and has a rare distribution.
