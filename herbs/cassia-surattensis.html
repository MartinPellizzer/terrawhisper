What's the taxonomical classification of Cassia surattensis?

Cassia surattensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. As a member of the Fabaceae family, it is placed in the genus Cassia, eventually reaching its specific identification as the species surattensis.
