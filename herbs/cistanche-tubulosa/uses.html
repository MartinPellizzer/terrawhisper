Cistanche (Cistanche tubulosa) Uses

Cistanche tubulosa is used to treat fatigue and sexual dysfunction by boosting vitality and enhancing libido. It supports kidney health, helping with impotence and urinary incontinence. It also aids in managing diabetes and digestive issues like constipation and diarrhea. Its properties may alleviate menopausal symptoms and reduce the risk of bladder infections.

Cistanche (Cistanche tubulosa) Monograph

1. Fatigue

Cistanche tubulosa is used to treat fatigue by enhancing energy levels and improving physical endurance. It supports vitality by nourishing the kidneys and promoting the production of essential bodily fluids. This herb is particularly valued in traditional medicine for its ability to combat exhaustion and restore strength.

2. Diabetes

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney function and improve glucose metabolism. It may help regulate blood sugar levels by enhancing insulin sensitivity and promoting glucose uptake in cells, making it a potential adjunct in diabetes management.

3. Menopausal symptoms

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes and night sweats. It is believed to support hormonal balance and enhance vitality. Often combined with other herbs, it helps reduce discomfort and improve overall well-being during menopause.

4. Impotence

Cistanche tubulosa is traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat impotence by enhancing kidney function and improving blood circulation. It is believed to strengthen vitality and support sexual health, making it a key herb in formulations aimed at addressing male infertility and low libido.

5. Sexual dysfunction

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to address sexual dysfunction by enhancing vitality and promoting kidney health. It is believed to improve libido and support reproductive function, particularly in men experiencing erectile difficulties or reduced sexual desire. Its warming properties are thought to stimulate circulation and balance bodily energies.

6. Constipation

Cistanche tubulosa is used to treat constipation by promoting intestinal motility and reducing dryness in the large intestine. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its laxative effects. This herb is particularly effective for elderly individuals or those with chronic constipation due to its gentle yet potent action.

7. Diarrhea

Cistanche tubulosa is used to treat diarrhea by strengthening the digestive system and reducing intestinal inflammation. It helps regulate bowel movements and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in traditional herbal medicine.

8. Kidney dysfunction

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney function, particularly in cases of kidney deficiency. It is believed to enhance vitality, improve urine production, and address symptoms like fatigue and impotence. Its active compounds may help regulate kidney health by promoting metabolic balance and improving circulation.

9. Urinary incontinence

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to address urinary incontinence by strengthening the kidney and bladder functions. It is often combined with other herbs to improve pelvic floor tone and reduce leakage. Its effects are believed to support overall urinary control and vitality.

10. Bladder infection

Cistanche tubulosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney function and may help alleviate symptoms of bladder infections by promoting urinary health. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties, aiding in the treatment of urinary tract infections and related conditions.
