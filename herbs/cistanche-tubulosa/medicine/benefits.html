10 Health Benefits Of Cistanche Tubulosa (Desert Truffles)

Cistanche tubulosa, also known as desert truffles, has health benefits such as anti-aging, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

Its bioactive compounds, including saponins and phenolic acids, contribute to these benefits. Studies have shown that consuming Cistanche can improve cognitive function, boost immune systems, and protect against chronic diseases.

For instance, individuals with Alzheimer's disease may experience improved memory and mental clarity, while those with compromised immune systems may recover faster from illnesses, ultimately enhancing their overall quality of life.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Cistanche tubulosa.

1. Helps lower blood sugar

Cistanche tubulosa helps lower blood sugar because it stimulates insulin secretion and improves insulin sensitivity.

The polysaccharides present in the truffle have been shown to activate the AMPK pathway, which regulates glucose metabolism.

Additionally, Cistanche tubulosa has antioxidant properties that reduce oxidative stress, a common factor in the development of insulin resistance and high blood sugar.

2. Reduces cholesterol levels

Cistanche tubulosa reduces cholesterol levels because of its unique bioactive compounds and antioxidants.

Studies have shown that the extracts of Cistanche tubulosa inhibit the production of low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol, which is associated with cardiovascular disease. Additionally, it has been found to increase the levels of high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol, which is considered beneficial for heart health.

This dual action contributes to the cholesterol-lowering effects of Cistanche tubulosa.

3. Improves digestive health

Cistanche tubulosa improves digestive health because it contains prebiotic fibers that help feed good bacteria in the gut, promoting a balanced gut microbiome.

Additionally, its high water content and fiber content can help regulate bowel movements and reduce symptoms of constipation. This natural remedy has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to boost overall digestive health and wellness.

Regular consumption may support a healthy gut.

4. Boosts immune system

Cistanche tubulosa boosts immune system because it is rich in antioxidants, polysaccharides, and saponins.

These bioactive compounds help stimulate the production of white blood cells, which fight off infections and diseases. Additionally, Cistanche tubulosa contains immunomodulatory properties that enhance the body's natural defense mechanism, protecting it from pathogens and inflammation, thereby maintaining overall health and well-being.

Regular consumption may lead to improved immune function.

5. Relieves stress symptoms

Cistanche tubulosa relieves stress symptoms because it contains various bioactive compounds that interact with the body's neurotransmitters and hormones, promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety.

It has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to enhance mental clarity and calmness, and its antioxidant properties help to regulate the body's response to stress, leading to a sense of well-being and calmness.

6. Increases energy levels

Cistanche tubulosa increases energy levels because it is a rich source of essential amino acids, vitamins, and minerals.

Its high antioxidant properties help to boost the body's natural energy production, while its adaptogenic properties enable the body to better manage stress, which can be a major energy drain.

Regular consumption of Cistanche tubulosa may also enhance physical endurance and mental clarity, leading to increased overall energy levels.

7. Supports weight loss

Cistanche tubulosa supports weight loss because it contains high levels of bioactive compounds that help reduce inflammation and improve metabolic function.

Studies have shown that consuming Cistanche tubulosa extract can increase lipolysis, the breakdown of fat, and enhance fat oxidation, leading to significant weight loss.

Its anti-obesity effects are also attributed to its ability to modulate gut microbiota and reduce cortisol levels.

8. Enhances cognitive function

Cistanche tubulosa enhances cognitive function because it contains bioactive compounds that promote neuroprotection and neuroregeneration.

These compounds, including saponins and phenolic acids, have been shown to improve memory, attention, and executive function by inhibiting oxidative stress and inflammation in the brain.

This results in improved cognitive performance and reduced risk of age-related cognitive decline and neurodegenerative diseases.

9. Regulates blood pressure

Cistanche tubulosa regulates blood pressure because it contains bioactive compounds that help to balance the body's internal environment.

Studies have shown that its extracts have vasodilatory and antihypertensive effects, improving blood vessel flexibility and reducing blood pressure. Regular consumption of Cistanche tubulosa may also help to reduce stress and anxiety, which are known to contribute to high blood pressure.

This natural remedy has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine.

10. Reduces inflammation markers

Cistanche tubulosa reduces inflammation markers because it is rich in antioxidants and polyphenols.

These compounds have potent anti-inflammatory properties that help mitigate oxidative stress and inflammation. Studies have shown that Cistanche tubulosa extract suppresses the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thereby reducing inflammation markers such as IL-6 and TNF-alpha.

This suggests its potential use in managing chronic inflammatory diseases.
