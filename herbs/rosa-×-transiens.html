What is Rosa × transiens?

Rosa × transiens is a sterile hybrid rose species within the genus Rosa.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is native to Europe. The plant is characterized by its deciduous shrubs or small trees with thorny stems, bearing pinnate leaves with 5-7 leaflets that are 2-4 cm long. Rosa × transiens produces solitary, cupped flowers with 5 petals, ranging in color from pink to white, with a diameter of 3-5 cm.

Flowers are followed by red, berry-like hips that are 2-3 cm long, containing 1-3 seeds.
