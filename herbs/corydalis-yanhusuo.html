Distribution

Yanhusuo Corydalis (Corydalis yanhusuo) is native to regions of China, particularly growing in the mountainous areas of Sichuan, Yunnan, and Guizhou provinces, as well as parts of Nepal and northern India.

Traditional Uses

Yanhusuo Corydalis has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its analgesic, sedative, and antispasmodic properties, primarily to relieve pain, calm the mind, and treat conditions such as menstrual cramps, headaches, and gastrointestinal spasms.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Yanhusuo Corydalis has been historically used for its analgesic and sedative properties, particularly in treating pain and menstrual disorders.
