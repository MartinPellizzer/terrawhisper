Corydalis Yanhusuo Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Corydalis yanhusuo, commonly known as Chinese Corydalis or Yanhusuo, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This herb is known for its analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antidepressant properties, making it beneficial for treating pain, inflammation, and mood disorders.

The therapeutic actions of Corydalis yanhusuo are attributed to bioactive constituents such as tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), tetrahydrocannabinolic acid (THCA), and other alkaloids.

It can be prepared in various forms, including infusions, tinctures, and decoctions, to harness its medicinal benefits.

Corydalis yanhusuo enhances circulation by promoting blood flow through its vasodilatory effects, which help to improve oxygen and nutrient delivery to tissues and reduce swelling.
