Physaliastrum heterophyllum

What's the taxonomical classification of Physaliastrum heterophyllum?

Physaliastrum heterophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Solanales and falls within the family Solanaceae. Finally, it is defined by its specific genus, Physaliastrum, and its unique species designation, heterophyllum.
