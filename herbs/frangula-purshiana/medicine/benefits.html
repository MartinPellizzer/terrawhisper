10 Health Benefits Of Frangula Purshiana (Cascara Sagrada)

Frangula purshiana has health benefits such as relieving constipation, soothing digestive issues, and reducing inflammation.

Its medicinal properties include anthraquinone glycosides, which stimulate the colon to move stool and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

By addressing these issues, cascara sagrada can improve overall health and quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic digestive problems, allowing them to feel more comfortable, energized, and confident in their daily activities.
