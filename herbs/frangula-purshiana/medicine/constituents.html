10 Medicinal Constituents Of Frangula Purshiana (Cascara Sagrada)

Frangula purshiana has active constituents such as anthraquinones, particularly aloe-emodin and rhein.

These compounds possess laxative properties, helping to regulate bowel movements and alleviate constipation. The anthraquinones also exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, making cascara sagrada a natural remedy for digestive issues.

Regular use may improve overall health and well-being by reducing the risk of constipation-related complications and promoting a balanced gut microbiome.
