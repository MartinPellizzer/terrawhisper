10 Possible Side Effects Of Frangula Purshiana (Cascara Sagrada)

Frangula purshiana has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, abdominal cramps, and stomach pain.

These side effects can occur when the herb is consumed in excess or combined with other medications, causing an imbalance of fluids and electrolytes in the body.

In severe cases, it can lead to dehydration, fatigue, and even kidney damage, worsening one's quality of life and daily activities.
