Avena sativa

Avena sativa, also known as oats or common oat, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and soothing effects on the nervous system.

The health benefits of avena sativa include reducing stress and anxiety, improving sleep quality, and alleviating symptoms of menopause and PMS, as well as soothing digestive issues such as constipation and diarrhea.

Avena sativa is often prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or powder, which can be taken internally to harness its medicinal properties.
