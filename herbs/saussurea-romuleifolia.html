Saussurea romuleifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea romuleifolia?

Saussurea romuleifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the taxonomic order of Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Saussurea, with its specific designation being the species romuleifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

At the apex of this stem, small, yellowish-white flower heads are arranged in a compact, terminal cluster.
