Lysimachia decurrens has decurrent leaf bases that extend down the stem, forming narrow, winged margins along the internodes. The leaves are typically arranged in an opposite pattern and possess an elliptical to lanceolate shape. These plants feature slender, erect stems that often grow in dense, herbaceous clumps. The flowers are characterized by five bright yellow petals that form a star-like shape when fully open.
