10 Health Benefits Of Cinchona Calisaya (Jesuit'S Bark)

Cinchona calisaya, also known as Jesuit's bark, has health benefits such as reducing fever and relieving malaria symptoms.

Its medicinal properties include quinine, a compound that inhibits the growth of Plasmodium parasites, which cause the disease. This natural remedy has improved lives by providing a safe and effective treatment for millions of people worldwide, especially in areas where access to modern healthcare is limited.

With Jesuit's bark, patients can recover from malaria more quickly and without the risk of severe side effects associated with synthetic medications.
