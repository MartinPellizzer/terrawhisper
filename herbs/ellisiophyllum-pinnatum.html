Ellisiophyllum pinnatum has pinnately compound leaves characterized by multiple pairs of leaflets arranged along a central axis. The venation pattern within the leaflets is distinct, often showing secondary veins that arch toward the leaf tip. The plant structure also features specialized reproductive organs that are integrated into its overall vegetative form.
