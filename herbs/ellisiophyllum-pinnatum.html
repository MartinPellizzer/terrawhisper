What's the taxonomical classification of Ellisiophyllum pinnatum?

Ellisiophyllum pinnatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Lamiales and into the family Plantaginaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Ellisiophyllum, with the specific identifier of the species pinnatum.
