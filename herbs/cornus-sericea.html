Green Corn Dog (Cornus sericea)

Green Corn Dog (Cornus sericea) is a member of the Cornaceae family, native to North America, Canada, and United States. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, astringent, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
