Delphinium omeiense

What's the taxonomical classification of Delphinium omeiense?

Delphinium omeiense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further classification reveals that it is a member of the order Ranunculales and falls under the family Ranunculaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Delphinium, and its unique species designation, omeiense.
