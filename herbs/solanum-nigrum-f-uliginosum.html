Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum

Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum, commonly known as marsh black nightshade or bog black nightshade, is a plant species within the Solanaceae family.

Possible Side Effects of Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum

Solanum nigrum f. uliginosum can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high solanine content.

In severe cases, excessive solanine intake may cause more serious health issues, including kidney damage and respiratory problems.

Additionally, the plant's high solanine levels can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions.
