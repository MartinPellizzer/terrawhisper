This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include reducing inflammation and acting as an anti-spasmodic to alleviate muscle cramps and menstrual pain.

The therapeutic actions of Corn Mint include its ability to stimulate digestion and relieve nausea, as well as its antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its potential in treating various health conditions.
