What is Mosiera del-riscoi?

Mosiera del-riscoi is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is an evergreen shrub or small tree, typically growing 3-6 meters tall, with a single stem and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, measuring 2-4 centimeters long and 0.8-1.5 centimeters wide, with a wavy margin and a petiole 0.2-0.5 centimeters long. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing small, white flowers with a five-parted calyx and a four-parted corolla, 0.4-0.7 centimeters in diameter.

The fruits are small, yellowish-green berries, approximately 0.5-1.5 centimeters in diameter, containing 3-5 seeds.
