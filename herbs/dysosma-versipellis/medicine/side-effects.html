Dysosma versipellis enhances sensitivity to light when used.

Dysosma versipellis produces nausea and vomiting due to its active compounds, including alkaloids and saponins.

As a result, users may experience unpleasant symptoms when consuming Dysosma versipellis.

10. Aggravates existing conditions

Dysosma versipellis aggravates existing conditions such as digestive issues and respiratory problems.

The compound berberine present in its leaves and rhizomes is thought to be the primary culprit, as it can stimulate the gastrointestinal tract and cause increased mucus production in the lungs, potentially exacerbating symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, chronic bronchitis, or asthma.

This may lead to discomfort, bloating, and difficulty breathing for individuals who already struggle with these conditions.
