What's the taxonomical classification of Symplocos congesta?

Symplocos congesta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ericales and the family Symplocaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Symplocos, and its specific species epithet, congesta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Symplocos congesta has simple, alternate, oblong to elliptic leaves that are typically leathery with entire margins and acute or obtuse tips. Its stems are woody and can develop a somewhat rough texture as the plant matures into a shrub or small tree. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in dense, axillary clusters, which is reflected in the specific epithet congesta.
