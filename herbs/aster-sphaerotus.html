Aster sphaerotus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Furthermore, it is organized under the order Asterales and falls within the family Asteraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Aster, and its unique species designation, sphaerotus.
