Berberis Vulgaris Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Berberis vulgaris, commonly known as barberry, is a shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its yellow berries and sharp spines.

This herb is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally to support digestive and immune health.

The bioactive constituents in Berberis vulgaris include berberine, which has antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from its roots and berries to harness its therapeutic benefits.
