Koellia virginica

Koellia virginica is a plant species that belongs to the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the Koellia or American Koellia.

The plant is used for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional medicine, where it is valued for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

Koellia virginica is said to have various health benefits, including reducing inflammation, treating wounds, and relieving respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.

Traditionally, the plant is prepared as a tea or infusion, as well as a tincture, to harness its medicinal properties and reap its health benefits.
