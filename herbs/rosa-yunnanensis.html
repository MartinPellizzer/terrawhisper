Rosa yunnanensis

Rosa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rose, is a species of rose plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include treating various health issues such as fever, rheumatism, and inflammation.

The health benefits of Rosa yunnanensis include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which have been traditionally used to reduce pain and promote wound healing.

The therapeutic actions of this plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and volatile oils, which work to modulate the body's response to stress and promote overall well-being.
