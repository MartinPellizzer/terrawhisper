The scientific name of Withania coagulans is Withania coagulans. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Spermatophyta, class Caryophyllopsida, order Solanales, family Solanaceae, genus Withania, and species Withania coagulans. It is commonly known as the coagulant withania in South Asian regions and the wild tobacco plant in parts of Africa and the Middle East. The synonyms for this plant include Withania coagulans, Withania coccinea, and regional names such as kachur in India and dhanvantri in Nepal.
