According to a study published by "Molecules (Basel, Switzerland)", Melastomast normale contains polyphenols with potential anti-inflammatory, anti-tyrosinase, and anti-obesity cellular protective effects. The plant's root extract yielded four new ellagitannins, labeled 1, 2, 3, and 4, alongside 8 known polyphenols. In tests using LPS-stimulated RAW 264.7 cells, compounds 1-4 failed to inhibit nitric oxide production with IC50 values all > 50 μM. Regarding tyrosinase inhibition, compound 1 demonstrated weak activity with an IC50 value of 426.02 ± 11.31 μM. Meanwhile, compounds 2-4 displayed moderate tyrosinase inhibitory activities with IC50 values ranging from 124.74 ± 3.12 to 241.41 ± 6.23 μM.
