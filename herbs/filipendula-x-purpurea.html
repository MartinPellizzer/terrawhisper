Filipendula × purpurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Filipendula × purpurea has erect, rhizomatous stems that produce pinnate, compound leaves with serrated leaflets and clusters of small, deep pink to purplish flowers. Its inflorescences are typically dense, terminal panicles that bloom in mid-to-late summer. The stems can reach significant heights, often supporting heavy floral displays that sway in the wind.
