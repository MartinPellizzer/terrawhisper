Justicia prachuapensis

The therapeutic actions of Justicia prachuapensis include its ability to reduce inflammation, exhibit antimicrobial activity, and protect against oxidative stress.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and diterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of Justicia prachuapensis

Justicia prachuapensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing allergic reactions, causing skin irritation, and leading to interactions with medications.

Overconsumption of the plant's extract may result in stomach issues, diarrhea, and nausea.
