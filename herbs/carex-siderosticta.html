What's the taxonomical classification of Carex siderosticta?

Carex siderosticta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Poaales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Carex, specifically identified by its species name, siderosticta.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including respiratory infections, stomach aches, and skin inflammations. The medicinal properties of its rhizomes and leaves are often utilized to alleviate symptoms of coughs and colds. Local practitioners also apply crushed parts of the plant to wounds to prevent infection and promote healing. Additionally, decoctions made from the plant are sometimes consumed to settle digestive disturbances. These traditional applications highlight its importance in regional herbal medicine.
