What's the taxonomical classification of Mussaenda hirsutula?

Mussaenda hirsutula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Mussaenda, specifically identified by its species name, hirsutula.
