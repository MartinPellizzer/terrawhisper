Rinorea latifolia

Rinorea latifolia, also known as the broad-leaved Rinorea or the Chinese clerodendrum, is a plant species in the family Violaceae.

The plant is traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Rinorea latifolia include the treatment of fever, cough, and respiratory infections, as well as its use as an anti-aging agent due to its high antioxidant content.

Rinorea latifolia can be prepared into various herbal forms, including teas, infusions, and extracts, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote overall well-being.
