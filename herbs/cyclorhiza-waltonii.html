Cyclorhiza waltonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Apiaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Cyclorhiza and the species waltonii.
