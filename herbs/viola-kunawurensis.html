Viola kunawurensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Viola kunawurensis?

Viola kunawurensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Malpighiales. As a member of the family Violaceae, it is categorized under the genus Viola, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species Viola kunawurensis.
