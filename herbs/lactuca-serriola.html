Lactuca Serriola Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Lactuca serriola, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a wild plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its hairy leaves and yellow flowers.

This herb has been traditionally used for its calming and sedative properties, offering potential health benefits such as reducing anxiety, promoting sleep, and alleviating digestive issues.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include lactucin, lactucopicrin, and various flavonoids, which contribute to its sedative and anti-inflammatory effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from the leaves and flowers to harness its medicinal properties.
