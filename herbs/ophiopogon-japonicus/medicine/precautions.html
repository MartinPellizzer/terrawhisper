10 Precautions To Take When Using Ophiopogon Japonicus (Mondo Grass)

Ophiopogon japonicus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding excessive doses and pregnant or breastfeeding women.

Taking high doses may cause gastrointestinal upset, such as diarrhea and stomach discomfort. For pregnant or breastfeeding women, consuming this herb can stimulate uterine contractions, leading to miscarriage or premature labor.

Additionally, individuals with bleeding disorders or taking anticoagulant medications should consult a healthcare professional due to potential interactions.
