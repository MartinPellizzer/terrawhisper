10 Best Pilocarpus Microphyllus Health Benefits

The plant also has stimulating effects on the nervous system, potentially enhancing mental alertness and cognitive function. Additionally, it may aid in reducing symptoms of colds and respiratory issues due to its expectorant properties.

Pilocarpus microphyllus is also used in traditional medicine for its potential to support digestion and reduce stress.
