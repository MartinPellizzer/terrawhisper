What's the taxonomical classification of Leontopodium ochroleucum?

Leontopodium ochroleucum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated under the order Asterales. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its complex inflorescences. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Leontopodium, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name ochroleucum.
