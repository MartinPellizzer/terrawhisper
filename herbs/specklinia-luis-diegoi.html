What is Specklinia luis-diegoi?

Specklinia luis-diegoi is a species of orchid within the family Orchidaceae.

The inflorescence is a raceme with several flowers, each having a white to yellowish-green labellum with a yellowish-green column and sepals, and a white to yellowish-green petals. The plant grows in shaded, humid environments at elevations between 1,200 and 2,300 meters above sea level in Mexico.

Specklinia luis-diegoi is endemic to the Mexican states of Chiapas and Oaxaca.
