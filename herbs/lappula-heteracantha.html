What's the taxonomical classification of Lappula heteracantha?

Lappula heteracantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae, which is part of the order Boraginales. This plant is a member of the family Boraginaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Lappula, with its unique designation being the species heteracantha.
