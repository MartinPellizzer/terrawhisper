10 Precautions To Take When Using Teucrium Canadense

Teucrium canadense has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential interactions with certain medications, like blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Taking this herb with these medications can lead to bleeding complications or blood sugar imbalances, which may cause dizziness, headaches, or fatigue.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional to weigh the benefits and risks of using wood sage, especially if you're already taking medication for these conditions.
