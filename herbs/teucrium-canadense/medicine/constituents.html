10 Medicinal Constituents Of Teucrium Canadense (Wood Sage)

Teucrium canadense has active constituents such as flavonoids, iridoid glycosides, and diterpenoids.

These compounds provide the plant with its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic effects. The flavonoids contribute to the plant's antioxidant capabilities, while the iridoid glycosides exhibit spasmolytic and bronchodilatory activities.

For example, the constituents of wood sage have been used to treat respiratory issues such as asthma and bronchitis, improving breathing and overall quality of life for individuals affected by these conditions.
