Possible Side Effects of Senecio glandulosus

Senecio glandulosus can have side effects if used improperly, such as liver damage and increased risk of liver disease due to the presence of pyrrolizidine alkaloids.

Prolonged consumption of the plant may also lead to symptoms like nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.

In severe cases, improper use of Senecio glandulosus can cause pulmonary hypertension and even death.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using the plant for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
