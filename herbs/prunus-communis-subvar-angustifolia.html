The therapeutic actions of the narrow-leaved plum include reducing fever, relieving cough and respiratory issues, and supporting digestive health.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of the plant's extracts can lead to cyanide poisoning, a life-threatening condition.

Other potential side effects include allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing, especially in individuals with pre-existing allergies.

Additionally, long-term use of Prunus communis subvar. angustifolia extracts may interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications.
