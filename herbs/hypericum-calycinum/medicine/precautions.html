10 Precautions To Take When Using Hypericum Calycinum (St John'S Wort)

Hypericum calycinum, also known as St John's Wort, has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional to discuss potential interactions with prescription medications, including antidepressants, blood thinners, and immunosuppressants.

This is crucial to avoid adverse effects, as taking St John's Wort with these medications can lead to increased risk of serotonin syndrome, bleeding, or organ transplant rejection.

For instance, not taking this precaution may result in severe bleeding or worsening of existing conditions.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Hypericum calycinum medicinally.
