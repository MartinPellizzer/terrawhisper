10 Medicinal Constituents Of Hypericum Calycinum (St John'S Wort)

Hypericum calycinum has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and hyperforin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

These compounds have shown antidepressant, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, thereby alleviating symptoms of mild to moderate depression, anxiety, and insomnia.

For example, the hyperforin content helps regulate mood, while flavonoids and phenolic acids provide pain relief, thereby improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.
