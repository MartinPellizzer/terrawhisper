Symplocos stellaris belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Ericales and is a member of the family Symplocaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Symplocos and the species stellaris.
