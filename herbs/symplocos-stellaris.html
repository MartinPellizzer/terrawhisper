Symplocos stellaris

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Symplocos stellaris has simple, elliptic to oblong leaves that are dark green, leathery, and feature prominently parallel-veined surfaces. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub with smooth, brownish bark and a compact branching habit. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in axillary clusters throughout the canopy. The fruit develops into small, fleshy drupes that transition in color as they mature.
