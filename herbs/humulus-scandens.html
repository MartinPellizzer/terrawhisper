Humulus scandens

Humulus scandens, commonly known as Japanese hops or American hops, is a perennial climbing plant of the hemp family (Cannabaceae).

It is used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, which include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anti-anxiety properties.

The therapeutic actions of Humulus scandens include sedation, relaxation, and pain relief, making it a popular herbal remedy for stress and insomnia.

The plant's bioactive constituents include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects and antioxidant properties.

Possible Side Effects of Humulus scandens
