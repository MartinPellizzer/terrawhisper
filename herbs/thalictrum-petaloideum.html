What's the taxonomical classification of Thalictrum petaloideum?

Thalictrum petaloideuom belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, falling under the genus Thalictrum, and is specifically identified by its species name, petaloideuom.
