Leontopodium brachyactis

What's the taxonomical classification of Leontopodium brachyactis?

Leontopodium brachyactis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, a diverse group of flowering plants, and is specifically identified by its genus Leontopodium and its species designation brachyactis.
