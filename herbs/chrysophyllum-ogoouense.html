Chrysophyllum ogoouense

What's the taxonomical classification of Chrysophyllum ogoouense?

Chrysophyllum ogoouense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is further categorized into the family Sapotaceae, within the genus Chrysophyllum, and is specifically identified by the species name ogoouense.

While specific clinical studies on human toxicity are limited, potential adverse effects may include gastrointestinal discomfort if any part of the plant is ingested improperly. Individuals with known sensitivities to the Sapotaceae family should exercise extreme caution when handling its leaves or fruit. Monitoring for unexpected dermatological or digestive responses is recommended during initial exposure. Always consult a medical professional if any symptoms arise after contact or consumption.
