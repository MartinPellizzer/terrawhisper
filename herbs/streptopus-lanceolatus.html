Streptopus lanceolatus

Streptopus lanceolatus, also known as twisted stalk, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Streptopus lanceolatus include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and support the immune system.

Herbal preparations of the plant can be made into teas, tinctures, and infusions, and are often used to treat colds, coughs, and other minor ailments.
