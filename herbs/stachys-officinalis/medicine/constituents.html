10 Medicinal Constituents Of Stachys Officinalis (Betony)

Stachys officinalis has active constituents such as caffeic acid, ursolic acid, and rosmarinic acid.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and neuroprotective properties, making betony an effective remedy for anxiety, insomnia, and memory loss. By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, betony constituents can alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, improving overall mental clarity and well-being.
