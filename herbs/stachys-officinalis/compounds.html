Wood Betony (Stachys officinalis) Compounds

Wood Betony contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Coumarin and essential oils provide mild sedative effects, while terpenoids and glycosides support its traditional use in respiratory and nervous system support. Saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal properties.
