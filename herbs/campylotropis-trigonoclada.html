Campylotropis trigonoclada

What's the taxonomical classification of Campylotropis trigonoclada?

Campylotropis trigonoclada belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. Further taxonomic refinement places it in the family Fabaceae, specifically within the genus Campylotropis, with its unique identification as the species trigonoclada.
