Elsholtzia heterophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Elsholtzia heterophylla?

Elsholtzia heterophyllia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Lamiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Lamiaceae, belonging to the genus Elsholtzia, and is specifically identified by its species name, heterophylla.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various inflammatory conditions, skin infections, and digestive issues in traditional medicinal practices. The bark and leaves are often prepared as decoctions to alleviate symptoms of stomach aches and intestinal parasites. Some cultures utilize the plant's extracts to manage skin ailments such as rashes or minor wounds through topical applications. Its properties are also believed to assist in reducing swelling and managing pain associated with joint inflammation. These applications highlight the plant's role in local ethnobotanical remedies.
