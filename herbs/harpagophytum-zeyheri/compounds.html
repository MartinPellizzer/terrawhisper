Feverfew (Harpagophytum zeyheri) Compounds

Feverfew contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties. Coumarin and saponins are also present, offering potential antispasmodic and digestive benefits. Other constituents like glycosides, tannins, and essential oils support its traditional use in treating headaches and inflammatory conditions.
