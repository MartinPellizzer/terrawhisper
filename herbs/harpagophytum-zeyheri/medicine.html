What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Harpagophytum Zeyheri (Devil'S Claw)?

Harpagophytum zeyheri, also known as devil's claw, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain.

Its medicinal constituents, including iridoid glycosides and harpagoside, contribute to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. Harpagophytum zeyheri is commonly prepared in capsule or tablet form, with a recommended dose of 3-4 grams per day. Common side effects include gastrointestinal upset and allergic reactions.

What are the health benefits of Harpagophytum zeyheri?

Harpagophytum zeyheri, also known as devil's claw, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain, particularly in the joints.

What are the possible side effect of using Harpagophytum zeyheri improperly?

Improper use of Harpagophytum zeyheri, also known as devil's claw, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

High doses may also lead to headaches, dizziness, and allergic reactions in some individuals.

Additionally, devil's claw may interact with certain medications, including blood thinners, diabetes medications, and sedatives, which can exacerbate side effects or lead to adverse health consequences.
