What's the taxonomical classification of Adiantum pedatum?

Adiantum pedatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within the family Pteridaceae, this plant is identified by the genus Adiantum, and it is specifically designated as the species pedatum.
