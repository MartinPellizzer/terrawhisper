Mentha × verticillata var. elata

Mentha × verticillata var. elata, commonly known as Elatior Mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family, known for its distinctive characteristics and fragrance.

Elatior Mint has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including reducing inflammation, alleviating digestive issues, and exhibiting antimicrobial properties.

The plant's therapeutic actions are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including rosmarinic acid, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic effects.

Additionally, Elatior Mint has been used to soothe headaches, improve respiratory health, and enhance cognitive function, making it a valuable plant for natural remedies and herbal medicine.
