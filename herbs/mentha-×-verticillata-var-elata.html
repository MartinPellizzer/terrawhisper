Mentha × verticillata var. elata

Mentha × verticillata var. elata, also known as Elata Peppermint or Elate Peppermint, is a natural hybrid of the peppermint plant, characterized by its unique vertical growth habit and compact leaves.

The medicinal properties of Mentha × verticillata var. elata include its high menthol content, which gives it a cooling effect and makes it a popular ingredient in various herbal remedies.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to aid digestion, relieve nausea and headaches, and provide relief from stress and anxiety, making it a valuable addition to herbal health routines.

Mentha × verticillata var. elata can be prepared into various herbal products, such as teas, tinctures, and essential oils, which can be used to harness its medicinal properties and promote overall well-being.
