What is Stelis ligulata?

Stelis ligulata is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short stem and ovate leaves, measuring 1-2 cm in length. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing 2-3 flowers, each 1-2 cm in diameter, with a yellow-brown labellum and a white dorsal sepal. The plant grows in tropical and subtropical regions, including Central and South America, from sea level to 1,500 meters in elevation.

The flowers are fragrant, blooming from May to July.
