What's the taxonomical classification of Cyperus michelianus?

Cyperus michelianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Poales and falls within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is placed in the genus Cyperus, with its specific designation being the species michelianus.
