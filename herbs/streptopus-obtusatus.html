Streptopus obtusatus

What's the taxonomical classification of Streptopus obtusatus?

Streptopus obtusatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical progression, it falls under the order Liliales and is a member of the family Liliaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Streptopus, specifically as the species obtusatus.
