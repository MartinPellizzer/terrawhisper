Annona Squamosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Annona squamosa, commonly known as custard apple, is a tropical fruit-bearing tree native to the Americas, known for its fragrant, sweet fruit.

This herb is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments.

The health benefits of Annona squamosa include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which support immune function and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves, bark, and fruit to harness its medicinal properties.
