Corydalis laucheana

What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis laucheana?

Corydalis laucheana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ranunculales and sits within the family Papaveraceae. Finally, its taxonomic position is completed by its placement in the genus Corydalis, specifically as the species laucheana.
