Asyneuma chinense

What's the taxonomical classification of Asyneuma chinense?

Asyneuma chinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asterales and the family Campanulaceae. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Asyneuma, with the specific epithet chinense designating its unique species identity.
