Jasminum Sambac Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Jasminum sambac, commonly known as Arabian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Asia, renowned for its fragrant white flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb is widely used in traditional medicine for its calming and therapeutic effects on the mind and body.

Health benefits include stress relief, improved sleep, and anti-inflammatory properties, attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and essential oils.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from Jasminum sambac to harness its aromatic and therapeutic benefits.
