Paspalum scrobiculatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Paspalum, with its specific identification designated as the species scrobiculatum.

Its inflorescence consists of several distinct, spike-like panicles that are arranged in a somewhat contracted or spreading manner. Each spikelet is small and contains a single fertile floret, often displaying a subtle purplish hue during maturity.
