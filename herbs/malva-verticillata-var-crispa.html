Malva verticillata var. crispa

What's the taxonomical classification of Malva verticillata var. crispa?

Malva verticillata var. crispa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Malvales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Malvaceae and the genus Malva, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety Malva verticillata var. crispa.
