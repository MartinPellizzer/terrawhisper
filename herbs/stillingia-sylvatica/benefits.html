10 Best Stillingia Sylvatica Health Benefits

Stillingia sylvatica, also known as wild buckthorn, has been traditionally used for its potential health benefits, particularly in supporting digestive health.

It contains compounds such as anthraquinones, which may help stimulate bowel movements and alleviate constipation. Some studies suggest that it may have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that could contribute to overall well-being. However, it is important to note that the plant is toxic if ingested in large amounts and should be used with caution.

Due to its potency, it is generally recommended to consult a healthcare professional before using Stillingia sylvatica for medicinal purposes.
