What's the taxonomical classification of Illigera rhodantha?

Illigera rhodantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Laurales and is a member of the family Hernandiaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Illigera, with its unique classification completed as the species rhodantha.
