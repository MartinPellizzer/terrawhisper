Allium Cepa: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Allium cepa, commonly known as the onion, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family, widely cultivated for its bulbous stem.

It is rich in bioactive constituents such as sulfur compounds, flavonoids, and quercetin, which contribute to its various health benefits.

The herb is known for its therapeutic actions, including antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

It may support respiratory health, aid digestion, and help reduce oxidative stress in the body.

Allium cepa has many health benefits, such as reducing inflammation, which helps alleviate symptoms of various chronic conditions, and lowering blood pressure, allowing individuals to live a more energetic and active lifestyle.
