10 Possible Side Effects Of Allium Schoenoprasum (Chives)

Allium schoenoprasum has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin irritations.

These side effects are caused by individual sensitivities, excessive consumption, or poor handling of the herb.

In severe cases, allergic reactions can lead to anaphylaxis, causing individuals to experience respiratory distress, nausea, and dizziness, severely impacting their quality of life.
