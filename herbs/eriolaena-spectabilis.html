Eriolaena spectabilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Eriolaena spectabilis?

Eriolaena spectabilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Malvales. It falls within the family Malvaceae and is represented by the genus Eriolaena, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species spectabilis.

Eriolaena spectabilis is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical paste derived from its various plant parts.
