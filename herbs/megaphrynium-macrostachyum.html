Megaphrinium macrostachyum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Zingiberales and is a member of the family Marantaceae. Finally, the specific classification is defined by its genus, Megaphrinium, and its unique species designation, macrostachyum.
