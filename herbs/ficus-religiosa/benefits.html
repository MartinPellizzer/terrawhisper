1. Lowers blood pressure

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a chronic condition characterized by elevated pressure in the arteries.

This condition occurs when the force of blood against the artery walls is consistently too high, potentially leading to serious health complications.

It is commonly experienced by adults, especially those with a family history of cardiovascular issues, poor diet, or sedentary lifestyles.

The ficus religiosa plant has been studied for its potential to lower blood pressure by promoting relaxation of blood vessels and reducing stress-related factors.

Incorporating this plant into one's environment may offer natural support in managing hypertension.

How this herb helps with lowers blood pressure?

Ficus religiosa lowers blood pressure because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which have vasodilatory and antioxidant properties.

These compounds help relax blood vessels, improving blood flow and reducing vascular resistance. Alkaloids like ficusine and flavonoids such as quercetin contribute to the plant's ability to regulate blood pressure naturally. The synergistic effect of these compounds supports cardiovascular health by reducing oxidative stress and inflammation.

Regular consumption of Ficus religiosa may therefore aid in maintaining healthy blood pressure levels.
