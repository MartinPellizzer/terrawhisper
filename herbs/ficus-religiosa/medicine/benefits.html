10 Health Benefits Of Ficus Religiosa (Bodhi Tree)

Ficus religiosa has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which can help alleviate various health issues.

The plant's leaves, bark, and seeds have been used in traditional medicine to treat fever, respiratory problems, and digestive issues. For example, its antimicrobial properties can aid in wound healing, while its anti-inflammatory effects can reduce pain and swelling.

By incorporating bodhi tree into one's healthcare routine, individuals may experience improved overall health and wellness, leading to enhanced quality of life.
