What's the taxonomical classification of Syzygium bullockii?

Syzygium bullockii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Myrtales and falls within the family Myrtaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Syzygium, with its specific designation being the species bullockii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
