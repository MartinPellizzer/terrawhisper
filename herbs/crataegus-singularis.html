Singleflower Hawthorn (Crataegus Singularis)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

Singleflower hawthorn, scientifically known as Crataegus singularis, is a rare medicinal herb native to parts of Asia and the Middle East, valued for its adaptogenic properties and unique therapeutic potential. This plant is traditionally used to support cardiovascular health, with its primary benefits including the regulation of heart rhythm, reduction of arterial stiffness, and enhancement of overall circulatory function. In traditional Chinese medicine and Ayurvedic practices, Singleflower hawthorn has been employed for centuries to treat anxiety, insomnia, and digestive ailments, reflecting its historical significance in holistic healing. Today, it is increasingly incorporated into modern wellness regimens as a natural supplement for stress management and heart health, often found in herbal teas and tinctures. Its distinctive floral aroma and rare active compound, such as flavonoids and oligomeric proanthocyanidins, contribute to its unique profile, making it a sought-after ingredient in both traditional and contemporary herbal medicine.
