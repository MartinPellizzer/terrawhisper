What's the taxonomical classification of Xylosma longifolia?

Xylosma longifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed under the order Malpighiales. This plant is a member of the family Salicaceae, falling specifically within the genus Xylosma, and is identified by its unique species name, longifolia.
