White Cinquefoil (Potentilla anserina) Actions

White Cinquefoil exhibits several therapeutic actions including astringent, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties. It acts as a tonic and bitter stimulant, aiding digestion and improving appetite. Additionally, it has carminative, sedative, antispasmodic, and expectorant effects, supporting respiratory and digestive health.
