Possible Side Effects of croton lechleri

In rare cases, it may also cause allergic reactions, skin irritation, or interact negatively with certain medications, exacerbating underlying health conditions.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Croton lechleri to minimize the risk of adverse effects.
