What's the taxonomical classification of Pollia condensata?

Pollia condensata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is positioned in the order Commelinaales. Within this order, it is the representative of the family Commelineaceae. Specifically, this plant is categorized under the genus Pollia, with the biological designation of the species condensata.
