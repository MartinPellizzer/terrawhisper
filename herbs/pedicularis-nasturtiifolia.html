What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis nasturtiifolia?

Pedicularis nasturtiifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Orobanchaceae and is further categorized into the genus Pedicularis, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species nasturtiifolia.
