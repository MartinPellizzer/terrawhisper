Devil'S Club (Oplopanax horridus) Uses

Devil's Club is traditionally used to address chronic fatigue and mental exhaustion, including conditions like chronic fatigue syndrome. It may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders by supporting nervous system function and promoting overall vitality. Its effects are believed to stem from its ability to reduce inflammation and support adrenal health.
