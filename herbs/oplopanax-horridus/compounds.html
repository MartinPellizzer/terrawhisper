Devil'S Club (Oplopanax horridus) Compounds

Devil's Club contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which are antioxidants, and phenolic acids, which have anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and antimicrobial effects. Terpenoids and glycosides are also present, supporting its traditional use in herbal medicine.
