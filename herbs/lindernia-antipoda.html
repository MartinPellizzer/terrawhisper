Lindernia antipoda

What's the taxonomical classification of Lindernia antipoda?

Lindernia antipoda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Lamiales. This specific plant is a representative of the family Linderniaceae and is categorized under the genus Lindernia, specifically identified by the species name antipoda.
