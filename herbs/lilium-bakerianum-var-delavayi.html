Lilium bakerianum var. delavayi

What's the taxonomical classification of Lilium bakerianum var. delavayi?

Lilium bakerianum var. delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Liliales. As a member of the family Liliaceae, it is placed in the genus Lilium, specifically categorized as the variety delavayi within the species Lilium bakerianum.
