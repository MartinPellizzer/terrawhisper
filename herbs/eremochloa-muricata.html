Eremochloa muricata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Poaceae, which characterizes its botanical structure. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Eremochlloa, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species name muricata.

What are the herbal preparations of Eremochloa muricata?

Eremochlloa muricata is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical poultice depending on the intended therapeutic use.
