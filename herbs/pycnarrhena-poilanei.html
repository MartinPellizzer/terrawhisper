Pycnarrhena poilanei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ranunculales and falls within the family Menispermaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Pycnarrhena and its designation as the species poilanei.
