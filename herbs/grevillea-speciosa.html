Grevillea speciosa

Grevillea speciosa, also known as the Red Spider Flower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat a variety of ailments, with its medicinal properties attributed to the presence of flavonoids and other bioactive compounds.

The plant has been reported to have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may provide health benefits such as reducing inflammation, fighting infections, and supporting immune function.
