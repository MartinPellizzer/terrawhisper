Lemon Balm (Melissa officinalis) Actions

Lemon balm exhibits several therapeutic actions, including sedative, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory properties. It also acts as a carminative, diuretic, and tonic, helping to soothe the nervous system and support digestive health. Its bitter and expectorant qualities aid in clearing mucus, while its astringent nature can help reduce excess secretions.
