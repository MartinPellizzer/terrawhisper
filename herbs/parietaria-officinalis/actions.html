Parietary (Parietaria officinalis) Actions

Parietaria officinalis exhibits several key therapeutic actions. It acts as an anti-inflammatory and diuretic, helping reduce swelling and support kidney function. Its bitter properties stimulate digestion, while its expectorant and tonic effects aid respiratory health and overall vitality. Additionally, it serves as a carminative, antispasmodic, astringent, sedative, and stimulant, addressing a range of health concerns.
