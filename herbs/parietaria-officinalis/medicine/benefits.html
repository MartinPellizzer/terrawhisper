10 Health Benefits Of Parietaria Officinalis (Pellitory)

Parietaria officinalis has health benefits such as relieving respiratory issues, reducing inflammation, and soothing digestive problems.

The plant's medicinal properties include its high levels of antioxidants, flavonoids, and volatile oils, which provide anti-inflammatory and expectorant effects. For example, pellitory tea can help alleviate asthma symptoms by loosening mucus in the airways, allowing for easier breathing.

Similarly, its anti-inflammatory properties can reduce pain and discomfort associated with digestive issues like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), improving overall quality of life.
