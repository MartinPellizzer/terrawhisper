10 Medicinal Preparations Of Parietaria Officinalis (Pellitory)

Parietaria officinalis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures.

These preparations are traditionally used to relieve pain and inflammation, particularly in the case of earaches, ear infections, and tonsillitis. The plant's anti-inflammatory properties also make it useful for reducing fever and alleviating respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma.

Additionally, its soothing properties make it a popular ingredient in topical creams and ointments.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Parietaria officinalis.
