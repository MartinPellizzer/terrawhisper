What's the taxonomical classification of Melanthera prostrata?

Melanthera prostrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy through the order Asterales, it is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Melanthera, and its unique species designation, prostrata.
