Cyanus sibiricus

Cyanus sibiricus, commonly known as Siberian cornflower, is a plant species in the daisy family.

It is used in traditional medicine due to its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Cyanus sibiricus include reducing inflammation, soothing digestive issues, and supporting the immune system.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
