Dillenia luzoniensis

Dillenia luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon Dillenia or Philippine Jabon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dilleniaceae.

Dillenia luzoniensis has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help to treat conditions such as fever, cough, and skin infections.
