10 Precautions To Take When Using Citrus Aurantium (Bitter Orange)

Citrus aurantium has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use in people with high blood pressure, as it can interact with medications and exacerbate hypertension.

Consuming excessive amounts can also cause anxiety, jitters, and palpitations. Failing to monitor dosage or ignore individual sensitivities may lead to adverse effects, such as insomnia, digestive issues, and heart problems.

Proper dosing and consultation with a healthcare professional are essential to minimize risks.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Citrus aurantium medicinally.

1. Store safely out of reach

When using Citrus aurantium medicinally, it's important to take under expert supervision because this herb has been linked to serious side effects when used improperly.

The stimulant properties of Citrus aurantium can cause high blood pressure, heart palpitations, and even stroke or death in rare cases.

Additionally, the herb's interactions with other medications, such as antidepressants and MAOIs, can be unpredictable and potentially dangerous.
