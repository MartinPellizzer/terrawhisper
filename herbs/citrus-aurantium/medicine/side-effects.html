10 Possible Side Effects Of Citrus Aurantium (Bitter Orange)

Citrus aurantium has some side effects when used improperly, such as palpitations, headaches, and nausea.

These side effects are often caused by the herb's stimulatory compounds, synephrine and pseudoephedrine, which can interact with certain medications and worsen conditions like high blood pressure and anxiety.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen a person's quality of life, leading to anxiety attacks, sleep disturbances, and decreased productivity.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Citrus aurantium if used imporperly.
