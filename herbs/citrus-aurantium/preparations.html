10 Best Citrus Aurantium Preparations

The best medicinal preparations of Citrus aurantium are teas, tinctures, capsules, decoctions, and essential oils, each offering unique benefits for health and wellness.

Tinctures provide a concentrated form of the herb, often used for mood regulation and energy support.

Capsules offer a convenient and standardized dose for daily use.

Decoctions, made by simmering the herb, are valued for their potency in addressing respiratory and circulatory ailments.

Essential oils extracted from the peel are commonly used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and mental clarity.
