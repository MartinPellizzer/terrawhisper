How this herb helps with boosts immune system?

Citrus aurantium boosts immune system because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which exhibit anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The synergistic effects of these compounds contribute to improved immune function.

Regular consumption of citrus aurantium may therefore aid in maintaining a robust immune response.
