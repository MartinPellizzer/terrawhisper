What's the taxonomical classification of Schoepfia jasminodora?

Schoepfia jasminodora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Santalales. It is specifically situated within the family Schoepfiaceae and is categorized under the genus Schoepfia, ultimately reaching its scientific designation as the species jasminodora.

What side-effects this plant can have?

While certain parts of the plant are studied for medicinal properties, the specific toxicity levels for humans remain insufficiently documented in clinical literature. Ingestion of untreated parts could lead to nausea, vomiting, or abdominal cramping due to unidentified bioactive compounds. Therefore, cautious handling and professional guidance are necessary to avoid adverse reactions.
