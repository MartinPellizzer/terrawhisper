Foenum-graecum officinale

It has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including lowering blood sugar levels, improving digestion, and reducing inflammation.

The therapeutic actions of Fenugreek include its ability to act as an anti-diabetic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant agent.
