What's the taxonomical classification of Altingia yunnanensis?

Altingia yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Saxifrages, where it is placed in the unique family Altingiaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Altingia, and its unique species designation, yunnanensis.
