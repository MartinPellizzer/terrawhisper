Coleus blumei var. marshallii

Coleus blumei var. marshallii, also known as Coleus marshallii, is a variety of the Coleus blumei plant, a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects.

The plant is believed to have various health benefits, such as reducing fever, relieving digestive issues, and acting as an anti-anxiety agent.

In herbal preparations, Coleus blumei var. marshallii is often used as a tea or tincture, and its leaves can also be applied topically to the skin for its therapeutic properties.
