Red Silk Cotton Tree (Butea superba) Compounds

The primary compounds of Red Silk Cotton Tree include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to astringency. Phenolic acids and coumarin are also present, offering anti-inflammatory effects. Alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins provide additional therapeutic benefits, while terpenoids and essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile. Anthocyanins give the plant its vibrant color and may support cardiovascular health.
