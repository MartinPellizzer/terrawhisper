Ottelia acuminata

What's the taxonomical classification of Ottelia acuminata?

Ottelia acuminata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Alismatales and falls within the family Hydrocharitaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Ottelia and the species acuminata.

There is currently limited toxicological data regarding the internal ingestion of this species, so its safety for consumption remains unverified. Caution should be exercised when working in habitats where this plant grows, as many aquatic species harbor microorganisms. Avoiding skin contact is the most effective way to prevent potential adverse reactions.
