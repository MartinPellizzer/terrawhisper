Gentiana carinata

Gentiana carinata, commonly known as Chinese gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the Gentianaceae family, native to China and the Himalayas.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can aid in the treatment of various ailments such as fever, respiratory issues, and digestive problems.

The therapeutic actions of Gentiana carinata include stimulating digestion, reducing inflammation, and modulating the immune system.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include xanthones, flavonoids, and iridoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties and have been shown to exhibit antiviral, antibacterial, and antifungal activities.
