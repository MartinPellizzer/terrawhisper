Chaste Tree (Vitex agnus-castus) Compounds

The primary compounds in Chaste Tree include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and essential oils, known for their aromatic and therapeutic effects. Coumarin contributes to the plant's mild sedative qualities, while phenolic acids and tannins provide anti-inflammatory benefits. Terpenoids and glycosides support hormonal balance, and saponins may aid in detoxification.
