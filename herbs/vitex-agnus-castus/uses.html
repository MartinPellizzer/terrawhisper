Chaste Tree (Vitex agnus-castus) Uses

Chaste tree is commonly used to address menstrual disorders, including irregularities, cramps, and premenstrual syndrome. It may also help with breast tenderness, fibrocystic breast condition, and menopausal symptoms like hot flashes. Its effects are thought to stem from its influence on hormonal balance, particularly regulating estrogen and progesterone levels.

Chaste Tree (Vitex agnus-castus) Monograph

1. Menstrual disorders

Chaste tree is used to support hormonal balance and alleviate symptoms of menstrual disorders such as irregular cycles, heavy bleeding, and premenstrual tension. It is often taken as a tincture or in capsule form, with typical dosages ranging from 20 to 40 drops twice daily. Its effects are generally mild and can complement other herbal remedies for menstrual health.

2. Breast tenderness

Chaste tree is traditionally used to alleviate breast tenderness, particularly during hormonal fluctuations like menstruation. It may help regulate estrogen and progesterone levels, reducing discomfort. Its effects are often noticed within a few weeks of regular use. It is commonly used as a supportive treatment in herbal medicine.

3. Menopausal symptoms

Chaste tree is commonly used to alleviate menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep disturbances. Its active compounds, including flavonoids and essential oils, may help regulate hormonal imbalances. It is typically taken as a tincture or capsule, with dosages varying based on individual needs and practitioner guidance.

4. Menstrual irregularities

Chaste Tree is traditionally used to support menstrual regularity and alleviate symptoms of hormonal imbalance. It may help regulate menstrual cycles, reduce cramping, and ease premenstrual symptoms. Its phytoestrogenic compounds are believed to modulate hormonal activity in the body.

5. Breast pain

Chaste tree is used to alleviate breast pain, particularly during hormonal fluctuations like menstruation or menopause. It supports hormonal balance by regulating prolactin and estrogen levels. This makes it effective for cyclical breast tenderness and related discomfort.

6. Menopause symptoms

Chaste tree has been traditionally used to support women during menopause by helping regulate hormonal imbalances. It may alleviate symptoms such as hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep disturbances. Its phytoestrogen compounds interact with estrogen receptors, providing mild hormonal support. It is often recommended as a natural alternative to conventional treatments.

7. Premenstrual syndrome

Chaste tree (Vitex agnus-castus) is used to support women's hormonal balance and alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome. It may help with mood swings, irritability, and breast tenderness by regulating progesterone levels. Standardized extracts are typically taken in the second half of the menstrual cycle for optimal effect.

8. Menstrual cramps

Chaste tree is used to alleviate menstrual cramps by regulating hormonal imbalances, particularly in women with premenstrual syndrome. It may reduce pain and discomfort by supporting the menstrual cycle's natural rhythm. Its effects are thought to stem from its influence on prolactin and estrogen levels. It is often recommended as a supportive herb for women experiencing irregular or painful periods.

9. Fibrocystic breast condition

Chaste tree (Vitex agnus-castus) is used to support women with fibrocystic breast condition by regulating hormonal imbalances. It may reduce breast tenderness and cyst formation through its phytoestrogenic and anti-inflammatory properties. Standardized extracts are typically taken in low doses for several months under professional guidance.

10. Hormonal imbalance

Chaste tree supports hormonal balance by regulating the pituitary gland and influencing progesterone levels. It is commonly used for symptoms like irregular menstruation, PMS, and menopausal discomfort. Its effects are attributed to compounds that modulate hormone receptors and reduce estrogen dominance.
