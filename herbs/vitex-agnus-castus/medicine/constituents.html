10 Medicinal Constituents Of Vitex Agnus-Castus (Chaste Tree)

Vitex agnus-castus has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and essential oils.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and estrogen-regulating properties, making it a popular herb for women's health issues.

For instance, iridoid glycosides may help alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS) and polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), while flavonoids may support uterine health and reduce inflammation.
