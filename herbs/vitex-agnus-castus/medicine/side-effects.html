10 Possible Side Effects Of Vitex Agnus-Castus (Chaste Tree)

Vitex agnus-castus has some side effects when used improperly, such as dizziness, headaches, and digestive problems.

These side effects are often caused by an excessive intake or individual sensitivity to the herb's flavonoids and terpenoids. In severe cases, they can worsen a person's quality of life, making everyday activities challenging.

For instance, dizziness may lead to accidents, while digestive problems can cause nutrient deficiencies and further health issues.
