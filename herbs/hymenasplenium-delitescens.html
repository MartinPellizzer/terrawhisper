What is Hymenasplenium delitescens?

Hymenasplenium delitescens is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small to medium-sized fronds, which are pinnate with 20-40 pairs of pinnae. The pinnae are lanceolate to elliptical in shape, and the margins are ciliate with 1-2 cilia per segment. The rachis is glabrous, and the sporangia are densely arranged in sori on the dorsal side of the pinnae.

Hymenasplenium delitescens is native to China, Japan, and parts of Southeast Asia.
