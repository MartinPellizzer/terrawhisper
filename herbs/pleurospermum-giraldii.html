What's the taxonomical classification of Pleurospermum giraldii?

Pleurospermum giraldii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Apiales. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Pleurospermum, and is identified by the specific epithet giraldii.
