1. Treats fever

The cola acuminata plant is traditionally used to treat fever due to its antipyretic properties, helping to reduce body temperature and alleviate symptoms.

How this herb helps with treats fever?

Cola acuminata treats fever because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which possess anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties.

These compounds help reduce body temperature by influencing the hypothalamic thermoregulatory center. The alkaloids, including compounds like caffeine and theobromine, contribute to the plant's ability to lower fever through their stimulatory effects on the central nervous system. Flavonoids in Cola acuminata also support immune function and may enhance the body's response to fever.

Traditional use of this plant aligns with its modern pharmacological potential in managing fever symptoms.
