Veronicastrum villosulum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and falls within the family Plantaginaceae. Ultimately, its specific identification is defined by the genus Veronicastrum and the species villosulum.
