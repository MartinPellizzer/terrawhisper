What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum betulifolium?

Viburnum betulifoliuum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Viburnaceae, falling within the genus Viburnum, and is specifically identified by its species name, betulifolium.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Small, white, five-petaled flowers emerge in dense, flat-topped cymes during the blooming season. These floral clusters eventually give way to small, fleshy drupes that change color as they ripen.
