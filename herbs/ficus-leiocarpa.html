Ficus leiocarpa

Ficus leiocarpa, also known as the small-leaved fig or small-leaved banyan, is a species of fig tree native to Australia and New Guinea.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antifungal activities, which are believed to help with various health issues.

The health benefits of Ficus leiocarpa include aiding in the treatment of fever, sore throats, and skin conditions, as well as providing relief from digestive problems and respiratory issues.

In herbal preparations, the leaves of Ficus leiocarpa are often used to make teas, infusions, and topical creams, although the effectiveness and safety of these preparations have not been extensively scientifically studied.
