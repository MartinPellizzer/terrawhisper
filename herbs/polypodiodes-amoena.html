Polypodiodes amoena

What's the taxonomical classification of Polypodiodes amoena?

Polypodiodes amoena belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is part of the order Polypodiales and falls under the family Polypodiaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Polypodiodes and the species amoena.
