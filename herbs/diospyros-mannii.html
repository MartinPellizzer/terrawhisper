Diospyros mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Ericales and the family Ebenaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its specific genus, Diospyrpos, and its unique species designation, mannii.
