Albizia multiflora

Albizia multiflora, also known as the siris tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Asia, characterized by its broad canopy and showy white flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including antibacterial and anti-inflammatory compounds that can help to soothe skin irritations and reduce fever.

The health benefits of Albizia multiflora include its ability to support cardiovascular health, lower blood pressure, and reduce the risk of certain diseases.

Albizia multiflora can be prepared into various herbal products, such as teas, tinctures, and extracts, which can be used to harness its medicinal properties.
