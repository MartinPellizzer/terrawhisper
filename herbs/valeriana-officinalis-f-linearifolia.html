Valeriana officinalis f. linearifolia

Valeriana officinalis f. linearifolia, commonly known as a variant of valerian, is a flowering plant species that belongs to the Valerianaceae family.

It is known for its calming and sedative properties, providing relief from anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness.

The health benefits of Valeriana officinalis f. linearifolia include reducing stress, promoting relaxation, and improving sleep quality.

The bioactive constituents of this plant, such as valerenic acid, isovaleric acid, and valepotriates, contribute to its therapeutic actions of sedation, anxiolysis, and muscle relaxation.

Additionally, valeriana officinalis f. linearifolia may exacerbate conditions such as glaucoma, thyroid problems, and sleep apnea when used in conjunction with other treatments.
