Artemisia waltonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, a diverse group of flowering plants, and is specifically categorized under the genus Artemisia. Its unique botanical identity is finalized by its specific designation as the species waltonii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
