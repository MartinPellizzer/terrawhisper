Lithospermum hancockianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Boraginales and the family Boraginaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Lithospermum, and its unique species designation, hancockianum.
