Phalaris arundinacea

What's the taxonomical classification of Phalaris arundinacea?

Phalaris arundiacacea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Phalaris, and its precise biological designation is the species arundiacacea.
