Cotula (Anthemis cotula) Uses

Cotula is used to treat skin infections, inflammatory conditions, and minor wounds. It helps with skin inflammation, itching, and irritation, supporting wound healing and reducing symptoms of dermatitis and insect bites. Its properties make it a useful herb for addressing various skin issues.

Cotula (Anthemis cotula) Monograph

1. Skin infection

Anthemis cotula is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce bacterial growth and soothe irritated skin, making it effective for conditions like eczema and minor wounds. Its essential oils have been traditionally applied to promote healing and prevent infection.

2. Inflammatory skin conditions

Anthemis cotula is used topically to treat inflammatory skin conditions due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and irritation, making it effective for eczema, psoriasis, and minor wounds. Its soothing effects promote skin healing and provide relief from discomfort associated with these conditions.
