What is Haumaniastrum katangense?

Haumaniastrum katangense is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb with a woody base, growing up to 60-80 cm in height. The stem is quadrangular, hairy, and branched, with leafy nodes. Leaves are opposite, simple, ovate-lanceolate, 2-5 cm long, and 1-2 cm wide, with a serrate margin and a hairy surface.

Flowers are purple, in axillary clusters, and 4-merous, with a calyx of 4 unequal lobes and a corolla of 4 equal lobes.
