Pale Coneflower (Echinacea pallida) Compounds

The primary compounds in Pale Coneflower include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and glycosides, which may support immune function. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects, while saponins and terpenoids aid in immune modulation. Coumarin and alkaloids provide additional therapeutic benefits, and essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its overall medicinal profile.
