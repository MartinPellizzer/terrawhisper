10 Medicinal Constituents Of Echinacea Pallida (Pale Coneflower)

Echinacea pallida has active constituents such as phenolic acids, flavonoids, and volatile oils.

These constituents contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and immunomodulatory effects. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to exhibit antioxidant activity, which can help protect against oxidative stress and cell damage.

By consuming pale coneflower tea or supplements, individuals may experience improved immune function, reduced inflammation, and enhanced overall well-being, potentially alleviating symptoms of colds, flu, and other respiratory infections.
