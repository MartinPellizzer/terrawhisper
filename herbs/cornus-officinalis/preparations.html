10 Best Cornus Officinalis Preparations

Tinctures provide a concentrated form using alcohol, enhancing absorption and potency.

Syrups are often used for their soothing effects, particularly for respiratory or digestive ailments.

Mucillages, derived from the herb, are valued for their ability to coat and protect mucous membranes, aiding in healing and soothing inflammation.
