10 Medicinal Preparations Of Cornus Officinalis (Chinese Dogwood)

Cornus officinalis, also known as Chinese dogwood, has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and decoctions.

These preparations are commonly used to treat fever, rheumatism, and inflammation. The fruit of the plant is used to make teas that are said to relieve digestive issues and soothe sore throats.

The roots and stems are used to make tinctures and decoctions that are used to treat cardiovascular and respiratory issues.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Cornus officinalis.
