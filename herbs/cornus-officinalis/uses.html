Chinese Cornel (Cornus officinalis) Uses

Chinese Cornel is used to treat gastrointestinal issues such as diarrhea, gastritis, and gastric ulcers. It helps manage symptoms of dysentery and gastroenteritis by reducing inflammation and promoting digestive health. It is also used for hepatitis and kidney stones, supporting liver and urinary tract function.

Chinese Cornel (Cornus officinalis) Monograph

1. Diarrhea

Cornus officinalis is used to treat diarrhea due to its astringent properties that help firm stools and reduce intestinal inflammation. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in managing acute or chronic digestive issues. Its ability to regulate bowel movements makes it a valuable component in traditional Chinese herbal formulations for gastrointestinal disorders.

2. Gastric ulcers

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat gastric ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and protective effects on the stomach lining. It helps reduce ulcer severity by promoting mucosal repair and inhibiting harmful bacteria. Its astringent properties also aid in controlling excessive acid secretion.

3. Dysentery

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat dysentery due to its astringent properties that help reduce inflammation and stop intestinal bleeding. It is often combined with other herbs to strengthen the spleen and resolve dampness, which are believed to contribute to the condition. This makes it a key component in formulas aimed at restoring digestive balance and alleviating symptoms of acute dysentery.

4. Gastritis

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to soothe gastritis by reducing inflammation and neutralizing stomach acid. It helps alleviate symptoms like pain, bloating, and excessive acidity. The fruit's astringent properties promote healing of the gastric lining. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations tailored for digestive disorders.

5. Hepatitis

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support liver health and treat hepatitis. It is believed to detoxify the liver, reduce inflammation, and promote regeneration. The fruit contains compounds that may enhance liver function and aid in the recovery process. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations tailored for liver support.

6. Constipation

Cornus officinalis is used to treat constipation due to its laxative properties. It helps soften stool and promote bowel movement. The fruit's high mucilage content aids in reducing intestinal dryness. It is commonly prepared as a decoction or powder for internal use.

7. Gastroenteritis

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat gastroenteritis due to its astringent and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce diarrhea and inflammation in the digestive tract. The fruit is often decocted and consumed to alleviate symptoms and restore digestive balance. It is particularly effective in cases of dampness and heat in the stomach and intestines.

8. Gastrointestinal discomfort

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate gastrointestinal discomfort such as bloating, indigestion, and nausea. It helps regulate digestion and reduce inflammation in the stomach and intestines. Its astringent properties help firm stools, making it beneficial for diarrhea. It is often prepared as a decoction or powder for internal use.

9. Stomach ulcer

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat stomach ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and mucoprotective properties. It helps reduce gastric acid secretion and promotes the healing of the stomach lining. The fruit is often prepared as a decoction or powder to soothe irritation and prevent ulcers. Its astringent qualities aid in protecting the digestive tract from further damage.

10. Kidney stones

Cornus officinalis is used in traditional Chinese medicine to help dissolve and expel kidney stones. It is believed to promote urinary flow and reduce stone formation. The fruit's diuretic properties support kidney health and are often combined with other herbs for enhanced efficacy. It is typically prepared as a decoction or powder.
