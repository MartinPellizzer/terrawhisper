Amomum neoaurantiacum

What's the taxonomical classification of Amomum neoaurantiacum?

Amomum neoaurantiacum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Zingiberales. This plant is a member of the family Zingiberaceae, specifically falling within the genus Amomum, and is identified by its unique species name, neoaurantiacum.
