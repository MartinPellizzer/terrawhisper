10 Medicinal Constituents Of Clematis Armandii (Chinese Clematis)

Clematis armandii, also known as Chinese clematis, has active constituents such as flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and rheumatism. The flavonoids, in particular, can help reduce oxidative stress and improve cardiovascular health, potentially lowering the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Regular use of Chinese clematis may also improve respiratory health and alleviate symptoms of allergies.
