Lindera metcalfiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Laurales and the family Lauraceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Lindera. As the specific epithet indicates, the plant is identified as the species Lindera metcalfiana.
