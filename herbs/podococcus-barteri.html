What's the taxonomical classification of Podococcus barteri?

Podococcus barteri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Arecales and belongs to the family Arecaceae. It is further categorized under the genus Podococcus, with the specific epithet barteri identifying it as the species Podococcus barteri.
