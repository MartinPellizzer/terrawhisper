Lyonia ovalifolia var. elliptica

What's the taxonomical classification of Lyonia ovalifolia var. elliptica?

Lyonia ovalifolia var. elliptica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ericales and is specifically situated in the family Ericaceae. Within this botanical hierarchy, it is identified by the genus Lyonia, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety elliptica of the species Lyonia ovalifolia.
