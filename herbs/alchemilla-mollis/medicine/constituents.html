10 Medicinal Constituents Of Alchemilla Mollis (Lady'S Mantle)

Alchemilla mollis has active constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and phenolic acids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and astringent properties, which can alleviate symptoms of menstrual disorders, menopausal issues, and digestive problems.

For example, the flavonoids can help to reduce inflammation and improve the symptoms of conditions such as endometriosis, while the tannins can help to soothe digestive issues and reduce menstrual bleeding.
