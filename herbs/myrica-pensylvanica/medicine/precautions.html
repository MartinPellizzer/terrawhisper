10 Precautions To Take When Using Myrica Pensylvanica (Bayberry)

Myrica pensylvanica has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional before consuming its extracts or supplements.

This is crucial as Myrica pensylvanica may interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications. For instance, not consulting a professional may lead to increased bleeding or uncontrolled blood sugar levels.

Moreover, pregnant women should avoid using this herb due to its potential to induce uterine contractions.
