10 Possible Side Effects Of Myrica Pensylvanica (Bayberry)

Myrica pensylvanica has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal issues, and liver damage.

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, individual sensitivity, and poor quality of the herb. For instance, an allergic reaction can worsen a person's life by causing anaphylaxis, a life-threatening condition.

Additionally, liver damage can lead to chronic health problems, such as cirrhosis and liver failure, severely impacting daily life.
