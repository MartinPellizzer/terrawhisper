Coptis chinensis var. brevisepala

What's the taxonomical classification of Coptis chinensis var. brevisepala?

Coptis chinensis var. brevisepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Ranunculales and falls under the family Ranunculaceae. This specific variety is part of the genus Coptis, ultimately identified by its complete taxonomic designation as the species Coptis chinensis var. brevisepala.
