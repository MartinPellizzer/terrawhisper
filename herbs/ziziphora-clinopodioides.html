Summer Savory (Ziziphora clinopodioides)

Summer Savory (Ziziphora clinopodioides) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Middle East, Central Asia, and Southern Europe. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and flowers have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.

Stems
Erect, unbranched, hairy, with opposite leaf scars and a woody cortex.
