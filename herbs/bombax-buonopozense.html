What's the taxonomical classification of Bombax buonopozense?

Bombax buonopozense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is part of the order Malvales and the family Malvaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Bombax, with its specific designation being the species buonopozense.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
