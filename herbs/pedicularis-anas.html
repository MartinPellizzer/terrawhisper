What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis anas?

Pedicularis anas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and falls within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Pedicularis, and its specific species designation, anaas.
