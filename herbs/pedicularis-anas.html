Pedicularis anas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and falls within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Pedicularis, and its specific species designation, anaas.
