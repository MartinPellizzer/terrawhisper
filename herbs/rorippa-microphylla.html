Rorippa microphylla

Rorippa microphylla, also known as micro leaf cress or watercress, is a type of aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its high content of vitamins A, C, and E, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The health benefits of consuming Rorippa microphylla include reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and supporting immune function.

Herbal preparations of Rorippa microphylla can be made into teas, infusions, or added to salads and other dishes to reap its nutritional and medicinal benefits.
