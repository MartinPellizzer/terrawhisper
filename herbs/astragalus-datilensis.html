Astragalus datilensis

Astragalus datilensis, commonly known as Datil yucca, is a type of plant in the legume family that is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This plant is valued for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it effective in treating various conditions such as arthritis, wounds, and infections.

The therapeutic actions of Astragalus datilensis include anti-inflammatory, immunomodulatory, and antioxidant activities, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.
