Sanicula Europaea Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Sanicula europaea, commonly known as European sanicle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It is valued in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits, including supporting respiratory health, reducing inflammation, and promoting digestion.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from the dried roots and leaves of Sanicula europaea.
