Hypoestes purpurea

What's the taxonomical classification of Hypoestes purpurea?

Hypoestes purpurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Lamiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Acanthaceae, which contains the genus Hypoestes, and is specifically identified by its species name, purpurea.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hypoestes purpurea has a woody, scrambling shrub habit characterized by slender, arching stems and oppositely arranged, elliptic to oblong leaves. The foliage is typically glossy and dark green, often featuring a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, usually presenting in subtle clusters. These blooms are typically pale or yellowish, contrasting with the plant's deep green canopy.
