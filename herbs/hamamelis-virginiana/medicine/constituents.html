10 Medicinal Constituents Of Hamamelis Virginiana (American Witch Hazel)

Hamamelis virginiana, also known as American Witch Hazel, has active constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and astringent properties, making it a popular herbal remedy for various skin conditions, including hemorrhoids, eczema, and acne. The medicinal properties of Witch Hazel can also help soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing redness and swelling.

For example, the topical application of Witch Hazel can provide relief from symptoms of varicose veins and minor cuts, promoting healthy healing and improving overall skin health.
