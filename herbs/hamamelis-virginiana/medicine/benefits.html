10 Health Benefits Of Witch Hazel

1. Soothes skin irritations

Witch hazel soothes skin irritations because it contains high levels of tannins, which have anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

These tannins help to reduce swelling, repair damaged skin, and restore the skin's natural barrier. Additionally, witch hazel contains gallic acid and polyphenols, which have antioxidant and antibacterial effects, further helping to soothe and protect the skin from irritation and infection. Its anti-inflammatory properties also make it effective in reducing redness, itching, and discomfort associated with skin irritations.

Overall, the natural compounds found in witch hazel make it a popular and effective remedy for soothing and calming irritated skin.

2. Reduces inflammation

Witch hazel reduces inflammation because it contains several active compounds, such as tannins, gallic acid, and essential oils, that have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds work by inhibiting the production of inflammatory molecules, such as prostaglandins and leukotrienes, which are released by the body in response to injury or infection. By reducing the levels of these inflammatory molecules, witch hazel helps to alleviate inflammation, swelling, and pain. Additionally, the astringent properties of witch hazel can help to tighten and soothe irritated skin, further reducing redness and discomfort.

As a result, witch hazel is a popular natural remedy for a variety of inflammatory conditions, including eczema, psoriasis, and hemorrhoids.

3. Alleviates puffy eyes

Witch hazel alleviates puffy eyes because of its anti-inflammatory properties.

The tannins present in witch hazel possess astringent qualities that help to tighten the skin, reducing the appearance of puffiness. Additionally, its soothing and cooling effects help to constrict blood vessels, thereby decreasing fluid buildup around the eyes.

Furthermore, its high antioxidant content aids in protecting the delicate skin around the eyes from damage caused by free radicals, promoting a more youthful and refreshed appearance.

4. Fights acne

Witch hazel fights acne because of its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

It contains tannins, which have astringent qualities that help to reduce redness, swelling, and inflammation of pimples. Witch hazel can also help to regulate the production of oil in the skin, preventing clogged pores that can lead to acne. Its antimicrobial properties can kill bacteria that cause acne, reducing the risk of infection and further breakouts.

By reducing inflammation, oil production, and bacterial growth, witch hazel can help to prevent and treat acne, making it a popular natural remedy for this common skin condition.

5. Relieves hemorrhoids

Witch hazel relieves hemorrhoids because of its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

It contains tannins, which help to reduce swelling and irritation, while its cooling effect soothes the affected area. Witch hazel also has healing properties that can help to alleviate pain and itching. Its ability to constrict blood vessels helps to reduce bleeding and promote healing.

When applied topically, witch hazel can provide immediate relief from the discomfort of hemorrhoids, making it a popular natural remedy for this common condition.

6. Improves varicose veins

Witch hazel improves varicose veins because of its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

Varicose veins occur when the valves in the veins weaken, causing blood to pool in the legs. Witch hazel contains tannins, which can help to strengthen the walls of the veins and reduce inflammation.

This can help to alleviate the symptoms of varicose veins, such as swelling, pain, and itching. Additionally, the astringent properties of witch hazel can help to reduce the appearance of varicose veins by shrinking the swollen veins.

Witch hazel can be applied topically to the affected area in the form of a compress or cream, providing relief and improving the overall appearance of varicose veins.

7. Heals minor cuts and burns

Witch hazel heals minor cuts and burns because of its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

The plant contains tannins, which have the ability to help constrict blood vessels and reduce inflammation, helping to stop bleeding and prevent infection. Additionally, witch hazel's high levels of gallic acid and polyphenols contribute to its antioxidant and antibacterial properties, further aiding in the healing process. When applied topically, witch hazel can help soothe and cleanse the affected area, promoting faster healing and reducing the risk of scarring.

Overall, the natural healing properties of witch hazel make it a popular and effective remedy for minor cuts and burns.

8. Tightens pores

Witch hazel tightens pores because it contains tannins, which have astringent properties that help to tighten and tone the skin.

These tannins work by temporarily constricting the skin's cells, reducing the appearance of pores and giving the skin a smoother, more even appearance. Additionally, witch hazel has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce redness and soothe irritated skin, making it an ideal ingredient for those with sensitive or acne-prone skin.

By using witch hazel as a toner or a part of your skincare routine, you can help to refine the appearance of your pores and promote a clearer, more radiant complexion.

9. Controls oil production

Witch hazel controls oil production because it contains tannins and volatile oils that have astringent properties.

These components help to shrink and constrict the pores, reducing the amount of oil that the skin produces. Witch hazel is also an anti-inflammatory, which can help to soothe irritated skin and reduce redness. Its natural properties make it an effective and gentle way to manage oily skin, without stripping the skin of its natural moisture.

By regulating oil production, witch hazel can help to prevent acne and other skin conditions caused by excess oil.

10. Eases sunburn pain

Witch hazel eases sunburn pain because of its anti-inflammatory properties.

The tannins present in witch hazel help to soothe the skin and reduce redness, while its astringent properties help to draw out heat and relieve pain. Additionally, witch hazel has been shown to promote skin cell regeneration, which can help to speed up the healing process of sunburned skin. Its natural antioxidant and moisturizing properties also help to protect and hydrate the skin, preventing further damage and discomfort.

Overall, the use of witch hazel can provide a natural and effective solution for easing the pain and discomfort associated with sunburn.
