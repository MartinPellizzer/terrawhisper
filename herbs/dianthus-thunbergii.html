What is Dianthus thunbergii?

Dianthus thunbergii is a perennial plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It has a compact, bushy habit, and its stem is erect, reaching 10-30 cm in height, with simple, ovate leaves, 3-6 cm long, and 1-2 cm wide. The leaves are glabrous, with serrated margins and a cuneate base. The plant produces pink to rose-purple flowers, 2-3 cm in diameter, with 5 petals, a long, cylindrical tube, and a fringed calyx.

The flowers are borne in a terminal, corymbose inflorescence.
