Dorema Ammoniacum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Dorema ammoniacum, commonly known as the ammoniacum herb, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia and is valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its ability to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and promote detoxification in the body.

The therapeutic actions of Dorema ammoniacum include expectorant, antispasmodic, and mild analgesic effects, making it useful in treating coughs and respiratory congestion.

Bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and essential oils contribute to its pharmacological activities and health benefits.
