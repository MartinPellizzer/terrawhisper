What's the taxonomical classification of Lomatogonium oreocharis?

Lomatogonium oreocharis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Gentianaales and falls within the family Gentianaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Lomatogonium and the species oreocharis.
