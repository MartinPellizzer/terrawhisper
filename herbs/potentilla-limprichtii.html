Potentilla limprichtii

What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla limprichtii?

Potentiella limprichtii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its higher taxonomic ranks, the plant is part of the order Rosales and falls under the family Rosaceae. Finally, it is categorized within the genus Potentiella, specifically identified by its species name, limprichtii.
