10 Possible Side Effects Of Prunus Cerasus (Sour Cherry)

Prunus cerasus has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, headaches, and dizziness.

These side effects can occur when the herb is consumed in excessive amounts or without proper preparation. For instance, taking large doses can lead to stomach pain and nausea, affecting one's daily activities and productivity.

In severe cases, it can even lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalance, compromising overall health.
