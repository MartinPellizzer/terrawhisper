10 Precautions To Take When Using Prunus Cerasus (Sour Cherry)

Prunus cerasus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being aware of potential allergenicity and gastrointestinal upset.

The tannins in Prunus cerasus can cause stomach discomfort, nausea, and diarrhea if ingested in large amounts. Failing to take this into account may lead to side effects such as stomach cramps, bloating, and even vomiting.

It's essential to start with a low dose and gradually increase as needed to minimize the risk of adverse reactions.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Prunus cerasus medicinally.
