Sagittaria sagittifolia f. angustifolia

Sagittaria sagittifolia f. angustifolia, also known as narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, which can help soothe digestive issues and wounds.

The health benefits of Sagittaria sagittifolia f. angustifolia include its potential to lower blood sugar levels, reduce inflammation, and act as a diuretic.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat a variety of health conditions.
