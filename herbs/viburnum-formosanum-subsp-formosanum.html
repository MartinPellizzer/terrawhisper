Viburnum formosanum subsp. formosanum

What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum formosanum subsp. formosanum?

Viburnum formosanum subsp. formosanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Dipsacales and is a representative of the family Viburnaceae. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Viburnum, specifically identified by its scientific name, Viburnum formosanum subsp. formosanum.
