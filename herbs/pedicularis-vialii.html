Pedicularis vialii

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis vialii?

Pedicularis vialii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its evolutionary path follows the order Lamiales, placing it within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, the plant is situated in the genus Pedicularis, with its specific designation being the species vialii.
