Castanopsis spectabilis

Castanopsis spectabilis, also known as the Taiwanese chinquapin, is a species of tree that belongs to the beech family and is native to Taiwan.

Traditionally, the plant has been used in Chinese medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and gastrointestinal issues, due to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Castanopsis spectabilis include reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and supporting digestive health, making it a valuable herbal remedy.
