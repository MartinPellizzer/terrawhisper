What's the taxonomical classification of Abelia biflora?

Abelia biflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this hierarchy, the plant is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae and is categorized under the genus Abelia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species biflora.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Small, tubular, white to pale pink flowers emerge in clusters from the leaf axils during the summer months. These blossoms are followed by distinctive, papery, reddish-brown calyxes that persist through the winter.
