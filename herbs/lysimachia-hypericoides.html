What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia hypericoides?

Lysimachia hypericoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Lysimachia, and is specifically identified by the species name hypericoides.

The slender, spreading stems allow the plant to colonize ground cover effectively through vegetative spreading.
