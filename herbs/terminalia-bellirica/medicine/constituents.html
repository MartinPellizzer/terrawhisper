10 Medicinal Constituents Of Terminalia Bellirica (Bahera)

Terminalia bellirica has active constituents such as ellagic acid, gallic acid, and oleanolic acid.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The plant's constituents can improve people's lives by reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing, as well as protecting against chronic diseases such as cancer and diabetes.

Regular consumption of Terminalia bellirica is also believed to enhance cognitive function and promote overall health and well-being.
