10 Precautions To Take When Using Terminalia Bellirica (Bahera)

It's essential to follow these guidelines to ensure safe and effective use.

1. Store safely out of reach

When using Terminalia bellirica medicinally, it's important to consult your doctor first use.

This precaution is crucial because Terminalia bellirica can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, and may exacerbate underlying health conditions like high blood pressure or kidney disease.

Additionally, Terminalia bellirica can cause allergic reactions or gastrointestinal upset in some individuals, making it essential to discuss individual circumstances and potential risks with a healthcare professional before incorporating this herb into your treatment plan.
