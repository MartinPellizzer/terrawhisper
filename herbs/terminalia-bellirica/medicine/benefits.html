10 Health Benefits Of Terminalia Bellirica (Bahera)

Terminalia bellirica has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties that have been traditionally used to treat various ailments.

The fruit of the plant is particularly beneficial in improving digestive health by reducing inflammation and relieving symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). Its antioxidant properties also help protect against oxidative stress and cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases like cancer and diabetes.

By incorporating bahera into one's diet or using its extracts as a supplement, individuals can experience improved overall well-being and quality of life.

1. Reduces inflammation

Terminalia bellirica reduces inflammation because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

The plant's extracts have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, thereby reducing the body's inflammatory response.

Additionally, its compounds have been found to possess potent anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate conditions such as arthritis, asthma, and other chronic diseases characterized by excessive inflammation.

2. Treats respiratory disorders

Terminalia bellirica treats respiratory disorders because of its potent antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The herb contains various bioactive compounds such as tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids that help to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues like bronchitis, asthma, and chronic coughs.

Its expectorant property helps to loosen and clear mucus from the lungs, providing relief from congestion and shortness of breath.

3. Improves digestion

Terminalia bellirica improves digestion because it contains a unique combination of bioactive compounds that stimulate digestive enzymes and enhance gut motility.

The fruit extract has been shown to increase the secretion of gastric amylase, lipase, and trypsin, thereby facilitating the breakdown of carbohydrates, proteins, and fats.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties help soothe the digestive tract and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating.

4. Boosts immune system

Terminalia bellirica boosts immune system because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

The fruit extract has been shown to stimulate the production of white blood cells, which play a crucial role in fighting off infections.

Additionally, bahera's antimicrobial properties help to prevent the growth of harmful bacteria and viruses, further strengthening the body's natural defenses against disease-causing pathogens.

5. Reduces blood sugar levels

Terminalia bellirica reduces blood sugar levels because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The fruit and leaves of this tree contain flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids that have been shown to inhibit α-amylase and α-glucosidase enzymes responsible for carbohydrate digestion and absorption.

By reducing the breakdown of complex carbohydrates into simple sugars, bahera extract helps slow down glucose release into the bloodstream, thereby decreasing blood sugar levels and potentially improving insulin sensitivity.

6. Lowers cholesterol

Terminalia bellirica lowers cholesterol because of its rich content of tannins and flavonoids.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol and triglycerides in the gut, thereby reducing their levels in the blood.

Additionally, the antioxidants present in Terminalia bellirica help to protect LDL ("bad") cholesterol from oxidation, further contributing to its cholesterol-lowering effects.

7. Prevents premature graying

Terminalia bellirica prevents premature graying because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The fruit of this tree contains flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids that help to protect the hair follicles from damage caused by free radicals and oxidative stress.

This protection leads to healthy hair growth, reducing the likelihood of premature graying, while also promoting a more vibrant and lustrous mane.

8. Promotes liver health

Terminalia bellirica promotes liver health because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

The fruit extract has been shown to inhibit oxidative stress and lipid peroxidation in the liver, reducing inflammation and damage caused by free radicals.

Additionally, it enhances the activity of liver enzymes responsible for detoxification, improving the body's natural ability to eliminate toxins and promote overall liver function and health.

9. Improves eye health

Terminalia bellirica improves eye health because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The fruit extract has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various ocular disorders such as cataracts, conjunctivitis, and glaucoma.

Bahera's potent antioxidants help protect the eyes from damage caused by free radicals, reducing the risk of age-related macular degeneration and promoting overall eye health and vision clarity.

10. Treats gum diseases

Terminalia bellirica treats gum diseases because of its impressive antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The fruit of this tree contains compounds like tannins, flavonoids, and alkaloids that have been shown to reduce inflammation and prevent the formation of plaque, which is a major contributor to gum disease.

Additionally, the antibacterial properties of bahera help to combat bacteria that can cause infections and promote healing in the gums.
