Belleric Myrobalan (Terminalia bellirica) Uses

Belleric Myrobalan is used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and cough. It helps reduce lung congestion and inflammation in the respiratory tract. It is also effective for allergic rhinitis and respiratory infections, supporting the treatment of tuberculosis and chronic cough.

Belleric Myrobalan (Terminalia bellirica) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Belleric Myrobalan is used in herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce mucus production and soothe respiratory irritation, supporting natural healing in chronic bronchial conditions. Its astringent qualities also help strengthen lung tissue and improve breathing.

2. Allergic rhinitis

Belleric Myrobalan (Terminalia bellirica) is used in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis. It possesses anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties that help reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose. Its active compounds, such as tannins and flavonoids, support immune modulation and tissue repair, making it a valuable remedy for managing seasonal allergies.

3. Asthma

Belleric Myrobalan is used in traditional medicine to support respiratory health. It helps reduce inflammation in the airways and may ease symptoms of asthma by improving bronchial function. Its astringent properties help clear mucus, promoting easier breathing. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations aimed at managing chronic respiratory conditions.

4. Cough

Belleric Myrobalan is used to alleviate cough by its astringent properties, which help reduce mucus production and soothe irritated throat tissues. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations to enhance its effectiveness against persistent or chronic cough conditions.

5. Respiratory allergies

Belleric Myrobalan is used in traditional medicine to alleviate respiratory allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce symptoms like sneezing, coughing, and nasal congestion by modulating immune responses. Its astringent qualities also soothe irritated mucous membranes in the respiratory tract.

6. Lung congestion

Belleric Myrobalan is used to alleviate lung congestion due to its demulcent and expectorant properties. It soothes irritated respiratory tissues and helps loosen mucus, making it easier to expel. This makes it beneficial in treating conditions like bronchitis and chronic cough associated with excess phlegm. Its anti-inflammatory effects further support respiratory health.

7. Respiratory tract infections

Belleric Myrobalan is used to treat respiratory tract infections due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and mucus production. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations to enhance its therapeutic effects on the respiratory system.

8. Mucosal inflammation

Belleric Myrobalan is used to treat mucosal inflammation due to its astringent and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce swelling and irritation in the mucous membranes of the digestive and respiratory tracts. Its high tannin content contributes to its healing effects on inflamed tissues. It is commonly used in formulations for conditions like gastritis and sore throat.

9. Tuberculosis

Belleric Myrobalan is used in traditional herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It is believed to help reduce inflammation and enhance immune function, which may aid in managing tuberculosis symptoms. Its astringent properties are thought to help clear mucus and improve lung function. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations for respiratory conditions.

10. Chronic cough

Belleric Myrobalan is used to treat chronic cough due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the respiratory tract. It is often combined with other herbs in traditional formulations to enhance its effectiveness. Regular use may support long-term respiratory health.
