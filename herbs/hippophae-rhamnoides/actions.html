Sea Buckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides) Actions

Sea Buckthorn exhibits several therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic properties. It acts as an astringent and carminative, aiding digestion and reducing gas. Additionally, it has expectorant, bitter, antispasmodic, stimulant, and sedative effects, supporting respiratory health and overall bodily function.
