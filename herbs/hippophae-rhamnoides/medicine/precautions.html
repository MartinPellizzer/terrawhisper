10 Precautions To Take When Using Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn)

Hippophae rhamnoides has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding excessive intake of its juice or oil, as it may cause gastrointestinal upset.

High doses can lead to side effects like nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. In rare cases, not adhering to proper dosage may result in liver toxicity.

It's essential to follow recommended amounts to prevent such adverse effects and ensure safe use of the herb.
