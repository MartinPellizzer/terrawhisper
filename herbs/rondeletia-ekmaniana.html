Possible Side Effects of Rondeletia ekmaniana

Rondeletia ekmaniana can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory problems due to its volatile oils and saponins.

Ingesting the plant can cause nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, as well as interact with medications and exacerbate existing health conditions.

Overuse or prolonged exposure to the plant's essential oils can lead to headaches, dizziness, and even liver damage.

Furthermore, the plant's sap may cause skin burns or blisters if not handled carefully.
