Hosackia humilis

Hosackia humilis, commonly known as Purple Lupine or Lupine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and providing antioxidant effects.

The therapeutic actions of Hosackia humilis include its ability to act as a vasodilator, diuretic, and anti-inflammatory agent, which can help alleviate symptoms of various conditions.
