Crataeva Nurvala: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Crataeva nurvala, commonly known as the "Indian Banyan" or "Kapikachhu," is a medicinal plant native to India and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is valued for its diverse therapeutic properties and is used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

The herb offers various health benefits, including aiding digestion, reducing inflammation, and supporting liver function.

Its therapeutic actions include detoxification, antipyretic effects, and anti-inflammatory properties, attributed to bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and tannins.
