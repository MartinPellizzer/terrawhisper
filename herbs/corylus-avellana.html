Corylus Avellana: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Corylus avellana, commonly known as the hazel tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and parts of Asia, known for its edible nuts and medicinal properties.

This herb is valued for its high nutritional content and various health benefits, including supporting immune function, reducing inflammation, and promoting cardiovascular health.

Therapeutically, it is used to alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions, improve circulation, and support nervous system function.

The bioactive constituents of Corylus avellana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, vitamin E, and essential minerals such as zinc and magnesium.
