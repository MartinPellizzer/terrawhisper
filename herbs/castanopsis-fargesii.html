Castanopsis fargesii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Fagaceae, placing it among a diverse group of nut-bearing trees and shrubs. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Castanopsis, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species epithet fargesii.
