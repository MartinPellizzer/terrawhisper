Fissistigma oldhamii

Fisstigma oldhamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Magnoliales, the plant is placed into the family Annonaceae. Its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Fisstigma and the species oldhamii.
