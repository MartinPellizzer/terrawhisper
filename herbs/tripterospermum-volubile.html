What's the taxonomical classification of Tripterospermum volubile?

Tripterospermum volubiile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Gentianales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Gentianaceae, specifically falling under the genus Tripterospermum, with its precise biological designation being the species volubiile.
