What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium thomsonii?

Cremanthodium thomsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Asteraceae, which places it within the genus Cremanthodium, and is specifically identified by the species name thomsonii.
