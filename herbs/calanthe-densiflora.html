What's the taxonomical classification of Calanthe densiflora?

Calanthe densiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and into the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Calanthe, and its unique species designation, densiflora.
