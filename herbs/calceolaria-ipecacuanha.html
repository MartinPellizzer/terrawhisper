It has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, which include treating respiratory issues, such as bronchitis, coughs, and asthma, due to its expectorant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Calceolaria ipecacuanha are attributed to its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve nausea, and induce vomiting, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
