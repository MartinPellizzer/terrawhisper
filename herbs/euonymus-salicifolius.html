What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus salicifolius?

Euonymus salicifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, its taxonomic position is completed within the genus Euonymus, specifically identified by the species name salicifolius.
