Silk Tree (Albizia lebbeck) Actions

Silk Tree (Albizia lebbeck) exhibits several therapeutic actions, including sedative, tonic, and anti-inflammatory effects. It also acts as a diuretic, carminative, and antispasmodic. Additionally, it has bitter, astringent, stimulant, and expectorant properties, making it versatile in herbal medicine for various health conditions.
