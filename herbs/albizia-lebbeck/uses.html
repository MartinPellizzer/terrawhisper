Silk Tree (Albizia lebbeck) Uses

Silk Tree is used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and depression due to its calming effects. It helps with stress-related fatigue and mood disorders by balancing emotions. The herb also alleviates headaches and mental fatigue, and may reduce skin inflammation through its anti-inflammatory properties.

Silk Tree (Albizia lebbeck) Monograph

1. Anxiety

Albizia lebbeck is used in herbal medicine to alleviate anxiety due to its calming properties. The plant contains compounds that may regulate neurotransmitters like serotonin, promoting relaxation. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture. Its use supports emotional balance and stress relief in traditional practices.

2. Insomnia

Albizia lebbeck is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat insomnia due to its mild sedative properties. The plant contains flavonoids and alkaloids that help calm the nervous system, promoting relaxation and better sleep. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to alleviate anxiety-related sleep disturbances.

3. Depression

Albizia lebbeck is used in traditional medicine to alleviate symptoms of depression. Its bark and roots contain compounds that may help regulate mood by influencing neurotransmitter activity. It is often prepared as a decoction or tincture and is valued for its calming and anxiolytic properties.

4. Stress-related fatigue

Albizia lebbeck is used in herbal medicine to alleviate stress-related fatigue due to its adaptogenic properties. It helps regulate the nervous system and reduce anxiety, promoting mental clarity and energy. Its compounds support emotional balance, making it a valuable remedy for those experiencing chronic stress and exhaustion.

Albizia lebbeck is used in traditional medicine to treat sleep disorders due to its calming properties. 6. Mood disorders

Albizia lebbeck is used in traditional herbal medicine to support mood regulation. Its compounds, such as flavonoids and alkaloids, may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression by influencing neurotransmitter activity. 7. Headache

Albizia lebbeck is used to alleviate headaches by promoting relaxation and reducing stress, which are common causes of tension headaches. 9. Mental fatigue

Albizia lebbeck is used to alleviate mental fatigue by calming the nervous system and improving mood. Its mild sedative properties help reduce stress and promote relaxation, making it beneficial for individuals experiencing prolonged mental strain or burnout.

Albizia lebbeck is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat skin inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties.
