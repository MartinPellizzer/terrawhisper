What is Denisophytum bessac?

Denisophytum bessac is a plant species belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is a climbing or scrambling shrub with a woody stem and a height range of 2-10 meters. The leaves are compound, bipinnate, and have 3-7 pairs of pinnae, with each pinna bearing 5-9 leaflets. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing pink or purple flowers with 5 petals, and the fruit is a legume, 3-5 cm long, containing 2-3 seeds.

Denisophytum bessac is native to tropical Africa, specifically in the countries of Cameroon and Equatorial Guinea.
