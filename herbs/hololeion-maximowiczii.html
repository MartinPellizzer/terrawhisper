Hololeion maximowiczii

What's the taxonomical classification of Hololeion maximowiczii?

Hololeion maximowiczii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Hololeion and the species maximowiczii.
