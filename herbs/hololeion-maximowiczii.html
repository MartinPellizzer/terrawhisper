What's the taxonomical classification of Hololeion maximowiczii?

Hololeion maximowiczii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Hololeion and the species maximowiczii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hololeion maximowiczii has slender, ascending stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and feature small, lanceolate to ovate leaves with entire margins.

The foliage is characterized by a smooth texture and a relatively thin structure. Its flowers are small and delicate, often displaying a pale color with subtle markings on the corolla lobes. The reproductive structures include a short style and a central ovary that develops into a small capsule.

This plant generally presents a compact and herbaceous growth habit suitable for shaded environments.
