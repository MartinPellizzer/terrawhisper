Changnienia amoena

What's the taxonomical classification of Changnienia amoena?

Changnienia amoena belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling under the genus Changnienia, with the specific designation of the species amoena.

What are the therapeutic actions of Changnienia amoena?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat gastrointestinal disorders including diarrhea, dysentery, and stomachaches. Its medicinal properties are primarily derived from its ability to act as an astringent within the digestive tract. Practitioners often utilize the plant to alleviate inflammation in the intestines and manage symptoms of indigestion. The decoctions made from its leaves or stems are traditionally applied to stabilize bowel movements. These therapeutic applications have been documented in various traditional herbal medicine practices.
