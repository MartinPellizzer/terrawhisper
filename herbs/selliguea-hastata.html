Selligeua hastata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Selligeua, and its unique species designation, hastaata.
