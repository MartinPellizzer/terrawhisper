Selligeua hastata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Selligeua, and its unique species designation, hastaata.
