10 Possible Side Effects Of Vitis Vinifera (Grape)

Vitis vinifera has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, diarrhea, and stomach cramps.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts or taking the herb in a concentrated form.

For example, a person with an existing allergy to grapes may experience severe hives or anaphylaxis after using Vitis vinifera, which can be life-threatening and disrupt daily activities.
