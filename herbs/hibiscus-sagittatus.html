Hibiscus sagittatus

Hibiscus sagittatus, also known as the pointed hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.

The health benefits of Hibiscus sagittatus include reducing blood pressure, lowering cholesterol levels, and providing antioxidant properties.

The herbal preparations of Hibiscus sagittatus can be made into teas, capsules, or extracts, which can be consumed for its medicinal and health benefits.
