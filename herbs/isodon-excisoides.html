Isodon excisoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of herbaceous and woody species. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Isodon and the species excisoides.
