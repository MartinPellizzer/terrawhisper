What's the taxonomical classification of Camchaya loloana?

Camchaya loloana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Asterales, this plant is a member of the family Asteraceae. Its specific biological classification is defined by the genus Camchaya and the species epithet loloaana.
