What is Koeleria macrantha?

Koeleria macrantha is a perennial grass species belonging to the family Poaceae.

It has a tufted growth habit, typically reaching heights of 30-100 cm. The leaves are narrow, flat, and linear, measuring 1-3 mm in width and 5-20 cm in length, with a smooth margin. The inflorescence is a panicle, consisting of numerous spikelets, each containing 1-3 florets with 2-3 stamens and 1-2 pistils.

The plant produces a large number of seeds, which are 1-2 mm in length, with a smooth surface and a single seed coat.
