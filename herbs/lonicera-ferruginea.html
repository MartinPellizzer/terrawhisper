Lonicera ferruginea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is situated within the family Caprifoliaceae, which characterizes its botanical structure. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Lonicera, with its unique identity defined by the species ferruginea.
