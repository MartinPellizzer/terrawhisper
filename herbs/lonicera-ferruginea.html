What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lonicera ferruginea has opposite, simple leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape and possess finely serrated margins. During the flowering season, the plant produces tubular, fragrant white or pale pink corollas that emerge from the leaf axils.
