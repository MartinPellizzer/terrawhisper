10 Medicinal Constituents Of Euphorbia Pekinensis (Chinese Milkweed)

Euphorbia pekinensis, also known as Chinese milkweed, has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-cancer effects. The flavonoids and saponins can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis and skin conditions, while the alkaloids may inhibit the growth of cancer cells.

By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, these constituents can improve overall health and quality of life.
