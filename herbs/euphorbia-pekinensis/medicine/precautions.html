When using Euphorbia pekinensis, also known as Chinese milkweed, medicinally, it's important to avoid internal use abruptly.

This precaution is crucial because sudden consumption can lead to gastrointestinal irritation, nausea, and vomiting due to the presence of irritating sap and latex in the plant.

Furthermore, abrupt internal use may cause allergic reactions or interact with other medications, potentially resulting in adverse effects on the digestive system and overall health.
