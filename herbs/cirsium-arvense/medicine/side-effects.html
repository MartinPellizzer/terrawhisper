10 Possible Side Effects Of Cirsium Arvense (Canada Thistle)

Cirsium arvense, also known as Canada thistle, has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin irritation.

These side effects occur due to its high concentration of sesquiterpene lactones, which can be toxic to some individuals. If not monitored, these reactions can worsen, causing respiratory problems, diarrhea, and even life-threatening conditions.

For people with pre-existing conditions, these side effects can significantly impact their quality of life, requiring extensive medical treatment and lifestyle adjustments.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Cirsium arvense if used imporperly.
