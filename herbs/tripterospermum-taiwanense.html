What is Tripterospermum taiwanense?

Tripterospermum taiwanense is a species of flowering plant in the family Gentianaceae.

It is a perennial herb that grows 15-50 cm tall, with a woody caudex and a few whorled leaves. The leaves are elliptical to lanceolate in shape, 2-10 cm long, and 0.8-3 cm wide. Tripterospermum taiwanense produces white or pinkish flowers in terminal inflorescences, with four petals, four sepals, and numerous stamens.

The flowers are typically 4-6 mm in diameter and bloom from April to June in its native Taiwan.
