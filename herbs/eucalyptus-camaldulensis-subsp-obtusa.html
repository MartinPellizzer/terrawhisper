Eucalyptus camaldulensis subsp. obtusa

The therapeutic actions of Eucalyptus camaldulensis subsp.

obtusa include decongesting and soothing the respiratory tract, and its bioactive constituents, including eucalyptol, limonene, and alpha-pinene, contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant's essential oil has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions and is often used in aromatherapy and herbal medicine due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
