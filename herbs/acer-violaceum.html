Possible Side Effects of Acer violaceum

In some cases, Acer violaceum has been associated with allergic reactions, such as skin irritation, respiratory problems, and anaphylaxis in rare instances.

Excessive consumption of the plant may also interact with certain medications, exacerbating health conditions.
