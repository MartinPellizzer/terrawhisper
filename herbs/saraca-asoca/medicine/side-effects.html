10 Possible Side Effects Of Saraca Asoca (Asoka)

Saraca asoca has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and dizziness.

These side effects can occur if the herb is ingested in large doses or without consulting a healthcare professional. For instance, excessive vomiting can lead to dehydration, while dizziness can cause accidents and injuries.

Furthermore, if left unchecked, these side effects can worsen over time, negatively impacting daily life and overall well-being.
