Passion Fruit (Passiflora edulis) Uses

Passion fruit is used to treat insomnia and sleep disorders by promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality. It helps alleviate anxiety and stress-related conditions due to its calming properties. The fruit may also reduce headaches and migraines, as well as ease nerve irritation and mood disorders. Its natural sedative effects support overall emotional and mental well-being.
