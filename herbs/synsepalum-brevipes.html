What's the taxonomical classification of Synsepalum brevipes?

Synsepalum brevipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is further categorized into the family Sapotaeciae, within which it is placed in the genus Synsepalum. Finally, its specific identification is completed by its species name, brevipes.
