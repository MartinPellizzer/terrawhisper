Java Plum (Syzygium cumini)

Java Plum (Syzygium cumini) is a member of the Myrtaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
