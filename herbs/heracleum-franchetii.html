Heracleum franchetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Apiaceae, which is characterized by its complex floral structures. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Heracleum, with its distinct biological identity defined by the species name franchetii.
