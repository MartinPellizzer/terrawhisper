Periwinkle (Vinca minor) Compounds

The primary compounds in Periwinkle (Vinca minor) include alkaloids, which have medicinal properties. Flavonoids and glycosides contribute to its antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins provide astringent qualities. Terpenoids and coumarin add to its complex chemical profile. Saponins, essential oils, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic potential.

Periwinkle (Vinca minor) Monograph

1. Alkaloid

Vinca minor contains the alkaloid vincamine. This compound is known for its ability to improve cognitive function and treat neurological conditions. Vincamine works by enhancing blood flow to the brain and reducing inflammation. It is a key component in traditional herbal medicine for its therapeutic benefits.

2. Flavonoid

Flavonoids are a group of compounds found in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). They contribute to the plant's antioxidant properties and may support immune function. These compounds are part of the broader therapeutic profile of Vinca minor in herbal medicine.

Glycoside is a key active compound in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in treating various ailments. Glycosides are often responsible for the plant's medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

Phenolic acid is a key active compound found in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). It contributes to the plant's antioxidant properties, helping to neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress. This compound supports the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.

5. Tannin

Tannin is a key active compound in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). It contributes to the plant's astringent properties and may support wound healing and inflammation reduction. Tannins in Periwinkle are often used in herbal formulations for their potential therapeutic effects.

6. Terpenoid

Terpenoids are a class of compounds found in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). They contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. These compounds are often extracted for use in herbal remedies and pharmaceutical applications.

7. Coumarin

Coumarin is a compound found in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). 8. Saponin

Anthocyanin is a flavonoid found in Periwinkle (Vinca minor). It contributes to the plant's vibrant blue and purple pigmentation. While not the primary medicinal compound, anthocyanin may support antioxidant activity and has been studied for its potential health benefits.
