Beesia calthifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Beesia calthifolia?

Beesia calthifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Ranunculales and belongs to the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus Beesia and its specific species designation, calthifolia.

What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

This plant contains a variety of specialized medicinal compounds including beesiosiide A, beesiosiide I, beesiosiides G, beesiosiides H, and beesiosiides J-N. These specific chemical constituents are primarily recognized for their unique biological activities. Researchers study these compounds to understand their potential therapeutic applications in modern medicine. The presence of this diverse group of molecules highlights the complex phytochemical profile of the species. Each specific beesiosiide may contribute differently to the overall pharmacological properties of the plant.

As discussed in a study, seven new cycloartane glycosides (1-7) were isolated from the rhizomes of Beesia calthifolia. These medicinal compounds include beesiosiides G, H, and J-N, which are identified as 20xi(1),24xi(2)-epoxy-9,19-cyclolanostane-3beta,16beta,18,25-tetraol-3-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside (1) and 20xi(1),24xi(2)-epoxy-9,19-cyclolanostane-3beta,16beta,18,25-tetraol-3-O-[beta-D-glucopyranosyl-(1-->6)]-beta-D-glucopyranoside (2). The plant also contains (20S,24R)-15alpha,16beta-diacetoxy-20,24-epoxy-9,19-cyclolanostane-3beta,18,25-triol-3-O-beta-D-xylopyranoside (3), (20S,24S)-16beta-acetoxy-18,24;20,24-diepoxy-9,19-cyclanosatane-3beta,15beta,25-triol-3-O-beta-D-xylopyranoside (4), and (20S,24S)-16beta-acetoxy-18,24;20,24-diepoxy-9,19-cyclanosatane-3beta,25-diol-3-O-beta-D-xylopyranoside (5). Additional compounds found in the rhizomes are 20xi(1),24xi(2)-epoxy-15alpha-acetoxy-9,19-cyclolanostane-3beta,16beta,25-triol-3-O-beta-D-xylopyranoside (6) and 20xi(1),24xi(2)-epoxy-9,19-cyclolanostane-3beta,12alpha,15alpha,16beta,25-pentaol-3-O-beta-D-xylopyranoside (7). Together with beesioside I (8) and beesioside A, these constituents make up the chemical profile of Beesia calthifolia.
