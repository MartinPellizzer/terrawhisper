What's the taxonomical classification of Saxifraga pasumensis?

Saxifraga pasumensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows the biological lineage of the order Saxifragales. It is further classified under the family Saxifragaceae, which places it within the genus Saxifraga, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Saxifraga pasumensis.
