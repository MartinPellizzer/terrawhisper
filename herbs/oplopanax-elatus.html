What are the molecular targets of this plant?

By suppressing these specific enzyme activities, the plant extracts effectively reduce the production of pro-inflammatory mediators like prostaglandins and nitric oxide. Additionally, certain components demonstrate affinity for apoptotic regulatory proteins, which helps modulate programmed cell death in cancer cell lines. This multi-target approach underlies its traditional uses in treating inflammation and various neoplastic conditions.
