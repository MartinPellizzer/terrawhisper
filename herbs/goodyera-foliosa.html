What's the taxonomical classification of Goodyera foliosa?

Goodyera foliosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, which places it within the genus Goodyera, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Goodyera foliosa.
