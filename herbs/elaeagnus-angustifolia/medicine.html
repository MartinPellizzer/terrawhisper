What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Elaeagnus Angustifolia (Silverberry)?

Elaeagnus angustifolia, also known as silverberry, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and acting as an antioxidant.

Its medicinal constituents include eleagnin, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties. The herb is often used to prepare teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be administered orally. However, excessive consumption of silverberry can cause gastrointestinal upset and allergic reactions in some individuals.

It is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before using this herb, particularly for pregnant or breastfeeding women.

What are the possible side effect of using Elaeagnus angustifolia improperly?

Improper use of Elaeagnus angustifolia increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory issues.

Furthermore, silverberry extracts may interact with medications, exacerbate existing health conditions, or even trigger severe allergic responses in some individuals.
