1. Use under medical supervision

When using Elaeagnus angustifolia medicinally, it's important to consume in moderation due to its potential interactions with other medications and its effects on the nervous system.

The plant contains alkaloids that can cause adverse reactions at high doses, such as vomiting, diarrhea, and increased heart rate. Additionally, it may enhance the activity of sedatives, antidepressants, or antihistamines, leading to excessive drowsiness, dizziness, or respiratory depression.

To avoid these risks, it's crucial to follow recommended dosages and consult with a healthcare professional before using silverberry medicinally.
