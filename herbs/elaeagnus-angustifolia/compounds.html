Russian Silver Bush (Elaeagnus angustifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds of Russian Silver Bush include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Terpenoids and saponins contribute to its antimicrobial and immune-supporting qualities. Coumarin and essential oils provide aromatic and potential antispasmodic benefits, while glycosides and anthocyanins support cardiovascular health. Alkaloids may offer additional therapeutic properties.
