Festuca obtusata

Festuca obtusata, also known as sharp fescue or obtuse fescue, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

Traditionally, this plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, making it useful in treating various health issues.

The health benefits of Festuca obtusata include reducing inflammation, soothing digestive issues, and supporting the immune system.

Herbal preparations of this plant can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, and it is often used in traditional medicine to promote overall well-being.
