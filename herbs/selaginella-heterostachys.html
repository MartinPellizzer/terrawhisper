What's the taxonomical classification of Selaginella heterostachys?

Selaginella heterostachys belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Lycopodiidae and is organized within the order Selaginellales. Its taxonomic placement continues through the family Selaginellaceae, which contains the genus Selaginella, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Selaginella heterostachys.
