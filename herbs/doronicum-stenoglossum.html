What's the taxonomical classification of Doronicum stenoglossum?

Doronicum stenoglossum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Asterales and the family Asteraceae, which characterizes it as a member of the sunflower or daisy family. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Doronicum, and its unique species designation, stenoglossum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
