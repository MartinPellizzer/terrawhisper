Polium Germander (Teucrium polium) Uses

Polium Germander is used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, cough, and asthma. It helps alleviate symptoms of the common cold, tonsillitis, and sinusitis by reducing inflammation and clearing lung congestion. Its anti-inflammatory properties make it effective for soothing an inflamed throat and supporting the body's response to respiratory infections.

Polium Germander (Teucrium polium) Monograph

Teucrium polium has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis.
