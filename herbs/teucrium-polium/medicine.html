What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Teucrium Polium (Wood Sage)?

Teucrium polium, also known as wood sage, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and alleviating digestive issues.

The herb contains terpenes and flavonoids, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal properties. Preparations such as teas and tinctures can be made from the plant, and it has also been used in traditional medicine as a poultice. However, excessive use of wood sage may cause digestive upset, and it is contraindicated for individuals with peptic ulcers.

Precautions should be taken to avoid overuse, especially when using the plant as a medicinal treatment.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Teucrium polium.

What are the medicinal preparations of Teucrium polium?

What precautions to take when using Teucrium polium medicinally?

Before using Teucrium polium for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage and usage.
