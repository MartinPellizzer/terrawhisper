10 Medicinal Constituents Of Teucrium Polium (Wood Sage)

Teucrium polium has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The iridoid glycosides have been shown to reduce inflammation and improve wound healing, while the flavonoids exhibit antispasmodic effects, helping to soothe digestive issues. Additionally, the phenolic acids may help protect against oxidative stress, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer.

Overall, these constituents can significantly improve people's lives by providing natural relief from a range of health concerns.
