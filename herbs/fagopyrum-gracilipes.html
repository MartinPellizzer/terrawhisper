Fagopyrum gracilipes

What's the taxonomical classification of Fagopyrum gracilipes?

Fagopyrum gracilipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Caryophyllales and falls under the family Polygonaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Fagopyrum and the species name gracilipes.
