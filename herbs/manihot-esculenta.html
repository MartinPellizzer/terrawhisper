Manihot Esculenta Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Manihot esculenta, commonly known as cassava, is a tropical plant native to South America, widely cultivated for its starchy root.

This herb is rich in carbohydrates and contains various bioactive constituents such as saponins, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

It offers health benefits including supporting digestion, reducing inflammation, and potentially aiding in weight management.
