What's the taxonomical classification of Cymbidium lancifolium?

Cymbidium lancifoliuum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is positioned in the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling under the genus Cymbidium, and is specifically identified by its species name, lancifoliuum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
