Narcissus redoutei

Narcissus redoutei is a species of flowering bulbous perennial plant, commonly known as a daffodil, belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include having anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antispasmodic effects.

The health benefits of Narcissus redoutei include its ability to reduce anxiety and stress, as well as its potential to have a positive impact on cardiovascular health due to its antioxidant properties.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in the form of essential oils, extracts, or dried flowers, which can be used to create remedies for various health issues, such as digestive problems and respiratory issues.
