A synonym for this condition is contamination, while a semantic variation could be disease caused by pathogens.

People with compromised immunity, such as the elderly, children, or those with chronic illnesses, are more likely to experience infections.

Ocimum tenuiflorum, also known as holy basil, has been shown to possess antimicrobial properties that can help the body combat and heal infections.

These natural compounds have been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat a variety of infectious conditions.

Scientific studies support the plant's efficacy in supporting the immune system and accelerating recovery from infections.
