What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia hemsleyana?

Actinidia hemsleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Ericales and the family Actinidiaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Actinidia. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, hemsleyana.
