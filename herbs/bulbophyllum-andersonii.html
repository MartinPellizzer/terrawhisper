Bulbophyllum andersonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized into the order Asparagales. As a member of the diverse family Orchidaceae, it is placed within the genus Bulbophyllum, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species andersonii.
