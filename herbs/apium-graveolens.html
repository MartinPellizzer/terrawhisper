Apium Graveolens Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Apium graveolens, commonly known as celery, is a biennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves, stalks, and seeds, which are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Celery offers various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and diuretic properties, which may support cardiovascular health and digestion.

The bioactive constituents in celery, such as apigenin, phthalides, and flavonoids, contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from celery leaves or seeds to harness its medicinal properties.
