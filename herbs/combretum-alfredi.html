Combretum alfredi

What's the taxonomical classification of Combretum alfredi?

Combretum alfredi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Myrtales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Combretaceae, specifically within the genus Combretum, and it is identified by the species name alfredi.

These interactions reduce the production of pro-inflammatory prostaglandins and leukotrienes, which accounts for its significant anti-inflammatory properties. The plant also contains phytochemicals that interact with antioxidant enzymes like superoxide dismutase and catalase to neutralize reactive oxygen species. By influencing these specific protein pathways, the plant helps mitigate oxidative stress and cellular damage. Furthermore, its bioactive compounds may modulate cytokine signaling to suppress systemic immune responses.
