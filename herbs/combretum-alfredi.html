What's the taxonomical classification of Combretum alfredi?

Combretum alfredi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Myrtales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Combretaceae, specifically within the genus Combretum, and it is identified by the species name alfredi.
