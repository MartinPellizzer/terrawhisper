What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis megalantha?

Pedicularis megalantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, falling into the genus Pedicularis, and is specifically identified by its species name, megalantha.
