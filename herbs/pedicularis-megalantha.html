What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis megalantha?

Pedicularis megalantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, falling into the genus Pedicularis, and is specifically identified by its species name, megalantha.
