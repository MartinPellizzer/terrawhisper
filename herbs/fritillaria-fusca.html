Fritillaria fusca

What's the taxonomical classification of Fritillaria fusca?

Fritillaria fusca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Liliales and is a member of the family Liliaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Fritillaria, and its specific species designation, fusca.
