Rhynchosia volubilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, this plant is situated within the genus Rhynchosia, ultimately being identified by its specific species name, volubilis.
