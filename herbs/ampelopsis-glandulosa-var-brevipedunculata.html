Ampelopsis glandulosa var. brevipedunculata

What's the taxonomical classification of Ampelopsis glandulosa var. brevipedunculata?

Ampelopsis glandulosa var. brevipedunculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Vitales. It is a member of the family Vitaeaceae and is further categorized within the genus Ampelopsis, belonging to the species Ampelopsis.
