Actinidia callosa var. discolor

What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia callosa var. discolor?

Actinidia callosa var. discolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Actinidiaceae and is nested within the genus Actinidia. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is identified as the species Actinidia callosa var. discolor.
