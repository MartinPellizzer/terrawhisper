What is Athyrium huttonii?

Athyrium huttonii is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

It has fronds that are bipinnate to tripinnate, with pinnae being linear to lanceolate in shape, and pinnules being linear to ovate. The fronds are typically 30-80 cm in length, and the stipe is brown to black in color, with scales that are lanceolate to ovate. This species is native to China and is found in shaded, humid areas at high elevations.

The fertile fronds are smaller than the sterile ones, and the sporangia are borne on the underside of the pinnules.
