Alyssum racemosum

Alyssum racemosum, commonly known as White Alyssum, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

The therapeutic actions of White Alyssum include its analgesic, antiseptic, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it a valuable remedy for various ailments.
