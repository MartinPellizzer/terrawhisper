Golden Ginkgo (Ginkgo Biloba Aureovariegata)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Ginkgo biloba aureovariegata.

Golden ginkgo, scientifically known as Ginkgo biloba 'aureovariegata', is a medicinal herb renowned for its adaptogenic properties and historical significance in traditional medicine. This variant of the ginkgo tree is prized for its unique golden foliage and its rich content of flavonoids and terpene lactones, which contribute to its cognitive and circulatory benefits. Traditionally used in Chinese medicine, it was believed to enhance memory, improve circulation, and support mental clarity, with records dating back over two millennia. In modern wellness practices, golden ginkgo is commonly used as a supplement to promote brain function, reduce anxiety, and support cardiovascular health. Its distinctive appearance and rare active compounds make it a valuable plant both for its medicinal potential and its ornamental beauty.
