Brunfelsia latifolia

Brunfelsia latifolia, commonly known as the "Yerba de Toro" or "Sleeping Plant," is a plant species native to Central and South America, belonging to the Brunfelsia genus.

Brunfelsia latifolia's therapeutic actions include sedative and anxiolytic properties, making it a potential natural remedy for stress relief.
