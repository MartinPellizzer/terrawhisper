Asafoetida (Ferula asafoetida)

Asafoetida (Ferula asafoetida) is a member of the Apiaceae family, native to Iran, Afghanistan, and Pakistan. Traditionally, its resin, oleoresin, and bark have been used for powders, decoctions, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its carminative, antispasmodic, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
