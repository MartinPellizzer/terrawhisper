10 Precautions To Take When Using Hyoscyamus Niger (Henbane)

Hyoscyamus niger has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in low doses and for short periods.

It's crucial to exercise caution due to its potent anticholinergic effects, which can lead to side effects like dry mouth, blurred vision, and confusion.

If not used judiciously, excessive doses can cause more severe consequences, including hallucinations, delirium, and even death.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Hyoscyamus niger medicinally.
