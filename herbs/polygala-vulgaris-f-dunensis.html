Polygala vulgaris f. dunensis

Polygala vulgaris f. dunensis, also known as the common jointgrass, is a plant species in the milkwort family (Polygalaceae).

It is traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help alleviate various health issues such as digestive problems and skin conditions.

The health benefits of Polygala vulgaris f. dunensis include its ability to soothe and calm the body, as well as its potential to support the immune system and reduce stress.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in teas, infusions, and tinctures, although its use should be consulted with a healthcare professional due to the lack of extensive scientific research on its effects.
