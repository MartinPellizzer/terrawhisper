Giant Dianthus (Dianthus superbus) Uses

Giant Dianthus is used to treat respiratory infections, cough, bronchitis, and sinusitis. It helps alleviate mucous congestion and symptoms of respiratory allergies and asthma. Its properties support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and clearing airways. It is commonly used for upper respiratory tract infections and other related conditions.

Giant Dianthus (Dianthus superbus) Monograph

1. Respiratory infection

Giant Dianthus is used to treat respiratory infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion by clearing mucus and soothing irritated airways. Traditionally, it is prepared as a decoction or tincture for internal use. Its active compounds support immune function and aid in recovery from respiratory ailments.

2. Cough

Giant Dianthus is used to treat coughs due to its expectorant properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the respiratory tract. Typically prepared as a decoction, it is effective for both dry and productive coughs. Its anti-inflammatory effects further support respiratory health.

3. Bronchitis

Giant Dianthus is used to treat bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. It helps reduce mucus buildup and soothe irritated airways. The plant's compounds may alleviate coughing and ease breathing in cases of chronic bronchial inflammation. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture for respiratory support.

4. Sinusitis

Giant Dianthus is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat sinusitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and mucus production, alleviating symptoms associated with sinus infections. The herb is often prepared as a decoction or tincture for internal use.
