Giant Dianthus (Dianthus superbus) Actions

Giant Dianthus exhibits several key therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, astringent, and diuretic properties. It also acts as a tonic and carminative, helping to support digestion and reduce gas. Additionally, it has antispasmodic and sedative effects, aiding in muscle relaxation and calming the nervous system. The plant is also noted for its expectorant and stimulant qualities, supporting respiratory health and energy levels.
