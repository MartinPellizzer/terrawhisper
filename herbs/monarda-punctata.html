Monarda Punctata Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Monarda punctata, commonly known as lemon bee balm, is a flowering plant native to North America, belonging to the mint family.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and flowers, which have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.

The herb offers various health benefits, including reducing inflammation, supporting respiratory health, and aiding digestion.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as rosmarinic acid, flavonoids, and volatile oils.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and salves can be made from Monarda punctata to harness its healing properties.
