What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Diospyros diversilimba has leaves that are elliptical to oblong in shape, typically measuring between 5 and 15 centimeters in length with entire margins and an acute to obtuse apex.

The underside of the foliage often exhibits a different color or pubescence compared to the upper surface.
