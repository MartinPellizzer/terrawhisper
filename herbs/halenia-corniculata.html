Halenia corniculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Gentianaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Halenia and the species corniculata.
