What's the taxonomical classification of Amomum dolichanthum?

Amomum dolichanthum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through the hierarchical ranks, the plant is a member of the order Zingiberales and falls under the family Zingiberaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is established within the genus Amomum, with the distinct species designation dolichanthum.
