This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia, as well as its antimicrobial action that can aid in wound healing.

The therapeutic actions of Salvia mellifera include its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve coughs, and reduce fever, making it a useful remedy in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents of Salvia mellifera include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
