What is Diaphananthe gabonensis?

It is a terrestrial orchid with elongated pseudobulbs, 8-20 cm long, bearing 2-4 linear, lanceolate leaves, 8-15 cm long. The inflorescence is a raceme, 10-25 cm long, with 3-6 flowers. Each flower is 2.5-3 cm long, with a white lip and a yellow labellum with a red stripe.
