Cyclea sutchuenensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cyclea sutchuenensis?

Cyclea sutchuenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and into the family Menispermaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Cyclea, specifically identified by the species epithet sutchuenensis.
