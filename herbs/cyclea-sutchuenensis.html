Cyclea sutchuenensis has woody climbing vines characterized by trifoliate leaves with ovate to elliptical leaflets and small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers arranged in axillary racemes. Its stems are flexible yet robust, allowing it to anchor itself to surrounding vegetation for upward growth.
