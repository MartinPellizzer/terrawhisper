Trichosanthes ovigera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Cucurbitaceae, which is characterized by its distinct botanical traits. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Trichosanthes, and its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the species name ovigera.
