What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum brachypodum var. crispulum?

Aconitum brachypodum var. crispulum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and is a member of the order Ranunculales. Furthermore, it is situated within the family Ranunculaceae and is categorized under the genus Aconitum, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety crispulum of the species Aconitum brachypoduim.
