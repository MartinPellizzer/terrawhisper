What is Microsorum wilkesii?

Microsorum wilkesii is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial, perennial herb with a rhizome, growing up to 50 cm in length and producing fronds that are 20-50 cm long and 5-10 cm wide. The fronds have pinnate or bipinnate venation, with pinnae that are lanceolate to elliptical in shape and have a cuneate base. The leaves are simple, entire, and have a leathery texture, with a dark green color on the adaxial surface and a lighter green color on the abaxial surface.

Microsorum wilkesii is native to the tropical forests of the Pacific Islands, specifically in Fiji and Samoa.
