10 Possible Side Effects Of Maclura Pomifera (Osage Orange)

Maclura pomifera has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These side effects can be caused by the herb's high concentration of urushiol, a chemical similar to that found in poison ivy and oak.

Severe reactions can worsen people's lives by causing fever, chills, and debilitating pain, potentially leading to missed workdays, social isolation, and strained relationships due to discomfort and fatigue.
