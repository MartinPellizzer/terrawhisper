10 Medicinal Constituents Of Maclura Pomifera (Osage Orange)

Maclura pomifera has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These compounds possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions. For instance, the flavonoids in Maclura pomifera may help alleviate symptoms of arthritis by reducing inflammation and pain, while its antimicrobial properties can aid in wound healing and prevent infection.

These medicinal properties can significantly improve people's lives by providing natural relief from chronic pain and promoting overall well-being.
