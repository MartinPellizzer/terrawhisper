Asplenium polyodon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Asplenium polyodon has pinnate fronds characterized by numerous narrow, lanceolate leaflets that are arranged symmetrically along a central rachis.
