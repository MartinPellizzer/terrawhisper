What is Senegalia hereroensis?

Senegalia hereroensis is a tree species in the family Fabaceae.

It has a spreading to upright growth habit, with a height range of 5-10 meters. The bark is grey to brown, often with longitudinal fissures. The leaves are bipinnate, with 3-5 pairs of pinnae, each pinna comprising 10-20 pairs of leaflets; the leaflets are linear to lanceolate, 10-40 mm long, and dark green.

The flowers are yellow, borne in long, drooping racemes, and are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.
