Artemisia stricta var. stricta

What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia stricta var. stricta?

Artemisia stricta var. stricta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Asterales and is a representative of the family Asteraceae. Within this botanical hierarchy, the plant is identified by the genus Artemisia, specifically categorized under the species Artemisia stricta and the variety stricta.
