As discussed in a study, Pseudognaphalium affine contains high contents of phytochemicals such as flavonoids and polyphenols that provide anti-inflammatory and tissue-protective activities. Researchers isolated 1,5-, 3,4-, 3,5-, and 4,5-diCQAs from the P. affine methanol extract to investigate their potential. Among these diCQAs, 1,5-diCQA was found to significantly inhibit apoptosis and enhance viability in human corneal epithelial cell cultures under hyperosmolar stress.
