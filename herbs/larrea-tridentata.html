Horticulturally, the bush is prized for its ability to thrive in arid environments, making it an excellent choice for xeriscaping and drought-resistant landscaping.

Botanically, creosote bush has been found to contain a unique combination of lignans and phenolic acids, contributing to its distinctive aroma and medicinal properties, while historical records dating back to ancient Native American cultures attest to the plant's long-standing importance in traditional medicine and spirituality.
