Agastache urticifolia var. glaucifolia

Agastache urticifolia var. glaucifolia, commonly known as Pale Giant Hyssop, is a perennial herb in the mint family (Lamiaceae) native to the United States.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include health benefits such as reducing inflammation, relieving respiratory issues, and exhibiting antimicrobial properties.

The therapeutic actions of Pale Giant Hyssop include its ability to act as an expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic agent, making it useful for treating coughs, colds, and other respiratory conditions.
