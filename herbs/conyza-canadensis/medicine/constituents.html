10 Medicinal Constituents Of Conyza Canadensis (Canadian Fleabane)

These compounds are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The sesquiterpenes can help reduce inflammation and alleviate pain, while the flavonoids can protect against oxidative stress and cell damage.

These medicinal properties can improve people's lives by providing relief from arthritis, gout, and other inflammatory conditions, as well as offering protection against chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease.
