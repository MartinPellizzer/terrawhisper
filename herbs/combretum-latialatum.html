What's the taxonomical classification of Combretum latialatum?

Combretum latialatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is part of the order Myrtales and falls under the family Combretaceae. Finally, it is defined by its specific genus, Combretum, and its unique species designation, latialatum.
