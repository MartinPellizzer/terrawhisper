Cistus Clusii (Cistus clusii) Compounds

Cistus clusii contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and digestive benefits. Terpenoids and essential oils are responsible for its aromatic and antimicrobial properties.
