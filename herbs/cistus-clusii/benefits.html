10 Best Cistus Clusii Health Benefits

Cistus clusii, also known as the rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region that has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties.

It is particularly valued for its high content of flavonoids and polyphenols, which are powerful antioxidants that help neutralize free radicals in the body. These compounds may contribute to reducing inflammation and supporting immune function, making Cistus clusii beneficial for overall health. Some studies suggest that extracts from this plant may support respiratory health and help alleviate symptoms of colds and coughs.

Additionally, its antimicrobial properties may aid in preventing infections and promoting skin health.
