Possible Side Effects of Rubus ambifarius var. senticetorum

Rubus ambifarius var. senticetorum can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea and stomach pain, due to its high content of anthraquinones.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to kidney damage and liver toxicity.

Additionally, some individuals may experience allergic reactions, such as skin rashes and itching, after coming into contact with the plant's sap or leaves.

In rare cases, improper use of Rubus ambifarius var. senticetorum may cause cardiovascular problems, including hypertension and arrhythmia.
