Bacopa humilis

Bacopa humilis, commonly known as Water Hyssop, is a perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its ability to enhance cognitive function, improve memory, and reduce anxiety and stress levels.

The therapeutic actions of Bacopa humilis are attributed to its adaptogenic properties, which help to balance the body's response to stress, as well as its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities.

The bioactive constituents of Bacopa humilis include bacosides, a group of saponins that are responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as other compounds such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids.
