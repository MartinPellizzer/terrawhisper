What is Trichotosia buruensis?

Trichotosia buruensis is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a creeping rhizome, producing pseudobulbs that are covered with a sheath-like leaf at the base. The leaves are linear, lance-shaped, and have a prominent midrib, measuring 12-30 cm in length. The inflorescence is a raceme that produces 1-5 flowers, which are greenish-yellow or white in color, and have a lip that is three-lobed and slightly notched at the apex.

The species is endemic to the Buru Island in Indonesia, and has been found growing in humid, shaded areas at elevations of 500-1000 meters above sea level.
