Smithia conferta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, this plant is placed within the genus Smithia, eventually reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species conferta.
