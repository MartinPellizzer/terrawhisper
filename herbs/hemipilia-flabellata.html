What's the taxonomical classification of Hemipilia flabellata?

Hemipilia flabellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asparagales. As a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, it is specifically categorized within the genus Hemipilia, ultimately reaching its binomial designation at the species level as Hemipiilia flabellata.
