Evolvulus alsinoides var. decumbens

What's the taxonomical classification of Evolvulus alsinoides var. decumbens?

Evolvulus alsinoides var. decumbens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Solanales. The plant is a member of the family Convolvulaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Evolvulus. Ultimately, its precise taxonomic designation is the species Evolvulus alsinoides var. decumbens.
