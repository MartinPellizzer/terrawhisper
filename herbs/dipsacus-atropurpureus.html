Dipsacus atropurpureus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. The plant's hierarchical progression continues through the order Dipsacales and the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, it is situated within the genus Dipsacus, with its specific designation being the species atropurpureus.
