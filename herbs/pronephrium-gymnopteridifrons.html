What's the taxonomical classification of Pronephrium gymnopteridifrons?

Pronephrium gymnopteridifrons belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Taxonomically, this plant is situated within the family Aspleniaceae, specifically placed in the genus Pronephrium, with the specific epithet gymnopteridifrons denoting its unique species identity.
