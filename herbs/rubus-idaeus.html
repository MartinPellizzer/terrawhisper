Possible Side Effects of rubus idaeus

Rubus idaeus can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

Some users may experience nausea, dizziness, or stomach cramps after consuming Rubus idaeus in excess.

In rare cases, overconsumption can lead to kidney problems or an increased risk of bleeding, especially when taken with anticoagulant medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Rubus idaeus, especially for individuals with pre-existing medical conditions or taking prescription medications.
