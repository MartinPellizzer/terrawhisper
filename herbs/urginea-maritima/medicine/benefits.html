10 Health Benefits Of Urginea Maritima (Squill)

Urginea maritima has health benefits such as treating cardiovascular diseases, reducing inflammation, and improving respiratory issues.

Its medicinal properties include saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids that exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and vasodilatory effects.

These benefits can improve people's lives by helping to lower blood pressure, alleviate symptoms of asthma and bronchitis, and reduce the risk of heart disease.
