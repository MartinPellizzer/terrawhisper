1. Reduces inflammation markers

Inflammatory markers are abnormal levels of proteins or chemicals in the body that signal chronic inflammation.

Chronic inflammation is a persistent, low-level immune response that can damage tissues over time.

It is often caused by prolonged exposure to irritants, infections, or genetic factors, and is commonly experienced by individuals with autoimmune disorders, metabolic syndrome, or chronic diseases like arthritis and diabetes.

Peganum harmala plant has shown potential in reducing these markers through its bioactive compounds, which may modulate inflammatory pathways.

This effect could offer therapeutic benefits for those suffering from persistent inflammatory conditions.

How this herb helps with reduces inflammation markers?

Peganum harmala reduces inflammation markers because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory properties by modulating key inflammatory pathways in the body. Alkaloids like harmine and harmaline inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines. Flavonoids contribute by scavenging free radicals and reducing oxidative stress.

Together, these constituents help lower markers of inflammation, supporting overall immune balance and health.

Scientific Research

According to "Endocrine, metabolic & immune disorders drug targets", Peganum harmala reduces inflammation markers by significantly attenuating poly-arthritis and restoring the levels of C-reactive protein, rheumatoid factor, alanine transaminase, aspartate transaminase, and alkaline phosphatase in poly-arthritic rats.
