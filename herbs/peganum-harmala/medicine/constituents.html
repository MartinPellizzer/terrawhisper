10 Medicinal Constituents Of Peganum Harmala (Syrian Rue)

Peganum harmala, also known as Syrian Rue, has active constituents such as alkaloids (harmine, harmaline, and harmalin), phenolic acids, flavonoids, and saponins.

These constituents possess medicinal properties that include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anticonvulsant activities. The plant's alkaloids have been traditionally used to treat various conditions such as anxiety, insomnia, and depression, while its flavonoids may help protect against cardiovascular disease and cancer.

By utilizing the medicinal properties of Syrian Rue, individuals may experience improved mental clarity, reduced stress, and enhanced overall well-being.
