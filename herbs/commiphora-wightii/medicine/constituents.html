10 Medicinal Constituents Of Commiphora Wightii (Guggulu)

Commiphora wightii has active constituents such as triterpenes, resin, and essential oils.

These constituents provide medicinal properties including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects. The triterpenes, specifically guggulsterone, help reduce cholesterol levels, improve triglycerides, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression.

This can improve the lives of individuals with high cholesterol, anxiety, and depression, enabling them to maintain a healthy lifestyle and achieve overall well-being.
