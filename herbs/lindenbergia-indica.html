Lindenbergia indica

Lindenbergia indica, commonly known as Indian Cuckoo Flower, is a plant species belonging to the Lindenbergia genus, native to the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia.

The therapeutic actions of Lindenbergia indica include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which help in soothing and protecting the body from various health issues.

Possible Side Effects of Lindenbergia indica

Lindenbergia indica can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and increased heart rate.

Prolonged use or excessive dosage can cause anxiety, insomnia, and dizziness in sensitive individuals.

Additionally, Lindenbergia indica may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, exacerbating underlying health conditions.
