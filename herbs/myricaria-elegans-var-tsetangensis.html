Myricaria elegans var. tsetangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Myricaria elegans var. tsetangensis?

Myricaria elegans var. tsetangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Tamaricaceae. This specific variety is nested within the genus Myricaria, eventually reaching its full taxonomic designation as the species Myricaria elegans var. tsetangensis.
