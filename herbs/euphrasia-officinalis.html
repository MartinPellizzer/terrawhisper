What to know about Euphrasia officinalis before using it medicinally

Euphrasia officinalis, commonly known as eyebright, is a medicinal herb primarily used to treat eye and respiratory infections, such as conjunctivitis and bronchitis, as well as skin conditions like eczema and acne.

The herb has been found to possess several health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. The main therapeutic properties of Euphrasia officinalis include its ability to reduce inflammation, kill bacteria, and scavenge free radicals. The herb is rich in bioactive compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoid glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

People often prepare Euphrasia officinalis in the form of a tincture or a tea infusion, which can be used to treat various health conditions and promote overall well-being.

Featured Study:

According to a study published by "Fitoterapia", the aqueous extract of the leaf of Euphrasia officinalis has been found to have a positive effect on blood glucose levels in alloxan-diabetic rats, effectively lowering raised glucose levels. Additionally, the extract did not cause a drop in blood glucose levels in normal rats, indicating its potential safety in use. This suggests that Euphrasia officinalis may be a useful natural remedy for managing diabetes in some cases.

What are the main medicinal uses of Euphrasia officinalis?

The main medicinal uses of Euphrasia officinalis are for the treatment of eye conditions, particularly dry eye, due to its ability to increase tear production and reduce inflammation.

Euphrasia officinalis contains flavonoids, such as rutin and quercetin, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it effective in alleviating pink eye and conjunctivitis. Red eyes are also relieved by Euphrasia officinalis, thanks to its ability to reduce vascular permeability and alleviate symptoms of allergic conjunctivitis, such as itching and swelling. The plant's anti-inflammatory properties also make it useful for treating eye inflammation, which can lead to secondary conditions like glaucoma, for which Euphrasia officinalis may help lower intraocular pressure.

Additionally, Euphrasia officinalis can help alleviate eye strain and infections, and its antiseptic properties can soothe itchy eyes.
