Sweet Flag (Acorus calamus) Actions

Sweet Flag exhibits carminative, tonic, and bitter properties, aiding digestion and promoting overall vitality. It acts as an antispasmodic and stimulant, relieving cramps and boosting energy. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant effects help reduce inflammation and clear mucus. Additionally, it functions as a diuretic, sedative, and astringent, supporting urinary health, calming the nervous system, and tightening tissues.
