Acorus Calamus Benefits

The anxiolytic effects of Acorus calamus have also been observed to benefit individuals with conditions such as generalized anxiety disorder (GAD) and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

By promoting relaxation and reducing stress, Acorus calamus may help individuals manage anxiety symptoms and improve their overall mental well-being.
