10 Best Acorus Calamus Health Benefits

Acorus calamus, also known as sweet flag, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its various health benefits.

It contains compounds such as alkaloids and volatile oils that may support cognitive function and help with memory improvement. The plant is also believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can contribute to overall wellness. In some cultures, it has been used to treat respiratory conditions and digestive issues.

However, it should be used with caution due to potential toxicity if consumed in large amounts.
