Gonocaryum lobbianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Gonocaryum lobbianum?

Gonocaryum lobbianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Aquifoliales. This plant is specifically nested within the family Cardiopteridaceae, falling under the genus Gonocaryum, and is identified by its specific species designation, lobbianum.
