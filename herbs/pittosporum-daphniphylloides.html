Pittosporum daphniphylloides

What's the taxonomical classification of Pittosporum daphniphylloides?

Pittosporum daphniphylloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Apiales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Pittosporaceae and the genus Pittosporum, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species daphniphylloides.

This plant is used to treat various skin ailments, including inflammatory conditions, minor wounds, and topical irritations. Traditional medicinal applications often focus on the leaves to soothe localized discomfort or swelling. It is important to note that these uses are largely rooted in folk medicine rather than standardized clinical protocols.
