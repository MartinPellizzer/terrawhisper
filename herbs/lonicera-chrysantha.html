What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera chrysantha?

Lonicera chrysantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Dipsacales, it is placed within the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Lonicera, with its specific designation being the species chrysantha.
