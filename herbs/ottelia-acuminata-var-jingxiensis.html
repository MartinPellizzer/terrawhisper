Ottelia acuminata var. jingxiensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Ottelia acuminata var. jingxiensis?

Ottelia acuminata var. jingxiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Alismatales. Its taxonomic placement continues through the family Hydrocharitaceae and the genus Ottelia, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety jingxiensis of the species Ottelia acuminata.
