Gossypium Hirsutum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

It is also used in herbal medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

The bioactive constituents in this herb include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Medicinal Parts of gossypium hirsutum

Gossypium hirsutum seed, commonly known as cottonseed, is the primary medicinal part of the plant, valued for its nutritional content and potential therapeutic properties.

The main herbal preparations from the seed include oil extracts, which are used for their emollient and anti-inflammatory properties, and seed powders that may support digestive health.
