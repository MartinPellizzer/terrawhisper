Side Effects of gossypium hirsutum

Gossypium hirsutum results in allergic reactions, particularly in individuals with a predisposition to hypersensitivity.

Exposure to this herb can trigger symptoms such as sneezing, runny nose, and even asthma-like symptoms in the respiratory system. The herb is also known to cause skin irritation, leading to redness, dryness, and a burning sensation upon contact with the skin.

Itching and rashes are common side effects, often appearing as small, raised bumps or patches on the skin. Additionally, Gossypium hirsutum may lead to swelling, particularly in the facial or hand areas, which can be uncomfortable and, in severe cases, require medical attention. These health side effects highlight the importance of caution when handling or using this herb, especially for those with known allergies or sensitive skin.

Individuals experiencing severe symptoms should seek medical advice promptly to prevent complications.
