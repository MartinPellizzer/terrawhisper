10 Possible Side Effects Of Origanum Compactum (Winter Savory)

Causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, allergic predisposition, and interaction with other medications.

These side effects can worsen people's lives, for example, causing stomach cramps and diarrhea that lead to dehydration and decreased productivity.
