Festuca mediterranea

Festuca mediterranea, commonly known as Spanish fescue, is a type of grass native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its compact growth habit and fine leaves.

This plant is valued for its health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation and improve respiratory function, due to its therapeutic actions such as anti-inflammatory and bronchodilatory properties.

The bioactive constituents of Festuca mediterranea include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various ailments, including digestive issues and skin conditions, due to its potential therapeutic actions.

Possible Side Effects of Festuca mediterranea

Festuca mediterranea can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal issues in some individuals.
