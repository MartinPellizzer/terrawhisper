Neolitsea cambodiana

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Neolitsea cambodiana has lanceolate to elliptic leaves that are evergreen, coriaceous, and possess entire margins with pinnate venation. The leaf surfaces are typically lustrous and dark green on the upper side, while the undersides may appear slightly paler. Its stems exhibit a woody texture and are characterized by a smooth to slightly lenticellate bark. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in panicles, eventually giving rise to small drupes. The overall growth habit of this species is that of a medium to large tree with a dense canopy.
