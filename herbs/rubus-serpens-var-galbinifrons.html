Rubus serpens var. galbinifrons

Rubus serpens var. galbinifrons is a lesser-known variety of the trailing blackberry (Rubus serpens) plant, native to the western United States.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating various skin conditions and wounds due to its astringent and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Rubus serpens var. galbinifrons include its potential to reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and exhibit antimicrobial activity, making it a valuable resource in traditional medicine.

Herbal preparations of this plant can be made into teas, tinctures, or infusions, allowing for its active compounds to be extracted and utilized for their medicinal properties.
