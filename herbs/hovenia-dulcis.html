Chinese Date (Hovenia dulcis)

Chinese Date (Hovenia dulcis) is a member of the Rhamnaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
