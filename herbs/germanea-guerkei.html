Germanea guerkei

Germanea guerkei, also known as German chamomile, is an annual plant in the daisy family that is native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its small white daisy-like flowers and apple-like fragrance.

The medicinal properties of German chamomile lie in its essential oils, which have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects, making it a popular ingredient in herbal remedies.

The health benefits of German chamomile include reducing anxiety and stress, improving sleep quality, soothing digestive issues, and alleviating menstrual cramps.

German chamomile can be prepared as a tea, infused oil, or taken as a supplement, and is also used in skincare products due to its calming and soothing properties.
