10 Possible Side Effects Of Trillium Erectum (Stiff Trillium)

Trillium erectum has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

These side effects can be caused by overdosing, incorrect preparation, or individual sensitivities. In severe cases, they can lead to worsened digestive issues, skin irritation, and compromised treatment efficacy.

For instance, if not properly managed, the allergic reactions can escalate into anaphylactic shock, and the interactions with medications can lead to reduced treatment effectiveness, complicating health issues.
