Wake Robin (Trillium erectum) Actions

Wake Robin is a tonic and stimulant that supports overall vitality. It has bitter properties that aid digestion and is astringent to tone tissues. Its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic actions help with respiratory and digestive issues. It also acts as a diuretic, carminative, and expectorant, promoting healthy elimination and clearing mucus.
