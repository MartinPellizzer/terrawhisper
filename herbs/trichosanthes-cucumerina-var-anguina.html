Trichosanthes cucumerina var. anguina

What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes cucumerina var. anguina?

Trichosanthes cucumerina var. anguina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Cucurbitales and falls into the family Cucurbitaceae. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Trichosanthes, specifically categorized under the species Trichosanthes cucumerina as the variety anguina.
