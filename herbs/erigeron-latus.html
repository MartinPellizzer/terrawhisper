What is Erigeron latus?

Erigeron latus is a perennial herbaceous plant, native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, and is part of the subfamily Asteroideae and tribe Astereae. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves with linear to lanceolate shapes, 2-5 cm long, and a corymbiform inflorescence with white to pinkish flowers, 3-4 mm in diameter. The flowers have 5-7 petals, and the achenes are 1.5-2 mm long with a pappus of 8-10 bristles.

The plant grows 10-30 cm tall and thrives in dry to moist environments with full sun to partial shade.
