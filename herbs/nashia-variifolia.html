Nashia variifolia

Nashia variifolia is a plant species that belongs to the family Annonaceae and is commonly known as Chinese prickly ash.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly its bark, which contains a high concentration of alkaloids that have anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.

Nashia variifolia has been used to treat various health issues, including pain relief, fever reduction, and gastrointestinal problems, due to its ability to reduce inflammation and improve digestion.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction or infusion, with herbal preparations including tea, capsules, and tinctures, to leverage its health benefits and medicinal properties.
