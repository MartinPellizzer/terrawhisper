Tanacetum Cinerariifolium Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Tanacetum cinerariifolium, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to North America, characterized by its blue-green foliage and yellow flowers.

This herb is valued for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties, making it beneficial for reducing anxiety, improving sleep, and supporting respiratory health.

The therapeutic actions of blue mistflower are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and volatile oils.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves can be made from its leaves and flowers to harness its medicinal benefits.
