Acacia yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Acacia yunnanensis?

Acacia yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Acacia, specifically identified as the species yunnanensis.
