Coral Bean (Caesalpinia crista)

Coral Bean (Caesalpinia crista) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and flowers have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and european herbal medicine.
