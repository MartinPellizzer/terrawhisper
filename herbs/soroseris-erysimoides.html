Soroseris erysimoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Soroseris erysimoides?

Soroseris erysimoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Soroseris, specifically identified as the species erysimoides.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat respiratory ailments such as coughs, asthma, bronchitis, and various types of influenza. It is frequently utilized in traditional medicine to soothe inflamed airways and reduce congestion. Practitioners often prepare decoctions or infusions from the plant to alleviate symptoms of chest congestion. These herbal applications aim to clear phlegm and ease the difficulty of breathing. The plant's properties are specifically targeted at calming the lungs and supporting general respiratory health.
