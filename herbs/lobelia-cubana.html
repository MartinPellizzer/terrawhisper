Lobelia cubana

Lobelia cubana, a species of flowering plant in the bellflower family, is native to Cuba and is also known as Lobelia cubana or Lobelia cubana var. cubana.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and expectorant properties.

The health benefits of Lobelia cubana include relief from respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its potential to reduce inflammation and improve digestion.

Herbal preparations of Lobelia cubana can be found in various forms, including teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat respiratory and digestive issues.
