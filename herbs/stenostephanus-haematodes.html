What is Stenostephanus haematodes?

The plant produces purple flowers in axillary clusters, each comprising 2-3 flowers with a tubular corolla, and measures 2-4 cm in length.

Stenostephanus haematodes is native to tropical Africa, specifically in the countries of Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda.
