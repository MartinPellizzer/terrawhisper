Aloe glabrescens

Aloe glabrescens, also known as the Aloe aristata or Lace Aloe, is a succulent plant species native to the Eastern Cape Province of South Africa.

This plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it effective in treating various health issues such as skin irritations, wounds, and mouth ulcers.

The health benefits of Aloe glabrescens include soothing and calming irritated skin, promoting wound healing, and supporting oral health.
