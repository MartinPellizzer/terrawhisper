What's the taxonomical classification of Ligularia achyrotricha?

Ligularia achyrotricha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, which is one of the most diverse families of flowering plants. Within this family, it is specifically categorized under the genus Ligularia, with its unique identity defined by its species name, achyrotricha.
