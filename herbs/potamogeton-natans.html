What's the taxonomical classification of Potamogeton natans?

Potamogeton natans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Alismatales and the family Potamogetonaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Potamogeton, and its unique species designation, natans.
