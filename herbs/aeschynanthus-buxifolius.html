What's the taxonomical classification of Aeschynanthus buxifolius?

Aeschynanthus buxifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Gesneriaceae, specifically within the genus Aeschynanthus, and is identified by its unique species designation, buxifolius.
