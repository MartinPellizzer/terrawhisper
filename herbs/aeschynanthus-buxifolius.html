What's the taxonomical classification of Aeschynanthus buxifolius?

Aeschynanthus buxifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the taxonomic order Lamiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Gesneriaceae, specifically within the genus Aeschynanthus, and is identified by its unique species designation, buxifolius.
