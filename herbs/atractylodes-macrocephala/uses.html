Chinese Atractylodes (Atractylodes macrocephala) Uses

Chinese Atractylodes is primarily used to treat gastrointestinal discomfort, including dyspepsia, diarrhea, gastritis, and nausea. It helps alleviate gastric distress and food poisoning by strengthening the stomach and reducing inflammation. It is also effective for gastroenteritis and gastrointestinal gas, promoting digestion and easing digestive disturbances.
