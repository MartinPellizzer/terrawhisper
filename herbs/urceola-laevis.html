Urceola laevis

The plant contains compounds with anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties, which have been shown to provide health benefits such as reducing fever, relieving respiratory issues, and treating wounds.

Urceola laevis is typically prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and its herbal preparations are used to support immune function and alleviate symptoms of various health conditions.
