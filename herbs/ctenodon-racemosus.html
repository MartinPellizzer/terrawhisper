Ctenodon racemosus

Ctenodon racemosus, commonly known as Gokshura, is a plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Gokshura is known for its various health benefits, including its ability to improve kidney function, lower blood pressure, and act as an anti-inflammatory agent.

The therapeutic actions of Gokshura are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include saponins, flavonoids, and glycosides.

The saponins and flavonoids present in Gokshura are responsible for its diuretic and antioxidant properties, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine.

Ctenodon racemosus has many medicinal parts, such as the roots, which are commonly used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever and inflammation, due to their content of saponins and glycosides.

Furthermore, improper preparation of the plant may cause skin irritation and other dermatological issues in sensitive individuals.
