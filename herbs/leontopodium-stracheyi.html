Leontopodium stracheyi

What's the taxonomical classification of Leontopodium stracheyi?

Leontopodium stracheyi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic lineage through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, a large group of flowering plants, and is specifically categorized under the genus Leontopodium, with its unique identity defined by the species name stracheyi.
