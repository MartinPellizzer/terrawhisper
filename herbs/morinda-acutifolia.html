Morinda acutifolia has many medicinal parts, such as its roots, leaves, and stems.

Possible Side Effects of Morinda acutifolia

Additionally, individuals with allergies or sensitivities may experience skin irritation, itching, or rashes when using Morinda acutifolia improperly.
