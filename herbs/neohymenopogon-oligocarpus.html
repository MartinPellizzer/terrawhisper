What's the taxonomical classification of Neohymenopogon oligocarpus?

Neohygeno-pogon oligocarpus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Neohygeno-pogon, and its species designation, oligocarpus.
