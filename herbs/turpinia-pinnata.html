Turpinia pinnata

Turpinia pinnata, also known as the 'Red Back', is a tropical plant species native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia, characterized by its dark red to purple stem and compound leaves.

The plant has been found to have medicinal properties, particularly in its ability to inhibit platelet aggregation and exhibit antioxidant activity.
