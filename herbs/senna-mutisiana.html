Senna mutisiana

Senna mutisiana, commonly known as Colombian senna, is a plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family.

This plant is used for its medicinal properties, particularly for its laxative effects, and has been traditionally employed to treat various gastrointestinal disorders, such as constipation and dyspepsia.

The health benefits of Senna mutisiana include its ability to stimulate bowel movements, relieve symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, and provide relief from menstrual cramps and other menstrual-related issues.

The therapeutic actions of this plant can be attributed to its bioactive constituents, including anthraquinone glycosides, which have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
