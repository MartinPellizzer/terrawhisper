Polystichum alcicorne

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Polystichum alcicorne has lanceolate to narrowly elliptical fronds characterized by pinnate dissection and a distinctively coriaceous texture. The individual pinnae are arranged symmetrically along a central stipe that exhibits a sturdy, fibrous structure. Each leaflet possesses serrated margins and a dark green coloration that provides a high level of contrast against the lighter midrib. The underside of the foliage contains specialized sori protected by indusia, which are essential for its reproductive cycle. These morphological features collectively allow the plant to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
