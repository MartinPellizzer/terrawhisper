Possible Side Effects of galium verum

Galium verum can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Excessive consumption can also lead to dizziness, headaches, and fatigue.

Some individuals may experience allergic reactions, such as skin rashes and itching, due to the herb's volatile oils.

Additionally, galium verum may interact with certain medications, exacerbating conditions like high blood pressure and kidney disease.
