Eupatorium parviflorum

Eupatorium parviflorum, also known as thoroughwort or bitter thoroughwort, is a perennial wildflower native to North America and parts of Europe.

The plant has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects, making it useful for treating conditions such as arthritis, muscle pain, and menstrual cramps.

The health benefits of Eupatorium parviflorum include reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and supporting the immune system, as well as exhibiting antimicrobial and antioxidant properties.

Eupatorium parviflorum can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, tinctures, and salves, with the root and leaves being the most commonly used parts for medicinal purposes.
