Bauhinia variegata var. candida

What's the taxonomical classification of Bauhinia variegata var. candida?

Bauhinia variegata var. candida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Further taxonomic refinement places it within the genus Bauhinia, specifically identified as the variety candida of the species Bauhinia variegata.
