Berneuxia thibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Diapensiaceae and is uniquely represented by the genus Berneuxia, specifically within the species thibetica.
