Axonopus compressus

What's the taxonomical classification of Axonopus compressus?

Axonopus compressus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Poales and into the family Poaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by the genus Axonopus and is specifically designated as the species compressus.

The plant contains bioactive secondary metabolites like terpenoids and flavonoids that modulate these specific protein pathways. These multi-target mechanisms contribute to the plant's documented anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

While extensive clinical studies on human toxicity are limited, cautious handling is recommended to prevent unpredictable dermatological distress.
