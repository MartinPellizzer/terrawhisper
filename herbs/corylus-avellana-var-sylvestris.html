Corylus avellana var. sylvestris

The bark, leaves, and nuts of the wild hazel have been used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can help to soothe digestive issues, reduce fever, and support the immune system.

The health benefits of wild hazel include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and support the health of the digestive system.
