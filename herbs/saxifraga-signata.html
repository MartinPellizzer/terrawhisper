What's the taxonomical classification of Saxifraga signata?

Saxifraga signata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Saxifragales and falls under the family Saxifragaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its placement within the genus Saxifraga, specifically identifying it as the species signata.
