Saxifraga signata has a basal rosette of fleshy, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs and feature distinct, dark, mottled patterns. From this central rosette, a slender, upright flowering stalk emerges to support small, star-shaped blossoms. The flowers typically display white petals marked with unique reddish or purplish spots, which give the species its name.
