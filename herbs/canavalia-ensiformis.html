Canavalia ensiformis leaf is widely used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects.

Side Effects of canavalia ensiformis

Some people may experience headaches as a secondary effect due to the herb's impact on the body's systems. Furthermore, Canavalia ensiformis can cause respiratory issues such as coughing or difficulty breathing in sensitive individuals, and it may lead to skin irritation or rash upon contact.

These side effects highlight the importance of cautious use and consultation with a healthcare professional before incorporating this herb into one's regimen.
