What's the taxonomical classification of Acer erianthum?

Acer erianthum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it is placed in the order Sapindales and the family Sapindaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by the genus Acer and the specific species name erianthum.
