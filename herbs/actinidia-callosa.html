What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia callosa?

Actinidia callosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Actinidiaceae, specifically belonging to the genus Actinidia, with the distinct species designation callosa.
