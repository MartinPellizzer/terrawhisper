Euphrasia officinalis, also known as eyebright, has been traditionally used to alleviate such inflammatory responses.

It is commonly used by individuals suffering from eye inflammation, such as conjunctivitis or blepharitis, as well as those with systemic inflammation due to conditions like arthritis.

How this herb helps with reduces inflammation?

Flavonoids further enhance its anti-inflammatory effects by scavenging free radicals and reducing oxidative stress.
