10 Medicinal Preparations Of Euphrasia Officinalis (Eyebright)

Euphrasia officinalis has several medicinal preparations, such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are useful for relieving eye irritations, conjunctivitis, and meibomianitis.

Eyebright tea, made by infusing the leaves and flowers, can be applied topically to reduce redness and swelling around the eyes.

Tinctures of eyebright can also be taken internally to address eye problems related to colds, flu, and sinus infections.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Euphrasia officinalis.

1. Teas

Euphrasia officinalis teas is used to alleviate a range of health issues.

It is traditionally employed to treat eye problems such as conjunctivitis, blepharitis, and dry eyes due to its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties. The tea is also used to soothe respiratory issues like bronchitis, asthma, and sinusitis by reducing congestion and promoting expectoration.

Additionally, eyebright tea has been used to calm digestive issues, such as indigestion and diarrhea, and as a mild laxative.
