Ardisia faberi

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ardisia faberi has simple, entire, elliptic to oblong leaves that are dark green, leathery, and possess an entire margin with an acute apex. The foliage is arranged in an opposite pattern along the woody stems of this evergreen shrub. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils, typically displaying a yellowish or pale coloration. These blossoms eventually give rise to small, fleshy drupes that transition in color as they mature. The overall structure of the plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and dense canopy.
