10 Health Benefits Of Taxus Baccata (Yew)

Taxus baccata, also known as yew, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-cancer properties.

The alkaloids present in the plant, including taxine and taxol, contribute to these effects. For instance, the anti-inflammatory properties can alleviate symptoms of arthritis, while the antioxidant properties can protect against cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of certain cancers.

Improved quality of life can be achieved through the use of yew extracts in traditional medicine.
