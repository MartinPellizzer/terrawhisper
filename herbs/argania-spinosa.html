Argan Tree (Argania spinosa)

Argan Tree (Argania spinosa) is a member of the Santalaceae family, native to Mauritania, Morocco, and Algeria. Traditionally, its leaves, fruits, and resin have been used for oil infusions, poultices, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and european herbal medicine.
