Cimicifuga Racemosa

Cimicifuga racemosa, commonly known as black cohosh, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is used to treat various health conditions, including menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes and night sweats, due to its reported health benefits of reducing stress and promoting relaxation.

The therapeutic actions of black cohosh include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its use in treating menstrual cramps, anxiety, and insomnia.
