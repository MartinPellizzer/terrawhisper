As discussed in a study, four compounds were isolated from the bark of Illicium difengpi K.I.B. et K.I.M. These medicinal compounds were identified as 3 beta-O-acetyl-mangiferolic acid (I), mangiferonic acid (II), mangiferolic acid (III) and butulinic acid (IV). Compound I is characterized as a new compound. These triterpene acids (I-IV) were isolated from the Illicium genus for the first time. Identification of these 4 compounds was achieved through various spectral analyses including UV, IR, EIMS, 1HNMR, 13CNMR, NOEDE and 2D NMR.
