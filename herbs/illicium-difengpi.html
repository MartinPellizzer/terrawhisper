What are the pharmacological activities of Illicium difengpi?

This plant has anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and insecticidal pharmacological activities derived from its bioactive secondary metabolites. The anti-inflammatory properties help mitigate swelling and immune responses within biological systems. Its antioxidant activity allows it to neutralize harmful free radicals and prevent oxidative stress in cells. Additionally, the plant exhibits significant insecticidal potential by acting as a natural deterrent or toxin against various pest species. These diverse chemical functions make it a valuable subject for both medicinal and agricultural research.

According to a study published by "Fitoterapia", the stem barks of Illicium difengpi have been widely used in Traditional Chinese Medicine for the therapy of rheumatoid arthritis and traumatic injury. Phytochemical investigation on the branches and leaves of the plant led to the isolation of one undescribed neolignan named difengpienol C and three known lignans. This new compound, difengpienol C, showed the strongest anti-inflammatory activity in lipopolysaccharide (LPS)-stimulated RAW264.7 cells. It powerfully inhibited the production of nitric oxide (NO), interleukin 6 (IL-6), and tumor necrosis factor α (TNF-α) while suppressing the mRNA transcription of inducible nitric oxide synthase (iNOS), IL-6, and TNF-α. Furthermore, difengpienol C blocked the activation of the TLR4/MyD88/NF-κB signaling pathway.
