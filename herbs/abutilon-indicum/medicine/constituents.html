10 Medicinal Constituents Of Abutilon Indicum (Indian Mallow)

Abutilon indicum, also known as Indian mallow, has active constituents such as flavonoids, glycosides, and terpenoids.

These compounds are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The plant's medicinal properties can help alleviate pain and reduce inflammation, improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.

By reducing oxidative stress and promoting wound healing, Abutilon indicum also holds potential in treating skin and soft tissue injuries.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Abutilon indicum .
