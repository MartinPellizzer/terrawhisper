      What are the herbal preparations of Peristylus tentaculatus?

      Peristylus tentaculatus is prepared as a liquid tincture, a decoction of the dried aerial parts, or a topical poultice for external applications. The tincture is typically made by soaking the plant material in high-proof ethanol to extract its active alkaloids.
