What's the taxonomical classification of Rhamnus dumetorum?

Rhamnus dumetorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Rosales and is a member of the family Rhamnaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Rhamnus and the species epithet dumetorum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The plant produces small, fleshy drupes that transition from green to a dark black color upon ripening.
