Potamogeton perfoliatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Potamogeton perfoliatus has long, slender, submerged leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape and arranged alternately along a thin, flexible stem.

Small, white, three-petaled flowers emerge from the water surface on specialized stalks during the blooming season.
