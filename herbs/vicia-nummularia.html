What's the taxonomical classification of Vicia nummularia?

Vicia nummularia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is classified under the order Fabales. It is a member of the family Fabaceae, specifically falling within the genus Vicia, and is identified by its unique species name, nummularia.
