Primula dryadifolia subsp. congestifolia

Primula dryadifolia subsp.

congestifolia is a plant species in the Primulaceae family, commonly known as the Dryad's Primrose.

This plant is used for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is valued for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of Primula dryadifolia subsp.

congestifolia include reducing fever, relieving coughs, and treating respiratory issues.

It can be prepared as an herbal tea, decoction, or tincture for medicinal use.
