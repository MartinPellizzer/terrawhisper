Carpinus turczaninovii

What's the taxonomical classification of Carpinus turczaninovii?

Carpinus turczaninovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Fagales. This plant is a member of the family Betulaceae, falling under the genus Carpinus, and is specifically identified by its species name, turczaninovii.
