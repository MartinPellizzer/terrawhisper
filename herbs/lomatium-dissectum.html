Lomatium Dissectum (Lomatium dissectum)

Lomatium Dissectum (Lomatium dissectum) is a member of the Araliaceae family, native to North America, Western United States, and Rocky Mountains. Traditionally, its roots, stems, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
