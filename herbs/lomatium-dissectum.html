What is Lomatium Dissectum (Lomatium Dissectum)?

Lomatium dissectum, also known as dissected lomatium, is a perennial herb native to North America, primarily found in the Pacific Northwest, characterized by its finely dissected leaves and yellow flowers, and used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory properties.

Lomatium dissectum is a biennial herb with a stout, branched stem that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring finely dissected, fern-like leaves with linear lobes and a terminal umbel of small, yellowish-green flowers.
