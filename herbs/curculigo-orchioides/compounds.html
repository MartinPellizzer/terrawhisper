Curculigo Orchid (Curculigo orchioides) Compounds

Curculigo Orchid contains several primary compounds, including alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Terpenoids and saponins also play roles in its therapeutic applications.
