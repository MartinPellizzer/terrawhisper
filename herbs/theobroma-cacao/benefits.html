1. Boosts antioxidant levels

Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and antioxidants in the body, leading to cellular damage.

This health issue, often referred to as oxidative damage or free radical-induced harm, can result from environmental toxins, poor diet, and lifestyle factors.

It is commonly experienced by individuals with chronic diseases, aging populations, and those under significant stress.

Theobroma cacao plant helps boost antioxidant levels, which can neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress.

Incorporating cacao into the diet may support the body's natural defenses against this harmful process.

How this herb helps with boosts antioxidant levels?

Theobroma cacao boosts antioxidant levels because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

Together, these compounds support overall cellular health and may lower the risk of chronic diseases.
