Gurmar (Gymnema sylvestre)

Gurmar (Gymnema sylvestre) is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Southern Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
