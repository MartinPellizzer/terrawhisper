What to know about Gymnema sylvestre (guduchi) before using it medicinally

As an ornamental plant, guduchi's vining habit and clusters of yellow flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners, while its roots can be used in herbal remedies.

According to ancient texts such as the Charaka Samhita, guduchi has been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and even snakebites.
