What to know about Gymnema sylvestre before using it medicinally

Gymnema sylvestre, commonly known as gurmar, is a plant-based herb that has been widely used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine to help manage various health conditions, including diabetes, obesity, and digestive issues.

The herb is valued for its numerous health benefits, which include its potential to lower blood sugar levels, improve lipid profiles, and enhance weight management. Gymnema sylvestre is rich in therapeutic properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-diabetic activities, which contribute to its medicinal uses. The herb contains a range of bioactive compounds, including gymnemic acids, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its bioactivity.

In traditional medicine, Gymnema sylvestre is often prepared as a decoction, made by boiling the leaves and stems of the plant in water, which is then consumed to reap its health benefits.
