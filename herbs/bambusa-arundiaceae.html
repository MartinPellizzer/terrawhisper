Bambusa Arundiaceae: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Bambusa arundiaceae, commonly known as the bamboo grass, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its ability to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote detoxification in the body.

Therapeutically, it acts as a mild diuretic, antispasmodic, and mild laxative, aiding in the treatment of gastrointestinal disorders.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, phenolic compounds, and essential oils, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.
