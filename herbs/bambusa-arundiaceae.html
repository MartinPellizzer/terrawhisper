What are the pest and diseases tips of Bambusa arundiaceae?

can also infect the plant, causing root rot and damping off, which can lead to reduced growth rates, stunted plants, and even death. In addition, the bamboo is prone to bacterial leaf blight caused by Xanthomonas oryzae, which can cause yellowing of leaves, defoliation, and reduced yields.

Finally, nematodes such as Meloidogyne incognita can also infect the plant, causing root knots and stunted growth.
