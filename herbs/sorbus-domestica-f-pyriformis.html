Sorbus domestica f. pyriformis

Sorbus domestica f. pyriformis, commonly known as the Dwarf Whitebeam, is a deciduous shrub or small tree belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is traditionally used to treat various health issues, including inflammatory and respiratory conditions.

The plant's health benefits are attributed to its ability to soothe and calm the body, and it is also used to treat digestive issues due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The therapeutic actions of Sorbus domestica f. pyriformis are primarily due to the presence of bioactive constituents such as triterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities.
