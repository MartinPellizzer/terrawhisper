Glochidion wrrightii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Glochidion and the specific species designation wrightii.
