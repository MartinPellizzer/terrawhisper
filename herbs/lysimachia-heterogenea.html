What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia heterogenea?

Lysimachia heterogenea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Lysimachia, with its specific designation being the species heterogenea.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
