Lysimachia heterogenea

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysimachia heterogenea has erect, branched stems that reach heights of up to 60 centimeters and feature opposite, lanceolate leaves with entire margins. The foliage is typically bright green and possesses smooth, somewhat fleshy textures. Small, star-shaped flowers emerge from the leaf axils, displaying five bright yellow petals. These blooms are characterized by their solitary arrangement and delicate central stamens. The plant often develops a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread through its habitat.
