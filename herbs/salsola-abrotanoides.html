Salsola abrotanoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Salsola abrotanoides?

Salsola abrotanoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Amaranthaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Salsola, with its unique biological classification completed by the species designation abrotanoides.

Salsola abrotanoides has antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties that contribute to its various therapeutic applications. Researchers continue to investigate its potential bioactive compounds for their antimicrobial and antioxidant effects.
