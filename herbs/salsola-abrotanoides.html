What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Salsola abrotanoides has succulent, cylindrical to subcylindrical stems that are often branched and possess a light green to glaucous color. These minute leaves are typically fleshy and serve to minimize water loss in arid environments. The plant develops small, inconspicuous flowers that are often tucked between the leaf bases.
