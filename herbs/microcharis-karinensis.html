What is Microcharis karinensis?

Microcharis karinensis is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a climber that has twining stems and leaflets with three to five pairs of pinnae, each pair having 6-12 pairs of leaflets. The leaflets are elliptic to lanceolate in shape, 1-5 mm long, and have a cuneate base and an acute apex. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers in a raceme, with each flower having five petals and a standard petal that is 1.5-2.5 mm long.

The flowers are followed by a linear pod that is 3-5 cm long, containing 4-6 seeds.
