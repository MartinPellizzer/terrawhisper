What is Xanthoxalis crassicaulis?

Xanthoxalis crassicaulis is a perennial herbaceous plant, belonging to the family Oxalidaceae.

It has simple, alternate leaves with 3-5 lobes, 1-2 cm long, and 5-7 mm wide, and a petiole 1-2 cm long. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in corymbose cymes, with 5 petals, 8-10 mm long, and a calyx with 5 sepals, 3-4 mm long. The fruit is a capsule, 5-6 mm long, with 3-4 seeds.

Xanthoxalis crassicaulis is native to the southeastern United States.
