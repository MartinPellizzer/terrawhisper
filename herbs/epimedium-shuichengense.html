What's the taxonomical classification of Epimedium shuichengense?

Epimedium shuichengense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This specific plant is further categorized under the family Berberidaceae, which places it within the genus Epimedium. Finally, its unique identity is defined by its specific epithet, marking it as the species shuichengense.
