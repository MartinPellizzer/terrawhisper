Gnetum gracilipes

What's the taxonomical classification of Gnetum gracilipes?

Gnetum gracilipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Gnetidae and the order Gnetales. This plant is a representative of the family Gnetaceae, falling under the genus Gnetum, with its specific biological identity defined by the species name gracilipes.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Gnetum gracilipes has slender, woody stems that branch extensively to form an open, shrubby architecture characterized by small, lanceolate leaves and distinct, scale-like structures.

Its stems are often reddish or brownish in color and serve as the primary structural support for the plant's sparse foliage. The reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous cones that develop along the branches.
