Gnetum gracilipes

What's the taxonomical classification of Gnetum gracilipes?

Gnetum gracilipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Gnetidae and the order Gnetales. This plant is a representative of the family Gnetaceae, falling under the genus Gnetum, with its specific biological identity defined by the species name gracilipes.
