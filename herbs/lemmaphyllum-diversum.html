Lemmaphyllum diversum

What's the taxonomical classification of Lemmaphyllum diversum?

Lemmaphyllum diversum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the family Polypodiaceae, which contains the genus Lemmaphyllum, ultimately identifying this specific organism as the species diversum.
