Arisaema intermedium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Alismatales and the family Araceae, which places it within the genus Arisaema. Ultimately, it is identified by its specific species name, intermedium.
