10 Medicinal Constituents Of Myroxylon Balsamum (Peruvian Balsam)

Myroxylon balsamum, also known as Peruvian balsam, has active constituents such as resin, benzoic acid, cinnamic acid, and vanillin.

These constituents are responsible for its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties.

The resin and benzoic acid can help alleviate skin irritations, wounds, and infections, while vanillin has been shown to have antiseptic and antiviral effects, improving wound healing and reducing the risk of infection, ultimately improving the quality of life for those suffering from chronic conditions.
