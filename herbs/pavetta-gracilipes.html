What's the taxonomical classification of Pavetta gracilipes?

Pavetta gracilipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Gentianales. This plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae, which is further organized into the genus Pavetta, specifically designated as the species gracilipes.
