Albizia kalkora

What's the taxonomical classification of Albizia kalkora?

Albizia kalkora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression through the order Fabales, it falls under the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of legumes. Finally, it is identified by the genus Albizia and is specifically designated as the species kalkora.

Albizia kalkora is prepared as a decoction, a fine powder, or a paste for topical application.
