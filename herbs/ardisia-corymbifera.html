What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ardisia corymbifera has simple, elliptical to oblong leaves that are dark green, glossy, and arranged oppositely along its woody stems. Its inflorescence consists of small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense, flat-topped corymbose structures. These flowers eventually give rise to small, fleshy drupes that serve as the plant's characteristic fruit.
