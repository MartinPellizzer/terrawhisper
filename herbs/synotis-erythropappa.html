What's the taxonomical classification of Synotis erythropappa?

Synotis erythropappa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asterales and the family Asteraceae, which characterizes its botanical relationship to other sunflowers and daisies. Finally, it is defined by its specific genus, Synotis, and its unique species designation, erythropappa.
