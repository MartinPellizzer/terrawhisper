10 Health Benefits Of Phyllanthus Niruri (Galangal)

Phyllanthus niruri has health benefits such as treating liver and kidney diseases, reducing fever and inflammation, and improving respiratory issues.

Its medicinal properties include its antioxidant and antimicrobial compounds, which help to neutralize free radicals and combat infections.

For example, the plant's ability to treat liver disease can significantly improve the quality of life for those suffering from this condition, allowing them to resume normal activities and reduce the risk of complications.

1. Reduces kidney stone size

Phyllanthus niruri reduces kidney stone size because it has been shown to inhibit the growth of certain bacteria that can contribute to stone formation.

The herb's flavonoids and alkaloids help to dissolve existing stones by breaking down calcium oxalate crystals, allowing for easier passage through the urinary tract.

Additionally, its diuretic properties increase urine flow, which can also aid in removing smaller stones from the body.

2. Inhibits kidney stone formation

Phyllanthus niruri inhibits kidney stone formation because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and triterpenoids that have been shown to inhibit the growth and adhesion of crystals on the renal tubular cells, thereby preventing the formation of calcium oxalate stones.

Additionally, galangal's antioxidant properties help reduce oxidative stress, which is a known risk factor for kidney stone development.

3. Boosts liver function

Phyllanthus niruri boosts liver function because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Galangal has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various liver-related ailments, including jaundice, cirrhosis, and hepatitis.

It has been shown to stimulate the production of liver enzymes, improve bile flow, and protect against oxidative stress and damage caused by free radicals, ultimately promoting overall liver health and function.

4. Possesses anti-cancer properties

Phyllanthus niruri possesses anti-cancer properties because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenes that have been shown to inhibit the growth of cancer cells by inducing apoptosis, inhibiting angiogenesis, and reducing inflammation. Additionally, galangal's anti-oxidant activity helps protect against DNA damage and oxidative stress, further contributing to its anti-cancer potential.

These properties make galangal a promising natural agent for cancer prevention and treatment.

5. Reduces oxidative stress

Phyllanthus niruri reduces oxidative stress because of its rich antioxidant properties.

The herb contains a variety of flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids that scavenge free radicals, thereby preventing their harmful effects on cells. Additionally, galangal's phenolic compounds exhibit potent anti-inflammatory activity, further reducing oxidative stress by suppressing pro-inflammatory pathways.

This dual action helps to mitigate the damage caused by reactive oxygen species (ROS), thereby maintaining cellular homeostasis and promoting overall health.

6. Combats viral infections

Phyllanthus niruri combats viral infections because of its unique bioactive compounds.

The plant contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids that exhibit potent antiviral properties, inhibiting the replication of viruses such as HIV, herpes, and influenza.

Additionally, galangal's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects help to modulate the immune response, reducing oxidative stress and promoting a healthy environment that prevents viral multiplication and enhances the body's natural defenses against infection.

7. Lowers high blood pressure

Phyllanthus niruri lowers high blood pressure because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The herb contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins that exhibit potent vasodilatory properties, which help to relax blood vessels and improve blood flow. This reduces peripheral resistance, leading to a decrease in blood pressure levels.

Additionally, galangal's antioxidant activity helps to mitigate oxidative stress and inflammation, further contributing to its hypotensive effects, making it a valuable natural remedy for managing hypertension.

8. Regulates blood sugar levels

Phyllanthus niruri regulates blood sugar levels because of its unique phytochemical composition.

The plant's extract has been shown to possess antidiabetic properties, which help to reduce glucose absorption in the small intestine and increase insulin sensitivity.

Additionally, galangal's flavonoids and alkaloids have been found to inhibit the activity of enzymes involved in carbohydrate metabolism, leading to a decrease in blood sugar levels.

9. Improves digestion

Phyllanthus niruri improves digestion because it has natural anti-inflammatory and carminative properties.

The plant's essential oils soothe the digestive tract, reducing inflammation and discomfort associated with conditions like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and acid reflux. Additionally, its bitter compounds stimulate digestive enzymes, promoting proper nutrient absorption and relieving symptoms of indigestion and bloating.

This natural remedy can be consumed as a tea or supplement to support healthy digestion and alleviate digestive discomfort.

10. Reduces inflammation

Phyllanthus niruri reduces inflammation because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The herb contains flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have been shown to possess potent anti-inflammatory properties. These compounds work by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, thereby reducing the inflammatory response in the body.

This natural mechanism helps to alleviate symptoms associated with inflammation, such as pain, swelling, and redness.
