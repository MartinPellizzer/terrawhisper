Stonebreaker (Phyllanthus niruri) Uses

Stonebreaker, or Phyllanthus niruri, is primarily used to treat urinary tract infections, kidney stones, and bladder infections. It helps reduce inflammation in the urinary tract and may alleviate pain associated with these conditions. It is also used for chronic kidney disease and renal inflammation, supporting kidney health and function.
