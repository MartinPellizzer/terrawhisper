Stonebreaker (Phyllanthus niruri) Compounds

The primary compounds in Stonebreaker include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to anti-inflammatory actions, while saponins may support digestive health. Terpenoids and glycosides are also present, offering potential antimicrobial and immune-supporting benefits.
