Possible Side Effects of Isachne kunthiana

Isachne kunthiana can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues.

Ingesting large amounts of the plant can lead to nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, while prolonged exposure to its sap can cause skin burns and blisters.

The plant's essential oils may also cause respiratory problems, such as coughing and shortness of breath, in individuals with pre-existing lung conditions.
