Scurrula ferruginea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Santalales and the family Loranthaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Scurrula. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species name, ferrugineae.
