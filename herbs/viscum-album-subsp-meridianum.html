Viscum album subsp. meridianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Viscum album subsp. meridianum?

Viscum album subsp. meridianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Santalales. As a member of the family Santalaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Viscum, eventually reaching its specific designation as the subspecies Viscum album subsp. meridianum.
