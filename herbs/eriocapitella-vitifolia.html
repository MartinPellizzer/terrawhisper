Eriocapitella vitifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and into the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Eriocapitella, and its species designation, vitifolia.
