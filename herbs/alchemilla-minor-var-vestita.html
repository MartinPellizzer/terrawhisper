Alchemilla minor var. vestita

Alchemilla minor var. vestita, also known as dwarf lady's mantle, is a plant species belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its delicate white flowers and hair-like appendages on its stems.

Traditionally, it has been used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, making it a popular remedy for skin issues and wound healing.
