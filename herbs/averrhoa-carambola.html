Starfruit (Averrhoa carambola)

Starfruit (Averrhoa carambola) is a member of the Sapotaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, Pacific Islands, and Indo-Malesia. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for culinary uses, infusions, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
