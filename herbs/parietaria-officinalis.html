Possible Side Effects of parietaria officinalis

Parietaria officinalis can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing allergic reactions in some individuals, which may manifest as skin rashes, itching, and swelling.

Overuse of the herb can also lead to gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, diarrhea, and abdominal pain.

Furthermore, Parietaria officinalis may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and lower the body's ability to clot, potentially increasing the risk of bleeding.

Additionally, using the herb without proper guidance can cause hormonal imbalances, particularly in women, leading to menstrual irregularities and other reproductive issues.
