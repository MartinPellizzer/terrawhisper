What's the taxonomical classification of Synotis duclouxii?

Synotis duclouxii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, which is a diverse group of flowering plants, and is specifically identified by the genus Synotis and the species duclouxii.
