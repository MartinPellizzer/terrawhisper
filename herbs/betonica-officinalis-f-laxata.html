Betonica officinalis f. laxata

Betonica officinalis f. laxata, also known as a laxative form of Mountain Betony, is a flowering plant in the mint family Lamiaceae, native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The medicinal properties of Betonica officinalis f. laxata lie in its ability to relax the muscles, particularly in the digestive tract, and reduce spasms, making it an effective treatment for digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and constipation.

The health benefits of this plant include relief from stomach pain, diarrhea, and nausea, as well as its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

Betonica officinalis f. laxata can be prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or infusion, and is often used in traditional medicine to promote digestive health and alleviate symptoms of various gastrointestinal disorders.
