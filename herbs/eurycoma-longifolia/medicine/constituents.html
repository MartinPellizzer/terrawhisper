10 Medicinal Constituents Of Eurycoma Longifolia (Tongkat Ali)

Eurycoma longifolia has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These constituents confer medicinal properties such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and testosterone-boosting effects. The antioxidant properties can help protect against cell damage and oxidative stress, while the anti-inflammatory properties can alleviate symptoms of pain and inflammation.

The testosterone-boosting effect can improve muscle mass, strength, and fertility, thereby enhancing physical performance and quality of life.
