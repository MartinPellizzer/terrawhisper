10 Health Benefits Of Eurycoma Longifolia (Tongkat Ali)

Eurycoma longifolia, also known as tongkat ali, has health benefits such as improved testosterone levels, enhanced endurance, and reduced stress levels.

Its medicinal properties include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds, such as eurycomanone and 9-hydroxyeurycomanone. This can help improve men's lives by increasing libido and energy, and women's lives by reducing anxiety and promoting better sleep.

Additionally, its adaptogenic properties may aid in recovery from exercise and daily stress.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Eurycoma longifolia.

1. Increases testosterone levels

Eurycoma longifolia increases testosterone levels because of its ability to stimulate the production of luteinizing hormone (LH) and follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH).

These hormones play a crucial role in regulating testicular function, including sperm production and testosterone synthesis. By increasing LH and FSH levels, tongkat ali helps to boost testosterone production, leading to improved male fertility, libido, and overall sexual performance.

This makes it a popular natural remedy for men seeking to enhance their masculinity and overall well-being.

2. Boosts athletic performance

Eurycoma longifolia boosts athletic performance because it increases testosterone levels, enhancing muscle mass and strength.

This herb has been traditionally used in Southeast Asian cultures to improve physical endurance and vitality. Studies have shown that tongkat ali supplementation can increase muscular power, speed, and agility, making athletes more competitive and resilient.

Its adaptogenic properties also help reduce stress and fatigue, allowing athletes to push themselves harder without exhaustion.

3. Enhances libido and sexual health

Eurycoma longifolia enhances libido and sexual health because of its ability to increase testosterone levels in men.

Testosterone is a key hormone responsible for regulating sex drive, sperm production, and overall male fertility. Tongkat ali has been shown to boost testosterone production by stimulating the hypothalamic-pituitary-testicular axis, leading to increased libido, improved erectile function, and enhanced overall sexual performance.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties help protect against oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, further supporting overall sexual health and well-being.

4. Reduces stress and anxiety

Eurycoma longifolia reduces stress and anxiety because of its adaptogenic properties.

It helps the body to adapt to changing circumstances by regulating cortisol levels and promoting a sense of balance and calmness. Additionally, tongkat ali has been shown to reduce symptoms of depression and insomnia, common co-morbidities with stress and anxiety.

Its natural ability to promote relaxation and reduce fatigue makes it an effective herbal remedy for managing stress and anxiety-related disorders.

5. Improves muscle mass and strength

Eurycoma longifolia improves muscle mass and strength because it increases testosterone levels in the body.

Tongkat ali has been shown to boost testosterone by up to 122% in just one week, leading to increased muscle growth and strength.

Additionally, its ability to enhance nitric oxide production and improve blood flow to the muscles further supports muscle growth and repair, ultimately resulting in improved overall athletic performance.

6. Supports bone health

Eurycoma longifolia supports bone health because of its rich content of antioxidants and flavonoids.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to stimulate the production of osteoblasts, which are cells responsible for building and maintaining bone tissue, thereby promoting bone density and strength.

Additionally, tongkat ali's anti-inflammatory properties may help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in bones, further supporting overall bone health and potentially reducing the risk of bone-related disorders such as osteoporosis.

7. Combats fatigue and enhances energy

Eurycoma longifolia combats fatigue and enhances energy because of its unique bioactive compounds, particularly flavonoids and saponins.

These compounds work synergistically to increase testosterone levels, boost mental clarity, and improve physical endurance.

Tongkat ali has been traditionally used in Southeast Asian medicine to alleviate exhaustion and enhance vitality, making it a popular natural remedy for individuals seeking to overcome fatigue and feel more energized throughout the day.

8. Possesses anti-cancer properties

Eurycoma longifolia possesses anti-cancer properties because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, including quassinoids and alkaloids.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit cytotoxic effects on various cancer cell lines, inhibiting cell proliferation and inducing apoptosis (programmed cell death).

Additionally, tongkat ali has been found to suppress angiogenesis, thereby reducing tumor blood flow and limiting its growth.

9. Helps control blood sugar levels

Eurycoma longifolia helps control blood sugar levels because it possesses bioactive compounds that have been shown to improve insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake in the body.

Tongkat ali has been found to increase the production of insulin-like growth factor-1 (IGF-1), which enhances insulin secretion and reduces glucose levels.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties help protect pancreatic beta cells from damage, thereby maintaining optimal insulin production and glucose regulation.

10. Reduces inflammation

Eurycoma longifolia reduces inflammation because it contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and terpenoids that exhibit potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thereby reducing inflammation and oxidative stress.

Additionally, tongkat ali's ability to modulate the immune system helps to mitigate inflammatory responses, making it a potential natural remedy for various inflammatory conditions.
