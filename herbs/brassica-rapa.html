Brassica Rapa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Brassica rapa, commonly known as Chinese cabbage, is a cruciferous vegetable belonging to the Brassicaceae family, widely cultivated for its edible leaves, stems, and flowers.

This herb is rich in nutrients such as vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like calcium and potassium, offering numerous health benefits including antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and immune-boosting properties.

The therapeutic actions of Brassica rapa are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as glucosinolates, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which have been linked to cancer prevention and digestive health.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and seeds to harness its medicinal properties.
