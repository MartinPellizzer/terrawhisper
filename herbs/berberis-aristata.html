Indian Barberry (Berberis aristata)

Indian Barberry (Berberis aristata) is a member of the Berberidaceae family, native to Himalayas, Central Asia, and Tibet. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, tibetan medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
