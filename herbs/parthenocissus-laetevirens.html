    What side-effects this plant can have?

    Parthenocissus laetevirens can cause severe skin irritation, redness, itching, and blistering upon direct contact with the plant's sap. This vine contains irritants that trigger a localized allergic reaction known as contact dermatitis in sensitive individuals. Symptoms typically manifest as painful, raised bumps or small vesicles on the skin where the plant touched. Once the reaction begins, the affected area may remain inflamed for several days. It is important to wash the skin immediately with soap and water to minimize the severity of the reaction.
