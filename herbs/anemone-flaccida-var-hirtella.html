Anemone flaccida var. hirtella

What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone flaccida var. hirtella?

Anemone flaccida var. hirtella belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. It is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is categorized under the genus Anemone. Ultimately, the specific classification of this plant is defined by its botanical name, Anemone flaccida var. hirtella.
