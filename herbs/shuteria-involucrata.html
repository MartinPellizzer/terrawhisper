Shuteria involucrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a part of the family Fabaceae, a group known for its diverse botanical characteristics, and specifically falls under the genus Shuteria. Its complete scientific designation is identified at the species level as involucrata.
