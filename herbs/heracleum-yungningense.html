Heracleum yungningense

What's the taxonomical classification of Heracleum yungningense?

Heracleum yungningense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. Its taxonomic placement continues within the family Apiaceae, specifically situated in the genus Heracleum, where it is identified by the unique species name yungningense.

What is the geographical distribution of this plant?

This plant is endemic to the Yungning region of China, specifically localized within the mountainous areas of the Yunnan province. Its range is highly restricted to specific high-altitude habitats within this particular geographical zone. The species thrives in the unique ecological niches found along the slopes of these central Chinese mountains. Because it is a narrow endemic, it does not occur naturally in any other part of the world.
