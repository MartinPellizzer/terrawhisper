What's the taxonomical classification of Heracleum yungningense?

Heracleum yungningense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Apiales. Its taxonomic placement continues within the family Apiaceae, specifically situated in the genus Heracleum, where it is identified by the unique species name yungningense.
