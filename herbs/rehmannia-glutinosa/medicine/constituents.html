10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rehmannia Glutinosa (Chinese Foxglove)

Rehmannia glutinosa, also known as Chinese Foxglove, has active constituents such as anthraquinones, starches, and alkaloids.

These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-boosting effects. The constituents can help alleviate symptoms of chronic diseases like diabetes, kidney stones, and gout, while also improving cognitive function and reducing anxiety.

For instance, the anthraquinones may improve blood sugar control, while the starches may soothe digestive issues, ultimately enhancing overall well-being and quality of life.
