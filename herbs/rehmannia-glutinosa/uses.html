Chinese Foxglove (Rehmannia glutinosa) Uses

Rehmannia glutinosa is primarily used to treat kidney-related conditions such as dysfunction, failure, inflammation, and deficiency. It also supports diabetes management and alleviates symptoms like fatigue, insomnia, and chronic fatigue syndrome. Additionally, it is used for arthritis due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

Chinese Foxglove (Rehmannia glutinosa) Monograph

1. Kidney dysfunction

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney function. It is believed to tonify the kidneys, improve urinary health, and reduce symptoms of kidney deficiency. The herb is often prescribed for conditions like proteinuria and chronic kidney disease. Its active compounds help in maintaining renal function and balancing fluid metabolism.

2. Kidney failure

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney function and manage kidney failure. It is believed to tonify the kidneys, reduce inflammation, and improve urinary function. Its active compounds may help in detoxification and maintaining fluid balance in the body.

3. Kidney inflammation

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support kidney health and reduce inflammation. It is particularly valued for its ability to alleviate symptoms of kidney inflammation by promoting diuresis and balancing fluid metabolism. Its roots are often processed into decoctions or pills for internal use. This herb is considered essential in treating chronic kidney conditions.

4. Kidney deficiency

Rehmannia glutinosa is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine to tonify the kidneys and support kidney function. It is particularly valued for treating symptoms of kidney deficiency such as fatigue, lower back pain, and impotence. Its ability to nourish Yin and reduce internal heat makes it a key herb in kidney-related formulations.

5. Diabetes

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support blood sugar regulation. It is believed to enhance pancreatic function and improve insulin sensitivity. It is often combined with other herbs in formulas designed to manage diabetes symptoms. Its roots are traditionally prepared as decoctions or powders for therapeutic use.

6. Diabetes mellitus

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support blood sugar regulation in diabetes mellitus. It is believed to enhance insulin sensitivity and improve pancreatic function. The herb's polysaccharides and alkaloids contribute to its hypoglycemic effects. It is often combined with other herbs in formulations tailored for diabetic management.

7. Fatigue

Rehmannia glutinosa is used to combat fatigue by nourishing the kidneys and strengthening vitality. It is particularly effective in cases of chronic fatigue linked to kidney deficiency. Its tonic properties help restore energy and improve overall physical endurance.

8. Insomnia

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to address insomnia by nourishing the kidneys and calming the mind. It helps regulate the body's energy flow and reduce anxiety, promoting restful sleep. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its soothing effects on the nervous system.

9. Chronic fatigue syndrome

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to address chronic fatigue syndrome due to its ability to nourish the kidneys and improve energy levels. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance vitality and support the body's natural recovery processes. This herb is particularly valued for its role in restoring balance and stamina in individuals experiencing long-term fatigue.

10. Arthritis

Rehmannia glutinosa is used in traditional Chinese medicine to support joint health and alleviate arthritis symptoms. It is believed to reduce inflammation and improve circulation, helping to ease pain and stiffness. Its root, rich in bioactive compounds, is often prepared as a decoction or supplement for long-term management of inflammatory conditions.
