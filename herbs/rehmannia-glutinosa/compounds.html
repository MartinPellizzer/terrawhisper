Chinese Foxglove (Rehmannia glutinosa) Compounds

The primary compounds of Chinese Foxglove include glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal effects. Flavonoids and anthocyanins act as antioxidants. Alkaloids provide various therapeutic properties, while tannins and saponins support anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits. These compounds work synergistically to enhance the herb's overall efficacy.

Chinese Foxglove (Rehmannia glutinosa) Monograph

1. Glycoside

Glycoside is a key active compound in Chinese Foxglove (Rehmannia glutinosa). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in kidney health and anti-inflammatory effects. Glycosides in Rehmannia glutinosa are known for their role in regulating fluid balance and supporting renal function.

Flavonoids are bioactive compounds found in Rehmannia glutinosa, a key herb in traditional Chinese medicine.
