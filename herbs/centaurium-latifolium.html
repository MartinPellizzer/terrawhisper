What is Centaurium latifolium?

Centaurium latifolium is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It has a stout stem that grows up to 30 cm in height, with green, lanceolate leaves that are 3-8 cm long and 1-2 cm wide. The leaves are opposite, sessile, and have a cuneate base. The flowers are blue-purple and arranged in a terminal inflorescence, consisting of 5-6 calyx lobes and 5 petals that are 8-12 mm long.

The flowers bloom from July to September and produce 1-2 seeds per capsule, which is 10-12 mm long and 5-6 mm wide.
