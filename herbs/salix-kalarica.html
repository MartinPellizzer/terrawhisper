Salix kalarica

Salix kalarica, also known as Kalarica willow, is a species of willow tree native to the Himalayan and Tibetan regions.

The plant is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and astringent properties, which help to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of Salix kalarica include reducing fever, relieving pain, and treating digestive issues, as well as supporting the immune system and reducing inflammation.

Salix kalarica is typically prepared as an herbal tea, infusion, or decoction, and is also used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine preparations to promote overall well-being.
