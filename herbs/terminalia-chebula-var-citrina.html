Terminalia chebula var. citrina

Terminalia chebula var. citrina, also known as the yellow myrobalan, is a variation of the Terminalia chebula tree, native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It possesses medicinal properties, including antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its numerous health benefits.

The health benefits of Terminalia chebula var. citrina include its ability to aid in digestion, lower cholesterol levels, and exhibit anti-cancer properties, as well as its potential to reduce stress and anxiety.

In traditional Ayurvedic medicine, the fruit of the Terminalia chebula var. citrina tree is often used in herbal preparations, such as decoctions, infusions, and powders, to promote overall well-being and longevity.
