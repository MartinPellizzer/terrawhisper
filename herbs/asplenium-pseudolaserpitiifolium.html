What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium pseudolaserpitiifolium?

Asplenium pseudolaserpitiifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This specific fern is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, situated within the genus Asplenium, and is identified at the species level as pseudolaserpitiifolium.
