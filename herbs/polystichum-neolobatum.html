What's the taxonomical classification of Polystichum neolobatum?

Polystichum neolobatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Polystichum, and its unique species designation, neolobatum.
