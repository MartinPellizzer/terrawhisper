Potentilla glandulosa

Potentilla glandulosa, also known as Five-Fingered Rose or Sticky Cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and astringent qualities.

The plant's health benefits include reducing inflammation and soothing skin irritations, as well as exhibiting antimicrobial properties that can aid in wound healing.

Potentilla glandulosa can be prepared as a herbal infusion, tea, or tincture, which can be used to treat various health conditions, including skin issues and digestive problems.
