Possible Side Effects of leonurus sibiricus

Leonurus sibiricus can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Furthermore, Leonurus sibiricus may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions like high blood pressure and thyroid disorders.

In rare cases, improper use of the herb can cause more severe side effects, including seizures and respiratory problems.
