Leonurus Sibiricus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Leonurus sibiricus, commonly known as Siberian Motherwort, is a perennial herb native to Asia and parts of Europe, valued for its medicinal properties.

It is known for its calming effects and is used to support cardiovascular health, reduce anxiety, and promote relaxation.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or decoction to harness its beneficial properties.
