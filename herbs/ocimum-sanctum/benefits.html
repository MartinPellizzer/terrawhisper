10 Best Ocimum Sanctum Health Benefits

Ocimum sanctum, commonly known as holy basil, is renowned for its numerous health benefits due to its rich content of antioxidants, essential oils, and phytochemicals.

The herb also possesses anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can support immune function and aid in the treatment of various ailments. Studies suggest that Ocimum sanctum may help regulate blood sugar levels and improve cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol and triglycerides.

Additionally, it has shown potential in combating oxidative stress, which is linked to chronic diseases such as cancer and neurodegenerative disorders.
