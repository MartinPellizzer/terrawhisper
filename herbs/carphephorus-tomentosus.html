What is Carphephorus tomentosus?

Carphephorus tomentosus is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It has a taproot and a woody base, growing up to 1-2 meters in height. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, 5-20 cm long, and 1-3 mm wide, with a tomentose (velvety) surface. The inflorescence is a capitulum, consisting of 20-100 florets, with yellow or purple flowers, and a central disk of disk flowers, surrounded by ray flowers.

The achenes are 3-4 mm long, with a pappus of 10-15 mm, and are dispersed by wind.
