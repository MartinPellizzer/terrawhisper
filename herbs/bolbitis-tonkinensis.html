What is Bolbitis tonkinensis?

Bolbitis tonkinensis is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial fern with a rhizome and stoloniferous stems that are dichotomously branched, reaching up to 50 cm in length. The fronds are bipinnate, consisting of a rachis with pinnules that are pinnatifid and have a toothed margin. The frond is 20-80 cm in length, with the largest pinnules reaching 3-10 cm in length.

The plant is epiphytic and lithophytic, and thrives in humid environments with filtered or dappled light.
