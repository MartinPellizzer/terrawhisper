What is Eurya cerasifolia?

Eurya cerasifolia is a species of flowering plant in the family Pentaphylacaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree with smooth, gray bark and a rounded crown, reaching 5-15 meters in height. The leaves are opposite, elliptic, and glossy, measuring 6-12 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, with a serrated margin and a petiole 1-2 mm long. The flowers are small, white, and borne in corymbose cymes, with 5 petals and 5 stamens.

The fruit is a red or orange berry, 5-6 mm in diameter, containing 5-6 seeds.
