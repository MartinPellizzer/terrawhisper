What's the taxonomical classification of Ligularia jamesii?

Ligularia jamesii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, which is part of the genus Ligularia, and is specifically identified by its species name, jamesii.
