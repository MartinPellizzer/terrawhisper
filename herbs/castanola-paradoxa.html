What's the taxonomical classification of Castanola paradoxa?

Castanola paradoxa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Oxalidales. Its taxonomic classification continues within the family Connaraceae, specifically placing it in the genus Castanola, with its unique identity defined by the species name paradoxa.
