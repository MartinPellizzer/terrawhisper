Centrosema virginianum

Centrosema virginianum, also known as the White Sweet Clover or Virginian Sweet Clover, is a perennial legume native to North America.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, primarily due to its high concentration of isoflavones and coumarins, which have been used to treat various health issues.

The health benefits of Centrosema virginianum include its potential to lower cholesterol levels, improve cardiovascular health, and exhibit antioxidant properties.

The plant can be used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, as well as in topical applications like creams and salves, making it a versatile natural remedy.
