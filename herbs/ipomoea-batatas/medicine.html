What are the possible side effect of using Ipomoea batatas improperly?

Improper use of Ipomoea batatas increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal issues like bloating, gas, and stomach cramps.

Consuming excessive amounts can lead to increased blood sugar levels, weight gain, and an imbalance in gut bacteria, potentially causing diarrhea, constipation, or even more severe symptoms like liver damage and kidney problems.

What precautions to take when using Ipomoea batatas medicinally?

Before using Ipomoea batatas for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage and potential interactions with other medications.

Additionally, ensure that the sweet potatoes are organic and free from contaminants to avoid adverse reactions or complications.

It's also crucial to properly identify the species, as some varieties may be toxic or cause allergic reactions.
