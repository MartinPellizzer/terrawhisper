Ipomoea batatas, also known as sweet potato, has medicinal preparations such as juice and extract.

What are the possible side effect of using Ipomoea batatas improperly?

Improper use of Ipomoea batatas, also known as sweet potato, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin problems.

Consuming large quantities can lead to gastrointestinal discomfort, bloating, and gas. Additionally, the high fiber content in sweet potatoes can cause stomach upset in individuals with sensitive stomachs.

Some people may also experience skin irritation or itching after consuming sweet potatoes.

Before using Ipomoea batatas, also known as sweet potato, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a qualified healthcare professional.
