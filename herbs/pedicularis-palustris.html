Pedicularis palustris belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pedicularis, and its species designation, palustris.
