Stellaria neglecta

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Stellaria neglecta has an annual herbaceous growth habit characterized by slender, procumbent to ascending stems that often form dense, low-growing mats. The leaves are small, entire, and typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. Its flowers are diminutive and white, featuring five petals that are often deeply notched at the apex. The plant produces tiny, ovoid capsules that contain numerous small seeds upon maturation. These morphological features allow the species to thrive in moist, disturbed habitats where it can quickly colonize open ground.
