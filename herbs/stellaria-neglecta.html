Stellaria neglecta

What's the taxonomical classification of Stellaria neglecta?

Stellaria neglecta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is further categorized into the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically within the genus Stellaria, ultimately reaching its scientific designation at the species level as Stellaria neglecta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Stellaria neglecta has an annual herbaceous growth habit characterized by slender, procumbent to ascending stems that often form dense, low-growing mats. The plant produces tiny, ovoid capsules that contain numerous small seeds upon maturation.
