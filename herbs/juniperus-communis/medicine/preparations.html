10 Medicinal Preparations Of Juniperus Communis (Juniper)

Juniperus communis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, essential oils, and extracts.

These preparations are useful for alleviating digestive issues, particularly for relieving menstrual cramps, and for treating urinary tract infections due to its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Additionally, juniper is used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, and to ease pain associated with rheumatism and arthritis.
