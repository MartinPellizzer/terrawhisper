10 Precautions To Take When Using Juniperus Communis (Juniper)

Juniperus communis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential interactions with medications like diuretics and blood thinners.

It's essential to be aware of these interactions to avoid complications like excessive bleeding or electrolyte imbalances. Failing to take this precaution may lead to side effects like dizziness, weakness, or nausea, which can worsen underlying health conditions.

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should also exercise caution due to the herb's potential effects on fetal and infant development.
