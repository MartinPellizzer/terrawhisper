Dryopteris Filix-Mas Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Dryopteris filix-mas, commonly known as the male fern, is a perennial fern native to temperate regions, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb has been traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory, antiparasitic, and detoxifying effects.

It contains bioactive constituents such as thujone, cineole, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations like infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from the dried fronds to harness its medicinal benefits.
