Ammophila villosa

Ammophila villosa, also known as beach grass or sand dune grass, is a species of perennial grass native to the coastal areas of Australia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which have been found to help alleviate symptoms of various health conditions such as wounds, skin irritations, and respiratory issues.

The health benefits of Ammophila villosa include reducing inflammation, promoting wound healing, and providing relief from digestive issues, making it a valuable herb in natural remedies.

Herbal preparations of Ammophila villosa can be made into teas, tinctures, and infusions, allowing for easy incorporation into a holistic health routine.
