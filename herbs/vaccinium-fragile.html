What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Vaccinium fragile has slender, drooping stems that are typically reddish to brownish in color and possess thin, deciduous bark. The leaves are small, elliptical to oblong, and feature finely serrated margins with an acute apex. These plants exhibit a low-growing, rhizomatous habit that allows them to form spreading mats across the ground. Flowers are small and bell-shaped, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink during the growing season.
