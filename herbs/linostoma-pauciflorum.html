Linostoma pauciflorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Linostoma pauciflorum?

Linostoma pauciflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized under the order Malvales. It is a constituent of the family Thymelaeaceae, specifically falling under the genus Linostoma, with its unique identification completed by the species name pauciflorum.
