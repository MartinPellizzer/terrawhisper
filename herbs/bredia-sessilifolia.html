Bredia sessilifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Bredia sessilifolia?

Bredia sessilifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Myrtales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant within the family Melastomataceae, specifically falling under the genus Bredia, with the unique specific epithet sessilifolia.
