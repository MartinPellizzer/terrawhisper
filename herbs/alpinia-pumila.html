What's the taxonomical classification of Alpinia pumila?

Alpinia pumila belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Zingiberales. This plant is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which places it within the genus Alpinia, and is specifically identified by its species name, pumila.
