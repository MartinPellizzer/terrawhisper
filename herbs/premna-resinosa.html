Premna resinosa

Premna resinosa, also known as the resinous Premna, is a plant species in the mint family (Lamiaceae) native to India and Southeast Asia.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral properties.

The health benefits of Premna resinosa include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and promote wound healing.

In herbal preparations, Premna resinosa is often used in the form of decoctions, infusions, or ointments, and is also used to make traditional remedies such as cough syrups and skin creams.
