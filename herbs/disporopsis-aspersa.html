Disporopsis aspersa

What's the taxonomical classification of Disporopsis aspersa?

Disporopsis aspersa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Asparagales. Further categorization identifies it as a member of the family Asparagaceae, specifically within the genus Disporopsis, with its unique designation being the species aspersa.
