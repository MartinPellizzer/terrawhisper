Caulophyllum Thalictroides: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Caulophyllum thalictroides, commonly known as blue cohosh, is a perennial herb native to North America, belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally by Indigenous peoples for various health purposes.

Blue cohosh offers health benefits such as supporting menstrual health, easing cramps, and promoting uterine tone, making it beneficial for women's wellness.

Its therapeutic actions include antispasmodic, emmenagogic, and uterine stimulant effects, while its bioactive constituents include alkaloids like berberine, protoberberine, and essential oils.
