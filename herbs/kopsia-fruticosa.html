What's the taxonomical classification of Kopsia fruticosa?

Kopsia fruticosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Kopsia, and its unique species designation, fruticosa.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Kopsia fruticosa has an erect, woody shrub habit characterized by slender, grayish-brown branches and lanceolate to narrowly elliptical leaves that are oppositely arranged on the stems. These blossoms are typically white or pale pink and possess a delicate, fragrant quality. The plant also produces small, fleshy fruits that develop from the fertilized ovaries.
