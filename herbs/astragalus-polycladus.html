What's the taxonomical classification of Astragalus polycladus?

Astragalus polycladus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Astragalus, and its specific designation is given by the species name polycladus.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
