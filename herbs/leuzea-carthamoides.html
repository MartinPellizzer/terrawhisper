Leuzea (Leuzea carthamoides)

Leuzea (Leuzea carthamoides) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Central Asia, Southwest Asia, and Eastern Europe. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
