Raphanus sativus subf. oleifer

Raphanus sativus subf.

oleifer, also known as oil radish or black oilseed radish, is a plant species of the Brassicaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, making it useful in treating various health conditions.

The health benefits of Raphanus sativus subf.

oleifer include its ability to improve cardiovascular health, reduce cancer risk, and support digestive health.

The plant is often used in herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and extracts, which are taken orally or applied topically to reap its medicinal benefits.
