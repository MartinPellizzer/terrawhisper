Pycnospora lutescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Pycnospora lutescens?

Pycnospora lutescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Pycnospora and the specific species epithet lutescens.
