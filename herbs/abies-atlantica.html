Abies atlantica

Abies atlantica, commonly known as the Grand Abies or Atlantic Silver Fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree species native to the mountainous regions of the Pacific coast in North America.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine due to its numerous health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, helping to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma.

The therapeutic actions of Abies atlantica include its ability to soothe and calm the respiratory system, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include sesquiterpenes, such as bornyl acetate, and alpha-pinene, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
