    What is Bidens exilis?

    Bidens exilis is a species of plant in the family Asteraceae.

    It is an annual or perennial herb that grows up to 60 cm in height, with a basal rosette of leaves and a stem with axillary leaves. The leaves are lanceolate to linear, 5-15 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, with an acute apex and entire margin. The plant produces white or yellowish flowers in a corymbiform inflorescence, with 4-5 petals and 20-30 disc florets.

    Bidens exilis is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.
