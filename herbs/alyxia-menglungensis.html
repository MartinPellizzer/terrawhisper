What's the taxonomical classification of Alyxia menglungensis?

Alyxia menglungensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical descent through the order Gentianales, the plant is placed within the family Apocynaceae. It is further categorized under the genus Alyxia, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Alyxia menglungensis.
