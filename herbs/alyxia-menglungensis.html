Alyxia menglungensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It is further categorized under the genus Alyxia, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Alyxia menglungensis.
