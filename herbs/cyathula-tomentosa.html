What's the taxonomical classification of Cyathula tomentosa?

Cyathula tomentosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its hierarchical progression, the plant is placed in the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Amaranthaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Cyathula, with the specific epithet tomentosa identifying the individual species.
