Filipendula × intermedia

Filipendula × intermedia, also known as meadowsweet, is a hybrid flowering plant that is a cross between Filipendula ulmaria and Filipendula vulgaris.

The medicinal properties of meadowsweet are attributed to its salicylic acid content, which is similar to aspirin.

The plant has various health benefits, including reducing inflammation and relieving pain, as well as soothing digestive issues and calming menstrual cramps.

Meadowsweet can be prepared in herbal teas, tinctures, and infusions, and is often used in combination with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
