Juncus setchuensis var. effusoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Juncus setchuensis var. effusoides?

Juncus setchuensis var. effusoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Poaales and is a specific representative of the family Juncaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Juncus, ultimately reaching its unique taxonomic designation as the variety effusoides of the species Juncus setchuensis.
