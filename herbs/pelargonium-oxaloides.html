What is Pelargonium oxaloides?

Pelargonium oxaloides is an evergreen perennial plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It has a woody base and slender stems that grow up to 60 cm in height, with ovate to lanceolate leaves that are 2-6 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, bearing small teeth along the margins. The leaves are glabrous or pubescent, with petioles that are 1-2 mm long. The plant produces white or pink flowers with five petals, which are 10-15 mm in diameter, and contain five stamens and a superior ovary.

The fruit is a loculicidal capsule that dehisces to release numerous seeds.
