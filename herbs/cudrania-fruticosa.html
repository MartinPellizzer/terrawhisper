Cudrania fruticosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the family Moraea (Moraeceae), which leads to its specific designation within the genus Cudrania. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is finalized by its specific epithet, making its full scientific name Cudrania fruticosa.
