Delphinium grandiflorum var. gilgianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Delphinium grandiflorum var. gilgianum?

Delphinium grandiflorum var. gilgianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Ranunculales. It further falls into the family Ranunculaceae and is identified by the genus Delphinium. Ultimately, the specific taxonomic designation for this plant is the variety Delphinium grandiflorum var. gilgianum.
