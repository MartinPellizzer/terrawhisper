What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone tetrasepala?

Anemone tetrasepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically falling within the genus Anemone, and is identified by its unique species designation, tetrasepala.
