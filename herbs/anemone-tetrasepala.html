What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone tetrasepala?

Anemone tetrasepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, specifically falling within the genus Anemone, and is identified by its unique species designation, tetrasepala.
