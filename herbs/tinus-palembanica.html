Possible Side Effects of Tinus palembanica

Tinus palembanica can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing liver damage, gastrointestinal issues, and allergic reactions.

In severe cases, improper use of tinus palembanica can also cause kidney damage, interact with medications, and exacerbate existing health conditions.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using any plant extracts, including tinus palembanica, to avoid these potential side effects.
