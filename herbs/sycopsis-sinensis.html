Sycopsis sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Sycopsis sinensis?

Sycopsis sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Saxifragales and the family Hamamelidaceae. Finally, the specific identification of this organism is defined by its genus, Sycopsis, and its species, sinensis.
