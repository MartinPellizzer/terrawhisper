What's the taxonomical classification of Grewia hirsuta?

Grewia hirsuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Malvales, it is placed within the family Malvaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Grewia, and its unique species name, hirsuta.
