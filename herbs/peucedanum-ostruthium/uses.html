Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium) Uses

Love-In-A-Mist is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, asthma, and sinusitis. It helps alleviate symptoms like nasal congestion and lung congestion. It may also support the immune system against respiratory infections and skin infections.

Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium) Monograph

1. Cough

Love-In-A-Mist is used to alleviate coughs by soothing irritated airways and reducing mucus production. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture to ease respiratory discomfort. Its expectorant properties help clear congestion, making it a valued herb in traditional herbal medicine for respiratory support.
