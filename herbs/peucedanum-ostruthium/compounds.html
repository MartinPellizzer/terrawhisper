Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium) Monograph

Coumarin is a key active compound in Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium). It contributes to the plant's traditional use in herbal medicine, particularly for its antispasmodic and sedative properties. Found in the roots and leaves, coumarin supports digestive and nervous system health in herbal formulations.

Flavonoids in Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium) contribute to its medicinal properties. These compounds may support respiratory health and have anti-inflammatory effects. The essential oil of Love-In-A-Mist (Peucedanum ostruthium) contains volatile compounds like limonene and phellandrene.
