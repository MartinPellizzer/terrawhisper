What's the taxonomical classification of Angelica laxifoliata?

Angelica laxifoliata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Apiaceae, which is characterized by its distinctive umbel inflorescences. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Angelica, and its specific species name, laxifoliata.
