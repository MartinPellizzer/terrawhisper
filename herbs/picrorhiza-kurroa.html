Kuroa (Picrorhiza kurroa)

Kuroa (Picrorhiza kurroa) is a member of the Labiatae family, native to Himalayas, India, and Pakistan. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, unani medicine, and tibetan medicine.
