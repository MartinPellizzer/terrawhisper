Elymus japonicus

Elymus japonicus, commonly known as Japanese wild ryegrass or Japanese wheat grass, is a type of grass native to East Asia.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which have been used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat various health issues, such as fever, inflammation, and respiratory problems.

The health benefits of Elymus japonicus include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its therapeutic actions in alleviating symptoms of colds, bronchitis, and other respiratory conditions.
