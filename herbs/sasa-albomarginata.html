Sasa albomarginata

Sasa albomarginata, also known as the white-edged bamboo, is a type of evergreen bamboo native to Japan.

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine, particularly in Eastern Asian cultures, for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Sasa albomarginata are believed to include reducing stress, improving digestion, and boosting the immune system.

It is often prepared as a tea or infused in hot water to make a decoction, which can also be used as a topical application for skin irritations and wounds.
