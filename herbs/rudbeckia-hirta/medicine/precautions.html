10 Precautions To Take When Using Rudbeckia Hirta (Black-Eyed Susan)

Rudbeckia hirta has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring liver function and potential interactions with blood thinners.

Failing to do so may result in liver damage or increased bleeding risk. For instance, if Rudbeckia hirta is combined with warfarin, it can exacerbate bleeding complications.

Regular monitoring of liver enzymes and blood clotting time is essential to prevent these adverse effects.
