10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rudbeckia Hirta (Black-Eyed Susan)

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The sesquiterpene lactones, in particular, have been shown to inhibit cancer cell growth and induce apoptosis.

The flavonoids and phenolic acids possess potent antioxidant activities, which can protect against cell damage and improve cardiovascular health.
