What's the taxonomical classification of Draba ladyginii?

Draba ladyginii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized under the order Brassicales. It falls within the family Brassicaceae, which is a group known for its diverse flowering characteristics. Specifically, the plant is identified by the genus Draba and is distinguished as the species ladyginii.
