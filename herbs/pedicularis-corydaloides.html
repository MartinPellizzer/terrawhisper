Pedicularis corydaloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, falling under the genus Pedicularis, and is specifically identified by the species name corydaloides.
