Pilocarpus Tree (Pilocarpus pennatifolius)

Pilocarpus Tree (Pilocarpus pennatifolius) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to Brazil, Peru, and Bolivia. Traditionally, its leaves, bark, and resin have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, native american herbal medicine, and european herbal medicine.
