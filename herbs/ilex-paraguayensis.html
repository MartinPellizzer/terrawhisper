Possible Side Effects of ilex paraguayensis

Ilex paraguayensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea due to its high levels of caffeine and theobromine.

Excessive consumption can also lead to anxiety, jitteriness, and insomnia, particularly in individuals who are sensitive to stimulants.

Additionally, using Ilex paraguayensis for prolonged periods can cause cardiovascular issues, including increased heart rate and blood pressure, as well as interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and high blood pressure medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Ilex paraguayensis to avoid these side effects and ensure safe use.
