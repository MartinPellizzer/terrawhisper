Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) Uses

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, is used to treat respiratory infections, inflammation, and related conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and sinusitis. It helps alleviate mucous congestion and is applied for asthma, allergic rhinitis, and the common cold. Its properties support the respiratory system and reduce symptoms of influenza.

Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and easing symptoms of infections. Its essential oils contain compounds like geraniol and citronellol, which have antimicrobial properties. It is often used in steam inhalations or as a topical application to soothe coughs and congestion.

2. Inflammation

Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) is used to reduce inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory compounds like geraniol and pelargonidin. It supports skin conditions and joint inflammation, often applied topically or ingested as a tincture. Its effects are attributed to its ability to modulate immune responses and inhibit inflammatory pathways.

3. Cough

Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) is used to support respiratory health, particularly for coughs. Its essential oil contains compounds like limonene and geraniol, which have expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the throat, making it a common remedy in herbal medicine for persistent or dry coughs.

4. Mucous congestion

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, is used to alleviate mucous congestion due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus in the respiratory tract, making it easier to expel. This makes it effective for conditions like bronchitis and sinusitis. Its essential oil is often used in steam inhalations or diluted topical applications.

5. Bronchitis

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, has been traditionally used to support respiratory health. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties may help alleviate symptoms of bronchitis by reducing mucus production and soothing airway irritation. It is often used in herbal formulations to support respiratory function during acute or chronic bronchial inflammation.

6. Allergic rhinitis

Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) is used in herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. Essential oils from its leaves are often diluted and used in aromatherapy or topical applications to reduce nasal congestion and sneezing. Its natural compounds help soothe respiratory irritation, making it a popular remedy for seasonal allergies.

7. Asthma

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, has been traditionally used to support respiratory health. Its essential oils contain compounds like limonene and eucalyptol, which may help ease bronchial congestion and reduce inflammation. Some herbal practitioners recommend it as a complementary therapy for asthma, though it should not replace prescribed medical treatments.

8. Common cold

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, is traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of the common cold due to its antimicrobial and decongestant properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and soothe sore throats, often prepared as a tea or inhalation. Its essential oil is also used topically to ease headaches and respiratory discomfort associated with colds.

9. Influenza

Geranium, or Pelargonium graveolens, has been used traditionally to support respiratory health during influenza. Its essential oils, rich in geraniol and citral, exhibit antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties. It may help reduce symptoms like congestion and fever when used as a steam inhalation or topical application. However, it should not replace medical treatment for severe cases.

10. Sinusitis

Geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) is used in herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of sinusitis due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. Its essential oil helps reduce nasal congestion and combat bacterial infections. It is often applied through steam inhalation or topical application to support respiratory health.
