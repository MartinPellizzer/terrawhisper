10 Precautions To Take When Using Pelargonium Graveolens (Geranium)

Pelargonium graveolens has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use during pregnancy and breastfeeding due to potential hormonal interactions.

This is particularly concerning as the essential oils may stimulate uterine contractions or affect milk production. Failure to observe this precaution may result in complications, such as premature birth or withdrawal symptoms in the infant.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using geranium medicinally, especially if you're pregnant or breastfeeding.
