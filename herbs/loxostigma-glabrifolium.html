Loxostigma glabrifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Loxostigma glabrifolium?

Loxostigma glabrifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically placed within the genus Loxostigma, and is identified by its specific species name, glabrifolium.
