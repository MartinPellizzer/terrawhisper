What's the taxonomical classification of Latouchea fokienensis?

Latouchea fokienensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This species is situated within the family Gentianaceae, falling under the genus Latouchea, and is specifically identified by the species name fokienensis.
