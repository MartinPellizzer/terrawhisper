Pennyroyal (Petiveria alliacea)

Pennyroyal (Petiveria alliacea) is a member of the Acanthaceae family, native to Central America, Caribbean, and Northern South America. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, native american herbal medicine, and european herbal medicine.

Cylindrical, erect, herbaceous stems with opposite branching, glabrous surfaces, and prominently veined cross-sections.
