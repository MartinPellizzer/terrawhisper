Mountain Sideritis (Sideritis montana) Compounds

The primary compounds of Mountain Sideritis include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and terpenoids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent and antimicrobial qualities. Essential oils provide aromatic and therapeutic benefits, while glycosides and saponins support digestive health. Coumarin and anthocyanin add additional antioxidant and anti-inflammatory support.
