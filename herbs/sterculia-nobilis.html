What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Sterculia nobilis has a large, spreading canopy supported by a thick, woody trunk with greyish-brown bark and trifoliate leaves consisting of three large, leathery, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically small, yellowish, or greenish, and they emerge from the axils of the leaves.
