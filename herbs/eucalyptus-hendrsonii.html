Eucalyptus hendrsonii

Eucalyptus hendrsonii, also known as Henderson's blue gum, is a species of eucalyptus that is native to Australia.

It is a tall tree with a broad trunk and a blue-green, aromatic bark, which is traditionally used for its medicinal properties.

The leaves of Eucalyptus hendrsonii contain eucalyptol, a compound that has anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.

Eucalyptus hendrsonii can be prepared in various herbal forms, including essential oils, teas, and tinctures, which can be used to alleviate symptoms of colds, coughs, and sinusitis.
