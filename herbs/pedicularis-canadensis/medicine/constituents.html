10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pedicularis Canadensis (Wood Betony)

Pedicularis canadensis has active constituents such as rosmarinic acid, caffeic acid, and flavonoids.

These compounds contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and astringent effects. The rosmarinic acid, in particular, has been shown to exhibit analgesic and antispasmodic properties, making it useful for relieving headaches, muscle cramps, and spasms.

The antioxidant flavonoids can help protect against oxidative stress and inflammation, potentially improving cognitive function and reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
