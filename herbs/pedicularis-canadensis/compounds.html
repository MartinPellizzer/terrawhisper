Canadian Lousewort (Pedicularis canadensis) Compounds

Canadian Lousewort contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Terpenoids and glycosides contribute to its medicinal properties, while tannins and coumarins provide astringent and antispasmodic benefits. Alkaloids, saponins, anthocyanins, and essential oils further support its use in herbal medicine.
