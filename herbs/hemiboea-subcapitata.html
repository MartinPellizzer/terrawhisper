Hemiboea subcapitata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Hemiboea, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species subcapitata.
