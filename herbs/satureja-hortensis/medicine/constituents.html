10 Medicinal Constituents Of Satureja Hortensis (Summer Savory)

Satureja hortensis has active constituents such as thymol, carvacrol, and rosmarinic acid.

These compounds exhibit antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it effective in treating various ailments. Thymol and carvacrol help combat respiratory infections, while rosmarinic acid reduces inflammation and improves cognitive function.

Consuming summer savory tea may alleviate digestive issues, such as bloating and cramps, while its essential oils can be used to treat skin conditions like acne and eczema.

1. Carvacrol

Satureja hortensis carvacrol is a volatile oil extracted from the herb's leaves and stems.
