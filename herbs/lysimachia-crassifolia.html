Lysimachia crassifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia crassifolia?

Lysimachia crassifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Lysimachia, and its specific species epithet, crassifolia.
