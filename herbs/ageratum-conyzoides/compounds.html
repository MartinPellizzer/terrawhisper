Whiteweed (Ageratum conyzoides) Compounds

The primary compounds in Whiteweed include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Terpenoids and essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile and potential medicinal uses. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and detoxifying properties, while coumarin, glycosides, and alkaloids may support various therapeutic applications. Anthocyanins add color and may offer additional health benefits.
