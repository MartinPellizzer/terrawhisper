10 Possible Side Effects Of Ageratum Conyzoides (Blueweed)

Ageratum conyzoides has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

These side effects occur when the herb is ingested in large quantities or for extended periods. For instance, consuming too much blueweed can lead to severe stomach cramps, which can hinder daily activities and worsen the quality of life.

Furthermore, excessive vomiting can lead to dehydration, making people more susceptible to infections and other complications.
