How is this plant cultivated?

This plant thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and requires well-draining soil that is kept consistently moist but never waterlogged.

High humidity levels are beneficial for its lush, glossy leaves, so frequent misting or using a pebble tray can help maintain optimal moisture.
