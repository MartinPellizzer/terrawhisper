Notoginseng (Panax notoginseng)

Notoginseng (Panax notoginseng) is a member of the Araliaceae family, native to China, Yunnan Province, China, Guizhou Province, China, and Sichuan Province. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and resin have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
