Pollia thyrsiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Pollia thyrsiflora?

Pollia thyrsiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Commelinaales into the family Commelinaeaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Pollia, with its specific classification being the species thyrsiflora.

These phytochemical constituents modulate the activity of various cytokines to mitigate systemic immune responses. By interacting with these specific protein structures, the plant extracts can suppress the signaling cascades that drive chronic pain and swelling. While specific toxicological studies on humans are limited, contact with the plant's juices may lead to redness, itching, or swelling. Some researchers suggest that certain alkaloids within the plant family could potentially cause gastrointestinal distress if accidentally ingested.
