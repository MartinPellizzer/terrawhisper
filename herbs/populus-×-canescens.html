Populus × canescens

Populus × canescens, also known as the hybrid black cottonwood, is a deciduous tree that is a cross between the black cottonwood (Populus trichocarpa) and the narrowleaf cottonwood (Populus angustifolia).

The medicinal properties of Populus × canescens include its ability to reduce inflammation and its antiseptic properties, which make it useful for wound healing.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to lower blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health, as well as its potential to reduce symptoms of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.

Herbal preparations of Populus × canescens can be made into teas, tinctures, and salves, and can be used to support immune function and promote overall well-being.
