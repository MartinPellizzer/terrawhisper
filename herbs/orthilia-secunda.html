What's the taxonomical classification of Orthilia secunda?

Orthilia secunda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down its taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Ericales and is a member of the family Ericaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Orthilia, with its specific designation being the species secunda.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Orthilia secunda has an erect, slender stem that typically reaches heights of 10 to 40 centimeters and supports several small, alternate, ovate leaves with entire margins. Its flowering structure consists of a terminal raceme where the small, white, bell-shaped flowers are arranged on one side of the stem. Each flower features four tepals and is supported by a short pedicel. The plant produces small, globose, reddish berries that develop after successful pollination.
