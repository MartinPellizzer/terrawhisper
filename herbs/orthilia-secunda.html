Orthilia secunda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Orthilia, with its specific designation being the species secunda.

Each flower features four tepals and is supported by a short pedicel. The plant produces small, globose, reddish berries that develop after successful pollination.
