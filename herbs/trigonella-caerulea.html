Trigonella caerulea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Trigonella, and its unique species designation, caerulea.
