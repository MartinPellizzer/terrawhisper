Piper'S Betony (Piper angustifolium)

Piper'S Betony (Piper angustifolium) is a member of the Piperaceae family, native to North America, Alaska, and Canada. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
