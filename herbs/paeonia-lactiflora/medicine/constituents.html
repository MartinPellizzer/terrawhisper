10 Medicinal Constituents Of Paeonia Lactiflora (Chinese Peony)

Paeonia lactiflora, also known as Chinese peony, has active constituents such as phenolic acids, flavonoids, and saponins.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antispasmodic properties, making it a valuable herb for treating various health issues. The medicinal properties of Paeonia lactiflora can help alleviate menstrual cramps, improve sleep quality, and even reduce anxiety and depression symptoms.

For instance, the flavonoids in this plant have been shown to increase dopamine levels, which can improve mood and cognitive function, while the saponins may help regulate blood sugar levels, benefiting individuals with diabetes.
