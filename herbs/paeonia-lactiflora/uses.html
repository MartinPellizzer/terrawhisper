Chinese Peony (Paeonia lactiflora) Uses

Chinese Peony is used to treat menstrual cramps and irregularities due to its blood-activating properties. It also alleviates inflammation and joint pain, making it effective for arthritis and inflammatory conditions. Additionally, it supports respiratory health by reducing coughs and easing skin irritations.

Chinese Peony (Paeonia lactiflora) Monograph

1. Menstrual cramps

Chinese Peony root is used to alleviate menstrual cramps by regulating blood flow and reducing inflammation. It helps ease pain and discomfort associated with dysmenorrhea. Its antispasmodic properties support smooth muscle relaxation in the uterus. It is commonly prepared as a decoction or powder for internal use.

2. Menstrual irregularities

Chinese Peony root is used to regulate menstrual cycles and alleviate symptoms of menstrual irregularities. It helps reduce blood stasis and pain, commonly associated with dysmenorrhea and irregular periods. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effect on balancing qi and blood flow in the body.

3. Inflammation

Chinese Peony root is used to treat inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and pain in conditions like arthritis and skin infections. The active compounds, such as paeoniflorin, inhibit inflammatory pathways in the body. It is often combined with other herbs in traditional formulas for enhanced efficacy.

4. Arthritis

Chinese Peony root is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat arthritis due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce joint pain and swelling by promoting circulation and balancing bodily energies. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in managing inflammatory conditions.

5. Inflammatory conditions

Chinese Peony root is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat inflammatory conditions such as arthritis and skin disorders. It possesses anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, helping to reduce swelling and pain. The active compounds, including paeoniflorin, contribute to its therapeutic effects in managing chronic inflammation.

6. Joint pain

Chinese Peony root is used to alleviate joint pain by reducing inflammation and improving circulation. It is particularly effective for conditions like arthritis and rheumatic pain. The herb works by promoting blood flow and reducing swelling in the joints. It is often combined with other herbs in traditional formulas for enhanced therapeutic effects.

7. Inflammatory joint pain

Chinese Peony root is used in traditional medicine to alleviate inflammatory joint pain due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It helps reduce swelling and stiffness, commonly used in formulations for rheumatism and arthritis. Its active compounds, such as paeoniflorin, contribute to its therapeutic effects on joint inflammation.

8. Respiratory infections

Chinese Peony is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat respiratory infections due to its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. It helps reduce symptoms like coughing and congestion by supporting immune function and clearing phlegm. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in respiratory conditions.

9. Cough

Chinese Peony is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat coughs by reducing inflammation and soothing the respiratory tract. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its expectorant properties. The root is typically decocted or processed into powders for internal use. It is particularly effective for chronic coughs associated with lung heat.

10. Skin irritations

Chinese Peony root is used topically to treat skin irritations due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. It helps reduce redness, swelling, and itching associated with conditions like eczema and psoriasis. The compound paeoniflorin in peony has been shown to modulate skin inflammation. It is often applied as a paste or incorporated into herbal ointments for localized relief.
