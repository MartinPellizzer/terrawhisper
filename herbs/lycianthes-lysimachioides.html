What's the taxonomical classification of Lycianthes lysimachioides?

Lycianthes lysimachioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order of Solanales and is a member of the family Solanaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Lycianthes, and its unique species designation, lysimachioides.
