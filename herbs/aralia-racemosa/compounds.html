American Angel'S Trumpets (Aralia racemosa) Compounds

The primary compounds in American Angel's Trumpets include saponins, which have surfactant properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to the plant's astringent qualities, while coumarin and glycosides may have anti-inflammatory properties. Terpenoids and essential oils provide aromatic characteristics, and alkaloids are noted for their psychoactive and medicinal potential. Anthocyanins contribute to the plant's vibrant color and may have antioxidant benefits.
