10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aralia Racemosa (American Spikenard)

Aralia racemosa, also known as American Spikenard, has active constituents such as lignans, terpenes, and saponins.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues. For example, the plant's lignans can help alleviate menstrual cramps and other PMS symptoms, while its terpenes have shown potential in combating respiratory infections.

The saponins also exhibit antioxidant activity, which may help protect against chronic diseases such as cancer and cardiovascular disease.
