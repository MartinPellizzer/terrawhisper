Anemopsis californica, also known as the California water plant, has been traditionally used to treat dysentery due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

Its natural compounds help alleviate symptoms and support the body's recovery process.

Its natural compounds make it a potential remedy for managing symptoms associated with dysentery.
