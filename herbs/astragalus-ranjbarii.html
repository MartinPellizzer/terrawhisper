Astragalus ranjbarii

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its adaptogenic and immunomodulatory effects, which have been used to boost the immune system and reduce stress.

The health benefits of Astragalus ranjbarii include its ability to lower blood pressure, improve cardiovascular health, and enhance exercise performance.
