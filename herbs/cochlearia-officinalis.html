Possible Side Effects of cochlearia officinalis

Furthermore, using Cochlearia officinalis in high doses or for extended periods can cause liver damage and interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Additionally, pregnant women should avoid using Cochlearia officinalis due to its potential to stimulate uterine contractions and affect fetal development.
