Cochlearia Officinalis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cochlearia officinalis, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family, known for its medicinal properties.

This herb has been traditionally used for its ability to support respiratory and digestive health, as well as for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The bioactive constituents in Cochlearia officinalis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from the leaves and flowers to harness its beneficial properties.
