Alysicarpus bupleurifolius

What's the taxonomical classification of Alysicarpus bupleurifolius?

Alysicarpus bupleurifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated in the order Fabales. It is a member of the family Fabaceae and falls under the genus Alysicarpus, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species bupleurifolius.
