Cymbopogon citratus

Cymbopogon citratus, also known as lemon grass, is a tropical grass native to Southeast Asia and used in traditional medicine and cooking.

It contains bioactive compounds with anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its medicinal value.

The health benefits of lemon grass include reducing fever, relieving digestive issues, and providing antioxidant protection against cell damage.

Lemon grass can be prepared as a tea, oil, or dried powder for use in herbal supplements and aromatherapy, or added to foods and beverages for its citrusy flavor and aroma.
