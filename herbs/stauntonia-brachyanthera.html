Stauntonia brachyanthera

What's the taxonomical classification of Stauntonia brachyanthera?

Stauntonia brachyanthera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Lardizabalaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Stauntonia, and its specific species name, brachyanthera.
