What is Cinnamomum amplexicaule?

Cinnamomum amplexicaule is a species of plant that belongs to the family Lauraceae.

The leaves are arranged in opposite pairs, with a pubescent petiole of 2-5 mm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in axillary panicles, with a calyx of 3-4 mm in length and a corolla of 3-4 mm in length.

The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is found in tropical forests at elevations of up to 1,500 meters.
