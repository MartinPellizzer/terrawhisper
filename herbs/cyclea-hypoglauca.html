Cyclea hypoglauca

What's the taxonomical classification of Cyclea hypoglauca?

Cyclea hypoglauca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ranunculales. This specific plant is a representative of the family Menispermaceae, situated within the genus Cyclea, and is identified by its unique species name, hypoglauca.
