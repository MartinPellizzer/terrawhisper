What is Arrowsmithia glandulosa?

Arrowsmithia glandulosa is a flowering plant endemic to the Western Cape province of South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is the sole species in the monotypic genus Arrowsmithia. The plant is an annual or biennial herb with a woody taproot, reaching heights of up to 50 cm and producing numerous glandular hairs on its stems and leaves. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and lance-shaped, measuring up to 10 cm in length.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers with a central disc and a ring of ligulate florets.
