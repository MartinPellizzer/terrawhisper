Senna divaricata

The health benefits of Senna divaricata include its use in traditional medicine to treat various ailments, such as fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to its ability to reduce fever, relieve pain, and act as a purgative, which helps in cleansing the body.
