Marshmallow (Althaea officinalis) Uses

Marshmallow is used to treat coughs, bronchitis, sore throat, and other respiratory conditions involving inflamed mucous membranes. It soothes irritation in the throat, nasal passages, and lungs, making it effective for respiratory infections and mucosal inflammation. Its mucilage content helps reduce inflammation and protect mucous membranes.
