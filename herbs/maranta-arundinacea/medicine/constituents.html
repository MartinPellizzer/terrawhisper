10 Medicinal Constituents Of Maranta Arundinacea (Prayer Plant)

Maranta arundinacea has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and anthraquinones.

These medicinal compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The constituents of Maranta arundinacea can help alleviate symptoms of various health issues, including skin irritations, wounds, and digestive problems.

For example, the antioxidants in this plant may improve cardiovascular health by reducing oxidative stress and inflammation, while its antibacterial properties can aid in wound healing and infection prevention.

1. Saponins

Maranta arundinacea saponins is a type of bioactive compound found in the leaves and stems of this species.

Maranta arundinacea flavonoids is a type of compound found in its leaves.

This makes them valuable in traditional medicine and potential candidates for the treatment of diseases associated with oxidative stress.

3. Alkaloids
