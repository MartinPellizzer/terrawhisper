10 Health Benefits Of Maranta Arundinacea (Prayer Plant)

Maranta arundinacea has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The plant's extracts have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including skin conditions like eczema and acne. Its anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate pain and reduce swelling, making it a potential treatment for arthritis and other inflammatory disorders.

For instance, the plant's topical application can provide relief from sunburns and insect bites, improving overall skin health and well-being.
