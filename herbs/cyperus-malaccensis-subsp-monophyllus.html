What's the taxonomical classification of Cyperus malaccensis subsp. monophyllus?

Cyperus malaccensis subsp. monophyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Poaales and falls into the family Cyperaceae. Specifically, this plant is a member of the genus Cyperus, which is further categorized into its unique taxonomic designation as the species Cyperus malaccensis subsp. monophyllus.
