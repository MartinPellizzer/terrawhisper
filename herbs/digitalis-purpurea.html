Possible Side Effects of digitalis purpurea

Digitalis purpurea can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea and vomiting, due to its high toxicity.

In severe cases, it can cause irregular heartbeats and arrhythmias, which can be life-threatening if left untreated.

Long-term use or excessive consumption can also lead to digitalis toxicity, characterized by symptoms such as fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath.

Additionally, digitalis purpurea can interact with other medications, such as beta blockers and certain antibiotics, exacerbating its side effects.
