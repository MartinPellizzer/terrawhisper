Asclepias Dependens

Asclepias dependens, commonly known as the showy milkweed, has been used for medicinal purposes by various indigenous cultures for centuries.

The plant contains cardiac glycosides, which have been traditionally used to treat heart conditions and respiratory ailments. Its sap, though toxic, has been applied topically to treat wounds and skin infections. In modern herbal medicine, it is sometimes used to support cardiovascular health and as a natural remedy for inflammation.

However, due to its potent and potentially harmful compounds, it should only be used under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.
