Wild Strawberry (Fragaria vesca) Actions

Wild Strawberry exhibits several therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and astringent properties. It also acts as a carminative, tonic, and stimulant, helping to relieve digestive discomfort and support overall vitality. Its bitter and antispasmodic qualities aid in soothing the digestive tract, while its expectorant and sedative effects can support respiratory health and calm the nervous system.
