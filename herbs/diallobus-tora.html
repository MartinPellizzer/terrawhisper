Possible Side Effects of Diallobus tora

Diallobus tora can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, headaches, and dizziness due to the high levels of toxic compounds it contains.

If not handled and used correctly, the plant's toxic compounds can accumulate in the body, leading to long-term health consequences.
