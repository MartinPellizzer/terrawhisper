Syringa × henryi var. eximia

Syringa × henryi var. eximia is a hybrid plant, specifically a variety of the Syringa × henryi, which is a cross between Syringa laeta and Syringa villosa.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is valued for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits associated with Syringa × henryi var. eximia include reducing stress and anxiety, improving cardiovascular health, and acting as an antibacterial agent.
