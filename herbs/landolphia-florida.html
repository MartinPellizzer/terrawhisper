What's the taxonomical classification of Landolphia florida?

Landolphia florida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated in the order Gentianales. It is a member of the family Apocynaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Landolphia, with the specific designation of the species florida.
