What's the taxonomical classification of Pseudolysimachion linariifolium subsp. dilatatum?

Pseudolysimachion linariifolium subsp. dilatatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Plantaginaceae. This specific taxon is situated within the genus Pseudolysimachion, ultimately reaching its precise identification at the subspecies level as Pseudolysimachion linariifolium subsp. dilatatum.
