What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Elsholtzia stauntonii has a small to medium-sized deciduous tree structure characterized by spreading, rounded crowns and finely textured, pinnately compound leaves. Its bark matures into a grayish-brown color with shallow longitudinal fissures that provide a textured surface. The reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous flowers that eventually develop into distinctive samara fruits.
