Euphrasia pectinata subsp. simplex

What's the taxonomical classification of Euphrasia pectinata subsp. simplex?

Euphrasia pectinata subsp. simplex belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Orobanchaceae and falls under the genus Euphrasia, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species Euphrasia pectinata subsp. simplex.
