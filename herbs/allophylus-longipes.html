Allophylus longipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Sapindales. It is further categorized into the family Sapindaceae, belonging to the genus Allophylus, with the specific species designation of longipes.
