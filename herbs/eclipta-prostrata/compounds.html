Goatweed (Eclipta prostrata) Compounds

Goatweed contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins aid in wound healing and digestion. Coumarin and terpenoids may support skin health. Essential oils and alkaloids contribute to its overall therapeutic profile. Glycosides and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal benefits.

Goatweed (Eclipta prostrata) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids in Goatweed (Eclipta prostrata) contribute to its medicinal properties. These compounds exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, supporting skin healing and immune function. Phenolic acid is a key active compound in Goatweed (Eclipta prostrata).
