What's the taxonomical classification of Lemmaphyllum carnosum?

Lemmaphyllum carnosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Polypodiaceae, specifically within the genus Lemmaphyllum, with its unique designation as the species carnosum.
