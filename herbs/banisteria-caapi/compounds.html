Banisteria Caapi Vine (Banisteria caapi) Compounds

Banisteria caapi contains various active compounds, including alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenoids. These compounds contribute to its traditional use in medicinal practices. Alkaloids like harmine and harmaline are particularly noted for their psychoactive and medicinal properties. Other compounds such as tannins and saponins support its role in herbal medicine.

1. Alkaloid

Banisteria caapi contains alkaloids, which are naturally occurring chemical compounds with significant pharmacological effects. These alkaloids, such as harmine and harmaline, play a crucial role in the plant's traditional use in Ayahuasca preparations, influencing psychoactive and therapeutic properties.
