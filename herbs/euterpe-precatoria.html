Red Cabbage Palm (Euterpe precatoria)

Red Cabbage Palm (Euterpe precatoria) is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to Brazil, Guyana, and Suriname. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, tonic, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and native american herbal medicine.
