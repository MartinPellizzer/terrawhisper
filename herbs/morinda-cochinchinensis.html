What's the taxonomical classification of Morinda cochinchinensis?

Morinda cochinchinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Gentianales, the plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Morinda, and its specific identification is defined by the species epithet cochinchinenis.
