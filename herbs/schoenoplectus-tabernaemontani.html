What's the taxonomical classification of Schoenoplectus tabernaemontani?

Schoenoplectus tabernaemontani belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It is further organized under the order Poales and falls within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Schoenoplectus, specifically as the species tabernaemontani.
