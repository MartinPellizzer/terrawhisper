Maiden'S Hair Fern (Adiantum capillus-veneris) Compounds

The primary compounds in Maiden's Hair Fern include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and coumarin, known for its anticoagulant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent qualities, while glycosides and saponins may support anti-inflammatory actions. Terpenoids, alkaloids, and essential oils add to its complex pharmacological profile. Anthocyanins provide color and potential antioxidant benefits.
