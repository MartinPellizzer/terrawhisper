Adiantum capillus-veneris has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in moderation and under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

This is because excessive use can cause gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

If not taken in moderation, it may also lead to an overactive thyroid or allergic reactions, such as hives and itching.
