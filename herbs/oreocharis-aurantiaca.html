Oreocharis aurantiaca has ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins, a hairy indumentum on both surfaces, and small, orange-colored tubular flowers. The leaf texture is often somewhat fleshy, and the presence of fine hairs serves as a distinguishing morphological feature.
