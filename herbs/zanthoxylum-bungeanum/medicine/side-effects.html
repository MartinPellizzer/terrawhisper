10 Possible Side Effects Of Zanthoxylum Bungeanum (Szechuan Pepper)

Zanthoxylum bungeanum, also known as Szechuan pepper, has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive issues, nausea, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption or individual sensitivities. For instance, consuming large amounts of the herb may exacerbate existing digestive problems, leading to severe stomach pain, diarrhea, or vomiting.

This can severely impact one's daily life, affecting work, social interactions, and overall well-being.
