Lablab (Lablab purpureus) Compounds

The primary active compounds in Lablab purpureus include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and detoxifying properties, while coumarin and anthocyanin offer additional antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits. Glycosides, terpenoids, alkaloids, and essential oils further support its medicinal applications in traditional herbal medicine.
