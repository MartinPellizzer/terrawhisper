What's the taxonomical classification of Selaginella kraussiana?

Selaginella kraussiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Selaginellales and the family Selaginellaceae, placing it firmly within the genus Selaginella. Ultimately, it is identified at the species level as Selaginella kraussiana.
