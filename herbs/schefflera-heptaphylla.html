Schefflera heptaphylla has large, leathery, elliptical leaves that typically grow in whorls of seven or more leaflets around a central stem. The plant exhibits a woody structure with branching stems that support the dense canopy of compound leaves. These morphological traits help the species thrive in its native tropical forest environment.
