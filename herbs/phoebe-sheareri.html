What's the taxonomical classification of Phoebe sheareri?

Phoebe sheareri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Laurales and is a member of the family Lauraceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Phoebe and is specifically designated as the species sheareri.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Phoebe sheareri has elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are dark green, coriaceous, and possess an entire margin with an acute apex.

These leaves are arranged alternately along the woody stems of the tree. The plant typically features small, inconspicuous flowers that emerge from the axils of the foliage. Its fruit consists of small drupes that transition in color as they reach maturity.

The bark is generally smooth and provides a protective layer for the underlying vascular tissues.
