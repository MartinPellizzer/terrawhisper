Polygonum chinense var. hispidum

What's the taxonomical classification of Polygonum chinense var. hispidum?

Polygonum chinense var. hispidum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Caryophyllales and further categorized into the family Polygonaceae. At the lower taxonomic levels, it is identified by the genus Polygonum, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety Polygonum chinense var. hispidum.
