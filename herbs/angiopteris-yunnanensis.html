Angiopteris yunnanensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Angiopteris yunnanensis has a rhizomatous growth habit with slender, creeping stems that produce small, deeply divided, pinnate fronds. The plant typically features delicate, highly dissected foliage that gives it a feathery appearance. Its reproductive structures are characterized by small sporangia located on the underside of the fertile leaves. These fronds are often thin and membranous, adapted to the moist environments of its native habitat. The overall structure is fine-textured and relatively diminutive in stature.

What are the therapeutic actions of Angiopteris yunnanensis?

Angiopteris yunnanensis has diuretic, hemostatic, and astringent therapeutic actions used to treat various inflammatory and bleeding conditions. Due to its astringent qualities, it helps to contract tissues and reduce swelling in affected areas. These combined medicinal effects make it a significant component in traditional herbal remedies.
