Chloranthus angustifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. More specifically, it is organized under the order Chloranthales and the family Chloranthaceae. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the genus Chloranthus, with its specific identity defined by the species angustifolius.
