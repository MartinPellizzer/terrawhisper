Epipactis mairei

What's the taxonomical classification of Epipactis mairei?

Epipactis mairei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, falling under the genus Epipactis, and is specifically identified by its species name, mairei.
