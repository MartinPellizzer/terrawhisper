What's the taxonomical classification of Madhuca pasquieri?

Madhuca pasquieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Sapotaceae, placing it within a group of diverse woody plants. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Madhuca, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name pasquieri.
