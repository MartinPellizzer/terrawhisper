Eurya weissiae

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eurya weissiae has elliptical to oblong leaves that measure approximately 3 to 7 centimeters in length and are characterized by an entire margin and a glossy, dark green upper surface. The leaf texture is typically coriaceous, meaning it possesses a leathery consistency that aids in moisture retention. Small, inconspicuous white flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in clusters. These plants generally exhibit a compact, shrubby growth habit with woody stems. The overall structure is dense, providing a lush appearance in its natural forest habitat.
