Cayratia japonica var. mollis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cayratia japonica var. mollis?

Cayratia japonica var. mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized under Equisetopsida, which leads into its placement in the order Vitales. This variety is a member of the family Vitaceae and specifically falls under the genus Cayratiia, with its full scientific designation being the species Cayratiia japonica var. mollis.
