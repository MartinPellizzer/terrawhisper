What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex angulata?

Ilex angulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae, following the order Aquifoliales. The plant is further categorized into the family Aquifoliaceae, which contains the genus Ilex, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Ilex angulatas.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

If the leaves or berries are consumed, individuals might experience nausea, vomiting, or abdominal pain. Some people may also exhibit systemic allergic reactions such as itching or hives. It is essential to exercise caution when working with this species to avoid these adverse physical responses.
