Apios fortunei

What's the taxonomical classification of Apios fortunei?

Apios fortunei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Fabales. It is a member of the family Fabaceae, a group well-known for its diverse range of legumes. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Apios, and its unique scientific designation is completed by the species name fortunei.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

These tubers contain complex carbohydrates and certain compounds that may lead to significant bloating or gas during digestion. Eating the plant raw or undercooked increases the risk of these unpleasant digestive reactions. Additionally, some individuals may experience mild allergic reactions such as skin irritation or itching upon contact with the plant. It is essential to ensure the tubers are boiled or roasted completely to neutralize these potential issues.
