Herb Types For Foundational Herbal Knowledge

Understanding the basics of herb types is essential for anyone seeking foundational herbal knowledge. Herbs can be broadly categorized into culinary, medicinal, and aromatic varieties each serving distinct purposes. Culinary herbs enhance flavor while medicinal herbs offer therapeutic benefits. Aromatic herbs are often used in perfumes and rituals. This article explores key herb types to build a strong foundation in herbal studies.
