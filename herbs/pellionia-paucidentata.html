Pellionia paucidentata

Pellioniia paucidentata has opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves with irregularly serrated margins and a distinctively textured surface. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils to facilitate reproduction. This species is characterized by its relatively small leaf size compared to other members of its genus.
