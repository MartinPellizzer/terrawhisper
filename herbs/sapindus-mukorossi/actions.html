Chinese Soapberry (Sapindus mukorossi) Actions

Chinese Soapberry exhibits several therapeutic actions including astringent, diuretic, and anti-inflammatory properties. It also acts as a tonic, antispasmodic, and carminative, helping to relieve digestive issues. Additionally, it has expectorant, stimulant, and sedative effects, supporting respiratory and nervous system health.
