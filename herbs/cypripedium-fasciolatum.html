Cypripedium fasciolatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Cypripedium fasciolatum?

Cypripedium fasciolatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, which places it within the genus Cypripedium, eventually reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species fasciolatum.
