These constituents contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. As a result, reed mace may help alleviate symptoms of arthritis and improve skin health by reducing inflammation and protecting against oxidative stress.

In addition, its antimicrobial properties can help combat infections and promote wound healing.
