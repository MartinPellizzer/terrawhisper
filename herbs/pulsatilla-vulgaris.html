Showy Pulsatilla (Pulsatilla vulgaris)

Showy Pulsatilla (Pulsatilla vulgaris) is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, native to Central Europe, Southern Europe, and Northern Africa. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and shoots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, sedative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
