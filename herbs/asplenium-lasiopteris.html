What is Asplenium lasiopteris?

Asplenium lasiopteris is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a perennial plant that grows up to 30-60 cm tall, with a rhizome that is black and scaly. The fronds are lance-shaped, 10-30 cm long, and have a petiole that is 5-15 cm long. The leaflets are linear, 2-4 cm long, and have a glandular midrib.

The plant produces sporangia on the underside of the fronds in a sori pattern, typically in the spring and summer months.
