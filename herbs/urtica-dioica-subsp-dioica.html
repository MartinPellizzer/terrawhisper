Urtica dioica subsp. dioica

What's the taxonomical classification of Urtica dioica subsp. dioica?

Urtica dioica subsp. dioica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the order Rosales, placing it firmly within the family Urticaceae. At the more specific levels of classification, this plant is identified under the genus Urtica, specifically as the subspecies dioica.
