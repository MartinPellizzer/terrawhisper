Phlomis mongolica

What's the taxonomical classification of Phlomis mongolica?

Phlomis mongolica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. This specific plant is identified by its genus, Phlomis, and is distinguished at the species level as Phlomis mongolica.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Phlomis mongolica has a perennial herbaceous habit characterized by erect, square-shaped stems that often reach heights of up to 50 centimeters. Its inflorescence consists of dense, terminal verticillasters that hold small, tubular, yellowish-white flowers. The calyx is typically two-lipped and covered in fine pubescence, providing protection to the developing floral structures.
