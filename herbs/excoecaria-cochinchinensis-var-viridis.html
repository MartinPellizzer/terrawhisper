Excoecaria cochinchinensis var. viridis

What's the taxonomical classification of Excoecaria cochinchinensis var. viridis?

Excoecaria cochinchiensis var. viridis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Malpighiales and is situated within the family Euphorbiaceae. At the more specific taxonomic levels, it is identified under the genus Excoecaria, ultimately reaching its unique designation as the variety viridis of the species Excoecaria cochinchiensis.
