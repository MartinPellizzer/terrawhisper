What's the taxonomical classification of Fissistigma bracteolatum?

Fissistigma bracteolatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Magnoliales, the plant is placed into the family Annonaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Fissistigma, and its unique species name, bracteolatum.
