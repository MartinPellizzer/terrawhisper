What's the taxonomical classification of Crawfurdia campanulacea?

Crawfurdia campanulacea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the family Gentianaceae, specifically falling under the genus Crawfurdia, with the species designation campanulacea.
