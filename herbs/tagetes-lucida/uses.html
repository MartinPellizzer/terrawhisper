Black Sage (Tagetes lucida) Uses

Black Sage is traditionally used to treat respiratory infections, influenza, and allergic rhinitis. It helps alleviate symptoms like sinusitis, nasal congestion, and sinus congestion. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties support the treatment of common cold symptoms and respiratory irritation.

Black Sage (Tagetes lucida) Monograph

1. Respiratory infection

Black Sage is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and clearing mucus. It may help alleviate symptoms of colds, coughs, and bronchitis. Its antimicrobial properties can aid in fighting infections. It is typically prepared as a tea or tincture for respiratory support.

2. Influenza

Black Sage is traditionally used to support respiratory health and may help alleviate symptoms of influenza due to its antimicrobial and expectorant properties. It is often prepared as a tea to reduce fever, soothe coughs, and ease congestion associated with viral infections. Its use aligns with historical practices in herbal medicine for immune support during seasonal illnesses.

3. Allergic rhinitis

Black Sage is used to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by calming the immune response. Often prepared as a tea, it provides a natural alternative for managing seasonal allergies.

4. Sinusitis

Black Sage is used to support respiratory health, particularly for sinusitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help reduce nasal congestion and combat infection. It can be prepared as a steam inhalation or tea to alleviate symptoms and promote clearer breathing.

5. Common cold

Black Sage is traditionally used to support the immune system and alleviate symptoms of the common cold. It contains compounds that may help reduce inflammation and ease congestion. Infusions or tinctures are commonly prepared to promote respiratory comfort during colds. Its antimicrobial properties are believed to assist in clearing infections.

6. Respiratory infections

Black Sage is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and soothing irritation in the throat and airways. It may help alleviate symptoms of coughs, colds, and bronchitis when consumed as a tea or tincture. Its antimicrobial properties can aid in clearing infections and promoting faster recovery.

7. Sinus congestion

Black Sage is used to alleviate sinus congestion due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce mucus buildup and soothe irritated nasal passages. Often prepared as a steam inhalation or infused tea, it provides relief for seasonal allergies and colds. Its effectiveness makes it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for respiratory support.

8. Cold symptoms

Black Sage is used to alleviate cold symptoms such as congestion, sore throat, and cough. Its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties help reduce mucus production and soothe respiratory discomfort. It can be prepared as a tea or tincture for internal use.

9. Nasal congestion

Black Sage is used to alleviate nasal congestion due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce mucus production and soothe irritated nasal passages. Traditionally, it is prepared as a steam inhalation or tea to relieve sinus pressure and improve breathing. Its aromatic compounds provide a natural remedy for respiratory discomfort.

10. Respiratory irritation

Black Sage is used to alleviate respiratory irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It helps soothe coughs, reduce mucus production, and ease symptoms of bronchitis and asthma. Its essential oil is often inhaled or used in steam therapy for respiratory relief. It is typically prepared as a tea or tincture for internal use.
