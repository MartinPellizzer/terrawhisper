10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tagetes Lucida (Mexican Marigold)

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, has active constituents such as thymol, geraniol, and linalool.

These constituents provide medicinal properties that have been used to treat various ailments, including digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions. The antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory effects of these compounds can help alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and asthma, improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.

Additionally, the antimicrobial properties of Mexican Marigold may aid in wound healing and reduce the risk of infection, promoting overall health and well-being.
