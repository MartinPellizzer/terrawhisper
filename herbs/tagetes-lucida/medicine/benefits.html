10 Health Benefits Of Tagetes Lucida (Mexican Marigold)

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican marigold, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and relieving anxiety.

Its medicinal properties include its high concentration of flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils. For example, the antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from digestive issues.

Additionally, its anxiolytic properties can help individuals manage stress and anxiety.

1. Reduces anxiety

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, reduces anxiety because of its unique composition.

The plant contains a high concentration of azulene compounds, which have been shown to possess anxiolytic properties. These compounds work by interacting with the brain's neurotransmitters, such as serotonin and GABA, to regulate mood and emotional response.

Additionally, the calming aroma of Mexican Marigold has been found to reduce stress and anxiety in individuals, promoting a sense of relaxation and tranquility.

2. Alleviates digestive issues

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican marigold, alleviates digestive issues because of its unique composition.

The plant contains flavonoids and phenolic compounds that have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce inflammation in the gut and alleviate symptoms such as bloating, cramps, and diarrhea.

Additionally, the herb has been traditionally used to stimulate digestion and relieve constipation due to its bitter principle, sesquiterpene lactones.

3. Fights inflammation

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican marigold, fights inflammation because of its unique blend of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains a rich array of terpenoids and flavonoids that possess potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, reduce oxidative stress, and promote the activation of natural killer cells, ultimately leading to a reduction in inflammation and related symptoms.

4. Boosts immune system

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, boosts immune system because it contains a rich source of flavonoids and terpenes that possess potent antioxidant properties.

These compounds help to neutralize free radicals in the body, thereby preventing oxidative stress and inflammation, which can weaken the immune system.

Additionally, the plant's essential oils have been shown to stimulate the production of white blood cells, further enhancing the body's natural defense mechanisms against infections and diseases.

5. Regulates menstrual cycle

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, regulates menstrual cycle because of its unique composition of essential oils and flavonoids.

These bioactive compounds interact with the body's hormonal balance, specifically progesterone and estrogen levels, to normalize menstrual patterns.

By reducing stress-induced inflammation and promoting uterine health, Tagetes lucida helps to regulate ovulation, menorrhagia, and even alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

6. Eases menstrual cramps

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, eases menstrual cramps because its essential oil contains a unique combination of flavonoids and terpenes that exhibit potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

When inhaled or applied topically, these compounds work to reduce prostaglandin production, alleviating uterine contractions and pain associated with menstrual cramps.

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, improves oral health because of its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

8. Acts as natural insecticide

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican marigold, acts as natural insecticide because of its unique chemical composition.

The plant contains a group of compounds called pyrazines, which are responsible for its insect-repelling properties. These chemicals disrupt the insect's nervous system, causing them to become disoriented and eventually die.

Additionally, the plant's strong scent also masks other attractive odors to insects, making it an effective deterrent against various pests, including mosquitoes, flies, and moths.

9. Supports bone health

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican Marigold, supports bone health because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids that help to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.

10. Enhances cognitive function

Tagetes lucida, also known as Mexican marigold, enhances cognitive function because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

Additionally, its flavonoids have anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce oxidative stress and neuronal damage associated with neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's.

This natural boost can lead to improved mental clarity and focus, making it an attractive option for those seeking a cognitive enhancer.
