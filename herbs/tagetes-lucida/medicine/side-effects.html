10 Possible Side Effects Of Tagetes Lucida (Mexican Marigold)

Tagetes lucida has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues.

These side effects can occur if the herb is ingested in large quantities, used in conjunction with other medications, or applied directly to the skin without proper dilution.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen daily lives by causing persistent itching, stomach cramps, and difficulty sleeping, affecting productivity and overall well-being.
