Amarella tortuosa

Amarella tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted lady's mantle, is a perennial plant species in the saxifragaceae family.

The health benefits of Amarella tortuosa include its ability to act as an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant, which can help to reduce inflammation and protect cells from damage.

The plant's therapeutic actions include its use in traditional medicine to treat conditions such as bronchitis and coughs, as well as its potential application in the treatment of respiratory diseases due to its expectorant properties.

The bioactive constituents of Amarella tortuosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of Amarella tortuosa

Amarella tortuosa can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, nausea, and dizziness.

Additionally, improper use of the plant can interact with other medications, exacerbating underlying health conditions.
