What's the taxonomical classification of Angiopteris caudatiformis?

Angiopteris caudatiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated under the subclass Marattiidae and falls within the order Marattiales. The plant is further categorized into the family Marattiaceae, which contains the genus Angiopteris, ultimately leading to its specific identification as the species caudatiformis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
