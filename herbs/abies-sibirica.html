Abies Sibirica Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Abies sibirica, commonly known as the Siberian fir, is a coniferous tree native to the cold regions of Siberia, known for its aromatic needles and resinous properties.

This herb is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and respiratory-supporting effects.

The bioactive constituents in Abies sibirica include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from its needles and resin to harness its medicinal properties.
