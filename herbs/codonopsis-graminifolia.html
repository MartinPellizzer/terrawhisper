What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis graminifolia?

Codonopsis graminifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Asterales, the plant is situated in the family Campanulaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Codonoopsis, with the specific epithet graminifolia.
