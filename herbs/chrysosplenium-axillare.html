    What side-effects this plant can have?

    Chrysosplenium axiella can cause allergic skin reactions, gastrointestinal distress, and potential liver toxicity if consumed in excessive amounts. Ingesting the botanical material may lead to nausea, stomach cramps, or diarrhea in some users. There is also limited clinical data regarding how high doses might interact with the metabolic functions of the liver. Careful monitoring is recommended for anyone attempting to use this plant for medicinal purposes.
