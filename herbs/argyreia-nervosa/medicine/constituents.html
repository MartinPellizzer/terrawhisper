10 Medicinal Constituents Of Argyreia Nervosa (Sweet Potato Vine)

Argyreia nervosa has active constituents such as flavonoids, glycosides, and alkaloids.

These constituents possess medicinal properties that include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and cardiotonic effects. The flavonoids can help improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels, thereby reducing the risk of heart disease.

Additionally, the glycosides can aid in wound healing and skin protection, improving overall well-being and quality of life.

1. Beraprost

Argyreia nervosa beraprost is a medicinal herb derived from its leaves and stems.

Beraprost is a prostaglandin analogue that serves as an endothelium-dependent vasodilator, helping to relax blood vessels and improve blood flow. It is primarily used to treat patients with peripheral artery disease (PAD) and chronic stable angina.

Additionally, beraprost has been studied for its potential benefits in reducing the risk of cardiovascular events and improving exercise tolerance.
