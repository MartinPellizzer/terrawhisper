Dahurian Angelica (Angelica dahurica) Uses

Dahurian Angelica is used to treat respiratory infections, sinusitis, and bronchitis. It helps alleviate symptoms like cough, nasal congestion, and common cold. It is also beneficial for asthma and allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

Dahurian Angelica (Angelica dahurica) Monograph

1. Respiratory infection

Angelica dahurica is used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It helps alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections by reducing inflammation and promoting mucus clearance. Its expectorant properties make it useful for coughs and bronchial congestion. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.

2. Sinusitis

Angelica dahurica is used in herbal medicine to alleviate sinusitis symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce nasal inflammation and mucus production, supporting clearer breathing. Often combined with other herbs, it is used in formulas to treat chronic or acute sinus infections. Its active compounds may help soothe sinus pain and promote drainage.

3. Asthma

Angelica dahurica may help alleviate asthma symptoms by acting as an antispasmodic and bronchodilator. It is believed to reduce bronchial inflammation and ease airway constriction. Traditionally used in Chinese medicine, it supports respiratory health when combined with other herbs in formulations. Its active compounds may enhance lung function and reduce coughing.

4. Allergic rhinitis

Angelica dahurica is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis due to its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion, sneezing, and runny nose by modulating immune responses. Often combined with other herbs, it supports long-term relief and reduces seasonal allergy flare-ups.

5. Cough

Dahurian Angelica is used to alleviate coughs by reducing inflammation in the respiratory tract and loosening mucus. It is often combined with other herbs to support expectoration and soothe irritation. Its expectorant properties make it effective for both dry and productive coughs. It is typically prepared as a tea or tincture for internal use.

6. Respiratory infections

Angelica dahurica is used to support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and easing symptoms of respiratory infections. Its expectorant properties help clear mucus, while its antimicrobial compounds may inhibit pathogen growth. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in treating conditions like bronchitis and coughs.

7. Cold symptoms

Dahurian Angelica is used to alleviate cold symptoms by promoting circulation and reducing inflammation. It helps relieve nasal congestion, sore throat, and fever associated with colds. Its antiviral and immune-boosting properties support the body's natural defenses during illness. It is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture for symptomatic relief.

8. Common cold

Angelica dahurica is used to treat common cold symptoms due to its antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce fever, congestion, and nasal inflammation. Often prepared as a decoction or tincture, it supports immune function and alleviates respiratory discomfort associated with colds.

9. Bronchitis

Angelica dahurica is used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health, particularly in treating bronchitis. It helps reduce inflammation and soothe coughing due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. The root is often prepared as a tea or tincture to ease symptoms and promote clearer breathing.

10. Nasal congestion

Dahurian Angelica is used to alleviate nasal congestion due to its decongestant properties. It helps reduce inflammation and mucus production in the nasal passages. Traditionally, it is prepared as a tea or tincture to relieve sinus pressure and improve breathing. Its active compounds support respiratory health and ease symptoms of colds and allergies.
