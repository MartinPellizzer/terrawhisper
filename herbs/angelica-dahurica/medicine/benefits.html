10 Health Benefits Of Angelica Dahurica (Baikal Angelica)

Angelica dahurica has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and relieving digestive issues due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Its bitter compounds, particularly sesquiterpenes, have been shown to soothe gastrointestinal problems and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

By alleviating these conditions, people can experience improved digestion, reduced bloating, and enhanced overall well-being, enabling them to live a more comfortable and active life.
