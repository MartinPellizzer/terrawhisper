10 Possible Side Effects Of Angelica Dahurica (Baikal Angelica)

Angelica dahurica has some side effects when used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal problems.

These side effects are often caused by overconsumption, poor quality of the herb, or interactions with other medications.

If left untreated, these side effects can worsen a person's life, leading to discomfort, anxiety, and potentially even serious health complications.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Angelica dahurica if used imporperly.

Angelica dahurica increases blood pressure.

This is due to its ability to stimulate the cardiovascular system and increase heart rate, leading to an elevation in blood pressure. Additionally, the herb's bitter compounds can cause vasoconstriction, which further contributes to the increased blood pressure.

As a result, people may experience hypertension when using Angelica dahurica, particularly if they have pre-existing blood pressure issues or are taking medications that interact with its effects.
