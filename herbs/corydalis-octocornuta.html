What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis octocornuta?

Corydalis octocorntuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is a member of the order Ranunculales and falls within the family Papaveraceae. Finally, it is specifically identified under the genus Corydalis and is designated by the species name octocornta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
