What is Roldana lineolata?

Roldana lineolata is a perennial plant native to Mexico, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

Its botanical characteristics include linear or lance-shaped leaves, measuring 5-15 cm in length, with a linear or elliptic base, and a linear or cuneate apex. The leaves are arranged oppositely on the stem and have a glabrous or hairy surface. The plant produces white or pinkish flowers in a capitulum, composed of tubular florets, with a diameter of 3-4 cm, and a corolla with five lobes.

The plant grows to a height of 50-100 cm, with a woody base and a herbaceous stem.
