      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Agrostis clavata can cause allergic reactions such as contact dermatitis, skin irritation, and respiratory distress due to its fine, irritating hairs and airborne pollen. For those with asthma, exposure to the plant may exacerbate breathing difficulties or lead to wheezing. It is important to avoid direct contact and minimize exposure in windy conditions to prevent these adverse effects.
