Possible Side Effects of pistacia lentiscus

Pistacia lentiscus can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

Some users may experience skin irritation, itching, or rashes due to the herb's volatile oils, while others may experience nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea if taken in excessive amounts.

Additionally, pistacia lentiscus may interact with blood thinners, diabetes medications, or certain antidepressants, potentially leading to adverse reactions or complications.

In severe cases, improper use of the herb can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention.
