Diospyros kaki var. silvestris

What's the taxonomical classification of Diospyros kaki var. silvestris?

Diospyros kaki var. silvestris belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this biological hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Ebenaceae and is categorized within the genus Diospyros, specifically identified as the species Diospyros kaki var. silvestris.
