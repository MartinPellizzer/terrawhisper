Humata repens

What's the taxonomical classification of Humata repens?

Humata repens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Polypodiales and the family Polypodiaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Humata and the species repens.
