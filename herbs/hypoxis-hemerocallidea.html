Hypoxis (Hypoxis hemerocallidea)

Hypoxis (Hypoxis hemerocallidea) is a member of the Asparagaceae family, native to Southern Africa, Southern Africa, and Eastern Africa. Traditionally, its rhizomes, leaves, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and european herbal medicine.
