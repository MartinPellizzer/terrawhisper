Ramps (Allium ursinum)

Ramps (Allium ursinum) is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family, native to Western Europe, Northern Europe, and Southern Scandinavia. Traditionally, its leaves, bulbs, and stems have been used for infusions, poultices, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and japanese kampo medicine.
