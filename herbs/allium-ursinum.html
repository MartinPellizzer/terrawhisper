Possible Side Effects of allium ursinum

Allium ursinum can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues like nausea, diarrhea, and stomach pain, which may be caused by consuming large quantities or taking it for extended periods.

In rare cases, it may also cause allergic reactions, including skin rashes, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, Allium ursinum may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, potentially leading to adverse effects.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Allium ursinum to minimize the risk of these side effects.
