Possible Side Effects of Pilea myriantha

Pilea myriantha can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive problems.

If ingested, the plant's sap may cause nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea in some individuals.

Additionally, prolonged exposure to the plant's volatile oils can lead to headaches and dizziness in people with sensitive respiratory systems.

In severe cases, improper use of Pilea myriantha can trigger anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention.
