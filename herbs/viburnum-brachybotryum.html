Viburnum brachybotryum

What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum brachybotryum?

Viburnum brachybotryum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Dipsacales and falls within the family Viburnaceae. Finally, it is placed in the genus Viburnum, with its specific identification being the species brachybotryum.

While specific toxicological data for this exact species is limited, most viburnum species contain compounds that may lead to nausea or vomiting if consumed.
