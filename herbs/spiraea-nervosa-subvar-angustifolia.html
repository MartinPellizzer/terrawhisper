Spiraea nervosa subvar. angustifolia

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, particularly in treating digestive issues, such as stomach ulcers and gastritis, as well as relieving symptoms of menopause and menstrual cramps.

The medicinal properties of meadowsweet have been shown to provide various health benefits, including reducing fever, alleviating headaches, and acting as a natural antacid.

Meadowsweet can be prepared as a tea, tincture, or dried herb for medicinal purposes, making it a versatile and accessible remedy for various health concerns.
