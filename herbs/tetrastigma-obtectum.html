Tetrastigma obtectum

What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma obtectum?

Tetrastigma obtectum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaeaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Tetrastigma and the specific epithet obtectum.
