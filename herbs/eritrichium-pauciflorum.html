Eritrichium pauciflorum has a low-growing, cushion-forming habit characterized by dense, compact mats of small, hairy leaves and bright blue, funnel-shaped flowers. Its flowering stems are short and emerge directly from the tightly packed basal rosette. Each flower possesses five fused petals that form a distinct corolla, typically appearing in small clusters.
