Themeda caudata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Poales and into the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Themeda, specifically identified as the species caudata.
