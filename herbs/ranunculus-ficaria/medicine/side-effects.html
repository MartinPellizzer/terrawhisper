10 Possible Side Effects Of Ranunculus Ficaria (Lesser Celandine)

Ranunculus ficaria has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal problems.

These side effects are usually caused by an overdose of the herb or an allergic reaction to its components.

For instance, severe skin irritation can lead to painful blisters and inflammation, affecting daily activities and causing discomfort.
