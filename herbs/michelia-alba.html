Michelia alba

What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia alba?

Michelia alba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Magnoliales and falls under the family Magnoliaceae. The plant is identified by the genus Michelia, and its specific scientific name is the species Michelia alba.
