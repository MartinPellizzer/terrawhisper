What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Asplenium sarelii has long, pinnate fronds characterized by lanceolate pinnae that are slightly asymmetrical and possess entire or minimally serrated margins. The texture of the foliage is typically leathery, providing a robust structure to the spreading plant body. The underside of the leaves often reveals the reproductive structures, where sori are positioned in neat rows near the margins.
