Asplenium sarelii

What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium sarelii?

Asplenium sarelii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This plant is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, specifically placed within the genus Asplenium, with its distinct designation as the species sarelii.
