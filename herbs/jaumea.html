Jaumea

Jaumea is a type of succulent plant native to the coastal regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina.

It has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.

The jaumea plant can be prepared in herbal remedies such as infusions, decoctions, and ointments to harness its medicinal properties.
