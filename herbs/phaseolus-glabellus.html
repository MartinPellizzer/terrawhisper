Phaseolus glabellus

Phaseolus glabellus, also known as the smooth bean or deer vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Americas.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antibacterial effects.

The health benefits of Phaseolus glabellus include its potential to support digestive health, lower blood pressure, and act as an antioxidant.

Herbal preparations of the plant can be made into teas, tinctures, or capsules, but more research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety for human consumption.
