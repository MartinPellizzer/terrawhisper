What's the taxonomical classification of Cardiocrinum giganteum var. giganteum?

Cardiocrium giganteum var. giganteum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Liliales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Liliaceae and the genus Cardiocrium, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species Cardiocrium giganteum var. giganteum.
