Pennisetum setosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Poales and the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Pennisetum and its designation as the species setosum.
