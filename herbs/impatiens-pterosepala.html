What's the taxonomical classification of Impatiens pterosepala?

Impatiens pterosepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Ericales. This species is a member of the family Balsaminaceae and is situated within the genus Impatiens, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Impatiens pterosepalas.
