Carex brownii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Carex, with its specific identification being the species brownii.
