Lecanthus peduncularis

Lecanthus peduncularis, also known as the Leucanthus plant, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the family of plants known as Labiatae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia and is widely used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties.

The plant is known to have anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antimicrobial properties, which can help to treat various health conditions such as fever, cough, and digestive issues.

The health benefits of Lecanthus peduncularis include reducing inflammation, improving respiratory health, and promoting digestive well-being, and it can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions for its medicinal use.
