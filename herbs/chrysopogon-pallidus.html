Chrysopogon pallidus

Chrysopogon pallidus, commonly known as Vetiver, is a tropical grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It has been traditionally used for its various health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation, improve sleep quality, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression.

The therapeutic actions of Vetiver include its sedative, calming, and anti-anxiety effects, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and sesquiterpenes.

These constituents work synergistically to produce a range of physiological effects, making Vetiver a popular herb in traditional medicine systems.
