What's the taxonomical classification of Polystichum craspedosorum?

Polystichum craspedosorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated under the subclass Polypodiinae and follows the taxonomic order of Polypodiales. The plant is further categorized under the family Polypodiaceae, specifically falling within the genus Polystichum, with its unique identification as the species craspedosorum.
