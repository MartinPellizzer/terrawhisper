Cynanchum callialatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Gentianaales. The plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae, falling within the genus Cynanchum, and is specifically identified by its species name, callialatum.
