Chile Ironweed (Bidens chilensis) Compounds

Chile Ironweed contains several key compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent effects. Coumarin and terpenoids may support anti-inflammatory actions. Glycosides, saponins, and essential oils add to its potential medicinal profile, while anthocyanins and alkaloids offer additional therapeutic benefits.
