Fissistigma latifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is a member of the family Annonaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Fissistigma, with its specific identification being the species latifolium.
