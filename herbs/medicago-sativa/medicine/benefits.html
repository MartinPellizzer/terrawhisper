10 Health Benefits Of Medicago Sativa (Alfalfa)

Medicago sativa has health benefits such as lowering cholesterol levels and reducing inflammation due to its rich content of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals.

The plant's medicinal properties include its ability to soothe digestive issues, improve skin health, and even treat diabetes.

For example, alfalfa tea can help alleviate symptoms of menopause, while the plant's isoflavones may reduce hot flashes and night sweats in women.
