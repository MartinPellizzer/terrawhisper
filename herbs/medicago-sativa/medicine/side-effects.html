10 Possible Side Effects Of Medicago Sativa (Alfalfa)

Medicago sativa has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive issues, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts or using contaminated products. For instance, people with sensitive stomachs may experience nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea, which can lead to dehydration, fatigue, and decreased productivity.

In severe cases, allergic reactions can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening condition that requires immediate medical attention.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Medicago sativa if used imporperly.

These allergenic compounds can trigger an immune response, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

As a result, individuals using Medicago sativa may experience acute or chronic kidney failure.
