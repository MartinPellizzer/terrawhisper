Synotis fulvipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, a diverse group of flowering plants, and is specifically identified under the genus Synotis. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species fulvipes.
