What's the taxonomical classification of Viola grandisepala?

Viola grandisepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is a member of the family Violaceae, which places it within the genus Viola, and is specifically identified by its species name, grandisepala.
