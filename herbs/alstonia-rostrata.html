What's the taxonomical classification of Alstonia rostrata?

Alstonia rostrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Gentianales, this plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Specifically, it is identified under the genus Alstonia, with its unique biological identity finalized as the species rostrata.
