What is Malva longiflora?

Malva longiflora is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It has 5-lobed, pink to purple flowers and 5-10 cm long, obovate leaves with serrated margins and a cuneate base, arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant grows up to 50-100 cm tall, with a stout stem and a branching habit. The leaves are gland-dotted and have a long petiole.

Malva longiflora is native to the Mediterranean region.
