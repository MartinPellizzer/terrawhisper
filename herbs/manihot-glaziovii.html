Manihot glaziovii

What's the taxonomical classification of Manihot glaziovii?

Manihot glaziovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Malpighiales, it is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae. Specifically, the plant is identified by the genus Manihot and is distinguished as the species glaziovii.

What medicinal compounds this plant contains?

These chemical constituents are primarily concentrated within the plant tissues to serve as defense mechanisms against herbivores. The presence of cyanogenic glycosides is particularly notable, as these compounds can release hydrogen cyanide when the plant tissue is damaged.
