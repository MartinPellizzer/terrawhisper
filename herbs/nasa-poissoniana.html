What is Nasa poissoniana?

Nasa poissoniana is an evergreen shrub belonging to the Loasaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves, measuring 1-3 cm in length, and white or pinkish flowers, arranged in clusters, which bloom from September to November. The plant grows to a height of 1-2 meters and a spread of 1-1.5 meters, with a woody base and branches. The leaves are opposite, ovate in shape, and have a serrated margin, with a glabrous and leathery texture.

Nasa poissoniana is native to the Andes mountains in Colombia, where it grows in subtropical forests at elevations of 2,000-3,000 meters.
