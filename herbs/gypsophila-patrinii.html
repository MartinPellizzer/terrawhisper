What's the taxonomical classification of Gypsophila patrinii?

Gypsophila patrinii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, falling specifically under the genus Gypsophila, and is identified by its unique species designation, patrinii.
