10 Possible Side Effects Of Fagopyrum Esculentum (Buckwheat)

Fagopyrum esculentum has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and inflammation.

Causes of these side effects include high consumption of its seeds, sensitization to its allergenic protein, and pre-existing conditions like celiac disease or wheat intolerance.

For example, severe digestive issues can lead to malnutrition and dehydration, while allergic reactions can cause respiratory distress and life-threatening anaphylaxis.
