Arnica Montana: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Arnica montana, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, known for its medicinal properties.

This herb contains bioactive constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and volatile oils, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Arnica montana is widely used for its health benefits, including reducing inflammation, alleviating pain, and promoting tissue repair.

Its therapeutic actions make it a popular remedy for muscle soreness, bruising, and skin conditions.
