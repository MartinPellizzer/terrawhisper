Arnica Montana Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Arnica montana, commonly known as the mountain dwarf, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Siberia.

It is renowned for its potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, making it a popular remedy for muscle pain, bruises, and joint inflammation.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and caffeic acid, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Arnica montana can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or salve, though it should not be ingested internally in large quantities due to its potential toxicity.
