What to know about Hamamelis virginiana before using it medicinally

Hamamelis virginiana, commonly known as American witch hazel, is a medicinal herb primarily used to treat conditions such as eczema, acne, and hemorrhoids, due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

The main health benefits associated with this herb include its ability to soothe and calm irritated skin, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing. The therapeutic properties of Hamamelis virginiana include its antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it a valuable remedy for various skin issues. The herb contains bioactive compounds such as tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, people often prepare a topical solution or salve with Hamamelis virginiana, which is used to treat skin irritations and promote wound healing.
