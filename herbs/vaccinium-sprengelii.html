What's the taxonomical classification of Vaccinium sprengelii?

Vaccinium sprengelii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ericales. It is a member of the family Ericaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Vaccinium, with its unique designation being the species sprengelii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These blossoms eventually develop into small, fleshy berries that serve as the plant's fruit.
