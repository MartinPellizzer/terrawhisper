This natural substance has been shown to inhibit the release of histamine and other allergic mediators in the body, thereby reducing inflammation and bronchial constriction.

As a result, ragweed extract has become a popular treatment for asthmatic patients, providing relief from symptoms such as wheezing, coughing, and shortness of breath.

Additionally, ragweed's expectorant properties help clear mucus from airways, further improving breathing difficulties.
