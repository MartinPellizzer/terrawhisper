What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus maackii?

Euonymus maackii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the order Celastrales and belongs to the family Celastraceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Euonymus and the specific species epithet maackii.
