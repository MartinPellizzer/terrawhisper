Alpine Aster (Aster alpinus)

Alpine Aster (Aster alpinus) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Alpine Regions Of Europe, and Mountainous Areas Of Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and roots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
