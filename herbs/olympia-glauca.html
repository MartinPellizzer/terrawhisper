Olympia glauca

Olympia glauca, also known as the Olympia blue-flowered or Olympia glaucous, is a perennial plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is primarily valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating digestive issues and respiratory problems.

Olympia glauca is rich in flavonoids, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, providing health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and boosting the immune system.

Olympia glauca can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, often used in herbal medicine to treat conditions like colic, bronchitis, and urinary tract infections.
