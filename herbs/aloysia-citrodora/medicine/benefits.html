10 Health Benefits Of Aloysia Citrodora (Lemon Verbena)

Aloysia citrodora has health benefits such as reducing anxiety and stress, alleviating insomnia and promoting a sense of calmness.

Its medicinal properties include its high content of essential oils, particularly citral and geraniol, which possess anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.

By incorporating lemon verbena tea into their daily routine, individuals can improve their overall well-being, enhance their mood, and even address chronic sleep disorders, leading to a more balanced and peaceful lifestyle.
