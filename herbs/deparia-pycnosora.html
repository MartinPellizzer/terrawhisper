Deparia pycnosora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following the order Polypodiales, the plant is a member of the family Aspleniaceae. Ultimately, its taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Deparia, specifically as the species pycnosora.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The sori are positioned on the underside of the pinnules, protected by relatively small, rounded indusia.
