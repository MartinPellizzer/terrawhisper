Trifoliate Coptis (Coptis trifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds of Trifoliate Coptis include alkaloids, which have antimicrobial properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent properties, while glycosides and saponins support its medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine.
