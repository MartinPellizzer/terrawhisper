10 Medicinal Constituents Of Coptis Trifolia (Golden Thread)

Coptis trifolia has active constituents such as berberine, palmatine, and coptisine.

These alkaloids possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties, making it a valuable remedy for various health issues. Berberine, in particular, has been shown to lower cholesterol levels and improve blood sugar control, while also exhibiting antibacterial activity against certain pathogens.

By utilizing these medicinal constituents, golden thread can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections, digestive problems, and even cardiovascular disease, ultimately improving the quality of life for those who utilize its therapeutic properties.
