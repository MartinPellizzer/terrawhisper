Trifoliate Coptis (Coptis trifolia) Uses

Trifoliate Coptis is used to treat gastrointestinal infections and inflammatory conditions such as gastritis and gastric ulcers. It helps reduce inflammation in the stomach and intestines, making it effective for peptic ulcers and diarrhea. Its antimicrobial properties support digestive health and alleviate gastrointestinal discomfort.

Trifoliate Coptis (Coptis trifolia) Monograph

1. Gastrointestinal infection

Trifoliate Coptis is used to treat gastrointestinal infections due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate symptoms like diarrhea and abdominal pain caused by pathogenic bacteria. Its alkaloid compounds inhibit bacterial growth in the digestive tract. It is often recommended as a natural remedy for mild to moderate gut infections.

2. Gastric ulcers

Trifoliate Coptis contains berberine, which has been shown to reduce inflammation and inhibit harmful bacteria in the stomach lining. It helps promote healing in gastric ulcers by enhancing mucosal protection and reducing acid secretion. Its traditional use in herbal medicine aligns with modern research supporting its efficacy in treating digestive tract conditions.

3. Gastritis

Trifoliate Coptis is used to treat gastritis due to its anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties. It helps reduce stomach lining irritation and combat Helicobacter pylori, a common cause of gastritis. Its alkaloids soothe inflammation and promote healing in the gastrointestinal tract. This makes it a valuable herb in traditional herbal medicine for digestive health.
