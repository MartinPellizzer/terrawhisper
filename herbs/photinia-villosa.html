Photinia villosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae, it is specifically identified within the genus Photinia, ultimately reaching its unique designation as the species villosa.
