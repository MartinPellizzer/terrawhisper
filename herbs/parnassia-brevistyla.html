Parnassia brevistyla

What's the taxonomical classification of Parnassia brevistyla?

Parnassia brevistyla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Celastrales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Celastraceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Parnassia, with the species name brevistyla.
