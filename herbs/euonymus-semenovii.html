Euonymus semenovii

What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus semenovii?

Euonymus semenovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Celastrales. It is further categorized into the family Celastraeaceae, which contains the genus Euonymus, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species semenovii.
