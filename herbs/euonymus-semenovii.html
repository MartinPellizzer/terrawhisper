What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Euonymus semenovii has opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves with entire or slightly serrated margins and a woody, deciduous shrub habit reaching heights of up to two meters. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge in clusters, often appearing in late spring or early summer.
