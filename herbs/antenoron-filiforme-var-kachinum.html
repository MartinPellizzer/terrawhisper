Antenoron filiforme var. kachinum

What's the taxonomical classification of Antenoron filiforme var. kachinum?

Antenoron filiforme var. kachinum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Caryophyllales. It is a member of the family Polygonaceae and falls under the genus Antenoron, ultimately identified by its specific botanical designation as the variety kachinum of the species Antenoron filiforme.
