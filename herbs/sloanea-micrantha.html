Possible Side Effects of Sloanea micrantha

Sloanea micrantha can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and vomiting, due to its high levels of glycosides and saponins.

Prolonged consumption may also lead to kidney damage and liver problems, as these compounds can cause oxidative stress and inflammation in the body.

Additionally, improper use of Sloanea micrantha may cause allergic reactions, such as skin irritation, respiratory issues, and anaphylaxis in severe cases.

It is essential to consult a medical professional before using this plant for medicinal purposes to avoid potential side effects.
