Argemone Mexicana Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Argemone mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican poppy or castor bean plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas, known for its bright orange flowers and toxic seeds.

This herb has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic and folk medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antidiabetic properties.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and flowers to harness its healing properties.
