What is Abacopteris liukiuensis?

Abacopteris liukiuensis is an extinct species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It has been fossilized and found in Japan, specifically on the island of Ryukyu. The plant had a rhizome and fronds with pinnae, with the frond base measuring approximately 12-15 cm in diameter. The pinnules were simple, ovate, and had entire margins, typically measuring between 2-5 mm in length.

Abacopteris liukiuensis is characterized by the presence of a distinctive rhizome with a central axis and lateral appendages.
