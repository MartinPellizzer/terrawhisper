Gout is a form of inflammatory arthritis characterized by sudden, severe attacks of pain, swelling, and redness in joints.

It commonly affects middle-aged and older adults, particularly men, and is often linked to lifestyle factors such as diet, alcohol consumption, and obesity.

The herniaria glabra plant is believed to help alleviate gout symptoms by promoting the excretion of uric acid and reducing inflammation.

7. Treats bladder infections

Bladder infection, also known as urinary tract infection (UTI), is a common bacterial infection that affects the urinary system.

It occurs when bacteria, most commonly Escherichia coli, enter the urinary tract and multiply in the bladder.

This condition is often characterized by symptoms such as painful urination, frequent urge to urinate, and a burning sensation.

Women are more prone to bladder infections due to anatomical factors, and they are more likely to experience recurrent episodes.

The herniaria glabra plant has been traditionally used to treat bladder infections by supporting urinary health and reducing inflammation.
