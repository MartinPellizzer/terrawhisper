Herniaria glabra reduces inflammation because of its rich content of flavonoids, terpenoids, and other bioactive compounds.

Its anti-inflammatory effects are also thought to contribute to wound healing and tissue repair.

Herniaria glabra lowers blood sugar because of its potential anti-diabetic properties.
