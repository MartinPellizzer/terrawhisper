Geranium linearilobum

What's the taxonomical classification of Geranium linearilobum?

Geranium linearilobum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls into the order Geraniales and the family Geraniaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Geranium and the species linearilobum.
