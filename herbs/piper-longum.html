Long Pepper (Piper longum)

Long Pepper (Piper longum) is a member of the Piperaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Nepal. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, stimulant, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and unani medicine.
