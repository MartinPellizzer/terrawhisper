10 Possible Side Effects Of Zanthoxylum Americanum (Prairie Toothpick)

Zanthoxylum americanum has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These issues are usually caused by excessive consumption, sensitivity, or allergic reactions to the herb's alkaloids and essential oils.

In severe cases, these side effects can worsen lives by causing persistent digestive issues, anaphylaxis, and skin conditions, leading to missed work or school, decreased productivity, and strained relationships.
