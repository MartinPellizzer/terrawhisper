Rhododendron calostrotum subsp. keleticum

Rhododendron calostrotum subsp.

keleticum, a lesser-known subspecies of rhododendron, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India and Nepal.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which are attributed to the presence of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

The health benefits of Rhododendron calostrotum subsp.

keleticum are said to include its potential to reduce fever, alleviate digestive issues, and provide relief from respiratory problems.

In traditional medicine, the plant is often used in herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are administered to treat various ailments.
