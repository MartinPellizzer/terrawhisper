1. Has anti-inflammatory effects

Chronic inflammation is a persistent, low-level inflammatory response that can affect various tissues and organs in the body.

This condition, also known as persistent inflammation or ongoing inflammatory activity, is often linked to long-term health issues.

It can be caused by factors such as autoimmune disorders, chronic infections, or prolonged exposure to irritants.

People with conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, inflammatory bowel disease, or asthma are commonly affected.

Pulmonaria officinalis, also known as lungwort, has been studied for its potential anti-inflammatory effects, which may help reduce the severity and frequency of such inflammatory responses.

How this herb helps with has anti-inflammatory effects?

Pulmonaria officinalis has anti-inflammatory effects because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds work by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and reducing oxidative stress in the body. Alkaloids in the plant help modulate immune responses, while flavonoids act as potent antioxidants. Together, they contribute to the plant's ability to alleviate inflammation associated with various conditions.

This makes Pulmonaria officinalis a valuable herb in traditional and complementary medicine.

Scientific Research

According to "Journal of ethnopharmacology", Pulmonaria officinalis is part of the BNO 1030 preparation, which has anti-inflammatory effects as it suppressed the secretion of IL-8 and hBD-2 in cultured epithelial A549 cells.
