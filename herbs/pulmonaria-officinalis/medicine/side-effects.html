10 Possible Side Effects Of Pulmonaria Officinalis (Lungwort)

Pulmonaria officinalis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

These can occur if the herb is not prepared correctly or if individual tolerance levels are exceeded. For example, some people may experience hives, itching, or swelling due to an allergic reaction.

This can worsen their lives by affecting their skin health and overall well-being, and may even lead to anaphylaxis in severe cases.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Pulmonaria officinalis if used imporperly.
