What is Bernardia major?

Bernardia major is a perennial shrub, native to tropical America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its glabrous stems, elliptical to lanceolate leaves, and a terminal inflorescence bearing small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces three-winged, greenish-yellow fruits, each containing three seeds. Bernardia major typically grows up to 2 meters in height, with a spread of approximately 1 meter.

The leaves are opposite, simple, and have a distinctive acuminate tip.
