Trigonella tibetana

Trigonella tibetana is a plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family, native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Tibet.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for its expectorant and antiseptic properties, which can help to relieve respiratory issues and promote wound healing.

The health benefits of Trigonella tibetana include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and support the immune system.

The plant is commonly prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, which can be consumed to harness its medicinal properties.
