Possible Side Effects of sanguinaria canadensis

Sanguinaria canadensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and vomiting due to its emetic properties.

Additionally, high doses of sanguinaria canadensis may cause dizziness, headache, and tremors in some individuals.

In severe cases, improper use of this herb can even cause respiratory failure, which is a life-threatening condition.
