Ligustrum robustum subsp. chinense

What's the taxonomical classification of Ligustrum robustum subsp. chinense?

Ligustrum robustum subsp. chinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the order Lamiales. It further belongs to the family Oleaceae and the genus Ligustrum, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species Ligustrum robustum subsp. chinense.
