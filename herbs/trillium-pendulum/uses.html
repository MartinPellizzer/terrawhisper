Pendulous Trillium (Trillium pendulum) Uses

Pendulous Trillium is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, respiratory infections, and sinusitis. It helps alleviate symptoms like cough, mucous congestion, and inflamed airways. It is also used for cold symptoms and asthma-related breathing difficulties. Its anti-inflammatory properties support mucosal health and ease respiratory congestion.
