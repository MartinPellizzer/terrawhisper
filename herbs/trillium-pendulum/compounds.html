Pendulous Trillium (Trillium pendulum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Pendulous Trillium include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to astringency. Phenolic acids and glycosides also play roles in its medicinal effects, while coumarin and terpenoids may support anti-inflammatory actions. Saponins, alkaloids, essential oils, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic potential.
