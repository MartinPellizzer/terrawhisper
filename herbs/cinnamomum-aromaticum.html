Cinnamomum Aromaticum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cinnamomum aromaticum, commonly known as star anise, is a small evergreen tree native to China, prized for its aromatic seeds used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

This herb contains bioactive constituents such as shikimic acid, lignans, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

Health benefits include supporting respiratory health, aiding digestion, and having antimicrobial effects.

Its therapeutic actions also encompass anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and expectorant properties, making it valuable in traditional medicine.
