Cinnamomum Aromaticum

The therapeutic actions of Cinnamomum aromaticum include its ability to warm the body, relieve pain, and reduce fever, making it a popular ingredient in herbal remedies for colds and flu.

Possible Side Effects of cinnamomum aromaticum
