This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which make it beneficial for treating various health conditions, including fever, pain, and digestive issues.

The health benefits of Korean prickly ash include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and boost the immune system, making it a popular herbal remedy in traditional Korean medicine.

Korean prickly ash is often prepared as a decoction, tea, or powder, and is also used in combination with other herbs in traditional medicine to enhance its effects.
