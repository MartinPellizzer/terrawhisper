What's the taxonomical classification of Selaginella davidii?

Selaginella davidii belongs to the kingdom Plantae, placing it within the diverse group of multicellular, photosynthetic organisms. It is classified under the phylum Streptophyta, which highlights its evolutionary connection to land plants, and falls under the class Equisetopsida. Within this lineage, it is organized into the order Selaginellales and the family Selaginellaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is established through the genus Selaginella, leading to its unique designation as the species Selaginella davidii.
