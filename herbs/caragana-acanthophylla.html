Caragana acanthophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Caragana acanthophylla?

Caragana acanthophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it in the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, this plant is categorized under the genus Caragana, with the specific epithet acanthophylla denoting its unique species identity.
