Indian Frankincense (Boswellia serrata) Uses

Boswellia serrata is used to treat rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, gout, and other inflammatory joint conditions. It also helps with inflammatory bowel disease, gastritis, and colitis due to its anti-inflammatory properties. Additionally, it may support respiratory health in conditions like asthma.

Indian Frankincense (Boswellia serrata) Monograph

1. Rheumatoid arthritis

Boswellia serrata has been shown to reduce inflammation and joint pain in rheumatoid arthritis by inhibiting inflammatory enzymes. Its active compounds, such as boswellic acids, help modulate immune responses and decrease swelling. Clinical studies support its use as a complementary therapy for managing symptoms in RA patients.

2. Osteoarthritis

Boswellia serrata, or Indian Frankincense, contains anti-inflammatory compounds that reduce joint inflammation and pain associated with osteoarthritis. Clinical studies suggest it may improve mobility and decrease reliance on NSAIDs. Its effects are attributed to the inhibition of enzymes involved in inflammatory responses. It is often used as a complementary therapy in herbal medicine for managing osteoarthritis symptoms.

3. Gout

Boswellia serrata, or Indian Frankincense, has shown potential in reducing inflammation and pain associated with gout. Its active compounds inhibit enzymes that contribute to inflammatory responses. Studies suggest it may help manage gout symptoms by decreasing uric acid levels and improving joint mobility. It is often used as a complementary therapy alongside conventional treatments.

4. Joint pain

Boswellia serrata, or Indian Frankincense, contains anti-inflammatory compounds that help reduce joint pain and swelling. It is commonly used for conditions like arthritis, offering natural relief by inhibiting inflammatory responses in the body. Clinical studies support its efficacy in managing chronic joint pain without the side effects of synthetic drugs.

5. Inflammatory joint conditions

Boswellia serrata contains active compounds that inhibit inflammatory pathways, making it effective for reducing joint inflammation and pain. It is commonly used for conditions like rheumatoid arthritis and osteoarthritis. Clinical studies support its role as a natural alternative to conventional anti-inflammatory drugs. Its anti-inflammatory properties help improve joint function and mobility.

6. Inflammatory bowel disease

Boswellia serrata, or Indian Frankincense, contains anti-inflammatory compounds that may help reduce intestinal inflammation in inflammatory bowel disease. Clinical studies suggest it can alleviate symptoms like pain and diarrhea, though it should be used under medical guidance alongside conventional treatments.

7. Gastritis

Boswellia serrata has shown potential in reducing inflammation associated with gastritis due to its active compounds that inhibit inflammatory pathways. Clinical studies suggest it may help alleviate symptoms like stomach pain and bloating. Its anti-inflammatory properties make it a valuable complementary therapy in herbal medicine for managing gastritis.

8. Gastrointestinal inflammation

Boswellia serrata reduces gastrointestinal inflammation by inhibiting pro-inflammatory enzymes like 5-lipoxygenase. Its active compounds, boswellic acids, suppress cytokine production, alleviating conditions like ulcerative colitis and irritable bowel syndrome. Clinical studies support its efficacy in reducing intestinal swelling and improving digestive health.

9. Colitis

Boswellia serrata has been studied for its anti-inflammatory properties, making it a valuable remedy for colitis. It helps reduce intestinal inflammation by inhibiting enzymes that contribute to digestive tract irritation. Clinical trials suggest it may alleviate symptoms such as pain and diarrhea in individuals with inflammatory bowel conditions. Its use aligns with traditional herbal medicine practices aimed at supporting digestive health.

10. Asthma

Boswellia serrata has been shown to reduce inflammation in the airways, making it beneficial for asthma management. Its active compounds inhibit enzymes that contribute to airway swelling. Clinical studies suggest it may help decrease asthma symptoms and reduce the need for conventional medications. It is often used as a complementary therapy in herbal medicine.
