10 Possible Side Effects Of Boswellia Serrata (Frankincense)

Boswellia serrata has some side effects when used improperly, such as diarrhea, stomach cramps, and nausea.

These side effects are often caused by excessive dosing, long-term use, or interacting with medications.

For instance, a person with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) may experience worsening symptoms like persistent diarrhea or abdominal pain, disrupting their daily activities and sleep patterns, and affecting their overall quality of life.
