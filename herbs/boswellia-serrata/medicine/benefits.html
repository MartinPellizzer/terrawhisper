10 Health Benefits Of Boswellia Serrata (Frankincense)

Boswellia serrata, also known as frankincense, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and anti-oxidative properties.

These medicinal properties help to reduce pain and inflammation, making it a natural remedy for conditions like arthritis and joint pain. By alleviating symptoms, individuals can enjoy improved mobility and reduced discomfort, allowing them to participate in activities they love and maintain a higher quality of life.

This, in turn, enhances their overall well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Boswellia serrata.

1. Reduces inflammation

Boswellia serrata reduces inflammation because it contains boswellic acids, which inhibit the production of inflammatory enzymes and hormones.

Specifically, frankincense's boswellic acids suppress the activity of 5-lipoxygenase, a key enzyme involved in the synthesis of pro-inflammatory leukotrienes.

This reduction in leukotriene production helps to alleviate inflammation and relieve conditions such as arthritis, asthma, and other chronic inflammatory diseases.

2. Relieves arthritis symptoms

Boswellia serrata relieves arthritis symptoms because of its potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The boswellic acids present in frankincense have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory molecules, thereby reducing joint inflammation and pain.

Additionally, frankincense has been found to increase the levels of antioxidants in the body, which helps to protect against further damage and promote healing in arthritic joints.

3. Improves joint mobility

Boswellia serrata improves joint mobility because it contains boswellic acids, which have potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These compounds help to reduce inflammation in the joints, leading to a decrease in pain and stiffness. Additionally, frankincense has been shown to increase lubrication and flexibility in the joints, allowing for smoother movement and improved range of motion.

As a result, individuals experiencing joint discomfort can enjoy increased mobility and reduced symptoms with regular use of boswellia serrata.

4. Alleviates asthma symptoms

Boswellia serrata alleviates asthma symptoms because it has potent anti-inflammatory and bronchodilatory properties.

The resin of this tree contains boswellic acids, which have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory compounds, reducing airway constriction and inflammation in the lungs.

This helps to ease symptoms such as wheezing, coughing, and shortness of breath associated with asthma, allowing individuals to breathe more easily and comfortably.

5. Supports gut health

Boswellia serrata supports gut health because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The resin's bioactive compounds, such as boswellic acids, have been shown to reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, which can alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and other gastrointestinal disorders.

By decreasing inflammation, frankincense may also improve gut barrier function and promote a healthy balance of gut microbiota.

6. Eases anxiety and stress

Boswellia serrata eases anxiety and stress because of its unique bioactive compounds that inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory chemicals in the brain.

This reduces inflammation and oxidative stress, which are often linked to anxiety and stress responses.

Additionally, boswellic acids in frankincense have been shown to interact with GABA receptors, promoting relaxation and calmness by slowing down neuronal activity, thereby alleviating symptoms of anxiety and stress.

7. Boosts oral health

Boswellia serrata boosts oral health because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Frankincense has been shown to reduce gum inflammation and prevent the formation of plaque, which can lead to tooth decay and gum disease. Additionally, its ability to inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi helps to prevent bad breath and mouth infections.

As a result, incorporating frankincense into your oral care routine may help promote healthy gums, teeth, and overall mouth wellness.

8. Enhances skin health

Boswellia serrata enhances skin health because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

It has been shown to reduce inflammation and irritation in the skin, making it an effective treatment for conditions such as acne, psoriasis, and eczema.

Additionally, frankincense's antioxidant properties help protect the skin from environmental stressors and damage caused by free radicals, promoting healthy collagen production and reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

9. Reduces risk of cancer

Boswellia serrata reduces risk of cancer because it exhibits potent anti-cancer properties.

Its boswellic acids have been shown to inhibit the growth and proliferation of cancer cells, induce apoptosis (cell death), and suppress angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation).

Additionally, frankincense has been found to modulate the immune system, enhancing its ability to recognize and attack cancerous cells, thereby reducing the risk of cancer development and progression.

10. Protects brain health

Boswellia serrata protects brain health because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

It has been shown to reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the brain, which are major contributors to neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.

Frankincense also promotes the production of neurotransmitters, such as serotonin and dopamine, which regulate mood, cognition, and memory, supporting overall brain function and well-being.
