10 Precautions To Take When Using Boswellia Serrata (Frankincense)

Boswellia serrata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional to determine the right dosage and potential interactions with other medications.

Taking more than the recommended dose can lead to side effects like dizziness, stomach upset, and headaches, while interactions with blood thinners, diabetes medications, or certain antidepressants can exacerbate conditions like bleeding or serotonin syndrome.
