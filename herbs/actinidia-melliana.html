What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia melliana?

Actinidia melliana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its biological classification, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Actinidiaceae. Ultimately, it is situated within the genus Actinidia, specifically identified by its species name, melliana.
