Potentiella griffithii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further classified under the family Rosaceae, which places it among a diverse group of flowering plants. Within this family, it is situated in the genus Potentiella, and its specific taxonomic designation is completed by its species name, griffithii.
