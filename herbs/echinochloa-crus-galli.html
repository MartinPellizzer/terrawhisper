What's the taxonomical classification of Echinochloa crus-galli?

Echinochloa crus-galli belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Poales and the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Echinochloa, and its species name, crus-galli.
