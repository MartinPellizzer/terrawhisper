Echinochloa crus-galli belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Poales and the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Echinochloa, and its species name, crus-galli.
