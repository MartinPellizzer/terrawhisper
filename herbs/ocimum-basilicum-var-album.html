Ocimum basilicum var. album

Ocimum basilicum var. album, commonly known as white basil, is a variety of sweet basil that belongs to the Lamiaceae family of plants.

The therapeutic actions of white basil include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties, which make it useful in treating various health conditions such as skin issues, wounds, and respiratory problems.
