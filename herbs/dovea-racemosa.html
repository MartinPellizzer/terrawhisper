Dovea racemosa

Dovea racemosa, commonly known as the Coral Root or Coralroot, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

This plant is used for its medicinal properties, primarily for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects, making it useful in treating various health conditions such as wounds, skin irritations, and infections.

The therapeutic actions of Dovea racemosa are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which exhibit antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties.

The bioactive constituents of Dovea racemosa may also contribute to its traditional use in treating respiratory issues, such as coughs and colds, and as a treatment for digestive problems.
