Buddleja lindleyana

What's the taxonomical classification of Buddleja lindleyana?

Buddlejia lindleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Scrophulariaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Buddleja, and its unique species designation, lindleyana.
