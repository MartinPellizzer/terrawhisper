Corydalis curviflora subsp. rosthornii

What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis curviflora subsp. rosthornii?

Corydalis curviflora subsp. rosthornii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the order Ranunculales and is a member of the Papaveraceae family. Taxonomically, this specific plant is identified by the genus Corydalis, with its precise biological designation being the species Corydalis curviflora and the subspecies rosthornii.
