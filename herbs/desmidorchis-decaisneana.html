Desmidorchis Decaisneana

Preliminary studies suggest that it may possess antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a candidate for treating infections and inflammatory conditions. However, further scientific research is needed to validate its medicinal efficacy and safety for human use.

Despite its traditional significance, Desmidorchis decaisneana remains underexplored in modern pharmacology.
