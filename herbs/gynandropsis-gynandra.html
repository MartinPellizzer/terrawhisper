African Leafy Vegetable (Gynandropsis gynandra)

African Leafy Vegetable (Gynandropsis gynandra) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to East Africa, West Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and shoots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
