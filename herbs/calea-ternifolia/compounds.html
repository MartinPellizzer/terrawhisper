Blue Mistflower (Calea ternifolia) Compounds

Blue Mistflower contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarin and glycosides are also present, offering potential benefits in supporting cardiovascular and digestive health. Tannins and terpenoids provide astringent and antimicrobial properties, while saponins and alkaloids may support immune function. Essential oils and anthocyanins add to its overall therapeutic profile.
