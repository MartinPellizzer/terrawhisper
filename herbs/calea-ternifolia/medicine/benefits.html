10 Health Benefits Of Calea Ternifolia (Jimsonweed)

Calea ternifolia has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and sedative properties that can improve people's lives.

The plant's medicinal properties, including its flavonoids and phenolic acids, help reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia.

1. Relieves anxiety

Calea ternifolia relieves anxiety because its active compounds interact with GABA receptors in the brain, promoting a sense of calm and reducing stress levels.

The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to treat anxiety-related disorders, and studies have shown that it can effectively alleviate symptoms such as restlessness, insomnia, and muscle tension.

2. Aids sleep

Calea ternifolia aids sleep because its unique compounds, such as flavonoids and terpenoids, possess sedative properties that help calm the mind and body.

4. Eases digestion

Calea ternifolia eases digestion because it has a natural ability to calm the digestive tract and reduce inflammation.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties help soothe and heal the mucous membranes in the stomach, intestines, and colon, alleviating symptoms such as bloating, cramps, and diarrhea.

5. Soothes menstrual cramps

Calea ternifolia soothes menstrual cramps because it contains a unique blend of compounds that target the root causes of dysmenorrhea.

Its flavonoids and terpenes have been shown to relax uterine muscles, reducing spasms and inflammation that can exacerbate cramping.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties help reduce prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that stimulate contractions, leading to a significant decrease in menstrual pain and discomfort.
