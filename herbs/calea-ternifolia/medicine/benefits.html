1. Relieves anxiety

Mexican dream herb relieves anxiety because it contains various compounds that may have anxiolytic effects.

One of the key components is a chemical called crisilineol, which has been shown to have anti-anxiety properties in animal studies. Additionally, Calea ternifolia may help to regulate the neurotransmitter GABA, which plays a crucial role in regulating anxiety and promoting relaxation. Furthermore, the herb has been traditionally used in Mexico for its calming and sedative effects, and some studies suggest that it may help to improve sleep quality and reduce insomnia.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the mechanisms behind Calea ternifolia's anxiety-relieving effects and to establish its safety and efficacy as a treatment for anxiety disorders.
