Geranium Latum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Geranium latum, commonly known as the broadleaf geranium, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its distinctive leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote mental clarity.

The therapeutic actions of Geranium latum are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which exhibit antioxidant and antimicrobial properties.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or poultice, making it a versatile herb for various herbal remedies.
