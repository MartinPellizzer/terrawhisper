Geranium Latum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Geranium latum, commonly known as the broad-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family, native to Europe and parts of Asia.

This herb is valued for its aromatic leaves and flowers, often used in traditional medicine for its diverse health benefits.

It is believed to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and aid in stress relief due to its therapeutic actions.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
