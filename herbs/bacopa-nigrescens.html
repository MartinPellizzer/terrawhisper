Medicinal Parts of Bacopa nigrescens

Bacopa nigrescens has many medicinal parts, such as its leaves, roots, and aerial parts.

The leaves of Bacopa nigrescens are rich in alkaloids like brahmine and amyrin, while the roots contain saponins like bacopasides and bacopaside II, and other compounds like aucubin.

The aerial parts of the plant contain flavonoids like isorhapontigenin and acacetin, which are known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Additionally, the flowers of Bacopa nigrescens may also possess medicinal properties, although less is known about their composition and effects compared to other parts of the plant.

Furthermore, it may also cause anxiety, insomnia, and an increase in heart rate in sensitive individuals.
