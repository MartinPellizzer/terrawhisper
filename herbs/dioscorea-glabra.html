Chinese Yam (Dioscorea glabra)

Chinese Yam (Dioscorea glabra) is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family, native to Asia, China, and India. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and tubers have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
