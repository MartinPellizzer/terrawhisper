Dioscorea Glabra: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Dioscorea glabra, commonly known as Chinese yam, is a medicinal herb native to Asia, valued for its nutritional and therapeutic properties.

It is a tuberous plant rich in starch, fiber, and various bioactive compounds that contribute to its health benefits.

The herb is known to support digestive health, boost immunity, and help regulate blood sugar levels.

Its therapeutic actions include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-modulating effects, largely attributed to constituents such as alkaloids, saponins, and polysaccharides.
