Seneciio oryzetorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a specific representative of the family Asteraceae, characterized by its distinctive floral structures. Within this family, it is classified under the genus Senecio, and its unique biological identity is defined by its specific epithet, oryzetorum.
