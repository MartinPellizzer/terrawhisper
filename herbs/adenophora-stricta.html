What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Adenophora stricta has an erect, perennial herbaceous habit characterized by slender, upright stems that typically reach heights of 20 to 60 centimeters. The inflorescence consists of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. These blossoms emerge from terminal clusters or axillary positions, supported by thin pedicels.
