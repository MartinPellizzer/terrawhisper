What's the taxonomical classification of Lagotis macrosiphon?

Lagotis macrosiphon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its lineage through the order Lamiales, the plant is a member of the family Plantaginaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Lagotis and the species macrosiphon.
