Sauropus spatulifolius

What's the taxonomical classification of Sauropus spatulifolius?

Sauropus spatulifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Malpighiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Phyllanthaceae, where it is placed within the genus Sauropus. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, Spatulifolius.

The plant contains phytochemicals that may contribute to its antibacterial and analgesic effects. These applications demonstrate its versatility in addressing both external and internal ailments.

Sauropus spatulifolius is prepared as a decoction, infusion, or topical paste using its leaves and stems for medicinal purposes.
