Crataegus Laevigata: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Crataegus laevigata, commonly known as the common hawthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to Europe, western Asia, and northern Africa, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is renowned for its cardiovascular benefits, helping to support heart health and improve circulation.

The therapeutic actions of Crataegus laevigata include anti-arrhythmic, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it useful in treating conditions like hypertension and heart failure.

Its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, triterpenes, and oligomeric procyanidins, which contribute to its wide range of health benefits.
