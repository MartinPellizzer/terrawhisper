Devil'S Ivy (Cissus quadrangularis) Actions

Devil's Ivy exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic properties. It acts as a stimulant and carminative, helping to relieve gas and promote digestion. Additionally, it has astringent and bitter qualities, which can aid in wound healing and appetite stimulation. Its antispasmodic and expectorant effects help ease muscle spasms and clear respiratory secretions, while also providing a mild sedative effect.
