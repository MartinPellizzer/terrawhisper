What are the possible side effect of using Cissus quadrangularis improperly?

Improper use of Cissus quadrangularis, also known as veldt grape, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

Additionally, high doses may cause interactions with medications, particularly blood thinners, and exacerbate conditions like high blood pressure and kidney disease.

Some individuals may also experience allergic reactions, such as rashes or itching, when consuming veldt grape.

What precautions to take when using Cissus quadrangularis medicinally?

Before using Cissus quadrangularis, also known as veldt grape, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a healthcare professional, as it may interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications.
