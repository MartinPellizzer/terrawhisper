10 Health Benefits Of Cissus Quadrangularis (Veldt Grape)

Cissus quadrangularis, also known as veldt grape, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and collagen-building properties.

These medicinal properties help improve joint health, reduce pain and inflammation, and promote bone density. For example, individuals suffering from osteoarthritis may experience reduced joint pain and improved mobility, allowing them to engage in daily activities with greater ease and comfort.

This can significantly improve their quality of life.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Cissus quadrangularis.

1. Speeds up wound healing

Cissus quadrangularis speeds up wound healing because it exhibits potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The plant's bioactive compounds, including flavonoids and phenolic acids, help reduce swelling and inflammation at the wound site, creating a conducive environment for tissue repair.

Additionally, its antioxidant activity neutralizes free radicals, protecting newly formed tissues from damage and promoting collagen synthesis, which is essential for wound closure and strengthening of the healing process.

2. Reduces joint pain

Cissus quadrangularis reduces joint pain because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The herb's unique compound, ursolic acid, has been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, which can exacerbate joint inflammation and pain. Additionally, its antioxidant activity helps neutralize free radicals that can damage joints and contribute to chronic pain.

As a result, Cissus quadrangularis may provide relief for individuals suffering from arthritis, osteoarthritis, and other joint-related conditions.

3. Improves bone density

Cissus quadrangularis improves bone density because of its rich composition of bioactive compounds, particularly flavonoids and glycosides.

These compounds have been shown to stimulate osteoblast activity, increasing bone formation and density. Additionally, the herb's ability to inhibit osteoclast activity reduces bone resorption, further contributing to improved bone health.

This natural mechanism of action makes Cissus quadrangularis a promising adjunctive treatment for conditions such as osteoporosis and related disorders.

4. Lowers blood sugar levels

Cissus quadrangularis lowers blood sugar levels because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's leaves and stems contain flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes that have been shown to inhibit alpha-glucosidase activity, thereby slowing down carbohydrate digestion and absorption in the small intestine.

This delay in glucose uptake into the bloodstream reduces the peak blood sugar levels after a meal, providing natural relief for individuals with impaired glucose tolerance or type 2 diabetes.

5. Reduces inflammation

Cissus quadrangularis reduces inflammation because of its potent anti-inflammatory compounds.

The plant contains flavonoids and other bioactive molecules that inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines. Additionally, it has been shown to reduce oxidative stress by scavenging free radicals, which can contribute to chronic inflammation.

These mechanisms make Cissus quadrangularis a promising natural remedy for alleviating inflammation and related diseases such as arthritis, gout, and other inflammatory conditions.

6. Boosts immune system

Cissus quadrangularis boosts immune system because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids that help to neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress.

The plant's compounds have been shown to modulate the immune response by activating Natural Killer cells and increasing cytokine production, which helps to fight off infections and diseases.

This natural immunomodulatory effect makes Cissus quadrangularis a valuable adjunctive therapy in supporting overall health and wellbeing.

7. Prevents oxidative stress

Cissus quadrangularis prevents oxidative stress because of its rich antioxidant properties.

The plant's bioactive compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, exhibit potent free radical scavenging activity, thereby neutralizing oxidized molecules and preventing the accumulation of damage-causing reactive oxygen species (ROS).

This natural protection can mitigate the effects of oxidative stress on cellular components, preserving overall health and reducing the risk of chronic diseases associated with ROS-related inflammation.

8. Supports weight loss

Cissus quadrangularis supports weight loss because it contains a unique compound called ursolic acid.

This acid has been shown to suppress appetite and increase fat burning in the body.

Additionally, Cissus quadrangularis has been found to reduce inflammation and improve insulin sensitivity, which can help to regulate blood sugar levels and prevent overeating.

9. Enhances liver function

Cissus quadrangularis enhances liver function because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's bioactive compounds, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, have been shown to protect the liver from damage caused by oxidative stress and inflammation.

This helps to improve liver detoxification, reduce oxidative damage to liver cells, and even support regeneration of damaged liver tissue, ultimately promoting overall liver health and function.

10. Reduces risk of cancer

Cissus quadrangularis reduces risk of cancer because it contains a unique combination of flavonoids and saponins that have been shown to exhibit potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help to neutralize free radicals in the body, preventing oxidative damage to DNA and cellular structures, which can lead to mutations and uncontrolled cell growth, hallmarks of cancer.

Additionally, veldt grape has been found to inhibit the growth of cancer cells and induce apoptosis (programmed cell death), thereby reducing the risk of tumor formation and progression.
