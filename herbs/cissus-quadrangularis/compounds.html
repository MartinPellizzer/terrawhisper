Devil'S Ivy (Cissus quadrangularis) Compounds

Devil's Ivy contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins provide astringent and digestive benefits. Coumarin and glycosides support circulatory health, while terpenoids and essential oils offer antimicrobial activity. Alkaloids and anthocyanins contribute to its therapeutic and protective properties.
