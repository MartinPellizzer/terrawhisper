Schefflera brevipedicellata

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Schefflera brevipedicellata has elliptical to oblong leaves that are typically dark green, coriaceous in texture, and characterized by a distinctively short pedicel. The leaf margins are generally entire and smooth, while the venation pattern is prominent on the underside. Its stems are woody and can develop a somewhat branched structure as the plant matures. The inflorescence is composed of small flowers that emerge from the leaf axils on very short stalks, which is a defining trait of the species. These botanical features distinguish it from other members of the Rubiaceae family.
