What's the taxonomical classification of Calanthe odora?

Calanthe odora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically falling under the genus Calanthe, and is identified by the species epithet odora.
