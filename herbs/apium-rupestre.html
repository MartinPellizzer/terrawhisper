Apium rupestre

Apium rupestre, also known as rock celery, is a perennial plant species belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly for its carminative and diuretic effects.

The plant is valued for its health benefits, including reducing inflammation, alleviating digestive issues, and supporting urinary function.

Apium rupestre can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures for medicinal use, although more research is needed to confirm its efficacy and potential interactions.
