Lophanthus krylovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Lophanthus, and its species name, krylovii.
