Thelasis pygmaea

What's the taxonomical classification of Thelasis pygmaea?

Thelasis pygmaea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This specific plant is a part of the Orchidaceae family, and its taxonomic classification is completed through its designation within the genus Thelasis and the specific species epithet pygmaea.

Its medicinal properties are primarily applied topically to soothe irritation and promote healing in damaged tissue.
