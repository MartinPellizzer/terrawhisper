What's the taxonomical classification of Arabis pendula?

Arabis pendula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Brassicales. This plant is a member of the family Brassicaceae, which places it within the genus Arabis, and it is specifically identified by the species name pendula.
