Dracunculus vulgaris

Dracunculus vulgaris, also known as the Dragon's Breath or Wild Dragon Arum, is a flowering plant species native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to stimulate digestion and relieve flatulence.

The root of the Dracunculus vulgaris plant has been used to treat various health issues, including indigestion, bloating, and stomach discomfort, making it a valuable herbal remedy.

The plant can be prepared into various herbal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, to harness its medicinal properties and reap its health benefits.
