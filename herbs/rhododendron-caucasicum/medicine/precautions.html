1. Avoid excessive consumption levels

When using Rhododendron caucasicum, also known as Caucasian Rhododendron, medicinally, it's important to take proper dosage carefully, as excessive consumption can lead to adverse effects on the nervous system and heart.

This is particularly crucial because the plant contains flavonoids, which, if not taken in controlled amounts, can cause anxiety, palpitations, and even hypertension. Furthermore, Rhododendron caucasicum's bitter compounds can interact with other medications, potentially leading to harmful side effects.

Therefore, it's essential to consult a qualified healthcare professional or follow established dosing guidelines to ensure safe and effective use of this herb.
