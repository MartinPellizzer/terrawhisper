What's the taxonomical classification of Pauldopia ghorta?

Pauldopia ghorta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales. Within this order, it is positioned in the family Bignoniaceae.
