Syringa pubescens subsp. patula

What's the taxonomical classification of Syringa pubescens subsp. patula?

Syringa pubescens subsp. patula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Oleaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Syringa, ultimately reaching the species level as Syringa pubescens subsp. patula.
