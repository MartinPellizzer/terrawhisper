What's the taxonomical classification of Spatholobus harmandii?

Spatholobus harmandii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fabales. The plant is a member of the family Fabaceae and is further distinguished by its placement within the genus Spatholobus, ultimately identified as the species harmandii.
