What's the taxonomical classification of Parsonsia goniostemon?

Parsonsia goniostemon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It is further categorized into the order Gentianales and the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Parsonsia, specifically identified as the species goniostemon.
