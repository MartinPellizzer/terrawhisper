Callicarpa formosana has opposite, simple leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape with serrated margins and an acute apex. Small, axillary clusters of flowers emerge from the leaf axils, eventually giving way to distinctive spherical fruit clusters. These drupes transition from green to a vibrant purple or reddish hue as they mature. The overall structure is characterized by its spreading branching pattern and dense foliage.
