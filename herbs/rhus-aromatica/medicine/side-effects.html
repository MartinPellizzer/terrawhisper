10 Possible Side Effects Of Rhus Aromatica (Fragrant Sumac)

Rhus aromatica has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and respiratory issues.

These side effects can be caused by overexposure to the herb's essential oils, skin contact with sap, or consumption of large quantities.

For instance, severe allergic reactions can lead to anaphylaxis, while respiratory issues can worsen asthma symptoms, limiting individuals' daily activities and compromising their quality of life.
