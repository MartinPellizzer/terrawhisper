10 Medicinal Preparations Of Rhus Aromatica (Fragrant Sumac)

Rhus aromatica has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures.

These preparations are useful for relieving fever, headaches, and other respiratory issues. The leaves and stems of the plant are used to make a tea that acts as a diuretic and helps to reduce inflammation. The tincture is also used to treat digestive issues and skin irritations.

Additionally, the oil extracted from the seeds is used in aromatherapy to relieve stress and anxiety.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Rhus aromatica.
