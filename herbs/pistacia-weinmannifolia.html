Pistacia weinmannifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Pistacia weinmannifolia?

Pistacia weinmannifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Sapindales and is a representative of the family Anacardiaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Pistacia, eventually reaching its specific identification as the species Pistacia weinmannifolia.
