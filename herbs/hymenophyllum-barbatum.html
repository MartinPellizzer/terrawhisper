Hymenophyllum barbatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Hymenophyllaceae and the genus Hymenophyllum, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species barbatum.
