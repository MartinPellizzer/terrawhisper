What is Urostachys meridensis?

Urostachys meridensis is a species of lycopod, belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small, perennial plant with a creeping rhizome and erect, sterile stems that bear needle-like leaves. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, 2-5 mm long, and arranged in two rows on the stem. Urostachys meridensis is dioecious, with male and female reproductive structures on separate plants, the latter bearing sporophylls with ovate to lanceolate sporangia.

The plant grows in humid, shaded areas, typically in tropical regions of South America.
