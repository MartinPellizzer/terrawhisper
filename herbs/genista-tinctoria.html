Yellow Broom (Genista tinctoria)

Yellow Broom (Genista tinctoria) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and North Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and flowers have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, bitter, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
