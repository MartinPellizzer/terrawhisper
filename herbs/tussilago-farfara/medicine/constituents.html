10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tussilago Farfara (Coltsfoot)

Tussilago farfara has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory and antitussive properties, making it a traditional remedy for respiratory issues like bronchitis, asthma, and chronic coughs. The antioxidants in coltsfoot can help soothe irritated mucous membranes, reducing inflammation and congestion. Additionally, its expectorant properties can aid in clearing mucus from the airways, improving breathing and overall lung health.

By consuming coltsfoot tea or supplements, individuals with respiratory conditions may experience improved lung function and reduced symptoms, leading to enhanced quality of life.
