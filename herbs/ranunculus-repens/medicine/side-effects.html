10 Possible Side Effects Of Ranunculus Repens (Creeping Buttercup)

Ranunculus repens has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin and mucous membrane irritation, and gastrointestinal problems like vomiting and diarrhea.

These side effects can occur if the plant is ingested in large quantities or if its sap comes into contact with sensitive areas.

For example, people with compromised skin or digestive health may experience worsened symptoms like infections or malnutrition.
