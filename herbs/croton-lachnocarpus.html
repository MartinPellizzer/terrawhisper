What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Croton lachnocarpus has woody, shrubby stems with elliptic to oblong leaves that are typically glabrous and possess entire or slightly wavy margins. The plant also produces specialized cup-shaped structures near the inflorescence, which is a defining feature of its specific epithet.
