What's the taxonomical classification of Dipteris chinensis?

Dipteris chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Gleicheniales and the family Dipteriaceae, eventually reaching the genus Dipteris. Ultimately, its specific identification is defined by its species name, chinensis.
