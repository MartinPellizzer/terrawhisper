Sambucus canadensis var. maxima, also known as American elderberry, is a large shrub or small tree belonging to the Adoxaceae family, characterized by its towering height and clusters of small white flowers that give rise to dark purple berries.

The medicinal properties of Sambucus canadensis var. maxima include its antiviral, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant compounds, which have been used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments.
