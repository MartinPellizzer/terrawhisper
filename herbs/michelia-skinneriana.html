What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia skinneriana?

Michelia skinneriana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its descent through the order Magnoliales, the plant is placed within the family Magnoliaceae. It is specifically identified under the genus Michelia, with its unique botanical designation being the species skinneriana.
