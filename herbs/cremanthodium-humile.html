Cremanthodium humile

What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium humile?

Cremanthodium humile belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asterales. It is a member of the family Asteraceae, falling under the genus Cremanthodium, and is specifically identified by the species name humile.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Cremanthodium humile has a prostrate or decumbent growth habit characterized by slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes.

Flowers are produced in small, terminal clusters and feature delicate, pale petals that contrast with the green foliage. The reproductive organs consist of small stamens and a central pistil tucked within the floral structure.

This diminutive herbaceous perennial maintains a compact profile suited for low-growing environments.
