Adiantum davidii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Adiantum davidii has delicate, fan-shaped pinnules that are dark green, oblong, and characterized by finely serrated margins. The plant features a distinctive dark, wiry, and highly branched rhizome structure that supports its light, airy foliage. Each frond is composed of multiple small leaflets attached to thin, dark stalks that give the fern a lacelike appearance. These leaflets are arranged in a complex, bipinnate pattern which creates a sense of depth and texture. The overall growth habit is spreading and graceful due to the fine texture of the individual segments.
