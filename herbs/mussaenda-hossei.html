Mussaenda hossei

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Mussaenda hossei has woody stems and large, elliptical leaves with prominent veins and entire margins. The most striking feature of this plant is its large, showy calyx lobes that are bright red or pinkish in color. These colorful structures serve to attract pollinators to the much smaller, white, tubular true flowers. The leaves are arranged oppositely along the branches and possess a glossy texture. This species typically grows as a shrub or small tree within its tropical habitat.
