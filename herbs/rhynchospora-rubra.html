Rhynchospora rubra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Poales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Rhynchospora, and its species designation, rubra.
