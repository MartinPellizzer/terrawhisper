What's the taxonomical classification of Isodon lophanthoides var. graciliflorus?

Isodon lophanthoides var. graciliflorus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the family Lamiaceae, where it is situated within the genus Isodon.
