What is Claytonia acaulis?

Claytonia acaulis is a perennial herbaceous plant with a rosette of 5-8 basal leaves, each 2-5 cm long, lanceolate, and acute.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae. The leaves have a green color, glabrous, and have a cuneate base. The plant produces a single, erect, and branched stem, 5-20 cm tall, bearing white or pale pink flowers in raceme, with 5 petals, and 10 stamens.

The plant is native to the high-altitude areas of the Andes mountain range.
