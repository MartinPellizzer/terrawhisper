Rauvolfia Root (Rauvolfia Sessilifolia)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Rauvolfia sessilifolia.

Rauvolfia root, scientifically known as Rauvolfia sessilifolia, is a medicinal herb native to the tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. This plant is renowned for its rich alkaloid content, including reserpine, which has historically been used for its potent hypotensive properties. Traditionally, Rauvolfia root has been employed in Ayurvedic and indigenous medicine to treat hypertension, anxiety, and insomnia due to its calming and sedative effects. In modern wellness practices, it is still valued for its natural ability to support cardiovascular health and promote mental tranquility, often used in herbal formulations for stress relief. One of its unique features is the presence of rare alkaloids that contribute to its efficacy, making it a significant plant in both traditional healing and contemporary herbal medicine.
