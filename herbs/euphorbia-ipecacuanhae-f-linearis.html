Possible Side Effects of Euphorbia ipecacuanhae f. linearis

Euphorbia ipecacuanhae f. linearis can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea due to its ipecac alkaloids.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption can lead to stomach ulcers, electrolyte imbalances, and muscle weakness.

In severe cases, it can cause cardiac problems, including arrhythmias and decreased blood pressure.

Additionally, individuals with pre-existing kidney or liver issues may experience exacerbated symptoms when using this plant.
