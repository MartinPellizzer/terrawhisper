Ardisia fordii

What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia fordii?

Ardisia fordii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Ardisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, foridii.

    What are the herbal preparations of Ardisia fordii?

    Ardisia fordii is prepared as a decoction or a concentrated aqueous extract by boiling the dried leaves, stems, or roots in water. This liquid preparation is frequently consumed orally to access the plant's bioactive compounds.
