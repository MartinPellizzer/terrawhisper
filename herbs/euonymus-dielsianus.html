Euonymus dielsianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls into the order Celastrales and is a member of the family Celastraceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Euonymus and its designation as the species dielsianus.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

Because of these potential risks, children and pets should be kept away from the plant to prevent accidental poisoning. Proper precautions are necessary when pruning or handling this species to avoid contact with its irritating compounds.
