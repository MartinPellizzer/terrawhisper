Anil viscosa

Anil viscosa, also known as Asarum caulescens, is a type of flowering plant belonging to the Aristolochiaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to reduce inflammation and alleviate pain.

The health benefits of Anil viscosa include its use as an anti-inflammatory and analgesic, making it useful in treating conditions such as arthritis and muscle aches.

The herbal preparations of Anil viscosa include teas and infusions, which can be made by steeping the plant's leaves and roots in hot water.
