Marsdenia longipes

What's the taxonomical classification of Marsdenia longipes?

Marsdenia longipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae, falling specifically within the genus Marsdenia, with its unique identity defined by its species name, longipes.

These flowers are often pale or creamy in hue and possess a tubular structure typical of the Apocynaceae family.
