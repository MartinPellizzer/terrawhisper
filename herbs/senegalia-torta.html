What is Senegalia torta?

Senegalia torta is a perennial shrub or small tree belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It has a height range of 1-5 meters, with a spread of 1-2 meters, and a greyish-brown bark. The leaves are bipinnate, composed of 10-20 pairs of leaflets, each measuring 5-15 mm in length, with a greyish-green color and a length of 4-10 cm. The plant produces yellow flowers in axillary racemes, 3-6 cm in length, with a diameter of 10-15 mm, from January to April.

The seeds are flat, elliptical, and 5-7 mm in length, with a brown color.
