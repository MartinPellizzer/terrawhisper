Clematis terniflora f. maximowicziana

Clematis terniflora f. maximowicziana, also known as sweet autumn clematis, is a flowering plant species in the clematis genus, characterized by its fragrant white flowers and feathery foliage.

This plant has been traditionally used in Eastern medicine, particularly in China, where it is valued for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of clematis terniflora f. maximowicziana include its potential to reduce fever, alleviate headaches and body aches, and support respiratory health, making it a popular herbal remedy for colds and flu.

In herbal preparations, clematis terniflora f. maximowicziana is often used in teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as in traditional Chinese medicine formulas, where it is combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
