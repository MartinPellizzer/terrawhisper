Oreocharis henryana

What's the taxonomical classification of Oreocharis henryana?

Oreocharis henryana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized under the order Lamiales. It is a representative of the family Gesneriaceae, falling under the genus Oreocharis, and is specifically identified by its species name, henryana.
