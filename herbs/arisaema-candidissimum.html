Arisaema candidissimum

What's the taxonomical classification of Arisaema candidissimum?

Arisaema candidissimum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Alismatales. As a member of the family Araceae, it is categorized under the genus Arisaema, with its specific designation being the species candidissimum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Arisaema candidissimum has a morphology characterized by a single, erect spathe containing a white, fleshy spadix that emerges from a deeply channeled, pale green to white spathe. These leaves are arranged in a way that creates a lush, spreading canopy around the central flowering structure. The underground portion consists of a fleshy tuber that serves as the primary nutrient storage organ. This tuberous structure allows the plant to survive dormant periods between growing seasons.
