Begonia eduledis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the taxonomic order Cucurbitalees. Within this lineage, the plant is a member of the family Begoniaceae, specifically situated in the genus Begonia, and is identified by its unique species name, edulis.
