What's the taxonomical classification of Acer davidii?

Acer davidii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its order, Sapindales, it falls into the family Sapindaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Acer, specifically identified by its unique species name, davidii.
