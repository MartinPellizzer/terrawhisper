Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba)

Cubeb Pepper (Piper cubeba) is a member of the Piperaceae family, native to Indonesia, Malaysia, and Philippines. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and bark have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
