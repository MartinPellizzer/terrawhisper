Piper Cubeba Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Piper cubeba, commonly known as cubeb pepper, is a spice derived from the dried berries of the Piper cubeba plant, native to Southeast Asia.

This herb is valued for its warming properties and has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its digestive and antimicrobial benefits.

The bioactive constituents in Piper cubeba include essential oils, alkaloids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
