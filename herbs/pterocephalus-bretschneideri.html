Pterocephalus bretschneideri

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Pterocephalus bretschneideri has lanceolate leaves that are typically 5 to 15 centimeters long and feature finely serrated margins. The foliage is characterized by a deep green color and a somewhat leathery texture. Its stems are woody and can become quite robust as the plant matures into a shrub or small tree. Small, inconspicuous flowers often emerge from the leaf axils during the growing season. The root system is well-developed to support its structural stability in diverse soil types.
