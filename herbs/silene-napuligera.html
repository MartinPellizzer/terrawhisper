Silene napuligera

What's the taxonomical classification of Silene napuligera?

Silene napuligera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Caryophyllales. It is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically falling within the genus Silene, with its unique designation being the species napuligera.
