What is Winter'S Canella (Canella Winterana)?

Winter's Canella (Canella winterana) is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of South America, known for its aromatic, medicinal leaves and bark used in traditional herbal medicine.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Winter's Canella is Canella winterana. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Liliopsida, order Gentianales, family Gentianaceae, genus Canella, and species Canella winterana. It is known as Winter's Canella in the Caribbean and as Canella de invierno in parts of South America. The synonyms for this plant include Canella acuminata and Canella glaberrima.
