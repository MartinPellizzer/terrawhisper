Cyananthus fasciculatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is part of the order Asterales and the family Campanulaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Cyananthus, and its unique species designation, fasciiculatus.
