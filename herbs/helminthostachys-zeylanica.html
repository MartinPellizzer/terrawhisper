What's the taxonomical classification of Helminthostachys zeylanica?

Helminthostachys zeylanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Ophioglossidae. Following its placement in the order Ophioglossales, the plant is further organized into the family Ophioglossaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Helminthostachys, and its unique species designation, zeylanica.
