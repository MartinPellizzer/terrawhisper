Agathosma Serratifolia Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Agathosma serratifolia, commonly known as the Cape golden bush, is a medicinal plant native to South Africa, valued for its aromatic properties and traditional use in herbal medicine.

This herb is known for its ability to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and promote mental clarity.

The bioactive constituents in Agathosma serratifolia include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it a versatile herb for natural health support.
