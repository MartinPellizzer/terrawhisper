What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron chrysodoron?

Rhododendron chrysodoron belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Ericaceae, falling under the genus Rhododendron, and is specifically identified by its species name, chrysodoron.
