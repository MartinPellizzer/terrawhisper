What is Eulophia buettneri?

Eulophia buettneri is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

The inflorescence is a raceme that can grow up to 60 cm tall, bearing 10-20 flowers with a white and yellow coloration, and a lip that is 2-3 cm long and 1-2 cm wide.
