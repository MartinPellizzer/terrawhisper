What's the taxonomical classification of Pennisetum alopecuroides?

Pennisetum alopecuroides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Pennisetum and its designation as the species alopecuroides.
