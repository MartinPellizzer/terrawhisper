Tetradium calcicola

What's the taxonomical classification of Tetradium calcicola?

Tetradium calcicola belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the taxonomic order Sapindales. It is further classified within the family Rutaceae, specifically situated in the genus Tetradium, with its unique identification as the species calcicola.
