Liparis macrantha var. sootenzanensis

Liparis macrantha var. sootenzanensis, also known as Japanese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan, characterized by its vibrant white flowers with a long spur.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, making it a potential remedy for various health issues.
