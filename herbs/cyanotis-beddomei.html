What is Cyanotis beddomei?

Cyanotis beddomei is a species of flowering plant in the family Commelinaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb with prostrate to ascending stems, 10-30 cm long, and leaves that are 3-10 cm long, linear to lanceolate, and often have a reddish tint. The inflorescence is a terminal raceme, 3-10 cm long, bearing 3-5 blue flowers with a white or pinkish mid-vein and 6 stamens. The fruit is a 3-locular capsule, 6-8 mm long, containing 3-6 seeds.

This species is native to the Western Ghats in India and is commonly found in moist, shaded areas.
