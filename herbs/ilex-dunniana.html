Ilex dunniana

What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex dunniana?

Ilex dunniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Aquifoliales and the family Aquifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Ilex, specifically identifying as the species dunniana.

    What side-effects this plant can have?

The plant contains secondary metabolites that may trigger redness or itching upon direct contact with sensitive skin. Inhalation of dust or pollen from the foliage might also induce respiratory discomfort in some individuals. There is currently limited clinical data regarding the long-term toxicological effects of this specific species on humans. Users should exercise caution and avoid direct exposure to prevent these potential adverse reactions.
