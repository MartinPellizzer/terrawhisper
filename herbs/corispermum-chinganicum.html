What's the taxonomical classification of Corispermum chinganicum?

Corispermum chinganicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant further falls into the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Amaranthaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its membership in the genus Corispermum, with its unique designation being the species chinganicum.
