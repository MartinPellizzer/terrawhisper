What's the taxonomical classification of Rubia manjith?

Rubia manjith belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the Rubiaceae family, falling under the genus Rubia, and is specifically identified by its species name, manjith.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Rubia manjith has a perennial climbing or creeping habit characterized by slender, woody stems that grow from a thick, fleshy, and branched rhizomatous root system. Small, tubular, and inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in dense, terminal clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy drupes that transition from green to a deep red color upon ripening.
