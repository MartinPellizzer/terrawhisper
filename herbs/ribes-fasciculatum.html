What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes fasciculatum?

Ribes fasciculatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Saxifragales and the family Grossulariaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by the genus Ribes and is specifically designated as the species fasciculatum.
