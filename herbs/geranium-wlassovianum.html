What's the taxonomical classification of Geranium wlassovianum?

Geranium wlassovianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Geraniales and the family Geraniaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Geranium, and its specific species designation, wlassovianum.
