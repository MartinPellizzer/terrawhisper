Picea nigra, commonly known as Black Spruce, is a species of evergreen coniferous tree native to northern Europe.

The essential oil extracted from Black Spruce has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help to reduce stress and anxiety while promoting relaxation and improving respiratory issues.

The bioactive constituents of Black Spruce oil include alpha-pinene, beta-pinene, and delta-3-carene, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, such as expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties.

The therapeutic actions of Black Spruce oil include its ability to relieve congestion, reduce muscle and joint pain, and promote wound healing.
