Tragopogon Pratensis

Tragopogon pratensis, commonly known as Salsify or Meadow Salsify, is a perennial herb belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) that grows in temperate regions of the world.

The plant's root and leaves have been traditionally used for their medicinal and culinary properties, providing various health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving digestive issues.

The therapeutic actions of Tragopogon pratensis include acting as a demulcent, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant, helping to soothe irritated tissues and clear congestion.

The bioactive constituents of the plant include saponins, flavonoids, and inulin, which contribute to its medicinal properties and potential health benefits.
