10 Health Benefits Of Citrus × Limon (Lemon)

Citrus × limon has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and boosting immunity due to its rich concentration of flavonoids and limonoids.

These medicinal properties can help alleviate symptoms of colds and flu, while also supporting cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol levels and blood pressure.

Additionally, lemons' antibacterial and antifungal properties make it effective in wound care and skincare, allowing individuals to naturally combat acne and other skin issues, leading to a more radiant and confident appearance.
