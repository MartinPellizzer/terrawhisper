What's the taxonomical classification of Cleisostoma rostratum?

Cleisostoma rostratum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically falling under the genus Cleisostoma, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name rostratum.
