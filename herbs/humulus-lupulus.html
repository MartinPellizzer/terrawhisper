Humulus Lupulus: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Humulus lupulus, commonly known as hops, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America, widely used for its aromatic and medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its calming effects and is often used in traditional medicine to support relaxation and sleep.

Health benefits of hops include reducing anxiety, improving sleep quality, and having anti-inflammatory properties.

Therapeutic actions of humulus lupulus include sedative, antispasmodic, and diuretic effects, while its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, polyphenols, and lupulins.
