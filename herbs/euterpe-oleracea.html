Acai Berry (Euterpe oleracea)

Acai Berry (Euterpe oleracea) is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to Amazon Basin, South America, Brazil, and Amazon Rainforest. Traditionally, its fruits, seeds, and leaves have been used for infusions, decoctions, and culinary uses.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and african traditional medicine.
