The rauvolfia vomitoria plant has been studied for its potential to lower blood pressure due to its bioactive compounds that may help relax blood vessels and reduce strain on the cardiovascular system.

As a result, Rauvolfia vomitoria has been used traditionally and is now studied for its potential in managing hypertension.
