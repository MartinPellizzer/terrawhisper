10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rauvolfia Vomitoria (Devil'S Breath)

Rauvolfia vomitoria has active constituents such as alkaloids like reserpine, ajmaline, and ajmaline, which possess anti-hypertensive, anti-arrhythmic, and anti-epileptic properties.

These properties enable the plant to help manage hypertension, arrhythmias, and epilepsy, thereby improving the quality of life for individuals affected by these conditions.

By controlling blood pressure and heart rate, individuals can reduce the risk of cardiovascular complications and enjoy a more stable and healthy life.

2. Serpentinine

3. Reserpine

Rauvolfia vomitoria reserpine is a alkaloid extracted from the roots of the plant Rauvolfia serpentina.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat various ailments such as anxiety, insomnia, and hypertension.

Rauvolfia vomitoria ajmalicine is a bioactive alkaloid extracted from the plant.

It is a potent cardiovascular agent, exhibiting both α-adrenergic blocking and calcium channel-blocking properties. Ajmalicine has been found to effectively lower blood pressure by reducing peripheral resistance and cardiac output, making it a potential natural remedy for hypertension treatment.
