10 Precautions To Take When Using Rauvolfia Vomitoria (Devil'S Breath)

Rauvolfia vomitoria has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood pressure and heart rate closely.

It's essential to be aware of these precautions to avoid side effects like hypotension, dizziness, and cardiac issues. For instance, if left unmonitored, excessive consumption can lead to severe drops in blood pressure, resulting in fainting or even cardiac arrest.

Therefore, proper dosage and regular medical check-ups are crucial when using Rauvolfia vomitoria for medicinal purposes.
