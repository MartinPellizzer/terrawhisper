What to know about Rauvolfia serpentina before using it medicinally

Rauvolfia serpentina, commonly known as sarpagandha, is a medicinal herb used to treat various health conditions, including hypertension, anxiety disorders, insomnia, and epilepsy.

The main health benefits of this herb include its ability to lower blood pressure, reduce stress and anxiety levels, and improve sleep quality. The herb's therapeutic properties are attributed to its sedative, anticonvulsant, and anti-hypertensive properties, which make it a valuable remedy for managing stress-related disorders. The bioactive compounds present in Rauvolfia serpentina include alkaloids such as reserpine, ajmaline, and yohimbine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Indian medicine, the herb is often prepared as a decoction or powder, and used to make various herbal preparations, including sarpagandha churna, a popular herbal powder used to treat anxiety and insomnia.
