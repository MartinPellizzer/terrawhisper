Indian Snakeroot (Rauvolfia serpentina)

Indian Snakeroot (Rauvolfia serpentina) is a member of the Rubiaceae family, native to India, Southeast Asia, and Indo-Malayan Region. Traditionally, its roots, bark, and rhizomes have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its sedative, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and european herbal medicine.
