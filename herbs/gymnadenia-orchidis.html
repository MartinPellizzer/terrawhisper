Gymnadenia orchidis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is placed in the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is defined by the genus Gymnadenia and the specific species epithet orchidis.
