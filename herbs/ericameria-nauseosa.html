What is Ericameria nauseosa?

Ericameria nauseosa is a shrub or subshrub that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It has a height of 1-3 feet and a spread of 1-2 feet, with gray or gray-brown branches. The leaves are linear or lance-shaped, 1-3 inches long, and 1/8 inch wide, with a glandular margin and a hairy underside. The inflorescence is a panicle or a corymb, bearing 10-20 flower heads that are 1/2 inch in diameter, each containing 20-30 florets with 5-7 yellow or yellow-brown ray florets and 20-30 yellow disc florets.

The fruit is a cypsela, 1/8 inch long, with a pappus of 40-50 bristles.
