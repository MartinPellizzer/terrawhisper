Cumin (Cuminum cyminum) Actions

Cumin exhibits several therapeutic actions including carminative, which aids in expelling gas from the digestive tract. It acts as an antispasmodic to relieve muscle spasms and has anti-inflammatory properties to reduce swelling. Additionally, it functions as an expectorant, stimulant, diuretic, tonic, bitter, astringent, and sedative, supporting various bodily functions and conditions.
