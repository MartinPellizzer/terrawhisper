Clausena emarginata

What's the taxonomical classification of Clausena emarginata?

Clausena emarginata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Sapindales. It is further categorized under the family Rutaceae, specifically falling into the genus Clausena, and is identified by its unique species name, emarginata.
