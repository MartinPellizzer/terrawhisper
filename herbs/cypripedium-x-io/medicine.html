What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Cypripedium × Io (Lady'S Slipper)?

Cypripedium × io, also known as lady's slipper, has health benefits such as treating respiratory and urinary tract issues.

The herb contains alkaloids, including cypripedium alkaloids, and phenolic compounds that contribute to its medicinal properties. The roots and rhizomes are typically used to prepare teas, infusions, and tinctures. However, excessive consumption may cause dizziness and gastrointestinal upset, so moderate use is recommended.

It is also essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Cypripedium × io, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding individuals.

What are the possible side effect of using Cypripedium × io improperly?

Improper use of Cypripedium × io increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as dizziness, headaches, and gastrointestinal issues like nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Long-term misuse can lead to kidney damage, increased blood pressure, and altered heart rhythms. In extreme cases, it may cause allergic reactions, respiratory distress, and even cardiovascular complications.

Additionally, improper use has been linked to impaired cognitive function, mood disturbances, and sexual dysfunction.
