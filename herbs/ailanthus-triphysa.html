Ailanthus triphysa

Ailanthus triphysa, also known as the Chinese tree of heaven, is a deciduous tree native to China and widely cultivated for its medicinal properties and ornamental value.

The plant contains compounds such as ailanthone and ailanthin, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The medicinal properties of Ailanthus triphysa are said to have several health benefits, including reducing fever, alleviating respiratory issues, and supporting liver function.

Ailanthus triphysa can be prepared as an herbal tea, infusion, or tincture, and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote overall well-being.
