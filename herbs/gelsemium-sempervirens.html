Gelsemium Sempervirens Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Gelsemium sempervirens, commonly known as yellow jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to the southeastern United States, valued for its ornamental flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its calming effects and is known to support nervous system health.

The bioactive constituents in Gelsemium sempervirens include alkaloids such as gelsemine and gelsemium, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including muscle relaxation and anxiety reduction.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from its dried flowers and leaves for various wellness purposes.
