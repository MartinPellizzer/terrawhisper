Fritillaria thunbergii var. chekiangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Fritillaria thunbergii var. chekiangensis?

Fritillaria thunbergii var. chekiangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Liliales and falls into the family Liliaceae. Specifically, it is a member of the genus Fritillaria, categorized under the species name Fritillaria.
