Wild Yam (Dioscorea villosa) Compounds

Wild Yam contains several key compounds, including saponins, glycosides, and flavonoids. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Other notable components include phenolic acids, tannins, and alkaloids, which support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
