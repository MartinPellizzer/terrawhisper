10 Possible Side Effects Of Zingiber Officinale (Ginger)

Zingiber officinale has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts of ginger or taking it in supplement form, especially for those with pre-existing conditions like stomach ulcers or acid reflux.

In severe cases, these side effects can lead to dehydration, electrolyte imbalances, and even hospitalization, causing significant disruptions to daily life and affecting work, social interactions, and overall well-being.
