Maesa argentea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Ericales and is a member of the family Primulaceae.
