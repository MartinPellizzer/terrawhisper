Junellia clavata

Junellia clavata, commonly known as Clavito, is a plant species belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

The therapeutic actions of Junellia clavata are attributed to its ability to modulate the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant activity.

Additionally, it may cause an imbalance in the body's potassium levels, leading to muscle weakness and fatigue.

It is crucial to consult a healthcare professional before using Junellia clavata for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
