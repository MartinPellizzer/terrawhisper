What is Hackelia intermedia?

Hackelia intermedia is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America, belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It grows to be 1-3 meters tall with an upright stem, bearing lanceolate, gray-green leaves that are 5-10 cm long and 1-2 cm wide. The inflorescence is a terminal spike of small, blue-purple flowers, which are 6-8 mm long and have five petals. The flowers are borne on pedicels that are 0.5-1 cm long, and are arranged in a whorled pattern.

The plant's roots are taprooted, and its leaves are opposite and decussate.
