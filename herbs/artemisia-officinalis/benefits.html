11 Best Artemisia Officinalis Benefits

1. Cures malaria

Artemisia officinalis cures malaria because it contains bioactive constituents such as artemisinin, quercetin, and flavonoids, which exhibit potent antimalarial properties.

These compounds work by disrupting the life cycle of the Plasmodium parasite, which causes malaria.

This health benefit also helps relieve related ailments such as fever, chills, and fatigue commonly associated with malaria infections.

Additionally, the plant's compounds may support immune function and reduce inflammation in individuals suffering from these conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine to support digestive health and combat parasitic infestations.
