What's the taxonomical classification of Urophysa henryi?

Urophysa henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the taxonomic order Ranunculales. This plant is a specific representative of the family Ranunculaceae, situated within the genus Urophysa, and is ultimately identified by its unique species name, henryi.
