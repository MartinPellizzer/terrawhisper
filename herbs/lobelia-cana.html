The therapeutic actions of Lobelia cana include relieving respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as reducing pain and inflammation.

The bioactive constituents responsible for these properties include alkaloids like lobeline, lobelanine, and lobelanidine, which have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory and spasmolytic activities.
