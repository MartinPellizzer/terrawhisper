Kari Leaf (Murraya koenigii) Compounds

The primary active compounds in Kari Leaf include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and digestive properties. Essential oils and terpenoids provide antimicrobial benefits, while coumarin and glycosides support various therapeutic actions.
