Orostachys minuta

What's the taxonomical classification of Orostachys minuta?

Orostachys minuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Saxifrages, it falls under the family Crassulaceae. The taxonomic hierarchy concludes with its placement in the genus Orostachys, specifically identified as the species minuta.
