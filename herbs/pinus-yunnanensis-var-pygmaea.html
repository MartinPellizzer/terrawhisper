Pinus yunnanensis var. pygmaea

What's the taxonomical classification of Pinus yunnanensis var. pygmaea?

Pinus yunnanensis var. pygmaea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Pinales. The plant is a member of the family Pinaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Pinus. Ultimately, its complete taxonomic designation is Pinus yunnanensis var. pygmaea, representing a distinct variety within its species.
