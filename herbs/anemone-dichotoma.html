Anemone dichotoma

What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone dichotoma?

Anemone dichotoma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated under the order Ranunculales. The plant is further categorized into the family Ranunculaceae, falling under the genus Anemone, and is ultimately identified by its specific species name, dichotomica.
