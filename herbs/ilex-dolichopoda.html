Ilex dolichopoda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving further down the lineage, it falls under the order Aquifoliales and the family Aquifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Ilex, and its specific species designation, dolichopoda.
