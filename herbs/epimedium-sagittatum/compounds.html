Fairy Bells (Epimedium sagittatum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Fairy Bells include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and glycosides, known for their cardiovascular benefits. Saponins and terpenoids contribute to immune support and anti-inflammatory effects. Phenolic acids and coumarins offer protective benefits, while alkaloids and tannins provide antimicrobial properties. Essential oils and anthocyanins add to the plant's diverse therapeutic potential.
