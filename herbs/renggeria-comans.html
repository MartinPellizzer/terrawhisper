What is Renggeria comans?

Renggeria comans is a species of flowering plant in the family Clusiaceae, native to South America.

It is an evergreen shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 3 meters in height, with a smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape, 5-15 cm long and 2-6 cm wide. The leaves are glabrous, with a serrated margin and a petiole up to 2.5 cm long. The flowers are small, greenish-yellow in color and borne in clusters at the branch tips, with four petals and a prominent calyx.

The fruit is a capsule, 2-3 cm long, containing four seeds.
