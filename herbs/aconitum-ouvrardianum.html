What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum ouvrardianum?

Aconitum ouvrardianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, following the evolutionary lineage of the order Ranunculales. As a member of the family Ranunculaceae, this plant is further categorized under the genus Aconitum, with its specific identity defined by the species name ouvrardianum.
