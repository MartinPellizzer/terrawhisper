Glaux maritima

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Glaux maritima has succulent, fleshy leaves that are oval to lanceolate in shape and a creeping or ascending stem structure. The foliage is typically a bright green color and possesses a smooth, somewhat waxy surface texture. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils, often appearing in clusters. These flowers are characterized by their tiny size and lack of prominent petals. The plant also develops small, fleshy fruits that contain several tiny seeds.
