What's the taxonomical classification of Pteris actiniopteroides?

Pteris actiniopteroides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This fern is a member of the family Pteridaceae, falling under the genus Pteris, and is specifically identified by its species name, actiniopteroides.
