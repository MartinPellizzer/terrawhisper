Calpocalyx dinklagei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Fabaceae, which places it among the legumes. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Calpocalyx, with its specific designation being the species dinklagei.
