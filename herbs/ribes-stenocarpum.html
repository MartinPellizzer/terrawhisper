What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes stenocarpum?

Ribes stenocarpum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This species is further categorized under the order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Grossulariaceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Ribes, with its specific designation being the species stenocarpum.
