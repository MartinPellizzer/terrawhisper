Lysimachia deltoidea

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysimachia deltoidea has lanceolate to deltoid leaves that are arranged oppositely along a slender, upright stem. The foliage typically exhibits a smooth margin and a bright green coloration that can vary in intensity depending on light exposure. Its flowers are small and yellow, emerging from the leaf axils in a terminal or axillary fashion. Each bloom consists of five distinct petals that form a star-like shape when fully open. The plant's overall structure is herbaceous and relatively delicate, often growing in moist or shaded environments.
