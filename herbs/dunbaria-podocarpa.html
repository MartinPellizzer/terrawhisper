What's the taxonomical classification of Dunbaria podocarpa?

Dunbaria podocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Fabales, it is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Dunbaria, and its species name, podocarpa.
