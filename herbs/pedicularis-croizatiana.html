Pedicularis croizatiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis croizatiana?

Pedicularis croizatiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Lamiales and the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Pedicularis, and its specific species designation, croizatiana.
