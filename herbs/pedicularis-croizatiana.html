What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Pedicularis croizatiana has a perennial life cycle characterized by upright, leafy stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, terminal spike composed of zygomorphic flowers with a distinctive tubular corolla. The petals are often brightly colored, ranging from pink to reddish hues, and are structured to facilitate pollination by specific insects.
