What's the taxonomical classification of Ficus ischnopoda?

Ficus ischnopoda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Rosales and falls within the family Moraceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Ficus, with its specific designation being the species ischnopoda.
