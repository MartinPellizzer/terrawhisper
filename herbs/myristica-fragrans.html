Nutmeg (Myristica fragrans)

Nutmeg (Myristica fragrans) is a member of the Myristicaceae family, native to Malesia, Indo-Malaya, and Southeast Asia. Traditionally, its fruits, seeds, and resin have been used for powders, oil infusions, and decoctions.

This herb is particularly valued for its carminative, tonic, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and european herbal medicine.
