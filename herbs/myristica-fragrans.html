Myristica Fragrans Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Myristica fragrans, commonly known as nutmeg, is a spice derived from the seed of the tropical evergreen tree *Myristica fragrans*.

It is widely used in both culinary and medicinal contexts for its aromatic properties and potential health benefits.
