What's the taxonomical classification of Schizophragma hypoglaucum?

Schizophragma hypoglaucum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Hydrangeaceae, which defines its closer botanical relationships. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Schizophragma, with its specific designation being the species hypoglaucum.
