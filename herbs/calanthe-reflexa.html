What's the taxonomical classification of Calanthe reflexa?

Calanthe reflexa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Calanthe, and its unique species designation, reflexa.
