Cynanchum thesioides

What's the taxonomical classification of Cynanchum thesioides?

Cynanchum thesioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Gentianaales. It is a member of the family Apocynaceae, which places it within the genus Cynanchum, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Cynanchum thesioides.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including skin diseases, wounds, fever, and inflammatory conditions. It is frequently applied topically to manage dermatological issues such as eczema or small skin infections. Traditional medicinal practices also utilize the plant to help reduce body temperature during febrile episodes. Some preparations are believed to aid in the healing process of physical injuries and open sores. The plant's therapeutic properties are often leveraged in folk medicine to address general bodily discomfort and inflammation.
