Bothriospermum secundum

What's the taxonomical classification of Bothriospermum secundum?

Bothriospermum secundum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Boraginales and is a member of the family Boraginaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Bothriospermum, and its species designation, secundum.

Always avoid direct contact or consumption to prevent unpredictable adverse reactions.
