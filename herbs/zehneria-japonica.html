What's the taxonomical classification of Zehneria japonica?

Zehneria japonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Cucurbitales. It is a member of the family Cucurbitaceae, which places it within the genus Zehneria, ultimately identifying it as the specific species japonica.
