Caulokaempferiia coenobialis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Specifically, this plant is categorized within the genus Caulokaempferia, with its unique identity defined by its specific designation as the species coenobialis.
