What's the taxonomical classification of Schisandra henryi subsp. yunnanensis?

Schisandra henryi subsp. yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, its lineage follows the order Austrobaileyales, leading to its placement in the family Schisandraceae. Specifically, this plant is classified under the genus Schisandra, with its precise taxonomic designation being the species Schisandra henryi and the subspecies yunnanensis.
