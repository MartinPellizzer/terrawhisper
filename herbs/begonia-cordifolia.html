Begonia cordifolia

Begonia cordifolia, commonly known as the Heart-Leaf Begonia, is a species of flowering plant in the Begoniaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Begonia cordifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which exhibit therapeutic actions such as wound healing, antiseptic, and anti-anxiety properties.
