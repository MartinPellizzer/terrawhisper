Hippophae rhamnoides, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a medicinal herb used to treat various health conditions, including gastrointestinal disorders, respiratory issues, and skin conditions such as acne and wounds.

In traditional medicine, Hippophae rhamnoides is often used to make a juice or oil, which is used topically to treat skin conditions and taken orally to support overall health and well-being.
