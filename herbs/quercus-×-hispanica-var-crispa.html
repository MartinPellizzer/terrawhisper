Quercus × hispanica var. crispa

Quercus × hispanica var. crispa is a hybrid oak plant, a cross between the English oak (Quercus robur) and the sessile oak (Quercus petraea), known for its unique, curled or twisted leaves.

The medicinal properties of Quercus × hispanica var. crispa include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.

The health benefits of this plant include reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and supporting the immune system, as well as exhibiting potential anti-cancer properties.
