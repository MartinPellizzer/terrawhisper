What's the taxonomical classification of Clerodendrum hainanense?

Clerodendrum hainanense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is further categorized into the family Lamiaceae and falls within the genus Clerodendrum, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Clerodendrum hainanense.
