Salvia scapiformis var. hirsuta

What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia scapiformis var. hirsuta?

Salvia scapiformis var. hirsuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a representative of the family Lamiaceae. Within this family, the plant is organized under the genus Salvia, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety hirsuta of the species Salvia scapiformis.
