What's the taxonomical classification of Neottia puberula var. puberula?

Neottia puberula var. puberula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Orchidaceae.
