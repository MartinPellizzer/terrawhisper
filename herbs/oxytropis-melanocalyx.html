What's the taxonomical classification of Oxytropis melanocalyx?

Oxytropis melanocalyx belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Fabales. It is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is a large group of flowering plants commonly known as legumes. Within this family, it is specifically identified under the genus Oxytropis, ultimately reaching its unique biological designation at the species level as Oxytropis melanocalyx.

Because of these dangerous properties, the plant should be strictly avoided to prevent accidental toxicity.
