Ferulago galbanifera

Ferulago galbanifera, also known as Galbanum, is a perennial plant that belongs to the carrot family and is native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its essential oil, which has been traditionally used to treat respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs, as well as skin conditions like acne and wounds.

The health benefits of Ferulago galbanifera include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help to reduce inflammation and prevent infections.
