What's the taxonomical classification of Stephania cephalantha?

Stephania cephalantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales, placing it within the family Menispermaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Stephania, and its unique species designation, cephalantha.
