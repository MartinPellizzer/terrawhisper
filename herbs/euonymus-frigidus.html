What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus frigidus?

Euonymus frigidus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Celastrales and is a member of the family Celastraceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic placement is defined by the genus Euonymus and the species frigidus.
