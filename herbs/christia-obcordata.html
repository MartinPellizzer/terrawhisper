Christia obcordata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Fabales. This species is a member of the family Fabaceae and is specifically identified under the genus Christia, with its unique designation being the species obcordata.
