What's the taxonomical classification of Smilax discotis?

Smilax discotis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Liliales and is a member of the family Smilacaceae. At the most specific levels of its taxonomic identity, the plant is defined by the genus Smilax and the species discotis.

During the flowering stage, the inflorescences emerge from the leaf axils to produce small berries that eventually ripen into a dark color.
