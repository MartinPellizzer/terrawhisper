Asparagus trichophyllus

What's the taxonomical classification of Asparagus trichophyllus?

Asparagus trichophyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Asparagaceae. Ultimately, its biological identity is defined by the genus Asparagus and the specific species trichophyllus.
