Ficus mollis var. albipila

Ficus mollis var. albipila, also known as the white-stemmed banyan or white-stemmed ficus, is a variety of the Ficus mollis tree, characterized by its distinctive white or light-colored stems.

Traditionally, the roots, leaves, and stems of Ficus mollis var. albipila have been used in herbal medicine for their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Ficus mollis var. albipila are attributed to its ability to reduce fever, alleviate pain, and aid in wound healing, making it a valuable remedy for various health issues.
