Valeriana gilgiana

Valeriana gilgiana, commonly known as the Himalayan valerian or Chinese valerian, is a species of flowering plant in the valerian family.

The therapeutic actions of Valeriana gilgiana include sedative, anxiolytic, and spasmolytic effects, which contribute to its use in traditional medicine for treating restlessness and muscle spasms.
