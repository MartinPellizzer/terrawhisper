Cordyline splendens

Cordyline splendens, also known as the Red Dracaena or Red Cabbage Palm, is a tropical plant species native to Australia and New Caledonia, characterized by its striking red or purple leaves and slender stems.

Traditionally, the plant's rhizome has been used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties.

The health benefits of Cordyline splendens include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve wound healing, and support immune function.

Herbal preparations of Cordyline splendens are often made by drying and processing the rhizome, which can be consumed as a tea, capsule, or added to topical creams and ointments.
