10 Possible Side Effects Of Myristica Fragrans (Nutmeg)

Myristica fragrans has some side effects when used improperly, such as headaches, dizziness, and nausea.

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption of the herb's volatile oil, myristicin. In severe cases, myristicin can cause hallucinations and seizures.

For individuals with pre-existing medical conditions, these side effects can worsen their condition and impact their daily lives significantly, making it challenging to maintain work or social routines.
