1. Fights oxidative stress

Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and the body's ability to neutralize them, leading to cellular damage.

This health issue, also known as oxidative damage or reactive oxygen species overload, occurs when there is an excess of harmful molecules that the body's antioxidant defenses cannot fully counteract.

It is commonly experienced by individuals exposed to environmental pollutants, smokers, and those with chronic diseases such as diabetes or cardiovascular conditions.

The myristica fragrans plant, also known as nutmeg, has been shown to combat oxidative stress due to its rich content of antioxidants that help neutralize free radicals and protect cells from damage.

How this herb helps with fights oxidative stress?

Myristica fragrans fights oxidative stress because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which act as potent antioxidants.

These compounds neutralize free radicals in the body, reducing cellular damage and inflammation. Alkaloids like myristicin and elemicin contribute to its antioxidant properties. Flavonoids present in the plant also enhance the body's defense mechanisms against oxidative damage.

Together, these constituents support overall health by mitigating oxidative stress.

Scientific Research

According to "BMC chemistry", Myristica fragrans fights oxidative stress in a positive way by showing good percentage of neuroprotection against H2O2-induced neurotoxicity with 86.28% at 100 µg/mL.
