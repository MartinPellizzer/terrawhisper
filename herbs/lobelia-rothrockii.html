Rothrock'S Lobelia (Lobelia Rothrockii)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Lobelia rothrockii.

Rothrock's lobelia, scientifically known as Lobelia rothrockii, is a rare medicinal herb native to the southeastern United States, particularly in the Appalachian region. This plant is valued for its unique properties and is often referred to as an adaptogen due to its ability to help the body manage stress and fatigue. Traditionally, Native American tribes used Rothrock's lobelia to treat respiratory ailments, such as coughs and bronchitis, and as a calming agent for anxiety and nervousness. In modern wellness practices, it is used in herbal formulations to support respiratory health, enhance mental clarity, and promote emotional balance. One of its most distinctive features is the presence of a rare alkaloid called lobelamine, which contributes to its potent medicinal effects and makes it a subject of ongoing scientific interest.
