Citrus × aurantium var. amara

Citrus × aurantium var. amara, also known as bitter orange or Seville orange, is a variety of the sour orange tree that is native to Asia and the Mediterranean region.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties, which include antispasmodic, antiseptic, and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Citrus × aurantium var. amara include reducing nausea and digestive issues, as well as aiding in weight loss and improving circulation.

In herbal preparations, the peel and fruit of the bitter orange tree are often used to make teas, extracts, and essential oils, which can be used to treat various health conditions.
