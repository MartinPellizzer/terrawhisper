What's the taxonomical classification of Delphinium hirticaule var. mollipes?

Delphinium hirticaule var. mollipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. It is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Delphinium.
