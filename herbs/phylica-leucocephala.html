Phylica leucocephala

Phylica leucocephala, also known as the white-flowering phylica or white-head phylica, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is a shrub native to South Africa and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
