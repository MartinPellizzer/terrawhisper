Coelogyne calcicola

What's the taxonomical classification of Coelogyne calcicola?

Coelogyne calcicola belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further classified under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling into the genus Coelogyne, with its specific designation being the species calcicola.
