Valeriana rigida var. tenuifolia

Valeriana rigida var. tenuifolia, commonly known as Japanese valerian, is a plant species in the valerian family, native to East Asia.

The plant's therapeutic actions are attributed to its ability to calm the nervous system, promote relaxation, and improve sleep quality.
