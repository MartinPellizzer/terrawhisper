    Phyllagathis elattandra is prepared as a decoction, a paste, or a poultice for various therapeutic uses. Some individuals might experience redness or itching upon direct contact with the plant's foliage or sap. It is essential to exercise caution and avoid self-administration without professional guidance to prevent unexpected physiological responses. Always consult a medical professional if symptoms occur after exposure to this species.
