Paris polyphylla var. yunnanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Paris polyphylla var. yunnanensis?

Paris polyphylla var. yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Liliales and is a specific variety within the family Melanthiaceae. Taxonomically, it is situated in the genus Paris, specifically identified as the variety yunnanensis of the species Paris polyphylla.
