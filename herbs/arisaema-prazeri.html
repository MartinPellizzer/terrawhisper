What's the taxonomical classification of Arisaema prazeri?

Arisaema prazeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Alismatales and the family Araceae. Finally, the plant is identified within the genus Arisaema, specifically categorized as the species prazeri.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Arisaema prazeri has a single, large, cordate leaf that emerges from a subterranean tuber and is supported by a long, fleshy petiole. The leaf blade typically exhibits a glossy texture with prominent venation spreading from the base. This plant produces a distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spathe that encloses a central spadix. The spadix serves as a specialized structure to attract pollinators within the protected chamber of the spathe. During its growth cycle, the plant relies on its robust underground rhizome for nutrient storage and survival.

      What side effects this plant can have?
