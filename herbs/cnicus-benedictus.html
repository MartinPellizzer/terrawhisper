Cnicus Benedictus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cnicus benedictus, commonly known as St. Benedict's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its use in supporting liver function, reducing inflammation, and aiding digestion.

The bioactive constituents in Cnicus benedictus include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
