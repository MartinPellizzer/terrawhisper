Dipteris conjugata

What's the taxonomical classification of Dipteris conjugata?

Dipteris conjugata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Gleicheniales and is a member of the family Dipteriaceae. Ultimately, this plant is defined by its genus, Dipteris, and its specific species designation, conjugata.
