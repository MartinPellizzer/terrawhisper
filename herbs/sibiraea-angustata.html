Sibiraea angustata

What's the taxonomical classification of Sibiraea angustata?

Sibiraea angustata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Rosales, it is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Sibiraea, and its unique species designation, angustata.
