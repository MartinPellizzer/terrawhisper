What side-effects this plant can have?

Rhaphidophora hookeri can cause localized skin irritation, such as redness or itching, if the sap comes into direct contact with sensitive areas. While the plant is generally considered non-toxic to most pets, ingestion could potentially lead to mild gastrointestinal upset or digestive discomfort. It is advisable to wash your hands thoroughly after handling the leaves to minimize these risks. Monitoring for any adverse reactions is recommended when introducing this plant into a new environment.
