Pararuellia delavayana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Pararuelliia and is distinguished as the species delavayana.
