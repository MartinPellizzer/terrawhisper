Helicia hainanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Helicia hainanensis?

Helicia hainanenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Proteales and is a member of the family Proteaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized within the genus Helicia, specifically identified as the species hainanensis.
