What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Neillia thyrsiflora has a woody, perennial habit characterized by small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in an opposite or sub-opposite pattern along its stems. These inflorescences are supported by sturdy peduncles that elevate the blooms above the vegetative mass.
