Neillia thyrsiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Neillia thyrsiflora?

Neillia thyrsiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaecae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Neillia and the species thyrsiflora.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including skin infections, inflammation, and digestive issues. It contains bioactive compounds that help reduce swelling and soothe irritated tissues. Local practitioners often apply the plant to wounds to prevent bacterial growth and promote healing. These medicinal applications rely on the plant's natural antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
