10 Possible Side Effects Of Ribes Nigrum (Blackcurrant)

Ribes nigrum has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, gastrointestinal issues, and interactions with medications.

These reactions can be caused by consuming excessive amounts or using the herb in conjunction with other substances.

For instance, an allergic reaction to blackcurrant can lead to anaphylaxis, a severe and life-threatening condition, which can worsen a person's quality of life and even cause hospitalization.
