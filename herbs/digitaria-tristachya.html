Digitaria tristachya

Digitaria tristachya, also known as Crowfoot or Crowfoot grass, is a type of tropical grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antibacterial properties.

The health benefits of Digitaria tristachya include reducing fever, treating skin conditions, and aiding in digestion, as well as being used as a diuretic to treat urinary tract infections.

The herbal preparations of Digitaria tristachya typically involve drying the leaves and roots, which can be made into teas, infusions, or added to other herbal remedies.
