Nelumbo Nucifera Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Nelumbo nucifera, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a sacred plant in many cultures, revered for its spiritual and medicinal properties.

This herb, native to Asia, is valued for its leaves, flowers, and seeds, which are used in traditional medicine for their numerous health benefits.

It is known to support digestion, reduce inflammation, and promote mental clarity due to its bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds.

Herbal preparations like infusions, decoctions, and extracts can be made from the leaves and flowers to harness its therapeutic actions.

Medicinal Parts

Nelumbo nucifera seed is widely used in traditional medicine for its rich content of nutrients and bioactive compounds, including proteins, fats, and minerals.

They are also used to treat respiratory conditions and improve cognitive function due to their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.

The seed's oil is sometimes applied topically to relieve muscle pain and skin ailments, highlighting its versatility in both internal and external applications.
