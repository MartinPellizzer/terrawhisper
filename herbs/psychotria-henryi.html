Psychotria henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Psychotria, specifically identified by the species name henryi.
