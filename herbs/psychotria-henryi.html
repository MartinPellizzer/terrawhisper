What's the taxonomical classification of Psychotria henryi?

Psychotria henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical descent through the order Gentianaales, the plant is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Psychotria, specifically identified by the species name henryi.
