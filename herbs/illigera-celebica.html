Illigera celebica

What's the taxonomical classification of Illigera celebica?

Illigera celebica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Laurales and the family Hernandiaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Illigera, and its unique species designation, celebica.
