Cuscuta Epithymum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cuscuta epithymum, commonly known as yellow dodder, is a parasitic plant that grows on other plants and is widely used in traditional medicine.

It is valued for its potential to support kidney health, enhance vitality, and promote reproductive wellness.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or powder for internal use, and is often combined with other herbs in traditional formulations.
