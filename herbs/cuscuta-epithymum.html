Cuscuta Epithymum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cuscuta epithymum, commonly known as yellow dodder, is a parasitic plant that grows on other plants, drawing nutrients from its host.

This herb is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including supporting kidney function and promoting vitality.

Therapeutically, it is believed to aid in the treatment of urinary disorders and enhance overall energy levels.

The bioactive constituents of Cuscuta epithymum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
