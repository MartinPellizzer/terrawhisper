What's the taxonomical classification of Dryopteris barbigera?

Dryopteris barbigera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, it falls under the order Polypodiales. This fern is a representative of the family Polypodiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Dryopteris, with its unique identity defined by the species name barbigera.

Each leaflet features finely serrated margins and a prominent midrib that contributes to its intricate, feathery appearance.
