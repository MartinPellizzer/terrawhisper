What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis angustiloba?

Pedicularis angustiloba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Lamiales and is situated within the family Orobanchaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Pedicularis and the specific epithet angustiloba.
