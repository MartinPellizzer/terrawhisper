Hydrangea xanthoneura

What's the taxonomical classification of Hydrangea xanthoneura?

Hydrangea xanthoneura belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Cornales. This plant is a member of the family Hydrangeaceae and is situated within the genus Hydrangea, specifically identified by its species name, xanthoneura.
