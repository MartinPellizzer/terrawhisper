Thladiantha hookeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Cucurbitaceae, which places it among the gourd and melon relatives. Within this family, it is specifically identified by the genus Thladiantha and the species hookeri.
