10 Possible Side Effects Of Gentianella Amarella (Yellow Gentian)

These issues can worsen people's lives by causing dehydration, fatigue, and disrupted daily routines.

1. Leads to addiction patterns

Gentianella amarella causes allergic reactions due to the presence of quercetin and isorhapontigenin, two flavonoid compounds that can trigger an immune response in some individuals.

These compounds may stimulate the release of histamine, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and flushing.

The allergenic properties of these flavonoids are thought to be responsible for the hypersensitivity reactions observed in some people who use Gentianella amarella.

The plant's bitter compounds, such as gentiopicrin and gentianine, can irritate the digestive system, leading to abdominal discomfort and cramping.

This is because these compounds slow down digestion and increase gastric acid production, which can cause nausea, bloating, and pain in some individuals.
