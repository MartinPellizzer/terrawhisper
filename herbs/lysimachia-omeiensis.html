What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia omeiensis?

Lysimachia omeiensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Lysimachia, with its specific designation being the species omeiensis.

Small, yellow, funnel-shaped flowers emerge from the leaf axils, typically appearing in small clusters. Each bloom consists of five delicate petals that surround a central cluster of stamens.
