Fokienia hodginsii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Pinidae and the order Pinales. Its stems are characterized by a textured bark that develops as the plant matures into a perennial shrub.
