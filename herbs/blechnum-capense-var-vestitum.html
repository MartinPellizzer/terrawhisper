Blechnum capense var. vestitum

Blechnum capense var. vestitum, also known as the Cape beechnum or beech fern, is a variety of the Blechnum capense plant, a type of fern native to southern Africa.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for treating wounds, skin conditions, and digestive issues.
