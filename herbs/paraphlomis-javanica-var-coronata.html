Paraphlomis javanica var. coronata

What's the taxonomical classification of Paraphlomis javanica var. coronata?

Paraphlomis javanica var. coronaata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Lamiales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Lamiaceae, within the genus Paraphlomis. Ultimately, this specific variety is identified by its scientific designation, Paraphlomis javanica var. coronaata.
