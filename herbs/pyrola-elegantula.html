Pyrola elegantula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Ericaceae, placing it within the genus Pyrola, and ultimately identifying it as the specific species elegantula.
