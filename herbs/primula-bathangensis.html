What's the taxonomical classification of Primula bathangensis?

Primula bathangenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling under the order Ericales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, specifically categorized under the genus Primula, and is identified by its unique species designation, bathangenensis.
