What's the taxonomical classification of Leptochilus pedunculatus?

Leptochilus pedunculatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and is organized within the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic journey continues through the family Polypodiaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Leptochilus and the species pedunculatus.
