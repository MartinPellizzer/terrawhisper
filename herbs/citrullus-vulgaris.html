Possible Side Effects of citrullus vulgaris

Furthermore, citrullus vulgaris may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and decrease their effectiveness.

In rare cases, improper use of citrullus vulgaris has been associated with allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.
