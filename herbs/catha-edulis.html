Catha Edulis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Catha edulis, commonly known as khat, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It contains bioactive constituents such as cathinone and cathine, which are responsible for its stimulant effects and therapeutic properties.

The herb is traditionally used for its ability to enhance alertness, reduce fatigue, and improve mood, though it may also have potential for dependency.

Herbal preparations of Catha edulis include infusions, chewed leaves, and sometimes made into teas or tinctures.
