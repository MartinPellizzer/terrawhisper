What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium wrightii?

Asplenium wrrightii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, following the order Polypodiales. The plant is further categorized into the family Aspleniaceae and the genus Asplenium, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species wrightii.
