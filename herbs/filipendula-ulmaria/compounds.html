Queen Anne'S Lace (Filipendula ulmaria) Compounds

The primary compounds in Queen Anne's Lace include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and coumarin, known for its anticoagulant effects. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while glycosides and saponins support digestive health. Essential oils and terpenoids provide aromatic and antimicrobial benefits. Anthocyanins and alkaloids add to its complex pharmacological profile.
