What's the taxonomical classification of Desmos grandifolius?

Desmos grandifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Magnoliales. The plant is a member of the family Annonaceae, falling under the genus Desmos, and is specifically identified by the species name grandifolius.

What are the pharmacological activities of Desmos grandifolius?

This plant has anti-tumor, antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant pharmacological activities that make it a subject of significant medicinal interest. Beyond its anti-tumor properties, the plant exhibits a strong ability to combat bacterial and fungal infections. These diverse therapeutic effects suggest that its extracts could be utilized in developing new pharmaceutical agents.

According to a study published by "Zhongguo Zhong yao za zhi = Zhongguo zhongyao zazhi = China journal of Chinese materia medica", the root of Desmos grandifolius was investigated to find new compounds with anti-tumor activities. Six compounds were isolated from the CHCl3 extract of this plant and identified as lawinal(I), isolawinal(II), desmethoxymatteucinol(III), benzoic acid(IV), beta-sitosterol(V) and sitgmasterol(VI). All 6 of these compounds were found from this plant for the first time. Additionally, 3 dihydroflavonoids isolated from this species were characterized by A-ring substitution and no B-ring substitution.
