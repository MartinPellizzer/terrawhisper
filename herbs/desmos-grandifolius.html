What's the taxonomical classification of Desmos grandifolius?

Desmos grandifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Magnoliales. The plant is a member of the family Annonaceae, falling under the genus Desmos, and is specifically identified by the species name grandifolius.
