Eleusine coracana

Eleusine coracana, also known as finger millet, is a type of small-grained cereal crop native to Africa and Asia, belonging to the family Poaceae.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities.

The health benefits of finger millet include its ability to lower cholesterol levels, regulate blood sugar, and provide essential nutrients like iron, calcium, and zinc.

Finger millet can be consumed as a whole grain, or used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, making it a versatile and nutritious addition to a healthy diet.
