Phyllostachys makinoi

What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllostachys makinoi?

Phyllostachys makinoi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Poales and falls under the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identification is defined by the genus Phyllostachys and the species makinoi.
