What's the taxonomical classification of Hylodesmum podocarpum subsp. szechuenense?

Hylodesmum podocarpum subsp. szechuenense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Fabales and falls into the family Fabaceae. More specifically, it is part of the genus Hylodesmum, ultimately identified by its precise botanical designation as the species Hylodesmum podocarpum subsp. szechuenense.
