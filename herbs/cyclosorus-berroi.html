Cyclosorus Berroi

Cyclosorus berroi, a species of liverwort, has been traditionally used in medicinal practices for its potential therapeutic properties.

It is often employed in herbal remedies to support liver function and detoxification processes in the body. The plant contains bioactive compounds that may possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, contributing to its medicinal value. Due to its historical use in folk medicine, further scientific research is needed to validate its efficacy and safety.

As a result, Cyclosorus berroi remains an intriguing subject in the field of ethnopharmacology.
