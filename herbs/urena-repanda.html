What's the taxonomical classification of Urena repanda?

Urena repanda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, falling under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Malvales and the family Malvaceae. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, it is assigned to the genus Urena, ultimately identifying it as the specific species repanda.

Its flowers are small, inconspicuous, and arranged in dense, axillary clusters that often appear greenish or white. The fruit is a small, globose berry that develops from the inferior ovary.
