Beaumontia murtonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is situated within the genus Beaumontia, with its specific designation being the species murtonii.
