What's the taxonomical classification of Pleione hookeriana?

Pleione hookeriana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling specifically within the genus Pleione, with its unique biological identity defined by the species hookerianana.
