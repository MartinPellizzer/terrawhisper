10 Possible Side Effects Of Rosa Centifolia (Damask Rose)

Rosa centifolia has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive issues.

These side effects occur due to an individual's sensitivity or prolonged use. For instance, an allergic reaction can lead to symptoms like hives, itching, and swelling, which may impact daily activities.

If severe, it can affect sleep, social interactions, and overall well-being, making life more challenging.
