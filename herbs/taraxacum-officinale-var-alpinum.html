Taraxacum officinale var. alpinum

Taraxacum officinale var. alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine Dandelion, is a subspecies of the dandelion plant, characterized by its smaller stature and narrower leaves compared to the common dandelion.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include its potential to treat digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant and antimicrobial activities, contributing to its numerous health benefits.
