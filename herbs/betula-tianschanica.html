Betula tianschanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Fagales. This plant is a member of the family Betulaceae, which contains the genus Betula, and is specifically identified by the species name tianchanica.
