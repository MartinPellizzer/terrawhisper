Cydonia Oblonga: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cydonia oblonga, commonly known as the quince, is a fruit-bearing plant native to the Middle East and South Asia.

It is valued for its aromatic, edible fruit, which is rich in antioxidants and nutrients.

The health benefits of quince include supporting digestion, boosting immunity, and promoting heart health due to its high fiber and vitamin C content.
