What's the taxonomical classification of Pyrola atropurpurea?

Pyrola atropurpurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its classification through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed under the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Ericaceae, specifically situated within the genus Pyrola, with its unique identity defined by the species name atropurpurea.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These blossoms are pendulous and hang from thin pedicels, often appearing in small clusters. The floral structure includes a prominent calyx that matches the dark intensity of the corolla.
