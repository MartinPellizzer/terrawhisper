Yellow Milkvetch (Astragalus membranaceus) Uses

Yellow Milkvetch is used to treat chronic fatigue and chronic fatigue syndrome by boosting energy and immune function. It supports autoimmune disorders and diseases by modulating immune responses. It also helps reduce chronic inflammation and may aid in managing asthma, allergic rhinitis, chronic bronchitis, and the common cold.
