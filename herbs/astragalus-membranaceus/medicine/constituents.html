10 Medicinal Constituents Of Astragalus Membranaceus (Huang Qi)

Astragalus membranaceus has active constituents such as polysaccharides, flavonoids, and saponins.

These bioactive compounds contribute to its immunomodulatory, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. The plant's medicinal properties can help boost the immune system, reduce oxidative stress, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory diseases like bronchitis and pneumonia.

For example, astragalus supplements may improve lung function in patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and enhance chemotherapy efficacy in cancer treatment.

1. Saponins

Astragalus membranaceus saponins is a type of triterpenoid glycoside compound found in the plant's roots.

These compounds have been extensively studied for their potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Saponins are believed to contribute to Astragalus' traditional use in Chinese medicine to boost immune function, reduce stress, and alleviate respiratory issues.

Flavonoids in Astragalus membranaceus play a crucial role in enhancing the plant's adaptogenic effects, making it a popular herb in traditional Chinese medicine for boosting immune function and overall health.
