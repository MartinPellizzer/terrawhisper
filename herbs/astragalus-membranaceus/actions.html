Yellow Milkvetch (Astragalus membranaceus) Actions

Yellow Milkvetch is a tonic that supports immune function and vitality. It has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and pain. As a stimulant, it enhances energy and circulation. It also acts as a diuretic to support kidney health and a carminative to relieve gas and bloating.

Yellow Milkvetch (Astragalus membranaceus) Monograph

1. Tonic

Yellow milkvetch has a tonic action, supporting overall vitality and immune function. It strengthens the body's defenses and promotes recovery from fatigue and illness. This herb is particularly valued for its ability to enhance energy and restore balance in the body.

2. Anti-inflammatory

Yellow milkvetch has an anti-inflammatory action, primarily due to its high content of flavonoids and polysaccharides. These compounds help reduce inflammation by modulating immune responses and inhibiting pro-inflammatory cytokines. It is commonly used in traditional medicine to support conditions involving chronic inflammation.

3. Stimulant

Yellow milkvetch has a stimulant action, enhancing energy and vitality by supporting the body's natural processes. It promotes circulation and strengthens the immune system, making it valuable in herbal medicine for its invigorating properties. This action is particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing fatigue or weakness.

4. Diuretic

Yellow milkvetch has a diuretic action, promoting the excretion of urine to help eliminate excess fluids from the body. This effect supports kidney function and can aid in reducing swelling caused by fluid retention. It is often used in traditional medicine to manage conditions involving water retention or mild edema.

5. Carminative

Yellow milkvetch has a carminative action, helping to relieve gas and bloating by stimulating digestion and reducing intestinal fermentation. It supports the digestive system's natural processes, promoting comfort and reducing discomfort associated with gas buildup. This makes it beneficial for digestive health and gastrointestinal discomfort.

6. Sedative

Yellow milkvetch has a sedative action, helping to calm the nervous system and reduce anxiety. It promotes relaxation by modulating neurotransmitter activity, making it useful for stress-related conditions. Its calming effects are gentle and support overall emotional balance.

7. Bitter

Yellow milkvetch has a bitter action, which helps to clear heat and detoxify the body. It stimulates digestion and enhances the body's natural defenses. This action supports the elimination of pathogens and promotes overall vitality. The bitter taste is associated with its ability to strengthen the immune system.

8. Astringent

Yellow milkvetch has an astringent action, which helps to tone and tighten tissues. It is used to reduce secretions and soothe inflamed mucous membranes. This property supports its traditional use in respiratory and digestive health. It can be particularly beneficial for conditions involving excessive mucus or bleeding.

9. Antispasmodic

Yellow milkvetch has an antispasmodic action, helping to relieve muscle spasms and cramps. It works by relaxing smooth muscle tissue, making it useful for conditions like menstrual cramps, gastrointestinal spasms, and respiratory issues. This effect is supported by its traditional use in Chinese medicine for easing tension and promoting relaxation.

10. Expectorant

Yellow milkvetch has an expectorant action, helping to loosen and expel mucus from the respiratory tract. It stimulates the secretion of bronchial fluids, making it effective for conditions like bronchitis and coughs. This action supports clearer breathing and reduces congestion in the lungs.
