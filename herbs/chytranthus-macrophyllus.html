What's the taxonomical classification of Chytranthus macrophyllus?

Chytranthus macrophyllus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated under the order Sapindales. It falls within the family Sapindaceae, which characterizes its broader botanical group. Specifically, the plant is identified by its genus, Chytranthus, and its unique species designation, macrophyllus.
