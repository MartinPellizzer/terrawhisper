Fordiophyton faberi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Myrtales and the family Melastomataceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Fordiophyton, and its species designation, faberi.
