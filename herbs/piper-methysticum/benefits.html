How this herb helps with reduce anxiety stress?

Piper methysticum reduce anxiety stress because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

According to "Phytomedicine : international journal of phytotherapy and phytopharmacology", Piper methysticum is associated with anxiolytic activity in humans, suggesting it can reduce anxiety.
