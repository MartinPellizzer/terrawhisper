Ficus macrocarpa

Ficus macrocarpa, commonly known as the Moreton Bay fig or Strangler Fig, is a large evergreen tree native to Australia.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality due to its health benefits, which include its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Ficus macrocarpa are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which may contribute to its anxiolytic, sedative, and antispasmodic effects.

Additionally, the bioactive constituents of Ficus macrocarpa may also provide cardiovascular protection, improve cognitive function, and exert antimicrobial activity.
