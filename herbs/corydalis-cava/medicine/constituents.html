10 Medicinal Constituents Of Corydalis Cava (Corydal)

Corydalis cava has active constituents such as protopine, berberine, and isoferulaldehyde.

These compounds possess analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and anti-anxiety properties, making corydal a valuable herbal remedy. Berberine, for instance, can help manage blood sugar levels and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, improving the lives of individuals with these conditions.

Protobufin, another constituent, exhibits neuroprotective effects, which may benefit individuals with neurological disorders such as Parkinson's disease.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Corydalis cava.

1. Berberine

Corydalis cava berberine is a natural compound extracted from the root of the Corydalis cava plant.

This alkaloid has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues and respiratory problems. Research has also demonstrated its potential in regulating blood sugar levels and cholesterol.
