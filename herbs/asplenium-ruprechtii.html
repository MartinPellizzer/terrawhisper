Asplenium ruprechtii

Asplenium ruprechtii, also known as the Ruprecht's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family, typically found in mountainous regions and often associated with limestone outcrops.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties.

The health benefits of Asplenium ruprechtii include its ability to aid in the treatment of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its potential to help lower blood pressure and alleviate symptoms of arthritis.
