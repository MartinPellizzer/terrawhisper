Asplenium ruprechtii has large, dark green, pinnate fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length with individual pinnae that are lanceolate and possess slightly wavy margins. Each leaflet is arranged along a central rachis that is often smooth or slightly textured depending on the specimen's maturity. On the underside of the mature fronds, small, linear sori are arranged in two neat rows along the veins to facilitate spore production.
