What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcococca orientalis?

Sarcococca orientalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Sarcococca, with its specific identity defined by the species name orientalis.
