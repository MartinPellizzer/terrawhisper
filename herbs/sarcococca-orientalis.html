Sarcococca orientalis

What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcococca orientalis?

Sarcococca orientalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This species is a member of the order Buxales and falls under the family Buxaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Sarcococca, with its specific identity defined by the species name orientalis.
