What's the taxonomical classification of Brazzeia soyauxii?

Brazzeia soyauxii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Lecythidaceae, which falls under the genus Brazzeia, and is specifically identified by its species name, soyauxii.
