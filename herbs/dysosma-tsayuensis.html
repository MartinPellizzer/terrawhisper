Dysosma tsayuensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Dysosma tsayuensis?

Dysosma tsayuensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Berberidaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Dysosmoma and is specifically distinguished as the species tsayuensis.
