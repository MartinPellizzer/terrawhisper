Biebersteinia odora

What's the taxonomical classification of Biebersteinia odora?

Biebersteinia odora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized under the order Sapindales and is the sole representative of its family, Biebersteiniaceae. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Biebersteinia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species Biebersteinia odora.
