Pottsia grandiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Pottsia grandiflora?

Pottsia grandiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the botanical hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Gentianales and falls within the family Apocynaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pottsia, and its species name, grandiflora.
