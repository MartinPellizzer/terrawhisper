What's the taxonomical classification of Heisteria zimmereri?

Heisteria zimmereri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Santalales. It is specifically categorized under the family Olacaceae, falling within the genus Heisteria, with the specific epithet zimmereri completing its scientific designation.
