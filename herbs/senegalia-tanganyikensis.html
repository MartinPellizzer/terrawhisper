What is Senegalia tanganyikensis?

Senegalia tanganyikensis is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae, subfamily Mimosoideae.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 10 meters in height, with a spread of 6-8 meters. The bark is brown, becoming gray with age, and the branches are terete, with a glabrous surface. The leaves are bipinnate, with 4-8 pairs of pinnae, each with 2-4 pairs of pinnules, and are 10-20 cm long.

The flowers are yellow, in axillary clusters, and are 1-2 cm long, with a calyx that is glabrous and a corolla that is 10-15 mm long.
