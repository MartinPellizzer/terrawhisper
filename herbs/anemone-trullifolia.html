Anemone trullifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and into the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Anemone, with its specific identification as the species trullifolia.
