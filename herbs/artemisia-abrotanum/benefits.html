Southernwood's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties also help to alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve digestion, and even protect against cell damage, reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease.

Artemisia abrotanum treats fever because of its rich content of bioactive constituents such as sesquiterpenes, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, which exhibit antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The antipyretic properties of Artemisia abrotanum make it an effective remedy for various ailments related to fever, such as the common cold, influenza, and malaria.

By reducing inflammation and fever, Artemisia abrotanum provides relief from symptoms and promotes overall well-being.
