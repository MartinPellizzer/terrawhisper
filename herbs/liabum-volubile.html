    What is Liabum volubile?

    Liabum volubile is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

    It is an annual herb with a sprawling habit, up to 1 meter tall, and a branching stem with a woody base. The leaves are lanceolate to elliptical in shape, 5-10 cm long, and have a leathery texture with a ciliate margin. The inflorescence is a large, terminal corymb of numerous, small flowers, 5-10 mm in diameter, with 3-5 yellow petals and a yellow center.

    The plant produces achenes with a pappus of 20-30 white bristles and a single seed.

Studies have also found that Liabum volubile contains analgesic compounds that help alleviate pain by blocking the action of pain receptors.

    Furthermore, the plant's anti-inflammatory properties make it useful in treating conditions such as arthritis.
