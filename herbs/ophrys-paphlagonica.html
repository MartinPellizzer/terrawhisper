Ophrys paphlagonica

Ophrys paphlagonica, commonly known as the Paphlagonian Bee Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

This plant is traditionally used in folk medicine due to its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Ophrys paphlagonica are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids that exhibit antimicrobial and antispasmodic activities.
