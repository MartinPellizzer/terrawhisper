What's the taxonomical classification of Argyreia pierreana?

Argyreiia pierreana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Solanales. Specifically, this plant is a member of the family Convolvulaceae, categorized under the genus Argyreiia, and is identified by the specific epithet pierreana.
