10 Best Prunus Dulcis Preparations

The best medicinal preparations of Prunus dulcis are teas, tinctures, decoctions, syrups, and mucillages, each offering unique therapeutic benefits.

Decoctions, made by simmering the bark or seeds, are valued for their ability to extract potent alkaloids and nutrients.

Syrups are popular for their soothing effect on sore throats and respiratory ailments, while mucillages, derived from the seed coat, are used to ease digestive discomfort and as a natural demulcent.
