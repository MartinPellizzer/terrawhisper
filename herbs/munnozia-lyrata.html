What is Munnozia lyrata?

Munnozia lyrata is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, with leaves that are lanceolate and serrated, and arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant produces yellow flower heads, each comprising 10-20 tubular florets, which are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The florets are surrounded by a ring of scale-like phyllaries, and the plant flowers from January to April.

Munnozia lyrata is endemic to the Andes mountain range in South America.
