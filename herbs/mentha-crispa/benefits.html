10 Best Mentha Crispa Health Benefits

Mentha crispa, commonly known as catnip, offers a variety of health benefits due to its rich content of essential oils and antioxidants.

It is traditionally used to alleviate digestive issues such as bloating, gas, and indigestion, thanks to its carminative properties. The plant also has calming effects on the nervous system, making it useful for reducing stress and anxiety when consumed as a tea. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties support immune function and may help in treating minor infections.

Additionally, Mentha crispa is believed to aid in respiratory health by easing symptoms of coughs and colds when used in steam inhalation or as a herbal remedy.
