Oreocharis maximowiczii

What's the taxonomical classification of Oreocharis maximowiczii?

Oreocharis maximowiczii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a member of the family Gesneriaceae. At the most specific levels of its taxonomy, this plant is identified as being part of the genus Oreocharis, with its specific designation being the species maximowiczii.

Oreocharis maximowiczii has medicinal properties that are primarily utilized in traditional practices to treat respiratory ailments, inflammation, and digestive discomfort. Additionally, some studies suggest its components may aid in regulating gastrointestinal functions. Local practitioners often prepare infusions or decoctions to harness these specific systemic benefits.
