What's the taxonomical classification of Disporum cantoniense?

Disporum cantoniense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its order, Liliales, the plant is placed into the family Colchicaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Disporum, and its unique species designation, cantoniense.
