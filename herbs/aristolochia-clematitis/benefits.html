10 Best Aristolochia Clematitis Health Benefits

Aristolochia clematitis, also known as birthwort, has been traditionally used in herbal medicine, but it is important to note that it contains aristolochic acids, which are toxic and have been linked to severe kidney damage and even cancer.

Due to these harmful effects, the use of Aristolochia clematitis is strongly discouraged, especially in medicinal applications. Despite its historical use for ailments such as respiratory issues and skin conditions, modern scientific research has largely discredited its safety and efficacy.

As a result, regulatory bodies in many countries have banned or restricted the sale and use of products containing Aristolochia clematitis to protect public health.
