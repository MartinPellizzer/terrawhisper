10 Precautions To Take When Using Aristolochia Clematitis (Birthwort)

Aristolochia clematitis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring kidney function and blood pressure regularly.

Failure to do so may lead to kidney damage and other serious complications. Long-term use can also cause oxidative stress and potentially worsen conditions like kidney disease, hypertension, and certain cancers.

It's crucial to follow these precautions to avoid adverse effects and ensure safe use.

When using Aristolochia clematitis medicinally, it's important to take under medical supervision.

This is crucial because Aristolochia clematitis contains aristolochic acid, a nephrotoxic compound that can cause irreversible kidney damage and even cancer if not used correctly.

Without proper guidance from a qualified healthcare professional, incorrect dosing or combination with other medications could lead to severe adverse effects.
