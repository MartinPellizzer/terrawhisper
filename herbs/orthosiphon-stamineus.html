Java Tea (Orthosiphon stamineus)

Java Tea (Orthosiphon stamineus) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, Indonesia, and Malaysia. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
