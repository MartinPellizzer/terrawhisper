Chamaemelum arvense

Chamaemelum arvense, also known as English chamomile, is an annual flowering plant in the daisy family Asteraceae, with a strong, sweet apple-like fragrance.

The medicinal properties of Chamaemelum arvense include its anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and antiseptic properties, which have been used to treat a variety of health issues.

The health benefits of English chamomile include promoting relaxation, reducing anxiety and insomnia, soothing digestive issues, and alleviating menstrual cramps.

English chamomile is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and essential oils, which can be consumed or applied topically to reap its medicinal benefits.
