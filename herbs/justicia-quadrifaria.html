What's the taxonomical classification of Justicia quadrifaria?

Justicia quadrifaria belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Lamiales, it is placed into the family Acanthaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Justicia, and its unique species designation, quadrifaria.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Justicia quadrifaria has opposite, decussate leaves that are lanceolate to oblong in shape with entire margins and a distinctively short petiole. Its stems are herbaceous and square in cross-section, which is a characteristic feature of the Acanthaceae family. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that emerge from the leaf axils in a structured arrangement.
