This herb is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which contribute to its numerous health benefits, including relief from respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, and skin conditions like acne and wounds.

The therapeutic actions of Cedrus libani include its ability to stimulate circulation, reduce pain and swelling, and exhibit antioxidant properties.
