Cedrus Libani Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cedrus libani, commonly known as the Lebanese cypress, is a type of coniferous tree native to the Eastern Mediterranean region, particularly Lebanon.

This herb is valued for its aromatic resins and essential oils, which have been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

The health benefits of Cedrus libani include antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which may support respiratory and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include terpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oil diffusions can be made from Cedrus libani to harness its medicinal properties.
