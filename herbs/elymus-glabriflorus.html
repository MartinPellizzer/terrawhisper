Elymus Glabriflorus

Elymus glabriflorus, commonly known as the smoothflower needlegrass, is a native grass species that has been traditionally used in various medicinal practices.

It is valued for its potential anti-inflammatory and astringent properties, which may help in treating skin conditions and wounds. The plant contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. In traditional herbal medicine, Elymus glabriflorus has been employed to support digestive health and reduce fever.

However, further scientific research is needed to fully understand its medicinal potential and confirm its efficacy in modern healthcare applications.
