What is Commicarpus tuberosus?

Commicarpus tuberosus is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Nyctaginaceae.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which store water and nutrients, and its succulent, fleshy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape and measure up to 7 cm in length. The leaves are also covered with small, hair-like trichomes, giving them a soft and downy texture. Commicarpus tuberosus produces small, white to pink flowers in axillary clusters, which are followed by greenish-yellow fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This plant is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found growing in sandy or stony soils.
