What is Dysphania tibetica?

Dysphania tibetica is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It has a simple or branched stem, typically 10-50 cm tall, and a woody base with a taproot. The leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, 2-10 cm long, and have a smooth margin. The inflorescence is a terminal spike, 1-6 cm long, containing small, greenish or yellowish flowers with 5 tepals and 5 stamens.

The plant produces small, brownish, 3-winged achenes 1-2 mm long, which are the fruiting structures.
