10 Possible Side Effects Of Brassica Oleracea (Broccoli)

Brassica oleracea has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interference with certain medications.

Causes of these side effects include eating large amounts, consuming raw broccoli, or having an underlying allergy or intolerance.

For example, people who experience severe allergic reactions may have difficulty breathing, while those with digestive issues may suffer from bloating, gas, and stomach cramps, which can impact daily activities and overall quality of life.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Brassica oleracea if used imporperly.
