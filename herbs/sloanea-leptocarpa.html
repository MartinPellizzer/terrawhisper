Sloanea leptocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Sloanea leptocarpa?

Sloanea leptocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Oxalidales and is a member of the family Elaeocarpaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Sloanea, and its specific species designation, leptocarpa.
