Malva cathayensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic journey continues through the order Malvales and the family Malvaceae, eventually reaching its specific designation within the genus Malva.
