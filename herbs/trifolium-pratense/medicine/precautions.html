10 Precautions To Take When Using Trifolium Pratense (Red Clover)

Trifolium pratense has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood pressure and clotting time.

This is because the herb contains isoflavones that can affect the body's ability to clot blood and may interact with blood thinning medications. Not taking these precautions can result in prolonged bleeding or increased risk of hemorrhage.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using red clover medicinally to avoid potential side effects.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Trifolium pratense medicinally.
