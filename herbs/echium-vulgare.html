Echium Vulgare: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Echium vulgare, commonly known as common flax, is an annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is valued for its high content of omega-3 fatty acids and lignans, which contribute to its various health benefits.

The herb is known to support cardiovascular health, reduce inflammation, and promote hormonal balance due to its therapeutic actions.

Bioactive constituents such as alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), flavonoids, and mucilage are responsible for its medicinal properties.
