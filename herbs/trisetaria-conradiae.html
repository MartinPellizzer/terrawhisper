    What is Trisetaria conradiae?

    Trisetaria conradiae is a species of grass belonging to the family Poaceae.

    It is a perennial plant that grows from 10-50 cm tall, with a basal rosette of leaves that are 2-10 mm wide and 1-5 cm long. The leaves are flat, linear, and have a glabrous surface. The inflorescence is a panicle 5-15 cm long, with spikelets that are 6-8 mm long and have 3-5 florets, each containing 3 stamens and 1 pistil.
