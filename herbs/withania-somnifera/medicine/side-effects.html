10 Possible Side Effects Of Withania Somnifera (Ashwagandha)

Withania somnifera has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive problems, diarrhea, and stomach ulcers.

These side effects are often caused by taking high doses or consuming it for extended periods.

For instance, excessive consumption of ashwagandha can lead to chronic diarrhea, which can cause dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, and even weight loss, affecting a person's daily routine and overall well-being.
