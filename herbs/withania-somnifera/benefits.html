1. Supports immune system

Immune system support.

The immune system is the body's defense mechanism against pathogens and diseases.

A weakened immune system can result from factors such as stress, poor nutrition, or chronic illness, making individuals more susceptible to infections and illnesses.

People with compromised immunity, such as the elderly, those with chronic conditions, or individuals under significant stress, often experience frequent illnesses and slower recovery times.

Withania somnifera, also known as ashwagandha, has been shown to enhance immune function by modulating inflammatory responses and boosting the production of immune cells.

How this herb helps with supports immune system?

Withania somnifera supports immune system because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds help modulate immune responses by enhancing the activity of immune cells. Alkaloids like withanolides have anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory properties. Flavonoids contribute to the plant's antioxidant capacity, which protects immune cells from oxidative stress.

Together, these constituents promote a balanced and robust immune function.

Scientific Research

According to "Journal of ethnopharmacology", Withania somnifera supports immune system function by stimulating immunological activity, enhancing the total WBC count, increasing bone marrow cellularity, and promoting phagocytic activity of peritoneal macrophages.
