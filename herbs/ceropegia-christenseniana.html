Ceropegia christenseniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Ceropegia, and its unique species designation, christenseniana.
