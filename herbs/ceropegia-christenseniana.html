What's the taxonomical classification of Ceropegia christenseniana?

Ceropegia christenseniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Ceropegia, and its unique species designation, christenseniana.
