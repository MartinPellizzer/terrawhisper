10 Medicinal Constituents Of Benincasa Hispida (Loofah)

Benincasa hispida has active constituents such as saponins, flavonoids, and triterpenoids.

These compounds confer medicinal properties like anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The constituents of loofah have been shown to alleviate symptoms of respiratory disorders, improve wound healing, and even exhibit antidiabetic effects.

For instance, the saponins in loofah have been found to reduce inflammation in the lungs, while the flavonoids can protect against oxidative stress and DNA damage, potentially improving overall health and quality of life.
