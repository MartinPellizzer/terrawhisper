Arabis incarnata

Arabis incarnata, commonly known as Snow Buttercup, is a small, perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties that may help alleviate symptoms of various conditions such as eczema, acne, and respiratory issues.

The therapeutic actions of Arabis incarnata are attributed to its ability to calm and soothe the skin, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing, making it a popular ingredient in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Possible Side Effects of Arabis incarnata

Arabis incarnata can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing skin irritation, allergic reactions, and gastrointestinal problems.

High doses of Arabis incarnata extracts can cause dizziness, headaches, and nausea, while low doses may result in insomnia and anxiety.

In severe cases, improper use of the plant can trigger anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention.
