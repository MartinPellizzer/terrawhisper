Eurya obtusifolia var. aurea

What's the taxonomical classification of Eurya obtusifolia var. aurea?

Eurya obtusifolia var. aurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Pentaphylacaceae and falls under the genus Eurya, specifically identified by the botanical variety Eurya obtusifolia var. aurea.
