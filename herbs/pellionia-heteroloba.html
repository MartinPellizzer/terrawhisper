Pellioniia heteroloba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Small, inconspicuous flowers are produced along the stems, often tucked within the leaf axils.
