What's the taxonomical classification of Anemone rivularis?

Anemone rivularis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Ranunculales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, falling under the genus Anemone, and is specifically identified by the species name rivularis.
