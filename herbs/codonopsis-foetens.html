What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis foetens?

Codonopsis foetens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is specifically nested within the family Campanulaceae, which is the taxonomic group to which its genus, Codonopsis, is assigned. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species foetens.
