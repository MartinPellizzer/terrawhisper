Craibiodendron henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Ericaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Craibiodendron, and its unique species designation, henryi.
