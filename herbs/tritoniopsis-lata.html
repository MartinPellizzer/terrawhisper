What is Tritoniopsis lata?

Tritoniopsis lata is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Iridaceae.

It has linear to lance-shaped leaves that are 3-10 mm long and 0.5-1.5 mm wide, and are found in a rosette at the base of the stem. The plant produces a single, erect stem that grows up to 30 cm tall, bearing a solitary, axillary flower with three sepals and three petals. The petals are white with yellow markings at the base, and the flower is 3-4 cm in diameter.

The plant flowers from December to March and is native to the southwestern and western parts of South Africa.
