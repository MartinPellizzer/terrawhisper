What is Ajuga turkestanica?

Ajuga turkestanica is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by a height of 10-20 cm, with ovate, serrate leaves, measuring 2-5 cm in length and 1-3 cm in width, bearing a cuneate base and a rounded apex. The leaves are glabrous above, tomentose below, with a 5-7 mm petiole. The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme, 5-10 cm long, bearing blue flowers with a tubular calyx, 4-5 mm long, and a bilabiate corolla, 10-12 mm long, with a white or purple throat.

The flowers are arranged in a cymose pattern, and the plant blooms in May-June.
