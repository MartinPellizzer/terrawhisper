What is Odontarrhena serpentinicola?

Odontarrhena serpentinicola is a perennial plant species in the family Brassicaceae.

It has basal rosettes of leaves, with cauline leaves and inflorescences that are racemose or corymbose. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, serrate to dentate, and glabrous to sparsely pubescent. The flowers are small, white or yellowish, and arranged in clusters, with six stamens and a four- or five-lobed ovary.

The fruits are siliques, which are linear to lanceolate and glabrous, and contain numerous seeds.
