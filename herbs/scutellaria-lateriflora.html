Scutellaria lateriflora, commonly known as blue skullcap, is a medicinal herb used to treat anxiety disorders, insomnia, and restlessness.

The bioactive compounds present in Scutellaria lateriflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoids, such as baicalin and baicalein, which are responsible for the herb's medicinal properties.

Scutellaria lateriflora is often prepared as a tea by infusing the dried flowers and leaves in hot water, making it a popular herbal remedy for promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety.
