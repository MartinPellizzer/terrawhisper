Scutellaria Lateriflora

Scutellaria lateriflora, commonly known as blue cohosh, is a perennial herb native to North America and has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties.

It contains various bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and flavonoids, which are believed to contribute to its therapeutic effects.
