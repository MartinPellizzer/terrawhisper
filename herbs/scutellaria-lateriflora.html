Scutellaria Lateriflora Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Scutellaria lateriflora, commonly known as blue flag, is a perennial herb native to North America, characterized by its striking blue flowers and medicinal properties.

This herb has been traditionally used for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects, offering benefits such as reducing anxiety, improving sleep, and supporting respiratory health.

The therapeutic actions of blue flag are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and mucilage, which contribute to its antispasmodic and sedative properties.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves can be made from blue flag to harness its medicinal benefits.
