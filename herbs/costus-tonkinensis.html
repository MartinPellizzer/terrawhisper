Costus tonkinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These leaves are arranged in a distinct contorted pattern along the stem, featuring parallel venation and acute tips. The inflorescence consists of dense, cone-like structures that protect the developing flowers within colorful bracts. Once mature, the flowers emerge from these bracts to facilitate pollination.
