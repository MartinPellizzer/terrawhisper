Quassia (Quassia amara) Actions

Quassia amara exhibits several key therapeutic actions. It acts as a bitter tonic, stimulating digestion and appetite. It also has astringent properties that help reduce secretions and inflammation. Additionally, it serves as a diuretic and stimulant, aiding in cleansing the body and boosting energy. Its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects make it useful for digestive and respiratory conditions.
