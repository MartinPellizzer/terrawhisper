What's the taxonomical classification of Notholirion bulbuliferum?

Notholirion bulbuliferum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Liliales and the family Liliaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Notholirion and the specific epithet bulbuliferum, completing its scientific designation.
