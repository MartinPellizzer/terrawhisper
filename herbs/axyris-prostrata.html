Axyris prostrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is placed in the order Caryophyllales and the family Amaranthaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Axyris, with the specific epithet prostrata defining its species designation.
