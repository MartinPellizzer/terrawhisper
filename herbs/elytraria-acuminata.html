What is Elytraria acuminata?

Elytraria acuminata is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a short stem, often growing to 10-30 centimeters in height, and has basal leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape, measuring 5-12 centimeters in length and 2-5 centimeters in width. The leaves are dark green, slightly hairy, and have a pointed tip. The inflorescence is a terminal spike of purple flowers, each measuring 2-3 centimeters in length, with a prominent lip and two lateral petals.

The plant is native to tropical Africa and Asia, and is often found in moist, shaded areas.
