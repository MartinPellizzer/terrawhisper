Velvet Bean (Mucuna pruriens) Actions

Velvet Bean exhibits several therapeutic actions including tonic, stimulant, and anti-inflammatory properties. It also acts as an antispasmodic and diuretic, aiding digestion through carminative and astringent effects. Additionally, it has sedative and bitter qualities, while functioning as an expectorant to help clear respiratory secretions.
