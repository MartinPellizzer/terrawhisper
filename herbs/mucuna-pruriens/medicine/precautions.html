10 Precautions To Take When Using Mucuna Pruriens (Velvet Bean)

Mucuna pruriens has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as dosing cautiously and monitoring blood pressure and serotonin levels.

It's essential to take these precautions to avoid side effects like hypotension, anxiety, or serotonin syndrome, which can occur if taken in excess.

For example, failing to monitor blood pressure may lead to dizziness or lightheadedness, while neglecting to monitor serotonin levels may result in anxiety or tremors.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Mucuna pruriens medicinally.

1. Follow proper storage guidelines

When using Mucuna pruriens medicinally, it's important to take it under guidance.

This herb can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, which may lead to adverse effects or alter its efficacy. Additionally, Mucuna pruriens can cause stomach upset, nausea, and dizziness in some individuals.

Therefore, consulting with a healthcare professional is crucial to determine the appropriate dosage and ensure safe use, especially for people with pre-existing medical conditions or taking other medications.
