Tussilago Farfara

Tussilago farfara, commonly known as coltsfoot, is a perennial herb native to Europe and Asia, recognized for its unique appearance and medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Tussilago farfara include its ability to soothe coughs and respiratory issues due to its expectorant properties, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The therapeutic actions of the herb are attributed to its demulcent and mucilaginous properties, which help to protect and calm irritated tissues.

The bioactive constituents of Tussilago farfara include flavonoids, saponins, and mucilages, which contribute to its medicinal properties and make it a popular ingredient in traditional remedies for respiratory issues.
