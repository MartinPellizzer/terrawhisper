Mentha × timbalii

Mentha × timbalii, also known as the lime mint or lime balm, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the mint family and is a cross between Mentha aquatica and Mentha × villosa.

This plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include its ability to act as an antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial agent, making it useful for treating various health issues.

The health benefits of Mentha × timbalii include its ability to alleviate digestive problems, soothe respiratory issues, and provide relief from stress and anxiety.

The plant can be prepared for herbal use through the production of teas, infusions, and extracts, which can be used to take advantage of its medicinal properties.
