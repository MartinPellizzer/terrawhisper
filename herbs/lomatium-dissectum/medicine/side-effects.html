10 Possible Side Effects Of Lomatium Dissectum (Desert Parsley)

These side effects can occur due to its high alkaloid content, which can cause gastrointestinal issues if ingested in excess.

In severe cases, this can lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalances, potentially worsening pre-existing kidney or heart conditions, significantly impacting individuals' quality of life.
