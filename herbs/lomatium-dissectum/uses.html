Lomatium Dissectum (Lomatium dissectum) Uses

Lomatium dissectum is used to treat respiratory infections, bronchitis, cough, and mucus buildup. It helps alleviate symptoms of allergic rhinitis, influenza, and asthma by reducing inflammation and supporting lung function. It may also assist with sinusitis and sinus infections by clearing congestion and improving breathing.
