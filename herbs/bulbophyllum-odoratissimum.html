What's the taxonomical classification of Bulbophyllum odoratissimum?

Bulbophyllum odoratissimum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Bulbophyllum, and is identified by the unique species name odoratissimum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Bulbophyllum odoratissimum has creeping rhizomes with small, ovate pseudobulbs that bear single, leathery leaves at their apex. Each flower features a distinctively shaped labellum and delicate, yellowish petals that contribute to its intense scent.
