Asparagus Altilis (Asparagus Officinalis Var. Altilis)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Asparagus officinalis var. altilis.

Asparagus altilis, scientifically known as Asparagus officinalis var. altilis, is a medicinal herb widely recognized for its adaptogenic properties and historical use in traditional medicine. This variant of the common asparagus is valued for its ability to support overall health by enhancing vitality, reducing stress, and promoting longevity. Traditionally, it has been used in Chinese and Ayurvedic systems to improve digestion, detoxify the body, and strengthen the immune system. In modern wellness practices, Asparagus altilis is incorporated into supplements and herbal formulations to support hormonal balance, liver function, and skin health. Its unique feature lies in its high concentration of asparagine, an amino acid that aids in energy production, and its historical significance as a symbol of purity and renewal in ancient cultures.
