What's the taxonomical classification of Vitis piasezkii?

Vitis piasezkii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Vitales and into the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Vitis, with its specific identification completed at the species level as piasezkii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Vitis piasezkii has woody, climbing vines characterized by trifoliate or pentafoliate leaves with serrated margins and large, cordate bases.

The leaves are typically dark green and possess a somewhat rugose texture on the upper surface. Its stems are equipped with specialized tendrils that facilitate climbing by wrapping around surrounding vegetation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in compact panicles.

These blossoms eventually develop into small, globose berries that change color as they reach maturity.
