Archangelica (Angelica archangelica) Actions

Archangelica exhibits antispasmodic properties, helping to relieve muscle spasms. It acts as a tonic to strengthen the body and a carminative to reduce gas. Its bitter nature stimulates digestion, while its anti-inflammatory effects reduce swelling. It also functions as an expectorant, diuretic, and sedative, aiding respiratory and urinary health.
