Cyclopia (Cyclopia intermedia)

Cyclopia (Cyclopia intermedia) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to South Africa, Eastern Cape Province, and Western Cape Province. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and flowers have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
