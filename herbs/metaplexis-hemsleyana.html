Metaplexis hemsleyana

What's the taxonomical classification of Metaplexis hemsleyana?

Metaplexis hemsleyana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Gentianales, this plant is a member of the family Apocynaceae. It is further categorized under the genus Metaplexis, with the specific identifier being the species hemsleyana.
