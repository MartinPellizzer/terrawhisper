Laggera crispata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a specific member of the family Asteraceae, a large and diverse group of flowering plants, falling under the genus Laggera. Finally, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific classification as the species crispata.
