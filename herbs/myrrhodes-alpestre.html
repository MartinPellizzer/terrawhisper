    What is Myrrhodes alpestre?

    Myrrhodes alpestre is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Apiaceae.

    It has a taproot and a basal rosette of pinnate, bi- to tri-pinnate leaves, which are 5-12 cm long and 2-6 cm broad. The plant produces umbels of small, white or yellowish flowers, 5-7 mm in diameter, in late spring and early summer. Each umbel is subtended by 2-3 bracts, and the pedicels are 1-2 mm long.

    The plant grows up to 30-60 cm tall in alpine and subalpine habitats.
