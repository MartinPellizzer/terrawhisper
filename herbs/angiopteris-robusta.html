What's the taxonomical classification of Angiopteris robusta?

Angiopteris robusta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is situated under the subclass Marattiidae and falls within the order Marattiales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Marattiaceae, which contains the genus Angiopteris, ultimately identifying this specific plant as the species robusta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The plant lacks true leaves, instead utilizing its photosynthetic stems to capture sunlight for energy production. Small, specialized sporangia are arranged in dense, terminal clusters at the tips of the branches to facilitate reproduction.
