10 Precautions To Take When Using Paederia Foetida (Skunkvine)

Paederia foetida has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in moderation due to its strong fragrance and potential skin irritation.

Prolonged exposure to the herb's oils may cause contact dermatitis, leading to redness and itching.

It's essential to avoid applying it to broken skin or sensitive areas, as it can exacerbate existing conditions like eczema or allergic reactions.
