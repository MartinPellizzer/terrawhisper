What's the taxonomical classification of Ceratopteris thalictroides?

Ceratopteris thalictroides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Polypodiales and the family Pteridaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Ceratopteris, and its unique species designation, thalictroides.
