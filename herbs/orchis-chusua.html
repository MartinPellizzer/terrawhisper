Orchis chusua

What's the taxonomical classification of Orchis chusua?

Orchis chusua belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Asparagales. As a member of the family Orchidaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Orchis, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species chusua.
