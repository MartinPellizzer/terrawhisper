Asplenium sampsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Asplenium, with its unique designation being the species sampsonii.
