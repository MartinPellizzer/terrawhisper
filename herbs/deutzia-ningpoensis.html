What's the taxonomical classification of Deutzia ningpoensis?

Deutzia ningpoensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Cornales. It is further categorized under the family Hydrangeaceae, falling into the genus Deutzia, and is specifically identified by its species name, ningpoensis.
