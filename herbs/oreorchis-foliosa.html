Oreorchis foliosa

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Oreorchis foliosa has narrow, lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette and a single stem bearing an inflorescence of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant features a slender, upright flowering spike that can reach heights of several decimeters depending on the environment. Each flower possesses a distinct labellum that is often slightly larger than the other perianth segments. The vegetative structure is characterized by its relatively thin foliage which reflects its preference for specific light conditions. Its overall morphology is typical of many terrestrial orchid species found in specialized habitats.
