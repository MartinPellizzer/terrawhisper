What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex kwangtungensis?

Ilex kwangtungensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Ultimately, this plant is identified by the genus Ilex and is specifically designated as the species kwangtungensis.
