1. Relieves nausea symptoms

Nausea is a common condition characterized by an uneasy sensation in the stomach, often accompanied by the urge to vomit.

It can be caused by a variety of factors, including motion sickness, gastrointestinal infections, or even stress.

This condition is frequently experienced by individuals undergoing chemotherapy, those suffering from migraines, or people with inner ear disorders.

Mentha spicata, commonly known as peppermint, has been traditionally used to alleviate nausea due to its calming and digestive properties.

Its essential oils may help soothe the digestive system and reduce the discomfort associated with this condition.

How this herb helps with relieves nausea symptoms?

Mentha spicata relieves nausea symptoms because it contains bioactive constituents such as menthol, flavonoids, and alkaloids.

These compounds work together to soothe the gastrointestinal tract and reduce feelings of nausea. Menthol, in particular, has a calming effect on the digestive system. Flavonoids contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Alkaloids help regulate muscle contractions in the stomach, further alleviating nausea.
