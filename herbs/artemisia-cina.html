Artemisia Cina: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Artemisia cina, commonly known as wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers.

It has been traditionally used for its digestive and antimicrobial properties, offering health benefits such as aiding digestion, reducing inflammation, and supporting liver function.

The therapeutic actions of Artemisia cina include antiparasitic, antispasmodic, and choleretic effects, making it valuable in herbal medicine.

Its bioactive constituents include essential oils, flavonoids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal profile.
