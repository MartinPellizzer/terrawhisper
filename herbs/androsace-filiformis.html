What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace filiformis?

Androsace filiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Ericales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Primulaceae, specifically falling under the genus Androsace. Finally, its unique biological identity is completed by its specific designation as the species filiformis.
