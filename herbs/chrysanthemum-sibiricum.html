Chrysanthemum sibiricum

Chrysanthemum sibiricum, commonly known as the Siberian chrysanthemum or Korean chrysanthemum, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The therapeutic actions of Chrysanthemum sibiricum are attributed to its ability to calm the nervous system, reduce anxiety, and promote relaxation.
