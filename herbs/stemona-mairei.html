Stemona mairei

What's the taxonomical classification of Stemona mairei?

Stemona mairei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Pandanales and is a member of the family Stemonaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Stemona and the species mairei.
