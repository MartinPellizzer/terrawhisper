Brassaiopsis glomerulata

What's the taxonomical classification of Brassaiopsis glomerulata?

Brassaiopsis glomerulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Araliaceae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Brassaiopsis, and its unique species designation, glomerulata.
