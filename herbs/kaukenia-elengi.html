Kaukenia Elengi

Kaukenia elengi, commonly known as the Indian banyan or Vitex negundo, is a versatile medicinal plant widely used in Ayurvedic and traditional systems of medicine.

It is valued for its various parts, including leaves, flowers, and roots, which are employed to treat a range of ailments such as skin disorders, respiratory issues, and digestive problems. The plant contains bioactive compounds like flavonoids, alkaloids, and tannins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties. In traditional practices, it is often used as a remedy for inflammation, fever, and even as a mild sedative.

Due to its broad pharmacological potential, Kaukenia elengi continues to be a subject of scientific research for its possible applications in modern medicine.
