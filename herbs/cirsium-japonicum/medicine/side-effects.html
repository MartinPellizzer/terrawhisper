10 Possible Side Effects Of Cirsium Japonicum (Japanese Thistle)

Cirsium japonicum, also known as Japanese thistle, has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions and gastrointestinal problems.

Causes include consuming excessive amounts or taking it without proper preparation. These side effects can worsen people's lives, leading to digestive issues, skin irritation, and even respiratory problems.

For example, severe allergic reactions can cause anaphylaxis, a potentially life-threatening condition.
