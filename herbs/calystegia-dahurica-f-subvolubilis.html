Calystegia dahurica f. subvolubilis

Calystegia dahurica f. subvolubilis, a lesser-known variety of the Calystegia dahurica plant, is a type of flowering vine native to certain parts of Asia, often referred to as a form of the Dahurian vine.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The plant has been used to treat various health issues, including fever, sore throats, and respiratory problems, offering benefits such as immune system support and reduced inflammation.

Herbal preparations of Calystegia dahurica f. subvolubilis typically involve drying the leaves and stems, then making tea or infusions, or using them in traditional medicine formulas.
