Small-Leaf Lime (Tilia platyphyllos) Compounds

The primary compounds in Small-Leaf Lime include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and digestive qualities. Coumarin and terpenoids provide antimicrobial benefits, while glycosides and anthocyanins support cardiovascular health. Alkaloids may influence nervous system function.
