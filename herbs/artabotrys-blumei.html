What's the taxonomical classification of Artabotrys blumei?

Artabotrys blumei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, falling under the order Magnoliales. The plant is a member of the family Annonaceae, specifically categorized under the genus Artabotrys, and is identified by the species name bluemi.

      What side-effects this plant can have?
