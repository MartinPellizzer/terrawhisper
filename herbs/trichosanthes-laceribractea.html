Trichosanthes laceribractea

What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes laceribractea?

Trichosanthes laceribractea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Cucurbitales. It is further categorized into the family Cucurbitaceae and is a member of the genus Trichosanthes, specifically identified by the species name laceribractea.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Trichosanthes laceribractea has prostrate, herbaceous stems that feature serrated or lacerate bracts and small, inconspicuous flowers.

The foliage is typically characterized by slender, lanceolate leaves that grow along the creeping vines. Its reproductive structures are small and often tucked within the leaf axils, reflecting its climbing habit. The plant develops specialized tendrils that allow it to anchor itself to surrounding vegetation.
