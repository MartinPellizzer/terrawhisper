What is Malaxis macrostachya?

Malaxis macrostachya is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae, native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, green, and leafy stems that grow up to 30-40 cm in height, with 2-4 linear-lanceolate leaves that are up to 10-15 cm long. The plant produces a single, greenish-yellow flower spike, 10-15 cm long, that bears 10-15 small, greenish-yellow flowers with a yellow lip and a white mid-lobe. Each flower is 0.8-1.2 cm long and has a 1.2-1.5 cm long spur.

The seeds are small, brown, and irregularly shaped, approximately 0.2-0.3 cm in diameter.
