Scutellaria omeiensis var. serratifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria omeiensis var. serratifolia?

Scutelariia omeiensis var. serratifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Lamiaceae. This specific botanical entity is categorized under the genus Scutellaria, eventually reaching its precise taxonomic designation as the variety serratifolia within the species Scutellaria omeiensis.
