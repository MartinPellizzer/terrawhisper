Habenaria diphylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically situated within the genus Habeanaria, and is identified by the unique species epithet diphylla.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Habenaria diphylla has a slender, unbranched stem that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and bears a pair of opposite, lanceolate leaves located near the middle.

The inflorescence is a terminal raceme containing several small, white to greenish-white flowers. Each flower possesses a long, nectar-producing spur and complex labellum structures typical of the orchid family.
