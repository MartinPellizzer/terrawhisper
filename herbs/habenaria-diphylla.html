Habenaria diphylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Habenaria diphylla?

Habenaria diphylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically situated within the genus Habeanaria, and is identified by the unique species epithet diphylla.

What diseases this plant treats?

Local practitioners utilize the plant's properties to soothe digestive discomfort and expel parasites from the gut.
