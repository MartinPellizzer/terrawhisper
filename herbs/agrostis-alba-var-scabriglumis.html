Agrostis alba var. scabriglumis

Agrostis alba var. scabriglumis, also known as scabrous bent grass, is a type of grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

Traditionally, this plant has been used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Agrostis alba var. scabriglumis include its ability to aid in wound healing, reduce fever, and alleviate respiratory issues.
