Hydrocotyle wilsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its order, Apiales, the plant is further assigned to the family Araliaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed through its classification within the genus Hydrocotyle, specifically as the species wilsonii.
