What is Haplopteris stenophylla?

Haplopteris stenophylla is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae.

It is a rhizomatous fern with a creeping habit, producing long, thin rhizomes and sterile leaves that are smaller than fertile leaves. The fertile leaves are lanceolate to ovate in shape, with a serrated margin and a pinnate venation pattern, and bear sori on the lower surface. Each sorus contains numerous sporangia, which produce spores for reproduction.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded areas.
