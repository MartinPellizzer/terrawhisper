What's the taxonomical classification of Paraquilegia anemonoides?

Paraquilegia anemonoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Paraquilegia and the species anemonoides.
