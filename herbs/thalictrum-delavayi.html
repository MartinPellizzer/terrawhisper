What's the taxonomical classification of Thalictrum delavayi?

Thalictrum delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, which is further categorized into the genus Thalictrum, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species delavayi.
