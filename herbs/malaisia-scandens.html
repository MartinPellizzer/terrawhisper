What's the taxonomical classification of Malaisia scandens?

Malaisia scandens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized under Equisetopsida, which further descends into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is part of the order Rosales and is a member of the family Moraceae. Ultimately, its taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Malaisia, specifically as the species scandens.
