Saxifraga cordillerarum var. bonplandii

Saxifraga cordillerarum var. bonplandii, also known as the Bonpland saxifrage, is a plant species within the saxifrage family, characterized by its white or pale yellow flowers and grayish-green leaves.

The plant is used in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

Saxifraga cordillerarum var. bonplandii has been reported to have various health benefits, such as reducing fever and alleviating respiratory issues, making it a valuable herbal remedy for colds and flu.
