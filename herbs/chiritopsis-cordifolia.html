Chiritopsis cordifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Chiritopsis cordifolia?

Chiritopsis cordifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Lamiales. It is further categorized under the family Gesneriaceae, specifically falling into the genus Chiritopsis, with its unique identity defined by the species epithet cordifolia.
