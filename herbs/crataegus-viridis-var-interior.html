Green Hawthorn (Crataegus Viridis Var. Interior)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Crataegus viridis var. interior.

Crataegus viridis var. interior, commonly known as the interior green hawthorn, is a lesser-known variety of hawthorn that has been historically used in traditional medicine for its cardiovascular benefits. This plant contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and oligomeric procyanidins, which are known to support heart health by improving circulation and reducing oxidative stress. In herbal medicine, it is often used to treat symptoms of heart failure, hypertension, and arrhythmia due to its ability to strengthen cardiac muscle function. The fruit and leaves of Crataegus viridis var. interior are typically prepared as tinctures or teas to harness its therapeutic properties. While research on its medicinal potential is still emerging, its use in complementary and alternative medicine continues to be explored for its natural cardiovascular support.
