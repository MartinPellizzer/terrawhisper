What's the taxonomical classification of Tetradium daniellii?

Tetradium daniellii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its evolutionary lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it under the order Sapindales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Rutaceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Tetradium, with its unique identification designated as the species daniellii.
