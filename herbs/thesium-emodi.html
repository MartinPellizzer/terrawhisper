Thesium emodi

What's the taxonomical classification of Thesium emodi?

Thesium emodi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant follows the order Santalales and is a member of the family Santalaceae. More specifically, it is identified under the genus Thesium, with its unique classification completed at the species level as Thesium emodi.

    What diseases this plant treats?

When ingested or prepared in specific decoctions, it is believed to aid in alleviating stomach pains and intestinal discomfort.     Thesium emodi is prepared as a decoction, an infusion, or a topical paste using its various plant parts. In some traditional practices, the plant material is crushed into a fine pulp to be applied directly to the skin. This topical application is often utilized to address localized inflammation or skin irritations. These different methods allow practitioners to tailor the delivery of the plant's active constituents to the specific ailment being treated.
