Pedicularis longiflora var. tubiformis

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis longiflora var. tubiformis?

Pedicularis longiflora var. tubiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Orobanchaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Pedicularis, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety tubiformis within the species Pedicularis longiflora.
