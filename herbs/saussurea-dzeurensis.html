What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea dzeurensis?

Saussurea dzeurensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized into the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, specifically belonging to the genus Saussurea, and is identified at the species level as dzeurensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Saussurea dzeurensis has a perennial herbaceous habit characterized by a thick, woody rhizome and basal leaves that are typically lanceolate or oblong with entire or slightly dentate margins. Its inflorescence consists of several heads clustered in a dense, terminal panicle, with each individual flower head protected by involucral bracts. The florets themselves are tubular and possess a vibrant color, often ranging from yellow to light purple depending on the specific variety.
