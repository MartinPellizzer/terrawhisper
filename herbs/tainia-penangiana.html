What's the taxonomical classification of Tainia penangiana?

Tainia penangiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Asparagales, the plant is a member of the family Orchidaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy is completed by its designation within the genus Tainia, specifically identifying it as the species penangiana.
