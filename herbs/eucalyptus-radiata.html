Radiata Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus radiata)

Radiata Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus radiata) is a member of the Myrtaceae family, native to South Australia, Victoria, and New South Wales. Traditionally, its leaves, bark, and resin have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
