What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes jinggangshanica?

Trichosanthes jinggangshanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Cucurbitales. It is further categorized within the family Cucurbitaceae, placing it among a diverse group of flowering plants. Specifically, it is situated in the genus Trichosanthes, with its unique identity defined by the species epithet jinggangshanica.
