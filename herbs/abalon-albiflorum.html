What is Abalon albiflorum?

Abalon albiflorum is a perennial herbaceous plant, belonging to the family Melanthiaceae.

It has linear-lanceolate to elliptic leaves, 10-40 cm long and 2-7 cm wide, that are alternate, entire, and glabrous. The plant produces a terminal, racemose inflorescence with white to pale yellow flowers, 4-5 mm long, that are 3-merous and 6-8 mm in diameter, with 3 petals and 3 sepals. The flowers are fragrant and borne on a peduncle 10-30 cm long, which is glabrous and terete.

The plant grows in shaded, humid areas at elevations of 900-2,500 meters.
