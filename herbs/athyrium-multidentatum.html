What's the taxonomical classification of Athyrium multidentatum?

Athyrium multidentatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Polypodiales. This fern is specifically situated within the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Athyrium, and is identified by its unique species designation, multidentatum.
