Iris potaninii var. ionantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Iris potaninii var. ionantha?

Iris potaninii var. ionantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the order Asparagales and falls under the family Iridaeaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Iris, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety Iris potaninii var. ionantha.
