Cleisostoma striatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Cleisostoma striatum?

Cleisostoma striatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is further categorized within the family Orchidaceae, specifically belonging to the genus Cleisostoma, and is identified by its unique species name, striatum.
