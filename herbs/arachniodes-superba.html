What is Arachniodes superba?

Arachniodes superba is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It has a rhizome with long, thin, and brittle stipes bearing lanceolate to ovate, pinnate fronds, typically 30-80 cm long, with 20-30 pairs of pinnules. The fronds are green on top and pale green on the underside, with a rachis that is pale green and hairless. The stipes are also pale green and hairless.

This species is native to China.
