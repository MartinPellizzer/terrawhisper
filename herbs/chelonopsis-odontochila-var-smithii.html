Chelonopsis odontochila var. smithii

What's the taxonomical classification of Chelonopsis odontochila var. smithii?

Chelonopsis odontochila var. smithii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized under Equisetopsida, which leads into its placement in the order Lamiales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Lamiaceae and the genus Chelonopsis. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its status as the variety smithii within the species Chelonopsis odontochila.
