What's the taxonomical classification of Stauntonia cavalerieana?

Stauntonia cavalerieana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its evolutionary path continues through the order Ranunculales, placing it within the family Lardizabalaeceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Stauntonia, and its unique species designation, cavalerieana.
