Not heeding this warning can lead to severe consequences, especially for individuals with pre-existing heart conditions.

1. Dispose of properly always

When using Banisteriopsis caapi medicinally, it's important to take under supervision due to the potential for adverse effects and interactions with other medications.

As a powerful plant-based psychedelic, ayahuasca can cause intense physical and emotional responses, including nausea, vomiting, and anxiety, which can be overwhelming if not properly monitored. Additionally, combining ayahuasca with certain medications or substances can increase the risk of serious adverse reactions.

Therefore, it's crucial to work with a qualified practitioner who can provide guidance and support throughout the treatment process.
