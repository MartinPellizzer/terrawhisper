Peristylus affinis

What's the taxonomical classification of Peristylus affinis?

Peristylus affinis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and into the family Orchidaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Peristylus, with its specific designation being the species affinis.
