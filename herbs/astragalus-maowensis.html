What is Astragalus maowensis?

Astragalus maowensis is a perennial legume species in the family Fabaceae, native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It grows to a height of 10-40 cm, with a slender stem bearing 5-10 leaflets that are 10-20 mm long and pinnately compound. The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme with 10-20 flowers, each 10-15 mm long, with purple or blue-purple corolla and yellow anthers. The fruit is a pod, 5-10 mm long, containing 2-3 seeds, which are 4-5 mm long and brown in color.

The plant is found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at elevations of 3200-4200 meters.
