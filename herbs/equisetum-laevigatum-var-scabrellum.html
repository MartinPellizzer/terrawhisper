Smooth Horsetail (Equisetum Laevigatum Var. Scabrellum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Equisetum laevigatum var. scabrellum.

Smooth horsetail, scientifically known as Equisetum laevigatum var. scabrellum, is a rare medicinal herb with a long history of use in traditional medicine. It is valued for its high concentration of silica, which supports joint health, bone strength, and skin integrity, along with its adaptogenic properties that help the body manage stress. Historically, it has been used in European folk medicine to treat urinary tract infections, respiratory issues, and as a diuretic. In modern wellness practices, it is incorporated into supplements and herbal blends to promote detoxification and enhance physical performance. Notably, its unique silica content and historical significance in herbal traditions make it a distinctive and sought-after plant in natural health circles.
