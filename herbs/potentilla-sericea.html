What's the taxonomical classification of Potentilla sericea?

Potentiilla sericea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Potentiella, with its specific designation being the species sericea.
