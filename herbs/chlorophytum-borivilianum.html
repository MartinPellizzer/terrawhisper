Chlorophytum Borivilianum (Chlorophytum borivilianum)

Chlorophytum Borivilianum (Chlorophytum borivilianum) is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family, native to Himalayas, India, and Nepal. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and rhizomes have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and stimulant actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
