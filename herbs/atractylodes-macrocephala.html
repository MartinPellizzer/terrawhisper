Atractylodes Macrocephala Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Atractylodes macrocephala, commonly known as *Chinese Atractylodes*, is a perennial herb native to China, widely used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It is valued for its ability to regulate digestion, reduce dampness, and strengthen the spleen, offering benefits such as improved digestion, reduced bloating, and enhanced immune function.

The herb contains bioactive constituents like volatile oils, alkaloids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

It can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effects.
