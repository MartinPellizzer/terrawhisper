Atractylodes Macrocephala

Atractylodes macrocephala, also known as Atractylodes rhizome, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb widely used for its drying and spleen-strengthening properties.

It is commonly employed to treat conditions such as dampness-related diarrhea, edema, and respiratory issues like coughs and asthma. The rhizome contains various bioactive compounds, including sesquiterpenes and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. In traditional medicine, it is often combined with other herbs to enhance its efficacy in treating digestive and respiratory disorders.

Due to its adaptogenic properties, it is also used to support immune function and reduce fatigue.

Health Benefits

Atractylodes macrocephala has several health benefits, such as aiding in digestion and reducing gastrointestinal discomfort.

It is known to support the immune system by enhancing the body's natural defenses against infections. This herb also helps in managing symptoms of respiratory conditions like coughs and bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory properties. Additionally, it may help regulate blood sugar levels and improve metabolic functions.

Overall, Atractylodes macrocephala is a valuable traditional remedy that contributes to holistic wellness.
