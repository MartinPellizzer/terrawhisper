Atractylodes macrocephala, commonly known as *Chinese Atractylodes*, is a perennial herb native to East Asia, valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

The bioactive constituents include volatile oils, alkaloids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and immunomodulatory effects.

It can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is often used in herbal formulas to support overall wellness.
