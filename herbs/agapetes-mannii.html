Agapetes mannii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Agapetes mannii has large, elliptical to oblong leaves that are dark green, glossy, and arranged in an opposite pattern along its woody stems. The foliage typically features prominent venation and a smooth, entire margin. This species produces distinctive bell-shaped flowers that emerge from the leaf axils. These blooms are often characterized by their deep pigmentation and delicate, waxy texture. The overall growth habit is that of a compact shrub with a spreading structure.
