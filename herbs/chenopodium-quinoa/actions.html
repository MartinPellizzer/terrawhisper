Quinoa (Chenopodium quinoa) Actions

Quinoa acts as a tonic to boost vitality and energy. It has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and pain. It also functions as a diuretic to support kidney health. Additionally, it is a mild stimulant and carminative, aiding digestion and relieving gas.
