What is Tragacantha tomentosa?

Tragacantha tomentosa is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legumes.

It has a woody base with a height of up to 60 cm, featuring a single stem with a few branches, and a taproot. The leaves are alternate, bipinnate, and have 2-4 pairs of pinnae, each with 8-12 leaflets that are 1-3 cm long and elliptical in shape. The inflorescence is a spike with 10-20 flowers, each 7-10 mm in diameter, with a calyx of five fused sepals and a corolla of five distinct petals, and produces a pod that is 4-6 cm long and contains 2-4 seeds.

The plant has a hairy tomentose stem and leaves, which are also glandular hairy.
