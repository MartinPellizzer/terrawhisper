Pinus tabuliformis var. henryi

What's the taxonomical classification of Pinus tabuliformis var. henryi?

Pinus tabuliformis var. henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the order Pinales. It further falls into the family Pinaceae and is part of the genus Pinus. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific taxonomic designation as the species Pinus tabuliformis var. henryi.
