Vicia amoena

What's the taxonomical classification of Vicia amoena?

Vicia amoena belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the biological order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. At the most specific taxonomic levels, it is categorized under the genus Vicia, with the specific epithet amoena, completing its scientific designation as Vicia amoena.
