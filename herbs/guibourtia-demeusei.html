Guibourtia demeusei

What's the taxonomical classification of Guibourtia demeusei?

Guibourtia demeusei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Fabales. It is a representative of the family Fabaceae, falling specifically under the genus Guibourtia, with its unique biological identity defined by its species designation, demeusei.
