Filifolium sibiricum

What's the taxonomical classification of Filifolium sibiricum?

Filifolium sibiricum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the biological hierarchy, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic ranks, this plant is situated in the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Filifolium and the species sibiricum.
