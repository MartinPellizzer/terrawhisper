Blue Ginger (Curcuma aeruginosa) Compounds

The primary compounds in Blue Ginger include terpenoids, which contribute to its aromatic properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Essential oils provide its distinctive scent, while phenolic acids and glycosides support anti-inflammatory actions. Coumarin and tannins add to its medicinal profile, and saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins contribute to various therapeutic benefits.
