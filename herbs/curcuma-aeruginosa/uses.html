Blue Ginger (Curcuma aeruginosa) Uses

Blue Ginger is used to treat various skin conditions such as infections, irritations, and inflammatory issues. It aids in wound healing and reduces symptoms of acne, psoriasis, and skin allergies. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties make it effective for managing skin itching and inflammation.

Blue Ginger (Curcuma aeruginosa) Monograph

1. Skin infection

Blue Ginger, or Curcuma aeruginosa, is used topically to treat skin infections due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce bacterial growth and soothe inflamed tissues, making it effective for conditions like fungal infections and minor skin wounds. Its active compounds support natural healing and prevent secondary infections.

Blue Ginger, or Curcuma aeruginosa, is used topically to treat skin irritation due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
