Side Effects

Turnera diffusa var aphrodisiaca can have some side effects, such as gastrointestinal discomfort, including nausea and vomiting, due to its high concentration of alkaloids.

Additionally, it can lead to increased heart rate and anxiety in some users, especially at higher doses.

As with any herbal supplement, it is important to consult a healthcare professional before use to minimize risks.
