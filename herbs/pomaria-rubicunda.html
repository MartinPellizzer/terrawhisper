Pomaria rubicunda

Pomaria rubicunda, also known as the Redflower Pomaria, is a plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family.

Traditionally, the plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The health benefits of Pomaria rubicunda include its potential to reduce fever, alleviate digestive issues, and support the immune system.

Pomaria rubicunda can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, making it a versatile herb used in various herbal preparations.
