10 Precautions To Take When Using Pfaffia Paniculata (Jambu)

Pfaffia paniculata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional, particularly for individuals with bleeding disorders or those taking anticoagulant medications.

Failing to do so may lead to increased bleeding or interactions with medications. For instance, consuming large amounts of Pfaffia paniculata without proper guidance may cause gastrointestinal issues or interactions with blood thinners.

It's essential to adhere to recommended dosages and potential contraindications.
