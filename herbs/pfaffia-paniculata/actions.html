Pfaffia (Pfaffia paniculata) Actions

Pfaffia paniculata is known for its tonic and stimulant properties, which help boost energy and overall vitality. It also exhibits anti-inflammatory and diuretic effects, supporting immune function and fluid balance. Additionally, it acts as a carminative, aiding digestion, and has antispasmodic and expectorant qualities to relieve respiratory and muscular discomfort.
