Pfaffia (Pfaffia paniculata) Compounds

The primary compounds of Pfaffia paniculata include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and saponins, which may support immune function. Phenolic acids and terpenoids contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects. Glycosides and tannins provide additional antioxidant and antimicrobial benefits, while coumarin and alkaloids may influence hormonal balance. Essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its overall therapeutic potential.
