Pfaffia (Pfaffia paniculata) Uses

Pfaffia paniculata is used to treat asthma and respiratory infections by reducing inflammation and supporting lung function. It also aids in wound healing and managing skin infections, acne, and inflammatory skin conditions. Additionally, it helps alleviate fatigue by boosting energy levels and overall vitality.
