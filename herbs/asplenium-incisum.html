What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium incisum?

Asplenium incisum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This plant is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, with its specific designation being the species incisum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The texture of the blades is relatively thin and membranous, allowing for efficient light absorption in shaded habitats.
