Coelogyne leucantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Coelogyne leucantha?

Coelogyne leucantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Coelogyne, with its unique species designation being leucantha.
