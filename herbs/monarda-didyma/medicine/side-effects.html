10 Possible Side Effects Of Monarda Didyma (Bee Balm)

Monarda didyma has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, allergic reactions, and interactions with medications.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption, allergic sensitization, or interactions with other herbs and medications.

In severe cases, gastrointestinal upset can lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalances, causing symptoms like nausea, vomiting, and fatigue, which can worsen people's quality of life and even lead to hospitalization.
