Mandarin Orange (Citrus reticulata)

Mandarin Orange (Citrus reticulata) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to Southern China, Eastern Asia, and East Asia. Traditionally, its fruits, leaves, and flowers have been used for infusions, decoctions, and culinary uses.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.

Flowers
