Cephaelis Blue Poppy (Cephaelis acuminata) Actions

Cephaelis Blue Poppy exhibits several primary therapeutic actions. It acts as a bitter tonic and stimulant, enhancing digestion and energy. It also has anti-inflammatory and diuretic properties, aiding in reducing swelling and supporting kidney function. Additionally, it serves as a carminative, expectorant, and antispasmodic, helping with gas, coughs, and muscle spasms. Its astringent and sedative effects can soothe irritation and promote calm.
