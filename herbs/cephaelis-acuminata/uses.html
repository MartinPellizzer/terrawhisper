Cephaelis Blue Poppy (Cephaelis acuminata) Uses

Cephaelis acuminata is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and asthma. It helps alleviate symptoms of respiratory tract infections and mucous buildup. The plant is also used for allergic rhinitis and lung infections, including tuberculosis, due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Cephaelis Blue Poppy (Cephaelis acuminata) Monograph

1. Cough

Cephaelis acuminata is traditionally used to alleviate coughs due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce irritation in the respiratory tract. Often prepared as a tea, it is valued for its soothing effect on coughing fits. Its use is common in herbal medicine for respiratory support.
