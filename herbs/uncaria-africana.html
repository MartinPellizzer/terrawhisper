What's the taxonomical classification of Uncaria africana?

Uncaria africana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is categorized under the order Gentianales and the family Rubiaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Uncaria, and its specific species name, africana.
