10 Health Benefits Of Angelica Sinensis (Dong Quai)

Angelica sinensis, also known as dong quai, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and estrogen-modulating properties.

These medicinal properties contribute to its ability to alleviate menstrual cramps, menopausal symptoms, and cardiovascular issues.

For example, dong quai has been used to treat hot flashes and night sweats in postmenopausal women, improving their quality of life and reducing the discomfort associated with menopause.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Angelica sinensis.

1. Alleviates menopause symptoms

Angelica sinensis alleviates menopause symptoms because of its rich content of phytoestrogens and isoflavones.

These plant compounds mimic the effects of estrogen in the body, helping to regulate hormonal imbalances that often occur during menopause.

As a result, dong quai has been traditionally used to alleviate hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and vaginal dryness associated with menopause, providing relief for many women seeking natural solutions to manage these symptoms.

2. Eases menstrual cramps

Angelica sinensis eases menstrual cramps because it has a unique ability to relax uterine muscles and reduce inflammation.

Its flavonoids and lignans work together to block the production of prostaglandins, which are hormone-like substances that cause uterine contractions and pain.

Additionally, dong quai's anti-inflammatory properties help to reduce swelling and discomfort associated with menstrual cramps, providing relief from dysmenorrhea and other period-related issues.

3. Regulates menstrual cycle

Angelica sinensis regulates menstrual cycle because of its ability to balance the body's energy and restore hormonal harmony.

Its active compounds, such as ferulic acid and angelicin, help to normalize ovulation and menstruation by stimulating the pituitary gland and uterus, promoting progesterone production, and reducing inflammation and cramping.

This natural remedy has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to alleviate menstrual irregularities and symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS).

4. Improves blood circulation

Angelica sinensis improves blood circulation because it contains a unique combination of bioactive compounds that stimulate blood vessel dilation and reduce blood viscosity.

Specifically, its flavonoids and lignans help to relax vascular smooth muscle, increase blood flow, and decrease platelet aggregation.

This results in improved oxygen delivery to tissues, reduced inflammation, and enhanced overall cardiovascular health, making dong quai a popular herb for promoting circulatory wellness.

5. Reduces blood pressure

Angelica sinensis reduces blood pressure because it has a direct effect on vascular smooth muscle relaxation, causing blood vessels to dilate and thereby decreasing peripheral resistance.

Additionally, dong quai has been shown to increase nitric oxide production, which is a potent vasodilator that further contributes to the decrease in blood pressure.

This natural herb has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine to treat hypertension and related cardiovascular disorders.

6. Alleviates migraines

Angelica sinensis alleviates migraines because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

It is believed to help reduce inflammation in the blood vessels that can cause migraine pain. Additionally, dong quai may help regulate hormonal imbalances, which can contribute to migraines.

Its flavonoids and alkaloids may also act as natural pain relievers, providing relief from headache symptoms.

7. Boosts immune system

Angelica sinensis boosts immune system because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

These compounds help to neutralize free radicals, reduce oxidative stress, and inflammation in the body. By doing so, dong quai enhances the body's natural defense mechanisms, increasing the production of white blood cells and activating the lymphocytes, which work together to fight off pathogens and prevent illnesses.

This results in a stronger immune system that is better equipped to combat infections and diseases.

8. Increases energy levels

Angelica sinensis increases energy levels because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains ferulic acid, a powerful antioxidant that helps to improve blood flow and oxygenation to the body's tissues, thereby boosting energy production.

Additionally, dong quai has been shown to regulate cortisol levels, reducing stress and fatigue while promoting a sense of vitality and well-being.

9. Improves bone health

Angelica sinensis improves bone health because it has been shown to increase calcium absorption and density in bones.

The herb's flavonoids and lignans have been found to stimulate the production of osteoblasts, the cells responsible for building new bone tissue, while also inhibiting the activity of osteoclasts, which break down bone.

Additionally, dong quai has anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce inflammation in joints and bones, further supporting overall bone health.

10. Reduces inflammation

Angelica sinensis reduces inflammation because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's roots contain flavonoids, lignans, and terpenoids that possess potent anti-inflammatory properties. Dong quai has been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, such as TNF-α and IL-1β, while also enhancing the activity of anti-inflammatory enzymes, like COX-2 inhibitors.

This dual action helps to reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms associated with various chronic conditions.
