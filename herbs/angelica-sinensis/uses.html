Chinese Angelica (Angelica sinensis) Uses

Chinese Angelica is used to treat menstrual cramps, postpartum recovery, menopausal symptoms, and anemia. It supports lactation and helps with digestive disorders and gastrointestinal spasms. It is valued for its ability to ease menstrual pain and promote overall women's health.

Chinese Angelica (Angelica sinensis) Monograph

1. Menstrual cramps

Chinese Angelica is used to alleviate menstrual cramps by promoting blood circulation and regulating hormonal balance. It helps reduce pain and inflammation associated with dysmenorrhea. Its active compounds support uterine function and ease discomfort during menstruation.

2. Postpartum recovery

Chinese Angelica is widely used in postpartum recovery to promote blood circulation, reduce swelling, and support uterine healing. It helps replenish blood lost during childbirth and alleviates symptoms like abdominal pain and fatigue. Its warming properties also aid in restoring energy and preventing postpartum complications.

3. Menopausal symptoms

Chinese Angelica is used to alleviate menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes and mood swings due to its estrogen-like compounds. It helps balance hormonal fluctuations and supports emotional stability during menopause. Often combined with other herbs, it is valued for its gentle yet effective action on the body.

4. Lactation support

Chinese Angelica is used to support lactation by promoting breast milk production. It is traditionally used in herbal formulas to enhance milk flow and address low milk supply in nursing mothers. Its effects are attributed to its ability to regulate qi and blood circulation in the mammary glands.

5. Menopause symptoms

Chinese Angelica is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate menopause symptoms such as hot flashes and night sweats. It is believed to nourish the blood and regulate the liver, helping to balance hormonal fluctuations. Its mild warming properties support overall hormonal harmony during this transition.

6. Menstrual pain

Chinese Angelica is used to alleviate menstrual pain by promoting blood circulation and reducing inflammation. It helps ease cramps and discomfort associated with dysmenorrhea. Its properties support uterine health and are often included in traditional formulas for women's health.

7. Anemia

Chinese Angelica is used to treat anemia due to its rich content of iron, hemoglobin, and other nutrients that support red blood cell production. It helps improve blood circulation and alleviate symptoms like fatigue and pale complexion. Its traditional use in herbal medicine aligns with modern understanding of its role in addressing deficiencies associated with anemia.

8. Digestive disorders

Chinese Angelica is used to treat digestive disorders by promoting healthy digestion and reducing bloating. It helps regulate gastrointestinal function and may alleviate symptoms like indigestion and abdominal discomfort. Its active compounds support gut health and improve nutrient absorption.

9. Digestive issues

Chinese Angelica is used to aid digestion by promoting the movement of qi and reducing stagnation. It helps relieve symptoms like bloating, indigestion, and abdominal discomfort. Its mild warming properties support healthy gastrointestinal function. It is often combined with other herbs in formulas tailored for digestive health.

10. Gastrointestinal spasms

Chinese Angelica is used to alleviate gastrointestinal spasms due to its antispasmodic properties. It helps relax smooth muscle in the digestive tract, reducing cramping and discomfort. This effect is attributed to its compounds like ligustilide and butylphthalide, which influence gastrointestinal motility. It is commonly prescribed in traditional Chinese medicine for digestive disorders involving spasms.
