13 Best Angelica Sinensis Benefits

1. Reduces inflammation

Inflammatory conditions involve persistent swelling, pain, and tissue damage due to an overactive immune response.

Chronic inflammation is a common health issue that can affect various organs and systems in the body.

It is often caused by prolonged exposure to irritants, autoimmune reactions, or chronic infections.

This condition is commonly experienced by individuals with conditions like arthritis, digestive disorders, or metabolic syndromes.

Angelica sinensis, also known as dong quai, has been traditionally used to reduce inflammation by modulating immune responses and supporting anti-inflammatory pathways in the body.

How this herb helps with reduces inflammation?

Angelica sinensis reduces inflammation because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

These compounds work by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes like cyclooxygenase-2 (COX-2). Alkaloids, such as ligustilide and butylphthalide, have been shown to modulate immune responses and reduce oxidative stress. Flavonoids in angelica sinensis possess antioxidant properties that further support anti-inflammatory effects.

Together, these compounds contribute to the plant's traditional use in treating inflammatory conditions.

According to the study, Angelica sinensis has a positive effect in reducing inflammation, as it showed notable anti-inflammatory effects and inhibited the production of various pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes.
