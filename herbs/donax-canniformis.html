What's the taxonomical classification of Donax canniformis?

Donax canniformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Zingiberales and is a member of the family Marantaceae. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Donax, and its specific designation is the species canniformis.
