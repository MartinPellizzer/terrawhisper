Canadian Collinsonia (Collinsonia canadensis) Uses

Canadian Collinsonia is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and respiratory tract infections. It helps reduce congestion, clear mucus, and soothe inflammation in the respiratory tract and mucous membranes. It is also used for tonsillitis and lung congestion due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.
