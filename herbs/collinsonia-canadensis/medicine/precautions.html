10 Precautions To Take When Using Collinsonia Canadensis (Stone Root)

Collinsonia canadensis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional to determine a safe dosage and usage.

It's essential to take these precautions to avoid side effects, including gastrointestinal upset and allergic reactions.

For example, taking high doses can lead to stomach cramps, diarrhea, and nausea, while allergic reactions can cause skin irritation and respiratory issues.
