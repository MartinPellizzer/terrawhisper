Canadian Collinsonia (Collinsonia canadensis) Actions

Canadian Collinsonia exhibits several key therapeutic actions including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and carminative effects. It also acts as an expectorant, astringent, and diuretic, while providing sedative and antispasmodic properties. Additionally, it is considered a stimulant and bitter tonic, supporting digestion and respiratory health.
