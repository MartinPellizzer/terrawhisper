Canadian Collinsonia (Collinsonia canadensis) Compounds

The primary compounds in Canadian Collinsonia include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory qualities, while coumarin may support circulation. Glycosides, saponins, and terpenoids also play roles in its medicinal properties, along with essential oils, alkaloids, and anthocyanins.
