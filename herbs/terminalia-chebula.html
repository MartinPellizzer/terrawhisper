Chebuli (Terminalia chebula)

Chebuli (Terminalia chebula) is a member of the Combretaceae family, native to India, Myanmar, and Bangladesh. Traditionally, its fruits, bark, and resin have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and unani medicine.
