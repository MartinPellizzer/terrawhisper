Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp. pedunculatus

What's the taxonomical classification of Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp. pedunculatus?

Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp. pedunculatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Gentianaales. This specific plant is a part of the Rubiaceae family and is nested within the genus Lasianthus, ultimately reaching its unique taxonomic designation as the species Lasianthus rhinocerotis subsp. pedunculatus.
