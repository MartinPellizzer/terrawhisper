Gnaphalium Uliginosum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Gnaphalium uliginosum, commonly known as swamp cudweed, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas and is widely used in traditional herbal medicine.

This herb is known for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to its various health benefits.

Therapeutically, it is used to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and aid in detoxification processes.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
