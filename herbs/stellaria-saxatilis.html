What's the taxonomical classification of Stellaria saxatilis?

Stellaria saxatilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Caryophyllales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Stellaria, with its unique designation as the species saxatilis.
