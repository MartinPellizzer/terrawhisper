It has been used in herbal medicine for its health benefits, which include reducing inflammation, relieving respiratory issues, and acting as an expectorant to help ease congestion.

The therapeutic actions of lobelia include its ability to stimulate the nervous system, improve circulation, and increase the production of sweat, making it useful for treating conditions such as colds, fever, and rheumatism.
