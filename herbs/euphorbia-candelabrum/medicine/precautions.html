10 Precautions To Take When Using Euphorbia Candelabrum (Candelabra Tree)

Euphorbia candelabrum has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use by pregnant and breastfeeding women.

This is due to the potential of causing uterine contractions and affecting milk production. If taken by pregnant or breastfeeding individuals, Euphorbia candelabrum may lead to preterm labor or decreased milk supply.

It's also advisable for individuals with liver or kidney issues to consult a healthcare professional before using this herb.
