10 Medicinal Constituents Of Euphorbia Candelabrum (Candelabra Tree)

Euphorbia candelabrum has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids.

These constituents are responsible for the plant's anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Additionally, the plant's antimicrobial properties can help combat infections and improve wound healing, ultimately enhancing the overall quality of life.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Euphorbia candelabrum.

Euphorbia candelabrum flavonoids is a group of compounds found in its latex and leaves.
