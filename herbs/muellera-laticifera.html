Possible Side Effects of Muellera laticifera

Muellera laticifera can have side effects if used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea due to its high levels of saponins and other compounds.

In some cases, excessive use of Muellera laticifera may cause symptoms of hypoglycemia, including dizziness, weakness, and tremors, particularly in individuals with pre-existing diabetes or blood sugar imbalances.
