10 Medicinal Constituents Of Hypericum Annulatum (St John'S Wort)

Hypericum annulatum, also known as St John's Wort, has active constituents such as flavonoids, hyperforin, and hypericin.

These constituents contribute to the plant's antidepressant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties. Flavonoids, for example, may improve mood and reduce symptoms of anxiety, while hyperforin has shown potential in reducing pain and inflammation.

These medicinal properties can improve people's lives by alleviating symptoms of depression and anxiety, as well as supporting wound healing and immune function.

1. Flavonoids
