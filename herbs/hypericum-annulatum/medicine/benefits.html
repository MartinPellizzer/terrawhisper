Hypericum annulatum reduces anxiety because of its unique composition of bioactive compounds, particularly hyperforin.

This potent flavonoid has been shown to inhibit the reuptake of serotonin, a neurotransmitter crucial for regulating mood and emotional responses.

By increasing serotonin levels in the brain, st john's wort helps to alleviate feelings of anxiety and promotes a sense of calm and relaxation, making it a popular natural remedy for anxiety disorders.
