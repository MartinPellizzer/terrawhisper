Annual St. John'S Wort (Hypericum annulatum) Actions

St. John's Wort exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, tonic, and stimulant properties. It acts as a carminative to relieve gas, a diuretic to support kidney function, and an antispasmodic to ease muscle cramps. Its astringent and expectorant effects aid in reducing inflammation and clearing mucus. Additionally, it has sedative qualities that promote relaxation and calmness.
