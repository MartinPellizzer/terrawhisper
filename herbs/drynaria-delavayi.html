What's the taxonomical classification of Drynaria delavayi?

Drynaria delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, its taxonomic lineage continues through the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. It is further nested within the family Polypodiaceae, falling under the genus Drynaria, and is specifically identified by its species name, delavayi.
