Sangre De Grado (Uncaria Tomentosa Var. Dioica)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Uncaria tomentosa var. dioica.

Sangre de grado, scientifically known as Uncaria tomentosa var. dioica, is a traditional medicinal herb native to the Amazon rainforest, widely recognized for its adaptogenic properties and potent health benefits. This plant, also known as "dragon's blood" due to its deep red resin, has been used for centuries by indigenous communities in South America for its healing properties. Its primary benefits include immune support, anti-inflammatory effects, and the ability to help manage stress and fatigue, making it a popular remedy in traditional medicine. In modern wellness practices, Sangre de grado is used in supplements and tonics to enhance vitality, support digestive health, and promote overall resilience against environmental stressors. Notably, it contains unique compounds such as the alkaloid pentacyclic triterpenes, which contribute to its therapeutic effects, and its historical significance as a sacred plant in Amazonian cultures adds to its cultural and medicinal value.
