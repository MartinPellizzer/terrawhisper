1. Fights infections

Infections.

Infections are harmful conditions caused by the invasion of pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi into the body.

These pathogens can affect various parts of the body, leading to symptoms like fever, inflammation, and tissue damage.

They commonly occur in individuals with weakened immune systems, such as the elderly, children, or those with chronic illnesses.

The cananga odorata plant has been found to possess antimicrobial properties that help combat these infections by inhibiting the growth of harmful microorganisms.

How this herb helps with fights infections?

Cananga odorata fights infections because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi, making the plant effective against a variety of pathogens. Alkaloids like canangine and canaridine contribute to its broad-spectrum antimicrobial activity. Flavonoids in the plant also support immune function and reduce oxidative stress, enhancing the body's defense mechanisms.

Overall, these natural compounds make Cananga odorata a valuable resource in traditional and modern medicine for combating infections.
