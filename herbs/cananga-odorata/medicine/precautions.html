10 Precautions To Take When Using Cananga Odorata (Ylang-Ylang)

Cananga odorata has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential skin irritation and allergic reactions.

If not taken seriously, these can lead to redness, itching, and even anaphylaxis. It's essential to start with small amounts and patch test the oil on a skin area before using it extensively.

Not taking this precaution may result in skin issues, such as eczema or rosacea-like symptoms.
