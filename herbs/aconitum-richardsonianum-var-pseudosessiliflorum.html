Aconitum richardsonianum var. pseudosessiliflorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Aconitum richardsonianum var. pseudosessiliflorum?

Aconitum richardsonianum var. pseudosessiliflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. As a member of the family Ranunculaceae, it is specifically identified within the genus Aconitum, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation at the variety level as the species Aconitum richardsonianum var. pseudosessiliflorum.
