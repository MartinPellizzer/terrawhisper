What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its flowers appear in clusters and possess numerous prominent stamens that contribute to their striking appearance. The fruit consists of a cylindrical legume that contains several small, dark seeds. What side effects this plant can have?

This plant can cause severe gastrointestinal issues including abdominal cramps, diarrhea, nausea, and intense abdominal pain. Prolonged or excessive use often leads to dangerous electrolyte imbalance and dehydration. These systemic complications can progress to hypokalemia, which is a critical deficiency of potassium in the blood. Long-term consumption is also associated with colon melanosis, a dark pigmentation of the intestinal lining. In extreme cases, users may experience melena, which is the passage of dark, tarry stools indicating internal bleeding.
