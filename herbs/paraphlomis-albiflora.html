What's the taxonomical classification of Paraphlomis albiflora?

Paraphlomis albiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This species is a member of the family Lamiaceae, specifically falling within the genus Paraphlomis, with its unique identity defined by the species name albiflora.
