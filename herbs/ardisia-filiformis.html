Ardisia filiformis

What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia filiformis?

Ardisia filiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Ericales. Further specialization occurs at the family level under Primulaceae, where it is categorized under the genus Ardisia. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific species name, filiformis.
