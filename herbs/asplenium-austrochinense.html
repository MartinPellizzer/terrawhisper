What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium austrochinense?

Asplenium austrochinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This specific fern is a member of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, and is identified by its unique species designation, austrochinense.
