What's the taxonomical classification of Asplenium austrochinense?

Asplenium austrochinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This specific fern is a member of the family Aspleniaceae, falling under the genus Asplenium, and is identified by its unique species designation, austrochinense.
