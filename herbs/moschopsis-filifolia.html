Possible Side Effects of Moschopsis filifolia

Moschopsis filifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea due to its high concentration of alkaloids.

Ingesting large amounts of the plant's extracts can lead to allergic reactions, including hives, itching, and respiratory issues.

If taken orally, Moschopsis filifolia may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate conditions like high blood pressure.

Furthermore, applying its extracts to the skin without proper dilution can cause irritation, redness, and skin burns.
