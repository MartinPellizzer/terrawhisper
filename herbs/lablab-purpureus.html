Lablab Purpureus

Lablab purpureus, commonly known as hyacinth bean, has been widely used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

It is rich in nutrients such as proteins, fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it beneficial for improving digestion and boosting the immune system. The plant's leaves and seeds have been traditionally used to treat ailments like diarrhea, inflammation, and hypertension due to their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Recent studies have also shown that Lablab purpureus may have potential in managing diabetes and lowering cholesterol levels.

Its versatility and nutritional value make it a valuable resource in both traditional and modern medicinal practices.
