To plant Lablab purpureus, start with fresh seeds in spring or early summer when soil temperature reaches 20°C. Sow seeds 2-3 cm deep and 10-15 cm apart in rows 1-2 m apart. Lablab purpureus pods are ready for harvest 60-80 days after sowing. Pick pods regularly to encourage continuous production. Harvest the pods when they are bright red or purple, as this indicates maturity. Leave some pods to dry on the plant to allow seeds to mature.
