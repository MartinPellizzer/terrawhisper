Lablab (Lablab purpureus)

Lablab (Lablab purpureus) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to Southern Africa, Eastern Africa, and Northern Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and shoots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and culinary uses.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
