What is Lablab (Lablab Purpureus)?

Lablab purpureus, also known as hyacinth bean or lablab, is a leguminous plant native to Africa and widely used in traditional herbal medicine for its nutritional and therapeutic properties.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Lablab is Lablab purpureus. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Fabales, order Fabales, family Fabaceae, genus Lablab, and species Lablab purpureus. It is commonly known as hyacinth bean in South Asia, and as lablab or Egyptian beans in Africa. The plant is also referred to as bird's wing in Australia and as pinto bean in parts of the Americas. Synonyms include Vigna purpurea (botanical) and various regional names such as kala chana in India and mogo in East Africa.
