Schisandra henryi subsp. marginalis

What's the taxonomical classification of Schisandra henryi subsp. marginalis?

Schisandra henryi subsp. marginalis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Austrobaileyales. As a member of the family Schisandraceae, it is placed within the genus Schisandra, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Schisandra henryi subsp. marginalis.
