Bowringia callicarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Bowringia callicarpa?

Bowringia callicarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Bowringia, and its species name, callicarpa.
