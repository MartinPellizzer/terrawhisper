1. Cytokinin

Trichopus zeylanicus cytokinin is a type of plant hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating cell growth and division.

It stimulates the production of chlorophyll and promotes cell elongation, leading to an increase in plant height and biomass. Additionally, cytokinin helps regulate the balance between cell growth and differentiation, ensuring proper root development and leaf expansion.

This hormone has been widely studied for its potential applications in agriculture and biotechnology.
