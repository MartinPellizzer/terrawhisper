10 Medicinal Constituents Of Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose)

Oenothera biennis has active constituents such as gamma-linolenic acid (GLA), linoleic acid, and beta-sitosterol.

These constituents contribute to its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can improve skin conditions like eczema and acne, as well as alleviate symptoms of menopausal and menstrual discomfort.

By reducing inflammation and promoting skin health, GLA and linoleic acid can also improve cardiovascular health and support overall well-being.
