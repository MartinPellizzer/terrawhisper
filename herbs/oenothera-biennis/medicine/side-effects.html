These compounds can stimulate the immune system to produce immunoglobulin E (IgE) antibodies, triggering an allergic response characterized by symptoms such as hives, itching, flushing, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, the plant's alkaloid composition may also contribute to these adverse reactions.
