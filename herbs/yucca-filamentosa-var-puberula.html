Yucca filamentosa var. puberula

Yucca filamentosa var. puberula, also known as the Mohawk Yucca, is a variety of the Yucca filamentosa plant, which is a type of flowering plant native to North America.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful in treating wounds, skin conditions, and digestive issues.

The plant's health benefits include reducing inflammation, improving wound healing, and supporting digestive health.
