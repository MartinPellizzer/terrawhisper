10 Medicinal Constituents Of Geranium Thunbergii (Japanese Crane'S Bill)

Geranium thunbergii, also known as Japanese crane's bill, has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and coumarins.

These constituents are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiseptic properties. The plant's medicinal properties can help alleviate skin conditions, wound healing, and even reduce anxiety and stress.

For instance, the flavonoids in Geranium thunbergii can improve cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels, promoting overall well-being and improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic diseases.
