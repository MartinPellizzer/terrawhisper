Schoenoplectus triqueteer belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Schoenoplectus triqueter is prepared as decoctions, infusions, powders, and sometimes topical poultices using its dried rhizomes or whole plants. Because scientific research is limited, users should exercise caution to avoid potential adverse physiological responses.
