What's the taxonomical classification of Buxus latistyla?

Buxus latistyla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually reaching the order Buxaales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Buxaceae and is part of the genus Buxus. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, latistyla.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Some studies suggest that the toxic compounds within the species may impact the central nervous system if absorbed in significant quantities. Due to these unpredictable biological reactions, extreme caution is required when interacting with this plant.
