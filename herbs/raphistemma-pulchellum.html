Raphistemma pulchellum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the hierarchy further, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Raphistemma, and its species designation, pulchellum.
