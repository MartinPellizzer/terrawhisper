What's the taxonomical classification of Raphistemma pulchellum?

Raphistemma pulchellum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchy further, it falls under the order Gentianales and is a member of the family Apocynaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Raphistemma, and its species designation, pulchellum.
