Medicinal Parts of Pinus montana var. pseudopumilio

Pinus montana var. pseudopumilio has many medicinal parts, such as its resin, which is rich in terpenoids like pinosylvin and pinocarveol, exhibiting antimicrobial properties.

The needles of this plant contain flavonoids like kaempferol and quercetin, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The bark of Pinus montana var. pseudopumilio is rich in phenolic compounds, including stilbenes like pinostilbene, which have been shown to have antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties.

The cones of this plant contain fatty acids like oleic and linoleic acid, which have been used to treat various skin conditions and promote wound healing.
