Ranunculus cuneliifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae, which defines its broader botanical group. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is established within the genus Ranunculus, where it is identified as the species cuneliifolius.
