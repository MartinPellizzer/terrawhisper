Psychotria gabonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Gentianaales. This plant is a member of the Rubiaceae family, which is further defined by its membership in the genus Psychotria, ultimately leading to its specific identification as the species Psychotria gabonica.
