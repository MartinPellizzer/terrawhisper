Linear Lobelia (Lobelia Linearis)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Lobelia linearis.

Linear lobelia, scientifically known as Lobelia linearis, is a medicinal herb native to North America, valued for its adaptogenic and respiratory-supporting properties. It is traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions such as asthma and bronchitis due to its ability to relax bronchial muscles and reduce inflammation. In Native American traditions, it was often used in smudging ceremonies and as a ceremonial plant to promote spiritual clarity and healing. Modern wellness applications include its use in herbal formulations for respiratory support and as a natural remedy for stress and fatigue. This unique plant is notable for its striking blue or purple flowers and its rare active compound, lobeline, which has been studied for its potential cognitive and therapeutic benefits.
