What is Otachyrium inaequale?

Otachyrium inaequale is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, perennial habit, and grows up to 1-2 meters in height. The leaves are linear, flat, and 3-10 mm wide, with a ligule 1-3 mm long and a leaf sheath 3-6 mm long. The inflorescence is a panicle, 5-15 cm long, with spikelets 4-6 mm long, each containing two florets.

Otachyrium inaequale is native to South America, specifically in the Andean regions of Argentina and Chile.
