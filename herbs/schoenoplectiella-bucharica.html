What is Schoenoplectiella bucharica?

Schoenoplectiella bucharica is a species of sedge that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb, typically growing up to 50-100 cm in height, with a short rhizome and culms that are triangular in cross-section. The leaves are distichous, with a linear-lanceolate shape, and are 3-10 mm wide, often with a reddish-brown base. The inflorescence is a spike, 5-15 cm long, with a brownish-purple color and a rounded apex, bearing 3-5 spikelets.

Schoenoplectiella bucharica is found in dry, mountainous areas of Central Asia.
