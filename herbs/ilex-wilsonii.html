What's the taxonomical classification of Ilex wilsonii?

Ilex wilsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Aquifoliales and is a member of the family Aquifoliaceae. Ultimately, this plant is identified by its genus, Ilex, and its specific species name, wilsonii.
