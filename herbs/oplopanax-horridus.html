Devil'S Club (Oplopanax horridus)

Devil'S Club (Oplopanax horridus) is a member of the Araliaceae family, native to North America, Alaska, and Pacific Northwest. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, stimulant, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
