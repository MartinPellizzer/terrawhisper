Stellaria Media Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Stellaria media, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is widely used in herbal medicine for its mild diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and skin-soothing properties.

The bioactive constituents in Stellaria media include flavonoids, tannins, mucilage, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, poultices, and tinctures can be made from the fresh or dried leaves and stems of this plant.
