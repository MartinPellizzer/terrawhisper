Thladiantha henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Cucurbitales. As a member of the family Cucurbitaceae, this plant is placed within the genus Thladiantha, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as Thladiantha henryi.
