Rosa Gallica

Rosa gallica, commonly known as the French rose or Red rose, is a perennial herb belonging to the Rosaceae family.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and alleviate menstrual cramps.

Possible Side Effects of rosa gallica

Rosa gallica can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal issues.

Some people may experience increased heart rate, high blood pressure, or interact with medications like blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Using rosa gallica in excessive amounts or for extended periods can lead to kidney or liver damage, as well as exacerbate conditions like bleeding disorders or thyroid problems.

In severe cases, improper use of rosa gallica can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction requiring immediate medical attention.
