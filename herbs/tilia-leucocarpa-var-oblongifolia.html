Tilia leucocarpa var. oblongifolia

Tilia leucocarpa var. oblongifolia, also known as the Chinese linden, is a variety of the Tilia leucocarpa tree, native to China and used in traditional medicine.

The bark and leaves of the plant have been used for their medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which are believed to contribute to its health benefits, such as soothing digestive issues and reducing stress and anxiety.

The plant is often used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be consumed to reap its benefits.

Additionally, the plant's leaves and flowers can be used to make a calming and anti-inflammatory salve or cream.
