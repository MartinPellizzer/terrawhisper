Withania Coagulans (Withania coagulans) Compounds

Withania coagulans contains several primary compounds, including alkaloids, saponins, and flavonoids. These compounds contribute to its pharmacological properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Other notable constituents are tannins, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
