Ajuga Reptans: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Ajuga reptans, commonly known as carpet bugle or bugleweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, belonging to the Valerianaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Ajuga reptans include supporting cardiovascular health, reducing anxiety, and promoting respiratory wellness.

Its therapeutic actions encompass sedative, antispasmodic, and diuretic effects, while its bioactive constituents include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and tannins.
