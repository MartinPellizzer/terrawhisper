      What side-effects this plant can have?

      Carpesium abrotanoides can cause severe gastrointestinal distress including intense abdominal cramping, nausea, vomiting, and violent diarrhea if ingested. Excessive consumption may lead to significant dehydration and electrolyte imbalances due to the rapid loss of fluids. In some cases, the toxic effects can manifest as systemic irritation or more serious metabolic disturbances. Monitoring for these reactions is crucial when dealing with any part of the plant.
