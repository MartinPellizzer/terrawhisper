Cotoneaster dammeri var. radicans

What's the taxonomical classification of Cotoneaster dammeri var. radicans?

Cotoneaster dammeri var. radicans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it follows the taxonomic lineage of the order Rosales and falls under the family Rosaceae. At the more specific levels of classification, it is identified by the genus Cotoneaster, specifically categorized as the variety radicans within the species Cotoneaster dammeri.
