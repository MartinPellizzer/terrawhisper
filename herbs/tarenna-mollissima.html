Tarenna mollissima

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Tarenna mollissima has small, woody, perennial stems that support soft, densely pubescent leaves and tiny, inconspicuous flowers. The foliage is characterized by its velvety texture due to a covering of fine hairs on both the upper and lower surfaces. These leaves are typically arranged in an opposite pattern along the branching stems. The inflorescences are generally small and may appear in axillary clusters. Overall, the plant exhibits a delicate and soft structural appearance.
