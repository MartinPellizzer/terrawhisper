What's the taxonomical classification of Tarenna mollissima?

Tarenna mollissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Its evolutionary lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it in the order Gentianaales. As a member of the family Rubiaceae, this plant is classified under the genus Tarenna, with its specific identification being the species mollissima.
