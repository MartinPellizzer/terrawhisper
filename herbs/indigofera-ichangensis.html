What's the taxonomical classification of Indigofera ichangensis?

Indigofera ichangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated within the order Fabales. It is further categorized under the family Fabaceae, a group well-known for its diverse range of legumes. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Indigofera, and its specific biological designation is given by the species name ichangensis.
