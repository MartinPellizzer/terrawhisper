Indigofera ichangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Fabaceae, a group well-known for its diverse range of legumes. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Indigofera, and its specific biological designation is given by the species name ichangensis.
