What is Hemionitis belangeri?

Hemionitis belangeri is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern with a rhizome that produces pinnate fronds with linear to lanceolate pinnae, the rachis and costae being brown and pubescent. The pinnae are ovate to lanceolate, acuminate, with entire margins, and the veins are free or anastomosing. The fronds are 30-70 cm long, the largest pinnae being 10-20 cm long and 3-6 cm wide.

The sporangia are borne on the abaxial surface of the pinnae in two rows along the veins.
