Bolboschoenus planiculmis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Poales. This plant is a member of the family Cyperaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Bolboschoenus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species planiculmis.
