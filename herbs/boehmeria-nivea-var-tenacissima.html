Boehmeria nivea var. tenacissima

What's the taxonomical classification of Boehmeria nivea var. tenacissima?

Boehmeria nivea var. tenacissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Rosales. The plant is a member of the family Urticaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Boehmeria. Ultimately, its precise taxonomic designation is the variety tenacissima of the species Boehmeria nivea.
