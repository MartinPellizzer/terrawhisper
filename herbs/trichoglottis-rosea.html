What is Trichoglottis rosea?

Trichoglottis rosea is a species of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a monopodiale, epiphytic orchid with a short, cylindrical pseudobulb and 2 to 3, fleshy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces a single, terminal inflorescence with 3 to 5, greenish-yellow to pinkish flowers, each with a greenish-yellow labellum and white, fleshy lip. The flowers are small, approximately 4 to 6 millimeters in diameter, and are arranged in a raceme.

Trichoglottis rosea is endemic to the Himalayas and is typically found growing on trees at high elevations.
