Muhlenbergia Tenuiflora

Muhlenbergia tenuiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a native grass species found throughout much of the United States, particularly in the central and eastern regions.

Historically, it has been used by indigenous peoples for its potential medicinal properties, though modern scientific research on its therapeutic applications is limited. However, more research is needed to confirm these effects and to determine safe and effective methods of preparation and use.
