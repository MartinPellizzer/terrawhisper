What is Laportea cuspidata?

Laportea cuspidata is a species of plant in the family Urticaceae, commonly known as the nettle family.

It is a perennial herb with simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape, measuring 7-14 cm long and 2.5-5.5 cm wide, with a serrated margin and a pointed apex. The leaves are green on the upper surface and have a whitish-green or yellowish-green tomentum on the lower surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white or yellowish flowers in a panicle, with each flower measuring 2-3 mm in diameter.

Laportea cuspidata is native to tropical Africa and has been naturalized in parts of Asia and the Pacific.
