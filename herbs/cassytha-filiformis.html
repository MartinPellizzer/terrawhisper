What's the taxonomical classification of Cassytha filiformis?

Cassytha filiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Laurales and is a member of the family Lauraceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Cassytha, and its specific identification is determined by its species name, filiformis.
