Turmeric (Curcuma aromatica) Actions

Turmeric exhibits several primary therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, carminative, and tonic properties. It also acts as a stimulant and antispasmodic, helping to relieve digestive issues. Additionally, it has diuretic, expectorant, astringent, and sedative effects, making it a versatile herb in herbal medicine.
