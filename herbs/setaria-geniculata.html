What's the taxonomical classification of Setaria geniculata?

Setaria geniculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy places it in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the lineage, it is categorized under the order Poales and falls within the family Poaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Setaria, and its specific species, geniculata.
