Polygonum ochotense

What's the taxonomical classification of Polygonum ochotense?

Polygonum ochotense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Caryophyllales. It is a member of the family Polygonaceae, falling within the genus Polygonum, and is specifically identified by its species name, ochotense.

    What are the herbal preparations of Polygonum ochotense?
