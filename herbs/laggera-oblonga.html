What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Laggera oblonga has woody, perennial stems that produce lanceolate to oblong leaves with entire margins and an acute apex. The plant features terminal clusters of flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a prominent yellow center. Each blossom consists of tubular corollas that flare into five spreading lobes.
