What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Amitostigma basifoliatum has a basal rosette of leaves and a single, slender, erect stem that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters.

As the stem ascends, the leaves become significantly smaller and more sparse, transitioning into much narrower bract-like structures. The inflorescence consists of small, delicate flowers that are often arranged in a terminal cluster.
