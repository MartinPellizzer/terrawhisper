Filipendula purpurea

What's the taxonomical classification of Filipendula purpurea?

Filipendula purpurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and is a member of the order Rosales. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the family Rosaceae and is categorized under the genus Filipendula. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, Filipendula purpurea.
