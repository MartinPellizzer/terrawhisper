Possible Side Effects of rhamnus purshiana

Rhamnus purshiana can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, nausea, and diarrhea due to its high tannin content, which can cause stomach upset and discomfort.

Excessive consumption of the herb may also lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and hives, in some individuals.

Additionally, improper use of Rhamnus purshiana can cause kidney damage and interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, which can exacerbate underlying health conditions.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Rhamnus purshiana for medicinal purposes to avoid potential side effects.
