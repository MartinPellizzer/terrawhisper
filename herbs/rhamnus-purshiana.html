Rhamnus Purshiana Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Rhamnus purshiana, commonly known as cascara sagrada, is a shrub native to the western United States, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its laxative effects and is known to support digestive health.

The bioactive constituents in Rhamnus purshiana include anthraquinones and glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures can be made from the dried bark of the plant.
