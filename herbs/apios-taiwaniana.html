Apios taiwaniana

What's the taxonomical classification of Apios taiwaniana?

Apios taiwaniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae, which identifies it as a member of the legume family. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Apios and the unique species epithet taiwaniana.

While specific clinical studies may be limited, its application in folk medicine remains a significant part of local healing practices.
