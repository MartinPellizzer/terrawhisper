Lathyrus palustris belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It further falls under the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of legumes. Within this family, the plant is identified by the genus Lathyrus, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species palustris.
