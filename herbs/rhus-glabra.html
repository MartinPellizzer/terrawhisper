Possible Side Effects of rhus glabra

Rhus glabra can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high tannin content.

Prolonged or excessive consumption may also lead to kidney problems and damage to the liver.

In some cases, Rhus glabra may cause allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and hives, in sensitive individuals.

Furthermore, it may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse health effects.
