What's the taxonomical classification of Desmodium styracifolium?

Desmodium styracifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it among the legumes, and it is specifically identified by the genus Desmodium. Ultimately, its unique scientific designation is completed by its species name, styracifolium.
