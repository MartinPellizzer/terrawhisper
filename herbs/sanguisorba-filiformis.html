What's the taxonomical classification of Sanguisorba filiformis?

Sanguisorba filiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Sanguisorba, with its specific designation being the species filiformis.
