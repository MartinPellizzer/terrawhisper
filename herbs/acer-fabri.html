What's the taxonomical classification of Acer fabri?

Acer fabri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Sapindales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Sapindaceae, which contains the genus Acer. Ultimately, its specific scientific designation is identified at the species level as fabri.
