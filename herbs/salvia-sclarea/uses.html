Clary Sage (Salvia sclarea) Uses

Clary sage is commonly used to alleviate menstrual cramps and irregularities. It helps ease breast tenderness and menopausal symptoms such as anxiety and mood disorders. It may also support sleep by reducing stress-related insomnia and headaches, including migraines.

Clary Sage (Salvia sclarea) Monograph

1. Menstrual cramps

Clary sage is traditionally used to alleviate menstrual cramps due to its antispasmodic and calming properties. It helps relax uterine muscles and reduce pain associated with menstruation. Often used in herbal teas or essential oils, it supports hormonal balance and eases discomfort during the menstrual cycle.

2. Menopausal symptoms

Clary sage has been traditionally used to alleviate menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes and anxiety. Its estrogen-like compounds may help balance hormonal fluctuations. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture. Clinical studies suggest it can reduce symptom severity when used consistently.

4. Breast tenderness

Clary sage is traditionally used to alleviate breast tenderness, particularly during menstrual cycles. 5. Menopause symptoms

Clary sage is used to alleviate menopause symptoms such as hot flashes and mood swings. Its phytoestrogens may help balance hormone levels. It can be consumed as a tea or tincture. It is often recommended for its calming and supportive properties during this transition.

6. Anxiety

Clary sage is used to alleviate anxiety due to its calming properties. It contains compounds like linalool and linalyl acetate that promote relaxation. Studies suggest it may reduce stress and improve mood. It is commonly used in aromatherapy and herbal teas for its soothing effects.

7. Stress-related insomnia

Clary sage is used to alleviate stress-related insomnia due to its calming properties. It helps reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, making it easier to fall asleep. Essential oils from the plant can be diffused or applied topically to ease restlessness and improve sleep quality. Its mild sedative effects support a more restful night's sleep.

8. Mood disorders

Clary sage is used in herbal medicine to support mood regulation. Its essential oil contains compounds like linalool and linalyl acetate, which may help reduce anxiety and promote calmness. It is often used in aromatherapy or as a supplement for mild mood imbalances. Clinical studies suggest it may offer relief for symptoms of depression and stress.

9. Headache

Clary sage is traditionally used to alleviate headaches, particularly tension-type headaches. Its calming properties help reduce stress and muscle tension. It can be consumed as a tea or applied topically in diluted form. This makes it a versatile remedy in herbal medicine for headache relief.

10. Migraine

Clary sage is used to alleviate migraine symptoms due to its calming and antispasmodic properties. It helps reduce tension and inflammation, often associated with migraines. Essential oil inhalation or topical application can provide relief by promoting relaxation and easing vascular tension. It is commonly used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies for migraine management.
