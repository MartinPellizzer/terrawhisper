10 Precautions To Take When Using Salvia Sclarea (Sage)

Salvia sclarea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in moderation due to its potential to cause dizziness and disorientation.

This is especially important for individuals with a history of seizure disorders or those taking medications that affect the nervous system, as excessive use can trigger seizures or interact with medications.

If not taken cautiously, it may cause nausea, headaches, and stomach upset.
