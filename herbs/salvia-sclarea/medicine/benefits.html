10 Health Benefits Of Salvia Sclarea (Sage)

Salvia sclarea has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving cognitive function, and supporting digestive health.

The medicinal properties of sage include its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory compounds, which help alleviate symptoms of menopause and osteoarthritis. For example, consuming sage tea may improve memory and cognitive abilities in older adults, while using topical creams containing sage oil can soothe skin irritations and reduce joint pain.

These benefits can greatly improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.
