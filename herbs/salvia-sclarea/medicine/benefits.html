Additionally, sage has anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause uterine contractions and pain during menstruation.

By soothing the uterine muscles and reducing inflammation, sage can provide relief from menstrual cramps and promote a more comfortable menstrual cycle.
