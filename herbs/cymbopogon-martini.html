Cymbopogon Martini Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cymbopogon martini, commonly known as lemongrass, is a fragrant perennial grass native to Asia, prized for its citrusy aroma and various medicinal properties.

This herb is widely used in traditional medicine for its calming and digestive benefits.

It contains bioactive constituents such as citral, myrcene, and limonene, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from lemongrass to support digestive health and promote relaxation.
