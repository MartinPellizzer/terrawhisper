10 Medicinal Constituents Of Abrus Precatorius (Precatory Bean)

Abrus precatorius has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties, which contribute to the plant's medicinal uses. For example, the flavonoids present in the plant have been shown to exhibit anti-cancer and antibacterial activities, potentially improving cancer treatment outcomes and reducing infection risk.

Additionally, the alkaloids may help alleviate pain and inflammation, making it a promising natural remedy for arthritis sufferers.
