10 Precautions To Take When Using Abrus Precatorius (Precatory Bean)

Abrus precatorius has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in small doses and under medical supervision.

This is crucial as the bean contains abrin, a highly toxic protein that can cause severe gastrointestinal symptoms, respiratory failure, and even death if ingested in large amounts.

Ingesting excessive amounts can lead to severe side effects, including abdominal pain, diarrhea, and vomiting.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Abrus precatorius medicinally.
