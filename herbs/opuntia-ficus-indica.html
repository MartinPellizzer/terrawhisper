The therapeutic actions of Opuntia ficus-indica include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, which contribute to its use in treating various conditions such as arthritis, diabetes, and skin problems.

Possible Side Effects of opuntia ficus-indica

Opuntia ficus-indica can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea, nausea, and stomach cramps.

Ingesting large amounts of the herb can also lead to allergic reactions, skin irritation, and an increased heart rate.

Additionally, taking opuntia ficus-indica with certain medications, such as blood thinners or diabetes medications, can interact and exacerbate health conditions.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of the herb can cause kidney damage or worsen underlying liver conditions.
