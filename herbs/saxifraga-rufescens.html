Saxifraga rufescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Saxifraga rufescens?

Saxifraga rufescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Saxifrages (Saxifragales) and falls within the family Saxifragaecae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Saxifraga and the species epithet rufescens.
