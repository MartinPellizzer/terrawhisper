1. Reduces blood alcohol levels

Hovenia dulcis reduces blood alcohol levels because it contains a unique compound called magnolol.

Magnolol is believed to inhibit the activity of enzymes in the body that are responsible for breaking down ethanol into acetaldehyde, the toxic metabolite that is responsible for many of the negative effects of drinking.

By slowing down this process, Hovenia dulcis can help reduce the amount of acetaldehyde produced and subsequently lower blood alcohol levels, potentially reducing the severity of hangover symptoms and improving overall health.

Hovenia dulcis protects liver from damage because of its rich antioxidant and flavonoid content.

The extracts of this plant have been shown to inhibit lipid peroxidation and reactive oxygen species (ROS) formation in the liver, thereby preventing oxidative stress and subsequent cell damage.

Additionally, its bioactive compounds have been found to activate detoxification enzymes, enhance glutathione levels, and suppress inflammation, ultimately leading to improved liver health and protection against toxic insults.
