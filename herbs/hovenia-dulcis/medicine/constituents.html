10 Medicinal Constituents Of Hovenia Dulcis (Oriental Chesnut)

Hovenia dulcis has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which have been attributed to the plant's medicinal uses. The flavonoids, in particular, are believed to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, by reducing inflammation in the airways.

Additionally, the antioxidants present can help protect against cellular damage caused by free radicals, potentially improving overall health and well-being.

1. Quercetin

Additionally, it may help alleviate symptoms associated with respiratory allergies and asthma.

Its potential health benefits have made it a popular ingredient in supplements and functional foods.
