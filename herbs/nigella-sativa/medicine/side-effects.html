10 Possible Side Effects Of Nigella Sativa (Black Cumin)

Nigella sativa has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, nausea, and dizziness.

These side effects can be caused by consuming large doses or using it for extended periods. For instance, if someone with a history of allergies takes it, they may experience anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction.

Furthermore, if they have a pre-existing condition, such as hypertension, excessive consumption can exacerbate their condition, potentially worsening their quality of life.
