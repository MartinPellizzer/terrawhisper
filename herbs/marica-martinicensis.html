It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, providing relief from digestive issues, inflammation, and respiratory problems.

Possible Side Effects of Marica martinicensis

Marica martinicensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea and diarrhea, due to its high concentration of alkaloids and saponins.

Ingesting large amounts of the plant can also cause vomiting and stomach cramps.

Additionally, improper use of Marica martinicensis may lead to allergic reactions, such as skin irritation and respiratory issues, particularly in individuals with pre-existing conditions.

In severe cases, improper consumption of the plant can result in cardiac problems and increased blood pressure.
