Carex lanceolata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further organized under the order Poales and falls within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its placement in the genus Carex, specifically identified by the species name lanceolata.

Its inflorescence consists of several distinct spikes, often featuring a mix of male and female flowers arranged along the stem.
