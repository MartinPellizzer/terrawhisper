What's the taxonomical classification of Carex lanceolata?

Carex lanceolata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Poales and falls within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its placement in the genus Carex, specifically identified by the species name lanceolata.

Most people encounter no adverse effects unless they have a pre-existing sensitivity to similar sedges.
