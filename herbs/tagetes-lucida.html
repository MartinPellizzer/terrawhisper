Tagetes Lucida

Tagetes lucida, commonly known as golden wedge or sweetroot, is a medicinal herb native to the southeastern United States and widely used in traditional herbal medicine.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and flowers, which contain compounds such as thymol and carvacrol, known for their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. This plant has been traditionally used to treat digestive issues, including indigestion and gastrointestinal discomfort, due to its carminative effects. It is also employed in herbal remedies for respiratory conditions like coughs and colds, and for its ability to promote mental clarity and reduce stress.

Modern research suggests that Tagetes lucida may have potential in supporting immune function and as a natural remedy for various ailments.
