Phoenix loureiroi

What's the taxonomical classification of Phoenix loureiroi?

Phoenix loureiroi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae, following the order Arecales. The plant is a member of the family Arecaceae and falls under the genus Phoenix, specifically identified by its species name, loureiroi.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Phoenix loureiroi has a single unbranched trunk reaching up to 5 meters in height, topped by a dense crown of pinnate leaves that can extend up to 2 meters in length. Small, sharp spines are located at the base of the leaf petioles, serving as a defensive mechanism. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers followed by fleshy, oval-shaped fruits that turn dark when mature.
