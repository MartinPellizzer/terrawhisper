10 Possible Side Effects Of Rheum Rhaponticum (Turkey Rhubarb)

Rheum rhaponticum has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, diarrhea, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts of the herb or having an allergic sensitivity. In severe cases, they can lead to dehydration, malnutrition, and impaired mental function, significantly impacting one's daily life.

For instance, a person with severe diarrhea may struggle to work or engage in activities, while others may experience mood swings and irritability.
