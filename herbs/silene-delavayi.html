What's the taxonomical classification of Silene delavayi?

Silene delaavay belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized into the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae and is specifically placed within the genus Silene, eventually reaching its unique designation at the species level as Silene delaavay.
