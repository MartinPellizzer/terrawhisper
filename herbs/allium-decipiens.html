Allium decipiens

What's the taxonomical classification of Allium decipiens?

Allium decipiens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asparagales, it falls under the family Amaryllidaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Allium, and its specific species designation, decipiens.
