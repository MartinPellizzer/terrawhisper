Possible Side Effects of linum usitatissimum

Linum usitatissimum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin irritation in some individuals.

In rare cases, excessive consumption of the herb may lead to symptoms like dizziness, headaches, and nausea.

Additionally, when used topically, linum usitatissimum oil may cause skin sensitivity and increased risk of phototoxicity, a condition that makes the skin more susceptible to sunburn.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using linum usitatissimum for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
