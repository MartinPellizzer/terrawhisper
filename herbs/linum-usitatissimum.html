Linum Usitatissimum: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Linum usitatissimum, commonly known as flax, is an annual herb native to the Mediterranean region and widely cultivated for its seeds and fiber.

This herb is valued for its high content of omega-3 fatty acids, lignans, and dietary fiber, which contribute to its numerous health benefits.

It supports heart health, reduces inflammation, and may aid in managing cholesterol levels and digestive issues.

Therapeutically, flax exhibits antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and mild laxative properties, making it a versatile natural remedy.
