Brassica napus var. napobrassica

What's the taxonomical classification of Brassica napus var. napobrassica?

Brassica napus var. napobrassica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Brassicales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Brassicaceae and the genus Brassica, specifically categorized under the species designation Brassica napus var. napobrassica.
