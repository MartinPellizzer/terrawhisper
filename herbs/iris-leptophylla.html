What's the taxonomical classification of Iris leptophylla?

Iris leptophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Iridaceae, which contains the genus Iris, and is specifically identified by its unique species designation, leptophylla.
