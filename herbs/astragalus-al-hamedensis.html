Al-Hamed Alkaline (Astragalus al-hamedensis)

Al-Hamed Alkaline (Astragalus al-hamedensis) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to Middle East, and North Africa. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
