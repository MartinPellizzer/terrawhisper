Euphorbia Pekinensis: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Euphorbia pekinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spurge, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, characterized by its milky sap and distinctive foliage.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties that may support immune function.

Therapeutically, it is believed to aid in detoxification and skin health, though more research is needed to confirm these effects.

The bioactive constituents of Euphorbia pekinensis include alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
