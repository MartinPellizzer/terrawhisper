Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides?

Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Caryophyllales and is a representative of the family Polygonaceae. Within this family, its taxonomic placement is further refined under the genus Parogonum, specifically identifying it as the variety Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides.
