Possible Side Effects of taraxacum officinale

Taraxacum officinale can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

Some people may experience nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea after consuming large amounts of the herb.

Additionally, using taraxacum officinale in high doses or for extended periods may lead to liver or kidney damage, and even worsen conditions like high blood pressure or kidney stones.

In rare cases, people may also experience symptoms such as dizziness, headaches, or skin rashes.
