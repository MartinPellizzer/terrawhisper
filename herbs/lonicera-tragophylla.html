Lonicera tragophylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Lonicera tragophylla?

Lonicera tragophylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Dipsacales and is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Lonicera, and its specific epithet, tragophylla.

These traditional uses rely heavily on the plant's perceived antimicrobial and soothing properties.
