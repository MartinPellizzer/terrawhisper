What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia pusilla?

Ardisia pusilla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Ericales and into the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Ardisia, and its specific epithet, pusilla.
