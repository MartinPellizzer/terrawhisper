Pilea symmeria belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Rosales and the family Urticaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pilea, and its unique species designation, symmeria.
