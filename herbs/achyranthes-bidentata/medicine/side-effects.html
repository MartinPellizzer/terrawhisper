10 Possible Side Effects Of Achyranthes Bidentata (Chinese Hogweed)

These side effects can occur due to excessive consumption, interaction with other medications, or sensitivity to the herb's compounds.

In severe cases, they can lead to severe abdominal pain, difficulty breathing, and even liver failure, significantly impacting daily life and overall well-being.
