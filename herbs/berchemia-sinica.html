What's the taxonomical classification of Berchemia sinica?

Berchemia sinica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, the plant is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls under the order Rosales and is a member of the family Rhamnaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Berchemia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species sinica.
