Angiopteris helferiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Angiopteris helferiana?

Angiopteris helferiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Marattiidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Marattiales and belongs to the family Marattiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Angiopteris, and its species designation, helferiana.
