Lumnitzera racemosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Lumnitzera racemosa?

Lumnitzera racemosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent further, it falls under the order Myrtales and the family Combretaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Lumnitzera, and its unique species designation, racemosa.
