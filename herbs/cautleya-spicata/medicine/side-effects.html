10 Possible Side Effects Of Cautleya Spicata (Spicata)

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption or interactions with other medications. For instance, gastrointestinal issues can lead to diarrhea, stomach pain, and nausea, making daily activities, such as work and socializing, unbearable.

Additionally, cardiovascular problems can result in high blood pressure, palpitations, and shortness of breath, compromising one's quality of life and overall health.
