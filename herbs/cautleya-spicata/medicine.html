What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Cautleya Spicata (Spicata)?

Cautleya spicata, also known as spicata, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The medicinal constituents of this herb include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. The herb is used to prepare various medicinal preparations, including decoctions, infusions, and poultices, which are applied topically to treat skin conditions. Possible side effects of Cautleya spicata include skin irritation and allergic reactions.

Caution is advised when using the herb, especially for individuals with sensitive skin or allergies.
