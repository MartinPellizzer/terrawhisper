10 Possible Side Effects Of Ocimum Basilicum (Basil)

Ocimum basilicum has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues.

These side effects can be caused by the herb's essential oils, which can be too potent for some individuals. For instance, if consumed in excess, basil can cause stomach upset, nausea, and vomiting, potentially leading to malnutrition and weight loss.

In severe cases, it may exacerbate underlying medical conditions.
