Possible Side Effects of Salacia impressifolia

Excessive consumption of Salacia impressifolia may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse reactions.

In rare cases, the plant may cause allergic reactions, including skin rashes and respiratory issues, in sensitive individuals.
