What is Trichocentrum microchilum?

Trichocentrum microchilum is an epiphytic orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It has pseudobulbs and two to four linear to elliptical leaves that are 10-30 cm long and 2-5 cm wide. The inflorescence is a raceme of 10-30 flowers, each measuring 3-4 cm long and 3-4 cm wide. The flowers have sepals and petals that are dark brown to reddish-brown with a yellowish-green lip and a white or yellowish-green column.

The species is native to Central and South America, particularly in countries such as Mexico, Costa Rica, and Ecuador.
