10 Medicinal Constituents Of Gossypium Hirsutum (Cotton)

Gossypium hirsutum has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids.

These compounds possess antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, making cotton a valuable medicinal plant. The flavonoids can help alleviate respiratory issues by reducing inflammation, while the saponins may aid in wound healing by promoting tissue regeneration.

Additionally, the antioxidants present in cotton can protect against cell damage caused by free radicals, potentially improving cardiovascular health.
