    What is Doryopteris triphylla?

    Doryopteris triphylla is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae, native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

    It is a terrestrial fern that grows up to 1 meter in height, with a rhizome that produces numerous fronds. Each frond consists of three pinnae, which are further divided into numerous pinnules with serrated margins, giving the plant a distinctive, lacy appearance. The fronds are dark green on the upper surface and lighter green on the lower surface, with a wiry stipe that supports the frond.

    The sporophylls are produced on the undersurface of the frond, forming a large, brownish-yellow sorus.
