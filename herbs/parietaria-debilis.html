Parietarica debilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Parietararia debilis has slender, scrambling stems that are branched and often prostrate or climbing with a diameter typically ranging from 1 to 2 millimeters. These leaflets exhibit entire margins and can be either glabrous or slightly pubescent depending on the specific environment. The inflorescence is characterized by small, inconspicuous green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.
