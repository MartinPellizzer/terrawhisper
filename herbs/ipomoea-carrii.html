Ipomoea carrii

What's the taxonomical classification of Ipomoea carrii?

Ipomoea carrii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Solanales. This species is a part of the family Convolvulaceae, specifically situated within the genus Ipomoea, and is identified by its unique specific epithet, carrii.
