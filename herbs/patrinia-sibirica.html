What's the taxonomical classification of Patrinia sibirica?

Patrinia sibirica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Dipsacales and is a member of the family Caprifoliaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Patriinia, with the specific epithet sibiriica defining its unique species identity.
