Centella Asiatica Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Centella asiatica, commonly known as gotu kola, is a creeping herb native to Asia, prized for its medicinal properties.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as asiatic acid, madecassic acid, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Centella asiatica can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or capsule, making it versatile for various herbal formulations.
