From a horticultural perspective, gotu kola is a perennial plant that thrives in wet, marshy areas and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the world. Botanically, gotu kola is a member of the Apiaceae family, which also includes carrots and parsley.

Historically, gotu kola has been used in traditional Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine for thousands of years, and is often referred to as "the fountain of life" due to its rejuvenating properties.
