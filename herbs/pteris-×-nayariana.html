Pteris × nayariana

Pteris × nayariana is a sterile hybrid fern, specifically a natural hybrid between Pteris multifida and Pteris neraudii.

The plant has been found to possess medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which contribute to its potential health benefits.

These benefits may include reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health, as well as exhibiting antimicrobial and antiviral properties.

Pteris × nayariana is traditionally used in herbal preparations, such as teas and infusions, although further research is necessary to confirm its efficacy and safety for human consumption.
