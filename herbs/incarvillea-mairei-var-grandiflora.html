Incarvillea mairei var. grandiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Incarvillea mairei var. grandiflora?

Incarvillea mairei var. grandiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a representative of the family Bignoniaceae. Taxonomically, this plant is placed within the genus Incarvillea, specifically identified under the species Incarvillea mairei, with the variety grandiflora denoting its specific morphological characteristics.
