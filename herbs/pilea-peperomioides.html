What's the taxonomical classification of Pilea peperomioides?

Pilea peperomioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Rosales. It is a member of the family Urticaceae, falling specifically within the genus Pilea, and is identified by its unique species name, peperomioides.
