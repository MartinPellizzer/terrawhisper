What's the taxonomical classification of Crataegus kansuensis?

Crataegus kansuensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its hierarchical lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Rosales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Rosaeaceae, falling under the genus Crataegus, and is specifically identified by its species name, kansuenis.
