Belleric Myrobalan (Terminalia bellirica)

Belleric Myrobalan (Terminalia bellirica) is a member of the Combretaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. Traditionally, its bark, fruits, and resin have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and unani medicine.
