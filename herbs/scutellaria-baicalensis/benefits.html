1. Has anti oxidant

Oxidative stress is a condition characterized by an imbalance between free radicals and the body's ability to neutralize them, leading to cellular damage.

This health issue, also known as oxidative damage or free radical-induced harm, occurs when the body's antioxidant defenses are overwhelmed by reactive oxygen species.

It is commonly experienced by individuals exposed to environmental toxins, smokers, and those with chronic diseases such as diabetes or cardiovascular conditions.

Scutellaria baicalensis, also known as Huang Qin, has been found to possess anti-oxidant properties that help combat oxidative stress by neutralizing harmful free radicals.

These properties make it a valuable natural remedy for supporting overall cellular health and reducing the risk of age-related diseases.

How this herb helps with has anti oxidant?

Scutellaria baicalensis has anti oxidant because it contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids and alkaloids.

These compounds help neutralize free radicals in the body, reducing oxidative stress. Flavonoids like wogonin and baicalin are particularly known for their strong antioxidant properties. Alkaloids such as baicalein also contribute to the plant's ability to combat oxidative damage.

Together, these constituents support the plant's role in promoting cellular health and preventing oxidative-related diseases.
