Enkianthus deflexus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Ericales, it is placed in the family Ericaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Enkianthus, with its specific designation being the species deflexus.
