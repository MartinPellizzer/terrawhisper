Colebrookea oppositifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Colebrookea oppositifolia?

Colebrookea oppositifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is further categorized into the family Lamiaceae and is a member of the genus Colebrookea, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species oppositifolia.
