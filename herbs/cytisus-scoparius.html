Cytisus Scoparius: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cytisus scoparius, commonly known as yellow broom, is a shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and widespread use in traditional medicine.

This herb is known for its various health benefits, including its potential to support digestive health and reduce inflammation.

Therapeutically, it is valued for its antispasmodic, diuretic, and mild sedative properties.

The bioactive constituents of Cytisus scoparius include flavonoids, alkaloids, and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
