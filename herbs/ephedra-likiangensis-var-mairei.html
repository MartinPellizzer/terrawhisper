Maire'S Ephedra (Ephedra Likiangensis Var. Mairei)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Ephedra likiangensis var. mairei.

Maire's ephedra, scientifically known as Ephedra likiangensis var. mairei, is a rare medicinal herb native to the high altitudes of Yunnan Province in China. It is traditionally valued as an adaptogen and stimulant, prized for its ability to enhance energy and endurance. The plant contains ephedrine, a rare alkaloid with potent bronchodilatory properties, making it historically significant in traditional Chinese medicine for treating respiratory conditions. In modern wellness practices, it is used in herbal formulations to support respiratory health and as a natural stimulant for improving focus and physical performance. With its unique, bitter-sweet flavor and deep cultural roots in Tibetan and Chinese medicine, Maire's ephedra remains a fascinating and historically rich botanical with both traditional and contemporary applications.
