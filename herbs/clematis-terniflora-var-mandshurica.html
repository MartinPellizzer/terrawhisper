Clematis terniflora var. mandshurica

What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis terniflora var. mandshurica?

Clematis terniflora var. mandshurica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Ranunculales. The plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is categorized within the genus Clematis, specifically identified under the species Clematis.
