What's the taxonomical classification of Zelkova schneideriana?

Zelkova schneideriana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within its taxonomic hierarchy, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Rosales and is a member of the family Ulmaceae. Specifically, it is placed within the genus Zelkova, with its unique identification defined by the species name schneideriana.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its bark is dark gray and develops shallow longitudinal fissures as the tree matures. The branches often exhibit a zig-zag growth pattern that contributes to the tree's structural complexity.
