10 Precautions To Take When Using Myrtus Communis (Myrtle)

Taking large amounts or using the herb excessively can also lead to dizziness, headaches, and stomach upset.

Furthermore, individuals with diabetes or low blood sugar should be cautious as the herb can lower blood sugar levels, potentially causing hypoglycemia.
