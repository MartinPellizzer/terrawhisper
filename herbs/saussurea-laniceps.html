Saussurea laniceps

What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea laniceps?

Saussurea laniceps belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, this plant is situated within the genus Saussurea, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as laniceps.

What are the therapeutic actions of Saussurea laniceps?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including inflammation, respiratory conditions, skin infections, and digestive disorders. In traditional medicinal practices, it is often employed to reduce swelling and alleviate pain caused by injuries or internal issues. The plant's components are also utilized to soothe coughs and assist in clearing the lungs during respiratory distress. Additionally, its properties are applied topically to manage skin irritations and promote healing. Practitioners frequently rely on its therapeutic effects to stabilize stomach discomfort and improve overall digestive health.
