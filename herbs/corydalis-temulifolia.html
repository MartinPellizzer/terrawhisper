Corydalis temulifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Corydalis temulifolia?

Corydalis temulifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Papaveraceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Corydalis, with the specific designation of the species temulifolia.

The stems are relatively slender and support both the complex leaf structures and the flowering parts of the plant.
