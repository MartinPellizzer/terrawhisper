Platanthera mandarinorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Platanthera mandarinorum?

Platanthera mandarinorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Asparagales. It is a specialized member of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Platanthera, with its unique identification completed at the species level as mandarinorum.
