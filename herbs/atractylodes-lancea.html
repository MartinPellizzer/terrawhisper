Lanceleaf Atractylodes (Atractylodes Lancea)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

Atractylodes lancea, also known as Chinese Atractylodes, is a widely used herb in traditional Chinese medicine for its various therapeutic properties. It is primarily valued for its ability to strengthen the spleen, promote digestion, and dispel dampness from the body, making it effective in treating conditions such as diarrhea, vomiting, and edema. The root of the plant contains essential oils, alkaloids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its medicinal effects, including anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory activities. In modern pharmacology, it has been studied for its potential in treating gastrointestinal disorders and respiratory infections. Due to its versatility and efficacy, Atractylodes lancea remains a key component in many herbal formulations aimed at improving digestive health and overall well-being.
