Sagina humifusa

Sagina humifusa, also known as wintercress or yellow wintercress, is a low-growing, annual or biennial plant that belongs to the mustard family.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Sagina humifusa include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and boost the immune system.

The plant can be prepared as a tea, tincture, or infused oil, and it is also used as a garnish in salads, adding a peppery flavor and potential health benefits.
