10 Medicinal Constituents Of Prunus Africana (African Plum)

Prunus africana, also known as African plum, has active constituents such as ursolic acid, scopoletin, and isorhapontigenin.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antiprostatic properties, which are beneficial in treating prostatic hyperplasia and other urological issues.

The constituents can also improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from benign prostatic hyperplasia by reducing urinary frequency and discomfort, allowing them to lead a more active and comfortable life.
