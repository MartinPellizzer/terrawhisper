10 Precautions To Take When Using Harpagophytum Procumbens (Devil'S Claw)

Harpagophytum procumbens has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels and thyroid function, as it may interact with diabetes and thyroid medications.

It is also essential to consult with a healthcare professional before taking it if pregnant or breastfeeding, as high doses may cause uterine contractions or affect infant growth.

Failing to monitor these conditions can lead to worsened symptoms, such as hypoglycemia or hyperthyroidism.

Devil's claw is a potent anti-inflammatory that may lower blood pressure and sugar levels, potentially causing adverse reactions when combined with other treatments.
