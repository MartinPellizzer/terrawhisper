10 Health Benefits Of Harpagophytum Procumbens (Devil'S Claw)

Harpagophytum procumbens, also known as devil's claw, has health benefits such as pain and inflammation reduction.

Its medicinal properties include iridoid glycosides and harpagoside, which have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. For example, people with arthritis can experience reduced joint pain and improved mobility, allowing them to perform daily tasks with more ease and comfort.

This can also benefit those with fibromyalgia, who can experience decreased muscle pain and improved sleep quality.

1. Reduces pain in osteoarthritis

Harpagophytum procumbens reduces pain in osteoarthritis because of its unique composition of sesquiterpenoid compounds, particularly harpagoside and iso-harpagoside.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which help alleviate joint pain and stiffness associated with osteoarthritis.

Additionally, devil's claw has been found to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines, further contributing to its analgesic effects and making it a valuable natural remedy for managing osteoarthritis-related pain.

2. Alleviates lower back pain

Harpagophytum procumbens alleviates lower back pain because of its unique properties.

The herb contains compounds such as harpagoside and beta-sitosterol, which have potent anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects. When consumed orally, these compounds can help reduce inflammation and alleviate pain associated with conditions like sciatica, arthritis, and fibromyalgia.

As a result, many people have reported relief from chronic lower back pain when using devil's claw supplements or applying the herb topically to affected areas.

3. Improves joint mobility

Harpagophytum procumbens improves joint mobility because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's root extract has been shown to reduce inflammation and pain in the joints, while also improving flexibility and range of motion.

Devil's claw's anti-inflammatory properties help to reduce swelling and stiffness, allowing for greater freedom of movement and alleviating discomfort associated with conditions such as arthritis and gout.

4. Decreases inflammation

Harpagophytum procumbens decreases inflammation because of its rich composition of harpagosides and other bioactive compounds.

These molecules have been shown to possess potent anti-inflammatory properties, which inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines. Additionally, devil's claw has been found to exhibit antioxidant activity, helping to neutralize free radicals that can contribute to oxidative stress and inflammation.

As a result, Harpagophytum procumbens has become a popular herbal remedy for the treatment of various inflammatory conditions, including arthritis, musculoskeletal pain, and fibromyalgia.

5. Relieves headaches

Harpagophytum procambens relieves headaches because its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties help to reduce pain and discomfort.

The plant contains compounds such as harpagoside and beta-sitosterol, which have been shown to inhibit the production of prostaglandins, which are hormone-like substances that cause pain and inflammation in the body.

By reducing these painful responses, devil's claw may help to alleviate headache symptoms, providing relief from tension headaches, migraines, and other types of headache pain.

Harpagophytum procumbens soothes tendonitis because its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties help reduce swelling and relieve pain in affected joints.

7. Eases muscle pain

Harpagophytum procumbens eases muscle pain because its unique compounds, harpagosides and beta-sitosterol, have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

These components work together to reduce inflammation and discomfort in the muscles, tendons, and joints, providing relief from conditions such as fibromyalgia, arthritis, and back pain.

The herb's ability to block pain signals to the brain also helps to alleviate muscle cramps, spasms, and stiffness, making it a popular natural remedy for alleviating chronic muscle pain.

8. Reduces gout symptoms

Harpagophytum procambens reduces gout symptoms because it exhibits potent anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

The plant's active compounds, such as harpagoside and procumbides, have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and reduce inflammation in joints, thereby alleviating gout-related pain, swelling, and stiffness.

Additionally, devil's claw may also help to decrease uric acid levels in the blood, a primary contributor to gout development.

9. Helps manage rheumatoid arthritis

Harpagophytum procumbens helps manage rheumatoid arthritis because of its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
