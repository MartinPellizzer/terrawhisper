10 Possible Side Effects Of Harpagophytum Procumbens (Devil'S Claw)

These side effects may occur if taken in large doses, combined with other medications, or used for extended periods. For instance, stomach upset can worsen symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and make daily activities, such as eating and socializing, even more challenging.

Additionally, dizziness can cause falls and injuries, especially in older adults or individuals with mobility issues.
