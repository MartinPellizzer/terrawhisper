Vinca Major Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Vinca major, commonly known as periwinkle, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its potential health benefits.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, including vincamine and vincadifformin, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including improving circulation and cognitive function.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical applications can be made from Vinca major to harness its medicinal properties.
