Possible Side Effects of Dupatya obtusifolia

Dupatya obtusifolia can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, as well as headaches, dizziness, and fatigue in some individuals.

In severe cases, overconsumption of the plant can lead to liver damage and kidney problems due to its toxic compounds.

Additionally, Dupatya obtusifolia may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions like diabetes and hypertension.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Dupatya obtusifolia for medicinal purposes to avoid potential complications.
