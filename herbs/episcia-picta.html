Episcia picta

Episcia picta, also known as the painted Episcia or Polka Dot plant, is a species of episcia native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

This episcia plant is known for its vibrant, colorful leaves with spots, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Episcia picta has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its ability to reduce inflammation and fight off infections.
