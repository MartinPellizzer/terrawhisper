What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia trijuga?

Salvia trijuga belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical framework, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further follows the taxonomic path through the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Ultimately, it is identified by its specific genus, Salvia, and its unique species designation, trijuga.
