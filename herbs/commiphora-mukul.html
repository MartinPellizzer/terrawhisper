Commiphora Mukul: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Commiphora mukul, commonly known as guggul, is a resinous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

This herb has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for its various therapeutic properties.

The health benefits of guggul include supporting healthy cholesterol levels, improving joint mobility, and promoting skin health.

Its therapeutic actions involve anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and lipid-lowering effects, attributed to bioactive constituents such as guggulsterone, guggulipid, and monoterpenoids.
