10 Medicinal Constituents Of Malva Sylvestris (Mallow)

Malva sylvestris has active constituents such as mucilage, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, soothing, and protective properties, which can help alleviate digestive issues, skin irritations, and respiratory problems.

For example, the mucilage can soothe irritated stomachs and mouths, while the flavonoids and phenolic acids can protect against infections and promote wound healing, improving overall health and quality of life.

Malva sylvestris flavonoids is a class of plant compounds that have been found to possess various biological activities.
