Chinese Brake Fern (Pyrrosia lingua)

Chinese Brake Fern (Pyrrosia lingua) is a member of the Pteridaceae family, native to East Asia, China, and Japan. Traditionally, its leaves, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, astringent, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
