Anamirta Cocculus: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Anamirta cocculus, commonly known as the sacred lotus or sacred water hyacinth, is a medicinal herb native to Southeast Asia, valued for its therapeutic properties.

This herb is traditionally used in Ayurvedic and traditional medicine for its calming and sedative effects.

The health benefits of Anamirta cocculus include reducing anxiety, improving sleep quality, and supporting digestive health.

Its therapeutic actions involve antispasmodic, sedative, and antidiarrheal effects, while its bioactive constituents include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins.
