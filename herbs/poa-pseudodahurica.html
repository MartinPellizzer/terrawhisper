Poa pseudodahurica

Poa pseudodahurica, also known as Dahurian Blue Grass or False Dahurian Wheat Grass, is a type of perennial grass native to East Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds, which contribute to its health benefits such as reducing fever, alleviating respiratory issues, and supporting immune function.

In traditional herbal medicine, Poa pseudodahurica is often prepared in tea or tincture form to harness its medicinal properties, with some also using it in infusions or as a raw material for supplements.

The plant's health benefits are also attributed to its potential to lower blood pressure, improve digestion, and even exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a sought-after ingredient in herbal remedies.
