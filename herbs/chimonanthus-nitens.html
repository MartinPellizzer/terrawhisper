Chimonanthus nitens

What's the taxonomical classification of Chimonanthus nitens?

Chimonanthus nitens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic lineage through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed under the order Laurales. The plant is a member of the family Calycanthaceae, which falls under the genus Chimonanthus, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Chimonanthus nitens.

This plant is used to treat skin infections, digestive ailments, respiratory issues, and inflammatory conditions. The extracts from the plant possess antimicrobial properties that help combat various bacterial and fungal pathogens on the skin.
