Populus Balsamifera Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Populus balsamifera, commonly known as the balsam poplar, is a deciduous tree native to North America, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and expectorant qualities, making it useful in treating respiratory and skin conditions.

The bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, alkaloids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves can be made from its leaves, bark, and buds to harness its health benefits.
