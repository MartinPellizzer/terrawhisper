What's the taxonomical classification of Silene jeniseensis?

Silene jeniseensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The species is a member of the order Caryophyllales and falls under the family Caryophyllaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Silene, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species jeniseensis.
