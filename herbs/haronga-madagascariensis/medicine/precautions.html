10 Precautions To Take When Using Haronga Madagascariensis

Haronga madagascariensis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use by pregnant or breastfeeding women due to a lack of research on its effects on fetal or infant development.

If not taken carefully, it may cause side effects like diarrhea, stomach cramps, and dizziness.

This is why pregnant and breastfeeding women should exercise caution and consult a healthcare professional before using Haronga madagascariensis.

When using Haronga madagascariensis medicinally, it's important to consume responsibly.
