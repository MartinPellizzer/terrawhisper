What are the therapeutic actions of Murdannia keisak?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat skin infections, wounds, stomachaches, and various types of inflammation. The leaves are often crushed or prepared into topical applications to soothe irritation on the skin. When ingested, the plant material can assist in alleviating digestive discomfort and abdominal pain. These traditional medicinal properties are frequently utilized by local communities for primary healthcare needs. Its application focuses on both external dermatological issues and internal gastrointestinal distress.
