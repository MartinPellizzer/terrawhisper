Aculeate Rock Fern (Cheilanthes Aculeata)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Cheilanthes aculeata.

Aculeate rock fern, scientifically known as Cheilanthes aculeata, is a resilient, drought-tolerant fern commonly found in arid and rocky regions, valued for its medicinal properties. It is traditionally regarded as a medicinal herb with adaptogenic qualities, helping the body resist stress and maintain balance. The plant is believed to offer primary benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and diuretic effects, making it useful for treating skin conditions, urinary tract infections, and respiratory ailments. In traditional Native American and South Asian cultures, it has been used for centuries to support kidney health and as a remedy for wounds and infections. Modern wellness practices continue to explore its potential, with some studies highlighting its unique compounds like flavonoids and tannins, which may contribute to its therapeutic effects.
