Pedicularis confertiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Pedicularis confertiflora?

Pedicularis confertiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Orobanchaceae, which contains the genus Pedicularis, and is specifically identified by its species name, confertiflora.

    What are the herbal preparations of Pedicularis confertiflora?

    Pedicularis confertiflora is prepared primarily as a dried herb for infusions, decoctions, or tinctures to extract its active constituents. Precise medical data regarding long-term human exposure to this specific species remains limited in current scientific literature.
