Long-Leaved Sundew (Drosera longifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds in Long-Leaved Sundew include tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its astringent properties and antioxidant effects. Essential oils and glycosides are also present, offering potential antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory benefits.
