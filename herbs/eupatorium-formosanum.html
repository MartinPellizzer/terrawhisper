Eupatorium formosanum

What's the taxonomical classification of Eupatorium formosanum?

Eupatorium formosanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Asterales. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, placing it among the sunflowers and their relatives. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Eupatorium, with its specific identification being the species formosanum.
