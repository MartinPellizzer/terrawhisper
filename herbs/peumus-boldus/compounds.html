Peumo Boldus (Peumus boldus) Compounds

Peumo Boldus contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Tannins contribute to its astringent effects, while essential oils provide aromatic and antimicrobial benefits. Coumarin and phenolic acids support anti-inflammatory actions. Terpenoids, glycosides, saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its medicinal profile.

Peumo Boldus (Peumus boldus) Monograph

Flavonoids in Peumus boldus are bioactive compounds known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. They contribute to the plant's traditional use in treating digestive and respiratory ailments. These compounds help reduce oxidative stress and support overall health.
