10 Possible Side Effects Of Peumus Boldus (Chilean Boldo)

For instance, digestive issues can lead to stomach pain, diarrhea, and nutrient deficiencies, affecting people's ability to work and maintain daily activities.

These bioactive compounds can trigger an immune response in some individuals, leading to symptoms like hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

The severity of these reactions can vary depending on the individual's level of sensitivity and the amount of Peumus boldus ingested or applied topically.
