10 Health Benefits Of Citrus Oto (Kaffir Lime)

Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving digestion.

Its medicinal properties include high levels of limonene and beta-pinene, which exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. The benefits of this plant can improve people's lives by providing relief from digestive issues and reducing the risk of chronic diseases.

For instance, a person suffering from IBS may experience relief from symptoms, and a person with compromised immune function may benefit from its antimicrobial properties.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Citrus oto.

1. Reduces inflammation

Citrus oto reduces inflammation because of its high content of limonoids, a class of natural compounds with potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thus reducing inflammation in the body.

Additionally, the essential oils present in Citrus oto, such as limonene and citral, also contribute to its anti-inflammatory effects, making it a popular ingredient in traditional medicine.

2. Lowers cholesterol

Citrus oto lowers cholesterol because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids that help to improve lipid profiles.

The essential oils present in the rind of the fruit, particularly limonene, have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the gut, thereby reducing circulating cholesterol levels.

Regular consumption of kaffir lime may also enhance the body's natural lipid-lowering mechanisms, contributing to overall cardiovascular health.

3. Kills bacteria

Citrus oto kills bacteria because it contains high levels of antimicrobial compounds, particularly limonene and linalool.

These essential oils have been shown to exhibit potent antibacterial properties, inhibiting the growth of various microorganisms.

The antimicrobial activity of kaffir lime has been studied in several contexts, including food safety and wound healing, highlighting its potential as a natural antimicrobial agent.

4. Treats fever

Citrus oto treats fever because of its high concentration of vitamin C and flavonoids.

The essential oils present in kaffir lime have antiviral and antibacterial properties that help reduce the severity of fever and fight against underlying infections.

The peel of the kaffir lime can be used to make tea or infusion, which is consumed to alleviate fever and promote overall well-being.

5. Relieves stress

Citrus oto relieves stress because its essential oil has a calming effect on the mind and body.

The oil's high concentration of limonene and beta-pinene has been shown to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, making it a popular ingredient in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.

Its scent also has a grounding effect, helping to reduce feelings of overwhelm and promote a sense of calm and well-being.

6. Boosts immunity

Citrus oto boosts immunity because it is rich in vitamin C, which is essential for a healthy immune system.

The high antioxidant content in kaffir lime also helps protect the body from free radical damage, reducing inflammation and promoting overall well-being.

Additionally, kaffir lime contains flavonoids and limonoids, which have been shown to exhibit antimicrobial properties, further supporting the immune system.

7. Soothes digestion

Citrus oto soothes digestion because of its potent essential oils.

These oils have been traditionally used in Southeast Asian medicine to alleviate digestive issues. The leaves and zest of the kaffir lime contain flavonoids and limonoids, which have anti-inflammatory properties that help calm digestive discomfort and reduce symptoms of nausea, bloating, and indigestion.

Regular consumption can promote healthy gut function.

8. Eliminates worms

Citrus oto eliminates worms because of its unique properties.

The essential oils present in its peel, particularly limonene and beta-pinene, have potent antimicrobial and insecticidal effects. These compounds disrupt the worm's nervous system, ultimately leading to their death.

Additionally, kaffir lime's acidity helps to repel and destroy parasites, making it a popular natural remedy for worm infestations in both humans and animals.

10. Combats cancer

Citrus oto combats cancer because of its potent bioactive compounds.

Studies have isolated naringenin, a flavonoid with anti-proliferative and pro-apoptotic properties, which inhibits cancer cell growth and induces cell death. Additionally, the herb's extracts have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory and antioxidant activities, further contributing to its anti-cancer effects.

Its potential as a natural cancer therapeutic agent is promising and warrants further investigation.
