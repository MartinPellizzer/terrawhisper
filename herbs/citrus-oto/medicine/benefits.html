10 Health Benefits Of Citrus Oto (Kaffir Lime)

Citrus oto has health benefits such as treating digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and exhibiting antimicrobial properties.

The plant's bioactive compounds like limonoids and flavonoids contribute to these medicinal properties. For instance, its antibacterial effects can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections, while its anti-inflammatory properties may ease joint pain and improve cardiovascular health.

These benefits can significantly improve people's lives by reducing the risk of chronic diseases and enhancing overall well-being.
