10 Medicinal Constituents Of Citrus Oto (Kaffir Lime)

Citrus oto has active constituents such as limonoids, flavonoids, and alkaloids that exhibit antimicrobial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties.

These medicinal properties can help alleviate symptoms of various health issues, including respiratory problems, digestive disorders, and skin infections.

For instance, the limonoids in kaffir lime may improve cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol levels and triglycerides, while the flavonoids may boost cognitive function and memory.
