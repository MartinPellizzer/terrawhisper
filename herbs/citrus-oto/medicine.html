What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Citrus Oto (Kaffir Lime)?

Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, has health benefits such as reducing fever, alleviating indigestion, and treating anxiety.

The herb contains flavonoids, limonoids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties. Various medicinal preparations, including infusions, decoctions, and oils, can be made from the herb. While generally safe, excessive consumption may cause gastrointestinal irritation, skin irritation, or allergic reactions.

To use Citrus oto safely, individuals should consult with a healthcare professional or use it under the guidance of an experienced practitioner.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Citrus oto.

What are the health benefits of Citrus oto?

Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and aiding in digestion.

Its essential oils have antimicrobial and antiseptic properties, which can help prevent the spread of diseases. The oil's citral content also exhibits anti-inflammatory effects, making it beneficial for skin and respiratory issues.

Its juice can also help relieve symptoms of indigestion, nausea, and other gastrointestinal problems.

What are the active constituents of Citrus oto?

Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, has active constituents such as limonene, limonin, and naringin.

Limonene has been found to have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, while limonin has shown potential in reducing cholesterol levels and combating certain diseases.

Naringin, a flavonoid glycoside, has been shown to have antioxidant and anti-cancer properties, contributing to the fruit's potential therapeutic benefits.

What are the medicinal preparations of Citrus oto?

Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, has medicinal preparations such as its essential oil, which is used to treat digestive issues, nausea, and fever.

The leaves and bark of the plant have been traditionally used to relieve rheumatism, asthma, and skin conditions. The juice of the fruit is used to treat scurvy and skin problems, while the seeds are used as a diuretic to treat kidney issues.

These preparations are commonly used in traditional medicine in Southeast Asia.

What are the possible side effect of using Citrus oto improperly?

Improper use of Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as dizziness, nausea, and stomach upset due to its high citric acid content.

Additionally, excessive consumption can lead to allergic reactions, such as skin irritation and respiratory issues, in some individuals.

Furthermore, the high vitamin C content can cause an overload in the body, potentially leading to kidney stone formation.

What precautions to take when using Citrus oto medicinally?

Before using Citrus oto, also known as kaffir lime, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting a healthcare professional to determine safe dosage and usage.

Additionally, individuals with citrus allergies, pregnant or breastfeeding women, and those taking certain medications, should exercise caution.

Ingesting the essential oil undiluted can be toxic, so proper dilution is necessary to avoid adverse reactions.
