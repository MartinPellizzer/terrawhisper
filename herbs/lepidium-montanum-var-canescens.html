Lepidium montanum var. canescens

Lepidium montanum var. canescens, commonly known as Mountain Pepper Cress, is a subspecies of the perennial herb Lepidium montanum.

This plant is known for its peppery flavor and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

The health benefits of Mountain Pepper Cress include its ability to aid digestion, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of this plant include its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, which can be attributed to its bioactive constituents, including isothiocyanates and glucosinolates.
