Rosa elliptica subvar. cheriensis

Rosa elliptica subvar. cheriensis, also known as Rosa rugosa var. cheriensis, is a type of flowering shrub native to East Asia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, due to its high content of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

The health benefits of Rosa elliptica subvar. cheriensis include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve wound healing, and exhibit antioxidant effects.

The plant can be prepared as herbal teas, infusions, or decoctions, and its leaves, stems, and flowers are often used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions.
