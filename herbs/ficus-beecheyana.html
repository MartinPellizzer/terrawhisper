Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Ficus, specifically identified by its unique species name, beecheyana.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ficus beecheyana has a large, woody habit characterized by a spreading crown and a trunk often adorned with numerous adventitious aerial roots. The venation is prominent, with secondary veins extending toward the margins in a pinnate fashion.
