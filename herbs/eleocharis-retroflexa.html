What is Eleocharis retroflexa?

Eleocharis retroflexa is a perennial aquatic plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It grows to 10-60 cm in height and 0.5-2 mm in diameter, with a slender, smooth stem and linear-lanceolate leaves that are 2-5 mm long and 0.5-1.5 mm wide. The leaves are typically flat, green, and arranged in two rows along the stem, with a sheathing base that clasps the stem. The plant produces a solitary spike 2-20 cm long, with spikelets 2-4 mm long that are arranged in a single whorl.

The spikelets contain 2-4 florets, each with a 1-2 mm long, green, lanceolate bract.
