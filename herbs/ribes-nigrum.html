Ribes Nigrum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

This herb is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, offering numerous health benefits such as supporting immune function, reducing inflammation, and improving skin health.

The bioactive constituents include anthocyanins, flavonoids, vitamin C, and polyphenols, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
