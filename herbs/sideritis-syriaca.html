What is Sideritis Of Syria (Sideritis Syriaca)?

Sideritis syriaca, also known as Sideritis of Syria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Syria, and is widely used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanical Classification and Taxonomy

The scientific name of Sideritis of Syria is Sideritis syriaca. This plant belongs to the kingdom Plantae, division Magnoliophyta, class Lamiales, order Lamiales, family Lamiaceae, genus Sideritis, and species Sideritis syriaca. It is commonly known as "Syringa" in the Levant region and "Sideritis" in the Balkans. The synonyms for this plant include Sideritis syriaca, Sideritis syriaca var. syriaca, and Sideritis syriaca subsp. syriaca.
