What's the taxonomical classification of Peristrophe japonica?

Peristrophe japonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Lamiales and falls under the family Acanthaceae. Taxonomically, it is identified by the genus Peristrophe, with its specific designation being the species japonica.
