What's the taxonomical classification of Daphniphyllum oldhamii?

Daphniphyllum oldhamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Saxifragales. It is a member of the family Daphniphyllaceae, specifically falling within the genus Daphniphyllum, with its unique biological classification completed by the specific epithet oldhamii.
