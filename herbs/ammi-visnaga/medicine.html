What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Ammi Visnaga (Khella)?

Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, has health benefits such as reducing anxiety, treating hypertension, and alleviating respiratory issues.

Its medicinal constituents, including coumarins and terpenoids, contribute to its therapeutic effects. Traditionally, khella is prepared as an infusion, tincture, or decoction to utilize its medicinal properties. Possible side effects include gastrointestinal upset and interactions with anticoagulant medications.

Precautions include consulting a healthcare professional before using khella, particularly for pregnant or breastfeeding individuals and those taking certain medications.

What are the health benefits of Ammi visnaga ?

Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, has health benefits such as lowering blood pressure and reducing fluid retention.

The plant's seeds contain a compound called visnagen, which has been shown to inhibit the production of aldosterone, a hormone that regulates electrolyte balance.

Studies have demonstrated that khella extract can be effective in treating conditions like edema, hypertension, and kidney stones, making it a valuable natural remedy for various health issues.

What are the active constituents of Ammi visnaga ?

Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, has active constituents such as visnagin and isovisnagin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The seeds and roots of the plant contain essential oils, including sesquiterpenes and aldehydes, which contribute to its pharmacological effects.

These compounds have been traditionally used to treat various conditions, including hypertension, asthma, and kidney stones, due to their vasodilatory and diuretic properties.

What are the medicinal preparations of Ammi visnaga ?

Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, has medicinal preparations such as tinctures and infusions that are used to treat various ailments.

The seeds of Ammi visnaga are rich in khellin and cymarin, compounds that have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, bronchodilatory, and cardiotonic properties.

These preparations are often used to treat respiratory problems, such as asthma and bronchitis, as well as heart conditions like palpitations and arrhythmias.

What are the possible side effect of using Ammi visnaga improperly?

Improper use of Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as stomach upset, diarrhea, and vomiting.

Some individuals may also experience allergic reactions, including hives and itching. Furthermore, taking high doses or prolonged use can lead to kidney damage and electrolyte imbalance.

People with pre-existing conditions, such as kidney disease, should consult with a healthcare professional before using khella to minimize potential risks.

What precautions to take when using Ammi visnaga medicinally?

Before using Ammi visnaga, also known as khella, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting a healthcare professional to determine the safe dosage and potential interactions with other medications.

Additionally, pregnant and breastfeeding women should avoid using Ammi visnaga as its effects on the fetus and infant are not well-documented.

Use with caution in patients with kidney or liver disease.
