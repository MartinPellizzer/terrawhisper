Possible Side Effects of Solanum sparsipilum

Solanum sparsipilum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its toxic glycoalkaloids.

Prolonged exposure to its leaves or berries can lead to cardiac problems, including irregular heartbeat and palpitations, in some individuals.

In rare cases, improper consumption of Solanum sparsipilum can also cause neurological symptoms, such as dizziness, headaches, and tremors.

It is essential to use this plant under the guidance of a medical professional or a qualified herbalist to avoid potential health risks.
