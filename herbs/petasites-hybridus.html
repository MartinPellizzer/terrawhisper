What to know about Petasites hybridus before using it medicinally

Petasites hybridus, commonly known as butterbur, is a perennial herb that has been used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of health conditions, including migraines, tension headaches, and allergic rhinitis.

People commonly prepare butterbur as a herbal supplement in the form of capsules or tablets, often standardized to contain a specific concentration of the herb's bioactive compounds.
