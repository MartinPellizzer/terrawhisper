10 Medicinal Preparations Of Cornus Canadensis

Cornus canadensis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and decoctions.

These are useful for treating gastrointestinal issues, such as diarrhea and dyspepsia. The plant's berries can also be made into a jam or syrup to treat coughs and sore throats. The roots and stems can be used to make tinctures, which are useful for treating fever and inflammation.

These preparations are also said to have antiseptic and antispasmodic properties.
