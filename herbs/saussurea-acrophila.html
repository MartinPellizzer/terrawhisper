What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea acrophila?

Saussurea acrophila belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae and is classified under the genus Saussurea, specifically identified by the species name acrophila.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Saussurea acrophila has a rosette-forming growth habit characterized by dense, basal leaves that are lanceolate to oblong in shape with entire or slightly dentate margins. Its inflorescence consists of a compact, many-flowered capitulum typically surrounded by specialized, colorful involucral bracts. The individual florets are tubular and adapted for high-altitude pollination environments.
