Bupleurum pusillum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a prominent representative of the family Apiaceae, which is characterized by its complex flowering structures. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Bupleurum, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species name pusillum.
