What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita linearifolia?

Chirita linearifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically situated within the genus Chirita, and is identified by its unique specific epithet, linearifolia.
