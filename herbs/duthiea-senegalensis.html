Duthiea Senegalensis

Duthiea senegalensis, commonly known as the African breadfruit, is a tropical tree native to West Africa, valued for its medicinal properties.

The leaves and seeds of this plant have been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat a variety of ailments, including digestive issues, skin infections, and respiratory conditions. Its high nutritional content, rich in vitamins and minerals, makes it beneficial for improving overall health and boosting immunity. The plant also contains bioactive compounds that exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, supporting its use in traditional healing practices.

Due to its versatile therapeutic potential, Duthiea senegalensis continues to be an important resource in both traditional and complementary medicine systems.
