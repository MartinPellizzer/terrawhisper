Laxa Anthemis (Anthemis laxa)

Laxa Anthemis (Anthemis laxa) is a member of the Compositae family, native to Central Asia, Southern Europe, and Middle East. Traditionally, its flowers, leaves, and resin have been used for infusions, decoctions, and oil infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and sedative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and african traditional medicine.
