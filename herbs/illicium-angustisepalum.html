Illicium angustisepalum

What's the taxonomical classification of Illicium angustisepalum?

Illicium angustisepalum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Austrobaileyales. Taxonomically, it is a member of the family Schisandraceae and is placed within the genus Illicium, ultimately identified by its specific species designation, angustisepalum.
