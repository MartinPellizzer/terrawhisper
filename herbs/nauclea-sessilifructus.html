Nauclea sessilifructus

Nauclea sessilifructus, commonly known as Quinine Tree or Nauclea, is a plant species native to Southeast Asia and China, known for its medicinal properties.

The therapeutic actions of Nauclea sessilifructus include its ability to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimalarial, and antibacterial properties, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents, such as naucleadin, naucleatin, and quinine.
