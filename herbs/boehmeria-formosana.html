Boehmeria formosana

What's the taxonomical classification of Boehmeria formosana?

Boehmeria formosana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Rosales and is a member of the family Urticaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Boehmeria, with its specific designation being the species formosana.
