Homalomena occulta has large, leathery, elliptical leaves that often exhibit prominent venation and a glossy texture.       What side-effects this plant can have?

      Homalomena occulta can cause severe skin irritation, blistering, and allergic contact dermatitis upon direct contact with its sap or foliage. The plant contains irritant compounds that react with human tissue to produce redness and intense itching. Some individuals may experience swelling or localized inflammation if the sap touches sensitive areas like the eyes. While systemic toxicity is not extensively documented in humans, caution is necessary to prevent accidental ingestion.
