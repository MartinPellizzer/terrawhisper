What's the taxonomical classification of Pogostemon brevicorollus?

Pogostemon brevicorollus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its evolutionary lineage, it falls under the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pogostemon, and its unique species designation, brevicorollus.
