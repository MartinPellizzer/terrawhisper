Armoracia Rusticana: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Armoracia rusticana, commonly known as horseradish, is a biennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its pungent, spicy root that is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications.

This herb is known for its potent antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which contribute to its various health benefits, including supporting digestion and boosting the immune system.

Therapeutically, horseradish is used to alleviate respiratory congestion and promote detoxification.

Its bioactive constituents include glucosinolates, isothiocyanates, and essential oils, which are responsible for its strong aroma and medicinal effects.
