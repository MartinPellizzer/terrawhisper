Garden Horseradish (Armoracia rusticana)

Garden Horseradish (Armoracia rusticana) is a member of the Brassicaceae family, native to Europe, Asia, and North America. Traditionally, its roots, leaves, and shoots have been used for decoctions, culinary uses, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, bitter, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and traditional chinese medicine.
