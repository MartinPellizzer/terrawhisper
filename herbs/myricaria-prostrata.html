What's the taxonomical classification of Myricaria prostrata?

Myricaria prostrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it in the order Caryophyllales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Tamaricaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Myricaria, with the specific designation of the species prostrata.
