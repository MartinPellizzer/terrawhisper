Adenophora khasiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Adenophora khasiana?

Adenophora khasiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic lineage further, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Campanulaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus, Adenophora, and its specific species name, khasiana.

What are the therapeutic actions of Adenophora khasiana?

Adenophora khasiana has potent antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties that are primarily utilized for treating respiratory infections and skin ailments. In traditional practices, the plant serves as a remedy for managing systemic infections and febrile conditions. Adenophora khasiana is prepared as a decoction, a powder, or a juice derived from its various plant parts to treat different ailments.
