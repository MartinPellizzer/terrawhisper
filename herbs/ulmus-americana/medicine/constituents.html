10 Medicinal Constituents Of Ulmus Americana (American Elm)

Ulmus americana, also known as American Elm, has active constituents such as tannins, flavonoids, and salicylic acid.

These constituents have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help alleviate skin conditions, wounds, and fever. The salicylic acid in American Elm can also help reduce pain and inflammation, making it a potential natural alternative for managing conditions like arthritis.

This plant has been used to create medicines that can improve people's quality of life by providing relief from chronic pain and promoting overall well-being.
