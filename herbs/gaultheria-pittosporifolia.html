What is Gaultheria pittosporifolia?

Gaultheria pittosporifolia is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

Gaultheria pittosporifolia produces small, greenish-white flowers in a raceme, which are followed by red or purple berries that are 8-10 millimeters in diameter.
