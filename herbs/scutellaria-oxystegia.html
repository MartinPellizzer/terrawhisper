What is Scutellaria oxystegia?

Scutellaria oxystegia is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is an annual herb with a stem height of up to 40 cm, having 4-angled, hairy, and glandular branches. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, 5-10 cm long, and 2-5 cm wide, with a serrated margin and a petiole of 5-10 mm. The inflorescence is a terminal spike of 5-10 mm long, with a tubular calyx and a tubular corolla of 10-15 mm long, which is blue or purple in color and has 4 lobes.

The fruit is a schizocarp, consisting of 4 nutlets, each 3-5 mm long.
