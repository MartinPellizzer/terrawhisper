10 Best Stellaria Media Health Benefits

Stellaria media, commonly known as chickweed, is a nutrient-rich plant that offers several health benefits due to its high content of vitamins A, C, and E, as well as minerals like calcium and iron.

Chickweed also has mild diuretic properties, which may aid in reducing water retention and supporting kidney function. Its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties may contribute to skin health, helping to reduce redness and irritation.

Additionally, chickweed is sometimes used in herbal teas to relieve stress and promote relaxation due to its calming effects on the nervous system.
