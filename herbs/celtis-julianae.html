Celtis julianae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Rosales and falls within the family Cannabaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Celtis and the species julianae.
