Herniaria suffruticosa

Herniaria suffruticosa, also known as dog's tooth grass, is a perennial plant species within the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine due to its diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce swelling and alleviate pain.

In herbal preparations, Herniaria suffruticosa is often used to treat conditions such as kidney stones, urinary tract infections, and rheumatism, offering relief from symptoms like painful urination and joint inflammation.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to improve digestion, reduce fever, and promote overall well-being when consumed in moderation as a herbal tea or tincture.
