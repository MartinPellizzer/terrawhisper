10 Precautions To Take When Using Matricaria Discoidea (Pineapple Weed)

Matricaria discoidea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as pregnant or breastfeeding women taking caution.

It's essential to note that ingesting this herb may stimulate the uterus and cause contractions. If precautions are not taken, it can lead to miscarriage or premature birth.

Additionally, allergic reactions can occur, manifesting as skin rashes or respiratory issues, emphasizing the need for proper dosing and usage guidelines.
