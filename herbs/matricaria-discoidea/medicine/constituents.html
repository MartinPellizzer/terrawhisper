10 Medicinal Constituents Of Matricaria Discoidea (Pineapple Weed)

Matricaria discoidea has active constituents such as flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and essential oils.

These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it a valuable plant for various health issues. Its ability to reduce inflammation can alleviate symptoms of arthritis, while its antimicrobial properties can combat skin infections and wounds.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties can protect against cell damage, potentially improving overall health and well-being.
