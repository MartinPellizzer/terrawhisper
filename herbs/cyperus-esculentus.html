Cyperus Esculentus: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cyperus esculentus, commonly known as yellow nutsedge, is a perennial herb belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its tuberous roots and clusters of yellow flowers.

This herb is valued for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

Therapeutically, it is used to support digestive health, reduce swelling, and enhance immune function.

The bioactive constituents of Cyperus esculentus include alkaloids, flavonoids, saponins, and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
