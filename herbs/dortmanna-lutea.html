Dortmanna lutea

Dortmanna lutea, commonly known as Golden Dock or Yellow Dock, is a perennial plant in the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae) native to Europe and Western Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, providing various health benefits such as aiding in the treatment of digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and acting as a natural laxative.

The therapeutic actions of Dortmanna lutea include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve bowel health, and exhibit antioxidant properties.
