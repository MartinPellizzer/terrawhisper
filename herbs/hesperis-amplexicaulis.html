Hesperis amplexicaulis

Hesperis amplexicaulis, also known as the Alpine Sweet Rocket or Alpine Rocket, is a flowering plant that belongs to the mustard family, characterized by its tall spikes of fragrant, white or pale purple flowers.

Traditionally, Hesperis amplexicaulis has been used in herbal medicine for its expectorant and antispasmodic properties, helping to relieve respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs, as well as muscle spasms.

The plant is also valued for its health benefits, including its ability to reduce inflammation, improve sleep quality, and act as a natural antiseptic.

In herbal preparations, Hesperis amplexicaulis can be consumed as a tea, tincture, or infused oil, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal effects.
