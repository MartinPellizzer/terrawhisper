Phoebe hunanensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Phoebe hunanensis?

Phoebe hunanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Laurales. It is further nested within the family Lauraceae, falling under the genus Phoebe, and is specifically identified by its species name, hunanensis.

Detailed toxicological studies on this specific species remain limited, so caution is necessary when approaching unknown flora.
