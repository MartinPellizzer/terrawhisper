10 Possible Side Effects Of Pulsatilla Chinensis (Chinese Windflower)

Pulsatilla chinensis, also known as Chinese windflower, has some side effects when used improperly, such as headaches, dizziness, and nausea due to excessive consumption or interaction with other medications.

These side effects can be caused by the herb's high content of sesquiterpene lactones, which can stimulate the nervous system and cause adverse reactions.

If not managed properly, these symptoms can worsen, leading to decreased productivity, strained relationships, and reduced quality of life.
