Devil'S Ivy (Cissus quadrangularis)

Devil'S Ivy (Cissus quadrangularis) is a member of the Rhamnaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. Traditionally, its stems, leaves, and roots have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and african traditional medicine.
