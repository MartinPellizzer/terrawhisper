Impatiens davidii

What's the taxonomical classification of Impatiens davidii?

Impatiens davidii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Ericales. The plant is a member of the family Balsaminaceae and falls under the genus Impatiens, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species Impatiens davidii.
