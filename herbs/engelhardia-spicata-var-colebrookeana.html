Engelhardia spicata var. colebrookeana

What's the taxonomical classification of Engelhardia spicata var. colebrookeana?

Engelhardia spicata var. colebrookeana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Fagales and further categorized under the family Juglandaceae. This specific botanical variety is identified by its genus, Engelhardia, and is formally designated as the species Engelhardia spicata var. colebrookeana.
