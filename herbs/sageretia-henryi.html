What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Sageretia henryi has opposite, decussate leaves that are elliptical to lanceolate in shape, featuring serrated margins and a distinctively textured, somewhat rugose surface. These flowers often possess delicate petals and reproductive structures characteristic of the Rubiaceae family.
