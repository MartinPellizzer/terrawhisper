What's the taxonomical classification of Ampelopsis glandulosa var. hancei?

Ampelopsis glandulosa var. hancei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Vitales.
