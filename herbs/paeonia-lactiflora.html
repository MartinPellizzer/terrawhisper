What to know about Paeonia lactiflora before using it medicinally

Paeonia lactiflora, commonly known as peony, is a medicinal herb used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and anxiety disorders.

Main health benefits of Paeonia lactiflora include its anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and sedative properties. This herb is rich in therapeutic properties such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal effects. Bioactive compounds present in Paeonia lactiflora include paeoniflorin, paeonol, and benzoylbenzene, which have been studied for their potential health benefits.

People often use Paeonia lactiflora to prepare a root-based tea or tincture, which is traditionally used to alleviate menstrual cramps and promote relaxation.
