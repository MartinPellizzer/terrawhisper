Althaea Officinalis Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Althaea officinalis, commonly known as marshmallow, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, valued for its soothing properties.

This herb is renowned for its ability to reduce inflammation, soothe irritated tissues, and promote healing, making it beneficial for respiratory and digestive health.

The therapeutic actions of marshmallow are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including mucilage, polysaccharides, and flavonoids, which have demulcent and anti-inflammatory effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, syrups, and poultices can be made from its roots and leaves to harness its healing properties.
