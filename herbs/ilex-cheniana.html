What is Ilex cheniana?

Ilex cheniana is an evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by elliptical to lanceolate leaves that are 4-12 cm long, with crenate margins and a glossy surface, and produce small white flowers in axillary clusters. The plant is dioecious, with male and female flowers on separate plants. The female plants produce red, berry-like drupes that are 6-8 mm in diameter.

Ilex cheniana typically grows to a height of 2-4 meters.
