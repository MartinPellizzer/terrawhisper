What is Vincetoxicum lateriflorum?

Vincetoxicum lateriflorum is a perennial plant native to China and Korea.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is a member of the genus Vincetoxicum. The plant grows to a height of 0.3-1.2 meters, with erect stems and ovate-lanceolate leaves that are 5-10 cm long and 2-4 cm wide. The leaves are opposite, glabrous, and have a cuneate base and a serrulate margin.

The plant produces white or yellowish flowers in lateral racemes, with corolla lobes that are 10-12 mm long and have a tubular shape.
