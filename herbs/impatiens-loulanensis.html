What's the taxonomical classification of Impatiens loulanensis?

Impatiens loulaneensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it resides in the order Ericales and is a member of the family Balsaminaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Impatiens, with its specific identity defined by the species name loulaneensis.
