Abies fargesii var. faxoniana

What's the taxonomical classification of Abies fargesii var. faxoniana?

Abies fargesii var. faxoniana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Pinales and is a part of the family Pinaceae. More specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Abies, where it is identified as the particular variety faxoniana of the species Abies fargesii.
