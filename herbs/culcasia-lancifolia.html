Culcasia lancifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Culcasia lancifolia?

Culcasia lancifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls into the order Alismatales and is a member of the family Araceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Culcasia and the species lancifolia.

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including skin infections, wounds, stomach aches, and intestinal problems. Its leaves are often applied topically to soothe inflammation and promote the healing of cuts or rashes. When prepared for internal use, the plant acts as a remedy for digestive distress and discomfort. Local traditional practices frequently utilize its medicinal properties to manage minor illnesses and infections. This botanical resource serves as a vital component in many regional herbal treatments.
