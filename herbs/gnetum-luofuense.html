Gnetum luofuense

What's the taxonomical classification of Gnetum luofuense?

Gnetum luofuense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Gnetidae and the order Gnetales. Within this lineage, it is placed in the family Gnetaceae, falling under the genus Gnetum, and is specifically identified by the species name luofuense.
