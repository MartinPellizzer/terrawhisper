What's the taxonomical classification of Paraprenanthes longiloba?

Paraprenanthes longiloba belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Asterales, the plant is a member of the family Asteraceae. At the most specific levels of its classification, it is identified as belonging to the genus Paraprenanthes, with its specific designation being the species longiloba.
