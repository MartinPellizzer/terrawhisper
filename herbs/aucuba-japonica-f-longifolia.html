Aucuba japonica f. longifolia

Aucuba japonica f. longifolia, also known as gold dust plant, is a cultivar of the Aucuba japonica species, a type of evergreen shrub native to East Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which can be used to treat various health issues.

The gold dust plant has been traditionally used to promote health and well-being, with potential benefits including improved digestion, reduced stress and anxiety, and enhanced immune function.

In herbal preparations, the leaves of the Aucuba japonica f. longifolia plant are often used in teas, infusions, and tinctures, and can also be used topically to treat skin conditions and wounds.
