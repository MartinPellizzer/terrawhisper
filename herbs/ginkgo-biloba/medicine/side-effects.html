10 Possible Side Effects Of Ginkgo Biloba (Maidenhair Tree)

Ginkgo biloba has some side effects when used improperly, such as bleeding, headaches, and nausea.

These side effects occur due to the herb's ability to thin blood, which can cause bleeding in people with bleeding disorders or those taking blood-thinning medications. In severe cases, this can lead to dizziness, fainting, and falls, making daily activities more challenging and increasing the risk of accidents.

This can worsen lives by limiting mobility and independence.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Ginkgo biloba if used imporperly.
