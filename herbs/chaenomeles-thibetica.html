Chaenomeles thibetica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Chaenomeles, and its species name, thiibetica.
