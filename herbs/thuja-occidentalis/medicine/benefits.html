10 Health Benefits Of Thuja Occidentalis (Arborvitae)

Thuja occidentalis has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that have been used to treat various ailments.

Its medicinal properties include its ability to reduce swelling and ease pain, making it effective in treating arthritis, gout, and other inflammatory conditions. Additionally, its antifungal and antibacterial properties can help combat skin infections and respiratory issues.

These benefits can improve people's lives by providing relief from chronic pain, promoting healthy skin and respiratory systems, and boosting the immune system, ultimately enhancing overall well-being.

1. Strengthens immune system

Thuja occidentalis strengthens immune system because it contains a compound called thujone, which has been shown to have antibacterial and antiviral properties.

This compound helps to stimulate the body's natural defenses against infection and disease, boosting the production of white blood cells and activating the body's natural killer cells to fight off pathogens.

Additionally, thujone has anti-inflammatory effects, reducing inflammation and oxidative stress that can weaken the immune system.

2. Reduces inflammation

Thuja occidentalis reduces inflammation because of its rich abundance of bioactive compounds, particularly cedrol and thujone.

These compounds exhibit potent anti-inflammatory properties, inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes that contribute to swelling and pain. Additionally, arborvitae's antioxidant properties help scavenge free radicals, further mitigating oxidative stress and inflammation in the body.

This natural remedy has been traditionally used to soothe joint pain, skin irritations, and respiratory issues.

3. Alleviates respiratory issues

Thuja occidentalis alleviates respiratory issues because of its natural antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oils present in the plant's leaves and twigs have been shown to help reduce congestion and inflammation in the lungs, making it an effective natural remedy for conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and chronic coughs.

Additionally, arborvitae has been traditionally used to ease respiratory distress and promote healthy breathing patterns.

4. Improves digestion

Thuja occidentalis improves digestion because it contains compounds that have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These compounds help to soothe the digestive tract, reducing inflammation and discomfort associated with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) and other digestive disorders.

Additionally, arborvitae has been traditionally used to treat symptoms of indigestion, nausea, and bloating, making it a natural remedy for promoting overall digestive health and well-being.

5. Relieves menstrual cramps

Thuja occidentalis relieves menstrual cramps because of its unique blend of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains cedrol, a sesquiterpene that has been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which can help alleviate the painful contractions associated with menstruation. Additionally, Thuja's essential oils have been found to relax uterine muscles, reducing spasms and cramping.

As a result, arborvitae has become a popular natural remedy for menstrual relief, offering a safe and effective alternative to conventional painkillers.

6. Boosts skin health

Thuja occidentalis boosts skin health because of its rich antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The essential oils extracted from the plant have been shown to reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the skin, which can help alleviate symptoms of conditions such as acne, rosacea, and eczema.

Additionally, Thuja occidentalis has antimicrobial properties that can help combat bacterial and fungal infections, promoting a healthy and balanced skin microbiome.

7. Reduces arthritis pain

Thuja occidentalis reduces arthritis pain because of its unique chemical composition.

The essential oil extracted from its leaves and twigs contains beta-thujone, a compound that has anti-inflammatory properties.

When applied topically or taken internally in supplement form, this oil helps to reduce inflammation and ease joint stiffness and pain associated with conditions such as osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.

8. Eliminates headaches

Thuja occidentalis eliminates headaches because of its unique essential oil composition.

The oil contains compounds like borneol, bornyl acetate, and alpha-pinene, which have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. When inhaled or applied topically, these compounds interact with the brain's neurotransmitters to reduce pain perception and inflammation, effectively alleviating headache symptoms.

Additionally, arborvitae's essential oil has been shown to have a calming effect on the mind and body, further contributing to its headache-relieving properties.

9. Kills bacteria and viruses

Thuja occidentalis kills bacteria and viruses because of its unique composition.

The essential oil extracted from its leaves contains a compound called thujone, which has potent antimicrobial properties. Thujone disrupts the cell membrane of microorganisms, ultimately leading to their death.

Additionally, other components in the oil, such as terpenes, have been shown to exhibit antiviral activity, making arborvitae an effective natural remedy against a range of microbial pathogens.

10. Stimulates appetite

Thuja occidentalis stimulates appetite because it contains a unique combination of essential oils and compounds that have been shown to increase digestive enzymes and improve gut health.

The aromatic properties of arborvitae have been traditionally used to treat indigestion and nausea, and its stimulating effects on the stomach can help to increase hunger and desire for food.
