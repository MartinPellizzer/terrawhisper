Carob (Ceratonia siliqua) Actions

Carob exhibits several key therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, astringent, and tonic properties. It acts as a carminative to relieve gas, a diuretic to support kidney function, and a bitter stimulant to enhance digestion. Additionally, it has antispasmodic, sedative, and expectorant effects, making it useful for respiratory and digestive ailments.
