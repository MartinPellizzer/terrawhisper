Campylotropis delaavay belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Fabales. The plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, specifically falling under the genus Campylotropis, with its unique designation as the species delaavay.
