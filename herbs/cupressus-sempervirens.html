Italian Cypress (Cupressus sempervirens)

Italian Cypress (Cupressus sempervirens) is a member of the Cupressaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Southern Europe, and North Africa. Traditionally, its resin, bark, and wood have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, antispasmodic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
