Cupressus Sempervirens Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Cupressus sempervirens, commonly known as the Mediterranean cypress, is a small evergreen tree native to the Mediterranean region, often used in herbal medicine for its therapeutic properties.

This herb is valued for its ability to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and promote mental clarity.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include flavonoids, terpenes, and essential oils, which have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and essential oils can be made from Cupressus sempervirens to harness its medicinal benefits.
