Possible Side Effects of cupressus sempervirens

Cupressus sempervirens can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing allergic reactions in some individuals, particularly those with respiratory issues, resulting in symptoms like congestion, coughing, and shortness of breath.

In rare cases, improper use of Cupressus sempervirens can lead to interactions with certain medications, such as blood thinners, which may exacerbate bleeding or bruising.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Cupressus sempervirens for medicinal purposes to minimize the risk of these potential side effects.
