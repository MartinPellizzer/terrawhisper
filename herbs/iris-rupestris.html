Iris rupestris

Iris rupestris, also known as the Alpine Iris, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Iris genus and is commonly found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that can help to soothe and protect the skin.

The health benefits of Iris rupestris include its ability to reduce swelling and alleviate pain, making it a popular remedy for treating bruises, wounds, and minor injuries.

Iris rupestris can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and salves, which are often used to treat various skin conditions and promote wound healing.
