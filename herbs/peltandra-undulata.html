Arrowhead Plant (Peltandra Undulata)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Peltandra undulata.

Arrowhead plant, scientifically known as Peltandra undulata, is a medicinal herb native to the southeastern United States, valued for its therapeutic properties and adaptogenic qualities. It is traditionally used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote detoxification due to its high concentration of bioactive compounds like saponins and alkaloids. In Native American traditions, the plant has been employed for centuries to treat ailments such as digestive disorders, respiratory infections, and skin conditions, often prepared as a tea or poultice. Modern wellness applications include its use in natural remedies for stress relief, immune support, and as a dietary supplement to enhance overall vitality. One of its unique features is its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and the presence of a rare compound called undulatine, which contributes to its medicinal potency and historical significance in herbal medicine.
