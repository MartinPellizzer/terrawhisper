Possible Side Effects of lysimachia vulgaris

Lysimachia vulgaris can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing allergic reactions, skin irritation, and increased sensitivity to sunlight, which may lead to phototoxicity.

Additionally, using Lysimachia vulgaris topically can cause skin redness, itching, and rashes, particularly in individuals with sensitive skin.

Furthermore, improper use of the herb may also cause hormonal imbalances and affect menstrual cycles in women.
