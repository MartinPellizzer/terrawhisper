Photinia villosa var. sinica

What's the taxonomical classification of Photinia villosa var. sinica?

Photinia villosa var. sinica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is a member of the family Rosaceae and is placed within the genus Photinia. Ultimately, the specific botanical identity of this plant is defined by its varietal designation, Photinia villosa var. sinica.
