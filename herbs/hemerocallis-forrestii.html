What's the taxonomical classification of Hemerocallis forrestii?

Hemerocallis forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the family Asphodelaceae, falling under the genus Hemerocallis, and is specifically identified by its species name, forrestii.
