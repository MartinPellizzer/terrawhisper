What's the taxonomical classification of Piper thomsonii?

Piper thomsonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Piperales, it falls under the family Piperaceae. Specifically, this plant is categorized within the genus Piper, and its unique scientific designation is defined by its species name, thomsonii.
