Lespedeza floribunda

What's the taxonomical classification of Lespedeza floribunda?

Lespedeza floribunda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic lineage through the order Fabales, the plant is a member of the family Fabaceae. It is specifically identified within the genus Lespedeza, ultimately reaching its unique species designation as Lespedeza floribunda.
