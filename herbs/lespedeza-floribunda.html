Lespedeza floribunda has trifoliate leaves consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets and small, pea-like yellow flowers arranged in dense terminal clusters. The plant produces small, flattened legume pods that turn brown as they mature. These botanical features collectively define its structural identity within the Fabaceae family.
