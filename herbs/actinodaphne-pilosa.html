What's the taxonomical classification of Actinodaphne pilosa?

Actinodaphne pilosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Laurales and belongs to the family Lauraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Actinodaphne, and its unique species designation, pilosa.
