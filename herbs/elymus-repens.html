Additionally, Elymus repens may lead to eye redness and irritation, potentially causing discomfort or blurred vision. In some cases, it can result in dizziness, indicating a possible systemic reaction. These side effects highlight the importance of caution when handling or consuming this herb, especially for individuals with known allergies or sensitive skin.
