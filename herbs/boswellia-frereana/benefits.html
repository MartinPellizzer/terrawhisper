10 Best Boswellia Frereana Health Benefits

Boswellia frereana, also known as Indian frankincense, is a resin derived from the tree Boswellia sacra, which is native to regions of Africa and the Middle East.

This natural supplement is renowned for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of conditions such as arthritis and inflammatory bowel disease. Studies suggest that the active compounds in Boswellia frereana, known as boswellic acids, inhibit the production of inflammatory enzymes in the body. Additionally, it may support joint health by reducing pain and improving mobility in individuals with osteoarthritis.

The resin is also valued for its potential to enhance cognitive function and reduce stress, making it a versatile natural remedy for overall wellness.

1. Reduces inflammation

Boswellia frereana reduces inflammation by containing active compounds called boswellic acids, which inhibit the production of inflammatory enzymes such as 5-lipoxygenase.

These enzymes are responsible for the synthesis of leukotrienes, which are key mediators of inflammatory responses in the body. This anti-inflammatory effect makes Boswellia frereana beneficial for conditions like arthritis, asthma, and inflammatory bowel disease. Studies have shown that it can help alleviate pain and swelling associated with chronic inflammatory disorders.

Its natural origin and potential for fewer side effects compared to synthetic anti-inflammatory drugs make it an appealing alternative in holistic medicine.
