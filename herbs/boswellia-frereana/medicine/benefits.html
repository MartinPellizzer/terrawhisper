10 Health Benefits Of Boswellia Frereana (Frankincense)

Boswellia frereana, also known as frankincense, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and pain, making it a natural anti-inflammatory agent.

Its medicinal properties include boswellic acids, which inhibit the production of inflammatory enzymes. This can alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve digestive issues, and even help manage anxiety and stress.

For example, studies have shown that frankincense can improve the quality of life for people with arthritis, allowing them to engage in physical activities without discomfort.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Boswellia frereana.

1. Relieves arthritis symptoms

Boswellia frereana relieves arthritis symptoms because of its potent anti-inflammatory and anti-oxidative properties.

The resin of Boswellia frereana contains boswellic acids, which inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, thereby reducing inflammation and pain in joints. This natural compound has been shown to improve joint mobility and function, making it a popular natural remedy for osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis sufferers.

Regular use can bring significant relief from joint pain and stiffness.

2. Reduces inflammation instantly

Boswellia frereana reduces inflammation instantly because its unique constituents, such as boswellic acids, target COX-2 enzymes that drive inflammation.

By inhibiting these enzymes, frankincense blocks the production of pro-inflammatory mediators, leading to a rapid decrease in swelling and pain.

This instant reduction in inflammation is attributed to the immediate action of boswellic acids, making frankincense a promising natural remedy for inflammatory conditions.

3. Heals wounds faster

Boswellia frereana heals wounds faster because of its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The resin contains boswellic acids that reduce swelling and promote tissue repair. Additionally, frankincense has been found to stimulate the production of collagen, which accelerates the healing process.

Its ability to combat infection and promote healthy tissue growth makes it an effective natural remedy for wound care.

4. Eases back pain

Boswellia frereana eases back pain because of its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The resin of the Boswellia tree contains compounds called boswellic acids, which have been shown to reduce inflammation and block the production of pain-producing chemicals. As a result, frankincense has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat chronic back pain, improving mobility and reducing discomfort.

Its benefits are supported by modern scientific research.

5. Lowers blood pressure

Boswellia frereana lowers blood pressure because of its ability to inhibit the production of inflammatory eicosanoids, which contribute to vasoconstriction.

This leads to a reduction in peripheral resistance and, subsequently, a decrease in blood pressure.

Studies have also shown that frankincense extract has antioxidant properties, which can help to mitigate oxidative stress, another factor in hypertension.

6. Prevents cancer growth

Boswellia frereana prevents cancer growth because it contains boswellic acids, which inhibit the formation of pro-inflammatory compounds that promote tumor growth.

By suppressing the expression of genes involved in cell proliferation and survival, frankincense has been shown to induce apoptosis and prevent angiogenesis, a process critical for tumor growth.

This makes Boswellia frereana a promising natural agent in cancer prevention and treatment.

7. Kills cancer cells

Boswellia frereana kills cancer cells because of its unique composition of terpenoids and boswellic acids.

These compounds inhibit the enzyme COX-2, which is responsible for tumor growth and metastasis. By blocking COX-2, frankincense creates an environment that is inhospitable to cancer cells, ultimately leading to their death.

This action is also enhanced by frankincense's ability to induce apoptosis, or programmed cell death, in cancer cells.

8. Relaxes mindfully

Boswellia frereana relaxes mindfully because of its unique properties that soothe the nervous system.

Its terpenes and boswellic acids have anti-inflammatory and calming effects, reducing anxiety and stress. As it absorbs into the body, frankincense promotes a sense of balance and tranquility, quieting the mind and calming the senses.

Regular use may also improve mood and promote a deeper state of relaxation and inner peace.

9. Improves cognitive function

Boswellia frereana improves cognitive function because of its anti-inflammatory properties, which reduce neuroinflammation and promote neural health.

Research suggests that boswellic acids, the key active components of frankincense, inhibit inflammatory cytokines and improve memory and learning abilities.

By modulating neuroplasticity and enhancing synaptic function, frankincense may even protect against neurodegenerative diseases, such as Alzheimer's and dementia.

10. Reduces anxiety naturally

Boswellia frereana reduces anxiety naturally because it contains boswellic acids, which inhibit inflammatory enzymes and modulate the body's stress response.

This reduction in inflammation leads to a decrease in the production of stress hormones, such as cortisol, and a subsequent decrease in anxiety symptoms.

The anti-inflammatory and neuroprotective properties of frankincense also promote relaxation and calmness.
