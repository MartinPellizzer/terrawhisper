What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Tamarix androssowii has a shrubby or small tree habit characterized by slender, woody stems and extremely small, scale-like leaves that are arranged in dense, overlapping patterns. The flowers are small and pink to purplish, appearing in crowded spikes along the branchlets. These delicate floral structures are often accompanied by tiny, deciduous scales that help the plant manage salt and moisture.
