Saussurea gossipiphora has a perennial growth habit characterized by a basal rosette of leaves and a single, erect flowering stem topped with a dense, head-like inflorescence. The flowering heads consist of numerous small, tubular florets clustered together to form a compact structure. These inflorescences are often surrounded by specialized bracts that provide protection and visual attraction.
