Saussurea gossipiphora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Asterales and falls under the family Asteraceae. It is specifically identified within the genus Saussurea, with the specific epithet gossipiphora completing its scientific designation.
