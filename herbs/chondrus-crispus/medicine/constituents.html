10 Medicinal Constituents Of Chondrus Crispus (Irish Moss)

Chondrus crispus, also known as Irish moss, has active constituents such as carrageenan, fucoidan, and mucilages.

These constituents provide anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-modulating properties. The medicinal properties of Irish moss include soothing digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and promoting wound healing.

Its constituents can improve lives by alleviating symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, reducing the risk of chronic diseases, and supporting healthy skin and mucous membranes.

1. Fucoidan

Chondrus crispus, also known as Irish Moss, fucoidan is a type of sulfated polysaccharide found in the cell walls of this red algae.
