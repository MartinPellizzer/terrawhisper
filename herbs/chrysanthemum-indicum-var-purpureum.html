Chrysanthemum indicum var. purpureum

Chrysanthemum indicum var. purpureum, commonly known as Purple Chrysanthemum, is a plant species within the Compositae family that is widely cultivated for its medicinal and ornamental value.

The therapeutic actions of Purple Chrysanthemum include immune system modulation, antipyretic effects, and the ability to alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia.

Possible Side Effects of Chrysanthemum indicum var. purpureum

Chrysanthemum indicum var. purpureum can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal problems.

Prolonged use or high doses may lead to liver damage and interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using chrysanthemum indicum var. purpureum for medicinal purposes to avoid these potential side effects.
