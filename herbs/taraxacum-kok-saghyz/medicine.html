What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Taraxacum Kok-Saghyz (Wild Potato)?

Taraxacum kok-saghyz, also known as wild potato, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.

The herb is rich in medicinal constituents like inulin, flavonoids, and triterpenes, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. Preparations of wild potato include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which can be used to treat various ailments. While generally considered safe, excessive consumption can cause gastrointestinal upset, nausea, and diarrhea.

Precautions should be taken when using wild potato, especially in pregnant or breastfeeding women, as it may interact with certain medications.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Taraxacum kok-saghyz.
