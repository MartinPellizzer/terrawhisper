Russian Dandelion (Taraxacum kok-saghyz) Compounds

The primary compounds in Russian Dandelion include coumarin, which has anticoagulant properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties, while essential oils provide aromatic and digestive benefits. Other compounds like terpenoids and saponins support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
