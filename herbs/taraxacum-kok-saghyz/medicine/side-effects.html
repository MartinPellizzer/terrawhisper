10 Possible Side Effects Of Taraxacum Kok-Saghyz (Wild Potato)

Taraxacum kok-saghyz has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

These side effects are often caused by consuming excessive amounts of the herb or having underlying sensitivities.

For instance, severe gastrointestinal issues can lead to malnutrition and dehydration, while allergic reactions can trigger anaphylaxis, a potentially life-threatening condition.
