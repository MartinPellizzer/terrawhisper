Bonduc Tree (Caesalpinia bonduc)

Bonduc Tree (Caesalpinia bonduc) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. Traditionally, its seeds, fruits, and bark have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its astringent, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, african traditional medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
