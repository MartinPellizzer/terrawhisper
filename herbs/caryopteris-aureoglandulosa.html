Caryopteris aureoglandulosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Caryopteris aureoglandulosa?

Caryopteris aureoglanduosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. It is specifically categorized under the genus Caryopteris, with its unique identification finalized at the species level as aureoglanduosa.
