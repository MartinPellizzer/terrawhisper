Hieracium Orthocladum

Hieracium orthocladum, commonly known as upright hawkweed, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Traditionally, it has been used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including flavonoids and sesquiterpene lactones, which may contribute to its therapeutic effects. While it has been used historically to treat skin conditions and respiratory ailments, modern scientific research on its medicinal potential remains limited.

Due to its potential toxicity and lack of extensive clinical studies, it is generally recommended to consult a healthcare professional before using Hieracium orthocladum for medicinal purposes.
