What's the taxonomical classification of Viburnum setigerum?

Viburnum setigerum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Dipsacales and falls within the family Viburnaceaae. Finally, its taxonomic placement is completed by its designation within the genus Viburnum, specifically as the species setigerum.
