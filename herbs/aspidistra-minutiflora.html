What's the taxonomical classification of Aspidistra minutiflora?

Aspidistra minutiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Asparagaceae, specifically falling into the genus Aspidistra, with its unique identification as the species minutiflora.
