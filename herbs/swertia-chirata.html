The herb contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and iridoids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Swertia chirata can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is also used in herbal formulations to address digestive and liver-related disorders.
