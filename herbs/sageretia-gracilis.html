Sageretia gracilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Sageretia gracilis?

Sageretia gracilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further organized into the order Rosales. It is a member of the family Rhamnaceae, falling under the genus Sageretia, and is specifically identified by the species name gracilis.
