Cymbopogon Grass (Cymbopogon schoenanthus) Compounds

The primary compounds in Cymbopogon schoenanthus include essential oils, which are responsible for its aromatic properties. Flavonoids and phenolic acids contribute to its antioxidant effects. Terpenoids and coumarin provide anti-inflammatory benefits. Tannins, glycosides, and saponins support digestive health, while alkaloids and anthocyanins offer additional therapeutic potential.

The essential oil of Cymbopogon schoenanthus contains bioactive compounds like citral and geraniol.
