10 Medicinal Constituents Of Cymbopogon Schoenanthus (Lemon Grass)

Cymbopogon schoenanthus has active constituents such as citral, geraniol, and limonene, which exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antifungal properties.

These constituents contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including reducing fever, soothing digestive issues, and repelling insects. The antifungal properties, in particular, can prevent the growth of fungal infections, improving the quality of life for individuals with compromised immune systems or skin conditions.

Regular consumption of lemon grass tea or essential oil can also boost immunity.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Cymbopogon schoenanthus.
