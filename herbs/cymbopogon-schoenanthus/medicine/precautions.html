10 Precautions To Take When Using Cymbopogon Schoenanthus (Lemon Grass)

Cymbopogon schoenanthus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding its use in large quantities or for extended periods.

This is because high doses can cause nausea, vomiting, and headaches, and prolonged use may lead to liver damage.

If not taken in moderation, lemon grass can also interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, potentially leading to adverse effects.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Cymbopogon schoenanthus medicinally.
