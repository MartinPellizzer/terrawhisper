What is Ocotea citrosmoides?

Ocotea citrosmoides is a plant species within the family Lauraceae, native to South America.

The flowers are small, greenish-yellow in color, and arranged in axillary panicles, while the fruit is a berry, typically dark blue or purple when ripe, containing a single seed.

Ocotea citrosmoides is also known for its aromatic bark and essential oils, which are rich in citronellal and other organic compounds.
