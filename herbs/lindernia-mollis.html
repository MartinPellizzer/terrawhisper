Lindernia mollis

What's the taxonomical classification of Lindernia mollis?

Lindernia mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical classification, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It is further categorized under the order Lamiales and belongs to the family Linderniaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Lindernia, specifically identifying as the species mollis.
