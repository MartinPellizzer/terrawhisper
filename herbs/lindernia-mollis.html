Lindernia mollis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the order Lamiales and belongs to the family Linderniaceae. Finally, the plant is situated within the genus Lindernia, specifically identifying as the species mollis.
