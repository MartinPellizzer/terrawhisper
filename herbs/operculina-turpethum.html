What are the medicinal properties of Operculina turpethum?

Improper use of Operculina turpethum can lead to side effects such as gastrointestinal irritation, nausea, and vomiting. The plant's glycosides can also cause allergic reactions in some individuals, including skin rashes and itching.

To use Operculina turpethum medicinally safely, it is essential to follow proper guidelines.
