Sweet Wormwood (Artemisia annua)

Sweet Wormwood (Artemisia annua) is a member of the Asteraceae family, native to Asia, China, and Eastern Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and resin have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
