What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcopyramis bodinieri?

Sarcopyramis bodinieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Myrtales. More specifically, this plant is a part of the family Melastomataceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Sarcopyramis, with its specific identification being the species bodinieri.
