What to know about Dioscorea villosa before using it medicinally

Dioscorea villosa, commonly known as wild yam, is a medicinal herb traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of menopausal disorders, arthritis, and other inflammatory conditions.

Main health benefits of this herb include its ability to reduce pain and inflammation, improve mood, and support hormonal balance. Dioscorea villosa is valued for its therapeutic properties, which include anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antispasmodic effects, making it a popular remedy for conditions such as fibromyalgia and menstrual cramps. The herb contains bioactive compounds like diosgenin, a steroidal saponin with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, as well as other glycosides and alkaloids that contribute to its medicinal properties.

Wild yam is often used to make a herbal preparation, known as wild yam root extract or tincture, which can be taken orally to support menopausal health and alleviate symptoms of hormonal imbalance.
