Wild Yam (Dioscorea villosa)

Wild Yam (Dioscorea villosa) is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family, native to Eastern North America, Eastern Canada, and Northern United States. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and tubers have been used for decoctions, poultices, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, diuretic, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
