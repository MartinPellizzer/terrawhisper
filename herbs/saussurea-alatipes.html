Saussurea alatipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is placed in the order Asterales and falls within the family Asteraceae. Finally, it is identified under the genus Saussurea, with its specific designation being the species alatipes.
