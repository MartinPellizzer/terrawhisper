1. Cures fever

Fever.

A fever is an elevated body temperature, often a sign of infection or inflammation.

It occurs when the body's immune system responds to pathogens such as bacteria or viruses.

This condition is commonly experienced by individuals suffering from colds, flu, or other infectious diseases.

The melilotus officinalis plant is believed to help reduce fever by promoting sweating and supporting the body's natural cooling processes.

How this herb helps with cures fever?

Melilotus officinalis cures fever because it contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids and alkaloids.

These compounds possess antipyretic properties that help reduce body temperature. Flavonoids work by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, which are involved in fever regulation. Alkaloids in the plant also contribute to its fever-reducing effects by modulating the central nervous system.

Together, these constituents make Melilotus officinalis an effective natural remedy for fever.
