10 Possible Side Effects Of Melilotus Officinalis (Sweet Clover)

Melilotus officinalis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and skin irritation.

Causes of these side effects may include individual sensitivities, excessive dosage, or interaction with medications.

For instance, severe allergic reactions can lead to anaphylaxis, causing breathing difficulties and potentially life-threatening complications, while digestive issues can result in nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, impacting daily activities and productivity.
