Astragalus polygala

Astragalus polygala, also known as milk vetch or huang qi, is a flowering plant that belongs to the legume family.

Traditionally used in Chinese medicine, it is believed to have adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to boost the immune system and reduce oxidative stress.

The health benefits of astragalus polygala include reducing fatigue and stress, improving respiratory function, and enhancing overall well-being.

Astragalus polygala can be prepared as a tea, capsule, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
