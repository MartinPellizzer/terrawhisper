Distylium myricoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Saxifragales and the family Hamamelidaceae, eventually narrowing down to its specific genus, Distylium, and its unique species designation, myricoides.
