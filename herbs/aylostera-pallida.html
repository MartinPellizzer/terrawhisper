What is Aylostera pallida?

Aylostera pallida is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is characterized by its cylindrical to subglobose stems, typically 5-8 centimeters in height and 3-5 centimeters in diameter. The stems are covered with short, fine, and yellowish spines, and are usually solitary, but occasionally produce offsets. The flowers are funnel-shaped, white to pale yellow, and about 2 centimeters in diameter, with a tube that is as long as the flower's diameter.

The fruits are small, red to purple, and globular, containing numerous seeds.
