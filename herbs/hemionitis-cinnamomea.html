The fronds are dark brown to blackish-brown when young, becoming greyish-green to dark green when mature, and the rachis and costa are covered with scales that are dark brown to blackish-brown. The sporangia are located on the underside of the fronds, in two rows along the veins, and are 0.5-0.6 mm long.

Hemionitis cinnamomea is native to tropical Asia.
