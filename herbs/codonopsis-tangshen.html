Codonopsis tangshen

What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis tangshen?

Codonopsis tangshen belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls into the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asterales, it is situated within the family Campanulaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Codonopsis, and its species designation, tangshen.
