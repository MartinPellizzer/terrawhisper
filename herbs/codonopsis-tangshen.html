Codonopsis Tangshen Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Codonopsis tangshen, commonly known as Chinese codonopsis or "dang shen," is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb derived from the tuberous roots of the Codonopsis pilosula plant.

It is valued for its ability to tonify the spleen and lungs, enhance energy, and support immune function.

The herb contains bioactive constituents such as polysaccharides, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects, including anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory properties.

Codonopsis tangshen can be prepared as an infusion, decoction, or incorporated into herbal formulas to support digestive and respiratory health.
