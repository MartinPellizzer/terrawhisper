Desmodium zonatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Desmodium zonatum?

Desmodium zonatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical order of its classification, it falls under the order Fabales and the family Fabaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Desmodium, with its specific designation being the species zonatum.
