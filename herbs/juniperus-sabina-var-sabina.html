Juniperus sabina var. sabina

What's the taxonomical classification of Juniperus sabina var. sabina?

Juniperus sabi sabina var. sabina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Pinales and is a representative of the family Cupressaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Juniperus, specifically identified by its botanical designation as the species Juniperus sabina var. sabina.
