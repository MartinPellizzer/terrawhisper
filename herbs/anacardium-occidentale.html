Anacardium Occidentale: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Anacardium occidentale, commonly known as the Brazilian nut or *Anacardium occidentale*, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America, whose seeds are used in herbal medicine.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including supporting cardiovascular health, reducing inflammation, and enhancing immune function.

Its therapeutic actions include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in treating various ailments.

The bioactive constituents of Anacardium occidentale include cardol, anacardic acid, and other phenolic compounds that contribute to its medicinal value.
