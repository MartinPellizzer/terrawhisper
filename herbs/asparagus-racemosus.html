Asparagus Racemosus

Asparagus racemosus, commonly known as shatavari, is a plant-based herb used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

It is a perennial climber with a rich history of being used to promote overall well-being and alleviate various health issues.

The health benefits of Asparagus racemosus include its ability to enhance lactation in nursing mothers, support reproductive health, and exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

In some cases, Asparagus racemosus may interact with certain medications, causing adverse effects like decreased blood pressure or increased bleeding risk.
