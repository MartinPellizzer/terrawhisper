What's the taxonomical classification of Atriplex fera?

Atriplex fera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the evolutionary lineage of the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further organized into the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Amaranthaceae. Specifically, it is identified by its genus, Atriplex, and is designated as the species fera.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Atriplex fera has prostrate to ascending stems that typically reach heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and feature small, triangular, succulent leaves measuring 5 to 15 millimeters in length. These leaves are often fleshy and can range in color from light green to a reddish hue depending on environmental stress. The inflorescences consist of small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters along the stem or at the nodes.
