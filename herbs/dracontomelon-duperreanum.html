Dracontomelon duperreanum

What's the taxonomical classification of Dracontomelon duperreanum?

Dracontomelon duperreanum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Sapindales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Anacardiaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Dracontomelon, with the precise species designation being duperreanum.
