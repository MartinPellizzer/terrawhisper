Greek Micromeria (Micromeria graeca) Compounds

The primary compounds of Greek Micromeria include essential oils, flavonoids, and terpenoids. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids and coumarin also play roles in its therapeutic potential. Tannins, glycosides, and saponins further support its use in traditional herbal medicine.

The essential oil of Greek Micromeria (Micromeria graeca) is a key active compound, containing volatile aromatic compounds like terpenes and phenols.
