10 Medicinal Constituents Of Micromeria Graeca (Mountain Mint)

Micromeria graeca has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The iridoid glycosides, in particular, have been shown to exhibit analgesic and anti-spasmodic effects, providing relief from pain and muscle cramps.

This can greatly improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic pain and muscle spasms, allowing them to engage in daily activities with more ease and comfort.
