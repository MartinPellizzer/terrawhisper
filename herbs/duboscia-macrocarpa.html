Duboscia macrocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Duboscia macrocarpa?

Duboscia macrocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Malvales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Malvaceae, where it is situated within the genus Duboscia, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species macrocarpa.

What are the therapeutic actions of Duboscia macrocarpa?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various ailments including skin infections, gastrointestinal issues, and inflammatory conditions. The medicinal properties of its components are often applied topically to soothe irritations and promote healing of the skin. When used internally, certain extracts are believed to help alleviate stomach discomfort and digestive irregularities. These traditional uses rely on the chemical compounds found within the plant's bark and leaves.
