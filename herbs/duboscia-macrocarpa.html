Duboscia macrocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Malvales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Malvaceae, where it is situated within the genus Duboscia, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species macrocarpa.
