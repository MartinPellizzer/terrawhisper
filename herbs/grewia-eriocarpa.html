What's the taxonomical classification of Grewia eriocarpa?

Grewia eriocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Malvales. This plant is further categorized under the family Malvaceae, which places it among a diverse group of flowering plants. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Grewia, with its specific taxonomic designation being the species eriocarpa.
