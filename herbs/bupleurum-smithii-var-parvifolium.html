What's the taxonomical classification of Bupleurum smithii var. parvifolium?

Bupleurum smithii var. parvifoliuum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Apiales and is situated in the family Apiaceae. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the genus Bupleurum, ultimately reaching the specific classification of the species Bupleurum.
