What's the taxonomical classification of Cryptocarya yunnanensis?

Cryptocarya yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant follows the taxonomic order Laurales and is a member of the family Lauraceae. Finally, it is defined by its specific genus, Cryptocarya, and its unique species designation, yunnanensis.
