Clerodendrum peii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further organized into the order Lamiales and falls under the family Lamiaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Clerodendrum, and its unique species designation, peii.
