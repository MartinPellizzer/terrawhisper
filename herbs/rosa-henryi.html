Rosa henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a part of the family Rosaceae, which is characterized by its diverse and economically important flowering plants. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Rosa, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species Rosa henryi.
