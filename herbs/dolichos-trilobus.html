What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Dolichos trilobus has trifoliate leaves consisting of three distinct, ovate leaflets with serrated margins and a climbing stem structure. The plant's growth habit is characterized by elongated, flexible vines that allow it to scramble over surrounding vegetation.
