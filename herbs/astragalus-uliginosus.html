What's the taxonomical classification of Astragalus uliginosus?

Astragalus uliginosus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Fabales. Furthermore, the plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, falling under the genus Astragalus, and is specifically identified by the species name uliginosus.
