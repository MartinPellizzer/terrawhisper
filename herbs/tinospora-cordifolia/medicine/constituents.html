10 Medicinal Constituents Of Tinospora Cordifolia (Giloy)

Tinospora cordifolia has active constituents such as alkaloids, terpenoids, and glycosides that possess immunomodulatory, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.

These constituents help to modulate the immune system, reduce oxidative stress, and alleviate inflammation, which can improve the quality of life for individuals with chronic diseases such as diabetes, arthritis, and HIV/AIDS.

By enhancing immune function and reducing inflammation, giloy can also help to prevent infections and promote overall health and well-being.
