Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia avellanedae) Compounds

The Trumpet Tree contains several primary compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent effects. Terpenoids and coumarin are known for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities. Saponins, glycosides, and essential oils also play roles in its medicinal properties, while alkaloids and anthocyanins add to its complex therapeutic profile.

Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia avellanedae) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids in the Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia avellanedae) contribute to its medicinal properties.
