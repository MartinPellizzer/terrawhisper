What's the taxonomical classification of Hypericum seniawinii?

Hypericum seniawiini belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Malpighiales. The plant is a member of the family Hypericaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Hypericum, and is identified at the species level as seniawiini.
