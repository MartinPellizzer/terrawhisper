What's the taxonomical classification of Didymocarpus hancei?

Didymocarpus hancei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Lamiales, it falls under the family Gesneriaceae. This specific plant is identified by the genus Didymocarpus and is distinguished as the species hancei.
