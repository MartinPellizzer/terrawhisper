Dalbergia benthamii

What's the taxonomical classification of Dalbergia benthamii?

Dalbergia benthamii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the biological order Fabales. The plant is further categorized into the family Fabaceae, which is known for its diverse range of legumes. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Dalbergia, and its specific biological identity is defined by the species name benthamii.
