10 Precautions To Take When Using Bixa Orellana (Annatto)

Bixa orellana has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as avoiding excessive consumption due to potential liver and kidney strain.

It's crucial to monitor the dosage, as overuse can cause gastrointestinal issues, headaches, and allergic reactions. For instance, neglecting to adhere to recommended dosages may lead to jaundice, a condition characterized by yellowing of the skin and eyes.

Taking the necessary precautions can help minimize side effects and maximize the herb's benefits.
