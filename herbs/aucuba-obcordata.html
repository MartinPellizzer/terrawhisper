What's the taxonomical classification of Aucuba obcordata?

Aucuba obcordata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Garryales and the family Garryaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Aucuba, with its specific identification being the species obcordata.
