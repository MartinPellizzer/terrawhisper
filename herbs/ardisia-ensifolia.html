Ardisia ensifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia ensifolia?

Ardisia ensifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is further categorized into the family Primulaceae, within which it is placed in the genus Ardisia, specifically identified by its species name, ensifolia.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ardisia ensifolia has lanceolate, leathery leaves that are arranged oppositely on woody stems and possess prominent parallel venation. The foliage is characterized by a deep green color and a smooth, entire margin. This plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that typically emerge from the leaf axils. The fruit develops into a fleshy drupe that often turns a distinct color upon maturity. These morphological features are consistent with other members of the Rubiaceae family.
