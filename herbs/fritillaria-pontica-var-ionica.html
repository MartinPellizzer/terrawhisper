Ionian Fritillary (Fritillaria Pontica Var. Ionica)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Fritillaria pontica var. ionica.

*Fritillaria pontica* var. *ionica*, a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, has been historically used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties. The bulb of this variety contains bioactive alkaloids, which have been studied for their possible anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. In folk remedies, it has been employed to treat respiratory conditions such as coughs and bronchitis, as well as for its expectorant properties. However, due to its toxicity and limited availability, its medicinal use remains largely restricted and requires further scientific validation. Despite its medicinal interest, conservation efforts are essential to protect this endangered species from overharvesting and habitat loss.
