The plant extract and its isolated active constituent, Sub-Fr0.2, were shown to have a significant promoting effect on wound healing in a circular excision wound healing model. Additionally, the expression of TGF-β1 and α-SMA was inhibited on wounds treated with an ointment containing Sub-Fr0.2 to reduce scar formation.
