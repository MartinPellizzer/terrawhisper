Sarcococca vagans

What's the taxonomical classification of Sarcococca vagans?

Sarcococca vagans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Buxales and falls under the family Buxaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Sarcococca, specifically as the species vagans.
