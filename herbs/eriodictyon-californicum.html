California Yerba Santa (Eriodictyon californicum)

California Yerba Santa (Eriodictyon californicum) is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, native to California, Usa, Baja California, Mexico, and Southwestern United States. Traditionally, its roots, bark, and leaves have been used for decoctions, poultices, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
