Mussaenda laxiflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Mussaenda laxiflora?

Mussaenda laxiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Gentianaales. It is a member of the family Rubiaceae, falling within the genus Mussaenda, and is specifically identified by the species name laxiflora.
