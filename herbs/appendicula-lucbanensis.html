What is Appendicula lucbanensis?

Appendicula lucbanensis is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, stout stem and several leaves. The leaves are ovate and elliptical in shape, measuring up to 7.5 cm in length and 3.5 cm in width. The inflorescence is a raceme bearing several flowers, each with a white labellum and a greenish-yellow column.

The plant is native to the Philippines and was first described in 1987.
