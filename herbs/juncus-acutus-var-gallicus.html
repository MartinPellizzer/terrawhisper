Juncus acutus var. gallicus

Juncus acutus var. gallicus, also known as rush or Spanish rush, is a type of aquatic plant belonging to the Juncaceae family, characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, flat leaves.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which have been used to treat various health conditions such as wounds, skin irritations, and respiratory issues.

The health benefits of Juncus acutus var. gallicus include its ability to soothe and calm the skin, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions such as bronchitis and asthma.

In herbal preparations, the plant is often used in the form of extracts, tinctures, and infusions, which can be applied topically or ingested to reap its medicinal benefits.
