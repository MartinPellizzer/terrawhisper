Astragalus molybdenus

Astragalus molybdenus, also known as Huang qi in traditional Chinese medicine, is a perennial plant native to China and Mongolia.

It is a member of the legume family and is prized for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory effects.

The plant is believed to have several health benefits, including boosting the immune system, reducing fatigue, and improving cardiovascular health.

Astragalus molybdenus is often used in herbal preparations, such as teas, capsules, and tinctures, and is commonly consumed in Asia to promote overall well-being and longevity.
