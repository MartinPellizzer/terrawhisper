Mentha vallesiaca

Mentha vallesiaca, commonly known as Valsalian mint, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

This plant is known for its calming and digestive properties, and it has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of indigestion, nausea, and irritable bowel syndrome.

The health benefits of Mentha vallesiaca include reducing inflammation, relieving stress and anxiety, and promoting relaxation.

The therapeutic actions of this plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include menthone, menthyl acetate, and limonene, that exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and sedative properties.
