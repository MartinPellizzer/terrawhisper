10 Precautions To Take When Using Vaccinium Vitis-Idaea

Vaccinium vitis-idaea has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels, as it may lower blood sugar levels and interact with diabetes medications.

If not monitored, it can cause hypoglycemia, leading to symptoms like dizziness, confusion, and fatigue.

Additionally, pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their healthcare provider before using cranberry, as it may stimulate the uterus and affect milk production.

When using Vaccinium vitis-idaea medicinally, it's important to keep away from children and pets.
