10 Medicinal Constituents Of Dorema Ammoniacum (Iranian Galbanum)

Dorema ammoniacum, also known as Iranian galbanum, has active constituents such as sesquiterpene hydrocarbons, particularly bornyl acetate, which have analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties.

These constituents contribute to the plant's ability to relieve pain, reduce swelling, and ease muscle cramps.

By incorporating Iranian galbanum into herbal remedies, people can experience improved management of chronic pain and inflammation, enabling them to engage in daily activities without discomfort.
