10 Precautions To Take When Using Frangula Alnus (Alder Buckthorn)

Frangula alnus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in moderation and under professional guidance.

It's a powerful laxative and can cause dehydration, electrolyte imbalances, and potassium loss if used excessively. Failure to take this precaution may result in side effects like headaches, dizziness, and muscle weakness.

Proper use is also crucial to avoid interactions with other medications and to ensure effective treatment.

When using Frangula alnus medicinally, it's important to take consult a doctor first.

This precaution is crucial because Frangula alnus can interact with certain medications, such as diuretics and blood thinners, which may lead to adverse effects or decreased effectiveness. Additionally, the plant contains anthraquinone glycosides, which can cause gastrointestinal issues like diarrhea and stomach cramps in large doses.

By consulting a doctor first, you can ensure safe and effective use of this herb, especially if you have pre-existing medical conditions or take regular medication.
