Goodyera biflora

What's the taxonomical classification of Goodyera biflora?

Goodyera biflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, which places it among the orchids, and is specifically identified by the genus Goodyera and the species biflora.
