What are the active constituents of Nicotiana tabacum?

The health benefits of Nicotiana tabacum comes from its active constituents, such as nicotine, anabasine, and nornicotine.

These alkaloids have been found to possess various medicinal properties, including analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.

Nicotine has also been shown to exhibit bronchodilatory and vasodilatory activities, while anabasine has been found to have antioxidant and antispasmodic properties.

What are the medicinal preparations of Nicotiana tabacum?

Nicotiana tabacum has different medicinal preparations, such as leaves, stems, flowers, and roots.

The leaves are used to make cigarettes, pipes, and chewing tobacco, which are used to treat respiratory problems like bronchitis and asthma. The stems are used in traditional medicine to treat fever and rheumatism. The flowers are used in herbal teas to soothe sore throats and coughs.

The roots are used to treat skin conditions like eczema and acne.

What are the possible side effect of using Nicotiana tabacum improperly?
