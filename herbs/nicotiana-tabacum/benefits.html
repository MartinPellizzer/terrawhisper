1. Prevents cancer growth

Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells.

This condition, often referred to as malignant neoplasia, arises from genetic mutations that disrupt normal cell regulation.

These mutations can be caused by factors such as exposure to carcinogens, genetic predisposition, or lifestyle choices like smoking.

It commonly affects individuals across all age groups, though the risk increases with age.

Some studies suggest that compounds in the nicotiana tabacum plant may inhibit cancer cell proliferation, though further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.

How this herb helps with prevents cancer growth?

Nicotiana tabacum prevents cancer growth because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids, which exhibit anti-carcinogenic properties.

These compounds can inhibit the proliferation of cancer cells by inducing apoptosis and reducing oxidative stress. Alkaloids like nicotinamide have been shown to interfere with DNA synthesis in malignant cells. Flavonoids in tobacco may also suppress tumor progression through their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.

However, it is important to note that these benefits are often studied in isolated compounds rather than in whole plant extracts.
