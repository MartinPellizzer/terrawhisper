What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes kerrii?

Trichosanthes kerrii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Cucurbitales and is a member of the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trichosanthes, and its species name, kerrii.
