Teucrium omeiense

What's the taxonomical classification of Teucrium omeiense?

Teucrium omeiense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Lamiales, it falls into the family Lamiaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its placement within the genus Teucrium, specifically as the species omeiense.
