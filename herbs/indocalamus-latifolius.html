What's the taxonomical classification of Indocalamus latifolius?

Indocalamus latifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy through the order Poales, it is placed within the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its membership in the genus Indocalamus and its designation as the species latifolius.
