Zea mays subsp.

This plant has been traditionally used for its potential health benefits, including antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which may help to protect against cell damage and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

tunicata include anthocyanins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.
