Cabbage Palm (Sabal serrulata)

Cabbage Palm (Sabal serrulata) is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to Southeastern United States, Florida, and Caribbean. Traditionally, its leaves, roots, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, astringent, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
