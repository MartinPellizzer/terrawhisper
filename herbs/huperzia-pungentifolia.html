Huperzia pungentifolia

Huperzia pungentifolia, commonly known as the "Forked Lycopodium" or "Shan Lycopodium", is a species of club moss that belongs to the family Huperziaceae.

It is native to Asia and has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The therapeutic actions of Huperzia pungentifolia include reducing inflammation, improving wound healing, and exhibiting neuroprotective effects.
