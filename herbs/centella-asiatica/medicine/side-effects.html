10 Possible Side Effects Of Centella Asiatica (Asiatic Pennywort)

These side effects can occur due to an overdose of the herb or an allergic reaction to its chemical composition.

In severe cases, skin irritation can lead to blistering, scarring, and chronic pain, significantly impacting daily life and relationships.
