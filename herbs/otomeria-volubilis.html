Otomeria volubilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Otomeria volubilis?

Otomeria volubilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Gentianales. It is a member of the family Rubiaceae, falling specifically within the genus Otomeria, with its unique identity defined by the species name volubilis.
