Iberis Amara (Iberis amara)

Iberis Amara (Iberis amara) is a member of the Brassicaceae family, native to Southern Europe, North Africa, and Western Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, flowers, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, astringent, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and ayurvedic medicine.
