Gastrodia (Gastrodia elata) Compounds

The primary compounds of Gastrodia elata include alkaloids, which have neuroprotective properties, and glycosides, known for their cardiovascular benefits. Flavonoids and phenolic acids contribute to its antioxidant effects, while saponins and tannins support anti-inflammatory actions. Coumarin and essential oils add to its aromatic and therapeutic profiles.
