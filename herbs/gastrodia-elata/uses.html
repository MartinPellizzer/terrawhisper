Gastrodia (Gastrodia elata) Uses

Gastrodia elata is used to treat conditions like migraine, vertigo, and tinnitus by calming the nervous system. It helps with neurosis, anxiety, and insomnia due to its sedative properties. The herb also alleviates tremors and seizures, and is effective for dizziness and headache.

Gastrodia (Gastrodia elata) Monograph

1. Migraine

Gastrodia elata is traditionally used to treat migraines due to its ability to regulate liver and spleen functions and reduce internal heat. It is often combined with other herbs to alleviate headache symptoms, particularly those associated with liver fire. Its calming effects help relieve tension and improve blood circulation, making it a key herb in migraine management in traditional herbal medicine.

2. Vertigo

Gastrodia elata is used in traditional medicine to alleviate vertigo by improving circulation and reducing inner ear disturbances. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effects on balance and nervous system function. Its calming properties help relieve dizziness and motion sickness.

3. Neurosis

Gastrodia elata is used in traditional medicine to treat neurosis due to its calming properties. It helps alleviate anxiety, restlessness, and insomnia by regulating the nervous system. Often combined with other herbs, it supports emotional balance and reduces stress-related symptoms.

4. Tremors

Gastrodia elata is used to treat tremors by calming the nervous system and reducing excessive movement. It is particularly effective for hand and limb shaking, often seen in conditions like essential tremor. Its action is attributed to its ability to regulate liver and kidney functions, which are linked to muscle control in traditional Chinese medicine.

5. Headache

Gastrodia elata is used to treat headaches, particularly those caused by liver yang rising or internal heat. It helps calm the liver and reduce inflammation, making it effective for tension headaches and migraines. It is often combined with other herbs in traditional formulas to enhance its therapeutic effects.

6. Insomnia

Gastrodia elata is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat insomnia due to its calming properties. It helps reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, aiding in better sleep quality. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its sedative effects and support restful sleep.

7. Anxiety

Gastrodia elata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate anxiety and nervous tension. It is believed to calm the mind and stabilize emotions by regulating the nervous system. Often combined with other herbs, it supports emotional balance and reduces restlessness. Its effects are attributed to compounds that influence neurotransmitter activity.

8. Seizures

Gastrodia elata is used in traditional medicine to treat seizures due to its calming effects on the nervous system. It is believed to reduce excessive neuronal activity, thereby preventing seizure episodes. This herb is often combined with other herbs to enhance its anticonvulsant properties. Its use in seizure management highlights its role in addressing neurological imbalances.

9. Tinnitus

Gastrodia elata is traditionally used to treat tinnitus by improving blood circulation and reducing ear pressure. It is believed to calm the liver and clear heat, which can alleviate ringing or buzzing sounds in the ears. This herb is often combined with other tonics to enhance its effectiveness in traditional Chinese medicine.

10. Dizziness

Gastrodia elata is used to treat dizziness by calming the liver and suppressing wind, which helps alleviate symptoms caused by liver yang rising or internal wind. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in reducing vertigo and stabilizing the mind.
