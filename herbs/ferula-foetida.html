Ferula Weed (Ferula foetida)

Ferula Weed (Ferula foetida) is a member of the Apiaceae family, native to Middle East, Central Asia, and Southern Russia. Traditionally, its resin, roots, and stems have been used for oil infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, carminative, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
