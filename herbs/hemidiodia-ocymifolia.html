Hemidiodia ocymifolia

Hemidiodia ocymifolia, also known as the 'wild sage', is a shrub-like plant native to tropical Africa.

This plant is used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Hemidiodia ocymifolia include treating fever, cough, and respiratory issues, as well as wounds and skin infections.

Herbal preparations of the plant are made by infusing its leaves in water or using them in a tea to harness its medicinal properties, although more research is needed to confirm its full range of benefits and uses.
