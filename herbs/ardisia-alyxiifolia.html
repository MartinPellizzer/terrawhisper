Ardisia alyxiifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia alyxiifolia?

Ardisia alyxiifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is organized under the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling within the genus Ardisia, and is specifically identified by its species name, alyxiifolia.
