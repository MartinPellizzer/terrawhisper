Agrimonia pilosa f. dahurica

Agrimonia pilosa f. dahurica, also known as the Dahurian agrimony or Agrimony, is a plant species in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to East Asia and has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral activities.

The health benefits of Agrimonia pilosa f. dahurica include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and boost the immune system, making it a popular herbal remedy for various health issues.

In herbal preparations, Agrimonia pilosa f. dahurica is often used in teas, infusions, and tinctures, and can also be found in combination with other herbs in traditional Chinese medicine formulas.
