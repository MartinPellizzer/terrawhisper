10 Health Benefits Of Hamamelis Hybr (Witch Hazel)

Hamamelis hybr, also known as witch hazel, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and soothing skin irritations.

Its medicinal properties include astringent and anti-inflammatory compounds like tannins and phenolic acids. These properties can improve lives by providing relief from eczema, acne, and rosacea.

For example, applying witch hazel to minor cuts and scrapes can promote healing and reduce redness, while its antiseptic properties can help prevent infection.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Hamamelis hybr.

1. Relieves itchy skin

Hamamelis hybr relieves itchy skin because of its remarkable anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties.

The plant's extracts contain tannins, which have a soothing effect on the skin, reducing redness and swelling. Additionally, witch hazel's flavonoids and phenolic acids help to neutralize histamine, a chemical that causes itching.

When applied topically, witch hazel's combination of anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties provides quick relief from itchy skin, making it a popular natural remedy for eczema, hives, and other skin irritations.

2. Soothes sunburned skin

Hamamelis hybr soothes sunburned skin because of its unique combination of anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The astringent and anti-irritant compounds present in witch hazel help to reduce redness and itching, while its antioxidant properties work to prevent further damage from free radicals.

This natural remedy effectively calms and cools the skin, providing relief from the discomfort and pain associated with sunburn.

3. Reduces acne severity

Hamamelis hybr reduces acne severity because of its potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Its extracts contain gallic acid, a compound that has been shown to inhibit the growth of Propionibacterium acnes (P. acnes), the bacteria responsible for acne. Additionally, witch hazel's anti-inflammatory effects help to reduce redness and swelling, allowing the skin to heal and preventing further breakouts.

This natural remedy has been used for centuries to treat skin issues, and its efficacy in reducing acne severity has been confirmed through scientific studies.

4. Treats hemorrhoids effectively

Hamamelis hybr treats hemorrhoids effectively because of its unique combination of anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

The tannins and phenolic acids in witch hazel help to constrict blood vessels, reducing swelling and discomfort associated with hemorrhoids.

Additionally, the astringent properties of witch hazel help to shrink and soothe irritated tissue, promoting healing and reducing the risk of further irritation.

5. Aids in wound healing

Hamamelis hybr aids in wound healing because of its unique combination of antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and astringent properties.

The extract of witch hazel has been shown to reduce the formation of biofilms, which can impede wound healing, while its anti-inflammatory effects can help minimize tissue damage and promote the healing process.

Additionally, witch hazel's astringent properties can help to contract the skin and reduce the size of the wound, further promoting the healing process.

6. Fights skin fungal infections

Hamamelis hybr fights skin fungal infections because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant's extracts have been shown to exhibit antifungal properties, which help to combat the growth of fungi that can cause skin infections.

Additionally, witch hazel has anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it an effective natural remedy for treating skin conditions like ringworm, athlete's foot, and nail fungal infections.

7. Relaxes muscle spasms

Hamamelis hybr relaxes muscle spasms because of its unique combination of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins.

These bioactive compounds work together to reduce muscle contraction and inflammation, providing relief from spasms and cramping. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to inhibit the release of muscle contracting neurotransmitters, allowing for a more relaxed muscle state.

This natural muscle relaxant properties make witch hazel a popular ingredient in topical creams and ointments for soothing sore muscles and joints.

8. Lowers blood pressure slightly

Hamamelis hybr lowers blood pressure slightly because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, including gallic acid and hamamelic acid.

These compounds have been shown to cause blood vessels to dilate, which in turn increases blood flow and reduces blood pressure.

Additionally, witch hazel's flavonoids may help to relax the smooth muscle cells in blood vessel walls, further contributing to its blood pressure-lowering effects.

9. Reduces inflammation levels

Hamamelis hybr reduces inflammation levels because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds, including gallic acid, tannins, and flavonoids.

These compounds possess potent anti-inflammatory properties, which help to calm irritated tissue and reduce the production of pro-inflammatory chemicals.

By targeting the root cause of inflammation, witch hazel extracts can effectively soothe and calm the skin, providing relief from conditions like eczema, rosacea, and dermatitis.

10. Prevents cold sores outbreaks

Hamamelis hybr prevents cold sores outbreaks because of its potent antiviral and anti-inflammatory properties.

The extracts of witch hazel have been shown to inhibit the replication of the herpes simplex virus, which causes cold sores.

Additionally, the astringent and anti-inflammatory properties of witch hazel can reduce redness and swelling associated with cold sore outbreaks, promoting a faster recovery and reducing the severity of the condition.
