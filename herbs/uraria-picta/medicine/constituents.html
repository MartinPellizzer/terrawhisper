10 Medicinal Constituents Of Uraria Picta (Painted Leaf)

Uraria picta has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making them useful in treating various health conditions. The flavonoids in Uraria picta can improve cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Its antioxidant properties can boost immunity and protect against cell damage, while the saponins can help manage diabetes and high blood pressure.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Uraria picta.
