10 Precautions To Take When Using Uraria Picta (Painted Leaf)

Uraria picta has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it under the guidance of a qualified practitioner.

This is important because Uraria picta can cause harm if not used correctly, particularly with excessive consumption. For instance, consuming large quantities can lead to vomiting, dizziness, and even kidney damage.

Furthermore, it may interact with certain medications, such as diuretics, blood thinners, and high blood pressure medications.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Uraria picta medicinally.
