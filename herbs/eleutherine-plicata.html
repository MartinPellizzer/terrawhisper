Eleutherine plicata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Asparagales and the family Iridaceae, eventually reaching its specific classification within the genus Eleutherine.
