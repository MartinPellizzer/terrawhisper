The therapeutic actions of the plant are primarily attributed to its calming and relaxing effects, achieved through its ability to modulate the central nervous system.

The bioactive constituents of Valeriana rubens include valerenic acid, valeranone, and isovaleric acid, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
