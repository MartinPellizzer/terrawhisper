What's the taxonomical classification of Embelia pauciflora?

Embelia pauciflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Ericales and placed within the family Primulaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Embelia and the species name pauciflora.
