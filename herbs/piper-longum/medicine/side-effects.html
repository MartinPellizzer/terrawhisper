10 Possible Side Effects Of Piper Longum (Long Pepper)

Causes include excessive consumption, individual sensitivities, and interactions with other medications. These side effects can worsen people's lives by causing discomfort, respiratory problems, and even cardiovascular issues.

In severe cases, they can lead to hospitalization and prolonged recovery periods, affecting daily routines and overall well-being.
