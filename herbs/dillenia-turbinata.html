Dillenia turbinata

What's the taxonomical classification of Dillenia turbinata?

Dillenia turbinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Dilleniales and is a member of the family Dilleniaceae. Specifically, it is placed within the genus Dillenia, with its unique biological identity defined by its species name, turbinata.
