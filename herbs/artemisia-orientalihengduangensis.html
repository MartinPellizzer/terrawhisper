What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia orientalihengduangensis?

Artemisia orientalihengduangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Asterales, the plant is a member of the family Asteraceae. It is further identified within the genus Artemisia, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species orientalihengduangensis.
