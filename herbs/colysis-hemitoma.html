What's the taxonomical classification of Colysis hemitoma?

Colysis hemitoma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Taxonomically, this plant is situated within the family Polypodiaceae, falling under the genus Colysis, and is specifically identified by its species name, hemitoma.
