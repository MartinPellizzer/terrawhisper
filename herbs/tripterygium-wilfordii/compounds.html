Fleeceflower (Tripterygium wilfordii) Compounds

The primary compounds of Fleeceflower include alkaloids, which have anti-inflammatory properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Terpenoids and glycosides contribute to its immunomodulatory and antitumor activities. Saponins and tannins provide additional anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial benefits.
