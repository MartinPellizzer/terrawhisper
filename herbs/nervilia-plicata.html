Nervilia plicata

What's the taxonomical classification of Nervilia plicata?

Nervilia plicata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a distinguished representative of the family Orchidaceae, specifically situated within the genus Nervilia, and is identified by the species name plicata.
