Vitex viticifolia

Vitex viticifolia, commonly known as the Bissy tree, is a plant species that belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is native to West Africa.

The health benefits of Vitex viticifolia include its use in traditional medicine to treat various ailments, such as fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal issues.

The therapeutic actions of the plant are attributed to its bioactive constituents, which include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, and have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.

The bioactive constituents of Vitex viticifolia have been found to include flavonoids like vitexin and isovitexin, as well as alkaloids like laudanosine and aporphine, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
