This herb has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, and it has been traditionally used to aid in digestion, reduce fever, and support the immune system.

The therapeutic actions of Prunus armeniaca include anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antiparasitic activities, which may help to alleviate symptoms of various health conditions.

Possible Side Effects of prunus armeniaca

Additionally, consuming large amounts of Prunus armeniaca can cause allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and swelling.

In some cases, excessive consumption of the herb may also lead to liver and kidney damage due to its high content of toxic compounds.

Furthermore, Prunus armeniaca can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, potentially exacerbating their side effects.
