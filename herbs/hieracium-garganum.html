Additionally, improper use of Hieracium garganum has been linked to increased risk of bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties, which can worsen bleeding disorders or interact with medications.

It is crucial to consult with a healthcare expert before using Hieracium garganum to avoid these side effects and ensure safe consumption.
