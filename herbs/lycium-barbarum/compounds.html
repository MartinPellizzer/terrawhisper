Wolfberry (Lycium barbarum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Wolfberry include flavonoids, which are antioxidants that support eye health. Phenolic acids and anthocyanins also contribute to its antioxidant properties. Glycosides and tannins provide anti-inflammatory effects, while saponins and terpenoids may enhance immune function. Coumarins, alkaloids, and essential oils add additional therapeutic benefits.

Flavonoids are a group of bioactive compounds found in Wolfberry (Lycium barbarum). These compounds contribute to the plant's medicinal properties by interacting with biological systems to support eye health, immune function, and antioxidant activity.
