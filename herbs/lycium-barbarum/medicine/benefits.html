10 Health Benefits Of Lycium Barbarum (Goji Berry)

Lycium barbarum, also known as goji berry, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These properties help to reduce oxidative stress and inflammation, which can lead to improved cardiovascular health and a reduced risk of chronic diseases. For example, goji berries have been shown to help manage blood sugar levels in people with diabetes, allowing them to live more comfortably with their condition.

Additionally, their high antioxidant content may also improve eyesight and reduce the risk of age-related macular degeneration.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Lycium barbarum.

1. Boosts immune system

Lycium barbarum boosts immune system because of its rich antioxidant content and high concentration of polysaccharides.

These compounds stimulate the production of white blood cells, such as macrophages and natural killer cells, which play a crucial role in fighting off pathogens and diseases.

Additionally, goji berries have been shown to inhibit the growth of harmful bacteria and viruses, making it an effective natural supplement to support immune function and overall health.

2. Slows aging process

Lycium barbarum slows aging process because of its rich antioxidant and polysaccharide content.

These compounds have been shown to neutralize free radicals that cause oxidative stress and cellular damage, which are major contributors to the aging process. Additionally, goji berries' polysaccharides stimulate the production of collagen, hyaluronic acid, and glycosaminoglycans, which help maintain skin elasticity, hydration, and firmness, promoting a more youthful appearance.

This natural antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effect helps delay signs of aging such as wrinkles, age spots, and joint pain.

3. Improves eye health

Lycium barbarum improves eye health because it is rich in antioxidants and flavonoids that help protect the eyes from oxidative stress and inflammation.

The berries' high levels of zeaxanthin and lutein, two powerful carotenoids, have been shown to reduce the risk of age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and cataracts by filtering blue light and reducing oxidative damage in the retina and lens.

This natural supplementation can help promote healthy vision and reduce the likelihood of eye diseases.

4. Increases alertness

Lycium barbarum increases alertness because of its unique combination of nutrients and bioactive compounds.

The high levels of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals in goji berries help to improve circulation and oxygenation of the brain, leading to enhanced cognitive function and mental clarity.

Additionally, goji berries contain adaptogenic properties that help the body adapt to stress, allowing individuals to stay focused and alert throughout the day.

5. Enhances athletic performance

Lycium barbarum enhances athletic performance because it provides a natural boost of antioxidants and adaptogens.

The berries contain high levels of beta-carotene, lutein, and zeaxanthin, which help to reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, allowing athletes to recover faster from intense training.

Additionally, goji berries have been shown to increase endurance, improve mental clarity, and enhance overall physical performance by stimulating the production of nitric oxide, which improves blood flow and oxygen delivery to muscles.

6. Reduces fatigue

Lycium barbarum reduces fatigue because it is rich in antioxidants and essential amino acids that help to combat oxidative stress and inflammation, which are common causes of fatigue.

The berries' high concentration of vitamins B and C, as well as its polysaccharides and other bioactive compounds, work together to improve energy production, reduce oxidative damage to cells, and enhance overall physical performance.

This leads to a reduction in feelings of fatigue and an improvement in overall quality of life.

7. Regulates blood sugar

Lycium barbarum regulates blood sugar because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds and phytonutrients.

The berries contain a high amount of polysaccharides, anthocyanins, and flavonoids that have been shown to inhibit the activity of key enzymes involved in carbohydrate metabolism.

This helps to slow down the absorption of glucose into the bloodstream, reducing insulin resistance and improving glucose uptake by cells, ultimately leading to better blood sugar regulation and management.

8. Lowers cholesterol

Lycium barbarum lowers cholesterol because of its rich content of dietary fiber, antioxidants, and polyphenols.

The soluble fiber in goji berries helps bind to bile acids, reducing their reabsorption into the bloodstream and increasing excretion, thereby decreasing LDL (bad) cholesterol levels. Additionally, goji berries' antioxidant properties help prevent oxidative stress-induced inflammation, which can contribute to high cholesterol.

This natural combination makes goji berry a promising dietary supplement for heart health.

9. Prevents cellular damage

Lycium barbarum prevents cellular damage because of its rich antioxidant and polysaccharide content.

These compounds scavenge free radicals, neutralizing their destructive effects on DNA, proteins, and lipids. Additionally, the polysaccharides in goji berries stimulate the production of superoxide dismutase, a vital enzyme that protects cells from oxidative stress.

By reducing oxidative damage, Lycium barbarum helps preserve cellular integrity and maintain overall health and well-being.
