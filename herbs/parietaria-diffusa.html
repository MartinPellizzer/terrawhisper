The bioactive constituents in Parietaria diffusa include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

In addition to teas, decoctions of Parietaria diffusa are also used, involving boiling the plant material for a longer period to extract more potent compounds.

These decoctions are often recommended for more severe conditions, such as chronic skin irritations or inflammatory disorders.
