Mareya brevipes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is identified specifically within the genus Mareya, reaching its unique designation as the species brevipes.
