What's the taxonomical classification of Styrax suberifolius?

Styrax suberifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Styracaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Styrax, with its unique identity defined by the species name suberifolius.
