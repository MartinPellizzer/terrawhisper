Cinnamon Fern (Dryopteris crassirhizoma)

Cinnamon Fern (Dryopteris crassirhizoma) is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family, native to China, Japan, and Korea. Traditionally, its rhizomes, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
