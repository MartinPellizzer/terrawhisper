Arenaria quadridentata

What's the taxonomical classification of Arenaria quadridentata?

Arenaria quadridentata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized into the order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic position is defined by its placement within the genus Arenaria, with the unique designation of the species quadridentata.
