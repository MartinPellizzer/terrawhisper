Picea bifolia

Picea bifolia, commonly known as Siberian spruce, is a coniferous tree species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and Eastern Europe.

This plant has been found to possess various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can aid in reducing inflammation and fighting off infections.

The therapeutic actions of Picea bifolia include its ability to stimulate the immune system, improve respiratory health, and exhibit antioxidant properties.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include terpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
