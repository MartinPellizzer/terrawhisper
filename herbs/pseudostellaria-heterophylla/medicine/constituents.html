10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pseudostellaria Heterophylla (Twisted Stalk)

Pseudostellaria heterophylla has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, alkaloids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and immunomodulatory properties, contributing to the plant's medicinal properties.

Examples of improved quality of life include relief from menstrual cramps, improved fertility, and enhanced immune function, making it a valued herb in traditional medicine for reproductive and women's health issues.
