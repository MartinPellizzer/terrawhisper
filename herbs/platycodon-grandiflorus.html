Balloon Flower (Platycodon grandiflorus)

Balloon Flower (Platycodon grandiflorus) is a member of the Campanulaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its roots, flowers, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in korean traditional medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
