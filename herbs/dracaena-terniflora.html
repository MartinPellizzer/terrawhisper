What's the taxonomical classification of Dracaena terniflora?

Dracaena terniflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of botanical classification, it falls under the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Asparagaceae. At the most specific taxonomic levels, the plant is identified by the genus Dracaena and the specific epithet terniflora.
