Cascarilla acutifolia

Cascarilla acutifolia, also known as Sweet Woodruff, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

The medicinal properties of Cascarilla acutifolia lie in its ability to act as an antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant, which is attributed to its high concentration of coumarin and essential oils.

The health benefits of this plant include its potential to alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues, as well as its ability to support heart health and boost the immune system.

Cascarilla acutifolia is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which can be consumed to harness its medicinal properties and promote overall well-being.
