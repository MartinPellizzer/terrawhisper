What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Alstonia Constricta (Devil'S Breath)?

Alstonia constricta has health benefits such as relieving respiratory issues and reducing fever.

Its medicinal constituents include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids that contribute to its therapeutic properties. The herb is prepared in various forms like teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to treat conditions like asthma and bronchitis. However, excessive consumption of devil's breath may lead to side effects like nausea, vomiting, and allergic reactions.

What are the medicinal preparations of Alstonia constricta?

Alstonia constricta has different medicinal preparations, such as powdered bark, decoctions, tinctures, and extracts.

Additionally, devil's breath has been known to interact with certain medications, leading to adverse reactions such as headaches, fatigue, and muscle weakness.

What precautions to take when using Alstonia constricta medicinally?

Before using Alstonia constricta for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a healthcare professional to determine the proper dosage and administration method.
