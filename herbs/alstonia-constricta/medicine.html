What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Alstonia Constricta (Devil'S Breath)?

Alstonia constricta, also known as devil's breath, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and alleviating fever.

The herb contains medicinal constituents like alkaloids, glycosides, and terpenes that contribute to its therapeutic properties. In traditional medicine, devil's breath is prepared into teas, tinctures, and infusions to treat various ailments. However, excessive consumption can cause dizziness, nausea, and stomach upset.

It is recommended to consult a healthcare professional before using devil's breath, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding women and those with underlying medical conditions.

What are the medicinal preparations of Alstonia constricta?

Alstonia constricta has different medicinal preparations, such as powdered bark, decoctions, tinctures, and extracts.

Tinctures are used to treat digestive issues and fever, while extracts are used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments including cancer, diabetes, and hypertension.

These preparations can be administered orally or topically, depending on the condition being treated.

What are the possible side effect of using Alstonia constricta improperly?

Improper use of Alstonia constricta increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as nausea, vomiting, and dizziness.

It can cause rapid heartbeat, palpitations, and even arrhythmias. In some cases, it may lead to increased blood pressure, anxiety, insomnia, and agitation. Long-term use can result in kidney or liver damage, respiratory issues, and cardiovascular problems.
