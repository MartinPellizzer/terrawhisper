10 Health Benefits Of Alstonia Constricta (Devil'S Breath)

Alstonia constricta has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving respiratory issues.

Its medicinal properties include alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenes, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. By alleviating symptoms of bronchitis, asthma, and coughs, devil's breath can improve breathing and overall quality of life for individuals with respiratory conditions.

This plant's benefits can also help alleviate menstrual cramps, fever, and other common health issues, making it a valuable addition to natural medicine practices.
