10 Precautions To Take When Using Alstonia Constricta (Devil'S Breath)

Alstonia constricta has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting a healthcare professional due to its potential interactions with medications.

Failure to do so may lead to adverse effects like excessive sedation or respiratory depression, particularly when combined with opioids or sedatives.

Additionally, patients with pre-existing conditions like hypertension or diabetes should exercise caution, as Alstonia constricta may exacerbate these conditions.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Alstonia constricta medicinally.

1. Keep away from children

When using Alstonia constricta medicinally, it's important to take under medical supervision due to the potential for adverse interactions with other medications and the risk of overdose.

As a powerful plant with potent alkaloids, unmonitored use can lead to serious side effects such as hypertension, respiratory distress, and even cardiac issues.

Furthermore, self-administration may mask underlying conditions or worsen existing health problems, making expert guidance crucial for safe and effective treatment.
