Heteropanax chinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Heteropanax chinensis?

Heteropanax chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, representing a specific lineage within the order Apiales. As a member of the family Araliaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Heteropanax, with its specific designation being the species chinensis.
