What side-effects this plant can have?

Lathyros davidii can cause severe poisoning through the ingestion of toxic alkaloids that disrupt the central nervous system. These chemical compounds are particularly dangerous because they can lead to symptoms such as muscle weakness, tremors, and loss of coordination. In high doses, the plant may result in permanent paralysis or even death due to respiratory failure. Because the toxicity is concentrated within the seeds and certain parts of the plant, even small amounts can be life-threatening to humans and animals. Caution is necessary to ensure that all parts of this species are kept away from living beings.
