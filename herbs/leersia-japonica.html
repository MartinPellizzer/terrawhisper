What's the taxonomical classification of Leersia japonica?

Leersia japonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Poales, it is a member of the family Poaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Leersia and the species japonica.
