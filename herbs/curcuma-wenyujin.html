Yunjin Curcuma (Curcuma wenyujin)

Yunjin Curcuma (Curcuma wenyujin) is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, native to China, Southeast Asia, and India. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and tubers have been used for decoctions, powders, and infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, carminative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
