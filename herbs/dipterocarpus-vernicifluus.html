Dipterocarpus Tree (Dipterocarpus Vernicifluus)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Dipterocarpus vernicifluus.

Dipterocarpus tree, scientifically known as Dipterocarpus vernicifluus, is a valuable medicinal tree native to Southeast Asia, renowned for its rich pharmacological properties and traditional significance. This tree is primarily recognized as a medicinal herb and adaptogen, valued for its ability to enhance physical and mental resilience. Its primary benefits include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which support immune function and overall wellness. Traditionally, it has been used in indigenous and Ayurvedic medicine to treat ailments such as fever, digestive disorders, and skin infections, while its resin has been prized for its unique aromatic and therapeutic qualities. In modern wellness practices, extracts from the tree are incorporated into supplements and skincare products, and its rare active compounds continue to attract scientific interest for their potential in pharmaceutical development.
