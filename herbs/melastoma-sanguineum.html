What's the taxonomical classification of Melastoma sanguineum?

Melastoma sanguineum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Myrtales and is a member of the family Melastomataceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Melastoma, and its specific species name, sanguineum.
