What is Agoseris alpestris?

Agoseris alpestris is an annual plant, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It has a taproot, slender stem, and a rosette of basal leaves that are lance-shaped, 5-15 cm long, and 1-3 cm wide. The inflorescence is a corymb of 10-30 capitula, each with 8-15 purple to pink flowers, 2-4 cm in diameter, and a yellow center. The capitula are 2-5 mm long, and the phyllaries are lance-shaped, 1-2 cm long, and 1-3 mm wide.

The plant grows in dry to mesic areas, typically in sandy or rocky soils, at elevations of 2,000-3,500 meters.
