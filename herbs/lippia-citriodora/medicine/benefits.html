10 Health Benefits Of Lippia Citriodora (Lemon Verbena)

Lippia citriodora has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it effective in soothing digestive issues, reducing pain and inflammation.

Its calming effects can help alleviate anxiety and insomnia, promoting a good night's sleep. Additionally, its antioxidant properties may reduce the risk of chronic diseases like cancer and heart disease.

By incorporating lemon verbena into one's diet or using its essential oil topically, individuals can experience improved overall well-being and enhanced quality of life.
