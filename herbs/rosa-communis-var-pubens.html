Rosa communis var. pubens

Rosa communis var. pubens, also known as the downy dog rose, is a variety of the wild rose plant that is characterized by its downy leaves and stems.

The health benefits of Rosa communis var. pubens include reducing inflammation, improving digestion, and supporting the immune system, as well as its potential to act as an antimicrobial agent.
