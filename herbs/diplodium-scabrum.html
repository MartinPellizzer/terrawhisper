The therapeutic actions of Diplodium scabrum include its ability to reduce inflammation, fight off infections, and exhibit antioxidant activity.

Possible Side Effects of Diplodium scabrum

Diplodium scabrum can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and digestive problems.
