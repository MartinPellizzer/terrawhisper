Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii

What's the taxonomical classification of Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii?

Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and follows the order Apiales. It is a member of the family Apiaceae and is further defined by the genus Bupleurum, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Bupleurum longicaule var. franchetii.
