What's the taxonomical classification of Trigonotis cavaleriei?

Trigonotis cavaleriei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Boraginales and is a member of the family Boraginaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Trigonotis, and its unique species designation, cavaleriei.
