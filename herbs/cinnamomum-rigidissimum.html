What's the taxonomical classification of Cinnamomum rigidissimum?

Cinnamomum rigidissimum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Laurales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Lauraceae, specifically falling under the genus Cinnamomum, with the specific epithet rigidissimum identifying the individual species.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
