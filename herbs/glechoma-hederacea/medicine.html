What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Glechoma Hederacea (Ground Ivy)?

Glechoma hederacea, also known as ground ivy, has health benefits such as soothing digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and improving respiratory function.

Its medicinal constituents include rosmarinic acid, ursolic acid, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Ground ivy is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or infusion, with the leaves being the most commonly used part of the plant. Side effects are generally mild and rare, but excessive consumption may cause allergic reactions or interact with certain medications.

Precautions include pregnant or breastfeeding women, as well as individuals with severe kidney or liver disease, using ground ivy in moderation.
