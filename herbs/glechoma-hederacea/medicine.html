What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Glechoma Hederacea (Ground Ivy)?

Glechoma hederacea, also known as ground ivy, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and anxiety.

The herb contains bioactive compounds like rosmarinic acid, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Ground ivy is typically prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture to utilize its medicinal constituents. However, excessive consumption may lead to gastrointestinal upset, and individuals with allergies should exercise caution when using this herb.

Precautions include consulting with a healthcare professional before using ground ivy for medicinal purposes, especially during pregnancy or breastfeeding.

Glechoma hederacea has different medicinal preparations, such as tincture, tea, and infusions.

What are the possible side effect of using Glechoma hederacea improperly?

Improper use of Glechoma hederacea increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as allergic reactions, respiratory distress, and stomach upset.

In severe cases, it may even trigger seizures or anaphylaxis.
