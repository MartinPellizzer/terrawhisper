What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Coelogyne sanderae has creeping rhizomes that support numerous small, fleshy, ovate-shaped pseudobulbs arranged in a single row. These blossoms feature creamy white to pale yellowish petals and a distinctively shaped, often colored, lip.
