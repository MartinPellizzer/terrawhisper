Golden Everlasting (Helichrysum Lancifolium)

Golden everlasting, scientifically known as Helichrysum lancifolium, is a medicinal herb native to South Africa, prized for its adaptogenic properties and unique therapeutic potential. It is traditionally used to support respiratory health, reduce inflammation, and enhance immune function, with its primary benefits stemming from its high concentration of flavonoids and volatile oils. In traditional African medicine, it has been employed to treat ailments such as coughs, colds, and digestive issues, while also being valued for its calming and uplifting effects. Modern wellness applications include its use in aromatherapy, herbal teas, and topical formulations to alleviate stress and promote skin health. This rare plant is notable for its distinctive golden-yellow flowers that retain their color when dried, and it has a long history of being used in both culinary and medicinal contexts.
