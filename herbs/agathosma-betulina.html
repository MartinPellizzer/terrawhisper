Agathosma Betulina

Agathosma Betulina, scientifically known as agathosma betulina, is a medicinal plant native to South Africa, traditionally used for its various therapeutic properties.

The plant contains bioactive compounds such as sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Agathosma Betulina is often utilized in herbal medicine to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.

Its growing popularity in modern herbal formulations highlights its potential as a natural remedy for a range of health conditions.
