Amherstia nobilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Amherstia nobilis?

Amherstia nobili belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows a distinct evolutionary lineage that leads to its placement in the order Fabales. It is a representative of the family Fabaceae, commonly known as the legume family, which defines its genus as Amherstia. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is finalized under the species name nobilis.
