What's the taxonomical classification of Cotoneaster horizontalis var. perpusillus?

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. perpusillus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this botanical hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Rosaceae and the genus Cotoneaster, ultimately reaching its specific classification as the variety Cotoneaster horizontalis var. perpusillus.
