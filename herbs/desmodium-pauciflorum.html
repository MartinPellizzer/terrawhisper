What is Desmodium pauciflorum?

Desmodium pauciflorum is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, subfamily Faboideae. The plant typically grows 1-2 meters tall, with a woody base and an herbaceous upper part. Its leaves are trifoliate with 3 leaflets, 5-10 cm long, and 2-4 cm wide, having a rounded tip and serrated margins.

The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme with small, pink or purple flowers, 1-2 cm long, containing 10-15 stamens.
