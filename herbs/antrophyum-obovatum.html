What's the taxonomical classification of Antrophyum obovatum?

Antrophyum obovatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This fern is a member of the family Pteridaceae, specifically falling within the genus Antrophyum, and is identified by its specific species name, obovatum.
