What's the taxonomical classification of Neolitsea levinei?

Neolitsea levinei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows a taxonomic lineage that places it under the order Laurales. Within this order, it is situated in the family Lauraceae, where it is categorized under the genus Neolitsea, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species levinei.
