Round-Leaf Thyme (Thymus Serpyllum F. Cyclophyllus)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

Round-leaf thyme, scientifically known as Thymus serpyllum f. cyclophyllus, is a rare and aromatic medicinal herb prized for its adaptogenic and antiseptic properties. This variant of common thyme is characterized by its distinctive, slightly sweet and minty flavor, which makes it a unique spice and herbal remedy. Traditionally used in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cultures, it has been valued for its ability to support respiratory health, ease digestion, and boost immunity. In modern wellness practices, it is often incorporated into herbal teas, tinctures, and topical salves for its calming and healing effects. Historically, its rare active compounds, such as thymol and carvacrol, have been linked to its potent antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory actions, making it a versatile herb for both traditional and contemporary herbal medicine.
