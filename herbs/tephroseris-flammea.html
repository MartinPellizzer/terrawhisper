Tephroseris flammea

What's the taxonomical classification of Tephroseris flammea?

Tephroseris flammea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Tephroseris, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name flammea.
