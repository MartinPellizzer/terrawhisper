What's the taxonomical classification of Arenga westerhoutii?

Arenga westerhoutii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant is placed in the order Arecales and belongs to the family Arecaceae. Ultimately, it is identified within the genus Ahenga as the specific species westerhoutii.
