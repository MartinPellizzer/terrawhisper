Hordeum crinitum

Hordeum crinitum, commonly known as bearded spelt, is a perennial grass species within the Poaceae family, often found growing in the wild.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, helping to protect against various diseases and promote overall well-being.

The therapeutic actions of bearded spelt are attributed to its ability to soothe digestive issues, reduce anxiety, and support the immune system.

Possible Side Effects of Hordeum crinitum

Hordeum crinitum can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues, including nausea and vomiting, due to its high saponin content.

Additionally, improper use of hordeum crinitum may cause allergic reactions, such as skin irritation and respiratory issues, in some individuals.
