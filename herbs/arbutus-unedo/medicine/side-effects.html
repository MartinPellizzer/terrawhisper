10 Possible Side Effects Of Arbutus Unedo (Strawberry Tree)

Arbutus unedo has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, allergic reactions, and increased blood pressure.

These side effects may be caused by excessive consumption, sensitivity to the herb's compounds, or interaction with other medications.

For example, gastrointestinal upset can lead to dehydration, fatigue, and decreased appetite, while allergic reactions can cause hives, itching, and difficulty breathing, significantly impacting daily life and overall well-being.

Arbutus unedo causes allergic reactions in some individuals.

This is due to the presence of certain allergenic compounds, such as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which can trigger an immune response in susceptible people.

Additionally, the plant's sap and leaves contain latex, a common allergen found in many plant species, further increasing the risk of allergic reactions.
