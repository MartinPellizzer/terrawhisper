What is Roldana sinuata?

Roldana sinuata is a perennial shrubby plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Mexico and has silvery-gray, hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and deeply lobed. The leaves are 10-20 cm long and have wavy margins. The plant produces numerous small, yellow or orange flowers with 5-7 ray florets and a yellow or purple disk, in a dense, cylindrical inflorescence.

Roldana sinuata grows up to 1-2 meters tall and has a spread of up to 1 meter.
