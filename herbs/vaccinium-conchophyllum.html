Vaccinium conchophyllum

What's the taxonomical classification of Vaccinium conchophyllum?

Vaccinium conchophyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Ericaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Vaccinium and the species conchophyllum.
