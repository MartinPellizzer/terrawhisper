10 Medicinal Constituents Of Boswellia Carteri (Frankincense)

Boswellia carteri has active constituents such as boswellic acids, including alpha-boswellic acid and beta-boswellic acid, which have potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These constituents help to reduce pain and inflammation, making frankincense a natural remedy for arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.

The anti-inflammatory properties can improve lives by reducing chronic pain, improving mobility, and promoting healing, leading to an enhanced quality of life for individuals suffering from chronic conditions.

This article explains in details the 10 best active constituents of Boswellia carteri.

1. Akba (acetyl-11-keto-β-boswellic acid)

Boswellia carteri akba (acetyl-11-keto-β-boswellic acid) is a bioactive compound extracted from the resin of the Boswellia tree.
