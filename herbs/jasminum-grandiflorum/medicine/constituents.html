1. Volatile oils

Jasminum grandiflorum, also known as Arabian Jasmine, volatile oils is a complex mixture of aroma compounds extracted from the flowers of this evergreen climbing plant.

The essential oil is highly valued for its sweet, floral, and slightly spicy scent, often used in perfumes, aromatherapy, and traditional medicine.

It contains mainly benzyl acetate, linalool, and phenethyl alcohol as major components, which contribute to its characteristic fragrance and therapeutic properties.
