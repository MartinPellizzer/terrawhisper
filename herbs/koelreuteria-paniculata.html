What's the taxonomical classification of Koelreuteria paniculata?

Koelreuteria paniculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is categorized under the order Sapindales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Sapindaceae, which contains the genus Koelreuteria, ultimately identifying this specific plant as the species Koelreuteria paniculata.
