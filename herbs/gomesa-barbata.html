What is Gomesa barbata?

Gomesa barbata is a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to South America, specifically in the subtropical and tropical regions of Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay. The plant features a sympodial habit, with elongated, keeled pseudobulbs that are 2.5-5 cm long and 1-2 cm wide. The leaves are 2-4 cm long, elliptical, and leathery, while the inflorescence is a raceme with 5-10 flowers, each 3-4 cm long and 2-3 cm wide, with a yellow lip and sepals and a purple-brown labellum.

The flowers have a sweet fragrance and bloom from September to November.
