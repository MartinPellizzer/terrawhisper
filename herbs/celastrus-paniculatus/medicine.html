What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Celastrus Paniculatus (Malkangani)?

Celastrus paniculatus has health benefits such as reducing anxiety and stress levels, improving sleep quality, and boosting cognitive function.

The herb is rich in bioactive compounds like alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties. Malkangani is prepared into various forms, including capsules, tablets, and powders, which can be consumed orally or used topically.

However, it may cause allergic reactions, stomach upset, or interact with certain medications in rare cases, making it essential to consult a healthcare professional before using the herb, especially for extended periods or in high doses.

What are the health benefits of Celastrus paniculatus?

Celastrus paniculatus has health benefits such as reducing anxiety and stress levels due to its adaptogenic properties.

Its extract has been shown to improve cognitive function, enhance memory, and alleviate symptoms of depression. Additionally, it is believed to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help protect against chronic diseases like arthritis and cardiovascular disease.

Overall, malkangani is considered a natural remedy for promoting overall well-being and mental health.

What are the active constituents of Celastrus paniculatus?

The health benefits of Celastrus paniculatus come from its active constituents, such as alkaloids, glycosides, flavonoids, and saponins.

These compounds have been found to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may contribute to the herb's traditional uses in treating fever, cough, and respiratory disorders.

Additionally, the plant's extracts have been shown to exhibit neuroprotective and anti-anxiety effects, making it a potential remedy for neurological and psychiatric conditions.

What are the medicinal preparations of Celastrus paniculatus?

Celastrus paniculatus, also known as malkangni, has different medicinal preparations, such as capsules, tablets, oil, and powder.

Capsules and tablets are used to treat anxiety, stress, and insomnia due to their adaptogenic properties, promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality. Oil extracted from the seeds is used topically to relieve pain and inflammation, while powder is taken orally to address digestive issues and respiratory problems.

Each preparation serves a specific purpose, offering a range of benefits for overall well-being and health.

What are the possible side effect of using Celastrus paniculatus improperly?

Improper use of Celastrus paniculatus, also known as malkangni, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as dizziness, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, headache, fatigue, insomnia, restlessness, anxiety, palpitations, increased heart rate, and blood pressure fluctuations.

It may also cause allergic reactions like rash, itching, and hives. In severe cases, it can lead to seizures, kidney damage, and liver failure.

Additionally, malkangni may interact with other medications, decreasing their effectiveness or increasing the risk of adverse interactions.

What precautions to take when using Celastrus paniculatus medicinally?

Before using Celastrus paniculatus, also known as malkangni, for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage and treatment duration.

Additionally, pregnant or breastfeeding women should avoid using it due to potential risks.

It is also important to monitor blood pressure and sugar levels, as well as liver function tests, during treatment.
