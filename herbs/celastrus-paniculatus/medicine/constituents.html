10 Medicinal Constituents Of Celastrus Paniculatus (Malkangani)

Celastrus paniculatus has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and triterpenes.

These constituents are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and adaptogenic effects. The plant's flavonoids can improve cognitive function and memory, while its triterpenes exhibit neuroprotective and anti-anxiety properties.

By alleviating stress and anxiety, malkangani constituents can improve sleep quality, boost mood, and enhance overall well-being, leading to a better quality of life for individuals.

1. Alkaloids

Celastrus paniculatus alkaloids is a complex mixture of bioactive compounds derived from the seeds of the plant.

The most significant alkaloids found in Celastrus paniculatus are panaxatriol, withanolide A, and celastrol, which have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and cytotoxic properties.

These alkaloids have potential therapeutic applications, including the treatment of various diseases such as cancer, arthritis, and neurological disorders.

2. Triterpenoids

Celastrus paniculatus triterpenoids is a group of compounds isolated from the plant's roots and seeds.

3. Flavonoids
