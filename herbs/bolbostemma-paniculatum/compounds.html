Chinese Bellflower (Bolbostemma paniculatum) Compounds

The primary compounds of Chinese Bellflower include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and saponins, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Phenolic acids and glycosides contribute to its antimicrobial activity, while tannins and alkaloids support its traditional use in treating inflammation and infections.
