Elaeocarpus duclouxii

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Elaeocarpus duclouxii has simple, alternate, elliptical to oblong leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface with finely serrated margins. The resulting fruits are drupes that transition from green to a dark color upon ripening. These botanical structures are essential for the plant's identification within its tropical habitat.
