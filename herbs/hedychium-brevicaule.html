Hedychium brevicaule

What's the taxonomical classification of Hedychium brevicaule?

Hedychium brevicaule belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is a member of the order Zingiberales. More specifically, this plant is part of the family Zingiberaceae, situated within the genus Hedychium, and is identified by its specific species name, brevicaule.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hedychium brevicaule has erect, rhizomatous stems that typically reach heights of 1 to 2 meters and feature large, lanceolate leaves arranged in a distichous pattern. Small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers emerge from the terminal spikes, characterized by delicate petals and prominent yellow labella. The inflorescence is often compact, displaying a dense cluster of blossoms that contrast with the elongated structure of the leaves.
