What's the taxonomical classification of Ptychococcus paradoxus?

Ptychococcus paradoxus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Arecales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Arecaceae, which contains the genus Ptychococcus, ultimately identifying the specific species as P. paradoxus.
