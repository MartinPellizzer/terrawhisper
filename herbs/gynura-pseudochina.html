Gyunura pseudochina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales.         What side-effects this plant can have?

        Gynura pseudochina can cause allergic skin reactions, gastrointestinal distress, and potential toxicity if consumed in improper dosages. Ingesting the plant might lead to symptoms such as nausea, abdominal cramping, or vomiting. There is also limited research regarding its long-term effects on organ function or systemic toxicity.
