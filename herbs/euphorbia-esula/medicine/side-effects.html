10 Possible Side Effects Of Euphorbia Esula (Leafy Spurge)

Euphorbia esula has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal issues.

These side effects can be caused by the plant's milky sap, which contains latex containing urushiol, a known allergen.

If not treated, these side effects can worsen to anaphylaxis, skin lesions, or digestive problems, significantly impacting daily life and productivity, leading to lost time from work or school.
