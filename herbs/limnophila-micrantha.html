Limnophila micrantha

Limnophila micrantha, also known as Marshall's Buttons or Blue Button Flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Asia, commonly found in wetlands and along rivers.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties, which make it effective in treating various health conditions.

The health benefits of Limnophila micrantha include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and support the immune system, while also exhibiting potential anticancer properties.
