What's the taxonomical classification of Deutzia glabrata?

Deutzia glabrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Cornales. This plant is further classified under the family Hydrangeaceae, falling into the genus Deutzia, with its specific identification being the species glabrata.
