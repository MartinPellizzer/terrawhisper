Ribes fasciculatum var. chinense

What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes fasciculatum var. chinense?

Ribes fasciculatum var. chinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Saxifragales. It is a member of the family Grossulariaceae and is specifically identified under the genus Ribes. Ultimately, its precise taxonomic designation is the variety chinense of the species Ribes fasciculatum.
