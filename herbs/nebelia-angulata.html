Possible Side Effects of Nebelia angulata

Nebelia angulata can have side effects if used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues in some individuals.

In rare cases, prolonged exposure to the plant's sap or leaves can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction.

Prolonged consumption of nebelia angulata extract may also lead to gastrointestinal problems, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

Furthermore, improper handling of the plant can cause skin contact dermatitis, characterized by redness, itching, and blistering on the skin.
