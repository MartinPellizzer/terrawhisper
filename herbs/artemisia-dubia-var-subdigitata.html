Artemisia dubia var. subdigitata

What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia dubia var. subdigitata?

Artemisia dubia var. subdigitata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. At a more specific level, the plant is placed within the genus Artemisia and belongs to the species Artemisia.
