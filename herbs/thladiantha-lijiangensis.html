Thladiantha lijiangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Thladiantha lijiangensis?

Thladiantha lijiangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Cucurbitales. This species is a part of the family Cucurbitaceae, which is characterized by its diverse range of climbing and trailing plants. Within this family, it is positioned in the genus Thladiantha, specifically identified by the species epithet lijiangensis.
