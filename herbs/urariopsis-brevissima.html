What's the taxonomical classification of Urariopsis brevissima?

Urariopsis brevissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant follows a specific evolutionary lineage that leads to the order Fabales. It is a part of the family Fabaceae, which is a diverse group of flowering plants, and is further classified under the genus Urariopsis. Ultimately, the specific identity of this organism is defined by its species name, brevissima.
