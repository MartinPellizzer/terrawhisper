What's the taxonomical classification of Spondianthus preussii?

Spondianthus preussii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further categorized under the order Malpighiales. This plant is a member of the family Phyllanthaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Spondianthus, with its unique designation being the species preussii.
