Microtoena delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Microtoena and the species delavayi.
