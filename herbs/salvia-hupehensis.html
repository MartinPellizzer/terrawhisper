What's the taxonomical classification of Salvia hupehensis?

Salvia hupehensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. As a member of the genus Salvia, it is specifically identified by the species name hupehensis.

Its inflorescence consists of dense, terminal racemes that bear tubular, bilabiate flowers typically colored in shades of purple or blue. The calyx is persistent and often displays a distinctively hairy or glandular texture.
