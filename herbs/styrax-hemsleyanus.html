Styrax hemsleyanus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Styrax and the species name hemsleyanus.
