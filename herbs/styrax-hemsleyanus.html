What's the taxonomical classification of Styrax hemsleyanus?

Styrax hemsleyanus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical structure of its lineage, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Styracaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Styrax and the species name hemsleyanus.
