Limaciopsis loangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Limaciopsis loangensis?

Limaciopsis loangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic progression continues through the order Ranunculales and the family Menispermaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Limaciopsis, and its unique species designation, loangensis.
