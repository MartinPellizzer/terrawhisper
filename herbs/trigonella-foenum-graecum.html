Medicinal Parts

Trigonella foenum-graecum seed, commonly known as fenugreek, is one of the most widely used medicinal parts of this plant, renowned for its numerous health benefits.

The seeds are rich in nutrients such as fiber, protein, and essential minerals like iron and magnesium, making them valuable for digestive health and metabolic support. They are often used in traditional medicine to help regulate blood sugar levels and improve cholesterol profiles, making them beneficial for individuals with diabetes or cardiovascular conditions. The seeds also contain compounds like saponins and alkaloids, which contribute to their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

In addition to their internal benefits, fenugreek seeds are used topically to treat skin conditions and promote hair growth, highlighting their versatility in both culinary and medicinal applications.
