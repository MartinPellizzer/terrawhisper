10 Possible Side Effects Of Erythrina Variegata (Coral Tree)

Erythrina variegata has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal disturbances, allergic reactions, and skin irritation.

The causes of these side effects are primarily due to individual sensitivities, excessive dosage, and contaminated plant extracts. For instance, gastrointestinal disturbances can lead to abdominal pain, diarrhea, and nausea, causing significant discomfort and impacting daily activities.

Severe allergic reactions can result in anaphylaxis, which requires immediate medical attention.
