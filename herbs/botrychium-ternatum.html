What's the taxonomical classification of Botrychium ternatum?

Botrychium ternatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Ophioglossidae. It further belongs to the order Ophioglossales and is a member of the family Ophioglossaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Botrychium, specifically identifying as the species ternatum.
