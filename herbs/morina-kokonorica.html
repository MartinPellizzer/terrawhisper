Morina kokonorica

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Morina kokonorica has a basal rosette of thick, leathery leaves that are elliptical to lanceolate in shape and feature prominently serrated margins. These leaves are typically dark green and possess a somewhat rugose surface texture. From the center of this rosette, a single, upright flowering stalk emerges to support a dense inflorescence. The flowers are small and arranged in compact clusters at the top of the stem. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, alpine environments.
