Lepidomicrosorium buergerianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Lepidomicrosorium buergerianum?

Lepidomicrosorium buergerianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further organized into the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This fern is nested within the family Polypodiaceae, which contains the genus Lepidomicrosorium, and is specifically identified by its species name, buergerianum.
