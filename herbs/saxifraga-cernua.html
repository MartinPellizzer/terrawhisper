Saxifraga cernua belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Saxifragaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Saxifraga and the species cernua.
