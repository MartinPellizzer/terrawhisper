What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium bupleurifolium?

Cremanthodium bupleurifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its complex flower structures. Within this family, it is specifically categorized under the genus Cremanthodium, with its unique identity defined by the species name bupleurifolium.
