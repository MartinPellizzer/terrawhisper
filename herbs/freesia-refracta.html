What's the taxonomical classification of Freesia refracta?

Freesia refracta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further descends through the order Asparagales and is a member of the family Iridaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Freesia, and its unique species designation, refracta.
