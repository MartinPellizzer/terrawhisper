10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pergularia Extensa (Devil'S Claw)

Pergularia extensa has active constituents such as harpagoside, meso-orientalide, and beta-sitosterol.

These constituents have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including reducing pain and inflammation.

The anti-inflammatory effect of these constituents can improve the lives of people suffering from arthritis, fibromyalgia, and other inflammatory conditions, enabling them to regain mobility and participate in daily activities.
