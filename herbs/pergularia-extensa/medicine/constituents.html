10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pergularia Extensa (Devil'S Claw)

Pergularia extensa has active constituents such as iridoid glycosides, alkaloids, and flavonoids.

These compounds are responsible for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antioxidant effects. The iridoid glycosides, specifically harpagoside and isoanguililoside, have been shown to reduce pain and swelling by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes.

This can improve quality of life for individuals suffering from arthritis, gout, and other inflammatory conditions, allowing them to engage in daily activities with reduced discomfort and pain.
