Pergularia Extensa (Pergularia extensa) Compounds

The primary compounds of Pergularia extensa include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while coumarin may support blood circulation. Glycosides, saponins, and terpenoids also play roles in its medicinal profile.
