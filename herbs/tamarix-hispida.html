What's the taxonomical classification of Tamarix hispida?

Tamarix hispida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This species is a part of the family Tamaricaceae, specifically situated within the genus Tamarix, where it is identified by its unique species name, hispida.
