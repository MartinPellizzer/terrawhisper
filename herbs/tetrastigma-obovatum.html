Tetrastigma obovatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma obovatum?

Tetrastigma obovatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Vitales. This plant is a representative of the family Vitaceae, specifically situated within the genus Tetrastigma, and is identified by its unique species designation, obovatum.
