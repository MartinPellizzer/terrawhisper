Jiaogulan (Gynostemma pentaphyllum) Actions

Jiaogulan exhibits several therapeutic actions including tonic, anti-inflammatory, and diuretic properties. It acts as a stimulant and carminative, aiding digestion and reducing gas. The herb also functions as an expectorant and sedative, helping with respiratory issues and calming the nervous system. Its antispasmodic and astringent effects support muscle relaxation and tissue tightening.
