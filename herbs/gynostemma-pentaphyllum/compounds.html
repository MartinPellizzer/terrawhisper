Jiaogulan (Gynostemma pentaphyllum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Jiaogulan include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and saponins, known for their immune-boosting effects. Glycosides and terpenoids contribute to its adaptogenic qualities, while phenolic acids and coumarin offer anti-inflammatory benefits. Tannins and alkaloids provide astringent and calming effects, and essential oils and anthocyanins enhance its overall therapeutic profile.
