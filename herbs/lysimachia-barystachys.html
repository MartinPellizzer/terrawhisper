Lysimachia barystachys

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia barystachys?

Lysimachia barystachys belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. This plant is specifically classified within the family Primulaceae, belonging to the genus Lysimachia, and is identified by its unique species designation, barystachys.
