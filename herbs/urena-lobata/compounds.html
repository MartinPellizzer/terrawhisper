Urena (Urena lobata) Compounds

Urena lobata contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties. Tannins and phenolic acids contribute to its astringent effects. Coumarin and saponins may support anti-inflammatory actions. Glycosides, terpenoids, and alkaloids also play roles in its medicinal effects, while essential oils and anthocyanins add to its therapeutic profile.

Flavonoids in Urena lobata are bioactive compounds known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
