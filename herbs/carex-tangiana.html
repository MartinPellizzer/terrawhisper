What's the taxonomical classification of Carex tangiana?

Carex tangiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within its hierarchical classification, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It follows the order Poales and is a member of the family Cyperaceae. Ultimately, this plant is defined by its placement within the genus Carex, specifically identified by the species name tangiana.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Carex tangiana has perennial, rhizomatous growth habits characterized by long, slender leaves and small, yellowish-green spikes.

The scales of the perigynia are a defining morphological feature, often possessing specific shapes or colors that assist in identification.
