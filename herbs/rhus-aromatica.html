Possible Side Effects of rhus aromatica

Rhus aromatica can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, skin irritation, and gastrointestinal problems.

Ingesting large quantities of the herb can cause nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to liver damage, kidney strain, and increased risk of bleeding.

Furthermore, Rhus aromatica can interact with certain medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, potentially exacerbating underlying health conditions.
