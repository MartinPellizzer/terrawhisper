Garcinia Indica: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Garcinia indica, commonly known as kudzu, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, widely used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

This herb is known for its rich content of hydroxycitric acid (HCA), which is believed to support weight management by inhibiting fat production.

Garcinia indica offers therapeutic actions such as appetite suppression, digestive support, and anti-inflammatory properties.

Its bioactive constituents include HCA, flavonoids, and polyphenols, which contribute to its potential health-promoting effects.
