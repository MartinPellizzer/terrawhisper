What's the taxonomical classification of Spiraea myrtilloides?

Spiraea myrtilloides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its evolutionary hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Spiraea, specifically identified by its species name, myrtilloides.

These plants produce flat-topped corymbs of blossoms that emerge in late spring or early summer to create a profuse floral display.
