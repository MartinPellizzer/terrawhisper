Possible Side Effects of Primulina bipinnatifida

Primulina bipinnatifida can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues, including nausea and vomiting, due to its high saponin content.

Additionally, improper use of Primulina bipinnatifida may cause allergic reactions, such as skin rashes and itching, in sensitive individuals.
