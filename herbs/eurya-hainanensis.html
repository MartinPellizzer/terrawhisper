Eurya hainanensis

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eurya hainanenxis has small, lanceolate to ovate leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and often possess serrated margins. The foliage is characterized by a leathery texture and a slightly glossy sheen. Its stems are woody and branch out to form a dense, shrub-like growth habit. Tiny, white, tubular flowers emerge in clusters from the leaf axils during the blooming season. These flowers eventually give way to small, fleshy drupes that serve as the plant's fruit.
