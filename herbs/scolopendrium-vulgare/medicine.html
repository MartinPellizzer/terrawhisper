What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Scolopendrium Vulgare (Hart'S Tongue)?

Scolopendrium vulgare, also known as hart's tongue, has health benefits such as soothing coughs and treating respiratory problems.

The herb contains flavonoids, tannins, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Infusions and tinctures are prepared from the leaves, which are harvested in the wild or cultivated in controlled environments.

However, excessive consumption may lead to stomach upset or allergic reactions, and individuals with sensitive skin should exercise caution when handling the plant.
