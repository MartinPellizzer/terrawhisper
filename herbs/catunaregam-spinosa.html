Catunaregam spinosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Catunaregam spinosa?

Catunaregam spinosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Gentianaales. This plant is specifically situated within the family Rubiaceae, which is the group to which its genus, Catunaregam, is assigned. Finally, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species name, spinosa.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Individuals with known allergies to similar botanical species should exercise extreme caution to avoid swelling or redness. There is also limited clinical data regarding the long-term systemic effects of consuming its extracts. Consequently, medical professionals advise avoiding direct contact or unverified ingestion to prevent unexpected adverse reactions.
