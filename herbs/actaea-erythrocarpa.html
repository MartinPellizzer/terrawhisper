Actaea erythrocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Actaea erythrocarpa?

Actaea erythrocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is specifically categorized within the genus Actaea, with its unique scientific designation being the species erythrocarpa.
