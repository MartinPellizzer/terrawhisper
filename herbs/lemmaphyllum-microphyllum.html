What's the taxonomical classification of Lemmaphyllum microphyllum?

Lemmaphyllum microphyllum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Polypodiinae and follows the taxonomic order Polypodiales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Polypodiaceae. Its specific classification is defined by its membership in the genus Lemmaphyllum, with the unique species designation being microphyllum.
