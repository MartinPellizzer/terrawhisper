10 Precautions To Take When Using Cedrus Atlantica (Atlas Cedar)

Cedrus atlantica has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious when consuming large quantities or using it for prolonged periods.

High doses or prolonged use can cause liver toxicity, which can be exacerbated by pre-existing liver conditions. Failing to heed this warning may lead to symptoms like jaundice, fatigue, and nausea.

It is essential to follow recommended dosages and consult a healthcare professional before using Cedrus atlantica for medicinal purposes.

When using Cedrus atlantica, also known as Atlas Cedar, medicinally, it's important to avoid during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

This is because the essential oils extracted from this tree can stimulate uterine contractions, potentially leading to miscarriage or preterm labor. Additionally, the oils may also be excreted in breast milk, posing a risk of toxicity to the infant.

Therefore, pregnant and lactating women should exercise caution when considering the use of Cedrus atlantica medicinally, and consult with a healthcare professional before doing so.
