What to know about Huperzia serrata (club moss) before using it medicinally

Club moss, scientifically known as Huperzia serrata, is a versatile herb that has been revered for its numerous health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to reduce stress and improve overall well-being.

In the culinary world, club moss is often used in traditional Chinese medicine to flavor soups and stews, while its delicate shoots are also used as a garnish or added to salads. Horticulturists appreciate club moss for its ability to thrive in a variety of environments and its use as an ornamental plant in landscaping. Botanically speaking, club moss is a member of the Lycopodiaceae family, characterized by its unique spore-producing structures and evergreen foliage.

With a history dating back thousands of years, club moss has been mentioned in ancient Chinese texts as a valuable medicinal herb, making it a fascinating subject to explore in the world of herbalism.
