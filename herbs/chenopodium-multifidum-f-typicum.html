Chenopodium multifidum f. typicum

Chenopodium multifidum f. typicum, also known as multifid wormwood or multifid goosefoot, is a subspecies of the Chenopodium multifidum plant, which is a type of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help to soothe digestive issues and relieve pain.

The health benefits of Chenopodium multifidum f. typicum include its ability to support the immune system and reduce inflammation, making it a potential natural remedy for conditions such as arthritis and digestive disorders.
