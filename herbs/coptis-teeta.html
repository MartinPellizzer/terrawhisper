Coptis Teeta (Coptis teeta)

Coptis Teeta (Coptis teeta) is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, native to Himalayas, Tibet, and Nepal. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and stems have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in ayurvedic medicine, traditional chinese medicine, and tibetan medicine.
