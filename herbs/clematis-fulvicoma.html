Clematis fulvicoma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is categorized under the order Ranunculales. This plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Clematis, where it is distinguished as the species fulvicoma.
