10 Medicinal Preparations Of Verbena Officinalis (Lemon Verbena)

Verbena officinalis has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and essential oils.

These preparations are useful for treating anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. The essential oil is also used to soothe skin irritations and wounds, while the tincture is used to treat menstrual cramps and other female health issues.

The herb is also used to calm nervous headaches and promote relaxation.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Verbena officinalis.

1. Teas

Verbena officinalis teas is used to soothe digestive issues such as bloating, gas, and stomach cramps due to its calming properties.

It has also been traditionally used to treat anxiety and insomnia by promoting relaxation and reducing stress levels.

Additionally, verbena tea has anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making it effective in treating respiratory issues like bronchitis and sore throats.

2. Tinctures

Verbena officinalis tinctures is used to promote digestive health and relieve symptoms of indigestion, bloating, and nausea.
