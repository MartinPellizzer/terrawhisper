Podocarpus forrestii

What's the taxonomical classification of Podocarpus forrestii?

Podocarpus forrestii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Pinidae, following the order Pinales. As a member of the family Podocarpaceae, it is placed within the genus Podocarpus, specifically identified by the species name forrestii.
