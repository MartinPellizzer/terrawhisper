Thladiantha dubia

What's the taxonomical classification of Thladiantha dubia?

Thladiantha dubia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving through its taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Thladiantha, and its unique species name, dubia.
