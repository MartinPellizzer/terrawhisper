This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can aid in the treatment of various conditions such as fever, digestive issues, and skin problems.

The therapeutic actions of Rubia cordifolia f. sylvatica include its ability to stimulate the immune system, reduce pain and inflammation, and exhibit antiviral and antibacterial properties.
