Adinandra glischroloma var. macrosepala

What's the taxonomical classification of Adinandra glischroloma var. macrosepala?

Adinandra glischroloma var. macrosepala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ericales and is a specific representative of the family Pentaphylacaceae. Within this family, the plant is categorized under the genus Adinandra, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety macrosepala of the species Adinandra glischroloma.
