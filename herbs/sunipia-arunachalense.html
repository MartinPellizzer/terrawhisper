Sunipia arunachalense

Sunipia arunachalense is a plant species endemic to the Arunachal Pradesh region in India.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly due to the presence of bioactive compounds that have been found to have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities.

The health benefits of Sunipia arunachalense include the potential to alleviate various health issues, such as fever, cough, and respiratory problems, due to its antipyretic and expectorant properties.

In traditional medicine, Sunipia arunachalense is prepared as a herbal decoction or infusion, and it is often used in combination with other herbs to enhance its medicinal effects.
