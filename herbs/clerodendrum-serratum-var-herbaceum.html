Clerodendrum serratum var. herbaceum

What's the taxonomical classification of Clerodendrum serratum var. herbaceum?

Clerodendrum serratum var. herbaceum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is situated in the family Lamiaceae. At the more specific level of classification, this plant is identified by the genus Clerodendrum, and its precise botanical designation is the species Clerodendrum serratum var. herbaceum.
