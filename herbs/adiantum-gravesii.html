What's the taxonomical classification of Adiantum gravesii?

Adiantum gravesii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Polypodiinae and falls under the order Polypodiales. This fern is a member of the family Pteridaceae and is specifically categorized within the genus Adiantum, ultimately identified by its unique species name, gravesii.
