Globba barthei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its descent through the order Zingiberales, it is a member of the family Zingiberaceae. Specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Globba, and its unique biological identity is defined by its species name, barthei.
