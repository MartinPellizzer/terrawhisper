Syringa oblata subsp. oblata

What's the taxonomical classification of Syringa oblata subsp. oblata?

Syringa oblata subsp. oblata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the order Lamiales and falls into the family Oleaceae. This specific plant is a member of the genus Syringa, and its precise taxonomic designation is the species Syringa oblata subsp. oblata.
