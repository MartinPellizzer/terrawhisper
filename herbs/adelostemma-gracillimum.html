Adelostemma gracillimum

What's the taxonomical classification of Adelostemma gracillimum?

Adelostemma gracillimum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Gentianaales. This plant is further categorized under the family Apocynaceae, which places it within the genus Adelostemma, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species gracillimum.

It is also applied topically to alleviate symptoms of localized infections and skin irritation. Some traditional practitioners utilize the plant to calm swelling and redness on the surface of the body. This botanical remedy serves as a natural component in managing minor dermatological ailments.
