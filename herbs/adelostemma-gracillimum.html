Adelostemma gracillimum

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Adelostemma gracillimum has slender, herbaceous stems that support small, lanceolate leaves arranged oppositely along the length of the plant. The foliage is characterized by a delicate texture and a relatively thin profile, which contributes to its gracile appearance. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils or at the terminal ends of the stems. These blooms typically feature a subtle structure that complements the fine architecture of the vegetative parts. The overall morphology reflects a highly refined and lightweight physical form.
