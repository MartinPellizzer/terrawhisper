What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ormosia fordiana has pinnately compound leaves with numerous small, oblong leaflets and woody stems characterized by a dense, spreading growth habit. The most distinctive morphological feature is the production of bright red, hard-coated seeds that contrast sharply against the dark woody structures. These seeds are encased in woody pods that eventually dehiscence to release the colorful contents.
