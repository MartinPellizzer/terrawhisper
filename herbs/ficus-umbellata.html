Ficus umbellata leaf is commonly used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Ficus umbellata results in irritation because its sap contains irritant compounds that can cause inflammation when they come into contact with mucous membranes or sensitive tissues.

Ficus umbellata causes skin irritation as the sap contains latex and other irritants that may provoke redness, itching, and blistering upon contact.

Ficus umbellata results in eye irritation because the irritant compounds in its sap can cause redness, burning, and excessive tearing if they come into contact with the eyes.
