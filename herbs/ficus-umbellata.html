Ficus Umbellata: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Ficus umbellata, commonly known as the weeping fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae, native to Southeast Asia.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

Therapeutically, it may support respiratory health, reduce stress, and promote skin healing.

The bioactive constituents of Ficus umbellata include flavonoids, tannins, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
