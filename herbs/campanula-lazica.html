Campanula lazica

Campanula lazica, also known as Armenian bellflower, is a flowering plant species native to the Caucasus region.

Traditionally, the plant has been used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Campanula lazica include its potential to reduce fever, alleviate respiratory issues, and soothe digestive problems.

The plant can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to harness its medicinal properties.
