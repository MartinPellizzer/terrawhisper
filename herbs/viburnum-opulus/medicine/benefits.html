10 Health Benefits Of Viburnum Opulus (Guelder Rose)

Viburnum opulus, also known as guelder rose, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and alleviating pain.

The plant's anthraquinone and flavonoid content give it anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. This makes it effective in treating arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.

For example, a study found that guelder rose extract reduced pain and improved mobility in patients with osteoarthritis, enabling them to engage in daily activities with greater ease and comfort.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Viburnum opulus.

1. Reduces inflammation

Viburnum opulus reduces inflammation because of its rich flavonoid and phenolic acid content.

These compounds have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to mitigate the production of pro-inflammatory chemicals in the body. As a result, guelder rose extract has been shown to effectively alleviate symptoms associated with inflammatory conditions such as arthritis, asthma, and other chronic diseases.

Its anti-inflammatory effects may also contribute to its traditional use in folk medicine for wound healing and skin conditions.

2. Alleviates pain

Viburnum opulus alleviates pain because of its unique composition of compounds that possess potent analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's leaves, stems, and roots contain a range of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes that work synergistically to reduce inflammation, inhibit the production of prostaglandins, and modulate pain pathways in the body.

This natural pain relief is particularly effective in alleviating musculoskeletal and arthritic pain, making guelder rose a valuable addition to herbal pain management regimens.

3. Boosts immune system

Viburnum opulus boosts immune system because of its rich antioxidant and phenolic content.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce oxidative stress and promote a healthy response to infection.

Additionally, guelder rose has been traditionally used to treat respiratory infections, suggesting that it may play a role in supporting the immune system's natural defenses against pathogens.

4. Fights bacteria

Viburnum opulus fights bacteria because of its rich content of bioactive compounds, particularly phenolic acids and flavonoids.

These molecules have been shown to exhibit antimicrobial properties, inhibiting the growth and replication of bacterial cells by disrupting their cell membranes and interfering with their metabolic pathways.

As a result, guelder rose has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various infections and diseases caused by bacteria.

5. Combats viruses

Viburnum opulus combats viruses because of its rich composition of bioactive compounds, particularly phenolic acids and flavonoids.

These molecules have been shown to exhibit potent antiviral properties, inhibiting viral replication and fusion, thereby preventing the spread of infections.

Additionally, guelder rose's extracts have been found to stimulate immune cell activity, enhancing the body's natural defense mechanisms against viral invaders.

6. Regulates blood sugar

Viburnum opulus regulates blood sugar because it contains anthocyanins and flavonoids, powerful antioxidants that help to improve insulin sensitivity.

The plant's active compounds have been shown to increase glucose uptake in the body, reducing the risk of developing type 2 diabetes.

Additionally, guelder rose extracts have been found to inhibit alpha-glucosidase activity, thereby slowing down carbohydrate digestion and absorption, which further helps to regulate blood sugar levels.

7. Lowers cholesterol

Viburnum opulus lowers cholesterol because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains polyphenolic acids and flavonoids that have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the gut, thereby reducing serum cholesterol levels.

Additionally, guelder rose has been found to possess antioxidant properties, which can help to protect against oxidative stress-induced lipid peroxidation and inflammation, both of which are associated with high cholesterol levels.

8. Improves circulation

Viburnum opulus improves circulation because of its unique composition of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes that have been shown to possess vasodilatory properties, allowing blood vessels to relax and dilate. This increased blood flow helps to improve oxygen delivery to tissues and organs, reducing inflammation and discomfort associated with poor circulation.

Additionally, the anticoagulant properties of guelder rose may also contribute to its ability to promote healthy blood flow.

9. Reduces blood pressure

Viburnum opulus reduces blood pressure because it contains a unique combination of flavonoids and terpenoids that have been shown to relax blood vessels and improve cardiovascular function.

The plant's extracts have been found to inhibit the production of angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE), which plays a key role in regulating blood pressure.

As a result, guelder rose may help to lower systolic blood pressure and reduce the risk of hypertension-related complications.

10. Supports liver function

Viburnum opulus supports liver function because of its rich composition of bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help protect the liver from damage caused by free radicals and toxins.

Additionally, Viburnum opulus has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various liver-related disorders, including jaundice and hepatitis.
