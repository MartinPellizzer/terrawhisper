Pseudognaphalium hypoleucum has a perennial herbaceous growth habit characterized by erect stems and leaves that are typically lanceolate or narrowly elliptical in shape. Its inflorescences are composed of small, clustered flower heads that emerge from the leaf axils or terminal positions.
