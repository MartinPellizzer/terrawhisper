Camellia mileensis var. microphylla

What's the taxonomical classification of Camellia mileensis var. microphylla?

Camellia mileensis var. microphylla belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is placed in the order Ericales and the family Theaceae. Further taxonomic refinement identifies it within the genus Camellia, specifically as the variety microphylla of the species Camellia mileensis.
