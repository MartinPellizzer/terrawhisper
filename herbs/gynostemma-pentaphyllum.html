Jiaogulan (Gynostemma pentaphyllum)

Jiaogulan (Gynostemma pentaphyllum) is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and shoots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, anti-inflammatory, and bitter actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
