Jiaogulan

Gynostemma pentaphyllum

Jiaogulan (Gynostemma pentaphyllum) is a climbing vine native to China, known for its adaptogenic properties and traditional use in promoting health and longevity. It is primarily used to support cardiovascular health, reduce stress, and improve energy levels by enhancing the body's ability to cope with physical and mental fatigue.

Botanical Identification

Gynostemma pentaphyllum is a climbing vine native to China. It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is also known as Chinese Jiaogulan or Southern Ginseng. The plant is a perennial with heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers, and its scientific name is sometimes cited as Gynostemma pentaphyllum or Gynostemma pentaphyllum var. sibiricum in traditional texts.

The following list summarize the taxonomy of plant:

  • Kingdom: Plantae
  • Division: Magnoliophyta
  • Class: Liliopsida
  • Order: Cucurbitales
  • Family: Cucurbitaceae
  • Genus: Gynostemma
  • Species: Gynostemma pentaphyllum

Safety Profile

Jiaogulan is generally considered safe when used in recommended doses. However, it may interact with certain medications, particularly those affecting the liver or blood pressure. Consult a healthcare professional before use, especially if you have existing health conditions or are on medication.
