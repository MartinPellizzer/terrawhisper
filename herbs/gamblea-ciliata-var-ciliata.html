Gamblea ciliata var. ciliata

What's the taxonomical classification of Gamblea ciliata var. ciliata?

Gamblea ciliata var. ciliata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Apiales. As a member of the family Araliaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Gamblea, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Gamblea ciliata var. ciliata.
