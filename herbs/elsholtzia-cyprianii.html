Elsholtzia cyprianii

What's the taxonomical classification of Elsholtzia cyprianii?

Elsholtzia cyprianii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Lamiaceae, falling within the genus Elsholtzia, and is specifically identified by its species name, cyprianii.
