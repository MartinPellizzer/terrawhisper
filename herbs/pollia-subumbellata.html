Pollia subumbellata

What's the taxonomical classification of Pollia subumbellata?

Pollia subumbellata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Commelinales and the family Commelinaceae, eventually leading to its placement within the genus Pollia. Ultimately, the specific identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, subumbellata.
