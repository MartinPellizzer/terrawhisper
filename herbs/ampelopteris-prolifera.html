Ampelopteris prolifera

What's the taxonomical classification of Ampelopteris prolifera?

Ampelopteris prolifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. The plant follows the order Polypodiales and is a member of the family Aspleniaceae. Taxonomically, it is defined by its placement in the genus Ampelopteris, with its specific identification being the species prolifera.
