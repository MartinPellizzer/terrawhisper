10 Medicinal Constituents Of Aristolochia Fangchi (Fang Chi)

Aristolochia fangchi has active constituents such as aristolochic acid, isopropyl and β-sitosterol.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, anti-cancer, and antioxidant properties. They can improve lives by potentially preventing cancer, reducing inflammation, and alleviating symptoms of chronic diseases.

For instance, aristolochic acid has been shown to inhibit cancer cell growth, while isopropyl may help alleviate pain and swelling associated with inflammation.

1. Aristolochic acid

Aristolochia fangchi aristolochic acid is a type of bioactive compound found in the plant Aristolochia fangchi.

This naturally occurring compound has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to treat various health conditions, including arthritis and respiratory issues.

However, it has been linked to serious kidney and liver damage when taken in high doses or for extended periods of time.

2. Β-sitosterol

Additionally, it has been shown to inhibit platelet aggregation, reduce blood lipid levels, and have immunomodulatory properties, making it a promising compound for the prevention and treatment of cardiovascular and inflammatory diseases.
