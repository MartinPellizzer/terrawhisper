Camellia (Camellia sinensis) Actions

Camellia sinensis exhibits several therapeutic actions, including stimulant, tonic, and anti-inflammatory effects. It also has antispasmodic and astringent properties, aiding in digestion and reducing inflammation. Additionally, it acts as a carminative, diuretic, and expectorant, while its bitter nature supports digestive health and can have a mild sedative effect.
