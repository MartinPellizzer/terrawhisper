10 Precautions To Take When Using Camellia Sinensis (Tea)

Camellia sinensis has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as pregnancy, breastfeeding, and kidney or liver disease.

Consuming excessive amounts can lead to adverse effects, including increased caffeine levels, which can exacerbate conditions like high blood pressure and anxiety.

Furthermore, interactions with certain medications, like blood thinners and certain antidepressants, can occur if not monitored properly, highlighting the importance of consulting a healthcare professional before use.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Camellia sinensis medicinally.
