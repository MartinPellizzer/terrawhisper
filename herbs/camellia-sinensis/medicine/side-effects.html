10 Possible Side Effects Of Camellia Sinensis (Tea)

Camellia sinensis has some side effects when used improperly, such as caffeine sensitivity, digestive issues, and anxiety.

Excessive consumption can cause these problems to worsen, leading to sleep disturbances, heart palpitations, and decreased focus. Moreover, interactions with certain medications, like blood thinners, can increase the risk of bleeding and bruising.

In severe cases, this may lead to more serious health complications, such as cardiovascular disease and kidney stones.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Camellia sinensis if used imporperly.

This is often attributed to the presence of specific proteins and polyphenols in the plant, which can stimulate an immune response leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.

Additionally, cross-reactivity with other plants or environmental allergens may contribute to the development of allergies to Camellia sinensis.
