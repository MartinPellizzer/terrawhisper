Monosperma Juniper (Juniperus monosperma) Uses

Juniperus monosperma is used to treat urinary tract infections and bladder inflammation due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate kidney stones and gout by promoting urinary flow and reducing uric acid levels. The plant also supports joint and muscle pain relief, easing arthritis and inflammation through its analgesic and anti-inflammatory effects.

Monosperma Juniper (Juniperus monosperma) Monograph

Juniperus monosperma has been traditionally used to support urinary tract health. Its antimicrobial properties help reduce bacterial growth in urinary tract infections.
