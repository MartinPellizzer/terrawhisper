Eucalyptus radiata subsp. sejuncta

Eucalyptus radiata subsp.

sejuncta, also known as 'Manna Gum', is a subspecies of the Eucalyptus radiata tree, native to Australia.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its essential oil, which is rich in compounds like alpha-pinene and beta-pinene, providing analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Eucalyptus radiata subsp.

sejuncta include relief from respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its potential to aid in wound healing and skin conditions.

Eucalyptus oil from this plant is commonly used in herbal preparations like inhalers, salves, and ointments to harness its medicinal properties.
