Pendulous Trillium (Trillium pendulum)

Pendulous Trillium (Trillium pendulum) is a member of the Melanthiaceae family, native to Eastern United States, Southeastern Canada, and Eastern Ontario. Traditionally, its roots, rhizomes, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, bitter, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
