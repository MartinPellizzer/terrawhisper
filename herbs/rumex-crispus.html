Possible Side Effects of rumex crispus

Rumex crispus can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

Additionally, using Rumex crispus in conjunction with blood thinners or diabetes medications can increase the risk of adverse interactions and complications.
