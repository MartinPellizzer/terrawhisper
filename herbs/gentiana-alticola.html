Gentiana alticola

Gentiana alticola, commonly known as Alpine gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family, native to the mountainous regions of China, India, and Nepal.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities, contributing to its health benefits such as reducing fever, alleviating pain, and supporting digestive health.

The therapeutic actions of Gentiana alticola include its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve respiratory issues, and exhibit anti-cancer properties, making it a sought-after natural remedy.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its medicinal effects include gentiopicrin, gentianine, and amarogentin, which have been identified as the primary compounds contributing to the plant's therapeutic actions.
