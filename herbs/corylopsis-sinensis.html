Corylopsis sinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Corylopsis sinensis?

Corylopsis sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Saxifragales and falls within the family Hamamelidaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Corylopsis and the species sinensis.
