What's the taxonomical classification of Myricaria rosea?

Myricaria rosea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Caryophyllales and is a member of the family Tamaricaceae. Finally, it is placed within the genus Myricaria, with its specific designation being the species rosea.
