Primula denticulata subsp. sinodenticulata

What's the taxonomical classification of Primula denticulata subsp. sinodenticulata?

Primula denticulata subsp. sinodenticulata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Ericales and is a representative of the family Primulaceae. At a more specific level, this plant is situated within the genus Primula, eventually reaching its precise taxonomic designation as the species Primula denticulata subsp. sinodenticulata.
