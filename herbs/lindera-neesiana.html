Lindera neesiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Lindera neesiana?

Lindera neesiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Laurales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Lauraceae, falling under the genus Lindera, with its specific designation being the species neesiana.
