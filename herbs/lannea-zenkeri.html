What's the taxonomical classification of Lannea zenkeri?

Lannea zenkeri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant falls under the order Sapindales and is a member of the family Anacardiaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Lannea and the specific epithet zenkeri.
