Lannea zenkeri

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lannea zenkeri has a woody climbing shrub morphology characterized by slender, twining stems and elliptical to lanceolate leaves with entire margins. The foliage typically features a glossy texture and a deep green color on the upper surface. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in panicle-like inflorescences that emerge from the leaf axils. These flowers eventually develop into fleshy drupes that serve as the plant's reproductive fruit. The overall structure allows the plant to navigate through forest canopies using its flexible stem system.
