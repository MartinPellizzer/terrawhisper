What's the taxonomical classification of Tetrastigma triphyllum var. hirtum?

Tetrastigma triphyllum var. hirtum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Vitales and falls into the family Vitaceae. Specifically, this plant is identified by the genus Tetrastigma, with its precise botanical designation being the species Tetrastigma triphyllum var. hirtum.
