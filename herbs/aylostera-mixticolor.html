    What is Aylostera mixticolor?

    Aylostera mixticolor is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

    It is a small to medium-sized cactus, typically growing up to 10-20 cm in height, with a columnar to cylindrical stem. The flowers are funnel-shaped, white to pale yellow, and 2-3 cm in length, occurring in the spring and early summer.

    The fruits are red to pink, and 5-7 mm in diameter, containing two to four seeds.
