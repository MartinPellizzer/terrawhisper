Cirsium vlassovianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Cirsium vlassovianum?

Cirsium vlassovianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Asterales. This plant is a specific representative of the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the aster or sunflower family. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Cirsium, and its unique biological identity is defined by its specific epithet, vlassovianum.
