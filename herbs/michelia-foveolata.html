Michelia fooveolata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its woody stems support these leaves in an alternate arrangement along the branches. The plant produces fragrant, cream-colored flowers that emerge from the leaf axils during its blooming period.
