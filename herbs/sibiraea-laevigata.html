Sibiraea laevigata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Rosales and is a member of the family Rosaceae. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by the genus Sibiraea and the species designation laevigata.
