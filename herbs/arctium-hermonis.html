Arctium hermonis, commonly known as the Burdock plant, is a biennial herb belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The therapeutic actions of this plant are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, which help to soothe and calm the body.

Possible Side Effects of Arctium hermonis

Additionally, the plant's use during pregnancy or breastfeeding may lead to birth defects or adverse effects on the fetus or infant.
