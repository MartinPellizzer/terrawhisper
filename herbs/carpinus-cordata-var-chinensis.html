Carpinus cordata var. chinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Carpinus cordata var. chinensis?

Carpinus cordata var. chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls into the order Fagales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Betulaceae, specifically positioned within the genus Carpinus. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its specific scientific name, Carpinus cordata var. chinensis.
