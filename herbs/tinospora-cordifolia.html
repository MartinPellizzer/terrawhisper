Tinospora Cordifolia Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Tinospora cordifolia, commonly known as Giloy, is a climbing shrub native to India and Southeast Asia, widely used in Ayurvedic medicine for its therapeutic properties.

This herb is renowned for its immune-boosting, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, making it beneficial for conditions like diabetes, respiratory infections, and skin disorders.

The bioactive constituents in Tinospora cordifolia include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids, which contribute to its healing and protective actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from its leaves and stems to harness its medicinal benefits.
