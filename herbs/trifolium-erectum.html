Trifolium Erectum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Trifolium erectum, commonly known as white clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, known for its trifoliate leaves and small white or pink flowers.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may support immune function and digestive health.

The bioactive constituents in Trifolium erectum include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
