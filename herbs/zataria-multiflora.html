Zataria (Zataria multiflora)

Zataria (Zataria multiflora) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Iran, Turkey, and Central Asia. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and resin have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its carminative, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
