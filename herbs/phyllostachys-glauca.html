Phyllostachys glauca

What's the taxonomical classification of Phyllostachys glauca?

Phyllostachys glauca belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It further falls into the order Poales and is a member of the family Poaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its specific placement within the genus Phyllostachys and its unique species designation, glauca.
