Carum buriaticum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the order Apiales, it is further categorized into the family Apiaceae, which is the characteristic group for this plant.
