Saussurea leontodontoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Saussurea leontodontoides?

Saussurea leontodontoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, which places it within the genus Saussurea, specifically identified by its species name, leontodontoides.
