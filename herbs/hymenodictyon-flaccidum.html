What's the taxonomical classification of Hymenodictyon flaccidum?

Hymenodictyon flacciidum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Gentianaales and is a member of the family Rubiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus, Hymenodictyon, and its specific species name, flacciidum.
