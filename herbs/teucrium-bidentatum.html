Teucrium bidentatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Teucrium bidentatum?

Teucrium bidentatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Lamiales. Furthermore, it is a member of the family Lamiaceae, falling under the genus Teucrium, and is specifically identified by its species name, bidentatum.
