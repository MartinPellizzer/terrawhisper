What's the taxonomical classification of Neoboutonia mannii?

Neoboutonia mannii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Malpighiales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae, specifically placed within the genus Neoboutonia, with its unique designation as the species mannii.
