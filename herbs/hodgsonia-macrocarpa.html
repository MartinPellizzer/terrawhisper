Hodgsonia macrocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Hodgsonia, and its unique species designation, macrocarpa.
