Rosa Canina Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Rosa canina, commonly known as dog rose, is a shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its edible red berries and medicinal properties.

This herb is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, offering numerous health benefits such as supporting immune function, improving heart health, and reducing inflammation.

The bioactive constituents in Rosa canina include vitamin C, flavonoids, proanthocyanidins, and ellagitannins, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

It can be prepared as an infusion, tincture, or syrup, making it a versatile herb for various herbal remedies.
