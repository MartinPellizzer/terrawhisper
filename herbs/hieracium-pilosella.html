Hieracium Pilosella Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Hieracium pilosella, commonly known as mouse ear hawkweed, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its fuzzy leaves and yellow flowers.

This herb has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and immune-supporting properties.

It contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, triterpenoids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and salves can be made from Hieracium pilosella to harness its medicinal benefits.

Hieracium pilosella alleviates pain through its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which help to block pain signals and reduce discomfort associated with various conditions.
