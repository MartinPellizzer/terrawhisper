What is Cleistocactus laniceps?

Cleistocactus laniceps is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

It is a columnar cactus, typically growing up to 1.5 meters in height, with a stout stem that is 10-20 centimeters in diameter. The stem is covered with 5-7 radial spines, which are brown to black in color, and with 0-4 central spines, which are also brown to black. The flowers are funnel-shaped, 4-5 centimeters in length, and are yellowish-green in color, with a yellow center.

The plant produces red or reddish-brown fruits, which are 4-5 centimeters in length.
