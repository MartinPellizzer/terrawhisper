Libanotis spodotrichoma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Apiaceae, which places it among the carrot and parsley relatives. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Libanotis, with its specific classification being the species spodotrichoma.
