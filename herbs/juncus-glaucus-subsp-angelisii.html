Juncus glaucus subsp. angelisii

Juncus glaucus subsp.

angelisii, also known as the Angel's Rush or Angel's Rush sedge, is a type of flowering plant in the sedge family (Juncaceae).

Traditionally, this plant has been used for its medicinal properties, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which are utilized to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of Juncus glaucus subsp.

angelisii include relieving digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and promoting wound healing.

In herbal preparations, this plant is often used in the form of teas, infusions, and tinctures, although more research is needed to confirm its effectiveness and safety.
