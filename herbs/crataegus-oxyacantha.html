Possible Side Effects of crataegus oxyacantha

Crataegus oxyacantha can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high tannin and flavonoid content.

High doses of Crataegus oxyacantha may also cause dizziness, headaches, and fatigue, while prolonged use can lead to kidney damage and decreased liver function.

In some cases, it may also trigger allergic reactions, such as hives and itching, in sensitive individuals.
