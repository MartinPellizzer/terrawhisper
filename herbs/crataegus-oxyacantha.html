Crataegus Oxyacantha Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Crataegus oxyacantha, commonly known as hawthorn, is a shrub native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, known for its medicinal berries and flowers.

This herb is widely used in traditional medicine for its cardiovascular benefits and its ability to support heart health.

The bioactive constituents in Crataegus oxyacantha include flavonoids, proanthocyanidins, and triterpenic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions such as improving circulation and reducing oxidative stress.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from the flowers and berries to harness its health benefits.
