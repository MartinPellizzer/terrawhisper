The bioactive constituents in Crataegus oxyacantha include flavonoids, proanthocyanidins, triterpenes, and oligomeric procyanidins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from its flowers, leaves, and berries to harness its health benefits.
