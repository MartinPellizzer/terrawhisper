Botrychium strictum

What's the taxonomical classification of Botrychium strictum?

Botrychium strictum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Ophioglossidae. The plant further follows the order Ophioglossales and is a member of the family Ophioglossaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is defined by its membership in the genus Botrychium, with its specific designation being the species strictum.
