What's the taxonomical classification of Itea yunnanensis?

Itea yunnanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Iteaceae and the genus Itea, ultimately reaching its specific identification at the species level as yunnanensis.
