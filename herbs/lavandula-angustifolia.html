Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia, commonly known as English lavender, is a fragrant, flowering plant belonging to the mint family.

This plant has been used for centuries to promote relaxation and reduce stress, thanks to its numerous health benefits, including its ability to calm anxiety, improve sleep quality, and alleviate symptoms of depression.

The therapeutic actions of Lavandula angustifolia include its sedative, antiseptic, and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe skin irritations and wounds.
