Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula

What's the taxonomical classification of Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula?

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Alismatales. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the family Araceae and the genus Cryptocoryne, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the variety Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula.
