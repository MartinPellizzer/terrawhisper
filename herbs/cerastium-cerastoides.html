What's the taxonomical classification of Cerastium cerastoides?

Cerastium cerastoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a representative of the family Caryophyllaceae, situated within the genus Cerastium, and is specifically identified by the species name cerastoides.
