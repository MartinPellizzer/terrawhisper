Speranskia cantonensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Speranskia cantonensis?

Speranskia cantonensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Malpighiales and falls within the family Euphorbiaceae. Specifically, it is identified by the genus Speranskia, with the specific epithet cantonensis denoting its unique species designation.
