Fritillaria yuzhongensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its perianth consists of six tepals that are often marked with distinct maculations and may exhibit a slight recurved shape. The reproductive structures include central stamens and a single style, which are tucked within the bell-shaped floral architecture.
