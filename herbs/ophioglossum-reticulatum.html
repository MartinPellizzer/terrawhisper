Ophioglossum reticulatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Ophioglossum reticulatum?

Ophioglossum retiiculatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Ophioglossidae and the order Ophioglossales. This plant is further categorized into the family Ophioglossaceae, belonging to the genus Ophioglossum, and is specifically identified by its species name, retiiculatum.

What are the therapeutic actions of Ophioglossum reticulatum?

Ophioglossum reticulatum has medicinal properties that primarily involve treating skin ailments, digestive issues, and various inflammatory conditions. Traditional practitioners utilize extracts from the plant to manage topical infections and promote the healing of wounds. It is often applied in various formulations to alleviate localized swelling and skin irritation. Furthermore, certain preparations are used internally to assist with gastrointestinal discomfort and metabolic regulation. These therapeutic actions are deeply rooted in regional ethnobotanical practices.
