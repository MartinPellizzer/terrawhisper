What's the taxonomical classification of Myrsine semiserrata?

Myrsine semiserrata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Ultimately, this specific plant is identified by its genus, Myrsine, and its species designation, semiserraata.
