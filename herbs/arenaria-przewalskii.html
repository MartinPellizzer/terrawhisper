Arenaria przewalskii

What's the taxonomical classification of Arenaria przewalskii?

Arenaria przewalskii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the taxonomic order Caryophyllales. It is further categorized under the family Caryophyllaceae, which places it within the genus Arenaria. Ultimately, its specific biological identity is defined by its species designation, przewalskii.
