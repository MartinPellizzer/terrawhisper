What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia deversa?

Artemisia deversa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Artemisia, and its unique species designation, deversa.
