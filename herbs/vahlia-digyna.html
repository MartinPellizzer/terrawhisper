What is Vahlia digyna?

Vahlia digyna is an evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Vahliaceae, an ancient lineage of plants with unique floral morphology. Botanically, Vahlia digyna features large, showy flowers with five yellow petals and 10-12 stamens, arranged in a dichasial cyme. The leaves are elliptical to ovate, often with a pointed apex, and have a leathery texture with a prominent midrib.

The plant typically grows up to 60-120 cm in height, with a robust rhizome system that produces new shoots and roots.
