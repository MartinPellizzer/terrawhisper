Oreocnide obovata

What's the taxonomical classification of Oreocnide obovata?

Oreocnide obovata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and placed in the order Rosales. This plant is a member of the family Urticaceae, specifically falling within the genus Oreocnide, with its unique biological identity defined by the species epithet obovatas.
