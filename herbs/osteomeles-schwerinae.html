Osteomeles schwerinae

What's the taxonomical classification of Osteomeles schwerinae?

Osteomeles schwerinae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is a prominent representative of the family Rosaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Osteomeles, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species Osteomeles schwerinae.
