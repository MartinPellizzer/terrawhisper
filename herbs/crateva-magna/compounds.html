Cape Gooseberry (Crateva magna) Compounds

The primary compounds of Cape Gooseberry include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Saponins and glycosides contribute to its medicinal value by supporting immune function and reducing inflammation. Phenolic acids and anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant benefits, while coumarin and terpenoids offer antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory actions.
