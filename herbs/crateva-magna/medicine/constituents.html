10 Medicinal Constituents Of Crateva Magna (Giant Chandelier Tree)

Crateva magna has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These compounds possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. They help to alleviate various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions.

The constituents in Crateva magna can also improve cardiovascular health, reduce oxidative stress, and promote wound healing, ultimately improving the quality of life for individuals who use this plant medicinally.

Crateva magna flavonoids is a group of bioactive compounds that have been isolated from its leaves and stems.
