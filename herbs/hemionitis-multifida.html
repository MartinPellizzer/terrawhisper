What is Hemionitis multifida?

Hemionitis multifida is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae, native to tropical Asia.

This epiphytic fern has fronds that are pinnatifid, with pinnae being linear-lanceolate to elliptic in shape, and the rachis is dark brown. The frond length is between 30-50 cm, with the leaflets being 3-5 cm long. The sori are linear, parallel to the margin, and have 8-16 sporangia per sorus.

The fertile leaves are similar to the sterile leaves but have a narrower and more lanceolate shape.
