10 Possible Side Effects Of Argemone Mexicana (Prickly Poppy)

Argemone mexicana has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.

These side effects are usually caused by the presence of toxic compounds, such as sanguinarine and dihydro-sanguinarine, which can be toxic to the body.

If left untreated, these side effects can worsen the lives of people, causing them to lose productivity, experience weight loss, and develop depression due to chronic pain and discomfort.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Argemone mexicana if used imporperly.
