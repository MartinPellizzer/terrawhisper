Parthenocissus dalzielii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy through the order Vitales and the family Vitaeaceae, the plant is placed into the genus Parthenocissus, eventually reaching its specific identification as the species dalzielii.
