What's the taxonomical classification of Begonia augustinei?

Begonia augustinei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Cucurbitaleales. This plant is a member of the family Begoniaceae, falling under the genus Begonia, and is specifically identified by its species name, augustinei.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Begonia augustinei has asymmetrical, ovate leaves that are characterized by a deep reddish-brown or bronze coloration and a noticeably puckered, bullate texture. Small, delicate flowers typically appear in clusters, contrasting with the heavy, textured appearance of the leaves.
