Adinandra millettii

What's the taxonomical classification of Adinandra millettii?

Adinandra millettii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the taxonomic order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Pentaphylacaceae and is further categorized under the genus Adinandra, specifically identified by its species name, millettii.
