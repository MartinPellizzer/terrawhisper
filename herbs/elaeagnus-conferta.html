What's the taxonomical classification of Elaeagnus conferta?

Elaeagnus conferta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Rosales and is a member of the family Elaeagnaeae. Finally, its taxonomic hierarchy is completed by its placement within the genus Elaeagnus, specifically identified as the species conferta.
