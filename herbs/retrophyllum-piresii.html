Researchers are exploring its use in treating infections and inflammatory diseases, as it may offer a natural alternative to conventional pharmaceuticals. Despite its promising attributes, further clinical trials are needed to validate its efficacy and safety for human use.

It is important to handle Retrophyllum piresii with care and follow safety guidelines to minimize these health risks.
