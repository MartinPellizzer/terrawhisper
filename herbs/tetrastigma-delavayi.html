Tetrastigma delavayi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaceae. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its genus, Tetrastigma, and its specific species name, delaayi.
