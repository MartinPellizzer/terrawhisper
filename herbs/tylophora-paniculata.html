Tylophora Vine (Tylophora Paniculata)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Tylophora paniculata.

Tylophora vine, scientifically known as Tylophora paniculata, is a medicinal herb native to Southeast Asia, widely recognized for its adaptogenic properties and traditional use in enhancing vitality and cognitive function. This vine is valued for its primary benefits, including its potential to support mental clarity, reduce stress, and improve immune function due to its rich content of alkaloids and other bioactive compounds. In traditional Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine, it has been used for centuries to treat ailments such as insomnia, fatigue, and respiratory issues, often prepared as a tonic or infusion. Modern wellness applications include its incorporation into herbal supplements aimed at boosting cognitive performance and emotional resilience, reflecting its growing popularity in holistic health practices. A unique feature of Tylophora vine is its distinctive bitter taste and the presence of the rare alkaloid tylophorine, which has sparked interest in scientific research for its potential therapeutic applications.
