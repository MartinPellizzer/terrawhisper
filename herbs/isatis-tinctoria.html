Woad (Isatis tinctoria)

Woad (Isatis tinctoria) is a member of the Brassicaceae family, native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, roots, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, bitter, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and european herbal medicine.
