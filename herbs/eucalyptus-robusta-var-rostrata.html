Eucalyptus robusta var. rostrata

Eucalyptus robusta var. rostrata, also known as the swamp gum or blue gum, is a tree species native to Australia, characterized by its distinctive blue-green foliage and tall, straight trunk.

The leaves and essential oil of this plant are valued for their medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The health benefits of Eucalyptus robusta var. rostrata include relief from respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as wound healing and anti-cancer properties.

Herbal preparations of this plant include essential oil, capsules, and tea, which can be used to alleviate symptoms, promote overall well-being, and support natural health.
