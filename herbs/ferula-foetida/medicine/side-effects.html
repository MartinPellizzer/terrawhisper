10 Possible Side Effects Of Ferula Foetida (Asafoetida)

Ferula foetida has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive problems, nausea, and diarrhea.

These side effects can be caused by ingesting excessive amounts, using low-quality or contaminated products, or having individual sensitivities to its compounds.

In severe cases, these issues can disrupt daily routines, reduce productivity, and lead to malnutrition, especially in vulnerable populations, like pregnant women and young children.
