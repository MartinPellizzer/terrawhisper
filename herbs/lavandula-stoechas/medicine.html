What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Lavandula Stoechas (Spanish Lavender)?

Lavandula stoechas, also known as spanish lavender, has health benefits such as reducing anxiety and promoting relaxation due to its calming effects.

Its medicinal constituents include camphor, borneol, and linalool, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. Lavender oil can be extracted for topical preparations, such as creams and salves, or inhaled as a calming steam treatment. Possible side effects include skin irritation or allergic reactions, and users should dilute the oil with a carrier and avoid ingesting it.

Precautions include caution with pregnant or breastfeeding women, as well as potential interactions with certain medications.
