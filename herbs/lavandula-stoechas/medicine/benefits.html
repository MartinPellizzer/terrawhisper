10 Health Benefits Of Lavandula Stoechas (Spanish Lavender)

This natural anxiolytic properties make Spanish lavender a popular choice for aromatherapy and herbal remedies.

4. Regulates blood pressure

Lavandula stoechas, also known as Spanish lavender, regulates blood pressure because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The plant contains a significant amount of luteolin, a flavonoid that has been shown to have potent vasodilatory effects, helping to relax blood vessels and reduce blood pressure. Additionally, Spanish lavender's saponin content can also contribute to its blood pressure-regulating properties by inhibiting the formation of angiotensin II, a potent vasoconstrictor.

This natural synergy makes Spanish lavender a promising adjunct for managing hypertension.
