Polygonum lapathifolium var. salicifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Polygonum lapathifolium var. salicifolium?

Polygonum lapathifolium var. salicifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Caryophyllales. As a member of the family Polygonaceae, it is categorized under the genus Polygonum, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the variety Polygonum lapathifolium var. salicifolium.
