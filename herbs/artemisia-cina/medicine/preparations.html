10 Medicinal Preparations Of Artemisia Cina (Wormseed)

Artemisia cina has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are useful for treating intestinal parasites, specifically tapeworms and roundworms.

These preparations are also used to alleviate symptoms of worm infestations, such as abdominal pain and diarrhea.

Additionally, they may be used to treat other conditions like indigestion and menstrual cramps, although the efficacy of these uses is not as well-documented.
