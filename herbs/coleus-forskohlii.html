Coleus Forskohlii

This herb is known for its potential health benefits, including weight loss, improved cardiovascular health, and enhanced exercise performance, attributed to its ability to increase cyclic AMP levels in the body.

The therapeutic actions of Coleus forskohlii are primarily attributed to its bioactive constituents, including forskolin, which is responsible for its medicinal properties.

Possible Side Effects of coleus forskohlii

Coleus forskohlii can have side effects if used improperly, such as stomach upset, diarrhea, and nausea due to the high concentration of forskolin, a compound that can stimulate digestive enzymes and muscles.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before adding this herb to your regimen to minimize the risk of adverse side effects.
