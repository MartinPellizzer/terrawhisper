What to know about Coleus forskohlii before using it medicinally

Coleus forskohlii, commonly known as forskohlii, is a plant-based herb that has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including high blood pressure, asthma, and glaucoma.

The main health benefits associated with the use of Coleus forskohlii include its ability to lower blood pressure, improve cardiovascular health, and enhance exercise performance. The herb is also valued for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its therapeutic effects. The bioactive compounds responsible for the medicinal properties of Coleus forskohlii include forskolin, a diterpenoid that has been shown to stimulate the enzyme adenylate cyclase, leading to increased cyclic AMP levels in cells.

In traditional Ayurvedic medicine, Coleus forskohlii is often prepared as a decoction or infused in water to make a tea, which is consumed to harness its medicinal benefits.
