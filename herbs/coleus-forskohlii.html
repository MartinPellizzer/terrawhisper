Coleus Forskohlii Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Coleus forskohlii, commonly known as Indian coleus, is a flowering plant native to India and widely used in Ayurvedic medicine.

This herb is valued for its bioactive compound, forskolin, which has various therapeutic properties.

Health benefits include supporting cardiovascular health, reducing inflammation, and potentially aiding in weight management.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules can be made from the leaves to harness its medicinal effects.
