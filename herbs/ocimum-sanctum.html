Ocimum Sanctum Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ocimum sanctum boosts immune system due to its rich content of antioxidants and phytochemicals, such as rosmarinic acid and eugenol, which help in reducing oxidative stress and enhancing the body's natural defense mechanisms.

Medicinal Constituents of ocimum sanctum

Ocimum sanctum carvacrol is a bioactive compound known for its potent antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, contributing to the herb's traditional use in treating respiratory infections and digestive disorders.

Ocimum sanctum polyphenols exhibit strong anti-inflammatory and cardioprotective effects, helping to reduce oxidative stress and improve overall cardiovascular health.

Ocimum sanctum essential oils contain a complex mixture of volatile compounds that provide soothing, antiseptic, and calming effects, often used in aromatherapy and for respiratory relief.

Medicinal Parts of ocimum sanctum

Ocimum sanctum leaf is widely used in traditional medicine for its aromatic and therapeutic properties.

Side Effects of ocimum sanctum

Ocimum sanctum may trigger allergies because it contains volatile oils and phytochemicals that can act as allergens, especially in people with pre-existing sensitivities.

Ocimum sanctum leads to nausea as its essential oils and compounds may stimulate the digestive system and affect the gastrointestinal tract.
