10 Medicinal Preparations Of Impatiens Balsamina

Impatiens balsamina has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and salves, which are useful for treating skin irritations and wounds.

The plant's leaves and stems are rich in enzymes that help break down urushiol, the allergenic compound found in poison ivy and poison oak.

The preparations can be applied topically to soothe and reduce itching and inflammation, making them a popular natural remedy for summer skin irritations.
