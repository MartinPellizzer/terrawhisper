What's the taxonomical classification of Ampelopsis aconitifolia?

Ampelopsis aconitifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is situated under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Vitales and belongs to the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Ampelopsis and is specifically designated as the species aconitifolia.
