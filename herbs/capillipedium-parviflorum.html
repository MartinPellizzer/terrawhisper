Capillipedium parviflorum has a slender, herbaceous growth habit characterized by fine, thread-like stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its inflorescence consists of minute blossoms that are often clustered in a way that mimics the appearance of fine hair. The root system is relatively shallow, supporting the light aerial parts of the organism.
