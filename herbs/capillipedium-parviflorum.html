What's the taxonomical classification of Capillipedium parviflorum?

Capillipedium parviflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Poales. This plant is a member of the family Poaceae, specifically situated within the genus Capillipedium, and is identified by its unique species name, parviflorum.
