Chinese Goldthread (Coptis chinensis) Compounds

The primary compounds of Chinese Goldthread include alkaloids, which have antimicrobial properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Glycosides and phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while tannins provide astringency and support wound healing.
