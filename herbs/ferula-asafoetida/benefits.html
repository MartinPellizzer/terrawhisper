Ferula asafoetida reduces gas formation because it contains bioactive constituents such as ferulic acid and umbellulone, which possess carminative properties that help alleviate digestive discomfort.

The reduction of gas formation can also alleviate symptoms of conditions such as gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).

By consuming Ferula asafoetida, individuals can experience relief from these digestive issues and improve their overall gut health.
