Tripartite Tickseed (Bidens tripartita) Compounds

The primary compounds in Tripartite Tickseed include flavonoids, which act as antioxidants, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and digestive effects, while coumarins may support blood circulation. Glycosides, terpenoids, and essential oils provide additional therapeutic benefits, and alkaloids and anthocyanins contribute to its medicinal profile.
