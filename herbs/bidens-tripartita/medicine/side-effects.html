10 Possible Side Effects Of Bidens Tripartita (Bur Marigold)

Bidens tripartita has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal irritation, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

The causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, individual sensitivities, and combining with other substances.

These side effects can worsen people's lives by causing discomfort, respiratory issues, or increased risk of bleeding, making everyday activities challenging and potentially life-threatening in severe cases.

Bidens tripartita causes allergic reactions in some individuals.

This is likely due to the presence of certain compounds found in the plant's leaves and flowers, such as sesquiterpenes and triterpenoids. These chemicals can trigger an immune response, leading to symptoms like skin irritation, hives, and respiratory issues.

Additionally, the plant's high content of flavonoids and terpenoids may also contribute to its allergenic potential.
