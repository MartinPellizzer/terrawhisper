Caesalpinia brevifolia

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its ability to treat various health conditions, such as fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, due to the presence of flavonoids and saponins.

The health benefits of Caesalpinia brevifolia include antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which may help to protect against cell damage and reduce inflammation.

Caesalpinia brevifolia can be prepared as an herbal tea, infusion, or tincture, and it is also used as a food source, with its leaves being edible and nutritious.
