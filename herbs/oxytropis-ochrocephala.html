Oxytropis ochrocephala

What's the taxonomical classification of Oxytropis ochrocephala?

Oxytropis ochrocephala belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Fabales. It is a member of the family Fabaceae, which places it among the legumes, and is specifically categorized within the genus Oxytropis. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific designation as the species ochrocephala.
