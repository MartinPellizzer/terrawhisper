The therapeutic actions of boldo include stimulating digestion, relieving gastrointestinal issues, and supporting the immune system, which is attributed to its bioactive constituents such as boldine, flavonoids, and alkaloids.

Possible Side Effects of peumus boldus

Peumus boldus can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, nausea, and vomiting, especially when consumed in large quantities or without consulting a healthcare professional.

Additionally, using peumus boldus for an extended period may cause stomach irritation, diarrhea, and allergic reactions in sensitive individuals.

It is essential to use peumus boldus under the guidance of a healthcare expert to minimize the risk of adverse effects.
