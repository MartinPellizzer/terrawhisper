Peumus boldus

Peumus boldus, also known as Boldo, is a plant native to South America, particularly in the Andes mountains.

It is a shrub-like evergreen with medicinal properties, primarily due to the presence of boldine, a bioactive compound that has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.

The health benefits of Boldo include its ability to aid digestion, alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome, and act as a natural diuretic, making it beneficial for urinary tract issues and kidney problems.

Herbal preparations of Boldo can be found in teas, capsules, and tinctures, often used to support liver and kidney function, as well as to reduce inflammation and alleviate digestive discomfort.
