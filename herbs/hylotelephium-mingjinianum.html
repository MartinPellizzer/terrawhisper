Hylotelephium mingjinianum

What's the taxonomical classification of Hylotelephium mingjinianum?

Hylotelephium mingjinianum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Saxifragales. It is a member of the family Crassulaceae, specifically situated within the genus Hylotelephium, with its unique identity defined by the species epithet mingjinianum.
