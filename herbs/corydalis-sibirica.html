Corydalis sibirica

Corydalis sibirica, also known as Siberian corydalis, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia and Europe, characterized by its blue or white flowers and yellow or green foliage.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries, with medicinal properties that include analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic effects.

The health benefits of Corydalis sibirica include relief from pain, inflammation, and muscle spasms, as well as improved sleep quality and reduced anxiety levels.
