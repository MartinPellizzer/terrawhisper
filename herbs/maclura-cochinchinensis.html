Maclurura cochinchinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat various skin infections, intestinal parasites, and inflammatory conditions through its medicinal properties. Local traditional practices often involve applying extracts to wounds or skin lesions to promote healing.
