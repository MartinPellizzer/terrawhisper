10 Health Benefits Of Terminalia Belerica (Bahera)

Terminalia belerica, also known as bahera, has health benefits such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antibacterial properties, which can improve heart health, boost brain function, and support digestive health.

The antioxidants in bahera can help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation, while its antibacterial properties can aid in treating infections and wounds.

By incorporating bahera into their diet or using it in traditional medicine, people can improve their overall well-being and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Terminalia belerica .

1. Reduces fever

Terminalia belerica reduces fever because of its inherent anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

The presence of various tannins and alkaloids in its bark and leaves contributes to its therapeutic effects.

These compounds help in inhibiting the growth of pathogens and reduce inflammation in the body, thereby lowering the body temperature and alleviating symptoms of fever.

2. Lowers cholesterol

Terminalia belerica lowers cholesterol because it is rich in flavonoids and phenolic acids, which have been shown to inhibit the activity of HMG-CoA reductase, a key enzyme involved in cholesterol synthesis.

The bioactive compounds in bahera also help to improve lipid profiles by increasing high-density lipoprotein (HDL) levels and decreasing low-density lipoprotein (LDL) levels, making it an effective natural remedy for cholesterol management.

3. Relieves constipation

Terminalia belerica relieves constipation because its rich fiber and mucilaginous properties help soften and move stool through the digestive tract.

The presence of bioactive compounds like terpenoids, flavonoids, and alkaloids stimulates digestive enzymes and enhances gut motility, promoting regular bowel movements and alleviating symptoms of constipation.

This natural remedy also soothes the gastrointestinal tract, reducing inflammation and discomfort associated with constipation.

4. Combats diabetes

Terminalia belerica combats diabetes because it contains flavonoids and terpenoids that inhibit the activity of alpha-glucosidase, an enzyme responsible for carbohydrate digestion and glucose absorption.

By slowing down the absorption of glucose, bahera helps regulate blood sugar levels and reduce the risk of hyperglycemia.

This property makes Terminalia belerica a potential natural remedy for diabetes management and prevention.

5. Controls blood sugar

Terminalia belerica controls blood sugar because of its unique properties.

The compound, terchebin, present in it has been found to increase the production of insulin in the body, thereby lowering the blood sugar levels. Additionally, the flavonoids and terpenoids in bahera help to enhance the sensitivity of the body's cells to insulin, making it easier for glucose to enter the cells.

This results in balanced blood sugar levels.

6. Inhibits cancer growth

Terminalia belerica inhibits cancer growth because of its high antioxidant and flavonoid content.

The plant's extracts have been shown to induce apoptosis in cancer cells, thereby preventing their proliferation. Additionally, its flavonoids exhibit anti-inflammatory properties, which help in reducing oxidative stress and inhibiting cancer cell growth.

These findings suggest that Terminalia belerica has potential anti-cancer properties, making it a promising area of research in the field of oncology.

7. Relieves menstrual cramps

Terminalia belerica relieves menstrual cramps because of its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The bark of the tree is rich in flavonoids and alkaloids, which help to reduce prostaglandins, the hormone-like substances responsible for causing pain and inflammation during menstruation.

These compounds work together to relax the uterine muscles, providing relief from cramps and discomfort, making Terminalia belerica a popular natural remedy for menstrual issues.

8. Eases inflammation

Terminalia belerica eases inflammation because of its rich content of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have potent anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, thereby reducing inflammation and swelling.

Additionally, bahera's bioactive compounds exhibit antioxidant activity, which helps to neutralize free radicals and further mitigate inflammation, promoting overall well-being and health.

9. Supports eye health

Terminalia belerica supports eye health because of its potent antioxidant properties.

The presence of ellagic acid, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds in bahera helps to neutralize free radicals and prevent oxidative stress, which can damage the eyes. This natural remedy may also improve vision by reducing inflammation and protecting the retina, thus promoting overall eye health and potentially alleviating age-related eye conditions.

Regular consumption of bahera may help prevent cataracts and macular degeneration.

10. Treats respiratory issues

Terminalia belerica treats respiratory issues because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

The herb's flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids possess potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which help alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and chronic coughs.

By modulating the body's immune response and reducing inflammation in the lungs, Terminalia belerica provides relief from respiratory issues.
