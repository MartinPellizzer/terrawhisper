Indigofera squalida belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is categorized under the order Fabales. The plant is a member of the family Fabaceae and is specifically identified within the genus Indigofera, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation at the species level as Indigofera squalida.
