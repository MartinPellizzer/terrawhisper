Hovenia trichocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Hovenia trichocarpa?

Hovenia trichocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is situated under the order Rosales. It is a specific representative of the family Rhamnaceae, falling under the genus Hovenia, and is identified by its unique species designation, trichocarpa.
