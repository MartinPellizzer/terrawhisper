What's the taxonomical classification of Rabdosia yuennanensis?

Rabdosia yuennanensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant follows the taxonomic order Lamiales and is a member of the family Lamiaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Rabdosia and the unique species designation yuennanensis.
