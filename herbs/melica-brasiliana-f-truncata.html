Possible Side Effects of Melica brasiliana f. truncata

Melica brasiliana f. truncata can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its saponin content.

Prolonged use or high doses of the plant can lead to liver and kidney damage, as well as interact with certain medications.
