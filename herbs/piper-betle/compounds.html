Betel Leaf (Piper betle) Compounds

The primary compounds in betel leaf include flavonoids, which are antioxidants, and alkaloids, which have stimulant properties. Phenolic acids and tannins contribute to its astringent taste and potential anti-inflammatory effects. Essential oils and terpenoids provide aromatic qualities, while coumarin and glycosides may offer additional therapeutic benefits.

Betel Leaf (Piper betle) Monograph

1. Flavonoid

Flavonoids are bioactive compounds found in Betel Leaf (Piper betle). They contribute to the plant's antioxidant properties, which may support respiratory and digestive health. These compounds are believed to enhance the therapeutic effects of Betel Leaf in traditional herbal medicine.
