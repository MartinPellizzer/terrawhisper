Salix caprea var. androgyna

Salix caprea var. androgyna, also known as the goat willow or silver willow, is a variety of the sallow tree that is characterized by its dual-sex catkins and slender branches.

The bark and leaves of this plant are rich in flavonoids, salicin, and other salicylic compounds, which have anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and pain-relieving properties, making it a valuable medicinal plant.

The health benefits of Salix caprea var. androgyna include reducing fever, alleviating rheumatic and arthritic pain, and supporting the immune system, while also possessing antioxidant and antimicrobial properties.

Salix caprea var. androgyna can be prepared as a tea, tincture, or ointment, and is often used in traditional herbal medicine to treat various ailments and promote overall well-being.
