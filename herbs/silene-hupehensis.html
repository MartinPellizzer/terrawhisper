What's the taxonomical classification of Silene hupehensis?

Silene hupehensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually placing it in the order Caryophyllales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Silene, with its specific designation being the species hupehensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Silene hupehensis has an erect herbaceous habit characterized by stems that are typically slender, branched, and covered in glandular hairs.

Its flowers are borne in terminal cymes and possess a distinct calyx with long, narrow teeth. The petals are deeply notched or bifid, creating a delicate appearance during the blooming period.
