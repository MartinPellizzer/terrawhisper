What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia quinquegona?

Ardisia quinquegona belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Primulaceae. Ultimately, the plant is defined by its genus, Ardisia, and its specific species designation, quinqugonia.
