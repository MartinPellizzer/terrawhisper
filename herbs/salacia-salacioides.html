What is Salacia salacioides?

Salacia salacioides is a species of flowering plant in the family Celastraceae.

It is an evergreen shrub or small tree with a height range of 2-10 meters, having a glabrous stem with a brownish color. The leaves are alternate, simple, and entire, measuring 5-13 centimeters in length, with an elliptical or lanceolate shape and a pointed apex. The inflorescence is a panicle, and the flowers are small, greenish-yellow in color, with five sepals and five petals.

The plant is native to tropical regions of Australia and New Guinea, where it typically grows in rainforests and along streams.
