Michelia maudiae

What's the taxonomical classification of Michelia maudiae?

Michelia maudiae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified into the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Magnoliales. This plant is a member of the family Magnoliaceae and is specifically situated within the genus Michelia, reaching its final taxonomic designation at the species level as maudiae.

While specific clinical studies on human toxicity are limited, many members of the Magnolia family contain bioactive compounds that may trigger contact sensitivity in sensitive individuals. If accidentally consumed, the plant might induce nausea, vomiting, or stomach discomfort due to its complex chemical makeup. It is generally recommended to wear gloves when pruning this species to avoid potential dermatological reactions.
