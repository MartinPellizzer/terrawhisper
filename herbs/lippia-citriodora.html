In some cases, improper use of Lippia citriodora can also lead to interactions with prescription medications, such as anticoagulants, blood pressure medications, and diabetes medications, which may exacerbate pre-existing conditions or increase the risk of adverse reactions.

Effective management strategies include regular monitoring for pests and diseases, maintaining good air circulation, providing adequate moisture, and using integrated pest management techniques to minimize damage and promote healthy growth.
