Lippia Citriodora Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Lippia citriodora, commonly known as citronella mint, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions, known for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.

This herb is valued for its ability to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and alleviate stress due to its calming effects.

The bioactive constituents include essential oils such as citral, geraniol, and limonene, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

You can prepare Lippia citriodora as an infusion, tincture, or essential oil for various wellness applications.

Health Benefits of lippia citriodora

Lippia citriodora has many health benefits, such as reducing inflammation due to its high concentration of anti-inflammatory compounds like flavonoids and terpenoids, which help suppress the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines in the body.

It reduces stress by containing essential oils with calming properties, such as citral and geraniol, which interact with the central nervous system to promote relaxation and lower cortisol levels.

It reduces pain through its analgesic properties, which may be attributed to its ability to inhibit pain signaling pathways and modulate neurotransmitter activity.

It improves digestion by stimulating the production of digestive enzymes and promoting the secretion of bile, thereby aiding in the breakdown of food and enhancing nutrient absorption.
