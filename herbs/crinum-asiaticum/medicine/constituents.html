10 Medicinal Constituents Of Crinum Asiaticum (Swamp Lily)

Crinum asiaticum has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These constituents have been found to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antidiabetic properties. The anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate pain and reduce swelling, while the antimicrobial properties can combat infections and promote wound healing. Furthermore, the antidiabetic properties can help regulate blood sugar levels, improving the lives of people with diabetes.

Regular consumption can also boost the immune system, reducing the risk of illnesses.
