Melilot (Melilotus officinalis)

Melilot (Melilotus officinalis) is a member of the Fabaceae family, native to Europe, West Asia, and Southwest Asia. Traditionally, its flowers, leaves, and stems have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, antispasmodic, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and japanese kampo medicine.
