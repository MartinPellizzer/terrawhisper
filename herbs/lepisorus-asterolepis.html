Lepisorus asterolepis

What's the taxonomical classification of Lepisorus asterolepis?

Lepisorus asterolepis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Following the order Polypodiales, it is placed in the family Polypodiaceae. The plant is further categorized under the genus Lepisorus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species asterolepis.
