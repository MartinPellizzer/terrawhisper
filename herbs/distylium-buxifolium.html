Distylium buxifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Distylium buxifolium?

Distylium buxifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Saxifragales. The plant is further categorized into the family Hamamelidaceae, within which it is situated in the genus Distylium. Finally, its specific biological identity is defined by its species name, buxifolium.
