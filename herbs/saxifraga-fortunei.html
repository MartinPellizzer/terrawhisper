Saxifraga fortunei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Saxifragales and is a member of the family Saxifragaceae. Ultimately, its specific biological identification is defined by the genus Saxifraga and the species fortunei.
