These effects occur due to its stimulant properties, particularly when consumed in excess. For individuals with pre-existing anxiety disorders, these symptoms can worsen their condition, making everyday activities like work and social interactions increasingly challenging.

In severe cases, excessive use can lead to dependence and exacerbate underlying health conditions.
