Catigua (Trichilia catigua) Compounds

The primary compounds of Catigua include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and phenolic acids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins and saponins contribute to its astringent and cleansing actions, while coumarins and terpenoids may support circulation and muscle function. Glycosides, alkaloids, and essential oils also play roles in its medicinal effects. Anthocyanins provide additional antioxidant support.
