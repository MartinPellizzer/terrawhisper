Possible Side Effects of Nubelaria wushanensis

Nubelaria wushanensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing respiratory issues due to the release of volatile oils, leading to symptoms like coughing, wheezing, and shortness of breath.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's essential oils can also cause skin irritation, resulting in redness, itching, and blistering.

In severe cases, improper use of Nubelaria wushanensis can lead to allergic reactions, including anaphylaxis, which requires immediate medical attention.

Additionally, the plant's oils may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions, like asthma.
