What's the taxonomical classification of Gaertnera paniculata?

Gaertnera paniculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Gentianaales and falls under the family Rubiaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Gaertnera, and its specific designation is the species paniculata.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Gaertnera paniculata has woody stems and opposite, elliptical to oblong leaves that are typically arranged in decussate patterns.

The foliage features prominent pinnate venation and acute or acuminate tips. Its reproductive structures consist of large, terminal panicles composed of numerous small, actinomorphic flowers. These inflorescences are supported by branching peduncles that emerge from the upper nodes of the plant.

The floral components include distinct petals and stamens characteristic of the Rubiaceae family.
