Gaertnera paniculata

What's the taxonomical classification of Gaertnera paniculata?

Gaertnera paniculata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Gentianaales and falls under the family Rubiaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Gaertnera, and its specific designation is the species paniculata.
