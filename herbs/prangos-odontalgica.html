What is Prangos odontalgica?

Prangos odontalgica is a perennial plant, belonging to the family Apiaceae, native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It has a taproot, a thick, woody rhizome, and a hollow, branching stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with a diameter of up to 2.5 cm. The leaves are pinnate, with 7-17 pairs of pinnae, each with 3-5 leaflets, and are 10-20 cm long and 10-20 cm wide. The flowers are small, white or yellowish, and are arranged in compound umbels, with 5-10 rays, each with 5-10 flowers.

The fruit is a schizocarp, composed of 5-10 mericarps, each 5-10 mm long and 3-5 mm wide, with a ribbed, winged or wingless surface.
