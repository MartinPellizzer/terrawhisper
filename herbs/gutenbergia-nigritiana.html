Gutenbergia nigritiana

Gutenbergia nigritiana, commonly known as the Nigritiana bushwillow, is a species of plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is traditionally used in South African folk medicine for its various health benefits, including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and as a purgative.

The plant is reported to possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant therapeutic actions, which are attributed to its bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins.

These constituents are believed to contribute to the plant's ability to reduce inflammation, fight off infections, and protect against oxidative stress.
