What's the taxonomical classification of Trichodesma calycosum?

Trichodesma calycosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is established through its membership in the genus Trichodesma, with its unique designation being the species calycosum.
