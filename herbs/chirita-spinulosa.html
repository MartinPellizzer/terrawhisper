What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita spinulosa?

Chirita spinulosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, falling under the genus Chirita, and is specifically identified by its species name, spinulosa.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The foliage typically presents a dark green hue with a textured, rough surface caused by the specialized trichomes. Its flowers are tubular in shape and emerge from the leaf axils in small clusters.
