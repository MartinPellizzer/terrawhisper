What's the taxonomical classification of Carex scaposa?

Carex scaposa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Poales. This plant is a member of the family Cyperaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Carex, eventually reaching its unique identification at the species level as Carex scaposa.
