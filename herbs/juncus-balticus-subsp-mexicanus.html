mexicanus, also known as Mexican rush, is a subspecies of the Juncus balticus plant, a type of wetland grass that belongs to the Juncaceae family.

Traditionally, the plant has been used for its medicinal properties, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help in treating wounds and reducing pain.
