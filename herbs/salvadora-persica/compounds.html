Arak (Salvadora persica) Compounds

The primary compounds in Arak include tannins, which have astringent properties, and flavonoids, known for their antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids contribute to anti-inflammatory actions, while saponins may support immune function. Alkaloids and terpenoids are also present, offering various therapeutic potentials.

Tannin is a key active compound in Arak (Salvadora persica). It contributes to the plant's astringent properties and has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects. Tannins in Arak are used in traditional herbal medicine for treating oral health issues and skin conditions.
