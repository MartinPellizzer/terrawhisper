What to know about Ammi visnaga before using it medicinally

Ammi visnaga, commonly known as tournesol de mer, is a medicinal herb that has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, respiratory problems, and inflammation.

Its main health benefits include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate pain, and promote digestion. The herb is also rich in therapeutic properties, such as anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and expectorant activities, which contribute to its medicinal value. The bioactive compounds present in Ammi visnaga include coumarins, flavonoids, and volatile oils, which are responsible for its pharmacological effects.

The herb is often prepared as a tea or tincture, and is commonly used to treat conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and digestive disorders, particularly in traditional Middle Eastern and North African medicine.
