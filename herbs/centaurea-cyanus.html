As an annual flower, cornflower is relatively easy to grow and maintain, making it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists.

With a history dating back thousands of years, cornflower has been a staple in traditional medicine and cuisine for centuries, with references to its uses found in ancient texts such as the Greek physician Dioscorides' "De Materia Medica".

Centaurea cyanus, also known as Cornflower, is a delicate annual flower that requires careful attention when it comes to harvesting.
