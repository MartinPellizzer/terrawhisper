Centaurea Cyanus Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Centaurea cyanus, commonly known as the blueweed or bachelor's buttons, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its blue or purple flower heads.

This herb is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and mild sedative properties.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and poultices can be made from Centaurea cyanus to harness its medicinal properties.
