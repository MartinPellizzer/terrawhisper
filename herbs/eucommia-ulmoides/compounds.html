Eucommia (Eucommia ulmoides) Compounds

The primary compounds in Eucommia include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and saponins, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its antioxidant activity, while glycosides and terpenoids support cardiovascular health. Coumarin and tannins offer antimicrobial benefits, and alkaloids may influence nervous system functions. Essential oils and anthocyanins add to its therapeutic profile.
