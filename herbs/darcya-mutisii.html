What is Darcya mutisii?

Darcya mutisii is a plant species in the family Plantaginaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb with erect stems, 10-30 cm tall, bearing opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves, 2-5 cm long, and 1-2 mm wide. The inflorescence is a terminal spike, 5-15 cm long, bearing purple flowers, 6-8 mm long, with a 5-lobed calyx and a 4-lobed corolla. The flowers have four stamens and two styles.

Darcya mutisii is native to the Andes of South America, specifically in Colombia, Ecuador, and Peru.
