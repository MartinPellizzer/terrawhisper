Arctostaphylos Uva-Ursi Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Arctostaphylos uva-ursi, commonly known as bearberry, is a shrub native to North America and parts of Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, particularly in treating urinary tract infections and skin conditions.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include arbutin, tannins, and flavonoids, which have antibacterial and astringent properties.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves can be made from the leaves and berries of this plant.
