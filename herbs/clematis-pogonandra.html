What's the taxonomical classification of Clematis pogonandra?

Clematis pogonandra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Ranunculales. It is a distinctive representative of the family Ranunculaceae, falling under the genus Clematis, and is specifically identified by its species name, pogonandra.

While less documented than other Clematis species, cautious handling is advised to avoid these adverse reactions.
