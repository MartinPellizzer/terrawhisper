What's the taxonomical classification of Rhamnus hainanensis?

Rhamnus hainanenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is ordered under the Rosales and is a member of the family Rhamnaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Rhamnus, with its specific designation being the species hainanenensis.
