10 Precautions To Take When Using Corydalis Ambigua (Tanzen)

Corydalis ambigua has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it under the guidance of a qualified healthcare practitioner.

It's essential to monitor liver enzyme levels, as high doses can cause liver damage. Failing to do so may lead to symptoms like jaundice, abdominal pain, and fatigue.

Additionally, individuals with bleeding disorders or taking anticoagulant medications should avoid using Corydalis ambigua to prevent increased risk of bleeding.
