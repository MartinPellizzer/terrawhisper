Staphylea holocarpa

What's the taxonomical classification of Staphylea holocarpa?

Staphylea holocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Crossosomatales and falls under the family Staphyleaceae. Within this botanical framework, it is categorized under the genus Staphylea, specifically identified by its unique species designation, holocarpa.
