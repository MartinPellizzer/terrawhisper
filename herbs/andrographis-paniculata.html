Andrographis Paniculata: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Andrographis paniculata, commonly known as "Indian echinacea," is a medicinal plant native to India and widely used in traditional Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine.

This herb is renowned for its potent anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting properties, making it a popular remedy for respiratory infections and viral illnesses.

The therapeutic actions of Andrographis paniculata include antiviral, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, supporting overall immune function and reducing inflammation.

Its bioactive constituents, such as andrographolides, flavonoids, and rohitakine, contribute to its broad spectrum of health benefits.
