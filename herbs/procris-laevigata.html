What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Procris laevigata has an erect, herbaceous growth habit characterized by slender, smooth stems and lanceolate leaves with entire margins. The root system is relatively shallow but provides sufficient stability for its upright structure. Seed production occurs through small capsules that contain numerous minute seeds.
