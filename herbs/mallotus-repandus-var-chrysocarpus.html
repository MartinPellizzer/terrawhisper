Mallotus repandus var. chrysocarpus

What's the taxonomical classification of Mallotus repandus var. chrysocarpus?

Mallotus repandus var. chrysocarpus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the order Malpighiales. It is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae and falls under the genus Mallotus, specifically identified as the variety chrysocarpus within the species Mallotus repandus.
