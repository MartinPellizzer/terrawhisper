Ribes diacanthum

What's the taxonomical classification of Ribes diacanthum?

Ribes diacanthum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, this plant is situated in the order Saxifragales. It further falls under the family Grossulariaceae, which is characterized by its specific botanical traits. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Ribes and is specifically known by its scientific species name, Ribes diacanthum.
