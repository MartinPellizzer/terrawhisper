Stachyurus salicifolius

What's the taxonomical classification of Stachyurus salicifolius?

Stachyurus salicifolius belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Crossosomatales. The plant is further categorized into the family Stachyuraceae, within the genus Stachyurus, and is specifically identified by the species name salicifolius.
