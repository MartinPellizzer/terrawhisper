What's the taxonomical classification of Biebersteinia heterostemon?

Biebersteinia heterostemon belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, the plant falls under the order Sapindales and is a member of the family Biebersteiniaceae. Specifically, it is placed within the genus Biebersteinia, with the full biological designation being the species Biebersteinia heterostemon.
