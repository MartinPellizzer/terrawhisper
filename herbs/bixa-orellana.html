Bixa Orellana Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Herbal preparations like infusions, decoctions, and topical applications can be made from the seeds to harness its health benefits.

The leaf of Bixa orellana is also utilized, often prepared as a tea or poultice, to treat ailments such as fever and respiratory infections.
