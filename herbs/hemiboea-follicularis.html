Hemiboea follicularis

What's the taxonomical classification of Hemiboea follicularis?

Hemiboea follicularis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, falling within the genus Hemiboea, and is specifically identified by its species name, follicularis.

      What are the herbal preparations of Hemiboea follicularis?

      Hemiboea follicularis is prepared primarily as a decoction made by boiling the plant's leaves and stems in water for medicinal use. This liquid extract is often consumed orally to address various traditional ailments.
