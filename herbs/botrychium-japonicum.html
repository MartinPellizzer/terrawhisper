What's the taxonomical classification of Botrychium japonicum?

Botrychium japonicum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Ophioglossidae. Following its taxonomic descent, it falls under the order Ophioglossales and is a member of the family Ophioglossaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Botrychium, with its specific designation being the species japonicum.
