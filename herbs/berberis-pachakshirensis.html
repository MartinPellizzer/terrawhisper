Berberis pachakshirensis

Berberis pachakshirensis, a species of barberry shrub native to the Himalayan region, is a type of thorny deciduous shrub belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its high berberine content, which has been used to treat various health conditions.

The health benefits of Berberis pachakshirensis include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties, which have been traditionally used to treat digestive issues, skin problems, and respiratory infections.
