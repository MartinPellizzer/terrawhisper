Trollius pumilus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Trollius, with its specific designation being the species pumilus.
