White Lily (Lilium candidum) Actions

White Lily exhibits anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, helping reduce inflammation and ease muscle spasms. It acts as a tonic and stimulant, supporting overall vitality and energy. Additionally, it has diuretic and astringent effects, aiding in fluid balance and tissue tightening. Its sedative and carminative qualities promote calmness and relieve gas.
