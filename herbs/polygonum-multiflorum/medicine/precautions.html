1. Follow recommended storage conditions

When using Polygonum multiflorum medicinally, it's important to take under medical supervision because of its potential interactions with prescription medications and the risk of adverse reactions.

Fo-ti may lower blood sugar levels, which can be problematic for individuals with diabetes, and may also interact with anticoagulant medications, increasing the risk of bleeding complications.

Additionally, fo-ti's effects on the liver and kidneys are not fully understood, making it crucial to work with a healthcare professional to ensure safe and effective treatment.

When using Polygonum multiflorum medicinally, it's important to avoid interaction with MAOIs.
