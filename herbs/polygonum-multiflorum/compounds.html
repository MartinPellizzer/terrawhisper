Chinese Knotweed (Polygonum multiflorum) Compounds

The primary compounds in Chinese Knotweed include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while glycosides and saponins support various therapeutic effects. Coumarin and anthocyanin add to its pharmacological profile, and terpenoids, essential oils, and alkaloids further enhance its medicinal value.
