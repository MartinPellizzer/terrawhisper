10 Medicinal Constituents Of Sanguisorba Canadensis (Burnweed)

Sanguisorba canadensis has active constituents such as salicylic acid, flavonoids, and tannins.

These compounds contribute to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. Studies suggest that the constituents of Sanguisorba canadensis can help alleviate digestive issues, soothe skin irritations, and reduce fever.

For example, the salicylic acid content can aid in reducing pain and inflammation in patients with arthritis, while the flavonoids may help to improve cardiovascular health by promoting blood flow and reducing blood pressure.
