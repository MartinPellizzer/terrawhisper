10 Medicinal Preparations Of Datura Stramonium

Datura stramonium has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and ointments.

These preparations are traditionally used to treat conditions like anxiety, insomnia, and pain, as well as to induce sweating in cases of fever. However, due to its toxic nature, its use is highly regulated and requires careful administration under the guidance of a qualified practitioner.

Its antispasmodic and analgesic properties also make it useful for treating muscle spasms and inflammation.
