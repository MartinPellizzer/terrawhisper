Astragalus carinatus

Astragalus carinatus, also known as the Hooked Milkvetch, is a plant species within the legume family that is native to the Western United States.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of Astragalus carinatus include its ability to boost the immune system, reduce stress and fatigue, and alleviate symptoms of respiratory and gastrointestinal disorders.

Astragalus carinatus is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or capsule, and is also used in combination with other herbs in various herbal preparations.
