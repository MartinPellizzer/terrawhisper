Arctium alpinum

Arctium alpinum, commonly known as Alpine Burdock or Mountain Burdock, is a plant species within the thistle family (Asteraceae) that is native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It has been traditionally used to support digestive health, alleviate inflammation, and boost the immune system, showcasing its potential as a natural remedy.

The therapeutic actions of Arctium alpinum include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which can be attributed to its bioactive constituents such as phenolic acids, flavonoids, and saponins.
