10 Possible Side Effects Of Borago Officinalis (Borage)

Borago officinalis has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, digestive issues, and interactions with medications.

The causes of these side effects include excessive consumption, individual sensitivities, and combination with other herbs or supplements.

For instance, an allergic reaction can lead to anaphylaxis, which can be fatal, while digestive issues can cause nausea, diarrhea, and stomach cramps, interfering with daily activities and work productivity.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Borago officinalis if used imporperly.

These compounds can trigger an immune response, leading to symptoms such as hives, itching, and swelling in some individuals. The allergenic potential is thought to be increased by the presence of pollen and other proteins in the plant, which can exacerbate allergic reactions.
