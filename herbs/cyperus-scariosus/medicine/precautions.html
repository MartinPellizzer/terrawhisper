10 Precautions To Take When Using Cyperus Scariosus (Rice Grass)

Cyperus scariosus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as proper dosing and caution with its interaction with other medications.

It's essential to follow these precautions to avoid adverse effects, such as nausea, dizziness, and gastrointestinal issues. For example, taking high doses or using it concurrently with blood thinners may lead to bleeding complications.

Additionally, pregnant women should consult a healthcare professional before using Cyperus scariosus.
