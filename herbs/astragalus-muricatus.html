Astragalus muricatus

Astragalus muricatus, also known as the spiny milkvetch, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the legume family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects.

The spiny milkvetch is believed to have various health benefits, such as reducing stress and fatigue, improving immune function, and alleviating symptoms of respiratory infections like bronchitis and pneumonia.

Astragalus muricatus is typically prepared as a herbal supplement in capsule or tea form, and can also be found in combination with other herbs in traditional Chinese medicine formulas.
