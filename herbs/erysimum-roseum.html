Erysimum roseum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Brassicales and is a member of the family Brassicaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its genus, Erysimum, and its specific species designation, roseum.
