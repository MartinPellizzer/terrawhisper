Haloragis Salsoloides

Haloragis salsoloides, commonly known as saltbush or sea barley, has been traditionally used in medicinal practices for its potential health benefits.

This plant contains various bioactive compounds such as flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It has been employed in herbal remedies to treat respiratory conditions, digestive disorders, and skin ailments due to its soothing and healing effects. Recent studies suggest that Haloragis salsoloides may also support immune function and help in managing oxidative stress.

However, further research is needed to fully understand its therapeutic potential and ensure safe usage in modern medicine.
