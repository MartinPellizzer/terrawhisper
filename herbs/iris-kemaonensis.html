What's the taxonomical classification of Iris kemaonensis?

Iris kemaonensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the family Iridaeceae and is situated within the genus Iris, ultimately identified by its specific species name, kemaonensis.
