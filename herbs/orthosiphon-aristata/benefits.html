1. Treats urinary tract

Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs) are bacterial infections that affect any part of the urinary system, including the bladder, kidneys, ureters, and urethra.

These infections can cause symptoms such as painful urination, frequent urges to urinate, and lower abdominal discomfort.

UTIs are commonly caused by bacteria, most often Escherichia coli, entering the urinary tract through the urethra.

Women are more prone to UTIs due to their shorter urethra, and other risk factors include sexual activity, catheter use, and a weakened immune system.

The orthosiphon aristata plant is believed to support urinary health by reducing inflammation and promoting the elimination of toxins, making it a natural remedy for treating urinary tract issues.
