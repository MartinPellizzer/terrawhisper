10 Health Benefits Of Orthosiphon Aristata (Java Tea)

Orthosiphon aristata has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving kidney function.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids and triterpenoids, which have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. By consuming java tea, individuals with kidney stones or urinary tract infections can experience relief from symptoms and improved overall well-being.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate joint pain and swelling, allowing people to enjoy daily activities without discomfort.
