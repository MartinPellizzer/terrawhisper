These constituents possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and diuretic properties, contributing to the plant's medicinal value. Java tea is traditionally used to treat urinary tract infections and reduce inflammation.

The antioxidant properties of its constituents can also improve heart health and prevent cell damage, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases, thereby enhancing the overall quality of life.
