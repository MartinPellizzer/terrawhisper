10 Precautions To Take When Using Orthosiphon Aristata (Java Tea)

Prolonged use can lead to decreased kidney function, liver damage, and increased risk of bleeding. For example, ignoring this precaution may result in hematuria, kidney stones, and even liver failure.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Java tea for medicinal purposes.
