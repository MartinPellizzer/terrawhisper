Parsley (Petroselinum crispum) Actions

Parsley exhibits several therapeutic actions including carminative, which aids in expelling gas from the digestive tract, and anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling. It also acts as a diuretic, promoting urine production, and has antispasmodic effects that relieve muscle spasms. Additionally, it functions as a tonic, stimulating the body's functions and acting as a sedative to calm the nervous system.
