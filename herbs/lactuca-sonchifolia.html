Lactuca sonchifolia

Lactuca sonchifolia, commonly known as Chinese lettuce or small-spotted bitter lettuce, is a plant species within the dandelion-like genus Lactuca, which is native to Asia.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties and health benefits, including reducing inflammation and improving liver function, which are attributed to its therapeutic actions as a hepatoprotective and anti-inflammatory agent.

The bioactive constituents of Lactuca sonchifolia include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and sesquiterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Regular consumption of Lactuca sonchifolia has been associated with improved digestion and immune system function, as well as potential anti-cancer and antioxidant effects.
