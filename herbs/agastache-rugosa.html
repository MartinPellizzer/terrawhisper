Agastache Rugosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Agastache rugosa, commonly known as honey sage, is a perennial herb native to North America, prized for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.

This herb is known for its ability to support respiratory health, aid digestion, and promote oral hygiene due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects.

The bioactive constituents in Agastache rugosa include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and salves can be made from this herb to harness its beneficial properties.
