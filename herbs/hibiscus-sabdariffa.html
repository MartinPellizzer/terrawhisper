Hibiscus Sabdariffa

Possible Side Effects of hibiscus sabdariffa

Hibiscus sabdariffa can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high acidity and potential interaction with certain medications.

Additionally, excessive consumption may lead to allergic reactions, such as skin rashes, itching, and difficulty breathing, especially in individuals with allergies to certain plant-based compounds.

Hibiscus sabdariffa may also interact with blood thinners, such as warfarin, and decrease their effectiveness, increasing the risk of blood clots and bleeding.

Furthermore, its high vitamin C content may cause kidney stones in people with pre-existing kidney issues.
