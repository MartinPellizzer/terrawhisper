Hibiscus sabdariffa, commonly known as roselle, is a medicinal herb that has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including hypertension, hyperlipidemia, and type 2 diabetes.

In traditional medicine, Hibiscus sabdariffa is often used to make a tea or infused in water, which is commonly consumed for its medicinal properties and is also used to make a popular herbal drink called sorrel.
