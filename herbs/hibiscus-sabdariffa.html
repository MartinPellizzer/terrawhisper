Side Effects of hibiscus sabdariffa

Hibiscus sabdariffa causes stomach upset due to its high acidity and potential irritant properties, which can stimulate the digestive system and lead to discomfort.

It may also lead to nausea as the herb can affect the gastrointestinal tract, causing a feeling of queasiness or an urge to vomit. Individuals with allergies may experience triggers such as skin rashes or even more severe allergic reactions, including difficulty breathing. The herb can trigger heartburn by relaxing the lower esophageal sphincter, allowing stomach acid to reflux into the esophagus.

In addition, Hibiscus sabdariffa may result in headache, dizziness, and fatigue, possibly due to its impact on blood pressure or dehydration. It can also cause dry mouth, as it may alter saliva production or increase fluid loss. While these side effects are generally mild, they can be more pronounced in sensitive individuals or when consumed in large quantities.

It is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before incorporating Hibiscus sabdariffa into one's routine, especially for those with pre-existing health conditions or on medication.
