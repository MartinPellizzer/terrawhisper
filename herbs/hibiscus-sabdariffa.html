Possible Side Effects of hibiscus sabdariffa

Hibiscus sabdariffa can have side effects if used improperly, such as allergic reactions, which may manifest as hives, itching, or difficulty breathing.

Additionally, excessive consumption of hibiscus tea or supplements can lead to gastrointestinal issues, including stomach upset, diarrhea, or nausea.

In rare cases, hibiscus sabdariffa may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and decrease their effectiveness or increase the risk of bleeding.

Furthermore, individuals with certain health conditions, like kidney or liver disease, may need to avoid using hibiscus sabdariffa altogether or consult with a healthcare professional before doing so.
