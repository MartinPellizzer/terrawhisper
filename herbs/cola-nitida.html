Cola Nitida: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Cola nitida, commonly known as African cola, is a shrub native to tropical Africa, valued for its seeds and leaves.

This herb is traditionally used in African medicine for its stimulating and tonic properties.

The health benefits of Cola nitida include enhancing energy levels, improving mental alertness, and supporting cardiovascular health.

Its therapeutic actions involve acting as a mild stimulant, diuretic, and antioxidant, while its bioactive constituents include caffeine, theobromine, and various alkaloids.
