Kola Nut (Cola nitida)

Kola Nut (Cola nitida) is a member of the Sterculiaceae family, native to West Africa, Central Africa, and Southeast Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, seeds, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, tonic, and diuretic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
