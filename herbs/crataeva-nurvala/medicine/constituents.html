10 Medicinal Constituents Of Crataeva Nurvala (Star Aniseed)

Crataeva nurvala has active constituents such as alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenes.

These constituents possess medicinal properties that make it effective in treating various ailments. The plant's anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve digestion, and protect against chronic diseases like cancer. Its antiseptic and antibacterial properties can also be used to treat wounds and skin infections, promoting faster healing and reducing the risk of infection.

Overall, Crataeva nurvala has the potential to significantly improve the quality of life for many individuals.

1. Beta-amyrin

Crataeva nurvala beta-amyrin is a triterpenoid saponin extracted from its fruit.

Beta-amyrin has been found to possess anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, making it a potential treatment option for various diseases.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential benefits on cardiovascular health and as an anticancer agent.
