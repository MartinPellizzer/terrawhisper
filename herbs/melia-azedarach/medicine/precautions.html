2. Consult doctor before giving birth

When using Melia azedarach medicinally, it's important to consult a doctor before giving birth.

Furthermore, some studies have shown that Melia azedarach may increase the risk of preterm labor, miscarriage, and uterine contractions, making it crucial to seek medical guidance to ensure a safe and healthy pregnancy.

9. Perform regular blood tests

When using Melia azedarach medicinally, it's important to perform regular blood tests due to the potential risk of toxicity and interactions with other medications.

10. Pregnant women should avoid use

When using Melia azedarach medicinally, it's important to note that pregnant women should avoid its use due to the risk of adverse effects on fetal development.

The plant contains toxic compounds that may cross the placenta and harm the growing fetus, potentially leading to birth defects or even miscarriage.

Therefore, it is crucial for pregnant women to exercise caution and consult with a healthcare provider before using Melia azedarach medicinally, as the potential risks outweigh any potential benefits.
