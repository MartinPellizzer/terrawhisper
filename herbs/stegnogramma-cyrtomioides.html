What's the taxonomical classification of Stegnogramma cyrtomioides?

Stegnogramma cyrtomioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This fern is a representative of the family Aspleniaceae, situated within the genus Stegnogramma, and is specifically identified by its species name, cyrtomioides.
