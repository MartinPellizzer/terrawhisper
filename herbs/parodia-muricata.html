What is Parodia muricata?

Parodia muricata is a species of cactus that belongs to the family Cactaceae.

It is a small to medium-sized cactus with globose to short cylindrical stems, typically 5-10 cm in height and 6-12 cm in diameter. The stems are covered with short, sharp spines and are often brightly colored, with a green or yellowish-green epidermis. The flowers are funnel-shaped and yellow or reddish-purple in color, 6-7 cm in diameter, and arise from the top of the plant.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are 2-3 cm in length and contain seeds.
