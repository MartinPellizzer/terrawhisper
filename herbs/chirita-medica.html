What's the taxonomical classification of Chirita medica?

Chirita medica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Lamiales. The plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, falling under the genus Chirita, and is specifically identified by the species name medica.
