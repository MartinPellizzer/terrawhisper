Callerya bonatiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Fabales. As a member of the family Fabaceae, it is categorized under the genus Callerya, with its specific identification being the species bonatiana.
