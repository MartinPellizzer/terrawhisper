What's the taxonomical classification of Justicia leptostachya?

Justicia leptostachya belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further categorized under the order Lamiales and falls within the family Acanthaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Justicia, with its unique designation being the species leptostachyia.
