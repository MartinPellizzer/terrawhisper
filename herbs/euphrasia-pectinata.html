What's the taxonomical classification of Euphrasia pectinata?

Euphrasia pectinata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Lamiales. It is a constituent of the family Orobanchaceae, which places it within the genus Euphrasia, specifically identified by the species name pectinata.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Euphrasia pectinata has an erect, annual habit characterized by oppositely arranged, ovate to elliptical leaves that feature distinctively serrated or pectinate margins. These blossoms are borne in terminal racemes that emerge from the upper leaf axils. The plant also produces small, ovoid seed capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds for reproduction.
