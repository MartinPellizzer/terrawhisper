Boldea boldus

Boldea boldus, also known as Boldo, is a shrub-like plant native to South America, particularly in the Andes region of Chile and Argentina.

The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties, which include its ability to stimulate digestion, relieve gas and bloating, and act as a natural diuretic.

The health benefits of Boldo include reducing inflammation, improving liver function, and supporting the immune system, making it a popular herbal remedy for various ailments.

Boldea boldus can be prepared as a herbal tea, capsule, or tincture, with the tea being the most traditional and widely used form, allowing for the plant's medicinal properties to be easily absorbed by the body.
