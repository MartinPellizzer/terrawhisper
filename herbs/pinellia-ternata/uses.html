Pinellia (Pinellia ternata) Uses

Pinellia ternata is used to treat gastritis, gastric ulcers, and gastrointestinal disorders. It helps alleviate symptoms like vomiting, dyspepsia, and gastroesophageal reflux disease. It is also effective for gastric disturbances, hiccups, and hemorrhoids by reducing inflammation and soothing digestive tract irritation.

Pinellia (Pinellia ternata) Monograph

1. Gastritis

Pinellia ternata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat gastritis by reducing stomach inflammation and soothing digestive discomfort. It helps alleviate symptoms like nausea, vomiting, and excessive acid production. Its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties make it effective for managing chronic gastritis. It is often combined with other herbs in formulas to enhance its therapeutic effects.

2. Hemorrhoids

Pinellia ternata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat hemorrhoids due to its properties in clearing heat, detoxifying, and reducing swelling. It is often combined with other herbs to alleviate symptoms such as pain and inflammation associated with hemorrhoidal conditions.

3. Vomiting

Pinellia ternata is used to treat vomiting, particularly in cases of nausea and stomach discomfort. It helps to reduce excessive phlegm and soothe the digestive system. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in herbal formulations. This application is based on traditional Chinese medicine principles.

4. Gastrointestinal disorders

Pinellia ternata is used to treat gastrointestinal disorders such as nausea, vomiting, and indigestion. It helps regulate stomach function and reduce excessive mucus production. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in easing digestive discomfort. Its properties make it a key herb in traditional formulations for digestive health.

5. Gastric disturbance

Pinellia ternata is used to treat gastric disturbances such as nausea, vomiting, and epigastric pain. It soothes the stomach by reducing excessive dampness and heat, commonly associated with these conditions. It is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness in digestive disorders.

6. Gastroesophageal reflux

Pinellia ternata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate symptoms of gastroesophageal reflux by reducing excess stomach acid and strengthening the lower esophageal sphincter. It is often combined with other herbs to soothe irritation and improve digestion in patients with reflux-related discomfort.

7. Dyspepsia

Pinellia ternata is used to treat dyspepsia by soothing the stomach and reducing nausea. It helps alleviate symptoms like bloating, indigestion, and a feeling of fullness. Often combined with other herbs, it promotes digestion and relieves gastrointestinal discomfort. Its efficacy makes it a key herb in traditional formulas for digestive disorders.

8. Gastric ulcers

Pinellia ternata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat gastric ulcers due to its anti-inflammatory and mucosal protective properties. It helps reduce stomach acid and promotes healing of the gastrointestinal lining, often combined with other herbs in formulations designed for digestive disorders. Its efficacy is supported by both historical use and modern pharmacological studies.

9. Gastroesophageal reflux disease

Pinellia ternata is used in traditional Chinese medicine to alleviate symptoms of gastroesophageal reflux disease. It helps reduce excess stomach acid and soothe inflammation in the esophagus. Often combined with other herbs, it supports digestion and relieves heartburn and nausea associated with reflux.

10. Hiccups

Pinellia ternata is used to treat hiccups due to its ability to descend stomach qi and resolve phlegm. It is often combined with other herbs to harmonize the middle jiao and relieve epigastric distension. This makes it effective for persistent hiccups caused by dampness and stagnation. It is typically prepared as a decoction or powder.
