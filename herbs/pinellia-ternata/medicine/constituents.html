10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pinellia Ternata (Ban Xia)

Pinellia ternata has active constituents such as pinellin, pinelline, and tuberostemonine, which exhibit expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties.

These properties enable ban xia to effectively treat respiratory disorders like bronchitis and asthma by loosening mucus and reducing inflammation.

Furthermore, the constituents' analgesic and anxiolytic effects can help alleviate chronic coughing and anxiety-related sleep disturbances, thereby improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from respiratory conditions.
