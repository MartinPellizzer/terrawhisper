What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium ellisii?

Cremanthodium ellisii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, which places it among the composite flowering plants. Within this group, it is identified by the genus Cremanthodium, specifically reaching its taxonomic designation at the species level as Cremanthodium ellisii.
