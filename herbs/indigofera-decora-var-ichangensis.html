Indigofera decora var. ichangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Indigofera decora var. ichangensis?

Indigofera decora var. ichangensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. It is classified under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Fabales. Within the family Fabaceae, it is assigned to the genus Indigofera, specifically identified as the variety ichangensis within the species Indigofera decora.
