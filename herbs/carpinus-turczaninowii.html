Carpinus turczaninowii

What's the taxonomical classification of Carpinus turczaninowii?

Carpinus turczaninowii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical structure, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls within the order Fagales. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Betulaceae, which contains the genus Carpinus. Ultimately, the specific taxonomic designation for this organism is the species Carpinus turczaninowii.
