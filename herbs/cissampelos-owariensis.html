Cissampelos owariensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Cissampelos owariensis?

Cissampelos owariensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this classification, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Ranunculales and the family Menispermaceae. Finally, the specimen is identified by its specific genus, Cissampelos, and its species designation, owariensis.
