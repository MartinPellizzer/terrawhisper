Ranunculus tureholmiensis

Ranunculus tureholmiensis is a rare and endemic species of flowering plant in the buttercup family, Ranunculaceae, specifically found in the mountainous regions of China.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, as well as its ability to relieve pain and reduce fever.

The health benefits of Ranunculus tureholmiensis include its potential to lower blood pressure, improve cardiovascular health, and provide antioxidant protection.

In terms of herbal preparations, the plant is often used to make teas, infusions, and decoctions, although its use should be approached with caution due to its potential toxicity and interactions with other medications.
