What's the taxonomical classification of Actinidia glaucocallosa?

Actinidia glaucocallosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the taxonomic order Ericales and is a member of the family Actinidiaceae. Specifically, it is situated within the genus Actinidia, where it is identified by its unique species designation, glaucocallosa.
