What's the taxonomical classification of Sabia emarginata?

Sabia emarginata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Proteales and the family Sabiaceae. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Sabia, and its specific identity is defined by the species emarginata.
