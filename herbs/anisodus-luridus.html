Anisodus luridus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These flowers eventually develop into small, globose capsules containing numerous tiny seeds. The overall structure of the plant is relatively delicate and sprawling in its natural habitat.

Anisodamine is often studied for its ability to improve microcirculation and treat various types of shock. Additionally, the presence of apiigenin provides the plant with unique antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
