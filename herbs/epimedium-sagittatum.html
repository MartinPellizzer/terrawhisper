Fairy Bells (Epimedium sagittatum)

Fairy Bells (Epimedium sagittatum) is a member of the Berberidaceae family, native to China, Korea, and Japan. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and leaves have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its tonic, stimulant, and anti-inflammatory actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and korean traditional medicine.
