What's the taxonomical classification of Carex brunnea?

Carex brunnea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Poales and into the family Cyperaceae. Finally, the plant is identified within the genus Carex, reaching its specific designation at the species level as Carex brunnea.
