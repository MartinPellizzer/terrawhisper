Neocinnamomum mekongense

What's the taxonomical classification of Neocinnamomum mekongense?

Neocinnamomum mekongense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Laurales. This plant is a member of the family Lauraceae, falling under the specific genus Neocinnamomum, and is identified by its species name, mekongense.

Local practitioners often apply its components topically to alleviate irritations or infections on the skin. These medicinal applications highlight the plant's importance in traditional regional healing practices.
