Everlasting Flower (Helichrysum Engelianum)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Helichrysum engelianum.

Everlasting flower, scientifically known as Helichrysum engelianum, is a medicinal herb native to South Africa, revered for its adaptogenic properties and unique therapeutic benefits. This aromatic plant is often used in traditional medicine for its ability to support emotional resilience, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being. Its primary benefits include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and calming effects, making it a valuable remedy for anxiety, digestive issues, and respiratory ailments. Historically, it has been utilized by indigenous communities in South Africa for its soothing properties, often brewed into teas or used in poultices. In modern wellness practices, it is popular as a dietary supplement and essential oil, with its distinctive honey-like aroma and rare compound, rutin, contributing to its reputation as a natural remedy with enduring appeal.
