Medicinal Parts

Aloe ferox leaf is the primary medicinal part of the plant, known for its rich content of polysaccharides, enzymes, and anti-inflammatory compounds.

The leaf contains a gel-like substance within its layers, which is commonly used in traditional medicine for its soothing and healing properties.

The main herbal preparations made from the leaf include aloe vera gel, which is extracted by carefully removing the inner gel from the leaf, and aloe juice, which is obtained by processing the entire leaf.

These preparations are widely used for skin care, digestive support, and as a natural remedy for various inflammatory conditions.
