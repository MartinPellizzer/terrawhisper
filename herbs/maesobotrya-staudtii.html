What's the taxonomical classification of Maesobotrya staudtii?

Maesobotrya staudtii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic descent through the order Malpighiales, the plant is placed within the family Phyllanthaceae. Ultimately, it is defined by its specific classification under the genus Maesobotrya, with the unique designation of the species staudtii.
