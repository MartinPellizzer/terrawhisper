Cicuta Fern (Polypodium Cicutarium)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Polypodium cicutarium.

Cicuta fern, scientifically known as Polypodium cicutarium, is a tropical medicinal herb commonly used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. This fern-like plant, also called the water fern or the sword fern, is valued for its adaptogenic properties and is often used to support energy, immunity, and respiratory health. Historically, it has been employed in Ayurvedic and Chinese herbal practices to treat conditions such as asthma, coughs, and digestive ailments. In modern wellness, it is increasingly used as a natural remedy for respiratory support and as a dietary supplement to enhance vitality. One unique feature of Cicuta fern is its distinctive, slightly bitter flavor and the presence of bioactive compounds like flavonoids, which contribute to its traditional and contemporary medicinal appeal.
