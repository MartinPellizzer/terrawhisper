Narcissus micranthus

Narcissus micranthus, also known as the small-flowered daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

This plant is traditionally used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include antibacterial and anti-inflammatory activities.

The health benefits of Narcissus micranthus include its potential to treat various conditions such as bronchitis, coughs, and respiratory issues due to its expectorant properties.

The plant can be prepared into herbal remedies, including infusions and decoctions, which are often used to treat these conditions in traditional folk medicine.
