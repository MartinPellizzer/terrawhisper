What is Erodium saxatile?

Erodium saxatile is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It has a woody base with slender, branched stems that grow up to 60 cm in height, bearing alternate, pinnate leaves with 5-9 pairs of leaflets, each leaflet measuring 2-5 cm in length. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in clusters, each flower measuring 1-2 cm in diameter, with 5 petals and 10 stamens. The flowers are followed by seed pods that contain numerous small seeds.

Erodium saxatile is native to the Mediterranean region and is found in dry, rocky areas, often in full sun.
