10 Possible Side Effects Of Rumex Acetosa (Sorrel)

Rumex acetosa has some side effects when used improperly, such as digestive issues, nausea, and vomiting.

These side effects can be caused by consuming excessive amounts, high doses, or prolonged use of the herb.

For instance, overconsumption of sorrel can lead to an imbalance of electrolytes in the body, causing muscle weakness, fatigue, and dizziness, making everyday activities a challenge.
