10 Medicinal Constituents Of Rumex Acetosa (Sorrel)

Rumex acetosa has active constituents such as vitamins A, C, and E, as well as flavonoids, anthraquinones, and fatty acids.

These compounds contribute to the plant's medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities. The antioxidants in sorrel can help protect against cell damage and oxidative stress, while its antimicrobial properties may aid in wound healing and fighting infections.

Additionally, sorrel's flavonoids and anthraquinones have been shown to reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer.
