Yulania amoena belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Magnoliales and the family Magnoliaceae, eventually leading to its specific placement within the genus Yulania. Ultimately, the plant is identified by its unique species designation, amoena.
