Koeleria californica

Koeleria californica, also known as California June Grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western regions of North America.

This plant is rich in medicinal properties, containing flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its various health benefits.

The health benefits of Koeleria californica include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it useful for treating wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues.

Koeleria californica can be prepared as a herbal tea, infusion, or tincture, which are commonly used in traditional medicine to promote overall well-being and alleviate various health concerns.
