Eranthemum pulchellum

What's the taxonomical classification of Eranthemum pulchellum?

Eranthemun pulchellum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Lamiales. As a member of the family Acanthaceae, this plant is further categorized into the genus Eranthemum, with the specific identifier of the species pulchellum.
