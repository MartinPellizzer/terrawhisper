Oberonia ensiformis

What's the taxonomical classification of Oberonia ensiformis?

Oberonia ensiformis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the family Orchidaceae, falling within the genus Oberonia, and is specifically identified by the species name ensiformis.
