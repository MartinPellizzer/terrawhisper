Tournefortia sibirica var. angustior

What's the taxonomical classification of Tournefortia sibirica var. angustior?

Tournefortia sibirica var. angustior belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, its evolutionary lineage continues through the order Boraginales and the family Boraginaceae. Within this family, it is situated under the genus Tournefortia, specifically identified as the variety angustior of the species Tournefortia sibirica.
