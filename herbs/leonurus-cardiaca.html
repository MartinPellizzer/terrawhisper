Possible Side Effects of leonurus cardiaca

Leonurus cardiaca can have side effects if used improperly, such as dizziness, nausea, and stomach upset due to its high concentration of iridoid glycosides and volatile oils.

Additionally, Leonurus cardiaca may cause allergic reactions, including skin rashes and itching, in some individuals, especially when taken in large doses or combined with other herbs.

It is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Leonurus cardiaca to minimize the risk of adverse effects and ensure safe and effective use.
