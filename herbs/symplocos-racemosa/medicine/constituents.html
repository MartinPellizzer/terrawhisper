10 Medicinal Constituents Of Symplocos Racemosa (Sweetleaf)

Symplocos racemosa has active constituents such as flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These constituents impart anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. These properties can help alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and boost immunity. For instance, flavonoids like quercetin may help manage symptoms of arthritis, while alkaloids may exhibit antiviral activities.

Overall, these constituents have the potential to improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from various health conditions.

Additionally, it may help lower blood sugar levels and improve insulin sensitivity.
