10 Possible Side Effects Of Symplocos Racemosa (Sweetleaf)

These side effects may be caused by consuming excessive amounts or taking it in combination with certain medications.

In severe cases, these reactions can worsen a person's quality of life by causing discomfort, fatigue, and anxiety, potentially affecting their daily work and social activities.
