Microstegium ciliatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Poales and the family Poaceae, which ultimately places it within the genus Microstegium. As the specific identifier, the species name is ciliatum.
