Amur Corktree (Phellodendron amurense)

Amur Corktree (Phellodendron amurense) is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to Eastern Asia, China, and Russia. Traditionally, its bark, roots, and resin have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, korean traditional medicine, and japanese kampo medicine.
