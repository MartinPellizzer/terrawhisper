Phellodendroon amurensse is prepared primarily as a decoction made by boiling the dried bark or roots in water to extract its medicinal compounds. Phellodendroon amurense can cause skin irritation, redness, and itching upon contact with its foliage or stems. It is generally advised to wear protective clothing when handling this species to avoid these physical reactions.
