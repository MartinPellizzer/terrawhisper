What's the taxonomical classification of Stephania dolichopoda?

Stephania dolichopoda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified into the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant follows the taxonomic order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Menispermaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological classification, it is identified by the genus Stephania and the species dolichopoda.
