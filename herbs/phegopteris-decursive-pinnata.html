Phegopteris decursive-pinnata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Polypodiales and the family Aspleniaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Phegopteris, and its unique species designation, decurse-pinnata.

      Phegopteris decursive-pinnata can cause skin irritation, allergic dermatitis, or gastrointestinal distress if ingested due to its chemical composition. It is generally advised to handle this species with caution to avoid these adverse reactions.
