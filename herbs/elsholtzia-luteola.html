Finally, its taxonomic position is completed within the genus Elsholtzia, specifically as the species luteola.

The bark transitions from a smooth, grayish surface in younger specimens to a deeply fissured and furrowed texture as the tree matures. During the autumn season, the foliage undergoes a distinct color change to vibrant shades of yellow before falling.
