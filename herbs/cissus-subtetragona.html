What's the taxonomical classification of Cissus subtetragona?

Cissus subtetragona belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Cissus and is specifically designated as the species subtetragona.
