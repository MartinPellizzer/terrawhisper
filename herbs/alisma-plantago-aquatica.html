Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica)

Water Plantain (Alisma plantago-aquatica) is a member of the Alismataceae family, native to Eastern Asia, North America, and Europe. Traditionally, its rhizomes, roots, and leaves have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, japanese kampo medicine, and european herbal medicine.
