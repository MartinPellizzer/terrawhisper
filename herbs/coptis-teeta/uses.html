Coptis Teeta (Coptis teeta) Uses

Coptis teeta is used to treat gastrointestinal infections, gastritis, and peptic ulcers due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce inflammation in the stomach and intestines, making it effective for conditions like gastric ulcers and inflammatory bowel disease. Its active compounds also aid in managing diarrhea and gastroenteritis.

Coptis Teeta (Coptis teeta) Monograph

1. Gastrointestinal infection

Coptis teeta is effective against gastrointestinal infections due to its active alkaloids, such as berberine, which possess strong antimicrobial properties. It helps combat pathogens like E. coli and Salmonella, reducing inflammation and promoting healing in the digestive tract. Its use supports natural treatment for bacterial infections and digestive disorders.

2. Gastritis

Coptis teeta is used to treat gastritis due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps reduce stomach inflammation and combat harmful bacteria, promoting healing. It is often prepared as a decoction or tincture to soothe digestive discomfort and restore balance to the stomach lining.

3. Peptic ulcer

Coptis teeta is used in traditional medicine to treat peptic ulcers due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps neutralize harmful bacteria like H. pylori, reduces stomach acid, and promotes healing of the gastrointestinal lining. Its active compounds, such as berberine, support mucosal protection and tissue repair.

4. Diarrhea

Coptis teeta is used to treat diarrhea due to its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce intestinal inflammation and combat harmful bacteria. Traditionally, it is prepared as a decoction or powder to alleviate symptoms quickly. Its effectiveness makes it a valuable remedy in herbal medicine for digestive issues.

5. Gastrointestinal inflammation

Coptis teeta is used to treat gastrointestinal inflammation due to its high berberine content, which exhibits antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce inflammation in the stomach and intestines, making it effective for conditions like gastritis and inflammatory bowel disease. Its natural compounds support digestive health and may alleviate symptoms associated with gut disorders.

6. Gastric ulcer

Coptis teeta is used traditionally to treat gastric ulcers due to its alkaloid content, particularly berberine, which has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce stomach acid and protect the gastric lining, promoting ulcer healing. Its use is supported by both historical practice and modern pharmacological research.

7. Inflammatory bowel disease

Coptis teeta contains berberine, which exhibits strong anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It has shown potential in reducing gut inflammation and modulating gut microbiota, making it a promising candidate for managing inflammatory bowel disease. Clinical studies suggest it may help alleviate symptoms and support intestinal healing.

8. Gastroenteritis

Coptis teeta is used to treat gastroenteritis due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate symptoms like diarrhea and abdominal pain by reducing intestinal inflammation and inhibiting pathogenic bacteria. Its alkaloid content, particularly berberine, supports gut health and aids in recovery from digestive infections.
