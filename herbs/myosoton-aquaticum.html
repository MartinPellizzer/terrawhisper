Myosoton aquaticum

What's the taxonomical classification of Myosoton aquaticum?

Myosoton aquaticum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically situated within the genus Myosoton, with the specific species designation being aquaticum.
