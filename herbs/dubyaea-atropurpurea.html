What's the taxonomical classification of Dubyaea atropurpurea?

Dubyaea atropurpurea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Asterales and the family Asteraceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Dubyaea and the specific epithet atropurpurea, completing its formal scientific classification.
