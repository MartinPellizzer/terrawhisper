Cenocentrum tonkinense belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further follows the order Malvales and is a member of the family Malvaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by the genus Cenocentrum and the species tonkinense.
