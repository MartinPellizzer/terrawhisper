Canscora andrographioides

What's the taxonomical classification of Canscora andrographioides?

Canscora andrographioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, which further descends into the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy through its order, Gentianales, it is placed within the family Gentianaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Canscora, and its species designation, andrographioides.

What side-effects this plant can have?

Because the plant may influence metabolic processes, it could potentially interact with various medications or affect blood sugar levels. It is important to monitor for any adverse systemic responses when introducing this species into a wellness routine. Consult a healthcare professional to assess the risks of individual sensitivities before use.
