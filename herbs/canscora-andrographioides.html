Canscora andrographioides has an erect, herbaceous growth habit characterized by slender, green stems and lanceolate to ovate leaves with entire margins. The reproductive structures feature delicate petals and stamens that reflect its classification within the Scrophulariaceae family. These morphological traits allow the plant to thrive in its specific ecological niche.
