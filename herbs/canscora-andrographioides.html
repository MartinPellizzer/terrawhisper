Canscora andrographioides

What's the taxonomical classification of Canscora andrographioides?

Canscora andrographioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. At the class level, it is categorized as Equisetopsida, which further descends into the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy through its order, Gentianales, it is placed within the family Gentianaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Canscora, and its species designation, andrographioides.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Canscora andrographioides has an erect, herbaceous growth habit characterized by slender, green stems and lanceolate to ovate leaves with entire margins.

The leaves are arranged oppositely along the stem and possess a smooth, somewhat fleshy texture. Its inflorescence consists of small, inconspicuous flowers that typically emerge from the leaf axils. The reproductive structures feature delicate petals and stamens that reflect its classification within the Scrophulariaceae family.

These morphological traits allow the plant to thrive in its specific ecological niche.
