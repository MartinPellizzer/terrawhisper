What's the taxonomical classification of Onychium tenuifrons?

Onychium tenuifrons belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Further refinement places it within the family Pteridaceae, under the genus Onychium, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species tenuifrons.
