What's the taxonomical classification of Onychium tenuifrons?

Onychium tenuifrons belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, its taxonomic progression continues through the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Further refinement places it within the family Pteridaceae, under the genus Onychium, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species tenuifrons.
