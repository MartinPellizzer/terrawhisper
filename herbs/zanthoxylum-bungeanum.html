Botanically, Szechuan pepper is characterized by its unique blend of volatile oils, including zanthoxyline and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its distinctive flavor and aroma.

Historically, Szechuan pepper has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine, with records of its use dating back to the Ming dynasty.
