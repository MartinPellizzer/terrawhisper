Gentiana spathulifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Gentiana spathulifolia?

Gentiana spathulifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed under the order Gentianales. The plant is further categorized within the family Gentianaceae, falling under the genus Gentiana, and is specifically identified by the species name spatulifolia.
