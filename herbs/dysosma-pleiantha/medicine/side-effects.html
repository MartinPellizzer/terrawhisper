10 Possible Side Effects Of Dysosma Pleiantha (Chinese Mayapple)

Dysosma pleiantha, also known as Chinese mayapple, has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, dizziness, and allergic reactions.

These side effects are often caused by incorrect dosage or interaction with other medications. In severe cases, they can worsen people's lives by causing prolonged nausea, fatigue, and anxiety.

This can lead to missed workdays, strained relationships, and decreased overall quality of life.
