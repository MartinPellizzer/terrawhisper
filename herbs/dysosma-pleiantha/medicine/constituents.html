10 Medicinal Constituents Of Dysosma Pleiantha (Chinese Mayapple)

Dysosma pleiantha, also known as Chinese Mayapple, has active constituents such as iridoids, alkaloids, and flavonoids.

These constituents impart anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties to the plant, making it a valuable remedy for various ailments. The iridoids, in particular, have been shown to exhibit anti-diabetic and hypolipidemic effects, potentially helping individuals manage type 2 diabetes and high cholesterol levels.

Additionally, the alkaloids have been found to possess analgesic and sedative properties, providing relief from pain and promoting relaxation.
