What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Calea Sp. C (Dream Herb)?

Calea sp.

c, also known as dream herb, has health benefits such as promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety, making it a popular natural remedy for insomnia and stress-related issues. The herb is rich in flavonoids, including calanol, which are known for their sedative and anti-inflammatory properties.

Medicinally, the herb is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture to enhance its relaxing effects. However, excessive consumption may lead to dizziness and lightheadedness. It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Calea sp.

c, especially in combination with other medications.

What are the medicinal preparations of Calea sp. c?

Calea sp.

What are the possible side effect of using Calea sp. c improperly?

Improper use of Calea sp.

c increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as headaches, dizziness, and nausea. It can also lead to vivid and unsettling dreams, insomnia, anxiety attacks, and feelings of panic or unease.

In some cases, excessive use may cause agitation, restlessness, and even hallucinations. Additionally, users may experience rapid heartbeat, tremors, and changes in appetite or libido.

Furthermore, it can interact with certain medications, exacerbating conditions such as depression, anxiety disorders, and cardiovascular problems.

What precautions to take when using Calea sp. c medicinally?

Before using Calea sp.

c for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage and potential interactions with other medications. Additionally, it's essential to use high-quality extracts or dried herbs, as low-quality products may contain contaminants or adulterants. Furthermore, pregnant or breastfeeding women should exercise caution due to limited research on its safety for these populations.

Proper storage and handling are also crucial to maintain the herb's potency and efficacy.
