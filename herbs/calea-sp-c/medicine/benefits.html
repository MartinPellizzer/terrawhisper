10 Health Benefits Of Calea Sp. C (Dream Herb)

Calea sp.

c has health benefits such as promoting relaxation and reducing stress levels due to its sedative and anxiolytic properties. This plant contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins that work together to calm the mind and body, leading to improved sleep quality and reduced symptoms of anxiety and depression.

For example, people suffering from insomnia may find it easier to fall asleep and stay asleep, while those with anxiety disorders may experience reduced panic attacks and increased confidence.
