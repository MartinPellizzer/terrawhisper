10 Health Benefits Of Cystopteris Fragilis (Maidenhair Fern)

Cystopteris fragilis has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, gout, and other joint pains.

The plant's saponins and flavonoids work together to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and improve circulation.

For example, a tea made from the leaves can be used to treat respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma, while the plant's topical application can soothe skin irritations and wounds.
