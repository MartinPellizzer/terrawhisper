Phlegmariurus hamiltonii

What's the taxonomical classification of Phlegmariurus hamiltonii?

Phlegmariurus hamiltonii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Lycopodiidae. Following the biological hierarchy through its order, Lycopodiales, it is placed into the family Lycopodiaceae. The plant is further categorized under the genus Phlegmariurus, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species hamiltonii.
