What's the taxonomical classification of Prosaptia obliquata?

Prosaptia obliquata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the family Polypodiaceae, which contains the genus Prosaptia, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Prosaptia obliquata.
