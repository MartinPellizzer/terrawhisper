What is Hibbertia bracteata?

Hibbertia bracteata is a species of flowering plant in the family Dilleniaceae.

It is a shrub-like plant, 0.3-1.2 meters high, with simple, alternate leaves, 10-30 mm long, and 5-10 mm wide, having a crenulate margin and an obtuse apex. The leaves are glabrous on both surfaces and have a petiole 1-5 mm long. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers, 10-20 mm in diameter, in terminal clusters or panicles.

The flowers have 5 sepals, 5 petals, 20-30 stamens, and 2-3 carpels.
