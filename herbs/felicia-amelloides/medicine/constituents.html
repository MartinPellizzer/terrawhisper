10 Medicinal Constituents Of Felicia Amelloides (African Daisy)

Felicia amelloides, also known as African Daisy, has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids.

These constituents are responsible for the plant's anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to improve cardiovascular health by reducing cholesterol levels and preventing platelet aggregation, thereby reducing the risk of heart disease.

This can lead to improved quality of life for individuals with heart conditions.
