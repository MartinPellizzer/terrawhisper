Carex duriuscula subsp. rigescens

What's the taxonomical classification of Carex duriuscula subsp. rigescens?

Carex duriuscula subsp. rigescens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Poales and is specifically nested within the family Cyperaceae. At the lower levels of its hierarchical classification, it is identified by the genus Carex, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the subspecies Carex duriuscula subsp. rigescens.
