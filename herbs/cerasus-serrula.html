Cerasus serrula belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Cerasus, and its unique species name, serrula.
