What's the taxonomical classification of Boehmeria clidemioides?

Boehmeria clidemioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Rosalales. It is further categorized under the family Urticaceae, which places it within the genus Boehmeria, specifically identified by the species name clidemioides.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Boehmeria clidemioides has oppositely arranged, ovate to elliptical leaves with serrated margins and long petioles attached to a woody stem.
