Pterocaulon redolens

What's the taxonomical classification of Pterocaulon redolens?

Pterocaulon redolens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Pterocaulon, and its unique species designation, redolens.
