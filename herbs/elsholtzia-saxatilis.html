Elsholtzia saxatilis

What's the taxonomical classification of Elsholtzia saxatilis?

Elsholtzia saxatilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Lamiales. It falls within the family Lamiaceae and is identified by the genus Elsholtzia, with its specific designation being the species saxatilis.
