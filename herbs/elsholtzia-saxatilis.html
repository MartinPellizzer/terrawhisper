What's the taxonomical classification of Elsholtzia saxatilis?

Elsholtzia saxatilis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further classified under the order Lamiales. It falls within the family Lamiaceae and is identified by the genus Elsholtzia, with its specific designation being the species saxatilis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Elsholtzia saxatilis has a deciduous, spreading tree habit characterized by pinnately compound leaves typically composed of 7 to 15 small, ovate leaflets with serrated margins.

Its reproductive structures consist of small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that emerge in clusters during the spring. The fruit is a small, winged samara that facilitates wind dispersal through its aerodynamic structure.

The overall growth form reflects an adaptation to rocky, mountainous terrains where it often develops a rugged and twisted architecture.
