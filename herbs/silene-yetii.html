Silene yetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls into the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized under the order Caryophyllales. This plant is a member of the family Caryophyllaceae, specifically situated within the genus Silene, where it is identified by its unique species name, yetii.
