Saxifraga montana

What's the taxonomical classification of Saxifraga montana?

Saxifraga montana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually reaching the order Saxifragales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Saxifragaceae and the genus Saxifraga, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Saxifraga montana.
