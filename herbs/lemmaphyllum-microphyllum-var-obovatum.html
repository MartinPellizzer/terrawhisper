Lemmaphyllum microphyllum var. obovatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Lemmaphyllum microphyllum var. obovatum?

Lemmaphyllum microphyllum var. obovatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is placed under the order Polypodiales and falls within the family Polypodiaceae. This specific botanical variety is classified under the genus Lemmaphyllum, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the species Lemmaphyllum microphyllum var. obovatum.
