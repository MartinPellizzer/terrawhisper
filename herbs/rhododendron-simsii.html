What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Rhododendron simsii has evergreen, elliptical leaves that typically measure between 5 and 12 centimeters in length and feature a leathery texture with a dark green upper surface. Its woody stems grow in a dense, shrubby habit, often reaching heights of one to two meters in well-established specimens. Small, fine hairs may be present on the undersides of the foliage or along the young stems depending on the specific cultivar.
