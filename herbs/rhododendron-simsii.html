Rhododendron simsii

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhododendron simsii?

Rhododendron simsii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Within the order Ericales, it is a member of the family Ericaceae, which contains a diverse array of flowering plants. Finally, the plant is placed within the genus Rhododendron, with its specific identity defined by the species name simsii.

This plant is used to treat various ailments including cough, asthma, and respiratory inflammation. It is frequently employed in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of lung congestion. Some practitioners utilize its properties to address digestive issues and stomach discomfort. Additionally, the plant is sometimes applied to soothe skin irritation or external wounds. Its medicinal application remains primarily rooted in specific regional folk practices.
