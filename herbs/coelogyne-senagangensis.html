Coelogyne senagangensis

Coelogyne senagangensis, also known as the Senagang orchid, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral activities.

The health benefits of Coelogyne senagangensis include its ability to treat fever, cough, and respiratory issues, as well as its potential to reduce inflammation and improve immune function.
