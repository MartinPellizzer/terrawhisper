10 Health Benefits Of Acacia Catechu (Cutch)

Acacia catechu has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, soothing digestive issues, and treating skin problems.

Its medicinal properties include its antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory compounds. These properties can help alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis, improve digestion, and clear up skin conditions like eczema.

For example, people with chronic pain may find relief by using cutch oil as a topical treatment, while those with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) may benefit from its ability to calm digestive issues.
