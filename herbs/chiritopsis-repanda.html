Chiritopsis repanda belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Chiritopsis, with its unique identity defined by the species name repanda.
