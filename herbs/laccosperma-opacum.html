What side effects this plant can have?

These chemical compounds frequently trigger redness, itching, and swelling when the plant comes into direct contact with sensitive skin. Some individuals may experience more serious dermatological reactions, including blistering or localized inflammation, following prolonged exposure. If the plant is swallowed, it may lead to gastrointestinal distress or more complex internal complications. Because of these risks, careful handling and avoidance of skin contact are highly recommended.
