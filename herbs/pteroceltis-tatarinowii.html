Pteroceltis tatarinowii

What's the taxonomical classification of Pteroceltis tatarinowii?

Pteroceltis tatarinowii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the order Rosales. It is further categorized into the family Cannabaea, the genus Pteroceltis, and is specifically identified as the species tatarinoowii.

What are the therapeutic actions of Pteroceltis tatarinowii?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat gastrointestinal disorders such as diarrhea, abdominal pain, and inflammation of the digestive tract. It contains specific bioactive compounds that help soothe the lining of the stomach and intestines. These medicinal properties are often utilized in traditional healing practices to regulate bowel movements. Regular application of the plant extracts may also assist in reducing bacterial infections within the gut. Researchers continue to study its chemical profile to better understand these therapeutic effects.
