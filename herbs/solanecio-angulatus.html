What's the taxonomical classification of Solanecio angulatus?

Solanecio angulatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually reaching the order Asterales. As a member of the family Asteraceae, this plant is placed within the genus Solanecio, specifically identified by its species designation, anguletus.
