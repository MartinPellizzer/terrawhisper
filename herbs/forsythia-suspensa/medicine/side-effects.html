10 Possible Side Effects Of Forsythia Suspensa (Chinese Weeping Forsythia)

Forsythia suspensa, also known as Chinese weeping forsythia, has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, diarrhea, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption of the herb, pre-existing conditions, or interactions with other medications.

For instance, gastrointestinal issues can lead to nutrient deficiencies, exacerbating conditions like anemia, while allergic reactions can cause respiratory distress, impacting one's quality of life and daily activities.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Forsythia suspensa if used imporperly.

1. Triggers anaphylactic shock

Forsythia suspensa, also known as Chinese weeping forsythia, causes allergic reactions.

The allergenic compounds present in the plant's flowers, leaves, and stems are responsible for triggering this response. These compounds, including flavonoids and terpenes, can cause symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing in susceptible individuals.

Additionally, the plant's sap may also contain allergenic proteins that can elicit an immune response.
