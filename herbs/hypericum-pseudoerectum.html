What is Hypericum pseudoerectum?

Hypericum pseudoerectum is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

It has a woody base and slender stems that typically grow up to 30-60 cm in height, bearing paired, sessile, ovoid-cylindrical leaves, 8-15 mm long, with a flat base and an acute tip. The plant's flowers are small, yellow, and 4-5 mm in diameter, arranged in terminal cymes, and are produced from June to August. The flowers are 5-merous, with 5 sepals, 5 petals, and 5 stamens, and are followed by small, greenish-brown capsules containing 3-5 seeds.

Hypericum pseudoerectum is native to the Balkan Peninsula and southern Europe.
