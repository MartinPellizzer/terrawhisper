Safflower (Carthamus tinctorius) Actions

Safflower exhibits several key therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and antispasmodic properties. It also acts as a carminative and tonic, aiding digestion and supporting overall vitality. Its bitter nature stimulates digestion, while its sedative effects can help calm the nervous system. Additionally, it serves as an expectorant and astringent, supporting respiratory and mucous membrane health.
