What's the taxonomical classification of Chassalia curviflora?

Chassalia curviflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Gentianales and falls within the family Rubiaceae. Finally, its specific classification is defined by the genus Chassalia and the species curviflora.
