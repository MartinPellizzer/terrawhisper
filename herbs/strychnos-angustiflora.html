What's the taxonomical classification of Strychnos angustiflora?

Strychnos angustiflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is further categorized under the order Gentianaales. It is a member of the family Loganiaceae and falls under the genus Strychnos, with its specific designation being the species angustiflora.
