What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Lespedeza Capitata (Bush Clover)?

Lespedeza capitata, also known as bush clover, has health benefits such as lowering blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Its medicinal constituents include isoflavones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are known to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Bush clover can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote cardiovascular health. However, excessive consumption may lead to gastrointestinal side effects.

Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a healthcare professional before using bush clover due to its potential estrogenic effects.

This article explains the health benefits, active constituents, medicinal preparations, possible side effects, and precautions related to Lespedeza capitata.
