10 Health Benefits Of Hieracium Pilosella (Mouse-Ear Hawkweed)

Hieracium pilosella has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving respiratory issues.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and phenolic acids, which possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant activities. For example, the plant's extract can help alleviate symptoms of bronchitis and asthma by soothing airways and reducing swelling.

Additionally, its topical application has been shown to ease skin conditions like eczema and dermatitis, providing relief to those suffering from these chronic conditions.
