What are the active constituents of Anacyclus pyrethrum ?

Anacyclus pyrethrum, also known as doseena, has active constituents such as anacardic acid, anacyclin, and anacardol.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. Anacyclin is known to have a relaxing effect on the nervous system, while anacardic acid has been shown to possess analgesic and anti-pyretic activities.

What are the medicinal preparations of Anacyclus pyrethrum ?

Anacyclus pyrethrum, also known as doseena, has medicinal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and essential oil.

The infusion is prepared by steeping the leaves in hot water, while the decoction involves boiling the roots and leaves. The essential oil is extracted through steam distillation. These preparations are used to treat various ailments including fever, cough, and respiratory issues.

The root of Anacyclus pyrethrum is also used to make a herbal tea that aids in digestion.

Improper use of Anacyclus pyrethrum, also known as doseena, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as digestive issues, nausea, and dizziness.

Furthermore, doseena may interact negatively with medications, including blood thinners and diabetes medications, highlighting the need for careful monitoring and consultation with a healthcare professional.
