10 Medicinal Constituents Of Azadirachta Indica (Neem)

Azadirachta indica has active constituents such as nimbin, azadirachtin, and quercetin.

These compounds exhibit antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it an effective remedy for various ailments. For instance, its antiseptic properties can help heal wounds, while its antioxidant properties may prevent cell damage and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Additionally, its anti-inflammatory properties can alleviate symptoms of arthritis and other inflammatory conditions, improving overall health and well-being.

Its unique properties also make it a potential ingredient in personal care products.
