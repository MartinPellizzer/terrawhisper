Urtica Dioica Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Urtica dioica, commonly known as stinging nettle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is known for its stinging hairs that cause a skin irritation when touched.

Urtica dioica is rich in bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, polyphenols, vitamins, and minerals, which contribute to its therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-supporting properties.

This herb can be prepared in various forms, such as infusions, decoctions, tinctures, or capsules, and is used to support urinary health, reduce allergy symptoms, and enhance overall wellness.
