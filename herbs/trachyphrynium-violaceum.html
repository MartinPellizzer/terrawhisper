Trachyphrynium violaceum

What's the taxonomical classification of Trachyphrynium violaceum?

Trachyphrynium violaceuum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic lineage continues through the order Zingiberales and the family Marantaceae, which places it among a diverse group of flowering plants. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trachyphrynium, and its species designation, violaceuum.
