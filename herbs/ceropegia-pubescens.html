What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Ceropegia pubescens has slender, cylindrical, succulent stems that are covered in fine, soft, white hairs and feature distinct longitudinal ribs. These blossoms are often pale or creamy in color and exhibit a delicate, waxy texture. The presence of pubescence serves as a key diagnostic feature for distinguishing this species from related succulents.
