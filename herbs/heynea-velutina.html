Heynea velutina

What's the taxonomical classification of Heynea velutina?

Heynea velutina belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant falls under the taxonomic order Sapindales. It is further categorized into the family Meliaceae, which places it within the genus Heynea, ultimately identifying it as the specific species Heynea velutinina.
