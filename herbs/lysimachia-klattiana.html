Lysimachia klattiana

What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia klattiana?

Lysimachia klattiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Lysimachia, with its specific designation being the species klattiana.
