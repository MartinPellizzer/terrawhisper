10 Possible Side Effects Of Paeonia × Suffruticosa (Tree Peony)

Paeonia × suffruticosa has some side effects when used improperly, such as allergic reactions, stomach upset, and diarrhea.

These side effects are often caused by taking high doses or using the herb for extended periods without proper breaks. In severe cases, these side effects can worsen lives by leading to anaphylaxis, chronic digestive issues, or even organ damage.

This can significantly impact daily activities, relationships, and overall well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Paeonia × suffruticosa if used imporperly.
