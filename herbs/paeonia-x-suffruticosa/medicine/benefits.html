10 Health Benefits Of Paeonia × Suffruticosa (Tree Peony)

Paeonia × suffruticosa has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties.

These medicinal properties can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease, and even combat certain types of cancer. For example, the plant's extracts have been shown to reduce inflammation in the body, which can lead to improved joint mobility and reduced pain for individuals suffering from osteoarthritis.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties can help protect against cell damage, potentially leading to a reduced risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer.
