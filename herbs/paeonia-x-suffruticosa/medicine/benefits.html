10 Health Benefits Of Paeonia × Suffruticosa (Tree Peony)

Paeonia × suffruticosa, also known as tree peony, has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Its medicinal properties include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids. These properties have been shown to improve cardiovascular health, reduce pain and inflammation, and boost immune function.

For example, a study found that tree peony extract lowered cholesterol levels and improved overall heart health, reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease and stroke.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Paeonia × suffruticosa.

1. Reduces inflammation

Paeonia × suffruticosa reduces inflammation because it is rich in flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to neutralize free radicals and reduce inflammation.

Studies have shown that extracts from tree peony can inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thereby providing relief from inflammation and promoting overall health and well-being.

2. Relieves menstrual cramps

Paeonia × suffruticosa relieves menstrual cramps because it contains flavonoids and phenolic acids that exhibit anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit prostaglandin synthesis, which is responsible for pain and cramping during menstruation.

Regular consumption of Paeonia × suffruticosa extracts or tea has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to alleviate menstrual discomfort and promote overall female health.

3. Helps anxiety

Paeonia × suffruticosa helps anxiety because of its unique combination of bioactive compounds.

Research suggests that the plant's flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids contribute to its anxiolytic properties. These compounds have been shown to modulate neurotransmitter activity, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation, ultimately helping to alleviate symptoms of anxiety.

Regular consumption of tree peony or its extracts may offer a natural solution for anxiety relief.

4. Lowers blood sugar

Paeonia × suffruticosa lowers blood sugar because it contains flavonoids and polyphenols that have been shown to inhibit alpha-glucosidase, an enzyme that breaks down carbohydrates into glucose.

This action helps reduce glucose absorption in the gut, thereby lowering blood sugar levels.

Additionally, tree peony has been found to have a hypoglycemic effect in animal studies, making it a potential natural remedy for managing blood sugar.

5. Supports cardiovascular health

Paeonia × suffruticosa supports cardiovascular health because of its unique combination of flavonoids and phenolic acids.

These compounds have been shown to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to improve blood vessel function, lower blood pressure, and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

Regular consumption of Paeonia × suffruticosa may also help to mitigate oxidative stress and inflammation associated with heart disease.

6. Treats digestive issues

Paeonia × suffruticosa treats digestive issues because its roots and stems contain a wealth of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.

These bioactive compounds have been shown to soothe and protect the digestive system, reducing inflammation and promoting healthy gut function.

Regular consumption of Paeonia × suffruticosa has been found to alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, such as bloating, gas, and irritable bowel syndrome.

7. Soothes respiratory problems

Paeonia × suffruticosa soothes respiratory problems because it contains compounds that have anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

The flowers and roots of the tree peony have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma. By reducing inflammation and promoting the expulsion of mucus, Paeonia × suffruticosa can provide relief from congestion and coughing.

Its calming effects can also help alleviate stress-related breathing difficulties.

8. Relieves skin conditions

Paeonia × suffruticosa relieves skin conditions because it contains anti-inflammatory compounds such as paeonol and paeoniflorin.

These bioactive substances help to soothe irritated skin and reduce inflammation, making it an effective treatment for conditions like eczema, acne, and rosacea.

Additionally, the antioxidant properties of tree peony help to protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, promoting overall skin health and reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

9. Eases migraines

Paeonia × suffruticosa eases migraines because of its unique combination of flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These compounds help to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the blood vessels, a key trigger for migraines.

As a result, tree peony extract has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to alleviate migraine symptoms, providing relief from pain and frequency.

10. Boosts immune system

Paeonia × suffruticosa boosts immune system because it contains flavonoids and phenolic acids that exhibit potent antioxidant properties.

These compounds help to protect cells from damage caused by free radicals, which can lead to inflammation and compromise the immune system.

The antioxidants in tree peony also stimulate the production of white blood cells, which play a crucial role in fighting off infections and diseases.
