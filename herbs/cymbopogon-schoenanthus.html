Cymbopogon Grass (Cymbopogon schoenanthus)

Cymbopogon Grass (Cymbopogon schoenanthus) is a member of the Poaceae family, native to Southern Africa, and Madagascar. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and flowers have been used for infusions, decoctions, and oil infusions.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and carminative actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
