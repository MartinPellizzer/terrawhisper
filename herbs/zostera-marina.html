Zostera Grass (Zostera marina)

Zostera Grass (Zostera marina) is a member of the Zosteraceae family, native to Atlantic Coast Of Europe, Pacific Coast Of North America, and Coastal Regions Of Japan. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for infusions, decoctions, and herbal baths.

This herb is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory, expectorant, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in european herbal medicine, mediterranean herbal traditions, and japanese kampo medicine.
