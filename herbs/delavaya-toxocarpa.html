Delavaya toxocarpa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized into the family Sapindaceae, from which the genus Delavaya is derived. Finally, the specific identity of this organism is defined by its species name, toxocarpa.
