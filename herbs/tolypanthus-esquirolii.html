What's the taxonomical classification of Tolypanthus esquirolii?

Tolypanthus esquirolii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Santalales. Further taxonomic refinement places this plant in the family Loranthaceae, within the genus Tolypanthus, and is specifically identified as the species esquirolii.
