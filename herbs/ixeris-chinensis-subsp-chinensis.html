Ixeris chinensis subsp. chinensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Ixeris chinensis subsp. chinensis?

Ixeris chinensis subsp. chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a member of the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Asterales. Within this taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is situated in the family Asteraceae and the genus Ixeris, ultimately being identified as the species Ixeris.
