Hemipilia limprichtii

What's the taxonomical classification of Hemipilia limprichtii?

Hemipilia limprichtii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Asparagales. As a member of the Orchidaceae family, it is placed in the genus Hemipilia, specifically identified by the species name limprichtii.

This plant is used to treat various ailments including skin infections, stomach aches, and respiratory issues.
