Eriobotrya cavaleriei

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Eriobotrya cavaleriei has large, leathery, evergreen leaves that are elliptical to oblong in shape and feature a densely pubescent, velvety underside. Its woody stems develop a rugged bark texture as the plant matures into a small tree or large shrub. Small, fragrant white flowers emerge from axillary clusters, eventually giving way to fleshy, pome-like fruits. These fruits are characterized by their distinct shape and are often covered in a fine layer of pubescence.
