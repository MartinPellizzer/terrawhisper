What's the taxonomical classification of Spiraea blumei?

Spiraea blumei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Rosales and the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Spiraea and its specific species designation, blumei.
