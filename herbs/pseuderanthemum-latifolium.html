What's the taxonomical classification of Pseuderanthemum latifolium?

Pseuderanthemum latifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a representative of the family Acanthaceae, specifically situated within the genus Pseuderanthemum, with its unique identity defined by its species designation, latifolium.
