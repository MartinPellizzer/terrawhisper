10 Medicinal Constituents Of Scrophularia Nodosa (Figwort)

Scrophularia nodosa has active constituents such as iridoids, phenolic acids, and flavonoids.

These compounds are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiviral properties. The iridoids, particularly aucubin, have been shown to reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health, while the flavonoids can boost the immune system and protect against oxidative stress.

As a result, figwort is used to alleviate symptoms of fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, potentially improving the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.
