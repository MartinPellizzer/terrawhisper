10 Possible Side Effects Of Scrophularia Nodosa (Figwort)

The causes of these side effects often stem from taking excessive doses, using the herb in combination with other medications, or having underlying allergies.

These side effects can worsen lives by causing stomach discomfort, itchy skin, and even respiratory problems, potentially leading to decreased productivity, increased stress, and compromised overall health.
