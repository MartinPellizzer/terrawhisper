Scrophularia Nodosa (Scrophularia nodosa) Compounds

Scrophularia nodosa contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Coumarins and glycosides are also present, offering potential anticoagulant and cardiovascular benefits. Terpenoids and saponins support immune function, while essential oils and alkaloids contribute to its therapeutic effects in herbal medicine.

Flavonoids are bioactive compounds found in Scrophularia nodosa, a traditional medicinal plant used in herbal medicine. These compounds contribute to the plant's therapeutic properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, making them key components in its traditional applications.
