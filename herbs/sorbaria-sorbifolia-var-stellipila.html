Sorbaria sorbifolia var. stellipila

What's the taxonomical classification of Sorbaria sorbifolia var. stellipila?

Sorbaria sorbifolia var. stellipila belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosales. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the family Rosaecae and the genus Sorbaria, ultimately reaching its specific designation within the species Sorbaria.
