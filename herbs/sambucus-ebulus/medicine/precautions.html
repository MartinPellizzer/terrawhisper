10 Precautions To Take When Using Sambucus Ebulus (Dwarf Elder)

Sambucus ebulus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring kidney and liver function, as it can cause nephrotoxicity and hepatotoxicity with prolonged use.

Failing to do so may result in damage to these organs and increased risk of side effects like kidney stones and liver failure.

It is also essential to consult with a healthcare professional, especially when using it in conjunction with other medications, to avoid potential interactions and complications.

When using Sambucus ebulus medicinally, it's important to take under guidance only.

This is because Sambucus ebulus contains anthraquinone glycosides, which can interact with certain medications and exacerbate underlying health conditions if not properly monitored.
