    What side effects this plant can have?

    This plant can cause severe poisoning characterized by nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, dizziness, confusion, arrhythmia, and seizures. These symptoms occur because the plant contains grayanotoxins, which interfere with the sodium channels in cell membranes. Once ingested, the toxins can disrupt the electrical signaling in the heart, leading to dangerous heart rhythm irregularities. The central nervous system is also affected, potentially causing mental disorientation and neurological distress. In severe cases, the physical reaction can escalate to life-threatening convulsions or cardiac arrest.
