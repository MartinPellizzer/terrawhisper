Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta

What's the taxonomical classification of Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta?

Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Apiales and falls within the family Araliaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Hydrocotyle and is specifically designated as the species pseudoconferta.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta has small, kidney-shaped to orbicular leaves that are characterized by crenate or scalloped margins and a bright green color.

The foliage is typically arranged in a rosette pattern along creeping stolons that allow the plant to spread across moist substrates. Each leaf is supported by a slender, cylindrical petiole that extends from the central base of the plant. The leaf surface can appear slightly textured or smooth depending on the specific environmental conditions.

Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the leaf axils to complete its reproductive cycle.
