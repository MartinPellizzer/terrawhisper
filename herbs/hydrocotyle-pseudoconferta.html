What's the taxonomical classification of Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta?

Hydrocotyle pseudoconferta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the order Apiales and falls within the family Araliaceae. Finally, it is identified by the genus Hydrocotyle and is specifically designated as the species pseudoconferta.
