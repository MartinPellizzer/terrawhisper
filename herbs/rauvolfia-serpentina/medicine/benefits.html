10 Health Benefits Of Rauvolfia Serpentina (Indian Snakeroot)

Rauvolfia serpentina, also known as Indian Snakeroot, has health benefits such as reducing stress and anxiety, treating hypertension and insomnia, and alleviating symptoms of Parkinson's disease.

Its medicinal properties include alkaloids like reserpine and ajmalicine, which act as sedatives, anticonvulsants, and vasodilators.

By using Rauvolfia serpentina, people can improve their sleep quality, manage stress and anxiety, and alleviate the symptoms of chronic diseases, ultimately enhancing overall well-being and improving daily life.
