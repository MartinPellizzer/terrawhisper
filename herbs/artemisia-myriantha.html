Artemisia myriantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is a member of the widely recognized family Asteraceae, which is further narrowed down to the genus Artemisia. Ultimately, its specific scientific designation is completed by its species name, myriantha.
