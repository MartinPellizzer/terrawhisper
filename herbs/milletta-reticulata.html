Possible Side Effects of milletta reticulata

Milletta reticulata can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

In some cases, excessive consumption of milletta reticulata may lead to allergic reactions, such as hives, itching, and swelling.

Prolonged use or high doses of milletta reticulata may also cause liver damage, which can result in symptoms like fatigue, yellowing of the skin, and dark urine.

Additionally, improper use of milletta reticulata may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, and exacerbate underlying health conditions.
