Eritrichium mandshuricum

What's the taxonomical classification of Eritrichium mandshuricum?

Eritrichium mandshuricum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic path continues through the order Boraginales and the family Boraginaceae. Ultimately, this plant is categorized under the genus Eritrichium, with its specific designation being the species mandshuricum.
