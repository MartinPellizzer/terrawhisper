Side Effects of juniperus osteosperma

Juniperus osteosperma may cause nausea, particularly when consumed in large quantities or by individuals with sensitive stomachs.

The herb may cause dry mouth and throat irritation, which can be uncomfortable for those with existing respiratory conditions. Additionally, it has been reported to induce fatigue and, in more severe cases, may contribute to kidney damage.

While not all individuals will experience these side effects, it is important to use Juniperus osteosperma cautiously and consult a healthcare professional before incorporating it into one's regimen, especially if there are pre-existing health conditions or allergies.
