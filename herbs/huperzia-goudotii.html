What is Huperzia goudotii?

Huperzia goudotii is a species of lycopod, belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is a small, perennial plant with a short stem, typically growing up to 5-10 cm in height, and has linear, lanceolate to oblong leaves, measuring 2-5 mm in length. The leaves are dark green, glabrous and have a distinctive shape with a prominent midrib. Huperzia goudotii is a terrestrial plant, commonly found in shaded, humid areas with acidic soils in tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Oceania.

The plant reproduces via spores, which are produced in clusters on the underside of the leaves.
