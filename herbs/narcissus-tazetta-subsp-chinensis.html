Narcissus tazetta subsp. chinensis

Narcissus tazetta subsp.

chinensis, also known as Chinese paperwhite, is a perennial bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

Traditionally, this plant has been used for its medicinal properties, which include treating colds, fever, and rheumatism due to its antiviral, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Narcissus tazetta subsp.

chinensis include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve respiratory health, and alleviate symptoms of fever and cold.

Herbal preparations of this plant can be made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, although its use should be approached with caution and under the guidance of a qualified healthcare practitioner.
