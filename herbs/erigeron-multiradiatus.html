What's the taxonomical classification of Erigeron multiradiatus?

Erigeron multiradiatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic descent, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the hierarchy, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Erigeron, ultimately reaching its specific identification as the species multiradiatus.
