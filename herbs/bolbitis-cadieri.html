What is Bolbitis cadieri?

Bolbitis cadieri is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, specifically in the tropical regions of China, India, and Southeast Asia. Botanically, Bolbitis cadieri is characterized by its rhizomatous habit, erect to ascending fronds that are 50-100 cm long, and pinnae that are pinnatifid to pinnatipartite. The fronds are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, with a leathery texture.

The species produces spores in spherical or ellipsoidal sori that are located on the underside of the frond.
