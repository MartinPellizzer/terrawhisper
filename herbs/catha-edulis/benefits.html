10 Best Catha Edulis Health Benefits

Catha edulis, commonly known as khat, contains psychoactive compounds such as cathinone and cathine, which can produce stimulating effects similar to those of caffeine.

Some studies suggest that moderate consumption of khat may enhance alertness, improve concentration, and increase energy levels, potentially offering benefits for individuals with attention deficit disorders. However, excessive use can lead to adverse effects such as insomnia, anxiety, and dependency, highlighting the need for caution. The plant is traditionally used in social and cultural settings in East Africa and the Arabian Peninsula, where it is consumed for its invigorating properties.

Due to its potential for abuse and health risks, Catha edulis is classified as a controlled substance in many countries, despite its traditional use.
