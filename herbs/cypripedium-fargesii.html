Cypripedium fargesii

What's the taxonomical classification of Cypripedium fargesii?

Cypripedium fargesii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. As a member of the order Asparagales, it falls under the family Orchidaceae, which characterizes its identity as an orchid. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Cypripedium, with its specific designation being the species fargesii.

What are the therapeutic actions of Cypripedium fargesii?

What diseases this plant treats?

This plant is used to treat cough, phlegm, fever, and inflammation. It is commonly utilized in traditional Chinese medicine to address respiratory ailments and soothe irritated airways. The plant's properties are specifically applied to help reduce excess mucus in the lungs. Practitioners also employ it to lower body temperature during febrile conditions. Its medicinal applications focus heavily on clearing heat and resolving congestion.
