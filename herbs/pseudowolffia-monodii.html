Pseudowolffia Monodii

Preliminary studies suggest that it may possess antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which could be beneficial in treating various chronic diseases. Due to its high nutrient density and rapid growth rate, Pseudowolffia monodii is also being explored as a potential source of functional foods and dietary supplements.
