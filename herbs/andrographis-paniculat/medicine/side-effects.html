10 Possible Side Effects Of Andrographis Paniculat (King Of Bitters)

Andrographis paniculat has some side effects when used improperly, such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

These symptoms occur due to the high dose of andrographolide, a compound found in the herb. In severe cases, excessive use can lead to stomach ulcers and bleeding. For example, individuals who rely on a steady diet may suffer from malnutrition due to frequent vomiting and inability to retain food.

This can worsen their overall health and well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 most common side effects of Andrographis paniculat if used imporperly.
