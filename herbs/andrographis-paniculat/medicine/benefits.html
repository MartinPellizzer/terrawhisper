10 Health Benefits Of Andrographis Paniculat (King Of Bitters)

Andrographis paniculat has health benefits such as relieving respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma by its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

Its bitter compounds also aid digestion and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). Furthermore, Andrographis has antimicrobial and antiviral properties, which can help combat infections and viral diseases like the common cold and flu.

By using this herb, individuals may experience improved lung function, reduced digestive discomfort, and enhanced immune system response, ultimately leading to better overall health and quality of life.
