What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Andrographis Paniculat (King Of Bitters)?

Andrographis paniculat has health benefits such as alleviating fever, reducing inflammation, and treating respiratory issues.

The medicinal constituents responsible for these benefits include andrografin, andrographolide, and neoadrographolide, which have anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antibacterial properties. The herb is often prepared in capsule or tablet form, or as a tea infusion, to make it easier to consume. However, potential side effects of using Andrographis paniculat include gastrointestinal disturbances, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

To minimize these risks, individuals should consult with a healthcare professional before using the herb, especially if they have pre-existing medical conditions or are taking prescription medications.

What are the active constituents of Andrographis paniculat?

The health benefits of Andrographis paniculat comes from its active constituents, such as andrographolide, neoandrographolide, and palmitic acid.

What are the medicinal preparations of Andrographis paniculat?

Andrographis paniculat has different medicinal preparations, such as capsules, tablets, teas, and syrups.

Capsules are used to treat fever, respiratory infections, and digestive issues, while tablets are often prescribed for rheumatoid arthritis and other inflammatory conditions. Teas are consumed to reduce stress, improve sleep quality, and soothe digestive discomforts, whereas syrups are used as a demulcent to calm coughs and sore throats.

What are the possible side effect of using Andrographis paniculat improperly?

Improper use of Andrographis paniculat increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach pain, and bloating.

It can also cause allergic reactions like skin rashes, itching, and hives. In some cases, it may lead to liver damage, kidney problems, or an increased risk of bleeding due to its blood-thinning properties. Moreover, Andrographis paniculat can interact with certain medications, reducing their effectiveness or increasing the risk of adverse effects.

It may also exacerbate pre-existing medical conditions like asthma, diabetes, and hypertension.

What precautions to take when using Andrographis paniculat medicinally?

Before using Andrographis paniculat for medicinal purposes, you must take precautions such as consulting with a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage and treatment duration.

Additionally, it is crucial to monitor your liver function regularly, as high doses can cause liver damage.

Furthermore, avoid combining it with other medications that may interact with Andrographis paniculat, and discontinue use if you experience any adverse effects or allergic reactions.
