What's the taxonomical classification of Euonymus schensianus?

Euonymus schensianus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is further categorized into the order Celastrales. The plant is a member of the family Celastraceae and is specifically placed within the genus Euonymus, ultimately reaching its unique taxonomic identification at the species level as schensianus.
