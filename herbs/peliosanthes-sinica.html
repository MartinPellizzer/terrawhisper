What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Peliosanthes sinica has small, herbaceous stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that feature entire or slightly serrated margins and distinct pinnate venation. Its reproductive structures consist of tiny, inconspicuous flowers that lack showy petals, often appearing in small clusters. The root system is generally shallow, allowing the plant to stabilize itself in loose, organic forest soil.
