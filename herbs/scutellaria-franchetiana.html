Scutelariia franchetiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Scutellaria, and its species name, franchetiana.
