Grindelia Squarrosa Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Grindelia squarrosa, commonly known as the desert groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This herb is known for its resilience in arid environments and is used traditionally for its medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Grindelia squarrosa include its anti-inflammatory, expectorant, and skin-soothing effects, which may help with respiratory and skin conditions.

The bioactive constituents responsible for its therapeutic actions include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and mucilage.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and topical salves can be made from this herb to harness its healing properties.
