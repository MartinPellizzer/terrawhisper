Desert Gumweed (Grindelia squarrosa)

Desert Gumweed (Grindelia squarrosa) is a member of the Compositae family, native to California, Usa, Baja California, Mexico, Sonora, and Mexico. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, european herbal medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
