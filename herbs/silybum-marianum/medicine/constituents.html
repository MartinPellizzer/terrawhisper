10 Medicinal Constituents Of Silybum Marianum (Milk Thistle)

Silybum marianum has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids, which possess potent medicinal properties.

These compounds have been shown to exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and hepatoprotective effects.

For example, the flavonoid quercetin can help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in liver tissue, while silymarin, a complex of silybin, silydianin, and silychristin, has been demonstrated to protect against liver damage and improve symptoms in patients with cirrhosis and liver disease.
