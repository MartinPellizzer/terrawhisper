What is Calamus longistipes?

Calamus longistipes is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is a rhizomatous palm with a stemless habit, and it grows up to 1.5 meters tall. The leaves are pinnate, with 20-30 leaflets, each 30-60 cm long, and a petiole 1-2 meters long. The inflorescence is a panicle, 1-2 meters long, with 10-20 branches, and it produces red flowers.

The plant is native to Southeast Asia, specifically in Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia.
