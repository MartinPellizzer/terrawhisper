Hairy Lime (Tilia Hirsuta)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Tilia hirsuta.

Hairy lime, scientifically known as Tilia hirsuta, is a lesser-known medicinal herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its therapeutic properties and unique characteristics. It is traditionally used as an adaptogen to support the body's ability to manage stress and promote overall wellness. The primary benefits of Hairy lime include its calming effects on the nervous system, its ability to aid digestion, and its potential to reduce inflammation. In traditional European medicine, it has been used to treat respiratory issues, headaches, and digestive disorders, often prepared as a tea or tincture. Modern wellness applications include its use in herbal formulations for anxiety relief and as a natural remedy for mild digestive discomfort, while its rare active compound, tilifolin, contributes to its unique medicinal profile.
