Picea jezoensis var. komarovii

What's the taxonomical classification of Picea jezoensis var. komarovii?

Picea jezoensis var. komarovii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Pinales. As a member of the family Pinaceae, it is further defined by its genus, Picea, and is specifically identified by its botanical designation as the species Picea jezoensis var. komarovii.
