What's the taxonomical classification of Scirpus wichurae?

Scirpus wichurae belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its placement in the order Poales, the plant falls under the family Cyperaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is completed by its assignment to the genus Scirpus, specifically within the species wichurae.
