Jateorhiza Palmata (Jateorhiza palmata) Compounds

The primary compounds of Jateorhiza palmata include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to its astringent effects. Phenolic acids and saponins also play roles in its medicinal properties, while alkaloids and essential oils may support its traditional use in herbal medicine.

Flavonoids in Jateorhiza palmata are known for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
