10 Medicinal Constituents Of Anthemis Laxa (Yarrow)

Anthemis laxa has active constituents such as volatile oils, flavonoids, and sesquiterpenes.

These constituents contribute to yarrow's anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic properties. The presence of these compounds enables yarrow to treat various conditions, such as wounds, digestive issues, and menstrual cramps.

By utilizing yarrow's constituents, people can experience improved wound healing, reduced menstrual discomfort, and alleviated symptoms of gastrointestinal disorders, enhancing their quality of life.
