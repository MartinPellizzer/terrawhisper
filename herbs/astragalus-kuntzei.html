What is Astragalus kuntzei?

Astragalus kuntzei is a perennial legume that belongs to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legume or pea family.

It is characterized by its erect stems, which can grow up to 40 cm tall, and pinnate leaves consisting of 6-12 pairs of leaflets, with each leaflet being 1-2 cm long. The plant produces yellow flowers, 2-4 mm in diameter, in axillary racemes, which are 2-5 cm long. The flowers are zygomorphic and have 5-7 petals, with a calyx consisting of 5 sepals.

The plant's fruit is a dehiscent pod, containing 4-6 seeds, each seed being 3-5 mm long and brown in color.
