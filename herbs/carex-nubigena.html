What's the taxonomical classification of Carex nubigena?

Carex nubigena belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Poales. Within this order, it is a member of the family Cyperaceae, specifically situated in the genus Carex, and is identified by the unique species name nubigena.
