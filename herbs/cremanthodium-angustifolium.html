Cremanthodium angustifolium

What's the taxonomical classification of Cremanthodium angustifolium?

Cremanthodium angustifolium belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Asterales. This plant is further categorized into the family Asteraceae, which is a large and diverse group of flowering plants. Within this family, it is placed in the genus Cremanthodium, and its specific identity is defined by its species name, angustifolium.
