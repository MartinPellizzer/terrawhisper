Carex ligata var. formosensis

Carex ligata var. formosensis, also known as Taiwan sedge, is a type of perennial grass that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

Traditionally, it has been used in herbal medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral effects.

The plant is believed to offer various health benefits, such as relieving respiratory issues, reducing fever, and alleviating pain.

It can be prepared as an infusion or decoction, often combined with other herbs, to create herbal teas or tonics that promote overall well-being.
