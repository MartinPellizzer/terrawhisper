10 Health Benefits Of Annona Squamosa (Custard Apple)

Annona squamosa has health benefits such as treating fever, inflammation, and respiratory issues due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's seeds have been traditionally used to treat digestive problems and its leaves can be used to relieve pain and reduce swelling.

These benefits can improve people's lives by providing natural remedies for common ailments, reducing the need for pharmaceutical interventions and promoting overall health and well-being.
