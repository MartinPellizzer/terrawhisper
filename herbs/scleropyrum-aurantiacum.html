Scleropyrum aurantiacum

Scleropyrum aurantiacum, also known as Golden Yellow Scleropyrum, is a plant species belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

This plant is used in traditional medicine, particularly in China and Southeast Asia, for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antifungal activities.

The health benefits of Scleropyrum aurantiacum include its ability to relieve pain, reduce fever, and alleviate digestive issues, as well as its potential in treating skin conditions and respiratory problems.

Scleropyrum aurantiacum is typically prepared as an herbal tea, infusion, or decoction, and its extracts can also be used in topical creams and ointments for skin treatments.
