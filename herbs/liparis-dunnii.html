Liparis dunnii

What's the taxonomical classification of Liparis dunnii?

Liparis dunnii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, this plant is organized under the order Asparagales. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, specifically falling within the genus Liparis, and is identified by the species epithet dunnii.
