Pilea monilifera

What's the taxonomical classification of Pilea monilifera?

Pilea monilifera belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical order of Rosales, the plant is a member of the family Urticaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Pilea, and its unique species designation, monilifera.
