10 Precautions To Take When Using Rosa Laxa (Wild Rose)

Rosa laxa has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as potential interactions with blood thinners and diabetes medications.

This is crucial because taking wild rose without caution can lead to excessive bleeding or blood sugar fluctuations. For instance, combining wild rose with warfarin may increase the risk of bleeding complications, while using it with metformin may worsen hypoglycemic episodes.

Consultation with a healthcare professional is necessary to weigh the benefits and risks.
