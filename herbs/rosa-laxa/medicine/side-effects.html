10 Possible Side Effects Of Rosa Laxa (Wild Rose)

Rosa laxa has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and increased bleeding due to its anticoagulant properties.

These side effects can be caused by excessive consumption, pre-existing health conditions, or interactions with other medications.

For example, gastrointestinal issues can lead to digestive problems, malnutrition, and discomfort, impacting daily activities and productivity.

These compounds stimulate the muscles in the intestines, leading to increased bowel movement and abdominal cramping.

Additionally, the flavonoids present in Rosa laxa may contribute to stomach irritation by increasing the production of gastric acid, further exacerbating discomfort.
