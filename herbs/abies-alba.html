Abies Alba: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Abies alba, commonly known as the European silver fir, is a coniferous tree native to Europe, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is derived from the needles and bark of the tree and has been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

The health benefits of Abies alba include respiratory support, anti-inflammatory effects, and immune system stimulation.

Its therapeutic actions involve expectorant, antiseptic, and diuretic properties, while its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, terpenes, and essential oils.
