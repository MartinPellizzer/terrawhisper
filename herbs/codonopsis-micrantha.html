Codonopsis micrantha

What's the taxonomical classification of Codonopsis micrantha?

Codonopsis micrantha belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is further categorized within the order Asterales and the family Campanulaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Codonopsis, and its species designation, micrantha.
