Codonoopsis micrantha has long, lanceolate leaves that grow in a basal rosette and small, tubular, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense terminal racemes. The inflorescence consists of multiple small blossoms clustered tightly along a central stalk that rises above the leaves.
