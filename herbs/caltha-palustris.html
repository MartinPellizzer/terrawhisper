What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Caltha palustris has kidney-shaped, bright green leaves that are glossy, cordate to reniform in shape, and possess crenate or wavy margins. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, actinomorphic flowers with five to nine distinct petals that surround a central cluster of numerous stamens. Each flower is supported by a sturdy, succulent stem that can reach heights of up to thirty centimeters.
