Kalmia latifolia f. obtusata

Kalmia latifolia f. obtusata, also known as the mountain laurel, is a variety of the mountain laurel plant (Kalmia latifolia) that is characterized by its obtusate leaf shape.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial compounds.

The health benefits of Kalmia latifolia f. obtusata include reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and supporting immune function, although more research is needed to confirm these effects.

Herbal preparations of the plant are typically made by infusing the leaves in hot water or using the leaves in teas, tinctures, or salves, but caution is advised due to potential toxicity.
