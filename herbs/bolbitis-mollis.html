What is Bolbitis mollis?

Bolbitis mollis is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern with rhizomes that are thick, fleshy, and covered in scales, and it has a distinctive habit of forming dense colonies. The fronds of Bolbitis mollis are evergreen, pinnate, and have a long, slender stipe, with the leaflets being lanceolate and usually pointed at the apex. The frond margins are entire or have a few small teeth, and the sori are round and usually present on the underside of the leaflets.

Bolbitis mollis is native to tropical Africa and Southeast Asia, where it grows in shaded, humid environments.
