Spiraea japonica f. pubescens

Spiraea japonica f. pubescens, also known as Japanese Spirea or pubescent Spirea, is a deciduous shrub that belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is native to East Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities.
