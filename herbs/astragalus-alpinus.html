Astragalus alpinus

Astragalus alpinus, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a type of flowering plant in the legume family.

The plant is used in traditional Chinese medicine and has been found to have various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to boost the immune system, reduce stress, and improve cardiovascular health.

The therapeutic actions of Astragalus alpinus include adaptogenic, anti-fatigue, and immunomodulatory effects, making it a popular natural remedy for fatigue, anxiety, and immune system disorders.

The bioactive constituents of the plant include saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
