Baccharis Shrub (Baccharis trimera) Compounds

The primary compounds in Baccharis trimera include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory actions, while terpenoids and essential oils provide antimicrobial benefits. Coumarin, saponins, glycosides, alkaloids, and anthocyanins also play roles in its medicinal properties.
