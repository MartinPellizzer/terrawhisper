Baccharis trimera has active constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids.

These constituents are responsible for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The flavonoids and phenolic acids in coyote brush may help alleviate symptoms of allergies and asthma by reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, thereby improving the quality of life for individuals with respiratory issues.
