Briggsia mihieri

What's the taxonomical classification of Briggsia mihieri?

Briggsia mihieri belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Lamiales. This plant is a member of the family Gesneriaceae, specifically categorized within the genus Briggsia, and is identified by the species name mihieri.

This traditional application helps to soothe irritations and accelerate the healing of minor cuts.
