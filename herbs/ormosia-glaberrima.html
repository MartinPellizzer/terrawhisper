What's the taxonomical classification of Ormosia glaberrima?

Ormosia glaberrima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Fabales. This plant is a member of the family Fabaceae, specifically falling under the genus Ormosia, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name glaberrima.
