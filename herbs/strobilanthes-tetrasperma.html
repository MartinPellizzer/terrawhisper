Strobilanthes tetrasperma

What's the taxonomical classification of Strobilanthes tetrasperma?

Strobilanthes tetrasperma belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae. This plant is a member of the order Lamiales and is specifically situated within the family Acanthaceae. Taxonomically, it is identified under the genus Strobilanthes, with its specific designation being the species tetrasperma.
