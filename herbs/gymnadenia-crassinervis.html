What's the taxonomical classification of Gymnadenia crassinervis?

Gymnadenia crassinervis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it under the order Asparagales. As a member of the family Orchidaceae, it is further categorized into the genus Gymnadenia, with its specific designation being the species crassinervis.
