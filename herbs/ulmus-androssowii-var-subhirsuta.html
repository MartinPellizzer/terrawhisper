Ulmus androssowii var. subhirsuta

What's the taxonomical classification of Ulmus androssowii var. subhirsuta?

Ulmus androssowii var. subhirsuta belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is specifically nested within the family Ulmaceae. This particular variety is categorized under the genus Ulmus, ultimately reaching its specific taxonomic designation as the species Ulmus androssowii var. subhirsuta.
