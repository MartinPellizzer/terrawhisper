Possible Side Effects of Fragaria canadensis

Fragaria canadensis can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal problems, allergic reactions, and interactions with certain medications.

Allergic reactions to Fragaria canadensis can manifest as skin rashes, itching, and respiratory issues.

Furthermore, individuals with bleeding disorders or taking anticoagulant medications should exercise caution when using the plant, as it may exacerbate bleeding risks.
