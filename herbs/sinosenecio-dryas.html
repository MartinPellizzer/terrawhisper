Sinosenecio dryas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down its taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Sinosenecio, and its unique species designation, dryas.
