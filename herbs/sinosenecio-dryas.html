Sinosenecio dryas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Sinosenecio, and its unique species designation, dryas.
