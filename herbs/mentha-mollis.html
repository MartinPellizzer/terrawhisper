Mentha Mollis

Mentha mollis, commonly known as slender mint or soft mint, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is widely used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.

It contains essential oils rich in menthol, which provide cooling and soothing effects, making it effective for relieving headaches, muscle aches, and respiratory congestion. The plant is also valued for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which support its use in treating digestive issues and skin conditions. In herbal medicine, Mentha mollis is often prepared as a tea or essential oil to promote mental clarity and reduce stress.

Due to its mild and pleasant aroma, it is also used in aromatherapy and as a natural remedy for nausea and cold symptoms.
