Mentha mollis

Mentha mollis, commonly known as Hoary mint, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

This plant is valued for its various health benefits, including its ability to alleviate digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties.

The therapeutic actions of Mentha mollis include its use as a carminative, antispasmodic, and expectorant, making it a popular remedy for respiratory issues and menstrual cramps.

The bioactive constituents of Mentha mollis include volatile oils such as menthone, limonene, and linalool, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
