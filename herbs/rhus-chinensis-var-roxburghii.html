Rhus chinensis var. roxburghii

What's the taxonomical classification of Rhus chinensis var. roxburghii?

Rhus chinensis var. roxburghii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Sapindales. Within the family Anacardiaceae, it is identified under the genus Rhus, specifically designated as the variety roxburghii of the species Rhus chinensis.
