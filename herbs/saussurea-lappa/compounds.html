Gao Ben (Saussurea lappa) Compounds

The primary compounds of Gao Ben include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and terpenoids, known for their anti-inflammatory effects. Coumarins contribute to its anticoagulant properties, while essential oils provide aromatic and therapeutic benefits. These compounds collectively support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
