Gao Ben (Saussurea lappa) Actions

Gao Ben exhibits several therapeutic actions, including anti-inflammatory, carminative, and antispasmodic properties. It acts as a tonic and stimulant, aiding digestion and improving energy. Additionally, it has expectorant, diuretic, and astringent effects, helping to clear phlegm, reduce swelling, and tighten tissues. Its bitter nature also supports overall bodily balance.
