10 Best Saussurea Lappa Health Benefits

Saussurea lappa, also known as Tibetan rhubarb, is a traditional medicinal herb widely used in Ayurveda and Chinese medicine for its various health benefits.

Clinical studies have shown that Saussurea lappa extracts can significantly lower inflammatory markers in both animal and human trials.
