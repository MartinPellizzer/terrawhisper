What's the taxonomical classification of Artemisia phaeolepis?

Artemisia phaeolepis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the subclass Magnoliidae and is part of the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, a large group of flowering plants, and is specifically categorized within the genus Artemisia. Finally, its unique scientific identity is established at the species level as phaeolepis.
