What's the taxonomical classification of Xyris pauciflora?

Xyris pauciflora belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is part of the order Poales and falls under the family Xyridaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized within the genus Xyris, specifically identified by its species name, pauciflora.
