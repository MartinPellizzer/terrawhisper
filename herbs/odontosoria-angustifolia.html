What is Odontosoria angustifolia?

Odontosoria angustifolia is a species of fern in the family Lindsaeaceae, native to tropical Asia.

This epiphytic fern has a slender rhizome, up to 15 cm long, with scales bearing long hairs. Its fronds are bi-pinnate, with pinnae 10-20 pairs, pinnules 2-4 pairs, and a total length of 20-40 cm. The fronds are dark green and have a leathery texture.

The sporangia are located on the underside of the fronds and are of the same color as the fronds.
