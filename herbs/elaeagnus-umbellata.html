Morphologically, it features silvery-white leaves with a dense covering of fine, glandular hairs, and it produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by small, edible berries that range in color from yellow to red.

This versatile plant is characterized by its rapid growth, nitrogen-fixing ability, and adaptability to a wide range of soil conditions.
