Phoebe faberi

What's the taxonomical classification of Phoebe faberi?

Phoebe faberi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the biological lineage of the order Laurales. It is further categorized under the family Lauraceae, which places it within the genus Phoebe. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic designation is completed by its species name, faberi.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

These leaflets exhibit a smooth, glabrous texture on both the upper and lower surfaces. The plant also features small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in axillary panicles.
