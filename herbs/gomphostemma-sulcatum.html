What's the taxonomical classification of Gomphostemma sulcatum?

Gomphostemma sulcatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Following its taxonomic hierarchy, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Within this family, it is identified by the genus Gomphostemma, with its specific designation being the species sulcatum.
