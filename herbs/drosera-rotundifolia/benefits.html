10 Best Drosera Rotundifolia Health Benefits

Drosera rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf sundew, is a carnivorous plant that offers several health benefits due to its rich content of bioactive compounds.

It contains antioxidants such as flavonoids and polyphenols, which help neutralize free radicals in the body, potentially reducing oxidative stress and inflammation. In traditional medicine, it has been used to support respiratory health and treat conditions like bronchitis and asthma due to its expectorant properties. Additionally, its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties may aid in wound healing and the management of skin infections.

However, further scientific research is needed to fully understand and validate its therapeutic potential in modern medicine.
