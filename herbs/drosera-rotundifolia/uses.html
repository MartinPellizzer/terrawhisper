Roundleaf Sundew (Drosera rotundifolia) Uses

Roundleaf Sundew is traditionally used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, pneumonia, and lung congestion. It helps reduce excess mucus production and alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections and allergies. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties support clearing the airways and easing coughing.

Roundleaf Sundew (Drosera rotundifolia) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

Roundleaf Sundew has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its mucilage content helps soothe inflamed airways and reduce coughing. It is often prepared as a tea to ease congestion and promote clearer breathing. This makes it a valuable herb in herbal medicine for managing bronchial inflammation.

2. Respiratory tract infections

Roundleaf Sundew has been traditionally used to support respiratory health, particularly in treating symptoms of respiratory tract infections. Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties may help reduce coughing and clear mucus. It is often prepared as a tea to soothe irritated airways and ease breathing.

3. Pulmonary congestion

Roundleaf Sundew has been traditionally used to address pulmonary congestion due to its expectorant properties. It helps loosen mucus and reduce inflammation in the respiratory tract. This makes it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis and coughs associated with excess mucus. Its use aligns with herbal medicine's focus on supporting natural bodily functions.

4. Excess mucus production

Roundleaf Sundew is used to address excess mucus production due to its demulcent and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps soothe irritated mucous membranes and reduce mucus viscosity, making it beneficial for respiratory and digestive tract conditions. It is often prepared as a tea or tincture for internal use.

5. Pneumonia

Roundleaf Sundew has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health. It is believed to help alleviate symptoms of pneumonia by promoting mucus clearance and reducing inflammation in the airways. Its expectorant properties may aid in expelling phlegm and easing breathing in affected individuals.

6. Respiratory allergies

Roundleaf Sundew has been traditionally used to alleviate respiratory allergies due to its anti-inflammatory and mucolytic properties. It helps reduce nasal congestion and soothe irritated airways, making it a valued herb in herbal medicine for managing seasonal allergies and asthma symptoms.

7. Respiratory infections

9. Cough
