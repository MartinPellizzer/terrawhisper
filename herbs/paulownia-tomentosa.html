Furthermore, Paulownia tomentosa is also susceptible to nematodes, such as Meloidogyne incognita, which can cause root knot disease and stunt plant growth.

These pests and diseases can be managed through integrated pest management practices, including cultural controls, biological control agents, and chemical pesticides, to ensure the health and sustainability of Paulownia tomentosa plantations.
