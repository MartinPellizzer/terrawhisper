    What side effects this plant can have?

    This plant can cause severe mouth irritation, a burning sensation, swelling of the throat, difficulty swallowing, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, and respiratory distress. The presence of calcium oxalate crystals in the tissues leads to immediate localized irritation upon contact with mucous membranes. If the plant is ingested, the chemical reaction often triggers significant gastrointestinal upset such as nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.
