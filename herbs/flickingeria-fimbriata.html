Flickingeria fimbriata

What's the taxonomical classification of Flickingeria fimbriata?

Flickingeria fimbriata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is placed in the order Asparagales. This plant is a member of the Orchidaceae family, falling under the genus Flickingeria, and is specifically identified by its species name, fimbriata.
