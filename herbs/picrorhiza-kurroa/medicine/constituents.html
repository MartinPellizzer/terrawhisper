10 Medicinal Constituents Of Picrorhiza Kurroa (Kutki)

Picrorhiza kurroa has active constituents such as iridoids, phenolic acids, and saponins.

These constituents exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and hepatoprotective properties. The iridoids, particularly kutkin and picrorhizin, enhance liver function and reduce oxidative stress.

The phenolic acids contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Saponins exhibit adaptogenic properties, enabling the body to cope with stress and improve overall well-being.

These medicinal properties can alleviate symptoms of liver disease and improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions.
