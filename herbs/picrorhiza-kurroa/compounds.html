Kuroa (Picrorhiza kurroa) Compounds

Kuroa contains several primary active compounds, including alkaloids, terpenoids, and flavonoids. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Other notable components include glycosides, phenolic acids, and saponins, which support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
