Heracleum bivittatum

What's the taxonomical classification of Heracleum bivittatum?

Heracleum bivittatum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant is further organized under the order Apiales and falls within the family Apiaceae. Finally, its taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Heracleum, leading to its specific designation as the species bivittatum.
