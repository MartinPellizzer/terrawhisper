Urena (Urena lobata)

Urena (Urena lobata) is a member of the Malvaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, Australia, and Pacific Islands. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and seeds have been used for decoctions, infusions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and astringent actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and ayurvedic medicine.
