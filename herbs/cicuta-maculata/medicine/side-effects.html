5. Provokes cardiac arrhythmia

Cicuta maculata provokes cardiac arrhythmia due to its toxic compounds, specifically cicutoxin and cicutin.

These alkaloids can disrupt the normal functioning of the heart's electrical system, leading to irregular heartbeat rhythms.

When ingested or absorbed through the skin, these toxins can bind to sodium channels in cardiac cells, causing aberrant electrical activity that results in arrhythmias.
