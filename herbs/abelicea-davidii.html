Abelicea davidii

Ablicea davidii, also known as Abelmoschus, is a tropical plant native to Asia and Africa, belonging to the mallow family (Malvaceae).

The plant's leaves and roots are rich in medicinal properties, containing compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues.

The health benefits of Abelicea davidii include reducing inflammation, improving wound healing, and exhibiting antioxidant and anti-cancer properties.

Abelicea davidii is often prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and extracts for use in traditional medicine, as well as being used in modern pharmaceutical formulations.
