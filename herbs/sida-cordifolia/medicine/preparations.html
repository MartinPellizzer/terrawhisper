10 Medicinal Preparations Of Sida Cordifolia (Country Mallow)

Sida cordifolia has several medicinal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions.

These preparations are useful for various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and inflammation. The root of the plant is particularly valued for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for treating wounds, skin conditions, and joint pain.

Additionally, the plant's leaves and flowers are used to make a decoction, which is helpful in treating digestive issues and respiratory problems.

This article explains in details the 10 best medicinal preparations of Sida cordifolia.
