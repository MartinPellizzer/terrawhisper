Heartleaf Sida (Sida cordifolia) Compounds

The primary compounds in Heartleaf Sida include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, which contribute to astringency. Phenolic acids and glycosides also play roles in its medicinal effects. Saponins and coumarin provide additional therapeutic benefits, while alkaloids and terpenoids contribute to its pharmacological profile. Anthocyanins and essential oils add to its diverse chemical composition.
