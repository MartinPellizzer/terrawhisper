Hieracium lillense

The therapeutic actions of Hieracium lillense include its ability to stimulate the immune system and exhibit antimicrobial activity, making it a potential treatment for various infections and conditions.

Possible Side Effects of Hieracium lillense

Hieracium lillense can have side effects if used improperly, such as inducing vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach cramps due to its emetic and purgative properties.

Additionally, improper use of Hieracium lillense may also cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory issues in some individuals.

Prolonged exposure to the plant's toxic compounds can lead to more severe health complications, including liver and kidney damage.
