10 Precautions To Take When Using Geranium Sanguineum (Bloody Cranesbill)

Geranium sanguineum has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as being cautious with its use in pregnant or breastfeeding women.

Taking this herb without proper precautions can lead to uterine stimulation, potentially causing miscarriage or preterm labor. For instance, if a pregnant woman were to consume Geranium sanguineum without consulting a healthcare professional, it could trigger uterine contractions, resulting in a miscarriage.

Therefore, it's crucial to consult a healthcare expert before using Geranium sanguineum medicinally.

1. Follow proper preparation instructions

When using Geranium sanguineum medicinally, it's important to take in recommended doses.

This is crucial because the plant contains coumarin compounds that can interact with anticoagulant medications and increase the risk of bleeding when consumed in excessive amounts. Additionally, high doses may cause gastrointestinal side effects such as nausea and diarrhea.

By adhering to recommended dosages, individuals can minimize potential risks and ensure safe and effective use of this herb for medicinal purposes.
