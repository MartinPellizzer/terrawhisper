What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Saxifraga imparilis has a basal rosette of fleshy, spatulate leaves that are dark green, slightly succulent, and characterized by crenate or rounded margins. The blossoms feature five white petals that are often marked with delicate reddish or purplish spots near the center.
