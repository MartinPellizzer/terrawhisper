Dactylorhiza × dufftiana

Dactylorhiza × dufftiana, a rare hybrid orchid, is a cross between Dactylorhiza fuchsii and Dactylorhiza maculata.

It is believed to have medicinal properties, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.

The health benefits of Dactylorhiza × dufftiana include its potential to aid in wound healing, reduce swelling, and alleviate respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma.
