Leptaulus daphnoides

What's the taxonomical classification of Leptaulus daphnoides?

Leptaulus daphnoides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Aquifoliales. This plant is specifically situated within the family Cardiopteridaceae, falling under the genus Leptaulus, and is identified by its unique species designation, daphnoides.
