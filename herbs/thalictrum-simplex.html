Thalictrum simplex belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Thalictrum, with its specific designation being the species simplex.
