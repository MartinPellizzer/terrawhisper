Friitillaria przewalskii has bulbous rhizomes, lanceolate leaves with acute tips, and bell-shaped flowers that typically feature reddish-brown or purplish spots on a yellowish background. Its nodding, pendulous blossoms are characterized by a distinctive patterned surface and slightly recurved tepals. The stem is generally smooth and sturdy enough to support the weight of the drooping blooms.
