Aquilegia ecalcara belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Within this order, the plant is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Specifically, it is categorized under the genus Aquilegia, with its unique biological identity defined by the species name ecalcara.
