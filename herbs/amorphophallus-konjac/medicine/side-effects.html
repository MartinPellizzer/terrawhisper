10 Possible Side Effects Of Amorphophallus Konjac (Konjac)

These side effects can occur due to consuming excessive amounts of konjac, causing an imbalance of fiber and electrolytes in the body.

For instance, severe diarrhea can lead to dehydration, which can worsen lives by disrupting daily activities, and abdominal pain can cause discomfort and interfere with work or school.
