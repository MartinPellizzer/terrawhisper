Seriphidium amoenum

What's the taxonomical classification of Seriphidium amoenum?

Seriphidium amoenum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific identity is defined by the genus Seriphiidm and the species amoenum.

Some traditional medicinal practices also utilize the plant to manage symptoms related to inflammation and fever. Its phytochemical properties are believed to provide antimicrobial and analgesic effects to the patient. These applications highlight its importance in regional ethnobotanical knowledge for addressing common physical discomforts.
