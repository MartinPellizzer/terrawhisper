Seriphidium amoenum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it falls under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. Finally, its specific identity is defined by the genus Seriphiidm and the species amoenum.
