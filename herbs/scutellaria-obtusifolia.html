What's the taxonomical classification of Scutellaria obtusifolia?

Scutelariia obtusifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Lamiales. It is a member of the family Lamiaceae, specifically situated within the genus Scutellaria, with the specific epithet obtusifolia designating its unique species identity.
