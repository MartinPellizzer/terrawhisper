10 Possible Side Effects Of Crataegus Oxyacantha (Hawthorn)

These side effects are often caused by excessive consumption, interactions with other medications, or allergic reactions.

For instance, individuals with pre-existing kidney or heart conditions may experience severe complications, such as cardiac arrhythmias or kidney damage, which can severely impact their quality of life, leading to fatigue, pain, and reduced mobility.
