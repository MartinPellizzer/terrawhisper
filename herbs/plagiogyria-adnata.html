Plagiogyria adnata

What's the taxonomical classification of Plagiogyria adnata?

Plaigiogyria adnata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Polypodiinae, this plant is situated in the order Cyatheales. It is further categorized under the family Cyatheaceae, falling within the genus Plaigiogyria, and is specifically identified by the species name adnata.
