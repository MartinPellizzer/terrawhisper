10 Medicinal Constituents Of Solanum Dulcamara (Bittersweet)

Solanum dulcamara has active constituents such as glycoalkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These constituents have medicinal properties that include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and diuretic effects. The glycoalkaloids, particularly solamargine, can help to reduce inflammation and alleviate pain, while the flavonoids and phenolic acids have antioxidant properties that can protect against oxidative stress and promote wound healing.

These effects can improve people's lives by providing relief from arthritis, skin conditions, and urinary issues.
