What's the taxonomical classification of Androsace sarmentosa?

Androsace sarmentosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Ericales. This plant is a member of the family Primulaceae, falling under the genus Androsace, and is specifically identified by its species designation, sarmentoosa.
