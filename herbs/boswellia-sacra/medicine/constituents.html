10 Medicinal Constituents Of Boswellia Sacra (Frankincense)

Boswellia sacra has active constituents such as boswellic acids, alpha-pinene, and beta-boswellic acid.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, contributing to its medicinal use in reducing pain, swelling, and inflammation. For instance, boswellic acids have shown potential in reducing symptoms of osteoarthritis, while alpha-pinene may alleviate anxiety and promote relaxation.

Overall, the constituents of frankincense can improve people's lives by providing relief from chronic pain and anxiety.
