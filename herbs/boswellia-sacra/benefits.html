1. Reduces inflammation

How this herb helps with reduces inflammation?

Boswellia sacra reduces inflammation because it contains bioactive constituents such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

Its traditional use in Ayurvedic medicine aligns with its modern scientific validation for anti-inflammatory properties.
