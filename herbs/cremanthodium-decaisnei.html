Cremanthodium decaisnei has erect, herbaceous stems that typically reach heights between 30 and 100 centimeters with leaves arranged in opposite pairs. The plant's root system is comprised of slender rhizomes that allow for gradual spreading through the soil. These morphological traits distinguish the species within its specific ecological niche.
