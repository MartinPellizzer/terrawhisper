What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Salvia digitaloides has upright, square-shaped stems that support opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated margins and a textured, rugose surface. The flowers are tubular and bilabiate, featuring a distinct color palette often ranging from pale blue to violet.
