What are the herbal preparations of Scleria boivinii?

Scleria boivinii is prepared as a decoction, a topical paste, or a liquid infusion to treat various ailments. The dried roots are often boiled in water to create a concentrated decoction for internal consumption. For skin conditions, the fresh plant matter is crushed into a fine paste and applied directly to the affected areas. Some practitioners also utilize infusions where the leaves are steeped in hot water to extract medicinal properties. These diverse methods allow the plant's bioactive compounds to be utilized effectively for both systemic and localized treatments.
