What's the taxonomical classification of Scleria boivinii?

Scleria boivinii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is positioned in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The taxonomic hierarchy continues through the order Poales, placing it within the family Cyperaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Scleria, with its specific designation being the species boivinii.
