What's the taxonomical classification of Ainsliaea latifolia?

Ainsliaea latifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the subclass Magnoliidae and falls under the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, specifically nested within the genus Ainsliaea, and is identified by the unique species name latifolia.
