Girardinia diversifolia subsp. diversifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Girardinia diversifolia subsp. diversifolia?

Girardinia diversifolia subsp. diversifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Rosales and is a part of the family Urticaceae. At the most specific levels of its biological hierarchy, this plant is categorized under the genus Girardinia, ultimately reaching its taxonomic designation as the species Girardinia diversifolia subsp. diversifolia.
