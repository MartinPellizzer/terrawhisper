Prunus glabra

Prunus glabra, also known as the naked or smooth cherry, is a species of cherry tree that is native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas and parts of China.

The bark and leaves of this plant are used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, and it has been used to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive problems.

The health benefits of Prunus glabra include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and boost the immune system.

Prunus glabra is typically prepared as a herbal tea or decoction, and it can also be made into a tincture or added to other herbal remedies to enhance their effects.
