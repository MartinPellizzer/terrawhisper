10 Medicinal Constituents Of Marrubium Vulgare (Horehound)

Marrubium vulgare has active constituents such as marrubin, ursolic acid, and volatile oils.

These constituents are responsible for its expectorant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties. The expectorant properties of marrubin can help relieve respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, while the antimicrobial properties can combat infections.

By alleviating respiratory problems, horehound can improve the quality of life for individuals suffering from these conditions, allowing them to breathe and sleep better.
