Cyananthus inflatus

What's the taxonomical classification of Cyananthus inflatus?

Cyananthus inflatus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is situated in the subclass Magnoliidae and follows the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Campanulaceae and is specifically categorized under the genus Cyananthus, with its unique identification being the species inflatus.

Additionally, some traditional applications suggest it may assist in regulating bodily heat and improving overall pulmonary function. Such qualities make it a significant component in various herbal formulations used to treat chronic respiratory ailments.
