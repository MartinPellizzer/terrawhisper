What is Ocotea pittieri?

Ocotea pittieri is a species of plant belonging to the family Lauraceae.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that grows up to 20 meters in height, with a diameter of up to 30 centimeters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a length of 7-15 centimeters, with a width of 2-4 centimeters, and have a leathery texture. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in clusters at the ends of branches.

The plant is native to the tropical forests of Central America, particularly in Costa Rica and Panama.
