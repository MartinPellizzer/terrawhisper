What are the side effects of Senna alata when used improperly?

Additionally, Senna alata may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, which can exacerbate their side effects or alter their effectiveness. Moreover, it has been reported to reduce the absorption of nutrients from food, particularly fat-soluble vitamins like vitamin K, D, E, and A, which can lead to deficiencies over time.
