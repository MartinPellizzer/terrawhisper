Banisteria Caapi Vine (Banisteria caapi)

Banisteria Caapi Vine (Banisteria caapi) is a member of the Loganiaceae family, native to Amazon Basin, South America, and Brazil. Traditionally, its bark, stems, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and tinctures.

This herb is particularly valued for its sedative, tonic, and antispasmodic actions, and has a long history of use in native american herbal medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and traditional chinese medicine.
