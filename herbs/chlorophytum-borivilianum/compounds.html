Chlorophytum Borivilianum (Chlorophytum borivilianum) Compounds

Chlorophytum borivilianum contains several primary active compounds, including flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids. These compounds contribute to its medicinal properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Other notable constituents are glycosides, phenolic acids, and tannins, which support its traditional use in herbal medicine.
