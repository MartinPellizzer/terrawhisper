10 Medicinal Constituents Of Chlorophytum Borivilianum (Sarpagandha)

Chlorophytum borivilianum has active constituents such as flavonoids, saponins, and glycosides.

These constituents are responsible for its medicinal properties, including antihypertensive, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects. The flavonoids can help reduce blood pressure and cholesterol levels, improving cardiovascular health. The saponins can help in reducing inflammation and pain, providing relief from arthritis and other inflammatory diseases.

Overall, these constituents can significantly improve the quality of life for people suffering from hypertension, cardiovascular disease, and other conditions.
