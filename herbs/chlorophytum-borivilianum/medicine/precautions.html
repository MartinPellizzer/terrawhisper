10 Precautions To Take When Using Chlorophytum Borivilianum (Sarpagandha)

Chlorophytum borivilianum has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as consulting with a healthcare professional, especially for pregnant women or those with thyroid conditions.

Failing to do so may lead to adverse effects on the thyroid or fetal development. For example, not monitoring thyroid levels while using sarpagandha can result in hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism.

It's also essential to follow the recommended dosage to avoid side effects like dizziness, nausea, and digestive issues.
