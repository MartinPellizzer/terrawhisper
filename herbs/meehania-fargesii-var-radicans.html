Meehania fargesii var. radicans

What's the taxonomical classification of Meehania fargesii var. radicans?

Meehania fargesii var. radicans belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is further nested within the family Lamiaceae. The plant is identified at the genus level as Meehania, specifically categorized as the variety radicans within the species Meehania fargesii.
