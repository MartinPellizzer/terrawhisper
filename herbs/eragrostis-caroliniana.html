What is Eragrostis caroliniana?

Eragrostis caroliniana is a species of grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial, clump-forming grass that typically grows 30-120 cm tall. The leaves are linear, 3-15 mm wide, and have a prominent midrib. The inflorescence is a panicle, 5-20 cm long, with spikelets that are 2-4 mm long and have 2-3 florets.

The spikelets have a glume that is as long as or slightly longer than the lemma.
