What's the taxonomical classification of Primula vialii?

Primula vialii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Ericales. Its taxonomic progression continues through the family Primulaceae, where it is placed within the genus Primula, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species vialii.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Primula vialii has a basal rosette of large, crinkled, oblong leaves that are dark green and possess serrated edges. These striking inflorescences sit atop thick, leafless stalks that emerge from the center of the foliage. The stems are relatively sturdy to support the heavy, globular blooms during the flowering season.
