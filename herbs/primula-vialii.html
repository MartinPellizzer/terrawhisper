Primula vialii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is classified under the subclass Magnoliidae and the order Ericales. Its taxonomic progression continues through the family Primulaceae, where it is placed within the genus Primula, ultimately reaching its specific designation as the species vialii.
