Ainsliaea latifolia var. obovata

What's the taxonomical classification of Ainsliaea latifolia var. obovata?

Ainsliaea latifolia var. obovata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is organized under the order Asterales and is a member of the family Asteraceae. More specifically, this plant is situated within the genus Ainsliaea, ultimately falling under the specific botanical designation of the species Ainsliaea latifolia var. obovata.
