Manglietia patungenuis has elliptical to oblong leaves that measure approximately 10 to 20 centimeters in length and are characterized by an entire margin and an acute apex. These blossoms feature several thick, spreading petals and a prominent central receptacle. The woody stem of the plant is robust and supports a spreading canopy structure.
