Pueraria Mirifica

Pueraria mirifica, commonly known as Kwao Krua, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, and has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties.

It is renowned for its high content of phytoestrogens, such as miroestrol and dehydroepiandrosterone (DHEA), which are believed to support hormonal balance and enhance vitality. In traditional medicine, Pueraria mirifica has been utilized to treat conditions related to hormonal imbalances, including menopausal symptoms, breast pain, and fatigue. Modern research suggests that it may also possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and potential anti-cancer properties.

Due to its diverse bioactive compounds, Pueraria mirifica is increasingly being explored as a natural supplement for promoting overall wellness and addressing various health concerns.
