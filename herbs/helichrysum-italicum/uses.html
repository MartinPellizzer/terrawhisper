Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) Uses

Everlasting is commonly used for respiratory infections, asthma, cough, and bronchitis. It helps alleviate respiratory allergies, nasal and chest congestion, and sinus issues. It also reduces skin inflammation, making it useful for various inflammatory conditions. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties support these therapeutic applications.

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) Monograph

1. Respiratory infections

Helichrysum italicum supports respiratory health by reducing inflammation and soothing irritated airways. Its antimicrobial properties help combat bacterial and viral infections, while its expectorant effects ease coughing and clear mucus. Often used in steam inhalations or chest rubs, it is a valuable herb for managing symptoms of colds, bronchitis, and other respiratory conditions.

2. Asthma

Helichrysum italicum may support respiratory health by reducing inflammation and easing bronchial constriction. It is often used in herbal formulations to help manage asthma symptoms, though it should not replace prescribed medical treatments. Its expectorant properties may assist in clearing mucus, supporting easier breathing. Always consult a qualified practitioner before use.

3. Cough

Helichrysum italicum is used to support respiratory health, particularly for coughs. Its expectorant properties help loosen mucus, making it easier to expel. It also has anti-inflammatory effects that may reduce irritation in the throat and airways. This makes it a valuable herb in herbal remedies for persistent or productive coughs.

4. Bronchitis

Helichrysum italicum has been used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health, particularly in cases of bronchitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties help reduce bronchial irritation and ease coughing. It may also aid in clearing mucus and improving lung function. Traditionally, it is often combined with other respiratory herbs for enhanced efficacy.

5. Respiratory allergies

Helichrysum italicum supports respiratory allergy relief by reducing inflammation and suppressing histamine release. Its antihistaminic and anti-inflammatory properties help alleviate symptoms like sneezing, congestion, and itching. It is often used in herbal formulations for seasonal allergies and respiratory conditions.

6. Nasal congestion

Helichrysum italicum is used to relieve nasal congestion due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce mucus production and soothe irritated nasal passages. Often used in aromatherapy or as a steam inhalant, it provides natural relief for colds and sinus issues. Its essential oil is a common ingredient in herbal remedies for respiratory discomfort.

7. Chest congestion

Helichrysum italicum is used to support respiratory health, particularly for chest congestion. Its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties help loosen mucus and reduce inflammation in the airways. It can be used in steam inhalations or as a tincture to ease breathing and alleviate symptoms of respiratory discomfort.

8. Allergic rhinitis

Helichrysum italicum has been used in herbal medicine to support respiratory health, particularly for allergic rhinitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antihistaminic properties may help reduce nasal congestion and alleviate symptoms. It is often used in aromatherapy and as a tincture to support immune and respiratory function.

9. Sinus congestion

Helichrysum italicum is used to alleviate sinus congestion due to its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties. It helps reduce mucus production and soothe nasal passages, often used in steam inhalations or as a topical application. Its essential oil is particularly valued for its ability to support respiratory clarity and ease breathing in cases of sinusitis or colds.

10. Skin inflammation

Helichrysum italicum is used to reduce skin inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It helps soothe conditions like eczema and psoriasis by calming irritation and promoting skin healing. Essential oil applications or topical preparations are common methods for addressing inflammatory skin issues.
