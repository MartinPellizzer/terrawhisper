10 Best Helichrysum Italicum Health Benefits

Helichrysum italicum, also known as immortelle, is renowned for its wide range of health benefits due to its rich composition of bioactive compounds.

It possesses potent anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce swelling and pain, making it beneficial for conditions such as arthritis and muscle inflammation. The essential oil of Helichrysum italicum is also known for its ability to promote skin healing and reduce the appearance of scars and stretch marks. Additionally, it has been used in aromatherapy to alleviate stress and anxiety, supporting mental well-being.

Its antimicrobial and antifungal properties make it a valuable natural remedy for treating infections and maintaining overall health.

1. Reduces inflammation

Helichrysum italicum reduces inflammation by interacting with the body's inflammatory pathways and modulating key cytokines involved in the inflammatory response.

Its essential oil contains bioactive compounds such as sesquiterpene lactones, which have been shown to exhibit anti-inflammatory properties in scientific studies. This makes it a valuable natural remedy for conditions like arthritis, skin inflammation, and respiratory issues. The anti-inflammatory effects of Helichrysum italicum can be both topical and systemic, depending on the method of application.

As a result, it is widely used in aromatherapy and herbal medicine to support overall inflammatory health.
