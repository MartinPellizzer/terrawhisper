Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) Compounds

The primary compounds of Everlasting include essential oils, terpenoids, and flavonoids. These compounds contribute to its aromatic profile and therapeutic properties, such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. Phenolic acids and coumarin also play roles in its medicinal uses.

Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum) Monograph

1. Essential oil

The essential oil of Helichrysum italicum contains bioactive compounds like bisabolol and chamazulene. These compounds contribute to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties. The oil is widely used in herbal medicine for skin healing and respiratory support. It is extracted through steam distillation from the plant's flowers.

Terpenoids are a key class of compounds in Everlasting (Helichrysum italicum). They contribute to the plant's aroma and have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties.
