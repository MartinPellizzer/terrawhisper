The therapeutic actions of Acetosella micrantha include its ability to act as an anti-diabetic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant agent.

Possible Side Effects of Acetosella micrantha

Acetosella micrantha can have side effects if used improperly, such as stomach upset, nausea, and vomiting due to its high concentration of anthraquinone glycosides.

Ingestion of large amounts can also cause diarrhea, abdominal cramps, and electrolyte imbalance.

Additionally, improper use of the plant can lead to allergic reactions, such as skin irritation and respiratory issues in sensitive individuals.

In severe cases, excessive consumption of Acetosella micrantha may cause kidney damage and interact with certain medications, exacerbating underlying health conditions.
