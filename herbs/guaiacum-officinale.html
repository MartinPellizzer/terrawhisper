Guaiacum Officinale: Medicinal Uses And Benefits

Guaiacum officinale, commonly known as beach plum, is a small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of the southeastern United States.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The herb is believed to support joint health, reduce inflammation, and aid in the treatment of skin conditions.

Its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic actions.
