10 Medicinal Constituents Of Spigelia Marilandica (Indian Pink)

Spigelia marilandica, also known as Indian pink, has active constituents such as spigeline, spiggin, and indole alkaloids.

These compounds possess medicinal properties that include anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and expectorant effects. They help alleviate coughs, relieve muscle spasms, and reduce inflammation, providing relief to individuals with respiratory issues, arthritis, and muscle strain.

Regular consumption of Indian pink's extracts may improve overall well-being and reduce the burden of chronic conditions.
