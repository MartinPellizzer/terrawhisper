American Ginseng, scientifically known as Panax quinquefolium, is a medicinal herb native to North America, renowned for its adaptogenic properties and long history of use in traditional medicine.

This plant is valued for its ability to enhance energy, reduce stress, and support immune function, making it a popular choice for those seeking natural wellness solutions.

Traditionally used by Indigenous peoples and later by Chinese herbalists, American Ginseng was believed to promote vitality and longevity, often consumed as a tonic during times of physical or mental fatigue.

In modern wellness practices, it is commonly used to support cognitive function, improve focus, and aid in recovery from illness or stress.

One of its unique features is its distinctive sweet, slightly bitter flavor, and it contains a rare active compound called ginsenosides, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
