Purple Violet, scientifically known as viola violacea, is a medicinal herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, prized for its historical and contemporary uses in wellness.

This adaptogen is renowned for its calming properties and its ability to support respiratory and immune health, making it a popular remedy for colds, coughs, and stress-related ailments.

Traditionally, it has been used in herbal medicine to soothe sore throats and reduce inflammation, with cultural significance in European folk remedies and traditional Chinese medicine.

In modern wellness, it is valued for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, often incorporated into teas, tinctures, and supplements.

One unique feature of Purple Violet is its distinctive violet hue and subtle floral aroma, along with the presence of iridoid compounds that contribute to its therapeutic properties.
