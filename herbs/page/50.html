Scutellaria, scientifically known as Scutellaria lateriflora, is a medicinal herb commonly referred to as blue flag or skullcap, renowned for its adaptogenic and calming properties.

This perennial plant, native to North America, contains a variety of bioactive compounds, including flavonoids and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Traditionally used by Native American tribes for its calming and antispasmodic properties, it has been employed to treat anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues.

In modern wellness practices, Scutellaria is valued for its ability to support mental clarity, reduce stress, and promote emotional balance.

Its unique feature is the presence of the rare alkaloid baicalin, which has been studied for its anti-inflammatory and neuroprotective benefits.
