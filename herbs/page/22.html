Field Horsetail, scientifically known as Equisetum arvense, is a medicinal herb that has been used for centuries for its unique properties and therapeutic benefits.

It is also classified as an adaptogen, helping the body resist stress and maintain balance.

The plant is rich in silica, which supports bone and joint health, and contains compounds like equisetin, a rare active compound with potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Traditionally, it has been used in herbal medicine to treat respiratory conditions, urinary tract issues, and skin ailments, with historical records showing its use by ancient civilizations like the Greeks and Romans.

In modern wellness, Field Horsetail is valued for its role in supporting detoxification, enhancing skin health, and promoting overall vitality, making it a versatile herb in both traditional and contemporary herbal practices.
