Erect Potentilla, scientifically known as Potentilla erecta, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and the Himalayas, valued for its medicinal properties and adaptogenic qualities.

It is commonly used in traditional medicine for its ability to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote overall vitality.

Historically, it has been employed in Ayurvedic and Tibetan healing practices to treat ailments such as colds, fevers, and digestive issues, while its unique combination of flavonoids and tannins contributes to its therapeutic effects.

In modern wellness, it is increasingly used as a natural supplement to enhance resilience to stress and support respiratory health.

One of its distinctive features is the presence of a rare active compound called potentillin, which is believed to contribute to its adaptogenic properties and has sparked interest in contemporary herbal research.
