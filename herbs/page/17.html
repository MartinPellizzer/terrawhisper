Trifoliate Coptis, scientifically known as Coptis trifolia, is a medicinal herb native to East Asia, prized for its rich alkaloid content and traditional healing properties.

This plant is commonly used as an adaptogen and herbal remedy, offering a range of health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and digestive support properties.

Historically, it has been a key component in traditional Chinese medicine, where it was used to treat infections, fever, and gastrointestinal disorders.

In modern wellness practices, it is valued for its potential role in immune support, detoxification, and as a natural remedy for skin conditions.

One of its unique features is the presence of the rare alkaloid coptisine, which contributes to its distinct bitter flavor and potent therapeutic effects.
