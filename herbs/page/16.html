Tangshen, scientifically known as Codonopsis tangshen, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb prized for its adaptogenic properties and nourishing effects on the body.

This plant, often referred to as "the ginseng of the south," is valued for its ability to enhance vitality, boost immunity, and support digestive health.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Tangshen has been used for centuries to treat fatigue, respiratory conditions, and to strengthen the spleen and lungs.

Modern wellness applications include its use in herbal supplements to combat stress and improve energy levels, making it a popular choice for those seeking natural health support.

One of its unique features is its distinct sweet, slightly bitter taste, which is attributed to its bioactive compounds like polysaccharides, and it has a long history of cultivation in the mountainous regions of China.
