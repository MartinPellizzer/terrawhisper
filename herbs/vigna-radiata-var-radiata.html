Vigna radiata var. radiata

What's the taxonomical classification of Vigna radiata var. radiata?

Vigna radiata var. radiata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It falls under the class Equisetopsida and the order Fabales, placing it within the diverse family Fabaceae. Taxonomically, it is identified under the genus Vigna and is specifically categorized as the species Vigna.
