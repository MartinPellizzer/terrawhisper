What is Callianthe monteiroi?

Callianthe monteiroi is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae, native to Brazil.

It is a shrub that grows up to 3 meters in height, with a woody stem and a branched habit. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and serrated, measuring 4-12 cm in length and 2-6 cm in width. The flowers are showy and purple, with five petals and a prominent stamen, forming in axillary clusters.

The plant produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous seeds, and its growth habits and characteristics make it a unique member of the Malvaceae family.
