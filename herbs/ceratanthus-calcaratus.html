What's the taxonomical classification of Ceratanthus calcaratus?

Ceratanthus calcaratus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Its taxonomic progression continues through the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Ceratanthus, and its unique species designation, calcaratus.
