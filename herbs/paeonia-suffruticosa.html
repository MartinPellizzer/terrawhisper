Paeonia suffruticosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Paeonia suffruticosa?

Paeonia suffruticosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Saxifrages (Saxifragales). The plant is a member of the family Paeoniaceae and is specifically categorized within the genus Paeonia, eventually reaching its unique designation at the species level as Paeonia suffruticosa.
