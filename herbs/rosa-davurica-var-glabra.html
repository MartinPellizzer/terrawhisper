Rosa davurica var. glabra

What's the taxonomical classification of Rosa davurica var. glabra?

Rosa davurica var. glabra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is positioned in the order Rosales and falls under the family Rosaceae. This specific variety is a member of the genus Rosa, representing a distinct taxonomic group within the species Rosa davurica.
