Acuan illinoense

Acuan illinoense, also known as Illinois acuan or Illinois water dock, is a perennial plant species native to the eastern United States.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The health benefits of Acuan illinoense include treating fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, as well as being used as a diuretic.

Acuan illinoense can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, or salves for topical use, and its root is often used in traditional medicine.
