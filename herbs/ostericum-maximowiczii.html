What's the taxonomical classification of Ostericum maximowiczii?

Ostericum maximowiczii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is a representative of the family Apiaceae, falling specifically into the genus Ostericum, with the specific epithet maximowiczii defining the species.
