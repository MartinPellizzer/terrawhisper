Wightia speciosissima

What's the taxonomical classification of Wightia speciosissima?

Wightia speciosissima belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida, it is categorized under the subclass Magnoliidae and falls into the order Lamiales. As a member of the family Paulowniaceae, it is further distinguished by its genus, Wightia, and is specifically identified by its species name, speciosissima.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Wightia speciosissima has large, woody, evergreen leaves that are pinnately compound and reach significant lengths.

The reproductive structures produce woody capsules that eventually house the seeds.
