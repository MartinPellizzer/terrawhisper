What is Pentacalia caliana?

Pentacalia caliana is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb, typically 30-100 cm tall, with a woody base and a simple or branched stem. The leaves are oppositely arranged, elliptic, 5-15 cm long, and 2-5 cm wide, with a ciliate margin. The inflorescence is a corymboid or capitate array of 10-40 phyllaries, surrounded by 5-10 linear to lanceolate bracts, and 10-20 purple to pinkish flowers.

Each flower head has a tubular corolla, 10-12 mm long, with 5 lobes, and a discoid shape.
