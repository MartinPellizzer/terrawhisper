Sisymbrium altissimum

What's the taxonomical classification of Sisymbrium altissimum?

Sisymbrium altissimum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical classification, it is classified under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further falls under the order Brassicales and is a member of the family Brassicaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Sisymbrium, and its specific species name, altissimum.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?
