What is Nageia excelsa?

Nageia excelsa is a species of evergreen coniferous tree that belongs to the family Podocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, specifically in Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The tree can grow up to 50 meters in height, with a straight columnar trunk and a narrow, conical crown. Its leaves are small, scale-like, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem; they are dark green in color and have a leathery texture.

Nageia excelsa is a dioecious species, with male and female cones produced on separate trees.
