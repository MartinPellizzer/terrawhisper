10 Precautions To Take When Using Psidium Guajava (Guava)

Psidium guajava has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as monitoring blood sugar levels, as it may interact with diabetes medications and cause hypoglycemia.

Additionally, individuals with bleeding disorders or taking anticoagulant medications should be cautious, as guava may increase the risk of bleeding.

Failure to take these precautions can lead to adverse effects, such as hypoglycemic episodes or excessive bleeding.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Psidium guajava medicinally.

As a precautionary measure, it is crucial to consult with a healthcare professional before using Psidium guajava medicinally during these critical periods of life.
