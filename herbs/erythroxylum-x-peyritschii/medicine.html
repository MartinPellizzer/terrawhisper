What are the medicinal preparations of Erythroxylum × peyritschii?

Erythroxylum × peyritschii, also known as yopo, has medicinal preparations such as tinctures and decoctions that are traditionally used to treat various ailments.

The plant's seeds are said to have anxiolytic and antispasmodic properties, and are used to calm anxiety and relieve muscle spasms. Additionally, yopo is used to treat respiratory issues, including bronchitis and asthma, due to its expectorant properties.

Its medicinal applications are rooted in traditional Amazonian medicine.
