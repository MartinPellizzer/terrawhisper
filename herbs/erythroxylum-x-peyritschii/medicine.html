What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Erythroxylum × Peyritschii (Yopo)?

Erythroxylum × peyritschii has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving respiratory function.

The herb contains medicinal constituents like alkaloids, glycosides, and terpenes that contribute to its therapeutic properties. Yopo is often prepared in various forms, including teas, tinctures, and capsules, which can be used to treat conditions like bronchitis and asthma. However, side effects like dizziness and allergic reactions may occur, particularly in individuals with pre-existing medical conditions.

It is recommended to consult a healthcare professional before using yopo, as individual tolerance and interactions with other medications should be carefully considered.
