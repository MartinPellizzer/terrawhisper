Kava (Piper methysticum)

Kava (Piper methysticum) is a member of the Piperaceae family, native to Papua New Guinea, Vanuatu, and Solomon Islands. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and bark have been used for infusions, decoctions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its stimulant, sedative, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in traditional chinese medicine, ayurvedic medicine, and native american herbal medicine.
