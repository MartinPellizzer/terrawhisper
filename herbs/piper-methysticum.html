What to know about Piper methysticum before using it medicinally

Piper methysticum, commonly known as kava, has been utilized for centuries in traditional medicine to alleviate a range of health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness.

The main health benefits associated with kava consumption include reducing stress and anxiety, promoting relaxation, and improving sleep quality. Kava's therapeutic properties are attributed to its ability to modulate the nervous system, acting as a GABA receptor agonist and anxiolytic agent. The bioactive compounds responsible for kava's medicinal properties include kavalactones, particularly kawain, desmethoxyyangonin, and yangonin, which have been shown to possess neuroprotective and anti-inflammatory effects.

Traditionally, people prepare kava as a tea or decoction by steeping the roots in hot water, which is then consumed to promote relaxation and reduce stress.
