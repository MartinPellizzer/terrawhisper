What is Cleistocactus sepium?

Cleistocactus sepium is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is a shrubby cactus that grows up to 2 meters in height and 1 meter in diameter, with cylindrical stems branching from the base. The stems are covered in fine, white or yellowish spines, and are greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces white or yellowish flowers in the axils of the leaves, which are very small and not visible.

The flowers are funnel-shaped and have a diameter of about 3-4 centimeters.
