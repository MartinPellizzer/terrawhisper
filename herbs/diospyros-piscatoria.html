Diospyros piscatoria belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this group, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic hierarchy further, it falls under the order Ericales and is a member of the family Ebenaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Diospyros, with its specific designation being the species piscatoria.
