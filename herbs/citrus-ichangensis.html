Citrus ichangensis

What's the taxonomical classification of Citrus ichangensis?

Citrus ichangenensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic progression continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, placing it within the order Sapindales. Furthermore, this plant is a member of the family Rutaceae, falling under the genus Citrus, and is specifically identified by its species name, ichangenis.

The fruit develops from a fleshy ovary and eventually ripens into a citrus berry known as a hesperidium.
