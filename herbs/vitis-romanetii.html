What's the taxonomical classification of Vitis romanetii?

Vitis romanetii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the hierarchical descent, it falls under the order Vitales and is a member of the family Vitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Vitis, and its unique species designation, romanetii.
