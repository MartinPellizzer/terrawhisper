What's the taxonomical classification of Smilax darrisii?

Smilax darrisii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Liliales. This plant is further categorized under the family Smilacaceae, falling into the genus Smilax, and is specifically identified by its species name, darrisii.
