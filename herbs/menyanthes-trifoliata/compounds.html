Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) Compounds

Water Pennywort contains several primary active compounds, including tannins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its astringent and antioxidant properties. Coumarin and glycosides may support anti-inflammatory effects, while saponins and terpenoids have potential detoxifying and immune-stimulating actions. Essential oils, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its therapeutic profile in herbal medicine.

Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) Monograph

1. Tannin

Tannin in Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) contributes to its astringent properties, aiding in wound healing and reducing inflammation. Flavonoids in Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) contribute to its medicinal properties. Phenolic acid in Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) contributes to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The essential oil of Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata) contains volatile compounds like methyl salicylate and camphor. Anthocyanin, found in Water Pennywort (Menyanthes trifoliata), is a flavonoid responsible for the plant's vibrant blue and red pigments.
