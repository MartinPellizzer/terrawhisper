What is Cyclosorus liukiuensis?

Cyclosorus liukiuensis is a species of fern in the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a perennial plant with rhizomes and stipe bases covered in scales, and leaves that are pinnatifid with a deeply divided lamina, bearing 20-30 pairs of linear to lanceolate pinnae. The frond is 10-15 cm long, with the terminal pinna the longest, and the rachis is densely covered with scales. The sori are linear, located on the lower surface of the pinnae, and contain 48-50 spores per sorus.

This species is endemic to Japan, specifically in the Ryukyu Islands, where it grows in humid forests and rocky areas.
