Bloodtwig Dogwood (Cornus sanguinea) Compounds

The primary compounds in Bloodtwig Dogwood include flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties, and tannins, known for their astringent effects. Phenolic acids contribute to its anti-inflammatory qualities, while coumarin may support circulatory health. Glycosides and saponins are also present, offering potential cardiovascular and digestive benefits.
