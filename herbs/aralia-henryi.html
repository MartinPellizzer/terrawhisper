What's the taxonomical classification of Aralia henryi?

Aralia henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its hierarchical lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Apiales and is a member of the family Araliaceae. Within this botanical framework, it is categorized under the genus Aralia, specifically identified as the species henryi.
