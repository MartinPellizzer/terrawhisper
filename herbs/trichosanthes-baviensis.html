What's the taxonomical classification of Trichosanthes baviensis?

Trichosanthes baviensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it falls under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving further down the taxonomic hierarchy, it is organized into the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its specific genus, Trichosanthes, and its unique species designation, baviensis.
