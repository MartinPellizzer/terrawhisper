What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Pelargonium Sidoides (Umckaloabo)?

Pelargonium sidoides has health benefits such as relieving respiratory tract infections and soothing coughs.

The herb's medicinal constituents include its active compounds, including cinnamic acid, which exhibit antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. These constituents are often prepared into medicinal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and teas that can be used to treat various respiratory conditions. However, potential side effects of umckaloabo may include gastrointestinal upset, allergic reactions, and interactions with other medications.

Precautions should be taken when using this herb, particularly in pregnant or breastfeeding women, and individuals with underlying medical conditions should consult a healthcare professional before use.

What are the medicinal preparations of Pelargonium sidoides?

Pelargonium sidoides has different medicinal preparations, such as oral capsules and liquids, which are used to treat various respiratory tract infections.

The oral capsules are prescribed for treating acute bronchitis, acute exacerbations of chronic bronchitis, and uncomplicated acute sinusitis.

The liquid preparation is used to treat children with these conditions, while the dry extract is often used in combination with other herbal extracts to create a treatment blend.
