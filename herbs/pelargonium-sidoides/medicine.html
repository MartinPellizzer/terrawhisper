What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Pelargonium Sidoides (Umckaloabo)?

Pelargonium sidoides, also known as umckaloabo, has health benefits such as alleviating respiratory tract infections, reducing inflammation, and soothing sore throats.

Its medicinal constituents include phenolic compounds, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. Pelargonium sidoides can be found in medicinal preparations such as teas, capsules, and tablets. However, potential side effects may include allergic reactions, interactions with other medications, and digestive discomfort.

Precautions should be taken when using Pelargonium sidoides, particularly for pregnant or breastfeeding women.

What are the medicinal preparations of Pelargonium sidoides?

Pelargonium sidoides has different medicinal preparations, such as oral capsules and liquids, which are used to treat various respiratory tract infections.

The oral capsules are prescribed for treating acute bronchitis, acute exacerbations of chronic bronchitis, and uncomplicated acute sinusitis.

The liquid preparation is used to treat children with these conditions, while the dry extract is often used in combination with other herbal extracts to create a treatment blend.
