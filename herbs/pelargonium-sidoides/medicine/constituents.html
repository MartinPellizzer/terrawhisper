10 Medicinal Constituents Of Pelargonium Sidoides (Umckaloabo)

Pelargonium sidoides has active constituents such as phenolic acids, flavonoids, and terpenoids.

These compounds exhibit anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and immunomodulatory properties, making it a popular remedy for colds and flu.

The flavonoids, in particular, have been shown to reduce inflammation and boost the immune system, alleviating symptoms such as congestion and sore throats, thus improving the quality of life for those suffering from respiratory infections.
