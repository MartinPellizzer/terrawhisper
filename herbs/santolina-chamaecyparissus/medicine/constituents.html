10 Medicinal Constituents Of Santolina Chamaecyparissus (Gray Cottonweed)

Santolina chamaecyparissus has active constituents such as sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds confer antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, making the plant effective in treating various health issues. The medicinal properties of Santolina chamaecyparissus can improve people's lives by reducing inflammation and pain associated with arthritis, skin conditions, and respiratory problems.

Additionally, its antioxidant properties may help protect against chronic diseases like cancer and cardiovascular disease, promoting overall well-being.

1. Luteolin

Santolina chamaecyparissus luteolin is a bioactive compound extracted from this perennial herb.

Its potential therapeutic applications are being researched extensively, with promising results in the treatment of chronic diseases such as cancer, cardiovascular disease, and neurodegenerative disorders.
