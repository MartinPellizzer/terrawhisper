10 Possible Side Effects Of Santolina Chamaecyparissus (Gray Cottonweed)

Santolina chamaecyparissus has some side effects when used improperly, such as skin irritation, allergic reactions, and digestive issues.

These side effects are often caused by the plant's essential oils, particularly camphor and borneol.

In severe cases, prolonged use or overdose can worsen these side effects, leading to discomfort, anxiety, and even respiratory problems.
