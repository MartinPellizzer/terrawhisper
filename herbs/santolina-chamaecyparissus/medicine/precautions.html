10 Precautions To Take When Using Santolina Chamaecyparissus (Gray Cottonweed)

Santolina chamaecyparissus has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as using it in moderation and avoiding prolonged use.

Excessive consumption can lead to gastrointestinal upset, dizziness, and headaches, as seen in cases where individuals ingested large quantities of the herb to treat various ailments.

It is crucial to note that this herb can exacerbate certain health conditions, such as high blood pressure and kidney issues, if not used carefully.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Santolina chamaecyparissus medicinally.
