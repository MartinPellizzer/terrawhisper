10 Best Santolina Chamaecyparissus Health Benefits

Its anti-inflammatory properties may support respiratory health by alleviating symptoms of conditions like bronchitis and asthma.

Additionally, Santolina is often used in herbal teas to aid digestion and promote a sense of well-being.
