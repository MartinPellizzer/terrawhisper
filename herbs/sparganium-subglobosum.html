Sparganium subglobosum

What's the taxonomical classification of Sparganium subglobosum?

Sparganium subglobosum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant further descends through the order Poales and is a member of the family Typhaceae. Taxonomically, it is defined by its placement within the genus Sparganium, with its specific identity being the species subglobosum.
