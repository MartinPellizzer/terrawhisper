Tetrastigma pubinerve belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, the plant is part of the order Vitales and falls under the family Vitaceae. Finally, it is identified by its specific genus, Tetrastigma, and its unique species designation, pubinerve.
