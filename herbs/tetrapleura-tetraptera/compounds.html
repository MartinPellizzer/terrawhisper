Tetrapleura Tetraptera (Tetrapleura tetraptera) Compounds

The primary compounds of Tetrapleura tetraptera include flavonoids, tannins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its antioxidant properties. Saponins and coumarins are also present, offering anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. Terpenoids and glycosides provide additional therapeutic benefits, while essential oils, alkaloids, and anthocyanins enhance its medicinal profile.
