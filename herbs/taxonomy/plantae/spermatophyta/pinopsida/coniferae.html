The Coniferae Subclass

Coniferae is an order of plants that belongs to the division Pinophyta, characterized by cone-bearing trees and shrubs.

This order includes various species that are commonly known as conifers. Within the Coniferae order, some notable examples of families are Pinaceae (pines and spruces) and Cupressaceae (cypresses and arborvitae).

Other examples of orders that belong to different divisions include Asparagales (a division of flowering plants), Malpighiales (also a division of flowering plants), and Poales (a division of flowering plants).

Phyllocladales

Phyllocladales is an order of flowering plants in the family Restionaceae, within the class Liliopsida, subclass Commelinidae, division Magnoliophyta, and kingdom Plantae.
