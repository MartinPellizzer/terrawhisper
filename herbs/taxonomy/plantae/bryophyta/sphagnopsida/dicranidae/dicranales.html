The Dicranales Orders

The Dicranales is an order of plants, specifically a group of mosses that are characterized by their leafy stems and branches, often growing in dense, carpet-like masses.

This order is known for its diverse range of species, including the likes of mosses with complex leaf structures and those with simple, needle-like leaves. The Dicranales order includes several notable families, such as the Dicranaceae family, which comprises species like the forest moss and the dicranum moss, as well as the Orchidaceae family, which is more commonly known for its flowering orchids, but also includes some moss species.

Other families within the Dicranales order include the Euphorbiaceae and the Cyperaceae, although these two are more commonly associated with flowering plants.
