The Hymenophyllales Orders

The taxonomical rank Hymenophyllales is an order of plants, specifically a group of ferns that are characterized by their unique leaf structures and reproductive methods.

This order is comprised of several families, including the Hymenophyllaceae family, which is the only family within the order. Some examples of families that belong to other orders include the Orchidaceae, the Euphorbiaceae, and the Cyperaceae, which are part of different orders altogether.

Within the Hymenophyllales order, the Hymenophyllaceae family is notable for its diverse range of species and adaptations, including the ability to grow in a variety of environments.

Hymenophyllaceae

Hymenophyllaceae is a family of ferns commonly known as filmy ferns, characterized by their delicate, transparent fronds.
