The Marchantiales Orders

The rank Marchantiales refers to an order of liverworts, a group of non-vascular plants that belong to the class Marchantiopsida.

It is a taxonomic grouping that includes various families of liverworts. Some examples of families within the Marchantiales order include Marchantiaceae, Ricciaceae, and Blasiaceae.

Other examples of plant families that are not part of the Marchantiales order include the flowering plant families Orchidaceae, Euphorbiaceae, and Cyperaceae.
