The Polypodiopsida Class

The rank Polypodiopsida is a division of plants that includes the ferns and their allies.

It is a group of vascular plants characterized by the presence of true leaves and a distinctive life cycle involving two generations of plants, known as sporophytes and gametophytes. The Polypodiopsida division encompasses a wide range of fern species, from the small, delicate maidenhair ferns to the large, tree-like horsetails.

Other divisions of plants that belong to different ranks include the Commelinidae (monocots with three-veined leaves), the Rosidae (dicots with a rose-like flower structure), and the Asteridae (dicots with a daisy-like flower structure).
