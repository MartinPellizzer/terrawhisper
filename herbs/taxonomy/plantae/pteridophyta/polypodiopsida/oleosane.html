The Oleosane Subclass

The oleosane rank is a subgroup or a tribe within a family, often defined as a group of plants that share similar characteristics and are closely related.

The oleosane rank can be thought of as a level of classification between the family and the genus. For example, the orders Asparagales, Malpighiales, and Poales are all part of various families, and they can be further divided into oleosane subgroups.

These subgroups can then be classified into different genera within those families, allowing for a more specific and detailed understanding of plant relationships.

Polypodiales

Polypodiales is a subgroup of the order Polypodiales, which is classified within the subclass Polypodiidae, division Pteridophyta, class Polypodiopsida, and kingdom Plantae.
