The Nelumbonaceae Family

The Nelumbonaceae is a family of flowering plants that consists of aquatic species.

It is a distinct group of plants characterized by their unique water lily-like flowers and submerged or emergent leaves. The Nelumbonaceae family is primarily composed of two genera: Nelumbo and Nuphar.

These plants are commonly found in temperate and tropical regions, often thriving in shallow waters and ponds.
