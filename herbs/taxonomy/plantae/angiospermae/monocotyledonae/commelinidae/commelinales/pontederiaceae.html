The Pontederiaceae Family

The rank Pontederiaceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

Pontederiaceae is a family of aquatic plants that includes water hyacinths and pickerelweeds, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. This family is characterized by its distinctive flowers and submerged or emergent aquatic habitats.

Pontederiaceae is a relatively small family with about 15-20 genera and around 100 species.
