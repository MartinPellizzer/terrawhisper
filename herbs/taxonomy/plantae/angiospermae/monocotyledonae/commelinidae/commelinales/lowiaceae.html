The Lowiaceae Family

The Lowiaceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic grouping of plant species that share similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships. Lowiaceae includes a small group of tropical flowering plants native to the Indo-Pacific region, known for their unique and often brightly colored flowers.

The family Lowiaceae contains approximately 10 to 15 species across several genera.
