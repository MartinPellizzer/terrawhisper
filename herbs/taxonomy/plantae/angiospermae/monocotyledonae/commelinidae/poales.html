The Poales Orders

The Poales rank is an order of flowering plants that includes various families of grasses, sedges, and lilies.

This order is characterized by its unique flower structure, often with three petals and a distinctive arrangement of stamens and carpels. The Poales order includes families such as Orchidaceae, which comprises orchids, Euphorbiaceae, which includes spurge and poinsettia, and Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and rushes.

These families are found in diverse habitats worldwide, from tropical rainforests to arctic tundras.
