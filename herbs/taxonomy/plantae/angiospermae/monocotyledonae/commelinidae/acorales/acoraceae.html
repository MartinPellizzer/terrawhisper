The Acoraceae Family

The Acoraceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

Acoraceae consists of aquatic plants with unique characteristics, such as the presence of rhizomes and a distinctive leaf structure. This family includes the genera Acorus and Calla, among others, and is primarily found in temperate and subtropical regions.

The plants within the Acoraceae family are often characterized by their ability to thrive in wet environments.
