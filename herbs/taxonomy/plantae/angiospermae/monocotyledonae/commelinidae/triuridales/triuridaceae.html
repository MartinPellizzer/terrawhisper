The Triuridaceae Family

The Triuridaceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

This family is characterized by its small, often underground flowers and tuberous roots. Members of the Triuridaceae family are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, and are often associated with fungi.

With around 100 species, Triuridaceae is a relatively small and diverse group of plants.
