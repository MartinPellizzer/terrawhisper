The Commelinales Orders

The taxonomic rank of Commelinales is an order, which is a level of classification that groups related families of flowering plants based on shared characteristics.

This order comprises a diverse range of plant families, including the Orchidaceae, known for its vast array of orchid species, and the Euphorbiaceae, which includes the poinsettia and the spurge. The Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family, also falls within the Commelinales order.

Commelinales is a relatively small order, consisting of approximately 25 families and over 3,000 species, but it plays a significant role in the diversity of plant life.
