The Zingiberales Orders

The taxonomical rank of zingiberales refers to an order of flowering plants characterized by a distinct inflorescence and often associated with tropical or subtropical regions.

Within this order, several families can be found, including the ginger family (Zingiberaceae), which is the namesake of the order. Other notable families within the zingiberales order include the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and the family Euphorbiaceae, although they are not typically associated with this order.

The family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family, also belongs to the zingiberales order.
