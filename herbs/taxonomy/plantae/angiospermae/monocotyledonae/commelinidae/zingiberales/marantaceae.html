The Marantaceae Family

The marantaceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a grouping of species that share similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships, often including plants with heart-shaped leaves and purple or white flowers. Marantaceae is a family of approximately 550 species, with many species found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This family is commonly known as the prayer plant family, with some species popularly cultivated as houseplants.
