The Misodendraceae Family

The Misodendraceae rank is a family of plants.

It is a small, monotypic family of flowering plants that is endemic to the southern Andes. The plants in this family are typically vines or shrubs with distinctive features.

Misodendraceae is considered one of the most ancient lineages of flowering plants, with fossil records dating back to the Cretaceous period.
