The Doryanthaceae Family

The rank Doryanthaceae is a family of flowering plants, specifically a group of orchids that are native to Australia and New Zealand.

It is a monogeneric family, meaning it only contains one genus, Doryanthes. This family is characterized by its unique, tall flowering spikes and strap-like leaves.

The Doryanthaceae family is relatively small and endemic to the region, with only a few species known to exist.
