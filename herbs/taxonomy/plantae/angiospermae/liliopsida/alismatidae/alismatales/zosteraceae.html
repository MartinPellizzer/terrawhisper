The Zosteraceae Family

The rank of Zosteraceae is a family of flowering plants, commonly known as the seagrass family or shoal grass family.

It is a group of monocots that primarily inhabit coastal and marine environments, providing habitat and food sources for various marine species. Zosteraceae consists of approximately 10 genera and 20 species, including Zostera, which is a well-known seagrass species.

These plants play a crucial role in maintaining the health of marine ecosystems and are often used as indicators of environmental quality.
