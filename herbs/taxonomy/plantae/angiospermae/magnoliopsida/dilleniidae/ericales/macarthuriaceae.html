The Macarthuriaceae Family

The taxonomical rank of Macarthuriaceae is a family of plants.

It is a classification rank that groups plants with similar characteristics and genetic relationships. Macarthuriaceae is a family of flowering plants, specifically a type of grass or sedge, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This family is relatively small and contains a limited number of species.
