The Crypteroniaceae Family

The Crypteroniaceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

This family is primarily composed of shrubs and small trees, native to tropical Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plants within the Crypteroniaceae family are characterized by their showy, funnel-shaped flowers and distinctive seed pods.

There are approximately 17 species within this family, with many being used in traditional medicine and landscaping.
