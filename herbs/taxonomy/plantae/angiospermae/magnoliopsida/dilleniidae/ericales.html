The Ericales Orders

The taxonomic rank of ericales is a clade or a group of plant families that share a common ancestor.

It is an order of flowering plants that are primarily found in temperate regions. Families such as Orchidaceae, which includes orchids, Euphorbiaceae, which includes spurge and euphorbias, and Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, are all part of the ericales order.

The ericales order is characterized by its diverse range of plant species and its widespread distribution across the globe.
