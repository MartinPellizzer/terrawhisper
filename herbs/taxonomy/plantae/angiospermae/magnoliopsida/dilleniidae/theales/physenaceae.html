The Physenaceae Family

The Physenaceae rank is a family of plants.

It is a taxonomic rank that groups together plants that share similar characteristics and are closely related. The Physenaceae family is part of the water blite tribe and is known for its aquatic or semi-aquatic nature, with plants such as Physena and Atriplex species being part of this group.

Physenaceae plants are typically found in wet environments and are often adapted to life in or near water.
