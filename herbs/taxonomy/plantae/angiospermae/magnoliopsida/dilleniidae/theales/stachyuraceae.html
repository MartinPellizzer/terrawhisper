The Stachyuraceae Family

The Stachyuraceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants that belong to the order of Saxifragales.

This family includes approximately 60 species of plants found primarily in Asia, particularly in China, India, and Southeast Asia. The Stachyuraceae family is characterized by its diverse range of plants, including shrubs and small trees, often with showy flowers and edible fruits.

These plants are adapted to a variety of habitats, from tropical rainforests to temperate mountain regions.
