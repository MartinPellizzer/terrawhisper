The Achatocarpaceae Family

The Achatocarpaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

Achatocarpaceae is a small family of plants that is considered monotypic, consisting of a single genus, Achatocarpus. It is a relatively rare and poorly understood family, with species found primarily in the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The family is characterized by its unique flowers and fruits, which are adapted for seed dispersal by ants.
