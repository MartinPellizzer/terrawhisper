The Mayacaceae Family

The Mayacaceae rank is a family of flowering plants, consisting of a group of species that share similar characteristics and are closely related.

Mayacaceae is a small family of plants, primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Members of this family, such as Mayaca, are known for their unique growth habits and adaptations to their environments.

The Mayacaceae family is one of the many distinct groups of flowering plants that are classified and studied by botanists.
