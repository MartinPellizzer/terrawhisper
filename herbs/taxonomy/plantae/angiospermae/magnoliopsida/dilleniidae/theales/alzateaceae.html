The Alzateaceae Family

The alzateaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

Alzateaceae is a small family of flowering plants, commonly known as the Mexican buckwheat family, that comprises four genera and around 15 species. These plants are typically found in Central America and Mexico, and are known for their distinctive flowers and edible seeds.

The alzateaceae family is a relatively small and understudied group within the plant kingdom, but they play an important role in the ecosystems they inhabit.
