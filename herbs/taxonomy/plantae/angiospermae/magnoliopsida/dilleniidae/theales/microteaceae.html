The Microteaceae Family

The Microteaceae rank refers to a family of flowering plants.

Microteaceae is a small family of flowering plants, consisting of about 12 species found in tropical regions. They are typically low-growing, herbaceous plants with small, inconspicuous flowers.

The Microteaceae family is part of the larger plant classification system and is used to group plants with similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships.
