The Limnanthaceae Family

The rank Limnanthaceae refers to the family of flowering plants, which is a taxonomic grouping that includes several genera of aquatic and terrestrial plants.

This family is characterized by its unique combination of characteristics, including its flowers, leaves, and growth habits. Limnanthaceae is a relatively small family of plants, but it includes several species that are of interest to botanists and horticulturists.

Some common examples of plants in the Limnanthaceae family include Limnanthemum and Limnanthus.
