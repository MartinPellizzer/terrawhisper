The Saururaceae Family

The saururaceae rank refers to a family of plants, specifically a group of flowering plants that are commonly known as the lizard tails or waterwort family.

This family consists of approximately 35 species of plants, primarily found in temperate and subtropical regions. Members of the saururaceae family are often aquatic or semi-aquatic, with adaptations such as submerged leaves and stems that allow them to thrive in wet environments.

The unique characteristics and adaptations of saururaceae plants have made them an interesting subject for botanical study and research.
