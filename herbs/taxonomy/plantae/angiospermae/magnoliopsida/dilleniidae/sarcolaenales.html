The Sarcolaenales Orders

The Sarcolaenales is an order of flowering plants, characterized by a unique set of characteristics that distinguish it from other plant orders.

This order is comprised of families such as Sarcolaenaceae, which is the only family within the Sarcolaenales order, and others are not classified within this order. However, for the sake of example, some plant families that are not classified within the Sarcolaenales order include Orchidaceae, Euphorbiaceae, and Cyperaceae, which belong to different orders.

The Sarcolaenales order is primarily found in the island of Madagascar and is known for its unique and endemic species.
