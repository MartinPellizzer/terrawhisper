The Gisekiaceae Family

The Gisekiaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

It is a small group of plants, primarily found in Japan, that are known for their unique characteristics and adaptations. The Gisekiaceae family is often recognized by its distinctive leaf morphology and growth habits.

As a family, Gisekiaceae is a key component in understanding the diversity and evolution of flowering plants, particularly in the Asian region.
