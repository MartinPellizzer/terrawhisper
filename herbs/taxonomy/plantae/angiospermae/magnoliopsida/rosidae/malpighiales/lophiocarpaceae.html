The Lophiocarpaceae Family

The Lophiocarpaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

Lophiocarpaceae is a relatively small family of plants, consisting of approximately 15 species found primarily in the Americas and South Africa. This family is characterized by its unique characteristics, including small flowers, narrow leaves, and a distinctive growth habit.

Members of the Lophiocarpaceae family are often found growing in dry, sandy areas and are known for their ability to thrive in arid environments.
