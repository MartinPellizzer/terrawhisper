The Pentadiplandraceae Family

The rank Pentadiplandraceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic group that consists of approximately 10 species of plants, primarily found in tropical regions of Africa. The Pentadiplandraceae family is characterized by its unique combination of leaf and flower morphology, setting it apart from other plant families.

This distinct family plays an important role in the biodiversity and ecosystem balance of its native regions.
