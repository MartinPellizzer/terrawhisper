The Balanopaceae Family

The taxonomical rank of Balanopaceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

A family in botanical taxonomy is a rank that groups together related genera of plants that share common characteristics and evolutionary traits. The Balanopaceae family is a small, monogeneric family that includes the genus Dipterocarpaceae's relative, the Balanopaceae genus Balanops, and a few other species.

The Balanopaceae family is mainly found in tropical Africa.
