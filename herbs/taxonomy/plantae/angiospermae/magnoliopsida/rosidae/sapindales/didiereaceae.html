The Didiereaceae Family

The Didiereaceae rank is a family of plants, which is the second-highest taxonomic rank after the order.

It is a group of succulent plants that are native to the island of Madagascar and are characterized by their thick, fleshy stems and leaves. Didiereaceae plants are also known for their unique shapes and sizes, and some species can grow up to several meters tall.

This family includes three genera, with the most well-known being Alluaudia, Didierea, and Alignya.
