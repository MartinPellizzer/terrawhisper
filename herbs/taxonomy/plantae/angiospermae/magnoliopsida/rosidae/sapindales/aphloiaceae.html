The Aphloiaceae Family

The taxonomical rank Aphloiaceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a rank within the plant classification system that groups plants with similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships into a specific family. The Aphloiaceae family is a small group of flowering plants, primarily found in Africa, and is characterized by their unique floral and vegetative features.

This family is part of the broader plant classification system, used to categorize and understand the diversity of plant life.
