The Crossosomataceae Family

Crossosomataceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic rank that groups together a set of related genera sharing similar characteristics. Crossosomataceae is primarily composed of shrubs and small trees native to the Americas, often producing small white or yellow flowers.

The family is further divided into multiple genera, each with unique features and adaptations.
