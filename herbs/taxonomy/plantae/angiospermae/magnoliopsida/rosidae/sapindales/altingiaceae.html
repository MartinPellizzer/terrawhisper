The Altingiaceae Family

The altingiaceae rank is a family of plants.

It consists of a group of flowering plants that are native to the Northern Hemisphere, particularly in Asia and North America. The altingiaceae family includes around 30 species, commonly known as the goldenraints or the prince's pine, which are deciduous trees or shrubs characterized by their umbrella-shaped leaves.

Members of the altingiaceae family are often valued for their wood, which is durable and resistant to decay.
