The Incertae_Sedis Family

The incertae sedis rank is a taxonomical designation used to classify plants whose placement within the plant kingdom is uncertain or unknown.

It is a temporary or provisional classification, indicating that the plant's relationships to other organisms have not been sufficiently established. This rank is often used when there is limited information or research available about a particular plant species.

By placing a plant in the incertae sedis category, taxonomists can acknowledge the uncertainty surrounding its classification without assigning a definitive position within the plant kingdom.
