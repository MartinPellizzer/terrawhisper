The Ixonanthaceae Family

The Ixonanthaceae is a family of flowering plants.

It is a rank in the taxonomic classification of plants that comprises a group of plants sharing similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships. The Ixonanthaceae family is typically defined by its distinct morphological, anatomical, and reproductive features.

This family includes various species of plants found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions.
