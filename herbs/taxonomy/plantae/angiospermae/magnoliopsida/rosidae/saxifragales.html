The Saxifragales Orders

The Saxifragales rank is an order of flowering plants, characterized by their diverse range of growth habits and reproductive strategies.

This order includes a variety of families, such as Saxifragaceae, to which its name refers, as well as the families Crassulaceae, Grossulariaceae, and others. The Saxifragales order also encompasses the families Orchidaceae, known for their intricate and showy flowers, Euphorbiaceae, which includes the spurge and poinsettia, and Cyperaceae, a family of sedges and rushes.

These families within the Saxifragales order showcase the order's unique and varied diversity of plant life.
