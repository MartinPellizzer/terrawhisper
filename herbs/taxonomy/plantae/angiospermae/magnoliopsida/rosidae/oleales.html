The Oleales Orders

The oleales rank is an order of flowering plants, characterized by their showy flowers and often woody stems, typically with opposite or whorled leaves.

Within the oleales order, several notable families are found, including the olive family (oleaceae), as well as families such as orchidaceae (orchids) and euphorbiaceae (spurges). The cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, is another example of a family within the order oleales.

These diverse plant families are united by their shared characteristics and evolutionary relationships within the oleales order.
