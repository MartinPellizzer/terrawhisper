The Nitrariaceae Family

Nitrariaceae refers to a family of flowering plants that belongs to the nitraria genus.

It is a group of plants that are primarily found in arid and semi-arid regions around the world. The Nitrariaceae family includes species such as Nitraria tangutorum, commonly known as the Chinese saltwort or Zygophyllum.

Characterized by their succulent leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers, Nitrariaceae plants are known for their ability to thrive in harsh environments.
