The Platanaceae Family

The Platanaceae rank is a family of flowering plants, commonly known as the plane tree family.

This family is characterized by its distinctive, large, and deciduous trees, often found in temperate and subtropical regions. The Platanaceae family includes species like the London planetree (Platanus x acerifolia) and the western planetree (Platanus occidentalis), which are known for their broad, flat leaves and large, showy flowers.

The family Platanaceae is a part of the larger group of flowering plants, and its members are widely cultivated for their ornamental value and shade-providing capabilities.
