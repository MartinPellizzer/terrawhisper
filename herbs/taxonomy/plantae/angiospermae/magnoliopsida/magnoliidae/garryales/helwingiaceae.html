The Helwingiaceae Family

The helwingiaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic group that consists of a group of organisms that share common characteristics and are closely related. Helwingiaceae is a small family of flowering plants, commonly known as the Helwingia family, which consists of three to four species of evergreen shrubs or small trees native to East Asia.

The Helwingiaceae family is characterized by its distinctive flowers and unique leaf morphology.
