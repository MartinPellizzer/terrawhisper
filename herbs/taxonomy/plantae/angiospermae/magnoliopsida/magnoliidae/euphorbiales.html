The Euphorbiales Orders

The taxonomical rank of euphorbiales is an order of flowering plants, characterized by a distinct set of characteristics such as the presence of milky sap and often, highly modified leaves or stems.

This order includes several families of plants, including the Orchidaceae, Euphorbiaceae, and Cyperaceae. These families are found in various parts of the world, and they exhibit a wide range of adaptations to different environments.

The euphorbiales order is a significant group within the plant kingdom, comprising over 10,000 species of flowering plants.
