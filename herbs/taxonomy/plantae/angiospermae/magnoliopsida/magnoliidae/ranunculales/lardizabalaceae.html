The Lardizabalaceae Family

The Lardizabalaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

It consists of approximately 11 genera, including Akebia, Decaisnea, and Sinofranchisia, and is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. This family is characterized by its unique flowers, which often have a distinctive shape and color.

Lardizabalaceae plants are also known for their ability to thrive in a variety of environments, from forests to grasslands.
