The Canellales Orders

The Canellales rank is a taxonomic order in the plant kingdom, characterized by woody plants with opposite, often leathery leaves and small, often solitary flowers.

This order includes families such as Canellaceae, which comprises the genus Canella, and other families like the Lauraceae, which is often mentioned alongside Canellaceae in discussions about this order. Some notable members of the Canellales order also include the family Annonaceae and the family Myristicaceae.

However, not all the mentioned families are part of this order, they are just mentioned as examples of families within the broader plant taxonomy.

Canellaceae

Canellaceae is a family of evergreen, flowering plants commonly known as the wax lemons or canelos, native to Central and South America.
