The Canellaceae Family

The Canellaceae rank is a family of flowering plants, comprising a group of species commonly known as the canella family.

This family is characterized by its unique combination of characteristics, including leathery leaves and showy white flowers. The Canellaceae family is found in tropical regions of Central and South America, and is often associated with humid, shaded environments.

With approximately 10-15 species, the Canellaceae family is a relatively small but diverse group of plants.
