The Daphniphyllaceae Family

The Daphniphyllaceae rank, also known as the family Daphniphyllaceae, is a group of flowering plants that are primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

This family comprises around 30-40 species of evergreen shrubs or small trees that are often characterized by their unique, star-shaped flowers and leathery leaves. The Daphniphyllaceae family is relatively small and diverse, with species exhibiting a range of growth habits and adaptations to different environments.

Members of this family are often found in humid, shaded areas and are prized for their ornamental value due to their distinctive foliage and flowers.
