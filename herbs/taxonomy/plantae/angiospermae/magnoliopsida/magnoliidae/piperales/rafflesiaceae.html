The Rafflesiaceae Family

The Rafflesiaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

It is a group of parasitic plants that are native to Southeast Asia and consist of about 15 species within five genera. Rafflesiaceae is characterized by its unique, large and complex flowers, which are often found in association with specific host plants.

The family is considered one of the most biologically diverse and fascinating groups of flowering plants, with some species producing the largest individual flowers in the world.
