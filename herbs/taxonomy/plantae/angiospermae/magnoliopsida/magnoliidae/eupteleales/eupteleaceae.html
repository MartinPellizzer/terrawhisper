The Eupteleaceae Family

Eupteleaceae is a family of flowering plants, specifically a group of small to medium-sized deciduous trees and shrubs.

This family is native to Asia and is characterized by its unique, often solitary flowers that grow at the end of branches. The members of Eupteleaceae are typically found in mountainous regions and are known for their striking foliage and vibrant fall colors.

There are approximately 10 species within this family, which is considered to be relatively rare and understudied.
