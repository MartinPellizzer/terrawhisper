The Dilleniidae Subclass

The rank Dilleniidae refers to a subgroup within the class of flowering plants, specifically a superorder that comprises several orders of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic rank that groups plants with similar characteristics and evolutionary relationships. Examples of orders within the Dilleniidae superorder include Asparagales, Malpighiales, and Poales, which are all distinct groups of flowering plants with unique characteristics and features.

The Dilleniidae superorder is a key part of the plant kingdom's taxonomic classification, helping to organize and understand the diversity of flowering plant species.
