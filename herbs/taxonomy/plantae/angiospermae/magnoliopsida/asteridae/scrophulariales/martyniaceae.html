The Martyniaceae Family

The rank Martyniaceae refers to a family of flowering plants.

It is a taxonomic grouping that consists of approximately 18 species, mainly found in tropical regions. This family is characterized by its diverse range of plants, including annuals and perennials, and is known for producing showy flowers.

The Martyniaceae family is often considered a part of the order Lamiales, although some sources may group it under the order Scrophulariales.
