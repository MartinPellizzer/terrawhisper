The Celastrales Orders

The Celastrales is an order of flowering plants, characterized by its diverse array of species, including shrubs, vines, and trees.

This order is defined by its unique characteristics such as simple, alternate leaves and small, often four- or five-merous flowers. Celastrales includes a variety of plant families, such as Celastraceae, which is its namesake, as well as some species within the families Orchidaceae, Euphorbiaceae, and Cyperaceae.

These diverse families within the Celastrales order showcase the complexity and diversity of plant life.
