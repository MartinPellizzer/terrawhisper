The Schlegeliaceae Family

The Schlegeliaceae rank is a family of flowering plants.

This family is composed of approximately 14 species of plants, primarily native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia. Schlegeliaceae plants are characterized by their showy flowers, often white or pink, and long, slender stems.

They typically thrive in humid environments and are commonly found in wetland areas, such as riverbanks and mangrove forests.
