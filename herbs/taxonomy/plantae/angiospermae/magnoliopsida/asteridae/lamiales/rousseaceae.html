The Rousseaceae Family

The rank Rousseaceae is a family of flowering plants, consisting of a small group of species primarily found in South Africa and Australia.

This family is part of the larger classification of dicotyledonous plants and is characterized by its unique leaf morphology and flower structure. Rousseaceae contains around 13-15 species, which are often shrubs or small trees with distinct floral features.

The family Rousseaceae is considered a distinct group within the broader plant classification system due to its distinct characteristics and limited geographic distribution.
