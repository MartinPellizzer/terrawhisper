The Dipteridaceae Family

The dipteridaceae rank refers to a family of plants that belongs to the division Pteridophyta, a group of vascular plants that reproduce via spores.

The family dipteridaceae comprises approximately 70 species of ferns, primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions. These plants are characterized by their simple or compound leaves and often exhibit a distinctive growth habit, such as rhizomes or stipes.

Dipteridaceae is a relatively small and diverse family of ferns, with species varying in size, shape, and distribution.
