The Campynemataceae Family

The Campynemataceae rank is a family of flowering plants, specifically a clade within the monocots.

It is a grouping of plants that share similar characteristics and are more closely related to each other than to other plant families. The Campynemataceae family is relatively small, comprising only a few genera and species.

This family is known for its unique and often specialized characteristics, setting it apart from other plant families.
