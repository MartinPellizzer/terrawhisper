The Phellinaceae Family

The phellinaceae rank is a family of plants that consists of two genera, Phellinea and Phyllium, previously classified under the family Hymenophyllaceae.

Phellinaceae is a small family of ferns commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. Members of this family are typically epiphytic or terrestrial ferns with delicate, lacy fronds.

Phellinaceae is a relatively newly recognized family within the plant kingdom, previously considered part of the larger family Hymenophyllaceae.
