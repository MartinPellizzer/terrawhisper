What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Clematis yunnanensis has climbing stems with opposite, trifoliate leaves featuring three ovate to elliptical leaflets that are typically dark green and slightly pubescent on the underside. These blooms often emerge in clusters and are supported by slender, twining petioles that allow the vine to scale surrounding vegetation. The foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, providing a dense textural backdrop for the striking floral displays.
