What is Poincianella laxa?

Poincianella laxa is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae, native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree, typically growing to 3-8 meters in height, with a broad, spreading canopy and a single main stem. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of 5-9 pairs of leaflets, which are elliptical in shape and 10-20 millimeters long. The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface.

The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers with five petals, which are followed by flat, linear seed pods 10-15 centimeters long.
