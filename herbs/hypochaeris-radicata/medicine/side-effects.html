10 Possible Side Effects Of Hypochaeris Radicata (Cat'S Ear)

Hypochaeris radicata has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, skin irritation, and allergic reactions.

These side effects can occur due to individual sensitivity or improper dosage.

In severe cases, they can worsen lives by causing chronic digestive problems, skin conditions, and even respiratory issues, requiring medical attention and impacting daily activities and productivity.

This is due to the plant's unique composition, which includes a combination of sesquiterpene lactones and other bioactive compounds that can stimulate an allergic response in some individuals.

The exact mechanisms behind this reaction are not fully understood, but it is thought to be related to the way these compounds interact with the body's immune system, triggering an overactive response that leads to symptoms such as itching, redness, and swelling.
