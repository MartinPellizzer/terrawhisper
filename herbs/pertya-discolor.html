Pertya discolor

What's the taxonomical classification of Pertya discolor?

Pertya discolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is further organized into the order Asterales. This plant is a member of the family Asteraceae, falling under the genus Pertya, and is specifically identified by the species epithet discolor.
