Aster altaicus var. hirsutus

What's the taxonomical classification of Aster altaicus var. hirsutus?

Aster altaicus var. hirsutus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Asterales and is a representative of the large and diverse family Asteraceae. At the generic level, it is categorized under the genus Aster, eventually reaching its specific designation as the variety hirsutus of the species Aster altaicus.
