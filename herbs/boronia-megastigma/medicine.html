What Are The Medicinal Properties Of Boronia Megastigma (Australian Rose)?

Boronia megastigma, also known as Australian rose, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and treating anxiety.

The herb contains essential oils, including geraniol and linalool, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and anti-anxiety properties. Medicinal preparations of Boronia megastigma include teas, infusions, and essential oil extracts, which can be used topically or ingested. However, high doses of the herb may cause gastrointestinal side effects, such as nausea and diarrhea.

To use Boronia megastigma safely, pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult with a healthcare professional before use.

What are the possible side effect of using Boronia megastigma improperly?

Improper use of Boronia megastigma, also known as Australian rose, increases the chances of experiencing side effects such as dizziness, nausea, and headaches.

It may also interact with certain medications, exacerbating conditions like high blood pressure and allergies. Furthermore, excessive consumption can lead to digestive problems, including diarrhea and abdominal pain.
