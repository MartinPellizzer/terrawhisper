What is Odontonema glaberrimum?

Odontonema glaberrimum is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Acanthaceae.

It is an evergreen perennial herb that grows up to 120 cm in height, with a woody base and leaves that are simple, elliptical, and entire, measuring 10-20 cm in length and 4-6 cm in width. The leaves are dark green, glabrous, and have a cuneate base and a rounded apex. The plant produces spikes of white or pink flowers, which are tubular and 2-lipped, with a 2-lobed upper lip and a 3-lobed lower lip, and are borne on a peduncle of 5-7 cm in length.

The inflorescence is terminal and axillary, and the flowers are zygomorphic and hermaphroditic.
