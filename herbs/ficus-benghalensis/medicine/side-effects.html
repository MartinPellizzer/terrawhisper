10 Possible Side Effects Of Ficus Benghalensis (Banyan)

Ficus benghalensis has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea.

These side effects may be caused by excessive consumption, allergic reactions, or interactions with other medications.

For example, if a person with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) consumes Ficus benghalensis regularly, they may experience worsening of symptoms like bloating, abdominal pain, and altered bowel habits, significantly impacting their daily life and quality of life.
