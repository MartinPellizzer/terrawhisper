10 Health Benefits Of Eupatorium Cannabinum (Hemp Agrimony)

Eupatorium cannabinum has health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

Its medicinal qualities make it effective in treating digestive issues, wounds, and respiratory problems. The plant's flavonoids and sesquiterpenes have antioxidant effects, reducing inflammation and promoting healing.

For example, a warm bath infused with hemp agrimony can soothe arthritis pain, while its tea can ease cold symptoms and promote relaxation.
