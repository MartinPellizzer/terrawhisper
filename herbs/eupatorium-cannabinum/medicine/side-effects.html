10 Possible Side Effects Of Eupatorium Cannabinum (Hemp Agrimony)

Eupatorium cannabinum has some side effects when used improperly, such as gastrointestinal upset, skin irritation, and allergic reactions.

Causes include excessive dosage, individual sensitivities, and interactions with other medications. Examples of how these side effects can worsen people's lives include debilitating stomach pain, prolonged recovery from treatment, and compromised immune systems.

In severe cases, these side effects can be life-threatening.
