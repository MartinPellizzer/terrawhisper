Podocarpus macrophyllus var. maki

What's the taxonomical classification of Podocarpus macrophyllus var. maki?

Podocarpus macrophyllus var. maki belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Pinales. It is a member of the family Podocarpaceae, specifically categorized under the genus Podocarpus, with the species designated as Podocarpus.
