What to know about Pygeum africanum before using it medicinally

Pygeum africanum, commonly known as pygeum, is a traditional herbal remedy used to alleviate various health conditions, primarily benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), a non-cancerous enlargement of the prostate gland.

The main health benefits associated with pygeum include improved urinary flow, reduced frequency of urination, and alleviated symptoms of BPH. The herb's therapeutic properties are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-androgenic activities. Pygeum africanum contains a range of bioactive compounds, including ferulic acid, isoflavones, and pentacyclic triterpenes, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

People often prepare pygeum in the form of a standardized extract, which is commonly used as a dietary supplement to support prostate health and alleviate associated symptoms.
