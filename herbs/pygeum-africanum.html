Pygeum (Pygeum africanum)

Pygeum (Pygeum africanum) is a member of the Rubiaceae family, native to Central Africa, West Africa, and Southern Africa. Traditionally, its bark, leaves, and roots have been used for decoctions, infusions, and powders.

This herb is particularly valued for its diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in african traditional medicine, european herbal medicine, and mediterranean herbal traditions.
