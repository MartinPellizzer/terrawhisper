What's the taxonomical classification of Humata griffithiana?

Humata griffithiana belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it is further classified under the subclass Polypodiinae and the order Polypodiales. This plant is a representative of the family Polypodiaceae, falling specifically under the genus Humata. Ultimately, its unique biological identity is defined by its specific epithet, making its complete scientific name Humata griffithiana.
