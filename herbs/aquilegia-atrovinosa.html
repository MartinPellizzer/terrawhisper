Aquilegia atrovinosa

What's the taxonomical classification of Aquilegia atrovinosa?

Aquilegia atrovinosa belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Moving through its taxonomic hierarchy, it is placed in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. It falls under the order Ranunculales and is a member of the family Ranunculaceae. Finally, the plant is categorized under the genus Aquilegia, with its specific designation being the species atrovinosa.
