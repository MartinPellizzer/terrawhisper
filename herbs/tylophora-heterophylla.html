Chinese Bellflower (Tylophora Heterophylla)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

This score reflects the overall reliability of the information presented in this article. It is based on the quality of scientific evidence, accuracy of sources, and the transparency of references related to Tylophora heterophylla.

Chinese bellflower, scientifically known as Tylophora heterophylla, is a medicinal herb widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its adaptogenic and rejuvenating properties. It is valued for its ability to support cognitive function, enhance memory, and reduce mental fatigue, making it particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing stress or age-related decline. Historically, it has been used in Chinese herbal formulations to treat neurological disorders and improve overall vitality, often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects. In modern wellness practices, it is gaining attention as a natural supplement for cognitive support and stress relief, with research exploring its potential neuroprotective benefits. One unique feature of this plant is its rare active compound, tylophorine, which has shown promise in pharmaceutical applications, highlighting its significance in both traditional and contemporary medicine.
