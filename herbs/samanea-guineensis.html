Samanea guineensis

Samanea guineensis, commonly known as African samanea or yoruba samanea, is a plant species native to West Africa.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties and health benefits, which include reducing inflammation, fever, and pain, as well as treating respiratory issues such as coughs and bronchitis.

The therapeutic actions of Samanea guineensis are attributed to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antipyretic properties, which are a result of its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These bioactive compounds have been found to possess antioxidant and immunomodulatory activities, making Samanea guineensis a valuable natural remedy for various health conditions.
