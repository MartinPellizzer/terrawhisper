Erycibe schmidtii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is situated under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Solanales and is a member of the family Convolvulaceae. Within this family, it is categorized under the genus Erycibe, and its specific scientific designation is the species schmidtii.
