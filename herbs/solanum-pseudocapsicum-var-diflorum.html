Solanum pseudocapsicum var. diflorum

What's the taxonomical classification of Solanum pseudocapsicum var. diflorum?

Solanum pseudocapsicum var. diflorum belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Solanales. It is a member of the family Solanaceae, which is a large group of flowering plants, and specifically resides within the genus Solanum. Ultimately, its complete taxonomic designation is Solanum pseudocapsicum var. diflorum.
