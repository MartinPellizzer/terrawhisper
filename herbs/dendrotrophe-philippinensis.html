Dendrotrophe Philippinensis

Dendrotrophe philippinensis, a medicinal plant native to the Philippines, has been traditionally used for its various therapeutic properties.

It is known for its antifungal, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it valuable in treating skin infections and wounds. The plant contains bioactive compounds such as flavonoids and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal value. In local communities, it is often applied topically to reduce inflammation and promote healing.
