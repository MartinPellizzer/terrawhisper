Microdesmis caseariifolia

What's the taxonomical classification of Microdesmis caseariifolia?

Microdesmis caseariifolia belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Malpighiales. This plant is further categorized into the family Pandaceae, which contains the genus Microdesmis, ultimately defining its specific identity as the species Microdesmis caseariifolia.
