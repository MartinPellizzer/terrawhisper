Polium Germander (Teucrium polium)

Polium Germander (Teucrium polium) is a member of the Lamiaceae family, native to Mediterranean Region, Middle East, and North Africa. Traditionally, its leaves, stems, and flowers have been used for infusions, decoctions, and poultices.

This herb is particularly valued for its bitter, anti-inflammatory, and tonic actions, and has a long history of use in mediterranean herbal traditions, european herbal medicine, and african traditional medicine.
