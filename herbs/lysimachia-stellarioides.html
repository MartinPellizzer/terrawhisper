What's the taxonomical classification of Lysimachia stellarioides?

Lysimachia stellarioides belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Ericales and the family Primulaceae. Finally, it is identified by its genus, Lysimachia, and its specific species designation, stellarioides.
