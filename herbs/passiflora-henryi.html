What's the taxonomical classification of Passiflora henryi?

Passiflora henryi belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its progression through the order Malpighiales, the plant is situated within the family Passifloraceae. Finally, it is identified under the genus Passiflora, specifically as the species henryi.
