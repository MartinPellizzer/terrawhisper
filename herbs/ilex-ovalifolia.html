Ilex ovalifolia

Ilex ovalifolia, commonly known as the Chinese Holly, is a plant species native to southern China.

Prolonged use of Ilex ovalifolia can also disrupt blood sugar levels and interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications.

Furthermore, improper use of the plant can exacerbate existing conditions, including high blood pressure and heart disease.
