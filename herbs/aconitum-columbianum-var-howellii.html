Aconitum columbianum var. howellii

Aconitum columbianum var. howellii, also known as Howell's monk's hood or Western monk's hood, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Aconitum genus.

Traditionally, the plant's roots and leaves have been used in herbal medicine, particularly in Chinese and Ayurvedic practices, due to their purported medicinal properties.

The plant is believed to possess anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antiseptic properties, which may contribute to its health benefits, including pain relief and wound healing.

Aconitum columbianum var. howellii can be prepared as an herbal tea, tincture, or infused oil, although caution is advised due to its toxic properties and potential side effects.
