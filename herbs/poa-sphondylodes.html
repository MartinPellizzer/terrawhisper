What's the taxonomical classification of Poa sphondylodes?

Poa sphondylodes belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. Its taxonomic hierarchy continues through the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, eventually reaching the order Poales. Within this order, the plant is situated in the family Poaceae, which contains the genus Poa. Ultimately, the specific biological identity of this plant is defined by its species designation, sphondylodes.
