What's the taxonomical classification of Cryptotaenia japonica?

Cryptotaenia japonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Apiales and within the family Apiaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Cryptotaenia, and its unique species designation, japonica.
