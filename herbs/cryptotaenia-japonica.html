Cryptotaenia japonica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Following the taxonomic hierarchy further, it is placed in the order Apiales and within the family Apiaceae. Finally, the plant is defined by its specific genus, Cryptotaenia, and its unique species designation, japonica.
