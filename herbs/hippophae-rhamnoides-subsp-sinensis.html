What's the taxonomical classification of Hippophae rhamnoides subsp. sinensis?

Hippophae rhamnoides subsp. sinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and falls under the order Rosalales.
