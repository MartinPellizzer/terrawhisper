Lastrea petrophila

Lastrea petrophila, commonly known as the petrophilous lastrea fern, is a species of fern in the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its medicinal properties.

The Lastrea petrophila plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its health benefits and therapeutic actions.

Lastrea petrophila can have side effects if used improperly, such as causing gastrointestinal issues, allergic reactions, and skin irritation in some individuals.
