Entada gigas belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

The plant produces large, woody, oblong pods that can grow up to 1 meter in length and contain several dark, heavy seeds. Its flowers are typically cream-colored or white and are arranged in dense clusters. The overall structure is designed to scale the forest canopy using its vigorous, twisting stems.
