Lady'S Mantle (Alchemilla Vulgaris Subvar. Semisecta)

Information Reliability Score: 2/10

*Alchemilla vulgaris* subvar. *semisecta*, commonly known as the lesser lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, valued for its medicinal properties. It contains a variety of bioactive compounds, including flavonoids, tannins, and mucilage, which contribute to its traditional use in treating digestive and urinary tract ailments. The plant is often used in herbal medicine to soothe inflammation and promote healing, particularly in conditions such as gastritis and urinary infections. Its astringent properties make it useful in formulations for reducing excess mucus and supporting intestinal health. Due to its mild nature and adaptability, *Alchemilla vulgaris* subvar. *semisecta* is a popular choice in both traditional and modern herbal remedies.
