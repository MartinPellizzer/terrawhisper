Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) Uses

The Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) is used to treat various respiratory conditions, including bronchitis, asthma, and influenza. It is also effective for allergic rhinitis, otitis media, and laryngitis. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties help alleviate symptoms of respiratory tract infections, cough, and upper respiratory tract infections.

Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) Monograph

1. Bronchitis

The Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) is used in traditional herbal medicine to treat bronchitis due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. Its bark and leaves are prepared as decoctions to help relieve coughing and clear mucus from the respiratory tract. The active compounds in the plant support bronchodilation and reduce airway inflammation, making it a valuable remedy for respiratory conditions.

2. Asthma

The Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) is used in traditional herbal medicine to help manage asthma symptoms. Its bark contains anti-inflammatory compounds that may reduce airway swelling. Preparations are often made as decoctions or tinctures, and they are believed to support respiratory health. However, it should be used under professional guidance to ensure safety and effectiveness.

3. Influenza

The Pink Trumpet Tree (Tabebuia rosea) is used in traditional herbal medicine to alleviate symptoms of influenza.
