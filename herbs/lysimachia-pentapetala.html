What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Lysimachia pentapetala has opposite, elliptical leaves with entire margins and five-petaled, bright yellow flowers that emerge from terminal clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. These morphological traits distinguish it from other members of the Primulaceae family.
