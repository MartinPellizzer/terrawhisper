Cynanchica oetaea

Cynanchica oetaea, also known as the Oetaa Cynanchum or Oetaea, is a plant species in the Apocynaceae family, commonly found in the Mediterranean region.

The medicinal properties of Cynanchica oetaea are attributed to its alkaloids, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including fever, inflammation, and respiratory problems.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to reduce fever and alleviate symptoms of colds and flu, as well as its potential to act as an anti-inflammatory agent.
