10 Medicinal Constituents Of Andrographis Paniculata (King Of Bitters)

Andrographis paniculata has active constituents such as andrographolide, neo-andrographolide, and flavonoids.

These compounds contribute to the plant's anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antimicrobial properties, which have been traditionally used to alleviate respiratory issues like bronchitis and asthma.

By reducing inflammation and opening up airways, these constituents can improve breathing capacity and relieve congestion, thereby improving quality of life for individuals suffering from respiratory conditions.
