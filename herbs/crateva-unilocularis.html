Crateva unilocularis

What's the taxonomical classification of Crateva unilocularis?

Crateva unilocularis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it follows the evolutionary lineage of the order Brassicales. This plant is further categorized under the family Capparaceae, which places it within the genus Crateva, and it is specifically identified by its species name, unilocularis.
