What's the taxonomical classification of Crateva unilocularis?

Crateva unilocularis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Capparaceae, which places it within the genus Crateva, and it is specifically identified by its species name, unilocularis.
