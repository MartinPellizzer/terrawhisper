Wood Betony (Stachys sylvatica) Compounds

Wood Betony contains several key active compounds, including flavonoids, coumarin, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Tannins and glycosides support its astringent effects, while terpenoids and essential oils contribute to its aromatic profile. Saponins, alkaloids, and anthocyanins further enhance its medicinal value.
