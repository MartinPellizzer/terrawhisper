10 Precautions To Take When Using Pelargonium (Scented Geranium)

Pelargonium has some precautions to consider before using it medicinally, such as pregnant and breastfeeding women exercising caution due to potential estrogen-like effects.

This is because excessive use may lead to uterine stimulation and hormonal imbalances. For instance, if a pregnant woman consumes excessive amounts, it may cause preterm labor. Similarly, breastfeeding women may experience changes in milk supply or altered infant behavior.

Therefore, moderation is key.

This article explains in details the 10 most important precautions to take when using Pelargonium medicinally.
