10 Health Benefits Of Pelargonium (Scented Geranium)

Pelargonium, also known as scented geranium, has health benefits such as reducing inflammation and stress.

The medicinal properties of this plant include its high antioxidant and antibacterial content, as well as its ability to modulate the nervous system. Examples of its benefits include improving sleep quality, reducing anxiety, and alleviating symptoms of menopausal hot flashes.

These benefits can lead to a better quality of life, increased energy, and improved overall well-being.

This article explains in details the 10 best health benefits of Pelargonium.

1. Relieves anxiety attacks

Pelargonium relieves anxiety attacks because of its calming and balancing properties.

The plant's leaves and flowers contain geraniol, a natural oil that has a soothing effect on the nervous system. When inhaled, geraniol can calm the mind and body, reducing feelings of anxiety and stress.

This can help to slow down rapid heartbeat and breathing, making it easier to manage anxiety attacks.

2. Reduces inflammation

Pelargonium reduces inflammation because of its high content of flavonoids and phenolic acids, which have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds work by neutralizing free radicals and inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, thereby reducing inflammation and oxidative stress.

Regular consumption or topical application of Pelargonium extracts may provide relief from various inflammatory conditions, including arthritis and digestive issues.

3. Kills bacteria

Pelargonium kills bacteria because of its potent essential oils.

These oils, particularly linalool, linalyl acetate, and geraniol, have antimicrobial properties that inhibit the growth of bacteria.

Research has shown that these oils can reduce bacterial colonies and prevent the formation of biofilms, making Pelargonium a natural and effective tool against bacterial infections.

4. Eases digestive issues

Pelargonium eases digestive issues because of its potent anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The plant's essential oils, particularly linalool and geraniol, have been shown to calm the digestive system, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).

Regular use of Pelargonium tea or infusions may also support healthy gut bacteria, promoting overall digestive well-being.

5. Lowers blood sugar

Pelargonium lowers blood sugar because of its flavonoid and rosmarinic acid content.

These compounds have been shown to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce insulin resistance and increase glucose uptake in the body.

Studies have demonstrated that Pelargonium extract can decrease blood glucose levels in diabetic mice and humans, suggesting its potential as a natural therapeutic agent for managing blood sugar levels.

6. Helps lower cholesterol

Pelargonium helps lower cholesterol because of its flavonoid and phenolic content.

These compounds have been shown to inhibit the absorption of dietary cholesterol in the gut and reduce the levels of low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol in the blood.

Studies have demonstrated that regular consumption of Pelargonium can help lower cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart disease associated with high cholesterol.

7. Reduces stress levels

Pelargonium reduces stress levels because its aromatic properties have a calming effect on the mind and body.

The plant's essential oils, including geraniol and linalool, promote relaxation and reduce anxiety by slowing down the heart rate and promoting a sense of tranquility.

By inhaling the fragrance of Pelargonium, individuals can experience a reduction in stress levels and a more peaceful state of mind.

8. Improves sleep quality

Pelargonium improves sleep quality because it promotes relaxation and calms the mind.

The plant's sweet fragrance and soothing scent have a calming effect on the nervous system, reducing anxiety and stress. Additionally, its essential oil has been shown to regulate the body's circadian rhythms, promoting a deeper and more restful sleep.

This can lead to improved mood and overall well-being.

9. Treats menstrual cramps

Pelargonium treats menstrual cramps because of its analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's essential oil, geraniol, has been shown to relax muscles and reduce prostaglandins, which cause cramping. The antispasmodic effects of Pelargonium help to ease cramp severity, while its flavonoids and tannins may reduce pain and inflammation associated with menstrual cramps.

This makes it a popular natural remedy for PMS symptoms.

10. Relaxes muscles

Pelargonium relaxes muscles because of its unique combination of fragrance and bioactive compounds.

The essential oils within the plant's leaves and stems interact with the body's limbic system, promoting a sense of calm and reducing muscle tension.

As a natural muscle relaxant, Pelargonium has been used for centuries to soothe aching muscles and joints, providing relief from stress and promoting overall well-being.
