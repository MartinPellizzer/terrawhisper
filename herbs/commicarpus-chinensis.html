What's the taxonomical classification of Commicarpus chinensis?

Commicarpus chinensis belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. This plant is further categorized under the family Nyctaaginaceae, which places it within the genus Commicarpus, ultimately identifying it by its specific species name, chinensis.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Commicarpus chinensis has a woody, climbing shrub morphology characterized by twining stems, ovate to elliptical leaves with entire margins, and small, inconspicuous flowers. During the flowering stage, the tiny, yellowish blossoms emerge from the axils of the foliage. The fruit consists of small, globose drupes that transition in color as they mature.
