Landolphia owariensis

Landolphia owarienensis has large, elliptical to oblong leaves that are typically coriaceous with entire margins and prominent pinnate venation. Its woody stem structure supports a canopy characterized by dense, spreading branching patterns. Small, inconspicuous flowers emerge from the axils of the leaves to facilitate pollination.
