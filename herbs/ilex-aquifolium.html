Possible Side Effects of ilex aquifolium

Ilex aquifolium can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, due to its high concentration of iridoid glycosides.

Furthermore, individuals with allergies or sensitivities may experience skin irritation, rash, or hives when handling the herb.

In rare cases, Ilex aquifolium can cause more severe side effects, including liver damage and kidney failure, especially when combined with other medications or substances.
