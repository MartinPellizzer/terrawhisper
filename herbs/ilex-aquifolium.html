Ilex Aquifolium Uses, Benefits, And Remedies

Ilex aquifolium, commonly known as the common holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and western Asia, valued for its medicinal properties.

This herb is traditionally used for its stimulating and tonic effects on the nervous and cardiovascular systems.

The health benefits of Ilex aquifolium include improving circulation, enhancing mental alertness, and supporting immune function.

Its therapeutic actions are attributed to bioactive constituents such as caffeine, theobromine, and flavonoids.

Herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and decoctions can be made from its leaves and berries.
