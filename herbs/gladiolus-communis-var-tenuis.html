Gladiolus communis var. tenuis

Gladiolus communis var. tenuis, also known as the narrow-leaved glad or narrow-leaved sword lily, is a perennial plant that belongs to the iris family.

Traditionally, the plant has been used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects, which have been attributed to the presence of saponins and flavonoids.

The health benefits of Gladiolus communis var. tenuis include its potential to lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties.

The plant can be prepared for medicinal use through various herbal preparations, such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, although its use should be consulted with a healthcare professional due to potential interactions and side effects.
