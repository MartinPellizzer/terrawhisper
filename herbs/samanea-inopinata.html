What is Samanea inopinata?

Samanea inopinata is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae, native to the Philippines.

The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, arranged in axillary racemes, while the pods are flat, 5-7 cm long, and contain 2-4 seeds.

The wood is dense and durable, used for furniture and construction.
