Possible Side Effects of curcuma aromatica

Curcuma aromatica can have side effects if used improperly, such as gastrointestinal issues, including nausea, diarrhea, and stomach ulcers, due to its high turmeric content.

Prolonged use or excessive consumption may also lead to liver damage and increased risk of bleeding, especially in individuals taking blood-thinning medications.

In addition, curcuma aromatica can interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners and diabetes medications, resulting in adverse effects.

Furthermore, people with allergies or sensitive skin may experience skin irritation, rashes, or itching after using curcuma aromatica products.
