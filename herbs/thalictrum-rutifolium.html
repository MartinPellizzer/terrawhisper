Thalictrum rutifolium

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Thalictrum rutifoliuum has pinnately compound leaves that are typically 10 to 25 centimeters long and feature leaflets with reddish or purplish undersides. The foliage is characterized by a delicate, airy texture due to the deeply divided segments of the leaves. Its flowering stems are slender and upright, bearing small, inconspicuous flowers that lack true petals. These flowers consist primarily of numerous yellowish or greenish stamens that create a fuzzy appearance. The plant eventually produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain multiple tiny seeds.
