Dioclea hexandra belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. It is further categorized under the family Fabaceae, which is a large group of flowering plants commonly known as legumes. Within this family, the plant is situated in the genus Dioclea, specifically identified by its species name, hexandra.
