Pileostegia tomentella

What's the taxonomical classification of Pileostegia tomentella?

Pileostegia tomentella belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is categorized under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is classified under the order Cornales. This plant is a member of the family Hydrageaceae and is specifically identified by the genus Pileostegia, with the unique species designation tomentella.
