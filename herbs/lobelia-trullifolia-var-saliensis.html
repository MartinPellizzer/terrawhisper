Lobelia trullifolia var. saliensis

Lobelia trullifolia var. saliensis is a type of flowering plant, also known as the creeping lobelia, which is native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its ability to act as a respiratory stimulant and a diuretic, and to treat conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and edema.

The health benefits of Lobelia trullifolia var. saliensis include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and boost the immune system, although more research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
