Malus baccata

What's the taxonomical classification of Malus baccata?

Malus baccata belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Following its taxonomic progression, it is placed in the order Rosales and falls within the family Rosaceae. Finally, the plant is identified by its genus Malus and its specific species designation, baccata.
