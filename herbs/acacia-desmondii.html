What is Acacia desmondii?

Acacia desmondii is a species of tree in the family Fabaceae.

It is an evergreen tree that grows up to 10 meters tall, with a broad, rounded crown and a straight, unbuttressed stem. The bark is smooth, grey and becomes fissured with age. Its leaves are pinnate, with 4-8 pairs of linear leaflets that are 1-3 cm long and glabrous above and below.

The tree produces yellow flowers in axillary racemes, which are 2-4 cm long and have a sweet, fragrant scent.
