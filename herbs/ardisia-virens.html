What's the taxonomical classification of Ardisia virens?

Ardisia virens belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it falls under the order Ericales. The plant is further categorized into the family Primulaceae, within which it is placed in the genus Ardisia, eventually reaching its specific designation as the species virens.
