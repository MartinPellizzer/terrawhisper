Panzerina lanata var. alaschanica

What's the taxonomical classification of Panzerina lanata var. alaschanica?

Panzerina lanaata var. alaschanica belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. As a member of the class Equisetopsida, it falls under the order Lamiales and is a representative of the family Lamiaceae. At the most specific taxonomic levels, it is categorized under the genus Panzerina, specifically identified as the variety alaschanica of the species Panzerina lanaata.
