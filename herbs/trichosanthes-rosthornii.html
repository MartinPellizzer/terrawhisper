Trichosanthes rosthornii belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified within the phylum Streptophyta. The plant is further categorized under the order Cucurbitales and the family Cucurbitaceae. Finally, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement in the genus Trichosanthes, with its unique designation as the species rosthornii.
