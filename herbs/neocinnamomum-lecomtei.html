What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Neocinnamomum lecomtei has woody, evergreen leaves that are lanceolate to elliptical in shape and possess entire margins with distinctively revolute edges. Small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in axillary clusters along the stems. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that serve as a key diagnostic feature for the species.
