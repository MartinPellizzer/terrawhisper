Neocinnamomum lecomtei

What's the taxonomical classification of Neocinnamomum lecomtei?

Neocinnamomum lecomtei belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is situated in the order Laurales. This species is a member of the family Lauraceae, falling under the genus Neocinnamomum, and is specifically identified by the species name lecomtei.
