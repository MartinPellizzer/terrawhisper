Grammitis tahitensis

Grammitis tahitensis, also known as Tahitian fern or Tahiti maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the islands of Tahiti and Moorea in French Polynesia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health issues due to its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties.

The health benefits associated with Grammitis tahitensis include reducing inflammation, improving respiratory health, and providing antioxidant protection.
