What's the taxonomical classification of Callicarpa peichieniana?

Callicarpa peichienianna belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae, it is categorized under the order Lamiales and the family Lamiaceae. This specific plant is further identified by its genus, Callicarpa, and its unique species designation, peichienianna.
