What's the taxonomical classification of Oenothera rosea?

Oenothera rosea belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is categorized under the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Moving down the taxonomic hierarchy, it falls under the order Myrtoales and is a member of the family Onagraceae. Finally, the plant is identified by the genus Oenothera and the specific epithet rosea, forming its unique scientific name.
