What's the taxonomical classification of Vanda concolor?

Vanda concolor belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. Further taxonomic refinement places the plant in the order Asparagales and the family Orchidaceae. Finally, it is categorized under the genus Vanda, with its specific designation being the species concolor.

What are the morphological characteristics of this plant?

Its roots are aerial and covered in a specialized velamen layer, which allows the plant to absorb moisture and nutrients directly from the air.
