Spatholobus uniauritus

What's the taxonomical classification of Spatholobus uniauritus?

Spatholobus uniauritus belongs to the kingdom Plantae and is classified under the phylum Streptophyta. Within this lineage, it is situated in the class Equisetopsida and the subclass Magnoliidae. The plant follows the order Fabales and is a member of the family Fabaceae. Ultimately, its specific taxonomic identity is defined by its placement within the genus Spatholobus, with the unique species designation of uniauritus.
